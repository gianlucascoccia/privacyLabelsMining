Description

Realistic Slime Simulator with MANY cool slimes such as: Unicorn Slime, Rainbow Slime, Glitter Slime, Candy Slime, Chocolate Slime and much much more!

From the creators of Unicorn Slime and Unicorn Chef, we bring you our newest game: Unicorn Slime Simulator.
This version is a realisitic simulation game.

You can stretch it, squish it, knead it, pop it, swirl it and poke it. Parents won't buy you a slime? No worries, this Unicorn Slime Simulator feels exactly like the real thing!

Features:
- Make slime in the mobile device anywhere, anytime. Just like playing the real slime!
- Make your own slime: Over 20 slime types, you can choose, such as putty, milky, glitter, jam, metallic. Tons of decorations especially with unicorn decorations for girls.
- Save every slime you've ever made and re-play them!
- The best anti stress: Unicorn + Slime = perfect anti stress game. Do you have a stress at school? No worries ASMR + Unicorn + Slime will help you RELAX!

How to play:
- Make the slime step by step by following the instructions.
- Combine different colors, decorations, and texture types to make the slime that you like.
- We also have pre-made slime for you to play directly. Just press and play!

Pretty good...

So, your game is pretty great. I do have a few complaints, and suggestions. :)
1. Most of the slimes you can’t poke, and when you move it around on your screen, it doesn’t really move smoothly. It kind of glitches under the other part of the slime.
2. The fun little decorations don’t really blend into the slime part, it seems like they just sit above the slime and don’t move until you swipe your finger through the slime and across the decoration.
3. Lots and lots of the colors, textures, tops and decorations are premium or ads. Some people don’t want to watch ads for everything, or have to pay for a texture or color.
4. There aren’t many different textures to choose from, and almost all of them are premium or ads. Please add a few more textures, that are free!
5. When you try to name your new slime, it has a 4-6 character limit. Please fix this, because I really want to name my slime without an unreasonable character limit.
