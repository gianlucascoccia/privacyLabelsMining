Description

A quick start to your music production – tap to generate a base, customize, master and export as a midi, audio or video. Share to finish the track in your favourite DAW.

Make new music with the help of an AI-powered music generator. Your next song is just a few taps away.

"I've gone from doing 3 songs a week to 3 songs a day with this app"

Songen is a one-of-a-kind mobile app for iOS that generates unique songs including chords, lead melodies, bass and drums. Perfect addition to a music producer's toolkit. Every generated song is your own, royalty-free and can be used instantly.



WHO IS THIS APP FOR?
Music Producers: new inspiration and help in creative block. An easy way to discover demo songs for artists.
Artists: Ideas for new songs and backing tracks for vocals. Full songs without having to know the secrets of music production
Non-musicians: Most easy entry to making music and learning how to make songs

SELECT A GENRE OR MULTIPLE GENRES
Songen has multiple up-to-date music genres that you can you use. Songen's algorithm has been trained to know the characteristics for many of today's most popular music styles. You can combine multiple genres or let Songen create new combinations for you.



GENERATE UNIQUE SONGS
With a just a tap of the Generate button Songen creates totally unique songs. Just a few more clicks and you have a whole song that is completely owned by you.



SAVE YOUR SONGS
Once you find a suitable song for your production you can save it. Your saved songs are stored and ready for listening when ever you want. After saving you can fine tune the the song and make it sound just the way you want.



EDIT & MIX
Edit your song's parameters such as transposing the octave of the instruments and slowing or speeding up individual tracks. Choose instruments to your tracks from a collection of premium-quality sounds.



SHARE YOUR SONG
Once you are ready with your song you can export it as a midi file and keep working on it in your DAW. You can also export audio files and share them on social media or to your artist, producer or a friend instantly.



IT'S YOURS!
In the end the song you have generated and mixed is totally royalty-free and you own it!



PREMIUM
With the Songen Premium you get all the functionalities and unlimited amount of new music at your disposal. Found a nice key or tempo? Lock a number of musical aspects to generate just the kind of music you want. Fine tune generated songs before saving.



HOW DOES IT WORK?
Under the hood there's a sophisticated algorithm that is trained to generate various music genres. As a low-level stochastic machine it doesn't involve any premade midi files and everything is generated on the fly.



HOW TO UNSUBSCRIBE?
1. Open the Settings app.
2. Tap your name.
3. Tap Subscriptions.
4. Tap Songen.
5. Tap Cancel Subscription. If you don’t see Cancel, the subscription is already canceled and won't renew.

I keep hoping they will make a “one time” purchase!

At first I gave this app 1 star, because of my dislike for subscription based apps!
But I find myself coming back to this app OVER and over! Normally by now, I would have paid for the app, (because in all fairness this app is pretty legit!
It’s more than a toy, it really generates some pretty ill music.
Always looking for inspiration, I find myself using my iPad to lay out quick demos.
I am not a fan of electronic made music, I love that nasty sound of a mic in front of a tube amp, and real drums with overhead cymbals, real bass and cello along with a grand piano.
Even though the song generated by Songen feel more hip-hop/trap and pop, the melodies and complex drums can spark my amazing ideas!

However I am stuck using the free version (which is still pretty dope) please developers, consider a one time fee, with maybe IAP for more content packs, or more instruments.

There is some pretty amazing algorithms at work here!
