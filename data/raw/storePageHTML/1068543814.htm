Description

Don’t spend another Sunday in the kitchen meal prepping when you could be enjoying your life (or your couch, no judgments).

FitGenie lets you get healthy, local, chef-prepped meals delivered whenever you want them, then makes it oh-so-easy to make sure those meals help you reach your goals with FREE macro tracking & personalized nutrition plans.

We’re committed to giving our customers easy access to the best local meal delivery services and data-backed, customizable nutrition plans to make sure those meals work for you.


Do you serve my area?

We’re an Atlanta-based company and currently serving our neighbors and working with the best healthy meal delivery services in our area. But keep an eye out because we’ll be expanding our options.


Do I have to subscribe to anything?

Nope, you can get meals delivered whenever you want them. No subscription required.


Do I have to track my food?

You don’t have to use the food log to get meals delivered (but all the research seems to tell us that tracking food intake is one of the most effective ways to lose weight/gain muscle/change your body if that’s what you’re into).


What happens when I reach my goal?

You’ll be prompted to check-in weekly and our AI dietician will help you adjust your food plan to keep you on track.


Ready to reclaim your weekends from meal prep?

Download the app.

Glad I switched

I used MyFitnessPal for like 5 years religiously and I don’t really have any complaints about it. I switched to FitGenie (continuing to update both) assuming I would hate it and switch back. I stopped updating MyFitnessPal after a month of using FitGenie. It’s easy to use and my favorite feature is setting your goal and getting the weekly update with change suggestions. This is really helpful for me because I don’t have a coach or really know when to do what. So far, the weekly updates have been putting me towards my goals exactly. I also like that they get feedback and constantly make changes and add new features. I’m looking forward to more additions in the future! Thank you!
