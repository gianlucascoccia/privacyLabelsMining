Description

Duckr is the Ultimate Duck Hunting App for the serious Duck Hunter.

- Full 10 day weather forecast with Wind, Precipitation, and Snow cover
- Save, comment, and share each of your hunting spots securely
- Add notes on number of Ducks on the water when scouting
- Upload pictures and leave breadcrumbs to find the blind in the dark
- Share location, pictures, and chat with hunting buddies.
- Show wind speed, direction, distance, for today, as well as the next 10 days
- Show sun trajectory for the day
- View advanced statistics built from your personal harvest and nationwide scouting history
- Real-time tide data from local tide stations
- View duck species with descriptions, pictures, and duck calls
- Hunter SOS for contacting your own Emergency Contact
- Hunting License are now stored and can be purchased from the App

Duckr functionality is provided on a low-cost subscription basis:
- Either purchase a 1 month subscription, or purchase an entire year at a nice discount!
- Use the same subscription on *any* of your iOS devices that use the same iTunes account
- There are two tiers of subscriptions available
* Basic - allows access to all of the Weather, Mapping Features, Point Services, Statistics, Chat, Groups, Flyway Reports, Duck Species, Season Schedules and Limits by State, Guide Services, and Application Support
* Professional - All of the Basic features, as well as Sun Direction, Wind Conditions, as well as the ability to share points with other Duckr users
- Basic is either $0.99/month or $9.99/year (A nearly 20% discount!)
- Professional is either $1.99/month or $14.99/year (A nearly 40% discount!)
- Payment will be charged to iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase
- Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period
- Account will be charged for renewal within 24-hours prior to the end of the current period, and identify the cost of the renewal
- Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user's Account Settings after purchase
- No cancellation of the current subscription is allowed during active subscription period
- Any unused portion of a free trial period, if offered, will be forfeited when the user purchases a subscription to that publication

*Continued use of GPS running in the background can dramatically decrease battery life.
