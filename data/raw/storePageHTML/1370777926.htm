Description

Scoreholio makes running tournaments more interactive and fun!

Players, you’ll check yourself into events with a quick scan, and stay on top of the action to “you’re up!” alerts and a real-time dashboard. Scoreholio also includes a scoreboard you can use to keep score when you’re playing at home and on lockdown.

Tournament organizers, hold on to your hats -- Scoreholio virtually automates the process of running cornhole and pong tournaments so you can relax, play in your own events with less stress, or use newfound free time to engage players, run side games or sell swag.

Legal stuff: If you decide to purchase a subscription to unlock advanced features, it will be charged to your credit card through your iTunes account. Your subscription will automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage your subscriptions in Account Settings after purchase.

By using Scoreholio, you agree to our Terms of Use: https://scoreholio.com/tou


FEATURES
• Formats: Round robin, single elimination bracket, double elimination bracket
• Playoffs: Run round robin then advance any number of teams to single or double elimination playoff
• Checkin: Quickly register players by scanning their QR code or adding them manually
• Scoreboards: Colorful interactive scoreboards at each court, on any tablet or phone
• Dashboard: Real-time scores, standings and brackets displayed at event and viewable via app or URL
• Real-Time Alerts: App notifies players when they're up, tells them which court they're on
• Flexibility: Easily set # of courts and rounds, time limit, etc., even change them during the event

Scoreholio is easier to use and offers players and tournament directors a like a much more interactive and real-time experience than old-fashioned solutions like Brackelope or Challonge.

Organizers: Join the "Scoreholio Organizers & Players" group on Facebook for help and tips from our growing community of users!

Best. App. Ever.

It’s only a matter of time before the ACL and ACO start using this App to run their tournaments.
This App is for people just playing pickup games AND for people who organize and run tournaments alike.
For people who organize tournaments, Scoreholio makes it SUPER easy! And having the ability to wirelessly have all of the LIVE scores and brackets up on a TV for everyone to see, adds the ‘cool’ factor! All you need to do is scan in the players and then sit back and let Scoreholio do all of the work for you! As an organizer, Scoreholio allows me to actually play in my own tournaments again! Whether in the backyard with friends, running a weekly league night or organizing a BIG fundraiser tournament, Scoreholio does it all!
For players, Scoreholio not only gives you an easy way to keep the score on any ‘pickup’ game, but it also now gives you SPR+ (Scoreholio Player Rating). This ranks all players in your area and the world, taking into account each player’s win/loss record, skill level and the point spread in how much you won or lost by. It then allows you to see every game you ever played and even lookup other player’s SPR from around the world!
And the Developers are amazing! Responsive within minutes and actively seeking feedback.
Definitely one of my top-used Apps!
