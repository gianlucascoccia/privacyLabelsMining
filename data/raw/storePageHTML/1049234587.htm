Description

BRAND NEW FOR INTERNATIONAL WOMEN’S DAY: Feel strong and empowered with our International Women’s Day workout collection, available in your On Demand dashboard now!

Work out at home with the largest fitness community of women worldwide, now with Pilates, barre and yoga! Get the fitness motivation you need with Sweat, the personal training app featuring Kayla Itsines and elite female personal trainers.

FREE trial for new members!

Join millions of women to live your best life through health and fitness, with workouts, nutrition guides and community support.

Programs include:

Kayla Itsines
-BBG, suitable for at-home workouts, includes high-intensity plyometric training, plus 8 foundation weeks of lower intensity, low-impact workouts
-BBG Zero Equipment, a no-equipment program using bodyweight exercises you can do anywhere, anytime, with 2 weekly express workouts
-BBG Stronger, combining bodyweight exercises and gym equipment into 28-minute high-intensity workouts
-Post-Pregnancy program that supports mothers to rebuild their strength and fitness

Kelsey Wells
-PWR at Home & PWR weight-training programs, including home-based and gym-based resistance workouts
-Post-Pregnancy program using resistance workouts to increase core strength and improve posture for mothers

Stephanie Sanzo
-LIFTING at Home program, ideal for women who want to weight train at home
-BUILD powerbuilding program, perfect for women wanting to improve their lifting performance

Chontel Duncan
-FIERCE at Home & FIERCE strength and high-intensity workouts are ideal for women who want to burn fat and increase fitness

Cass Olholm
-High Intensity Strength program builds lean muscle, boosts your metabolism and increases cardio fitness with high-energy workouts

Sara Colquhoun
-Pilates made for you at home, with challenging workouts to improve technique, increase mobility and bring the burn

Britany Williams
-A barre program inspired by elements of ballet, designed to define and strengthen your body with low-impact exercises

Phyllicia Bonanno
-Vinyasa yoga program to build flexibility and stability, and release stress and tension

Ania Tippkemper
-Yoga program with Vinyasa dynamic flows and postures, using your breath to move you seamlessly through the practice

Sjana Earp
-Yoga program with Vinyasa-style resistance and yin-based recovery flows to improve flexibility and posture

Available in 8 languages: English, German, French, Italian, Spanish, Portuguese, Dutch and Chinese (simplified)

Fitness expert Kayla Itsines wants you to reach all your health and exercise goals—and her amazing app has just what you need to get there. It’s packed with everything that’s made her Bikini Body Guides an international sensation, including short 28-minute workouts (minimal equipment needed), customizable meal plans, and global fitness challenges. pemager , 01/21/2019

Love this app!!

I absolutely love this stupid app. It’s got me excited to go to the gym and setting up a schedule and I’ve never had that before. I don’t use the meal planner because I’m a poor college student, so I don’t do much grocery shopping, but the option is there and I really like the meals it offers.

There are a few things I would change, but the pros so outweigh the cons that they’re easy to pass over when deciding between 4 or 5 stars. One, I wish that it gave you the option to change the default warm up setting. Sometimes I forget to change it and it jumps right into the workout and I don’t want that. Also, as someone else said, the mile tracker is wayyy off, but I’m not super concerned with miles right now, I’m just trying to build a cardio base, and I could always just use another app. Lastly, all the tapping is annoying at times, but so sometimes I just don’t do it. I just remember what exercise is coming next and I do it. For the supersets I just use the clock at the top to count my 30 seconds.

Overall, such a good app. I can’t keep the subscription going because, like I said, poor college student and I’m trying to afford a trip to Japan this summer, but holy cow I’m still debating paying for it. I really do love this app. The workout counter keeps me motivated and on track and the workouts are so much fun. I’m really seeing changes and I’m really happy. Thanks Sweat Team!
