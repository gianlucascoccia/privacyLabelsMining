Description

The SingaporeAir app offers you greater travel convenience from booking to boarding and beyond.

From the user experience to the personalised features, everything has been designed to be fast, intuitive and delightful to use.

More features will be progressively added in future updates of the SingaporeAir app, but here are some of the key functions you can enjoy now:

1. EXPLORE, BE INSPIRED AND GET THE LATEST DEALS ON THE GO
Get inspired to travel with our new city guides to the destinations we fly to. Browse fare deals to your favourite destinations, and share them with friends and family. Planning your next getaway has never been easier.

2. SEARCH, BOOK AND MANAGE YOUR FLIGHTS
With a faster and more full-featured interface, it’s much easier to book your next adventure or view details on your upcoming and past trips. You can now also use your miles, AliPay, or PayPal to reserve your flight, preferred seats, insurance and much more. After a few taps and swipes, you’ll be ready to sit back and relax.

3. SKIP THE CHECK-IN QUEUES
Breeze through the airport by checking in on your mobile for yourself and anyone else on the same booking. And to make it even simpler, you can download your mobile boarding pass to Apple Wallet and view it anytime.

4. MANAGE YOUR KRISFLYER ACCOUNT
Keep your important information close to you. Just log in once, and you’ll be able to track your KrisFlyer miles balance, transactions and PPS Value easily. PPS Club members can also connect to customer service representatives via PPS Connect*.

5. EXPERIENCE THE FUTURE OF FLYING
Find out what's playing on our award-winning KrisWorld inflight entertainment system, and create your personal play list on your iPhone or iPad. Lean back and launch your favourite movies, directly from your own phone/tablet, or view your flight’s path and progress**.

*This service is currently only applicable to registered PPS Club members with valid Singapore mobile numbers.
**This feature is available on A350 and selected Boeing 777-300ER aircraft.

Please also note that by downloading the SingaporeAir app, you agree to the terms and conditions, including the privacy policy, which can be found at http://www.singaporeair.com/en_UK/terms-conditions/ and http://www.singaporeair.com/en_UK/privacy-policy/
