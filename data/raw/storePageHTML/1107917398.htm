Description

Listen to audiobooks in our new, more convenient mobile application. Log in to your account and try it now!

Audioteka is a free app, where you can download or listen to audiobooks directly from your device. We offer you a possibility to listen free chapters, buy, download and listen to bestsellers wherever you are. The app allows you to find thousands of audiobooks on Fiction, Non-fiction, Arts & Entertainment, Biography and Autobiography, Business, Children, Classics, Crime & Thrillers, Science - Fiction & Fantasy, Health & Personal Development, Young Adult, Podcasts & Newspapers and many more.

The Audioteka application is in short:

- the ability to create a list of favorite items

- integration with Apple CarPlay
- Google Chromecast support
- access to your shelf
- the ability to easily recommend and share content with friends
- offline listening to previously downloaded content
- snooze function (auto-turn off the audiobook)
- option to set the speed of listening to the audiobook (from 0.5 up to 2x faster)
- possibility to rate audiobooks
- the ability to listen to podcasts and broadcasts
- constant help and customer service

More features coming soon!

If your shelf is empty, because you do not have an account at Audioteka yet, listen to the free broadcasts and podcasts that we have included in the new version of the application.
