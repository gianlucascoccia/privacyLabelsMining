Description

Our latest update lets you know exactly where to exit the Interstate to find the cheapest gas prices. Works for unleaded, mid grade, premium, and diesel fuel.

The iExit Interstate Exit Guide is a must-have on your next road trip. It figures out where you are on the Interstate, and shows you what's ahead.

Need gas? iExit knows all the gas prices in the United States, and can tell you which station is cheapest at each exit. It also makes it easy to compare prices by the exits ahead, letting you know exactly where to exit to save the most. It works for Unleaded, Mid Grade, Premium, and Diesel.

Need food? iExit has you covered, no matter if you're looking for the usual fast food or something local. It also integrates Yelp ratings and reviews to help you decide.

Need a place to stay for the night? iExit lists all hotels within 3 miles of an exit ramp. But if you're looking for a deal, we have thousands of same-day discounted rates that are cheaper than you'll find anywhere else.

Looking for a specific chain, like Cracker Barrel? Or maybe a specific amenity, like WiFi? Or maybe a rest area? Just search for it and we'll show you the exits ahead with a match.

Don't take another road trip without iExit!

** Important Notes **
● Your device must have GPS hardware or a connection to the internet for iExit to display results based on your location.
● iExit is compatible with iOS 11.0 and later.
● iExit only works in the United States on limited access highways, most of which are official Interstate highways.

What’s New

Aug 6, 2020

Version 10.0.93

Fixed a bug where the app crashes when searching for nearby highways.

-------------------------------

Welcome to iExit version 10! This update brings a completely redesigned and updated iExit, and we think you're going to love it.

- First and foremost, GAS PRICES. If you're needing to exit the Interstate for gas, iExit now tells you the cheapest station at each exit. It also lets you compare exits by gas prices, so that you can be sure you exit where prices are the cheapest. No other app out there lists gas prices by Interstate exit, so be sure to use iExit on your next trip and save at the tank.

- We have redesigned the home page to let you 1) change location easily (if you want to search somewhere besides your current location), 2) select a nearby highway and see upcoming exits, even if not on the Interstate, and 3) search for places around you. If you search for gas stations nearby, you can easily find the cheapest gas station within 5 miles of you.

- When you are viewing the exits ahead of you, we have made it easy to search for the major categories: Gas, Food, and Hotels. We also make it easy to perform a custom search, as has always been the case.

- We have updated some parts of iExit to list businesses horizontally instead of vertically. This will help cut down on endless scrolling at exits that contain many businesses.

- We've added Dark Mode to make it easier on the eyes when using iExit for night driving.

- We've also introduced a banner ad at the bottom of the app to help pay the bills. If you would like to remove the banner ad, you can pay a one-time, in-app purchase of $1.99.

Please let us know how you like the new update. Feel free to leave a review, but if you have questions or feedback, please get in touch at info@iexitapp.com.

Thanks and safe travels!
-The iExit Team
