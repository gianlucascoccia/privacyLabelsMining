Description

$0 Account Minimums
Begin with as little or as much as you want. The earlier you start, the more your money grows.

Your Money on Autopilot
Enjoy automated deposits, dividend reinvesting, and portfolio rebalancing. Without having to do a thing.

Get Real Human Advice
We're loved for our technology. But we also have smart financial advisors ready to help you. You can schedule a portfolio review, ask an account question, and get advice when you need it. And our money experts have a fiduciary responsibility to give advice that's in your best interest.

Invest Your Spare Change
Our Roundup feature turns your everyday purchases into smart investments. When you link your credit and debit charges, we'll round up to the nearest dollar and put your spare change to work. So your $3.30 cappuccino becomes an automatic $0.70 investment. Like magic!

Socially Responsible Investing
By investing in socially responsible initiatives across the globe, you can have a well-diversified portfolio that helps build the world you want.

High-Interest Savings
With returns 10x higher than the national average, Wealthsimple Save is the perfect place to keep money for emergency expenses, a down payment, or your next vacation. There are no account minimums, no low balance fees and unlimited free transfers and withdrawals.

Low Fees = More Money
Low-fee ETFs and smart technology to keep costs low, and we tell you our fees up front: 0.5% for accounts under $100K; 0.4% for accounts over $100K. We never charge trading, account transfer, or rebalancing fees. And we'll never hide the fees you pay.

We Keep Your Money Safe
Over 175,000 clients trust us with their assets. We use state-of-the-art security and encryption, and accounts are SIPC protected. Backed by some of the biggest names in finance and tech, we're growing quickly and globally.

Get Smart About Money
Our award-winning magazine features human stories to entertain and ideas to help you succeed. While watching our Investing Master Class will make you a personal finance expert in less than 45 minutes.

Smart Tech, Simple Design
Wealthsimple is the two-time Webby Award winner for the world's best financial website because our platform is simple, intelligent and user-friendly.
