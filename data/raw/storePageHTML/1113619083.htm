Description

Run far & fast across beautiful lands with the 10 Horse Guardians to save the Everbloom Forest!

Budge Studios™ presents: EverRun™! Assemble the ten legendary Horse Guardians of Everbloom and join them on the most magical adventure to save their forest! Brush, feed, take care of your horses and decorate each of their stables with divine decorations! Run far and fast across magnificent lands to recover and return to the Everbloom Tree all of its lost magical petals!

AN EPIC ENDLESS RUNNER ADVENTURE
• RUN with 10 Horse Guardians, each with their own mystical personality!
• EXPLORE the world’s breathtaking landscapes, from radiant rainforests to dusty deserts!
• GATHER all lost petals to bring the Everbloom Tree back to life
• USE the Guardians’ super powers to dash past obstacles, blast through them or even take flight!
• COLLECT Embers to awaken the full power of your Horse Guardians
• DRESS UP your Horse Guardians from head to hoof with mythical armor sets!
• GROOM your Horse Guardians with magical brushes and feed them to replenish their energy!
• ENHANCE your runs with epic trails made of furious flames, sweet snowflakes and more!
• DECORATE your stable with divine decorations for glorious rooms worthy of the horse heroes
• RACE through all of nature’s challenges, blizzards or sandstorms, day or night!
• ENDLESS RUNNER MODE: Gallop without limit, gather as many petals as you can and set your own record!

Total Scam

This game is really fun. It’s great. But I feel totally scammed out of my money. Several months ago, before the update that changed this from a flat fee game to a pay as you go game, I paid $7 to unlock all the horses for my daughter. Not long after that they updated the game and added a bunch of horses and now you have to earn “embers” (the game currency) and spend them on various things. This would be ok in and of itself but it was just after I spent the money on upgrading to unlock everything so that money I spent was a total waste.

In addition to that, my daughter has now played enough to unlock many new horses again with her earned embers. But she keeps finding horses she unlocked to be locked again and she has to pay again to unlock them. They’ve also now implemented a feature where you have to feed the horses to keep them alive. Perhaps this is what’s locking them again? And she has to spend more and more embers to keep and earn horses.

You can no longer play this game for free or even for a reasonable amount of money. Plus what you spend disappears. It’s incredibly frustrating and feels like a total money grab now. What game takes away things you’ve already paid for??

If the restore purchase button doesn’t solve this, please contact our support team and we will sort it out for you immediately. We are also releasing an update in a few days that will fix any other bugs you are encountering. Thanks for bearing with us while we sort this out for you!

GodGirl77 , 01/16/2018

I love this game.

I am a 26 year old Mom of three girls. My oldest is four, and I downloaded this game for her on an older model phone so we could play it together. She loves games. And I’m not really much of a gamer, but taking turns with her has given me a little big of a game bug. I downloaded it on my phone and play on my own now because I like the way the game is created. It’s an awesome game! You should download it if you like temple run or pitfall, but always wished you had the ability to level up in the game. It’s easy going in the beginning of the game, but you can collect power ups or petals to move onto the next level while avoiding obstacles that get in your way. You get to power up your horse by feeding it, brushing it, and buying armor. Each time you beat a level, a flower blooms on the tree (game-board). You can purchase horses too for different levels. It does get more complicated as the game progresses. It’s very fun though.
