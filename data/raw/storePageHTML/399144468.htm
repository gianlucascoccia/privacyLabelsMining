The best of both worlds

I have been a subscriber to the print version for a few years and love it. Then when I got an iPad I saw in the magazine that I can get the iPad version for free, so I decided to give it a try...I mean why not, right? I'm certainly glad I did! The iPad version is simple to navigate has really cool extras like videos and high resolution images as well as occasionally some articles you you don't find in the print version. When I receive the print magazine the digital version is not available yet, not too surprising, so I read it and when finished download the electronic version and see what more they have included. I love it as I am not one who enjoys reading long articles/papers on a screen. Once you have read an article and follow it up with the movie, you're hooked... Hearing the tire squealing and the engine whaling you get the sense of what the author was saying in the article. The Lamborghini Aventador piece is a perfect example. And if you have issues tell the publisher so they can fix it... No one correct what they do not know about.
