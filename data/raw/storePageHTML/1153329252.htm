Description

March your way through the evil lands, crush the gigantic bosses and free the princess in the epic idle role-playing game, Crush Them All.

Tap with one finger, collect and upgrade hundreds of heroes, find powerful artifacts and progress in this never-ending journey even while taking a break!


Features:
● COLLECT and UPGRADE heroes to crush stronger enemies along your journey
● LAUNCH visually impressive skills
● EXPLORE 1000+ stages
● ASCEND to new worlds to earn prisms and make your army stronger.
● CRAFT 50+ powerful artifacts to give your heroes more powers
● PROGRESS while being away, earning gold and battling enemies.


PLEASE NOTE! Crush Them All is free to download and play, however, some game items can also be purchased for real money. If you don't want to use this feature, please disable in-app purchases in your device's settings. Also, under our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, you must be at least 13 years of age to play or download Crush Them All.


A network connection is also required.

Best idle play to date

The game itself is set on a good pace with easy to follow and simple upgrade system,so no more complicated and long drawn out grinding to move up in the game,the characters are awesome with the ability to change appearance as they level weapons which is rare to see in games like this,the daily rewards are set with a day to week schedule as the rewards get better each week which helps keep you coming back for more,graphics are really good and love the art of the background,the guilds are amazing and a fast track for grinding the needed points to level your characters and so far i havent been in a guild that isn’t active and helpful to new players,which is rare to see on some games,and speaking of rare to see the in game purchases are set up nice and neat where the developers aren’t greedy,you get what you need (if you actually need to buy packs)at a decent price where you don’t over pay for a few resources,a few bucks goes a long way with out breaking the bank,I’ve been playing for a few months now and haven’t had to purchase a whole lot really unless i just wanted to,and if you do it rolls to a VIP system set to bring you in more rewards which is another plus as well,all in all the developers did one amazing job across the graphics,system,and pvp/guild of this game and i recommend it to all my coworkers and friends

Wjucdnsbjs , 08/02/2018

Good fun

I find this game very fun, I have been playing for about 45 days now and I’m almost at stage 2000 and I find that I’m rewarded for everything I do. I love how much flooz is given to me for doing things like tapping the gold chests or beating certain stages and I love how so far no hero’s are locked behind a pay wall (as far as I know), and I love the whole vip status it’s an awesome idea that you guys benefit from me watching ads and playing your game and you in turn reward me for doing that. And not a whole lot seems broken or underpowered. Sometimes though the hero progression can be a little slow like I wish it was easier to level up hero’s that I wanted to instead of requesting only common hero’s to my guild, or maybe more than 3 hero’s in the 24 hour rotation. Another thing is I wish the weapon system was explained better because I didn’t use it for like a week because I was intimidated and didn’t know how to use it, and the weapon catalyst are super expensive at I think 250 blitz coins for just one. But other than that I see myself playing this game for a while, thanks for a fun game!
Edit: also I don’t like how I feel like I hit a brick wall in progression because I reached the last village that produces gold, it takes to long to produce and it gives too little gold

Ev3yx , 12/29/2019

Great and truly free game

I have been playing Crush’em all for over a year now. The biggest pros to this game is that you can actually earn everything for free besides hero skins. With dedication and time, you can get everything you want in the game. I love this game cause I am a busy person that enjoys a game you can get off of right away. A lot of games in the App Store aren’t truly “afk” but Crush’em all is very close to that. I’m not usually a gamer that sticks with games long but this game is so easy to put down whenever. You don’t feel like you missed out on a ton of rewards cause you weren’t on the game for hours a day. This game is great for busy college kids, working parents, and honestly everyone who wants to have a social life and more. Love this game and I hope it never stops being amazing! If anyone is curious about cons, the only one I can think of is the in game currency, Flooz is very expensive. But honestly that isn’t even that bad either cause you can watch ads to earn free flooz. It just takes a lot of dedication and over time you will earn plenty of flooz to buy in game content with. If you haven’t tried this game yet, try it! If you want a social life and are busy in real life, this is the game for you!
