Description

Experience a world where you can collect & trade your favorite Star Wars characters, weapons, spacecraft, memorable moments, and more with Star Wars fans across the galaxy! Introducing Star Wars™: Card Trader by Topps.

Fun app but not perfect!

I’ve had this app for a LONG time now and I feel I can give a very accurate review on what makes this collector good, and what it’s lacking.
For starters this is a very fun game for any people who like to collect things; contrary to what some reviewers would say you can get good stuff in this in particular one you can get some pretty good stuff without EVER spending a cent. However the rarities of cards very from common-legendary. It is extremely rare, and I mean EXTREMELY rare to get any legendary without at least spending a dollar or two. Even on the very very rare occasion where they even release a set where you can get legendary for the normal credits, the pull rate is practically impossible. What I would really like to see is the realistic possibility of pulling a legendary with credits. Fixing that would definitely make this app a 5⭐️ I also have the walking dead collector and the marvel collector and those are definitely worse than this but I’m not reviewing those. If you’re a die hard Star Wars fan like me, who also collects cards and other things like a borderline hoarder than this is the app for you. To paraphrase this whole review for people who just scroll all the way to the bottom for final judgement

Paraphrase: great app but could be perfect if you didn’t HAVE to spend money to get the absolute best stuff, but in a sense it makes sense because not only is it an app but also a card collector

- good day all

The boss king awsome , 04/04/2020

Game is ruined for me

I wrote a review for this app a long time ago and this is how it went. I have been playing this game for almost a year now, and am still addicted. New cards are constantly being added so it never gets old. The community is great, active, and helpful. The game is incredible with the diversity between cards that I have collected 3,500 of. And that's only 6% of the cards I the game. THIS IS NO LONGER HOW I FEEL.

Before the updates there were no card packs that required paid in game crystals. I believe this was a horrible addition to the game and ruined it for me. It makes those with money to spend get the cards much quicker before they’re sold out. The new UI in the game is often buggy and unintuitive I greatly prefer the old more simple layout. The community is still great, all great people, but it has diminished greatly. The in game can feed used to always been roaring with comments. It’s no where near where it used to be now. Trading is not the same nor are old features like the smugglers den.

This game used to be great, now it is a pay to win game where the developers do not respond to criticism by the community on their socials, and utterly have been working in the wrong direction for years now.

effyouandyournicknames , 10/31/2020

Odds/content

Not a fan. Ever since the new update a year or so ago, the odds of winning previously in-won content has decreased, and I’m tired of “winning” the same cards that i have at least 45 copies of. At least recently, with the free giveaways, you have a chance of winning a newer card that may not be in your collection. So, that’s good. BUT it should be the same for the “free packs” you win by meeting the challenges (ie: opening 10 packs, etc). Also, you need to work on your odds system. If it says there is a 1 in 5 chance, it shouldn’t be 15 packs later when I am finally winning a “basic” card. And this happens A LOT. It makes me not want to play on this app. Also, there is no clear info on what, exactly, the “lock” system is. Why do i need to lock cards? Do my rare cards I’ve collected for years now have a chance of disappearing? Why is there no better info on this?? Idk. When this app first started out, you won more money as a daily prize. Which was good. And you had different “free” cards you could win. Now, you don’t win a lot of money, and the free cards are the same old-same old. It just gets realllllly boring.
