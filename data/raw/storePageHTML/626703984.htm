Description

Cafeyn is the perfect space to go beyond the headlines and discover full articles and publications. With easy access to more than 1600 magazines and newspapers - and the most comfortable reading experience - users can feel free to be curious.

A LARGE CATALOGUE ALL IN ONE PLACE

From now, enjoy unlimited access to a large choice of magazines all in one place for £7,99 per month. First month £0,99. Cancel anytime.

WHEREVER, WHENEVER YOU WANT

Read your favourite magazines on all devices. You can now browse your favourite magazines on your smartphone, tablet or computer whenever and wherever you want.

DON'T NEED A CONNECTION TO BE CONNECTED!

In the subway, on the plane, in a tunnel or on Mars, enjoy your readings, even offline.

THE BEST READING EXPERIENCE

Enjoy our Smart Reading function, which enables you to scroll through articles without zooming. For the most comfortable reading experience, you can now control the font size and change to night mode.

CREATE YOUR OWN EXPERIENCE

You can now save your favourite articles to read whenever suits you. You can bookmark your favourite magazines so you never miss the latest issue. Use our search bar to find all the articles of a topic you are passionate about.
