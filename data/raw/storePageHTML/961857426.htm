FEATURES

• Intuitive, gesture-based navigation — Getting around in apps can be so confusing. With NeuBible's spine navigation, it's always easy to remember where you are and what you were doing. Plus, it's always within a thumb's reach, and even easy to use with the larger screens of the iPhone 6 & 6 Plus!

• A beautiful, considered reading experience — We've licensed fonts from Hoefler & Co, Linotype, Commercial Type & DSType, and hand-tuned the margins, line spacing and font sizes to give you as considered a reading experience as you'll find anywhere on iOS.

• Lightweight...AND LIGHTNING FAST — From its insanely quick loading speed, to its inventive use of navigation, everything about NeuBible has been optimized and engineered to get you to books and verses, and get reading as quickly as possible.

• Quick Search — Double tap anywhere while reading to quickly access search and easily find books, keywords, and even jump directly to verses.

• Timeline View — Quickly access your highlighted verses, along with any verse(s) you've bookmarked.

• Easy chapter navigation — Swipe left in the book view or while reading to quickly navigate to other chapters.

• Reading mode customization — Customize your reading experience with control of font formatting and day & night reading modes.

• Reading Reminders — No unwanted push notifications here, just the ones you set yourself.

• Gesture-based verse highlighting — Simply tap, hold, and drag your finger down the screen to highlight and copy verses.

• Make NeuBible your own w/ themes — Since the Bible is one of the most personal reading experiences you'll ever have, we wanted to help you make it feel even more personal. Themes are basically like going to the bookstore and picking out that ONE perfect Bible for yourself when you were a kid...

• iCloud Sync — Keep all of your highlights* and timeline backed up and synced across devices seamlessly with iCloud Sync

*We currently only support iCloud sync for highlights at this time. We've put a lot of thought into this and feel that even though it would be easy to do, given the different places and ways most people use their iPhones and iPads, also syncing reading position and bookmark wouldn't provide the best user experience.

Fantastic Vibe

Wow! i’ve noticed that this is my preferred app for iOS, and the newest update is just super lovely... it has this extra polish that’s so hard to find in apps, especially Bible apps. The elegant play of the views is unique, but so wise compared to how most reading apps work. I wonder sometimes about two features: What if there was a way to turn off the “stop at the bottom of the chapter” bounce, because sometimes I simply want to keep reading... especially since chapters aren’t original to the words. And I wonder about some sort of really thoughtful Bible reading plan flow (or perhaps there’s a way for me to download one as a shared Reminder app list or something). It’s fun to have someone else curate a daily reading for me, so I don’t get stuck. I’m fantastically thankful for this app - it makes me not dread opening it, like many of the apps on my phone, and always impresses me with the black levels and smooth, thoughtful screen views and placements of controls.

ConcernedCitizen1234567898765 , 03/24/2018

Good app - Search function could be better

This is a great app and it’s completely worth the money. I give it four stars because the search function is very frustrating. For instance if you barely remember a verse and want to find it you need to know the full exact word in it. For instance, if you are trying to find Mt 19:12 but don’t remember the book/chapter of it but you do remember Jesus mentioning the word eunuch in that verse, the search function won’t return that verse if you search “eunuch.” He says “eunuchs” (going off KJV) so you have to search that to find it. The search feature also deletes your search term after you select a verse to look at. If you realize it’s not the verse you were thinking of, you have to go back and type it again. This makes it really frustrating to do any kind of serious study in the app other than simple reading. With a really simple fix to the search bar, this app could work great as a concordance. I’d pay double for it if it actually did that.

Another note - I saw a typo when reading the ESV in Jeremiah (possibly it was in Job). I forget where exactly but it was obviously wrong as in there was a space missing between two words. I checked my paper ESV and it had it right. This severely undermined my faith in this app - I want to know that what I’m reading is really accurate and not cross check everything that looks strange! I have not seen anything wrong in the KJV however.
