Description

Get in the best shape of your life from the comfort of your own home!

Since 2008, BodyRock has been providing health and fitness coaching that you can follow at home without a gym membership. We launched Bodyrockplus so you can access all of our workouts and videos in one place, with awesome features that help you remove excuses and reach your fitness and health goals.

It's like having a personalized trainer on your device for a fraction of the price! Subscribe to get access to exclusive workout series including HIIT, Tabata, Combat, Yoga, Strength Training. There's something for everyone!

-Already have an account? Sign-In to access your subscription.
-New? Try it for free! Subscribe in the app to get instant access.

Bodyrockplus offers a Monthly Subscription with a Free Trial.
You'll receive unlimited access to content on all your devices. Payment is charged to your account at confirmation of purchase. Pricing varies by location and is confirmed prior to purchase. After the free trial, subscription automatically renews at the monthly rate unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the trial period. Subscription automatically renews each month unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. Manage your subscription in Account Settings.

Effective workouts for mom’s with limited time!!

I started with BodyRockers about 8 years ago after my first daughter was born. It helped me get back into shape and keep me motivated while I was home. I’ve used on and off ever since. I would like to stay more on top of my workouts but I do find it hard sometimes now that I have my third child. But the workouts are super effective and I can find short videos that I can usually squeeze in. The one thing I was wish was different was that the videos in collections were better labeled. For example, the Beginner Boot Camp is difficult to find the right day when I stream from my fire stick. I have to count the days and find the video and it would be nice to see it at a glance. I definitely enjoy my workouts. I’m hoping to add some gear this year!

AnnaDager , 07/26/2019

Best workout you’ll find

I been working out with Body Rock for a year. It is absolutely the best quality workout you can find on the internet, gym instruction, and almost anywhere without paying an arm-and-leg for. I would highly recommend it for anyone to try.
Start out with the introductory challenges if you’re new to working out and ramp it up to the longer workouts when your ready.
They premier several trainers, but the favorites are Sean and Lisa. When you’re ready for a longer workout with heavy weights, go to Lisa’s Lives. If you want a shorter workout with more bouncing, stability challenges, and loads of ab work then go to Sean. Of course, mix and match or try other trainers or different styles: yoga, pregnancy, etc... It’s all there. Give it a try! What do you have to lose? Maybe some weight.
