Description

Make it rain with your mining empire! Become the next mining trillionaire, rule the industry, and be the king of all mines!

DISCOVER AND ENJOY DIVERSE CONTENT
Throughout the game, you will see your environment evolve visually with new buildings, new vehicles, new machines to help you reach your goal of operating the most successful mining company!

UNCOVER PRECIOUS GEMSTONES
You will start with one production line- a gold mine. As the game progresses, you can unlock mines that are full of diamonds, rubies, and other rare gemstones!

IMPROVE YOUR PRODUCTION
With the money you earn, you can unlock and upgrade buildings to increase the output of your mining company!

HIRE YOUR DREAM TEAM
Gather the best managers to tackle all your challenges! Assign them wisely to optimize your production! Hire managers with various unique skills to assign them to different parts of your operation!

COLLECT UNIQUE BOOSTERS
When you reach specific prestige ranks, you will win unique boosters to make your mining enterprise grow even faster!

EMBRACE THE MINING CHALLENGE
The user journey is filled with numerous challenging quests. Complete them all to unlock new content!

Start drilling today!

Gilnockie+ , 02/15/2021

Idle game the way they should be done

This is definitely a change of pace for the genre. It involves a lot more strategy and you need to balance the use of cash in order to keep production flowing as seamlessly as possible. Mines, transports, processing, etc all have to be calibrated to maximize profit.
My favorite thing is that not once have I been forced to watch an add nor do you have to in order to properly play the game. The animations are great and the ad bonuses are worth the time. The only thing that’s annoyed me so far is that sometimes in order to rank up you have to open chests or collect daily bonuses so you end up stuck for a couple of days on a level without much else to do. Remove those as rank up criteria and you’ll have a bullseye with this game.

almapa93 , 08/23/2020

Game renders itself pointless very quickly

You can only level your equipment up to certain tiers before you have to level up completely. I’ve been stuck on level 7 for awhile now because it requires rare manager cards which in my daily chests I haven’t gotten any and even spent in game cash on the epic chests that only gave me regular managers too. I can’t level up my equipment anymore so I can’t play anything in the game since I can’t level up without those cards. It’ll also ask you for 3+ chests to level up so you have to wait hours for that since you only get one every 4 hours. Even if everything is maxed out and you need to level up to continue playing, you have to wait hours and sometimes days to get one you need to level up.

Also in most idle games, when you level up, it starts you over but allows you to build back to the place you were at really quickly. In this game when you level up, you just start over with nothing. Any money you accumulated is lost and you are back to square one. You can level your equipment up to the next tier now, but you’ll start at the bottom tier again and have to spend hours getting yourself up to that point again.

The game has a good idea going but how they executed it is bad. Waiting hours and hours accumulating money while waiting for the managers cards or daily chests in order to level up, only to lose all of that money and start the game completely over and have to wait hours or days again to level up again.

Schizmo , 07/16/2020

Bugged manager drops?

I’ve been playin this game regularly for weeks, and have been stuck on level 12 for quite some time because the final challenge to beat level 12 is to have 21 manager cards equipped. The issue is that out of all manger cards, the final two cards that I am missing (both of them are for buildings with an open slot, which would take me from 20 to 21) have not dropped even once in any roll. I have a timer set to go off every four hours, and I regularly buy Epic chests in game, and yet these final two managers elude me. I’m starting to get concerned that there’s a bug in the drop system that is preventing me from getting these final two cards, as all my other cards are floating around the same level depending on rarity, which says to me I get them pretty evenly across the board, except for these last two cards. I hope it sorts itself out.

Oh and all the other bugs are still present, I can’t upgrade anything, but that’s not a barrier for progress for me at the moment because I’d already completed all the other missions for this level and am just waiting for my 21st manager card.

PS please add managers for the underground gold mine, it’s a pretty expensive but comparatively useless building
