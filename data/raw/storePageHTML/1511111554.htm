Evang Ismail , 09/22/2020

Best Sandwiches Ever

I love eating from Charleys. You can choose all the toppings you like, or have it especially made your way. The bread is so good, fresh, and soft, that you could eat it without the meat and other toppings. The sandwiches are not greasy nor salty. In my opinion, it’s very healthy eating. I’m so glad they have reopened since the shut down from the Covid 19 Pandemic. You can bet, I’m going to be a regular again. If you haven’t tried them, you owe it to yourself to at least try them once. You will not be disappointed. The food is pricey, but you know what they say, you get what you pay for. Charley Philly Cheese Steaks are EXCELLENT and worth every penny!!!!!

mom 2 my 3 sons , 11/24/2020

Philly cheesesteak the best!

So many places have this on their menu but they don’t compare to the one I just had at Charley’s. It was one of the best I have had anywhere outside of Philadelphia.
It was full of moist, tender and flavorful beef topped with melted cheese grilled onions and peppers. It will definitely be a weekly meal and a place I will tell others about.
The service was speedy and the server very friendly!
A place to go for Philly Cheesesteaks. While there try their strawberry lemonade. Delicious and refreshing!
