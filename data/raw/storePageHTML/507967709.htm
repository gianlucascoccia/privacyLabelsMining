Description

Large selection of white noise, sounds of nature and lullabies to calm your baby. Get to sleep quickly and sleep well through the night.
Features:
- white noise sounds including fan, vacuum cleaner, rain, womb, and more…
- soothing lullabies, including traditional favorites and modern renditions
- sound self-activates at first cry – you might not even have to get out of bed to soothe your baby!
- record your own sounds
- sleep tracking (learn your baby's sleep pattern to help her sleep through the night)
- WiFi baby monitor and Remote Control to let you control Sound Sleeper right from your bedroom
- night light and clock

Sound Sleeper is a three-in-one sleep solution for you and your baby that will accompany you from birth to the toddler years. Use Sound Sleeper to put your baby to sleep in minutes with Play Mode, help her stay asleep with Listen Mode, and enable her to develop healthy sleep habits with Sleep Tracking Mode.

Sound Sleeper is also great for kids who share rooms; use the soothing sounds to keep baby asleep while you get your toddler ready for bed.

Use Sound Sleeper on two iOS devices to keep an eye on your baby as well as turn on sounds, all from your own bedroom!

We are young parents who developed Sound Sleeper to help us stay sane when our first child was born in 2011, and are still using it for our family! We invite you to download Sound Sleeper for free, and treat the whole family to a good night’s sleep!

A Life Saver

I hardly give apps reviews because they either do just fine or I don’t care enough to write one but this app is different.

This apps “Ocean” noise is the ONLY thing that will get my baby to sleep! It calms him down when he’s fussy, it was the only thing to stop him from screaming after an hour and a half on a plane ride (if you were on that plane ride I sincerely apologize it was the first time we rode by ourselves), and the only noise that he will fall asleep to at night!

He has a noise machine with a projector in his room that plays an ocean noise but it’s more muffled than this one. This ocean noise sounds like you’re standing with your feet in the sand waiting for the water to hit your toes.

I thought the free version would do us justice because 30 minutes is a long time, right? Wrong. The other night at 3am I caved and bought the full version because I wanted to go back to sleep but if I brought my phone back into the master bedroom, I knew our little one would wake up instantly. So I bought it, set it for continuous and went back to sleep for 4 more hours.

This app is amazing and I will never stop using it!

(I’ve had both of his grandmas download it and the nanny as well because it’s a godsend for them too :) )
