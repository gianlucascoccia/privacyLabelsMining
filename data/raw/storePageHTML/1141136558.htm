Description

ALGO Traffic provides live traffic camera feeds, updates on Alabama roads, and access to exclusive ALDOT information such as message sign readouts, incident and construction information, and current road congestion levels.

Key Features:
• Rich information on road events directly from ALDOT
• Access to a network of ALDOT cameras across the state
• Live message sign readouts
• Audible alerts while driving about upcoming events
• NWS weather alerts

Create a profile on algotraffic.com to customize your default map settings, or check interstate traffic by viewing our live camera feeds. Learn more about active alerts for the state, or use driving mode to be notified of upcoming events on your route. Currently supporting Birmingham, Huntsville, Mobile, Montgomery, and Tuscaloosa regions.

Sponsored by the Alabama Department of Transportation (ALDOT) and the Alabama Law Enforcement Agency (ALEA). Do not use this application while driving.

App layers option finally worked without having an account. Thank you

Developer Response ,

The camera layer on the app can be turned on and off by tapping the map layers button in the bottom right corner on the map screen (middle button looking like a stack of paper). This will show all cameras on the map.

Edit: You don’t have to sign up for an account or anything to do what is outlined above. Not sure how any of what we said suggested that an account was required. What you want to do is very much possible in 2 taps no account required.
