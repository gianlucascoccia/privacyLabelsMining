Description

From Zoella and A Color Story comes a brand new video effects + filtering app for iOS!

Filmm makes it simple to mix and stack professional color grading filters with authentic film textures, light effects, and subtle distortions. The app includes—

• Filmm packs pairing over 100 filters + effects
• Realistic film-inspired frames + watermark control
• Multi-clip editing with professional transitions
• Advanced adjustment tools, including curves
• Audio + music control, including custom royalty-free music
• Story splitting save options
• Crop detection, pre-ready for social media dimensions
• Clip trim, cut, reverse + duplicate

Get unlimited access to all Filmm packs, frames, transitions, music, watermark control, and all upcoming features with Filmm+!

Singinstarr44 , 07/13/2019

BEST video editing app...

Edit July 13, 2019 -

So, I’m in love with the new Music feature! It was exactly what I was looking for and I love all the choices.

The one thing that REALLY irritates me is; if you are using the camera IN the app, you better make sure no one calls you and you have all your alarms off. If someone calls me, my alarm goes off, or I accidentally hit Siri it not only stops your recording but DELETES EVERYTHING you just recorded. I’ve lost so much footage this way - I like to use the camera in the app instead of the iPhone one because it zooms out further. I have to text people telling them not to call if I’m about to make a video. If it stops the recording it should still have what you’ve done so far instead of erasing everything.

Original Review -

That I’ve played around with, to this date. With the endless choices of filters and effects you can get as creative as you’d like! It’s simple to use but still has advanced tools for editing the colour and tone of your video. The option to save as a single video or as stories makes it easy for you to upload on any platform.

The only thing that I wish was included would be a feature to add music. You can do so in IG but if your video is split into multiple stories it’s difficult to get the music to flow right. This is a hard thing to get the rights for though, so I understand why it’s not an option.

Developer Response ,

Hi, thanks again and again for the kind words as well as bringing this issue to our attention. We've gotten it resolved in version 1.2.2, which is now available for you to update, so now our camera behaves like the native iOS camera when you receive a call during recording and saves your footage. If you have any other questions or concerns please contact our support team at filmm.co/support ❤️
