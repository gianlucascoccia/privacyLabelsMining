Description

Want to lose weight, get strong, improve flexibility or simply de-stress? Fiit lets you stream hundreds of workouts straight to your iPhone, iPad or TV.

Membership gives you unlimited access to 10, 25 and 40 minute classes, with industry-leading personal trainers. Whenever you want, wherever you are. Your first 14 days are totally free!

WHAT CLASSES ARE THERE?
Choose from on demand and group leaderboard classes. Whether you’re just starting out or a regular gym-goer, keep progressing with entry level, beginner, intermediate and advanced workouts. Including:

HIIT
Strength training
Kettlebell
Dumbbell
Resistance band
Combat cardio
Low impact cardio
Yoga
Pilates
Breathwork
Mobility training
Postnatal recovery


ARE THERE TRAINING PLANS?

Of course! We have over 20 training plans tailored to specific goals and fitness levels. So whether it’s weight loss, a six pack or mood boosts you’re after — our training plans will get you there faster than any other fitness app or gym class.

HOW IS FIIT DIFFERENT TO OTHER FITNESS APPS?

Track your stats with Apple Watch or 25 other compatible devices
See your heart rate, calorie burn and reps in every class
Train with friends in group leaderboard workouts
Set personal bests, then come back — and beat them!
Fiit syncs with Healthkit to export your workout data into the Health app
Take classes at home, in the gym or on holiday. Either straight from your iPhone or connected to a TV
Join a motivating online community to share your successes and stay accountable
Customer support 7 days a week

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST?

Access to our Premium service requires an active subscription, available on a monthly, quarterly or yearly basis. All Premium subscriptions come with a 14 day free trial. Subscription renews automatically and you can cancel anytime.

The Fiit device (£45) or Fiit device Lite (£25) and accessories are available for purchase separately (international shipping available to 175 countries). If you already have a fitness tracker, such as Apple Watch, Wahoo, Polar and Cardio Sport devices, you can connect to Fiit and track your stats during classes.

Fiit also syncs with Healthkit to export your workout data into the Health app.
