Description

ShapeShift is the easiest way to get started with top cryptocurrencies such as bitcoin, ethereum, chainlink, litecoin, dogecoin, dash, stablecoins and many more! Buy, trade, track, receive, share, and even win! A ShapeShift user is rewarded with free crypto everytime someone makes a trade. The more of our loyalty tokens, FOX you have, the better your chances of winning! Available in more than 10 languages. Use the ShapeShift App to easily buy crypto with your debit or credit card and trade one crypto for another in your wallet. Watch the value of your crypto in real-time. Your account is secured with client-side encryption and your crypto is always in your control. ShapeShift has been building leading bitcoin technology since 2014.

ALL OF THE FEATURES, NONE OF THE HEADACHES..

RAINFALL: WIN CRYPTO
Rainfall lets you win free crypto and is powered by FOX Tokens. Everytime someone trades, a ShapeShift user wins a random amount of USDC! The more FOX Tokens you have, the better your chances to win. You can earn FOX Tokens for verifying your account, referring your friends, and trading. Follow along with our activity board to see each time someone trades and someone wins. Why not get rewarded and have fun while you manage your crypto portfolio?


INSTANTLY BUY BITCOIN WITH A DEBIT & CREDIT CARD
Buy bitcoin, ethereum, USDT, chainlink, and litecoin in seconds with your debit or credit card. Available in over 160 countries and with competitive rates, the ability to purchase bitcoin and crypto has never been easier. No need to wait several business days for your order to process, immediately take advantage of market trends wherever you are.

CRYPTO TRADING MADE EASY
Want a different cryptocurrency? It’s as simple as two clicks of a button. You can diversify your crypto portfolio quickly and easily. Invest in your favorite assets whenever you want and wherever you are. ShapeShift has been an industry leader in exchanging one cryptocurrency for another since 2014 and have always maintained fidelity to one core principle: giving our users complete control over their digital assets.

STORE YOUR CRYPTO IN A SECURE WALLET
Setup a ShapeShift wallet in seconds to store your crypto. With our blockchain technology we make getting started in cryptocurrency easy. You take care of the email and password, we take care of the rest. You can access your backup phrase at any time. Don’t give control of your crypto to centralized wallets and exchanges that hold your funds and can suffer from hacks. ShapeShift makes self-custody easy, never holding your coins, so you have complete control over your assets.

EASILY SEND & RECEIVE BITCOIN
When you use ShapeShift, you are only a few clicks away from sending or receiving top cryptocurrencies, like bitcoin, ethereum, Chainlink, Litecoin, Dogecoin, Dash, stablecoins, and ERC-20s.

WORLD-CLASS SUPPORT
If you’re new to crypto, our support team is available 24/7 to recommend reliable resources and help you have the best possible ShapeShift experience. You can count on our team for accurate and efficient help every step of the way. Visit our Help Center: https://shapeshift.zendesk.com/hc/en-us

CRYPTO BEGINNERS TO EXPERTS
ShapeShift was built to help you effortlessly onboard your friends and family into the cryptocurrency space. With ShapeShift you can get them into bitcoin in seconds. Whether you’re just getting started with bitcoin or you’re a crypto expert, check out our resource library at ShapeShift.com or sign up for our email list.

ABOUT SHAPESHIFT
Founded in 2014, ShapeShift is an internationally renowned cryptocurrency trading platform. It is guided by four business principles, all of which focus on serving users: security and self-custody, free trading and high liquidity, 24/7 customer service, and an effortless user experience. ShapeShift allows you to buy bitcoin, track, trade, and secure your digital wealth through a beautiful web interface and mobile app.
