Description

FAR AIM by Aviation Mobile Apps, is the finest app available for pilots who consume the US Federal Aviation Regulations and the Aeronautical Information Manual. This app is always up-to-date, synchronizes your bookmarks and highlights across all of your Apple devices and sends you an alert when regulations you care about change.

The app includes all of CFR Title 14 and Title 49 Subtitle B along with a regularly updated editorial compilation of CFR material and Federal Register amendments. We also include a guide to understanding the Federal Aviation Regulations and the latest copy of the Aeronautical Information Manual.

Features include:
* Daily updates
* Shows you exactly what changed and when
* Fast and comprehensive search
* Resizable text
* Synchronized bookmarks & highlights
* Share bookmarks with your friends
* Alerts
* Works off-line

Users tell us that they love the fact that they can finally see what changed between regulation updates. Our exclusive before and after feature highlights new regulation text in green and modified text in red.

They also tell us that they love how easy it is to find relevant information with our fast search engine, and they appreciate how natural it is to access frequently referenced material on all of their devices with our bookmarks feature. They also like the ability to make the text bigger or smaller and love that we don't mess up their highlights or bookmarks when new updates download to their device.

One time fee. No subscription is necessary.

Give FAR AIM a try today and see why everyone who tries this app, loves it!
