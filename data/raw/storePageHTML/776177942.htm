Description

With locations worldwide, WeWork offers you workspace where and how you need it, whether your goal is to be closer to home, empower your team in different cities, or have a go-to private space where you can actually focus.

The WeWork app is exclusively for members, so you must have an account to access the app. Once logged in, WeWork members are able to:
• Book workspace or conference rooms where and how you need it
• Register guests, so the Community Team can greet them upon arrival
• Submit requests to the WeWork support team with any questions
• View and RSVP to member-exclusive WeWork events
