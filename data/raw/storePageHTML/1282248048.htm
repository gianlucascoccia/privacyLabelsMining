Description

Powered by the Langston AI Engine, Black® collects the news & stories from the world’s greatest influencers of black culture.

With all of your black news in one place, it has never been easier to discover the stories that keep you informed, engaged, and inspired. Langston™ does the work behind the scenes to make sure our voices will always be heard, shared within our communities, and that our narratives remain pure.

JOIN US ON OUR MISSION TO CHANGE THE GAME.
- Create your own feed of stories from the publications you know and trust.
- Read, recommend, and share stories with your friends using Facebook, Twitter, iMessage, and Email.
- Explore stories that are just for you and based on your interests.
- Receive timely push notifications that will keep you in the know.

Featured Publications:
- The Root
- Atlanta Black Star
- OkayAfrica
- News One
- Blavity
- BET
- Black Enterprise
- Ebony
- Essence
- HuffPost Black Voices
- Hello Beautiful
- and more!

Black® is now ad-supported, so we can hire more black engineers!

We'd love to hear any feedback you have about Black®. Reach out to our team at team@black.news. Oh, did we mention that we're 100% Black Owned?

This app is a great way to stay informed about what’s going on in the black community.
