VisionaryWelding , 08/28/2017

Excellent- Weather+ since ~2012

Weather+ has been in the top 3 "best weather apps" for years. I've used it since ~2012, and every design upgrade has been meticulously crafted to retain function and simplicity. No weather app is everything-for-everyone in every situation, so I use three. 3rd is Accuweather, in 2nd place is MyRadar, and Weather+ is my main go-to. The blend of intuitive use, with fast one-touch shifting of the 5-day forecast to the details of the day I want to see, in visual 2-hour summaries. Nearly as awesome is being able to define and arrange other cities that you occasionally need, and then just swipe to another city instantly. I have 13 cities defined... is there any limit to the number? Recommendation: Do NOT settle on any weather app until you try Weather+.

Sabor Místico , 07/16/2020

Great - However

I love the visuals. Great imagery of the sky and various landscapes under different weather conditions. The app reflects the weather reasonably well although there is a pretty significant lag (often there is an hour delay after it starts raining or snowing before the app displays those conditions). This should be improved.

The other thing I would like to see is some additional scenes for clear nights. For the longest time the only nighttime scenes that display [when the weather is clear] are one beautiful moonlight beach scene and a few scenes that feature wind turbines at dusk. That’s pretty boring - and repetitive. After all, there ARE other things to see at night. Why not add a scene featuring a starry sky- (or a few different ones)? What about other moonlight scenes (during whatever moon phase) over a lake, river or mountains? There are definitely other options to consider. I wish you would find some and add them!

Chuck Fitz , 10/03/2017

My go to app for weather.

I'm an amateur photographer and Weather+ is my go to app when I'm going on a shoot outside, it's been very accurate. I like that you can see the week at a glance. I love the background videos of what the weather is going to be like that day, it lets you know what to expect with just a glance from across the room.
What keeps me from giving it 5 stars is that it occasionally take some time to update. There's been a number of times of when I checked the weather for a city and it's still showing the day before. That's a minor but sometimes annoying thing.
There are two things that would make me give it a 5+ stars. Add an alarm feature to it so I could wake up and see what the weathers going to be like that day. Secondly add a dimmer feature on the app so on those mornings when it's still dark and the newly added alarm wakes me up it's not glaring at me.
