Description

- Unique features
o Smart Battery Prep: warms-up battery according to your schedule
o Smart Charging: Stops charging at the specified time. Useful for time-of-use electricity plans
o Scheduling Climate to turn on at configured days/times
o A graph that shows your battery health
o A graph that shows your efficiency vs. others
o A graph that shows your cost savings (EV vs. gas)
o A graph that shows firmware distribution across users
o A graph that shows miles driven per day/week/month
o A graph that shows stats related to your charging sessions
o A graph that shows your driving efficiency history
o A graph that shows you phantom drain stats per day/week/month
and location of each phantom drain
o Export your stats to a spreadsheet to analyze on
your desktop computer
o Sends notification if you leave one of the doors or trunks open
(which prevents the car to lock itself) and you are not in the car
o Sends notification if you leave one of the doors unlocked and
you are not in the car. It also optionally locks the car for you.
o Receive a notification (after a few minutes) if climate was tuned on
remotely and you are not in the car. This helps prevent depleting
the car battery if you turn the climate on remotely and then forget
about it.
o Several Siri Shortcuts to turn Climate on (and set it to your desired
temperature using Siri), open charge port
o Send addresses from your iOS device to your car

- Charging reminder notifies you if the car's battery level is low and
it is parked at home and it is unplugged

- Use the Widget to do all this without launching any apps:
o check status of all doors
o battery level
o time to complete charge (when charging)

- Displays driving efficiency over past 30 miles
- Supports multiple vehicles under the same account
- Displays several detailed information related to charging the car
- Simple, clean, modern user interface
- Supports metric as well as imperial units
- App reminds you if the battery level is low (user specified) and the
car is at home (user specified location) and it's not plugged in.
- You can change the cost for a charging session by
tap-and-hold on the cost bar-chart
- User can specify the unit of currency
- Integrated several forums and new sites

-- All stats can be exported to spreadsheets --

*** This app is for Model-S/X/3/Y owners ***
** This app requires iCloud Drive to be enabled **

Stevegshi , 04/20/2019

The best car now has the best App

Yes, this app is bit pricy, but it is totally worth it. In addition to all the features that the official Tesla app provides, this app offer much much more: all the stats you could imagine on battery, efficiency and range. Not only you can see your data, you can compare it with other app users. I found myself use this feature a lot when compare my car’s firmware and efficiency. With firmware, you could almost predict a firmware will arrive soon when big chunk of other users are already on a newer firmware. With efficiency, you can tell if your car is acting abnormal or has potential issues. Sure there are enormous amount of numbers and graphs packed into this app when you open it, but the developer carefully planted explanations right at your fingertips to explain what these numbers and graphs mean. Plus, you get Smart Sentry Mode, Siri control, more advanced schedule charging and Apple Watch app, which you won’t find in other Tesla app. Furthermore, the developer actively participates discussions on Tweeter and Tesla owner’s club forum. You get tech support there from the person who develops this app! And just like your Tesla, the app keeps getting better over time. The developer constantly improve the app experience with people’s positive feedback. That’s amazing! I recommend this app for any Tesla owners who want to know more about their cars!

AlanJB45 , 03/30/2019

Great App & Great Support!

Have used this app for 2 1/2 months and love it! They have added several new features in the time I have used the app. Originally I bought the app to use the battery health and the scheduled cabin conditioning features. Through use I have liked seeing the vehicle fleet firmware versions. It lets me get an idea how the rollout of the new Tesla firmware updates is progressing. Recently they added the Model 3 smart battery conditioning setting. At first I didn’t see a need for it for me. After using it for a few weeks I can’t imagine not using it. I set my cars charging to 70%. When I get home and plug in the car charges to 70% Incase in need to run errands . In the morning the app sets the charging limit to 80% 40 minutes before I leave to work. In winter the precharging warms the battery enough so I get good regen. Two hours later it resets the limit back down to 70% ready for the next day. Much easier then manually setting the charging times in the car. I had trouble with the one feature the day after a Tesla Firmware update. The developer responded to my question in an hour through their support link in the app. My favorite app!
