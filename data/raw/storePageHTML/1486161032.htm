Description

Original, breathtaking stories are a good idea to pass the time during a break, on a road or while waiting for somebody or something.

Original, breathtaking stories are a good idea to pass the time during a break, on a road or while waiting for somebody or something. Just choose the genre: novel or legend, hot romance or mystery&myth, comedy or sci-fi, adventures or life&moral and read short interesting stories in the our app.

Each story looks like a chat in a messenger, plus photos and videos. It’s as if you are reading the real chat, completely immersed in some investigation or love story or who knows in what else! Depends on your choice only. But be sure the plot and the ending is absolutely unexpected! And don’t tell us you don’t have a temptation to read someone else chats!)

Why our app?
• This is interesting and easy: reading of one chapter takes up to 5 minutes!
• This is exciting: secret chat stories are full of unexpected twists.
• This is real: intriguing media content in the form of video, photos and calls gives you the effect of presence.
• This is for everybody: scary, funny, famous, romantic, life stories, horror stories, stories for adult, love stories and many-many others are waiting for you!
