Description

* Unlimited FREE Book Summaries of Bestsellers & New Releases
* Featured #1 by Apple, Google and The Guardian worldwide
* Top 10 in 80+ countries with 0 paid ads

SO MANY BOOKS, SO LITTLE TIME

Nothing replaces reading a great book cover-to-cover. But have you ever left one unfinished? Instead, use StoryShots to learn or review the key takeaways of bestselling nonfiction books in minutes; instead of hours or never. Remember, you can't make more time. But you can make the most of it.

FREE STREAMING

Enjoy the key takeaways of 400+ bestsellers online for free. No other app makes such an offer.

MULTIMEDIA - LEARN THE WAY YOU LIKE

Some people like to read. Some like to listen. Others like to watch an animation. You can do all three with StoryShots for every book!

PDF & SEND TO KINDLE SUPPORT

Do you prefer to read on the desktop, Kindle or your Kobo device? We've got you covered.

OUR MISSION

We want to make the core lessons of the world's best books accessible to everyone, regardless of their budget, time, and learning preference.

TRY BOOKS BEFORE BUYING THEM TO SAVE TIME AND MONEY

The key takeaways of each book are on average 15 minutes long. If you read just two books a day using StoryShots, every year, you can save more than $10,000 not buying books that will end up gathering dust on your bookshelf and 9 weeks not reading books that will disappoint you. Invest that time and money in books or other means of education that excite and benefit you.

MULTIPLE CATEGORIES

The books fall into these categories: business, entrepreneurship, management, leadership, self-improvement, personal development, productivity, time management, psychology, habits, health, fitness, nutrition, money, investing, economics, marketing, sales, history, philosophy, science, mindfulness, happiness, communication skills, dating, and relationships.

MULTIPLE LANGUAGES

We have content in 18 different languages including Spanish, Portuguese, Hindi, French, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Russian, Italian, Persian, German, and Turkish.

StoryShots™ is for you if you:

* get frustrated when an author repeats the same concept over and over
* want to challenge yourself to read more
* are a visual learner and prefer to watch a video or animation
* feel guilty about not reading a recommended book
* can't get through your growing stack of must-read books
* want to be more efficient and productive by learning on the go
* need to learn the big ideas of a book before attending an event or walking into a meeting
* want to review the key takeaways of a bestseller you've read before

The key ideas are carefully handpicked from thousands of Youtube channels, TED talks, podcasts, and blogs. Think of them as condensed ebooks, audiobooks, and cartoon adaptations of bestsellers. Learn the wisdom the authors have gained over 10000 hours of hard work in just 15 minutes.

YOU CAN'T MAKE MORE TIME BUT YOU CAN MAKE THE MOST OF IT

Download the app now and discover your next life-changing book.

STORYSHOTS SUBSCRIPTION TERMS

StoryShots offers a free version with a monthly and annual premium subscription for offline downloads. not displaying ads, highlighting, note-taking, and more. To get the subscription, go to the side menu and tap on Get premium benefits.

If you choose to subscribe, you will be charged a monthly or annual subscription fee according to your country. The subscription fee will be shown in the app before you complete the payment. Your subscription will automatically renew at the end of each subscription term unless auto-renewal is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the term. Subscription renewals cost the same as the original subscription, and your credit card will be charged through your iTunes account at confirmation of purchase.

You may manage your subscription and turn off auto-renewal at any time after purchase by going to your iTunes Account Settings. Refunds will not be given for any unused portion of the term.
