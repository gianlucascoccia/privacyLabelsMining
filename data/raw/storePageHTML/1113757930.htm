Description

FlixLatino is a subscription video on demand service for $2.99/month, that offers more than 250 movies, series and documentaries originally produced in Spanish. Start your 7 Day Free Trial. Our movie catalog consists of theatrical releases from all over Latin America and Spain. All movies in our catalog are carefully screened for quality and variety in genre, country of origin and gender appeal. Enjoy a world of Spanish-language entertainment at your fingertips.

Start your 7 Day free trial to enjoy our programming at a time of your choosing, through any device. After your free trial is over, enjoy FlixLatino for $2.99 per month (auto-renewed). Our service continuously adds new movies to cater to our subscriber demands.

Subscribe now! You can cancel anytime.
