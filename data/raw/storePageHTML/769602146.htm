Description

Wherever you are Barometer gives you the local atmospheric pressure, directly on your iPhone iPad or iPod. With an intuitive and accurate design, Barometer offers a pleasant experience and reliability in its use.

Make sure you turn on location services of your device and that you are connected to the Internet. At this point the application will detect your geographic location. The display will show the name of the location and the current barometric pressure, also indicating the climatic conditions. The data are provided by apixu.com

Barometer gives you the option to choose the internal barometer to your device or to use the service offered by apixu.com to detect the pressure of your city.

[ Features ]
• Real time measuring of the atmospheric pressure (main black needle)
• Support of internal barometer
• Ability to select the internal or external Barometer
• Units supported in hPa, mbar, mmHg, torr, inch
• Possibility to keep track of the last atmospheric pressure reading (secondary gray needle)
• Atmospheric pressure forecasts for today and tomorrow (available through In-App purchase)
• Maximum and minimum atmospheric pressure today and tomorrow (available through In-App purchase)
• Place detection
• Revelation weather
• Map current position
• Search City
• Save favorite places (unlimited)
• Reliability in its use (data from apixu.com)
• Intuitive Graphics
• Ease of use

[ Units ]
You can easily choose to see the barometric pressure measured in hPa, mbar, mmHg, torr, inch.
