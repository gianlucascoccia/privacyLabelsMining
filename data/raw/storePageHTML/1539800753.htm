Description

AdvanceClub app is here! Take your favorite Advance Classes with you on the go and stream all our videos through the app. Watch Bugha, MrSavage, Bizzle, and more!

ADVANCECLUB | LEARN FORTNITE FROM THE BEST
Achieve your own greatness in Fortnite by learning from the top professional Fortnite players. Join the 2019 World Cup Champion Bugha plus MrSavage and Bizzle in their Advance Class where they break down everything you’ll need to become a better Fortnite player.

YOUR ESPORTS JOURNEY BEGINS HERE
- Start by learning from the best Fortnite pros in our Advance Classes where you’ll get Fortnite tips, instruction, and VOD reviews straight from the top pros.
- Get weekly videos in our Club Now section with the most up-to-date Fortnite meta updates available from the best analysts in Fortnite.
- Apply your new knowledge and sharpen your Fortnite skills by participating in community events alongside all AdvanceClub members

UNLIMITED ACCESS
The Advance Pass grants unlimited access to everything we have to offer:
- Every Advance Class from all of the top pros
- Weekly video content and training maps
- Access to our forums and Discord communities
- Instant access to our Members Cup

CLASSES ON THE GO
Stream our Advance Class and Club Now content anywhere, anytime! Binge our cinematic, ultra high-quality Advance Classes on your Mac or PC or enjoy them on your phone or tablet while waiting for the bus, between classes, or on a break.

ACHIEVE YOUR OWN GREATNESS
Whether you’re looking to get more wins to keep up with your friends, increase your social presence by creating content or streaming, or getting into the professional esports scene, AdvanceClub has all the tools you’ll need to improve.

MEMBERSHIP TERMS

There are no cancellation fees, and you can continue to use your account until the expiration date.
