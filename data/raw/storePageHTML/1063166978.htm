Description

Quidd is the world’s largest marketplace for digital trading cards and more!

Remember trading cards? They’re back, in digital form.

Build the ultimate collection by choosing the right mystery packs from hundreds of your favorite brands, shows, anime, movies, comic books, and games!

Join millions of fans who are chasing limited-edition digital stickers, digital trading cards, and digital 3D figures!

Be the first to own rare items and level up to become the ultimate fan with the most valuable collection!

Here’s how you do it:

-> Find brands you love, like Disney®, Rick & Morty, Game of Thrones, MARVEL, Transformers™, My Little Pony™, Naruto: Shippuden, and more
-> Open mystery packs and discover what’s inside
-> Keep what you love and sell the rest for coins
-> Level up as your Collection Value grows
-> Become one of the best collectors in the world

With new sets dropping every day and over 2.1 BILLION individual items in circulation, you’re sure to find something you love — and something valuable — for your collection.

You can also share the stickers that you collect with your friends on iMessage! We’ll never track your keystrokes.

Are you ready to become the best collector? Join the Quidd community now!

Great, but..

Quidd is an awesome app it just has some quirks that make it sometimes annoying. (Reasons why it’s awesome at the bottom) first of all the feature probably 80% of the people on quidd probably hate the special editions/ hyper shots/scattershot/etc..The idea that only a few people can get something super rare is cool but annoying. Special editions cost a lot of quidd coins and take forever to earn quidd coins for.(only takes a few days but still) and you can only by like less than half of the set and by the time you same enough coins for the rest of the set it’s all sold out! The largest problem with quidd is the hypershots/scattershot/etc. you can usually only buy them with REAL Money for the set. Some of them make it so you have to buy a certain 100 card pack just to receive it meaning people can’t buy because they either don’t want to waste their money on something virtual or just 18 or younger (me) which is probably most people who can’t purchase them. With all of these issues aside the basic quidd is really cool and fun Shelfies are semi interesting and the idea of being able to collect stickers of your favorite fandoms is awesome and being able to use them in iMessage is even cooler. If quidd didnt have the issues it did I would rate it 1 million at of 5 just without those stupid special edition/scattershot/hyper shots. I would give this app a 4 out of 5 stars

It’s fun but some issues

Alright so I just started a few days ago and I was thrilled to collect things. Of course in a way this is kind of dumb as you can’t actually get these but collecting is still fun when it’s digital. Anyways, since I started “late” all of the items that are here are sold out, which makes you needing to trade with people. There’s no problem with trading things but sometimes people are at high demands for things you don’t have. Another thing is that there should be more fandoms. There’s just so little options and out in the real world, there are more movies, games and comic funko pop vinyl figures to choose. Just add a new fandom like The Walking Dead, or Stranger Things, it literally any other show or video game. Finally, the coin system. I’m not the one for buying in-game currency so I usually watch an ad to get 500 coins. But sometimes it doesn’t want to give me the coins so I just wasted 30 seconds for no reason. I’ve watched 5 videos in a row and on the final try, I got my 500 coins and ONLY 500 coins. It didn’t add up all the other times so I just got robbed of time. And one last and final thing is the showcase. Now I thought it would be a good idea to fill up a thing with pop vinyls. But ever since I added like 12 of them, the app just crashes over and over again. Maybe it’s because the figures take up so much space but it’s just ridiculous.
Any ways, it’s still fun. It just needs some things tweaked and fixed
