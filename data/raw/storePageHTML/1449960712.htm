Description

CSE will help you on your journey to a healthier lifestyle. With this app in hand, you can:

-Easily calculate your daily macro intake
-Plan out your meals on a daily/weekly basis
-Make custom menu lists to help you shop for your CSE meals

We hope you enjoy this tool and that you find it helpful. By choosing to pay monthly, you'll be charged $14.99/month. If you pay for a year upfront, you'll only be paying $9.99/month.


SUBSCRIPTION PRICING AND TERMS

CSE is free to download. Access to premium content requires an active subscription, available on monthly or yearly basis. Yearly subscriptions bill the total annual fee, monthly subscriptions are billed each month.

Payment will be charged to your credit card through your iTunes account at confirmation of purchase. Subscription renews automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours prior to the end of the subscription period.

Subscriptions can be managed and auto-renewal turned off in Account Settings in iTunes after purchase. Once purchased, refunds will not be provided for any unused portion of the term. Read our full Terms of Service (https://cleansimpleeats.com/terms), Billing Terms (https://cleansimpleeats.com/billing-terms), or Privacy Policy (https://cleansimpleeats.com/privacy-policy)

Tracking needed

I LOVE the CSE recipes and products and workouts and that it’s all in an app now! I love everything about it - except that I need a separate app to track everything I ate for the day and make sure I’m staying within my calories and macros. If you added a tracker where it would let you add the meal from the plans portion of the app and automatically populate the calories and macros and your tally for the day, that would be amazing and make it a one stop shop. I currently use fitness pal to track my meals and exercises but it’s time consuming to add all the meals from CSE and it doesn’t calculate the calories the same based on the ingredients so I usually just add the calorie total but then I can’t track the macros too.

Anyway - love you guys and your products - just some thought for maybe a next step for people who have made CSE a way of life and an easier way to stick to it!!
