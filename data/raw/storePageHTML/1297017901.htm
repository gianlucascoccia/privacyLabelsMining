Commander Storm , 04/15/2020

Very low quality and poorly made

I was out on to boohoo man by a friend of mine, he said that he had order one or two items from the site and had no issue. I looked around the app and couldn’t believe how inexpensive the clothing was especially men’s jeans. I should note that i am in the middle on gaining weight for a bodybuilding competition, so I needed a bigger size pants than usual. Everything seemed to be discounted as if they were going out of business. I ordered two pairs of shorts and three pairs of pants. My order arrived only after three days. Once I got my clothing and tried everything on that’s when I understood why everything was discounted. I took one garment out of the box which was missing a button, another had the button hanging on for dear life, and all the clothing had no real fit or contour at all. Mind you all of the jeans I ordered were skinny, they were very baggy especially about the knee area and one could tell they had just been slapped together and thrown in a box. Rather than return the items which would be I feel an exhausting process am writing about it so other will know. From here on out I will pay for Great Quality clothing which you won’t find at BooHoo man.
