Description

The Ultimate Hawaii Travel Guide app from the makers of the #1 best-selling Hawaii Revealed travel books.

This Hawaii Travel Guide app gives you access to all four islands: OAHU, MAUI, KAUAI and the BIG ISLAND, with custom maps that work offline, so you know where you are on adventurous hiking trails or when you’re in just the right spot to enter a hidden abandoned beach.

The app can be your ideal planner for Hawaii vacations.

While planning a Trip to Hawaii, find everything including:

- RESORTS

- HAWAII MAPS

- RESTAURANTS

- HIDDEN GEMS

- HAWAII BEACHES

- HAWAII TOURS

- HAWAII ACTIVITIES

- SIGHTS

- HAWAII VOLCANO UPDATE

Whether it's Maui vacations, a relaxing spa at one of Honolulu’s resorts or an action-packed trip featuring helicopter tours and witnessing real lava flow, it’s all available at your fingertips on Hawaii Travel Guide App. All businesses included in the app are reviewed anonymously, so we’re unafraid to let you know if you’ve found a real gem or if you should stay away. We update the app daily, so you’re always armed with the most accurate information including weather updates and safety warnings.

Get access to the Maui Travel Guide, Oahu Travel Guide, Big Island Travel Guide and Kauai Travel Guide exclusively on this best travel guide app.



**Written by the author of the Best Selling Travel Book in the U.S: Ultimate KAUAI REVEALED, MAUI REVEALED, OAHU REVEALED, and BIG ISLAND REVEALED Guidebooks.



**Recently updated with custom-made maps that replicate the ones readers love in the books.



Features that make exploring paradise incredibly easy:



REVIEWS - Anonymous reviews of resorts, restaurants, sights, activities, beaches and more.

HAWAII HIDDEN GEMS - Nearby feature to let you properly explore local gems, gorgeous waterfalls, culinary adventures, hikes off the beaten track, guided tours, and more.

HAWAII OFFLINE MAPS - High-quality interactive maps of the islands that work offline.

FILTERS - Use filters on the app to choose which part of Hawaii you’ll reveal next: Maui, Kauai, Oahu, or Big Island.



All of this information fits on your phone, so leave the travel books in your hotel room and access everything you need on-the-go.

clreedy21 , 11/02/2018

Great Information, Good App

First I’ll lead off by saying this is a wealth of information about the islands that is constantly being updated and an absolute must have with the subscription to any island you’re spreading more than a day on. Beach info is updated daily with surf/snorkeling conditions which is awesome. Restaurant info is spot on. We ate at almost exclusively recommended places and never came away disappointed.

The only reason I docked a star is for the app itself which, while fairly well laid out and easy to navigate, had some annoyances like a structures that were sometimes circular referencing and sometimes had multiple locations that seemed like they had *almost* the same info but were slightly different for some reason. The layout also makes it really easy to go way down a rabbit hole of links and redirects that you have to backtrack through or jump all the way home. Just made it hard to go back to that exact page you were looking for sometimes. One other quibble was that the vertical infinite scrolling effect was far too sensitive at the bottom of the page which let to me scrolling past where I wanted about 450 times on the trip.

Developer Response ,

Aloha, Mahalo for the great feedback on the app. We really appreciate comments like yours. We are constantly trying to decide where the fine line is between linking internally (sort of like the page refs in our printed books). And we appreciate the scroll comments, too. We did give option to go to a list format within sections or to scroll depending on user preferences. We will be looking again at the scroll feature in future releases. We hope you return to Hawaii again soon for another visit.

Good app but needs multi-device syncing

Very nice resource for travels to paradise! Sold as a “companion” to the book (which I bought first), it is essentially identical to the book (as far as I can tell?), which kinda makes the book itself obsolete... so bear that in mind if you’re looking at both this and the ebook.

One star subtracted because I wanted to use this both on my iPad and my iPhone... the tablet while on the plane (or lanai!), phone while out & about. It works beautifully on both devices, BUT: favorites aren’t synced! So I’ve ended up with essentially two parallel resources that I have to reconcile manually. The website says they’re working on it... but I think that’s a semi-major gap (at least IMHO). Since I’ve signed up for an account, can’t the two devices just “talk?”

Overall nice work... if the device syncing worked, this would be a solid 5-star review.

Mamaabcdefg , 01/26/2019

Not worth it if you already have the book

I purchased the Big Island Revealed book and found it to be such a wealth of knowledge that I also checked out the app. With all the recent changes in the Big Island with the 2018 eruption, I was hoping to find updated information from the printed book, but the sections are the same. If you prefer an app to a book, this would be a great resource. However, personally, I prefer the book because it has more maps and guidance so you have a better understanding of where things are. The app has a map but you have to zoom in close to see the mile markers and you lose the view of the whole island. I also found the book better organized for the way i personally think. The only benefit of the app is that you can save favorites which might be easier to access on a trip instead of carrying a book with you.

I realize they did add a section on the lava flow update, but the information was provided in the free version. I was hoping for more detailed information on how to visit volcano National Park post-2018 and where in Puna we can legally and respectfully see the effects of the 2018 lava flow.

This is a great resource if you do NOT have the book, but not necessary if you do.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for using our book and the app. We were sorry to hear that you were not happy with the app, but we are confused. We have published thousands of changes on the app, mainly due to the lava flows, since the print version of our book came out. We provide some of that information for free in the app as a courtesy, but many, many more changes have occurred in the last year and are reflected in our up-to-date app. Also, you requested that the map show all the mile markers while zoomed out. Unfortunately, because the island is 4,000 square miles, in order to see all of the mile markers at the zoomed out level, we would have to cover the entire island with nothing but mile markers, and even then we would not be able to show them all. That’s why it is necessary to zoom in to see the mile markers.

Moreover, our app is regularly updated, and the majority of our users have both the app and the book. They find the books are much better when they are away from the island and planning their trip whereas the app is more useful when they are on the island, and need easy access to our content. However, the preference of digital vs physical book differs from user to user.

Feel free to reach us at aloha@hawaiirevealed.com if you have any additional problems. We would love to guide you on how to use the maps and find any info you require. Mahalo!
