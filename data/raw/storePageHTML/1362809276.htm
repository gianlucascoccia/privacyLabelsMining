Description

One of the 'Best Hypnobirthing Apps to Download for a Calmer Labour' -Mother&Baby

A Christ-centred birthing app, helping you feel more relaxed and connected to God throughout your pregancy and birth.

Using Christian Hypnobirthing, thousands of women around the world have experienced easier, more relaxed births - some even completely pain-free without any medicated pain relief! This is because the scripture-based audio tracks help to program your mind and body to come into a faith-filled state, trusting in God, and allowing you to have the best birth possible.

'With God all things are possible'
(and that includes an amazing birth!)
Matthew 19:26

If you believe that God is with you throughout your pregnancy and that birth is a positive and wonderful opportunity to connect with Him and your baby, then that will completely transform your birth experience.

WHY USE CHRISTIAN HYPNOBIRTHING?

* It can help you feel more connected to God and your baby during pregnancy and birth
* It's a natural form of pain management (there are no medications with potential side effects for you or your baby)
* It can decrease stress and fear during childbirth
* It can provide comfort, relaxation, and relief during labor

CAN HYPNOBIRTHING BE CHRISTIAN?

Yes!

There are a lot of misconceptions around what hypnobirthing is (some people worry that it’s a form of mind control or ‘not right with God’ etc - which could not be further from the truth).

Simply put, hypnobirthing brings you into a deeply relaxed state, where you’re able to absorb positive affirmations and beliefs about birth while remaining alert and completely in control. In fact, as a Christian, you’ve probably already experienced being in this relaxed state many times, during prayer or worship activities, etc.

Listening to Christian Hypnobirthing helps to bring you into this calm, faith-filled state, which can help make birth a more relaxing and joyful experience.

THE APP FEATURES:

- Almost an hour of audio tracks including:
* Breathing and Visualisation exercises
* Encouraging Scriptures for birth
* Positive Affirmations
* And Prayers for strength

- A ‘Positive Birth’ section with encouraging blog posts, links to birth videos, and more.

- A 50% discount code for our Printable Birth Affirmation Cards.

Download Christian Hypnobirthing and benefit from both the relaxing and calming effects of hypnobirthing, combined with the huge strength, love, and support that comes from our Heavenly Father, Lord Jesus, and Holy Spirit.

Pain Free Natural Dream Birth

I absolutely loved using this app during my pregnancy in preparation for my home birth in January. I read the Hypnobirthing book and really loved the ideas they presented but wasn’t sure if I would be able to do it, but this app really helped bridge the gap for me. I happened upon this app while searching for a Hypnobirthing app to learn more. It gave the scripture I needed to be able to be confident in my ability to do this. I used the techniques I learned through the book and this app during the 72 hours of prodromal labor prior to active labor beginning and then during actual labor. I can honestly say my labor never registered as being painful. There were times I would label as intense, but never painful. On January 13, 2019 at 11:38 pm, I gave birth to our third beautiful baby boy in our home after about 12 hours of active labor . It was the birth I had always dreamed of and I am now a firm believer that you can experience a natural birth without pain and that birth is not something to be feared. This was such a peaceful and beautiful experience that as soon as he was born, I wanted to do it all over again. My wish for every mother is to have a birth as calm and comfortable as mine was. Thank you for providing this app to contribute to that experience.

Great resource for believers

This app is an excellent companion to the hypnobirthing class I took through the Positive Birth Company. It’s so encouraging to have tracks that point back to our strength coming from God rather than simply within myself. As a mom-to-be, listening to the Scriptures being read along with the affirmations track has helped put me in a good head space.

I wish there were an accompanying class, but like I said, it paired nicely with the secular online class I was already taking.

I would definitely like to see a few tweaks to the app for future use such as a higher text:background contrast (it’s very hard to read the words on the main menu), a simple way to download tracks within the app for offline use, and a general tightening up of the design and flow. The app is a little clunky, but I still really appreciate the value added to my life from the quality of the audio tracks! (Also, I love the reader’s accent!)
