Description

The Papa John's App makes ordering your favorite pizza even easier. You can earn free food, find Papa John's locations near you, get special online offers, and track your order right to your door.

FEATURES

Earn Free Food
Earn Papa Rewards points and redeem your Papa Dough towards any menu item (account login required).

Push Notifications
Get special offers and notifications delivered to your mobile device.

Special Offers
Get offers from your neighborhood Papa John’s. Receive and store super deal offers in the app inbox.

Faves and Past Orders
Save up to four of your favorite orders, and save past orders for 90 days (account login required).

Find a PJ Near You
Enter your zip code or city to find your local Papa John’s for delivery or carryout.

Pay Your Way
Choose from cash, credit card or Apple Pay.

Plan Ahead
Use Future Ordering to plan ahead. Order tomorrow’s lunch or this weekend's party spread right now. Choose when you'd like your order to be delivered or ready for pick-up.

Papa Track
Track your delivery order from dough to door. Get push notifications when your order is on its way.

For use at U.S. locations only. Have questions or comments about this app? Contact us at http://www.papajohns.com/contact-us/

Depoppy , 09/11/2020

Great app

A easy to use and navigate app. It allows you to order anywhere so you can place your order and it can arrive minutes after you arrive at home or whenever. We love the convenience and the fact that this app works when others don’t. Only changes that need to be made are to provide a space for the customer to add any specific instructions such as cooking the wings longer so they are more crispy or if you are adding to or modifying a regular menu item or that whoever is making it understands your request. We have had multiple issues where they didn’t follow the customization of a specific pizza on the menu and yet they still charged the extra amount of $$ for it. Also the tip area of the checkout needs to be more visible because it has added in a much higher tip than necessary when I didn’t see the tip section. The area to leave instructions for the driver needs to be larger so that the instructions can actually be longer than 5 words. Our street name is very similar to 2 other streets in our city and we regularly have to give detailed information on which one we live on. Other then those changes it’s a great app.

spindrift ledge , 08/21/2020

Outstanding!

I have been patronizing a competitor because I lived outside PJ’s delivery area. It was like Russian Roulette. Some days, food came promptly, hot and properly cooked. Too many times, it came quite late, lukewarm, and nearly every item was way overcooked, often burned and inedible. As advertised, I called to ask them to make it right. First time, I got my money back. Next time the employee argued with me. (“How dare I complain” attitude) A couple interim times, I didn’t bother to call. This last time I wrote, I asked them to re-create the order done right another night, no charge. I never heard back. In the meantime PJ’s moved their store closer to my home. I could now get delivery. I began ordering from them and have been thrilled with the results. Every order has been perfect—hot, cooked right every time, delicious, and super courteous and light delivery persons. Clearly, it’s a tightly-run ship with impeccable training. As a retired corporate executive, I’m impressed! Your loss, competitor!
