Description

High Vibe TV is a positive lifestyle media network, infused with spirituality, and the curious quest for what is going on in this universe? The network is based on a root foundation of raising the individual and collective positive vibrations, in a way that is entertaining, inspirational, and educational. High Vibe TV offers daily video horoscopes, tarot and angel card readings, sun sign horoscopes, and programming content from personalities in leading spiritual, and inspirational markets, and creates awesome live content, as well as documentaries.

Developed by 12th House Media, Inc. based in Southern California. High Vibe TV has the expertise in delivering exciting content using the newest technologies, social media approaches, and finds a way to always connect with the viewer. Media content is also made with a core algorithm, of infusing positive energy into every piece of content that comes out.

Another interesting fact about High Vibe TV, is it is not about talking about subjects that are vanilla. It is about, “Waking” people up, and doing it in way that has a positive edge. Edge is important to High Vibe TV, because standing side by side in this new OTT media market place, you need to stand out, and be loud enough to wake people up to information that will change their lives forever.

Founded by David Lawrence Palmer, “The Leo King”, and Abby Moneyhun
