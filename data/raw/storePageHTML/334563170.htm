Description

Use your iPhone or iPod Touch as real Weather station. Big Temperature display, windchill temperature, humidity, wind speed, wind direction, barometer, moonphases and more values can hardly be desired. With this design-weather globe you can have fun with the weather. You can select a City and get a more precise weather-info. A 4-days weather forecast is built-in.

Displaying weather data:
- Shows actual weather data
- autom. location determination > weather-data nearby, world wide
- Shows the Station name resp. the town
- 16 Design-backgrounds: clouds, sun, rain, mist, snow, Frost etc.
- Temperature in °C or °F
- windchill Temperature
- Humidity in %
- Wind force (+ Windcone/sock in 5 steps)
- Compass displaying the wind direction
- Air pressure (+ Balloon & animated Rotator)
- Actual moonphases displaying
- Date + time of the weather data
- Simulation of 16 Weather sceneries, Windsock, Balloon (Air pressure)
- Data: Internet, online (only a few Byte)

Settings:
- Temperature in °C or °F
- Barometer in mb or hPa or Torr or psi or inHg
- Wind speed in kph or mph or kn or m/s or Bft
- City: select the city myself

More features:
- Button for updating Data
- Animation (fade in of Background, Windsock, Balloon, Compass)
- New: 4 Day Preview
- New: more precise Weather
- New: At snow: animated snowfall
- New: View the Drewpoint temperature
- New: moonphases
- New: Wiki City (Informations of your city)
- New: Ice Forecast (for your car windshield)

Systemrequire:
- iPhone or iPod Touch with OS 8.x or higher
- Internet Connection for get the weather data
