Description

Learn on the go with ITProTV.

ITProTV delivers binge-worthy learning™ to IT professionals in every stage of their careers. With training that’s more like a talk show, you’ll watch a subject matter expert engage with a host and an online audience to create a better-than-classroom experience that you’ll look forward to watching.

Learn server administration, network administration, security, pen testing and more. Cisco, CompTIA, Linux, Apple, Microsoft – all of the vendors you need. A searchable database provides a go-to resource for the information you need to succeed in IT.

Access 3,600+ hours of content or watch the daily live stream. With ITProTV, you’re a member of an IT community that turns IT education into a conversation that connects learners to experts and each other.


Use this app to:
• Stream the daily live feed
• Access the entire course library with your Premium Membership
• Sync your viewing history across all your iOS devices and the ITProTV website
• Stream episodes on your iOS device and directly to your TV via Airplay
• Resume watching episodes where you left off
• Download episodes (MP4-video MP3-audio only) to your iOS device for offline viewing (annual memberships only)

drew_dizzle , 02/06/2019

Poor user interface from a tech teaching company.

Content itself is like a 7/10. HOWEVER the app for iPhone/iPad is not very user friendly and it not something I’d expect from a company that specializing in teaching tech skills.

Some of the more frustrating problems with the app are:
-App frequently logs me out when switching between watching on iPhone and iPad.
-No bookmark feature to add series to the homepage/user dashboard.
-No button to download all videos in a series at once. Have to go to every single individual video you want to watch and press download. For example it would be easier if you could just go to the “Security+” series page and download all videos in that series.
-Home page shows you recently watched videos. But I don’t care if I just watched a recently viewed video. I want to watch the next video. Would suggest showing the most recent watched SERIES (not episode) on the home page.

Not as annoying but still annoying:
-Videos take a bit longer to download than other on similar apps
-prefer an audio option only to listen on my commute.

ITProTv definitely could benefit from using CBT Nuggets app as an example ;)
