Description

8/19/19: HDPT completely changed the schedules, all routes are updated for Fall 2019 semester. Normal schedule begins Monday 8/26. Welcome JMU Class of 2023!

8/23/17: All routes have been updated for the fall 2017 schedule. Note that many routes have changed since last semester, so make sure you check your schedules!
-------
The original, the best, and still unofficial. The JMU Bus App.

This product is not officially endorsed by either HDPT nor JMU.

Made by a JMU CS '13 Student.

Email me at MattBlooJeans@gmail.com , follow me on twitter @JMUBusApp , or via facebook page for any problems, route requests, suggestions, or anything else! I'm happy to help with any and all problems, but I can't fix what I don't know about.

-Location awareness
-Schedule estimation
-Late Night Routes location estimation
-Limited NextBus Support
-GPS enabled
-Off Campus Routes
-Saved Routes
-Holiday schedules, planned schedule changes, & unplanned schedule changes (ex. snow days) are downloaded straight to your phone, so you're always up to date

Note: Route updates occur automatically when you open the app.

Check our facebook and follow us on twitter to keep up to date with schedule changes and updates

We reserve the right to anonymously track and report users activity inside the app
