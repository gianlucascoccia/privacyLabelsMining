Description

Card App is the best way to get the most from your Renasant Bank debit cards.
• Clearly see your purchases with cleaned up merchant names, location on a map and store info.
• Keep a close eye on your spending with spend summaries to see where you’re using your card online and in-store.
• Get 24-hour self service at your fingertips. Activate cards, tell us when you’re traveling and report cards lost or stolen.
• Stay in control with real-time alerts and controls. Lock your card, set spending limits and determine where your card can be used.

Download now to get the most from your cards.

Won't log in

I have an iPhone8. It logged in through the main Renasant app once, and looked fairly neat while I poked around in it that time, but following that one experience, it won't let me log in. It opens to the screen saying it will launch the Renasant app, but it never does anything. If I open the Renasant app at this point and manually log in directly through there, nothing happens. If I try to log in through the card app with my Renasant login credentials, it says they're wrong. I was never prompted to make unique login credentials for the card app, so I don't know what it wants. There is nothing like a "Forgot password" option to select. Finally, I deleted the app and reloaded it. It gave the same prompt saying it would now log in through the main Renasant app, but nothing ever happened. Dead app. So much time and frustration over an app that I still know basically nothing about. This app launch was a total failure.

We're sorry that you are having difficulties. This issue should be resolved now. Please try force closing both apps (including anything opened in the background). If you are still having problems please call our customer support @ 877-367-5371.
