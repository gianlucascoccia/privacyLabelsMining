Easy to pour hrs into this game and still want more

First off this game does not make you feel bad for not spending money on it (huge plus). Great gatcha collector where even low lvl characters can be useful in some endgame content due to their unique skills. Great graphics and music. Beat the game before I wanted to spend money on it as I was still playing heavily.
2 things I wish was different tho. Endgame money requirements need to be heavily reduced. I’ve beaten some of the endgame content and I still need to put a heavy focus on gold farming just to do little things to attempt to get better at this game. Spending multiple days just to get a few attempts at better gear or seeing if it maxes into amazing gear really is a bummer because the answer is usually no. Not to mention promoting characters (you do that a lot!) removing or changing gear, efficient summoning (tavern is the best), and like 3 other things.
Raid should change elements once a week or something. Having a majority of a raid being fire even if the bosses are not all fire means the requirements to start raiding are usually limited to newer players. I’m more than qualified for raid but some of my strongest players are green and they get destroyed while blue is my weakest and they’re what I need. I’m forced to use weaker guys and it’s taken awhile for me to clear much because of that fact.

Jekkylove , 05/25/2019

Great game, two major needs for improvement.

I enjoy epic seven and it is a really great game overall. But I do share concerns as the “most helpful” reviews: drop rates are a bit unfair with the amount of skystones you are able to acquire daily. I can really grind for skystones (scarce when you finish world difficulty as well) and still not receive any decent units to further my progress in the game. Sometimes I wouldnt have any other choice and would have to purchase packs, I’m sure that’s the gimmick. If given another easier way to acquire skystones or increase drop rates, it could help alleviate some of the difficulties of this issue.

I’d also like do address how incredibly irksome it is to put together gear for your unit when you can not view all armor, including the ones that are already equipped. There could be an equipment manager where we can piece sets together to see the before and after stats of your unit. And then total up the amount of gold based on what piece of armor moves from one place to another. You waste so much gold trying to move your gear around and gold isn’t easy to farm when you use it for many other things as well. I can go from a few million gold to ten thousand really quickly. In order to reattain that amount, it would take so much time and use of energy which could be utilized somewhere else. It’s a big waste and this could be improved.
