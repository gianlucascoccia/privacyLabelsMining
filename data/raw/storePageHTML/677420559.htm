Description

Do just about everything you can do from a branch on your Apple™ device with our mobile app. Best of all, it’s completely secure and at your fingertips 24/7. Welcome to a seamless new world of banking possibilities.

Apple Face ID®
• Log in by simply glancing at your phone, using Apple’s secure biometric authentication
• No need to remember a username and password

Touch ID®
• Log in securely with your unique fingerprint
• No need to remember a username and password

Accounts
• View account balances and transaction details

Transfers
• Transfer money instantly between CommunityAmerica accounts

Bill Pay
• Schedule and pay your bills quickly and easily

Mobile Check Deposit
• Snap a pic and deposit your check. From anywhere.

Live Chat
• Chat with us on the go directly from your mobile app.

Loan Payments
• Easily and securely pay your CommunityAmerica loan any time — even if the payment comes from another financial institution

Popmoney
• Pay friends and family simply and securely
• All you need is an email or cell phone number

Apply for/Open Another Account
• Apply for a loan or open another account in just a few steps

Drop the Pin
• Locate the nearest CommunityAmerica branch or ATM

Insured by NCUA.

Everything that used to not work has been fixed. I am now able to deposit checks almost perfectly, use the quick balance widget to view my basic account information, and log in with Touch ID. Ms GJW , 02/02/2021

Mobile deposit camera

I love the app for the most part. It’s great for checking balances and ease of use with the exception of the mobile deposits.

Every time I try to take a picture of the back of my checks, I get some sort of error. Crazy ones too like “the image isn’t recognized” or “image size doesn’t match the front of the check.” I painstakingly make sure to hold the camera at the same angle and distance and yet every singe time I get one or both of those error messages. It’s maddening. I live in Florida so I don’t have the luxury of branch depositing or direct deposit from my employer. I share accounts with my husband who still lives in MO so I don’t really have an option to change banks. Nor do I really want to. I love CACU. They’ve been wonderful for us for the last 10 yrs.
