Description

Achieve your fitness goals with fun, home workouts . Based on Carrie Underwood’s personal fitness routine, every fit52 workout is different! Free trial for new members!

fit52 is a fun, community powered fitness platform that makes it easier for you to find your path to a stronger, healthier you, 52 weeks of the year. Inspired by Carrie Underwood’s passion for an active lifestyle, fit52 follows her home fitness routines to help nourish your body, mind and soul to achieve your personal wellness goals.

14 day free trial available!

Each of the 52 cards reveals an exercise and number of reps, providing a fun, full body workout that can be completed in less than 30 mins!

fit52 is simple, fun and takes the guesswork out of working out, which is why this has been Carrie’s go-to fitness routine for years! Now Carrie’s fitness routine is available to all, appealing to anyone who’s ready to get active and build healthy habits. All you need is you and fit52!

fit52 workouts are different every time, yet adaptable to the demands of busy lives. Work out on your schedule, with friends, or complete the same workouts as Carrie herself!

With fit52, every card reveals an exercise and number of reps, removing boredom from repetitive fitness routines by offering a variety of fresh workouts. Choose a ‘Path’ based on your fitness level and personal goals, making it easy to fit a workout into your busy schedule.

Best Workout app ever

It’s everything I’ve ever wanted. Keeps working out fun with the card “game” you play for what exercise you do and how many reps to complete. Offers quite the variety of workouts to choose from. Can doing something super intense and challenging but if your not feeling like a full workout like that, they have half workout options available as well. Perfect for getting your feet wet if you’re beginner or someone trying to get back into working out on a regular basis or just don’t have too much time on your hands some days but still want to get a workout in. Also, helps keep me motivated by connecting me with others that are following the same workout path as me and being able to post about it in a specialized feed. Fit52 is just all around a fantastic workout app and I would highly recommend it to anyone at any level of fitness!!

Thank you so much for your in-depth review. We love how well you understand the workings of the app and the reasons we made the features the way we did. You sound like you are getting a lot of use out of it which pleases us immensely. Enjoy your fit52 Path and we look forward to seeing your posts soon.

Holly9015 , 06/13/2020

Sadly not what I was looking for

I’m so sad because I have been LOVING doing the six days of workouts in Carrie’s book. I’ve gone through them a number of times. I love using the cards variety and unpredictability of what’s coming next. And they are amazing workouts!! I downloaded the app to get some new workouts to switch it up. I hate the way the app works. I like using my own cards and going at my own pace. But the app sometimes is too slow, sometimes too fast. I had to pause it multiple times then press the fast forward button and then it told me I couldn’t skip anymore exercises even though I didn’t actually skip any workouts. I felt like I was on my phone half the time. Plus none of the moves were any different from the ones in the book. I love the concept so I’ll probably just find some workouts online and adapt them to use the cards because I have loved that. You can’t even look through different workouts without starting and going through the whole thing. It keeps giving me notifications that I’m changing “my path.” I’ll stick with it for another week until my trial runs out, but then I’m out.

Developer Response ,

Sorry you are having this trouble with the app. We are improving it as we go along and hopefully some of the hiccups you mentioned will be taken care of in the next few builds. We are still very happy you are following a good fitness Path and are getting what you need out of the book. Give it another shot soon and you may find the improvements you are looking for.

rbl00 , 06/18/2020

Great concept but needs more variety

The app works very well in it’s simplicity. While there are multiple avenues for improvement the biggest issue I’m having right now that’s making me stop using it is the variety. I fit a quick workout in in the morning, the half workouts are fantastic for this, but doing 2 to 3 exercises for a half hour just doesn’t work for me. You finish lunges and it tells you to get ready for lunges, Repeat that three times. Or maybe throw superman in the middle but then you’re doing lunges again. With 52 cards you would think you wouldn’t get the same exercise 2! 3, or 4 times in a row or not even with one different one in the middle and then again.

One addition I would love to request is the ability to set what equipment you have for the possibility of harder or more advanced exercises. I love being able to get a half workout in on the two days a week I don’t have a lot of time in the morning and then get a full work out but something more advanced the than the other days.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for the feedback. What you mentioned was very helpful. Although we are trying to find the balance of muscle workout through repetition, we understand that many people would rather have a little more variety. We are adding new exercises in the next few updates and will be reworking some of the workout flow. Setting the equipment is also one of the features we are hoping to add soon. Thanks again for the great feedback!
