Description

Ghost Radar® is the original application designed to detect paranormal activity. Ghost Radar® attempts to detect paranormal activity by making various readings on the device. Traditional paranormal equipment can be easily fooled when simple mundane bursts of normal energy occur. Ghost Radar® sets itself apart by analyzing the readings and giving indications only when interesting patterns in the readings have been made. Ghost Radar® also includes a voice to let you know when interesting words have been detected.

This is the Legacy version of the ORIGINAL Ghost Radar®. Please visit http://www.ghostradar.com/GhostRadarLegacy for more information on the advanced features of including:
* Advanced user interface
* Energy translation to words
* Energy translation to radar indicators
* Energy translation to numeric values
* Energy translation to graphics
* Word logs
* Word log management
* Advanced settings options
* Reporting capability
* Spoken words
* Multi-language support
* Multi-platform support

Visit http://www.ghostradar.com/ for more information.

Please note we offer no guarantees of accuracy or any warranties, therefore, since results from this application cannot be verified scientifically the app should be used for entertainment purposes.
