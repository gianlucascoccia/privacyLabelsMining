Description

Learn your favorite songs with Six String Country's interactive guitar lessons. With well over 500 song lessons to choose from there are plenty of options in modern country, classic country, alternative and red dirt country, blues, fingerstyle guitar, and more. Each song lesson comes with chord charts, tabs, and full demonstrations on acoustic and electric, and break out, note-by-note instruction.

Add songs you want to learn to your favorites lists, and move them to your conquered list when you've mastered it. Six String Country offers 8 full length guitar courses and master classes. Guitar 101 is designed to be a resource from the day you bring home your first guitar. From there, choose from courses in beginning improvisation and soloing, a country pickin' guitar course, a guitar theory workshop, an acoustic fingerstyle course, or master classes in Chet Atkins style, Jerry Reed's style, or the Total Electric Blues Guitar Master Class.

Enjoy a free lesson upon downloading the app, and then become a member to unlock everything on the site. Six String Country's active members-only community forum is packed with knowledgable guitarists from all over the world that are eager to share tips on gear and answer questions that come up along the way.
