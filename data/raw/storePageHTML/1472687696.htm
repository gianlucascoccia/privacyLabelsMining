Description

** Super FUN Bakery Game for Girls **
Many baking recipes and food such as oreo & ice cream cake, cupcake, cakepop, cookies, donuts, birthday cakes, chocolate cake & more

Features:
- A super fun food cooking game with TONS of UNICORN BAKERY FOODS！
- Keep updated with new foods EVERY WEEK!
- Cook yummy bakery foods such as unicorn puff tower, hidden galaxy inside cake, flamingo cake, alpaca birthday cake, blooming succulent cake, and so much more
- Tons of realistic cooking tools to play: bowls, spatulas, spoons, freezer, oven, food blender, piping bags, plates, cream mixer, food processor, induction cooker, knife, cutting board, cake molds, cream scraper, and so much more
- Tons of food materials and decorations to try: fruits, candies, sprinkles, milk, flour, salt, sugar, butter, eggs, rainbow food coloring, chocolate, ice cream, cream, water, cotton candy, cookies, caramel and so much more

How to play:
- Use interactive controls to play the game
- Try different tools to make your foods
- Mix various ingredients to create the unique flavor and color of your foods
- Decorate your foods with beautiful sprinkles, candies, fruits, straws, and sugar coating toys
- Eat desserts to trigger the rainbow & falling stars magic
