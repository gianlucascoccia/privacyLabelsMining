Do you love your houseplants and want to grow a beautiful garden? Simply add all the plants you have to the Smart Plant Home digital garden and receive care reminders so that you can grow happy plants and create a happy home.

Have a plant and wonder what it is? See a plant and want it in your garden? Snap a pic and send it to our horticulture experts. Our experts will identify your plants, answer your specific plant questions, and help you fix pest and disease problems so that your plants will keep thriving.


KEY FEATURES

Plant Identification
Horticulture Experts Identify your plants with the snap of a photo

Plant Care
Monthly Plant Care broken down into tasks specific to each one of your plants. Customize your care calendar by placing tasks on the days you want to do them.

Chatting with Experts
Hundreds of our plant experts ready for questions about your plants, pests, diseases and more.

My Plants
All your plants in your pocket for easy tracking

Wishlist
Keep a digital wishlist for your next plant shopping spree.

Barcode Scanning
Instantly add plants by scanning retail barcodes and begin tracking plant care immediately

Product Recommendations
Get ideas for the best products each of your plants needs

Inspiration
Browse plants by room, by category. An extensive beautiful plant library is at your fingertips. In-App Plant Shopping and shipping.

Order & Pickup
Find plants at shops near you then order just what you for pickup

WHY SMART PLANT HOME?
If you follow a few handy gardening tips from us, weekly or bi-weekly plant care is enough. We are here to simplify all of the mystery of it for you. We have a team of seasoned horticulturists who offer guidance and help for all kinds of plants from around the world.

You can add all your plants to your digital garden and receive monthly plant care without being a premium member. We also have partner retail shops where you can walk in with your plants and scan the barcodes to get free gardening tips and tricks for the particular plant.

When you upgrade to become a premium user, our experts will personally identify your plants and pests and answer any of your plant questions. Our horticulture experts are dedicated to helping people with plants thrive. Premium subscription will be renewed if the auto-renewal is not turned off which is why you should follow a few steps. Go to the app settings and turn off the auto-renewal 24 hours before the renewal or else you will be charged unnecessarily.

kawarimono21 , 02/01/2016

Like having a plant expert by your side!

We recently moved to a large property in a different region of the country. The landscaping had been neglected for many years and we have a lot of decisions to make about how to get it under control again. I know very little about plants, particularly those in our new climate zone, and after finding it impossible to identify anything using Google, was thinking I would need to shell out for an expert to walk through the yard with me. Luckily, I found this app instead! The annual fee for premium membership is well worth it for the unlimited plant IDs. I've only had the app for a few days and have already successfully identified every plant in the front yard!

Update:
Revising from 5 to 4 stars because there is no feature to edit or delete your IDs. If you accidentally upload the wrong photo, it gets sent to the expert that way and confuses them. Also once you get a lot of IDs going, it becomes confusing when you can't delete a duplicate or a misidentification.

NewKitty1 , 02/12/2021

Terrible App

I used the ask the expert feature to identify a known plant (Ctenanthe setosa “Compact Star”) with a good clear photo and after several weeks received a reply of “prayer plant”. While this is technically accurate, it is not what I expected from a so-called expert since there are literally hundreds of plants that could be called a prayer plant. This app is definitely a work in progress, as after re-downloading it I see that they’ve corrected the scientific name of the Easter cactus. However, their Christmas Cactus page still shows a photo of a Thanksgiving Cactus.
The inaccuracy of this app, combined with their petty response to my feedback: (“Thank you for the compliment. Our users know your claims are false and see right through you trying to promote your own app!”) caused me to delete this app again. I still prefer Planta since I don’t have the time or interest to waste with these developers. And I have no interest inPlanta, except being able to trust the information in the app!!
