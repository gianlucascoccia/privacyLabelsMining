Description

Take a sneak peek of what awaits you in the future. Your star sign and palm lines carry valuable information about your life path.

With our app you get:
 - An updated general horoscope for any period of time daily
 - More detailed predictions on such areas as relationships, compatibility with other people, luck, health and career
 - A general prediction based on your palm lines
 - Details on each principle and secondary palm line

All the horoscope and palmistry data is based on your personal information (name, gender and the date of birth) and the analysis of your palms’ photo.

What’s more, you’ll have a personal profile with the following statistic:
 - physical activity

 - biorhythms
