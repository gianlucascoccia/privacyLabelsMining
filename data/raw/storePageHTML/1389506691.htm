Description

Build muscle, lose weight and get more fit than ever! Powered by Bodybuilding.com, BodyFit will help you start your fitness journey with a 7-day free trial. Work out in the gym or at home with whatever equipment you may have: from bodyweight exercises to full weight lifting programs, our exclusive training plans are designed to help you reach your personal fitness goals.

Discover thousands of workouts that you can do in a gym or from the comfort of your own home. Each plan was designed by fitness experts to give you all the guidance you need: daily workouts, nutrition plans, recipes, tips, replacement exercises, and more. We offer strength training, powerlifting and bodybuilding programs, nutrition programs, and much more!

5 REASONS TO DOWNLOAD BODYFIT

• Programs for every goal: want to lose weight? Tone up? Increase muscle mass? We have everything you need.
• Clear instructions: thanks to our video tutorials, you’ll get easy-to-follow instructions from the simplest movement to the most complicated. You’ll never be lost again.

• Fitness tracker: record your number of reps, sets, and the difficulty level of a workout. With clear data points, you get to see that your performance is improving over time and that what was once a personal best is now a warm-up.
• Healthy meal planning and nutrition: we’ve made meal planning easy. Take the guesswork out of calorie counting, and simply trust our recipes to help you achieve the results you want.
• Supplement information: learn about ingredients found in supplements and how to get the most out of your workouts, whether you want to gain strength or lose weight.

Pick the program that motivates you! We offer over 60 training plans, from intense strength training to aerobic exercises. Track progress on each workout with our workout tracker and join the fitness community for tips and encouragement! You’ll also learn how to balance your nutrition with each program, so don’t hesitate anymore - you’ll never be more supported and ready than with us.

With 20 years of strength, fitness, and nutritional expertise, Bodybuilding.com will help you hit your goals. From weight management to muscle building, we’ll give you all the tools for a killer workout to gain strength and increase fitness levels.

SUBSCRIPTION PRICING & TERMS

BodyFit is free to download but ongoing use requires an active subscription, available on a monthly or yearly basis. All customers are eligible for a free 7-day trial to work out, begin weight training, or assess their overall fitness after the initial subscription.

Payment will be charged to your credit card through your iTunes account on the eighth day from a selection of a subscription program. Subscription renews automatically unless it’s cancelled at least 24 hours prior to the end of the subscription period. There’s no increase in price when renewing.

You can cancel auto-renew and manage your subscription at any time by adjusting your iTunes account settings. Once purchased, refunds will not be provided for any unused portion of the term.

Supper cool me , 10/26/2020

Great app could be better

Let me start out by saying the videos and workout plans are way worth the price. Prior to covid I would pay about $1,500 for 20 personal training sessions with a master trainer at 24hr fitness. The videos and trining programs on here out beat what I was doing with 24hrs fitness. However, since we now live in an app based lifestyle and you have to pay for the app, it would be greatly beneficial if the developers would integrate their workouts with other apps like “My Fitness Pal” by Under Amour to track my calories and protein intake since that app does great at keeping track of it. Also, there is no place to track what weights you used or the number of reps for each exercise. It would be great to be able to track if you’re getting stronger or know if you’re at the same weight and go on from there. Over all, it’s a very good app and it’s way worth the cost but much improvement is needed.

Faceschmuck , 02/19/2020

Needs Watch App & Health Integration

I’d give this a 5 for content, 4 for the app itself. The app is very stable and I never have an issue with it - kudos to the engineering & QA teams for their effort. There are a few features it should have to make it a 5:

Integration with Apple Health - why not just read my weight & body fat from my smart scale that records to Health?
Apple Watch app - be great to do a workout and have it record to the Workout app
Videos in landscape mode - I rotate my phone but still get the portrait mode, ugh
Customizable rest timer - some plans use talk about different rest periods between sets but it looks like there is just a global setting
Same exercise between two programs with different names & history - some of the programs prepend a prefix to the exercise name, most likely due to some programs have their own videos for the exercises, but the history should be applied to both
Offline functionality for airplane mode weekends - I like to take breaks from always being connected and I’m not able to do that
There are other apps that have the Apple Health integration & watch app, but they lack the content of the BodyFit app.

Developer Response ,

We appreciate your feedback! You should be able to click the square icon to the right of the video timer to switch to landscape mode if your device does not automatically switch when turned horizontally. If clicking that icon does not work to switch to landscape mode, please reach out to us at appsupport@bodybuilding.com so we can gather some additional information.

Jalix , 02/14/2020

Rest Day/Workouts Not Being Tracked

This app is seamless and straightforward when it comes to making a plan and sticking to it. I am fortune enough to have a majority of the equipment necessary to perform nearly all exercises asked for in each plan, which is great. However..

This has happened multiple times over, particularly in Jim Stoppani’s 6-weeks to Shred and the Alpha Men workout, where my workouts simply wont be logged onto the calendar after I’ve fully completed the workout. I’ve tried restarting the app and re-logging all the weights and reps again, but it still won’t appear. I’ve also tried to visit the website itself and track it there, but to no avail. This app’s failure to log my workouts after consistent attempts is making it difficult to track my efforts, and can sabotage my progress in the future because I won’t be able to remember every single weight I’ve lifted as time goes on when I’m trying to do progressive overload. If there are workarounds or fixes done to this app, I’d be thrilled to give 5 stars, but this hassle of failure to record my progress is detrimental to me.
