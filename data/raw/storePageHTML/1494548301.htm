Description

Video speed is an application that allows you to watch your favorite videos in fast, slow motion. You can manually edit your video playback speed to see details you never saw before.
You can also edit videos by cutting, add a filter, add text and add music to videos

Special features of this application

- Add music to video
- Add Text to video
- Merge Video
- Video editor & slow down motion - Edit video with the selected time interval and generate slow-motion video.

- Slow Motion Video app is very fast and easy to use with user-friendly

- Share your generated slow-motion video.

- Play slow motion video or fast video

- An edited video has the same quality as the original video.
