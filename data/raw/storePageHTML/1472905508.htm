Description

Sign up and get 30,000 points. Earn 20,000 points when you link payroll to Brex Cash and 10,000 points when you spend $3,000 in 3 months on your Brex Card.

Elevate your Brex experience on mobile. With the Brex app, we’re making it easy to track company expenses.

Capture a photo of your receipt for every transaction and add a memo on the go. Instant push notifications make it easy to remember to upload all of your receipts!

Quickly visualize and search for all of your transactions in the app. Pull up your Brex card without touching your wallet to make a purchase online. Lost your card? Lock it in real time. Found it? Unlock in a tap.
