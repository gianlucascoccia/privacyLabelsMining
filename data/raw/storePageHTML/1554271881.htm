Description

Lure Cast is the future of fishing.

Using sophisticated algorithms and frequently updated databases of lure data, fishing patterns, and fish behavior, Lure Match takes the guesswork out of selecting your lures, colors, location, and depth. Lure Cast's recommendations are consistent and accurate and will help you have a more successful day of fishing.

FEATURES

LURE MATCH
- Instant lure recommendations
- Percent match for the lure
- Lure Photo
- Lure color recommendations
- Additional information linked to each row including lure photo, lure description, where to fish, and how to fish

DEPTH MATCH
- Instant minimum, ideal, and maximum depth recommendations
- Instant lure recommendations at the recommended depth
- Additional information linked to each lure including lure photo, lure description, where to fish, and how to fish
- Area and percent of the water column to fish

LOCATION MATCH
- Instant location recommendation
- Percent match for the location
- Location photo

LURE CATALOG
- Collection of 30 bass fishing lures
- Additional information linked to each row including lure photo, lure description, where to fish, and how to fish
