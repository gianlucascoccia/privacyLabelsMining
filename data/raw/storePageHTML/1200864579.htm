Description

Hi there! Welcome to the fun-filled tropical islands of Funky Bay!

Build your own town and farm in a tropical paradise. Set out on amazing adventures and find your fortune. Exotic plants, animals, and adorable pets await you! The charming and colourful characters have lots of interesting stories to tell!

Harvest crops, process them at your factories, fulfil your orders and sell goods to develop your town and farm. Explore new lands, mine resources, solve quests and unearth pirate treasures. Become an Adventurer, start your own business, escape your routine and get some real relaxation on an island paradise! What are you waiting for!

FEATURES:

• Breathtaking scenery and stunning graphics;
• Create and customize your very own farm with tons of different buildings and decorations;
• Harvest crops and fruit to craft valuable goods;
• Trade with island neighbours and friends;
• Begin your adventure with EXPEDITIONS on different islands in Funky Bay;
• Get pets and animals for your own farm zoo;
• Buy and sell valuables on the global marketplace with other players;
• Explore and expand your beautiful island;
• Embark on exciting and risky adventures for more resources;
• Play 100s of different quests with guaranteed bonuses and souvenirs!
• Interact with the fun local characters!

Funky Bay is free to download and install. However, some game items can also be purchased for real money.

CherylStone , 06/27/2019

Cute but buggy

This is a cute game. I love the concept, the characters, the tasks and games, the islands. But it is buggy, even glitchy. The market is full of items that are already sold, making it nearly useless. Many players have complained but there’s no response. The cost of items increasing as you move up levels is ridiculous. An apple tree should cost the same on every level. The in-game chat for neighbors works sporadically and is very limiting. The islands are cute and I love this separate game, but the cost to clear them is high. Again, complaints from everyone but silence from the developers. I’ve paid real money for them in the past, as I want to support the game. It’s at the point that I am not sure that I will give the game any more money until problems are fixed. And finally, the regatta. Fantastic concept, I love it and my neighborhood. But it needs an overhaul. The tasks are too low in points to make a serious run for placing in top 3, and this is after an update. So they made it harder. And there are teams, regularly from outside the United States, that rack up impossible points in the first few minutes of the race. It’s impossible, so something is going on. And again silence from the developers. I love this game but it needs attention, and the developers need to start communicating more than silly polls on their Facebook page.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for your detailed feedback. We are sorry you have this feeling and impression about the game, but appreciate your review anyway. We continue working on the game design and balance and it is important for us to receive as much info from our players as possible. We changed many mechanics in the game, and also changed the points system in the regatta. Also, we changed the value of many items in the game, including trees. Now we are working on new ideas and hope that this will make the game more interesting for our players.

AlexM555 , 11/08/2020

It’s a good game but...

This farm style game is pretty good but does have some elements that are maddening because it is not necessary for it to happen. Case in point, the Funky Market... you click on it and the market pops up with what looks like to be an assortment of items for you to click on, BUT even if you click on them immediately, there is NOTHING to select... everything is sold! My gripe is, why don’t the developers exclude all those that have sold everything from the list display. Only include things that are actually available at the moment. Yes, I know the stuff gets picked right away, but when I click on something that has 10 minutes to go, there is literally NOTHING in all the selections at the moment I click on it. That is not realistic, and it is frustrating.

I got this response back from the developer which gives me the impression that they either don’t want to fix it, or they don’t know how to fix it...

“Hello! Thank you for the review. We are sorry for the inconveniences and problems with the game. We are working on market and hope that we can fix it in future versions of the game. We understand your feelings and we are truly sorry that we cannot resolve this issue right now."

Developer Response ,

Hello! Thank you for the review. We’re sorry to hear you feel this way about the game and developers. We want to completely update the market and its mechanics, make it more player-friendly. Some features and decisions take a lot of effort before they can be applied. We would like to assure you that we have taken into consideration your feedback.

JEdwardsRN , 06/16/2020

Lots of bugs, NO customer service

I love the concept of this game! However, there are many many bugs that make this game so frustrating it’s not worth investing time in anymore. The challenges are impossible to finish or to make any progress on unless you spend money for gems to clear land. There is a gem trap on the challenges in the form of a pop up box that will pop up quickly at the same time you are clearly land and suddenly you’ve accidentally clicked on this random box and lost all of the gems you’ve worked hard to collect. There is a coon trap in the store where you click on items to buy, the store takes your coins, but you never get the items. Add to it the multitude of other problems that happen on a daily basis in this game and it’s worth taking a pass on spending time with an unreliable app as this seems to be. I have contacted customer support several times for lost items on my account. I never receive any response back except a predrafted response telling me how busy they are and how updates have been made. What updates? Where? And please tell us how that is helping the issue we are coming to you about! Telling us you have made updates without resolving our losses is as good as the zero response that I have also gotten in the past from support. Players, do yourself a favor and skip to another game that isn’t trying to scam you.

Developer Response ,

Hello! Thank you for your review. We’re sorry to hear you feel this way about the game. When developing Funky Bay we aimed at creating a challenging gameplay. We are still working on the game design and balance, so your feedback helps us to make the game even better. In the upcoming updates, we plan to make some innovations that will allow our players to get more energy. We will certainly announce this in our community.
As for your complaint, we will forward your it to our game design department for further consideration. Some features and decisions take a lot of effort before they can be applied. We would like to assure you that we have taken into consideration your feedback.

Have a nice day!
