Description

Get easy access to your child’s medical records, see a pediatrician on demand, and get tools to keep your child healthy.

Get the best of Nemours, including 24/7 access to our pediatricians and our award-winning educational content, at your fingertips. Gain secure access to your child's medical records, as well as other tools designed to help you keep your child healthy.

Note: This app replaces MyNemours. If you use MyNemours, please uninstall the MyChart app and download the new Nemours app. Use your MyNemours username and password to log in.

Key Features:
• View your child's test results, immunizations, growth charts and doctor visit notes
• Have video visits for urgent needs and scheduled appointments
• Send a message to your child's care team any time
• Request prescription refills
• Get reminders for upcoming appointments
• Complete pre-visit tasks from the comfort of home
• Search our library of KidsHealth educational content

The Nemours app also has custom features for children with specific conditions, like asthma and congenital heart disease.

About Nemours
Nemours is a world-renowned, nonprofit children's health system. We own two freestanding children's hospitals in Wilmington, Del., and Orlando, Fla., and operate over 85 pediatric primary, specialty and urgent care practices in six states. Nemours also creates KidsHealth®, the world's most-visited website for health information about children and teens.

Established in 1936 through the philanthropy of Alfred I. duPont, Nemours is committed to supporting families in the communities we serve with pediatric clinical care, research, education, advocacy and prevention programs.

What’s New

Mar 3, 2021

Version 2.90.0


We've updated the Nemours app to improve your experience! Here's what has changed in this version:

• My Nemours Story - Kids feel better when they know what to expect. That's why we created My Nemours Story. This new feature gives kids a first-hand glimpse into what will happen at their next doctor’s visit or procedure. Through pictures and captions, our stories help ease fear and anxiety, while also letting kids know what steps to take during the visit. Stories are for all Nemours patients, but are especially helpful for kids with autism or developmental disabilities who may need extra support. The story library will expand over time, so check back to see what we’ve added.
• Help! My Child is Sick or Hurt. What Should I Do? - Many families struggle to understand where to take their child when they are sick and hurt. That can result in overpaying for services or suffering long wait times needlessly. We want your child to get the most appropriate care when he or she is sick or hurt, so we now offer a new feature that will help you decide the best place to take your child.
