Description

Do you want that dress? All you have to do is say yes! Customers are lining up at your store looking for help. They are looking for that next style fad that will make them stand out with the stars. Pull out a basic dress and style it however you like. Change the color, add some sparkles, have a new cut or design; the final look is limitless in this fashion designer simulator.

Have you ever gone to the store and wished you could purchase any dress you want? Now you can create those looks for yourself. Make people's dreams come true and create the most beautiful dresses you can think of. Whether it’s for a party, or prom, or just a night out on the town, people want to look trendy with the latest fad, and it’s up to your keen eye to make this a reality.


Game Features:
1. Simple but addicting mechanics
Customers are looking for the next great designer - can you make the cut?

2. Amazing Physics
The final outcome of the designs will have you feeling breathless

3. Keep on upgrading!
Become the best shop in town - keep upgrading your business.

4. Relax and enjoy
No stress in this game. Just create, color and decorate. So many options to test your creativity.

Immense Potential

So this game could be one of the greatest games available but there are still a LOT of kinks that need to be resolved! 1. The program does not allow you to go back to the beginning of a design. The program freezes and instead of allowing you to select the garment, it gets stuck on the dyeing of the garment that you have not been able to select. As a result, you have to exit the program, turn off your phone, turn it back on and reboot the game. 2. The stickers are stupid and are applied inconsistently! If they are applied AFTER the garment has been dyed, the original dyed color of the garment should show through after removing the appliqué/sticker. Again, the stickers are stupid. Who wants a pig’s face or fat lips on an evening gown? To resolve this issue, the designer should be able to select the sticker to be applied, whether to use a sticker or not and when the sticker should be applied and what colors will show after the sticker has been removed. 3. The designer should have more control over the spray dyed and the ornamentation of the garments. 4. The decorations to be purchased for the shop are childish and trite and do not add to the objective of the game or to the enjoyment of the game. 5. Finally, the designer should be given the option of designing and selecting shoes to go with the custom garments. Thank you.

Cute, but...

I mean, it’s an ok game. But there’s a lot of room for improvement.
1. The “masks” are really annoying. They’re usually not cute and when you “peel” them away, you just see the unfinished white dress underneath. It would be better if there could be a color underneath them. Not only that, but you can’t choose when or when not to use the masks, and you can’t choose which mask to use. Very annoying.
2. The “jewels” that just randomly appear in the dress at the finish and ruin your design because you didn’t know they were coming and didn’t plan to compliment them.
3. There is just so much that the game chooses for you, like the masks and jewels, etc. It would be better if it would let you choose that kind of stuff in a way that works coincide with the customer’s wishes.
4. The spray paint almost always turns out hideous.
