Description

Read, learn, and play with fun songs and activities on Starfall! Covers reading, math, music, and more for preschool to third grade. Includes free and subscriber content.

Join Zac the Rat and his friends on an exciting learning journey, starting with the ABCs and 123s and advancing into grade 3 grammar and mathematics. Starfall's unique free-exploration model intuitively guides children through a variety of topics including reading, math, art, music, and social subjects like kindness and caring.

Core reading activities (ABCs, Learn to Read, Fun to Read, and I’m Reading) are free. Subscribe to support new features and gain access to hundreds of additional activities and songs.

This app requires an internet connection and is intended for Pre-K, Kindergarten, and Grades 1-3. It is also perfect for English language development, special education, and homeschool environments.

Features: *Math Songs *Numbers and Counting *Geometry and Measurement *Addition and Subtraction *Multiplication and Division *ABCs *Colors *Backpack Bear’s Books *Learn to Read *Talking Library *All About Me *Groundhog Day *Pumpkin *Turkey *Gingerbread Boy *Snowman *Selected Nursery Rhymes *Motion Songs *Starfall Sing-Alongs *ABC Rhymes *Historical Folk Songs *Calendar *It's Fun to Read *I'm Reading

Starfall's systematic approach was created by experienced educators using time-tested, research-based instructional methods and feedback from pilot programs across the country. Built to be instantly accessible, the Starfall app helps children build skill and confidence as they explore early literacy concepts and elementary mathematics with stories, songs, and much, much more.

About Starfall: Starfall's founder Stephen Schutz never forgot his childhood struggle to read. As an adult, he was naturally motivated to create a resource to help children like himself. Starfall.com began in 2002 as a free public service and was a pioneer of phonics-based online learning. Parents and teachers fell in love with Starfall as they discovered that children of all ability levels excelled on their own with minimal adult guidance. Over the years Starfall’s offerings have grown in the number of songs, books, and mathematics activities extending to third grade. In 2015, the Polis-Schutz family donated their full interest in Starfall to the Starfall Education Foundation, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.

Run45Rie , 10/06/2019

Needs new content

My 6 year old daughter has this app and she loves to use it. It has a wide variety of fun games and gets her excited about learning. I love that she actually uses it.

My only complaint is that the app content is repetitive. Each time she plays a game, the equations use the same numbers as the last time she played that game. So, all she has to do is memorize what the answer was last time and she gets the answer. I purchased a year’s subscription thinking the content would be new, but it’s still recycling the same equations.

I also wonder why the website has a different organization than the app. It looks like there are more advanced options on the website and more kindergarten options on the app. I’d like to see some more advanced options on the app, as she uses an iPad.

Developer Response ,

We have great news to share with you! This week, Starfall released a long-awaited update to the iOS version of the Starfall.com App, which now closely aligns to the content available on the website.

Here's whats new with this release:

The iOS app still includes our our classic Pre-K and Kindergarten content along with many new activities and content for higher grades. The update features a new layout & organization, with split indexes for Kindergarten & Pre-K and Grades 1, 2, and 3.

Other content additions include the Calendar, I'm Reading! books, Motion Songs, Word Machines, plus English, Grammar, and Punctuation for Grades 1-3, and more!

We hope you enjoy the new updates!
