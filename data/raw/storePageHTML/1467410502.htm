Description

It’s vacation time, and your Sago Mini friends got to the airport early. Luckily, there’s lots to do while they wait!

Watch the bags go by on the conveyor belt, check your belongings through the security x-ray, grab a snack at the vending machines or hang out at the indoor playground. When it’s time for takeoff, head out onto the runway and pick your ride – are you team helicopter or jumbo jet? Bon voyage!

ENDLESS EXPLORATION

Spend your layover exploring all the unique areas of the airport. Just like in real life, visit the arrivals gate, security checkpoint, food court, indoor playground, baggage carousel, airplane runway and much more. Each area has dozens of movable objects and surprises to uncover!

MEET THE FAMILY

Brand new characters like Jinja’s Grandma Nola, Jack’s nieces and nephews Buff, Fluff and Muff and Harvey’s uncle Jasper have come to wish you bon voyage! And don’t think we’d let Fins miss out on the fun – we’ve built him a special robot suit so he can vacation on land with his pals.

ADORABLE DETAILS AND FUN SURPRISES

Your Sago Mini friends travel in style with their custom luggage – can you guess whose bag is whose? Be sure to visit security next, where a working x-ray examines whatever you place on the belt in real time! Suffering from some pre-flight jitters? Shake it off at the indoor playground or grab a surprise treat at the vending machines.

Love the open-ended play of Airport? Check out our other Sago Mini Playsets: Farm, Big City and Zoo.

Features
• Explore unique locations like the security gate, indoor playground, runway, arrival gate, baggage carousel and much more
• Meet new characters and uncover dozens of fun surprises and props
• Fly your own propeller plane, helicopter or jumbo jet on the runway
• Ideal for toddlers and preschoolers, ages 2-5
• Whimsical artwork and custom sound design
• Play wherever you are - without WI-FI or Internet
• Safe and kid-friendly - no in-app purchases or third-party advertising, play without any interruptions!
