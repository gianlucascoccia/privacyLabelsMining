COLLECTIONS
Each collection is designed to give a specific area of your life some extra love. Got a challenge? We’re here for you. Collections and topics include:

Be Healthy: Receive the tools you need to keep your mind and body healthy
◦ Stress, Sleep, Deep Sleep, Calm, Eating, Pain, Fertility

Be Awesome: Boost your mood and find your inner awesome
◦ Happiness, Joy, Motivation, Work, Performance, Muse Mindset Talks, Confidence, Focus, Creativity, Life Transitions, Pep Talks

Be Kind: Cultivate a sense of forgiveness, compassion, and self-love
◦ Gratitude, Compassion, Grief, Relationships, Leadership

Be Curious: Find your perfect fit by exploring different meditation styles
◦ Day to Night, Travel, Outdoors, Mantras, Quick Breaks, Long Breaks, Zen, Singing Bowls

Just For: Meditations to meet the needs of specific communities, people, and careers
◦ Moms, Kids, Goodnight Kiddo, Teens, College, Quarterlife, Baseball, Entrepreneurs, Special Occasions, Climate Awareness, First Responders, Veterans

COURSES
Get step-by-step guidance for a deeper dive into specific meditation topics:
◦ Starter Series, Meditation Essentials, Foundations of Mindfulness, Deepening Your Practice, Sleep Basics, Uncovering Happiness, Mindful Relationships, Mindful Work, Changing Habits

FAVORITES
Tap the heart icon to mark a meditation as a favorite. Sort by Favorites in Studio, Collection Detail, Teacher Detail and Search Results to quickly access favorite meditations.

UNGUIDED
Create your own unguided meditations with start, interval and end sounds. Choose silence or background sounds to add to your custom meditation experience.

TEACHERS
Today’s leading meditation experts. Featuring over 60 teachers including Elisha Goldstein, Chrissy Carter, Michael Apollo, Ashley Turner and more, with a diverse range of styles to explore.

STUDIO
Build a personalized library of your favorite guided and unguided meditations. Play meditations anytime, anywhere – no internet connection needed.

SCHEDULE
Track your progress, schedule meditation sessions, and stay focused on your practice.

BACKGROUND SOUNDS
Choose from calming ambient sounds or original music tracks by tapping the musical note at the bottom of the player.

Meditation Studio saves your meditation sessions to your Apple Health App.

Tried many, this is the best.

I have tried many apps for meditation and anxiety refocusing techniques. Most were given to me as suggestions from psychiatrists and behavioral health specialists. The stuff they gave me was ok when in a pinch, but nothing I would stick to. This app is on a whole other level. I am relaxed in such a way that once I start a meditation it’s almost as if I have floated off into some peaceful dream as my anxiety melts away. I was disappointed on day 1 to see that there was a fee section, but for full access to most of the meditations there is a fee. But after a week or so I’m sold. I find more peace and reflection doing these than in any office sessions.
