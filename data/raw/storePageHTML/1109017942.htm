Description

"This is the most comprehensive CRM app I have found. It saves me time, reminds me when to do what, and just generally freed up a huge part of my mental energy. My monthly sales have gone up at least $500-$1,000 each month just be using this app." - Lauren P., using AMI since April 2018

Find out for yourself why Lauren and tens of thousands of other business builders using AMI every day. Increasing sales and growing business relationships has never been easier.

With the Sell With AMI app, you can run your entire business from the palm of your hand. All your contact information is stored in one place. You can easily send messages from Facebook Messenger, text, Instagram, email, or give a quick call all from one place. Contacts never fall through the cracks thanks to AMI's automated reminders that take the guesswork out of when to reach out and what to say. You can also easily send invoices and collect payments using your customers' preferred method of sending payments and track all your business-building activities from an intuitive dashboard updated constantly to track progress towards your business goals.

Based on a study from February 2020, those who use AMI consistently see 34% higher sales than those who don't use the AMI app at all.

These are some of the key ways AMI makes growing your business and building relationships incredibly easy — we can't wait for you to try them out!

BUILD AUTHENTIC RELATIONSHIPS

- Add contacts from your phonebook, CSVs, manually, or by asking contacts to fill their information for you using your web-based AMI Contact Form
- Send messages via text, FB Messenger, email, Instagram, or snail mail
- Record notes from conversations with follow-up tasks
- Organize contacts with custom labels
- Manage your pipeline of leads, customers, and more
- Filter for specific segments of contacts and easily look up notes using robust search filters

GROW YOUR CUSTOMER BASE

- Share product links easily and track views
- Record samples & receive automated follow-up reminders
- Send customer invoices and receive payments using Square, PayPal, Venmo, Cash app
- Link Instagram content to your top products to increase online sales

CONSISTENTLY WORK YOUR BUSINESS

- Daily reminders to complete business tasks
- Automated reminders to follow-up and reach out with new and past customers and leads
- Customizable message templates to easily send messages to dozens of contacts in minutes

TRACK BUSINESS GROWTH & ENGAGEMENT

- View key stats on a monthly basis
- Track engagement with any content shared from AMI
- Set monthly and yearly goals
