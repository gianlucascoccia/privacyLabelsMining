Description

Bored with superficial selfies and bad or empty profiles?

Want to be judged on more than just looks?

Put your personality first and join Jigsaw. We’ve solved the dating puzzle for over 100,000 singles just like you.

People are full of surprises. So judging them on how they look means missing out on hidden magic. That’s why faces on Jigsaw start off covered with a jigsaw puzzle.

Exchange messages to remove the puzzle and match the face with the personality you’re getting to know.

Here’s how it all works:
→ Send a message to your match to have a puzzle piece disappear.
→ Complete the puzzle within 72 hours and reveal the photo underneath.

Discovering the person behind the puzzle makes a connection worth waiting for. One based on personality, not just looks. Because you date people, not photos.

Over 100,000 people have already joined the Jigsaw community, moved millions of jigsaw pieces, had zillions of great conversations, and formed real bonds. With Jigsaw, you get better matches, enjoy better dates and create lasting relationships based on true compatibility.

So download Jigsaw the dating app now and find a new, anti-superficial way to date.

Jigsaw helps you match with local singles for free, but if your personality prefers all things premium then upgrade to Jigsaw+. Boost your dating prospects with daily access to additional features like:

* INSTANT MATCHES - See everyone who likes you straightaway.
* JUMP THE LINE - Let someone know you’re interested with Supersend.
* REMATCH - Didn’t reveal a photo in time? View any expired matches.
* UNLIMITED LIKES - Message and like potential matches for as long as you want.
* BE SEEN - Stand out and get your profile noticed by more people near you.

Join the dating revolution with Jigsaw and get better dates faster.

Not enough people yet in my area yet.

I legit can’t even really test the app since I only had 4 people within 20 miles have a profile. Setting up my profile though and looking at the profiles I could see made it nice and easy. The prompts given to everyone to use were plentiful and good enough to force people to actually write something really interesting about themselves. I even got to read one of the chicks went skydiving. Plus the puzzle feature. Whenever you post a pic of yourself, a small jigsaw puzzle covers your face. I’m used to Tinder so I didn’t care much, but the puzzle pieces come from their pics the more you talk to them. So what I’ve seen. The app is really well done. Just personally for me, in my area, there just isn’t enough people using the app.
