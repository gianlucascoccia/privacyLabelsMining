Description

Bimble is the app to save, discover and share your favourite places & hidden gems.

Create a list of independent local restaurants and shops to try out, plan your Mexico vacation, or create a bucket list of places to go! Bimble has you covered for everything!

Each place in Bimble is hand curated. Browse over 75,000 great little places and if you see somewhere special that catches your eye you can instantly save it or send it to a friend!

HIGHLIGHTS
• Save your favourite places
• Get automatic maps, directions and addresses
• Add photos, comments and notes
• Create area guides
• Easily share Bimble guides with guests and friends
• Plan for getaways, road trips, vacations, city breaks and holidays
• Discover new places others have shared
• Support local businesses by raising awareness
• Share independent, neighbourhood places you love

CURATED PLACELISTS
Use Bimble to recommend your favourite spots and browse lists of great little places recommended by other Bimblers in curated placelists, called Bimbles.

PLAN YOUR NEXT TRIP

Planning a vacation? Save ideas of places you want to go and get maps & photos automatically - so when you arrive at your destination, you’ll know exactly where to go.

REMEMBER YOUR GO-TO SPOTS
Keep a collection of all of the great little places you can’t stop going back to! You'll always remember where you had that fantastic sashimi or found the perfect trainers.

SUPPORT YOUR LOCAL COMMUNITY
Independent businesses are the life & soul of your community. List great little places in your neighbourhood on Bimble to shine a light on them and help them thrive!

Great UI and concept. Very overwhelming.

Amazing app, but navigation of it could be improved.
Adding your own places is so easy, as is viewing other features people’s places.
Not sure if overwhelming is a good word, but as it stands, the only way to see all places in a specific area is to search every permutation of the name of places after you select an area. I can’t find what I don’t know, and it looks like one aim of this app is to discover places. Let’s maybe make it easier to do that.
Here’s a suggestion: allow the user to pick a location, then provide either all the places/bimbles available on my map/list, or even sort it by category.

Developer Response ,

Hi Caleb, Thanks so much for taking the time to leave us your feedback. It's super useful for us, as a startup, to hear from you.
I'm glad you find adding places works well - we are just a year old and all the places on Bimble are added by the community, so our primary focus has been to encourage people to save and share the great little places they love. As content has grown, we've created some city-focused pages but we absolutely will be developing our map-based functionalities to facilitate discovery. I like your suggestion of the category filter here too. Stay with us and I hope and believe you'll enjoy Bimble more and more : ) The next big set of features that are coming will be making Bimble more social.
You can always drop us an email at hello@bimble.com if you have any questions or other feedback. thanks again, Julia

Albert Lawson , 01/04/2021

Not ready for prime time

This app is kind of a mess. Many restaurants have duplicate (in one case, triplicate) listings. It seems anyone can add anything and it won’t check to see if it’s already in the database. There’s no way to report duplicates, either, so it just becomes a mess. Meh.

The discovery function is terrible. You can’t just pull up a map or have it look for places near you. It just gives you a list of Bimbles that might be 3,000 miles away. It doesn’t even have location data.

Meh. Not the worst idea but oretty useless as it stands.

Developer Response ,

Hi Albert, I've just seen your review. Thanks for sharing your thoughts and I'm sorry that you've had some issues with Bimble. We're a very small team, less than a year old and improving every week.

Good news: we're about to release an update which will iron out the duplication problem. We pull place information from the internet when you add a new place to Bimble and sometimes places are registered elsewhere more than once! We're just finishing reviewing the code which will merge these places at our end and fix the issue. It was a gnarly little problem to solve but it's now done and we're just testing : )

As far as Discovery goes, we have a "near me" map function in the pipeline. In the meantime, you can easily click on the magnifying glass at the top of the Discovery screen and search for whatever you fancy. Hope this helps : )

Feel free to email me at julia@bimble.com if you have any other questions or thoughts.
