Peaceful and pleasant, it is by far my favorite game on my phone!

If you love cats and are looking for a game that is both peaceful, pleasant, and enjoyable I would definitely recommend! I have been playing this game for a couple months, and it is my favorite game I’ve played on my phone. I’ve struggled to find a game that i can put a lot of time in and one that i can enjoy at this same time. I’ve put hours into it! Reading the stories of each of the cats, and it’s really fun finding more to add to your collection and to the population of these lovely islands. This game is peaceful in various aspects: the music, the ambience, the actions of the cats, and even the tones of every cat in each of their letters you can read. I have also enjoyed every event in the game so far! It gives you the opportunity to do something new and earn new rewards. There are ads, and to get through the game at a reasonable speed you will have to watch some, however the creators made it so you are at least rewarded for watching them! I am so excited to continue playing this game and I think the creators have done a fantastic job of creating a story and a beautiful piece of work. I encourage them to keep up the good work!

Cute game, but overheats my phone

Super cute game. I had pre-ordered this game after seeing as ad on Instagram and thought it was adorable. The graphics is cute, the story is cute, the music is relaxing, but why so many ads? This game is really similar to Abyssrium and Isle which I both play. Those games don’t have as many ads as this one. But I just loooove the cute little cats with stories and accessories that you can collect. I do prefer having customizable cats instead of simply purchasing the same animal over and over agin like Isle or Abyssrium. I just wish that maybe the island can be customizable as well so that there’re more things to do in the game instead of just watching ads. It would definitely make the game more enjoyable. Not only that, but games heats up my phone really fast for some reason. I have played so many games on my phone without this issue. But this game overheats my phone to the point where I can smell smoke, it smells almost like my phone case is melting off! I thought this may be my phone’s issue. But after testing this out with other apps/games, my phone never overheated within 3mins if playing. But it does with Dear My Cat. Overall I really like this game and I see a lot of potential in it. I’m curious as to what new things they’ll come up with. Maybe then I’ll change my rating to 5 stars! :D
