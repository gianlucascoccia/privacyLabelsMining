Description

Mobile CRM and Auto Dialer

GoDial turns your phone into a Call Center

Want to handle call lists with a single click? Want to reach more prospects faster? Want to increase sales?

GoDial is your answer. Start GoDialing Today!

How It Works
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
1> Add a contact or Import your whole calling list
2> Click Start Dialing and Call will get placed
3> After the Call, set a Status like SALE, NO ANSWER, INTERESTED, BUSY etc
4> You may add a note or schedule a callback
5> Go to Next call

Features
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
◆ Auto Dialing
Simply import your call list and start dialing. Calls are automatically placed from your phone. No more manual dialing.

◆ Lead Stages
Move contacts between stages and visualize the current state of the contact.

◆ Bulk Messages
Send dynamic template messages via Email or Sms to your imported call lists

◆ Rechurn
Recycle dialed contacts to get the most out of your calling list. Godial allows you to rechurn call status like Busy, Not Available, etc to NEW and redial.

◆ Schedule
Never miss a Call. Set a Callback date and Time and get notification Reminders

◆ Import Export from CSV
Simple bulk Import any CSV sheet. You can even Export your List with all call details. Your data is safe and stored only your phone

◆ DNC
Set any call to DO NOT CALL. Your lists will be filtered and DNC contacts will never be called again

◆ Voice Notes
Add notes and remarks to the contact after a call. View anytime and easily export. You can even save notes by directly speaking. Our text-to-speech converts your voice to note.

◆ Template Messages
Setup a template message and simply click on Email, SMS, etc after a call to send an automatic message to the contact without saving the number on your phone

◆ Payment Tracking
Set a deal value to your prospect. Track payment received and sent.

◆ Attach Files
Attach files to your prospect for later viewing and easy management.

◆ Privacy
Your data is stored locally on your phone. No data is ever transmitted outside your phone. Making GoDial extremely secure and safe.
