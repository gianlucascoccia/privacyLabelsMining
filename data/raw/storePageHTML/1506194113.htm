Description

Apple TV+ Screeners allows award voters and members of the press to watch Apple Originals for awards consideration, as well as for reviews and feature consideration.

This app is available only to pre-authorized voters and members of the press. To access Apple TV+ Screeners, please use your unique code or pre-registered email and password. If you are not an awards voter or member of the press, please enjoy Apple TV+ using the Apple TV app.
