Description

Play 24 lightning-fast games featuring your favorite DC Super Hero Girls characters!

Barbara Gordon here! A.k.a. the one and only Batgirl. You might know my awesome friends and me as the DC Super Hero Girls! We're not your average high school students: we also fight crime by going head-to-head with Super-Villains. Do you want to join us for some crazy fast games and challenges? Snow720 , 06/01/2020

Expensive; Fun, needs update!

The game itself is a mini game collection that steadily speeds up as you progress. You collect stars which in turn allow you to purchase crates that grant you new cosmetics for your superheroes and new mini games. Each character has about 5 mini games per hero and 5 cosmetic wardrobe changes . I purchased this for my daughter and played multiple times with her. Very fun and time consuming.
My biggest nag towards this app is the high price for entry. To truly play you must purchase the full game, which unlocks all of the girls. It is in my opinion a hefty price to ask for a collection of mini games. For the price, too quickly you can collect everything.
I held out on this review in hopes that the high entry price came with future mini games in the way on an update. I have seen no update that adds further mini games or new cosmetics.
I’d be willing to pay a small fee for a collection of further mini games to better justify the $10 I spent on this game for my daughter .
To my point I spent $10 on this where 5.99 is fair. It is fun not knocking that but games like KOTOR cost 10...huge games.
