Description

Stay up to date on the latest scientific advances with Science News Magazine!

The Science News Magazine app includes the biweekly magazine and real-time news updates from all disciplines of science — from astronomy and physics to biomedicine, neuroscience, genetics, early humans, climate, ecology, animals, environment and more.

Current print/digital subscribers can log in to access issues. You may also purchase issues or subscriptions via the app.

MAGAZINE FEATURES:
– Current and past issues of Science News Magazine.
– Brilliant replica format, just like the print edition you know and love.
– Text-formatted articles designed for maximum mobile readability.
– Download each issue, then return any time for offline reading.
– Search the archive of available issues.
– Bookmark your favorite articles.

You'll also get real-time updates from ScienceNews.org, featuring our latest news and blogs.

This application is powered by GTxcel, a leader in digital publishing technology, provider of hundreds of online digital publications and mobile magazine apps.

1 year subscription for $29.99 or a single issue for $1.99.

Subscriptions will start from the most current eCommerce enabled issue going forward for one year from the date of purchase. Your subscription will automatically renew via your iTunes account 24-hours prior to the end of your subscription at the rate of $29.99 for one year. You can turn off the auto-renew feature up to 24-hours before the end of your subscription by going to your Account Settings after purchase. Subscriptions cannot be cancelled during the active subscription period.

vonlost1 , 02/23/2020

Always blurry on iPhone 11

There’s no way to get Retina-sharp text; every page is blurry with this app. When I get the new-issue email and tap Read Now, I get the web site .org version that’s even worse, impossibly blurry. The only way to get sharp text is to tap the icon in the upper right corner of the .org version, then tap Download, tap Download again, wait for the download, then tap the blue down arrow, then tap the file, and you’re in PDF mode with sharp text and landscape support. It should be easier to get sharp text and landscape support. The content is always excellent if you can just see it in sharp text.

FrankieMouse , 06/07/2018

Good—with a couple flaws

I am rather enjoying the app. I have an electronic subscription, but found I haven't been keeping up with reading the magazine since I've stopped receiving them in the mail. This app helps me to get back to reading every issue. I have found one bug so far and a little annoyance.

First for the bug. I am unable to share anything to social media. Every time I try to shave via Twitter, or Facebook the app crashes. So, effectively I can't share via social media.

Second the annoyance. If I close the app, when I re-open it I am taken to my library and not the article I was reading. I've tried downloading the issue to my phone, but the app still opens to my library. This app would be just about perfect if it saved my reading location. You can't really use the bookmark feature to save your issue progress, it basically puts a copy of the article on your phone. If you return to your bookmark you can't continue to the next article if the issue. This is fine as it allows you to keep certian articles handy regardless of the issue it was published in. I would just really appreciate being able to close the app and open it to where I left off.

Good App1211 , 04/23/2019

Better than a paper magazine but ...

... at least with a paper copy I can set the magazine down and it will not close itself and forget the page I was reading. My iPad version regularly forgets my current page requiring a search before continuing.

Added a star for the current release as a number of bugs have been corrected. However, the simple, incredibly simple, function of remembering my place in an issue hasn’t been addressed.
