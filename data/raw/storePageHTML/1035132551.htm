Description

"Ultimate Guide for Terraria" contains a full strategy guide giving you tips and tricks on bosses, items, enemies,NPCs, mechanics,....
"Ultimate Guide for Terraria" is the ultimate resource for finding what you need for each platform.
Features include:
+ Tips and cheats for Terraria
+ A fully searchable list of all items.
+ A filter option to specify which types of items you wish to see.
+ Crafting details including items recipes and what objects can be crafted with it.
+ Full list of enemies that you may battle with.
+ A complete guide to NPCs that you may encounter.
+ A full list of armor sets, plus the option to specify which types you own.
