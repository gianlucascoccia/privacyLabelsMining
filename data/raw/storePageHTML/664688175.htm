Description

MultiTracks Made Easy.

Prime is an easy and powerful way to play back all of your multitracks, loops and click tracks in worship or rehearsal.


GENERAL FEATURES:
• Change the Key of any song
• Change the Tempo of any song
• Customize Song Arrangements (easy drag and drop)
• Unlimited Tracks Mixer: Adjust the volume, mute and solo
• Multi-Out: With iOS Compatible Audio Interfaces
• Auto-Pan: Click and Cues are panned LEFT, Instrument Tracks are panned RIGHT
• Preview songs in full stereo or panned
• Waveform: Displayed for each song
• Create and Save Setlists
• Add a Click Track Metronome to any setlist
• Click Sounds: Choose from 5 different metronome sounds
• Cue Voices: English, Spanish & Portuguese
• Dark theme option


PLAYBACK CONTROLS
• Song Sections: Let you see where in the song you are!
• Section Looping: Loop and repeat any section
• Infinite Loop: Loop a section 1 time or infinitely
• Section Jumping: Jump in time spontaneously to different sections of the song
• Jump Time: Choose when Section Jumping takes place (end of section, after 1 bar, after 2 bars)
• Transitions: Crossfade, Auto-Advance, Overlap and more!
• Set Start / End Times: Trim the beginning and end of a song
• Set Fade Out: Add a fade out to the end of a song
• Auto-Fade In and Out Button


MIDI INTEGRATION
• Map any MIDI controller to various app functions
• MIDI Integration with the Looptimus USB Controller
• Control master volume with a MIDI expression pedal
• Map individual songs sections and looping functions to a controller
• MIDI Out: Send Program Changes, Control Changes and Notes over MIDI Network to control Lyrics, Lighting and more!


PRIME CLOUD
• Import your own audio files with Prime Cloud
• Custom Section Markers: Add your own sections markers to content you upload
• Dynamic Cues: When you change the arrangement of a song, the cues follow you
• Sync and manage setlists between all your devices
• Save custom arrangements to your library
• Share setlists with team members

Madeforworship , 01/10/2020

Use it every week

This has been a great way for our church to enhance our sound and stay tight to a click. We use it both on iPhone and Mac, and in the Mac we use a looptimus stop box to control basic things like looping a section, fading out, and starting the next song. Some of the other features like being able to route the audio to an audio interface id love to be able to do.

If you buy tracks, the biggest challenge I’ve noticed is that there isn’t consistent leveling song to song. So I use soundflower to create a midi multi output (built in on Mac) and then I can listen and level out each tracks parts by soloing those parts and watching a level meter in something like Studio one or probably any DAW. I usually level to the bass part first, then the drums and then make sure I set each track to overall not exceed a certain value, for us -12 dB typically works. The important part is that the volume isn’t changing a lot between tracks because that effects in ear mixes and creates a headache for the sound guy. If we could route the audio out to separate channels with an audio interface (which the app supports), this would probably be easier.

Overall love this app and integral to our worship service each week!
