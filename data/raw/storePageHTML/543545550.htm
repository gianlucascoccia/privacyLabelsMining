Description

An all in one activity center for children - complete child safe phone environment.

Enjoy the classic song, The Wheels on the Bus, with your little ones! With 11 beautiful and colorful verses filled with fun interactions, your children will play and learn at the same time! The 10 different games in the app provide hours of entertainment in which your children will not only have fun, but also develop their, memory, logic and fine motor skills. Wheels on the Bus is an intuitive and child friendly app filled with positive affirmation designed to boost self esteem and stimulate your child’s imagination.

ROAD TRIP
Play Road Trip! Collect puzzle pieces as you go to build as many vehicles as you can!

INTERACTIVE SING ALONG
The classic “Wheels on the Bus” children song comes to life in an animated, interactive rich song and dance experience.

COLORING BOOK
Enjoy The Wheels on the Bus coloring page and learn the different names of transportation vehicles.

PEEKABOO LETTERS
Where are the letters hiding? Practice and train cognitive skills in this adorable and entertaining hide and seek game.

HORN PIANO
Play and record your own music with the 5 different sets of horns on the honk piano.

CAR RACE
The happy race is on, select your car and race with 10 colorful cars in a vivid racing track. Tap your car to make it drive faster, the more you tap the faster it will drive.

HAPPY BUS MATH
Five unique preschool math games. Presented with adorable cars that make learning basic concepts of math so much fun.

TINY TRAIN
All aboard! Join the vehicle train! Learn about vehicle names, voices and vocabulary as you spell the names of the vehicles on the train

TAP THE CAR
Let’s learn vehicle names vehicle has its name, tap on the correct car every turn and move on to the next challenging and fun stage.

VEHICLE PUZZLES
High quality, super fun and simple to master puzzle game, with 12 unique vehicle puzzles.

MEMORY MATCH
Intuitive, educational memory matching games. Match pairs of cars to learn their sounds and names.

BUS RIDE
The bus ride driving simulator game lets your little ones feel like they are driving a real bus! Pick up all five passengers, while avoiding the road obstacles.
