* THE BEST JOURNAL APP TO IMPROVE YOUR MOOD *

CBT Thought Diary is a journal with a purpose: it uses effective tools from Cognitive Behavioral Therapy and Positive Psychology to help improve your mood.

Think of this app as a space to reflect on your thoughts, both positive and negative. Record your mood, reflect on your emotions, practice gratitude, and analyze your thoughts with proven & effective techniques.

Thought Diary will help you evaluate, understand, and change your thoughts and feelings. By using this application, you can work to identify your emotions, analyze how and why you're feeling this way, challenge those negative beliefs, change your thinking patterns for future situations, and remember positive experiences.

You can use this app as a mood journal, a thought record journal, and a gratitude journal all in one!

Handmaid of thr Lord , 01/12/2021

Truth vs. Lies

I’ve suffered from depression and anxiety for years. I found out about this app from a therapist, and over time it became a transforming lifestyle when I invested my time into it. Thoughts can be so convincing and seem true and emotions can be so controlling. This app opened my eyes to what was really going on so I could be in control of my thoughts and not the other way around! I have combined this app with therapy and medication and after years of challenges and growth I can see how far I’ve come from previous entries that I can look back on. Now I can also point out when the same cognitive distortions that affect me, come out in what other people say in conversations. It looks like A LOT of people could use this from what I can tell with recent events happening.

essiecab , 02/08/2021

Well Made and Helpful

I did not really think that this app would help me because it’s so hard for me to change the idea that all of my thoughts aren’t true. Therefore I didn’t think CBT would help me.

However, after working with this app for several weeks, I can see the value in it. It’s very helpful to be able to go back and think about my past entries and how it has or hasn’t changed me. Just the act of thinking about these CBT entries helps me see destructive patterns.

I like the way that you can search under different categories, search by mood, etc. I originally thought there was a flaw in the search function, but after contacting them, they fixed the problem! So they do listen to feedback.

ZhiLi1898 , 02/19/2020

Great app, bad pricing model

This app is exactly what I need to help me develop good thinking habit and stay positive in my life! I also recommended it to my therapist hoping this can help others.

However I don’t feel right about how the dev prices it. Of course you don’t have to pay for it; the free version has all the basic functionalities for your CBT record, but it happens that I need to share my thought entries with my therapist, which is locked until you pay. You need to pay almost 40 bucks a year for the record export, and this is kinda ridiculous.

I would rather pay upfront for one time cost and that’s it. I hope the dev can reconsider how they charge users for this app. It’s a solid app, and I would like to support a solid developer who really cares about mental health. But so far the pricing is not quite right.
