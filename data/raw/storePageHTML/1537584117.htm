Description

Purpose Fitness is a health and fitness app programmed for all levels of fitness. We provide on-demand workouts across multiple categories (HIIT, Strength, Yoga, Boxing etc) led by qualified fitness professionals. Whether you’re at home, outdoors or in a fully equipped gym, our app has carefully curated workouts and programs for beginners as well as seasoned athletes that will make you stronger, more agile, confident and healthier.

Benefits & features
- FREE workouts when you download the app to help you get started
- FREE 14-day Trial of our Premium Membership
- Easily search for workouts based on time, type (programs or singles), muscle or equipment focus
- NEW workouts added every week
- Integrates with Apple Health to keep track of your progress
- Get access to specially curated workout playlists that you can add to your Spotify account or integrate a playlist of your choice alongside the workouts
- Perfect your technique with form and exercise cues in follow-along videos
- Access to a community of members to hold you accountable and keep you motivated
- Coaches & Community forum to ask questions, post updates & get recommendations
- Workouts specially designed for limited to no equipment to perform at home
- Workouts can be pre-loaded in the app for smooth streaming


Pricing & Terms
- Purpose Fitness is free to download
- Premium membership is priced at $19.99/month or $179.99/year
- Premium membership comes with a 14 day trial period. After that, customers will be charged automatically on a monthly or yearly basis
- Payments will be charged to your credit card through your Apple account at confirmation of purchase
- Subscriptions can be managed and auto-renew can be turned off in Account Settings on the App Store after purchase
- Once purchased, refunds will not be provided for any unused portion of the term.

Darren Mack , 02/17/2021

My review of Purpose Fitness

I have been training over 20 plus years and this is my go to fitness app. This is the level up of virtual training as there is a selection of follow along workouts where you train with a small group in real time. Motivating as everyone is in it together and you don’t feel isolated working out at home. The app itself is easy to navigate as you can select from abs workouts, HIIT, tabata, yoga, etc. The classes also have music playlists as an added option and the music does not drown out the instructors voice. The classes are energetic and uplifting and will make you sweat and burn off unwanted calories. Darren M, MSc, NASM, RYT 200

Sebi Lim , 02/17/2021

Workout app with so much customization

So incredibly happy that this launched, I know this is a labor of love from a talented and dedicated coach.

I love the concept of adding your own music to a workout, which makes it so personal. The filters for times and types of workouts make it easy to navigate. And the energy and programming from Trish & Sarah is top notch, it’ll challenge you whether you are a beginner or advanced athlete.

Would love to see the app go even further and allow users to build their own playlists or have reminders for them to workout, to add that extra level of accountability!
