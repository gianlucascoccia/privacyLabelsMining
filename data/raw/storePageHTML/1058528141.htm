Super Gaming

I write this with reservation. Which means I’m torn in two directions.

Play the game or not. It can be expensive. But the enjoyment is second to none.

We cannot get a stronger, faster or more capable tank unless we have a subscription. We can move up slowly without being part of a clan, but the benefits make it worth $19 per month.

The game allows everyone to continue upgrading and moving up in rank and tanks.

However you won’t ya e the kind of battling power you need to crush your opponents unless you buy extras.

Decals, patterns, ammo, and crates which all enable you to make that tank a super hero.

The problem lies in that without all the extras it can take getting shot a million times over and over while scooping up stars and diamonds on the battle field. A long hard battle.

I suspect those with extra money or funds to buy the extras needed, do the best at upgrading the fastest, creating a top tier fully capable destroyer tank.

But even with all the money spending the tanks will not be completely invincible. You will get shot at and eventually picked off during the game by others who are sniping shots at you as you roll along crushing your enemy.

I gave it 5 stars as a premium member and do recommend you at least give it a try.

One awesome game that takes your breath away and engulfs you with nervous encounters. Time after time.
