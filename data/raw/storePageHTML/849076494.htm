Description

The Akron Beacon Journal/Ohio.com is known for in-depth local reporting in print and online, regularly attracting accolades from professional journalism associations at the local, regional, state and national levels.

• View every word, photo, and image exactly as it appears in print
• Tap any article to convert to text view
• Browse pages, zoom on articles, rotate to landscape and portrait modes
• Easily email or share articles and pictures
• Includes archive access to every issue published in the past 30 days

This application includes an option for an auto-renewable subscription for $10.99 per month through an in-app purchase.

ORAgeek , 01/30/2021

More than just an internet news site

The Akron Beacon Journal’s app (I have it on my iPhone and iPad) is literally a replica of the physical newspaper. You see full images of every pager, including ads, and touch an article to drill down to read it in a separate window. Swipe or click an arrow to move from page to page or touch the fast forward icon to move another sections.

This app is very different from a typical news website with animated ads popping up all over the place. The experience is as close as possible to reading an actual newspaper. The only thing I miss is having material to pack dishes and use as kindling in the fireplace.
