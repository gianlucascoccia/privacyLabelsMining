Description

The MyFax app lets you send and receive faxes from your iPhone or iPad and enjoy the convenience of full-featured faxing anytime, anywhere.

Go mobile – ditch the desk and fax from your phone.
Save money – eliminate fax machines, landlines, and ink, paper and maintenance costs.
Always on – your faxes go wherever your device goes, never miss a fax again.
Stay private – no more faxes sitting in open view on fax machines.

MyFax features include:

• Choose a personal local or toll-free fax number at no extra cost.
• Receive and view faxes on your iPhone or iPad.
• Send/receive up to 300 pages per month
• Upload files from Google Drive, Box or Dropbox, or scan faxes with your camera.
• Send faxes with a personalized cover page.
• Store faxes with unlimited MyFax cloud storage or download files and transfer PDF files to your cloud storage (Dropbox, Box or Google Drive).
• Print faxes using Apple AirPrint or share documents by fax or email
• 24/7 Customer Support.
• Fax by email, web or app.

Work smarter and get more done on the go. Get the MyFax app and start faxing now.

The MyFax fax app is designed for use with any MyFax subscription. New users can sign up for MyFax in the app, and also send faxes from the website or by email.

MyFax subscription details. $12.99 USD per month. Includes 300 combined inbound and outbound fax pages per month, and your choice of a local or toll-free fax number. Subscription will automatically renew monthly until you cancel. Once you confirm your in-app purchase, your subscription fee will be charged through your iTunes account. Manage your subscription and turn off auto renewal in your iTunes account. Monthly subscription will renew automatically unless your subscription is canceled through your iTunes account and auto-renew is turned off 24-hours before the end of the current period. Renewed accounts will be charged $12.99 for renewal within 24-hours prior to end of current period. No cancellation of current month allowed during active subscription period. For more terms and conditions see https://www.myfax.com/legal/terms.

We appreciate your reviews and feedback. Please email ios@myfax.com or contact MyFax support at https://www.myfax.com/support.
