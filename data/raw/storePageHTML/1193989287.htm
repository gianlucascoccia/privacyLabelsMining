Halzy69 , 01/21/2021

Why do subscribers have to pay for PPVs?

I have enjoyed the app however one of the major issues I have is that in order to watch PPVs we have to order it through cable or FITE TV. On top of that even after the live event it takes days (not hours) for the PPV to be made available. This is absolutely ridiculous. Other streaming platforms (WWE, ROH, etc) allow their subscribers to watch PPVs live. It doesn’t even make sense that after the PPV is over it takes days to be available on the app. Paying an additional $40 or so when you’re already an Impact Plus subscriber does not make sense. Pay per views are a thing of the past most of the other wrestling companies have moved to the streaming format (even though they have the option of watching the PPV on cable) and as long as you’re a subscriber you can watch their PPVs live on the streaming app. IMPACT you need to do the same. The only people that should have to order the PPVs through cable and FITE TV are those who are fans but not Impact Plus subscribers.

BDaddyL , 05/05/2018

Upload times and app restarts

I’m really liking the app; however, upload times are an issue with me. For example trying to watch the previous night’s Impact or Xplosion. They’re not uploaded the following day. Not even the following week. Impact is not too much of a deal because I can watch on demand on cable, but I’d like to watch Xplosion the next day if I could because I’m in the US... Also, I can’t leave the app for a few seconds without it restarting when I get back into it. I’ve had this app since the beginning, but it might be time to un-subscribe soon...
