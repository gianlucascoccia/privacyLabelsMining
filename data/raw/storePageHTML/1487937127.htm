Description

Craft is a tool for creating beautiful documents and notes and sharing your thoughts. Everything you create in Craft can be shared with one tap. Craft supports inline markdown, backlinks, code snippets, images, videos, attaching PDF files, and rich link previews.

## Available for iPhone, iPad, and Mac! ##

STRUCTURED & LINKED NOTES
Craft allows you to nest notes within notes - creating your own structure - and making thoughts easier to group and organize. It also allows you to create links and connections between pages via @page links easily.

FRICTIONLESS SHARING & EXPORT
With Craft, you can share your content via a link or collaborate with others in real-time. If you decide to export your content, you can choose between many export formats: Email, Markdown, TextBundle, PDF, Print Export.

WORKS ON ALL YOUR APPLE DEVICES
You can use Craft on iPhones, iPads, Macs - you no longer need to switch tools as you move between devices.

ONLINE & OFFLINE
Craft works online and offline as well - it always stores all your documents on your device - so these are available independent from your network connection. More than that - you can keep editing these even without an internet connection, and Craft will upload changes once your connection is restored.

VISUAL CONTENT AS A FIRST CLASS CITIZEN
Most writing apps don’t treat images and videos as first-class citizens. We believe that these are just as an important part of creating and sharing content as text - so we made sure Craft integrates media beautifully.

… AND ALL THE SMALL DETAILS
We've made sure the Craft is "crafted" so it deserves this name - it's built with love, care, and attention to every detail - with to goal of providing you with a frictionless experience - resulting in you having to only focus on what you want to write - and not fighting the tool.

RichardDaniels , 11/21/2020

An incredible start

If you're used to Notion, this app will feel like a breath of fresh air. Like Notion, it has you take notes in a block-based page structure, with child pages contained in parent pages. Unlike Notion, it's a real joy to use. In addition to looking better, running smoother, and absoslutely nailing the animations, it provides several aesthetic elements to make your pages pop. And it provides a full palette of commands at all times so you don't have to be an expert to use it effectively -- all the functionality is at your fingertips from the very start. Craft's offline functionality is icing on the cake.

I'm not quite ready to jump ship to Craft just yet -- I'm too reliant on tables, inline math, and the quick search that Notion has. But the fact that this is Craft's very first version is mindblowing. I look forward to their progress, and I expect them to become my tool of choice quite soon.

Truly amazing and functional!

Very few apps check all the right buttons. While Craft is still evolving, it has an amazing feature set and everything just seems so intuitive! I love creating docs with this and I've just scratched the surface of what it can be used for. I have so many ideas after starting to use it, and I think a good app should INSPIRE your creativity, like this does! Everytime I think, "I wish it could do this", I just try it and it usually works. This is really a rare app that is fun to use, functional, and well designed.

DG 123456789 , 01/03/2021

Brand new and already better than Bear and the like

I moved over to Craft from Bear, and I feel liberated. It is so much easier to organize, work in, and view than Bear. Importing Bear files was super easy with textbundle docs. While it does not have tags, I find the back links system work much better and between that and folders, it is easier to find what I need. Also, search is much more powerful in Craft. There are a few missing features that require workarounds, mostly around bringing content into the app, but the team is working on that. For a new app, I’m amazed at how much they have nailed right from the start. It actually feels like a more refined app than most that are years older, while also having a lot of the same functionality.
