Description

The best-selling Dr. Seuss classic “Oh, the Places You’ll Go!” comes to life with playful interactivity, delightful animations and fun activities!

*** Children's Technology Review Editor's Choice Award Winner ***
*** Academic's Choice "Smart Choice Media" Award Winner ***
*** Parents’ Choice Recommended Award Winner***
*** Best New Apps - Apple***
*** KidSAFE Listed ***

FEATURES
- Tap and drag to find playful surprises throughout the book
- Find hidden stars on every page to reveal fun Activities
- Read along with highlighting narration, read it yourself, or enjoy auto play
- Explore new vocabulary by tapping words and pictures
- Track minutes spent reading, pages read, and more in the Parents section

ACTIVITIES
- Play nine different mini-games like Memory Match, Jigsaw Puzzle, Word Search, Sequence, and more!
- Find all 23 unique games hidden throughout the book
- Enjoy activities again and again, directly from the main menu
- Try to beat your high score and best time

Great app for iPad, but issues with iPhone

My son loves all the Dr. Seuss apps. He is only 18 months and is already starting to recognize letters and numbers. I purchased the entire bundle and some additional ones as well. The only issue we are having is when we open the apps on my iPhone (when an iPad is too much to lug around), there are some pages where the star activity can't be found. For example, in this app, there is one page where the mountains are cut off from the screen and there is no way to get the star at all. Other than that, these are wonderful apps!

Developer Response ,

Thank you so much for your thoughtful review, and for alerting us to this issue. We have now fixed the issue, and ensured the star can be found on all pages of the iPhone version. Please look for the app update in the next 10 days. If you have any additional questions, please email support@oceanhousemedia.
