Description

Wisdo is a friendly, safe place to share life stories, create meaningful connections, find a friendly shoulder to lean on or even provide one to others. We’re so glad you’re thinking about joining and are eager to hear your stories.

Wisdo improves life by instantly surrounding you with people who will get you, because they've "been there."
Share your unique journey, connect within seconds with people who have walked your path and have helped others and in time, give back to others!
From Relationship Advice, to Personal Growth, Anxiety and everything in between - Wisdo is here to prove to you that you are never alone, especially when it matters.

1. Share Your Unique Story
Your personal profile will tell others what you have been through. The more you share, the more helpful people you can bond with

2. Connect to People Just Like You
Discover people you can benefit from connecting to based on shared experiences; also, join Mentors sessions.

3. Achieve your goals and help others achieve theirs

4. Meet friends for life
Explore more life experiences and people who have gone through them. Then, become a Guide to others!

Get this 👌🏻👌🏻👌🏻👌🏻

By first opening the app there are specific types of problems (mental or physical) to talk about in group chats. You can add more specific types of chats and people can join. There are ‘guides’ who monitor the conversation, keep it going and will talk to you with others in the chat and even direct message you if you don’t want all people to see. Even though the live chats are about specific things you can chat a guide or any person in general that’s willing to talk about any situation you might have.
Under another tab there are various subjects such as depression, MS, anorexia, child birth and even more mainstream things such as graduating from college, loosing a job, divorce and breakups that give some good advice (I’m bad at explaining just check it out for yourself). These really target specific problems.
I suggest everyone get this app. The only thing I don’t like is the Pinterest looking theme under the specific tabs and I also wish they could tell you what the specific thing is about Incase you just want to learn about it (but I guess part of the app is defining that for yourself per your own experience)
Overall great. (:
