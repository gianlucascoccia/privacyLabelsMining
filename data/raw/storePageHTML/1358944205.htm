【Main Features】
①Make Friends
In the moments area, you can see the posts from your friends or nearby location users. And you also can post your ideas, which will be viewed by friends and strangers. Users can engage with each other and this will enlarge people’s friendship.

②Go Live Free
The live streaming is available for everyone. No follower quantity requirement and level limits, you can just go live in the broadcasting room and set your favorite cover. As live streaming, you can mute, remove and admin the viewers in management.

③Watch Live Streams
Whenever you open the Facecast app, there are always people broadcasting. Millions of talented broadcasters, passionate dancers, amazing singers, and hilarious comedians, etc. You also can comment in their broadcasting rooms.

④Vlog Editing
Facecast has very many video filters, stickers, effects, and music. You can trim, crop and collage the video. In addition, Lip sync, slow-mo, and the transition will easily help you make popular videos here.

⑤Short Funny Video
Facecast got many talented and fun video creators. Upload your video to related topic and challenge with them. Once your video is mostly liked, you will receive prizes from official And you will be famous if your video is on the trending.

⑥Instant Message Chatting
You can have instant chatting with your friends in Facecast. Users can send and receive texts, photos, emojis, videos, and Voice Messages. If you are not friends, you can only send two messages to others.

⑦Filters & Stickers
The face filters can help you show your best style in the live streaming channel, short videos, and chat. You can use many cute, funny and cool video effects, you can create awesome videos with real-time effects. You can easily share the fun on Youtube, Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook,Tik tok or Flipagram and amaze your friends.And animated stickers will move with your face while broadcasting


⑧Get Followers & Earn Gifts
Users can get likes and followers easily here because Facecast will let everyone get viewers. If you got some talents and want to share with others, then you will get a lot of followers.
