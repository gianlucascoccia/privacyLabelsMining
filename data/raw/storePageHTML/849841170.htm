Description

TECHNOLOGY ENHANCED MEDITATION & SLEEP SUPPORT

The free Muse Meditation app can be used alone or in conjunction with all Muse: The Brain Sensing Headband devices - available at https://choosemuse.com.

Take the guesswork out of your meditation practice with real-time feedback. While you meditate, your Muse device will translate your brain activity into the guiding sounds of weather through real-time feedback to help you find focused calm. Busy mind? You’ll hear stormy weather. Calm mind? You’ll be able to calm the weather in real-time.

After your Muse session, you’ll get a graph showing you the amount of time spent in calm, active, and neutral states. Muse makes it easy and fun to stay on track with post meditation reports, challenges, and rewards.

Subscribers gain access to over 300 guided meditations from renowned meditation instructors including collections like sleep, performance, stress and more. When you use your Muse device, you’ll get a report on how your brain, heart, and body did during each guided meditation.

FEATURES:
* Real-time Feedback: Muse 2 & Muse S users get real-time feedback on brain, heart, body and breath activity during meditation
* Sleep Support: Muse S users get access to responsive Go-To-Sleep content for personalized sleep support including Go-to-Sleep Journeys
* Challenges and Rewards: Stay motivated with our Muse challenges, weekly goals and reminders
* Track & Measure Progress with easy to understand results and analytics
* Apple Health App: Sync your meditation minutes to your Apple Health app
* All non-subscribers who have a device can access all biofeedback meditations (Mind, Heart, Breath, Body) without a subscription

PAID SUBSCRIPTION FEATURES:
* 300+ Meditations on popular topics like sleep, focus, performance, stress and more
* 20+ Meditation Teachers for a wide variety of expert guidance
* Guided Meditation Post-reporting on brain activity, heart rate, and movement data when you use a Muse device
* New meditations released every month
* No headband required, ENG content only

MUSE IS HELPFUL BE AWARE OF PROBLEMS

Overall I would rate Muse as an excellent electronic device for meditation. I cannot see bees out with my friends who have for years meditated see the difference between that and using this device to help you get to where you need to be. What I do notice however is that the device sometimes has problems connecting with your phone. It is important to make sure that there are no oil‘s dust or residuals on the headband and to make sure that the area where the skin contact device is clean. And it is important to make sure that your phone is on your right side while using. For some reason I find it more difficult to use a device in enclosed spaces like inside a motor vehicle as opposed to an open space in the room. The extra money for the optional meditations is worth it. In that optional package you get extra meditations that are specific to certain situations and so far for me is proving all to be valuable resources. So overall I do recommend the device itself I also recommend the optional meditations that go along with that package.
