Description

** Best of 2019 ** –– Apple

“The camera of choice for countless influencers and brands” –– Apple

“Gorgeous App for Making Movies on Your iPhone” –– Wired

Spark Camera is an award-winning camcorder for your iPhone. Over 1 million creators on Instagram and YouTube use Spark for it’s simplicity and speed in creating vlog-like videos. Everyone from big organizations like TED, to Instagram influencers, to small businesses use Spark to make videos that stand out. And we also have everyday people use Spark as their camcorder to record weddings, vacations, parties and more. Unlike other video editors, Spark Camera is all about the camera. You shoot all of your clips in the app and when you’re done, your video is already edited together. From there you can add one of 30 beautiful filters and any song from our popular license free music library.

Features:
+ Simply press and hold to capture as many moments as you’d like in 1080p HD and 4K.
+ Easily access advanced camera controls for FPS and stabilization.
+ Choose from over 20 stunning filters by simply sliding left and right. Adjust the intensity by sliding up and down.
+ Add music from your iTunes library or our hand-picked music library to give your video the perfect mood
+ Narrate your videos with one-touch voice overs.
+ Trim, reorder, and delete clips from your video with a beautiful and simple interface
+ Adjust speed and volume on every clip for complete creative control.
+ Share your Spark with your friends on Instagram Stories, TikTok, IGTV, Snapchat, iMessage, or anywhere else.

Spark Camera gives everyone the ability to create beautiful video memories.

Have questions, problems, or feedback? Reach out to us at team@sparkcamera.com

ElanorMc , 07/25/2019

My Favorite App of All Time, My Memory Maker

I started using Spark 4 years ago. It was exactly what I wanted and imagined - a simple video app that can help me document my travels. The simplicity of the interface makes a world of difference! You simply film along, a few seconds at a time, your trip, and in the end easily turn it into a music video that you can look back on. However, at the time the app was far from perfect. It didn’t have transitions so the video always cut off in an abrupt way. But what I love the most about this app is that the people behind it keep improving it over the years! I wrote to them and right away they wrote me back this nice email telling me about new functions they added to help me achieve a smoother fade-in and fade-out. I also told them my problems with sound transition. And literally the next update came with just the functions I needed. I can’t say it was my suggestion that prompted this, but I do know that they genuinely listen to their users’ feedback and are making the app better all the time! For that reason alone, Spark is so worth supporting because its makers genuinely care about how us users feel and they incorporate our feedback. Keep up the good work Team Spark! I now have over 80 videos of travel memories to look back on, I look forward to making many many many more ❤️

Jared75684 , 02/23/2021

Exceptional App, Great Execution but, Subscription Yearly

Love this app. It’s absolutely phenomenal for what it’s intended purpose is. It isn’t a HARDCORE film editor, but you can certainly do quite a lot with it. It isn’t FilMic or anything like that, but it does give you a very impressive tools suite, a great app that doesn’t ever crash, a BEAUTIFUL UI...and it’s just intuitive. Swipes and Taps mostly to control most functions, which is natural to most people now.

My only complaint — the subscription fee. Why not just let me BUY the app once, and you charge me an appropriate purchase price? I hate subscribing yearly or monthly to anything, and I struggle with that on this app. I wish they’d give you the option to permabuy, subscribe monthly, or do a yearly sub — some LIKE subscriptions vs the large cost of some apps, but honestly at this point....consumer choice is key. Give us some options, Devs!

Otherwise AAA+++ with minimal gripes from me.

jaybroho , 05/27/2020

Great app but some issues

Really love the app. I use it every day as a way to record my own personal daily vlogs. But there are times when I’m exporting that when it completes the ring it doesn’t do anything. It just waits. So I usually have to force close reopen then try again. Usually it’ll work again but gets frustrating waiting twice. The other thing is after recording a fairly long single clip (> 50 sec) there’s a bit of a delay before I can start recording again. Sometimes I immediately press to record but if it’s in the middle of that delay it doesn’t capture anything. So I end up wasting a shot. And the last issue I’d really love to have and been asking for a few years now is to have orientation lock. I shoot in landscape and because you don’t have a lock I need to keep my phone “orientation free” to accommodate Spark. It’s not a big deal most of the time except when I’m lying down on my side and everything rotates. So having a lock on the video orientation would be great.

Otherwise great app!
