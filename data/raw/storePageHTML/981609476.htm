Description

The BuzzFeed News app brings you the most important and most interesting stories from around the world, hand-picked by our editors.

With the BuzzFeed News app for iOS, you’ll quickly catch up on what’s going on around the world.

We make the news easy to follow. Whether it’s a tweet or a Vine, a BuzzFeed News exclusive or a compelling piece of reporting from elsewhere, our editors will make sure you don’t miss out on what people are talking about.

We make the news easy to understand. Our editors know that when something happens, the who, why, when, and how matter just as much as the what. We’ll make sure you’ve got context and background to major news events as they happen.

We make the news easy to manage. Need to know when something big is happening in the world of technology, politics, or entertainment? Need the latest updates on major breaking news? Passionate about sports or LGBT issues? We’ve got you covered with customizable notifications.

We make the news easy to share. Read something in the app that moves you? Share it with a friend or colleague, straight from the app.

A friend recommended this app and I was pleasantly surprised at just how refreshingly different it is. DerbyBird , 07/16/2020

Great but has typos

Buzzfeed News is a great source for news. In fact, it’s my main source. They provide a great mix of mainstream news and unexpected or uncommon stories. I still think about a piece published about the treatment of children in the 20th century in orphanages. However, I have two big complaints, which is why two stars are missing.
1.) Grammar mistakes. For such a large company, it’s a little ridiculous how often a word isn’t quite right or there’s a random period with too many spaces. Mistakes with punctuation are more common then spelling, but still. It’s just unprofessional.
2.) During the pandemic, I have not seen very much about the actual pandemic other then a few statistics. It would be nice to see more because it’s so important to know about right now.
Despite this, it is still my main news source. These should be easy fixes.

joedavenport , 05/01/2017

From 5 star app to deleted.

Buzzfeed News started out as a clean app with straightforward headlines and nice summaries, but now it is a little too biased. I prefer news stories to be more centered and objective rather than a predictable lean in one direction or the other.

I deleted the app because my Apple Watch kept giving me updates even after I had turned them off. Then I deleted the app and I received another notification, which seemed impossible! I finally figured out the mystery, I think that BuzzFeed News had automatically liked itself on Apple News. If you're having issues with unwanted notifications, search for BuzzFeed News in your Apple News and click the heart icon at the top.
