Great for a Simple Websitr

This is a cool tool for someone who wants to quickly get a website up on the run. It is very easy to use. I do not think this makes a great long term plan for your website unless you plan on keeping it simple. The web domain is theirs you cannot put the site on your hosting so you have to forward your domain to their site. You also can only develop one webpage per subscription which makes the $80 a year seem a bit steep. If you could develop multiple sites for that cost I would probably subscribe for the year. But with the cost per year and the lack of ability to use your own web domain I don’t think I would spend that much long term. $7 a month for a couple of months until you get your website fully developed is not a bad deal however.

Developer Response ,

Thanks for taking the time to put virtual pen to paper. We are grateful. You are right that we are aiming to help people get set up quickly and easily, and I'm glad you agree Simpl does that. Just to clarify on web domains, you *can* use your own domain, as we can now connect a third party domain to your website. Just contact our Support desk at http://simpl.zendesk.com and we'll walk you through the steps. We hope to allow multiple websites in future to provide even more value to our customers. We're glad you appreciate the 30 day free trial too.
