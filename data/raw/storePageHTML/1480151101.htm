Description

Experience the Daf in a whole new light. Uncover groundbreaking insights and explore the topics you love through a personalized feed of shiurim, bite-sized clips and supplementary resources. Download All Daf for full access, FREE.

LEARN THE DAF, DAILY
Delve into the daf with dynamic & world-renowned lecturers such as Rabbi Moshe Elefant and Rabbi Shalom Rosner

EXPLORE THE DAF YOUR WAY
Whether you’re a history buff, thrive on a good halacha debate, or enjoy stories from Nach, discover bite-sized clips related to that day’s daf on subjects you’d love to learn more about.

SET YOUR OWN COURSE
You set the pace, you choose the content. Curate your feed with the speakers and topics you like at your preferred level and pace.


FITS YOUR SCHEDULE
Clips range from 60 seconds to 45 minutes, so you can make the most of whatever time you have.

CAREFULLY CURATED CONTENT
Every video, audio track, discussion, and article on the app has been carefully chosen for the valuable insights offered.

Dov+ , 03/09/2020

Feature requests

The app and website are great, you should definitely download it and more importantly, use it.

Feature requests:

1. Ability to go forwards/backwards by 15 seconds from the lock screen. Similar to a podcast app.

2. Have the Sefaria save your place and English on/off status when you go back and forth from the audio player screen to Sefaria screen.

3. Make the audio control buttons bigger.

4. Have the app save your place in a shiur when you exit the shiur. Sometimes I accidentally exit a shiur, and it takes a while to find my place again.

These are just suggestions that would make my workflow easier, can’t speak for everyone of course.

All in all a terrific, stable, and intuitive app that has made Daf Yomi learning and chazarah so much more accessible to me. Well done, and thank you!
