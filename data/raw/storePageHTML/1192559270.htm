Description

Sword Art Online: Memory Defrag is a free 2D anime adventure action RPG game inspired by classic arcade beat-em ups with simple intuitive one touch controls!

Experience the action RPG that contains every aspect of Sword Art Online as characters crossover from the manga, games, and side stories all in one epic anime adventure!

HUNDREDS OF Sword Art Online CHARACTERS AT YOUR FINGERTIPS
- Collect beloved characters such as Kirito, Sinon, Yuna, and LLenn and more daring sword fighters from the SAO manga and anime!
- Upgrade characters with unique weapons and equipment, such as the Elucidator and Dark Repulser swords!
- All new versions of beloved characters available only here!

FLUID, ACTION RPG GAMEPLAY
- Tap to attack and unleash blistering combos!
- Swipe to dodge devastating attacks from terrifying floor bosses!
- Block & parry, then SWITCH! to finish your foes.

ENTHRALLING STORY
- Relive the classic arcs of the popular Japanese anime adventure and manga series as our heroes traverse the virtual MMO RPG of Sword Art Online!
- Experience virtual action-packed game adventures from outside of the anime such as Ordinal Scale and SAO Alternative: GGO!
- Explore all-new stories unique to SAO: Memory Defrag!

LIVE ONLINE MULTIPLAYER
- Party up with friends and other players in LIVE co-op missions!
- Become a Healer, Attacker, or other traditional RPG roles and unleash devastating combo moves with your allies
- OR go lone wolf and fight to clear SAO in Solo mode
- Join a guild and work together to complete special quests for exciting rewards!

Guide Kirito and the Assault Team through the virtual RPG world of Sword Art Online, as they fight to clear 100 floors of Aincrad and free themselves from the death game! Gain new allies in Alfheim Online and battle to ascend the World Tree. Travel to the barren world of Gun Gale Online to stop the killer “Death Gun” before he strikes again!

What are you waiting for? The fate of the virtual world rests in your hands! LINK START!!!

What happened to the OGs?

Listen I played this game for 4 years now meaning I’ve been playing since the birth of this game and it was joyful during the first year or two. Then all the bug fixes screwed everything up. All the ogs lost their accounts and all their hard work. I was a level 351 on my account and lost all my stats! It was heart breaking and I was devastated. I just reinstalled this game and it’s still good but it does need a kink to it. See I when I reinstalled the game I instantly became a level 58 in 12 minutes of playing. I don’t know if it was my grinding tactics coming back to me but I leveled all my characters up to max level in 25 minutes. I don’t think starters should have the ability to max all their characters out at level 5. Also when your start and log in you get 200x of the level potions that level your character up 25x than the 1x level potion. See when I did that I then was able to 1 tap every single boss in main quests and extra quests. So if you could just not have the starters be more powerful than the people such as myself being an OG and a Professional Grinder of this game. That would be great. Over all it’s an amazing game and keep pushing out new updates.

TheIdleOne(Username) , 03/02/2018

Amazing!

I’m absolutely in love with this game! It takes up a good portion of my time, making sure I’m leveling up and getting better with each character I use! However, I have a few complaints I’d definitely like to address that bug me. For one, I don’t plan on putting money into the game, despite the fact I want this game to be more successful, so it makes it very very difficult to unlock new characters when the cost to unlock the x10 packs be 125 or 250 of income to unlock. Once you’re far into the game, you get stuck at a certain point where everything becomes challenging and you can’t even proceed. I also dislike how the level cap is 80, requiring you to get a special upgrading material to increase your leveling limits. The process is too abnormally long in my opinion and it frustrates me that I can’t even proceed in the game with my 2 level 80 characters. I’m forced to use my other characters and level them up, which still buys my time and I still enjoy to do, but I’d like to actually have a completely maxed out character instead of having to leave things off right before it’s finished. Aside from all this, I’m really enjoying the fact that you guys are going out of your way to add in new events so often! The constant work is very pleasing and I definitely encourage you to keep going! I hope that I won’t come to the point where I find the game boring because of the aforementioned complaints, but I’ll continue to enjoy this amazing game!

Crux010 , 06/28/2018

Favorite game, needs more team play

I just want to start by saying after years of downloading and playing games on the AppStore, this game has become my favorite game of all time. I’ve played for over 100 days now and I don’t have many complaints. For people talking about the scouting prices, as long as you do the events there will never be a scout you cannot do at least once. Keep up with what’s going on and you will be fine. Only thing that’s bugged me about this game is the lack of team play/guild development. Guilds don’t feel like anything more then random players put together. I’ve been in my guild for over 30 days now and haven’t talked to a single member of it once. And it’s a full 10 person active guild. Maybe you can make a guild chat and increase the guild sizes to make it feel more like a community. Another thing please add a “stay in party” button in coop. It is the most annoying thing when you find an amazing party to have to leave and join again. Please make it so you can just stay in the party, would make farming so much more easier. That’s all I have to say for now, would highly recommend the game to anyone looking for a fun competitive time.
