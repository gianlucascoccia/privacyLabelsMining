Description

Sticker Maker Meme Generator, a must-have app to create personal stickers, which has massive users around the world.
It provides a wide range of popular sticker packs and also supports to create your own personal stickers, hilarious stickers and memes packs by the functions of Cutout, Text, Stroke, Erase and more. What’s more, you can add them to WhatsApp, Telegram or iMessage with one click.

Awesome Features:
√ Easy to use, you can create your own personal stickers in a few minutes.
√ Wide range of popular interesting sticker packs, you can choose your favorite as you like.
√ Create stickers with A.I. & Magic Cutout.
√ Simply draw a circle around your target, the object will magically get separated from the background.
√ The brush can restore the selected area of the cutout, and you can get the shape you want
√ Draw to erase the needless background, you can also adjust the size of the brush.
√ Add a distinctive personal touch to your stickers with colorful borders.
√ Providing text templates, you can add your own text to let your stickers talk in an instant.
√ Varieties of popular sticker materials to help you make Meme Packs.
√ Choose your favorite image format to save, it supports jpg and png now.
√ Impress your friends by posting the stickers on social networks such as Facebook, Twitter, WhatsApp.
√ Use your custom stickers to create stickers pack then add it to WhatsApp, Telegram or iMessage.
√ Now you can search what stickers you want in our app

Want to make your chat more interesting? Just come and use Sticker Maker Meme Generator to make funny personal stickers.

I think this has potential to be great

I gave this app three stars instead of four because I felt there were some things missing that need to be changed to make it truly great.

I would like to say that I really think that making your own stickers and all the options given is phenomenal. It really is easy to make your own stickers and edit out things with the adjustable eraser.
When it comes to organizing and creating your own sticker packs that is a brilliant move.
I think it is also essential to be able to share your sticker pack with friends. The app flows well and has the nice keyboard capabilities to easily switch to the app.

Now for the reasons that I didn’t give this app full stars or even four.
I don’t like how the stickers that I’ve made are at the bottom of the list of stickers other people have made it that are offered in the app. I would think it would be better to have the ones made by the person to take priority and be at the top. Maybe a good fix would to have the option to pick the stickers you want to pop up in the keyboard function.

I almost don’t use the app because I don’t want to have to scroll all the way to the bottom to the count on packs I made every time I want to share a sticker. And some of the ones suggested by you guys aren’t that funny or great or to inappropriate. So it would be nice to have more customization so it becomes my number one five star sticker app. But for now it’s a number 3 good and has potential but isn’t quite there.

mkoggg , 07/30/2020

Fun but hard to use

I just downloaded this app and it’s pretty awesome. So fun to make your own stickers! However, within just 15 minutes of using it, I’ve had some real issues. First off, there are so many ads that it disrupts the experience. Every time I go to do something new or have to reopen the app, I get an ad. Second, If you want to send the sticker to someone via the app, there is no clear way to close the message after it’s been sent to go back to the app. Your only option is to close it and then reopen and watch an ad before you can do anything. Thirdly, unlike some of the other sticker apps I’ve used, this one has a pop up magnifying glass, which is great if you want to be really detailed, but extremely hard to use as it moves around on the screen while you’re making edits.

Overall, it’s a cool app but difficult and annoying to use. Will keep it for the times when I have a lot of patience but otherwise is kind of useless.
