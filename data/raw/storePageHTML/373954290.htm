Description

Subscribe to The Sun, Britain’s No1 newspaper, packed with the latest news, gossip & sport headlines, sent straight to your device every day.

Bursting with news, entertainment, showbiz, celebrity exclusives, sport and more, a subscription to The Sun Digital Newspaper lets you read all the latest stories from around the world in today’s edition, as well as the previous 8 day’s editions too.

• The best football action from The Sun Sport team
• Discover the latest and greatest shows in the TV Mag
• All the latest fashion, beauty tips and celebrity news in Fabulous
• Hundreds of puzzles ready to play
• All your daily favourites like Dear Deidre and Mystic Meg
• Simple navigation so you can turn each page just like the real paper
• Ready to read wherever you are
