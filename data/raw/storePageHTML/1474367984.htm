Description

vTag uses your voice, expressions, body movements, and style to create full-body, 3D animoji messages that live in the real world! Send vTags to your friends, drop them on the map or share your creations to your social channels.

Make Yourself
Design a vMe as individual as you are. Choose your look, choose your style, and change it up as often as you change your mood! Add 3D backgrounds, animations and more.

Be Yourself
Bring your avatar to life! Use your avatar, your facial expressions*, your moves** and your voice to create fun vTags to share with friends.

Lose Yourself
Level up your content creation with vTag! Dance, chat, and interact with your vMe to create unique videos for TikTok, Instagram Stories, Snapchat and more!

Leave Yourself
Use vTag’s AR camera to bring your vMe into the real world! DM your vTags to friends in full 3D or tag messages to real-world locations for them to discover later.

Got any feedback? Email us at feedback@vtag.com and let us know!

*Face capture is only available for users with an iPhone X or above, but all compatible iPhones can make awesome vTags.
**Body tracking available for iPhones XS and above.

Theogore , 01/30/2021

Excellent Avatar App

I try the newest avatar and digital puppet apps quite frequently and this by far is the most comprehensive, easiest and cheapest by far. I shudder to think that if this becomes a subscription app, what I would do, but suffice to say, it covers most of my needs to create videos.

The things that make this app great are ability to make multiple avatars (great for costuming a single character - can we duplicate an avatar?), the quickness with which it allows a user to jump in, the avatar guide at the beginning of onboarding, great animations, cool fashions, the option to do both 2D and 3D and various ways to share (not just on vTag’s site).

I have only begun to play with the possibilities of this great avatar app and look forward to a Vtag craze that will push it to more expansion and customizable options.

That said I have a few wishes that might make this a better app for myself and maybe others:

- The ability to do more tweeking on facial differences using only the initial snapshot as a guide.
- Opaque fifties style sunglasses (Beatnik).
- More androgynous clothing styles (glam rock, goth, etc).
- Landscape mode in recording and exporting.
- Sitting animations (PLEASE! This will make all the low budget Vtubers grab this app in a hot second).

I am going to share this app in my animation groups and forums and also to another developer who wants to know how to make a great avatar/vtuber app.

Thanks a million and I support vTag 100%.
