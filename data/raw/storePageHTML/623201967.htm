Description

The South China Morning Post (SCMP) has been the most authoritative voice reporting on China and Asia for more than a century. As Hong Kong’s English-language newspaper of record, our international team of Asia experts, reporters, journalists, photographers and filmmakers are dedicated to providing breaking news and analysis from Hong Kong, China and beyond.
Our unique home base of Hong Kong allows us to have in-depth understanding of the China story from an insider’s perspective, while enjoying a high level of press freedom and independence granted by the city’s special constitutional principle of “One country, two systems”. Today, our dedication to representing the region with our editorial motto, “truth and fairness” is more relevant than ever before.

AWARD-WINNING JOURNALISM
Download the South China Morning Post app and receive instant access to the SCMP’s award-winning journalism including coverage of the latest breaking news, both regionally and globally, including:
Coronavirus, US-China trade war, Trump vs Xi Jinping, the Hong Kong National Security law, Hong Kong protests, Brexit, Global politics, Climate change and much much more.

KEY FEATURES
Breaking news alerts to keep you updated with exclusives, live coverage and developing stories
Article bookmarking to save your favourite features and articles
‘My News’ feed personalisation allowing you to follow the latest from your favourite topic, column or author

CONTENT AND COVERAGE
Keep informed with the latest Hong Kong and China headlines, opinion pieces, plus stories from the rest of the world
Full access to all SCMP content, including publications such as Post Magazine, This Week in Asia and Style
Full access to SCMP’s award-winning journalism including Hong Kong news, China news, Asia news, Business, Politics, Economy, Lifestyle, Tech, Health, and Sports
Wide-range of regional topics from our Asia experts including North Korea, China’s Economy, Belt and Road, K-pop, as-well-as a spotlight on China’s tech-giants such as Tencent, Baidu and Alibaba, Huawei and Xiaomi
Explore new ways to discover our insightful, unique content and current issues with SCMP Films, SCMP Spotlight and SCMP Podcasts, plus video, infographics, pictures and special reports

SUBSCRIPTION OPTIONS
Download the app for free and enjoy a limited number of free stories each month. Get unlimited access for just US$10.49/month or US$124.99/year.
In addition to unlimited access on the South China Morning Post iPhone and iPad apps, your subscription comes with complementary web access to scmp.com.
Payment will be charged to your iTunes account when you have confirmed your purchase.
Your subscription will auto-renew for US$10.49 each month or US$124.99 each year unless you cancel 24 hours before the end of the existing subscription period. Auto-renewal can be disabled at any time in the Account Settings section of the App Store. Please note that it is not possible to cancel your current subscription during an active subscription period.
