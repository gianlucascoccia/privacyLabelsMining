Description

This application is based on the much talked about challenge whose goal is to save money. The idea is simple and ideal for those who can not plan and save money. Sure, this is a great way to save money during the year.

The challenge is to save a little bit each week. In the first, $1.00 in the second, $2.00, and so on. At the end of the challenge, that is, at the end of the 52 weeks that have the year, you will have $1,378.00 to help with Christmas shopping, on that vacation trip, anyway, use as you wish.

Anyone who has a little extra finances can save a greater amount of money every week. And if you deposit the money into the savings, you still get an extra gain.

App features include:

- Add a challenge, with value and start date, leaving with the app the responsibility to manage all dates and values ​​from there;
- Follow the challenge created over the weeks and have control of the deposits;
- Receive weekly notifications as a reminder for deposits.
