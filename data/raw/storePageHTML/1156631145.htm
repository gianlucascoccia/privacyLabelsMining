Description

MURAL is the leading digital workspace for visual collaboration in the enterprise. Teams depend on MURAL to understand and solve problems and build consensus using visual methods. More than an online whiteboard, MURAL enables innovation at scale by providing a platform for everything from product strategy and planning to leading immersive workshops using agile and design thinking methodologies. Industry-leading teams at companies including IBM, IDEO, Autodesk, Intuit, GitHub, and Atlassian use MURAL to work together — at any time and from anywhere.

The MURAL iOS app enables collaboration from your iPhone or iPad. Collaborate using MURAL’s iOS app with the capabilities you need to for visual thinking and guided teamwork.

- Freehand draw with a variety of colors and line sizes with support for the Apple Pencil on iPad.
- Share ideas using sticky notes and text boxes with formatting options at your fingertips.
- Add another dimension to your visual communication with easy access to the icon library, full of customizable shapes, symbols, emojis, and more.
- Lead more productive and engaging collaboration sessions with Facilitation Superpowers such as voting, timer, comments, and more.
- Enhance your mural by importing images and GIFs from the MURAL library, your camera roll, or by scanning sticky notes with MURAL Scan (beta).
- Easily invite collaborators to your mural with share links and customizable share settings.
- Export a mural as a PDF or an image.
- Send a mural to a touchscreen for in-person collaboration sessions on large screen displays.

With the MURAL iOS app, the leading visual collaboration platform is available to you wherever you go.
