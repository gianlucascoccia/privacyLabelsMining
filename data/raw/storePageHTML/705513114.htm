Description

Formerly known as APICS Learn It, the APICS Dictionary app gives users free access to 4,890 terms and definitions from the APICS Dictionary. Expand your supply chain vocabulary with Term of the Day and flashcard features. Learn more at apics.org/DictionaryApp. Please direct questions to service@apics.org.

Features:
- 4,890 terms and definitions from the APICS Dictionary, 16th edition
- Term of the day
- Flashcards study tool, with game end-of-game answer summaries, and “save for later” features to customize your learning.
- Flashcard categories for all APICS credential exams: CSCP, CLTD, SCOR-P, and the newly reconfigured 2-module CPIM exam.
- Offline access – no internet connection needed to use
