Description

Weesurf recommends you when and where you can go surfing, kitesurfing, windsurfing, sailing, stand-up paddle boarding, based on the most reliable weather forecasts, your location and your level. Become a member of the community, follow your friends, meet new people and share your sessions together.


*** OUR COMMITMENTS ***

> Weather forecast for the waves, the wind and the tide
> Reliable data
> A valued user experience
> Available all around the world
> Available for all water sports: surfing, bodyboarding, kitesurfing, windsurfing, stand-up paddle boarding, sailing, fishing, etc


*** THE UNIQUE ADVANTAGES ON WEESURF ***

** EXCLUSIVE ** get real-time conditions with LIVE REPORTS from the community, and find out if the spot is crowded
** EXCLUSIVE ** set a CUSTOM FORECAST ALERT to receive a push when it’s good to go on your favorite spots
** EXCLUSIVE ** add your OWN SPOTS anywhere on the map to get the full detailed forecasts on these spots

+ Explore the MAP with +4,000 waves and wind spots all around the world
+ All spots are sorted by LEVEL: beginner, intermediate, expert
+ Forecasts are EASY TO READ with the waves, the wind and the tide on the same view
+ Complete information of TIDES with water levels and tide times
+ The RADAR in satellite view makes it easy to see the best swell and wind directions
+ Save your best spots in your FAVORITES
+ Go to your spot easily thanks to the GPS

BE PART OF THE COMMUNITY, follow your friends, meet new people and share your sessions together


*** YOUR ALL-ACCESS PASS WITH YOUR PREMIUM MEMBERSHIP ***

Premium version surrf cams are just advertisements

I’ve been using the free version of this app for a while now and really like it. I decided to upgrade to premium to get access to the live surf camera feed that was available with a premium subscription. Unfortunately this is a complete scam. Once subscribed, I clicked on the cam to see how the surf looked and was taken to an external website called thesurfersview that was just a page of advertisements. I clicked around at what appeared to be the video I was trying to find, but each embedded video was just another advertisement.
