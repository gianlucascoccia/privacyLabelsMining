WhatsYerHeritage , 09/25/2020

I’m sold

At first I thought “do I really need this app. I already log all my practice session details, plan my routine items in advance, etc”. But the reality is this app does make the whole process more efficient and gives you handy stats at a glance. And it saves time. Without it you have to rewrite the same notes every day (15 minutes on diminished arpeggios, 15 minutes on whole tone arpeggios, etc.). With this app, all of that is preconfigured ahead of time so that you just create the list of things you want in your to-do list and then tick them off as you go. Serious musicians know that time one of our most precious commodities and this app helps to maximize it. Also, it’s really nice not to have to toggle between different apps to get drones, metronomes etc. I’m loving this thing!

allbassnotes , 03/18/2019

Super-effective Practice Tool

Modacity has truly helped me change practice routine in all the best ways! The ability to organize playlists and practice items allows me to leverage my practice time. The improvements and mastery features then further help me figure out what really needs my attention.
My biggest success with Modacity was in March/February 2018. I was preparing to go on tour with a bluegrass group playing bass and singing harmony (with a little lead thrown in there), and I had 20+ songs to memorize. It was a truly daunting challenge, and I procrastinated. About 2 weeks from the departure date, I finally decided that it was time to learn these tunes. Using the playlist feature, it helped me see all of what I needed to do, and the mastery feature let me see what needed work. With about 1.5-2 hours of practice a day and some interleaving strategies, I solidly memorized those songs. I didn’t even have to worry about memory lapse on the tour! Using Modacity as a tool for effectiveness let me just have fun. Hope it helps others keep pickin’ and grinnin’!

nschnitzs , 01/27/2020

Fantastic design and great features

After seeing this app recommended on auditioncafe by Jason Heath, I immediately downloaded it. I love all of the reminders and ways you can really customize your ability to practice. This system really works great with my calendar that I’ve planned out what I’ll be practicing when to allow me to remind me and keep me focused!

I am in absolute LOVE with the interface and UX design- incredible. I do wish, however, that features were able to be unlocked with a one-time payment rather than having to pay monthly for the service. I like to just get it out of the way rather than have the feeling of a recurring payment dragging on me for only a couple of features that could be replaced with an app that I already have (like TonalEnergy for the met and drone).

I do absolutely love how this app makes me feel and I can’t wait to start practicing with it.
