Description

"Amazing. I owe this app so much for lowering my stress levels and helping me avoid constant 24/7 conversation with clients.” Ben Louis, Tattoo Artist, @benlouistattoos

Like a personal secretary at your fingertips, Bookedin takes care of all of your time-consuming appointment booking tasks. The simple to set up, easy-to-use appointment booking system that’ll take your business to the next level!

Everything you and your team need to get booked solid, save time, reduce stress, and impress your clients.

POWERFUL APPOINTMENT SCHEDULER
• Secure & private appointment app for you & your staff
• Easily adjust availability: block time off, schedule lunch breaks, set business hours
• One-time & recurring appointment booking
• Handy notifications for new appointments, payments, cancellations

EASY ONLINE APPOINTMENT BOOKING
• Branded online booking link for your clients. Ex: bookedin.com/book/bobs-barbershop
• Share via Facebook, Instagram, Email, WhatsApp, etc.
• Clients don’t need to download the app or remember a password
• Directory listing for your business

APPOINTMENT CONFIRMATIONS & REMINDERS
• Automated text* & email reminders
• Clients can confirm or cancel appointment via email or text
• Unlimited messages sent

PAYMENT COLLECTION
• Eliminate no-shows! Collect deposit payments when clients schedule appointments online
• Clients pay via PayPal or credit card: Visa, Mastercard, AMEX, Discover
• Automatic payment receipts
• Works with any free business PayPal account

CLIENT HISTORY & DATABASE
• Track client list, profiles & write private notes
• Appointment & payment history
• Click to call, text or email clients directly
• Private, secure & continuously backed up data

BONUS WEB FEATURES
Log in to bookedin.com via desktop or tablet to access extra time-saving features!

• Web appointment manager: month, week, day, team split calendar view
• Unlimited staff logins
• Profile photo, logo & colors
• Custom appointment booking form fields
• Custom reminder emails
• Import / export client list
• Appointment cancellation policy
• Web & social appointment booking button & calendar integration
• 2-way personal calendar sync: Google, iCloud, Outlook, Office365, Microsoft Exchange
• Client email invoicing
• Automatic refund for cancelled appointments

Enjoy all Pro Bookedin features & unlimited appointment booking FREE for 14 days! Or choose Bookedin's basic free plan which includes limited features and 5 appointments per month.

*Text reminders available in USA & Canada
