Description

TOR Browser + VPN is a unique combo app, giving you the power of an anonymous Browser and the intractability of a VPN connection.

FREE features :
- TOR Network: Your Internet access will be tunneled over the TOR Network when you use Private Browser.
- No signup or registration, no complex settings required. Just one button to launch a VPN powered Onion Browser!
- No Logs: We do not track or keep any logs of users and their activities.
- Encryption: Data in and out of your device is encrypted completely.
- Stay Anonymous: Your IP address remains hidden and not revealed to the websites you visit. When you use a VPN the traffic from your browser will show up as being originated from one of our servers.
- Access .onion websites (dark web) only accessible through the TOR network.
- Block malicious scripts inside insecure wireless public networks authentication webpages.
- On top of privacy, don't miss out the most useful browser capabilities like multiple tabs, search, bookmarks and annoying popover blocking, cookie and cache management.
- Audio and Video playback is fully supported.

Privacy concerns are real and global use of Tor network is a reliable way of browsing the free internet.

Tor Browser + VPN is built with the simple belief that privacy is everybody's right and should be protected under all circumstances.

WHY USE Tor Browser + VPN
"Using Tor Browser + VPN gives you an extra layer of privacy because the VPN encryption prevents the Tor entry node from exposing your exit node IP address. The VPN will encrypt some Internet traffic that Tor network does not support, like ICMP traffic. It also prevents your ISP from knowing you are connecting to Tor network."

This app takes care of security breaches exposed by TOR network's entry and exit nodes, something no other app on App Store support. Enjoy the fastest and secure Tor Browser + VPN and the freedom to choose between VPN tunnels (Anonymous Connection) across the US, Europe, Asia, far-east and Australia.

One tap solution to hide your IP address, be anonymous and access desired content with privacy.

IMPORTANT!!! The app's anonymity feature does not encourage any violation of local laws, hence adherence to the local laws while using the app is mandatory.

HOW Tor Browser + VPN WORKS?
"The Onion Router Network protects you by bouncing your communications around a distributed network of relays run by servers all around the world. It makes it impossible for anyone watching your Internet connection to learn what sites you visit and your physical location. For international travellers, the ability to access different kinds of contents from abroad is essential. Access social media sites like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Snapchat, LinkedIn, Google services, Google maps and many more."
