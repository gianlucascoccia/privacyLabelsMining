Description

The Life Time Training app helps you achieve your fitness goals by delivering personalized programs created by certified Life Time Personal Trainers. Life Time Training will help you stay committed to a Healthy Way of Life through comprehensive training plans, interactive messaging and progress reports.

IN APP MESSAGING
Stay accountable through instant messaging with your Trainer and in app Community Groups.

CUSTOM PROGRAMS
Created by a certified Life Time Personal Trainer, your program will be customized to your goals and abilities.

EXERCISE VIDEO LIBRARY
View videos of every exercise prescribed by your Personal Trainer to see proper form and technique.

WORKOUT CALENDAR
See a calendar view of your program to know what is coming and what you've already accomplished.

PROGRAM TRACKING
Track your cardio and strength workouts, body measurements and tasks to stay motivated.

PROGRESS TRACKING
Take progress photos and record lifting measurements as you progress through your plan.

NUTRITION TRACKING
Sync with Fitbit or MyFitnessPal to track your daily nutrition.

INTEGRATION WITH APPLE HEALTH
Sync your daily stats to the app such as steps, sleep and more.

Missing Key Functions

1. Apple Watch support - You should be able to leave your phone in the locker. Bringing your phone to the workouts is a complete hassle.
2. Detect Gym Equipment integration - Apple Watch allows you to “tap” the gym equipment and sync all the work out data... treadmills, etc. You should be able to jump on the treadmill or other equipment, tap your Apple Watch, and have all the data sync into the app and into Apple Health/Activity.
3. Apple Health/Activity integration - The app should sync all your data to Apple Health/Activity. Too much data flowing into too many apps is very inconvenient. Having the one central location for all your data is great. This app should push data to Apple like most other workout apps do.

Crashes and turns my phone into a furnace

The app crashes after about 2 minutes of use, causes my phone (iPhone X) to heat up uncomfortably, and drained my battery very quickly too. Not a fan currently. Pretty much unusable when it crashes before I can enter my workouts.

ch196232 , 01/26/2020

Lots of Potential

There is a lot of potential with this app. The connection this app allows between you and your trainer - even the possibility for a completely virtual trainer - is great for goal tracking, accountability, and assistance/adjustments. The app has issues with connections. It currently doesn’t connect with MyFitnessPal and I’ve notified app support - they responded very quickly and said it was an issue on MFP’s side - so hopefully that will be corrected. I think a good feature would be to take the data from Health/Activity and pair it with your workout for better analytics and insights.
