I love this app

This app is so easy to use to log what you eat! It has a feature to scan bar codes on a lot of food/drink items. If scanned it logs the serving size and you adjust from there, if you are more or less, whatever quantity consumed. It is a great way to keep track of how much water you drank throughout the day and how many calories you have left after consuming foods. It also encourages you to eat healthy by giving a daily apple you try to fill. Another great feature is that it is able to sync it to your Apple Watch and it reads/adjusts your activity level to your calorie consumption. I have been using this for about a week now, (have tried others and lost interest or didn’t follow through) but I plan to continue to use this one. I would highly recommend this.
