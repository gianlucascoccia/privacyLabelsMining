Description

Enjoy unlimited listening and reading of audiobooks and e-books in English and other languages.

Find your favorite new stories through personal recommendations – discover everything from current bestsellers to acclaimed thrillers, unforgettable novels and gripping biographies.

UPGRADE YOUR READING EXPERIENCE

Popular features include:

• Download in advance, or stream on the go.
• Switch seamlessly between listening and reading.
• Adjust listening speed.
• Pre-set snooze with the sleep function.
• Set your own bookmarks and make notes.
• Discover new favorites through personal recommendations.
• Share your own book recommendations with friends.
• Let your child explore the library of children’s books with Kids Mode.

HOW IT WORKS

Try Storytel for free, and start discovering great stories. If you enjoy it, let your monthly subscription renew automatically –– you can cancel easily, anytime you want.
