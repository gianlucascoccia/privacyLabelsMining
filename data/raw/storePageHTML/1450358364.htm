Description

Rune finds teammates for games like Brawl Stars! Our matchmaking finds players with similar trophies and who speak the same language as you.

Connect on Rune’s voice chat so you can strategize with your teammate. Voice chat makes it much easier to win!

You can also play on voice chat with existing friends. Rune’s voice chat works in the background of any game and has team link support for Brawl Stars!

Try our app and let us know what you think at feedback@rune.ai

ATTENTION: You must provide a phone number to sign up for Rune. We use phone verification to keep Rune spam-free! :)
