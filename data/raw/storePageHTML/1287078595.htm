A Great App! But Does Have Stability Issues

First off, I truly love this app. The effects are incredible. Purchasing it was a no-brainer for me being an artist already.
I bought it to use with the latest ios on both an ipad mini and an ipod 6. Unfortunately, it just stopped working, some of the effects are not showing their respective thumbnails. And the ones that do show do not work. It goes to the processing screen but the progress bar just sits there frozen. The only way to get out of it is to close the app. Others are correct, there is no support anywhere in the app. To restore when reinstalled as I did to see if it would fix the app, there is a very tiny dollar sign and a circular arrow in the top left hand corner on the pay screen. Hardly visible, but it will restore your purchase. Unfortunately reinstalling the app did not fix it. Thumbnails for several effects are still missing and it still freezes at the process screen. I then dowloaded the app on my ipod6 to see if it was my ipad mini, but the same happens. Only wish the developer would respond to our reviews and correct the issues for us. This could have easily been a 5 star.
