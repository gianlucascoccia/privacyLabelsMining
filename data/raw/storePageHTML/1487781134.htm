Description

Get a new phone number and 20 FREE CREDITS without any contracts or providers! Get a burner number and keep your private information safe. Change your number anytime.

Numbr is a private phone number app specifically designed for iPhone to protect the caller‘s privacy. It is a simple and inexpensive way for those who need a disposable number to make international calls or to have an anonymous backup number for everyday situations. No contracts, transparent fees, absolute privacy.

NO HASSLES
Our numbers come without a contract. You don’t need a cell phone plan or another device to use your second phone number.

SIMPLE INSTRUCTIONS
Using your second phone number with Numbr is super-simple. Purchase a number, recharge credits and start calling.

TRANSPARENT FEES
Number calling fees are as transparent as possible. By using the in-app Tariff calculator, you can see how much it costs to call to different countries

UNRESTRICTED OWNERSHIP
Purchase and keep your number for as long as you need or dispose of them anytime.

GLOBAL COVERAGE
Make international calls to anywhere in the world for a fraction of the price.

Use Numbr in everyday situations:
• Buying or selling things on Craigslist, eBay, or other e-commerce platforms
• Dating
• Hosting apartments on Airbnb
• Using Uber
• Signing up (for platforms that prompt for a phone number)
• Applying for a job
• Working (e.g. as a second work number)

Phone numbers available in: USA, Canada, Australia, United Kingdom, Belgium.

PRICING
Numbr is free to download and comes with 20 free credits to make calls. Phone numbers and additional credits are available through subscriptions and in-app purchases.

Subscriptions for phone numbers:
• Weekly subscription
• Monthly subscription
• Yearly subscription

Subscriptions will be charged to your credit card through your iTunes account. Your subscription will automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You will not be able to cancel the subscription once activated. Manage your subscriptions in Account Settings after purchase.

IMPORTANT: Phone calls use prepaid credits. Area code availability varies. May not work with SMS shortcode services. Not for 911 or other emergency services.
