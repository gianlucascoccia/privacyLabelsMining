Description

Window shopping for those new shoes or that new dress? Want to be the first to know when it comes back in stock or when it goes on sale in your size and favorite color?

When you discover something you plan to buy, simply bookmark and save it to Shoptagr.

Shoptagr is your happy place to quickly save the products you plan to buy soon across multiple external sites and apps.

Save any clothing item from any store to Shoptagr and organize everything in one place. You'll automatically get notified in real-time when it’s on sale, low in stock or back in stock (right down to your size and preferred color).

Stop leaving items you want in the shopping cart or emailing yourself links - just install Shoptagr and never forget where you saw that beautiful purse.

Online window shopping has never been easier... and smarter!
--

SAVE IT NOW. SHOP IT LATER
The next time you discover an item you plan to buy soon, just save it to Shoptagr. It’ll automatically sync across your phone, tablet, and computer so you can come back to it whenever and wherever you’d like.

You can save to Shoptagr from most places, including your computer, Safari, Chrome, email, and your favorite shopping apps.

SMART NOTIFICATIONS
Receive real-time notifications when items you save drop in price, are low in stock or if they come back in stock, right down to your preferred size and color (including coupons)!

BEAUTIFUL AND SIMPLE
Keep everything you find in Shoptagr in a clean, easy-to-view layout that’s free of distractions. You can always get back to your items again when you are ready to finally make that purchase.

PRIVATE LISTS
Create your own private shopping lists with Shoptagr and organize items you discover across the web in one single place. Create lists such as Work Wardrobe, Birthday Wishes, Nights Out or Nice to Haves.
