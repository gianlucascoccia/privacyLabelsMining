Description

The official app of Model Rail magazine, a must-have for modellers and crafters alike.

Model Rail is packed full of the latest new model reviews, expert 'how to' projects and inspirational layouts. Fire your imagination with great track plans you can build, and inspiration from the real railway, not to mention Model Rail's exclusive range of model locomotives.

Our favourite features include:
- The full magazine delivered to your phone or tablet as soon as it arrives in shops.
- Browse and download back issues so you can learn new skills.
- Adjust the text size to make reading easy on your screen.
- Search for any term to learn something new or find a new product.

Do you love railway modelling? We do too! Model Rail magazine is jam-packed with the latest news, reviews, inspiring features, and the best practical techniques from some of the hobby’s most passionate modellers.

Every issue in this app is packed with:
- The country’s finest home and club layouts
- Stunning photography
- Unbiased and in-depth product reviews
- Expert tips and layout plans
- Inspirational real railway subjects

We cover every stage of the hobby – from buying a first train set, right through to museum standard finescale modelling.

In our unique ‘Workbench’ section, we tackle a wide range of modeling skills and projects. Our expert writers and model-makers will help you to get the most from your hobby and help you build the layout of your dreams.

Whether you're a novice or have enjoyed the hobby for many years, Model Rail magazine welcomes everyone.

We are the go-to publication to answer all your modelling questions, give in-depth step-by-step demonstrations and inspire your layouts.

Join the Model Rail community today!
