Description

Little Princess is the prettiest coloring book for girls! This activity provides hours of fun for little princesses and their parents!

We have created lots of cute and sweet drawings of beautiful unicorns, ponies, fairies, princesses and friendly animals. Little Princess is a complete magical world with everything girls like!

Girls can make masterpieces with our specially selected range of colors which fit together harmoniously in the finished artwork.

We have worked hard to create an easy-to-use app that makes coloring each drawing an enjoyable process. The app features a large number of smooth animations and transitions for your enjoyment.

We hope that you will enjoy our app and share it with your friends around the world!

The application is full of gorgeous illustrations created by professional artists:
Yaroslava Apollonova https://www.instagram.com/odnatamyara/
Julia Zhilyaeva https://www.instagram.com/yui_shi_/
Julia Volkova https://www.instagram.com/yaaawny/
