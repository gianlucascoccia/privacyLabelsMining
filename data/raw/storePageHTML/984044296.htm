Description

We've delivered more than 100 million orders!

You no longer have to leave your house or office to enjoy the best of your city. Rappi will bring you ANYTHING in minutes.

Need to do grocery shopping? Our trained shoppers will shop for you in the grocery store and deliver to your home at the exact same price as the stores. Want to eat at your favorite restaurant, but they don't deliver? We will pick up your food and deliver it in minutes. This is a new way of life. The very best of your city, delivered in minutes.

Ideal for FOODIES:
If you appreciate great food, you no longer have to order from the same 10 restaurants that offer fast delivery. Now, you can order from ANY restaurant in your city because we will order and deliver on your behalf.

Ideal for BUSY PEOPLE:
You no longer have to spend 3 hours going to the grocery store every two weeks. Save the trip to the grocery store for a very special occasion, or for when you are looking for that special cheese. Leave the rest to Rappi. Program an order. Save time and money.

Ideal for the GOOD LIFE:
With Rappi, everyone has a personal assistant that can do anything: from picking up medicine and bringing forgotten keys to paying invoices. When it comes to any errand you don't like to do, we are here to take care of you.

Ideal for YOU:
Rappi will change your life. Around the clock service, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Ideal for urgent needs. Ideal for any craving. Ideal for a better life.
