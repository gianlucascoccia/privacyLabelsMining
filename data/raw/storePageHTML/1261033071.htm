Description

The Aetna Health℠ app puts you in charge of your health care and benefits. With just a few taps, you can find a doctor and see what procedures may cost. Talk to a doctor by phone or video in just minutes. Pay your claims. And compare costs before you get care.

It’s simple. It’s all in one place. It’s all in the palm of your hand.

And we're not stopping there. We’re continuously adding new features and functionality to improve your experience.

This app is available for most Aetna® members. Members in select plans may not be eligible yet, but we’re continually working to expand access. If you aren’t eligible today, just check back in a few months.


Get plan details quickly and easily
• Pull up your ID card whenever you need it
• Track progress toward meeting your deductible for Medical and Dental plans
• See your personal PayFlex® HSA, HRA and FSA account balances
• Check your Explanation of Benefits and get info about what's covered for your medical and dental plans (for eligible members)
• View and filter up to 2 years’ claims by member name, provider, facility, service and date
• Pay claims using your bank account or credit card (for eligible members)

Search for and find care at your convenience
• Find in-network providers and offices by location, profile, specialty and cost
• Compare cost estimates for doctors’ visits and medical procedures
• Look up medications by their brand or generic names, and learn about side effects
• Search for MinuteClinic locations and services inside select CVS Pharmacy and Target locations, and schedule a MinuteClinic appointment
• Set up your Teladoc® account today to talk with a doctor anytime, from anywhere, when you need care (for eligible members)

Receive personal recommendations to help achieve your goals
• Get personalized health actions based on your profile. Set and track progress toward fitness and nutrition goals.
• Sync your data automatically by allowing Aetna Health to access your steps and exercise data from HealthKit

Eligible members with benefits through Aetna Pharmacy can also
• View prescription details for you and your family
• Get cost estimates for brand name or generic drugs
• Search for a nearby pharmacy: Filter by details, location and distance

* Aetna and MinuteClinic inside select CVS Pharmacy and Target locations (which either operates or provides certain management support services to MinuteClinic-branded walk-in clinics) are part of the CVS Health® family.
