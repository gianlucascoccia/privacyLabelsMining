Description

FormaCar is a unique automotive portal, which includes a 3D Configurator, a section of news from the world of cars, tuning and wheels, a catalog of wheels and tuning, and much more.

We offer fantastic graphics of cars in our 3D Configurator, the most accurate and authentic cars, wheels and tuning. A map with dealers and their contacts.

Mode - Augmented Reality (AR), now you can see any cars, tuning, wheels in reality, you just need to find the desired surface and point at it iPhone and will be able to see 1 to 1 any 3d object from our configurator.

We add cars every day, so if you do not find the car you are interested in, then you do not need to put us one star, and write us a letter on support@formacar.ru with the brand and model of the car that you want to see in our Configurator.

PS. Your comments and suggestions can be sent to support@formacar.ru
