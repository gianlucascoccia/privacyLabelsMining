Description

Keep track of your event task list directly in Seat Puzzle

Seat Puzzle is the best way to create lightweight mockups for your events. Easily organize your space, manage the guest list, and keep track of RSVPs.

Great for weddings and corporate events.

Download and try a free demo today!

Features include:

· Place tables and seats, the dance floor, the DJ Booth, and more!
· Maintain the guest list and keep track of RSVPs.
· Keep track of your task list and remaining todos. Check things off as you plan.
· Build the menu and see a breakdown of who ordered what.
· Import a guest list.
· Link guests together so that guests always sit with their plus-ones.
· Build a floor plan up to 180 ft by 180 ft. Supports metric system.
· Includes audio / video and outdoors items for even more types of events.
· Share your floor plans with your clients, event planner, wedding coordinator, or significant other.
· Print your floor plan or post it to social media.
· Works 100% offline.
· Automatically syncs with iCloud across all your devices.
· Create and manage unlimited events.


Professionals use Seat Puzzle to manage events for all their clients and look great on the job. Designed for iPhone and iPad.
