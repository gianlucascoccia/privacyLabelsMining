Description

“As sports officials, we cannot just know the rules – we must master the rules.” – Al Battista (NBA Eastern Regional Officials Scout)

RefHQ is your headquarters for studying, quizzing and mastering the rules – so you can have the answer in the heat of the moment.

Features:
- Quiz on multiple choice and T/F questions regarding rulebook and casebook topics. The question bank includes hundreds of quiz questions with new content added every month.
- Study specific rules, individual special topics (rule changes, etc.), or study all questions in shuffle mode
Compete with other users for the best streak of consecutive correctly answered questions
- Access IAABO pregame content to have on-hand for pregame conferences and preparation
Pregame notepad to store game-specific journals and notes

***RefHQ now supports the following sports and rule codes:
- Basketball (powered by IAABO)
- NFHS
- NCAA-M
- NCAA-W
- FIBA
- Lacrosse
- NFHS

Coming Soon:
- Soccer
- Baseball
- Lacrosse (NCAA)
- and more!
