Description

GUNSTRUCTION is a 3D weapon builder and customization tool that allows the user to prototype firearm builds using real world parts! This is not a toy gun builder, but a robust tool to help gun enthusiasts and newcomers plan out their dream build. With over 100 licensed manufacturers, and over 3,000 parts, we are the most extensive 3d builder, with new parts being added constantly!

GUNSTRUCTION features multiple weapon platforms, with more being added all the time. There is an in-app purchase available to unlock Photo Mode, Paint Mode, more save slots, and access to older "legacy parts".

Features:
•Over 20 points of customization per weapon platform
•Hundreds of accessories from bipods, vertical grips, and rail covers to red dots, night vision, and QD suppressors
•Accessory support for picatinny, keymod, M-lok and other mounting systems
•Select from off the rack rifles, or build your own from scratch
•New parts constantly added
•Real time conflict system helps take some of the guess work out of building a weapon
•Multiple caliber support for all platforms

In-App upgrade:
•Photo Booth mode allows you to snap shots of your virtual build in various environments
•Paint and Camo your weapon with over 300 choices
•Save and share up to 10 builds with friends
•Unlock more parts to customize with
•Keep track of cost, weight, overall length, and other key elements to help plan your build

littykittychickentitty , 12/01/2020

Best so far (updated dec 2020)

Great game super detailed customization, and the accuracy of the build aspect is great. I like the variety of parts but and lots of the glitching has gone away with the new update but it’s still not perfect and more in some of the smaller categories wouldn’t hurt. Quadrail options are lacking. The variety of build platforms and calibers has improved but some of the unique calibers are lacking in parts selection and at least one shotgun such as a mossberg 500/ Remington 870 would be great, and some of the muzzle brake/ suppressor options seemed to be removed which was very disappointing. The final area I feel could use some more attention is in the optics/ attachments categories, especially the bipods and red dot area. Its mostly micros and large tube designs where an eotech or aim point would be lovely. Some more trijicon would be nice too. But no other game comes close to the accuracy in disassembly and use of real part options.

crazymerc118 , 11/19/2018

The app I’ve been looking for over literally 10 years

I was told yesterday about this app by my buddy, who is a avid gun enthusiast like myself, and I have to say, I am very impressed. I’ve always loved apps that let me customize guns, but they rarely had the option to buy what you built, if they did, there weren't a lot of options. This solved that by including a great amount of products to choose from. On top of that, it lets you know if said products are compatible or not, which is fantastic. I definitely bought the full version so I could make multiple builds, I feel it was worth the $2.99. That being said, it’s 100% perfect, but nothing is. There are a few things that I saw that were off, like putting a flashlight attachment on some of the rails would look like the textures are clipping, which would mean it wouldn’t work on the real thing without moving it. Also there was some weird thing going on when putting some of the fore grips on, it would say it’s not compatible, even if was in the middle of the rail, but then I moved it slightly and it would work. Also when putting some attachments on a modular rail, it would clip through but still attach. Small things in my opinion and certainly not stopping me from using it. Love it and hope they add more products to work with.
