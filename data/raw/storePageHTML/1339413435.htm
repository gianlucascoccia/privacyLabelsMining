Subscriptions
If you choose to purchase a 1-month, 3-month, or 6-month subscription to access the Tankee Hero Pass including the ability to watch videos offline, disable video ads, and access exclusive avatars, payment will be charged to your iTunes account. Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hour prior to the end of each term. Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off at any time by going to your setting in the iTunes Store after purchase. Current price for a 1-month plan is $4.99 USD per month, the price for a 3-Month plan is $12.99 USD and the price for a 6-month plan is $21.99 USD; pricing may vary from country to country.
