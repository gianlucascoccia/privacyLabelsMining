Description

kiddZtube is a selection of safe Kids videos and learning games. Includes educational videos and nursery rhymes for children from popular Kids channels. All curated by teachers.

Start your FREE trial with access to full content. New videos available every week!

The safest videos for kids aged 2-8! A wide selection of the best Kids videos with no Ads, approved by Parents and Educators.

Videos are hand-picked from popular channels by real-life teachers who add in quiz questions to each.
Kids will learn spelling, counting, colors, and more - all by watching their favourite kids videos! Better yet, it is 100% safe, with teacher-approved material only.

Safe baby and toddler videos containing top nursery rhymes, songs and cartoons for children.

kiddZtube has a selection of kids videos divided in several categories: Songs, Cartoons, Stories, Learming Arts and Crafts, Workout at home, Funny videos, kids playing games and more.
