Description

VNTG is a vintage filter app and powerful photo editor.

EASY TO USE

VNTG is very easy to use. All you have to do is choose a filter you like, and that's it! Most of the work is already done. Then you can go to the editor and add parameters to your taste.


BECOME A PHOTO EDITING EXPERT

The editor provides limitless options from professional photography programs. You can change the color temperature, contrast and any other photograph parameters.

15+ HANDMADE RETRO FILTERS

The application has over 15 different retro filters made by hand by a professional photographer. They are universal and made for various kinds of photos and colors, imitating the effects of the 80s and 90s.
