Description

Your Home Screen, Your Way, Now Completely Clear!

Clear Spaces allows you to create Transparent Widgets that allow you to move your icons where ever you want.

It starts with taking a screenshot by putting your home screen into wiggle mode and swiping all the way to the right. Import that screenshot into 'Clear Spaces' and it'll crop the image down to fit perfectly and merge into the wallpaper.

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out at support@clearspaces.uk or @clearspaces_ on Twitter.

Developer Response ,

Hey! Thanks for your updated post. Apple has a limit on how many times a widget can update per day. We are working on providing more accurate time, but ultimately it boils down to how many times you open the app, so if the app isn’t opened periodically, your phone will assume you don’t care about the information on the widget, and therefore it updates less frequently. We are working to fix this as best as we can.
