Description

Book sitters, nannies and tutors for reliable child care —for everything from full-time to last-minute help. Trust and safety are our priority. All sitters are background checked, and reviewed by our team. Whether you need a nanny with teacher experience to help with distance learning or a learning pod, an after-school sitter, or backup childcare, UrbanSitter’s babysitting app makes it easier than ever to find, interview, book and pay child care.

With UrbanSitter, you have the power to unlock your personal network to book babysitters and nannies you’re connected to through friends, moms groups and parents from your kid’s school.

SIX REASONS TO LOVE URBANSITTER:

FOR PARENTS:


* All your needs, answered: find part-time or full-time nannies, occasional & backup childcare, tutors for learning pods or homeschool, after-school sitters, live-in nannies, split-shift sitters, virtual sitters, date night babysitters, pet sitters, senior care, household help (cooking, cleaning, laundry, errands)
* New for 2020: Easily add preset health screening questions to job posts, find sitters willing to work exclusively for your family, learn the latest best practices for hiring
* Safe & transparent: all caregivers are background checked. See how many 'repeat families' sitters have and if they have CPR training
* Plug into your existing networks to find caregivers your connections use and love
* Easy payment system: go cashless and pay your sitter by credit card through the app
* Last-minute bookings: you can book babysitters in a pinch, as most last minute jobs get a reply in less than 2 minutes!

FOR BABYSITTERS & NANNIES:

* Great pay & flexible hours. Top UrbanSitter babysitters earn over $1,000/wk
* No cut from your earnings: Keep 100% of what you make with our standard payment setting
* Trust & safety: your safety is our priority. Parents must verify their identity to book
* See how you’re connected to families through other sitters, nannies, families & groups
* Browse babysitting jobs, nanny jobs, elderly care jobs, household services jobs, and pet sitting jobs in your area or neighborhood

Whatever your childcare needs are now and in the future, UrbanSitter gives you the opportunity you find and book sitters hired by people you trust. We give you the most efficient and trusted childcare solutions, so you can pull off the world’s greatest balancing act.

Lifesaver when I was in college

When I was in college I used UrbanSitter a lot to pick up date night jobs during the weekend and even got a regular mother’s helper job during the week after classes. It was a great way to make extra money completely on my own schedule. You can set your availability for certain days and parents can book you, or you can look at posted jobs and apply for them. I met so many nice families and explored different parts of the city I may have never visited! When you set up your profile, definitely add a video and get reviews from past employers and other parents so you can start off with 5-Star reviews. I’ve seen other sitters complain but honestly if you do a great job babysitting and are professional and reliable (don’t no-show!) you shouldn’t have a problem and you’ll find so much benefit in UrbanSitter. Everyone at support has been helpful too with jobs and payments. I highly recommend you give it a try!!!

Best Sitter App out there, one major flaw for sitters

I've been babysitting for several years now, and been an US member for about 3 years. I tried 6 different sitting sites and fell in love with this one. My tech friend also confirmed that UrbanSitter has the best security out of the several sites his hiring company has used (data protection is important!!)

Sitters read to the end why this review is 4 stars, and not 5...

Pros
• Very easy/functional app (love this)
• Sitters - autonomy with setting your availability, creating your bio, setting your rate, and accepting/declining job requests (most, but not all sites allow all of these options!)
• Parents - from what I've heard, responsive customer service for families

Cons:
• Parents - Some families have struggled to book interview requests; however, this can be easily navigated by booking a $0/hr job request or using the website messaging system to message a sitter and arrange a chat or in-person meet

Thankfully, I have only needed to use customer service once, but it was incredibly stressful and disappointing.

The other 5 sitting sites I worked with was readily available the same-day to talk with me. This or 2 business days should be the standard like it is for other jobs.

bhecka , 01/15/2019

Changed the game!

Urban Sitter is truly an amazing app! It has helped me (a college student) to meet many new families around the bay area while gaining more experience in the child care field. It is very easy to navigate, I just have a couple of suggestions.

1) Make a feature where you can block a specific family (or sitter) so you don’t even have to view them in your search. If a certain family is not adequately compensating for their number of children or have included services in their posts that you don’t offer, you should be able to block them so you won’t see them each time you’re on the job board. Likewise, if a sitter does not fit your criteria (i.e. aren’t CPR certified) you should be able to block them, too. This will help save time for everyone involved during their searches for the perfect candidate/family. I know you can hide your availability from specific families, but it would be nice if you could completely hide them from view forever.

2) For sitters, only show jobs that match the availability posted to your calendar. This will save time during the job search so you won’t have to waste time hiding all the jobs you can’t even take.

Again, I love this app so much and can’t recommend it enough! I seriously talk about it to anyone who will listen. I hope you at least consider adding these features, as I know many other sitters and families who agree.

Best of luck on your continued job search!
