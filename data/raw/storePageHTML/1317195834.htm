Description

Voxel brings you innovative 3D and new 2D coloring experience, much more vivid and interesting than traditional number coloring book! Download and enjoy!

Special Themes, Cute Animals, People,Monsters, Food, Fashion, Ocean, and many other coloring pages for you to color. Generate cool time-lapse video and share it out with your friends or family members.

Features:
- Daily updated new templates. Give you a fresh coloring experience each day;
- 2D templates. Voxel provides you a new “2D” coloring experience with Color by number. If you’d like to try a new style of “2D” coloring games, you can’t miss Voxel!
- 3D Templates and AR Effects. Color beautiful and fun 3D models, and color in augmented reality as well. So cool!
- Coloring tools. Use Bomb to color the blocks nearby and use search tool to find the remaining blocks. You can also change the color to whatever you want on palette.
- Educational
It is important that even youngest can color in 3D, so that they become more aware of what a real world means. Having real objects colored, also, can get the children to better understand what they are studying.
