peekabooyou , 07/17/2020

Great game but...

I really enjoy this game. From the puzzles to the search and find scenes. The downfall is, you have to have a certain amount of collected flowers to go onto the next scene to keep going in the game. Once you reach a point where you can’t go further, and you need more flowers, you either might not have the money or the space on your property to buy more items, and it takes more of those items to get more flowers—a big chain reaction. It takes a long time to build or harvest these items to get more flowers. You have to wait 1-2 days or sometimes 20 hours or more for items to build in order to the gain more flowers to move forward. Why is this bad? You have to put the game down for 1-2 days and can’t play, and well it’s really fun. The concept is neat, but the time to build is wayyyyy too long. There are ads all through this game which is how they make it free, and I’m okay with that. Sometimes, you have to watch ads to get certain rewards. Advertisers who pay for the ads are missing out on players watching them with how this game is set up. That part doesn’t affect me, but if they are spending money to place ads, there are lots of people not watching them, based on how the game won’t advance and you have to wait 1-2 days. My suggestion would be make us watch ads to shorten the time on how long things take to build—at least then, we can play the game. Other than this, the game is really fun!
