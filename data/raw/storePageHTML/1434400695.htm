Description

Walk to burn calories and lose weight! Choose your top goals, set the difficulty and start walking daily to burn fat, reduce stress and get rid of negative thoughts. Achieve proper hydration with the smart water tracker. Weight loss has never been this easy with this walking App!!

TRAINING PROGRAMS:

- Daily walking programs for different goals
- Walk to reduce stress and sleep better
- Individual programs of interval training designed specifically for weight loss;
- ready-to-use 3-month training plan that includes 5-7 workout days a week;
- 3 program options for various levels: ideal both for those who just starting their healthy lifestyle journey or for those who are full on living healthy and working out regularly;
- suitable for out- and indoor workouts.

WATER TRACKER :

- Water Tracker that will remind you when and how much water to drink throughout the day;
- You can set your daily goal or we can help you with it based on you parameters;
- You can choose standard (oz) or metric (ml) units;
- Stay hydrated easily during your busy day.

EASY WORKOUT MODE:

- Instructions to each training;
- Detailed statistics;
- Easy access to music, audiobooks and podcasts right from the app;
- Enter your parameters and our app will automatically customize your daily step goal;

STAY MOTIVATED:

- daily reminders to make sure your walk off your daily step goal
- weight loss has never been this easy with walking programs

Enjoying this app

I’m currently using the free version of this app. Really enjoying the walking workouts and timely notifications. The Free version DOES work. Like others, I did get the pop up to subscribe after entering my fitness info. But I chose not to subscribe at that time. Closed the app and reopened with access to the free area.

The one downside to the app is you aren’t able to view the results of the previous day. How many steps did I finish with, how much water did I drink? Having the ability to see the recent history would make this a 5 star app.

I’m planning to upgrade and have recommended this app to friends & family. They are all currently using the free version of the app with no issues.

Deceptive

The app led me thru a series of start up questions such as height, weight, weight loss goal, etc all with a “continue” button at the bottom of the pages..until about the 3rd page which has a list of features such as “Professional walking programs”, “Audio guided walks”, “Daily statistics”, etc and that now familiar “continue” button at the bottom of the screen page. Except this page had small print after the continue button that you would not see unless you scrolled down. The small print informs you that pressing the above “Continue button” means you accept their privacy policy and the billing terms. You are agreeing to being billed $9.99 per month if you pressed the “Continue” button. No way for me to back out of this page or go to other areas of this app...I’m locked into agreeing to a subscription I do not want so I deleted the app. That small print further informs me that deleting the app will not cancel the subscription.

So beware. The app can lead you through a deceitful series of questions using a strategically placed “Continue “ button at the bottom of the pages until you get to the subscription page with its small print below the page fold after the “Continue “ button.

Developer Response ,

Hello! It is never our intention to mislead our customers. We have taken our users' feedback into account and are making changes accordingly. Our aim is to be as transparent as possible about subscription options and cost, while helping our esteemed users achieve their wellness goals. Best regards, Team BetterMe Walking
