Description

Ready to build your dream world? Channel your inner designer and bring your vision to life as you transform a rundown vineyard into a vibrant resort! Solve puzzles to renovate and decorate your resort, while discovering hidden secrets and mysteries along the way!

Nestled in the hills of Vineyard Valley is The Tangled Vines, a once-thriving resort and vineyard where you spent your childhood with your Aunt Margaret and best friend, Simone. Now the Tangled Vines is in need of repair! Help Simone restore and decorate the resort to its former glory! Solve challenging puzzles and let the renovation fun begin!

RENOVATE & DECORATE:
-Decorate your resort with a variety of stunning furniture, flooring, and lighting options!
-Customize the kitchen, dining room, entrance, guest rooms, garden, and more!
-Design each room with your own personal style! Modern, industrial, or chic? Your choice!
-Choose between hundreds of furniture and décor options!
-Master makeovers to increase your Prestige level and unlock awesome rewards!

SOLVE PUZZLES
-Solve addicting puzzles to makeover your resort!
-Tap 3 or more blocks of the same color to start clearing the board!
-Beat challenging levels faster when you activate the bottle rocket, barrel bomb, and rainbow token boosters! Combine adjacent power-ups for a more powerful explosion!
-Join social clubs to earn free lives, extra coins, and special perks that will help you progress through levels and makeover your resort faster!

FOLLOW THE STORY
-Meet and interact with a quirky cast of characters and watch the drama, romance, and mystery unfold with each chapter
-Discover hidden secrets and uncover the mystery of the Tangled Vines

Design, Puzzle, and Story - what more could you ask for? Unleash your creativity! Download Vineyard Valley and start building the world of your dreams today!

fyrgirl1 , 12/29/2020

Very Fun Game - Not Fun Errors

This is my current favorite game, but since I capped out at 12, I’m seeing a lot of broken bits. 1) The change you made to the decorating tool makes it harder to change or swap decorations when you want to because you can no longer see the item markers to select them by, and the now-hidden markers aren’t always aligned with the items, that is, I’m getting a floor or an archway when I try to change the side table or a chair. 2) The glitches other commenters mention have been a problem for several months, but don’t seem to be getting attention. Whole map areas or lines of text turn purple and I can’t see the images or text that should be there, like hearts, coins or levels. 3) I realize you’re in this to make money, but the inequity of the costs for things is frustrating, and when I almost beat a level, except for 1 (over and over again), then it’s pretty obvious that the game is cheating, and I’m being coaxed to spend money to beat that level. Also, the winter cottage prices are totally out of control. No one plays enough to decorate that whole cottage during the available time period, even if you spend a couple hundred dollars on game coin to keep playing the levels. C’mon, let’s tidy this stuff up and make things more equitable and fair. You may need to make money, but we need to enjoy the game more, and be frustrated less, or we’ll spend our money on something else....like I started doing a couple of weeks ago.
