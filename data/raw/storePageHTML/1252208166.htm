Absolutely Amazing!

I have been trying to get into the habit of journaling for years. And no matter how hard i tried i never could journal for more than a few weeks until i’d eventually give up or forget. I decided to give journaling one more try- this app!

Having your diary in you’re phone makes it 100 times more accessible. It is so much easier to simply tap an icon, rather then using a physical diary. I’d argue that this dairy is safer than your average diary too. You can pick a password of your choosing to use to get into your diary. And whenever you exit the app (even just for a second) the app closes and locks completely. There is no way anyone is getting into your diary! You can change font size, style of font, etc.

This app is genuinely amazing. And a few tips for anyone interested:
1-Write short, easy to read, interesting paragraphs instead of long and hard to read stuff. It makes journaling more fun!
2-Try to write everyday, but don’t push yourself too hard. Sometimes you’ll miss a day or two. That’s ok!
3-Know when to quit. If your diary becomes a chore- quit using it. Sometimes it’s best to just give up than to keep doing something you dislike.
I personally have written in this diary, everyday, for almost 5 months. You can too!

Quickening , 03/02/2021

Not bad. A little basic, though.

I was scouring the App Store looking for a journal that was an actual journal and DIDN’T require a monthly subscription to use (which is RIDICULOUS). Finally found this one and it’s pretty nice. I like that I can backup to iCloud so I can use it across all of my devices. Had to pay a flat fee of $5 to get that feature, as well as unlocking extra fonts, but that’s much better than a subscription fee.

I do think the app is a little TOO basic, though. It would be nice to have some basic formatting, like the ability to use italics and such. Changing font color would be nice, too, to make the journal less ... boring to look at. Or the ability to use Apple Pencil to sketch in the entries? It would also be nice if we could change the fonts for each entry we make, so they don’t all look the same.

Aside from that, it’s a decent enough journal for someone looking to not pay ridiculous monthly or yearly fees just to use. If you want something fancier though you might end up having to go with a more expensive option, unless the developers are willing to add a few more extra features.
