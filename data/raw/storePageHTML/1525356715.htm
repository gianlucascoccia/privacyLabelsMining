Description

Communicating overseas has never been so easy! Use the HoloTranslator app to communicate in any country across the globe. Our voice translator allows you to pronounce words at the drop of a hat, and our document translation feature means you’ll no longer be left guessing what’s on the menu at a foreign restaurant! Enjoy instant camera translation!

Simply talk into your device for verbal translation or point the app camera at a document or an object and tap to translate the contents. Our app will instantly translate your voice, or text, and convert it into the language of your choice.

HoloTranslator can be used as a learning tool for children and adult alike, simply open the app and start learning new phrases while perfecting your pronunciation.

Start using HoloTranslator today and never worry about communicating freely at the supermarket, your hotel, or the airport again!

Verbal Translation Features:
– Instant translation of real-time conversations
– Use your camera for immediate text translation
– Translate texts from your stored photos and pics
– Extended offline phrasebook
– 100+ languages working in document and object mode
– You can either speak or type to translate your text
– Speed up your text input with typing hints
– Convert text from languages with non-Latin alphabet
– Copy, paste and share translations in a single tap
– Intuitive dialogue conversation interface
– Speak the translated phrase out loud
– Control the speed of the voice
– Male and female voices available


Document and object translation features:
- Any object taken by the camera or uploaded from your gallery will automatically be translated to any language
- Text converted by the camera instantly
- Supports more than 100 languages
- Recognizes text documents with lots of formats
- Edits scanned texts inside the app
- Copies texts to share in any application
- Uses AI to translate accurately
