Description

Welcome to the official Saladworks® FREE app. Benefit from eating fresh by joining the Saladworks Rewards™ program and earn 1 point for every $1 spent in participating Saladworks restaurants. Rewards start poppin’ up after just 100 points. Keep earning points to grow your status. Also receive special offers, news, exclusive VIP invitations and more. 

Features:

Join Saladworks Rewards™

Learn about upcoming menu offerings, news and offers

Login and join with Facebook to earn points

Find a Saladworks location near you

Receive special push notifications and email offers

Share with your friends through social

Refer a friend for extra points

Send your ideas and feedback to Saladworks

GuineaPig83 , 05/20/2020

Food is great! App, not so much.

I love saladworks! I go maybe once every few weeks and I love the food there. I got the app hoping to get rewards and such because I’m such a frequent customer, but after buying somewhere around 20 salads and over a year of having the app, I only have a $7.00 credit. To get enough credit to buy a salad, you’d have to buy about $300 worth since each salad is about $10. That’s pretty ridiculous. Also, if you use a coupon or get a free salad or a two for one deal, scanning the receipt won’t give you any points. The receipt scanner only really works if you pay full price for a salad. The app is very hard to use, and doesn’t really have a purpose. You can’t even order food from the app! You have to go to the website.

I really feel that with some adjustments to the rewards system and adding a way to order, this app could be just as good as many other chain’s apps. Right now though, if you want Saladworks (which you should get, it’s great food!) just go to the website or a store location.

Kimster19380 , 10/08/2017

Clunky app, good food

I like Saladworks food, thanks for a good product and good service. I'm disappointed with the app, though. I'd like to be able to order from the app, as I can with my Panera app. Pulling up the app and getting it scanned in the store just to earn points after my transaction is clunky and slows down your staff and me. Not sure it's worth the trouble. Also agree with other commenters that daily notifications from the app to no benefit are too frequent.
