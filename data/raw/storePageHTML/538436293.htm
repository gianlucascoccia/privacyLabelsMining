ATTENTION SUBSCRIBERS: If you are having problems viewing your issues or subscription information after the iOS 7 update, please go to the Help section (along the bottom bar) and click “Restore Purchases.”

Take an exciting adventure with DISCOVER magazine as it reports captivating developments in science, medicine, technology, and the world around us. Spectacular photography and refreshingly understandable stories on complex subjects connect everyday people with the greatest ideas and minds in science.

Every issue of DISCOVER magazine delivers the latest news, theories, and developments in the world of science, compelling stories and breakthroughs in health, medicine, and the mind, environmental issues and their relevance to daily life, cutting-edge technology and its impact on our future, thought-provoking articles from award-winning editors, opinion makers, Nobel laureates, and renegade scientists.

Download this app to enjoy free previews of our magazine and occasional free article packages from the pages of our publication. Issues of our magazine are offered for purchase as single copies, month-to-month subscriptions or full-year subscriptions.

DISCOVER subscriptions available:

•1-year subscription (10 issues) for $19.99, automatically renewed until canceled

•1-month subscription for $1.99, automatically renewed until canceled

•Single issue purchase for $5.99, with no automatic renewal

When purchasing through iTunes, your account will be charged at confirmation of purchase.

SUBSCRIBERS’ AUTOMATIC RENEWAL FEATURE: Your subscription automatically renews unless you turn off the auto-renew feature in your user account settings at least 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period. iTunes will automatically charge your account for the same price stated above for each renewal period 24 hours prior to the end of the current subscription period.

No cancellation of the current subscription is allowed during the active subscription period and no refunds are provided by iTunes.

I have an active digital subscription that ends August of 2021. For some reason I cannot access any issue, nor can I **Restore Purchases** as per the disclaimer here. So I drilled down to the *Contact Us* link in the customer service section of the magazine’s web site and sent an email request. This was 4 business days ago and no response. So, I contacted Apple Support and requested a refund, which was not provided. So, this makes me upset at both Discover Magazine and Apple itself because I am not the only customer who seems to have to recourse to this problem. If services and products are not rendered AFTER payment has been provided, there is a problem. I don’t care if it’s over $30 or $3 or $300. Lousy Customer Service all around here. Keep this in mind if you are considering subscribing.
