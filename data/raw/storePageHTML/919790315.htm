Description

Brought to you by AMC Networks, Shudder was built to super-serve fans of thrillers, suspense, and horror. Home to the largest and fastest-growing human-curated selection of high-quality, spine-tingling, and provocative films, TV series, and originals there's always something new and unexpected for Shudder members to explore.

Don't be left in the dark - try Shudder commitment-free for 7 days and get unlimited access to the full library of films and TV series – unlimited, ad-free, and uncensored. Watch on your iPhone, iPad, or TV through Airplay with your AppleTV.
