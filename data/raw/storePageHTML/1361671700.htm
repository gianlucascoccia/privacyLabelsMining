Description

YOUR WAY TO FINANCIAL FREEDOM
Be the only one in charge of your money. Securely & Easily.
Ledger gives you full power over your crypto investment: a hardware wallet, combined with one single app, that offer the best security, ease of use and ownership of your crypto assets.
With Ledger, Secure, Buy, Sell, Exchange, Grow and Manage your crypto. All-in-one place.

SECURE & CONTROL
Ledger Live is backed up by Ledger Hardware Wallet - the most secure way to store your crypto.
When you own crypto what really matters is the key that gives access to your coins. The person that has access to this key really owns and controls the associated coins.
Ledger created a solution that enables you to secure and own this key and to become the only person in charge of your coins.
Ledger Live app, combined with our hardware wallet, is the best way to secure your crypto while giving you the freedom to manage everything on your own.


FULL POWER OVER YOUR CRYPTO WITH ONE APP

BUY & SELL CRYPTO, SECURELY
Buy and Sell crypto directly through Ledger Live with our partner Coinify.
You can buy Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Bitcoin Cash (BCH), Tether (USDT), Stellar (XLM) and Dash (DASH) by Credit Card or Bank Transfers, in 45 different countries, with the currency of your choice.
Once you bought your crypto through Ledger Live, it will immediately be sent to the safety of your hardware wallet.
You can also sell bitcoin directly through Ledger Live.

EXCHANGE ONE CRYPTO FOR ANOTHER
Exchange one crypto for another through Ledger Live with our partner Changelly, in a secure and fast environment.
You can swap more than 50 different crypto on our application, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Tether, Bitcoin Cash, Litecoin. All-in-one place and from the security of your hardware wallet.

GROW YOUR ASSETS
You can grow your assets directly from Ledger Live by staking or lending some of your assets.
You can stake your Tezos (XTZ), Tron (TRX), Cosmos (ATOM) or Algorand (ALGO) directly through Ledger Live. By staking, you can receive crypto rewards while keeping your assets secured in your hardware wallet. Let your crypto do the work for you and receive passive income.
You can also lend your USDT, USDC or DAI to grow your assets. Ledger uses Compound Protocol to give you access to lending services in Ledger Live. Once lent, you can withdraw your assets at any time directly from the app.

MANAGE YOUR CRYPTO
Check your balance in real-time directly in the app: you have an overview of all your cryptocurrency portfolio and of all your transactions history.
Send and Receive Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Tether (USDT), Ripple (XRP) and more digital currencies with Ledger Live. Every action is validated on your hardware wallet for optimal security.


ALL-IN-ONE PLACE
You can manage 27 coins directly in Ledger Live and more than 1500 different tokens, including ERC-20, TRC-10 and TRC-20 tokens.

List of crypto supported:
Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Ripple (XRP), Bitcoin Cash (BCH), Litecoin (LTC), Tezos (XTZ), Stellar (XLM), Tron (TRX), Ethereum Classic (ETC), Dash (DASH), Cosmos (ATOM), Zcash (ZEC), Dogecoin (DOGE), Digibyte (DGB), Bitcoin Gold (BTG), Decred (DCR), Qtum (QTUM), Algorand (ALGO), Komodo (KMD), Horizen (ZEN), Stratis (STRAT), PivX (PIVX), Peercoin (PPC), Vertcoin (VTC), Viacoin (VIA), Stakenet (XSN), Stealth (XST).

COMPATIBILITY
Ledger Live mobile application is fully compatible with the Ledger Nano X via Bluetooth connection.


Secure, Buy, Sell, Exchange, Grow and Manage your Crypto - including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Tether (USDT), Ripple (XRP), Litecoin (LTC), Tezos (XTZ) - through one single-app and your Ledger hardware wallet, without worrying about security.

Ledger Live and Nano S: Lost control of assets while value declined 52%

I Ledger Live which is the software to control a new Ledger Nano S device I bought. I transferred my assets, BTC, BCH, ETH, and XRP. In early March 2020, foreseeing the eminent crash of the crypto markets due to COVID-19, I transferred my assets to an exchange in order to liquidate them. Ledger Live would encounter an error whenever I tried to transfer assets out of the BCH wallet, so I was unable to withdraw the assets. I contacted customer support on March 6. I didn’t hear back from them until March 25. In that period BCH had lost a bit more that 52% of its value. The one and only reason that I suffered those losses was because of Ledger, their software and their device.

It was of course no surprise that customer support said that was a bug that had been fixed in version 2.0.1 of Ledger Live, that had been released on March 23 and that if that didn’t work they suggested updating the apps for all wallets on the device. Also it was no surprise that their solution did not work.

If you want to lose access to your assets you are looking at the right company. In my case that led to a loss of 52% in asset value between the time that I reported the issue and the time I got a response from customer support - which still has not resolved the issue.
