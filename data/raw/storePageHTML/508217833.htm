Description

MiLB First Pitch is the official mobile app for Minor League Baseball and its 160 clubs, from Triple-A to Rookie Advanced. Follow the top MLB prospects from every club and catch up on the best action from the 2019 season with free access to more than 6,500 archived MiLB.TV streams.

**** NEW FOR FIRST PITCH IN 2020 ****
- New team selector process to help you easily find and edit your favorite team(s).

- Updated layout for individual club pages, providing more customized content from each team.

- Support for Minor League Baseball’s newest teams – the Fort Myers Mighty Mussels, Fredericksburg Nationals, Kannapolis Cannon Ballers, Missoula Paddleheads, Norwich Sea Unicorns, Rocket City Trash Pandas and Wichita Wind Surge. Support for 2019 free MiLB.TV archives.

Other great features include:
- Customizable team icons to put your favorite club on your device display
- Complete stats
- Club schedules
- Team standings
- Latest team news, video and features
