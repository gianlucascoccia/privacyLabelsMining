Description

Join 60 million Stocard users and store all your rewards cards in one free app.


Wall Street Journal: "a clever way of turning a smartphone into a virtual wallet"
Techcrunch: "a simple but clever take on the mobile phone-as-loyalty-card-wallet"


DIGITIZE YOUR REWARDS CARDS
Unclutter your wallet by scanning the code on your plastic cards within seconds.

COLLECT REWARDS POINTS IN STOCARD
Whenever you're shopping, just pop-up your loyalty card's barcode on your phone and have it scanned by the cashier to receive your points.

DISCOVER EXCLUSIVE OFFERS
Browse coupons, discounts, flyers and catalogues in Stocard – all related to your favorite stores!

CARDS IN WALLET
Add any card in Stocard with a QR code to Wallet.

Awesome Rewards Card App!!!

It seems like every day another company is creating rewards programs, which they basically force you to sign up for because if you don't you are going to end up paying more if you don't get their card. Honestly it kind of ticks me off because why don't they just give everyone the same price. I know it's all marketing and they try to create loyalty to their stores but all of those dang little key cards are so annoying and get thrashed pretty quick. Well, as Lee Corso would say, "Not so fast my friends!" Now there is Stocard, a extremely simple and easy to use App that stores all of those tiny annoying key / rewards cards! All you have to do is download and open the app, scan those horrible little key cards and abracadabra your rewards card is stored in the app. Finally, now more digging around for a rewards card!!! Just open the Stocard app on your phone when you need a specific rewards card and bingo-bango the cashier in the store scans the card that's displayed on your phone. Download the app now!!! It's saved me time and money and I guarantee it can do the same for you. Oh and I do know work for Stocard and I didn't get paid to write this review, I just think the app is da shizzle. Although, they should pay me for such a glowing review.

CN💋 , 10/05/2019

What wallet should be and better

I’m not an app reviewer I think I may have reviewed 1-2. But I had to review this one bc I’m practically in love with it!!! Download this app RIGHT NOW if you have more then a few club cards or rewards cards or whatever they’re called. Or if you use Apple Pay or w/e and don’t always take your wallet or purse with you... or if you’re into simplicity... I rarely have the cards I need when I go wherever and only a few are tied to my phone number so I miss out on points and sales and all sorts of stuff but not anymore now that I have this insanely simple app! It scans and saves ALL your cards and keychain cards with ease, even the really rough ones, (I was shocked) including the ones to stores they don’t have yet on here. If your cards super rough you can enter the numbers manually and save it that way. You can even take pictures of the cards so you always know which is which, which mostly helps with the few cards the app didn’t know. It took a couple seconds to add each card which is a lot less time then it took to put those dumb keychain cards on the same key ring all facing the same way... this app is such a simple idea and what I’ve always wished Apple Wallet was. I threw out so many nasty old cards I’m SO GLAD I stumbled upon this app!
