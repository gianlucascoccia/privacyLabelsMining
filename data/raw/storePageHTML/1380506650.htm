Description

RECGO allows you to record your favorite games, apps or screen audio for tutorials, games, video demos and video call on your iPhone or iPad. After recording your screen, you can add Face Cam to make some video reactions to further enhance your recording!
Besides high-quality recording, RECGO also support video editing. You can start to make your YouTube videos after the recording finished with RECGO directly. Just come and join the millions of people who have fun in RECGO.

iPad gaming & screen recording

I play games on my iPad ALOT, mostly Fortnite and Pubg Mobile, but I spend atleast a couple hours each day playing, and I like to record all my matches. I tried every free recorder in the App Store, and this one was the best. Its also one of the only ones that allows you to change the resolution of your recording (from 1080p all the way down to 340p) which I absolutely LOVE. And it doesnt randomly stop on its own like the iOS system recorder does. Only complaint so far is that when I record for long periods, the recordings can get very choppy & laggy when you rewatch them, but its only on the recording itself, not in the game thankfully.
