Description

Back in 1992, founder Dan Oaks, a starving college student with one computer, no capital, and no connections created the first version of Cook'n. Since then, Cook'n has become the #1 best-selling recipe organizer with millions of copies sold!

Hundreds of copy cat recipe apps have come and gone over the past 28 years but no recipe keeper compares to the robust features and quality of Cook'n. And, no other developer is more committed to excellence or customer support than DVO.

Some people spend hours entering all of their favorite recipes into cheap recipe apps...then the developer goes out of business and they're stuck!

Get Cook'n! Cook'n truly is the most robust, feature-rich...and funnest recipe keeper, menu planner, and grocery list app available today!

Use the Cook'n recipe keeper to organize all of your favorite recipes. Import recipes from Pinterest, Snap pictures of your recipes, Capture Internet recipes, even dictate your recipes and Cook'n will organize them for you. Tell the Cook'n recipe app what you have on hand and Cook'n will tell you what you can make for dinner.

The Cook'n grocery list feature will help you make menu plans and shopping lists with just one tap. Grocery lists are organized by aisle to save you time at the grocery store. Cook'n menu planner will simplify meal preparation and make mealtime memorable!
