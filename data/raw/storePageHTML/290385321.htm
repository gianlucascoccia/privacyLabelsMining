Description

Track time, materials and send PDF invoices or estimates.

*** Apple iTunes "Staff Favorite" ***
*** Apple iTunes "New and Noteworthy" ***

Timewerks is a time tracking and invoicing application for freelancers, consultants, contractors, lawyers, salespeople, or anyone who needs to track time, materials and send invoices— all from your iPhone, iPad or iPod touch.

Timewerks is fully-functional by itself, and allows for 1 invoice. You can enhance Timewerks with the purchase of these additional modules:

* Unlock (unlocks an unlimited number of invoices)
* Unlock Pro (unlocks unlimited invoices and includes PDF invoicing, invoice logo and built-in data backup/restore)
* Full data synchronization with our companion website, mytimewerks.com, and all your iOS devices

See www.sorth.com/timewerks for screenshots, video tutorials and our online user manual.

Features:

* Track multiple projects and clients.
* Create, email and print HTML or PDF invoices and work estimates without a computer, complete with your company logo (visit www.sorth.com/timewerks/invoice.html for a sample invoice).
* Quickly and easily add timesheet hours and capture notes for each project task.
* Track and invoice billable items, materials and expenses.
* Built-in data backup and restore: back up and restore your data with one tap (without connecting to iTunes).
* Sync data with our web-based companion, www.mytimewerks.com (requires an optional timewerks sync subscription module.)
* Accept credit card payments via integration with Credit Card Terminal (sold separately by Inner Fence LLC)
* Time your work for each project via a built-in stopwatch (Task Timer).
* Customize different rates for different types of tasks or items.
* Calculate sales taxes, discounts and support multiple currencies.
* Export your data to comma-delimited (CSV) or tab-delimited (TSV) text files via a Wi-Fi connection and a standard web browser.
* Enter and view data without a network connection— all records are stored on the device.

We welcome your feedback. Please visit www.sorth.com and let us know what features you'd like to see in later versions. Our goal is to make Timewerks the best time tracker and invoicing tool on the App Store.
