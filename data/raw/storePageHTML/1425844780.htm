Description

Train like a pro

We are democratizing access to elite soccer training!
Train Effective is the #1 elite digital soccer academy available on-demand and right from your pocket. This soccer app delivers one of the most comprehensive learning hubs for aspiring soccer players of every level. Train Effective enables its members to experience professional academy-like soccer training at home.

START YOUR ONLINE SOCCER TRAINING, CREATED BY UEFA LICENSED SOCCER COACHES & WORLD CLASS SOCCER PLAYERS
Our soccer training app is compiled of over 150 soccer drills, soccer workouts and soccer programs from perfecting your first touch and improving your shooting to reaching your peak level of fitness.
Regardless if you're just starting to learn soccer or you’re already a soccer academy player, the wide variety of skills and drills will help you progress further into your soccer career.
With new content added monthly, you get access to the latest and best soccer training drills and never run out of ways to improve.

IMPROVE YOUR SOCCER MENTALITY BY JOINING OUR CLASSROOMS TAUGHT BY ELITE SOCCER PLAYERS
Imagine soccer stars like Rio Ferdinand and other world-class athletes giving you insights from their vast experience and training you on the mental skills needed to get that edge over your competition. Our classroom training sessions deliver exactly this. Episode after episode on topics such as what it takes to become the best soccer player in the world, self-belief, motivation, managing a soccer injury, discipline… to name just a few. Get prepared to make your next step towards the best version of yourself and your soccer goals!

PLAN & SCHEDULE YOUR SOCCER TRAINING, 365 DAYS OF THE YEAR
Our simple and easy to use tracker allows you to plan and schedule your soccer practice with ease.
Simply find your preferred soccer workout or course and add it to your preferred day.
The tracker is designed to keep you accountable, track your progress and help you improve your soccer skills FASTER!

BECOME A SMART SOCCER PLAYER THROUGH GAME BRAIN SESSIONS DELIVERED BY PREMIER LEAGUE ANALYSTS
Game Brain is a performance analysis training tool that was created to give soccer players a step-by-step understanding of professional soccer players’ decisions, tactics and strategies. Using the knowledge and expertise of our English Premier League analysts we have created the perfect tool to not only train like a pro but think like a pro.
