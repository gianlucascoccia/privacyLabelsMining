Description

Go all the way from a small roadside motel, where travelers stay overnight, to a luxury hotel where even stars dream of spending their holidays.

Manage your staff, respond to customer requests and have time to fulfill their whims to pass the levels.

Improve the hotel! Book your equipment and upgrade your rooms to keep your customers happy. The better the apartment, the more money you get from your guests!

Time-management is not that easy! Use impressive boosters to deal with hard episodes faster and easier.

A little motel with soda and sandwiches? Or a hotel with lobsters and champagne? It's up to you how this story ends.

Nynns , 06/05/2020

Just a few things l...

I don’t normally review games, they’re either good or they’re not 🤷‍♀️ I have been loving this game. It’s a great time filler and it’s fun! However, there are a few things that could be fixed to help make the game even better.
1. The coins and gems.. so far I haven’t had too hard of a time collecting coins, but gems are hard to come by. And since you have to have those to upgrade, then it makes it almost impossible to pass the levels. I have spent real money because I got tired of repeating the same level over and over again, so I decided I would just buy gems just so I could upgrade a few things. I have no problem purchases with real money once in a great while, but I’m not doing it all the time. Maybe come up with different ways to earn the gems other than the gift boxes you get for passing levels.
2. Please take away the confusion of the little guy!! It throws everything off and takes up time when you’re trying to get him to do other things. Which, again, makes it hard to pass levels that are already impossible to pass.
3. I go through the little tutorial when you add a new booster, and every single time it ends up freezing. So, I either have to quit the game I’m on or exit out of the game completely. I’ve had this happen OCCASIONALLY with other games in the past, but never this much. And every time I have to quit, it takes a life away.
