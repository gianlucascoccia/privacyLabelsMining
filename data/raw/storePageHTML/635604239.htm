Description

Turn your iPhone, iPad and Apple Watch into your FAVORITE teacher of Greek.
◆ The most essential words and phrases of the language, each with native speaker audio.
◆ Master your accent with the Speech Studio. Simply record yourself saying a phrase and then hear your voice next to the teacher’s. You’ll be AMAZED how quickly your foreign accent melts away.
◆ All audio is downloaded to the device and accessible even in airplane mode or without expensive international roaming fees.
◆ Nemo is NOT BUILT AROUND LESSONS at all. It is meant to be picked up and put down throughout the day, whenever you have a few minutes to spare.
◆ Builds a foundation of Greek empowering you to speak CONFIDENTLY in EVERYDAY CONVERSATIONS.
◆ NO PRIOR KNOWLEDGE of Greek required.

Nemo® is specially DESIGNED to tap the remarkable power of your mobile device, and your brain, to their fullest.


PERSONALIZED FOR YOU
Nemo is your personal trainer, tracking your PROGRESS across every single word and phrase.
◆ Practice Greek the way you'll use it. Nemo is fully customizable.
◆ Nemo immerses you in the sounds of Greek so you naturally speak with ease.
◆ Dive into WHAT YOU WANT to learn at any time.
◆ You control which words to practice – skip words you don’t want to learn or already know.
◆ Turn on Review Mode to study what you practiced earlier in the day to BOOST your memory retention.
◆ Hear English descriptions of all words, in a flashcard format.
◆ Quiz your mastery of the vocabulary.
◆ Nemo introduces words to you progressively to develop familiarity and reviews them at the right moment so they enter your LONG TERM MEMORY.


TARGET THE MOST COMMON WORDS
Maximize your time investment by targeting the most basic and high frequency words of the language.
Upgrade to the complete version to:
◆ Get off the beaten path or find comfort with DIRECTIONS, TRAVEL and HOTEL phrases.
◆ Decode menus with the most common FOOD words.
◆ Shop and find bargains like an insider with must-know SHOPPING phrases.
◆ Learn words useful for your time in greece.
◆ Build your proficiency with the most important verbs and SENTENCE BUILDING BLOCKS in Greek.
◆ Spark new friendships with COMPLIMENTS and CONVERSATION STARTERS.

Use your new skills to make friends, impress old friends, bring smiles to children, get out of jams, experience new things, speak confidently, and make your travel and cultural experience more rich and fulfilling.

Wandering Chemist , 09/16/2018

Simple & Effective

After visiting Greece once, I’ve dreamed of going back with the ability to converse in Greek. This app is great! Highly recommend. It helps you learn phrases and words (with the right pronunciation!). Just pick a category and click play. It will shuffle through words/phrases, adding new ones as you go. Continues playing even when the app is in the background. Listening to words repeated frequently for even 10 minutes daily ingrains then in my mind— both Greek pronunciation and English meaning. Since I have no Greek friends to immerse me in the language, this is the next best thing. It’s easy, fun, simple, and incredibly effective.

dhhouston , 06/02/2020

Great tool for learning but serious bugs to work out

I had been using the free app and recently purchased the extended version so I could get more serious.
My complaint is lack of explanation of what tile temperature means. Two default settings have these on and if one leaves them on, not all words in a category appear in the player to practice or appear less often because they’ve been deemed cool words that don’t need as much attention. While that may be helpful after one builds a vocab—deciding what are important words to emphasize—the default shouldn't be to exclude words, or at least offer an explanation of what that means so a user can choose what fits them best. I thought it was a buggy program; turns out I just didn’t like the app’s choices.

Thisnamealreadytakn , 01/16/2021

Better than other apps

I’ve been using Nemo Greek for several weeks and found I could progress faster with the Player and reversible Flash Cards features than I could with Duo Lingo (which had problems with recognizing my Greek inputs by key and voice on iOS). Nemo Greek is inherently motivating because I’m able to move ahead and view my stats across several simple metrics. I got quite far on the free program and then bought additional content. I was never motivated buy more on Duo Lingo even though they kill you with ads and invitations to upgrade to the subscription every few minutes.
