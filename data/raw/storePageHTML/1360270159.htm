Description

Color by number app that is completely unique - color with swipes instead of taps. You are unlocking more paintings while leveling up, and we’re not hiding any tiny spots to color from you (no magnifying glass needed!). It is all you would want out of a paint by number app and more!

Besides making your painting realistic, April Coloring provides you with top-notch handcrafted free content and overall premium app quality, to help you become a more relaxed and creative person. To make sure that April Coloring brings you realistic experience and relaxation, we focused on user experience, simplicity and great community around the app!

WHY USE APRIL

● We know you appreciate coloring activities and probably you are using plenty of other coloring apps. What we wanted to excite you with is a completely unique approach towards the way coloring feels. Just download the app and check it out yourself!
● We want to make things easier for you. Just tap on the palette and you will find the right spot instantly. You may also tap on any spot on the main coloring board and you will highlight all required fields to paint the color number needed.
● Almost all content is free - unlockable while gathering more coloring experience (stars) and leveling up or collecting coins. This makes the whole experience a lot more fun!
● There is a daily content posted within the app - make sure you claim it regularly - otherwise past dailies are available only for users which subscribed!
● If you subscribe you will receive plenty of goodies - exclusive content, access to past dailies and all ads will be removed. On top you will support our team as we’re not treating April Coloring as a one-off but a long term project to innovate the coloring evergreen!
● April Coloring is highly optimized for Apple Pencil. Check all the features that you can benefit from - including pressure and angle sensitivity / double tapping and more!

People all over the world are already enjoying our unique way of coloring – with over 1 000 000 paintings painted each month! Join them!

If you see anything we can improve, please, let us know! New reviews and support emails are really helpful to make our app better and smarter! Contact us via april@crimsonpine.com

Great! I love it! ❤️

I love this game so much! The premium doesn’t have ridiculous features, so you don’t HAVE to buy it for the game to work and everything works well. The controls are easy to figure out, and it’s nice how you actually get to color it in, instead of like in other apps where you just tap and the whole section is colored. Basically for me this game is incredible and worth it. Some other reviews say there are too many ads. I don’t understand this because it is a free game so of course there are gonna be some ads. And also, even if it feels like they are excessive ads, that’s not the case for every picture. Also, even if that were true, the game is such a great one that it balances out that anyway. I like how the pictures that would normally just be premium you can actually buy with the in game coins. I love how there are so many different galleries to choose from and the pictures are very well chosen. Another thing that I like is how they make the pictures look like real life when you finish coloring them, instead of cartoons. This game is amazing! I love it. The only thing I would like is maybe a little percentage thing around each of the colors like the circle for the total percentage done. I saw that suggestion in another comment, and I think it’s a good idea. That’s really just me trying to come up with something that is not perfect about this game! 🤩I know this review was really long.... 😅but I couldn’t help it. Thanks!

Infinitely Whelmed , 09/27/2018

Excellent app with excellent diversity

Okay I usually don’t write reviews but this app is great. First of all, it’s the first one I found where you actually make the motions of painting instead of just tap to fill and if there are any bugs I haven’t noticed them. Second of all, I like how you can use coins to get the premium stuff at the end of a pack, and how a good 70% of the app is usable without it. I’ve seen so many where a subscription is absolutely necessary to get anything out of an app. Last but not least, DIVERSITY!!! Seriously, this is the first app I’ve seen to feature so many women of color. Usually you get mainly white women with the occasional black woman, but there are all races featured in this app. In fact most of the women ive seen aren’t white, or at least clearly white. I’ve even seen a few men, which you usually don’t see in these kinds of apps. I really appreciate that this app displays the beauty of all colors instead of just white people, because too many of these kinds of apps don’t. The only improvement I could think of would be an option to choose different pallets, especially for the landscapes. Like there’s one where it’s a mountain range primarily in shades of pink, and as pretty as it is I’d like so see it in blues or greens, but that’s honestly just nitpicking.

All in all, fantastic app, completely recommend!
