Life Period Tracker lets you track your period, predict your ovulation, schedule cycle reminders, track your symptoms, moods, and more! INNOVATIVE CALENDAR
We weren't satisfied with the methods other apps used to enter women's cycle dates. We went through hundreds of prototypes before we were satisfied with the result.

PERIOD TRACKER
We wanted a display that was simple yet beautiful. A page that could be viewed with only a passing glance yet still have it's own personality.

OVULATION PREDICTIONS
Viewing your ovulation predictions is fast and easy with our calculator. Gone are the days of information clutter.

IRREGULAR CYCLE SUPPORT
By default, the app defines cycles as irregular if the cycle has a length less than 21 days or over 36 days but these values are easily personalised to fit your needs within Settings.

MENSTRUAL HISTORY
The menstrual history was visually optimised to include thoughtful choices with an easy-to-read vertical orientation.

CYCLE HISTORY
The days of difficult-to-create reminders are over. Our cycle reminders are easy to create, modify, and set.

SYMPTOM TRACKER
One of the best features: each symptom can be kept as a separate entry for later analysis or to share with your physician.

dementedaeryn , 10/16/2018

Rating & Suggestion

I started using this app to track my moods in conjunction with my cycle. It changed my life. I found out that my moods where very hard to control during ovulation and I took that information to my doctor. He prescribed me a specific medication that I’ve been taking for a year now, and things are so much better. I love how you can diary in it, track your medications and what’s going on etc.
My first and most important suggestion would be, if you could interface a moon cycle calendar into the app, that would be awesome!
Second, it would be nice if the ovulation bar that is on the current month view, stayed in the past months views, rather than just having your entries as a way to remember that’s when you were supposed to ovulate.
I am not the in-tuned to my ovulation, nor do I do anything else to track my ovulation (I am not trying to or avoiding pregnancy), so just having that to look back at would be cool. Other than that, this app is well worth the 99 cents I paid, and I highly recommend all women to track their moods and cycles together. It’s life changing when you’re mindful of your bodies changes!
