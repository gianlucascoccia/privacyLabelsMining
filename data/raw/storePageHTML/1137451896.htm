Description

Best High-Quality Wallpapers & Fan Arts for Fortnite. Personalize your screen with your favorite battle royale game. It will give your screen new awesome crispy looks.

- Cool themes to help you create a stylish look for both your Home & Lock Screen
- Pixel-perfect wallpapers in retina and standard resolutions

Good app. But only two problems.

It has a lot of good options for Pokémon wallpapers, but they really need to enhance their submission filters. I could have sworn I saw a Sonic the Hedgehog wallpaper in this Pokémon wallpaper app. Also, the Pokédex list only shows the first 151. I don’t know how old this app is, but this seriously implies that a Genwunner made the app (or, in non-expert language, someone who only cares about Red/Blue/Yellow). DragonKyro , 01/29/2017

Great app, but here's a suggestion~

I like this app as a whole. It's variety of backgrounds and videos suits my needs, with a pokedex to boot. It's a great app, don't get me wrong, but I find it hard to find backgrounds with CERTAIN Pokémon on them. I'd be browsing for ages, not finding a single Zoroark background. Now, I'm not the kind of person to address a problem without also suggesting a solution, so here goes. There should be tags, like say there's a picture with a tepid on it- #tepig. Then, when people want to look for a certain Pokémon, they can type 'Zoroark' into a search box and every picture with a 'Zoroark' tag will be pulled up. Again, just a suggestion, and the app is perfect besides that.
