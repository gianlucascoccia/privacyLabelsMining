Enjoy calmer day times and quicker bedtimes with this award-winning sleep & mindfulness app for kids.

Join the millions of parents who use Moshi to help their families relax, unwind, and sleep with over 75 hours of original audio stories, meditations, music, and sleep sounds that you won’t find in any other app.

- 97% of parents surveyed agree that Moshi helps get their kids to sleep quicker than usual.
- 95% said using the app makes bedtime less stressful*.

Includes special guest Moshi Stories narrated by legends of stage and screen, including Goldie Hawn and Patrick Stewart. Recommended by top sleep experts and physicians.

Moshi helps kids of all ages fall asleep faster, stay asleep longer, and wake up less, according to scientific research. In a recent study*, scientists discovered that, on average:

- Kids fall asleep 28 minutes earlier
- Kids sleep for 22 minutes longer
- Night wakings decrease by up to 50%

Make bedtime a dream:

- Remove anxiety & stress from the bedtime routine
- Reduce the time it takes for your kids to fall asleep
- Reclaim precious time in your evening

Moshi helps kids of all ages fall asleep independently with over 200 pieces of completely original sleepy content created by the BAFTA winning Moshi team.

Kids love the adorable characters from the world of Moshi including:

- SleepyPaws the Snoozy Koala and his eucalyptus-fueled imagination
- Yawnsy the Sleepwalking Otter and his enchanted yawn
- Nodkins the Bedtime Bunny and her ability to hop into any dream

Why Moshi Stories work:

- Moshi Stories are gentle rhyming journeys with dreamy lullaby-style choruses
- Musical transitions and ambient white noise are specifically designed to be relaxing
- As each story progresses the gentle narration becomes increasingly sleepy and ambient sounds diminish

Teach your kids the basics of mindfulness with guided meditations designed to help kids relax, reduce anxiety, and manage negative thoughts.

Mindfulness topics to encourage positive emotions include:

- Kindness
- Gratitude
- Positive thinking
- Recalling joyful moments
- Staying calm
- Patience
- Making friends
- The power of imagination
- Reflection
- Self-appreciation

Features to support calmer days and nights:

- Original content released every week
- Audio-only. No need for screens in the bedroom
- Learn deep breathing to help worries drift away
- Browse Day & Night to easily find the best content for any situation or time of day
- Loop and replay functions ensure sleepy content plays throughout the night if needed
- Explore curated playlists for specific situations like “Ease Anxiety,” “Sleep,” and “Calm Down”
- Create multiple personalized playlists for any mood or family member
- Tag your favorites to quickly and easily find your child's most requested stories

Pearlway , 04/29/2019

Moshi for Me, too!

I am a 70 year old who has had trouble sleeping for years. I have tried many things, including nature sounds, music and stories besides bedtime habits, reading, etc. I tried Moshi Twilight by mistake and have found it amazingly helpful. The stories are cute but not so interesting that they keep me alert (or irritated). I love the music attached to the Sleep Stories, and sometimes I switch to Music only or to Meditation. There is a library of items and I can relisten to a favorite or randomly select an old one. I enjoy new selections as they come along and may or may not like them. I use the app when needed at bedtime or when I wake up in the middle of the night. I have a wireless headband with speakers that connects to Bluetooth so it won’t bother my husband. Previous sleep “fixes” have helped me for temporarily, but Moshi has been working for 6 months. It helps me fall asleep probably 90% of the time. I am a little embarrassed that this is a product for children, but am so glad I have found it! I often have difficulty understanding the words in the songs, which bothers me (I guess I sing along in my head). The support staff have been very helpful with supplying the missing words. I don’t have little ones that I am putting to sleep, but I wholeheartedly recommend this for adults! Others may also find it just perfect.
