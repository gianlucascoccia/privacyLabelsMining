Description

Guideys helps you get the most out of Pokémon Sword and Shield!

Learn all about Galar Pokémon with this powerful reference app, which includes:
- Pokémon
- Dens
- Locations
- Moves
- Items
- Gigantamax Forms
- Regional Forms
- Curries
- Abilities
- Stats
- Evolutions
- Weaknesses
- Breeding
- And much more!

App Features:
- No ads
- Dark mode
- Instant search
- Works offline
- Advanced filters
- Large text support
- iPad support
- App customization
- And much more!

What’s New

Feb 11, 2021

Version 8.0

Added lots more helpful features!
- Pokéball Icons: Now in the Areas tab you can see which Pokémon you already have. This makes it even easier to find the ones you're missing.
- Curry Dex: All 151 curries can now be viewed in the Lookup tab. Tapping on a curry will show you which ingredients you'll need to cook it.
- Adventures: See the 270+ Pokémon you can get in the Dynamax Adventures with the new Adventures filter.
- Data Update: 100+ data improvements in Pokémon locations, moves, items, and more to help make the app even better.
- Privacy Protected: From day 1, this app chose to use no ads and no trackers to protect your privacy. It will continue to use no ads or trackers.

Fantastic App!

This app is a perfect companion for anybody who wants to play Sword and Shield and doesn’t want to keep looking up information. It’s super easy to navigate and see everything you really need like typings and weaknesses, and it even shows the chances to catch and the stats of everyone too. This app is excellent to see which Pokémon are available in every area as well.

One thing I wish was possible: when looking through the different areas, and some Pokémon are only available during a certain weather condition, I wish I could be able to filter out Pokémon that aren’t available due to the weather I have in game. So if I’m in a snowstorm, I would rather choose “snowstorm” in app, to see only the Pokémon available always, and the Pokémon available during the snowstorm weather. That would make it much easier to know exactly what I can catch, and also reduce a lot of scrolling (especially in the wild areas, where there can be upwards of 60 Pokémon, but only 5-6 are available at that moment). That is something that would make this app even better!

Anyways, this app is amazing! Great job!
