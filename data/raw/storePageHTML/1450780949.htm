Description

The professional photo editor application, Phonty allows you to make beautiful editings, fabulous designs and stunning collages with the high-tech tools, the simple usage, and the rich contents.

You can easily add text and graphics from the large selection of choices on your photos or images and make creative designs by applying various easy-to use editing options with this enjoyable application.

You will find a large number of artistic ready messages for birthdays, Christmas, special days, love messages and various inspirational messages you would like to express your feelings to your loved ones.

You can choose from a wide range of graphic collections, creative drawings, artistic shapes and graphics, colorize, shade, give color transitions, create your own style in seconds.

You do not need to know any design techniques, you can create wonders with a few taps.

You can remove the background of a person or an object, or change it with another background.

You can magically remove unwanted objects on your photos.

You can also enhance your photos and pictures with professional filters and editing options, and you can enrich them by overlays.

Some excellent features of the Phonty application:
- Magic eraser tool that removes unwanted objects on your photos.
- Amazing photo cut-out feature that can remove backgrounds of your photos.
- Options for changing backgrounds.
- A feature that allows you to cut your photos shapely.
- More than 50 stylish fonts and renews very frequently
- Various growing typographic messages for special days
- Ribbons, borders, dividers, doodles, and decoratives for enriching your designs
- Ability to apply transparency, color options, shadows and color gradients to your text and artwork
- Professional photo filters, overlays and adjustment options to polish your photo backgrounds
- Numerious stock photographs to use with search option
- Various crop options for social media and wallpapers
- High resolution output
- Various options to save and share your work

Start creating wonders with a few taps now!
