What’s New

Feb 17, 2021

Version 2.1.1

How is the community doing?

2020 was a tough year for group studying. Our goal in 2021 is to add features to our platform that help members study more effectively together, regardless of their location. We don’t want to say too much more, but in the short term, this update provides the ‘community score’ for each question when you are reviewing it. This means you can see on a per question basis just how hard questions are and how other people are answering. To view a breakdown of how the community answered and the percentage of people who answered correctly just press the community icon, 2 people, next to the flag icon in the lower tray area. This functionality will stay on as you review questions until you press the icon again.

Keep studying hard and we’ll keep the app working hard for you.

I used this app for about 2-3 months to help study for a national registration exam to become a dietitian. I found the content very relevant and helpful.
