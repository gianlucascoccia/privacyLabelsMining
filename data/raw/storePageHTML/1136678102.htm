Description

Have you got what it takes to become a world class surgeon and make your hospital the greatest medical center in the world?

Chrissyblueberrybabii , 08/19/2018

Money Gobbler, Unrelenting Ads, and Glitches

I give it 2-stars because it’s a cool game, and it would be really fun without its problems. In order to be successful at this game you’ll need to spend money (real life money). You’ll see what I mean by day 2 at the latest. I’m about to stop playing it because it’s ridiculous to spend money on a game (I’ve already spent $20 cuz I was hooked). The staff needs to be energized and you can only put 1 staff member into the break room, unless you upgrade it which is like 60,000, and there’s so many other things you need to buy that it’s a low priority. So instead you’ll need to buy tons of coffee and energy drinks. Leveling up staff is pricey (it costs money and skills which are in limited supply) and gets more important after level 10. The ads are obnoxious!! Every time I go back to the main screen at least 2 Ads pop up. Every time!! They’re the same Ads too, like get the picture that after the first 5 times I’m not going to buy these things. On top of that you’ll probably fail surgeries because of the game glitching, especially during sutures. The surgeries are the best part of the game so failing them is annoying and you need to have successful surgeries to level-up yourself and to get other opportunities, so failing is also time consuming since you’ll have to redo the surgery until you succeed. Overall, a cool game but not worth tons of money and frustration.

Me830105 , 01/25/2019

Game is OK

I just started playing a couple of days ago. The graphics are beautiful. The operations are simple to execute. I’m already a bit frustrated though because I can’t advance at my second hospital. I need a paramedic to assign to a task and only have one that I need at the other hospital. I spent $60,000 game money on a recruitment pack only to get a Doctor. It takes so long to build cash and everything is expensive. There should be a way to be able to pick which kind of staff member you want to hire when buying with game money. You can pick if you hire with CVs but I have no idea how to earn those.

UPDATE: A previous review was correct and you will get to a point where you cannot progress without spending real money. I currently need the highest level Nurse for stamina purposes to complete an assignment. The Nurse can only be purchased with hearts. I took a chance and spent the hearts I earned naturally on a Diamond recruitment pack and got an Auxiliary. I don’t even know what they are used for. I’ll repeat what I said in my earlier review that we should be able to pick what kind of staff member you get. I might consider spending a little actual money every now and then if there was a guarantee that I could get what I needed and not something random that (in this case) I don’t even know how to use. I’ll continue working on my other assignments for as long as I can but once I’m capped out, I’m done with this game.
