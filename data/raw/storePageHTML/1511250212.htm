Description

Walletry is a simple app to help you track your daily expenses and your subscriptions. You can add your daily transactions and categorize them by adding many categories and customize them also you can get notifications for transactions due date and attach photos in your transactions and include your location too.
Also, you can get reports for your spending and your transactions in the charts section, and track your weekly transactions and everything you need.

You can create your own budget to save your money and make it ( Daily / Weekly / Monthly ) and you will get notifications to remind you when it will renew and how you spend your money and the remaining in your budget.

The settings section lets you customize everything you need like change app accent color, app gradient colors, app icon, and your wallet theme also we care very well about your security. so you can lock and unlock your app using ( FaceID / Touch ID ) and many settings you will need.

All your data synced with your iCloud account so if you signed in on any device like your iPhone / iPad / Mac you will find all your data synced and continue where you left. also, you can delete all your data if you need this with only one click in the settings section.

Wallety is supporting the Universal Purchase so once you buy it you can download it on any other device with your Apple ID.

Walletry is developed and designed to make your life easier and helps you to track your daily expenses so your feedback is very appreciated and will help us to make Walletry great and more helpful.

Great Work in Progress App

Just downloaded it a few minutes ago and so far, so great!! Beautifully designed app with most of the features I wanted. Just a few things that I would like to see added/fixed please:
1) The menus for adding transactions on the Mac don’t work very well, specifically adding transaction titles.
