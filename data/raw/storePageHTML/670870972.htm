Love the notifications

I’m sure lots of people think multiple notifications a day are annoying (and you can adjust that in settings). But I personally love it so much. From “can you start X now?” to “can you try to finish Y before the end of the day?” it puts me in a productive mindset and reminds me to focus when I inevitably get distracted throughout the day.

I like how I can visually plan my day (separating tasks into morning, afternoon and evening; and highlighting high-priority tasks), and it’s easy to move tasks from one day to another if you didn’t get to them. I also like that it connects to my calendar (so it automatically autofills events into the correct section of my Today window).

I was looking for an app that would nudge me to complete certain tasks throughout the day, and I found it. Thanks!

Daniel DeVita , 06/03/2019

Wish the Free Version Wasn’t Just a Demo

I really, really enjoyed everything about this app. The functionality was great, the activities and goal linking were really motivating, and the evening routine was great for ending the day in a thoughtful note. Previously, I had rated this app five stars.

And then I hit my forty activity cap that comes in the free version. The app is essentially broken now unless I subscribe to the pro plan (which is a monthly fee). Honestly, the app is good enough that I AM considering going pro...but the fact that the free version essentially had a limited lifespan before basically forcing you into the paid plan by restricting the app’s usefulness is pretty disappointing. Would much rather have had the functions just limited to free version/paid version features, vs a free version that stops working as well (ie. Like Evernote, Todoist, Trello, etc). Leaves me feeling pretty let down as a consumer by an app I had been really enjoying...
