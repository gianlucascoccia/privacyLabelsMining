Description

TOPPS® KICK® Football Card Trader is the official digital collectibles app of UEFA Champions League, UEFA Europa League, MLS and Bundesliga! Unlock a world where you can celebrate the nostalgia of collecting & trading football cards with the added bonus of opening packs EVERY DAY!

Discover a NEW world of collecting!
• NEW football cards available every day!
• Claim FREE daily bonus packs of Topps collectibles!
• Claim FREE currency to increase pack-opening power!
• Trade with fans around the world – anytime, anywhere!
• Connect with and follow fellow collectors!

Bring your collection to life!
• Complete Missions to unlock special content!
• Create card Wish Lists & Trade Lists.
• Trade in cards to receive rarer ones!
• Track and complete sets to earn collectible awards!
• Spin the Wheel to win cards, coins, and more!

Customize your profile!
• Showcase your favorite Topps football cards!
• Choose & unlock profile avatars!

*For the best experience, we recommend devices be updated to iOS 12 or later.*
