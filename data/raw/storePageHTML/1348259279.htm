Description

Get personalized meal plans to lose weight quickly and naturally with Diet & Meal Planner by GetFit! A comprehensive, intuitive calorie counter and food tracker will help you stay on track and make smarter food choices.

Features:

- 42 club. Find the motivation, guidance, and tools to achieve life-long results without challenging your busy schedule. Build a healthy mindset in just 42 minutes a day.
- Tons of diet plans. Our practicing nutritionists have performed a great deal of work and developed many personalized diet plans with your food habits in mind.
- Junk food meter. Discover the pros and cons of the products that you consume. Our nutrition quality scanner rates tons of foods and helps you to shop smarter and eat healthier.
- Recipes at hand. With hundreds of delicious healthy recipes adjusted to your diet plan and food preferences, you're sure to satisfy your cravings.
- Food logging and diary. With an extensive food database combined with a smart search, voice search, and barcode scanner, you'll feel the difference compared to the good-old pen & paper method.
- Motivation and tips. We've got tons of inspiration, motivation tips, and daily goals to help you see your progress day by day. Enjoy the expert coaching, so you don't have to do it alone.

Weight loss is not about starving yourself. It's about eating smart. Set off on your journey today with a pocket nutritional coach guiding you to success!

HealthKit is used to synchronize with Apple Health and Activity for accurate calculations of your physical activity and 42club charts.

Easiest calorie tracker period , 02/13/2021

Great way to track nutrition

One of the biggest frustrations with diet/fitness apps for me is not being able to track what's important in addition to calories. Diet shows right at the top not total calorie intake, but carbs, proteins, and fats, and not just to tell you if you've had too much, but when you haven't had enough. Diet has an awesome interface that's easy to navigate, and puts everything into smaller categories that makes adding meals and snacks SO easy. I also love that for tracking water, you just tap on a "plus" button and it shows right there how much water you've had. In other apps I've tried, they often take you elsewhere, asking for more info on liquid intake or requiring you to add it to a meal. As someone who frequently struggles with keeping properly hydrated, this is the best and easiest way I've seen to keep track of my water intake on a diet app. The only thing I don't like is that allllll over the app there are ads for the premium version where you think youre clicking on something you can look at and it very often needs to be purchased.
