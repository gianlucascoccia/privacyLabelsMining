Description

"I wanted to create a place with all the tools that I felt I needed when trying to reach my own fitness goals. There just wasn’t anything out there that did that. I figured that if I wanted it for myself, my fans probably would too.” – Tammy Hembrow

Tammy Fit gives you exactly what you need to work out and eat like Tammy and see results. From 8-week programs to step-by-step individual workouts and nutritionist designed meal plans, you can choose what works for you and your schedule.

Nutrition is the key to long-term results. The app features 8-week meal plans that are tailored to your personal goal, be it lose, gain or maintain. The plans have been designed by leading nutritionists and have easy step-by-step recipe instructions, weekly grocery lists, daily intake/macro goals and tracking. You’ll even find some healthy treats there too.

Kauten20 , 10/05/2019

Overall good app

So let me start by saying the reason for only 4 stars is due to the lack of complexity in the app but I’ll get to that later.
For starters I just recently took the plunge of deciding to try Tammys app. I have always watched her on insta and thought why not? I originally used the app BBG and I did truly like it but it got boring to me as I wanted something more advance and focused on weights. Going from Kayla’s app to this app is probably why I feel like the layout of this app could be more organized and a little more sparkle so to speak. I find it bland🤷🏻‍♀️
As far as the workouts go, like I said I just recently started it but I do enjoy what I see. I love that there is no timer and it allows you to focus on form and you can track what weight your lifting to see progress! Another pro about this app for me is that the meal plan is simple and stuff you can buy at your local Walmart. Some cons I would say if you aren’t already experienced in the gym then this isn’t a beginners app and you may benefit from learning the basics prior to starting. I feel like in the future some improvements can be made such as beginner weeks for people learning, organizing the app a little more as far as the workouts go, and also the macros... I used a calculator online and I feel like it’s more accurate than the app. No way I could consume over 200 carbs in one day! Regardless I love the app and hope they will extend the 8 week program to a 12-24 week!
