Description

Voted Best Motivation App by Healthline.

FAST
Invest less than a minute daily to track, identify, and change your habits with Way of Life.

EFFICIENT
Changing habits is hard work. Having the right tool is half the battle. Way of Life is that tool - a beautiful, intuitive habit tracker that motivates you to build a better, stronger, and healthier you!

As you collect more and more information, you will be able to spot positive and negative trends in your lifestyle easily:

• Am I exercising as much as I thought?
• Eating less and less fast food?
• Getting the fruits and vegetables I need?
• Sleeping well?
• Avoiding too much sugar?

Or whatever is essential to you. There are no restrictions on what Way of Life can help you with when it comes to changing habits.

FEATURE RICH
• Chains - challenge yourself not to break a positive streak.
• Powerful reminders with flexible scheduling and custom messages.
• Themes - Dark Mode, Serenity, Retro C64, Light, Rose, etc.
• Charts - Pie charts and bar graphs with trend lines
• Today Widget
• Note-taking - quickly jot down a note
• Use URL-schemes for interaction with other apps
• Organize all your habits with customizable tags
• Scoreboard for instant weekly/monthly feedback
• Interactive walk-through
• Unlimited items (*)
• Backup to any Cloud storage provider that supports iOS (*)
• Export your data as CSV or Excel files for further analysis
• Passcode lock with Touch ID and Face ID support
• Color-blind mode

McD4444 , 07/17/2019

Helpful! Encouraging!

I find this app the best for habit forming. I bought several to test out which one I would like the best since forming a good habit is so critical to healthy living. This app has a notification on the app (red bubble with number of habits waiting to be completed that day). The notification is helpful to remind you as the day goes on and you can see the number go down.
I made the mistake of having 7 habits and it grew to 10. I'm down to 3 now since I was feeling defeated when I didn't accomplish all 10. Simple habits like "make your bed in the a.m." or "take vitamins" or "exercise for 10 minutes". I was sick for a few days but I checked off that I exercised and took my vitamins and wrote a note in that box that I would have exercised and taken vitamins if I wasn't puking. Or you can hit the skip button and write a note why you had to skip. If you didn't have to skip, check the "X" and use that as an oppty to get better. It's not a negative mark on you, it's an oppty to get more green check marks and improve. Keep it simple and support and encourage yourself.

RustVel , 11/01/2019

Needed this app! (basic spreadsheet export)

So many journaling and tracking apps out there really put some effort into their exports. Your only option here is a spreadsheet (which is good! And necessary! But not enough.) Take a look at the mood tracking app Daylio’s export, it’s a pdf, looks nice and it’s good to read over and review.

Another thing, exporting habit descriptions separately! Descriptions are stuck on the app! I use them to keep track of what I mean by “yes” or “no” for the habits at any given time and so it’s important for me to save my descriptions as well, it should be an option. My only choice is to copy and paste them which isn’t so bad.

Maybe what I’m asking is too much, or not that useful to other users, or not feasible. I don’t know so for that reason I’m not rating this app down. I love it so far thank you. I’ve been starting so many positive habits I started to lose track!
