Description

Quran Pro app is a collection of the best Quran reciters in the world, an amazing user interface design and the best set of features. Now available on the Mac App Store

Quran Pro app is FREE without any limitation.

With the Quran Pro app you can do:

• Translations
Listen to the Holy Quran in Arabic alongside its translation ( French, English, and Urdu).

• Offline mode
Download recitations, playlists, and tracks, and bring your Quran with you anywhere using our offline mode. Download all the surats or one by one surat.

• Favorites
Create your personal Quran collection simply by marking playlists as your favorite.

• Multi-Platforms
Thanks to the technology Quran Pro supports AirPlay & CarPlay.

• Audio features
Play audio in the background while you're using your Mac. Repeat, shuffle, ​and timer functions are also included

• Design
The app has been redesigned to be faster, simpler, snappier UI, and more beautiful. In addition to optimized for 4K Retina display on the Mac range.

REQUIREMENTS
- The application requires an internet connection with WiFi.

Great but...

This app is great, it has many reciters and cool features like different dua's. However, it's missing two things; first, it's missing the ability to have the same Surah repeat itself instead of having the reciter or otherwise stop after a certain time just go on and on. When I sleep, sometimes I want to listen to Quran, but I want it to stop after 15min or 30min, I wouldn't want it to go on for the whole night and drain my battery. Second thing that's missing from this app is having the ability to set it in Arabic. When going over the names of the Suras or reciters I want to listen to, I really prefer having it in Arabic; it's not only my mother language but it's more natural to have the Quran (Suras' names) in Arabic. When these two features get added to the app I'll give it a full rating 😊. Good job, nevertheless. Keep it on guys. Thank you and may you be rewarded the highest place in heaven (and us too). Amen.

Awesome app, one feature missing

This app is mashallah very user friendly and has a wide range of speakers. I like the feature where you can choose how long you can make the sheikh recite the Quran (e.g. for 30 minutes).

However, there is a small issue. The problem is that I was reading Quran (without this app) and reached Surat al An’am (the cattle) verse 152. When I tried to open the app and read with the sheikh, I clicked on the verse 152 and thought the sheikh would continue the recitation of the Quran from the point I selected (verse 152), however he wouldn’t recite from 152, he would recite it from the beginning of the Surah. When I clicked on the verse, I could only bookmark it, there isn’t a feature where you could select any verse (aya) from the Quran and the sheikh would recite from that point.

If you add this feature it would be awesome.
