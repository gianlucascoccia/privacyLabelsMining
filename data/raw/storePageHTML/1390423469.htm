Description

Aiming at perfection in everything, Peachy is a powerful & handy pro photo editor, especially for selfie editing & retouching, body shape editing.

Everybody is an excellent photographer with this simple retouch tool, it helps you meet the best version of you, smooth skin, whiten teeth, reshape face, fix blemishes, remove wrinkles, become taller, add makeup, tattoo, muscles & filters and so on.

Peachy provides lots of portrait and selfie photo editing features. Download it and give a try!
