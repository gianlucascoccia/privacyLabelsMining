Description

What you can expect with Choices:
• Romance your favorite characters.
• Fall in love, solve mysteries and embark on epic fantasy adventures.
• Customize your hair, outfits and character look.
• Choose your story from our constantly growing library with weekly chapter updates.
• Control what happens next in immersive visual stories!

Our most popular stories include:

THE ROYAL ROMANCE - In this rags to riches saga, ditch your waitressing job to travel to the beautiful kingdom of Cordonia... and compete for the hand of the crown prince! Will you win his royal proposal, or will another suitor command your affections?

OPEN HEART - Fresh out of med school, you’re the newest medical resident at Edenbrook Hospital working under the brilliant and demanding Dr. Ethan Ramsey. When faced with situations where you hold a patient's life in your hands, will you be able to make the cut?

BLADES OF LIGHT AND SHADOW - Human, elf or orc? Create your character, gain new skills, and become the hero you want to be in this epic fantasy adventure! The forces of darkness are rising.. will you be the light that defeats them?

MOTHER OF THE YEAR - Things take a turn for the worse when your ex-husband and your daughter's biological father shows up with a high-powered new job, a hot new girlfriend, and a plan to re-insert himself into your daughter’s life.

BABY BUMP - Finally, after graduating from business school and landing your dream job, your life is taking off when your life is interrupted by an unplanned pregnancy with Gracetown's most eligible bachelor.

THE ROYAL MASQUERADE - As the unlikely heir to a minor house in Renaissance-Era Cordonia, the death of a sibling thrusts you into a dangerous political masquerade, where secrets reign and no one is truly what they seem.

THE ELEMENTALISTS - Magick. Mystery. Mayhem. Are you really the normal, college freshman you thought you were? Or something much, much more? Discover what powers lie deep within you at Penderghast College.

DESIRE & DECORUM - In this regency era romance It's court or be courted when you discover you’re one of the most eligible heiresses in England! Does true love await you at the grand Edgewater Estate?

RIDE-OR-DIE - Logan isn’t the type of boy you can bring home to Dad... not when Dad’s the detective tasked with taking down Logan’s illegal street racing crew.

BLOODBOUND - You're applying for a job with brilliant CEO billionaire Adrian Raines. Urban fantasy meets romance in this sexy vampire thriller!

HIGH SCHOOL STORY - It's your first day at a new high school! Make friends, find love, and attend the Homecoming dance in your very own High School Story!

Choices has even more titles and releases new stories and chapters EACH WEEK!

ABOUT US

Choices is from the team that has been bringing you narrative games for over a decade. We created the top 25 hit games, Surviving High School and Cause of Death, the first episodic games, for EA. After leaving EA, our team regrouped as Pixelberry in a small office, set out to continue creating story driven games with heart and launched the two interactive hit games, High School Story and Hollywood U.

Our team has now grown to over a dozen of the most experienced game writers in the industry. In our 10 years of creating story games together, we’ve seen heartbreak, marriages, great adventures, and even Pixelbabies.

Stay tuned for more new interactive story games to pick from in Choices!

- The Pixelberry Team

This is THE ultimate game

I love this game! It’s super addicting but the only thing that bothers me is the diamonds. Yes I understand that diamonds can and SHOULD get you clothes but I just don’t like that sometimes you have to use diamonds for your choices. Sure you get a bonus part and stuff like that but it’s like- in reality I would never pay someone to do something (besides HAVING to pay like at a carnival, mall, etc).
I love this game because all of the stories make you dig your nails into the couch of wherever your at. The stories always have the choice if you want romance in your book with your choices or not. They will give you the option if you want to flirt with someone and usually if you do your relationship would change.
The outfits are also amazing. But I think you could lower the price on them a bit because that takes me days of trying to collect 25 diamonds for an outfit that I love. I have played and deleted this game over and over and over again. This game is almost better then Tic Tok. Reason I deleted this game is because of the addiction. It also makes you read which is great especially since kids don’t go to school on the weekends. (Parents) You can read them a book off of it, or have them read it themselves and you can do so many fun things with your child.
So as I said, so many possibilities, amazing game. Good job Pixelberry for creating one of the best games ever. Just please fix the diamonds a bit.
Thanks for reading.

Mandy Dope , 09/05/2018

CRAZY ABOUT CHOICES!

I’m borderline obsessed with these books. I’ve played them all! Every story is different from the next and, being a writer myself, I really appreciate all of the character development. There’s no question that a lot of thought goes into the customizing, the art, the music, and the dialogues. My favorites include The Freshman (makes me feel young again LOL), Royal Romance, and the newer books like Desire & Decorum, as well as Perfect Match. Once you get into it, the characters really come alive and It’s hard to stop playing! I discovered Choices about a year ago, and very quickly went through all the stories. I gave them 5 stars because they’ve truly mastered the art of virtual reality and I would absolutely die to work for Pixelberry someday! My only complaints are that I’ve paid more than I can really afford on diamonds, and you have to wait anxiously for new chapters and books. Like someone mentioned in another review, I would much rather spend $10-$20 on the app at initial download, rather than pay as I go. Or even offering 2-5 diamonds after watching an ad instead of just 1. Heck, I’d even watch more ads if it meant earning more diamonds! Otherwise, Choices is brilliant and beautifully done. I’ve laughed, I’ve cried, and I’ve learned a lot about myself through these characters- as cheesy as that sounds. These stories are never dull, and contain a lot of twists and turns. I highly recommend Choices over any other competitor apps! Loveee!

maykatric , 12/04/2018

Just the best

Choices, without a doubt, is the best game I have ever played. I have had it for almost a year and still look forward to new chapter being released, and reread my favorite stories. Choices’s books have taught me so much about family, relationships, love, and the importance of making a choice. Our choices truly do define us, which I think is the whole message of the game if you could boil it down. Have you ever wanted to attend a school of magic? Find love in Jane Austen’s England? Captain a star ship? Seek vengeance in Ancient Rome? Become a superhero? Marry a king? Fight for your crown? Or just try to survive high school? There is a book out there for everyone, I promise. However, If you only play one book series, please play Endless Summer. Inspired by the tv show Lost, this book is a tear jerking, heart string pulling, hair raising, mind bending beautiful adventure that I cherished every minute of and cannot wait to re read. Anyway, do yourself a favor and get this game. It sure changed my life. Thank you Pixelberry.

p.s. Many people complain about the diamonds and yeah, I sometimes wish there were less choices that require diamonds each chapter (Across the Void is notoriously bad at this) but there are plenty of games that only have one or two diamond choices per chapter, and you certainly don’t have to spend any diamonds to enjoy each book. I have never spent any real money on this game which has not hindered or lessened my game experience in any way.
