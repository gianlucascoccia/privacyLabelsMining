Description

Discover an easy way to lose weight, feel great and stay healthy! Get Window — your reliable intermittent fasting tracker.

Track your daily eating window, log your weight loss and form new eating habits. Don’t limit yourself in what you eat, just eat at the right time!

What features are available for Window users?
- Track your eating window and your fast all in one app.
- Schedule your eating windows in advance.
- Get notified when your eating windows open and close.
- Challenge yourself with fasting, mindfulness, nutrition, workouts or self-care tasks.
- Try water fasting.
- Use Siri to open or close your eating window from any device.
- Check on your eating window or fast from your Lock Screen, using the Window widget.
- Sync your data with Apple Health app.

Discover the Premium features:
- Choose from popular fasting plans
- Attach daily notes and photos
- Track your weight

BlueEyeBird , 04/19/2018

So helpful!!

I love this app. I use it every single day. I am doing the OMAD but it does help to be able to leave a note when I eat a particularly high carb meal or I want to remember that I went somewhere in particular and that’s why my window might have been open longer than another day. I prefer to use the flexible window because of my work and family responsibility don’t always go hand and hand with a reliable time routine. The ability to have a scheduled or a flexible window is a great option for people who never quite know what the day will hold. Plus I never know if I’ll be satisfied after an hour or if I’ll need a little extra food that day, but I don’t feel the need to keep eating because my window hasn’t closed yet. This app helps me not only keep track of my window and fasting time, it also helps keep track of my weekly (sometimes daily) weights. It not only gives me a place to log in my weight, it also has a very clear and easy to read graph above it tracking my progress. When I haven’t lost much or barely an ounce or two and I start to get down about it, I look at the graph and it really is a pick me up to see that the line of the graft is still going downwards. I would recommend this app for anyone who is entering the IF or OMAD lifestyle. So much more handy than caring a notebook around.

tturtle2007 , 02/08/2019

Worth the price! Trust me!

I never buy apps, especially health ones. But since starting fasting, I wanted to find something to help me keep track of my fast on the go. I downloaded a bunch of free ones and they weren't what I wanted.

Window is very flexible and user friendly. You can set daily reminders for when your fast ends and begins. Lots of times I see it pop up and I'm able to check in with myself and change my Window to later because I'm not hungry yet. It's much easier than watching the clock waiting for the fast to be over, allowing food and time to be in control. Just set your times and you'll be reminded. So forgetting about eating time helps you be more engaged with your body.

I love how you can track your fast, see it on the calendar, and see your weight on a graph. It also syncs up with the Apple Health app. Best part is the author of "Delay, Don't Deny" Gin Stevens' son made the app. Definitely worth it when I seen that. Great book and happy to have an app to assist me further.
