Description

The Imperial Court is on the brink of collapse. Selfish officials have forgotten their oath and plunder what they can. Fight corruption and see if you have what it takes to make the right decisions as you judge the destiny of those accused of crime.

Start your journey as a freshly appointed magistrate. Determined to bring prosperity back to the people.

You will not be alone in your quest to restore order to the empire. New friends are eager to join your cause and lend you their services. Expand your coffers and raise your troops to defeat familiar foes and unexpected adversaries.

FEATURES:

Recruit & Upgrade Retainers
No one man can do it alone. Recruit retainers with unique abilities to help you on your journey. Great generals, fearless warriors, and brilliant advisers are ready to join your cause. Upgrade them to help you overcome the many enemies and challenges.

Cherish Confidants
As you prestige, you will attract many beautiful ladies; perhaps even those of other lands. Cherish them for what they offer is more than meets the eye.

Fortify Alliances
Join an alliance or build your own, the choice is yours. Unite with your friends to take on others in the battle for supremacy. Work together to unlock Alliance-only perks such as unique buildings, boss fights and more...

Raise Offspring
Raise your children and nourish their talents. Establish your dynasty by arranging marriages with other players.

Establish Trade
Trade with the other ancient kingdoms of the Orient and make a fortune. Beware! The oceans are treacherous and others are looking to do the same.

Study in Academy
Brush up on your study of the arts. Your dedication to poetry and the arts may earn you the honorary yellow jacket.

Much more...

This is your destiny. Be the King and may your wisdom bring prosperity to the Empire.

El grande Jefé , 10/31/2019

One year of experience

I started playing this game exactly on October 31 at 11 pm going into November 1st of 2018. Here I am exactly one year later to tell you it was quite a fun ride. I have a full streak of 365 days of continuous log in, and apart from a few lazy days where I don’t do everything I can, I progressed pretty smoothly through the game. Yes, of course there are the classic p2w methods of any mobile game. But alas, even though there is some pressure to buy and become a VIP, that doesn’t mean it will halt your progress if you don’t- one of the better p2w mobile game models I’ve seen as of late. Even with my late start and not completely min maxing the living heck out of the game at every opportunity I was still able to stick pretty close to the mid/top of the players in my server. There are many events that give great rewards And keep the game refreshing and fun, and many constant updates keep you covered and show the interest the developers have in their own work. It’s a project made with care, and really does keep you engaged and wanting more. For me this game is a high 3 or low 4, but since I’ve had a particularly good time it is a 4 for me. Thank you, and I will continue playing this game.

Good game but it’s tends to be unfair to other players.

I’ve been playing this game for about 5 months this game came out somewhere in September or October and I really like this game and I’ve been addicted to this ever since. There are lots of things that you can do in the game. But my biggest problem is that the game tends to keep the same top 3 or 4 players with the same name tag with the same servers almost constantly and hardly rarely give other servers a chance to get rewards and always get pushed down to the last of the list no matter how many times they play it. This seems very unfair to a lot of players and it’s getting very ridiculous. It’s starting to seem that some players have been cheating their way through to win. I’ve been trying to collect some tokens for recruits to unlock new characters. I was very close to winning to receive a token but all of the sudden I get shoved down to the list of winning players and didn’t get any tokens. I’m not liking that I have to buy certain things with real money which I never did to get VIPs. I wish that I could use in game golds that I worked hard on earning to rank up on VIPs instead of real money.
