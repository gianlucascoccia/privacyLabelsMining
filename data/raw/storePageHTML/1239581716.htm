Description

TezLab is the most popular app for you and your vehicle. Get insightful drive details, track your trips, charges, and more to help you get the most from your Tesla.


A qualified electric vehicle is required to use TezLab.

Aoun Akram , 12/25/2020

Overall great app

I’m a new Tesla owner and I love this app more than any other app available on the AppStore (yes, even over the paid ones). It makes it really easy to see your drive efficiency. Not only that you can switch between battery percentage and miles with just a tap. With the Tesla app you have to change it within the car which can be frustrating and sometimes you’re just not near your car. You can see stats for each drive and charge session which is great! Overall a great app and highly recommended for new Tesla drivers!
