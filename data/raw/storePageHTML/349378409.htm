Description

Going skiing or snowboarding this winter? Download trail maps from your favourite ski resorts to your iPhone and use the built-in GPS to find your way around the slopes or track your activities.

The best thing is that you don't need an active internet connection, just download the maps you'll need in advance and everything you need on the slopes is right there on your iPhone. If you do have an internet connection available however, you can display webcams from Windy.com on the piste maps or check the weather forecast at your favourite resort.

SkiMaps is now also available on iPad, a great tool for making plans for your next trip!

Some of the available ski areas are:

Espace Killy (Tignes & Val d'Isère - France)
Les 3 Vallées (Val Thorens, Meribel, Courchevel,... - France)
Portes du Soleil (Morzine, Avoriaz, Les Gets,... - France & Switzerland)
Paradiski (Les Arcs, La Plagne,... - France)
Chamonix - Mont Blanc (France)
St. Anton am Arlberg (Austria)
Le Grand Masif (Flaine, Samoëns,... - France)
Alpe d'Huez (France)
Les 4 Vallées (Switzerland)
Les Deux Alpes (France)
Ischgl (Austria)
Whistler Blackcomb (Canada)
Zell Am See - Kaprun (Austria)
Zermatt & Cervinia (Switzerland & Italy)

... and more!

For a complete list of maps, please check our website.
