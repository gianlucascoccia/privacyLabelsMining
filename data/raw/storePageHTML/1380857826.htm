Description

NURSING.com (formerly NRSNG) is the best place to learn nursing. Take your nursing school studies and NCLEX® prep anywhere with the NURSING.com mobile app. NCLEX® mastery is just one click away.

NURSING.com is your best friend through nursing school and your must-have study tool for NCLEX®.

When you become a NURSING.com member, you get full access to the mobile app, with video lesson viewing, cheatsheets, and Nursing Practice Questions rationales to help you learn!

• 2000+ HD Video Lessons (viewable from your mobile phone)
• 135+ Nursing Care Plan Lessons
• 300+ Full Color Nursing Cheatsheets and Pathocharts™
• 215+ Nursing Mnemonics Lessons
• 300+ High-Quality MP3 Audio Lectures
• 6000+ NCLEX® Style Practice Questions


Ideal for the nurse and nursing students on the go (all of us, right). You can finally study anytime -- anywhere.

Find out why over 1,000,000 nurses and nursing students love NURSING.com and why Nurse.org, FreshRN.com, KevinsReview.com, NerdyNurse.com and more all recommend NURSING.com with 5 stars.

NURSING.com is your All-In-One, secret weapon for dominating nursing school and the NCLEX® exam.

This is so far the best study program I’ve used. I enrolled in Kaplan, Uworld, Klimek, even using Saunder’s book but combined all these and you got NRSNG. The app is worth every dime IF you can actually afford it

$84 a month for 6 months is a lot of money. I’m a single mother of 4 going back to school for nursing after more than 20 years since graduating high school so I needed help. Most resources I found were much more affordable but much less useful. (You get what you pay for I suppose). The number of topics/classes that they cover here is honestly pretty shocking and exceeds anything I’ve seen anywhere; I just wish there was a cheaper option for those of us barely scraping by, (subscribing to only the pre-nursing option, only the nursing courses or only the test prep instead of everything. Maybe lower payments over a 2 year period since everyone needs AT LEAST 2 years to get their RN, even spreading it out over 12 months would make it more accessible to the less financially secure of us.) If every nursing or pre-nursing student used this program then the failure rates would be much lower than they currently are. So this is worth every dime if you actually have the money to buy it. I don’t even have a home phone or cable tv - THATS how broke I am, so shelling out $84 a month for 6 months is A LOT of money.

uahnurse33 , 01/20/2019

Better than any other NCLEX prep

I love this app, and the online version is even better. I love how I can listen to this while I drive, workout, or go to the store. I recently failed my NCLEX (I used HURST review before that exam...it obviously did not help), so a friend referred me to NRSNG and now I am hooked. I wish I had NRSNG academy in nursing school, the content is explained so much better than my instructors had ever taught. This app has thousands of questions on each nursing concept, disease process, labs, medications, management of care...etc. There are cheat sheets, pictures (drawings/real life pictures), and patient stories (which I think is the best, it helps you remember the impact that these disease processes have on people). Lastly, the online content has the SIMCLEX (simulation NCLEX). It is EXACTLY like the real thing. The questions are adaptive to how you answer, just like the NCLEX. Try this before you start to see where you're at and then after you complete all the lessons to see where you're at!
