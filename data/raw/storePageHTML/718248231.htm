Description

Post your golf scores with USHandicap to establish a Handicap Index with USGA licensed golf club. Post scores, stats and track your friends. Try it free! Annual membership is just $29.99.   

- Post and view your entire score history
- Lookup your friends or other golfers Handicap Index
- Search for golf courses near you
- Create groups and outings
- Share leaderboards

USHandicap can be used for free without limits. The in-app purchase of $29.99 will give you a membership to a USGA licensed golf club, which can then compute your Handicap Index. All your scores, stats and handicap information will also be stored on the USHandicap cloud network of clubs, where your information can be retrieved and reviewed for USGA's peer review requirement. In addition to your licensed golf club membership, the in-app purchase will provide:

- USGA Handicap Index
- View your Handicap Revision history
- StrackaLine Putts
- Participate in Golf Forums

Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase. Subscription automatically renews for $29.99 unless it is cancelled at least 24 hours before the current period. Your account will be charged for the renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period. You can manage and cancel your subscription by going to your account settings on the App Store after purchase.

Our Terms of Use and Privacy Policy are located here: https://www.ushandicap.com/privacy

Professor Boogerpants , 04/15/2019

Last year would have been 5 stars

...Easily. The new HBH interface is really much more difficult to use than it was last year. I appreciate some of the changes, but the actual entry of HBH stats is really frustrating especially if you’re doing it mid round.

I know this is a golf app and everything, but you really made this worse by changing it.

Update: home page much better, but the HBH entry remains much more cumbersome than the previous version (which was really great.) Half of the HBH entry page is consumed by categories (score, drive direction, putts, etc) which leaves only the right half of the screen to scroll through selections on a single line. So if you hit one right, you have to scroll through the options to see it, but you’ll likely end up clicking another option that the scrolling menu then snaps to which undoes all of your scrolling efforts.

To even get to this HBH entry you must first click a small box on a digital scorecard, adding another step to the process.

Honestly, is there anyway to revert back to the old version? I’m serious. I just paid to continue my service, but this app is just too frustrating to use much longer. I know they can fix the issues, because it was really a great layout before.

Bill and Nasha , 06/03/2019

I Love this App

This is a great way to save your rounds and statistics. The only thing I wish is that it established a GHIN number but I realize now that has to be done through a club. As far as keeping my rounds and seeing my trends, this has a whole lot of capability. The other good thing about it is it will link other apps you may have (if I enter on GolfNet for instance) and will grab that score and put it in your Handicap tracker on here. I’m very happy being assured that all my scores are safe, especially because I am trying to practice and get back to a competitive level, so this helps me majorly in that aspect also. As far as a free app to store your scores, it can’t imagine it would ever get any better. I may even upgrade my service to their pay option considering I am so pleased with what they offer for free!
