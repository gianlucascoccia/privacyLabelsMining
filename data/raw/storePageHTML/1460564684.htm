Description

Take in a stray kitten

This heartwarming management simulation game all starts with a stray cat in the forest.
You're the owner of Animal Restaurant. Will you take in this clumsy, dirty kitty and let him work at your restaurant?

You can learn all kinds of recipes,
like taiyaki, strawberry pancakes, shaved ice, and spaghetti!
There's even pizza and avocado sandwich!

Mix and match all styles of furniture.
We've got European-style dessert tables, Japanese-style fences, and Mediterranean-style ovens!
You can also have an Alice in Wonderland-style garden tea party!

Hire cute feline staff,
including a ragdoll cat, a tabby cat, and a big orange cat!
You've also got to get on good terms with an eccentric chef!

As long as you work hard, you'll always have a steady stream of customers.
Will you chat with this diverse crowd of customers?
Will you listen to their thoughts, or argue with them?
Learn about the customers' stories through chats and letters. You can even participate and change their lives.
Hear about secrets, gossip and tear-jerking experiences.

All of this and more can be found at Animal Restaurant—a simple yet cozy and cute restaurant that's all yours!

Come open a restaurant and start your story!

The fishing game

I love this game and I think it’s super fun. There’s a lot of ads but they don’t bother me at all because watching the ads actually gives you things and it’s well worth it so I applaud this app for being able to put in a lot of ads but in a way that isn’t annoying at all.

But there’s only one thing I hate about this game and it’s the fishing game in it. The fishing pond is the most infuriating part of this game because catching fish is hard and there seems to be no rhyme or reason behind it. It says that if you keep trying and watch the fish’s mannerisms you’ll eventually get good at fishing and should be able to catch them easily but so far for me that hasnt been the case. The fish can squirm off the hook and there’s nothing you can do about that. The fish dart away quickly when they see the hook coming and it’s not like you can move the hook to still catch them or do anything about it. Unless I’m missing something and just haven’t figured something out yet, but catching fish really seems to be a luck and chance thing and I think it’s just up to the game to decide if you’ll be able to actually catch them or not. And most of the time, you’ll have to try a lot of times before you finally catch something. Idk maybe I’m just bad at it but I really don’t like that part of the game because there really doesn’t seem to be any specific method to catching them And it’s very hard. Other than that, I have no complaints
