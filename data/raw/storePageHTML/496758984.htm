Description

Scoreboard is an innovative and user-friendly scoring app!

No longer keep track of scores in your head or just with your fingers. Whether you are playing, refereeing, or just watching a game, this attractive new look allows you to keep scores in style. Impress your friends and family with the next generation of mobile scorekeeping. Choose from five scoreboard themes.

Features:
- Choose between five different scoreboard themes
- Tap number to increase the score
- Swipe down to decrease the score
- For classic flip scoreboard, go to negative numbers if needed
- Swipe side to side to switch home and guest scores
- Modify team names
- Game countdown timer
