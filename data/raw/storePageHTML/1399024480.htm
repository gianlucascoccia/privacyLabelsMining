Description

Join the best bookish community!

BookSloth gives you the best personalized reading experience and connects you with readers everywhere for you to share your love for books.

Discover your next favorite book: receive the best personalized recommendations based on your reading habits and preferences and browse curated lists that feature top recommendations, new releases and more.

Connect with your book community: Create a new bookish discussion and follow your friends and fellow book lovers everywhere. Share your favorite books with them.

Stay Active with your reading: Participate in our monthly reading challenges and earn achievements for reading some of the top classic books. The more you read, the more you win!

Organize your library and recommend books: Update your currently reading status for multiple books. Create shelves with books you've read, want to read, are currently reading, and books you recommend to others.

Share your book reviews: You can finally rate books based on the reading elements you enjoyed most from the story. Whether you prefer awesome characters or amazing worlds. Give badges to the books you've read so we can recommend your next favorite book.

Features:
• Discover new books, browsing curated lists with personalized recommendations, including new releases.
• Connect with your book community and start your own bookish discussion.
• Join our reading challenges and earn bookish achievements by adding books and reading top classics.
• Keep track of the books you've read, want to read and are currently reading.
• Update your currently reading status and track your reading progress.
• Rate and review your books based on your favorite reading elements by giving them badges, stars or leaving a comment.
• Following friends, family and book lovers everywhere.
• Recommend your favorite books.

Definitely Has Potential

BookSloth appears to be an up and coming alternative to Goodreads and I’m excited to see how it grows from here.

I’m enjoying it so far, the UI is intuitive and visually appealing. I like how straight-forward a process it is to add a title to your library. However, the app still needs a few tweaks. The search function leaves much to be desired. When I search a title, it rarely appears as the first result. Usually something completely unrelated appears before it. And just a suggestion, there should be more reading elements to pick from when reviewing books (i.e. humor).

I can’t wait until you have the GR import up and running. It’ll make it things a lot easier.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for your feedback. The search is something we want to improve and the reading elements is definitely something we want to expand on. We are gathering everyone's feedback and making a list of priorities for the next updates. We are currently testing the GR Import and hope to enable it this week. :)

574269536$9 , 07/30/2020

Still Exploring

I’m really happy to have found this app! Goodreads feels outdated and has so much to improve, and BookSloth seems to have already honed in on aspects that are important for book lovers. The fact that the first thing that pops up in my homepage is book lists separated by minority authors had me falling in love the moment I opened the app. I’ve been really wanting to widen my exposure to Black and Latino authors and I’ve already added multiple books to my “To Be Read” shelf.

Some things that I think still need improvement include the implementation of author profiles. When I click on an author, the only thing I can see is the different books that they published but I can’t see any information about them beyond that or a picture of them. I believe that would be a great addition to the platform.

I also hope that eventually there can be more options to create original shelves. I like to separate the books that I want to read into separate categories which doesn’t seem like something I can do on this app yet.

Anyway, this app shows a lot of potential, and I can’t wait to see what improvements are implemented in the updates!
