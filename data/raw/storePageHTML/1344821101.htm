Description

Wotja 21 is an awesome 'generative music' creativity system - a powerful & FREE all-in-one creator, lab, mixer & player. Get it now if you like creating, making or even listening to generative music!

With or without musical knowledge use it to make ambient & melodic soundscapes, lush drone mixes, text-to-music, melodies & MIDI for ideas, fun, relaxation, background music, installations etc.

USER REVIEWS
"Essential music app", "Nothing compares", "Masterpiece", "Incredible, fantastic, wonderful, inspiring"

CREATE & EXPLORE
Easily make mixes from 100s of editable templates. Optionally explore powerful generative, adaptive, text-to-music, sound design, cut-up, scripting, Text to Speech [TTS] (macOS 14+) & randomization techniques.

PLAY & RELAX
Use Flow to auto-generate & play a continuous succession of mixes for relaxation, meditation, sleep etc. No internet streaming is required!

FEATURE SETS
• Lite (Default; free & ad-free): A full save mode with a few restrictions (e.g. auto-timeouts, mix editing, MIDI etc.); Recordings licensed for Personal Use only (see EULA).
• Play (In-App Subscription): Unlocks all auto-timeouts & allows export of Flow mix to Box file.
• Pro (In-App Subscription): As 'Play' + unlocks 16 channel MIDI, 12 track mix editing, Randomization Schemes & ALL remaining restrictions; Recordings licensed for Commercial Use (see EULA).

Note 1: See the Wotja 21 webpage for the Feature Set table.
Note 2: Don’t like Subscriptions? Get the paid-for version, Wotja Pro 21!

KEY FEATURES
• NEW: See the Wotja 21 webpage
• 100s of gorgeous generative music templates & Sound/FX presets
• 48 independent content cells in 12 tracks for live mixing/generative cell sequencing
• Flow mode for a succession of freshly generated mixes
• Music Engine (IME) with 130+ parameters for generative (MIDI) music generation & 'text to music' capability for melodies
• Sound Engine (ISE) with ’Macro Oscillator 1’ plus many other synth & FX units + presets
• Text Engine (ITE) for creating 'cut-up’ text with word locking, syllable rules, 5 source fields
• Visual Engine (IVE) for visualizer, screensaver + mini games
• Wotja Script (ECMAScript AKA JavaScript) for programmatic control of parameters & adaptive music
• Support for: 16 channel MIDI In/Out; recording to WAV & MIDI; SF2; iCloud; AirPlay; TTS; Sleep timer etc.

What’s New

Feb 23, 2021

Version 21.4.1

NEW: ISE Audio Meter/Scope - To make sound design easier the Synth & FX Editor now has two new visual displays ("inspectors"), these being a meter for audio-rate units/junctions and an oscilloscope for control-rate units/junctions; Show or hide these inspectors with the new "Synth & FX Editor: Show Meter/Scope" toggle in Settings > General.
NEW: ISE Unit “Bypass?” toggles - To make sound design easier ISE TG/Control/FX units now have a “Bypass?” toggle and ISE Junction units have a “Bypass?” toggle in the junction toolbar; Bypassed units are marked with a subtle white X through the unit.
NEW: "Zero" option in Controller/Junction popup menu - To make sound design easier this provides a quick way to set the value to 0; Access the menu via double-tap or right-clicking the slider.
IMPROVED: ISE Synth & FX Editor layout & display changed to make it easier to see the text on any unit, the selected unit and Controller cables.
IMPROVED: The "Grid Display Mode" button is now "Grid Display Mode and Cell Actions" and includes the items previously in the "Cell Actions..." sub-menu making it easier to use; "Cell" text has been added to make it clearer these are Cell actions.
IMPROVED: Window positioning when moving to / restoring from full screen mode.
IMPROVED: Text to Speech can now use Mix-level FX and be recorded in Recording mode.
IMPROVED: Slowed-down macOS speech, to make it roughly match that of iOS speech.
IMPROVED: Sliders (e.g. Scale) now have increased accuracy (1000 points up from 100 points).
FIXED: FX Unit Wet/Dry modulation where if a value ever hit "zero" it would (for that cycle) pause the unit.
FIXED: Bug in Amplifier unit which could cause glitching when adjusting / modulating pan.
FIXED: Issue with 2 taps being required on toolbar button after exiting Full Screen mode.
FIXED: On exiting Full Screen mode the App Window position no longer changes (but means we no longer hide the App window titlebar in that mode).
CHANGED: Cell/Track mixers now auto-enable Generator envelopes or they could otherwise not work as expected.
CHANGED: Setting "Auto Play (& Full Screen) on File Open" is now "Auto Play on File Open" (Full Screen mode is now accessed via any Play Screen's Action menu).
CHANGED: Setting "Always Show Full Screen Top Panel" is now "Show Play Screen Top Panel" (as the panel never shows in Full Screen mode).

As ever, if anything is not working right then please do get in touch via our Help Page ‘Contact Us’ options.

T.j.V. , 02/06/2020

Generative/Modular/Expansive

Wotja can be as simple as a visual and auditory experience that mixes written words and generative music. Wotja includes many pre-generated mixes and makes it easy for you to create random mixes with just a a couple touches.

Wotja can also be a very deep modular environment where you can edit every aspect of your music and sound parameters. The music engine is very adaptive with rules and settings that you can set for how each part plays. The sound engine is where you get your hands dirty building and editing your modular “synth” creations using sound/tone sources, FX units, controllers and routing junctions.

I don’t think I’ll ever get to explore all of Wotja’s features, but I do learn new things every day with the help of Intermorphic. Anytime I hit a wall, it gets knocked down pretty quick with explanations for those little things that aren’t readily apparent to me.

If you are brave enough to take the dive into Wotja’s vast generative and modular music environment, understand that you have a vast amount of control and there is probably a setting that you can tweak to make your creation sound just the way you want.
