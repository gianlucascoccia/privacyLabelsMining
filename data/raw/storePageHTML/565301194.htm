Description

The Tithe.ly Church App is the leading container app serving nearly 4k churches. Tithe.ly Church App offers a completely customized app experience for churches and their congregations. Download the app for free and select your church to get started!

If your church is not on Tithe.ly please go to https://get.tithe.ly/church-app and signup today!

What does the Tithe.ly Church App offer my church?

• Customized app icon to match your church brand.
• Listen & download sermon audio and podcasts.
• Watch your church's live stream and recorded videos.
• Event and calendar information and add them directly to your device or share them with a friend.
• Use your church's Prayer Wall to post and reply to prayer requests.
• Read Blog entries or bulletins posted by your church or pastor.
• View photos posted by your church or ministry.
• Keep up to date on your church's Facebook & Twitter streams.
• Receive important alert push notifications.
• Map integration to your church and church events.
• Add bulletins and announcements.
• Free E-Giving and instant one click access.
• More!

Then I know what to troubleshoot.

This app basically just goes blank and nothing appears to search, besides it shows where I am cuz share phone location setting is set to on, or it gives a message that the app is offline right now!

I’m finally coming to grips with my autistic self and reasons for communication barriers. I’m a pretty literal person!

Saying the “app is offline right now” sounds like there’s an issue with the app. That apparent wasn’t the case. There was an issue with the internet connection.

If the app can’t connect because my phone doesn’t have an internet connection, PLEASE SAY THAT SPECIFICALLY.

Thank you very much for anyone else who will ever have this issue.
