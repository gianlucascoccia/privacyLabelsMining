Mindthefox , 11/29/2019

*Updated* Great app, allowed time to pay!

So far, this app has proven to be reliable. I’ve started a new job and my second paycheck was still 5 days away when I realized that my bills would barely be out of reach. I saw an ad on tv for Dave and thought it might be a good fit for me. When asking for an advance, I saw that it could take up to 8 hours to transfer, which inclined me to do it sooner than later just in case I would find myself needing the money and not having the time to wait. Next, Dave told me I could pay it back on my next payday, but when choosing an option they only allowed me to select one of the next 2 days. Dave could tell from my transaction history that my next payday wouldn’t be for another 4 days and I had a hard time finding out what penalties I would accrue for being late. But, my phone battery was dying, and internet would be shut off if I hadn’t gone through with it, so I picked the latest possible day and moved on. Without knowing my outcome of this experience, I’d have to say that so far the app has been straightforward, honest, and very handy for personal bookkeeping. I would just like to know more about my payback options and how I can avoid making a bigger hole than I started with, or else I would give 5 stars.

**update**
I was not charged an extra penalty for taking a little longer to pay back my advance. This was my only concern, but now I feel like Dave has shaken my concerns and fulfills my expectations. Would recommend

krzylkafox , 08/23/2018

Truly Awesome

I found Dave via Facebook and read a lot of comments from people making fun of the service, making it sound like people who need it don’t manage their money very well. I was curious to see what it was about and why others feel the need to make fun of people who use it, so I downloaded the app (not expecting to use it, but to just see what it was). As circumstances presented themselves, being a single mom with zero help from the other parent, and trying to provide for my kids (I work full time and go to school full time so I can put myself in a better place, but until then things are pretty tight) - I found myself facing an overdrawn check fee (I wrote a check to register my daughter for school and had something else clear my checking account that wasn’t supposed to go through, which means I didn’t have enough funds for the check I wrote). I remembered I’d installed the Dave app a couple of weeks ago and within a few minutes, I was approved for a small loan and because I paid the extra $4.99 expedite fee, the money was deposited into my bank account within the hour. Dave only charges a $1.00 fee to loan you the money plus any tip you’re willing to donate, but is not required. When your paycheck is due to deposit, Dave takes the loan and fee back out of your bank account and that’s all there is to it! It’s very easy and painless and definitely worth the $1.00 fee to not have to pay an overdraft/insufficient funds fee to your bank.
