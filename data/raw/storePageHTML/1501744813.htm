What’s New

Feb 17, 2021

Version 2.1.1

How is the community doing?

2020 was a tough year for group studying. Our goal in 2021 is to add features to our platform that help members study more effectively together, regardless of their location. We don’t want to say too much more, but in the short term, this update provides the ‘community score’ for each question when you are reviewing it. This means you can see on a per question basis just how hard questions are and how other people are answering. To view a breakdown of how the community answered and the percentage of people who answered correctly just press the community icon, 2 people, next to the flag icon in the lower tray area. This functionality will stay on as you review questions until you press the icon again.

Keep studying hard and we’ll keep the app working hard for you.

Feature-Rich Study App!

I tried many CompTIA study apps and the Pocket Prep offered many elements that others do not.

This app offers you stats of your progress and knowledge, matching it with the community, and lets you know your study streak. It lets you know your weaknesses in particular subjects and lets you focus on those often-missed questions. You have options of time quizzes, weakness quizzes, quick pop quizzes, etc. It even has a count-down to test day in case you have that plan, which is a great motivator. I really like their Question of the Day and you can personalize your reminders/times. Most importantly, they are very thorough in their explanation of the correct answer so you know thoroughly why you got it wrong and how to research it further. Finally, they have amazing customer service and contacted me back very quickly.

I feel confident I will pass with this app!
