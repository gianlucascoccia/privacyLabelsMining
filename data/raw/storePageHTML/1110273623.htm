Description

Browse, search and listen unlimited free music to your iPhone/iPod/iPad and support to iPhone 11.

Music X is an amazing music player which enables you to enjoy your favorite music Anywhere Anytime!!

Key Features:
- Full iPhone X, Xs and 11 Pro Support!
- Discover & search directly with different categories: Classical, Rock, Jazz, Dance, Country, Folk, Hip Hop and so on.
- Search for artist, DJ, song, remix or cover
- Easily find your watch history
- Auto play next music
- Repeat and shuffle songs
- Background playback
- Lock screen playback controls
- Sleep timer

Very good so far ...

I got this app last year I believe and so far it’s been very good 👌👌The songs don’t have bad audio or anything. You can customize your playlist and stuff like that. Some of the songs though don’t have the original artist but instead covers by other people, or remixes of that song. It doesn’t bother me too bad, but it would be nice to have original songs. Take for instance Panic! At The Disco, one song ( Saturday Night/ Say Amen) doesn’t have the original song only covers. But it’s not really a big deal to me. There’s also not a lot of adds which is good 👌👌And they don’t interrupt you either. I did have a problem one day where it wouldn’t let me play ANY songs at all, but I emailed the people who work with the app and it worked perfectly the next morning 👍👍 No serious problems so far, but we’ll see in the future. Highly recommend it it if you like totally free, no sign in necessary, music 👌👌💙💜🖤🔥
