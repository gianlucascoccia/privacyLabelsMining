Description

* Best Ever Status Saver For WhatsApp *
* Join thousands of users and Try it NOW *

Main Features:
1- WhatsApp Status Saver + Widget
2- Import WhatsApp contact from any WhatsApp to your iPhone Address Book.
3- Synchronize your iPhone contacts photos with your friends recent WhatsApp profile pics.
4- Open a WhatsApp chat box with someone not in your contact by entering their phone number.
5- Merge Duplicate/Similar Contacts.

Import WhatsApp contacts (and their photos) from any phone to your iPhone Address Book and start a conversation with anyone on WhatsApp without adding them as contact.

Do you deal with many walking customers that you can't save all their numbers? This app is for you. No More Ghost Contacts! Chat with anyone on WhatsApp!
