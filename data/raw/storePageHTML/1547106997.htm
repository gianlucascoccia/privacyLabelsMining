Description

Offline access to all bookmarks. Smart collections. Share extension, tag suggestions, drag-and-drop, Shortcuts app. Widgets, Handoff, multitasking.

Pins, a modern Pinboard app for macOS Big Sur.

* Access all of your bookmarks. Supports accounts of all sizes. 1,000 bookmarks? Piece of cake. 20,000? No problem. 50,000? Pins can handle that.
* Use Smart Collections to filter your bookmarks by title, description, tags, URL.
* View bookmarks you added on this day in history.
* Use share extension to quickly add bookmarks from browsers and other apps.
* View your bookmarks in Notification Center with Rediscover widget.

More features to come in 2021!

What’s New

Mar 6, 2021

Version 1.3

VERSION 1.3

Pins 1.3 brings thumbnails to your bookmarks for better visualization and a lot of improvements to the "Save to Pinboard" experience.

Bug fixes and improvements:

- Total overhaul of the "Suggest Title" feature. Pins will now attempt to get both the title and description of the page you're bookmarking.
- Better support for 3rd party apps for the share extension.
- Support for new line characters in bookmark descriptions when saving bookmarks.
- Some magic to address "Request timeout" errors.
- More verbose error messages.
- "Open in Safari" button in the in-app browser now opens the page you're on, not the original bookmark.
- Fix "Email Support" button not showing up when you have no accounts set up in the Mail app.

Good, modern Pinboard app

I happily paid for this to have a supported and maintained Pinboard app, and it’s worth it for that alone. In most ways it works as it should and doesn’t have any of the weird brokenness of the other years-old Pinboard apps available (like Pinner and Pushpin).
