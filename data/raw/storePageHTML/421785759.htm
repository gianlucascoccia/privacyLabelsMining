Description

This application converts photo to pencil sketch. Take the photo from photo album or take new photo using camera and convert to pencil sketch right away for free.
Features
1. Ability to use photos from photo album.
2. Ability to launch camera and take photos from application
3. Email your sketches from the application
4. Save the sketch to your photo album
5. Change the contrast and brightness of the sketch by using the settings
6. no stretching or shrinking of images, produce high quality pencil sketched
7. and its free, no need to pay for it
