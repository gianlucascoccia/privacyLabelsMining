Description

If you feel like you’re spending too much time & money on food, then MUNCH:ON is for you.
As a PRO member, you unlock meals at up to 55% off the restaurant price. Non members get discounts too, up to 10% off.
How? Well, every day, MUNCH:ON (previously LUNCH:ON) gives you curated menus, with up to 20 meal offers from 3-5 restaurants, for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Free delivery in the UAE and KSA. We currently deliver to offices across the UAE & KSA and are expanding our residential footprint.
Eat the same. Never pay the same.
How it works:
Check out your menu for the week.  Order the day you want it delivered, or plan a week in advance.
Simply pick your meal before the cut-off time shown on your menu.
Select your price. Pick ‘Regular’ to get the standard discount. Or become a PRO member and get it for up to 55% off. 
We’ll deliver your food within a set time.
Dig in! Get your munch on.
What you get:
Discounted meals, up to 55% off from a great variety of restaurants, famous and local.
Free delivery in the UAE & KSA.
Curated daily menus from 3-5 restaurants.
Ability to pre-order for the entire week.
What you get as a PRO member:
Much larger discounts up to 55% off the original price for 39 AED a month for UAE members & 99 SAR a month for KSA members.
Free trial. Cancel anytime.
Over 5000AED/SAR in savings a year.
By giving restaurants business during their down time, we can give you
meals for lower than the original price. This is why there’s a cut-off time.
The PRO membership fee allows us to give you an even better discount, up to 55% off all meals.
