Description

Wherever you go, take United Community Bank with you! United’s mobile banking app puts the power of online banking at your fingertips 24/7. Our convenient mobile banking app allows you to check your account balances, transfer money, deposit checks, set up alerts and much more all from your favorite mobile device – whenever and wherever you want.

Get back to living and do more with your time:

• Check your account balances and transaction history for your checking and savings accounts and loans
• Pay bills
• Set up alerts to monitor your transactions and account balances to help protect against fraud and overdrafts*
• Deposit checks from your smartphone or tablet**
• Transfer funds between your accounts
• Send money to anyone with a mobile phone or email address with Pay Friends
• View your account statements
• Manage your finances with our Financial Tools including Budgeting/Spending/Net Worth tools
• Link accounts from other financial institutions to show your full financial picture with one login
• Locate a nearby branch or ATM

United Community Bank’s mobile banking app gives you more control of your money without sacrificing our commitment to service.

We are strongly committed to maintaining the privacy of your personal information. For our complete Privacy Policy, visit ucbi.com/privacy.

Have questions? Contact a customer service representative at (866) 270-7100.

About United Community Bank

United Community Bank is headquartered in Blairsville, Georgia and proudly serves customers in Georgia, North Carolina, Tennessee, and South Carolina. Known as ‘The Bank That SERVICE Built®,’ United is committed to providing exceptional service and tailored banking solutions to its customers. For more information about United or our products and services, visit us online at www.ucbi.com.

* Message and data rates may apply. Please check with your mobile carrier for details.
** Mobile Deposit is available to Personal Online Banking customers with an active personal checking, savings or money market account in good standing.
