Description

iShow Screen is a call screen app for incoming calling screen with stunning HD themes. Customize your caller screen with our stylish iShow Screen. You can also download your ringtones by using our app.

FEATURE:
Fancy & Stylish Themes for caller screen
Tons of beautiful, dynamic and stylish caller screen available to beautify your incoming call. Pictures or videos all can be used as caller show.

Ringtones:
Download Ringtones, choose the best ringtone for your phone ringtone, and then choose some more cool ringtones as your contact ringtones. The ringtones are selected carefully with high, unique and diverse.

Beautiful widgets
Clock, date: Supports local time. Launches calendar application and clock application. Different widget themes for you, which can be purchased separately without being a subscriber, the price will be 0.99$ per theme.

Setting List
There will be a list of what you chose, you can check the history anytime you want.
