Description

Simple Soulful App by Shilpa Shetty is a Holistic Health App designed by experts with Yoga, Exercise, Immunity, & Diet Programs.

Bollywood actor & fitness enthusiast Shilpa Shetty Kundra has pledged to spread awareness about holistic wellness by encouraging a healthy lifestyle modification. As a 45-year old mother of two, her active lifestyle proves that she lives by her famous philosophy of 'Swasth Raho, Mast Raho' (Stay Healthy, Stay Happy).

The app offers customized goal-based yoga & fitness programs. The app also offers easy-to-do yoga asanas, exercises & recipes to maintain a healthy balance in life. It is especially made for working women & men, young mothers & also youngsters.

To aid your fitness and wellness journey, the App also offers non-restrictive daily diet plans, recommendations, and tips. The quick & healthy recipes are easy-to-make and appealing to the taste-buds, bringing a balance of delicious and nutritious.

The app boasts of several unique features:
- Workout with Shilpa Shetty: Exercises mentored by experts & performed by Shilpa Shetty herself
- Easy & quick home-based workouts: Workouts that can be performed anywhere, anytime
- Immunity-building: Programs designed to help boost immunity amid the pandemic
- Mind-body balance: Programs that help in de-stressing & developing focus
- Customized nutrition plans: Simple requirement-based recipes & nutrition plans
- Programs for women: Specially curated exercises for pregnancy, post-pregnancy weight loss, & menstrual pain

Yoga Programs:
Boosting immunity, De-stress, Reduce Belly Fat, Yoga Nidra (Good Sleep), Mind, Office, Controlling High & Low Blood Pressure, Diabetes, Anxiety, Partners, Relieve Constipation & Bloating, Back & Joint Pain

Fitness Programs:
Fat Loss, Build Endurance, Men Over 40, Beginners, Toning Body & Abs (Abdomen), Legs, Arms, Post-Pregnancy (Weight Loss), Quick Full Body Workout, Core Strengthening, Cardio

Meditation Programs:
Inner Peace, Pregnancy, Relaxation, Calming Stress, Mindfulness, Quick Mental Reset, Chakra Balance for Positive Thinking

There are also Pilates programs for all levels - Fundamentals, Beginners & Intermediate, along with a fusion of yoga & pilates, called Yogalates program.

The App has Apple’s HealthKit integrated. It will provide heart rate & calories metrics for Programs.** Metrics tracked & available are dependent on device model. With your Apple Watch you can:
- Start Program days
- Control video playback
- Easily track programs using the Activity Ring
- Track & view your Heart Rate while you workout
