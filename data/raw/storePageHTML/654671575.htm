Description

Join loads of players all over the world and become the master of your pirate kingdom. Attack, revenge and steal your friends’ loot in an extreme online pirate experience!

Who will become the richest Pirate King master of all?
- SPIN the wheel to become the richest pirate of them all!
- BUILD your pirate world one island at a time.
- ATTACK your friends’ islands! Show no mercy!
- STEAL loot and prizes from other pirates! Take what your heart desires!
- COLLECT coins and prizes to build your islands. Stash more treasure than any other pirate!
- BATTLE Friends for Gold and Glory to Become the Pirate King!

Pirate Kings combines the adventure of a pirate game and the competitive thrill of multiplayer games. Call all hands on deck and prepare to set sail on this pirate adventure!

For the best multiplayer game experience, invite your friends to join in the fun!
Gift coins and treasures to your friends - who said all pirates were bad?
Anything goes in the most pirate-tastic multiplayer game!

Bonuses and Loot A-Plenty In This Epic Island Adventure Game!
- Spin, win coins, and use them to upgrade your island.
- Once your pirate island is fully upgraded, set sail to your next island.
- Win chances to attack, steal, and raid in this true pirate game experience!
- Complete Pirate Missions! Team up with other pirates on the Totem Chase adventure and search for legendary treasures as you progress through the map.
- Collect colorful cards! Complete the Pirate album for epic rewards!
Pirate Kings adds new events and features all the time to keep the adventure going.

Love pirate games? Love pirate games with friends? Play Pirate Kings!

Become the Greatest Pirate Captain of all your friends. Pirate Kings is even more fun when you play with friends, so invite them all! This is Pirate Kings – the multiplayer game where you choose to be a friend or a foe. Will you take revenge or will you show no mercy?

So call all hands on deck, lower your sales, load your cannons and conquer the 7 seas! Get in the adventure game spirit and become the richest Pirate King of all!
