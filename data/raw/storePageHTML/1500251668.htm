Great game...and a couple of suggestions

I’ve been playing this game for several weeks and really enjoy it a lot. Here’s a couple of suggestions for the developers that would enhance game play.
1. When I earn a wild card from playing multi-card runs, many times I don’t have to use the wild card. When the wild card is not used, how about adding it to the other rewarded wild cards that were earned?
2. The jackpot bonuses are great! Instead of coins, please have the jackpot bonus have wild cards, +plus 5’s, undo’s, and free plays. That would add to the element of surprise...will it be coins, rewards, or both?!!

Scam

This Tripeak solitaire games are such blatant scams for money it’s pathetic, well, only if you actually shell out money for the various shiny packs of coins and prizes they offer, which I have, thankfully just a buck here and there, but still, I’m as pathetic as they come. This cruise one is definitely the worst of the bunch, because I’d hardly gotten into the game before I was tapped dry and looking at the flashing “buy this super cheap package of coins for a limited time only, buy now now now” in-app purchase with interest, but I was strong and deleted the game faster than it took to download. The messed up part is I even won 30,000 coins- I couldn’t believe it! I thought that surely I’d have an hour of playtime, but nope with each round costing me 4000 coins and wild cards and the like costing 4500, that loot was gone in seconds. Will this be the last Tripeak solitaire game I download? Likely not, because they are addictive during that low coin period, but I will never EVER give them another cent.
