Description

Canary is home security made simple. The Canary app works with our security devices to connect your home to your phone, allowing you to view and protect those that matter most.

Monitor your home: Watch live or recorded HD videos day or night. Access Canary’s timeline for a living history of your home. Save and bookmark your favorite clips.

Intelligent notifications: When Canary identifies something out of the ordinary, you’ll get a notification with recorded HD video of the event, as well as the option to watch live.

Automatic arming: Canary changes modes when you come and go. No complicated keypads or codes to remember.

- Away
When all members are away Canary monitors for activity and sends notifications

- Home
When a member is home Canary is customizable. You can set notification and privacy preferences.

- Night
Schedule Canary to monitor for activity while you sleep.

- Privacy
Camera and microphone are completely off.


Emergency Options: Respond to incidents directly from the Canary app. Trigger the 90 decibel siren, or be connected directly to your home’s local emergency responders.

A healthier home: Canary’s HomeHealth Technology™ monitors indoor air quality, temperature, and humidity. It all adds up to a smarter and safer home.

UI STILL needs a major overhaul

This app is a nightmare to navigate, and is inconsistent with modern mobile standards. It is impossible to download a video go your iOS photos app too - you have to either email it to yourself or save it to the local file system. How can this be acceptable, especially for something we have to pay a monthly fee to use?

What other UI employs a “bounce” effect for video? It makes no sense, and causes accidental exiting of the screen. Because video streams from the cloud each time it is opened, you have to buffer all over again and you lose your place. Because of this streaming situation, you have to “request” a download from the cloud which results in an extremely small video file with high levels of compression, and it doesn’t even save to your photos! So you have to text or email it to yourself just to watch the clip again offline... bizarre, right?

The hardware is excellent but the UI/UX is a nightmare. Please fix, it would take very little time to turn this around! Your potential customers’ first impressions are critical, and this app is likely to blame for loss of paid subscriptions. Four years later and no improvement... where is my money going?

DuckieDuck17 , 09/16/2020

Good concept, actual function needs improvement

As a whole, this app would be great if it worked the way they wanted it to. The camera is constantly disconnecting and reconnecting even thought the WiFi connection is fine. It often won’t register that we’re home and will alert us over and over again that “there’s a person at home” even though we’re there. Going into the app and adjusting the settings does nothing to help this. The app glitches often, and usually runs very slowly. When watching a video it recorded, there will often times be gaps in the video feed. Example: we use this to watch our pets when we’re not home and they’ll often set off the camera to start recording. But they’ll be walking across the living room, then the video will glitch and our pet will magically appear on the other side of the room. The two-way talk is only available if you purchase a premium membership (but to be honest, with the quality of the camera it’s really not worth it). The two-way talk is also very glitchy and sometimes comes across garbled and hard to understand.
Overall, it’s not entirely bad. It just needs a lot of improvement.
