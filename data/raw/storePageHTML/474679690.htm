Description

Big Ten Network+ is a subscription video streaming service offered by the Big Ten Network -- the only network dedicated to in-depth coverage of America's most storied collegiate conference, the Big Ten Conference!

With a Big Ten Network+ subscription, you get the following:

1. LIVE STREAMING (NON-TELEVISED GAMES ONLY)
Big Ten Network+ offers live streaming of hundreds of NON-TELEVISED Big Ten games and events produced by the Big Ten Network in partnership with the Big Ten Conference schools. Live streams include regional network productions, Student U productions, and simple camera feeds. A TV Provider is NOT required to subscribe to Big Ten Network+.

Attention: If you are looking for live streams of Big Ten Network TELEVISED games and events (such as B1G Football), those will be found on FoxSports.com or the Fox Sports app. In order to watch live streams of televised games and events on Fox Sports, you will be required to authenticate with separate TV Provider credentials.

2. NEXT DAY, ON-DEMAND REPLAYS
Big Ten Network+ includes next day, on-demand archives of all games and events produced by Big Ten Network+, as well as those televised games and events produced by the Big Ten Network, and Big Ten Conference media partners, including FOX, FS1, ABC, ESPN, & CBS. If you missed yesterday’s B1G game, you will be covered with a Big Ten Network+ subscription.

3. ON DEMAND ORIGINAL PROGRAMMING AND CLASSIC GAMES
Big Ten Network+ includes on-demand access to the vast library of Big Ten Network original programming and classic games. Re-live your favorite school's most incredible games! Or stream previous seasons of popular Big Ten Network shows such as The Journey, Big Ten Elite, and Big Ten Treasure Hunter.

Big Ten Network+ offers three different subscription options:
-Conference Pass: Access all content (annual or monthly)
-School Pass: Access specific school content (annual or monthly)
-Sport Pass: Access specific sport content (annual)

Please note: At this time, Big Ten Network+ streaming service is only available in the United States, Canada and select Caribbean Islands.
