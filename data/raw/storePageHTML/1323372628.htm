Description

Tune your ukulele with precision and ease, instantly!
Optimal for beginners, intermediate and advanced musicians!

· 5 tunings for Soprano, Tenor and Concert Ukuleles.
· Displays the exact sound frequency value in Hz.
· Visual and audible tuning indicators.
· Highly responsive.
· Universal app.
