cgoodermote , 12/10/2020

Great potential, but not there yet.

The app logs out randomly at least once a day. Most apps (unless they’re banking or medical) stay logged in. The JAXJOX app does not.

The Push UpConnect connected once and uploaded data. Since then it does not record any data — except the one time it randomly synced 7 reps to the app, when the Push UpConnect itself had registered 30. Even though it recorded those 7 reps, it erased them all the next day from the app and said “You’ve done 0 workouts this week” and there was no data. Not sure what’s going on here but the app is extremely buggy.

Developer Response ,

Hello and thank you for your feedback. We're so sorry you're having a difficult experience. Please write to us at support@jaxjox.com so we can troubleshoot with your directly and get your workouts back online. We're updating the app all the time for stability and improvements, so please keep your app updated. We look forward to hearing from you and making your workouts exactly as you want them to be. Thank you for your support.

Awful

Bought the kettlebell connect to try before I made a final decision between the JAXJOX interactive studio and Tempo fit. Both units are on back-order or pre-order status. So I thought this would be a good opportunity to check out jaxjox. I subscribed to the JAXJOX premium monthly subscription and the app worked once.
