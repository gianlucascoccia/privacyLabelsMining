Description

IMPORTANT: THIS IS ESSENTIALLY THE SAME APP AS ENDLESS LEARNING ACADEMY, BUT SPECIFICALLY FOR SCHOOLS USING THE VOLUME PURCHASE PROGRAM OR THOSE THAT CANNOT MAKE IN-APP PURCHASES. DO NOT PURCHASE THIS APP IF YOU'VE ALREADY PURCHASED OR SUBSCRIBED TO ENDLESS LEARNING ACADEMY.

Prepare your child for Preschool, Kindergarten, 1st grade, and beyond with this interactive educational app from Originator - Endless Learning Academy! Kids will learn across a broad, customized and ever growing curriculum all while having FUN with the adorable Endless monsters!

Based on classroom-proven learning techniques developed by Originator and now used by tens of millions of children, your child will be engaged and delighted while getting ahead in school.

Endless Learning Academy includes thousands of progressive lesson modules and interactive learning activities covering:

- ABC’s
- Reading
- Vocabulary
- Phonics
- Spelling
- 123’s
- Quantity
- Counting
- Sequences
- Arithmetic
- Music
- Spanish
- Art & coloring
- …and much more

In addition to the interactive lessons, this app includes hours of video lessons on hundreds of subjects to deepen your child’s early learning across a vast array of subjects including:

- Time & duration (e.g. morning, before, after)
- Colors & shapes (e.g. red, blue, circle)
- Size & weight (e.g. big, small, tall)
- Direction & orientation (e.g. top, bottom, over, under)
- Physical states & conditions (e.g. on, off, fast, slow)
- Actions (e.g. throw, catch, sit, stand)
- Anatomy (e.g. eyes, ears, hands, feet)
- Human senses & functions (e.g. sleep, smell, hear, yawn)
- Hobbies (e.g. bicycling, climbing, baking, dancing)
- Social emotions (e.g. share, help, determination, friendship)
- Feelings (e.g. brave, curious, excited, proud)
- Weather & seasons (e.g. rain, summer, thunder, wind)
- Geography & places (e.g. farm, garden, lake)
- Plants & animals (e.g. bird, elephant, fruit, flower)
- Physical science (e.g. sun, stars)

FEATURES:
- Thousands of interactive lessons let your child see, hear, and feel concepts come to life.
- Delightful animations reinforce basic concepts, deeper understanding, and subtle nuances.
- Customizable Common Core based lesson plans grow with your child: progress to more advanced lessons when they're ready, or review individual lessons or entire lesson plans.
- Multiple user profiles on each device allow the entire family to learn at their own pace.
- Freeplay mode allows kids to focus on specific subjects for more practice.
- Designed and developed for children of ages 2-6+.
- 100% free of advertisements and offers.
- Learn on the go: all lessons and activities are accessible when the device is offline.
- Endless Learning Academy was designed with your children in mind. There are no high scores, failures, limits or stress. Your children can interact with the app at their own pace.
