Description

You don’t need stress, you need a high-quality, trustworthy, easy to use voice recording app. That app is CleanRec. This simple app designed especially for iOS users will not only provide all the necessary features for easy and stress-free voice recording, it will also offer other useful features to help make the most of your recorded file.

We’ve all been there - someone explains something to you and you think you remember it all, but 5 minutes later it’s out of your head. You’ve made notes in your notebook but you can’t even read them now. It could be something important, like something your boss said during a meeting or your doctor during your check-up, or it can be something dear to you, like something your friend or parents said. Losing such important information can lead to unfortunate consequences, but not with CleanRec!

With CleanRec’s help you can:
Enjoy the gorgeous minimalistic design made in keeping with iOS aesthetics
Create names for your recordings to keep them organized neatly
Search files easily using tags
Convert your recording into a text right in the app
Edit your recordings - delete the parts you don’t need

Search files easily using tags
and edit your recordings

Your information and precious memories are safe with CleanRec!

Creative!!

I love to read my favorite books over and over. However, when I want to read when I travel or leave out of town, it's quite hard because my books are quite bulky, so I don't have much room to bring them along. My friend then suggested to me to get a voice recording app so I could listen to my books as I traveled without the need to haul them along! It was genius. I downloaded CleanRec thinking I was just going to use it as a normal voice recorder. At the time however, I did notice how minimalistic the app looked and fell in love with the design as well. I read my book out loud like normal for a couple chapters, and figured it was good to last me for my trip out of town. I quickly got ready for my trip the next day, and in the process forgot my earbuds. I was devastated. I had spent so much time getting this recording ready for me to enjoy during this long trip, and now I can't even listen to music. I pulled out the app to delete the recording when I noticed a feature where I could covert my audio into text. I was elated! I watched in awe as my audio turned into text right before my eyes! Now I had a couple chapters of my favorite book right in front of me, and it was all thanks to CleanRec!
