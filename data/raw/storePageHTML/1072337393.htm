How the app works?
=================================================
Video Maker with Music app is really easy to operate to add music to videos.

Just follow these 5 steps:

• Select source video (camera or videos on your iPhone) to add music to video.
• Add video background music from your iPhone, from app's track list, or add video background music by recording your own voice.
• This creative video editor let you mark where you want to add music to video: you can trim the part, if you want, to add to the video.
• Configure volume of audio or songs added to video. Just tap one button to merge video background music when done.
• Now it’s time, you can save it to camera roll like an expert movie maker.
• Enjoy your music video, it is now ready to be shared on social networking sites like Instagram, Facebook, Vine, and YouTube or via email.

I have enjoyed this so far 😉👌

I love it so far. I got it to make a video for my girlfriend and mines trip. It’s a gift to her because of how special she is😊I do need to complain about the recording aspect of it. It only allows one record at a time. Let me explain.. When you tap record, you can record something such as sweet words, music, or that meme your aunt thinks is so funny. However, what if you want to pause the recording and come back to it? Well. It has a button that says “Pause Recording” (after you have hit record of course) But this button actually stops the recording and you don’t end up getting to pick up where you left off. It can get quite annoying. Perhaps if they would allow you to actually “Pause” the recording and then come back to it later, then it would be a 5 star app in my eyes 🙌But all in all, good movie editor 👍
