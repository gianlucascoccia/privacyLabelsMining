Description

Players’ Lounge is a community of gamers competing in skill based head to head competitions and tournaments for cash prizes. Our mission is to give all gamers the opportunity to earn money from esports, not just professionals or streamers.

How It Works:

1. We match you with an opponent of equal skill, who is looking to play the game of your choice.
2. You play each other in that game, online, on PS4, Xbox One or PC.
3. Winner receives the money.

Supported Games:

Madden
NBA 2K
Call of Duty Modern Warfare
Fortnite
FIFA
