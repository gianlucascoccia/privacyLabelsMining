Description

Watch full episodes and live TV from Animal Planet anytime, anywhere. It’s FREE with your TV subscription. Just sign in with your TV provider username and password to get access to favorites like Pit Bulls & Parolees, Treehouse Masters, The Zoo and many more.

New episodes are added all the time.

+ Dr. Jeff: Rocky Mountain Vet

+ Monsters Inside Me
+ My Cat From Hell

+ Lone Star Law
+ Puppy Bowl
And many, many more!

FEATURES
+ Live TV

+ Browse and search for your favorite shows by genre

+ Save episodes to your watchlist
+ Closed-Captioning support

REQUIREMENTS
