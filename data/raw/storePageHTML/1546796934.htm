Description

Rock identification has got easier with the Rock Identifier App. Simply take or upload a picture of the rock, and Rock Identifier can help you identify it within seconds. Let’s start exploring, learning, and recording your geologic surroundings with Rock Identifier!

Key features:
-Identify many kinds of rock
-An impressive accuracy rate to meet your demand
-Rich learning source about rock
-Delicately-designed and user-friendly interface
-Record your geological observations, easily build your own rock collection in the app
