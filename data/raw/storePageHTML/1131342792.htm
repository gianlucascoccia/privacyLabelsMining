Description

Uber Driver app – the app for drivers.

Turn your spare time into earnings with the new Driver app — built with drivers, to bring you helpful information at your fingertips.

Help as you move people and things where they need to go. Drive whenever you want — no offices, no bosses. Wherever you want to go, we want you to enjoy the journey and the destination.

Sign up to drive inside the Uber Driver app. We’ll guide you through the steps and notify you when you’re all set to drive.

A smarter way to earn
Keep track of how much you’re making after every trip, right on the map.
Schedule driving around your life. Plan your days easier with estimated times until your next request and a forecast of rider activity in your area over the next 24 hours.

The support you need to use the Uber Driver app
Take the fear out of your first trips with Uber — you’ll learn how to use the app when you first open it.
Get the help you need with an easy in-app tool to report issues or ask questions.

*This app typically uses 2 GB of data per month. Using navigation can decrease your phone’s battery life.

mel-buf , 07/31/2020

Newer driver

I have driving for a few months and I do enjoy it and of course, love to make some extra money! With that being said, this app needs some work. Multiple times it has taken me to the wrong location to pick customers up. Clearly it’s annoying because I don’t get paid to have to drive somewhere else or trying to figure out where people are. Second, for instance, I was waiting in the driveway of someone’s house to pick them up. Uber told me I was “completely “ there yet and I literally had no where else to park and the customer made me wait like 10 minutes because of what they needed to bring. I did not get the wait time for that trip and I should have. Third, Uber should tell you the full trip details before accepting a ride. Some trips are not worth the drive, especially if I’m driving 15 minutes to get someone and only driving them 5 minutes. But if I don’t take the trip, my acceptance rating goes down. It shouldn’t be held against me. Also, there are times when I know I don’t want to drive anymore and I’m working on my last trip so when I drop the customer off, I’m going to go offline but Uber sends me multiple rides and knowing I’m done, I decline them. But I then get a bad acceptance rating because of it. There should be something that says “do you want this to be your last ride” so I don’t get anymore calls. Overall, I like doing it but there are definitely some cliches to the app.

Aviator1567 , 01/28/2021

No pay for waiting during a multi stop ride!

When you get a ride that has intermediate stops before reaching the final destination, you cannot slide the selector to confirm the stop until the rider is back in the vehicle. If you do it before they are back and ready to drive, you will tell the app that you are now riding to your next stop. This results in getting new trips added to you without being ready to accept them, which you have to decline and then it takes a toll on your acceptance rate. The problem is that while you wait unable to slide to confirm the stop, you are not getting paid for wait time. I’ve had to wait for a rider as much as 25 minutes at a pharmacy without pay. This needs to be fixed.
Another Issue, and this is more an Uber issue than the app, is the driver inability to interject for a customer bad review. It is a one sided conversation that is unfair to the driver. I had a customer give me a rating of 1 ( my only bad rating) for making a totally legal and safe left turn where there is a surface painting depicting this and the app directed the turn to get on course and she felt that it was an unsafe maneuver. It upset me that she didn’t address it with me. She got a very professional drive and went on to report me as unsafe. Uber should see the driver side to see if in the driver’s good judgment in the interest of safety and efficiency, found a good cause for a maneuver
