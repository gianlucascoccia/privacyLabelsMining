Description

SwingU is the top-performing, free golf GPS rangefinder & scorecard app in the world, with more than 5.5 million users around the globe!

This is not a "trial" version with "limited functionality" -- it is FREE FOR LIFE and extremely accurate, reliable, and battery-efficient, rivaling any other golf GPS app or expensive golf GPS handheld or watch on the market!

WHAT YOU GET WHEN YOU DOWNLOAD

* GPS rangefinder that works on every course in the world with distances to greens and every obstacle on the course

* Digital scorecard that easily tracks your score & putts and automatically advances hole-to-hole

* Free handicap after posting scores for three rounds

* On-the-course games including stroke play and skins

* Daily golf instruction tip from our team of top-100 instructors

* Apple Watch integration - get distances and keep score for you and your friends!

* HealthKit features have been included when using the Watch on the course. Track calories burned while you play!


WHY DO MILLIONS OF GOLFERS USE OUR APP?

* Reliable and easy to use - one tap and you’re playing

* Constantly improving with new functionality and new technology

* Commitment to user satisfaction with quick, reliable customer service

* Community-built course database: works on every course in the world


UPGRADE TO PREMIUM FOR A COMPLETE DIGITAL CADDIE EXPERIENCE & YOUR OWN STROKES GAINED ANALYSIS

Upgrade to SwingU Premium, our AI-powered digital caddie and instruction platform. With additional technology and functionality on-the-course, you can truly own your game through an integrated strokes gained analysis system, SwingU Versus, that tracks and analyzes every facet of your game.

With SwingU Versus, compare your game versus a target handicap, a scratch golfer or a tour pro; and see how you stack up across all five categories on the course: driving, approach shots, chipping and pitching, bunker play and putting.

Each facet of your game will be given a “relative handicap” so you can easily and accurately identify the area of your game that needs the most attention. SwingU’s AI-driven algorithm will assign you a prescriptive drill, focusing you on the game-improvement priority that will lower your handicap and scores FAST!

dcfire , 11/24/2017

Missing one feature

I have used this app for a full year and have recommended it to all my buddies. It is a great app! I would rate this app with 5 stars except I think it needs one feature added. I would like to be able to manually enter club distances. I sometimes forget to tag a club or I fail to finish the distance until I move to the next shot. I often know the distance, but just don't get it entered. Being able to manually enter my distances would ensure that I get more accurate club data. Please add this feature.

Additionally, the Apple Watch version needs some work. For instance, the app automatically goes to the scoring screen when near the green. However, if you leave that screen, to finish tracking a shot for example, there's no way to go back to the scoring screen. Also, there is no way to delete a tracked shot if you shank one and decide not to track it. If you have already entered the club, you have to delete it from your phone. In fact, I still have to keep my phone within easy reach, because the phone app version is so much more user friendly. I could not accurately score a round with just the watch.

I would also like to see the Apple Watch version use its own GPS and not the phone’s. I think this would improve the speed of the app since it’s slower than other apps I have tried.

One more thing. It would be nice if the Apple Watch version would automatically track a golf workout in the Workout app

Please upgrade. Thanks.

Developer Response ,

HI: If you would send an email to support@swingbyswing.com, we will be glad to respond to your suggestions and questions. Thank you

Quit working on my Apple Watch

I really liked the app and all the funtionality. The overhead satellite shots and distance calculation seems to be second to none. Recently I found that a local course did not match up with my scorecard. I went on the website to edit the golf course, and they did have the correct information on their website. However, in my app the back nine was incorrect for nearly every hole. Their support got back to me quickly, but something is not right between the scorecard on my app and what they show on the website. For the last three weeks, I have not been able to use my Apple Watch with the app. I work for a company that uses Apple exclusively, and I’m all up-to-date with the latest technology. I’ve gone online and spent half an hour going through mediocre instructions on how to set up the watch. It is all to no avail. It’s very frustrating having to pull out your phone all the time and not be able to use your watch. I am going to try GolfLogix. If the watch functionality works, it has five stars. Without the watch, three stars at best. To be clear, I used this for a year and a half with no problems, then all of a sudden around the beginning of June my watch no longer worked with the app.

CCrunk , 01/30/2021

Good Phone App, Horrible Watch App

The phone version of the SwingU app is pretty good. It tracks your handicap, it has an intuitive interface to input scores, and is pretty accurate on distances on the course. The club tracking is nice and the stat tracking is really informative on how you have played over several rounds too.

However, my issue with the app is mainly through the Apple Watch version. I’ve played 90 rounds with this app and the watch version FREEZES EVERY SINGLE TIME. I’ve even tried keeping my phone in my pocket while playing too, which is not very comfortable, and it still freezes. Restarting the app when this happens sometimes fixes the issue, but not for long. I even went out and bought the cellular version of the Apple Watch recently because I thought that it might help. No luck. It is very frustrating because I enjoy the phone app itself and get use out of many of the features. I just wish I could use the watch version more on the course and didn’t have to go to my phone after leaving every green to input my scores.

If the developers could figure out how to solve the Apple Watch bugs, this would be a perfect golf app. But for now 3/5.

Thank you for the review and feedback regarding the Apple Watch. We would be happy to work with you to improve your experience if you could please email support@swingu.com. Please keep in mind that currently the watch pulls all information from the phone, so you need to keep it on your person during the round, as we do not take any GPS data from the watch itself.
