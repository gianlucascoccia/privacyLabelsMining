Description

Don’t go the extra mile. Go Straightaway. Join tens of thousands of drivers already shaving hours off their routes everyday. Add up to 250 stops, optimize, get directions and be on your way faster than any other app. Whether you’re delivering packages or food, running routes or running errands, Straightaway can get you done faster. We’re the #1 Route Planner for delivery drivers across FedEx, UPS, DHL, onTrac, and Amazon. Use what the pros use. Get Straightaway.

Add stops with the snap of a photo.

Take a picture of an address and see it instantly on a map. You’ll be doing routing magic before you know it.
- Take a picture of your itinerary
- Take a picture of a shipping label
- Take a picture of the shipping manifest
- Take a picture of NMLS listings
- Take a picture of anything and be on your way.

Fastest search out there. We have a global database from hundreds of sources updating addresses daily. Search for stops manually and never miss a beat. Our intuitive, quick design makes adding stops to your route a breeze.

Optimize for the fastest route.

Get up to 250 stops is the quickest order in seconds with our best in class route optimization algorithm. We’ve been improving our technology for over a decade now to get you the best route possible, every time. We look at real-time traffic, road closures, speed limits and even weather conditions to get you where you need to go.

Navigate without switching apps.

We built turn by turn navigation right into Straightaway so you’ll never get lost again. With Straightaway autocomplete and auto advance your run through your stops and finish your route in no time.

How to use Straightaway to plan and optimize your route:
- Scan addresses: Use your phone’s camera to capture up to 250 addresses or add stops manually
- Optimize your route: Our proprietary, state of the art route optimization algorithm will get you the best order of stops available.
- Navigate to stops: Hit directions for any stops or all your stops and get audio guided, turn by turn directions built right in.

Free Version:

Pro Version - $9.99/month:

Add a few things and it would be PERFECT!

* add the option to print or export the route so that notes can be written (or an option to record notes in app). Would be extremely helpful, as I wouldn’t have to look through mixed up addresses on a separate sheet for each address to make sure I’m meeting the customer’s requests
* add the option to clear the “DONE” check marks without starting over (or unchecking each one) for those that reuse the same route each day
* Show the address on the map view/directions screen. I had to keep going back to check the address if I forgot the numbers.
* add the ability to start the route and keep going without stopping to physically click on the next stop and then click again to start directions (the check marks are good though - maybe once a stop is marked “DONE” automatically start directions for the next location.
* add a way to insert a temporary road closure (I ran into a road closure today out in the middle of no where) and get detour directions

Other than that, great app! The directions have been spot on - I even found 2-3 locations I haven’t been able to find before - and this is in rural Ohio - I’m talking 4-way dirt road intersections! It definitely made the morning run much more smoothly. Thanks!
