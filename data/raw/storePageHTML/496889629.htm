Description

FamilyWall is the easiest way to organize your family life and keep everyone in the loop. A leading family organizer that includes a shared calendar, Grocery lists, a Family Locator, a Photo/Video Album, a secure messenger and more.

With FamilyWall, you can spend more time doing what you love, and less time organizing it. The whole family can easily access FamilyWall with a phone, a tablet or any web browser to keep everyone on the same page.

==== FEATURES ====

SHARED FAMILY CALENDAR
- An easy to use color-coded calendar, view an individual's schedule or the whole family at once
- Add or edit appointments that everyone in the family can see
- Set reminders so no one misses soccer practice or an important event
- Import your current calendars with a single touch (Outlook/Google)

SHOPPING LISTS
- Share grocery & shopping lists with the whole family
- Browse your lists even offline when you’re at the store and quickly check items while you shop
- See items added by other family members. Never forget the almond milk again!
- Manage your shopping lists together with your smartphones, tablets and desktop

TO-DO LISTS
- Create a private or share to-do list, a wish list or a chore checklist for kids
- Assign to-dos to selected family members
- Track progress of personal or family to-do lists
- Create as many lists as you want like packing lists, the kids camp list, emergency supplies and more

FAMILY LOCATOR
- Parents can use location tracking to keep an eye on their kids
- Each member can easily manage to whom he/she wants to share his location.
- Set Family Places such as “home,” “work,” and “school” and receive automatic notifications for arrivals and departures

FAMILY MESSAGING
- Using Messaging, a member can directly post a short message to the family members who will be directly notified.
- Each member will be able to browse the conversation using a thread view and easily participate in the conversation.

FAMILY GALLERY
Share among your family and friends your best moments. The Gallery allows you to share photos, Videos in a simple and private way.

IMPORTANT CONTACTS
The Family directory allows to rapidly find a useful contact of the family. Any member can add a contact with its details (e.g. Babysitter, grandparents…).

Everything and more!

Such a great app. Initially using it to care for a loved one as well, but finding so many fun yet functional aspects. Being able to share one calendar that everyone can see and receives notifications on is absolutely essential. Also love the fact you can create do lists and assign to people, create grocery lists, see where members are at. The fun part? Sharing pics, notes, messaging to some members or all and just being able to communicate effectively with each other even though we’re separated by thousands of miles. Keep up the great work! One question though: how can you see each other’s location? Also, one idea for a function is to quickly do a video call with members.

Lito30 , 08/23/2019

Quite nearly perfect!

I really, really like this app. My family is a little communicationally challenged, where different people know bits and pieces of a thing and no one tells one another. Then they’re like “oh, you didn’t know?” *sigh* They’re still developing the habit of using this but even in this learning period it has done so much to get us on the same page. We have relatives out of state and it’s reduced the chaos of organizing their visits so, so much. It’s ever so nearly perfect.

The main reason I gave it 4 stars instead of 5 is the notification system. Notifications show up in iOS saying what’s new, no problem. When you go to the app itself there’s no indicators pointing you to the update. So if someone posts to the wall I’ll get a notification saying person X said this on the wall. When I go in the app, if I’d never seen that notification I’d have no idea what happened because there’s no counter or anything. The only thing that does it are messages, and even those you have to go into the messages before it updates and shows a dot. I really, really like this app. Once you make notifications a little more friendly, I’ll love it like a brother!

Developer Response ,

Thank you very, very much Lito30 for the great feedback. Your message confirms us the importance to improve the notifications rapidly. Feel free to send us any further suggestion on support@familyandco.com it really helps us make FamilyWall even more useful and easy to use.

winbley1530 , 04/19/2020

Awesome app. Map misleading.

I love (almost) everything about this app. After doing research I chose this app over other family planners available because of the plethora of features and how customizable it is! It’s like my family has its own “Instagram.” I was also blown away by the amazing customer support Family Wall provides. I gave four stars because when I am at home the apple map location shows my “current location” as my neighbors address. It’s frustrating because the “share my location” option is the first option when checking in on the map and I would love to be able to quickly tap that option, but, if I were to do that it would check me in at my neighbors address despite the fact the map shows my location to be pointing on my house when zoomed in. It would be nice if the Apple map in this app were more accurate. Especially since it has an SOS feature-that could prove to be very misleading in the event of an emergency!
