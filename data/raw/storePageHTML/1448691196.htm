麦当基叔叔 , 06/25/2020

Some weird stuff really stopped me playing

The game is really encourage camping, each Mecha almost deals the same DPS so there’s not much skill required but rather who started the damage first. If there’s another camper starting to attach you right after the fight then you are pretty much done. As the human form is pretty impossible to kill a mecha or even escape in open areas. Some game mechanics are super weird as well: if you jump while you are trying to use an item such as healing or vehicle, the progress immediately stops and you have to start over. This can be frustrating when you are trying to escape, as standing still is stupid in this game because the hit scan area is soooo huge, again anyone can kill you is just the matter of time. I’m a pretty good mobile FPS gamers and I almost never die in human vs human but always killed by mecha camper which made this game not fun at all because the mecha doesn’t requires that much skill. Another stupid mechanic is when I’m trying to escape by summoning a vehicle, and the progress was disrupted for some reason, the item icon switch to healing items immediately after I took a damage, and I have to switch it back to try to summon a vehicle to escape. Overall, I have to play one game a day to make my monthly pass worth it, but I don’t see myself play this game after because it’s so easy for campers yet so hard me who just wanna get as many kills as possible.
