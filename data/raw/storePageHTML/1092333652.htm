Description

Hello Dear,

Capsule is a new kind of pharmacy. One that is smarter, friendlier, faster and hand-delivers your medication, same-day, for free.

Capsule is free to use — you only pay your copay.

Get started with Capsule in 15 seconds. Or tell your doctor to e-prescribe your medication to Capsule. Any doctor can send prescriptions to us.

We’ll text you to schedule your free, same-day delivery. You can manage all your prescriptions from your phone. We’ll also coordinate refills with your doctor and copays with your insurance company. Chat or text with a pharmacist for any questions.

Never worry about your meds again and enjoy the peace of mind that comes with having your health looked after.

Love,
Capsule

Seriously great

No offense to Rite Aid, but waiting in line to find out they don't have your med in stock really blows, as does waiting until next week for them to get their shipment. Capsule is probably the only time I've seen an ad in the subway for some service startup that actually turned out to be as great as they say they are. Simply being able to track my scripts and refills in an app is useful, and I love that I get a notification right away when Capsule receives the script from my doc. But the cherry on top is the totally reliable delivery service THAT IS FREE. They offer 2-hour windows until 10 pm and if I need something fast there is always a window available withing the next few hours. Oh and also you can text them with questions or changes or whatever, and they get back to you promptly with helpful and friendly answers. I have no affiliation with Capsule and I almost never write reviews, but Capsule is so great that I want to help make sure they never go out of business!! Once you go Capsule, you'll never go back

AmandaBNYC , 05/01/2018

Highly Recommend

I can’t say enough great things about Capsule. I honestly wouldn’t have tried it unless one of my doctors had put me on, but I am so glad she did. Capsule not only conveniently delivers my prescriptions to all of my multiple jobs, but they have a 2 hour delivery window which is extremely reasonable. Capsule has open and easy communication and I have handled everything that I needed via text. I’ve had changes in insurance since I started using Capsule and once I sent a picture of my new insurance card, they handled the rest. I also had a long-standing refill at a pharmacy that no longer accepts my insurance all I had to do was send Capsule a text explaining my situation and my prescription was transferred and delivered to me later that day. Capsule also accepts insurance that many other pharmacies don’t. I rarely take the time to write reviews, even when I am satisfied, but Capsule really makes my life that much easier. The company and the app are both excellent.

ffisherb , 07/10/2019

Life changing so far

I was hesitant to use as my meds *are* so important, but with the customer support being on text it’s so easy. I’m bi-polar with extreme anxiety and am therefore on a very strict med. regimen - living and working in Manhattan makes it tough to be able to get my controlled substance prescriptions - meetings run long and you can’t transfer controlled meds. to different pharmacies that suit my home/office locations. Capsule is a game changer. It takes all the brainwork out of my monthly med pickup (something that used to be surprisingly complicated as a freelancer) my co-pays are actually less and I’m always able to get a same day delivery. As someone who works very hard to treat my illness as any other (diabetics need their insulin, I need my brain meds) but has had issues getting some of my prescriptions in time due to their controlled substance status, Capsule is life changing. Customer support is on top of everything, the UX is superb. Cannot say enough good things about this app.
