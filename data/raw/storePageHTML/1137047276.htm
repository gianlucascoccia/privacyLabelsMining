Description

Bald Head application allows you to turn any of your photos into Bald Headed photo with Funny face makeup and make people look funny.

Bald Head makeup is very easy to use

1. Take a new photo or select one from your gallery which you want to turn people into Bald Headed people.
2. Select the bald image using bald head makeup tool
3. Select other funny stickers like beard, hair, glasses which you want to add to the photo
4. Share your bald photos with your friends and family in Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, WatsApp and other social networks.
5. Bald your friends!

Also you can adjust every photo aspect

* Lot of customized magic mirror effects.
* Brightness
* Contrast
* Saturation
* Blur
* Temperature
* Shadows/Highlights
* Crop
* Focus

Enjoy and have fun which Bald Head makeup application!
