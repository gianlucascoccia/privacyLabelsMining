Description

Are you currently in a relationship? Start using Between! Between is an app for couples to communicate more lovingly and for storing precious memories easily.

#Couple conversations with more love!
Between is a messenger just for two where you can use free emoticons and GIF selfies to be more romantic when talking to each other!

#Precious memories can be stored easily!
You can store your photos, videos, and notes easily. Everything stays saved in Between even when you change phones!

#Schedules and special days saved in one place!
Check each other’s schedules and special days in Between! There is even a feature that automatically calculates the count down for your shared calendar and special days!

#Anytime, anywhere - a conversation just for two! Try out Between!
Use the free call feature and Between PC version to always keep the conversation going with your loved one! All Between data is encrypted and completely secure, so you can feel safe about using it!

Kass79 , 03/14/2020

Works for me

This app has many cute aspects. I really enjoy using the stickers to animate the conversation. My boyfriend and I have the lifetime membership so we can share photos in high definition and other small perks. I enjoy the calendar feature and being able to post pictures of our dates that later appear on the calendar itself. I’m the chat we can compose love letters with the paid membership and they usually make my day. I enjoy the variety of themes provided with the purchased membership but even if you don’t purchase it you’ll have a few themes to choose from. The app has mini video template you can use, the videos seem to be only around 3 seconds or so. Another perk is that you can call from the app.

Now that I’ve told you all the good things about the app I’ll tell you some of it’s less likable aspects. The purchased membership does not give you access to all stickers those still need to be purchased separately with a pack for both of you being 300 coins (6 dollars). The app has never really failed to send one of my text messages but it often fails on my boyfriends phone with no notification or loading messages. Videos can only be chosen one at a time and take a while to compress and send to your partner.

All of that being said I don’t think I would switch back to regular texting. I enjoy the app, the stickers, the themes and overall it’s a more fun experience using it to talk to your partner.

Love

My boyfriend and I love Between! We are both college students. It’s easy for us to put our class and work schedules into the calendar on Between and be able to see when we’re both busy and when we’re both free for a date. I also love the chat feature, because I can’t accidentally text someone else. I know my message is going to him. I love being able to upload photos into stories, we have so many different memories uploaded to Between. This app is truly great. I would love a feature where we could video chat or share our screens and video chat (so we could watch a show together and talk to each other at the same time long distance) and a map feature (wherein the photos I’ve tagged a location on will put a pin on the location). I just think it would be nice to be able to see the map and see all the places we’ve been together. Again, I truly love this app and have nothing bad to say about it. I just hope to see new features in the future! The love letters feature is awesome and I love surprising him with them.

So I started to using between as a alternative to couple. However couple became glitchy and then now it’s doesn’t even exist anymore.
