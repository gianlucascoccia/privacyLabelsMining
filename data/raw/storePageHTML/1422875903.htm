Description

Identity Theft Protection is hard. We make it easy for you with the LIFELOCK IDENTITY app and a NORTON 360 or LIFELOCK plan. You’re across town—and someone is taking out a bank loan in your name. We’ll send you an identity alert†. If it’s not you taking out that loan, you can respond right away, and we’ll go to work on your behalf.

LIFELOCK PLANS INCLUDE:

• LIFELOCK IDENTITY ALERT™ SYSTEM†: Alerts you if your Social Security Number, name, address, or date of birth are used in applications for credit or services.


• DARK WEB MONITORING: Continuously patrols the dark web and notifies you if we find your information.


• US-BASED ID RESTORATION SPECIALISTS: Should you become a victim of identity theft, an Agent will be dedicated to your case from start to finish to help restore your identity.


• MILLION DOLLAR PROTECTION PACKAGE†††: If you become a victim of identity theft, you’ll be covered for lawyers and experts up to $1 million, and stolen funds and expense reimbursement up to the limits of your plan.



ADVANTAGE AND ULTIMATE PLUS PLANS ALSO INCLUDE:

• EASY ACCESS TO CREDIT SCORE(S) & REPORT(S)1: Access to monthly or annual credit information (based on your selected plan), an essential part of credit protection.

The credit scores provided are VantageScore 3.0 credit scores based on data from Equifax, Experian and TransUnion, respectively. Any One-Bureau VantageScore mentioned is based on Equifax data only. Third parties use many different types of credit scores and are likely to use a different type of credit score to assess your creditworthiness.

• IDENTITY LOCK: With a single tap, lock your TransUnion credit file.‡

• TRANSACTION MONITORING: Monitors your bank, credit card, and retirement account activity.†

NORTON 360 plans without LifeLock include dark web monitoring only.

LIFELOCK IDENTITY ADVISOR includes dark web monitoring, breach notifications, and merchant resolution management. It does not include the Million Dollar Protection™ Package or additional alert features offered in higher-tiered plans.

No one can prevent all identity theft or cybercrime.

IF EITHER OF THE FOREGOING REQUIREMENTS ARE NOT MET YOU WILL NOT RECEIVE CREDIT FEATURES FROM ANY BUREAU. REShaman , 10/22/2020

Good to know

The App allows for Face ID, but only for the primary account holder. What about the primary account holder’s spouse also a member/user? Doesn’t make sense to exclude when a couple are member/users. On the plus side, having this information/ protection especially in these times when predators are lurking around and without consequences to them but to us. The app should also provide a spouse with the same categories of information as the primary account holder. I am a long time Norton client and I made the mistake of making my spouse the primary while I am the techie in the family. Norton makes it so only one primary is permitted. And for a married couple/family that’s absurd. There are other apps like 1Password that recognize and allow for sharing as if both are primary. Still, having Norton’s protection is worth the inconvenience. But given the feedback, Norton is slow or non-responsive to implementing changes to suit their customer base which is not only silly, it’s nonsensical given the cost. There’s no family membership saving. However, the app is worth having overall. Wish Norton would catch up to who they’re protecting not only how.

Update: The present state of the app build now allows one’s spouse to activate and use successfully Face ID. Don’t know what changed but that’s the good news!

Norton LifeLock

Not sure I am loving the change from LifeLock to Norton. At least with LifeLock all of my accounts were being monitored.
