Description

Mileage, Expense & Time tracking made easy: Automate mileage tracking with IRS compliant mileage and expense logs. Built for individuals and businesses of all sizes.

Rated #1 Mileage App and Expense Log for Individuals and Companies for IRS tax deduction and company reimbursement.
TripLog Mileage provides accurate mileage tracking for tax deductions and mileage reimbursement for every type of business, big or small. From the self-employed driving a car for their business, to enterprises offering mileage reimbursement to their employees, Triplog Mileage offers diverse options and features for every user.

Benefits
• TripLog Mileage App helps users save an average of $7,200 annually.
• Save time & money by automating mileage and expense capture you maximize your mileage tax deductions and reimbursements.

Feature Rich Mileage Expense App
• Multiple Tracking Options: MagicTrip™ automatic mileage using phone GPS, TripLog Drive™ GPS logging device, TripLog Beacon assistance device, or utilizing Car Bluetooth, Plug-N-Go, and Manual Tracking.
• Expense Capture: Upload your paper receipts, tolls and fuel receipts for easy reimbursement. Auto scan fuel receipts with text recognition.
• Track driving safety: Detect Hard Brake, Rapid Acceleration, Phone Calls, Speeding.
• Better Map Views: Multiple-stop route planning, Daily driving overview, Frequently traveled Heatmap, Most visited locations, Longest stayed places, Real-time fleet tracking.
• Data Integration: ADP, Concur, Chromeriver, QuickBooks, Xero, and Bank and credit card integration to track revenue and expenses.
• Calendar Integration: Read meetings from Google Calendar, Outlook Calendar, and Salesforce and import into TripLog.
• Tax Compliant Reports: Hassle Free Tax Logs
• Comprehensive Dashboard: Employers can manage their company's accountable plan and employee's requirements with a comprehensive dashboard featuring an easy-to-use interface.
•Enterprise Features: Approval management, Daily commute mileage, Department/office, Single sign-on, Google maps distance, Company address book, Time tracking reports, Custom tags, etc.
•HIPAA Compliance: TripLog encrypts protected health information (PHI) and personally identifiable information (PII) in a secure environment for your privacy.
•Real-time Location Monitoring: Helps companies and drivers stay on track throughout the day, including a map view with daily/weekly travel routes.

Miles=Money
Perfect for the Self-Employed, Employers, Employees and any business needs
Employers: Businesses rely on TripLog to track mileage expenses, reimbursement and minimize tax exposure.
Employees: Employees can easily track and submit mileage expense log for fast, accurate reimbursement. So you never miss a cent.
Business owners: Business owners can cover a single car or a small fleet of vehicles to ensure accurate and tax compliant mileage tracking. Great mileage tracker for Contractors, Salespeople, Real Estate Agents and Nurses..
Freelancers: Freelancers can easily segment between business use and personal use when tracking mileage related to work
Independent contractors: Contractors can easily keep their business and personal mileage separate and properly track mileage related to client work.
Rideshare drivers: TripLog makes it easy for rideshare drivers to track mileage related to service passengers and reporting expenses to the IRS. Uber / Lyft/ GrubHub
Personal use: Whether using a vehicle for charity work or personal reasons, TripLog helps keep track of virtually every mileage situation.
