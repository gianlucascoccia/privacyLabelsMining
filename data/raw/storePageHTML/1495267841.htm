It’s okay but....

This is a good game but it could use lots of improvement. First off, Whenever I was coloring it didn’t kick me out but once I left the game to play the next morning, it wouldn’t let me back in. 2nd, when your coloring there is a way to drag color but it’s really annoying. The reason for that is because you have to hold down your finger for longer than you should. It’s like 3 seconds. Although that may not seem long, it really is for swift coloring. Also you have this annoying magnifier in your face that nobody asked for and to make matters worse there’s no settings to turn it off. In all, I think this game is ok but there’s lots of room for improvement.
