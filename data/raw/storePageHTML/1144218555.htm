Description

Ace your next Cosmetology exam with the help of the new Cosmetology Exam Center app! Thousands of Cosmetology questions and their detailed explanations are now available at your fingertips.

Prepare for your State Board of Cosmetology exam by answering thousands of questions written by Cosmetology professionals.

Features:
* Detailed question explanations
* FREE Question of The Day
* Question I Got Wrong area that helps you revisit the questions you got wrong
* Inspired by Milady and Pivot Point methodologies
* 7 State Board of Cosmetology exams
* Dedicated exam for each Cosmetology chapter
* Tens of Cosmetology exams each with tens of questions
* Offline support - After installing, no need for an internet connection
* Native app optimized for speed and ease of use
* NO ads
* Clear right/wrong answer indications
* Exam summary with exam grades and more stats

Our mission at the Cosmetology Exam Center is helping students ACE their next Cosmetology exam. Please send us feedback or questions at:

The BEST creation ever!!!

I am currently a Student at Aveda Institute. I graduated high school in 2009 and can’t tell you the last time I took a major exam! My school printed out the mock exams so that I could study, but that did not work for me. I was not retaining the information. I would go over the questions & check my answers with the last sheet of the packet. Any questions/answers I was unsure of, I had to pull out my book to figure out the why’s. With this app, all I had to do was click the question “?” mark and it explained it to me right then and there. Not only does it give you the mock exams, it breaks down each individual section into its own tests! Anatomy & Physiology, Chemicals, Electricity, Skin Disorders, Nail Diseases & Disorders, etc. It also has a tab for “questions I got wrong” so that I could go back and easily look over what I missed. Talk about AMAZING! For only $10 a month! I spend $10 on food and snacks so why not put it towards my future right?! Needless to say I passed my state board practical....the first time!!!! Tears of joy!!!! I would recommend this app to the entire universe! It’s so worth it!!! Pay the $10! I promise you won’t regret it!❤️

Definitely Recommend

I had went to cosmetology school 6 years ago and didn’t really have interest afterwards on if I really wanted to do hair. Pregnancy and boards

I was 32 weeks pregnant when I graduated cosmetology school so I held off on my boards until after I gave birth. Two months later I realized how hard it was -trying- to study, write notecards, and take care of a baby that I almost gave up my dream career. I absolutely LOVE this app and how easy it is to use. Instead of carrying around heavy books, notecards and trying to fit them in a diaper bag, I have everything I need on my phone and can pick up and set down a test anytime I need to.
