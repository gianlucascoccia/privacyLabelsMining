Description

Join Bubbu and his friends in the funniest animal school ever!

Welcome to the amazing world of Bubbu School! Meet your cute virtual pets and make their learning awesome. Don’t worry if you like school or not - you rule in this animal school!

Dress up your pets in unique outfits and start with your favorite subject. Is it art, gym, music, language or maybe math? Doesn't matter, try them all and educate yourself while having fun.

ART
To express your creativity, you can draw a painting with a perfect set of colored pencils. In addition, your creations can be decorated with a collection of beautiful stickers. Furthermore, you can spark your imagination with an exciting coloring minigame or make some crazy decorations with fruit and vegetables.

MUSIC
Learn to play the piano or prepare a concert with your favorite instruments. You can enjoy a single instrument or a whole band with the guitar, piano, drums, trumpet, violin, cello, harp and singer. Decorate the scene with fog machine, confetti and disco ball. Dress up and fix the makeup. It's time to rock this school of fun!

GYM
Get some exercise in the fresh air. It's always exciting to go down the slide or swing with a friend. Organize an hour of gymnastics or shoot some hoops.

MATH AND LANGUAGE
Let's do some brain training. You can play with geometric shapes or learn about addition, subtraction, multiplication and division at the difficulty level of your choice. Learn some basic English words or train your writing to master the alphabet.

SCHOOL RESTAURANT
Become a masterchef. Prepare delicious sandwiches, sweet fruit and salad for the hungry pupils or serve them a yummy birthday cake.

To return home safely, you can practice safe walking in a legendary road crossing minigame.

Would you like to know our adorable virtual pets even better? Collect all the puzzles from their memory album, put them in the right place and reveal what memories they hide. In addition, you'll unlock a unique colorful fish for each album page. Decorate the school with a relaxing aquarium full of all kinds of beautiful fishes.

It's time to try out this endless fun! Bubbu cat, Duddu dog, Bunny, Crocodile, Piggy, Hedgehog, Owl, Penguin and Panda are waiting for you!

FEATURES
- highly interactive and cartoonish pretend play game,
- lots of exciting and educational activities that encourage exploration and creativity,
- fun and relaxing game for girls, boys and whole family,
- many cute animated animal characters,
- adorable jigsaw puzzle collection,
- luxurious and gorgeous decorations,
- beautiful high-quality and detailed graphics,
- no in-app purchases,
- can play offline anytime.
