Description

Stream 300+ dynamic stretching, toning & pain-relief Essentrics workouts!

The creators of the award-winning TV programs Aging Backwards®, Forever Painless®, Classical Stretch®, and Essentrics® bring you a unique workout that combines scientifically-designed strengthening and stretching exercises to create a flexible, balanced and pain-free body.

The Essentrics TV app provides unlimited streaming access to 300+ workouts designed for women and men of all ages and fitness levels. Each routine will engage and rebalance all of your 650 muscles and 360 joints – creating long, lean muscles, improving posture and mobility, enhancing range of motion, increasing energy and blood flow, and promoting healthy cells.

Essentrics TV members have access to…

- Classes ranging from 10-60 minutes in length
- Health challenges & fitness programs (5 to 30 days)
- Exclusive workouts available only to members
- New content added regularly
- A variety of Essentrics instructors
- Full collection of Essentrics & Classical Stretch dvds
- Educational features & documentaries

To access all features and content you can subscribe to Essentrics Workout on a monthly basis with an auto-renewing subscription right inside the app.* Pricing can vary by region and will be confirmed before purchase in the app. In app subscriptions will automatically renew at the end of their cycle.

* All payments will be paid through your Apple Account and may be managed under Account Settings after the initial payment. Subscription payments will automatically renew unless deactivated at least 24-hours before the end of the current cycle. Your account will be charged for renewal at least 24-hours prior to the end of the current cycle. Any unused portion of your free trial will be forfeited upon payment. Cancellations are incurred by disabling auto-renewal.

Restoration

Essentrics is a life-enhancing, supportive process of exercise that gives immediate benefits of refreshment & strength! The teachers are all very positive, directing each part of the process with attentive care.
I had Achilles- lengthening surgery on both legs in 2007, at the same time as an onset of Fibromyalgia put an end to my previously active life. Just being alive was constantly painful & my life felt diminished in every way. I remember that I put our plates in a plate rack on the countertop, because my shoulders were too stiff to reach up to the upper cupboards.
I then started streaming Essentrics & enjoyed how simple it was to do at home, with no special equipment or clothing needed. I held on to Miranda’s encouragement that the body changes “cell by cell”. I felt good being able to move again! Now that I do Essentrics daily, as a necessity, I have much less pain, am stronger & have the best posture in my life! I highly recommend this program to everyone!! I can now reach the highest cupboards, play with my granddaughters, travel & look forward to each workout, including the 5-10 min. ones on FB!
