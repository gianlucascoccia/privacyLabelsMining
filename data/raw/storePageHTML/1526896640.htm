Lovingpanda1343 , 02/06/2021

The Quality

The cute characters, unique story, simple gameplay, production and animations are what makes priconne a very quality experience for anyone who is into moe.

I put cute characters first for a reason because it’s probably why I love this game. All the characters are cute, voiced and have their own story and bonding moments with the main character and each other with main story being pretty much anime with surprising good animation and acting. And it also has guild stories too that roots a lot characters together to make the game world seem much even more believable and immersive.

The gameplay is simple due to it just being a standard free to play JRPG with autoplay feature attached but even so it at least takes a bit of thought and grinding when it comes to a good team composition for higher level play. One nit pick I have has to be the stamina and how tedious it is but it’s pretty standard for a free to play mobile game anyway so it’s understandable.

Micro-transactions are pretty fair in my opinion and you probably won’t have to spend much at all because you can earn a good bit of the premium currency in game but spend wisely if you do.

In conclusion, it is a very character focused moe JRPG with high quality production put behind a unique story and very simple gameplay. Would very much recommend.

P.s The anime is great as well, can’t wait for season 2.

Akairon , 01/30/2021

Subjective game review

I will mention four things about this game. First of is the target player for this game. If you like waifu anime and enjoy collecting your favorite characters of Princess Connect, this is a game for you. I actually wanted a gatcha game that has diverse character selection including male and monster units. So I reduced a star for review. Secondly I want to talk about the summon rate. It is low. Yes, you will get a bunch jewel in the beginning which is like many other games. However, later on you will find yourself not able to do any pull when you favorite character comes out. Basically only P2W will be able to collect their waifu. Third thing I want to talk about is the game mechanic. This is a casual game so don’t expect anything crazy complicated. Yes, there are some depth regarding position, skill timing, buff and so on but that is it. Not much to do due to limited stamina and how crazy expensive to refill it. More over, the rng that might or might not go against you for your wanted gear drop. You might spend 200 stamina to just get one piece gear. Last thing I want to talk about is how this game just got released globally but has so little events going on.... seriously, push more events to grab players attention. Make the game rewarding or else player like me won’t stay to play a game that is purely waifu collector. Plus, the animation and graphic is only fine. I do like character with 2.5D or 3D design. Thank you.

Cholik123 , 01/21/2021

Good game horrible translations

So I have played Priconne for a long time. It’s a fun game with great animations. I was excited to see it finally got an English version and to be fair I probably wouldn’t invest much time in it unless I got extremely lucky with my first few gacha pulls since I have most of my favorites on the Japanese version.

However, I decided to just stay away from the game all together because crunchyroll decided to take some weird cringeworthy liberties with the translation. Some examples are changing “onii-chan” to “Mr. Nice Guy”. I get it you don’t want your western cancel culture seeing a loli call a stranger big brother but you couldn’t think of a better less weird alternative or perhaps just leave it off completely? Another is adding “bruh” and “amirite” to another characters lines. I get she is considered dumb but wow that’s still sort silly.

Edit: Due to developer feedback and what seems like they’re listening I will change my rating. I will send my feedback directly with future translation grievances directly. I absolutely love Priconne and truly want it to succeed. Plus watching Ina play while drawing kokkoro made me want to give it another shot.
