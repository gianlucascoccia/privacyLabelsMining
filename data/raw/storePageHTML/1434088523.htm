BoyScout13 , 01/06/2019

Worst App Ever Used........

This is easily the worst app I have ever used. As an iTunes app intended for use by Apple users, why would you make an app incompatible with Apple’s Keychain? First, it doesn’t recognize that Keychain has a user name and password for the Military Star. Then when you force it to utilize Keychain it still fails. Sometimes it won’t recognize that there is data entered despite displaying characters, other times it just does nothing when you select a Keychain to enter.

Second,TouchID does not work and nothing shows up in settings that allows you to turn it on. I can’t even log on that way! While I don’t have FaceID, I can only assume it is the same from the number of complaints I see. I still have to log on via a web browser which defeats the purpose of actually having an app.

Lastly, who creates an app that can’t be rotated and viewed in landscape mode?

I am not surprised that it appears AAFES didn’t check the app they paid a developer to create, they aren’t really known for their customer service or attention to detail. You would think that when finally catching up to what nearly every other financial institution has been doing for the last 10-15 years, AAFES might want to get this one right.

Come on folks, we were all really looking forward to this one! If it starts working I will absolutely change my ratings.
