Description

Smart Baby Rattle is an amazing kids app developed for babies and toddlers. This user-friendly app with colorful animation and curious sounds works well as educational and entertaining game for infants to learn real-life objects while playing.
Smart Baby Rattle can be used as your baby’s first toy, as it is easy to handle and is also suitable for newborns who tend to get fussy and fidgety - let your baby discover new animals and plants or just listen to nature sounds and relaxing classical music which helps to calm down your little one by capturing his attention.

The game is provided with many opportunities:

- 4 different themes (11 in the full version) including Farm and Wild Animals, Christmas, Birds, Underwater World and Sea Animals, Vehicles, Musical Instruments and many other exciting things to play & learn.
- Awesome vibrant colors and outstanding real-life sound effects.
- Best classical music for babies – Mozart’s greatest pieces.
- Fine motor activities for your child’s coordination improvement.
- Easy and intuitive handling with touch-and-move screen.
- Rattle sound effect when shaking your iPhone or iPad.
