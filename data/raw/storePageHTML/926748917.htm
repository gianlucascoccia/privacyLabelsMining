Description

Discover great free books with a simple interface and beautiful images.

Make reading fun again! Create your own library from thousands of free audiobooks and eBooks. Read reviews from other users and stream audiobooks or download to listen offline. With featured books and top charts you'll quickly be on your way to enjoying the best free books available.

Michael W. Perry , 03/11/2015

Great sleep timer!

This is the first audiobook or podcast player I know that gets the sleep timer right. Others are like the sleep timers in bedside radios. Set it for 15 minutes, and the sound goes off after 15 minutes—period. To listen more, you have to reset the sleep timer and restart the playing manually.

Not so with the Loyal Books app. At my suggestion, the sleep timeout pauses play. Press the play button on your earphones, and it resumes play instantly with the sleep timer reset. You can keep resetting until you fall asleep.

That matters because other sleep timers either time out before we fall asleep, forcing a restart that wakes us up, or time out long after we go to sleep. The latter means that the next day we have to manually scroll back some unknown length of time until we reach the last part of a book or podcast that we remember; That’s a nuisance.

So many thanks for Loyal Books for being the first to get the sleep timer right. Perhaps others will follow and make that sleep timeout an easily reset pause.
