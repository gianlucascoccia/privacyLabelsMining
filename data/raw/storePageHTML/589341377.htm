Description

Virtual Hairstyling with Hair Zapp
============================

YOUR face with NEW hairstyles.

You are searching for a new style but don't know which one looks best on you? Then Hair Zapp is the virtual hairstyling app you need.

It's so easy!
==========
1. Start Hair Zapp and tap on the camera button.
2. Put back your hair.
3. Take a picture of yourself.
4. Try lots of hairstyles on your picture and find your ideal makover.

Feature Overview:
================
+ High-resolution imaging: Hair Zapp hairstyles look so real that the results are hard to tell from a photograph (provided that you are using a high-quality picture as well).
+ Hair Zapp is ad-funded, we hope you understand. If you buy the premium subscription, no ads will be shown.
+ Favorites: Compare your favorite hairstyles side by side and discuss them with your friends or your hairdresser.
+ Community: Still not sure which hairstyle looks best on you? No problem — upload your favorites to our Community anonymously and get feedback from other users, for free!

Premium features for hairdressers
================
+ Auto-renewable subscription
+ Subscription length: 1 month or 1 year
+ Multiple client management (projects)
+ Each hairstyle in three perspectives: half-profile, profile (new) and portrait (new)
+ Hairstyle flatrate: get 944 hairstyles instead of 400, and each new hairstyle as it becomes available
+ No advertisements anymore
+ Deforming
+ Coloring
+ Participate in our Ad network to promote your salon
+ Open projects sent by your clients

veggrrrl , 02/03/2019

Good selection of hairstyles , very expensive

Like the header says, this app has the best selection of haircuts that you might actually want. The ability to colorize your cuts is limited to non existent depending on the style, so don’t expect to change color much or to a shade of your preference. And it is very expensive to get the options with all the haircuts. My suggestion is to find a cut you want, within a month and take it to the hairdresser that month. Unless spending 10 a month for an app is reasonable to you.
Update: they've changed the pricing scale. I've tried other apps and I still like this one for styles the best, although there are styles I want to try on that aren't in the app. The thing I would like the most is to share pictures on instagram, while the are other social media platforms you can share in, instagram isn't one of them. But the ad free version is much more affordable now. And if you can put up with ads you get the same number of hairstyles as the ad free version.

Angel barns , 07/18/2018

So fun to try styles before you commit at a salon

I love that this app! It gives you so many different hairstyles current and retro. The possibilities are endless. Definitely makes you see what you look like with a different hairstyle instead of doing something drastic at the salon and not being happy and it’s too late to change. Now the only problem you have to worry about is hoping that the hairstylist can cut hair LOL.
The only thing I would change is the angle of the face it’s very hard to do a selfie and pose at that angle maybe you should update your software to detect your face.

Some improvement needed still

I would really like to see color be an option. Right now you pick a style and only see it on you in the color provided. It’s difficult to like a good style with the completely wrong color for me. Also, it’s very difficult to get a profile selfie. Lol on the plus sides, I love that it blurs the picture behind and around you so you can really focus on what your supposed to be looking at. And the sizing of the hair to face option works beautifully.
