Description

Create impressive video effects & movements in a creative way! Motion ninja is #1 motion & effects design app, providing professional tools and easy preset templates!

√ Multi-layer support of videos, graphics and audio
√ Professional keyframe & chroma key features

Features Details:
● Keyframe Video Editor & Animation Editor
Keyframe animation can be applied to masks, video collages, text, stickers, audio and more. A great tool for editing a motion trailer.

● Best Picture in Picture App
PIP with a well-designed video overlay function allows you to compose various fantasy scenes, like science fiction movies.

●Video Editor for iOS
Probably the best video editor mobile app you can find!
Glitch, VHS and 100 more dazzling video effects!
Flames, lasers and more animated stickers. Master superpowers with ease.
Use this powerful video editor to become a video guru and get tons of likes on Instagram & TikTok!

● Free Video Editor
As a Pro video trimmer & cutter, Motion Ninja provides editing features like crop, duplicate, detach audio from videos and reverse.
The most understandable video splitter. Just tap the scissors button lightly.
Unlimited undo and redo, no worry about making mistakes!
Combine different video clips into one video.
● Preset Templates
Multiple templates to help you recreate popular effects by just adding your photos or videos.
Effects including 3D Cube, Kirakira, Cartoon Filter and more coming soon.
Even if you are a video editing beginner, you can make amazing edits easily!

● Chroma Key & Green Screen
Offers several green screen resources in different styles. Use chroma key and green screen features to make stunning videos.

● Music Video Editor
Want to created videos to get more fans & followers on social media?
Motion Ninja offers powerful music editing and alignment features. Add FREE music, Vlog music or your own music, extract music/audio from any video.

● Motion Ninja Transition
Splice, Blur, Glitch, VHS, 3D... Add 50+ creative transitions in one click!

● Funny Video Editor
100+ Popular stickers, funny emoji, animated stickers and illustrations.
100+ Featured sound effects like animals, explosions, laughter.
100+ Well-designed fonts. Combine masks and keyframe animations to give your text a cool gradient effect.

● Multi-layer Timeline
An intuitive editing interface to zoom in & out precisely frame by frame in this video editor app.
Multiple layers of pip, text, music, images, stickers & special effects.

● Save and Share
Custom export resolution and frame rate including 720p, 1080p. Choose a suitable resolution to compress and convert your video.
Save videos from this Motion Ninja video maker & upload to YouTube, TikTok, Facebook, WhatsApp, Instagram.
