It’s a review

Normally don’t leave reviews. However, if you’re looking for a game to play for when you are avoiding work or not getting enough attention from someone in your house, this is the game for you! Avoiding the boss? Go to the corner and play. Your s/o is playing games? You can too! For maybe at most a ten minute interval though. The game play is short, which is why I like it so much. There isn’t any thinking involved. Just finishing up each quest really. Gems are pretty easy to get from them. It can keep my mind thinking it is having fun briefly during a boring work day. The game can be slow, especially at the beginning of each theater but hey I mean. It’s an idle game? What did you expect? Play that tap tap chicken game if you want a more fast pace. It’s idle, fun, and like I said, a game to keep you occupied during your most boring times. I will add, that if you actually want to play this game you will need to watch the ads to move further into the game. Better chance at getting more money in a shorter amount of time. Don’t mind it though. I’ve seen the hotel idle ad probably more times than I can count and have it by extreme memory, but if it means I can get level 7 seating for sunset cinema, hey! I’ll do it. It’s an idle game, it’s the point.

I’m giving this game 5 star because it is so much fun. BUT! Matthew Petruska , 10/05/2020

It’s fun but needs some changes, plus recommendations

So overall this is a really fun game. One I’d happily pay for *but* it needs some changes, also I’m including some recommendations for the games developers.

-1. *Most Important* I understand that a lot of work goes into creating games/apps, but honestly the prices for buying features in the game are WAY too high. I mean, $20 just to skip ads? Or $15-20 plus to add other features that’s a crazy high price for a simulation game on my phone that I would never pay. Prices closer to $10 & below & you’d probably make more in sales.
-2. Toggling between theaters/venues would be a huge help/time saver instead of having to zoom out & tap every time you want to change rooms
-3. Better quality graphics
-4. New/changing music, the same background music gets old after awhile
-5. More skins/faces for managers so it’s easier to tell them apart
-6. The ability to name your theatre’s, name your managers etc
-7. Adding more add-ons like bars, restaurants, bowling alleys, laser tag etc just some ideas.

Overall you’ve done a great job. The main two issues I have are A) The high prices for in app purchases & B) Adding the ability to toggle/click over to the next Theater/room etc. If you can ever lower your prices I’ll be happy to purchase the game.
Thanks!
