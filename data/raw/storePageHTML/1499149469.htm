Description

Bank anytime, anywhere from the palm of your hand!
Our mobile banking application allows you to manage your finances, deposit a check, transfer funds, check balances, view transactions and send us a messages from anywhere, anytime. It’s fast, free and available to all of our members!

Login Security
- Supporting both Touch ID & Facial Recognition
- Biometric Authentication - access your Honda FCU accounts quickly and seamlessly

Money Movement
- Quickly transfer money between your Honda FCU accounts or to another Honda FCU member
- Pay a loan, Pay a credit Card or save for a rainy day

Money Management Tools
- A dedicated Personal Finance Management center puts controls of your finances at your fingertips
- Vision into your budgets, spending trends, debts, & networth anytime, anywhere
- Financial tools to show you the big picture of your finances – even your accounts outside of Honda FCU.

Deposit A Check
- Deposit your check with only 5-taps using our Mobile Check Deposit service
- Receive your remote deposited funds in “real-time”

Pay Bill
- Easily setup and manage onetime and recurring payments
- Use existing Payees or add new Payees

Messages & Alerts
- Send and receive secure messages with our dedicated Digital Banking Member Service team
- Setup Account alerts and adjust where and why you receive them

ATMs, Branches & You
- Find surcharge-free ATM and shared branch location nearest you
- Speak with a dedicated Digital Services Member Service Representative

JasonL211 , 09/01/2020

3 Things:

1. The new layout does not have anywhere that I can see ALL of my accounts activities, transactions, deposits, etc. The Activity Center option only shows my transactions (like moving money from savings to credit card). It doesn’t show my deposits and withdrawals in an easy to view list (what should be the Activity Center). I have multiple sub accounts within my overall account. I really don’t want to have to click each one individually to view if there’s been activity. I want to be able to see it all listed for quick reference if money has come or gone in any of these sub accounts.

2. When I transfer money from an account to pay a credit card, it doesn’t show how much is due on the credit card when I click the Funds Transfer link. I have to write down or quickly memorize what is due. Minor, but annoying for efficiency.

3. Why doesn’t the Credit Union and Digital team communicate with customers on what is being addressed? An email status blast isn’t hard and would help us know we are heard and these errors are being corrected. It’s been plenty of time since this ill advised launch and still silly errors in efficiency on the site and here we are wondering if anything we feedback is being taken seriously.

Developer Response ,

Hey there, we're sorry the new app is missing the mark. Our team is gathering feedback and we will be working on making necessary updates.

mn10731 , 08/17/2020

Balance???

I agree with one other reviewer. For credit, the balance should not be what more is available to borrow. The balance is what is still owed. At the top it shows next payment due date but doesn’t show how much the payment is or what the real balance is. Using your logic, my vehicle loan balance should show $0! I can get used to the new format but the balance on credit needs to be fixed.

One additional point: I share accounts with my children where I can use the app to transfer money to their accounts. The old app showed the nicknames on the accounts.
