Description

Ahoy there! Captain Brickbeard here. Aaarrrrgh you ready to build the perfect day at LEGOLAND® California Resort? The app you’re about to download be every Pirate’s treasure trove when it comes to adventures at LEGOLAND California Resort. You’ll be able to:

• Take a peek at wait times

• Navigate through the Park

• Set reminders so you don’t miss your favorite shows

• Use our Planner tool to get all the information you need for your visit before you’ve even arrived. Tell the app who is in your party to see which rides are suitable for everyone. Then add your favourites and check them off as you go!

• Get access to insider tips that only be available to our most adventurous sailors

Best of all, an ancient sea legend foretold that this update would give you access to amazing discounts 'n insider tips... blimey! That be just a rumor though. You’ll have to download for yourself and see! Avast Ye! Haul up the sails and get your crew to LEGOLAND. See you on the high seas soon, mateys!

The app uses your location to keep you up to date with offers, events and activities relevant to your location.

Pretty good

Map is useful but GPS often too slow to update so routing still difficult. Wait times useful but often inaccurate (actual wait often shorter!). Would be good if one could remove rides already done from map. Also would be useful if one could make a fav list and not see all attractions that aren’t of interest.

Dash16 , 10/30/2018

Great alternative to paper maps

I recently visited the park and made sure everyone had this preloaded on their iPhone ahead of time. The ride locator and wait time estimates were super helpful, but it was a little confusing because some rides were closed for maintenance and the app didn’t know about them. Overall a good alternative to a physical map and good park companion!

Developer Response ,

Hi Dash16, good news! We've actually developed a new and improved app. If you update or re-download the app, you should be able to create an account if you choose. At this time, an account is not required. Hope this helps. Happy planning!

djcjdc , 09/01/2019

Meh

Overall the app wasn’t very helpful. The wait times on rides were wrong. It almost always said 5 min even if it was actually 30-60min. Show times also wrong which led to some frustrating experiences were we planned for a movie but was 15 min late. Having recently gone to Disney and their app was very helpful to planning a great day, this app was disappointing. Navigation also not intuitive.
