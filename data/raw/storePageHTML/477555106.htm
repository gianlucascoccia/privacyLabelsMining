Description

Get actionable information delivered directly to your mobile device in real-time!

NCR Pulse lets you know exactly how your business is performing, regardless of where you are. With up-to-date information collected automatically from the POS system, NCR Pulse Real Time gives you the power to make insightful and immediate decisions that directly impact the bottom line.

Get Pulse for your business today to:
• Get real time data (sales, labor, comps, voids)
• Get up to the second sales and labor forecasting
• Send messages to your employees.
• React to and track social media posts in real time (Twitter & Facebook)

Are you currently a NCR Restaurant Guard Customer? If so, retrieve real time Restaurant Guard alerts to your mobile device so you can take immediate action.

REQUIREMENTS
• Hardware: iPhone 5s, iPhone 6, iPhone 6s, iPhone 7, iPhone 8, iPhone X
• iOS version: 11 or greater
• To receive real time updates, a NCR POS system (Aloha) is required
• To use My Schedule, Schedule Manager or Mobile Inventory, NCR Back Office (NBO) is required
• To view check details in Customer Voice (CV), a subscription to Customer Voice is required
