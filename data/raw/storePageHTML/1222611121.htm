What a difference in my life!!!

I’ve been meaning to get into meditation for a while now. There are many apps out there and I tried a few of them that were both difficult and expensive. Then I found this app. What an amazing experience, and what a great different it has made in my life.!! I used it to learn all the techniques and I felt the difference in me after 2 to 3 meditation sessions. I have a lot going on in my life and I have trouble relaxing, I also don’t sleep very well, and in general I’m just stressed out, which is why I wanted to meditate in the first place. Using this app I learned how to do it really quickly and every session I did seemed to work even better and better. Their seven day program for beginners is just perfect. Then I decided to do the next 14 days program. It went into all the different advanced techniques that were extremely effective for me. Next I did their meditation for sleep program (I think it’s called sleep better in 7 days) and loved it. It really worked. They put focus on my exact problems and helped me feel so relaxed.!!

They also have quickie meditation sessions which are great for me - sometimes I just want to do a quick session and relax, and on other times I do their longer sessions, all depending on the time I have. Last thing - the guides voice is pure magic and the way she ties the techniques into real life thoughts and happiness is just perfect. Thanks for helping me change my life. I only wish I had started sooner !!!
