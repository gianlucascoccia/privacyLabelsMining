Description

Want to relax your mind? The game starts off easy but gets increasingly challenging. You can play more than 8000+ fun word puzzle levels and find 200+ attractive collectible cards.
Sometimes the game will get challenging, remember to use your spelling and vocabulary to beat it! (Don't forget the card-skills! Every time you complete a card set you will get a permanent card-skill or some special rewards!)
Unlike crossword, word search, scrabble, or wit puzzle, we will bring you an unexpected gaming experience with creative thinking and classic word puzzle.

How to play:
Swipe the letters to make words.
Fill up every block to beat the level.
Collect hidden cards when you fill in the specific answers.
Don’t hesitate to shuffle the letters or use hints, you will get so many rewards in the game!

Features:
TONS OF WORDS: Solve word puzzles with 80,000+ supported words!
HANDCRAFTED LEVELS: Explore 6000+ board game style epic levels!
DAILY PUZZLES: 3 NEW puzzles with increasing difficulties every day!
DAILY BONUSES: Login every day to claim your daily bonuses!
CARD COLLECTION: Collect 200+ beautifully designed cards and unlock powerful perks!
OFFLINE MODE: play online or offline, anywhere or anytime!
FREE FOR ALL: Nothing is behind the paywall! Please enjoy our free word game for iphone and ipad!
