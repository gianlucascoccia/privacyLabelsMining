Description

Take a mini-break to a hotel near you and get up to 40% cash back!

Get automatic cash back when you shop and dine with Dosh. Get extra cash back for dining, shopping, and booking hotels.

With Dosh, you get paid when you shop and dine at the places you already visit, including Walmart, Costco, Sephora, Dunkin, Office Depot, Forever 21, PetSmart, Ebay, DoorDash, Uber, Home Depot, and thousands more! You can even get real cash back on your next hotel booking.

Simply download the Dosh app, securely connect your credit and debit cards, and live life as usual. Every time you use those cards, Dosh searches for available offers. If it finds one, Dosh automatically redeems the offer and converts it into cold, hard cash, then deposits it directly into your Dosh Wallet. You don’t have to do a thing.

Five reasons you’ll love using Dosh to get cash back.

1. Dosh is Automatic - Securely link your credit and debit cards and whenever you pay with your linked cards, Dosh gets you cash back. It’s as simple as that!

2. Thousands of Local Restaurants - Pay with your linked card at thousands of local restaurants and get cash back automatically in your Dosh Wallet.

3. Cash Back On Travel - Book at thousands of hotels worldwide from the app and get up to 40% cash back in your Dosh Wallet.

4. Online Offers Are Here - Open the Dosh app, find your favorite brands, tap Shop from the app to launch the brand website (or app) and do your shopping. When you’re done check out and get your cash back!

5. Use Your Cash However You Want - Transfer your cash to your bank account, Venmo, PayPal, or donate to charity from the app.

FEATURES:

- Get cash back automatically - Unlike other cash back apps, there’s no need to cut coupons, scan receipts, or remember discount codes. Through secure credit and debit card connections, Dosh automatically finds existing offers, redeems them, then puts the cash into your Dosh Wallet.

- Choose from thousands of brands & merchants - Dosh is available at thousands of stores, restaurants, and hotels with new offers added every day like Walmart, Costco, Sephora, Dunkin, Office Depot, Forever 21, PetSmart, Ebay, DoorDash, Uber, Home Depot, and thousands more!

- Refer friends & family - Get paid for each friend who signs up with your referral code. When you share Dosh with people you love, it’s like putting cash right into their pockets.

Kgintovt , 12/20/2019

Convenient Cash Back

By linking your credit and debit cards to Dosh, you automatically receive cash back when you use those cards at participating locations without having to first logon to their website or activate the app. This makes it extremely convenient but there are caveats. You have to swipe your debit card as credit or you don’t get the cash back and if you have an app to one of the restaurants, for example, where you accumulate loyalty points/rewards, you don’t get Dosh cash back if you pay through that app and, conversely, you don’t get the loyalty points/rewards if forego the app and pay with your card. So you have to choose. Other than that, transferring your cash to your bank account once you have enough accumulated is painless but is not immediate and will take a few days to show up, which is par for the course with other types of online transfers. My only desire is to see more participating locations, which will vary depending upon where you are and not always the same. I travel a lot between DC and New England and while most Dunkin’ locations in New England participate, not all in the DC area do, so you have to check the app. But wherever you are in the country, assuming you allow the app to know your location, it’ll show you participating merchants at your current location.

We're working on bringing more brands to the app, so keep an eye out!
