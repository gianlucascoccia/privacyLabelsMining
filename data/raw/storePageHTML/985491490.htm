Description

New apple watch integration. Quickly remind yourself of your practice plans by looking at your watch.

With ready-made practice plans and hundreds of new and innovative drills, the Basketball Practice Planner App will help you plan your next practice in 7 seconds or less!

As a basketball coach, have you ever wanted to have your drills organized perfectly so you can find them easily and plan your practice in seconds? With a drills library, intuitive animations and audible time reminders, Practice Planner will help you do all of that from your iPhone!

With drills from coaches such as Dell Curry and Brian McCormick, we’ve designed Basketball Coach Practice Planner to be a place where you can learn hundreds of new drills as well as plan your practices in a few clicks.

APPLE WATCH INTEGRATION
• Quickly glance at your wrist to see your practice plans.
• Works even without your phone nearby.
• Tap once on your complications to open practice plans

ORGANIZED DRILLS
• Organized neatly in categories such as Fun Games, Shooting, Defense, Offense, Dribbling, etc. for easy access
• Add your own custom drills and insert them into each categories with just one click
• Filter by category, team/ individual drills, custom/ built-in to easily find the exact drill you want

BUILD YOUR OWN PRACTICE PLAN IN SECONDS
• Multiple drills can be selected at the same time to easily insert them into a practice plan
• Organize, insert and delete drills in your plans is as easy as changing songs in an iTunes playlist
• Save and edit practice plans to customize your next practice based on pre-made practices in the app

LEARN FROM THE BEST COACHES IN THE WORLD
• Dell Curry – Father of Stephen Curry and Charlotte Hornets' all-time leader in points and three-point field goals made
• Don Showalter – Head Coach of USA Basketball Junior National Team
• Chris Oliver – 3 time OUA Champion
• Brain McCormick – Youth Basketball Coaches Association founder and author of The 21st Century Basketball Practice

SAVE PRACTICE HISTORY AND NEVER FORGET WHAT YOU DID
• After each practice, the Practice Planner will automatically prompt you to save each practice
• Practice plans from history can be edited to create practices based on the last one
• Notes are attached so each practice so you can mark down how the practice went and what to improve on next time
