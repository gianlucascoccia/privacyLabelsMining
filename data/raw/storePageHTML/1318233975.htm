Description

Ever feel stressed? Try Dazzly for FREE!
This NEW unique Color by Number app is here to help you relax while creating art that will dazzle you and your friends. Play with beautiful Gems and Diamonds to sparkle up your day.

With Dazzly, follow the flow of Color by Number to match real handpicked Diamonds, Rubies and Sapphires with their corresponding numbers to create hundreds of beautiful artworks.

Dazzly Premium offers even more Gemstones and templates to color and create unlimited pictures.
Release your inner artist with incredible gemstones as never seen before!

Save, share and collect your very own Dazzly Wallpapers on your iPhone & your iPad!

Enjoy Dazzly with your family and friends: creating truly dazzling pieces of art has never been so easy and fun!

Features:
• Over 500 gemstones and pictures to color with!
• New templates offered every day!
• Family-friendly content: color new anti-stress pictures, mandalas, animals, jewels, and much more.
• Dazzly naturally helps develop fine motor skills for children.
• Dazzly Premium mode: choose your own color palette from hundreds of beautiful diamond and gems.
• Use your Dazzly Art as unique iPhone and iPad wallpapers.
• Share your Dazzly Art with your friends and on your favorite social network.

I love this game. I’ve been playing it for more than a few months and I’m obsessed. I’m so obsessed with the game that I bought the subscription to get new pages to color every day. My only issues are the glitches. I start 1 or 2 coloring pages and finish them both. Once I finish them I get busy doing something else, so I have to get off my game. Before the new update I would get off the game and come back an hour or two later to see that my pictures that I just finished were still fine, and they were saved. Now after the update, the pictures that I just finished hours ago are somehow not done. I still have to finish them and they didn’t save. I find it weird that even when I go back in the second time to finish the picture. Crystallinediva , 12/03/2020

Great Recent Updates

Edit: thank you SO MUCH for adding the option to work with a specific gem or birthstone and find all of the pictures that include it. I have finally collected all of the gems, and come back every day to see what new pretties I can make.

This might be the first time I’ve left a review for an app, but I wanted to express my enjoyment of the most recent updates. Now in the My Gallery section, there’s a breakdown of completed pictures, ones still in progress, and ones I’ve designed (chosen different gems to complete). That makes things easier when I need to go back and find one I didn’t get to finish, which is much appreciated. The other really great change came a little earlier- there are 3 different color schemes for most of the new pictures that are released daily. I’m not a huge fan of pink and pastel colors, so it’s great to still be able to work on a picture with different color options that I enjoy more.

I do have two pieces of feedback for consideration in future updates: 1) I’m close to collecting all of the different gems, and it would be cool to be able to tap the gems I’m missing and see what pictures they’re in, so I can get them. 2) Some of my “incomplete” pictures are actually complete, and there are no more gems to place, but the game froze or closed (before the update), so they’re showing incomplete with no way to finish them.

Overall, extremely relaxing and enjoyable! Thank you so much!
