Description

BUILD THE CITY OF YOUR DREAMS IN MAGICAL ELVENAR

Choose between elves and humans to build a beautiful, fantasy city. Discover a world of magic and mystery as you constantly build, develop and expand your realm. You decide how to advance your city as you gather resources, trade with other players and research ancient technologies. Whether you want to build a whimsical paradise or a well-organized metropolis, it’s easy to create a home for fantasy creatures and enjoy the detailed beauty of Elvenar.

CHOOSE YOUR RACE
Play as powerful humans or magical elves to build as you see fit

START INSTANTLY
City building is quick and easy with a friendly introduction and active community

EXPLORE THE WORLD
Discover new provinces to expand your city

TRADE WITH FRIENDS
Trade goods and resources with fellow players and merchants in the marketplace

ADVANCE YOUR CIVILIZATION
Upgrade your buildings to supply your growing population

WELCOME NEW CREATURES
Create a home for dwarves, fairies, dragons and other charming fantasy races

Elvenar is based on the successful browser city-builder, published by InnoGames. Now players can enjoy the online fantasy fun on mobile, tablet and PC browser – all from the same account.

Elvenar is free to download and install. However, some game features can also be purchased for real money. If you don’t want to use this feature, you can disable in-app purchases in your device’s settings. A network connection is also required.

Yaya6363 , 06/20/2018

This is a great game

Firstly, the negatives that only involve the app. You dont get ancient wonders, you cant truly explore other cities, and some things in the app just dont work like they do on web based game(i dont have comp based game but my fellowship mates tell me). That being said, these updates are coming. And i can patiently wait. Other than this, this game is fantastic. It has real substance and can be played for quite some time, im only on chapter 3(out of 12, with more coming), and it requires real strategy, whether that be in base layout(buildings dont rotate so you have to pay attention to where you put everything, and must move things and buildings increase in size via upgrades), resource management and trading, army structure, and what to research when(my advice is to find a good fellowship, and be active. You get an option to visit your neighbors and fellowship members daily, DO THIS. Its incredibly beneficial to both you and them). This game was exactly what i was looking for, and i look forward to future updates that make the app closer and closer to the web based experience.

BBsAmazon , 02/22/2020

Love this game!

I hate to say this, but I’m addicted to this game! Before real life kicked me around with time constraints, I had 10 cities!
Finding a good fellowship is sometimes a challenge, but once you do, it’s a blast. There are no other players raiding your city and stealing your goods, either. Not a fan of games like that.
Tournaments are weekly and important to the growth of your city. Fellowship Adventures are fun, but sometimes a challenge to coordinate. Graphics rock!
Events are great because you get time and supplies boosts, plus needed spells. When you first start the game, it looks simple. It is not! Makes you think! There’s a lot to know about this game and thankfully, there are resources in and out of the game that help. Plus, some players are a whiz at this game. You can play without buying boatloads of premium currency (diamonds) and that’s a challenge, too. I love challenges!
A couple of complaints are these: in-game chat is not stable and is frequently out of commission. Messaging is better and is *finally* available on the app - took them long enough! However, messages only last so long. Emojis are finally available on PC, but not the app. Anyway, we’ve had to resort to an outside gamer’s chat app to hold information we need and to chat.
Next request for the app: please make it so you can apply KP to other’s Ancient Wonders!
As with any game, there are good and bad aspects, but overall, this is a fantastic game!

Developer Response ,

Thank you so much for you fabulous feedback!
You will be pleased to hear that our developers are working on the Ancient Wonders for the app. Please keep a look our for announcements on the forum!

Via Family , 04/08/2018

Good but could be better!

I enjoy playing the game. The quests are manageable and require tactic, sure they take time but it’s cool because you don’t have quests that are leading you on to something you may not even care about. Building the civilization is fun and a good way to unwind. However, it takes forever to get through providences because your army does not level up as easy and as fast as you go through the open providences so it can take weeks for you to complete one Providence because you have to level up so much stuff in order to be able to level up your army and even then the army may still not be big enough. Also it is so difficult to set up your village when you have to keep adding stuff but have no efficient way to rearrange anything you already have set. I could make space for more of the buildings I need if I could completely rearrange my base but I can’t. It would be a good idea to have a reset button where you can rearrange your buildings so they fit better!

Overall this is a fun game but needs just a couple tweaks to be even better!
