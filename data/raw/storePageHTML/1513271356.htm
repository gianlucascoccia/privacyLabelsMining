Description

See the music you love most. PlayTally puts your favorites front and center. Watch your top songs and artists trend in real-time. Track your listening habits and activity. Get insights into your music collection with advanced stats. All perfectly integrated with your Apple Music library.

Features:

• Accurately track your listening history play by play.

• Create Replay-like playlists of your top songs for ANY time period.

• Trending songs refresh dynamically. No waiting for weekly updates.

• Exclude specific tracks like sleep sounds or white noise.

• Make playlists from your history.

• Easily browse large libraries with powerful sorting options.

• Earn awards as you listen more and grow your collection.

• Share your most impressive stats with friends and followers.

• Sync across multiple devices.

• Privacy as a priority. Your data never leaves your device and/or iCloud storage.

• No extra accounts, signups or subscriptions.

• Completely ad-free.

• PlayTally records its own data. Earlier playback history cannot be accessed or recreated.
