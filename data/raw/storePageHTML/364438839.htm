Description

A Universal (iPhone and iPad) version of the 24 hour KICK Map - the only subway App with both Day and Night maps to show the service cutbacks that occur every night in NYC.
Service Advisories with push notifications show the latest news from the MTA on service delays and bypasses that occur daily due to various unforeseen problems.
Calculate directions between stations and street addresses.
Touch and hold any station for 3 seconds to bring up train arrival times and a street map showing station entrances and exits.

FSCforkball , 04/18/2019

Made the NYC subway easy

We just returned from 4 days in New York and KickMap made navigating the city a breeze. Just put in where you want to go and it gives you step by step what to do. The first step will tell you which subway station to walk to from your location and which train you need to take. The key here is to push the map and it will show how to get to that station. The other apps I tried did not do this and without the map it’s real easy to go to the wrong station. The next steps will tell you which train to take and which stop to get off, and how many stops there will be in between. At the step where you exit it gives you a map with walking directions to your destination. I’m an old guy who lives in the country, so if I can do this anyone can. I highly recommend KickMap.

I have used KickMap from first on my IPod, then IPad and finally my Iphone. It is the most user friendly NYC Subway app ever!
