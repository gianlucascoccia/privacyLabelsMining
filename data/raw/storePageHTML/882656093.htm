Description

Migrate to Number 8 United States app for iPhone X support.

SuperLotto Plus Results : The simplest yet advanced way to check California Lottery’s SuperLotto Plus results.

FEATURES

Check latest SuperLotto Plus results
- Check the latest and past SuperLotto Plus results by swiping left or right on your iOS device.
- Navigate to any past SuperLotto Plus results quickly.

View all SuperLotto Plus results
- View all SuperLotto Plus results since June 2000.

View next SuperLotto Plus draw details
- Includes next jackpot and cash amounts.

Subscribe latest SuperLotto Plus results push notifications
- Receive push notification when the latest SuperLotto Plus results is out.
- Receive push notification when the latest SuperLotto Plus results jackpots is out.
- Receive push notification when the next SuperLotto Plus draw details is out.

Share SuperLotto Plus results with your friends
- Send SuperLotto Plus results to your friends using iOS built-in sharing.

Personal SuperLotto Plus Number Storage
- Keep a list of your SuperLotto Plus numbers that you bought or thinking of buying.

SuperLotto Plus Results Number Checker
- Check your SuperLotto Plus numbers against any SuperLotto Plus results.
- View SuperLotto Plus results with your favorite SuperLotto Plus numbers highlighted.
- View the prize group that you have won highlighted.

SuperLotto Plus Number Generator
- Generate random winning SuperLotto Plus numbers by shaking your iOS device.

Universal
- One app for your iPhone, iPod touch and iPad.

SuperLotto Plus Results works for SuperLotto Plus in United States only.
