Description

Angie’s List is here to help you complete all of your home projects, including major improvements and everyday maintenance and repairs — and it’s FREE to use.

View verified reviews in hundreds of home service categories, instantly connect with and compare quotes from your neighbors’ favorite contractors, and get exclusive deals from highly rated professionals in your ZIP code. All right from the app!

Use Angie’s List to:

Get a Customized List of Top-Rated Local Pros:
Tell us about your project and we’ll provide you with a set of Pros who can help get it done. You can then easily request quotes, ask questions and keep a record of your project right in the app.

Find Great Deals on the Home Services You Need:
Get your projects done at the right price! Browse the latest deals from local Pros on all services, from plumbing to landscaping.

Research Pros’ Reputations Before You Hire:
With more than 10 million verified reviews, Angie’s List makes it easy to see what your neighbors have to say about local home service professionals and contractors — giving you all the information you need to choose the right Pros for projects like painting, house cleaning, remodeling and more.

I contacted Lon Smith Roofing to look at possible wind damage. The guys were all very nice and I couldn’t be happier. 5 Stars!!
