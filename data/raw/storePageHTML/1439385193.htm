Description

The award-winning Ultimate Draft Kit is the most proven fantasy football tool to dominate your draft. Since 2015, no other draft kit has delivered more fantasy football championships. The UDK features the award-winning Fantasy Footballers' Top 5 Most Accurate Rankings.

This draft kit is for everyone, from brand new players wanting to get off to a hot start to the most avid fantasy expert who wants to dive deeper into analysis that you can't find anywhere else. So what all is in the UDK?

THE MOST ACCURATE 2020 RANKINGS
The Fantasy Footballers trio of Andy, Mike, and Jason have dominated expert-rankings over the last several seasons continually finishing in the top 5 of Fantasy Pros expert competitions year-in and year-out.

TIER BASED DRAFTING
While accurate rankings are a good start, if you want to really dominate your draft you must have tier-based rankings. Our rankings are separated into tiers of similar players at each position allowing you to easily see which position to draft or avoid throughout your draft allowing you to always know who the best available player to draft is.

SLEEPERS, BREAKOUTS, BUSTS, AND VALUES
Championships are built by grabbing and avoiding the right players. Last season we had breakout picks like Chris Godwin and Aaron Jones, bust alerts like Baker Mayfield, and sleepers and values like Mark Andrews and Michael Gallup. Get the full 2020 list for your draft this year!

2020 CUSTOM SCORING PROJECTIONS
See every stat for every player in detailed projections from three of the industry leaders. Customize the scoring for your league's scoring settings to make sure you are taking full advantage over your league-mates.

100+ PLAYER PROFILE VIDEOS
Watch Andy, Mike, and Jason break down over 100 players giving you a much more thorough understanding of the pros and cons of each player and their outlook for 2020.

MARK, FAVORITE, & TRACK YOUR DRAFT BOARD
Use the app to mark favorite players, do-not-touch players, and keep track of your draft board with a drafted players filter.

AUCTION / DYNASTY / TOP 200 RANKINGS
If you're looking for the best rankings for your non-traditional leagues, you've found it. With specific rankings for Auction leagues, Dynasty leagues, Rookie drafts and more we have you covered (non-IDP).

ALWAYS UP TO DATE
Unlike some apps or old-busted magazines, the UDK is always up to date. Breaking news, injuries, trades, and everything else along the way are quickly factored into all rankings, research, and analysis right up to NFL kickoff! The 2020 Season is yours for the taking!

PLUS MUCH MUCH MORE
Reports on rookies, coaching changes, strength-of-schedule, injuries, free agency, ADP, and so much more is included.
