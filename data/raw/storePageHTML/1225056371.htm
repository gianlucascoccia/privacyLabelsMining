Description

Learning languages is fun and free with the read-along audiobook Beelinguapp! Read text in 2 languages side-by-side and listen along to learn!

Learn a new language with Beelinguapp, the app that teaches you to read and speak a new language with bilingual audiobooks. Learn with stories in different languages when you read text in your language to reference words and phrases and listen to the audio in the language you want to learn.

If you’re familiar with language learning audiobooks, you’ll love Beelinguapp’s innovative method to learn a new language!

No need to spend time memorizing languages and vocabulary - just pick what languages you want to learn and start reading your favorite children’s stories, short stories, novels and more.

From Spanish to German to Arabic and more, Beelinguapp teaches you through fun and familiar text. Follow the words with karaoke-style scrolling text to learn a new language with ease.

Learn at your own pace with this fun and free language learning app and use your native language as a guide!

Beelinguapp Features:

Language Learning Made Easy
• Learn a new language by reading different stories in the language of your choosing! 
• Read the story simultaneously in YOUR language to reference what words or phrases means while you learn.

Audiobook Reader
• Spanish, German, French and more languages on easy to listen audiobooks.
• Listen to audiobooks in any language even if your phone is sleeping.
• Learn languages by following the audiobook reader with a karaoke-style animation.
• Spanish, English, French, German and more – choose whatever language you want to read!

Great Stories in Different Genres & Languages
• Learn languages at your own pace and choose only the stories you want to read.
• Scrolling text lets you keep up with the story in both languages.
• Find audiobooks with stories of every genre - fairy tales, classics, novels and more. 
• Sort stories based on your learning level to make learning languages easy.

Great Idea

I love the idea of this app. Reading with a voice-over as well as a direct translation to help decode new phrases and vocabulary makes something so intimidating much more accessible! It’s one of a few apps that is for intermediate learners rather than just beginners.

However, it seems that the comprehension questions either don’t have enough room to finish the question or don’t have the correct answers marked as such. There were a few that I KNEW I chose the right answer but it still says I’m wrong.

Overall, this app is incredible and could be very useful for many learners. Some of these fixes could make it much better!

Concept is nice but very buggy app

Well the concept is wonderful. Yet the functionality of app is horrible. I wouId not buy it if bugs persisted. I have tested it in Russian. 1. Downloading a story - it just stuck at 0% forever. Nothing helps. Switching between Wifi and 4G, opening and closing app etc. Not even single thing like going back to library etc not working. 2. Some stories won’t even open. 3. When I successfully opened one story, it is glitching on a reading mode (Not text mode) unless it is been read. Sometimes, I just want to read it without listening and that glitching makes it tiring for eyes. 4. Entering into text mode is horrendous task. It always takes several attempts. Still I had not figured it out how to switch between reading mode vs. text mode. When I try to switch to text mode it starts to read it and its very annoying. 5. Glossary... where to begin... I really wish that it could have been possible to delete some words added for me. 6. I don’t understand the concept here. How Beelinguapp expects one would want to go over and over again the same words. 7. Even some words are in English with English translations.

In summary, the app is barely usable. A lot of supposed to be great functions are not working and I really wonder how it got those five stars. By the way, I have checked the update And mine was the latest version.

Best Soccer Player 77 , 07/17/2020

Needs A Little Work

It’s got a good selection from what I see. There are lots of languages, though I have yet to change between them. My main problem is that the story, as the narrator is reading it, scrolls repeatedly to the bottom so that you cannot see the text being read.

Then, there’re some minor things, like how it’s annoying that you can add words to a glossary but you can’t translate it. Yes, that’s just a premium feature, but I bet if you asked a lot around, people would prefer to have a translation on the spot and they can revisit the story later to get the words. It interrupts the flow of the story when you have to constantly check back and forth and just translate everything later. Let me select a word, get its meaning, and move on.

Other than that, it’s good. Take this with a grain of salt. I haven’t wandered very far within the app.
