Disappointed. Same Story, Different Title

Roughly 50-60 players moved to this game together to Continent #41 around Halloween from another game, Ember Dragonstone Kingdoms. Initially, Survival Mobile 10,000 BC aka Chief Almighty was fun. Leveling my chief was exciting and resources didn’t feel like a grind. Unfortunately, when your Tribe Center gets above 14 or 15, you can’t adequately protect and save enough resources without making in-game resource package purchases and/or diamonds to purchase town shields. Developer promotes supremacy events that gets your resources looted and your troops killed. You can’t even heal your troops because you’ve been decimated by top-level players.

Overall, only start playing on a new continent if you choose this game. A new player cannot close the level gap and compete with level 20-30 players without spending thousands of dollars. Disappointed consumer...
