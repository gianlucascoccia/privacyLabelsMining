Description

Bring dinosaurs to life in Jurassic World Alive!

Dinosaurs have returned to rule the Earth, and they’re roaming free in your world. Explore your surrounding area to find your favorite Jurassic World dinosaurs, including new breeds that are more awe-inspiring and terrifying than ever before!

EXPLORE your world with location-based technology and discover dinosaurs on a map. Discover surprises around every corner – you’ll never know who you’ll run into!

COLLECT rare and stunning dinosaurs and maintain a full roster of thriving prehistoric animals.

CREATE unique hybrid dinosaurs in the Lab – science knows no limits!

My go-to game

I’ve been playing JWA almost since it came out and I don’t know why it’s taken over two years to write a review. I play this game daily, have never had any major complaints. I love collecting dinos and getting all the DNA. I’m a super casual player and have found a casual alliance but there are some super serious players out there as well if that’s more your style. I sometimes spend money but it’s definitely not needed if you’re a casual player.
I’m working from home so my hunting range has been reduced significantly so I guess with that my one complaint would be that the gym battles (v the AI) don’t provide a wide enough variety within certain spaces. I had 5 gyms within my hunting circle the other day and four them were the same. The levels suggested for those gyms really only focus on lower levels or super high levels, there’s not enough middle to high level gyms (16-20, 21-25).
I also ran out of achievements within the first year or so and a larger range of achievements haven’t been added for us long term players.
But overall it’s a solid game. Updates come out regularly so it doesn’t feel stagnant. Always new dinos to be on the look out for and to collect. It’s so satisfying to be working toward creating a new creature for months and finally getting that last bit of DNA needed. They art work is amazing and it really is a giant community if you want it to be.
10/10 would recommend

.:.khaleesi.:. , 11/06/2020

Love the game and the added raids!

I rarely review games because they are so costly. Not to mention the time it takes to refresh or start another section of the game. Jurassic World Live however can be played completely free. I admit I have put money into this game to level faster but it’s definitely not required. You can easily play this game for hours everyday and I love the pvp and raids. The map of the area you are in is really awesome as well. You obtain dino dna from literally your own backyard. You can walk or drive to obtain even more if you are so inclined but there is plenty in your own home area. Combining the dna to make stronger super dinos can be very slow at times but what fun would it be if you didn’t have to work for the “Unique” and “Apex” creatures. And to obtain the Apex creatures it does take the coordination of your alliance but that really turns into a lot of fun. There is limited chat capability and I’m sure that is to keep everyone safe but that can be a little frustrating at times. Especially when you are coordinating a raid with your alliance but Discord pretty much solves that issue. I’ve met a lot of great people. I highly recommend this game. It’s quite ingenious and they are constantly giving us new updates. (Developers please stop changing the dinos “powers” around, it’s frustrating when we work so hard on a creature and then it’s worthless at the next update). Thank you for making such an amazing game!!!!!
