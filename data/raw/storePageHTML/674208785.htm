Description

Content Creation, Made Easy.


Photofy is the easiest content creation platform available, putting the power of brand and creative assets into the hands of individuals, influencers, brands, and businesses alike.


Let your creativity loose with:

- Easy-to-use design templates, collages, artwork, and photo/video editing tools
- Access to 1m+ stock images or personal photo libraries and free fonts
- Schedule posts or repost your favorite branded content instantly
- Preset ratio options fit for all social and marketing asset needs
- Marketplace options for industry-specific templates, and graphic packages available

With Photofy, you can create eye-catching graphics, collages, photos, and marketing content quickly and easily with the right tools at your fingertips!

Love it!

I have been using Photophy for almost 5 years now. The ability to save projects to be able to go back in and edit them without starting from scratch is one of the number one things I love about it. (They save them in chronological order now! Yay! 👏🏼👏🏼👏🏼) They have great fonts, stickers, custom artwork, layout options and the ability to add logos is also a plus! On other apps I really dislike when you can’t “shadow” text to help it pop and stand out on various background images and that’s always been an option on Photofy since day 1 of using this app! There have been many improvements over the years! This app just gets better and better! One thing I really really hope to see in the future is some type of SMART GUIDE to help align and center objects and text. I recommend this app 100%! Thank you, Photofy!

Diannafer , 08/10/2018

Favorite app! But new update has me confused..

This has been my favorite app for years now! It has the largest selection of stickers & frames of any app I’ve found! Its well made & easy to navigate! Up until yesterday I would have given it 5 stars but there must have been a recent update because today I went to search the stickers for “family” stickers & normally I have a huge selection of little quotes & sayings to choose from but it seems all of the quotes/sayings have been moved to the purchased tab. I am ok with that thought of organization BUT there is no way to search them now because they are categorized by artist. So in the past when I searched “family” and all the quotes, saying & stickers came up relating to family making it super easy for me to choose from a wide selection, now I cannot find any of the quote/sayings that use to pop up under stickers to use relating to “family” because there is no way to search for the quotes/sayings that I used to much in the past! This was my favorite thing about that app that I had the ability to search under stickers and SO many stickers popped up. I would see quotes & sayings I liked for a particular project & had no problem purchasing the one I wanted. I wish you would bring the quotes/saying back into the sticker tab OR make the “purchased” tab searchable. Thanks for maintaining such an AWESOME fun app!
