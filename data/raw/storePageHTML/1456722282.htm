Description

Take charge of your health with the Meals App. Create weekly meal plans with healthy, quick & easy recipes.

Meals is a quick and easy meal planning app suitable for everyone with a busy lifestyle, trying to stay healthy. Join 1,00,000+ users and plan your meals in advance with highly customised meal plans. Stay on track with an automated pantry, save money with budgeting meals better, and reduce stress.

Meals starts you off on your health journey with a simple quiz to determine your preferences, and to filter out anything you don’t/can’t have. The app then recommends a meal plan best suited to your unique palate and lifestyle. You can choose from countless breakfast, lunch, and dinner recipes, and customise your own weekly meal plan.

Top app features:

1. COOK AND EAT HEALTHIER WHILE SAVING TIME
All recipes on meals have been carefully crafted to ensure you cook efficiently. Get prep/cooking time indications, recipe notifications, step-by-step instructions, ingredient lists, and required equipment. You can also tick-off steps you’ve completed in the process to ensure you never mess up.

2. MAKE DINING DECISIONS EASY
With an entire meal plan catered to your tastes and lifestyle, you never have to worry about the “what should I eat” puzzles. Make your dining decisions easy by looking at each recipe’s calorie and macros counts, health score, difficulty score, and cooking time.

3. EAT ONLY WHAT YOU LOVE
Meals creates a diet plan for you with ingredients you love. Choose from 18+ diet plans including Balanced, Vegan, Keto, Vegetarian, Paleo, Gluten Free, High Protein, Low FODMAP, Pescetarian, Indian, Thai, Japanese, Mexican and more. Mark off ingredients you don’t like or are allergic to and get personalised recipe recommendations.

4. SAVE MONEY WITH AUTOMATED GROCERY LIST & PANTRY
Save recipe ingredients to your grocery list directly or add them manually. Our app automatically moves ingredients from pantry to shopping list, and vice-versa based on what you’re cooking. Budget your meals better, and make use of all ingredients with minimal waste.

5. MORE EXCITING FEATURES
- Now scan ingredients directly with your phone’s camera to add them to the grocery list
- Track your water intake through the day and stay hydrated
- Mark recipes as favorites for easy access
- Real diet and nutrition guides to stay healthy
- with trophies and unlock achievements to stay motivated
