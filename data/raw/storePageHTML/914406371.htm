Description

Do you love music? Download JOOX, the FREE, easy-to-use, music player app for iPhones & iPads. Stream or download your millions of local and International songs, albums, radio, short videos and popular playlists. Have fun with karaoke and video streaming with friends. Download the latest hit songs and tracks for offline playing with lyrics too. Stream music with no audio ads. Discover the best new music with special playlists and radio from our editors for songs that fit your taste and mood, anytime, anywhere. Enjoy live broadcasts from top artists too with JOOX Live.

FREE FEATURES
* Huge Music Library - Instantly access music from all over the world.
*Trending Videos – Tons of popular short videos are waiting for you to explore. You will never get bored in JOOX.
* JOOX Radio - Access over 50 radios with an immense number of songs.
* Recommended Popular Music Playlists - Find playlists recommended by us, with songs to fit your mood.
* Personalised Music - Customise your playlists with your favourite songs and tracks for every occasion.
* Lyrics for all your favourite hits - Learn the words and lyrics to your favourite songs and albums to use in Karaoke.
* Real-Time Music Sharing - Share your songs and videos with your WeChat friends and on your Moments.
* Live Broadcasting - Get closer to your favourite artist and their hits in JOOX Live.

JOOX is there for all of your music moments with a large catalogue of hits to download. Enjoy your favourite local and international artists’ music from hip-hop, R&B, Pop, Dance, EDM and more music genres. Stream or download your favourite songs, albums and playlists now.
