Description

Challenge yourself in a new puzzle game, blast all the colorful cubes, solve fun puzzles, reach the target, and win awesome rewards!

Match and crush blocks in a super fun puzzle game - it’s free, it's awesome, and it’s addicting! Swipe and match 2 or more adjacent cubes to complete missions, reach high scores, and clear levels.

Play Cubes Empire Champions for free, now available on the App Store, and perfect for your iOS device. This amazing tapping game may look easy, but it is really challenging to master. With limited moves in every level, it’s all up to your thumbs - make smart blasts and collapse more colored blocks. Try to clear the board with fewer moves to get a higher score.

Puzzle games are much easier when you get some help!
Enjoy a Unique Gameplay and create blasting combos to pass tricky challenges. Use powerful boosts like the RAY GUN, PIŇATA, and YO-YO and pop the cubes!
Employ these super cool power-ups and boosts at the right time to create major explosions, clear the board and win awesome prizes.
Unlock AMAZING BOOSTERS that will help you crush the cubes and win levels:
* Match, blast, and explode 5 or 6 tiles to get a rocket
* Match, pop, and smash 7 or 8 toon blocks to get a bomb
* Match, crush and pop 9 cubes to earn a color wheel

Rockets, bombs, and color wheels will help you blast the blocks and beat the challenges!
Use these amazing boosts at the right time to pop cubes, clear the board, and win awesome prizes!

Play Cubes Empire Champions, and enjoy the best game FEATURES:
- Fun gameplay. Connect and smash 2 or more adjacent bricks of the same color to create a cube blast
- Plenty of fun levels filled with cool puzzles and challenges that will train your brain
- Amazing boosts and gifts to help you along the way
- Fun challenges to test your matching skills
- Easy to learn and play - crush and collapse blocks and win levels
- Relaxing blaster game to play with family and friends


Get ready to train your brain with hundreds of blasting levels filled with cool challenges and brain teasers. Test your logic and color-matching skills as you match and pop the cubes and blast your way to the top.


Cubes Empire Champions is a super fun and addicting matching game! Try this cool blaster game, fill your world with color, and smash all the colored blocks!
