It’s a good app, but...

I normally use manga bird, but i decided to change since they are not up to date with all the chapters. I found out that this app is really good, reading manga is super easy and comfortable, and they keep uploading new chapters, but there is one problem that keeps me from giving it 5 stars. The bookshelf where you keep the manga you are following only saves the manga in 1 language. I read manga both in English and Spanish and it gets really annoying having to switch the manga source every time I want to read the manga that I have saved in another language. It would be really cool that all the manga you follow, no matter the source, would be stored in one bookshelf. Other than that it is a really good app!

Developer Response ,

We will support this function. please upgrade to the latest version. Thank you!

Shymare_11 , 07/21/2020

A safe haven

This app delivers what is promised and is very flexible. The selection is pretty good though there are many repeats of titles of varying quality and reliability. The “UI” is very user-friendly and easy to understand but some aspects could use some work like customization of preferred genres and alerts of updated titles being in your face and then disappearing with no indication of which updated. So if a bunch of titles updated at the same time and you forgot which was the last one you read, you would have to go back a bit and through a few before you got to the oldest one that updated. The ads are understandable and are at most minimal on the toxic ad scale. Though there have been a few troubles when some non-skippable ads would not close forcing me to reset the app or straight after an ad ends the app crashes (but I’m think that’s on my phone). However, besides the few complaints above this is overall a very good source of manga and was very nice especially after the shutdown of manga rock. I would love to thank the devs for their work on this and hope they continue their efforts on improving our experience and the app’s functionality for the further future. ❤️❤️❤️❤️❤️❤️❤️❤️❤️❤️❤️❤️
