Description

Zirtue is a relationship-based lending application that simplifies and manages loans made between friends, family and trusted relationships with automatic ACH loan payments.

We get it. You need to loan money to friends and family with confidence. Zirtue allows you to get paid back with automatic monthly (ACH) loan payments from the Borrowers bank account to your bank account. Zirtue automates and simplifies the repayment process, so you can focus on your relationships.

We've all either loaned to or borrowed money from our friends and family with reasonable expectations that everything will go smooth. The reality is usually something different, unexpected or just awkward.

YOUR FRIENDS | YOUR MONEY | YOUR TERMS | AUTOMATED MONTHLY PAYMENTS

LEND & BORROW MONEY
Within minutes you can lend or borrow money with friends and family on your terms.
- Borrow money from friends and family
- Loan money to friends and family
- Digital agreement on loan terms
- Automatic monthly loan payments
- Payment notifications and reminders
- Easy messaging and personal notes
- Personal Dashboard to track your loans

RELATIONSHIP BASED LENDING
Zirtue is a private secure lending app that allows you to leverage your personal relationships instead of your personal credit score to lend and borrow money.

TRACK & MANAGE
Easily track and manage your loan activity using your private dashboard to view all loan activity and message lenders and borrowers. Set automatic deductions to make repayment friendly, easy and without having to think about it.

SIMPLY SAFE & SECURE
Zirtue simplifies loans between friends and family. We're serious about security and use cutting-edge technology to ensure your personal information is fully encrypted and securely stored.

SUBSCRIPTION BASED OPTIONS FOR BORROWERS
Zirtue is always free for Lenders. With a 7-Day Free Trial for Borrowers, Zirtue offers its Software-as-a-Service on a monthly basis ("Subscription Cost") monthly ($5.95), quarterly ($15.95), annually ($49.95) basis subject to auto-renewal. Borrowers can cancel your subscription anytime and all current loans will continue with no service interruption.

We're always listening so please continue to send us comments or suggestions to getintouch@zirtue.com or visit our Terms of Use at https://zirtue.com/legal.html

Reaching people most apps won’t.

This is my second time borrowing through Zirtue, and with good reason. Aside from the fact that it allows borrowers like me to be transparent with the need with actual PEOPLE, and not some lender-bot AI, the system itself is designed to access banks that lender-bot apps won’t (through Plaid’s ability to input account info manually). Having been a Zirtue customer, and being a seasonal worker (school bus driver), Zirtue has allowed me to borrow in times where I’ve needed it, be it for hard times over not-so-busy summer break, or in the most recent case, Thanksgiving break catching up with me. Thank you to the Zirtue team for giving the handshake loan more accountability.

Not ready for prime time

It took me and a friend much too long to successfully do a loan on here they didn’t respond to multiple requests for help.

First, they try to verify bank accounts with Plaid, which is currently not working for my major US bank. Plaid had to help me figure out a workaround, Zirtue didn’t respond to my questions.

Then when I tried to accept her loan request, I kept getting the same generic error that didn’t make sense.

All in all, I emailed them 3 times, got 0 answers, and it took 5 days to get the bank accounts linked (1 day) and get the loan made (4 days). Meanwhile the car my friend was trying to buy got sold to someone else.

They emailed today asking me to review the app but there was no link in the “Review the app” text and no other link to review it. That’s really the app in a nutshell. That made me annoyed enough to open the App Store and leave a review.

Potential but expect it might be messy. Not ready for prime time—and if they answered emails I would be a lot less irritated by that.
