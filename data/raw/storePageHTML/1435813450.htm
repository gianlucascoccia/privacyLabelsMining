Description

Vooks is the world’s first streaming service dedicated to animated storybooks. We transport stories off the page, promoting literacy, imagination and fun for children 2 - 8 years old.

Everything great about storybooks plus so much more. Watch all your favorite storybooks anytime, anywhere with the Vooks app! With the Vooks app you can watch the animated version of the books they love. Take a trip to where the wild things are or go on an adventure with scaredy squirrel right from your device. Better than audio books or ebooks, Vooks combines the best of all platforms creating a new way to enjoy books.

With new books added monthly, you’ll always have access to the best in children’s storybook entertainment.

Download Vooks today and watch 6 free books via free access or start your free trial with subscription and explore award-winning children’s books to help your child learn and grow. Cancel at anytime.

VOOKS FEATURES

● AD-FREE KID SAFE AGES 2-8
The Vooks app provides a safe, child-friendly viewing experience for all ages. Carefully selected award winning family friendly books that will engage and promote imagination.

● STREAMING OR OFFLINE VIEWING
Kids can easily watch Vooks at home, on the road, or anywhere via streaming over WiFi or with cellular data plans. Offline viewing capabilities for when your traveling or on the go, just download to your device while connected and watch offline.

PRIVACY & SAFETY
Your child’s privacy and safety is our top priority.


To access all features and content an adult can subscribe to Vooks on a monthly or yearly basis with an auto-renewing subscription right inside the app.* Pricing can vary by region and will be confirmed before purchase in the app. In app subscriptions will automatically renew at the end of their cycle.

* All payments will be paid through your iTunes Account and may be managed under Account Settings after the initial payment. Subscription payments will automatically renew unless deactivated at least 24-hours before the end of the current cycle. Your account will be charged for renewal at least 24-hours prior to the end of the current cycle. Any unused portion of your free trial will be forfeited upon payment. Cancellations are incurred by disabling auto-renewal.

New habit

This was a great find for our homeschooling family.. I can play books to extend school lessons and also great bedtime books which quickly became part of my kids’ nightly story routine.
Even seeing some of our favorites brought to life with various, creative methods of animation is quite an exciting experience for us. My early reader really likes that he can easily follow along with the words as they light up. The content and book choice has not been in the least bit disappointing, there’s some really great books for so many areas of life, brought to life for you! Gotta love it! I got a free trial but I’ll tell you what, this one might be worth paying for after it’s over, and frankly my kids might be very upset to lose this as an option. I can’t let them navigate my kindle reader as freely because they could be lead to books from all over. Vooks is a super safe bet if you need a quick thing to pop on for younger kids, because even if some topics are over their head, they’re never going to be inappropriate for kids. Love it!!! I say 10 stars!
