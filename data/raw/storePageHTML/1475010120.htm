Description

WORKOUT with Bikini Body Mommy™ is the #1 Most Relatable Workout Program for Women.

Join Bikini Body Mommy™ Founder and mother of five, Briana Christine, in your quest to better health, happiness, and body confidence!

If you are looking for a workout program that is relatable and inspiring -- regardless of your current weight, shape, or size, then WORKOUT with Bikini Body Mommy™ is the workout app and global body-positive community for you!

FEATURES
No gym or fancy workout equipment needed. Burn fat, lose weight, and gain confidence in under 20 minutes a day all from the comfort of your own home!

This App provides over 1,500 short and effective home-based specialty workouts including:
• 8 unique 90 Day HIIT Challenges!
• Ab Workouts
• Kettlebell Workouts
• Mommy & Baby Workouts
• Pregnancy & Postpartum Workouts
• Diastasis Recti (Abdominal Seperation) Rehab
• Legs & Booty Workouts
• Arms & Back Workouts
• Restorative Flow Workouts
• and even a "6-Week Couples workout" series!

What are you waiting for? Let Bikini Body Mommy™ help you get fitter, happier, and healthier today!
