SEND MONEY IN MINUTES

Sending money with SiguePay is easy. It takes seconds to create an account and it’s free. Then just login to your account, and start sending money. You can send a transaction to the same recipient next time by just clicking on the recipient’s name. No need to provide the same information over and over again.

CONVENIENCE AT THE TIP OF YOUR FINGERS

With the new SiguePay app, there is no need for you to stand in line or wait to send money to your loved ones. Simply pull out your phone and you are set. Don’t wait!
