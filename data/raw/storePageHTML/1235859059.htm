Description

SRP Federal Credit Union’s Mobile App makes it easy for you to bank on the go.
Do your banking right from your iPhone/iPad!
You can manage your accounts, pay bills, find ATMs and more!

Bank with confidence – SRP Federal Credit Union’s Mobile App allows you to manage accounts from your mobile device...anytime, anywhere.
Now it’s easy to do banking 24/7, right from your iPhone/iPad.

It’s fast, secure and free. With SRP Federal Credit Union Mobile, you can:

• Check available balances & transaction history
• Pay bills and credit cards
• Deposit Checks
• Transfer money between SRP Federal Credit Union accounts
• Find the nearest SRP Federal Credit Union branches and ATMs
• Send us a message

At first doubtful, now a believer.

When SRP first switched to a mandatory app for online banking, I was very upset, I was used to going to my favorites in safari and logging in with a name and password. But after going through the initial set up, it’s now one click on an app and my finger print, and everything I need is one click away. No more taking a five minute break to check my bank account, ten seconds and one finger is all I need to do all the online banking I require. Excellent app design. Convenient. Simple. Sleek. And small, barely taking up any hard drive space. Highly recommended.

Kick2012 , 11/02/2017

Crisp, Modern, New Features

A lot of people are giving the app grief for their own mistakes. I failed an attempt to login the day of release and remembered I received an email weeks before and a reminder the day before on how to login on the new app. When I plugged in the new information the app opened right up and prompted me to create a new password. After that it’s been quick, and clean. The only issue I’d have is I liked the way the old app displayed your available balance larger than what is in your account. This app has a lot more bells and whistles too.
