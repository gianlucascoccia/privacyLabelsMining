Description

Dickey’s Barbecue Pit: Legit. Texas. Barbecue.
Since 1941, Dickey’s Barbecue Pit has smoked our meat low and slow overnight, every night, in every location to ensure its authentic flavor. In more than 500 locations worldwide we serve our guest our Texas-style smoked meats and wholesome sides. At Dickey’s, our guests enjoy our barbecue in-store, at catering event, and delivered right to your door.
 Features:
EVERYDAY ORDERING: Order your favorite Texas-style slow-smoked barbecue with our selection of wholesome sides for in-store or curbside pickup or delivery

ORDER CATERING: Dickey’s Barbecue Pit is your ideal catering partner for life’s events, big or small. Order for your next catering event for in-store pickup or delivery.

LOYALTY PROGRAM: Big Yellow Cup Club Rewards. Earn 1 point for every dollar you spend. Get free barbecue every 35 points. Eat. Earn. Rewards

DICKEY’S DELIVERS: Order pit-smoked quality meats and wholesome sides from our Everyday or Catering menu and we’ll bring the ‘cue to you!

IN-STORE & CURBSIDE PICKUP: Order slow-smoked barbecue from our Everyday or Catering Menu for in-store or curbside pickup at your nearest Dickey’s Barbecue Pit.

ORDER TRACKING: Long before the craving hits Dickey’s Barbecue Pit began to slow-smoke your meal. Track your order from our pit to your plate.

Other Features:
Locate your nearest Dickey’s Barbecue Pit*
Create a profile and order your favorite menu items.
Quickly recall all of your previous orders View nutrition and allergen information.

Do better...but I’ll still go here.

I had to download this app since the website is so terrible. The only way to continue through this app was by adding food to my cart, closing and then re-opening the app every time I added food. Our small town mom and pop restaurants have better online ordering services. Only two stars because I THINK I was actually able to complete my order.

UPDATE:

Context is important. My online order to Dickeys a few weeks earlier never made it to the store even though I had the confirmation email. Very annoying since I was on a time crunch but not the worst thing to deal with. The employees at the Benson, AZ store more than made up for the inconvenience simply by their understanding demeanor. Two things are consistent, the food is great and the employees I’ve dealt with were very very understanding and accommodating. The fact that I got a response from their app developer let’s me know that they care...this warrants a two star update. Not perfect, but still very good.

BadBoy93 , 05/18/2020

Food is Great, App not so much

I am new to the world of eating at Dickey’s. When I first heard that they were opening up a restaurant in my area I thought it was a joke and I laughed. Why? Because I live in Kansas and Texas barbecue is inferior. (I lived in Texas a lot longer than I have in Kansas and so I do speak from experience, and barbecue is literally the only thing about Kansas that I like at all.) The first time I tried it I was very unimpressed and figured they wouldn’t be open long, but I did give them another chance and now love them.

The app, however, is very irritating. The app works just fine when I’m at home and just looking it over. The problem is that there are special deals that are only available by ordering from the app (the cashiers are unable to match the app’s prices on their end), yet it seems like the app doesn’t want to work while I’m at the restaurant and needing to order something. This has happened to me on numerous occasions.
