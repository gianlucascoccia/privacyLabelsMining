Description

The coolest virtual pet cat is back! Play Tom’s new game and have the best fun!

From the creators of My Talking Tom comes a new global hit game, My Talking Tom 2!
In this cool free game, the famous pet cat Tom is going on an amazing life adventure!

THE COOLEST CAT ON THE BLOCK: Talking Tom is funnier and more lively than ever before! He reacts to everything players do and there are new surprises every day!

EVERYDAY FUN: Players can feed Tom when he’s hungry, wash him when he’s dirty, put him to bed when he’s tired, and take him to the toilet when he’s… really got to go! They can watch him grow into a big happy cat!

NEW MINI GAMES: There are brand new mini games to play, including puzzle games, action games, and Tom’s first-ever multiplayer mini game!

EXPLORING NEW WORLDS: Tom’s plane can be used to discover exciting new worlds. Players can go flying and bring back amazing treasures and use them to dress Tom up or decorate his house.

TOM’S PETS: Yes, Tom has a pet too, and there are four more pets to discover! Each one has its own look and personality. Players can try feeding them to see what happens!

Fun game, gets boring

I played this game for about a month, then deleted it. Later I downloaded it again for less than a week and then deleted it. The concept of this game is cool. You get to take care of your own pet “cat” (it looks and acts like a kid except for different colored fur, a Tail, and ears). I am a bit older than the intended audience and I have noticed that some of the ads might be inappropriate or disturbing to little kids. It also gets boring after a while. In My Angela, another game by Outfit7, the gameplay is a bit more interesting and addictive. Another issue with this app is that you watch Tom go to the bathroom. You don’t see anything, but it is a bit inappropriate. One issue that many reviews talk about but I disagree with is the man in Tom’s eyes. First of all, in all of my gameplay I have never seen him. Second of all, from what I’ve heard, it’s just a few pixels in Tom’s eye that happen to resemble a person. Third, if there is a person in Tom’s eyes, it’s probably just details so that it looks like he sees you. Lastly, parents, you know that Tom repeats what your child says; it’s part of the game! This is my opinion on this issue, and I would only recommend this app to kids older than 6, with parents who understand that this app has a bit of questionable content. Thank you for reading.

xEllaBeex , 06/18/2020

I hate to admit it, but this is a decent game

I’m aware that this game is targeted towards kids 4-8 y/o. I’m 14 y/o and playing this game, mostly for nostalgia. I’ve played Outfit7 games ever since around the time Talking Tom came out (I was about 4), and I’ve played My Talking Tom from when it was first released to when I was 11 or 12. I’ve been seeing a bunch of ads lately for this game and I decided, “why not?”, so I downloaded it. I’ve been playing it for a few hours in total, and I would say that I like this game, I don’t love it, but that’s based on personal preference. I find this game to be more childish than the first My Talking Tom game (not that the first game wasn’t childish), the visuals are a whole lot softer and everything is 10 times easier. However, I can’t really complain about that since it’s targeted towards little kids, like I said earlier. I cant really see myself enjoying this game long-term like I did with the first My Talking Tom game (partially because of my age when MTT came out compared to my age now, and partially because of personal preference), but I 10/10 recommend it if you’re under the age of 8, or if you’re a parent or smth and you want to keep your kid(s) occupied for a while
