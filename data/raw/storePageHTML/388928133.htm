Description

RUN Powered by ADP®, in your pocket…

RUN Mobile Payroll is your essential companion to RUN on the web — designed to help SMALL BUSINESS OWNERS like you manage your business, while you’re on the go.

• Run payroll – Run your payroll start to finish, or pick up where you left off on the web. We’ll even send payday alerts!
• Manage your employees – View and update pay rate, department, contact info, and more
• Print and share reports – View, filter, print, and share easy-to-read charts and graphs
• Submit tax notices – Submit them to us and we’ll let you know as soon as the status changes
• Store and retrieve HR docs – Securely access and share crucial business info
• Navigate day-to-day HR challenges – With weekly HR tips from ADP
• View insurance policy info and manage certificates* – For workers’ comp policies through ADPIA®

Safe & Secure
At ADP, security is integral to our products, our business processes and infrastructure. And that extends to RUN Powered by ADP Mobile Payroll — so you can stay focused on your business.

Completely deficient with COVID19

This app is ONLY for processing payroll and reviewing payroll reports.

It DOES NOT ALLOW:
1. Changing personal info for employees
2. Changing payroll rates
3. Reviewing addresses
4. Reviewing start dates

This app FAILS to allow any semi-advanced functionality, which is needed for COVID 19 reports.

ADP does not even allow you to access the desktop site on your mobile phone. It forcibly moves you to the app WITHOUT OPTION.

For a company like this, I am surprised that they have not remedied these issues. Perhaps there is another platform that they offer that fulfills these needs, and that this version is meant to frustrate you into the up-sell.

Meager and disappointing.

iCrowe , 05/06/2020

Why even bother?

I would give this app negative stars if it were possible for lack of effort from such a large payroll firm. Why even bother developing an app that is so incredibly limited? Anytime I need to manage our employee’s payroll, I cannot from this mobile app. I need to have access to a desktop or laptop in order to do anything. You would think ADP (one of the largest payroll companies in the world) would have the resources to build a useful app for business managers and owners. This app is non-functional at best. Why did ADP even bother? I find it sad that lesser known and newer payroll systems on the market have much better mobile functions than ADP for remote processing on any platform. It’s 2020 and ADP seems to be stuck in 1999. In today’s world of mobile workforces, having a functional app is no longer just a luxury, it is mission-critical. I expect a lot better from ADP. We have been using ADP for four years, but we will be looking elsewhere for payroll services due to our need for mobile payroll functionality.

One&OnlyGrump , 12/20/2019

Not designed with Business Owners in mind

This app would probably do whatever an Employee needed as far as viewing your payroll, and if you need to do nothing but “run” a payroll that you have already edited and reviewed on the desktop site this would do it.
But in today’s mobile world it would be nice if I could access zip recruiter, access GL reports to download to QuickBooks, access the “full” employee record in case changes need to be made, add an employee or do most of the things an employer needs to do.
The worst part is that at least before I loaded the app, I could access the actual site via my phone’s browser but once the app was added, I can no longer even bypass the app for things I need, I have to either have a laptop handy or get to a desktop. The idea is Great, but the execution is terrible especially from the largest payroll processing company around.
