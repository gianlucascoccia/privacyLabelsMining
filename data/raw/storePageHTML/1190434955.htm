Description

DC Metro & Bus is the easiest, most info rich, and beautiful app to help navigate Washington DC! Providing information straight from WMATA (Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority), you'll always be updated with accurate real time info!

HIGHLIGHTS:
- Includes All Bus Routes
- Includes All Metro Lines
- Find Metro Stations Near You
- Find Bus Stops Near You
- Includes All Bus Stops
- Includes All Metro Stations
- View Bus Schedules/Arrivals
- View Metro Schedules/Arrivals
- View Bus Route Stop by Stop (in order and time of arrival)
- North & South Bound Buses Supported
- Map included for each Station or Stop to easily help you find the stop
- Metro Station Addresses Included
- Metro Alerts (Delays, Closures, Disruptions)
- Supports iPad & Watch

This is the ultimate app to find your way around the wonderful city of DC - bus, metro, stops/stations, schedules - we've got you covered.

Start navigating today - Download Now!
