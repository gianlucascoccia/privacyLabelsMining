FEATURES:
-----------------------------
Do you like to study while listening to music?
Do you find it hard to focus?

Sonata (formerly iLuvMozart) powered by the "Mozart effect" will increase your IQ and give you a boost in concentration.

A little background: "Mozart effect" was first coined by Dr. Alfred A. Tomatis, an internationally known otolaryngologist who used Mozart's music as the listening stimulus in his work to cure a variety of disorders. Later on, Dr. Rauscher, Shaw, and Ky took this research further and published an article in Nature that demonstrated an increase in IQ of 8 to 9 points when students studied while listening to Mozart's music.

- 10 free classical greatest hits
- Crowdsourced effects of each classical hit
- High quality stereo soundtracks
- Licensed collection of classical music

You can turn off auto-renew at anytime from your iTunes account settings. When your subscription ends, the songs you downloaded during your subscription period will be removed.

It isn’t too good to be true

I love this app. Trust me it’s everything you think it is. You can download recordings of classical music and listen to them with no WiFi. This helps in the car for someone who doesn’t have data. It is 100% free and requires no payment. The only downside is that to download the music you have to watch ads to get sonata coins, which then you can choose which app to get at the price of usually 45 coins. No real money is used however. There is an option for a $5.99 per month subscription to automatically download all of the songs at once but again, it isn’t required. Anyway, definitely download this app. It’s exactly what it advertises and I couldn’t be more satisfied with it.
