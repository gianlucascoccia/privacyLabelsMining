Description

Welcome to the PLAYMOBIL Horse Farm!
Build a paddock paradise for your horses and take care of your animal companions.

AT THE STABLES

• Feed and groom your four-legged friends.
• Style your horses with colorful saddles, head collars, and bandages.
• Choose from seven different coat colors.
• Take cute snapshots of your favorites.

AT THE PADDOCK

• Create your own equestrian course and let your horses gallop around it.
• Customize the paddock with shady trees and other items.
• Earn new items step by step.

100% FREE AND CHILD-SAFE

Alright, so I'm a 14 year old (I'm old, I know, but the simple things are fun!) and I'm actually really enjoying this game! I am a rather easy to please person, so... That might contribute to my love of this game though. The fact that everything is free is AMAZING!! Thank you for that! :)
I do have a question... On the opening page (the page that pops up when you open the app), there's a light(ish) bay horse (I think? I could be wrong) with a white stripe in a stall in the far left. Great game but wish there was more....

This game was fantastic when I first got it! The graphics were cute and the horses were nice. There were no glitches or bugs or anything that would stifle my gameplay. The only problem I have is why we can only have 4 horses? Also there is a 5th horse in the opening page with the name plate “Bella” but I don’t see her when I enter the stable. I also wish we could increase our yard space and have more horses and more tack, food, and item options. Once you level up to the maximum and get everything you are kinda done with the game. I wish we had the chance to get more horses and get a bigger stable/ yard space and more item options so we don’t ever have to just Stop playing.
