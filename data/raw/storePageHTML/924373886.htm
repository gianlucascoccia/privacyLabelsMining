Editors’ Notes

Hipster Whale's Apple Design Award–winning game takes a timeless arcade formula—cross a road bustling with traffic and deadly obstacles—and transforms it into an adorable infinite runner. As you progress, you’ll unlock new characters with wildly diverse looks and effects. There’s a teeny-tiny flea, an emo goose, and even a few characters from other games—like Epoch, who sprays cars with blocky bullets. The creativity and care put into Crossy Road’s wacky world are unmistakable.
