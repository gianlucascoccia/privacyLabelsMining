Description

Fitonomy App is designed to help you become healthy & fit, achieve your body goals with weight loss training. Lose weight, build muscle, tone and sculpt with our customized legs, butt, and ab workouts.

PERSONALIZED WORKOUTS
Fitonomy App generates weight loss workouts and tone & sculpt workouts that you can do at home or at the gym, regardless of your fitness experience. With weight loss workouts at home and at the gym the app serves as a health & fitness fit plan that helps you achieve your goals.

Fitonomy is made of workouts & exercises like:
-Weight loss workouts,
-HIIT workouts,
-Biceps,
-Leg workouts,
-Glutes,
-Ab exercises,
-Cardio,
-Arm exercises,
-Back and shoulder workouts,
-28 & 30-day challenges.

PERSONALIZED MEAL PLANS
The personally-tailored meal plans are created so you can get all the nutrition you need to achieve your fitness goals. Lose weight, tone your body, and get fit with the right diet for your goals.

TRACK YOUR FITNESS JOURNEY
Weight loss training health works best when you keep track of it. Getting fit or even losing weight requires you to see your progress and track your workouts, calories, water intake, steps, etc. Fitonomy offers you all that.

INSPIRING COMMUNITY
Fitonomy’s Community is a support system that will help you during your health journey, to stay motivated, inspired, and achieve your goals together. Community is created to keep things fun while connecting with others.

By working out with Fitonomy, you:
Have weight loss meal plans & workouts
Have a 24 hour fitness on your phone
Can get fit on my home or at the gym

Fitonomy has Apple’s HealthKit integrated. The app will provide calories and step counting metrics for all exercises and workouts.

SUBSCRIPTION PRICING & TERMS

Fitonomy is free to download. Ongoing use requires an active subscription, available on a weekly, or annual basis. Weekly subscriptions are billed every week. Yearly subscriptions are billed the total annual fee from the purchase date.

Payment will be charged to your credit card through your iTunes account at confirmation of purchase. Subscription renews automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours prior to the end of the subscription period. There is no increase in price when renewing.

Subscriptions can be managed and auto-renewal turned off in Account Settings in iTunes after purchase. Once purchased, refunds will not be provided for any unused portion of the term. Read our full Terms and conditions & Privacy Policy

What you SHOULD consider

The app is amazing. But I’m tired of having to hear the same song each time so I just play it through my music app instead. I wish I could build my own routine (leg day, arm day, abs day). I also wish if I wanted to skip a workout, it didn’t just go on to the next and count that workout. I wish it would give me another alternative. Some days I’m not in the mood for certain workouts and I don’t feel like I’m striving for the best if a workout it skipped and not replaced. Also the run should start off as a jog for beginners. I already hate running, and working out but I’m willing to for my health and mindset and it’s deceiving you’ve made this app into a one size fits all. I don’t have the usual gym equipment yet I need it to fit my needs. Do better please, and remember ONE SIZE does not fit all.

Hi there, thank you for your feedback, we really appreciate it. We are always looking for ways to improve Fitonomy and make it as user-friendly & personalized as possible.
