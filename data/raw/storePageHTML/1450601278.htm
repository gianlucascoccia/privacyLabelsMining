Description

Cally is an app that offers video and voice calls over the internet with your friends, family and loved ones. Cally cares about your privacy. You can hide all your conversations by setting a password to use the app. Cally appoints a unique ID code only for you, and you can make your calls with this ID code without your number appearing on the opposite side. You can only use Cally with the internet, so it does not reflect any additional charges on your phone bill.

Features;

• Make a video and voice calls with Cally ID number especially determined for you.
• The person you are talking to with Cally does not see your number, it protects your privacy.
• You need to have an internet connection to make a call. Your calls are not charged via your operator.
• You can make voice and video calls with your specific Cally IDs.
• Customize your search screen with the unique themes that we update frequently.

Protect Your Privacy

Nobody can listen or access your voice and video conversations with SSL encryption. Cally allows you to have a 100% secure conversation. Cally does not want access to your contacts, it only provides a secure search service with the internet. Your calls are made with your Cally ID.

Personalize the Search Screen

You can customize your call screen and answer button as you wish, and your phone will ring with a theme of your choice. You can personalize your search screen by exploring the unique themes we have prepared for you and constantly updated from the customization section.

Things You Need to Know:


• To use the Cally, you need to allow access to your camera and microphone transportation for full performance.
• Both parties must use Cally app to make voice and video calls from the app.
• You must have an active internet connection.
