Description

キティちゃんも遊んでいるアプリ！
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t88jwau9W8g
:*..サンリオキャラの世界へご招待:*..みんなに癒しをお届けします～キャラクターと一緒に、ほのぼのデイズを送りましょ！

キティ、マイメロ、キキララ、ポムポムプリン、シナモンなどサンリオの大人気キャラクターが大集合！
お洋服やお部屋をあなた好みにコーディネートしたり、キャラクターと楽しくお話ししたり、遊び方はいろいろ ！

サンリオのキャラクターたちが暮らす街「スマイルタウン」で、あなただけの特別な時間を過ごしてね！

《主な登場キャラクター》

かわいい!! Very cute!

This game is wonderful and totally caters to my tastes in design and cuteness! As a beginner in Japanese, though, the game's lack of English settings doesn't do it many favors. I hope one day the game gets an English setting! I understand America may not be your biggest demographic for this, and not many Americans play it compared to Japanese, but I think it would do some great favors, and maybe attract more people, since the Japanese may turn a lot of people off. That's all to complain about, though! That and the fact that it's a bit difficult to get currency, but I understand the reason why. Thank you for making such a cute and colorful game! 愛あなた！♡♡

Edit: so, I recently found out about your trading system! It could be improved a little. Maybe you could make a place for people to trade instead of having to wait until you bump into someone, or an AI that can match you with people who have something on your wishlist! That's all!
