Description

Happiness at your fingertips!

Learning and practicing meditation as an integral part of your day has been proven to increase the overall quality of your life. Our app Relax Meditation provides an immersive experience that approaches meditation from an everyday life perspective making it an easy, natural and simple activity for everyone.

From the creators of Relax Melodies, Ipnos is proud to introduce its new app specialized in mindfulness meditation. Make us part of your everyday life, and experience incredible positive changes. Quiet your mind, improve your awareness, your mood and overall health by learning how to experience every moment to its fullest and find the happiness that's been waiting for you.

Try our free, 5-day Introductory program to meditation and experience firsthand how mindfulness can help you in all aspects of your everyday life.

Some of the benefits of mindfulness meditation.

* Boost your overall Health
* Find Happiness everywhere you go
* Improve your Relationships
* Increase your Focus and Awareness
* Improve your Productivity
* Know yourself at a deeper level
* Sleep Better

CyndyML , 06/25/2017

Superb

We started using a noise machine to cover up snoring some years ago. It worked well, but the noise machine would break every other year and we'd replace it. Then I tried an app for a noise machine. I've tried a few, and this is the best, unquestionably. There is a great variety of sounds, and you can create your favorite combinations with each sound at its own volume, and save and label it for the future. I never wanted to get into a subscription payment, so had never gone pro. Now they offer lifetime access for a reasonable set fee, and it is well worth it! As much as I have used and enjoyed this app, I was happy to have the opportunity to compensate the company, and the current lifetime fee is at a proper point when comparing it to stand alone models and other apps. I suggest you try the free version for a few months and then don't be surprised if you upgrade!
One more thing - some other apps are just not as good as stand alone noise machines. This app is far better because of the quality of the sound options and the ability to really personalize the saved sounds.

Ook-bay over-lay , 11/15/2017

Stressing out!

I’ve always wanted to learn to meditate. I have really been stressing out this past year so I went looking for meditation apps and really liked this app right off the bat when I tried it. The voice of the meditation leader is perfect and I can individually adjust the sounds and the volume of each. I’m hard of hearing so this is actually a big deal to me otherwise I struggle to hear the meditations because the background noise is too loud. There are a lot of features to customize with the sounds, music and background settings. It’s easy to use and intuitive.

I went through the introductory learning week and chose to buy the lifetime membership at a reduced price, which is less than I’d pay for even a basic class to learn how to do this. I’m not a fan of monthly memberships so I thought it was worth it. The lifetime membership opens up a vast library of meditations, sounds and coaching to help me deal with problems I’m facing daily. My wife and I are making meditation with this app the first thing to start our day. Thanks for developing your product!

Leasuca , 05/22/2017

Music for Sleeping

Update to my previously submitted review (below): Hooray to the composers who created new sounds and melodies that happened to address my concerns and/or criticisms. There are more evocative music selections that don't get stuck with short repetitive melodies but rather wash over you or lull you into a state of calm and peace. Also there's an impressive number of new meditations. All in all, thanks for the welcome updates.

Previous review:
I appreciate all the choices of sounds and ability to customize ... but I wish the sounds with melodies (piano, flute, orchestra, etc.) had a longer loop cycle. Some of them consist of just four notes - whistleable, memorable notes, if you know what I mean - where instead of relaxing you, start to annoy or frustrate you because you're fixating on it, instead of letting it wash over you. I'm a music teacher, so perhaps I'm hyper sensitive to this. If you could offer more music choices with less melody and more of a droning, hypnotic quality, I'd give this app five stars. Thank you for a great app.
