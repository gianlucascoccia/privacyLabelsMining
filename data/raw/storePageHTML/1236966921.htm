Description

Wedding Planner is the perfect companion to have while preparing for your big day. It's packed with a personalized checklist of all the essential to-dos for you and your partner's "I dos." You'll feel so much more at ease to know what has been done and what still needs to be done - there's no better app to use for all your wedding needs, so start planning with Wedding Planner today!

And it's so much more than just a checklist. Here are some of Wedding Planner's amazingly helpful features:

• Sync & Backup: Invite your partner to participate or simply back up your data for peace of mind.
• Categories: If you ever feel lost, you can find any checklist item by category from Accessories to Stationery.
• Photos: Keep your favorite photos of you and your loved one right on the home screen to remind yourself of what this is all about every time you open the app.
• Music: Add your special song and listen while you get things done.
• Budget: Set a budget and get an estimate of how much to spend on each expense.
• Journal: Keep a wedding journal for all the days leading up to the wedding - these are memories worth saving.
• Day-of: Plan the timeline of events on your wedding day and easily share it with vendors, friends, and family.
• Contacts: Put all your important wedding contacts in one place and organize them by category.
• Apple Watch: Check off your to-dos and view your wedding countdown and photo on Apple Watch!
• And more!

In addition to the free features listed above, we also offer Wedding Planner Premium as a subscription. Wedding Planner Premium subscribers will have access to an ad-free experience and unlimited storage.

Price may vary by location. Subscriptions will be charged to your credit card through your iTunes account. Your subscription will automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage your subscription in Account Settings after purchase.

Real review

This is a great app for anybody doing their own planning. There is a design flaw in the budget system. It will not automatically re-calculate money when changing it to suit your needs. Plus sometimes when you go in to change the budget to a lesser or higher number it just stays a zero. The only way to really counter this issue is to do it by hand or on your own notes. I hate that but obviously nobody is fixing this app anytime soon because it still doesn’t work after updates and re-downloads.

anakcornelio , 07/16/2019

Perfectly Flexible!

This app is (to my expectations) a great app for the bride / coordinator who isn’t looking to be bombarded with vendors as soon as it is opened. So you simply enter your estimated date & automated checklists get allocated into the months in between. That to me is a wonderful amount of help when you’re new to weddings events in general. If you don’t know how to really “start from scratch” or to organize “only what you need” for the special day.
While I did have some tasks put into other months & some things already taken care of, this app allowed me to move the task’s ideal due date & even call it whatever I want (“Practice floral arrangements” in place of “Research Florists”). I could delete tasks that simply didn’t pertain to my wedding style choice. I think it eases the process so conveniently. So you can take it everywhere & do things as you want them. I had previously downloaded a more popular app (The Knot) & I was beginning to feel overwhelmed. Because I am having a more intimate gathering with less of the elaborate traditions/arrangements etc.
Highly recommend this for the planner in you that wants everything accessible and flexible to your needs/wants.

wallacebatgirl , 01/04/2021

Great way to keep your sanity!

Once I got engaged, I considered buying a wedding planning book to help me keep things organized. I’m so glad I decided to look for an app instead! Not only is it a free way to keep my sanity, but I love the way that it gave me a suggested checklist for each month leading up to our big day! That has helped us so much with our guess work! If we don’t like some of their suggestions, we can delete them or add some of our own.

We’ve actually gotten ahead of schedule, just doing things as it’s convenient. The way that it shows us what percentage we’ve accomplished of our checklists for each month makes us feel very accomplished! All in all, I recommend it to anyone getting married!
