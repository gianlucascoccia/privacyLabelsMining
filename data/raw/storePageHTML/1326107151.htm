spanish_r0se , 02/17/2019

This app is okay

This app has some pros and cons. First of all, some content is cute; but there’s no way to do-over a picture. So if you are doing one for fun, don’t do the nicer ones, in my opinion. If you were doing it for beauty or something like that, than sure, do a nicer one. This brings me to my next point. Like with most apps, there’s a membership you can buy (for 9.99 a week). While this can buy you even “cuter” content, its still 10 bucks a week; which to me is a waste of money. Also, something that frustrates me: If you don’t have the membership, the pictures you will have to require you to watch a 30 second commercial. I can deal with this, but say I don’t get to finish a picture, and I exit the app. When I’m ready to finish the pic, just to start again (it will save your progress), I have to watch a commercial, which I feel this app just promotes other apps. One specific app makes me leave the coloring app to go to that app; then I have to go back into the coloring app, which is REALLY annoying.
Overall, this app is okay. While there are pros to this app, I feel that cons outweigh them. I hope I was able to help you in your decision on whether to download this app.
