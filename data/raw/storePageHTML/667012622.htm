Description

Put your face in funny GIFs, eCards and Videos. Add your own voice or select from 1000's of audio clips. Make hilarious, funny GIFs, memes and videos that will make anyone laugh.

* Browse our collection of thousands of trending and popular GIFs, Ecards famous faces, and sounds.
* Add faces of friends, family, pets, cartoons and more to any of the GIFs, Ecards or your own photos.
* Personalize with Text, animated stickers and sound effects.
* Use the custom GIF editor and upload any GIF you want.
* Tap into the huge collection of faces, GIFs, sounds and stickers to make custom videos, animated gifs and talking photos.
* Adjust voice and audio pitch of any recording or audio clip you apply.
* Photo booth and cartoon effects for any face you create. Cartoon yourself, Elf yourself, and more with special effects, filters and animated stickers.
* Upload your own sounds and voice clips to use with your GIFs, Animated Photos and Ecards.
* Easy to use and Easy to share. Share to iMessage, Facebook, TikTok, WhatsApp and more.

HOW DOES AN ANIMATE ME MEMBERSHIP WORK?

All GIFs, Animations and Ecards in the Animate Me app are free to view and share as GIFS.

A membership provides access to upload your own sounds and GIFS, share videos with sound, and future premium features.

Payment will be charged to your iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase.

Memberships automatically renew for $2.99 each month or $24.99 per year unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period.

You may manage your auto-renewal by visiting your iTunes Account Settings after purchase.
