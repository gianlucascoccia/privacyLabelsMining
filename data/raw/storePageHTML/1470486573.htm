Description

Use the official app from the National Park Service to find detailed information about visitor centers, important sites, places to stay and eat and services throughout the park.

##App Features:
Map - Use the park map to find sites, get directions and locate services. Start the app and download the map before you arrive to use it when there is no internet connection.

Sites - Find trailheads, restrooms, overlooks and more throughout the entire park.

Save Favorites - Build an itinerary by saving your favorite sites and events. You can even get alerts when you're nearby a saved site.

Calendar - Get up-to-date information from the National Park Service about what is happening in Yosemite, including ranger talks and other learning opportunities.

Tours - Discover rich natural and cultural resources with self-guided walking tours.

Accessibility - This app includes up-to-date accessibility information for facilities and some trails in the park, audio-described sites, and alternative text for images.


##About The Park.
First protected in 1864, Yosemite National Park is best known for its waterfalls, but within its nearly 1,200 square miles, you can find deep valleys, grand meadows, ancient giant sequoias, a vast wilderness area, and much more.


##Notes
Download the app before you arrive. Cell service and Wi-Fi are extremely limited in Yosemite.
Continued use of GPS running in the background can dramatically decrease battery life.

Great info, gps lags

I used the map part of the app more than anything else. It was nice to see where I was on the map, but the gps was sometimes slow to update. NPS Yosemite

I just finished two days in Yosemite, and I love this app. Not only is it chock full of useful information, you can download its content to your phone so you have the fully functional app even when there’s no cell signal. Best of all, the map apparently uses the phone’s GPS, because it tracks you as you drive through the park, and has icons for every upcoming feature. Touch the icon, and up comes a full description of the feature. You always know where you are, and you always know what there is to see and do. Terrific!
