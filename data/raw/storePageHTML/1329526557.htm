Description

Discover a large and ever-growing collection of oracle decks. Get unlimited access to each deck for 7 days, for free!

Welcome to the Indie Goes Oracle Cards collection! In this app, you will find a large collection of oracle decks, covering many themes. If you already used our single apps, we recommend switching to this app instead.

Key features:
- All your decks in one place
- Exclusive decks only available in this app!
- Get unlimited access to each deck for 7 days
- Explore different types of spreads
- Save your readings to a journal for further reference
- Get your "Card of the day", drawn from our decks collection
- Decks take 50% less space on your device, compared to multiple single apps
- Stay updated about new releases and sales
- Higher resolution assets
- Improved sharing and email features. Use any social app on your device to share cards.
- If you already purchased our single apps, you will be able to unlock the decks in this app for free!

About the author: Indie Goes is a French studio founded by Frédéric Calendini in 2012. We all see times are changing, and people need access to more profound and meaningful products in order to connect to a deeper part of themselves. By getting access to beautiful and enlightening material, everybody will then be able to receive the proper inspiration and follow their own guidance. There are so many authors who have beautiful things to share, and our joy is to help them reach a wider audience through quality and fun projects. We strongly believe technology and spirituality are not incompatible. That's why we want to create software — but software with a soul.
We hope you will enjoy our creations, as they really are a work of love. Thank you!

Great company!

I’ve purchased a number of the Indie Oracle cards and enjoy all of them. They each have their own unique way of relating wisdom so I’ll choose different decks depending on my question or state of mind. I have my favorites of course, but they are all beautifully done and seem to be very insightful.
The main reason for my review though is to praise the developer. One of the apps I purchased for some reason wouldn’t load the full version even after the fee had been charged to my account. There are many app developers that don’t seem to put much care or energy into responding, let alone working to correct issues that customers have. So I was hesitant to even bother trying to contact them. I did though and got an immediate response. The developer, Frédéric Calendini, got right back to me. Then kindly and quickly gave me a fix for the problem. He obviously cares about every single customer and is available and willing to do what he can to help if there’s a problem.
This unfortunately doesn’t always seem to be the standard these days now that there’s so little face to face interaction.

Thank you Frédéric!
I appreciate, respect, and admire the way you run your company.
-Esme
