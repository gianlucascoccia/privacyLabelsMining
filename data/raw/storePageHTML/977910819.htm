Very fiddly to install but okay otherwise

Lots of people seem to have issues installing the cameras and getting the meShare app to recognize them. I found a stupid workaround, but it does work. Download the Zmodo app here on the App Store. Login with your meShare account info. Add the camera on the Zmodo app using “Zlink.” Don’t try any of the other options, they basically never work. Once you get the to the final camera setup page in the Zmodo app with the continuous loading wheel, boot up meShare and see if the cameras show up. It’s worked 100% for me over ten reboots, and multiple router changes and app updates. Once you’re done you can delete the Zmodo app entirely.

Three stars because no camera should be this obnoxious to install, but do they have decent functionality once you jump through all the hoops.
