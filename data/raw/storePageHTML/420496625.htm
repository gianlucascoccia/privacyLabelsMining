Description

The Merrill Edge mobile app lets you access and manage your accounts and investments where and when it’s most convenient.

Manage Your Accounts
• View balances, holdings, order status and pending and historical transactions
• Trade stocks, ETFs, mutual funds and options
• View your brokerage accounts or any linked external accounts you hold outside Merrill Edge

Stay On Top of the Market
• Up-to-date Market data and News
• Real-time streaming quotes
• Explore heat maps and Interactive charts

Deposit Checks
• Simply take photos of checks to deposit them¹
• View recent check deposits

Transfer Money
• Transfer money between your Merrill Edge investment accounts and your linked Bank of America, N.A. bank accounts
• Make transfers to and from accounts held at other financial institutions that you’ve set up on the website.
• Send and receive money using Zelle®²

Pay Bills (must be enrolled)
• Make single payments
• Pay eBills

Guidance & Retirement
• Plan for retirement & college costs using the Personal Retirement and College Planning calculators
• Other tools available to help with what matters most to you (IRA Selector calculator, My Life Priorities, Life Events)

Stay Secure
• Set up Touch ID® / Face ID® to securely sign in³
• Send messages and documents using the Secure Inbox

Apple Watch®
• Monitor market performance
• Receive alerts

¹Deposits are subject to verification and not available for immediate withdrawal. Other restrictions apply.
²Requires enrollment in the service and must be made from a Bank of America consumer checking or savings account. Recipients have 14 days to register to receive money or the transfer will be canceled. Dollar and frequency limits apply.
³Certain devices are eligible for enrollment in Touch ID® / Face ID®
