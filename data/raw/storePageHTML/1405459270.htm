Description

Be with your pet, even when you're not at home - With our Dog Camera App you keep in touch with your best friend via Live Video Stream using your iPhone, iPad, Mac.

Be with your pet, even when you're not at home - With PetCam App you keep in touch with your best friend via Live Video Stream using your iPhone, iPad, Mac.

PetCam App allows you to set alerts to inform you whenever your Pet is noisy or needs your attention. You can see anytime what is going on at home.

How to get started with PetCam App:

1. Choose your home station and activate it (e.g. your Mac or iPad)
2. Activate your receiver on your mobile device that will stay with you
3. To watch your pet while you are not at home, tap “open video”

Functionalities:

• Free Full version for 7 days with the first download.
• NEW!! - Live Video Chat with your Pet
• 8x Digital Video Zoom.
• Take Pet Photo - Create photos to capture unique moments - anytime.
• Talk to Pet - Talk to your pet whenever you want.
• Live Video Stream- Be part of your pet’s life even from abroad!
• Record Pet Video - Record the best moments of your pet.
• Auto Sound - If your pet gets stressed during your absence and you are enable to access the app, PetCam App will help to calm down your pet.
