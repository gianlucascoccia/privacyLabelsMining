Description

You can use Go Translate with more than 100 languages in any app such as Whatsapp, iMessage, Facebook, Twitter, and translate anything, anytime - voice, pics, text!

Go Translate is an indispensable voice, camera and text translator that allows you to communicate effectively in any country. Easily translate text, websites, or start voice-to-voice conversations in over 100+ languages.

FEATURES:

• Translation Keyboard
Realtime translate messages with more than 20 languages inside any app, such as Whatsapp, iMessage, Facebook, Twitter, etc.

• Conversation Mode
Have foreign language conversations. Now you can communicate effectively with people from all over the world. Go Translate translates conversations between a foreigner and yourself in real-time.

• Camera Translation
Translate any pic or text using your camera. Snap a shot of any printed source and Translate GO translates it instantaneously. You can easily translate menus, signs, maps, or any paper document.

• Offline Mode
Works OFFLINE. No need for internet connect.

• Voice Translation
Speak to translate. Dictate what you want to translate and let the app read out the translation, or read it on your screen. GO Translate enables you to speak a new language with confidence.

• Text Translation
Translate text into 100+ languages. Enter the phrase you want to translate to receive the results in text format or listen to the correct pronunciation.

• Instant Translation

• 100+ languages – English, Spanish, Italian, French, German, etc.

You can use it anytime and anywhere.
• Travel - Asking directions, hotel inquiries, menu translation, talking to taxi drivers, shopping,...
• Study - Learning new languages, understanding articles and speeches...
• Communication – Business meetings, conferences, parties...
