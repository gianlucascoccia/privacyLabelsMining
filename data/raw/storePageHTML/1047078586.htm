Description

Are preparing for Ranger School or looking for the perfect hip pocket training aid on Infantry tactics?

Ranger School Professional helps you fully prepare for the tactical and technical portion of Ranger School, deployment, CTC rotation or hip pocket training by giving you instant access to Infantry tactics classes given by actual Ranger experts, high-definition video instruction, FAQ, Ranger School Tips provided by instructors and former Ranger students, quick access to the information on ARTB website, and much more.

If you’re interested in attending Ranger School or to sharpen your tactical and technical knowledge, then this app will help you achieve your goals. With over 39+ instructional videos for all three phases of Ranger School, quizzes specifically designed to test your knowledge, quick access to the Ranger School handbook and other manuals, and tons more, you’ll be set for success.

Already graduated Ranger School? Ranger graduates often forget in detail the classes they were taught because of exhaustion and sleep deprivation, but now you can reference this app when you want to brush up on your knowledge or before you give a class to your Soldiers.

Ranger School Professional is constantly getting updated and gets the information from the source. This app is the ultimate resource if you are trying to significantly increase your chances of graduating or being a more proficient Infantryman. If you have a question or concern, you can message us from the app and get a quick response.

Ranger School Professional includes all of the following:

*Squad Classes:
FOOM, React to Contact, Squad Attack, Break Contact, React to Ambush, React to Indirect Fire, Cross Small Open Danger Area, Cross Large Open Danger Area, Cross Linear Danger Area, and Link up, Area Reconnaissance, Ambush, and Patrol Base

*Platoon Classes:
Operations Order, Fragmentary Order, Platoon Ambush, Patrol Base, and Raid

* Ranger Tactical Tasks:
Maintain M249 MG, Fire M249 MG, Maintain M240B MG, Fire M240B MG, Employ M18A1 Claymore Mine, Operate SINCGARS, Operate MBITR, and Acquire Position with DAGR

* Ranger Knots:
Bowline, Clove Hitch, Double Figure 8, Rerouted Figure 8, Figure 8 slip, Munter Hitch, Prusik, and Rappel Seat

*Ranger School:
Prerequisites, General Information, Environmental Considerations, Graduation Photos, and Rangers In Action Demonstration

*Extra:
Land Navigation Test, CWST, SKEDCO, Malvesti, Darby Queen, Rope Bridge, Lessons / Tips

* FAQ:
Ask a question you have about Ranger School; also look up past questions that were asked

*Quizzes

* Access to all publications that Ranger School Professional referenced

If you're serious about being an expert in your field or preparing for Ranger School and want to increase your chances of graduating, then this is the app for you. Don't take the chance when it comes to earning the Ranger tab!
