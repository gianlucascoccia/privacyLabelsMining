Description

Capture and 3D track your scene with ease. Bridge the gap between production and post-production with CamTrackAR, the only app that will capture video and your 3D tracking data simultaneously using Apple’s AR technology.

Perfect for professionals looking for pre-visualization options and any video creator wanting to speed up their post-production workflow.

Use it free with a limited export to HitFilm (.hfcs) and Blender (.py) and a single point anchor. Upgrade to export to After Effects (.jsx) and Plain Text (.json, .csv) where you can complete your post-production workflow, and add multiple anchor points to your scene.

FIRST OF ITS KIND
CamTrackAR is the first and only app to automatically capture video and 3D tracking data at the same time, simplifying the motion-tracking workflow for creators of all skill levels.

SUPERIOR TRACKING
We use Apple’s state-of-the-art AR technology to produce a fast and accurate 3D camera track. CamTrackAR utilizes all hardware advancements available to produce a better track.

EASY-TO-USE INTERFACE
CamTrackAR is just one simple screen with clean and clear buttons, designed to ensure the details of your shot aren’t obscured as you film.

AUTOMATIC CAPTURE
Set your floor plane with our green tracking grid and add in anchor points with the press of a finger. Then press record and CamTrackAR does all the hard work for you. Disable auto-focus with the click of a button for further control.

GAME-CHANGING FOR VIDEO CREATORS
A must-have for every YouTuber and creator, CamTrackAR ensures that lower thirds, composited 3D models, and anything requiring a camera-track stays locked in place.

PRE-VISUALIZATION FOR PROFESSIONALS
Test the viability of VFX shots immediately. Accurate tracking makes CamTrackAR perfect for any cinematographer or director wanting to preview shots on set.

EXPORT TO FREE SOFTWARE
With the free version of CamTrackAR, you can export your scene and tracking data to the free post-production software HitFilm (.hfcs) and Blender (.py).

UPGRADE FOR MORE
Access advanced features like unlimited export and multiple point anchors. Subscribe monthly or purchase outright to add more than one positional point to your scene and export your camera tracking data into After Effects (.jsx) and Plain Text (.json, .csv) quickly and easily.

Frog The Spy , 12/07/2020

Really good! Just needs some more features

I really really love the potential this has as a tool. The tracking itself is fantastic, very very accurate.

My only issues lie in the lack of features you get when buying the full version. The app forces you to shoot in landscape, but if you try to shoot vertically the motion data is much trickier to work with, especially in Blender. Aside from that, there’s just a general lack of information that is given to you within the app about what you are capturing, etc.

Overall, great app and I highly recommend, but it could use some fine tuning, especially if the full version costs what it does.

Thanks very much for the review! We're taken your comments into account, and please watch future updates for improvements.
