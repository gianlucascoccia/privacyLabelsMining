Description

Free Offline Audiobook & Music Player! Download your audiobooks or music and save device space!
Main Features:
- Download and play audio files offline without internet!
- Import tracks from all popular cloud services - Dropbox, Google Drive, iCloud Drive
- Full-featured player
- Advanced order for your audio: by title, added by date, artist, source, plays, and length
- Start listening in one tap
- Listen to tracks from iPod library
- Background play
- Create and manage playlists with multi-cloud support
- Shuffle and repeat playlists and favorites
- Collections and album covers
- Lockscreen control support
- Bluetooth, earphones and AirPlay support
- Powerful widget
* Please note that the app cannot play DRM protected files purchased on iTunes Store

Great App - Needs Further Development

I play my Kindle books on Fire tablet and convert them to MP3 audiobooks with 30 minutes per “chapter.” I store the books in Dropbox. This app fetches the chapters and I listen in their order. This is a pricey app, and can justify the subscription if I see further development. What I need for audiobooks are the common controls. Jump Forward and Jump Back 15, 30 60 seconds. Bookmark location. Remain in last heard spot of a track when re-starting the app. I have tried several music players and audiobooks creators in iOS, and this app is the best so far. Continue to work on this and I will continue to subscribe. Thank you!

Too expensive - $100+ per year

I was looking for an audiobook player that would access my cloud files where my audiobooks are stored, particularly iCloud which most players don’t seem to support. I thought I had found my app until I discovered that the app is on a subscription basis for use rather than purchase. The app seems to work well and definitely meets my needs; however, I certainly am not willing to pay $100+ per year for nothing but access to an app. No books, no content, no nothing other than the use of an app. No wonder there’s so few ratings. I’m sure the others that have tried out the app have done what I am going to do — delete it.
