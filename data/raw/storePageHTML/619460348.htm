maryc0501 , 02/18/2021

Fun

Well you’ll probably find yourself getting stuff that you don’t need but you can find real deals on stuff that you do need or want. Whenever I’ve had a question or a problem they have been very responsive. If something has arrived damaged or the wrong item has come they did not refund me my money but they did give me a credit immediately. If you bid impulsively and realize you don’t want the item they have issued me a credit for that as well. If you bid accidentally they will cancel your transaction if you let them know immediately. So no reason not to buy stuff and have fun. Shipping speeds are variable, some things taking a very long time to ship and other things coming very quickly. They encourage you to leave a rating on the items and if you look at the ratings from others ahead of time you will find information that you may have otherwise missed. The sizes of clothing can be very tricky as you normally have to purchase at least two sizes larger than what a US size would be. If you have to make a choice between several options I encourage you to take a picture that shows either the size, color, or the item number that you want so that when you go to make your purchase you are not confused because there’s no way to go back and review the multiple pictures of the items unless it’s up for bid again. You may forget that you wanted color number 5 for example.
