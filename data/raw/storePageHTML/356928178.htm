Description

LiveScore keeps you up-to-date with the latest scores and live sports action. From soccer goals to cards, fixtures to final results, LiveScore has everything you need, all in one place.

LiveScore delivers fast updates with the latest scores and sports info coverage for soccer, basketball, hockey, tennis and cricket so you can find out first.

KEY FEATURES

LIVE NOTIFICATIONS – Get fast updates to keep track of what’s going on, including scores, goals and red cards for multiple matches and sports at the same time. Just star a match to find out first.

FAVOURITE LEAGUES - Quickly get to the scores and details of matches you care about.

COMPETITIONS - Tap the Explore icon to search hundreds of your favourite competitions across the biggest sports. Including major cups and leagues with full match details on the MLS, NWSL, Premier League and La Liga.

LIVESCORE BALL TRACKER - Check the pitch view to follow the ball, scores and action.

MATCH STATISTICS – Check club form and the results of previous match meetings in the head-to-head section. Plus, possession, shots and fouls stats at the match end.

BREAKING NEWS - Read breaking sports news for major sports & leagues, including the US Open Series, MLS, Premier League, Champions League and La Liga. Turn on notifications to find out first.

FIXTURES AND RESULTS CALENDAR - Find out what matches are coming up with the calendar feature, helping you look further ahead for those all-important Fantasy Soccer and Fantasy Football decisions.

LIVE COMMENTARY - Detailed live match commentary, including goals scored, assists, corners, cards and descriptions of the action – ideal for Fantasy Soccer and Fantasy Football fans.

MANAGE NOTIFICATIONS – Select which match events you want to hear about in your settings. From when line-ups become available, or the start of the second half, there’s every detail you won’t want to miss.

LEAGUE TABLES - See champions made and teams relegated as the league table including for MLS and NWSL updates in real time.

WORLDWIDE SCORES AND SPORT
Whether you say “soccer”, “football”, “calcio” or “fussball”, you’ll be able to find your team. More than 1,000 live soccer and football matches are followed every week during the season for the premier cups and leagues worldwide.

Follow your favorite tennis players in their quest to become champions at ATP & WTA events and each of the Slams. Check all the tournament’s results and view upcoming matches, then follow in-play commentary and point-by-point details in the biggest tennis matches on the ATP or WTA World Tours.

If basketball is your thing then LiveScore is the best sports app for keeping up with the game, with NBA conferences, play-offs and finals all covered.

From court details in basketball’s top competitions, to up-to-the minute commentary about the action in the hockey, LiveScore will deliver fast updates directly to your app.
Hockey is covered too and thanks to LiveScore’s speed, the app gives you fast updates from national and international hockey leagues in the USA, Canada and Europe including the NHL.

Follow international tournaments and see cricket scorecards, ball-by-ball commentary and detailed descriptions of each wicket, from ODIs to T20.


ABOUT LIVESCORE
Since 1998, LiveScore has been giving you real-time delivery of live sport scores and data. As leaders and inventors of live score updates, LiveScore is trusted and used daily by millions of users globally. It specializes in the sports: soccer and football, tennis, cricket, basketball and hockey. LiveScore is proud to be an Official Global Partner of La Liga Santander.

JBF42 , 01/13/2021

Great app but needs Push Notification upgrade

Loving this app so far but the one significant downside is the football (soccer) push notifications (and possibly other sports too, I haven’t had a chance to test others).

1) When a goal is scored - it doesn’t say which player scored. Seems rather obvious that, if I’m subscribed to goal updates via Push, I’ll be interested in knowing which player scored. A mention of the player who was awarded the “assist” would be great as well, but not as important
2) When a goal is scored - rather than indicating which team just scored, it only shows the updated score for both teams. It’s not clear which team just scored. Again - just seems like a simple thing that would make sense and make the Push easier to comprehend.

The ESPN and theScore apps do a great job with both items above. Until LiveScore can offer something similar, I’ll unfortunately be switching back to the ESPN app solely for this reason.
