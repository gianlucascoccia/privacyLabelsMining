Description

Tops BONUS PLUS features include paperless, click-to-card coupons, interactive weekly ads, recipes, shopping lists, BONUS PLUS point balances, store mapping and information, and pharmacy refills.

Notification Features
• Optionally use location services in order to alert you of valuable specials and more when you arrive at the store.
• Optionally provide push notifications of special limited time offers.

eCoupons Features
• Paperless coupons available for all Tops BONUS PLUS accounts
• Scan product barcodes to find matching coupons
• “Tap” once to add to your BONUS PLUS account and your Shopping List
• Instant savings at the register when you shop
• Share coupons via social sites
• Recipe links on coupons

Weekly Ad Features
• Interactive digital Weekly Ad items
• Tap to add to your Shopping List
• Multiple Ads available online
• Tap eCoupons adjacent to the corresponding Weekly Ad items
• View associated recipes within Weekly Ad

Recipe Features
• Thousands of recipes
• Weekly featured recipes and browse by categories
• Tap to add all or selected ingredients to your Shopping List
• View special offers associated with ingredients

Shopping List Features
• Contains Click-To-Card coupons, Weekly Ad items, Recipes and easy entry items
• Multiple lists can be created, edited or deleted
• Lists are organized by department for easy shopping
• Tap the list as you shop, and the item moves to “groceries in cart” at bottom of screen

Pharmacy features
• Scan your prescription labels to add them to a refill request that can be submitted right from the app
• Resubmit prescriptions for refill from a history of prescriptions that you've previously refilled using the app
• Check the status of submitted refills to see if they are ready for pickup
• Submitted refills are automatically added to your current shopping list
• Access detailed information about each refill (number of refills, quantity, etc.)
• Access to pharmacy hours and phone number

Points Plus Features
• Once you’ve logged in, you can see the latest point balance
• Linked to your BONUS PLUS account

BONUS PLUS Features
• Your BONUS PLUS account provides access to Coupons, Shopping Lists, Points Balances and interactive Weekly Ad items
• BONUS PLUS ID Scan page displays your card's barcode so that it can be scanned during checkout

Store Locator Features
• View by map to find a store
• Browse by state and city
• Store hours and phone number listed
