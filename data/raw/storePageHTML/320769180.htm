Description

Over 700 terms commonly used by real estate professionals are defined and categorized in this handy mobile reference and flashcard application. Kaplan's Real Estate Terms for real estate professionals is designed to help you learn important terms you will need in both the workplace and for licensure exams. With this application you can quickly scroll through terms to find a definition, use the search feature, or study and test your knowledge with flashcards.

Kaplan Real Estate Terms includes an alphabetical listing of all terms for ready reference. Study sets are grouped into twenty-three categories:

- General Real Estate Terms
- Introduction to the Real Estate Business
- Real Property and the Law
- Fair Housing
- Interests in Real Estate
- Forms of Real Estate Ownership
- Land Description
- Real Estate Taxes and Other Liens
- Real Estate Contracts
- Transfer of Title
- Title Records
- Real Estate Financing
- Real Estate Brokerage
- Leases
- Property Management
- Real Estate Agency
- Land-Use Controls and Property Development
- Client Representation Agreements
- Environmental Issues and the Real Estate Transaction
- Closing the Real Estate Transaction
- Real Estate Appraisal
- Government Involvement in Real Estate Financing
- Investing in Real Estate

The Real Estate Terms App is designed as a mobile reference and learning tool. The application works in two modes; Reference and Flashcards.

Reference mode includes the ability to:
- Scroll alphabetically through the terms and quickly jump to a letter with an A-to-Z side panel
- Search for a specific term including terms within a definition
- Easy text scrolling makes learning complex information more manageable
- Try Landscape view if you prefer to view definitions that way -- just turn your device sideways

Flashcards give you the ability to choose from four study modes:
- Adaptive mode iteratively passes through cards, focusing on those items that need the most work
- Quickly evaluate your progress in the Self-Test Quiz mode
- Flashcard Boxes mode lets you file cards in “Unknown”, “Known” or Mastered” boxes so you can target weak areas
- Turn study time into play time with the concentration-style Matching mode
