Description

Convoy lets you find the loads you want and book them instantly without making a call.

Convoy lets you find the loads you want and book them instantly without making a call. We help you grow your business and keep your truck full on the lanes you like to run.

With Convoy, you can:
• Find relevant loads, view prices & instantly bid on and book loads.
• Dispatch loads to drivers, get turn-by-turn directions & take advantage of free fleet tracking.
• Receive real-time job notifications and electronically upload BOLs - reducing phone calls, manual processes and paperwork.
• Access industry-leading 24/7 bilingual customer service on every load.
• Get paid within days of job completion with free Convoy QuickPay™.
• Access to freight from some of the best shippers around

To register your company with Convoy, call 855-5-CONVOY or email fleet@convoy.com.

We operate in North America with dry van, flatbed, and reefer freight
