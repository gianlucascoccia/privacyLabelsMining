Description

Welcome to Holiday Inn Express in Santa Barbara, California!

Take the opportunity to enhance your travel experience by installing the new Holiday Inn Express Virtual Concierge iPhone app.

The new Holiday Inn Express Virtual Concierge iPhone app showcases the Holiday Inn Express highlights, amenities and special deals and offerings as well as the finest local attractions and dining hotspots within the city of Santa Barbara, California.

The Holiday Inn Express Virtual Concierge iPhone app includes:

• New and current hotel information including unique resort amenities and services
• Hotel Dining menus, special dining offers and meeting charts
• Includes latest dining menus for the best local restaurants in the city of Santa Barbara
• Local Maps and directions available for local island attractions in Santa Barbara, California
• Access to Restaurant Videos, Menus, Reviews and Dining Reservations

We hope you enjoy your stay with us while visiting the Holiday Inn Express in Santa Barbara, California.

The Holiday Inn Express Virtual Concierge Interactive iPhone app available for current and future resort guests.
