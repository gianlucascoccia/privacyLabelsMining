Description

Team communication & collaboration made easy

Beekeeper helps you reach and connect the non-desk employees in your company in an easy and simple way, without exchanging phone numbers or email addresses.
Integrate external systems in the Beekeeper platform to help your employees achieve more together.

Use Beekeeper for
* Real-time communication - chat with your co-workers and see what's happening across your company
* File Sharing - share photo, videos, and documents with your coworkers
* Shift Management - coordinate your shift schedule and tasks with your colleagues
* Centralize operations in one hub - automate your workflow by integrating the tools and services you already use, including Workday, ADP, Microsoft Azure, SAP, and more
* Boost employee engagement and reduce turnover - simple employee surveys let you gather critical feedback in minutes and make impactful changes
* Enterprise-level security and compliance - your data and privacy are protected with state-of-the-art encryption

Want to try Beekeeper at your company? Download the app and start a free trial.

Not sure if Beekeeper is right for your company? Contact us today and we'd be happy to share more and answer any questions you have at info@beekeeper.io.

Grwon , 04/14/2020

Poor user experience

This app is just a thrown together UI that sort of works for small teams. The notifications are not informative. Just a name and a group “posted in” notification and list of notifications makes it difficult to know what’s going on. In a large work force, 100 notifications from 10’s of groups, only one of which you might be interested in looking at, and with no context to navigate with, is worthless. The mark as read function doesn’t work reliably because it’s trying to dispatch asynchronous work that seems to depend on you leaving the app open or at least staying on that tab. If you switch tabs and come back you still see those unread notifications until the asynchronous work completes.

Overall this is just a horrible app which is useless.
