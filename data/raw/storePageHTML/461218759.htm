Description

You found it! Drum Beats+ is the metronome app that improves your timing using great sounding drum beats, not a boring click.

But that’s not all. It can also help you write songs, jam with friends, or even perform live. It's a learning, jamming and performing tool that keeps getting better, just like you.

"Awesome sounding and authentically grooving drum loops…but also a great practice tool for timing." ~ Ets5017

"Sorry drummers...but I use this on live gigs." ~ RBJ555

"A proper replacement for your deadbeat drummer." ~ Rolling Stone Magazine

- - -

FEATURES

Cheaper than a metronome, simpler than a drum machine.

• SUPER SIMPLE DESIGN - spend more time playing, less time learning the app

• NO WORK REQUIRED - Standard Library comes with 100+ PRE-MADE great sounding beats in a variety of genres (Add 250+ with the Pro Library)

• VERSATILE 45 to 200 Tempo Range -- play all the songs you want

• ADJUSTABLE - Play any beat at any speed WITHOUT CHANGING THE PITCH

• FAVORITE beats have their own tab. Play and practice faster.

• LOCK THE TEMPO to save time during practice (tap the padlock button on tempo screen)

• PERSONALIZE - Tap edit to RENAME the beats (e.g. "My Hit Song")

- - -

As you advance as a player, you’ll grow into the app’s advanced features.

• Improve your timing with Bar Break beats that rest for entire bars or more

• Refine your ear with beats that alternate between swing and straight feels, loud and soft volumes and beats per bar

• Connect to GarageBand and other music apps using Inter-app Audio

• Jam loud by using BlueTooth or AirPlay to play beats through your stereo or Apple TV

• Hit the randomizer button to challenge yourself with beats you might not choose

- - -

So many drummers, so little time. Drum Beats+ includes four style packs and four training packs—100+ beats in all—with tons more in our Pro Library.

• Break Beats. 16 bumping beats of classic funk and soul breaks
• Hit Songs I. 16 beats based on popular songs every musician should know
• Pop Rock I. 24 beats spanning funk, straight ahead rock, shuffles and more
• R&B Essentials I: 16 excellent modern and vintage-sound electronic beats
• Rhythm, Timing, & Dynamics Packs. 41 practice beats that alternate grooves, volumes, beats per bar and rests
• Every beat features different sounds—booming rock bass drums, snappy soul snares and more. All digitally mastered to make practicing a pleasure

- - -

Enjoy this powerful, yet easy-to-use tool to get the best music experience!

No need to replace the app with a complicated drum machine, or even a drummer. Just add more beats with our Pro Library. Preview everything inside the app.

Bap, bap boom! :-)

Look, let's be honest here. This is a totally simplistic app, it's not meant to do anything super sophisticated, but that said, it's a lot of fun and great practice tool. I love just scrolling through the different grooves and letting em' inspire a groove. Or finding a beat that fits a groove I'm working on. And it all helps with my timing and my tempo. Especially the practice section where the beats drop out for a bit and come back in. That really helps keep my playing honest and my focus sharp. All of the drum tracks sound great either in my headphones or jacked into the aux jack on my guitar amp, or jacked into my home stereo. Even through my iPhone's speakers it sounds good. They really EQ'd the sounds really well to sound great in a variety of situations. A lot of fun. Totally easy and simple to use....

San Antone! , 07/09/2016

Beats a Regular Click Click Click

This is a great app. I'm an old-school country and western swing guitarist and between the provided beats and the country package I have everything I need for practicing. Metronomes are much better than having something playing the chord progression a lot of the time since you can stop to work on a particular passage or fingering and pick right back up without having to restart or find your place in the chord progression. I wish you could select your own BPM instead of going by the five BPM increments the way the app is set up, and I wish it had more range in terms of tempo's. I would really rated about a 4.8 instead of 5 because of those limitations, but even so a great, great app and a lot of fun to play with.
