Description

Shop at the speed of culture

NTWRK offers shoppable shows featuring the best brands and biggest names in pop culture. Download our app to secure sneakers, streetwear, collectibles, and more during each episode. Products drop during each episode and are available exclusively on our app. Don’t miss out—culture moves fast, and so do our drops.

Forbes notes, “NTWRK is the much-hyped e-tail platform that’s putting a rocket under retailtainment.” Business of Fashion asks, “Is this the QVC of the YouTube generation?”

Featured on: Hypebeast, Forbes, Wall Street Journal (WSJ), Business of Fashion (BoF), Highsnobiety, Tech Crunch, Fast Company, Variety, The Atlantic, WWD, Yahoo! Business, New York Times, Crunchbase.

Watch
Watch our exclusive content on NTWRK with cultural icons and talent such as Billie Eilish, Juice WRLD, DJ Khaled, Odell Beckham Jr, Eddie Huang, Blake Griffin, Alexander Wang, FaZe Clan, Nadeshot, Jonah Hill, Gary Vee, A$AP Ferg, Wu-Tang Clan, Doja Cat and more.

Shop
Shop to gain early access to limited edition and exclusive sneakers, apparel, and collectibles from brands and talent like adidas, Levi’s, Nike, Puma, Adidas, Medicom, Funko, Beats by Dr. Dre, 2K Sports, NBA and more.

Chat
Chat and interact with other users or directly with our host in realtime.

Secure
Secure exclusive drops and products from your favorite brands, labels, and artists shipped directly to your door.

Drawings
The NTWRK Drawings give you a chance to purchase rare and highly coveted hypebeast items from companies such as Supreme, Yeezy, Be@rbrick, Jordan, Off-White, Nike, Adidas, Rolex, Louis Vuitton, and more. Winners are selected at random and announced at the end of each drawing episode. All drawings are free to enter. Enter to win.

Great stuff, if order goes smoothly.

Think of NTWRK like a StockX or a eBay where your order just isn’t guaranteed and you’ll be much happier. You just have to be on top of order tracking and hound customer service. I’ve had multiple instances of missing orders that one day will show up as cancelled but not refunded, that I contact support and they say “that got cancelled, but we will place a new order” or items taking 8 weeks to ship instead of the expected 2. I’ve never gotten a response from emailing their customer service without having to follow up multiple times myself (and yes, I wait 48+ hours for them to respond). They have some really cool stuff and I’ve had maybe 4 of 12 orders to date ship in a timely manner (it says 2-3 weeks but it will always take at least a month or two) with no problems, but if there are any delays or hiccups, it’s a nightmare to get support. Also, things sell out very quickly. Sometimes in seconds. The “stock” they have can’t be more than 5 items sometimes. Have low expectations and you’ll be pleasantly surprised.
