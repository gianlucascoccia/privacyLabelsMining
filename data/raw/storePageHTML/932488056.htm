Description

Perfect companion for practicing Indian classical music

Rhythm with Tabla & Tanpura is an innovative app that lets you sing or practice songs anywhere, anytime. Just select the taal and sing along with the rhythm. Its a handy tool for every singer, composer or a dancer. The beats have the flavour of real tabla and tanpura, giving an Indian classical feeling.

The beat counter helps to sing or practice with ease. We like to make things simple, hence controlling the volume of tabla and tanpura can be done with the help of sliders, same goes for the tempo. Tabla bols are highlighted in a karaoke like style that helps new learners and tabla enthusiasts.

* Hassle-Free
* Easy to use
* Must have for every singers, composers and dancers
* Beautiful tone of manual tabla and tanpura

Features:
* List of 10 taals (After purchasing Taal Pack, you get 62 taals)
* 1 Tanpura Kharaj (After purchasing Taal Pack, you get 18 tanpuras)
* C# scale (After purchasing Scale Pack, you get 12 scales)
* Tempo ranges from 20 -720
* Beat counter
* Karaoke style tabla bol highlighter
* No time limit, continues playing even when the screen is turned off
* Settings page lets you control Taal name sorting preference

charanodhwani , 09/29/2019

Excellent

Excellent App. I have been looking at various tabla app for iPad and getting the right sounds and tuning that is in sync with tabla shruthi to play with vocals and instruments has been a challenge. The free version has only a few basic taals with no variations. But the ones there are a joy to play with. Also note that the free app does not support inter app audio and AU. But does run in background. In spite of many limitations, which may be by design constrained in free version, I am rating this 5 stars as the best free tabla app. Yes intrusive ads are frustrating, but once the tabla is playing it does not intrude.i felt it would be remiss if I did not write this review to give the app the credit it truly deserves.

Sreerupa30 , 12/03/2018

Amazing app! Missing fillers

Love all the various versions of the taals. It would be even more amazing if there was some kind of an intro/filler/ending similar to synthesizer keyboards. It would make the stanza endings/song endings sound even more real!

Hope, the developer will notice this review and maybe give some thought into it! :)

Timou23 , 08/20/2017

Great app, does what it says.

Great and affordable Tanpura/Tabla app. Only features it's missing are a fine tuner for a pitch in case your instrument is off (flute or Harmonium for example), and also some of the town parade drones are on a lower and of the octave available, would be nice to have higher tunings available as well.
