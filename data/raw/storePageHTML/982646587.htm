Description

With Card Guard™ from America First, your iPhone puts you in control. Using America First Card Guard, you can determine where your Visa® cards can be used and where they can’t. You’ll set the amounts, locations, merchants, and/or transaction types your America First cards will process. Control or alert changes take effect instantly so you can make changes on the go.

-Receive real-time alerts anytime your card is authorized or declined or create them based on your preferences.

-Lock your card when you're not using it and turn it on when you're ready to pay.

-Set a dollar threshold that limits your card transactions to an amount you specify.

-Define a geographic location where your cards will work or select My Location to authorize charges in the vicinity of your iPhone.

-Specify which kinds of merchants or transaction types can clear your account.
