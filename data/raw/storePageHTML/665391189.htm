Solobo_ , 02/05/2021

Update Headphones to Fix Bluetooth Issues

There’s a lot of negative reviews for this app and most of them mention connectivity issues. Different people suggest different solutions to the problem but what worked for me was updating the headphones with a computer. At first I was having the same problem where the headphones connected via Bluetooth but the app wasn’t able to recognize it. One of the first things the guide says is to make sure the device is running on the most recent software so I decided to check if there was an update available by connecting it to my computer and what do you know, there was an update. After updating it I tried connecting it to the app again and it worked instantly. This might be the problem some of you reading this have so I hope this helps. I don’t think this app deserves the low rating it has since it’s simple and straightforward. There’s really nothing bad about it. Hopefully the connectivity issues go down and the rating goes up in the future.

Jposey08 , 03/08/2021

From 4/5 Star, to 1 Star!!

Originally I rated this app as a 4-5 Star back when I first installed & used the app back in early 2020. I installed it after purchasing the Elite Pro 2 w/ Super Amp for Xbox, and the app was simple and easy to use. The app made adjusting different sound dynamics easy, as well as visually changing the colors and patterns on the Power Amp itself. Well fast forward to the last 2/3 months, and the app has turned into a complete dumpster fire!!! No matter what I do, the app will not connect to the Power Amp via bluetooth even after attempting every step Turtle Beach has suggested. I have forgot device then reconnected it to not only my iPad pro, but my 2 iPhones as well. I have uninstalled and reinstalled the app numerous times. I also have connected both of my Power Amps to the Audio Hub program on my Mac and updated the Power Amp firmware as well, but nothing works. The worst part is I can't even use the mic because ever since this issue arose, mic function went down with it. Fix this Turtle Beach, or Astro's here I come for good!!!!
