Description

Audiofy is the best way to listen to audio news journalism, books or any text material keeping you informed. It turns any text into audio with a natural voice reader.

Audiofy app is the best way to listen to audio journalism from leading newspapers and magazines, keeping you connected to the world and yourself. Newsfeeds are curated and selected from the best-trusted news sources from more than 30 countries.

Audiofy app is a smart text-to-speech audio-reader that turns any news, book, or reading text into an interactive audiobook so that you can learn something new every day and save more time.

Listening is faster than reading. Audiofy app transforms any text document to an audio-reader with a beautiful natural voice. Simply you take a photo of any text document or you import it from your photo library and Audiofy turns it to an interactive audiobook.

Audiofy can read books, magazines, web pages and can support any text document or text format.

With Audiofy you can listen to your reading material while you're traveling, commuting, cooking, doing a workout, walking your dog, or any other activity.

Audiofy can help anyone who has Dyslexia, Low vision acuity, or other reading disabilities. The Audiofy reader offers a beautiful reading assistant that highlights each word as it reads and let the user change the reading speed.

Audiofy Allows you to translate any text Document to another language and let you listen to the translation with a native reading voice. Imagine if you are traveling to France you can just snap a photo of the restaurant menu to listen to it in French or English many languages are available.

Audiofy app Features:

● Audiofy has news feed from more than 40 countries with more than 30+ languages,
● Supports any Text Document: Book, Image, Pdf, Web text, photos, ...
● Supports any Natural enhanced voice on iPhone or iPad with more than 30+ languages, 70+ High-quality voices.
● Automatically detects the scanned language and choose the best natural voice for you.
● Control your listening speed from slow up to fast.
● Translate to more than 30+ languages and listen with a natural local voice.
● Perfect for users with reading disabilities.
