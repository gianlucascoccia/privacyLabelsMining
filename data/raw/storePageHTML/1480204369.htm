Description

S’More, Anti-superficial relationships

S’More, is a next-gen relationship / dating app that puts character and personality first! Get to know a person before deciding if you like them and before you see them. The more you chat, the clearer they become. S’More is the world’s first anti-superficial dating app.

The Buzz:

Featured in: Forbes, Elle, LA Times, Wall Street Journal, Washington Post, Esquire, Bustle, and more

Named a top dating app by: AskMen, Marie Claire, Self Magazine, Esquire, Refinery 29 and more.


Blurred Video: Video conversations are more fun when they start off blurred! When both chatters opt-in to a video chat, the first 2 minutes of the call are blurred. Get ready for the big reveal!


S’More-A-Scopes. Get free custom daily horoscope and put your relationship and love life into perspective! Get the tools you need to achieve relationship success!

How we use “Something More” on S’More

For relationship type: you are looking for a more serious relationship

Rei V , 01/25/2021

Good, but still needs some updates

I downloaded the app because I like the idea that the pictures are blurred, until you have a few conversation exchanges...very innovative! It feels like a blind date through an app. This app encourages me to just start a conversation, which I like, because I usually wouldn’t be the first to reach out. The only issue I have is not being able to see who I’ve already sent a message to. Because once I sent a message it goes back to the default 7 profile of the day, asking me to send a chat to unblur. I know it’s designed this way to focus on the people who actually reply back, however, I tend to lost track on whom I have already messaged versus on those I haven’t messaged. Like can there be a notification that you’ve messaged the person, it doesn’t even have to be on the inbox, just a checkbox of having sent a chat already. I just don’t want to send to two messages to the same person.

Developer Response ,

Hi Rei V! Thanks for downloading and sharing your super thoughtful feedback. We totally hear you, and we don't have a "sent message" or outbox because we want you to focus on those who reply back to you! We agree that some notification that you've messaged a person already would be helpful, so keep those app notifications turned on and you'll be the first to know when we have a new app update :) We wish you the best on your S'More journey!

Boston_Lady , 02/10/2021

Suggestions and Concerns

I live in Boston, but keep getting connected with people in other states in New England. There should be a setting for location proximity preference to weed out matches that are not feasible to pursue. I like the icons on the profile, but it would be nice to have more question options. It would be nice to have like a special time of the week you are able to see people’s photos before chatting. Also, I like the premise of the blurred photos, but I feel horrible when I delete someone after the photo is clear - I feel like this could be worse for people’s self esteem. I find I’m not physically attracted to anyone I’ve chatted with so far and don’t see me renewing the subscription if this continues. It’s not feasible for me to keep investing time in matches where there is not physical chemistry. Maybe you could swipe photos but then not know out of the swiped photos who is who until you chat and unblur?

Also, I keep getting people who are younger than me. I’m in my late twenties and would rather date men in their thirties than a twenty two year old. Please instead of doing the age range let us select what age range we would like to date. Thanks!

Developer Response ,

Hi Boston lady! Thanks for the thoughtful feedback. We totally hear you, and we are working on a new feature to help you filter matches by distance - that way you'll only see matches that are closest to you! We're also testing a new way to see profile photo previews, so keep those app updates turned on and you'll be the first to know when these are live :) Stay tuned!
