Description

In partnership with thousands of public safety agencies, Everbridge offers the most trusted information available at a neighborhood level to keep residents informed - all delivered directly to your mobile device. Messages range from emergencies and crime advisories to important announcements, reminders and community updates.

You can also connect with your employer or university to receive vital information during critical events or while managing your response to an incident. Your workplace, school, or similar organization may also allow you to view your schedule or create your own alert to send an SOS when in need of assistance.

Cal Cowboy , 11/27/2019

Great Idea, Poor Execution

This was originally recommended as an emergency warning system. It has my location (exact address). It has a loud warning signal when a message is sent. All good so far. In the approximately 3 years that I’ve had it I’ve received several alerts that didn’t pertain to me. I received an immediate evacuation alert at 2am that was a mistake, fortunately my wife called to verify the accuracy of the alert.
Now, along with emergency alerts I have started getting warnings. Last night I was woken at 10:20pm with a warning that rain was detected and could result in a debris flow. Then at 10:50pm I was awoken agin to receive the same warning in Spanish. I don’t speak Spanish. It wasn’t even rainy at the time that I received this “emergency” warning. This possible debris flow had been discussed on the local news for two days thus I didn’t need to be woken twice on a week night with this information. I don’t understand why they can’t send the warning in English and Spanish at the same time.
I will be cancelling this service and don’t recommend it.

Developer Response ,

Cal, I apologize for the issues you have experienced thus far with the service. From you description it sounds as though you just may need to access your account and adjust a few settings with your subscriptions. Please feel free to contact our support team at support@everbridge.com and they can walk through your account settings and help you make any changes to ensure you have a better experience.

User-hating software

Was sent here by my county health department to register for alerts on CV19 vaccine distribution. I wasn’t told that I must download an app. The registration process is dismal, and NEVER was confirmed as being completed. I did every single thing that was asked of me, only to find that when I agreed to accept their terms of use (after acct/login info was entered), there was no further action of ANY kind available. The end results are just another ten minutes of my life wasted, another useless app loaded on my phone, my email info in the wind with a failed app developer, and now I’m another day further from inoculation (my wife is in a high risk category, and already inoculated).

At one point, I was able to determine from a map, that there is something health related which is happening approximately 1300 miles away, but I don’t know what it might be.

The county is probably going to regret sending me here when their offices open tomorrow morning. This was supposed to be a simple registration for vaccine availability alerts. Before the maker replies to this review with “you did it wrong”, I want to know where their help was when I was struggling to set this up! If setup help is available at all, it is obscure.

Kerry AK , 12/15/2020

Not as helpful as nixle texts

I admit I’ve only used it for three days, but the nixle texts that I’ve gotten have been much more helpful. This app opens to the map and requires that I allow it to know my location all the time in order for that map to be any use. I would rather not give it that permission. So I signed up for the alerts like I had via text. It still opens to the map, even though I’m not sharing my location. I am not getting the same alerts as I was on text, have had to use both text and app (how can I get a text for Anchorage Water, but not be able to find it anywhere in the app?). My employer uses everbridge; couldn’t find them on the app at all either. I gave up. Hoping texts will work again now that I deleted it.
