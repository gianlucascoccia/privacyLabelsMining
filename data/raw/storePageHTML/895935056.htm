Description

Master the art of smoking, from prep to plate, with the new and improved app from Masterbuilt. With custom timers and alerts, no detail is missed while monitoring your masterpiece. Whether you’re a novice or a backyard BBQ pro, you’ll enjoy the ability to operate and control features of your smoker from your smart device. Set the smoking temperature and time, monitor the internal meat temperature, and enjoy the process.

With the smoker in mind, the Masterbuilt app features over 250 delicious recipes to get you started on the path to mastery. With the shopping list feature, you won’t forget your favorite wood chips or key ingredients at the store. With an improved design and Bluetooth connectivity updates, creating signature dishes that become family traditions has never been easier.


FEATURES
• Control multiple Masterbuilt digital electric smokers with a single mobile device.
• Control the same Masterbuilt digital electric smoker from multiple devices.
• Craft a masterpiece with recipes from cookbook author and leader of Masterbuilt, John McLemore.
• Capture all the ingredients for your masterpiece with the integrated shopping list.

App needs a lot of work before it’s truly useful

I thankfully didn’t buy the device expecting this app to be fantastic, since as of this writing it has 1.7 stars. I can connect to the device consistently, and more or less see that it’s up to temperature, however no matter what I do the safe mode overlay appears for me. It’s annoying, but not a dealbreaker...yet.

What really merits the 2 star rating is the lack of utility. I had to create an account with a password to use a Bluetooth-connected device that as far as I can tell is for absolutely nothing but information acquisition (and doesn’t nicely hook into keychain so I can provide a secure password easily), the recipes are nice but it would be nicer to have the ability to favorite them rathe than search for them every time I open the app, and incorporating widgets would be fantastic so I don’t have to go into the app to check on my thermometers. Also, definitely fix the safe mode overlay, or provide better instruction on how to fix it. Pressing the connect button on the device definitely doesn’t remedy anything, the door is 100% closed, and the cover is off.

Levidt33 , 02/13/2021

This is so simple.

Not sure why there are so many negative reviews on this app! This is so awesome.

Using the control panel on the grill takes some manual reading to actually figure out what the readout is saying. But I downloaded the app, connected the app, after making an account the device immediately updated in 20 seconds. After the smoker reset, I just set the temp and time on the app and it set off an alarm once the fan pushed the heat quickly to 400 degrees. And set off another alarm once the timer was up. This thing is awesome!

It takes all of the guesswork of charcoal, and is way cooler than the automated world of pellet grills for 2000$, that will never match the flavor of charcoal. I have never enjoyed grilling before this unit; because you can easily under or over estimate the amount of charcoal and have to add or push charcoal around to avoid overcooking your meat. But the masterbuilt takes out that guess work. Plus it is the coolest grill in the neighborhood that the guys can’t help but grab a cold one and stand around.
