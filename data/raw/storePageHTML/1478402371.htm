Description

Ready for some fierce competition on the basketball court?

Would you like to be a basketball star? Webelinx basketball simulator has a new game mode for true all-star players - compete in leagues and play 1v1 duels to win incredible prizes!

10 BASKETBALL LEAGUES TO COMPETE IN

9 regional leagues + a worldwide basketball championship for the top basketball players!
A chance to win unique customization items in each league - climb to the top of the leaderboard and walk off with a special set, ball & mask
Improve your shooting skills, win 1vs1 basket battles and unlock all leagues
Special rewards include in-game cash, boosters, and customization items
Not happy with your performance? Replay the league, improve your ranking and become a basketball champion!

KEY FEATURES:

REALISTIC 3D environments - play street basketball and shoot 3-pointers on more than 25 unique basketball courts in major world cities
FULLY CUSTOMIZABLE PLAYER - you can customize your player’s appearance from head to toe: jerseys, sneakers, wraps, bands, masks, and more
CHALLENGING MISSIONS - each level comes with 3 missions you must complete to advance to the next level
DAY AND NIGHT MODE - once you pass all levels during the day, more challenges await in the night mode!
BOOSTERS, SETS, AND BONUSES - step up your game with booster items and shoot for that basket with confidence!
DAILY MISSIONS offer extra practice in shooting hoops, as well as more experience points and rewards!

Take a deep breath and shoot for the hoop - just like in real life, there’s a minute on the clock and you must make as many baskets as you can to win and become a basketball star! Once you feel the thrill of shooting hoops around the world, there’s no going back - you will be hooked on this competitive basketball game!

Download this fun and free basketball game by Webelinx NOW and let us know what you think!
