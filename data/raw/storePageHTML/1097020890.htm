Description

Anyone with a YouTube channel can create a live video event to share with the world without 1000 subscribers.

◆ Live Now enables live streaming to any RTMP server via standard H.264/AAC encoded video.

Other features include:

• Use of the front or back camera
• Encrypted streaming via the RTMPS protocol
• Low latency streaming
• White label app – no branding on streams
• 4k live streaming with bitrates up to 20 Mbits per second
• Dark mode
• Support watermark
• Video stabilizer
• AE/AF lock
• Show comment (Only support after connect with Youtube)
• Show viewer
• Custom video resolution, audio quality and bitrate
• Save video
• Auto reconnect after lost connection

◆ Please note using this app does not mean you can for instance broadcast concerts of your favorite bands without permission, so please respect the YouTube rules concerning to what you are and are not allowed to stream.
