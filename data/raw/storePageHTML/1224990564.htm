Description

TVO Remote is the easiest and fastest way to control your TiVo from your iPhone, iPad, or Apple Watch.

FEATURES
- Automatically discovers TiVo devices on your network
- Compatible with Series 3, Series 4 (Premiere), Series 5 (Roamio), Virgin V6, and Series 6 (Bolt and Bolt Vox) TiVos
- Easy step by step setup instructions
- Control your TiVo from your wrist by installing the Apple Watch app (included with purchase)
- View status of TiVos in your home (for example: see what channel your kid is currently watching)
- COMPLETE KEYBOARD. Supports all the special characters supported on TiVo like @, $, *, &, etc., to make logging into services and pasting passwords easy. Keyboard has built in controls above the keyboard to navigate your TiVo and delete text
- Bonus buttons (closed-captions on/off). More coming soon.
- iPad: Split View supports allows you to keep your remote on the screen while you use other apps (see screenshot)
- AD FREE, fully unlocked
- Supports telnet and RPC based communications. RPC is available for Series 4+
