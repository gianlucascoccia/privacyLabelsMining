Description

One of the best apps for Face AI photo editing. Face aging, gender swap, cartoon, celebrity lookalike and tons of aesthetic filters. No photo editing skill required.

Face Story is one of the best mobile apps in AI auto portrait photo editing. Combining of AI filters, backgrounds, effects, and other tools to make a seamless and photorealistic transformation in one tap.
No more spending hours edit every small detail in your photos.
The app uses the new improved AI face scanning, analysis and then using AI face changing algorithm to create a new look for you.

Our AI analysis and auto editing photo offers:
+ CARTOON face app: AI cartoon cam turn your portrait to a cool cartoon character. Every features of your face will be recreate into hand drawing and coloring.
+ GENDER SWAP: See what you'd look like as a different gender. Auto detect to add a beard or mustache, change your hair color and hairstyle, add volume to your hair, add slightly makeup, smooth wrinkles
+ AGE STIMULATION - FACE AGING: This is also a true "Aging Booth Camera" which allows time to change your face. More wrinkles, different hair colors, not the same skin complexion.
+ CELEBRITY face morph: Morph your selfie with your lookalike celebrity doppelganger
+ ARTISTIC filters: many aesthetic filters. You will never get bored with these filters.
+ FACE READING: explore more about your self.
+ HOROSCOPE: personal detail report from daily to full year.

Just take a photo or upload from your gallery, waiting for scanning and analysis running in a few seconds and get great the result.

• Works with photos taken with your device camera or from your photo gallery
• Auto-cropping using face detection
• Transformation process instantly
• Free to share to your friend via any social network account
• Easy to use with no edit photo skills
