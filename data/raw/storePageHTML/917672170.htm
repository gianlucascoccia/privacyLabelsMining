Description

WebOutLoud is the smartest text-to-speech web reader on iOS. Now with premium voices, offline capabilities, desktop integration, iCloud sync and Dark Mode, you can save articles or e-novels to read later, or listen to them one after another in a playlist.

No Longer Limited to Websites!

* Use WebOutLoud to listen to PDFs--it highlights words as it reads.
* Use WebOutLoud to read aloud any text on your iPhone or iPad.
* Scan documents with your camera with state-of-the-art text recognition.
* Organize your saved content into collections.

About WebOutLoud's Leading Website Reader:

It's simple! Just navigate to any web page using the in-app web browser and touch play. Web Out Loud will read web pages using the high quality text-to-speech synthesis built-in to every iOS device.

Reading requires way too much of your attention. Multitask like a champ! Listen to articles in your car or while you work.

* Reads any web page
* Highlights words as it reads
* Tell the app where to begin reading using Touch Assist, or enable the powerful Reader View to isolate readable content automatically.
* Dozens of supported languages, each with their own unique voice
* Chrome and mobile Safari browser integration

But how does it know what to read!?!? WebOutLoud uses Artificial Intelligence to read web pages to you Out Loud.
