Description

From policies and procedures to training and accreditation documentation and much more – PowerDMS gives you a single, secure way to manage, distribute and track your most important documents.

Over 4,000 organizations use PowerDMS as their policy and procedure software to increase trust and efficiency by simplifying the way they share crucial information and handle their policy management.

The PowerDMS mobile app is the perfect companion for your team members on the go. They can access all of your policies and procedures from an iPhone or iPad anywhere there is an internet connection.

With PowerDMS on mobile, you can:

• Quickly access critical documents on an iPhone or iPad.
• Electronic signature with a simple swipe or fingerprint.
• Full-text search of all documents.
• Replace paper files and manuals with secure cloud storage.

An active PowerDMS license is required to access the PowerDMS app.
