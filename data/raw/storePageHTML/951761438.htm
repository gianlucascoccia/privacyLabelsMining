Description

This is unofficial app. The purpose of this app is let Pokemon fans enjoy wallpaper. Images are copyrighted of their respective owner. No copyright infringement intended.

This App bring to you beautiful Pokemon art wallpapers in HD resolution.
It make your iPhone beautiful, special and unique.
Don't miss this app if you love Pokemon !
Features:
- Pull down to load more wallpapers.
- Save wallpapers to your photo gallery.
- Share wallpapers via Facebook, Twitter, Google+...
All wallpapers are optimized for better definition.
Internet connection is required while using this app to load image from server.
