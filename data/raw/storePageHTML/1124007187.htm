Description

Why buy or lease a new car the old way? Rodo makes it fast and easy to buy or lease new cars from local dealerships, all from the comfort of your home. Complete the transaction in a few easy steps directly from your phone or computer.

Shopping for a new car is hard - visiting multiple dealerships, haggling over price, then driving home without even knowing if you got the best deal. At Rodo, we think the experience should be worthy of your new car, and now it is.

Download the app, provide some basic information, and we’ll show you personalized pricing on vehicles in your area. Search our live inventory of hundreds of thousands of cars and complete your order in just three easy steps. Place an order, snap a few photos of your documents, and schedule a convenient, contact-free delivery!

Rodo currently serves specific areas in AL, AR, AZ, CA, CO, CT, DC, DE, FL, GA, KY, IL, IN, LA, MA, ME, MD, MO, MS, NH, NJ, NV, NY, OH, PA, RI, TN, TX, VA, VT, WI and WV. Stay tuned - we are constantly adding new markets.

EASY REGISTRATION
Share a few basic details so we can run a soft credit inquiry, all in seconds on the app. We’ll then provide you personalized pricing that already includes taxes, discounts, rebates and incentives, so the price you see is the price you pay.

SEE CARS FROM DEALERSHIPS IN YOUR AREA
View our extensive, real-time inventory of cars and trucks by make and model, or filter by vehicle specifications. With thousands of dealer partners on Rodo, we have the perfect vehicle to fit your lifestyle.

360° VEHICLE TOURS
Explore 360° closeup views of the exterior, and panoramic views of the interior of your new vehicle. It’s like having your own personal dealership in your back pocket!

GET THE BEST PRICE
We've taken care of applying all rebates, incentives, and discounts. Just set your lease or loan term, and place an order.

LOCK IT IN
We make the ordering experience quick, easy, and secure. Our team of experts is here to help along the way.

CLAIM YOUR KEYS
Rodo has been doing contact-free order and delivery for years. We can help you get your car delivered the same day you order, or arrange for convenient pickup from the dealership. Drive it around the block to make sure everything is just as you expected.

Need help finding your next new car? Contact us at info@rodo.com

You can also make offers of your own and see if the dealer accepts them. The responses were prompt and clear.

The best part of my experience, that I was most impressed with, was when I called to speak with someone and was put in contact with Steven Lowe. He was absolutely amazing. He advocated for me with dealers to get me the price I wanted. He was knowledgeable, used all his resources, and searched multiple dealerships until he got what I wanted. He contacted me every single day to see how I was feeling and what I was thinking about the offers and how I wanted to proceed. Once the deal was made, he was extremely thorough in following through with all of the details with paperwork and returning my old lease. He guided me every step of the way. He had no connection whatsoever to my old lease but still cared and made sure I got it back safely and with as little charges as possible. I was so impressed with his care and concern. He is kind, honest, and capable. I will be coming back to him with all my cars in the future.

Pleasantly Surprised

Before actually using this app, I went on many forums and saw a lot of mixed reviews. Many said that “they’ll change the price on you last minute” and that “dealers use it to get you in the door” while that may be the case for a very small amount of Rodo users, that was the complete opposite of my experience. When you place an order you’ll be asked if you want Rodo concierge or not. Definitely go with the concierge. This is basically when a member of the Rodo team acts as the middle man between you and the dealer, and this helped me a lot. I started my order right when the US and NY went on lock down due to the covid-19 pandemic. My Rodo concierge was with me every step of the way and was alway there to answer questions or give me updates on the situation. The first dealer I chose actually did try to do a “bait and switch” on me and my concierge caught that and immediately let me know and found a new dealer for me with the same vehicle. I found a great deal on this app while scrolling through it, and everything was super legit and nothing was changed on me. The prices are made by the dealers, so whatever they may be is out of Rodos control. Also like any dealership, every month comes with new prices due to different deals, incentives, sales, and rebates so I f you keep searching with the app you’ll find what works for you. Overall the Rodo did well by me and I really appreciate them!
