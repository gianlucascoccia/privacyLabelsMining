Description

See instant replay of your own golf swing after the shot. Automatically create and print beautiful swing sequence photo. PGA show "Best Overall Product" Swing Profile is the only golf app that automatically captures and plays back your golf swing completely handsfree. No sensor required. Perfect for golf training, swing analysis and coaching. Download now and witness the world's Fastest Golf Swing Analyzer and Training Aid!
Swing Profile features include:
* Auto-Replay Golf Training - playback your golf swing in slow motion right after the shot so you can continuously refine your technique
* Auto Swing Detection - film your own golf swing hands-free on a mobile holder
* Auto Create Swing Sequence - like Golf Digest pull-out pages
* Auto Draw Swing Plane
* Auto Trim Video to just the 2-second swing motion
* Auto Synchronise any two golf swings
* Tools for golf schools and golf teams: Voice-over, High Speed Recording, Group Chat, Cloud Storage and more
* Opportunity to list your business in the Coach Directory to attract more students
* Already used by golf coaches worldwide for teaching

Awarded “Best Overall Product” and “Best Market Research” at the 2012 PGA Merchandise Show, SwingProfile golf swing analyzer, training aid and coaching is a must-have for golfers of all level.

Best golf swing analyzer

Downloaded just a few months ago and love the app. Swing Profile beats the other two that I tried hands down. Easy to use and automatic swing capture feature is extremely useful and allows you to take multiple swings without going back and forth to the phone! RRWFitness , 09/22/2020

Absolutely outstanding golf app that everyone should be using

Having been a Division 1 decathlete in college, I learned early on that practicing bad habits only makes it worse. I wanted eyes on me at all times to be sure I was practicing good habits. The same goes for golf, even more so. Not only do you have to learn what habits to avoid, you have to confirm that you definitely aren’t grooving those bad habits.

Swing Profile gives you the ability to easily film, review, compare, etc your swing. And miraculous, this app allows you to do that with no one’s help. It’s all automatic.

When I’m on the range, I simply clip my iphone to my bad and hit record. Swing Profile will record just your swing. I then just go back and hit the review button and see if I’m accomplishing what I want.

When I want to review my swing more in-depth, the app allows you to draw lines and circles and everything you need to see what your movements are. This is exactly what any good golf professional will do as well.

The history is also very useful. I can go back to past swings and see how I’ve progressed. Or even see particular moves I liked in the past that I’ve forgotten about.

We all know that quite often what we “feel” is not actually what is happening. We have to see it to be sure.

I highly recommend this app to anyone working on their swing.

Thank you for the glowing review. We're thrilled that Swing Profile app helped to improve your practice sessions.

Love The New Resolution Support

Unless I’m crazy and completely missed something before now, I love that SwingProfile on the iPad now supports vertical (portrait) swing filming. (IIRC, this was only supported on the iPhone before.)
