Description

At Alinma Bank, we value the importance of your time, and work continuously to bring convenience to your financial life. With the updated Alinma Bank application for smart devices, we provide you with a new set of features that allow you to securely conduct financial transactions and manage your accounts – anytime, anywhere:
Multiple services available from one single application:
· Financing calculator
· Branch and ATM locations
· List of current promotions/discounts/offers
· Currency exchange calculator
· Descriptions of bank products
· Alinma Virtual Cards

Gateway to all other Alinma applications
· Account opening application
· Alinma Token application
· Alinma Shariah Publications application
· Alinma Tadawul application

New Enhancements to the following services:
· List of accounts
· List of cards
· Account balances
· Account transactions
· Show/hide account balances in dashboard
· Account QR code sharing
· Ability to generate and send IBAN
· List of credit cards with payment information
· Alinma Traveler Card wallet details and fees
· Ability to add currencies to the Traveler Card currency wallet
· New card requests

What’s New

Feb 23, 2021

Version 1.1.9

As a continuation of the bank’s endeavor to provide the best digital services, an instant (SARIE) payment service has been added to transfer between local banks and its advantages are:
- The possibility of transferring without the need to add the beneficiary
- Immediate transfer around the clock and during vacations
- Ability to transfer by adding one of the identifiers (IBAN number, mobile number, email and ID number)
