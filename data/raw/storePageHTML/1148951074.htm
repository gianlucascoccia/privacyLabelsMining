Yoga Wave Review

This is a great app! I love how the classes are customized exactly to what you need to work on. I also love that you’re able to choose your level and length of the class. The only thing is that I wish was different was that it gave you the ability to type your own messages to your instructor. After you take a class they ask you how it went, like asking if it was too easy, good, or too hard. And then ask you how you feel. But they are automatic multiple choice messages (if that makes sense) i have been suffering from lower back pain and I saw the back pain class and thought, this will be perfect for me, but some of the poses increased my pain. I wish I could have told my instructor that, or that they’d have mortifications. Overall I love this app and would highly recommend it, and I have recommended it to a couple of friends already :)

Sounds robotic

Hi there!

Tried my first class today and I’m a beginner. I’ve been to many classes before, but still consider myself new to the practice because I haven’t been to a class that explains the small things when you form a certain position (like making sure to flex your feet to prevent knee damage). In any case, this class felt a bit robotic and rushed. I couldn’t center myself and find mental peace with any of the positions because it was literally all just back to back. Also, it ended to suddenly I didn’t even realize it. I love the personalized chat feature, but if you’re going to set the bar for that kind of environment, i would recommend that the classes are less robotic. Give the class some closure for sure. Happy Tweaking! 🙏🏻

LaMonicaT , 08/02/2019

Meh...

I’ve had the app for almost 2weeks and I deleted it today. Great concept in theory, but poor application in MY opinion. I’m not new to the yoga world, however, I’d still consider myself a beginner since I don’t do it often. Which is why I downloaded the app...to help me do it more regularly and at my own pace.

The videos/instructions go waaaay too fast. Even after doing it on and off for almost 2 weeks (was trying to give myself a chance to figure it out), there’s just not enough time to keep up with the instructors. I wish they could hold the poses a lot longer to give you time to look and follow along with them. By the time I’m on lose 4, the instructor is already on post 7!!! It can be overwhelming for a beginner and emotionally make you feel like you can’t do it-which is not true!!!! It’s just too fast of a pace if you’re just starting out...
