Description

Walk your way from poverty to wealth in this career simulator! Download HIT THE BANK: Life simulator game right now and make all your dreams come true!

Do you want to feel yourself in the shoes of a big businessman, the head of a corporation with a turnover of billions of dollars? Then HIT THE BANK is the game you have been waiting for so long! With this business simulator, you'll get a whole second life: find your first job, work hard to effectively organize your time and get promoted. Long story short, you will do everything to climb up the career ladder and become a millionaire.

And you will start reaping the benefits. You’ll get to know a luxurious lifestyle, start spending money on expensive cars, houses and helicopters, fashionable suits. And then, realizing that this is not the main thing in life, you will find a girl and build a perfect relationship with her.

In this game you will:
-FIGHT YOUR WAY OUT OF THE POVERTY LINE AND BECOME A PRESIDENT OF YOUR OWN BANK.
-FINISH YOUR STUDIES TO GET A MORE PRESTIGIOUS JOB
-LIVE LIFE OF A BILLIONAIRE: BUY LUXURIOUS CARS, CLOTHES AND HOUSES
-FIND YOUR WAY TO RELAXATION: PLAY AND WIN IN A CASINO SIMULATOR
-ADOPT PETS, COMMUNICATE WITH FRIENDS, WATCH YOUR HEALTH AND MOOD - LIVE A REAL LIFE.

Download HIT THE BANK life simulation game right now for free and make all your dreams come true!
