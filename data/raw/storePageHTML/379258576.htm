Description

There was a time when going head to head meant you had to draw quick to stay alive. Now you have to react quick to stay competitive. JEGS Perfect Start is a reaction time game that keeps you at your fastest. See if you can beat your best time or challenge a friend to beat your time. Try Pro or Sportsman mode, set your Reaction Time Delay and see how fast you really are; go for a Perfect Start (.000)!

Whether you are a racer looking for a competitive edge or a fan looking
to catch a glimpse grab this App...

Ready, set, go!

Features:
* Sportsman/Full practice Tree (.500)
* Pro practice tree settings (.400)
* Adjustable Reaction Time Delay to simulate vehicle

Try Pro or Sportsman
mode, set your Reaction Time Delay and see how fast you really are; go
for a Perfect Start (.000)!

Practice your Perfect Start. Hold the JEGS button down until the tree begins to cycle. Release the button to clock your time.

Adjust your Roll Out/Reaction Time Delay - Press options and dial in your desired time (0.000 - 1.500).
