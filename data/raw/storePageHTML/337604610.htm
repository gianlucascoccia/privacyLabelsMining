Description

Channel 2 Action News App delivers coverage you can count on in North Georgia. Explore important local stories that impact you and your community with 24/7 coverage from the major news of the day to local investigations, on-demand videos, and live newscast stream.

The Channel 2 Action News App is your guide to:

• Important local stories from Cobb, Fulton, Dekalb, Gwinnett, Clayton, Cherokee, Barrow, Bartow, Carroll, Clayton, Douglas, Fayette, Forsyth, Hall, Henry, Coweta, Spalding, Newton, Paulding, Rockdale, and Walton counties.
• Getting where you need to go on time with live drive times in and out of the Atlanta Metro Area.
• Tracking storms minute-by-minute with StormTracker 2 HD radar and 24/7 access to the 5-day forecast so you know how the weather will impact your day.
• Local sports coverage of the Atlanta Falcons, Atlanta Braves, Atlanta Hawks, Atlanta United, and more.
• The latest national and world news and how it impacts Georgia.
• Engaging content that you can share easily from the app to Twitter and Facebook.

Get accessible coverage that keeps you up-to-date with WSB-TV Channel 2 News.

Continued use of GPS running in the background can dramatically decrease battery life.
