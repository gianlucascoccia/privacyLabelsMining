Description

Main features:

Get a dedicated business phone line for your existing iPhone. Share your number with leads and customers directly or post it on your website or social media page—your personal number stays private and you’ll know if an incoming call is for business.

-Choose your own local number or get a customized number like 555-4MY-HOME so it's memorable for you and your clients.

-Automatically send a message with auto-replies when you miss a call so you never miss out on following up with a lead or an important client.

-Snooze and Nightly-Snooze allow you to pause call and message notifications while auto-replies allow you to stay connected to your leads and clients while you focus on family or work.

-Set up custom voicemail greetings so your voicemail is specific to your business.

-Voicemails are automatically transcribed to save you time and allow you to quickly respond.

-“Star” your most important text messages to find them later without digging through hundreds of texts.

-Keep your messages organized by marking as unread or even create a task from a message.

-Caller ID will show you it's a business call every time so you answer like a pro.





View and manage all your business contacts through the app so you can keep them separate from your personal contacts.

-You can scan business cards which will be automatically transcribed and added as a contact in the app.

-Manually add your contacts or import them directly from your device.

-View your customer details, notes, tasks, call history, messages, and more in one organized contact record so you don’t get caught without the info you need prior to a meeting or a call.

-View your Appointments or book appointments directly through the app so you can manage your time on the go (For users of Keap Lite, Keap Pro or Keap Max editions only)

-View, edit, create and send invoices allowing you to request payment on the go.


Second phone line feature available in the U.S. and Canada only.
