Description

Try the experience of a powerful but at the same time weave tool, a real Anemometer directly to your device.
With an innovative design, inspired by the instruments of the aircraft, this app combines the concept of the wind with that of the compass resulting in ultimate speed and wind direction integrated in the compass.

Wherever you are, Anemometer gives you the direction and the wind speed.
Search new positions and will be automatically added to your favorites.

Make sure you turn on location services on your device, at which point the App will detect your location.

[Features]
- Real time wind speed in any place
- Wind Direction
- Compass
- Choose from 5 units: knots, mph, km/h, m/s or beaufort
- Map current position
- Search Cities
- Save favorite places (without limitation)
- Intuitive Graphics
- Ease of Use
