Description

Create some monster-sized mayhem with Monster Truck Kit!

BUILD A TRACK
Select a stadium and then build a track, add some ramps for air time.  
Choose from a selection of big-wheeled smashing machines. King Crusher, Dino Truck, Ninja Van and many more.
Add multiple trucks for maximum carnage!

CREATE MAYHEM
Build a figure of eight track and watch your trucks smash into each other. Add some mud pits to get your trucks dirty!

CRUSH, SMASH AND DESTROY
Add some cars to crush under your wheels, some vehicles to smash through or find out what happens if your truck lands on a gas tank truck!

BECOME A MONSTER TRUCK DRIVER
In driver mode you become a monster truck driver, take to the sky and perform insane stunts to drive the crowd wild.

TV MODE
Record exciting videos of your trucks performing amazing stunts.

FEATURES
+ Track tiles snap together to make tracks
+ Lots of ramps to choose from, add as many as you want
+ Fun, themed monster trucks... Ninjas Van, King Crusher and many more
+ Crushable cars, smashable vehicles and exploding trucks to crash into
+ Truck driver view, drive your truck around the track you made!
+ TV mode allows you to create exciting stunt videos

ABOUT OUR APPS
Our range of Kit apps are designed to allow children to create fun immersive worlds to explore. We focus on open-ended creative play with no rules. This enables a stress-free gaming experience. We avoid distractions such as in app purchases, confusing settings and social media links.

PRIVACY
We do not collect user data within our apps
