Description

Payanywhere Point of Sale (POS) app and credit card readers are the business credit card payment solutions you need to transform your business’s payments.

Manage all your business and customer payments with the Payanywhere point of sale payment app. From farmers markets to coffee shops to mom & pop shops, Payanywhere POS app has got you and your customers covered! Easily accept debit and credit card payments with our mobile credit card readers, manage your inventory, send invoices, and generate receipts - all through our free POS mobile app for iOS.

With Payanywhere’s point of sale (POS) app and compatible card readers, your customers can swipe, insert, or tap their cards – no matter where you are! Your customer forget their wallet? Payanywhere’s 3-in-1 credit card reader accepts Apple Pay and other contactless payments.

The Payanywhere POS app and free online portal has everything you need to run your business successfully. Our point of sale (POS) solution makes accepting payments simple. Cash, credit cards, and contactless payments are all supported to help you serve your customers! Streamline your day-to-day operations and easily keep track of inventory, manage invoicing, view transaction reporting, and order additional credit card readers from wherever you do business.

Why Payanywhere point of sale (POS) app and compatible card readers?

- Your one stop credit card reader solution for on-the-go or Point of Sale (POS) register systems. Magstripe, chip, or contactless credit card payments are all accepted.
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, and AliPay — accepted all at competitive rates.
- Let customers easily pay with their phones with Apple Pay and other contactless payments.
- Manage invoicing — send invoices from the Payanywhere point of sale (POS) app. Allow your customers to set up autopay.
- Easily split payments, add tips, and accept signatures from your mobile point of sale (POS) system.
- Email, text, and print receipts.
- Create open tickets and tabs for food and drink orders.
- Keep track of stock with inventory management tools.
- Scan barcodes to add items to your inventory or cart.
