I want to give it 5 stars

Oh Growtopia, the many thing I have learned from you. I have played roughly 4 years and you have helped me through my most awkward time: middle school. Although there may be scammers and hackers, the friendships you make are the ones that will last! Don’t worry about WLS or stupid currency in the game, rather spend wls and get them moving! Donate to a new player! Help someone break their farmables for free! Seth and Hamumu gave a great basis to build off of, but sadly UBISOFT has let another customer down. The old GT days were filled with endless chats, farming, and building. As a more seasoned player I have three words of advice. 1: Mods will never help you out, nor emailing the makers of the games... it’s sad but true.
2: Don’t ask for donations. Be patient and farm and splice your way up in the world! Farming has gotten me almost all the DLS I have ever had!
3: Find good friends... the friends you make on GT will last forever, and will always be loved! My friends helped shape who I am today and I would be no where in life without them and GT! Thanks Christine and Nid. And most importantly thanks GT for giving me some of the most fun moments of my life!

TriciaDogPerson , 01/01/2018

Fun once you get used to it

Growtopia is definitely a game I love to play. The creators are always adding new updates and items which is awesome. I’ve made many friends from around the world through this game, it’s really a great community if you avoid dumb things like drop games and other obvious scamming events. My number one advice is to just be careful!! When trading, always take a few seconds to check the trade confirmation. Always avoid drop games no matter what. Don’t do anything suspicious. It’s that simple. And if you wanna be rich, don’t ask for donations. It’s ok to want to be rich but don’t bug other players. FARM! I know it sounds sad but if you want a head start, you might have to pay some money, then buy farmables and start farming. But your hard work will pay off. You don’t need to be rich anyways, you can talk to other players and play mini games if that’s your style. Growtopia is a game that takes a while to get the hang of, there are so many rules and little things that you need to know to really get the experience (and plus those rules keep Growtopia safe), but it’s fun. If you get scammed, it’s your own problem, not the creators’. I’ve been scammed at least 100wls worth of items in all my years of playing but trust me, it’s not that big of a deal, you’ll earn it back if you actually try. Just farm, it’s the only way you’ll earn your own riches.
