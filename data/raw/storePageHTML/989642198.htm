neblotr , 04/29/2020

Needs bank sync

If ever this app added bank sync it would be a top tier standard. As it is now it feels more like debit & credit, an app I used in college & about a year after graduating. It does train you to a certain mindset about expenses, but it’s easy to get behind in keeping track when all the entry is by hand. For an app that charges a $30/year sub I was surprised by the lack of web access bank connection. It’s effectively charging the cost of an app with actual AI built in (eg ability to set filters and predictive category assignment etc) but expecting the user to do all the data logging footwork.

I suppose it appeals to those without 3+ years of records to import, but given all entries seemingly need to be individually edited it’s not ideal for those not starting from square one.

Definitely love the concept, really really wanted to love it. Was ready to drop the $75/lifetime until I spent about 6 hours trying to fumble through all the data entry. Then realized I was working too hard to make an app work the way I wanted it to, which never works out in the long run.

Will keep my eye on it, definitely a promising developer team and concept.

lauragee33 , 09/25/2020

Good, but could be better

Got the app and paid for premium because it added some awesome features. However, upon starting to use the app, I found that you can’t split transactions into different categories. That’s a huge dealbreaker for me as most of the purchases I make are typically comprised of items from multiple different categories. I could go and make individual transactions for each portion of the full purchase, but I must be honest and say that’s a lot of extra work to create all of those transactions and it would just be faster for me to write it down at that point. It’d also be amazing to have the ability to link bank accounts. That’s always incredibly convenient. Overall great app, but missing some key features that I need in a budget/financial app.

**Also FYI the widget didn’t work which I was really sad about because that was what made me download the app. You guys have the most useful widgets in an finance app that I’ve seen.

Developer Response ,

Hi there. Thanks for your feedback. We are thinking about the split category feature, but in our testings it takes longer than just adding multiple transactions via Quick Entry for example. But we might consider adding it in the future. As for the widget, can you please get in touch with our support team so we can figure out what is going on as the widgets work? Let us know please!
