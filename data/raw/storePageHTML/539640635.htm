Description

Unlisted is your private second phone — in an app. Protect your personal life and free time. Add a second line for work, dating, anything!

An Unlisted phone number is perfect for posting in your online ad, using as a dedicated work number, or for giving out to students & parents (teachers LOVE Unlisted).

Add as many phone numbers as you need to your existing phone. Keep them as long as you want, or for as short as you want.

=> Here's how it works:
1. Tap to download Unlisted
2. Pick an Unlisted number in your area code (or anywhere). This will be your dedicated number, for your exclusive use.
3. Start your free trial. You can call or text from your new number as much as you want.
4. If you decide to continue, choose from our affordable plans (ranging up to $9.99/mo for unlimited)

You can delete the number, renew it, or add another at any time.

= OUR PROMISE TO YOU =
1. Nobody can use your Unlisted number to find you or your real phone number
2. We won't ever sell your data or show you ads
3. Unlisted calls & texts won't ever appear on your real phone bill

We at Unlisted Mobile rely on Unlisted numbers every day for our own needs. We promise to never sell your data, show you ads, or betray your privacy. Since 2012, customers have trusted us to provide them new phone numbers for all of their needs, and we hope you will too.

If you ever need help, or have something you'd like us to add to Unlisted, please send us an email at support@unlistedapp.com. We offer dedicated support and respond personally to each and every email.
