Description

Reelgood is here to help you find what to watch and where to watch it. We make streaming easier!

See everything that's on Netflix, Hulu, Prime Video, Disney+, HBO, and over 100 other subscription, TV everywhere, and free streaming services. Join the millions using Reelgood for searching, browsing, watching, and tracking TV shows and movies across all their streaming services in one app

How It Works:
- Select your streaming services from the over 50 available on Reelgood
- Browse and search everything you can watch in one app.
- Can't decide? Let recommendations, curated collections, IMDb scores and Reelgood scores guide you
- Track your TV shows and add movies you want to watch
- Let Reelgood keep you updated with alerts when new episodes are available and movies are added to your services
- Get all the info: ratings, trailers, genres, cast & crew, summaries and more
- See what's free to watch online across over 90 streaming services with free to stream options
- Browse what's new on Netflix, Hulu, Prime Video, HBO and others
- Try out Roulette for something random to watch.

Great with a Couple Suggestions

This app does most of exactly what I was looking for in a TV/Movie organizing way. There are a few improvements I would like to see. 1) The app consistently kicks me out if I use it for more than a few minutes, at least it has saved everything I’ve done to that point. 2) Shows that I’ve seen and no longer tracking need to go in a separate place. Most of the suggestions were things I had already seen and I had marked as such. This way if I want to see them again I know where to find them without them clogging my suggestions. 3) In the summary section maybe include a little more information. Can’t really get a sense of some descriptions maybe the top cast. FYI The Odd Couple original and newer version episodes are smushed together. Some dates are wrong but I know y’all are working on that. Keep up the good work!

IronicPants , 05/03/2020

Life changing

If you have a Roku, ReelGood will change your life.

No more wasting time hunting for something to watch or where to watch something. I spent 1 hour adding TV shows and movies to my watch list and now I’ve got enough to watch for at least a year and I’ll save god knows how many hours over that time not wasting my time looking for something to watch every time I turn on the TV.

With ReelGood I know if something is available on any of my services for free. I don’t have to hunt around in each of my services to see if it’s available. And, ReelGood will notify me if anything in my watch list becomes available for free on any of my services.

By far, though, the best best best part of ReelGood is that I can tap on something to watch in the app and it will just start playing on my Roku. I don’t have to go the channel on my Roku and search for the thing I want to watch. Instead, ReelGood launches the channel and starts playing it on my Roku for me.

My only nitpick is that I wish I could set defaults to get something playing on my bedroom Roku with one tap. We have 4 Rokus in the house so it asks me each time which Roku I want to play something on. 99.9% of the time I’m watching on the bedroom Roku. And, I’ll never want to play something on my daughter’s Roku in her bedroom. Also, if I tap to watch something on HBO it asks which version of HBO we have every time (HBO Go, HBO Now, Etc.). I don’t really care. We have HBO Now so just play it there.
