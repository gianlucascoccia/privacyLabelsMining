MrLibraryMan , 10/20/2019

Not ready for prime time

After experiencing the use of the app on the ship and after visiting the Princess “Navigators” (the cruise line’s tech support), it became apparent that this app along with the companion app, Ocean Compass, are absolutely not stable. The app, without warning, would crash and/or have me login at least multiple times in an effort to keep trying to make it a wonderful passenger tool. That in itself was a major frustration. And what is really frustrating is that both apps drain battery power at a much higher percentage than any other app I have on my iPhone 7. 20% battery usage for MedallionClass and 44% for OceanCompass after just 2 ½ hours of use and/or running in the background when not being used. Also, depending on where I am on the ship (such as Princess Theatre), the search for friends onboard would just fail. Somehow I get the feeling that the developers did not beta test these two apps.

Developer Response ,

We appreciate feedback from all of our guests, and we are sorry to hear about your experience with MedallionClass. We’d like to learn a bit more about the problems you encountered, to help improve the experience for others.
Please contact askoceanmedallion@carnival.com at your convenience to start a dialog.
