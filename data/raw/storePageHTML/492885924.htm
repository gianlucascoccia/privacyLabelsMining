*** TOPICS COVERED ***
StudyPad has the best math apps aligned to common core standards with virtually infinite number of questions. This app covers following topics:
1. Place Value - Read and write numbers up to a million in various forms. Generalize place value understanding to multi digit whole numbers
2. Number Sense - Compare, Order and Round multi-digit whole numbers
3. Algebra - Work with factor and multiples, recognize prime and composite numbers, extend number patterns
4. Addition - Add multi-digit whole numbers using the standard algorithm
5. Subtraction - Subtract multi-digit whole numbers using the standard algorithm
6. Multiplication - Multiply up to four digit numbers by one digit numbers and multiply 2 two digit numbers
7. Division - Divide up to four digit numbers by one digit numbers including scenarios that lead to remainders
8. Fractions - Mixed numbers, general equivalence of fractions, addition and subtraction of like fractions, multiplication of fractions by whole numbers
9. Decimals - Read, write and compare decimals to the hundredths place including decimals greater than 1
10. Measurements - Multiply up to four digit numbers by one digit numbers and multiply 2 two digit numbers
11. Geometry - Lines, rays, line segments, angles, parallel and perpendicular lines, lines of symmetry
