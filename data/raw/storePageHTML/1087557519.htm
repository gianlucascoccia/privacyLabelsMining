Nikmo86 , 07/05/2018

Not Really PTW

I’ve seen a number of reviews stating that this is an unoriginal Pay-To-Win idle clicker game; this is simply not true. Yes, it’s greasy. Yes, you could easily spend a lot of money in-game. However, I’ve been playing since it came out over a year ago and have never spent a single penny on it, despite winning over 80% of the events in that time & am currently sitting on well over 2000 hash coins. I’ve also spent a ton of hash coins in events and have only earned them in-game (no promotional offers, no cash spent). If you’re spending money on this, you’re probably just not very good at strategizing. I would recommend checking out r/GreasyMoney on Reddit, it’s a great community with spreadsheets and strategy advice.
As to it being unoriginal. Again, not true. The devs have done a nice job incorporating actual quotes, scenes & a diverse character list straight from all 12 seasons of the show. In terms of format, it may seem unoriginal now but the truth is the devs pioneered this particular format and if you’ve seen it before it’s likely that whatever other apps you’ve seen it in actually copied it from this one. If you like the show and enjoy a good idle clicker game, don’t miss this one!

shxulasgcoasc , 06/17/2019

Played this for a long time but I think it’s time to stop

I really enjoyed this game for a while, but it is nearly impossible sometimes to get the rewards you want during events if you want to get one of the top tier rewards. For example, I really wanted that wedding dress costume for Candy but it was only a reward for the top 1-20 people in your crew during the event, I had already spent money to boost my cash production/automation. I was on vacation and just didn’t want to be bothered to keep clicking on the collect cash button, I would literally have to do it obsessively for hours to even stay in the 11-20 ranking range to get the dress. It just isn’t worth it for me in the long run to spend my hard earned money on a virtual game continuously, when I could pay money to buy a full game I enjoy. It isn’t worth my time either, to just sit there and click the collect cash button because I don’t want to spend my real money. I really enjoyed this game for a while but since they took away the option to download/play apps to get free hashcoins, it became harder to get them and harder for me to get a leg up in the events (now you have to do those surveys with all the stupid popups). It is a shame because I LOVE the design and the work put into coordinating these events...they are great and I always looked forward to them, but I just don’t see myself enjoying the game anymore. I had a good run with it tho, which is why I gave it 4 stars.
