Description

Sellzee Scout allows you to screen prospective products to sell on Amazon using criteria such as customer demand, profitability and supply, allowing more data-driven sourcing decisions.

See buying advice, target pricing, offers and key profitability, demand, and supply data at a quick visual glance so you can make smarter sourcing decisions.

Try for free with 20 free scans per week after connecting your MWS Auth Token.

SMART SCOUTING
* See a full profit breakdown including all Amazon costs with support for both FBA and FBM
* Find products using keyword or title search
* Supports bluetooth scanners at no additional cost
* Don't have a bluetooth scanner? Use our camera scanner on a timer so the camera automatically opens on a set interval.
* View the Keepa chart directly on the scouting screen!
* Quickly access Keepa, CamelCamelCamel and the full Amazon offers page
* Scout stealthy and hands free with the Sellzee Apple Watch app

SCALE YOUR SCOUTING WITH A TEAM
* Outsource your sourcing and grow your business faster. Invite additional users ("shoppers") all across the country to your team.
* Hide pricing and other sensitive data from shoppers - they only see when to buy
* Put your exact buying criteria in your shoppers' hands by remotely publishing your triggers and settings to their devices.
* Get detailed reports in your email of each shopper session. See their scans' profit, rank, fees, and much more!
* Get session digests with helpful data that summarizes each scouting session and helps you calculate how much to pay your shoppers!

REALISTIC PRICING
* Set your sell price just like your repricer - filtering comparable offers and setting your price relative to the comps
* Filter comparable offers by condition, fulfillment, and featured offers (Amazon, new buy box, and used buy box)
* Set your sell price differently depending your item's key metrics like sales rank, condition, and offer counts
* Turn on a ceiling price so you never assume your Used item can sell for more than New again!
* Use our out-of-the box target pricing and triggers (designed for book scouting) or create your own tailor made for any strategy

TRUSTWORTHY BUYING TRIGGERS
* Now you can finally put your scouting decisions on "auto-pilot" with flexible buying triggers like profit and ROI. Set different triggers depending on the item's sales rank and offer counts
* Top rank % tells you if your item is in the top 1%, 3% or 5% of its category without having to remember the # of items in each category
* Use our out-of-the box buying triggers (designed for book scouting) or create your own for another strategy
