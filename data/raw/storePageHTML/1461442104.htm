Description

FF is the abbreviation of “Find Family”, Family is very important to everyone.

If you want to find your friends, family or phone, then don't worry, "FF" will help you.

No need to send text messages asking "when will you be home?" The "FF" will tell you. When you try to find your friends and family, all you need to do is to quickly join the FF circle.

"FF" can make it easier for you to communicate with the one you loved and help you find out where he is quickly.

You can exchange location with important people like good friends and family members, and you can check their location. This can help you meet friends and family in the real world.

With FF you will:
- View the realtime location of friends or family members on a private map .
- Create your own circles, share location with important people, and connect with your family.
- Find your friends and family quickly when they get lost.
- Help you gather with your friends or family when you need to play together.
- Receive realtime alerts when the phones of your friends and family are running out of power.

"FF" uses GPS positioning system, it will help you locate your family in real time. Of course, you should know these characteristics:
1. We pay great attention to privacy
Location is completely private and only the people in the same circle can see the location with each other.

2. Safety is always the first
With "FF", you won't be worried about the battery of your phone,when you travel in strange place . Your friends and family will know the last position of you before your phone turn off, which allows them to find you according to the navigation.

3. Warm and private circle
You can form your own circle with family, friends, and loved ones. This is your private circle. It's fun, isn't it?

Warm Tip:
Functions that require permanent locations:

-Share location temporarily or permanently: In our app, when a user shares his location, his friends can start following his location immediately and easily share their locations back. If the user do not want to be followed，he can hide his location ,too. So for best location accuracy performance, our product "FF" requires support for permanent locations.

-Locate friends and family: A user may locate his friends or family in the circle at any time, without permanent locations, the location we provided will be inaccurate. It will disturb the user.
Disclaimer: Continued use of GPS running in the background can dramatically decrease battery life.We are doing our best to optimize.
