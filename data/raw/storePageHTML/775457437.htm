Description

Hey there Vegetarians! We've been veg for two decades & we're so happy to help make others' lives easier. ▶This is a new & improved plan - let us know what you think!◀

Wouldn't it be great getting delicious and well rounded Vegetarian recipes every day of the week? Well that's exactly what you'll get with Vegetarian Meal Plans!

Ok so it won't actually make the recipes for you, but it can help you get started eating healthy and tasty vegetarian meals every day of the week.

Starting a vegetarian diet (or sticking to one) can be daunting for some. And, meal planning for the week can sometimes be impossible! With work, school, kids activities, and a social life to focus on, who's got time for all of this? Well, look no further because we've done it ALL for you!

Eating a vegetarian diet doesn't have to be difficult or boring. We're here to help make cooking and eating, more exciting and healthy. Everything you need to eat a vegetarian diet is right at your fingertips. This app is all you need to succeed on this diet. All recipes are healthy and have natural ingredients. You can eat nutritious meals and possibly lose weight at the same time!

Every week you'll get a new meal plan hand-picked by us, just for you. Meals include a daily breakfast, lunch, dinner and even a snack recipe. We’ve even added replacement recipes, in case something doesn’t fit your fancy. The recipes are endless and include a variety of vegetable, pasta, sandwich, soup, salad, stir-fry, tempeh and tofu ideas.

There's even a shopping list for your convenience, plus the app has been designed to work great on both the iPhone and the iPad.

If you choose to subscribe, you'll get 1 Free week of full access to all meal plans and recipes. If you continue to subscribe it's just a $9.99/month auto-renewable subscription plan until you cancel.

So make life easier and get the results you want with the Vegetarian Meal Plan app today!


And yes, we are a trusted developer following iTunes guidelines including the following:

- Payment will be charged to iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase
- Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period
- Account will be charged for renewal within 24-hours prior to the end of the current period, and identify the cost of the renewal
- Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user's Account Settings after purchase
- No cancellation of the current subscription is allowed during active subscription period
