Very very good

I’m teaching myself HTML and Javascript using Mimo. I’m about 80% of my way through this path, and I have to say Mimo is brilliant. I had previously taught myself Java and the Spring framework using textbooks mostly and was successful in becoming certified in both. So I can compare Mimo to that kind of self-learning, and I have to say it’s really good. Of course I’m doing it with some extensive background knowledge, so I can’t speak as someone totally green. But for me, the bite-size chunks, the requirement to only spend a few moments each day, the convenience of being able do do it on my ipad without setting up an IDE or any other software, and the thoughtful and gentle way in which they lead me through the content... this really works well for me, and I feel like I am laying a very solid foundation for further study in which I probably will have to do something a little more rigorous. What I think is most valuable about Mimo is that is helps me take a very big step (learning a coding language) in such a way that I don’t feel tempted to quit at some point due to difficulty. By the way, so far I’m only doing the free version, and the ads are not intrusive at all. Thank you again Mimo!
