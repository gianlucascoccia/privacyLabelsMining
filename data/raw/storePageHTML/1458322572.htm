Description

Add your own style and vibrancy, boost your natural beauty, or get a whole new look for your photos in just a few taps! You are Y-O-U-N-I-Q — show it!

Original Preset Collections of trendy effects & beauty tools! Create a perfect photo in seconds! All you need to do is select the palette of effects you like most, and here’s a perfect photo ready for you!

In the mood for an out-of-this-world surreal look? Youni got you covered! Don’t want to wear make-up? Having a lazy day? Youni got you covered! Up for a fun experiment? Not sure if a tan or changing hair color to green is a good idea? Youni got you covered!

►▻▹RETOUCH
- Edit your face, eyes, nose, lips, teeth, skin in a matter of seconds. Look your best in any photo!
- Smooth your skin, whiten your teeth, add a natural-looking pop of color to your lips, make your brows more prominent, and voilà: you look charming without any make-up!
- Wipe out the traces of tiredness and lack of sleep. Remove eye bags & dark circles and give your eyes a sparkle! Always look fresh and well-rested!
- See how you would look with a different eye color or enhance your natural one.

►▻▹PRESETS & EFFECTS
- Mystical, dreamy, fairytale and whatever you imagine! Set any mood with a whole range of double exposure, neon glow, fantasy & art presets.
- Add a mysterious double-vision effect with glitch-like Color Shift!
- Give your pictures a different feel, add some color, or just a nice aesthetic with stylish filters.
- Choose the intensity of tools — from light and airy to bright and saturated. Play it just the way you want.

►▻▹STYLE
- Don’t have time or desire to put on make-up? You don’t have to! Just take a photo and let Youni be your virtual make-up artist! Enjoy the process of transformation!
- Want to dye your hair but not sure which color suits you best? Use a set of our hairstyles to find out! Or just have fun trying on different looks!

►▻▹BACKGROUND
- Adjust the background to your liking. Zoom it in, blur it to bring yourself into focus, add a bokeh effect to make the photo more atmospheric, or make your portraits more dynamic with motion.

►▻▹AUTO-CORRECTION
- Too-hard-to-edit stuff out! No need to manually tweak the clarity, brightness, exposure, tint, etc. Just tap a magic auto-fix button to improve your pic automatically!

Please note: For better edits, choose a well-lit location while taking a photo and make sure the face is visible on it.
