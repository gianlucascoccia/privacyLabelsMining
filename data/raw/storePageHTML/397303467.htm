Description

The official app of Major League Soccer keeps you connected with the latest soccer news, schedules, match highlights, real-time video, live scores and standings all for FREE.

Choose your favorite club and customize your MLS app experience with easy access to all the match content, players and soccer news you care about.

Match Center brings the game to life with live scores, video timeline, live lineups and stat visualizations.

MLS Fantasy players can have their line-up at their fingertips within the app with an integrated design and gameplay.

Other highlights:

• Live scores for every official MLS match, Gold Cup, CONCACAF Champions League, U.S. Open Cup, Canadian Championship, Leagues Cup, Campeones Cup and many international soccer matches
• Free highlight videos and clips during games
• Chromecast support
• Real-time match lineups, stats, video & highlights for every game
• My MLS provides a single feed of matches, highlights and news, tailored to you
• Personalized notifications about your favorite teams and content series
• Easy access to breaking news, videos and analysis from North America's best soccer reporters
• Shop the latest gear from the MLS Store
• Manage your digital tickets (SeatGeek, Ticketmaster & AXS tickets only)

Hamish56 , 05/05/2018

Disappointing Redesign

I have been using the app since it first came out, and I loved all of it. Information was easy to find, game lineups posted as soon as they were given (before announced in stadium). The new design is fancier and prettier but far, far less useful. Some areas are far too touch sensitive - you accidentally open stories when you just try to scroll thru - or not nearly sensitive enough. Some areas are very difficult to open at times. For design, the redesign gets an A, for utility and ease of use, a D+.

For those lamenting about blackouts, it’s the broadcast rules. The games are controlled by the broadcasters (and that includes your local affiliate) for a period (usually 48 hours). That gives them the right to replay if they want. The reason local games are blacked out is because someone has paid for the right to broadcast and they deserve to get their money’s worth. With MLS Live (I can’t speak to ESPN+ because I haven’t tried it yet) I have never found a game I couldn’t watch or rewatch after the 48 hour window, even with my local team.
