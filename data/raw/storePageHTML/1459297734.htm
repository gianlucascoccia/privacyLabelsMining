Hgtapia , 06/05/2020

Great game, but be Leary!

Okay I can understand paying coins for wilds or more cards or reverting back one card to progress, it’s all about those micro transactions!! What I don’t understand is why you have an increasing amount of coin you have to pay for each level just to play the level... I enjoyed the game in the beginning and even purchased the ad free version when the levels were 1000 coins just to play. Not realizing that as you increase in levels the more you have to pay to play.. I am only at level 75 and have to pay 2000 coins now for each level. And I’m pretty sure as the levels increase so will the coins... please change this concept!! you already make enough money from coins on all the bonuses wilds, extra cards and reverting cards you don’t need to charge to play the level on top of that, or if you do just charge the 1000 coins for every level, your still getting some coin back!

Kwinkle1 , 02/29/2020

Spelling—-ugh

The game is fun and the graphics are pleasing. However, I have 2 main complaints—first the word “loose” and any forms of it means to set free. Lose means “not win.” In the daily challenges it says “without loosing.” It should be “without losing.” It’s irritating. Second, I am pretty good at this form of solitaire but after the first 35 levels or so the boards never are winners without using your coins for extra cards. I get it that you want people to buy more coins—but to blatantly force me to does not work for me. When I play a level and can predict, without a doubt, that I will have to use a couple thousand coins to finish the level it distracts from game play (and enjoyment) and doesn’t make me play more—in fact, it will cause me to stop playing

Developer Response ,

Thanks for bringing this to our attention! We'll make sure the game team knows so it can be fixed.
