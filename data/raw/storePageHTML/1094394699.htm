Description

Take your favorite spiritual teachers with you on the road with Spirit Stream - a stream player for iPhone.

Download Spirit Stream and listen to streams of your favorite topics - such as Money, Relationships, Health and Meditation - from your favorite spiritual teachers - Like Abraham Hicks, Bashar, Deepak Chopra, Eckhart Tolle, Louise Hay, Wayne Dyer and more - directly on your iPhone.

Create your own playlists of streams from searches.

Listen to your streams while commuting to work or relaxing on the beach without keeping your phone unlocked with Spirit Stream.

Keep track of newly uploaded streams and share your favorite streams with your friends and much more.

We have two subscription plans that give you full access to our app:

- A monthly subscription for $9.99 charged per month.

- A yearly subscription for $99.99 charged per year. This plan includes 14 days free trial where we do not charge you.

If you have not selected a plan after the 14 days free trial, you will not get charged. After the additional 14 days free trial from the yearly plan is over and you have not unsubscribed, we will charge your iTunes account the amount of your chosen plan. If you have set up auto-renewal on your phone, we will charge you automatically after end of each period in your plan.

Please follow this guide from Apple if you no longer want to subscribe to our app https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202039

- Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period 
- Account will be charged for renewal within 24-hours prior to the end of the current period, and identify the cost of the renewal
 - Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user's Account Settings after purchase 
Read more in our terms of service: http://spiritstream.dk/terms-service.html

We keep your privacy safe and keep your data only to improve our app. We do not give your information to any 3rd party.
Please read our policy to find out what we collect: http://spiritstream.dk/privacy-policy.html
