Description

SoSecure: Safety that’s mobile like you
SoSecure lets you take the safety and security of ADT with you on the go. SoSecure is a stand-alone, mobile safety and security app that can help you feel safe and protected in your day-to-day life. On a date, out for a run or wherever life takes you, there’s no longer a need for you to go it alone and compromise safety for convenience.

SoSecure features a free, monitored SOS function that lets you contact ADT if you’re feeling unsafe or have an emergency. It lets you use the safest, most appropriate communication method, whether that’s a voice call or a text.

SoSecure also lets you set up a “Guardians” list, which can include family members or friends, and we’ll send them a text with your location in the event of an emergency. And if you need help right away, we’ll alert first responders on your behalf, share your location and provide them any pertinent details for your protection.

TrackMe+: Do your thing without doing it alone
Walking to your car on your own? Headed out for a run or maybe going on that first date? Or maybe you have to regularly be in unfamiliar settings or around strangers. With TrackMe+, no matter where you go, there’s no need to go it alone.

TrackMe+ provides three safety features in one package – Activity Tracking and Live Monitoring Agent, with Voice Activation in an emergency.

• Activity Tracking lets you set a timer for any activity and invite ADT to track you via GPS for that period (up to 4 hours each). When you’re done, just stop the timer. If the activity tracking timer expires and we can’t confirm you’re safe, we’ll send emergency alerts to 911 and your Guardians. You also have the option to have your Guardians track you in real-time during your activity – even if they don’t have the app.

• TrackMe+ also gives you access to a Live Monitoring Agent, so you can have security by your side – virtually – for those times when things don’t feel right and you want to put someone on emergency alert in case something goes wrong. Think of Live Monitoring Agent as your ‘virtual companion’ who’ll stay connected with you via phone or text until you feel safe again. With live GPS tracking, the agent can get you help and protection quickly, if you need it.

• With Voice Activation, you can quickly alert ADT hands-free using your personalized secret phrase if you need to be discreet. Or, you can just say, “Hey Siri, start a SoSecure emergency.” Either way, it’s a fast, easy and safer way to call for help if you need it.

It’s a lot of extra peace of mind for just $3.99/month, or $39.99/year.
