Description

The challenge

Keeping up with conversations on the phone can be a challenge, especially when in background noise.

The solution

The Phonak myCall-to-Text app writes out what the person on the other end of the phone says, in real time. Communicate with anyone, anytime, anywhere and without worrying about missing out on the conversation.  

How it works

Just like subtitles on TV or at the movies, you can now read what you hear on your smart phone. It works optimally with the hand-free call capabilities of Phonak Marvel hearing aids.

------------------------------

Key highlights
• Live transcription of phone calls in more than 80 languages
• The other person can use any type of phone (mobile, office or fixed line) and does not need to install the app
• Save the transcription for later review


System requirements
• Active phone subscription
• Access to 3G/4G/5G or WiFi network 

How to start

• Download the myCall-to-Text app
• Install the app on your smart phone
• For the app to work, enter your mobile phone number
• You will get an SMS with a registration code
• Enter the registration code and you're all set!

Useful App for Hearing Impaired people with Speech Ability

This app is a great tool when it comes to speaking with people over the phone. In fact, this app helped me land a job since most of the interviews were through a phone with recruiter. Before this app, I had a tough time landing a job since it was difficult for me to speak with the recruiter unless it was an in-person interview.

Note to point, the live captioning is not perfect and some transcripts can get lost in the conversation. But I believe the developers are hard at work in closing those gaps. There are a few bugs like any other app. I am hoping for the best to come out of this so we can speak to people over the phone with more confidence.
