Description

Rated #1 and most downloaded Donuts Maker game. Download now for FREE

Here comes the best Donut Party! Tie on your apron and come fry up some warm, delicious donuts! Our friends are coming, Let's hurry up. Mix and bake your favorite flavor of donut, from chocolate to vanilla, strawberry and more. Wow~ Twist donut is available to make. Let's play.

Product Features:
- A super fun food theme cooking game
- Learn how to cook donuts in this entertaining cooking game
- Make all kinds of donuts step by step.
- Choose from various frostings, candies and glazes
- Be the best chef and food designer.
- Hold & enjoy the best Donut Party

How to play:
- Start the game by choosing a donut type. Pick normal donut or twist donut as you want.
- Add & mix all the ingredients by preparing the donut dough.
- Roll & cut the donut dough to fry. Do not let it burn~
- Decorate your donuts any way you like with candies, sprinkles, syrup, frosting and many more.
- Eat & serve to the friends. It will be the best donut party ever.
- Take a photo to show off your creations to your friends and family.
- You'll be the best donut baker ever!

I love the Doughnut Maker app. I got this app for my brother and I like it more than him!
