Description

Kid tested. Teacher approved.

Anywhere Teacher is the award-winning online learning tool your child deserves. Make mastering math, reading, and writing fun! Created by School Zone and designed by teachers to align to national curriculum standards, it has over 2,000 guided and self-directed activities. Just log in and go. Class is always in session anywhere, anytime! With so many ways to learn, including books, flash cards, games, videos, printables, interactive worksheets, a Play Zone, and more, your child will love spending time with Anywhere Teacher. Focused on kids ages 2-8, each child can learn and play at their own pace, on different devices at once, in a fun, safe and ad-free environment. Add our online program to your daily routine to build excited, confident, independent learners.

Sign up now and get 7 days free.
