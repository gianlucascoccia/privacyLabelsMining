Description

Scout Like a Pro and eliminate paper scouting reports using The Scout Hub app. Build your database of players, capture player videos, and objectively evaluate your players using customizable MLB scouting criteria, all from the palm of your hand.

Developed by former pro baseball players, coaches, scouts, and industry executives with over 40 years of combined experience, this app has been built by baseball people for baseball people.

Utilizing scouting criteria that MLB Scouts use to evaluate and draft players, The Scout Hub app combines old-school scouting with today’s cutting edge baseball technology to help you evaluate player’s mechanics, quantify your players, make better team decisions, and ultimately help your players and teams advance their game.

Whether you are a travel coach, private instructor, college coach, middle school coach, high school coach, or even a pro coach, this app will change the way you evaluate and recruit players and enhance your player development. Get started for free and put professional scouting technology in the palm of your hand!

……………………………………………………………………………………………………………….

Free Features:
CAPTURE VIDEO - capture player videos and attach it to your evaluations
DETAILED EVALUATING - evaluate in greater detail using the same 50+ criteria used by MLB Scouts
SAVE TIME - save time by going digital; no more writing up reports on paper and then transcribing to your computer
STAY ORGANIZED - stay organized and build your database of team players or players you want to recruit.
OBJECTIVE SCOUTING - each evaluation uses your grading inputs to calculate the player’s overall grade based on the position they play, which helps you more objectively compare player’s and make team decisions

Monthly Subscription Features:
REPORTS - see your player’s and team’s strengths and weaknesses and use that information to develop better practice plans and win more games
CUSTOMIZATION - customize which mechanics categories you want to grade and define your own averages for all metrics categories like Throwing Velocity, 60 yd dash, etc.
TECHNOLOGY - incorporate today’s cutting edge baseball technology like Blast, Diamond Kinetics, Pocket Radar, and more into your evaluations
COACH CONTENT - learn from Pro Scouts about scouting criteria and get better as a coach
PARTNER DEALS - gain access to The Scout Hub’s exclusive partner deals with brands like Under Armour, Blast, Pocket Radar, Infinitee, and more

IMPORTANT NOTE: This version of the app is designed for baseball coaches working with any level of player age and ability. We are already working on future development such as allowing multiple team coaches to collaborate together, allowing players to create an account, and allowing coaches to share their evaluations with their players if they wish to. Additionally, a softball version is in development with similar features and benefits.

I love this Scouting app- perfect for all coaches

I’ve been coaching for over 25 years and also scouted for 8 years. This app eliminates the paper scout forms and was built by Pro Scouts with the most advanced easy to use grading and evaluating system out there. Love how I can customize my scout cards to evaluate my your get teams and have another completely advanced scout card for my older teams. I have never used a scouting system that was this accurate in the grading of players like this one here and having the ability to generate reports on my players that I can filter and rank is amazing. I use the reports to help me identify the areas my players need work and helps me help them develop. Grading pitchers and players on this is really advanced for example you just don’t grade a pitcher out by their velocity or spin rate, this app allows you to grade the fastball and other pitches in more depth along with a hitter and their metrics in the swing. Love the way this grades and sorts players. Perfect app for the college coaches to recruit players based on what you think is average and perfect for the High School and travel ball coaches to evaluate their players and bios reports to help with building development programs to help them get better. Try the Bronze Free version but in all honesty the price is so affordable, I got the Gold version because I have a couple teams with one being an elite HS team. Great job Scout Hub, forward thinking and easy to use. Worth every dollar.
