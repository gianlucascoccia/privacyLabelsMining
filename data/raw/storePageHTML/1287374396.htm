Description

myLIBRO allows patrons and students to use voice & chat conversation to connect with libraries through Amazon’s Alexa and a mobile app. With myLIBRO, patrons can search the catalog, place holds, reserve & renew, check fines, download & play audiobooks on Overdrive. Patrons and library staff can also schedule and manage curbside pickups, passport appointments, printing services, and more.

Gep2002 , 09/30/2020

Useful app

I’ve generally had a positive experience using the app. It does need a few tweaks: there should be a quick and easy way to see your scheduled pickups. Instead you have to edit the existing scheduled pickups to see what you’ve set up. Or deal with the annoying assistant that speaks/chats - not ideal to have to go through FAQs.

The other thing is that there’s no easy way to consolidate 2+ pickups or indicate your preferences.
For example I was alerted about a new book then a day later I got an alert about a 2nd book. I didn’t want to schedule 2 separate pickups. The library staff figured out that I probably would want to do just one pickup and they put all the books together for me- but the app did not show that they had consolidated the books. I had no way to request that or indicate that I’d prefer fewer pickups if multiple books are ready within a short time.

Ipomoea , 08/11/2020

When will multiple system registration be available?

Many people have cards with multiple library systems in metropolitan areas, and MyLibro has been marketing to a ton of systems for curbside pickup. Unfortunately, despite spending time and money on marketing, they didn’t take this into account. I still can’t schedule for KCLS and SPL, so this is only 50% useful. Would like to see less defensive developer responses in the reviews and more actual implementation of this feature, which patrons need.

Developer Response ,

Version 2.5.13 does support multiple libraries. Login one library first and add multiple cards. If you are still not seeing the option, contact support team.
