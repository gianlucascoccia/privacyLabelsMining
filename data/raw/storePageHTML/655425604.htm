Description

Super Stocks is a free all-in-one stock market station, intelligent stocks portfolio tracker with comprehensive real-time alerts, and advanced options research.

ALL-IN-ONE MARKET STATION
-----------------------------
■ Live Advanced Stock Quotes (Pre & After hour)
■ Advanced Chart with dozens of Technical Indicators, Fibonacci lines, Moving Averages, Pivot Point, Price Bands, Overlay Bars
■ Multiple Watch-lists for Stock, Options, Futures, Forex
■ Price Alert & Strategic Alert based on TA Indicators Combination
■ Live News & Personalized Notes
■ Comprehensive Earnings & Dividend Calendar and history
■ Advanced IPO Calendar
■ Economic and Split Calendar
■ Market Index and Top Mover
■ Screening & Scanning for your own picks with Technical and Fundamental scanners & filters
■ Candle Pattern Based Scanner and Stock Report
■ Company Profile and SEC Filings (e.g. S-1, 8K, 10K, etc)
■ Analyst Rating
■ Extensive Financial Data & Key numbers
■ Insider Trading and Insider Transaction history
■ Cloud Sync on multiple devices
■ Touch ID protected app access

ADVANCED PORTFOLIO TRACKER
■ Manage Multiple portfolios
■ One Summary View shows all portfolios' balance, profit, & change
■ Live Tracking & Instant Reporting
■ Import Portfolio from US major brokers: Fidelity, E*Trade, TradeKing, TradeStation, Scottrade, Charles Schwab, OptionsHouse, Tradier
■ Support Stock, ETF, Mutual Fund, Options
■ Auto Refresh
■ Aggregated Portfolio News
■ Alert based on portfolio's value, gain / loss, performance, & stock events: Portfolio Summary Alert, News Alert, SEC Filing Alert, Insider Trading Alert, Earnings Alert, Dividend Alert, Top Mover Alert, Options Expiration Alert
■ Dividend Distribution & Reinvestment
■ Track Money Deposited / Withdrawn from Bank in Cash position
■ Well Guided setup of complex options strategy
■ Built-in Options Strategy Templates
■ Major Foreign Currency Supported
■ Performance Chart of 14 days
■ PDF Report of Portfolio Snapshot
■ Share portfolio securely through iMessage

ADVANCED OPTIONS
■ Unusual Options Activities Scanner and Alert
■ Live Scan of Big Money Options Activities
■ Top IV Scanner & Intraday IV Chart
■ Options Volume & OI in AR
■ Options Price Chart & Alert
■ Live Options Chain
■ Index Options supported
■ Extensive Options Greeks
■ Options Volume, OI, Max Pain Chart
■ Daily OI & Maximum Pain calculation
■ Interactive Strategy Payoff Diagram
■ Plan your options trading with the strategy simulator and do your analysis like playing game
■ Various Strategy Templates: Covered Call, Call Spread, Put Spread, Butterfly Spread, Straddle, Strangle, Calendar / Time Spread, Diagonal Spread, Iron Condor, Protective Collar, Covered Put, Married Put
■ Easy share to Facebook, Twitter, Weibo, and through iMessage
