Description

Do you really want to pass the CDL Test on your first attempt? Our CDL Practice Test prep questions are an awesome way to get ready for your written CDL test.

The Commercial Drivers License is a requirement if you want to drive school buses or tractor trailer commercial vehicles. You need to study in order to pass your tests and we are here to help you along with way. In some respects, you can call us your study buddy!
Preparing for your exam just takes commitment and time, as a matter of fact just spending 20 minutes a day on your CDL Prep work, increases your chances of success by 50%! Yes, that's right, just a small amount of time can help you along the way with your written cdl tests.

This app includes CDL practice tests (CDL Prep Tests) for the following categories:
• General Knowledge
• Hazardous Materials
• Passenger Vehicles
• Air Brake
• Combination Vehicles
• Doubles/ Triples Trailers
• Tanker Vehicles

Features:

• Over 800 questions to practice with.

• The questions are just like the actual CDL test, using official test questions as the basis for our practice tests.

• Immediate results: If you answer a question incorrectly, it will highlight the answer in red and allow you to try again.

• Immediate results: All of our practice tests not only randomize the questions so you never see the same questions in the same order, we also randomize the answers for these practice tests as well.

• No registration required

• This app is applicable for the following state exams: Alabama (AL), Alaska (AK), Arizona (AZ), Arkansas (AR), California (CA), Colorado (CO), Connecticut (CT), Delaware (DE), Florida (FL), Georgia (GA), Hawaii (HI), Idaho (ID), Illinois (IL), Indiana (IN), Iowa (IA), Kansas (KS), Kentucky (KY), Louisiana (LA), Maine (ME), Maryland (MD), Massachusetts (MA), Michigan (MI), Minnesota (MN), Mississippi (MS), Missouri (MO), Montana (MT), Nebraska (NE), Nevada (NV), New Hampshire (NH), New Jersey (NJ), New Mexico (NM), New York (NY), North Carolina (NC), North Dakota (ND), Ohio (OH), Oklahoma (OK), Oregon (OR), Pennsylvania (PA), Rhode Island (RI), South Carolina (SC), South Dakota (SD), Tennessee (TN), Texas (TX), Utah (UT), Vermont (VT), Virginia (VA), Washington (WA), West Virginia (WV), Wisconsin (WI), Wyoming (WY).

Our goal is to see that you not only pass your CDL Test on your first attempt, we also want you to succeed and as we all know, learning doesn't stop with getting your Commercial Drivers License. Rules change, laws are modified and best practices are improved over time. So, after you pass your exam, keep reviewing! It cannot hurt and will help you be a better driver.

We appreciate your support and look forward to hearing that you passed your CDL Test on that very first try!
