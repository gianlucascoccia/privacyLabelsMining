Description

Updated and ready for your 2021 fantasy baseball teams!

Manage your fantasy baseball lineups and dominate your leagues with My Playbook from FantasyPros.

My Playbook is a suite of powerful tools to help you make better decisions, manage your baseball lineups, and win your leagues!

Created by FantasyPros, the #1 fantasy sports advice and tools provider in the world, this app will let you import your team to get custom fantasy baseball news, rankings, and analysis.

Download now for iPhone and iPad for the ultimate edge.

---

What do you get with My Playbook?

*Custom Advice*
Import your team for custom news, rankings, and analysis.

*Breaking News and Updates*
Stay on top of the latest player news, notes, and updates.

*Accurate Player Projections*
Access daily, weekly, and season player projections.

*Quick Access to Stats & Analysis*
Timely research and reports from one convenient spot.

More Powerful Tools:

*Top Available*
Identify the top players to pick up in your league.

*Lineup Assistant*
See what your optimal lineup is each day/week.

*Waiver Assistant*
View suggested players to add and drop.

*Trade Assistant*
Find out if you should accept or reject a trade.

*League Analyzer*
See how your team stacks up against your league.

Beerad30 , 01/26/2019

App functions are fine but advice needs an upgrade

I had used for the final month of my season as I was given a discount to try it out. The waiver wire advice was awful. Kept telling me to drop Luis Castillo (who was solid) and add Hunter Pence who at the time was averaging 4 AB a week. All the recommendations would have made my team worse. The line up suggestions were just as bad. Please note this in points leagues I am referring too. The app it self works fine but the playbook in baseball simply needs some fine tuning. However no syncing issues, rosters up to date, player news up to date, for tracking purposes this app is great. Especially those in multiple leagues. Worth it if you plan in more than one league.

Simply the best!

While the logarithms seem to make some very odd (and very obviously wrong) choices from time to time, the overall information and advice they provide is second to none.

Coachbulldog , 05/07/2019

Do not waste your money

The “waiver assistant” function is not intuitive. I play in an NL only league and when I went to the screen to analyze a waiver wire pick up, the only players available to analyze are AL players. I contacted custom service and was told league settings cannot be changed on the app and I would have use the website. It is a shortcoming in the app that it is necessary to visit a website to modify basic settings. The “lineup assistant” function doesn’t work. In an email, Fantasypros acknowledges that it doesn’t work. It is frustrating to pay for a service only to be told what you’ve paid for doesn’t work. This app is slow to load and the data is not easy to interpret. Fantasypros received my $35 dollars but they shouldn’t get yours. There are better fantasy baseball apps that provide superior analysis and actually work as advertised.
