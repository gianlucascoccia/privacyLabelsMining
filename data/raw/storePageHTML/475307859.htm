Description

The perfect companion app for Sprout Social so you can manage your social media anywhere. You must have a Sprout Social account to use this app.

Sprout Social is a social media management and engagement platform that helps you manage your Twitter, Facebook, Google+ and LinkedIn profiles all in one place.

Your favorite Sprout features are now available on the go:
• See all of your messages in one place
• Easily reply, view conversation history, and assign a task from a message
• Quickly post, schedule, queue or draft a new message
• View retweets, clicks, shares, replies and more for individual posts
• Monitor and share rich content from your feeds
• Get notified of new social messages

Decent, save for a few issues

I love Sprout, I do. I’ve been using it for about 5 years and it’s a powerful tool. The app works great with an older iPhone, but if you’re using the app with an iPhone X, expect the keyboard to cover the area where you would be able to type a response. It doesn’t happen all the time but when it does it’s really frustrating.

I also wanted to share a Customer Care experience. Usually Sprout’s CC is amazing but we had a really weird experience yesterday. We had to reset our password like 5 times to be able to login (again, specific to the app). I don’t know exactly what happened but the agent who was helping us continued to say there was nothing he could do to help, and that we were typing the password incorrectly.

There is nothing more patronizing and frustrating to receive CC that assumes you and your 4 different employees with phones are all typing the password incorrectly when the login/pass worked all all of our MacBooks. When we pushed to log a bug, the agent said “there isn’t a history of these complaints so there was nothing I could do.”

After like 5 app uninstalls/reinstalls with all of us logged out, we finally got back into our account. I just wish that this agent would have tried to listen rather than assume we are all a bunch of idiots. Nothing worse than: “did you try to turn it off and back on again” when your whole team works in IT/tech.

Developer Response ,

We’re sorry to hear that we missed the mark in working with you, and that is definitely not reflective of the type of service we want to provide. Working in technical support, too, we would hate having that kind of experience. Can you please send us a Twitter DM to @sproutsupport, including your App Store username? We’d love to take a look, get your issue resolved if it isn’t already, and make this right.
