Description

A weather radar and rain forecast app. Track rain, snow, or any storm on the live radar map. RainViewer forecasts where the storm will be moving and notifies you of upcoming rain.

Gone are the days when you had to switch between apps, checking whether it’s going to rain in one, and seeing the weather forecast and temperature in another. RainViewer puts it all in one app.


WEATHER RADAR MAP
The weather radar map shows you where the storm is at the moment. You can track the whole path of the storm: how it moved for the past 48 hours and the forecast of where it will be moving the next 90 minutes.


FUTURE RADAR FORECAST
RainViewer is the only application that creates a future radar animation for 1000+ weather radars in original resolution using the latest forecasting techniques. A beautifully visualised radar animation gives you a clear picture of the sky above for the next 90 minutes.


PRECIPITATION CHART
Shows the intensity and amount of precipitation over the next hour in your area. Available for more than 90 countries, the precipitation chart visually displays when the rain starts and ends.


WEATHER WIDGETS
Check the weather radar map right from your home screen. Get updates on storm direction every 10 mins.


WORLDWIDE COVERAGE
RainViewer has the biggest weather radar coverage in the market. It’s available for 90 countries and shows a single map of all 1000+ doppler radars with the possibility to view info about every radar on the map.


WEATHER FORECAST
RainViewer displays a 48-hour and 14-day weather forecast on a beautifully streamlined graphic. Straightforward and simple, it highlights important data only: temperature and weather conditions.


FAVORITE LOCATIONS
Get weather reports for multiple locations: home, office, holiday destination or business trip. RainViewer’s weather reports are available for 90 countries. You can plan your travel outfits properly and check the weather conditions in your favorite locations from almost anywhere in the world.


RAIN ALERTS
Every time rain, snow, or any other precipitation is heading to your location, you will get a rain alert: “Rain starts in 23 mins and lasts 58 mins”. You can also set up in-radius notifications that keep you informed if rain is near any favorite location.


48-HOUR ARCHIVE
You can roll back in time to view precipitation data for the previous 48 hours of the composite map, and a single radar data.
