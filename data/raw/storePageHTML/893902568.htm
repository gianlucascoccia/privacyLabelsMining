Clima show you all weather information, just beautifully simple. A series of color bars can tell you at a glance exactly current temperature and weather, you can see much more information quickly with just a gesture, hourly forecast, daily forecast, precipitation chance, sunrise, sunset, cloudy, humidity, …

= Features =
• Simple gesture-based design that allows to focus.
• Change between Current weather, Hourly forecast and Daily forecast with just a Swipe.
• Tap in the screen show different information.
• Widget to get weather fast and easy.
• Works world wide. Supports both ºC and ºF.
• You can change between km/h, mph and m/s for Wind information.


Based upon Brisk, an idea of Eddie Lobanovskiy

Great, better than all the rest

This app presents the right amount of information, it doesn't get in the way. The themes are pretty but info is, first and foremost, center stage here. I can't stand Apple's weather app with all the kitchen sink clutter, so Clima was a breath of fresh air. Better than Weather Line which is irritating when trying to examine the high/low for the day (ugh) among other annoyances. I highly recommend replacing whatever app you currently use for weather info with Clima.

Kadrey , 06/07/2015

Beautiful and informative

The app is simple enough, but provides good weather data with fun and attractive animations. Small visual touches make this app a pleasure to use.

I do wish the notification settings were a little less granular. I don't want to set up rain notifications for each location and time; rather, give me the ability to get rain notifications for my current location, no matter the time.

I give this app four out of five for its beautiful design and awkward notification system.
