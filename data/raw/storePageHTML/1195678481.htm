Description

Kartoon Channel! is filled wall-to-wall with Fun, Family-Friendly, and Free shows for kids from ages 2-11.

You can feel safe with Kartoon Channel! because the entire app is COPPA compliant (Children’s Online Privacy Protection).

Your kids will love watching fun and enriching content like:
-NURSERY RHYMES and toddler shows such as “Baby Genius” and “Amber the Ambulance”

-ENRICHING & FUN shows like “Warren Buffet’s Secret Millionaires Club” and “Thomas Edison’s Secret Lab”

-LOTS OF LAUGHS for everyone with “Angry Birds” and “Om Nom”

-GAMING SHOWS like Minecraft "Journey to the End” and “Fairy Horse Quest”

We offer educational, entertaining content for preschoolers with fun lessons about colors, animals, shapes, and numbers set to toe-tapping songs and nursery rhymes. For older kids, our enriching, entertaining programming from well-known, recognized characters to rising stars designed to engage young minds with lessons including science, math, financial literacy, social navigation, and more. Curated and programmed by industry experts, parents can rest assured that their kids will always be enjoying SAFE and FUN content while watching Kartoon Channel!

Check out these great features:
-Thousands of episodes of free on-demand content perfect for kids of any age and updated regularly
-Stream video anywhere with a Cellular or Wi-Fi connection (your regular data fees may apply)
-Optimized for all versions of the iPhone/iPod Touch and iPad
-Kartoon Channel! is available on many other devices!
Visit www.kartoonchannel.com/where-to-watch to learn more

Download the app now to find out how much fun Kartoon Channel! channel is!
