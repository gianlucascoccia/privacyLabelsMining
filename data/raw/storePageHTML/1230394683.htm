Description

Ever wanted to remove something from a photo/video? Now you can. Photo Retouch can make objects disappear as if they were never there.
Very simple to use. Simply paint the items you want removed, and touch run erase.
Make fun pictures/videos, remove unwanted people, remove unsightly pimples, clean up the perfect shot.
