P to the G , 08/04/2020

Can Favor go from okay to amazing?

I’ve been running Favors for over a month and in general I am pleased with the experience. Tips are pretty good and runs are consistent enough, especially if you schedule your hours. There is one glaring problem however: runners are responsible for placing orders. Not only does this waste time as the orders are effective placed twice, but it creates problems when the customer has asked for something that is not available.

Here’s an example. A customer places an order for Outback Steak House in the Favor app. That order gets sent to me. I then call or go online to place the order with Outback Steak House. While on the phone with Outback they tell me that they don’t have baked potato soup. I hang up with Outback, call the customer to see what he wants instead, then call Outback and complete the order. Favor Fleet orders don’t have this problem and I think that feature should be expanded.

Delivery jobs are full of small hassles and this should not be one of them. Door Dash has engineered their way around. Favor, are you reading this? You should too.

cboswell87 , 12/07/2020

Way too stressful

I started running when the COVID-19 quarantine started bc I lost my job. I’m grateful to Favor as a way to earn money when I had no other way, but it is way too stressful. Unlike other food delivery apps, you can order anything on Favor, but about 85% of the time, what the customer wants is unavailable or out of stock. It takes forever to get in touch with the customers bc they’re rarely ever by their phone, and the customer’sorders don’t go straight to the restaurant, gas station, pharmacy, or store they want their product from. They just basically make a list of what they want, you the runner then have to call and place the order and/or drive and try to find everything they want. And nothing is completely streamlined all in one interface with the app. You get your order/run, navigation opens in another app, if the customer texts you it’s back in the runner app and you have to multitask with multiple apps open all at once and try to drive or focus on something else. It’s way too stressful, definitely needs to be made easier to use as a customer and runner. I prefer driving for UberEats
