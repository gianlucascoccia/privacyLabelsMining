Description

Burn & Earn with CardioCast! Work out with top coaches backed by hit music and get rewarded each month for building a healthy workout habit. CardioCast is the only audio fitness app that pays you back.**

CardioCast features cycling, elliptical, bootcamp, running, walking, jogging, and yoga classes. Work out from home with an indoor class on the bike, treadmill, or your living room carpet—or hit the streets or trails with an outdoor class.

Classes feature fun, music-driven intervals and easy to follow audio instructions delivered by some of the best coaches in fitness. Try it free for 7 days and discover why thousands of people call CardioCast their favorite way to work out.

A. Gunderson , 12/28/2019

Just as Good (If Not Better!) Than My Favorite Spin Studio

I fell in love with spin around this time a couple of years ago - mostly because a new studio opened near me and the vibe, rides, music and lights were all BOMB. I loved leaving dripping with sweat and would feel so exhaustedly accomplished at the end of class which was a nice change of pace from my regular bodybuilding and powerlifting style workouts. Cut to realizing that spending $200+ a month for a few classes a week just wasn’t feasible (would rather spend that money on traveling or pizza 🙃) and my husband and I bought a spin bike for our home gym. I’ve used it regularly, but not following a class or app. I tried the Peleton app for about a month but it was meh. Then, I found CycleCast just a couple of weeks ago and have been AMAZED at how great the classes are. I’m left feeling the same way I did when I would leave the actual studio - just as a sweaty, just as a proud, and the music, so far, has been fantastic. Thanks for putting out such an awesome app and letting us ride with you!

julied93 , 06/11/2020

Feels just like spin class at home!!

I recently got into spin class, but the drawback was how expensive it was for a monthly membership (around $250) and the commute & commitment to get to class. I stumbled across CardioCast while looking for a spin class alternative that I could use with my new exercise bike at home. Due to the pandemic, I could no longer go to the gym or spin class so I was desperate for another workout regime that would keep me motivated and on track. This app has been a LIFESAVER during these hard times! I look forward to my CardioCast spin workouts. They are very easy to follow, the music is great, and they are also very challenging and fun. I sweat even more than I did at spin class! I love all of the instructors (especially Shanda!!) and the music that keeps me on beat. I could not be happier with this app not only for the reasons listed above, but also for the low monthly price and its ability to keep me extremely motivated and engaged!

FelicityKC , 01/04/2019

Great App for spinning when you’re available

I love to do spin classes. It’s an amazing full body workout. However with my shift work of long hours, most classes take place when I’m either already at work or have to be driving there. I was thrilled when my husband bought me a spin bike for home and I then found this app that lets me do the classes on my own time frame. It’s perfect for my busy schedule. I also love the many options for length of the class. Due to asthma and recent surgery I haven’t been able to go to the gym and run on the treadmill like I enjoy so my body has gotten out of shape. Being able to start with a 20 minute ride and build my stamina back up has been so helpful. I do wish the classes had a visual where you can see the instructors but it’s not a deal breaker for me. I would recommend this app to anyone who loves spin and just can’t make to an in person class!
