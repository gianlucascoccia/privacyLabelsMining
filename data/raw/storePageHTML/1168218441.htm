Description

TaxCaddy makes gathering tax documents and sharing them with your tax professional a breeze. Let TaxCaddy retrieve your 1099s, 1098s and W-2s automatically. Upload or snap photos of your tax documents year-round and store them with bank vault security. Electronically sign your tax documents from anywhere. No more office visits. No more paper organizers.

The TaxCaddy app for iOS is the companion for TaxCaddy.com, a revolutionary solution that streamlines the way you collect your 1040 tax documents and share them with your tax professional.

Your TaxCaddy account is yours to keep, forever. You decide which tax professional to share your information with, and if you change accounting firms, you take your TaxCaddy account with you.

Gathering your documents year-round is easy. Link TaxCaddy to over 300 banks, brokerages, and payroll service providers to automatically fetch documents like 1099s, 1098s and W-2s the day they're issued. Photograph paper documents and upload them as you receive them. Categorize them on the spot then shred the paper. Browse your PC, Mac, tablet or mobile phone and upload PDF files, Excel spreadsheets or other files types.

Respond to your tax professional's document requests on your office PC or Mac. Use the TaxCaddy app for iOS to ask your tax professional a question while you're waiting to board your flight. View your tax return on your tablet from the comfort of your sofa. TaxCaddy enables you to collaborate, share and communicate with your tax professional from anywhere, on any device.

From your mobile phone, PC, Mac or tablet electronically sign engagement letters, consent requests, e-file authorizations and other documents. TaxCaddy's e-signature is 100% IRS compliant.

TaxCaddy was built with best-in-class services to be rock solid, safe and secure. TaxCaddy leverages Microsoft's Azure infrastructure to ensure data security and Amazon Web Services for secure, reliable storage. TaxCaddy is independently audited, tested and validated by C-Level Security, LLC and verified to use only the strictest security measures for safeguarding your data. TaxCaddy is used and trusted by some of the largest accounting firms in the world.

Finally, there's a solution that takes the headaches and hassle out of tax time. Your tax professional's work is complicated. Your part should not be. Simplify tax time with TaxCaddy.

PizzaFanatic247 , 03/31/2019

Modern Day Time Saver for ALL

This is my first year using the Tax Caddy app and it’s innovation at it’s finest for the tax industry! I’ve worked in tax and can appreciate the time saved by avoiding paperwork and having to coordinate, scan and save tax documents. Coordination of tax documents is streamlined, which brings so much value to the practice. I must say, I believe that the app is geared more toward the more modern day app-savvy customer in that you can take pictures or upload your tax documents easily, and having a pre-set document checklist better simplified the process. A general questionnaire, which is part of the normal industry practice, can fill in any holes one would believe would be caused by a program so slick. Plus, there is a feature that allows you to communicate with your tax preparer at any time during the preparation time of your tax return! The face-to-face aspect of interaction is not present, but with having an extremely busy lifestyle, with a million things on my plate, I can really appreciate that I can come and go into the app as I have time, and can deliver my documents without having to drive to the CPA firm or to the post office, on my time. One thing that I would like to see different is to have more notification from the app to get myself wrapped up with everything more quickly. Overall, this app is awesome and would recommend!!

Guido the Cat , 02/03/2021

Off to a Bad Start

My introduction to TaxCaddy was to fill out an on-line questionnaire on changes in the past year. Retirement. Should be easy. 62 questions, but many of them involve technical legal retirement program terms. Part way through, I wanted to save partial completion to check something (I could as easily have wanted to pause for dinner and complete it later); but there was no apparent way to partially save (and you cannot leave any question unanswered). The App was locked in the questionnaire until completion. Couple that apparent flaw with the statement at the end of it that once you “submit” your answers you can never change them. What if I made a mistake (or got new information) and wanted to revise an answer? Too bad, your error will be forever reflected in your tax return? Next, one of the “Questions” was a directive that if you have not already linked your bank account to this App, insert your account information here (or better yet upload a photo of a check). That’s an audacious assumption that I’d want to link my bank account to this App, especially at this awkward introductory part of getting used to the App. What additional irritating things await? If I wanted a do-it-yourself tax return, I’d buy TurboTax. I don’t like this.

Developer Response ,

Hello. Thank you for the detailed comments. The number of questions is controlled by your tax professional, not TaxCaddy. The TaxCaddy questionnaire does not need to completed all at once and it saves progress automatically so you can resume at any point. It sounds like your tax professional included a fillable PDF questionnaire. The TaxCaddy questionnaire should provide a better user experience. If you "made a mistake" your tax professional can always reset the questionnaire for you. Regarding the account linking, TaxCaddy's Smart Links feature is optional. We suggest people use the feature because it can save time but you're not required to use it.

If you need any assistance please don't hesitate to reach out to our support team: https://helpcenter.taxcaddy.com/hc/en-us/articles/226693168
