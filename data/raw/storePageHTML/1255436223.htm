Description

Go on a fun-filled shopping spree in Dr. Panda Town: Mall! With so many shops and other things to discover, there are endless stories to be told and adventures to be had.

EXPLORE THE MALL
Explore the entire mall with four huge floors to choose from! Check out the fire-breathing dinosaur in the toy store, try on the cool superhero costumes in the movie theater, or see if you can find all 30 hidden gift packages! Play fun games to earn rewards in the arcade, or play hide and seek in the indoor playground for even more fun! With surprises and fun around every corner, what will you discover?

CREATE YOUR OWN STORY
Be whoever you want to be as you tell your own stories! Become a hairstylist and run your own hair salon, or play as a veterinarian and take care of your furry friends. Dress up and put on a stage show in the toy store and even choose to get married. There's no limit to what you can do!

PLAY TOGETHER
Shop till you drop and play together on one device with your friends! Give each other a makeover in the hair salon and swap outfits in the clothes store. Visit the top floor and grab a bite to eat, or kick back with a movie before finishing up with the perfect selfie for a day out to remember!

KEY FEATURES:
● 8 unique locations to visit
● 4 floors with shops, a food court, a movie theater, arcade and more
● Choose from up to 30 characters to play with
● 15 adorable animals in the pet store
● 25 new outfits and costumes to dress up in
● Be a customer, hairstylist, veterinarian, actor & more
● Fun surprises & hidden secrets to uncover
● Supports multi-touch to play together
● Bring your characters’ outfits, hairstyles, pets, items and more between every app in the Dr. Panda Town series! Simply tap the orange map icon at the top of the screen to see all the areas you can visit. 
● Go on fun adventures & tell endless stories
● No third-party ads!

Additional areas in Dr. Panda Town: Mall can be added via one time in-app purchases. These are one-time purchases which will permanently unlock the content for your account. Please check your device settings for more detailed parental options regarding in-app purchases, and feel free to contact us at support@drpanda.com if you have feedback or concerns.
