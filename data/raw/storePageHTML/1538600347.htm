Description

It is designed to assist the user in reciting al-Quran al-Majeed in accordance with the rules of tajweed without having to learn them initially.

It will help in reading the entire Quran either in a week through manzil wise recitation, or in a month through Juz wise recitation

It will inspire the user to recite Quran daily, turning recitation into a lifetime habit.

By listening to the audio and following the text, the user will notice a gradual improvement in his/her tilawat as the app will reinforce correct articulation and pronunciation.

Finally, using this app will ease the process of Hifz (memorizing) al-Quran al-Majeed accurately and will also serve as a source for regular Muraja'at (revision).

Features:
• Tilawat of al-Quran – Manzil wise, Juz wise & Surah wise
• Tilawat of Adiyat Sharifa
• Ahkaam and Makharij videos
• Qibla Finder
• Salat Timings
