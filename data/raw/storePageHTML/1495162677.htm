Description

Auto Brawl Chess is an auto battler strategy RPG game, in the royal battle genre. Enjoy classic auto chess mechanics plus fast-paced 5-minute matches, ranked, and insane mode. On top of that, PvE and PvP mode, guild wars, dungeons, and a whole PvE campaign called “Journey”. The most unique and complete autochess game where strategy is a key, but luck also plays a role!

We explored the whole genre to bring to you all of the best features and mechanics all of the auto battles and much more of the new stuff from us:

● Quick matchmaking and Fast 5-Minute Battles;
● A variety of competitive modes: PvP mode, Ranked Clashes, Guild Wars, Battle Royale;
● A lore-rich campaign “Journey” with an epic story involving many different worlds;
● Numerous mechanics and idle quest system;
● High-quality polished visual style;
● 100+ fantasy heroes with their own storyline and a specific Faction and a Class
● Loads of hero combinations allowing you to build the strongest synergy set.

You can level up your heroes and craft/enhance their equipment outside the battle.

Join Auto Brawl Chess and prove yourself by unique gameplay, boost your Heroes, get rewarded just for playing the game. Complete the quests, join the battles and you will receive EXTRA bonuses from the CHESS PASS.
Good Luck, player, and try to win first place at ABC!

Note:
“Auto Brawl Chess: Battle Royale” is free to download and play, however, some game items can also be purchased for real money. If you do not want to use this feature, please set up password protection for purchases in the settings of your App Store app.

A network connection is required.

Serbow , 12/18/2020

Great game but...

This has to be one of the most addictive games I’ve ever played. It’s great for f2p players like me, as it’s easy to get heroes with just summoning and other stuff. The gameplay is just amazing and yes, it’s luck based but you also need some skill to find the best heroes possible. But, as all games, there’s a problem. Well, there’s 2 major problems. Firstly, the game crashes a lot, and when I say a lot, I mean a lot. Sometimes I crash twice every match. I was in a game that I was about to win and guess what? The game crashed, I lost the match and didn’t get any rewards. The other issue or problem is the matchmaking. I’ve been put up against players who have plenty legendary heroes at like level 10 meanwhile I have 1 at level 9. It’s just so unfair sometimes as if the best players gets into like a 3 winstreak, he will dominate the rest of the match with ease. If the devs ever fix one or both issues, I will rewrite this comment and give it 5*, as it’s one of the best games I’ve ever tried. Thanks for reading!

Ckdjdjf , 01/04/2021

No strategy, all luck

This game claims its about strategy when its not. Theres really no skill to it. Its all about luck. Certain characters are better then others so if your unlucky your opponent keeps getting good characters. This has happened to me quick a bit but ive also been on the other side where i demolish everyone. Theres also a button you can tap that will fix your lineup for you and give you the best chances possible. This option really takes the skill/strategy out of the game. In all honesty all you have to do to win get whatever characters cost more and hit the best line up button. If they dont switch anyone then everyone on your side line can go and hopefully you get more good people. This game is too simple/easy and doesnt require any attention to win. Overall the idea behind the game is nice. It reminds me of the magic chess on mobil legends, which i would say requires a little more skill/strategy but still heavily reliant on luck. If your just looking to pass time and dont want to really get too into what your playing than this is perfect. Also, if you've come to check out this game from the advertisements, then prepare to be disappointed. Its nothing like the ad. This game is F2P but can very easily be pay to win. I would recommend finding another game. Also, if your opponent has Mary then you lost. She has a massive AOE heal and she basically spams it. The most broken/OP character in the game. She really is unfair and needs a nerf.

Widukind27 , 02/19/2021

Has NOTHING to do with Strategy

This game has nothing to do with strategy. The available characters are given randomly and arbitrarily. So it’s more about luck than anything else. The determination of draws and victories also seem to be arbitrary. What factors are considered to decide who wins a draw? Is it based on the total number of skill points one has? Is it based on the number of characters still on the board? Seems inconsistent, so I’m never sure. Here’s an idea, if both players still have a character on the board, it’s always a draw...makes more sense, doesn’t it?

Perhaps the promise of winning through purchasing bonus packages may seem enticing, but I think we all know that a game that requires you to get upgrades to win and not rely on actual skill is a game that won’t last long. It’s skills that get better over time, the bonus packages can only go so far, unless they decide to offer a package that lets you automatically win. But then what’s the point? Buying your way to the top is not the same as developing your character line-up and your skills.

Saying this is a great game considering that it’s free is irrelevant. There are plenty of games that actually test skill and ability. Don’t be deceived by a game that lets you win sometimes just because the algorithm is in your favor. Let me download an actual chess game now...that at least tests my skills.
