Description

Trying to pick stocks is very challenging and time consuming. It requires spending lots of time analyzing charts, patterns, indicators, oscillators, and other statistical data to make a decision on whether to buy or sell a stock.

With Stockvisor, no longer do you have to spend hours analyzing stock data. A simple search of a stock, will tell you whether the stock is a buy, sell or hold based on the chart patterns, technical indicators, oscillators and other algorithmic patterns. It’s like having your own stock advisor in your pocket!

Features include:

* Stock Analysis, including technical analysis details + charts
* Weekly stock pick
* Stocks to watch for the upcoming week
* Alerts - create your own technical indicator alerts and get push notifications
