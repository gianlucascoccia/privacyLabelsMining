Description

Billy’s mother gave him a coin to spend on a toy at the zoo, but when the day came, it slipped down the drain, leaving the little boy blue. Now the coin is on an epic adventure at the zoo. From a chomping alligator to a bunch of crazy monkeys, Billy’s coin is going to meet all sorts of animals in this fun, interactive children’s story.

Follow the coin as it travels from animal to animal on it’s quest to return to Billy. This original, read-a-long story is sure to make your little animal lover smile.
