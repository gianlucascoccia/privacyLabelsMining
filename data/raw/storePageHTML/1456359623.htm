■ POWERFUL GLOBAL SERVERS WITH FAILOVER

We maintain industry leading ultra-fast VPN servers across the world with multiple failover mechanisms. Larva Dynamo technology detects when a VPN server IP is unreachable in a region and automatically creates a new server with a new IP within 120 seconds.

Larva allows you to browse the internet in private and maintains a no-log policy without exceptions, so you never have to worry about being monitored or tracked by our servers or anyone else.

Hello,

I apologise for the issue you experienced, it happens in a select set of circumstances.

If you turn on ‘on demand’ then your device will ALWAYS fire up the VPN any time a connection is requested to anywhere. This is what on-demand means and is there to protect you. It is also why when you are on cellular data it connects automatically. If you want manual on/off control then turn this option off.

The upgrade message is rare and we only ever need to trigger that when we make significant changes on our servers which force us to prevent the old version connecting. Because you had on demand on AND we had to force upgrade, every time the server disconnected with the upgrade message your app reconnected again.

This issue will be fixed in an update in a few days.

Regarding your final point, if you’re happy with the results there’s no need to wonder if it’s doing anything. That’s the whole point - it’s supposed to make it seamless for you. We don’t waste memory, cpu, storage, or time showing you stats you don’t really need. Larva is streamlined to do its job and do it fast and well, not as a PR machine to advertise numbers to you.

You’re always welcome to use another VPN but do remember when they spend so much effort showing you graphs and numbers what is that really for?

In any case of you ever have issues please send us a support email and you will get a response very quickly.

I hope that helps.
