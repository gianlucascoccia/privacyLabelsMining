Description

Looking for biker singles?
Download the Biker Planet app!
We are the premier motorcycle community on the web.

Looking for a biker dating app? Still trying to find that perfect motorcycle single to chat with? Join our community at Biker Planet! Download the Biker Planet app to meet single male and female riders from around the world.

The Biker Planet app focuses on mature biker singles looking for someone to date. Our community brings ALL riders together.

Here's how to get started:

1. Download the Biker Planet app

2. Create your profile to search single people in your area by using your email address, your Apple ID, or your Facebook account

3. Check out your matches feed. This will show you male and female bikers near you to meet.

4. Start something special. Choose a person you're interested in and send a message or a flirt.

The Biker Planet app also has additional features to find mature men and women to chat with:

- Rapid Match: Are you in a rush and only want to look at photos? Decide which singles you like here instantly by swiping right or left

- Search: Only want to date people near you? Use the Search feature to find matches based on specific criteria such as location and age. For example, you can search only for people who are over 30, over 40, over 50, over 60, or all senior bikers.

- Likes You: Want to know who likes you? See which member swiped ""yes"" on your profile here

- Who's Online: See who is currently online and chat instantly with them

Want to increase your chance of finding a perfect match? Upgrade to the Biker Planet premium plan and unlock these additional features:

1. Unlimited Messages and Unlimited Flirts: Don't let potential dates slip away because you ran out of messages or flirts

2. Who Viewed You: See who viewed your profile and is possibly interested in meeting or chatting

3. View All Photos: See all the photos of your matches

4. Advanced Search: Expand your search criteria to see what other interests your matches have. For example, you can see what their favorite motorcycle is or if they have pets.
