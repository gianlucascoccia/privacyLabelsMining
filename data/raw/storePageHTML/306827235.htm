Description

From the water bill to the car payment to the cell phone subscription, BillTracker for iPhone can help you stay on top of your bills.

Features of BillTracker for iPhone:
• All new design, now updated for iOS 9
• The new Upcoming screen makes it easy to keep tabs on what needs to be paid next
• With reminders, BillTracker can alert you to pay a bill before it's due
• BillTracker stores every bill and payment you add and then makes it easy to export your data in CSV format for importing into Excel, Numbers, or another finance app

We think BillTracker for iPhone is by far the best bill tracking app for your iPhone or iPod touch and we hope you agree. Of course we're always working hard to make it even better, so if you have any suggestions or run into a problem, let us know!

The only app I need....

I’ve been using this app for a very very long time. Of all the apps that are on my phone this is the only one that I couldn’t live without. It keeps us on track and informed and ensures that all of our bills are paid on time. It’s also great for being able to go back and see the trend of your bills and being able to say where/when/how you paid for a bill. I can’t tell you how many times I’ve had an issue with a bill and been able to go back and say it was paid on this date by this check number and have more information the recipient of the money. If I could give it 10 stars I would. It’s very simple, and easy to use. Every time I’ve gotten a new phone it has seamlessly transferred my data. I’ve never had an issue with it. 👍🏻👍🏻👍🏻

toytoy21 , 11/11/2016

Wonderful Bill Tracker

The app does exactly what it says. It tracks your bills and sends you reminders ahead of time. The thing about this app that sold me was the ability to view your due dates on an actual calendar. It helps to visualize which bills have been paid and which have not been paid. I wish the ability to see the bill that's due when you click on it, wasn't changed with the update. I really liked that feature. Now it goes to a whole new page. That is the only reason the fifth star is omitted. Other than that, it is a great app that has been a great help for years. Definitely worth it...

Megan5519 , 08/02/2019

Super Bill Tracker

I tried over a dozen other apps, and this is by far my favorite. Relatively user friendly in regards to adding your recurring monthly bills, which you can customize my number of weeks, monthly, quarterly, etc. The only reason it lost a star is because, many many moons ago, I had a free app that allowed you customize the view of the upcoming coming bills. For example, I receive a biweekly paycheck and like to pay all the bills due at the same time that have due dates prior to my next check. So to compensate for the lack of this feature, I added a bill titled “Pay Day” for 0.01 that repeats every 2 weeks. But it’s not as effective as knowing the amount of the bills due within that timeframe. Otherwise it’s a great app.
