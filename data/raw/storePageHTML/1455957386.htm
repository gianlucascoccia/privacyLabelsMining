Description

FUN Keyboard is a DIY keyboard which has dozens of colorful themes and trendy stickers. It is also having custom emoji stickers which can create avatar by yourself.

HIGHLIGHT FEATURES
- DIY your own keyboard with your favorite pictures.
- Creating personal memojis basic with your face.
- Easy to send cute emojis, lovely stickers, funny gifs, smiley faces.
- Auto-complete the words and offers you accurate next-word prediction!
- Easy to get cool fonts, makes your type no more boring.
- Support apps including but not limited: Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp and all your apps.


THEME STORE
- There are more cool, colorful, lovely, dark, gothic, imaging, rose, gold, dream, candy, ice-cream, tech, and business theme packs can focus on our free theme store!
- Customize your own keyboard with your favorite pictures! With FUN Keyboard, you can create your keyboard with cool music, colorful fonts and fantastic click effects.

CUSTOM EMOJI
- Creating personal memojis basic with your face.
- There are dozens of emoji stickers created by your own.
- Covering a variety of popular words such as Hi, OK, No, thanks and a lot in your DIY emoji keyboard.
- Custom memojis can also be shared on social platforms to play with friends.

STICKER STORE
- New exclusive, trendy stickers to download.
- Express your emotions to your friends by sending funny emoji faces.
- The Emoji Stickers is an exclusive extension to the FUN Keyboard that is used to send to your friends to make communicating more fun!


Upgrade your keyboard to FUN Keyboard to typing faster and send cool memojis to amaze your friends!

Last but not least, FUN Keyboard is absolutely FREE!
