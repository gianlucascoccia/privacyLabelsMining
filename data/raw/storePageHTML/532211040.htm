Description

The Automated Solution To Unify Your Entire Athletics Department.

ARMS is an integrated set of tools to unlock the true value of your entire athletic organization through process automation, streamlined communications, and real-time reporting and monitoring.

ARMS is a cloud-based software solution for Collegiate Athletic programs to automate and unify the entire athletic department for Athletic Directors, administrators, coaches, athletes, and recruits. The ARMS product is comprised of several different modules including:

• Process Automation
• Recruiting
• Compliance
• Team Management
• Department Management
• Advancement & Fundraising
• Departmental Workflow
• Camps
• Academics

Each of the modules provide for seamless contact and communications management along with process workflow and real-time reporting, all available on the web, smart phones, and tablet devices. All ARMS modules are integrated so that data can be aggregated, monitored, and reported on seamlessly at any level within the department in real time.

ARMS Mobile enables paid subscribers of ARMS to access their information on their iPhone and iPad devices. To use ARMS Mobile your organization must be an active subscriber.
