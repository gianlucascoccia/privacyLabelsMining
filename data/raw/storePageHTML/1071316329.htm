Great test of knowledge!

This app was a great study tool! Especially now that part one is on the computers, the clicking and flow are very similar. I had many questions on boards that were very similar questions, or questions that asked something different but on the same specific topic (such as saliva ducts). It was the only practice questions I did prior to the exam and I felt confident going in and when I came out. I would definitely still use other methods of studying obviously as this is not a stand alone system, but it was definitely great study tool and test of knowledge.

The only things that prevented me from giving it five stars was that there are a few wrong answers (I’d says less then 10 in the whole app) and also that they’re a little too generous with the scoring (however the boards scoring is so crazy I have no idea how they’d replicate that). Neither of which I would to affect my outcome on boards.

Developer Response ,

Thanks for your feedback, Gcavetttttt! Actually, since then we hired 2 experts on this matter. One which used to write test questions for Boards... So by now there shouldn't be any wrong answers, but in the event you have ANY questions regarding a specific question we also added a NEW FEATURE which lets every user report a question with a screenshot already attached to the report. :) We found this to be the main issue and while we agree we you, we also know that this issue has been fixed. We ASK that you consider changing your review so that we can achieve more stars and be more accessible to more students!! Thank you so much for your feedback, Gcavetttttt!
