Description

Gain access Eric inthe Morning with Melissa & Whip and Today’s New Music from Chicago, Illinois whenever, wherever!

Take advantage of App only contests, promotions & events. The more you listen on THE MIX App, the more you can win!

Make sure you complete the simple registration form on the app so we can notify if you are a winner. It’s that easy!

Once you have our app, be sure to add us to your tech gadgets and stream us anywhere in the U.S. on your smart speakers, phones, tablets, smart watches, gaming consoles, smart TVs and more.

Support for Apple CarPlay now included!

MIX App Features
Easy access to Eric in the Morning Podcasts with Melissa and Whip
Get notifications about concerts, contests, special deals and events.
Listen for your chance to win great rewards and prizes
Learn more about THE MIX artists you love.
Wake up with THE MIX Alarm Clock.
