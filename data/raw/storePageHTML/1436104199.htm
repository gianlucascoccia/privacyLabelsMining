Description

ALYSIA makes it possible for anyone to make original songs in minutes! Creating lyrics and melodies has truly never been simpler. Record your voice directly from the app!

ALYSIA makes it possible for anyone to make original songs in minutes! Creating lyrics and melodies has truly never been simpler. Record your voice directly from the app!

Whether it’s your first time making a song, or you’re already a seasoned songwriter, ALYSIA allows you to express yourself through the power of music.

CREATE SONGS
- ALYSIA helps you create original lyrics based on your topics
- Melody Composer suggests tunes that fit your lyrics and style
- Melody Editor lets you adjust the melodies to your taste
- Record your voice
- Video recording to share with your friends!

VOICE EFFECTS
- Apply voice-enhancing effects, such as Equalizer and Reverb
- Play around with funny voices, like ‘chipmunk’ or ‘monster’

EXPORT AND SHARE
- Share your song on your favorite social media platforms
- Download your full song
- Download Stems (.WAV and MIDI files):
- MIDI files of your ALYSIA melodies
- Background tracks
- Voice recordings

ALYSIA offers a wide variety of genres and styles, making it possible to finally create the music you’ve always had in your heart, but never knew how to unleash… until now!

If you’re a beginner: ALYSIA can help you discover your creative voice through lyrics, assist with creating the perfect melody to match, and guide you through the entire recording process - making you feel like a pro in no time!

If you’re more experienced or a professional musician, ALYSIA can help you break out of writer’s block, expand your musical horizons, spark creativity, enhance your skills, and practice your craft.

With ALYSIA you have a personal songwriting partner right at your fingertips to help inspire and guide you through the entire music-making process!

bohemian_vibes , 11/22/2020

Confusing, but fantastic idea

Reading the other reviews, I was really excited to use this app. I know that it is hard to come up with original melodies and so I was super stoked. When I tried to make a song, I HAD to choose a backtrack. It would not let me make my own. You also could only choose some because u needed a subscription to access others. I had read an article on science news for students about this app and it said that the melodies were chosen by the app to fit the lyrics depending gm on the words u had chosen, so to my understanding, you were supposed to type in your lyrics and it would show u melodies that fit YOUR song. This was kind of the case. There were lots of boxes that showed OTHER lyrics that you could put in the FOUR boxes that you had to type in your lyrics. I felt that it was pulling back in my creative freedom. There is more but I would just like to point out to the developer some of the things that I disliked.

I apologize for me going on and on about disliking this app. I do want to point out that this is a very good idea, just maybe a little bit of work on design :)
