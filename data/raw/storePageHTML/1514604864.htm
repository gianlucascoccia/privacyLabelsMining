Super fun.. but one problem

When you first get the game, the app is just like the ad. The game gives you a nice easy walkthrough of what to do and the different clothing designs you can do. It’s starts with all colors and T- shirts. Just after a couple of games you get different clothing peaces like girls bathing suits, tank tops, and dresses. Then you have costumers coming to your store asking for certain colors and designs for their shirts. And I love that it is sooo easy! I The game is super easy and doesn’t take much skill. It is an app where you can be creative and make stuff as if it were in real life. I have not gotten one single ad, but then that brings along the problem. In the app, you can get new cool supplies after getting a certain amount of money and working for a certain amount of days. But, you need ads to load some of them. I have tried so many times to get it to work but the game just replies with “ad not ready” each and every time. I still love the app and think it is awesome. Although it would be better if the ads for the new supplies would work. I hope this can help you before purchasing the free DIY Tie Dyeing app. Please stay safe and healthy during these times.
