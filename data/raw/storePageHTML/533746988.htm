Description

Over 900,000 downloads and overwhelming 5 star reviews show this is the must have app for all football fans!

Use the app to plan your football viewing so you never miss a match. We offer the most up-to-date, extensive and accurate listings of live football being shown legally in the UK.

- Every competition including domestic, continental, international, European, youth and women's football
- Every platform including TV, streaming services, apps and whatever they think of next…

If it's being shown live - it's listed in the app!

*** FEATURES ***

- Access all future match listings and details so you can plan your viewing in advance
- Create your personalised schedule that automatically syncs to your calendar and reminds you via notifications so you never miss a match
- Filter listings by team, competition and/or channels to only see the matches that matter most to you
- View the upcoming schedule at a glance in the new calendar view
- Dark mode, improved Match Details and much more!
