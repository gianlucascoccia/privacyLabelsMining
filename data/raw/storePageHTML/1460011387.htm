Description

MOZE is a personal expense tracker which has a beautiful interface and focus on user-experience. We want all of you to build a habit of tracking your daily expense.

-----

Tracking the expense is just the first step for your financial management.

Everyone should have a comprehensive view of their financial situation and understand where their money comes in and goes out.

◆ Keep track of your money
Tracking every expenses you have and understand your spending pattern

◆ Easily manage your budget
Creating the personalised budgets according to your preference. Allowing you have a more effective financial management.

◆ Reduce the unnecessary expense
Building a good spending habit by identifying the needs and wants

◆ Reach your financial goals
Keeping your every accounts and balances in one place to understand your financial life

-----

Have you ever used any expense tracker before?

Don’t worry, no matter you are new to expense tracking or experienced. MOZE provides a comprehensive workflow and advanced features for a better expense tracking experience.

Brooke J McNeil , 03/15/2020

Phenomenal for everyday money management

This app is exactly what I needed to keep on top of my money, and was the only one I could find with the functionality i needed.

This being said, a few minor details would make this app even more useful. Firstly, being able to edit the names and icons of sub-budget categories would be ideal. I don’t often use many of the preset categories, and it would be convenient to be able to budget for individual items inside of a project without having to use the preset categories and simply remember which category represents which item.

Additionally, another feature I would use if it was available would be the ability to allocate only a selected amount of an income into a budget project. It can be quite a hassle trying to separate an income into multiple parts if I want to put some of it away, especially if the income was not recent. I understand this capability may be available via the split payment premium feature, however, I am unsure of the exact specifications of this feature given that I use the free version.
