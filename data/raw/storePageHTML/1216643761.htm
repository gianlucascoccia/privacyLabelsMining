Description

Embark on a fantastic ride with Emily! Thrilling tracks, a ferris wheel, a carousel and much more are waiting for you in the Coaster City! Collect all the minis and toys!

Budge Studios™ presents Thomas & Friends™ Minis! Create your very own train set piece by piece and bring it to life with Thomas and all his friends. Customize endlessly and let your imagination run free with whirly waterslides, frozen loops, rainbow bridges, dino spine rails and more! Climb aboard your favorite Minis engine and experience every twist, turn and stunt! Ready, set, build!

THE ULTIMATE TRAIN SET BUILDER
• BUILD tons of exciting tracks, from twisting turns to rattling ramps
• ROCKET yourself through spectacular stunts, bold boosts and raging rollercoasters!
• DECORATE your train set with tall trees, beautiful buildings and dazzling decorations
• PAINT your set’s terrain with sandy beaches, bubbling lava and snowy blizzards
• DRIVE through your creations in 3rd person with Thomas, Percy and more!
• COLLECT golden gears to unlock epic engine skins like Hero Hiro or Spooky Spencer!
• CREATE your world from scratch or play with one of our awesome pre-made sets!
• IMMERSE yourself in your train set and experience it like never before with Augmented Reality!*
*Augmented Reality feature is only available with iOS 11 and on certain devices.

GET CREATIVE WITH 8 UNIQUE WORLDS TO DISCOVER
• THOMAS’ COUNTRYSIDE: Explore the windmills, train stations and workshops of verdant Sodor!
• GORDON’S WINTER WONDERLAND: Ride over frozen bridges and crash through ice walls!
• PERCY’S SPOOKY FOREST: Dare you enter the hollow log tunnel and haunted mansion?
• TOBY’S BUSY CITY: Visit the fire station, hospital and deliver the mail downtown!
• SPENCER’S AQUA PARK: Dive through the whirlpool tunnel and slide down the waterslide loop!
• JAMES’ JURASSIC COVE: Venture to the lost land of treasure-filled cars and triceratops skull tunnels!
• DIESEL'S ENCHANTED VALLEY: Ride through the magical forest and jump through incredible waterfalls to find the Medieval Castle!
• EMILY’S COASTER CITY: Embark on a rollercoaster adventure at the fair, will you dare to jump through the Fire Hoop?

Great little game — but how do you rotate pieces??

This is a fun game and I’ve enjoyed making tracks as much as my son has. The layout is simple. The graphics are fun and the trains do fun little things when interacting with one another. I love all the little obstacles and what they do to the trains.

But for the life of me I can’t figure out how to rotate a piece! Can the developer help? Surely you must be able to rotate a tunnel so it can go horizontally too? (EDIT: The developer has responded that there are two versions of each tunnel-like piece. I still only see one direction of each piece. For instance, the only tunnel piece is north-south.)

Developer Response ,

Hello! Yes you can have pieces go either direction by dragging the correct one out of the tray, there are 2 versions of each tunnel, etc/ Hope that helps!

Great game for the price!

My son and I enjoy building together but the repetitive advertisements for more stuff does get a little annoying. A previous reviewer said they couldn’t find tunnels to go different ways (horizontal vs. vertical) that took us a while to figure out, too: lay some plain tracks pieces across the area you want to put the tunnel (or whatever) then when you carry the tunnel over that area, it will automatically flip to match the existing track. This is different than the instructions! Hey developers, you could change the game instructions to reflect this :). Overall my favorite free game for my 3 yr old. So yeah, I’m about to buy one of the expansion packs because it’s hard to say no to a 3 yr old stuck at home in a pandemic... 😆
