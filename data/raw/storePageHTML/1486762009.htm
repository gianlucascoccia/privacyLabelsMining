Description

Oxygen is a modern digital banking platform built for the 21st century economy - the free thinkers, rebels and entrepreneurs.

Oxygen offers flexible banking solutions for both Personal and Business accounts that are elegant, simple and secure.

Don’t like hidden fees? Neither do we. Enjoy no minimum balance fees, no monthly maintenance fees, no overdraft fees, and access to over 55,000 fee-free Allpoint ATMs.

Keep spending goals in check by connecting external accounts, and get Cashback* Rewards from approved merchants on things you buy everyday - like 5% cashback at Trader Joe's, Walmart, Gas and your preferred Rideshares, and $1 off at your favorite coffee shop, among others.

Small business or entrepreneur? Manage employee spending with one time use virtual cards. Upload receipts to the app and tag deductible expenses.

Get started today in just one minute.

So... I have been banking with Oxygen for some time. And like others who have commented, oxygen is so much better than N26 and other solutions out there. The overall service ease of use is outstanding and the cash back categories are fair and reasonable.
