Description

Compete, track, connect, compare. The CrossFit Games App is your all-access pass to the largest international fitness competition in the world. Find out when new workouts are released, access movement standards, submit your scores, track and compare your results on the leaderboard, and more.

COMPETE: Sign up for push notifications to be among the first to know when new workouts are released. View workout details, movement standards and video tips.

SUBMIT SCORES: Never forget the submission deadline again. When you sign up for notifications, you'll receive reminders when it's time to log your score.

TRACK PROGRESS: See where you stack up locally and worldwide on your personalized leaderboards. Search to compare your performance to your friends, family members, fitness nemesis or favorite elite athletes.

GET SUPPORT: Have a question for the Games team? With the app, you'll have the Rulebook and Drug Testing Policy in the palm of your hand. If you can't find what you're looking for, reach out to Games Support with the click of a button.

The CrossFit Games season covers two stages of competition: the worldwide Open and the CrossFit Games. This is where grassroots meets greatness, and we all start together.
