Description

Welcome to the world’s largest network for LGBTQ+ news and entertainment! EDGE delivers the latest in national news and politics, movies, television and theatre, plus health & fitness, travel, business, sports, technology, weather, nightlife, giveaways and more. Staying connected on the go has never been so easy.

Our app technology has been completely revamped for 2020 – and it remains completely free. No subscription necessary.

Join us. Our team of award-winning independent journalists, photographers and technologists are working to deliver you the headlines you need to stay up-to-date… and always with an edge.
