Description

Stream your favorite shows with the Bravo app! Full episodes and live TV from The Real Housewives, Below Deck, Top Chef, Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen, Married to Medicine AND MUCH MORE.



Watch the most recent episodes of your favorite shows or watch live TV with the Bravo app. Catch up with live TV and past seasons anytime, anywhere!



Live stream TV from your phone or tablet and catch up on the latest seasons of your favorite shows. Get started by signing in with your TV provider (cable, satellite or digital). The Bravo app supports most TV providers, including DirecTV, Xfinity, Spectrum, AT&T and Verizon.



Sign up for an NBCUniversal Profile within the app using your Facebook, Google or email account and receive three credits to watch shows for free before signing in with your TV provider. Live stream your favorite shows and sync them across all your devices to continue watching right where you left off.



Watch now to catch the latest episodes of your favorite shows on the Bravo app today!



BRAVO APP FEATURES:



Watch Bravo Shows

Stream full episodes of your favorite Bravo shows.

Watch episodes of The Real Housewives of Atlanta and The Real Housewives of New Jersey.

Live stream Below Deck, Watch What Happens Live and so much more!



Stream Episodes Your Way

Watch episodes of new Bravo shows the day after they air on TV.

Watch shows by signing in with your TV provider and access the entire catalog of VOD and live content.

Live stream TV and access schedule 24/7.

Chromecast and AirPlay compatible! Start episodes in the app and stream to your TV.

TV on the go! If you can't finish an episode in one sitting, the Bravo app saves your place so you can resume watching later.

Watch TV and mark shows as your Favorites for quick access to the latest episodes.



NBCUniversal Content

Watch NBC shows on demand from the NBCUniversal family of networks (including NBC, E!, USA, SYFY, Oxygen, MSNBC, CNBC, Universal Kids, Universo and Telemundo).

Live stream NBCUniversal networks within the app.

Watch shows and filter by network and genre while browsing.



NBCUniversal Profile

Create an NBCUniversal Profile to save shows across all your devices.

Live stream your favorite shows and continue where you left off on any device.

Watch shows for FREE with three credits when you sign up!

Love Bravo

I’ve been committed to Bravo since the network started and love basically every show! Seriously, I love Bravo. However, my main issue with the app is the consistent glitches when viewing episodes. Sometimes if you pause the video you can lose your spot you stopped the episode at. My internet connection isn’t an issue but for whatever reason, sometimes the videos pause and take long to load. There’s too many commercials. I can handle like one to two ads but don’t make them two minutes a piece or like 5 different ads. I purposely use apps to avoid commercials. I love how the new episodes are quickly up on the app. Side note, it’d be really helpful to have the “Behind the Scenes/Never Before Seen” episodes in a separate category rather than with the season episodes. I get annoyed having to dig through the episodes looking for the NEW episode. Thanks Bravo, for breaking me away from my reality to focus on someone else’s problems and drama :)
