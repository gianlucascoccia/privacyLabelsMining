Description

This is a music ball game where you will have to hop the balls, survive by doing jump, bounce, hit and smash the music tiles. Drop the ballz to create beat in the edm music enviroment.
Keep the bouncing ball jumping on tiles but don't forget to follow and enjoy the rhythm with greatest hits of the edm, piano songs. Let's dance!

You will surely love:
1. Frequently updated hit EDM songs.
2. List of the hottest pop songs.
2. Stunning 3D visuals and effects where could could really dunk and smash the tiles while in perfect mode.
3. Simple one-finger control .
4. A seamless experience with music.
5. A lot of beautiful backgrounds.

Hop in and dune and enjoy the game for free!

Frozen Echos , 10/09/2020

Decent

Okay, this app is a very entertaining game, not gonna lie. But see... sometimes when you select a song thinking (for example) “Oh! Let You Down by NF is super popular! I’ll do that one!” It turns out that the song called Let You Down is by a different person who no one knows about and the song isn’t even a hit song. And of course there are the very annoying ads that pop up every single time you press something. And there are bugs and a whole lot of other issues I’m sure other customers have been experiencing. Okay, now let’s look on the bright side of this app. They DO have a lot of good hit songs on there including the very popular song “Faded.”
I do also select songs I have never heard before and I am very happy with some songs that I have now added to my playlist on Apple Music. I adore the endless feature and the maps are very nice. I think some things you could improve are by adding more maps, adding a search bar to look up a particular song and of course, maybe textures for the balls. Other than those and all the issues I described earlier in this review, this game is great! Please keep updating this app! I hope to bump up my review to five stars! ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Once was great...

I’ve played this since the very start! Back when it only had maybe 20 songs? Back before all this fancy stuff now. It was my favourite game! I spent months getting perfect scores on everything I could! Now it doesn’t remember anything I did, I never deleted this either. I’m still very upset, despite still playing, about all of it especially now that I can’t find half the songs I’ve finished.

As for making this game better. I of course can’t play without the music but I don’t like the extra sound to it and which I could turn it off. I also wish there was another column to view which songs you’ve finished, need to complete, and have bought so you don’t have to go searching through it all. I also have an extremely hard time with the flashing light that you now get getting perfects hits I wish I could turn that off as well.

Really, I miss my old scores and am glad to see the game improving though. I’m sure it’s a lot of work to do. I do wish you can get more improvements and hopefully some more customisation in settings.
