Description

Practice your favorite masterpieces with Metronaut! Explore more than 3,500 music sheets with high quality backing tracks for all instruments (including voice) and levels and enjoy practicing like never before, even when you’re alone.

——————————
FEATURES FOR EASY AND FUN PRACTICING

- Explore a growing catalog of thousands of classical music sheets and get music recommendations selected by our editorial team for your instrument and level.

- Play-along high quality recordings of professional musicians.
Playing with piano or orchestra accompaniment will develop your listening skills, which will come in handy when performing ensemble music or working with an accompanist. And all that while making practice more fun!

- Don’t worry about page turns thanks to our automatic music sheet scroll.
Metronaut zoom and automatic page turn features ensure optimal reading comfort on all screen sizes.

- Adapt the accompaniment tempo to your progress.
Slow down or speed up the tempo of the music accompaniment to adapt it to your level.

- Take the lead and play at your own rhythm.
Activate Magic Mode to have the tempo automatically adapt to your speed in real time.

- Annotate and Print all the scores of our catalog.
Add indications or highlight the score, as you would on a regular sheet music and if you’d rather work on paper, just print your score.

- Practice difficult sections using the loop feature and metronome.
Tackle and master difficult passages of the piece by playing them in loop, activating the metronome or the solo to hear the part you are playing.

- Don’t tear your hair out over transposition
Choose any piece to play in our catalog: Metronaut automatically transposes and changes the clef for your instrument, while preserving the audio quality of the accompaniment.

- Record your performance to listen back to it and make progress.
You can also share your performances with your teacher, friends or the whole world!

——————————
3,500+ PLAY ALONG MUSIC SHEETS FOR ALL MUSICIANS

- 33 instruments available : Violin, Viola, Cello, Flute, Piano, Voice, Clarinet, Trumpet and many others.

- 4 levels : Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert

- Play accompanied by: an Orchestra, a Piano, a Guitar, a Violin, a Viola, a Flute…

- Solo Pieces for Pianists: Practice your right hand while Metronaut plays the left one and vice versa

- Explore many genres: Baroque, Romantic, Classical, Gospel, Country, Celtic, Jazz, Traditional, Kids songs...
