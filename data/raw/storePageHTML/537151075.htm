Description

Learn how to play the songs you love, from Ed Sheeran to Metallica!
Fourchords is a beginner-friendly songbook that comes with all you need: Easy chords with lyrics, backing tracks, tutorials, tuner and real feedback! Includes play-along versions for over 2000 hit songs, with no knowledge of tabs or sheet music required. Gain confidence on your chord and transition skills while having fun playing your favorite songs.

FourChords breaks down your favorite songs into four simple guitar chords, so you can master them in an instant — it is the perfect learning tool for beginners and an awesome songbook for more advanced players! The app listens to you play and gives you feedback on how to improve, so grab your guitar and start your free trial now! Only 10 minutes of daily practice will get you playing your first song in no time!

lefty63w , 03/11/2019

Beginner has learned a lot

Has selection for rhythm parameters with an excellent how-to for chord construction and strumming. New songs added that provide chord and strumming variations across the group of songs. Also songs have lyrics if you’re talented enough to play and sing (I am not, at least for right now).
So far I his app has provided me with a platform to learn, the play and practice songs using basic chords, adjusting tempo and backing up to replay a section as needed. I like that it can be done with just a touch or swipe of my finger on the screen.
From the library of songs available I’ve built my own playlist of songs for learning and practice. As new songs are posted it’s easy to add them or later delete from the playlist.
As someone who previously had not spent a lot of time with an acoustic guitar, I find this app useful and worth the money.
What I haven’t tried yet is setting up a progress sharing account (mic on you device set to On) so I could receive “a more tailored user experience, song recommendations and playing tips”. Wanted to get my basics first so may try it, now that I’m more comfortable with my playing ability.
