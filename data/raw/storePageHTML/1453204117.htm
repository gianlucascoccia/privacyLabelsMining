Description

Stay up-to-date with the latest news and weather in Northwest Ohio and Southeast Michigan on the all-new free WTOL 11 app from WTOL 11.

Our app features the latest breaking news that impacts you and your family, interactive weather and radar, and live video from our newscasts and local events.

LOCAL & BREAKING NEWS
• Receive real-time notifications for breaking news
• Read the latest news as it happens in your area
• Explore exclusive investigative stories
• Browse photos, news clips, and raw video

LOCAL WEATHER
• Daily and hourly forecasts for your city
• The latest forecasts from our meteorologists
• Interactive radar maps
• Automated hyperlocal severe weather alerts

LIVE VIDEO
• Watch live video newscasts and breaking news from WTOL 11

PERSONALIZATION
• Discover and dive into topics that matter to you
• Favorite topics to receive personalized updates

Other Features:
• Share stories & videos directly with your friends and family
• See the latest updates and behind the scenes photos from your favorite anchors and reporters
• Get information on school delays & closings as they happen

This is the official app of WTOL 11 and WTOL.com, the CBS affiliate serving the Northwest Ohio and Southeast Michigan area.

Got feedback? Let us know how we can make this the best app experience for you! Send us a note at appfeedback@tegna.com.

No Live broadcasting

Update- June 12, 2019- this app still not performing with the advertised changes. Can you add the live and repeating daily broadcast feature back to the old format???

The live news broadcasting is no longer available on this app. The old format would play the live new broadcasts, take you into the 9 am morning show and then repeat the morning news. The live feature would play afternoon, evening and 11 pm news which allowed viewer to see everything all day. This app only shows portions of coverage.
I wish they would return it to the previous format so we could watch all of the broadcasting.

Developer Response ,

@MDAWG1995 Thanks for taking the time to submit a review, there is live broadcasting on the Watch section of the app (third tab at the bottom). Whenever a newscast is live on TV, it will also go live at the top of the Watch section of the app. Hope this helps!

43560 , 06/25/2019

The old app was better

I was using the old app and saw that there was a new one, so I deleted the old and downloaded this new one and am probably going to switch back. The app doesn’t scroll smoothly, has a ton of old news prominently featured on the home page, and is not as customizable as they want to make it seem. I have tried to add favorite topics but it doesn’t change anything with what you see on the home page. I don’t like that I can’t scroll from story to story anymore either. You read one, then have to go back to the home page, look for the next headline, read that story, go back to the home page, etc. Sometimes I can’t even find all of the new stories from the day. There are headlines listed as local news but they are from other parts of the country, and you don’t realize that it isn’t a local story until halfway through the article. This is a horrible news app.
