Description

Mileage and Fuel tracking made easy!

Free, Accurate, Private Ad-Free Automatic Mileage Tracker
Track your mileage along with driving behavior!
One of the best apps for self employed, uber/lyft drivers, real estate agents to create mileage expense reports, check real time fuel expenses and to record their driving behavior.

We do no share your location. Background and always on location is required by Apple for automatic trip detection and we don't share with any third party.

*FEATURED APP. Drive less, drive responsibly™

- Fuel Consumption Cost and driving Behavior in real-time
- Mileage log (US and Metric units), driving pattern, speed alerts, full route tracking

DASHBOARD
View fuel cost, speed, distance traveled in real time. Get sound and visual alerts on your driving pattern

TRIP LOG
. View trips; manually add trips; swipe on a trip to delete it.
. Analyze driving pattern graphs and improve your driving habits

CALENDAR
. View fuel expenses on a monthly calendar

REPORTS
. Generate and share trip log reports
. Reports can be generated in PDF or as a spreadsheet document (MS Excel, iWorks Numbers, etc).
. Include driving analysis as data points or graphical bar chart in the report.
. Create a personal report having consumed fuel costs or as a business expense using the IRS business travel rate. Preview the report before sharing the reports via email.
.Dropbox integration

Disclaimer: Continued use of GPS running in the background can dramatically decrease battery life.

Extra Mile's Privacy Policy may be found at: http://www.extramileapp.com/privacy.html

Extra Mile's Terms of Use may be found at: http://www.extramileapp.com/terms.html

Extra Mile can be used as a GPS Voice Navigation app, Gas Logger, Trip Logger, Mileage Tracker, Fuel expenses, Green Mile, Vehicle Log, fleet management, driver behavior.
