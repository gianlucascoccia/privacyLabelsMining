Description

Our Regions banking app has a new look, with the award-winning service and features you expect. Bank where you want, when you want.

It’s simple and easy to:
- Securely log in with fingerprint or facial recognition
- View account and routing numbers
- Search transactions by date, check number or amount
- Transfer funds between Regions accounts
- Make mobile deposits
- Send money to your family or friends with Zelle®
- Pay bills with Regions Bill Pay
- Access Services such as Regions LockIt, Open an Account and Activate a Card
- Set up alerts in Notification Center
- View and activate Rewards
- Schedule a Regions banker appointment
- Locate the nearest Regions branch, ATM or VTM

To contact us, email MobileApps@Regions.com.
