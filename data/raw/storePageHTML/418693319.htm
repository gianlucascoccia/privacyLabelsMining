Description

Unique Daily Affirmations is a free creative affirmation tool that helps you succeed in your endeavors, bring you out from under the clouds, and make positive changes in your life. Through uplifting statements and quotes, you'll believe in yourself with these one-a-day affirmations for daily motivation.

Hold down the large gray button to reveal your daily affirmation and effectively use the app by repeating the statement out loud. Saying the affirmation in front of a mirror is even better. It's been scientifically proven that the more stimuli your mind has, the more your mind will retain the information. By vocalizing in front of a mirror, you stimulate sight and sound. Envision your personal success.

Let these positive declarations become truth in your subconscious mind as you think about it throughout the day.

INSTRUCTIONS
Press and hold the large gray button to reveal today's affirmation.

Tap the bottom left icon for options on sharing, saving or skipping the affirmation. You can SHARE, SAVE, or VIEW the affirmation.

When viewing your list of Favorite affirmations you can swipe left to delete an affirmation. Tapping on a specific affirmation will let you view it.

To add your own affirmations, tap on the bottom left icon then tap on "Add Your Own". Any affirmation you add will appear in your favorites list.

To record yourself saying an affirmation, tap the top right REC icon and then on the red button to start recording. You can save one recording per affirmation.

To add your own backgrounds, tap on the bottom right icon, then tap on "My Backgrounds". Any background here will appear in one of your daily affirmations unless you set one as your current.
