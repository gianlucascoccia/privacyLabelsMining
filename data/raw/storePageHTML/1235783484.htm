Description

Stream poker with PokerGO®, the premier destination for live and on demand poker.

Watch your favorite professional poker players like Daniel Negreanu, Phil Ivey, Tom Dwan, and Phil Hellmuth plus celebrities like Don Cheadle, Norm Macdonald, and Kevin Hart throw down on the felt. Experience the high stakes action with a non-stop schedule of the biggest cash games and tournaments, including the World Series of Poker (WSOP), High Stakes Poker, Poker After Dark, and Super High Roller Bowl.

Pick up expert poker tournament strategy, tips & tricks, and poker tells while watching the world’s best live and on demand from the PokerGO Studio in Las Vegas and other famous worldwide poker venues.

POKER LIVE TV
Watch live Poker tournaments and events from the World Series of Poker, Super High Roller Bowl, World Poker Tour (WPT), US Poker Open, Poker Masters, Poker After Dark, and more.

POKER ON DEMAND
Stream Poker 24/7 on all your favorite devices. Binge watch your favorite seasons of High Stakes Poker and Poker After Dark, plus final tables from the World Series of Poker, World Poker Tour, and more. Get in the game with PokerGO. Anytime. Anywhere.

POKER ORIGINAL SHOWS
Enjoy exclusive episodes of PokerGO originals, including Beyond the Rail, Dead Money®, Poker Nights®, Stories from the Felt, Super High Roller Club™, and many more.

Caution ahead - updated

I purchased PokerGO to watch the content which I greatly enjoy. I bought on my AppleTV. It shows up in my purchases in my App Store subscriptions, however it won’t let me restore purchases successfully from my iPad, so I can’t watch on other devices. It also doesn’t allow me to figure out my password or reset it from my iPad. Since I’m now away from my Apple TV for a while, this subscription is useless to me. Will ask for a refund unless someone can help address this issue with me.

Great content is there, but it’s such a pain to use, I’ll just find other poker content elsewhere.

UPDATE - PokerGO contacted me and I was able to resolve the issue. The AppleTV signup didn’t require a password and I needed to reset the password to play on the iPad. The App still is not perfect (hard to surf), but the content is excellent and they honestly tried to help. Give it a try.
