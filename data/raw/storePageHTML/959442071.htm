Description

From the commencement of the 2021 Suncorp Super Netball season, stream every match live, fast and data free with the Netball Live Pass, available through the Netball Live App.

If you’re a Telstra mobile customer with a current Telstra month-to-month mobile plan, pre-paid mobile ($30+ active recharges) or mobile broadband plan, a Season Pass is included when you mantain your month-to-month plan or a $30+ active pre-paid recharge.

Not a Telstra mobile customer?
No worries, you’ll be able to purchase a weekly ($1.99), monthly ($3.99) or quarterly ($7.49) subscription so you can enjoy the benefits of a Live Pass.

The Netball Live App is packed with free features and content
- LIVE stats and scores
- Video highlights
- Fixture information
- Player profiles
- Regular news articles
- Pick your favourite club
- Match notifications

Your subscription will automatically rollover to a new subscription of the same period (eg. Weekly to weekly) unless you cancel your subscription.


With the Netball Live Pass, you will be able to enjoy:
- LIVE video streams & on-demand full match replays for every 2021 Suncorp Super Netball match
- LIVE video streams & on-demand full match replays for Quad Series & Constellation Cup matches
- Cast and mirror select Netball matches

Note: Live streams & on-demand match replays for the 2021 Suncorp Super Netball season are available to users in all regions. Quad Series & Constellation Cup streams and full match replays are available to users in all regions except New Zealand & United Kingdom.

Screen size restrictions apply for live match videos as vision produced by the broadcasters is currently filmed in 16:9 ratio. This may appear full screen on your device, provided it is 7 inches or less, or may cause black borders at the top and bottom of your screen.

Please refer to Crowd Support, https://crowdsupport.telstra.com.au/t5/Netball-Live/bd-p/Netball-Live, if you have any further queries.
