It still has some of the bells and whistles as other, like winning free duals and instant bingos, etc. but it’s still pretty simple and challenging. Just pick the number of cards and how much you want to wager. There are even side bets you can do, that are fun. I myself love the fact it’s not in a board game, level/stage like kind of game most mobile games are (think candy crush) I don’t want to play a board game I just want to play bingo and that’s why I love this game despite it hardness on getting bingo.
