Description

Store all your Bitcoin and other coins and tokens in one secure wallet. Guarda allows you easily receive, send, buy & exchange cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, Bitcoin Cash, BSV, Ethereum Classic, Litecoin, TRON, BEP2, TRC10, TRC20, ERC20 tokens and many more – all in one lightweight, user-friendly application.
Guarda Wallet functionality opens the gate to a brand new financial system and you are in charge here!

MULTICURRENCY CRYPTO WALLET
Now, you do not have to sign up for multiple wallets and exchange services for all of your cryptos. Favorite digital currencies can be stored and managed in one neatly designed application. With BTC, ETH,XRP, BCH, BSV, ZEC, DASH and more already on board. We never stop expanding!

NON-CUSTODIAL SECURITY
We give you full control over your crypto wallet private keys. Guarda encrypts all your data and securely stores it on the device itself – no one can gain access to your funds except you. Besides, you can enable Touch ID to access the crypto storage without having to type the password every time.

FAST CRYPTOCURRENCY TRANSACTIONS
Send and receive Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, Bitcoin Cash, EOS, TRON instantly. Scan the QR code.
