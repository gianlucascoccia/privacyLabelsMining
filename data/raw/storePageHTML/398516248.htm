Description

Start your home search with the real estate market leader in Minnesota and western Wisconsin. The Edina Realty real estate app provides you with instant access to virtually all regional MLS listings, including high resolution photos and full property details, updated every 15 minutes. Search active listings for sale, pending sales and homes recently sold across the Twin Cities, greater Minnesota and western Wisconsin.

Found a home you love? Save it to your favorites, see upcoming open houses or share it with friends. When you’re ready to take the next step, find a local Realtor who can provide you with expertise throughout the home buying and selling process.

Key Features for Every Step of House Hunting:

- Begin your search: Search by city, zip code, MLS number, price, style and more to find single family homes, condos, townhomes, farms, land and commercial properties for sale or recently sold. Find homes near you using built-in GPS or our augmented reality home search tool, Home Scan.

- Save and compare listings: Save your favorite properties to easily view again and compare features.

- Calculate payments: Use our mortgage calculator to see the estimated monthly payment for each home you love. Adjust the purchase price, down payment, interest rate and more to customize.

- Find an expert real estate agent: A local Realtor can help you take the next step in your home search.

Lost functionality

I just updated from the last version and am immediately disappointed with the design changes and loss in functionality.
On the summary panel you've eliminated one of my favorite features - the estimated commute time display - I know the function is still available, but without the display on the summary panel it is no where near as effective.
The new layout also prevents me from seeing as much information quickly. Being forced to flip between tabs to try to find all of the information that used to be available all in one panel is frustrating and dysfunctional. Making it work more like your website was not a good choice - it should have been the other way around - your App used to have much better functionality than your site, but no longer ...
And additionally, the layout doesn't scale properly to an iPad mini, so all of the type is cut off on the right side.
The last version had some challenges in loading photos, but I'd rather have that issue and the better design function than this new layout.
I will no longer be recommending this app to others searching for homes as I used to.
