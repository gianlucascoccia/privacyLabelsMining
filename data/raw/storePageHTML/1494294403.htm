Description

Create your own unique fan made Pokemon style cards from over 750 card backgrounds from the latest expansions!

Includes Sword and Shield, Sun and Moon, XY plus Trainer and Energy cards! All stages and elements plus V, V Max, GX, Tag Team, Ultra Beast, Prism, EX and Mega. Also supports GX Attacks, UB GX Attacks, Poke-body, Poke-power and abilities.

Compete in Weekly Events by submitting your own Poke Cards to compete against other players for the most votes!

This is an unofficial "Pokémon" app and is only intended for creation of fan made art.

FEATURES
• Customize everything on over 750 card backgrounds
• Weekly Events against other players
• Use any photo or draw your own
• View all your cards in a gallery and edit at any time
• View your cards in 3D and in the real world using AR
• Share with friends or family
• Print your cards

CUSTOMIZE
• Name
• HP
• Card Energy
• Card Description
• Abilities (Name, Description, Damage)
• Ability Energies
• Ability/Attack Bars and Icons
• Evolution Image and Line
• Weakness, Resistance, Retreat Cost

Outstanding App!

I have to say this app is SUPER fun to use. I have customized a few cards that are super cool.
Though, this app (as I would say) has one too many ads. Every time I want to make a slight tweak, an add pops up and I have to wait. (Not saying it’s a super long time, but sometimes it’s aggravating to have to wait 30 seconds just to change a spelling error.)
The weekly card votes are fun, there are a lot cards with pictures off the internet, (which as a person who makes my own art for cards,) to me, that always slightly annoys me. (Not saying you shouldn’t be able to do that, that is just a pet peeve of mine)
I also think it would be a fun improvement to add a customization to make your own Pokémon type, (such as light type, poison type, cyber type, and so on) which would expand the the artist’ level of creativity.
I definitely had to think hard to find improvements to add, because it’s just so fun and overall this app is definitely an app you should consider downloading, it is a very creative platform, and I love it!

Pokémon cards create app review

This is one of the best and most entertaining apps that I have used in a long time. It has awesome options for placing pictures for the illustrated dishing of the Pokémon card and your able to draw it on the app (although drawing on the app should be a little more sophisticated). Many different cards design including full art, basics, stage 1, stage 2, stage 3, EX, Mega EX, GX, V, VMax just to name a few. Some new ones could be Pokémon breaks which would go into The XY series. Also when making Mega EX there should be a golden M before creating the name. It would be really helpful if you were able too type a energy and the gold EX in your description of an attack or ability. Also creating a reverse hollow or a hollow shiny card. The card would flow a lot smoother if you could center the abilities and attacks when on the card. Last thing it would be really awesome if you could print the card or upload it somewhere else. I know I would place a printing order.
