Description

纽约时报中文网 (cn.NYTimes.com) 是纽约时报公司旗下的首个中文媒体产品，旨在向读者提供全球时事、商业及文化生活领域的高水准报道。网站内容特为中文读者量身打造，既包括《纽约时报》英文报道的中译版本，也包括中文写作者专为中文网所撰写的原创内容。

《纽约时报中文网》iOS App 功能包括：

- 最新图文新闻
- 中英双语阅读
- 推送每日新闻简报
- 收藏文章
- 夜间模式
- 全文截图
- 分享内容到 Twitter、Facebook、微博、微信等社交网络

BillyR___ , 08/11/2019

Great App with 2 flaws

I love this app. My only gripes are that it doesn’t allow you to use the portrait (vertical) mode on the iPad, and you can’t use the look up word feature within the app. Typically you can highlight a word and select the “look up” button. This would be a massive time saver when looking up an unfamiliar word. Otherwise, I have zero complaints. Would love to see those two issues resolved.

PodHead101 , 03/16/2018

It would be nice to support portrait mode on iPad

New York Times has always been one step ahead of other newspapers. Making this Chinese version app is one example. I love this app and its China tailored reporting. My petty grievance is the landscape-only mode in the iPad version. It would be nice to support the portrait mode as well. Keep up the good works!

Animation Slideshow , 12/25/2020

A great resource for Chinese!

A thumb up for NYT to bring the professional journalism to Chinese community. Many of its members are subjected to media distortion and disinformation campaign on social media such as WeChat. Time for people to read something real and know the difference between high-standard journalism versus self-made “news” that creates rumors and confusions. Need more efforts like NYT outreach to ethnic media, as it is dying without enough support.
