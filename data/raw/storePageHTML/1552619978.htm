Description

Manga Viewer is the go-to app for all your manga reading needs!


- Discover the latest manga
- Download one or whole chapter
- Save to local to optimize the experience (no need to wait the requesting for next time)
- Sort manga by date, alphabet, rating
- Bookmark a manga
- History reading manga
- Organized by genre

Due to copyrights, the licensed manga are not available for users. And if you think the content of some manga are objectionable, please report it to us: thanhtam050693@icloud.com
