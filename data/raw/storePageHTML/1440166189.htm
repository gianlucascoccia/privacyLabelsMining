Description

Track real-time location of your beloved ones on a private map. Get access to movement history. Stay connected with family and friends with Lap - real-time GPS tracker.

Lap - Find Family & Friends is a real-time location-sharing app and the best way to coordinate with family and friends. Get automatic notifications when your family comes and goes from home, work and school, and when they complete drives.

Use Lap - Find Family & Friends to:
* View the real-time location of friends or family members on a private map
* Receive real-time alerts when friends or family arrive or leave home, work, and school
* Get the location history: Even if the person you are following is not online, learn their latest location.
* Track a lost or stolen phone

Use GPS to find Friends and Family
Lap - Find Family & Friends uses state-of-the-art GPS location data to report the real-time whereabouts of your friends and family. Simply install the app on everyone’s phone. Once registered, each member appears as a unique icon on the map so you’ll always know where your family members are.

No need to send text messages asking “When will you be home?” Lap - Find Family & Friends tells you! Create geofence zones around your home, work, school, or anywhere you visit regularly. Whenever someone in your Circle arrives or leaves those locations, you’ll get a notification.

Please note that continuous use of location services in the background can drastically decrease battery life. But not to worry, our location technology and algorithms always keep battery usage to a minimum.
