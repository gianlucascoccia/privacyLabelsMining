Description

The only thing you need to make your New Year's Resolutions stick. Make 2021 YOUR year.

Customizable tools + progress at your own pace = success. You won't find this kind of flexibility anywhere else.

We've put a lot of TLC into building an interface that is beautiful, simple, and personal. Why? We want you to feel like this app is truly yours! Your empowerment translates into your success.

- We support all types of goals & projects -
Whatever you want to achieve — i.e., live healthier, buy a new car, learn to code, etc. — belongs in North Star.

- We'll keep track of your progress and help you stay organized -
How do you currently keep track of the things you want to achieve? Do you use different apps? Make mental notes? Write them down on a sticky note that ends up at the bottom of your pocket?

We've simplified the process by creating customizable tools — think of them as ''mini-apps" that you can activate:

- Habits: plan and track activities you want to do consistently & build your own routine
- Milestones: divide your goal into achievable steps
- Measures: track results over time to measure progress
- Planner: create to-do lists and set reminders

The app is fully functional, even if you only activate one tool. You can create and track as many goals as you want. Your goals are separated into different views, each containing different sets of tools.

With North Star, you'll have more energy to achieve your goals.

Subscription

North Star is free to download and use. You can use the whole app and all the tools for free. If you'd like to add more than one goal, you can upgrade to any of the following subscriptions:

1 month: $5.00/mo.
1 year: $2.50/mo. ($35.00 per year)
Lifetime: $50.00

The total cost of the purchase will be charged to your iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase. The subscription period will automatically renew unless the auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period.

Subscriptions can be managed, and auto-renewal turned off in Account Settings in iTunes after purchase. If you wish to stop subscribing, we'll help you out. You can cancel your subscription anytime through the subscription page in North Star.
