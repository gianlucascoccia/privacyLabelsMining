Description

Text Me That was developed for one simple purpose, so you never forget again.

An average person casually checks their phone 63 times a day. People, apps and advertisers are constantly competing for your valuable attention. Text Me That was built to cut through the noise.

Receiving actual texts directly in their built-in messages app has helped many TMT users prioritize their own reminders and never forget again. We hope it helps you too.

Text Me That is free to use but there are extra features unlocked with a TMT Pro subscription:

• Unlimited Recurring Reminders
• Additional Notes
• Location Reminders - iOS and iPadOs Only
• Quick Reminders
• Reuse Completed Reminders
• Group Reminder Credits
• Add Reminders to your own Calendars
• And more features are added regularly!

The upgrade to the Text Me That Pro provides access to a variety of released and upcoming features. Should you choose to upgrade to Text Me That Pro (Monthly/Yearly) plan, payments will be charged to your iTunes account at the confirmation of purchase. Your subscription automatically renews unless cancelled at least 24 hours prior to the end of the current period. If cancelled, you will still have access for the remainder of the current period. Auto-renewal may be turned off in your Account Settings in iTunes after purchase. You can also manage your subscriptions within Text Me That’s account settings after purchase.

PJ Alford , 03/10/2020

Great App!

Due to the time change this past weekend I have been looking for something to replace the app I was using for medicine reminders.

I have a complicated and complex medicine schedule. The app I was using changed the times I had set up in it for reminders - moved them an hour ahead and the only way to change them back was manually. I did that for Sunday thinking that Monday would have the correct times. No - they were showing up an hour earlier again.

Wel...I began searching for a new app I could use for this purpose and found Text Me That. I gave it a try and found it does what it says right away. So, I signed up for a subscription immediately.

The one thing that I would like to see added to TMT is the ability to customize schedules. I have med that is to be every other day and so I’ve had to resort to using another app to remind me for it. It would be great if the developers would make the app where those of us who need it could specify that the reminder is every 2 days, every 3 days, etc.

As far as I’m concerned this is the only thing missing from an otherwise great app!
