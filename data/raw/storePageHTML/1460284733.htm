Description

Not sure what to watch next? Not sure if you'll like a new TV show? Let us help! Give us 30 seconds and we'll predict your new favorite TV shows with amazing accuracy.

Let's face it: TV has gone from simple to overwhelming. Viewers now have hundreds of channels and streaming services to choose from, offering an endless amount of choices to watch. Over 500 new scripted TV shows premiered in 2019 alone!

Ain’t nobody got time for that!

So how do you find good shows to watch? Which shows are worthy of your time? What shows should you watch next?

If you’re like most people, you usually waste over 10 minutes each day trying to find what to watch. We've all been there. Flipping from app to app... scrolling through watchlist after watchlist... adding more to your watchlist but never actually watching anything...

Then, when you can't find anything, give up and just binge-watch *insert standby show like The Office* yet again.

Here’s where Watchworthy comes in.

We Personalize TV Recommendations

Watchworthy starts with crowd-sourced data from over 200 million votes on Ranker.com, by people like you, voting on and ranking TV shows they like and don’t like. We then combine this data with fancy science and complicated math to predict and recommend TV shows you’ll love (or not love).

But wait… there’s more!

Find Your Next Binge

Not sure you want to invest time in a new show? Only to realize after 3 episodes that you just wasted 3 episodes worth of your time?

Watchworthy has you covered as your very own personalized TV recommender. Before you invest valuable time on a new show, simply look it up to see how watch-worthy it is based on your tastes and preferences.

Your Personal Streaming Guide

Watchworthy lets you set the services you subscribe to, like Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, and over 200 other streaming services, letting you filter out shows you don't have access to.

Build a master Watchlist combining all your watchlists from all the different apps and channels you subscribe to. You can even start watching shows straight from your Watchlist with a single tap.

As our reviewers are saying - Watchworthy is the app you didn’t know you were missing!

But don’t take our word for it.

Watch More and Browse Less In Seconds

Start with a fun, interactive 30-second quiz so Watchworthy can learn what you like and don’t like

Get your Worthy List of personalized TV show recommendations and add the shows you think are binge-worthy to your Watchlist

Each of the personalized matches on your Worthy List comes with a Worthy score based on how much we know you’ll like it

Rate more shows you’ve already seen to improve your recommendations

Filter your Worthy List by genre, streaming service, content ratings, and more

Connect the channels and streaming services you are subscribed to and easily watch shows on your device, or via screencast, with a simple tap

All this for free!

Isn’t it time you had a watchlist as unique as you? Stop wasting time looking for something to watch and spend more time actually watching.

Hit the Install button now to download Watchworthy and find your next binge!

I’ve gotten to the point where I needed a watchlist for my watchlist and Watchworthy has been a beacon of light in the clutter I call my queue.

AmandaJ625 , 05/09/2020

Amazing for recommendations

Why I like it: This app is amazing for recommendations. The worthy percentage is so helpful to know how well the app thinks the show matches with your viewing preferences. I am bingeing a series that wasn’t even on my radar based on the app’s recommendation and I’m loving it. Other apps that give recommendations either don’t seem personalized to my viewing preferences or they use bad algorithms.

Areas for improvement: This would be a 5 star app if the watchlist was improved upon. I need the app to allow me to mark episodes as watched. The watchlist should show the next episode in a series instead of an entire series. This would also stop the app from showing series that I’ve already completed in my worthy recommendations. Currently, since the app doesn’t have this feature, I have to use a different app to track my viewing. It would be nice if the detail page for a series would show other things you might like. For example, on the page for The Mentalist, it might suggest that others that liked this series enjoyed Psych. This would allow me to find things based on my favorite show and those suggestions should rate highly worthy for me anyway. I also wish that it contained movies and documentaries.
