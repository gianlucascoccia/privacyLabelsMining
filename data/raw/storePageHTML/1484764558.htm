Description

ATTENTION: Download this directly from your watch's App Store.

If you have downloaded it from your iPhone and can't find it on your watch, on your watch's App Store, scroll down to 'Account', tap on 'Purchased' then download Nomi!

--

Meet Nomi, a pet for your Apple Watch that you keep alive with your movement and exercise.

--

HOW TO PLAY:

- Fill the heart-ring with your steps and exercise
- Once a heart-ring is full, tap it to feed your Nomi
- Feed your Nomi before all 3 of its hearts run out
- Try to keep your Nomi alive for as many days as you can!
- Survive for long enough and your Nomi will continue to evolve!

What will yours become?

--

Nomi integrates with the Health app to measure your movements and steps. This data is never transferred off your device.

BenboBaggins8 , 10/14/2020

A great watch pet app with high potential

This is a fun little watch pet that brings back great memories of childhood tamagotchi days. The concept is superb to move around to feed Nomi as well as keeping the watch owner active and healthy.

I left 4/5 stars as the highly interactive creator who responds very frequently has room to make this even better. More engaging interactions with Nomi would be so much fun such as bathing/cleaning and “flushing” if you will, after Nomi goes to the bathroom, especially when young. Watering Nomi as well as having a “medicine” option when Nomi gets sick. The hearts can count for feeding Nomi so that’s fine. Petting and being interactive with Nomi could fill a “cared for” type of gage. Lastly fun games we could play with Nomi itself, not just exercise could be fun.

It new and I’m sure it’s complicated and takes time to add these types of interactions but none the less, it’s a great pet app and great visuals that bring back those nostalgic memories.

Developer Response ,

I'm so grateful for this. Thank you for all of these brilliant thoughts and ideas. We'll be sure to take them into our future brainstorms!

debbyo81 , 11/25/2020

Cute little buddy, stay active so they don’t die! :)

I’ve kept my Nomi alive for 95 days and counting - it has definitely motivated me to walk throughout the day or be active when I don’t quite have the motivation. Overall, well worth the minimal cost because it’s cute and fun. Love that there are continual updates and it keeps getting better.

Suggestions for the developer: is there a way to show time left before a heart gets empty? Or a reminder like “one hour left before Nomi dies” 🤣I would love to interact with it more somehow or would love if it read my exercise minutes to earn a heart for the “game” without having to start the exercise on the app. It really screws up my workout tracking / data to only set it for the 7-9 min at a time. Like if I could start a run and at the end, Nomi would tell me I earned 2 hearts worth based on HR during the run.

Update: I’m now on my 3rd Nomi and they’re just so cute and motivating. Sadly, the last one died at 147 days and I didn’t get the “one hour left” notification - I think because I was asleep and watch was set to Apples new do-not-disturb sleep setting. Excited for the new evolution paths now with my newest Nomi!! Love the updates, great job!! Can’t wait to find some friends!

Developer Response ,

Hi debbyo81, we're currently working on figuring out how we can manage and build the 'banking' of hearts so it may be something in a future update.
Regarding warnings and how long it has left until the heart disappears, you should receive notifications when you lose a heart and when your heart is full (when its ready to feed Nomi) if you're not getting these please check out your notification settings. Each heart should last around 8 hours and goes down 1/4 of a heart each couple of hours. Hope this helps and thanks so much for the review!

Selurple , 04/14/2020

A great little app, perfect for the watch

This is the perfect kind of app for the Apple Watch. Simple, fun, and motivational, playing to the strengths of the platform. Very appealing visuals and a clever concept; staying healthy yourself keeps your little buddy alive too.

This isn’t the kind of thing that needs many features - the beauty is in its simplicity, and the very reasonable price reflects that - but further integration with the health app in some way (heart rate/exercise tracking) would be nice right now during these quarantine times when we’re still trying to exercise but can’t get those steps in. I also second other users ideas about a tiny bit more interactivity; everyone who plays this is going to instinctively try to poke/pet the little guy, and that tiny bit of extra interaction would go a long way.

Overall, great app and I hope the developer continues to support it because it fills the perfect niche on the watch.

Heartwarming feedback like this just makes it worth all the effort! We've worked so hard to keep it minimal.

In our latest update (which it looks like you have installed) allows you to select between Steps and Active Energy (kJ) the next time you hatch a Nomi. We're also currently obsessing about finding more ways for you to interact with your Nomi.

Stay tuned and thanks once again for the incredibly kind words.
