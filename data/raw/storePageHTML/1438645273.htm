Description

The Gainesville Times app delivers real-time content specially formatted for your mobile device. Stay in touch with the latest developments in Northeast Georgia and beyond with stories, photos and video provided by our team of journalists with more than 200 years of combined experience.

The app is free to download, and all users can access a sampling of Gainesville Times articles every 30 days. Print and digital subscribers receive unlimited access.

Gainesville Times digital subscription options:

- Unlimited Digital (Monthly): Enjoy 1 month of unlimited access to The Gainesville Times for ONLY 99 cents. After the first month, your subscription will automatically renew at $9.49 per month. Cancel anytime!

Your subscription comes with complimentary web access. Download today to begin your news reading experience.

Payment will be charged to your iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase. Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period at the rate of the selected plan. Subscriptions and auto-renewal may be managed by going to Account Settings after purchase. Any unused portion of a free trial period will be forfeited when purchasing a subscription.
