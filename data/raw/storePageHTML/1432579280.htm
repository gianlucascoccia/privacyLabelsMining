Ty Younce , 10/23/2020

Play for hours!

Angry Birds Dream Blast is my favorite phone game. Like other reviewers, I have gotten super frustrated on higher levels and ended up spending money to buy bonuses. Maybe a little too much! I am really enjoying Dream Peak and love the Halloween Pass and the similar one a few months ago. However, I noticed in the description it says pass rewards can be claimed when you want to claim them. That was my hope when I purchased the premium pass. ($2.99 for all those bonuses—totally worth it!) But every time I hit the x, it automatically claims the bonuses. I ended up playing for like five hours last night and still lost a lot of my infinite lives time that I wish I could’ve saved for another day. When I got the first Halloween update, I was also having issues with birds freezing when they were going to explode, but I did a second update last night which seemed to fix it. Lastly, honestly having all the bonus birds for several hours, which I got as a gift today, can sometimes hurt more than help. When there’s so many, they all explode at the top, making you lose any birds you’ve earned, and don’t help as you go through the level.

Feels like I’m being conned....

A person can get addicted to this game, the competitive feeling one gets can lead you to spend TOO MUCH MONEY on coins just to place and in the end if you’re lucky you might get coins (small amount) or a single power up. I enjoy the game but I wish it didn’t feel so rigged. This is not meant to offend you but it is insane how you might get through 2-3 levels then get stuck on some levels that you basically have to purchase coins and power ups. Now the amount that you receive for the price you pay is very little. To continue the level costs 100 coins. So after spending an embarrassing amount of money I feel like I’ve been swindled. Maybe you can come up with a better system for obtaining more coins and look at the fees you charge. I can spend $23 on my very popular poker game app and receive $4+ BILLION coins. I get 1000 on Dream Blast.....hope you all can make some improvements to help your customers. I am thankful for the 3 hours free play you’re gifting us ATM but like I said some levels are set up in a way that can take a person all the free play hours PLUS much more time and money.

I forgot to ask if I could get a refund???? It in the hundreds. Ugh.

PJ from CA , 04/07/2019

Okay for awhile

Do you actually think there will never be a person that will play your game that might actually have just a little intelligence. I have played your game for a week now and here is my assessment. Forgive me if I am wrong, but the game play is trivial based on the players willingness to play. You set your winning and loosing in a pattern. You let you players win a few matches to build the confidence only to bate them into a special purchase to continue their streak. I get that, Angry Birds is a household name, but don’t think that there aren’t people out there that have the intelligence enough to see the pattern of game play. Your game play is pretty transparent. The physics change according to how many times the person has tried a specific level. If a person doesn’t pass a level a certain many of times, the playing will be adjusted to offset their efforts to ensure the player doesn’t lose interest. Once the interest to continue playing has been established, you revert to more challenging physics in the hope the player will spend money buying coinage. This cycle repeats over and over. The bottom line is this does not feel like a game. This feels like a fishing derby and we’re the fish. I have played 100 or so levels, but I can’t really say I am enjoying it, because of what I have stated above. Not this time Rovio!
