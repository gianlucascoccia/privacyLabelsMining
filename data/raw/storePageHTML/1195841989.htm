Description

Run, jump, fly and restore the colors of the world with all your favorite ponies!

Budge Studios™ presents My Little Pony™ Rainbow Runners! A powerful color-stealing spell has been unleashed on Ponyville and it’s up to your favorite ponies to break it with the power of Friendship! Run, jump, awaken your ponies’ Rainbow Powers and use their epic abilities to bring the colors back to the world!

A COLOR FILLED ADVENTURE
• PLAY exciting missions all over Ponyville!
• RUN to close the color-stealing portals, one platform at a time!
• JUMP, FLY and SLIDE to avoid obstacles, holes and muddy puddles!
• TRANSFORM your ponies into their epic Rainbow Power form!
• COLLECT Pony Power and upgrade your ponies’ Rainbow Powers
• EMBARK on a personal quest with each pony

6 PONIES, EACH WITH THEIR OWN RAINBOW POWER AND SUPER FORM

• APPLEJACK: Turns obstacles into apple sauce with her Apple Strike
• FLUTTERSHY: Glides gracefully over and above anything with her Super Flight

Samuel Pierce , 07/06/2017

Not bad...Just Needs A Little Work

Overall I found myself enjoying the time I spent playing this app. The levels start out really easy, but gradually become a little more challenging later on. This gives the game a very nice difficulty curve that never spikes up at random.

I also like how the Rainbow Powers grow and change as you upgrade the two characters available. This keeps the upgrades from becoming too stale and gives the player something to strive for.

However, I do have to agree that putting a pay wall between the player and the other characters is a little bit much. So here's what I recommend:
Allow the player to play a demo version of the locked characters within their specific levels. This way the player isn't barred from the rest of the game, but you can still sell the characters for $2.99.

Alternatively, you could create 6 new characters and shift the price tag onto them, thus freeing up the main characters and giving the player full access to the game, while still having a way to make money from the game.

I hope you - Budge Games - will take my recommendations into consideration, and I look forward to hearing from you.
