Description

TechPort allows you to keep up to date with technology news and updates from the best news sources online such as The Verge, TechCrunch, Wired, Ars Technica, The Next Web and many more.

Main features include:
- Over 60 news sources
- Easy to use interface
- Dark and light themes
- Text size customization
- Saving favorite articles
- Adding custom sources
- Handoff support
- VoiceOver support
- And many more

Enjoy TechPort for your technology news and updates!

Toj Mora , 12/06/2017

This app is a beast!

Within seconds I realized this app was made for me. The brutally intuitive design instantly answered some major complaints I've had about other news apps. I literally went to my phone's home screen and deleted an entire folder filled with now useless tech news apps.

• For those who complain about having to open Safari to read full articles, I am sure there's plenty of hoops to jump through to legally get full-length articles displayed - and it'd probably mean more ads or a higher premium. For 2 bucks to remove the already unobtrusive ads, this is a steal!

• Also, using Safari means I can open the article in reader mode (Safari's night mode) - which for some dumb reason - other iOS browsers don't have this feature. Yes, I know you can use smart invert, but in many apps it inverts images too.

• I cannot overstate the ease of use for this app. At any time within the app, with one tap (or swipe) you can view the long list of major tech news publications. You can switch from list to thumbnail view. You can add favorites, etc.

It's brilliantly simple. Stop reading this stupid review and go download it before this app blows up and the developer charges for it. I'd pay 5 bucks for this app, easy.
