A fun new way to experience romance novels!

Each book released on Crave is serialized into bite-sized segments that are sent to you on a daily basis over the course of 30 days, offering a fun new way to enjoy romance novels without having to commit to hours at a time. You’ll get a notification each day when the next chapter is available, and a new encounter with the leading man awaits. The first week is free. After that you can purchase the book to continue the experience.

• Meet your next book boyfriend (this is really happening!)
• Get exclusive extras from the lead character and author including instant notifications, polls, quizzes, videos, photos, audio, and more.
• Message the author with questions (yes, for real)
• Enjoy books from bestselling authors

---------------------------------------------------------

Purchase a book for only $9.99 and receive the story over the course of 30 days. There's no limit to the number of books you can purchase at once. You can even read tomorrow's chapter today by referring Crave to friends or by purchasing credits. Payment will be charged to your iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase.
