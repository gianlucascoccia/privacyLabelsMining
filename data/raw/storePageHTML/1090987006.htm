LisaBdisappointed , 06/02/2020

Too many (bad) changes

I signed up for this app a long time ago, and the whole appeal of it was that you could pick some favorite stores you want to earn points at and then passively earn those points when you used your linked card at that store. You didn’t have to go into the app, complete an offer, etc. you could “set it and forget it”. That’s completely gone now, as they rather quietly did away with power offers and replaced them with “flash deals” which basically forces you to search for deals and purchase through the app, like Rakuten. Except the point value awarded is usually worth less than the cash back you’d get through Rakuten or a similar app, so it’s not worth it to use Drop.

Now it also offers points for completing surveys. The trouble with that is wasted time for surveys that disqualify you after you’ve already completed most of or in some case the entire thing. I’ve literally been “disqualified” on the thank you screen at the end more times than I can count. The surveys say you’ll be given “partial credit” if you’re disqualified during the survey, but I’ve found that to be true only about 60% of the time, and if you do get the partial credit it’s a measly 20 points. Not worth the time. The app also says each completed survey puts you a step closer to the little “snake game” they do to earn extra points, but that’s just a blatant lie. I’ve done several surveys and never got any progress toward a game reward.

Hey Lisa, thanks very much for all your feedback and we're sorry to hear that you're not a fan of the new platform features. If you reach out to our support team at support@joindrop.com, we'd be happy to apply a Snake Game to your Drop account to make up for the missed ones! Please be sure to reference this review to help us keep track of your case. Looking forward to hearing from you! :)
