Crashes too much

I like this game. Especially after a long day it’s just a great way for my mind to decompress. But it is super frustrating that it crashes all the time. Just when your finally about to beat a level...BAM... whole app shuts down and restarts.
Another problem for me are the sound on the ads. Sometimes I play in the middle of the night if I can’t sleep. If I forget to turn my phone down the ad comes blaring through between games. It would be nice if the ad volume was silenced if the game settings are set to silenced.
Please fix both these issues and I will gladly give 5 stars.
