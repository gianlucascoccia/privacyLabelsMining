Description

Educational Employees Credit Union (EECU) – California

The EECU mobile app allows you to access your money – anywhere, anytime. It’s fast, free and secure!

With EECU mobile banking you can:
•Check your balances
•View transaction history
•View check images
•Transfer funds
•Make bill payments
•View and activate your cash back offers
•Deposit checks
•Manage Purchase Rewards
•Find EECU locations and CO-OP ATMs.

Login to this app using your existing Online Access User ID and Password. If you do not have EECU Online Access, apply today at myEECU.org.



To learn how we protect your privacy, please visit https://www.myeecu.org/privacy
