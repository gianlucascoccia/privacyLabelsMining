Description

Learn how to write the alphabet and numbers with [Pinkfong Tracing World] app!

Let your kids master their ABCs and numbers with Pinkfong Baby Shark's best videos first, and then learn how to write them with fun tracing activities.
Interactive games and activities will also help kids master the alphabet, numbers, patterns, and shapes in a fun and easy way.
Let your kids improve their handwriting skills and associate with phonics with this educative app!

[Features]

1. Fun & Educative Videos for Kids
- Watch videos and learn the alphabet, numbers, and shapes in a fun way.
- Discover and navigate the different stations, including the rocket station, under the sea, crunchy land, and more!
- Learn how to differentiate between uppercase and lowercase alfabet.

2. Over 100 Tracing Activities
- Trace the different characters with the adorable aliens, sea animals, and jelly beans.
- It's easy to trace, with friendly guides for little children.

3. Interactive Alphbet and Number Games
- Includes kids' favorite bubble and card games.
- Review your ABCs, numbers, and shapes with fun and easy mini games.

4. Available in 6 Languages
- Watch and play the games in English, Spanish, French, Chinese, Korean, and Japanese.

All kids are invited to [Pinkfong Tracing World]!
