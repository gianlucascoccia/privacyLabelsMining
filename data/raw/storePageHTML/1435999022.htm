Description

Easily manage your mortgage online with the NewRez app. View account details, apply for a new loan, choose payment options, access important documents, and more!

The app can be used for current NewRez loans serviced by Shellpoint Mortgage Services or new loan applications. For existing LoanCare and PHH serviced loans, please refer to your loan servicing portal for additional options.

Download the NewRez app to:

View a snapshot of your current loan, including account details, recent activity, and payment info.
Apply for a new loan and check the status from application through funding.
Make a one-time payment, view pending payments, or schedule recurring payments.
Access statements and documents, including monthly statements, 1098 yearly statements, and tax and insurance documents.


Who is NewRez, LLC?

NewRez LLC (NewRez) is a leading nationwide mortgage lender and servicer. As a lender, NewRez focuses on offering a breadth of industry-leading products, supported by a loan process that blends both human interaction and the benefits of technology into an unparalleled customer experience. Founded in 2008, NewRez is headquartered in Fort Washington, Pennsylvania and operates multiple lending channels, including Correspondent Lending, Wholesale, Direct-to-Consumer, Retail, and a network of joint venture partners. The servicing business operates through NewRez’s servicing division, which consists of its performing loan servicing division, NewRez Servicing, and its servicing division, Shellpoint Mortgage Servicing. NewRez is a member of the New Residential Investment Corp. family. More information is available at www.newrez.com.
