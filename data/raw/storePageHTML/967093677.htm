Good for watching tv, but has some glitches

This app is pretty good for watching shows that can be found on the tv channels, but there’s a few glitches that bother me.
The ads are fine in my opinion, I mean like how else they gonna keep the app free, right?
But these glitches make it so I can’t even watch what I want to. I was in the middle of watching, and the ads come on, like they always do, and I paused it for a minute to go do something, I come back and my iPad screen had turned off, so I turned it back on and when I tried to unpause it, it unpaused for like 2 seconds, then the ads stopped playing and the screen went black. I could tap the screen and the buttons would come up, play/pause, done etc, but when I tried to make it play it wouldn’t. I tried countless times, restarting the app, waiting some, resetting where I was, rewatching the episode until that set of ads, nothing has worked. Every time I got to that particular set of ads, the app just seemed to stop working completely, and crash.
Another one is sometimes, if I pause or go out of the app during the ads, when I come back the play button doesn’t work, however, just restarting the app fixes this one.

If this is fixed, or I find a fix for this issue, I’ll edit this. However for now staying with 3 stars at the moment.

App_5t0r3_N1cknam3 , 12/26/2020

AirPlay Fails if Auto-Lock is Not Disabled

The big strongpoint of this app is that you can watch the shows with it. For an app to do what it is primarily designed for is expected. The worst flaw of this app is that in order to watch a show using AirPlay you need to turn off Auto-Lock in display settings. I have no idea how this hasn’t been fixed. I turn off Auto-Lock to watch shows with my Apple TV and then turn it back on when I am finished. This should be easily fixable with an update. Several people write off that bugs like this are to be expected because it’s a free app. I don’t feel it’s at all free with the number of ads I have to view when watching shows through the app. Someone is getting the ad revenue. I’m just stating that I would be more inclined to watch more if they fixed this feature.

Black_Pearl 21 , 01/27/2021

Not User Friendly

Great app in terms of the number of shows. So far on the TV series I am viewing, adverts come at reasonable intervals, much like live commercial television. What confounds me is finding a watch list or favorite show list and sending feedback. I also had the devil of a time figuring out how to enable closed captioning (fortunately for me, so did many, many others, so that answer appears in the Q&A). The “settings” do not have user tools. When I watch an episode of a series, I don’t see a way to either halt autoplay of the next or return to the series’ page.

Would be nice for this app to display a few of the standard features other free streaming services provide that allows viewers to customize their experience.
