Description

Simple & powerful video editor for Youtube, Tik Tok & Vlogging! Merge Multiple videos & photos into a single video easily!

Features
- SPEED: Adjust clip or video speed.
- ROTATION: Rotate video or photos every useful way.
- IMAGE SUPPORT: Now you can add image to make slide show video. Make an awesome video combining videos & images.
- FILTERS: Give a new look to the videos or photos with beautiful filters.
- THEMES: Select from the custom themes.
- MUSIC: Browse our 1000+ collection of music and choose from different categories.
- TRIM: Trim video clips precisely.
- TRANSITIONS: Apply transitions between videos.
- SPLIT: New split tool is to divide videos into multiple clips.
- RESIZE/CROP: Resize & Crop videos easily!
- FLIP HORIZONTAL/VERTICAL: Flip videos or photos horizontally or vertically.
- Full HD Output: Now support for full 1080p video output.
- VIDEO ORIENTATION: Set video orientation to Landscape, Portrait and Square.
- SHARE: Share videos to social media like Facebook, Instagram & Vine!
