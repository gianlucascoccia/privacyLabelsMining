Description

Design your dream home, create your character with fashion, and start playing with friends today!

Highlights:

⁃Design rooms in your home. Redecorate or expand for endless possibilities!
⁃Level up to unlock new features in the game.
⁃Earn money by completing jobs downtown.
⁃Take the tram to downtown to spend an afternoon shopping or discovering new neighbors!
⁃Sell items in your shop or browse other player’s for great deals.
⁃Visit other player’s houses to leave comments and ‘like’ their rooms.
⁃New furniture, home decor, and clothes are constantly added to the shops!

(Neighbors is free to play, though some in-game items can also be purchased for real money. If you don’t wish to use this option, simply turn it off in your device’s Restrictions menu.)

Follow along on Instagram @Play_Neighbors for a chance to have your room featured on our page, to vote on what you’d like to see next in the game, and so much more!

Losing Interest

This game is such a great concept but it seems that the developers can’t keep up with the players. I made it to level 43 but as I have exhausted all of the decor items in the shop there seems to be no point in adding anymore to my house as they all start to look the same after awhile. I love the items that have been added in the outside category but there hasn’t been any new items in a couple of weeks and at this point I just log in to collect money and start a new job. I also understand the long wait times for rooms to build as the developers hope the user gets bored and wants to buy coins to finish the build sooner. But to be honest it costs a LOT of coins to complete builds. And the wait times for the builds are getting in the realms of 18 hour waits. I wish that they’d let you decorate at least a couple rooms at a time but again I understand that’s where they are hoping the user spends actual money to play the game. I also would like for the game to let me unlike a room cause it’s definitely easy to accidentally like a room. Anyways good concept just getting boring. Nice game to play for a couple weeks at the most.

Update: Had to lower my review to two stars as the game has been really glitchy over the last week. Rooms won’t load, grass in outdoor rooms is not loading, the game freezes often. Very disappointed that these issues are not being resolved.
