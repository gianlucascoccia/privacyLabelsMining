Description

A one-stop-shop for all super moms to organize their daily chores. Create to-dos, schedule meals, maintain grocery lists, find recipes & coupons & do a lot more!

Are you a mom and is your daily routine taking a toll on you? Well, worry no more! Mom’s Daily Planner is here to help you manage yourself and your family in the simplest way possible. This app offers a comprehensive suite of features for moms to keep themselves and their families organized.

Moms Daily Planner is an extremely user-friendly app and it comes packed with over 10 awesome features to help you out with your day-to-day schedule.

Calendar
◆ Keep a track of everyone’s schedule in the family with Mom’s Daily Planner’s feature-rich calendar
◆ Set reminders, write notes and view your to-do tasks with your calendar entries simultaneously
◆ View your calendar in any format (Day, Month or List)

Meal Planner
◆ Schedule all meals for your family using our specialized meal calendar
◆ Easily label different meals as breakfast, lunch, supper or dinner
◆ Select a variety of meal options from the cookbook or simply write your own recipes

Grocery List
◆ Maintain a master list for your everyday grocery items
◆ Use our built-in menu or add items manually to fill in your grocery needs swiftly
◆ Create and update separate lists for each store to avoid any confusion

Budget
◆ Create monthly and annual budgets on the app to track your income and expenses easily
◆ Conveniently copy the previous year’s budget to get a head start

To-Do List
◆ Set alarms or on-screen audible alerts so that you never miss out on a task
◆ Smoothly cross off the tasks that you have completed

Cookbook
◆ Choose from over 200 exquisite recipes
◆ Find simple and easy to cook recipes and meal ideas for your family
◆ Feel free to add your own recipes to the cookbook and bookmark your favorite recipes from the web

Sync & Share
◆ Effortlessly sync Moms Daily Planner across your iPhone or iPad devices
◆ Sync the app’s calendar with your built-in iPhone or iPad calendar

Chores
◆ Assign chores for yourself and the family with the tap of a button
◆ Add chores to the app’s calendar and view them on the device’s calendar simultaneously
◆ Put alerts & reminders on the app to keep a track of everyone’s chores

Coupons (US markets only)
◆ Redeem various coupons and save on major brand names
◆ Make use of the new coupons that are refreshed every week

Birthday Reminders
◆ Add important birthdays to Moms Daily Planner and let it remind you to wish your loved ones
◆ Simply keep a track of all the birthdays as they are added to your calendar automatically

◆◆Our home screen badges inform you about due dates beforehand and regular push notifications remind you to check for new coupons and other value-added features.

ESAET , 02/25/2016

Free version too basic, does not inspire upgrade

From what I can tell this may be a great app for scheduling a busy family. I did not try the calendar features since my kids a re a little less active these days. I was hoping it would b a great one stop app once they get busy again. I might have upgraded and used it but I don't see how the meal planning potion of the app is functional or even useful with the limited recipes and no way to add additional recipes. If that is a features available in the meal planning upgrade it should be specified. A trial of that option (a set number of days or upload 5 recipes for example) would be helpful in deciding to upgrade. I already use mealboard for planning meals and they are uploaded to my regular apple calendar. The to do list and calendar provided by Apple are also sufficient for my needs and have additional options this to do list lacked like snoozing items, location alerts etc. I hope there are more options in the full version that make it worth the upgrade but from what I can see it isn't anything significantly better than what I can already do with the standard built in apps and one additional app on my devices.

J@3C1 , 07/16/2015

Almost there!

I find this app very nice. I appreciate all the sections because it combines my lists into fewer more organized ones. It could be a little more user friendly.
My suggestions:
1) recipe section- I can cook, I'd love to add some of my family favorite recipes to personalize the section so I add to the calendar what I actually want to eat not all new experimental recipes for me. Even just being able to add the name would be nice. With that, it would be nice if the grocery list were easier to navigate. Like checking off groceries instead of deleting them so you can double check the list or if you could add prices next to it to keep in mind your budget, etc.
2) I would like multiple views of the calendar with all the hours listed as an Option. I like to plan out my day a little more in depth, so for instance if there was a day view with set plans (like appointments involving other people) and other plans (like work on chores during this time) and then a back up plan section that would be fabulous!
A normal back button would also be nice.

I have only had this app a few days, maybe a week, and I really like the idea. I just don't use it as much as I would like to because I find it difficult to navigate. I like to have a little bit more steady of a routine like Monday grocery shopping etc and it's difficult to insert that in this app.
However, I definitely find this app as a good start to a magnificent thing. Thank you!
