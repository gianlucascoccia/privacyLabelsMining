Description

Nanit speaks baby.
Understand your baby’s days and nights. Nanit’s camera uses something called computer vision. Nanit learns how your baby moves, and tells you if they’re fussy, awake or sleeping like a dream.

Understand their night and conquer sleep.
Nanit Insights helps you identify sleep issues, so you can adjust and get back on track fast. Nanit tracks—and understands—sleep patterns, parent visits, room conditions and much more.

Your new morning briefing.
Every morning Nanit Insights delivers last night’s highlight reel. Plus a Sleep Score. So you’ll know instantly if baby’s sleep is improving.

Savvyvt , 07/24/2020

Parents Love Sleep Data

My husband and I fell in love with this product just reading about it, but now that we’ve been using it a couple of weeks, we love it even more. Our daughter started sleeping in her crib the first night we came home from the hospital. We keep our Fire 8 Plus in our room on loud with the app running in the background so we don’t burn the battery on our phones at night. There is barely a second of delay. When our daughter cries you hear her first and then an echo through the app. The key is getting the band situated correctly each night. We were using the band in the beginning and swaddling her (which you’re not supposed to do). We got a red alert on the second night (the most terrifying noise to wake up to). Our daughter was fine and breathing but the layers from the swaddle and band must have made the reading difficult. I will say the red alert will give you some false alarms which I would rather have happen than not. However, all in all we’ve really enjoyed watching the nightly summaries that are produced the following morning to see how many times our daughter woke up, for how long, and how long it took us to get back to sleep after a feeding. Additionally, we love that you can turn the camera off when it’s not in use so we keep ours turned off during the day. Last, but not least, when the time comes that we need to travel to visit family, I love that we can take the camera with us and utilize the stand for traveling. Such a great feature!

Best thing ever.

I have 4 kids. My kids have all co slept with me except for my 4th. I was ready to have my bed back and my sleep back. I never put the other 3 in their own room due to fear. With this monitor my daughter has been sleeping in her own room since she turned 3 months. She has slept so amazing and the advice I have gotten from the app made the transition that much smoother. I will sit in my room with my husband watching our show while I watch her sleep. I love watching her put herself to sleep. I have been able to put her back to sleep by shhhhshing her from the intercom. I have captured moments between her and her father and her brothers that I saw when they thought I wasn’t watching. I am going to be purchasing the breathing band and the sleep sack as I think having that additional peace of mind knowing her breathing will take this to the next level for me. My sisters in different states can watch her sleep. My mom can watch her. My husband can talk to her when he’s at work through the intercom if she wakes up while I shower. I absolutely love this monitor. 10/10 recommend. My only suggestion would be to be able add in when they fall asleep and for how long for better sleep Analysis. She doesn’t always sleep in her crib and if I don’t have her in her crib it will say that I put her to sleep late when she infect is just asleep in her crib in my room. But still the best thing ever!

JezsC , 03/03/2020

Best Monitor

Even thought we have had a few “connection” issues. This has been by far the best investment we have made for ourselves and our now toddler.
I love that I am able to see our baby’s room/crib no matter the distance. I have gone travel for work and still able to see and tell my daughter good night thought the monitor and app. I love the temperature and humidity features in the app, specially on those days my baby is sick. The integrated light is a ++ specially for those late night and early mornings and middle of the night accidents in bed. Just enough light to see but not too much to fully wake baby up. The integrated white noise feature is pretty amazing too, if for some reason I forget to turn on our white noise machine and I’m out of the room already, I don’t have to go back in and turn the machine on, I can take advantage of the app and monitor and turn on the build in white noise. Pretty awesome.
Lastly, the different stands make the minutos adaptable .. you are able to move it around if needed with the stand or set it up in the wall with minimal effort. Super easy to install. I would definitely be getting a second one and also use the breathing and heart rate feature that comes with the extra accessory the monitor provide. I wish I got this monitor sooner.
