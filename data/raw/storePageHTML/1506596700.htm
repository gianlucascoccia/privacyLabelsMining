Apple TV App Problems

I’ve been watching The First’s live stream on Apple TV through Pluto, but wanted to see some of their other shows on demand, so I downloaded the app. The app opens to the live broadcast, and these is a note at the bottom of the screen to press the menu button to return to the home section. However, when I press either the menu or home buttons, the app closes. When I press the menu button, the screen flashes before closing the app, which appears to be a crash. I tried reloading the app and cycling the power on my Apple TV, but nothing changed. So, I’m no better off with the app than I was watching on Pluto.
I still gave the app three stars because the mobile version works perfectly on my phone. I like that the audio still plays while the phone is locked.
