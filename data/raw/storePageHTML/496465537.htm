This is an extremely minimalist whiteboard app that brings convenience to your work, life and studies.

The app is applicable to a wide range of scenarios like creating drafts, board drawings, doodling and making demonstrations. You can also use it as your notepad, temporary notebook, or do dictation and arithmetic on it. Additionally, it is also suitable for scenarios like family education, school teaching, conference room presentation, sports coaching, on-spot guidance, teaching and children’s drawing. The most surprising and touching aspect of the app, as articulated by the thanks letters we received from speech-impaired users, is that the product is tremendously helpful in assisting them with communicating with others.

*Easy-to-use
1. Write on-screen with one finger;
2. Enlarge or shrink background images with two fingers;
3. Lightly double-tap the Apple Pencil to display or hide the Eraser.

*Simplistic Interface
The entire interface is comprised of only a canvas, four commonly-used colors, three pen stroke sizes and a few function buttons.

*Rich Features
1. The fourth “Pen” at the bottom: allows you to select an infinite number of colors;
2. The “Background Setting” button at the bottom: allows you to select an infinite number of colors apart from providing many useful background templates;
3. The third “Stroke Size” at the bottom: allows you to further select and customize more stroke sizes and save them as defaults;
4. The “Personal Folder” button on the left hand side of the screen allows you to save the file you have edited to the personal folder, or merge and export your file in PDF format and share it with your friends;
5. The “Tool” button on the left hand side of the screen provides a number of simple sketching tools and some special pens like color pens, brush pens and markers. The button also allows you to create special settings to prevent gesture disturbance while using the Apple Pencil, or settings for watermarks.
6. Sketches can be directly converted into HD images, saved in your album or shared on social media network;

RxIxVxAxL , 07/14/2020

Helpful! Just one thing I noticed...

I was hoping to find an app such as this one to keep track of things. I barely check my notes and reminders, and this would surely get my attention better.

After a few minutes of trying this out, I already enjoyed the options given. This is a simple whiteboard app that you can doodle on if you wish, but there is no layers. I’m fine with that. I can use the app for much more stuff!

However, I was checking the reviews and saw a review about the eraser didn’t work that well. And whilst the review was updated and the problem had been fixed, I found another issue with it.

Every time you use the eraser, it switched back to a marker. Not a big deal since there is an undo button, but I think it’d be better for that not to happen. It’s a bit of a pet peeve of mine, and it’s a little tough to remember at the beginning of figuring it out. This is just an issue I didn’t really like, and people probably already grew to remember it.

This is just a suggestion of a little pet peeve I had. I apologize if I sound demanding. It is the devs’ option and I do believe some, if not many people would agree that the eraser is just a little tough.

Other than that, great app! I really like the tools and the Apple Pencil compatibility! I have no other suggestions, this is a great and simple app I would probably use in the upcoming future!

EDIT: Thanks for the information! I’ll definitely change that! Thank you!
