Description

Preview art on your walls at home with a few simple taps. Add frames, add custom walls and upload your own photos or art to see how it looks, export it and share it.

Join thousands of art collectors and galleries in changing how you visualize art at home and on the go.

Download ARTSEE and take a few pictures of the blank walls you’d like to fill with art. ARTSEE makes shooting and sizing the walls easy. With ARTSEE on your phone, you can take photos of artwork anywhere, superimposing them on your walls at home, creating artwork wall previews that we call “hangups”. You can easily export your creations or share them with your partner, friends or family.

==========================

ARTSEE Features:

• Add custom walls into the app. ARTSEE makes scaling and sizing a breeze.
• Browse art from dozens of high-end galleries worldwide and save pieces to your own library.
• Add art of your own liking into the app by snapping a picture or uploading it.
• Choose from dozens of frames that you can easily add on to your custom artwork previews.
• Export your created previews in high quality, or share them directly with friends and family.
• A beautiful new look and feel, fully optimized for the newest devices with vibrant colors and a smooth user experience.

Getting started is simple. Download the app now!

==========================

How ARTSEE works:

When you see a piece of art you like at an Art Fair or a local Art Gallery, add it to your ARTSEE library using the camera on your device. Select an empty wall from the options provided in the app or an empty wall in your home and preview the artwork you’ve chosen.

You can share this hangup with friends, family or your interior designer and see what they say before buying the art piece.

Interesting new art-finding app that will only get better

This app has a really cool premise. For art collectors and aficionados alike, you can upload images of bare walls in your house and see how different artwork will look on them. It also has a pretty big directory of artists in order to discover new pieces, and not only gives you the gallery information of where it can be found, but allows you to contact them through the app. As of right now, you can't do direct purchases, but it seems like this app is still in development and I imagine there will be many new features soon. In the meantime, it's still a fun way to find new artwork and test out how it'll look hanging in your living room.
