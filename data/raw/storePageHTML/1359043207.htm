Description

Whether you are at home with the family, on a road trip or camping with friends… Thousands of catchy tunes await you at VOCA!

Sometimes you just NEED to sing. You want to express yourself by belting out your favorite songs. Take a couple of deep breaths and unleash your inner rockstar.

At VOCA, we believe that when the heart wants to sing, the mind should set it free! We’ve created an application that helps the beginning vocalist. Get discovered online or rock the house at karaoke.

What are you waiting for?
Millions of potential fans are awaiting your show! VOCA will put you at center stage for the performance of a lifetime. See what your personal recording studio can do for you:

Want a New Look?
We’ve selected a collection of inspirational masks to create your onstage persona (or just for fun and a laugh).

Forgot How It Goes?
Let VOCA guide you to the beat! We’ll lead you through your favorite tunes by highlighting lyrics and using animated pauses so you’ll feel like a natural.

Need More Confidence In Your Vocals?
Use our voice effects to sound like a star!

Looking For A Song To Sing?
We’ve compiled a convenient catalog of all your favorites. Check out our selection of everything from modern pop hits to the oldies you remember from your Sweet 16.
