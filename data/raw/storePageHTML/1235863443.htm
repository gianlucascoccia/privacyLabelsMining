adam.braley , 08/06/2019

Mixed feelings

On the one hand, it is impressive that they got this thing to play on mobile and I love Civ 6. On the other, it’s annoying that after buying all the expansions on Steam I’m being asked to buy them again here. $20 is a fair price, but I wish they would have credited me the expansions. Grumble, grumble.

At the moment I’m writing this, I’m also annoyed that I have been forced to stop playing a game I really liked because of a glitch preventing me from recruiting a great prophet and ending my turn. I had a similar problem once with diplomacy which turned out to be bypassed by going back a few turns and initiative the same diplomacy option myself instead of letting them ask for it, but in this case no matter what I do (complete with gold, reduce my points per turn to recruit later, etc) it appear impossible to either recruit the prophet or pass entirely. I really liked this map so I may just load back to an early turn and not build a holy site. I guess this is a known bug they plan to fix in the next patch. Sigh.

On the whole, I do recommend Civ 6. If you can, play it on PC. If you can’t or want it to go then get this and the expansions. Nubia is OP for early domination btw. Gotta love archers that move like scouts.

What is the point of a game you can’t even play properly let alone play to its full potential. I have other full AAA games ported over that play fine. Side note 3-4 minute wait times to load a map are ridiculous particularly with the crashing.

Electro Junky , 12/16/2019

Just One Bug

Probably the best gaming experience I’ve ever had on iPhone, or from any mobile game at all. It’s beautiful, it’s easy to navigate, it’s responsive and it’s well worth every penny. I love the long stand offs against my opponent, fending off every battle and making temporary peace just so I can work my way up to thermonuclear weapons and...well it’s all hell fire from there. One thing though...one very important fix that NEEDS to be made. As I said, I enjoy using the nuclear option. It’s just that it also nukes my game in the process. I of course want to witness the destructive power of the atom in all its classic civilization style glory. But if my camera is aimed at the event, it freezes. And boom, done, that’s it. Game frozen and I have to close the entire app and go back to the save, use the nuke again except this time I can’t watch the city burn to a crisp. This also happens when attack enemy units that carry nuclear weapons, and at that point there isn’t enough time between pressing attack and it actually happening for me to move the camera so as not to crash the game. Please please PLEASE fix this. This is a game breaking bug for me, this ruins the whole experience of the game, and this is probably my favorite strategy game of all time. Please fix this :(((((
