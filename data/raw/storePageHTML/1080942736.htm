It’s ok.

Well, it does what it says, as the many apps exactly like it...it allows you to slow down your video. That’s all the features you get for the free version. I don’t want to talk badly of the app, because yes...that’s its purpose..and it does it. However one thing I find very strange after trying a bunch of these apps.

Why is it that all of these camera apps all have the exact same FREE features, and the exact same PAID features?? Just strange to me.

I really get annoyed when an app claims its a free app, but 50% of it is locked behind a paywall...it just feels...dishonest in a way? Don’t advertise a “FREE APP” with features in the preview that actually have to be paid for, that is very dishonest to do. There’s nothing at all in the app info about any features costing money. One could say “well that’s a given that some will.” ...is it though!? See, I’d rather take the app devs at their word, if something is made to appear free, why should I assume they try and sneak in paid features?

I have nothing against someone wanting to get paid, but you should at least put that in your info. It’s so annoying to download a “free” app and then in the first 5 seconds, I’m being asked to buy things.

Be upfront, because we will see it...and then we will just become annoyed.
