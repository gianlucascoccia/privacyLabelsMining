Description

** This app is for homebuyers and requires an invitation from your Realtor. Ask your Realtor if they use Flexmls and if they can send you an invitation today**

The Flexmls app connects you to your REALTOR® with the same accurate, comprehensive and up-to-date listing information that they rely on every day.

You’ve already made a great decision to work with a licensed REALTOR®. Realtors use their local knowledge and expertise to help guide you through your home search journey, and we designed the Flexmls app with this relationship in mind.

Search for homes, view beautiful photos, select your favorites, hide homes you don’t like, and even directly message your Realtor - anytime and anywhere. Search, collaborate and find your dream home with the Flexmls app.

******
Flexmls App Features:
******

The Most Trusted Information Source
• Accurate, reliable property information
• Real-time data that provides the most up-to-date pricing and home details
• Directly from the source (the MLS)


Endless Search Options
• Search homes by city, address, zip code or even MLS #
• Filter your search by property type, bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage, list price and year built
• Use the map to view homes for sale in that location or neighborhood
• Search for homes in your desired school districts
• View open house information or attend a live virtual open house (where available)
• Sort your search results by price, new or recently changed, status, city, bedrooms or bathrooms

Hot Market? Customize Your Notifications
• Subscribe to get notifications of the types of homes you want to see; with push notifications directly to your mobile device
• Manage the types of push notifications you want to receive, including new recommendations from your Realtor and market updates: when homes hit the market, are pending sale, have price changes, sell, or go back on the market
• No digging through emails: View your personalized news feed to review all the property updates you have received

Easy To Use
• Swipe through photos, videos, virtual tours, immersive 3D home tours (where offered) and documents
• Save and rank your favorite homes and hide the ones you don’t want to see
• Beautiful, modern interface that's consistent with the home search you’ll use on your computer
• Touch ID for easy login

Collaborate With Your Realtor
• Quick and easy access to the home searches your Realtor created for you
• Direct in-app messaging allows you to contact your Realtor to ask for more information, request a showing, and more

Involve Your Family & Friends
• Quickly share listings you’re excited about with your loved ones via iMessages, airdrop, email, and other sharing options that are available on your mobile device
• Never get lost with quick access to turn-by-turn driving directions on every listing

Your Flexmls app gives you what you need to find the home of your dreams!

Please contact your Realtor if you have problems accessing or using this app.

DbeesApp , 12/11/2018

Great information BUT .......

I only use this app when I have an actual address to plug in because the app actually gives detailed information that Zillow does not give such as the real taxes (Zillow’s is a guesstimate), HOA fees (Zillow doesn’t give this information), etc. And I can read any legal documents about each property on FLEXMLS. BUT.......it is horrible for searching for properties. I get 277 properties in one search (with a sorting of high to low prices) but it only lets me see 100 of them. If I then do a sorting of low to high prices I will get 100 of the 277 from the opposite. However there is no way to see the 77 properties in the middle price range. Grrrr So, like I said, it has great detailed information but I keep it only for that. I do all of my searching on Zillow. Then use this app to get more info if I can plug in an actual address.
