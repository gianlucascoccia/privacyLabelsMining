SEC³URE Companion Mobile App
By IntelliCentrics

This companion mobile app makes it even easier for you to access and support every hospital you visit. From the convenience of your mobile device: Check the status at each of your healthcare facilities, snap a picture and upload documents for verification, add new facilities as you visit, and accept facility policies “on the go.”

MONITOR YOUR STATUS
• Identify compliance status by facility
• View outstanding policies by facility
• View outstanding credentials by facility
• Check REPScore
• Know before you go

EASILY PERFORM KEY TASKS
• Use your camera phone to snap images and upload documents for verification by IntelliCentrics
• Edit your profile photo
• Receive proof document updates
•
• Check into facilities remotely and receive an electronic badge on your mobile device (for participating facilities)
• Check out of facilities remotely (for participating facilities)
• Dispute negative REPScore events

FACILITY ACCESS
• Access nationwide database of hospitals
• Easily add and delete facilities you visit
• Read and accept facility policies before you visit
• View all of your facilities on a map
• Get directions via the Maps application

About IntelliCentrics, Inc.
For more than ten years, at more than 10,000 unique locations of care across six countries, the SEC3URE technology platform has been making it easier for vendors and their representatives to meet hospital regulations—all on a single instance of software. IntelliCentrics’ CVO is fully Certified by NCQA for 11 out of 11 verification services.

Christobal Hombrenegro , 10/01/2020

Barely functional

This credential app is the worst of any I’ve used so far. They routinely kick back credential submissions for vague reasons, you can literally submit a picture of a wall and it’ll be accepted as a credential, but then a flu shot requires 3 different submissions and a phone call to get approved. They randomly add new requirements such as Consent of Use, which is literally just allowing them to use things you’ve submitted, then the agreement section is bugged so you can’t actually agree to the required surprise requirement. If you call customer service, you’ll hear things like “The average wait time is 1,970 seconds”. Kiosks won’t tell you that you have missing requirements, they’ll just freeze when you try to login, then later you’ll get an email about a negative rep score event. The only part of this app that works well is the texting, reminders of expiring visits and such.
