Hvymetal58 , 11/09/2018

Great app for my lactose intolerance.

I’ve been a type one diabetic for 36 years and lactose intolerant for about 10. My daughter told me about this app and figured I’d try it out. Holy Cow! This is wonderful. I found it very self explanatory and extremely easy to use and understand. The search filter settings are a very nice feature to limit my option to no lactose and no sugar products and the large number of results gives me abundant options. The interface is exactly as I expect it to be to get more details about each product and add it to my list for shopping. Then after I’ve tried a product, being able to separate the product as “ liked” or “don’t like” so that I can keep track. I’m sure I haven’t mastered the features yet but the only thing I’d see any need for is more available coupons, I only saw one. Great job folks, keep it up.

Meohmy111111899 , 11/07/2018

Great app!

This app is really great to use if you’re lactose intolerant or have a dairy allergy. It shows you a bunch of different options for dairy free food. I really like it because it has so many categories to choose from whether I’m looking for dairy free ice cream or dairy free coffee creamer. It’s also great because it even gives you coupons for a bunch of different dairy free items. I think what really stood out to me was the fact that if you see an item you want to try you can add it to your shopping list and this makes it so easy for me when I’m grocery shopping. This app is so unique and helpful and I would definitely recommend it.
