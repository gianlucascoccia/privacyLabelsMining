Cyntrifical , 01/17/2019

Great video editor - terrible camera

So this is intended to be a vertical video editor which it does well, as well as standard sideways phone video editing. The editor is easy, intuitive and packed with nearly endless combinations of features and effects! Trimming, video speed, background audio, audio editing, etc are all there as well as features that go beyond your basic video editor app on iOS. Pro subscription is very flexible, offering by the year, by the week, and if you so please, a one time flat fee for lifetime purchase!
Now as stated, this excels as a video editor, but it’s execution on a camera recorder is poorly designed and lacking in many needed features. Not sure about most users but I experience lagging everytime I use their camera recorder, and even sometimes the app hangs up and shuts-down. Definitely needs manual controls (exposure, focus, white balance, etc) and more stable shooting, possibly image stabilization features akin to ProCam 6, etc. if Filmr can be fixed in that department then it’s a guaranteed 5 out of 5 stars!

Developer Response ,

Hey, thank you so much for the amazing feedback you gave us. We are super happy to know that you are enjoying Filmr! :) About the camera issue, we will consider your comments for future updates. Please feel free to comment every time you have a opinion about things that we can improve. Thank you.
