Description

The BBVA Mobile Banking App has already been named a ‘Leader’ by Javelin Strategy & Research in its Mobile Banking Scorecard six times in a row, but it just got better! Version 9.0 has been completely redesigned to give you faster access to the features that you already love and need, all with a fresh look and feel, both in English and Spanish.

Better Navigation: The new main navigation bar allows you to quickly access the most used features from all key areas of the app easily from the bottom of most screens. Plus, when navigating through your accounts, the new quick action bars will allow you to manage your accounts and customize features in less taps.

Transaction Details: See more clearly how a transaction has impacted your available balance, with improved details for posted and pending transactions. We have also added a warning badge to let you know if a transaction has been posted against insufficient funds.

Improved Dashboard: See all of your accounts and cards, from BBVA and other financial institutions, in one place for a full financial picture.

Alongside these improvements, there’s a fresh new look for all of the existing features that help you manage your money in an efficient way:

Find Features Fast: Use the search feature to tell us what you’d like to do. Use your voice or start typing for predictive search results to get answers faster.

Other features include:

Financial Tools to track your Spending and create Budgets
Save time paying bills by setting up AutoPay
Deposit checks using the simple, fast and convenient Mobile Deposit®**
Sign up to receive Notifications anytime we detect suspicious activity on your debit card
Apply for new BBVA accounts right from the mobile banking app in minutes

Pre-login QuickView
Statements you can view or download
Report cards lost or stolen
Turn your cards On or Off whenever you like
Order replacement cards
Change your card PIN
Create Travel Notifications
Pay Bills
Internal and External Payments and Transfers, including International Transfers
Schedule & set up recurring payments
PopMoney person to person money movement
Set up Alerts via Push Notifications
Activate Offers for Simple Cash Back*
Redeem Credit Card Rewards in real time
Quickly update your Home Address
Manage your Profile Settings

Existing Mobile Banking or Online Banking users just need to download the app, sign-in with their existing username and password, and start using it!

Existing bank customers without a username or password can enroll for Mobile Banking directly from the app by using their valid BBVA product.


For more information, go to: https://www.bbvausa.com/digital-banking-services/mobile-banking.html


If you need further help, call us at 1-800-273-1057


* To use Simple Cash Back℠, a customer must have a consumer BBVA debit card or credit card and be registered for Online or Mobile Banking. Eligible customers are enrolled in Simple Cash Back automatically, and offers will appear within Online and Mobile Banking

** Mobile Deposit® is subject to eligibility and further review. Deposits are subject to verification and not available for immediate withdrawal. Deposit limits and other restrictions apply.
