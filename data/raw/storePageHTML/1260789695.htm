Description

**Free with your ABCmouse.com membership**

Help build your child’s fundamental reading and math skills with the Magic Rainbow Traceables® app from the award-winning learning program ABCmouse! The Magic Rainbow Traceables® app features engaging tracing activities that help your child learn to recognize and write letters, numbers, and shapes while practicing eye-hand coordination and observation skills. Your child can trace and play these activities anywhere, anytime—even while offline!

Current ABCmouse members can use the Magic Rainbow Traceables® app for free just by logging in. If you’re not a member yet, you can subscribe through this app to access the full ABCmouse experience and begin your family’s learning adventure.

The ABCmouse Magic Rainbow Traceables® app offers
• More than 400 tracing activities
• Letters, numbers, shapes, words, Chinese characters, and more to trace
• Immediate feedback to help your child learn how to follow the lines, with correct stroke order where applicable
• Free Paint section for creative play
• For Parents section with curriculum information
This app contains NO third-party advertising and does NOT share any personally identifiable information about you or your child with third parties.
