Description

Your cycling app with route planner, navigation, tracking and more for cycling in and around the city. Download and start right away: Multi-stop route planning, navigation and many more features can be used for free in your self-selected Premium Area (7km diameter)!

FEATUES OF THE BIKE CITIZENS APP
• Worldwide availability
• Bicycle-optimised display of maps with highlighting of cycle routes
• Tracking of rides and visualisation in the overview and the personal heatmap in the profile
• Bicycle route planner with as many stopovers as needed and display of high metres;
• Routing gives preference to cycle paths and cycle-friendly routes
• Various routing options
• Find countless places (Points of Interest - POI) with the online search function
• Online route planning and navigation, as well as offline map download (Premium)
• The bike navigator gives precise voice prompts while cycling
• Recommended cycling routes in many cities and regions
• Local and international cycling campaigns, challenges and games such as Bike to Work, Bike Benefit, PINGifyoucare

AVAILABILITY OF THE CYCLING APP
The app is available worldwide. So you can start cycling right away.

YOUR FREE PREMIUM AREA
Use multi-stop route planning, navigation with precise voice prompts and offline maps in a 7km diameter for free! You can also use many of the app's features outside of this area.

BIKE CITIZENS PREMIUM MEMBERSHIP
If you want more of it, worldwide, get the Premium membership! You can use everything that is included in your free premium area worldwide. Plus you get: Map download for offline navigation, many options to personalise your routing settings and your personal profile and much more!

HOW DO I GET PREMIUM MEMBERSHIP?
• Get a Premium subscription in the app ($3.49 per month / $27.99 per year). The subscription will be charged monthly/yearly to your iTunes Account and renews automatically every month unless you disable it at least 24 hours before the end of the subscription period from Account Settings. The currently active subscription period cannot be cancelled.
• Redeem a voucher code
• Do you have existing city or regional maps in the app? -> You automatically start with Premium.
• If you live in a sponsored city or region, many premium features are available to you for free.

WHERE DOES THE MAP MATERIAL COME FROM?
The cycling app Bike Citizens is based on data from OpenStreetMap (OSM), the "Wikipedia of maps". We invite you to learn more about OSM at wiki.openstreetmap.org and to contribute to the accurate mapping of the cycling infrastructure in your area.

HOW DOES MY SMARTPHONE GET ON MY BIKE?
As a perfect complement to the cycling app we have developed a smartphone mount for cycling with Bike Citizens. FINN fixes any smartphone to any handlebar, whether city bike, mountain bike or road bike: http://getfinn.com

BNT1554 , 11/29/2020

Works like Google, but with better map data

Where I live (and I would guess most places) the bicycle infrastructure information is much more detailed and up-to-date in OpenStreetMap than other databases (Google or Apple). I have often wished someone would come out with an app that works like the big navigation apps, but uses OpenStreetMap data. So far, BikeCitizens is the closest thing to that I have found. Their map skin is not the best (bike routes don’t really pop unless you manually zoom out) and their routing algorithm has a tendency to route you along side streets that don’t have safe ways to cross major arteries, but at least it has voice navigation and I haven’t found any other app that comes remotely close to the interface of Google or Apple Maps for real-time bicycle navigation.

paydaddy , 07/16/2020

Works great in NYC

This app felt safer to me than google maps in real time on the road. But I would love to be able to add multiple destinations on my routes & I am disappointed that I can’t automatically set my current position as a starting point. Otherwise I’m very happy with this app in place of a bike computer.

Get more NYC people on here! I want more from the social aspects of this app! It has great potential for biking communities.

Disappointing, buggy, nice try

Downloaded the app and was hoping for a good experience. Managed to avoid the misleading purchase requests and got it working to try before buying. Did one ride, worked great. Tried to use it again, but kept getting GPS position is unknown error. Google Map had no such problem with my GPS position, and since it also does bike routes for free, I’m not seeing the point of this app. I did try to make a big report through the app, but I also got an error. Oops! Seems like my GPS position isn’t recognized when I choose to only share my location when I’m using the app. I love irony, but not from navigation apps.
