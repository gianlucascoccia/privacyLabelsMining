Description

Find Truck Service® is the leading and most accessible heavy duty service locator for truckers. Our National Service Locators are utilized by more than half a million users in the trucking industry, annually.

For nearly a decade, we've been helping truckers and fleet owners efficiently manage Truck Breakdowns, Over The Road Repairs and Scheduled Maintenance across North America. With thousands of service locations nationwide Find Truck Service® makes it easy to locate the nearest Road Service, Repair Shop, Towing Service and OEM Dealers for all your Truck Repair, Tire Repair, Trailer Repair and Refrigeration Service needs.

We are a service oriented company but it doesn't stop there, you will find that our Truck Stop Locator with Fuel Prices is second to none in the trucking industry. The Truck Stop Locator has multiple search categories (With Scales, Service, Parking, and Showers) and you can see location amenities as well as Truck Stop links with more information.
