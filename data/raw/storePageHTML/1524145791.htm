Description

Multifunctional keyboard is now on your Apple Watch! Type easily and send messages quickly right from your Watch with the full, universal keyboard for Apple Watch - WatchKeys.

Type stylish text messages using 50+ beautiful fonts, send text messages and emojis directly from your Apple Watch and change the keyboard theme in a few clicks.

The WatchKeys keyboard will be available on your Apple Watch right after the WatchKeys app is successfully installed on your iPhone.

Note: If the WatchKeys app does not appear automatically on your Apple Watch, go to the App Store on the Apple Watch and get it there.

Main WatchKeys App Features:
- A full, universal keyboard, including: QWERTY, numbers, symbols, emoji - everything is like on an iPhone keyboard;
- Colorful keyboard themes;
- Beautiful fonts.

What is included in WatchKeys app:
- 50+ beautiful fonts with regular updates (5 fonts for free);
- 3000+ colorful keyboard themes with daily updates, collected in 30 categories for every taste, color and mood;
- 5 keyboard themes in each category for free (150 keyboard themes for free);
- Convenient preview of fonts - You can see how the selected font will look on your watch before you start using it;
- No ads!
