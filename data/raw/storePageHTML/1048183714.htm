Description

Get unrivalled coverage of the UEFA Europa League with the competition's official app. We'll bring you the latest news, live coverage, next-day highlights and much more.

FOLLOW THE UEFA EUROPA LEAGUE

- Access all the fixtures and up-to-date standings.
- Read the latest news and analysis from UEFA experts.
- Dive straight into the news that matters to you with our personalised newsfeed.
- Don’t miss a single goal thanks to next-day highlights for every match.
- Listen to live match commentary on the go.
- Get live match statistics for every game.
- Track the full player rankings as the season progresses.
- Watch all the live draws.
- Get a heads up for all kick-offs, confirmed line-ups and draws.
- Keep up to speed on each team thanks to in-depth form guides.
- Have your say by voting for your Player of the Week.

EXPLORE THE ARCHIVES

- Access all-time player statistics: everything from the top goalscorer to the most yellow cards.
- Access all-time club statistics: everything from the most titles to the most goals conceded.
- Browse comprehensive reviews of each season.
- Watch match highlights from past seasons.
- Watch highlight compilations curated by UEFA experts.

Want to hear about exclusive UEFA competitions, watch classic clips and more?

- Like us on Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/europaLeague
- Follow us on Twitter: https://twitter.com/europaleague
- Follow us on Instagram: https://www.instagram.com/europaleague

Download the app for free now to get the definitive UEFA Europa League coverage, straight from the home of European football.

In the latest version of the app, you can get mini-cards in your home feed. Mini-cards give you quicker access to your team’s line-ups, stats, news, form guides, head-to-head records and highlights.

Update your app today to get the best possible Europa League experience.
