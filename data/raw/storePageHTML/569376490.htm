FREE: RSV-Bible text with entire Gospel of Mark audio (99 minutes) featuring Blair Underwood as Mark - plus full text of the RSV-CE Bible and access to EWTN Live content. At anytime you can purchase the complete New Testament audio.

The EWTN Bible is powered by the Truth & Life Audio Bible a celebrity-voiced "radio drama" of the entire RSV-CE New Testament. More than 70 actors help bring the scriptures to life in this 22-hour audio production, complete with movie-quality sound effects and an original music score. This award-winning audio New Testament is endorsed with an Imprimatur from the Vatican and including a foreword by Pope Benedict XVI. Voiced by internationally-renowned actors including: Neal McDonough, Stacy Keach, Kristen Bell, Sean Astin, Michael York, Blair Underwood,Malcolm McDowell, Brian Cox, Julia Ormond, John Rhys-Davies and many more.

70 actors - 20 audio engineers - 10 studios over 3 continents - more than 100 media development experts - and 10,000 production hours were needed to create this world-class audio New Testament.

In addition to this amazing audio, the iPhone/iPad app features:
• Complete text of RSV-CE synchronized with audio for the New Testament.
• Go directly to any verse and hear and see the Bible instantly.
• Use the built in playlist to hear the New Testament in 40 days or in Chronological order.
• Create your own playlist of verses.
• Remembers where you left off.
• Sleep timer
• Airplay support
• Support for headphone pause and play buttons
• Easily fit entire 22 hours of audio on your iPhone/iPad.

No purchases needed for New Testament!

Some wrote a review that the New Testament dramatized had to be purchased. The normal reading is free and has some dramatization! There is a menu item that offers a “full dramatized New Testament” more like a Hollywood production but it is not advertised nor pushed, just a menu item. There is not one single advertisement nor any pop ups in this app. So I do not know what the other reviewer was reviewing. This app is beautiful designed, easy to navigate, works with all my blue tooth listening devices as well as all my vehicles so I can listen as I drive. It is seamless and descriptive for easy selection for what you’re in the mood for. Fr. Wade Menezes’ Tuesday live call in show is on there so I can keep up! As well as the other open line hosts. Also the best of Mother Angelica which even our teen boys laugh and enjoy listening while I am cooking in the kitchen. There is even a section you can rent or buy some of the best Catholic videos. All regular EWTN programs are free to watch and or listen to as always.

proud-Catholic!!! , 12/16/2020

EWTN App latest release Dec 2020

Absolutely love EWTN and all of he great services they provide especially via the app. I I would have addressed the following via web developer link if such one worked/existed on the app. Please note I also have the most recent version of iOS (14.3) and as of this review. The only issue related to the mobile app is that those with limited internet access (ex poor cell signal or low bandwidth) may find the change to have one icon on the app’s home page for the On Demand content versus the previous separate ‘audio’ and ‘video’ icons to present performance issues in the loading of the list of available content. Basically if you want just audio or video or catalog content separately you will have to wait much longer for the page to load. I experienced a noticeable increase in time to load content and this despite having 2-3 bars of LTE signal. Additionally I have found the app may unexpectedly close when in the On Demand page.
