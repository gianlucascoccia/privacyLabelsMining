Description

Cultivating new habits in a fun way. Fortune City is a habit-building game from Fourdesire, creators of the Plant Nanny and Walkr with more than 25 million players!

Track your spending, grow a city! Fortune City gamifies bookkeeping with a fun city simulation game. Record your income and expenses, and watch as your city flourishes into a beautiful metropolis.

◈ Cultivate Good Habits in a Easy Way ◈
- Gamification gets you hooked on recording expenses! Build good habits while watching your city develop and grow.
- Easy to use interface let you track your spending and categorize transactions in simple taps.

◈ Analyze Expenses at a Glance ◈
- Pie charts and bar charts allow you to check income and expenses at a glance.
- Over 10 different categories and multiple accounts so you can keep clear records.
- View your balance weekly, monthly, or seasonally for both long and short term financial planning.
- Advanced searching functions allow you to quickly understand your personal spending habits.

◈ Build it your way ◈
- Choose from a variety of themes to personalize the look of your city.
- Collect over 100 different styles of buildings, unique vehicles, and friendly citizens to inhabit your town.
- Invite citizens to join your beautiful city. The happier they are, the more prosperous your city will become!
- Watch your ranking increase among your friends. See who can develop the most prosperous city!

But wait... there’s more!
- Automatic cloud storage backup
- Private password protecton
- Global currency exchange
- Awards for daily use
- Even more practical features!

Kellie.Kat , 01/22/2019

Actually works to encourage mindful spending

First of all, the icon for this app really sets up what the app is going to be about. It’s fun-looking, like a game, but it also has a serious name and a pencil in the design to indicate that the app does involve tracking and budgeting. The App Store description does a good job, and the app itself is so user friendly and cute. I would say this app is for anyone needing motivation or just starting out trying to budget. It makes it fun to pay attention to how money is spent, and the collection aspect is very addicting. The app is free and very professionally done.

The main focus of the app is motivating people to engage in mindful spending by having users track their expenses. By being aware of what you are spending, you can build a better budget plan. The design is absolutely adorable, and it uses a lot of game mechanics to create a motivating experience. The content, however, can be a little complicated to understand. I found that it throws you right into the game. Some scaffolded learning may benefit users. That being said, it was easy to navigate and use, the cost was perfect, and many people love this app, myself included.

JPuops , 07/22/2018

Fun Budgeting

I find this app to be both fun and useful. As I make purchases throughout my day I will log them into the game and have been able to categorize each one to my specifications. There are major categories and subcategories in order to track your purchases. It does incur you to spend more IRL just to gain new building types, but it’s not necessary, because those will come with time.
The free version itself offers a good range of capabilities, but for a few bucks a month you can also use more extensive graphing features, which is quite helpful for long-term budgeting.
The game interface on top of that is quite easy to maneuver and play around with. Even when not doing anything with your city for a day, you workers will build additional parks to liven things up.
Overall, I absolutely enjoy this app, and would also recommend some of the partner apps as well, Walkr and Plant Nanny. Those encourage positive and healthy daily routines, like exercise and drinking more water. Thank you dev. team! Well done!
