Description

Access your Alaska USA account in the palm of your hand

• Quick and secure log in with Touch ID® or Face ID
• Deposit checks remotely without waiting in line
• View balances and transaction activity
• Transfer funds
• Pay bills or schedule recurring payments
• Check current rates
• Locate an Alaska USA branch, surcharge-free ATM, or CO-OP® Shared Branch
• Get an insurance quote for your home, auto, and more

Lengthy approval time

The app works fine. It's quite annoying it takes so long for remote deposits to be available though. AlaskaUSA claims the funds will be available within 1-5 days. They should just say 5 days. We have made 20 some remote deposits and have NEVER received the funds in less than 5 days. Once my husband called and it was approved over the phone sooner. Which I don't understand? Anyway, it doesn't matter how big or small the amount is,
whether it's from a well-known entity such as the State of Alaska, whether we've received a check from the same entity in the past, what type of check it is, whether it's a money order. It doesn't matter. Five days. Just say it will take five days and stop claiming it will be less.

totallyfrozen , 04/23/2020

Needs intelligent updating

UPDATE, 2020: Trying to add a payment to a biller whose name starts with “T”, but the app won’t scroll down the list that far. Portrait or Landscape mode makes no difference. Great! Now how am I supposed to get this bill paid?! Once I finish the migration, all my banking will be with another institution and AKUSA will be history.
UPDATE, 2019: I don’t recommend this app. After almost 30 years with this institution, I just can’t even recommend using AlaskaUSA for any regular banking anymore, really. They changed the BillPay system and drastically complicated it. I used to schedule the bills to be paid ON my paydays and things went smoothly for years. Now the system includes both Send and Delivery dates for a payment. I found the system unnecessarily cumbersome and confusing. Several times now I’ve been hit by bills being paid when I don’t have the funds to cover them and ended up with hundreds of dollars overdrawn PLUS AKUSA’s $25 each overdraft fine! I don’t understand the reason for the change in the first place and I don’t understand how this is supposed to make my banking experience easier. After 29 as a member of AKUSA, I can’t take the financial burden of their BillPay “service”. I’m going to start migrating my business over to my USAA accounts.

Developer Response ,

Hi totallyfrozen, we don’t have any reported issues with our BillPay but we would like to look into this. Could you please email us at memberservice@alaskausa.org and reference your review?
