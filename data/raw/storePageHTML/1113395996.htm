Description

A walk through the history of photography! Inspired by the most iconic instant cameras of all time, with VNTG app you’ll feel the nostalgia and get your best vintage photos.

►►► VNTG CAMERAS
Select your desired VNTG Camera, take or import as many photos as desired, and transform them simultaneously in a single click! Each VNTG Camera includes its own distinctive style based on the period which it belongs to, with its unique Border Textures, analog Filters, aged Effects and exclusive Fonts. It´s easy, fast and there’s a whole bunch of possibilities!

Create and keep organized your photo albums based on your favorite VNTG Cameras. It´ll be easy to share your memorable moments with family and friends!

►►► VNTG PRINT SERVICE
Offering high-quality retro format printing. Unique and totally personalized prints, like never before! Choose your instant photos, confirm your order and get your prints home! The perfect gift and instant memory! :)

24h Worldwide Express Delivery available!

Ideal for travel memories, birthdays, love gifts, or any other special occasions. Printing of your photo albums´ll be easily done at any time. Don´t miss out and place your order!

►►► VNTG PRO Membership
VNTG PRO allows you to have full access to all the VNTG Cameras available and the new ones regularly added. And, stay tuned to the Special Edition Cameras for the holidays and other special occasions: Christmas, all Seasons, Halloween, Valentine’s Day, etc.

You will be charged a nominal monthly or annual subscription fee and your subscription will automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period. The subscription fee is charged to your iTunes account at confirmation of purchase. You can manage or turn off auto-renewal by going to App Store > Apple ID > Subscription > Manage your subscription; Change the membership options or turn auto renew off.

Memebership Options:
• VNTG Pro Month: Unlimited access to all VNTG Cameras for a month.
• VNTG Pro Year: Unlimited access to all VNTG Cameras for a year.
