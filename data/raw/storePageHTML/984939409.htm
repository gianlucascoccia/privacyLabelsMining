Description

Started by acclaimed actor (and closet drummer) Joseph Gordon-Levitt more than 10 years ago, HITRECORD is a positive community, a radically inclusive production company, and a platform for collaborative creativity.

Find a project that inspires you, and jump in.

■ Writing, Photography, Voice Acting, Filming, On Camera, Visual Art, Music, Editing, Animation

■ Quick and simple Creative Prompts to get you started right away

■ Collaborate on Short Films, Short Documentaries, Animated Shorts, Songs, Audio Stories, Illustrated Stories, Photo Series, or Customize your own project.

■ Project Tools help break down your process into manageable Steps, gather Contributions, Update your collaborators, and celebrate your finished piece.

■ Download anything. Everything is up for grabs to be remixed.

■ Contribute to high-profile productions. Our community has won an Emmy for our variety show HITRECORD on TV, screened short films at Sundance, published books with Harper Collins, partnered with non-profits like the ACLU and the National Parks, produced a commercial that played on the World Series, and of course, more! If and when any production makes money, contributors are paid through our unique, personal, and transparent payment process.

Being an artist on HITRECORD is not about getting Likes or amassing Followers. It’s about the joy of the creative process itself. Social networks do not have to be popularity contests. Digital technology can bring us together to accomplish things we couldn’t do alone.

Are we recording?
