Description

Welcome to Church Finder! If you are interested in learning more about churches in your area or need to find a church nearby this is the perfect app for you. With Church Finder you have access to the most comprehensive database of churches and cathedrals worldwide, along with pictures and reviews!

Church Finder is also a great tool if you are traveling or preparing to travel in an unfamiliar area. Simply type the name of the church or location you would like to search for in the search bar for instant results! You can sort your list by Distance or Prominence (according to Google) to see which churches are being viewed the most in any location. Discover churches nearby with the list view OR click on the Map button to view churches on a map!

Church Finder also includes a Bible Verse button, which provides a daily Bible verse and allows you to generate a random verse with a simple tap.

Thank you for downloading Church Finder - may the love of Jesus Christ shine down upon you!
