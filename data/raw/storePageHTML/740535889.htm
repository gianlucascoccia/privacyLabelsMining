Almost Great...but: Greed, Trumpism & Bugs

Addictive game, with persistent bugs. Sometimes it freezes, sometimes it doesn’t record high scores, sometimes exhibits degraded controls (aiming mechanics malfunction or freeze, bubbles become “sticky”, fired bubbles hang suspended mid-air instead of reaching target, etc.) but mostly it’s fun and an excellent time-eater.

If you do not play with real money on the table, higher levels and prizes are ridiculously unobtainable...unless you play for hours every day. Thus, gameplay is designed to encourage you to bet real money on your games. (#capitalism) Super high scores, when you get them, net you nothing, which is lame.

The pointless chat function tarnishes the game by offering a forum for the now all-too-familiar sophomoric Trump-era trolls, with their endless douche-baggery, obscene/racist comments, hitting on or insulting anyone they suspect is female or gay or different in any way from whatever they think they are...but it’s easy to not engage. Just don’t.
