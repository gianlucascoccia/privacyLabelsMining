Description

The free This Old House app features 1,400+ full episodes –– with new ones added weekly –– streaming to your TV, tablet or phone. Upgrade to Insider for ad-free viewing, and to unlock every episode in the archive.

This Old House is the number one home improvement brand, serving over 40 million consumers each month with trusted information and expert advice through the EMMY award-winning television shows This Old House and Ask This Old House, the Webby Award-winning site ThisOldHouse.com, the Insider membership club, the podcast Clearstory, and the highly regarded This Old House magazine.

Graphex , 04/18/2020

Software Craftspeople They Are Not

I’m a huge fan of TOH and the content is great to have access to, but unfortunately the app needs more work. In particular, it doesn’t keep track of what you’ve watched. There is no way to tell which episodes you’ve watched or where you were in an episode.
Being unable to navigate away from an episode and choose a different episode when in landscape orientation is a pain as well. I’m hopeful that the developers for TOH can add these sorts of features soon, and get the UX up to at least a professional range, and then take it further to show the type of craftsmanship with their software that they do in their homebuilding.
