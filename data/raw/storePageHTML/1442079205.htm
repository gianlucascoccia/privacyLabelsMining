Description

Play with a cute little virtual pony in this pet care & dress up game! Hatch, raise, feed, bathe & have fun with the cutest unicorn!

You got a gift - a super cute virtual unicorn baby! Become the best horse and pony nanny with fun My Baby Unicorn pet care game! Hatch a colorful egg and take care of the cutest newborn unicorn with rainbow hair! Bathe, feed, potty train, dress up, sing to sleep and play fun games with your tiny virtual pet friend! Decorate the baby unicorn’s room with rainbow colors! Play My Baby Unicorn virtual pet care game every day and watch your pony grow!

HATCH AN EGG & DRESS UP YOUR VIRTUAL PET UNICORN!
Surprise! You have a magical egg to hatch! Take care of it and watch a tiny baby unicorn hatch! Now you are a magical horse and pony babysitter! Take good care of the newborn unicorn! Design and decorate the baby’s room with rainbow colors and fun accessories! Dress up your baby unicorn in colorful outfits!

BATHE & POTTY TRAIN YOUR TINY UNICORN!
Your baby unicorn wants to be super clean! Bathe the little one and watch the magical pony’s fun reactions to rainbow soap or a slime bath! Time to potty train your tiny unicorn pet! Don’t forget to wash the hands!

FEED & SING TO SLEEP YOUR BABY PONY PET!
Feed your baby unicorn with fun and yummy foods: fruits, veggies, candy, donuts and even a cute unicorn cake! Give a squishy toy to your baby unicorn friend and sing the little one to sleep! Sweet dreams, baby unicorn!

PLAY GAMES WITH YOUR RAINBOW UNICORN!
Take good care of your magical pony pet and unlock new fun mini games every day! Play memory, coloring and decoration games with your virtual unicorn! Jump on clouds, slide down a rainbow and enjoy more surprise games.
