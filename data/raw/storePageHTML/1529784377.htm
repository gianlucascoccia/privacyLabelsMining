Description

With Story Viewer for Insta, you can view any public Instagram profile stories and posts to see it in full size. No sign-in required.

Story Viewer for Insta allows you to watch only the public users' stories and posts to see it in full size. Watching the stories of private accounts is unfortunately not possible at this time. You can still view the high-resolution(HD) profile pictures of private accounts.

Simply enter a username, search, and view any story or feeds!

No sign-in required.

FEATURES:

- View Instagram profiles without being noticed.

- View profile photos for all users in HD.

- View the users' stories without your username show up in the viewer list.

- Save stories & posts

- Share stories & posts with your friends

- Dark Mode Support

- Stories View with Video Player

- Posts View with %800 Zoom

We are willing to improve Story Viewer for Insta App. Please keep following us.
