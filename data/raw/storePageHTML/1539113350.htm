Description

Video Joiner & Trimmer was created to help you merge your various videos in one pack so you can enjoy and share your preferred clips or movie series without having to handle them individually.
You can add an unlimited number of videos as you like. With just a few taps, you can enjoy your movie clips without interruption.

Features:

- Join & merge
Add videos from album or shoot with camera. Merge result can be previewed immediately.

- Trim & cut
Select the start time and end time of the clips and trim it.

- Slow motion & fast motion

- Fit mode and full mode supported

- No watermark

- All visible, what you see is what you get
