Description

Group Text enable you to send mass text message individually.

Group Text is an easy, user-friendly, time-saving and well-designed app enabling individual text messages to a group of contacts at once. Easily create & change your groups. Quickly search your groups/contacts. Possibility to select multiple numbers per contact. Use “template” feature to instantly send recurrent content to your groups.

There is no advertising in the App!

GROUPS FEATURES
• Import your existing iCloud groups at first use
• Create your own groups: Family, Friends, Colleagues…
• Change your groups by assigning/removing contacts
• Already assigned contacts/numbers are always marked
• Use “Select All” feature to assign all your contacts at once
• Enabling multiple numbers selection per contact
• The total number of contacts is always shown for each group
• Easily find your groups or contacts to be assigned/removed
• Display order of contacts in line with your device settings
• Newly created groups are always highlighted for easy access

TEXT MESSAGING
• Instantly send text message to your groups
• Quickly add/remove contacts before sending

TEMPLATES
• Create predefined templates for your recurrent contents
• Instantly send text message by selecting a template
• Edit the selected template before sending if needed
