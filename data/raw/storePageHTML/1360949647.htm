Description

Game Your Core! Stealth’s Game Play technology turns the boring plank exercise into a fun workout that focuses on scoring points and high scores instead of counting reps and time. In as little as 3 minutes a day you can target over 29 core muscles anywhere. Start your day the Stealth way by firing up your core for a more productive day.

Getting started with Stealth couldn’t be easier. Just download the Stealth gaming app, place your smartphone into the designated slot, get into the plank position and start playing. Believe us when we say:

"You'll forget you are even working out!"

Stealth’s mobile games will have you tilting and twisting at various angles delivering one amazing core workout that is challenging, effective and fun.

eJules1 , 04/08/2019

This is outstanding!

I was skeptical based on the price, but the frequent eBay ads wore me down and I decided to buy it (on sale). ; )
I’m very glad I did!
The concept is brilliant, the board is sturdy / quality-made, and the workout games are quite challenging (for me, once I come up to about 3 min) but fun. I do have a few nits about the app’s functionality (for instance, where’s the leaderboard for the gliding game), but all in all I’m thrilled with the purchase.
My sons and I have all downloaded the app and have each completed our 3 minutes a day since it arrived (for the past three days). Compare that to the past three months, when we didn’t do any core workouts, and I consider it money well spent!
And my son made the top 5 under 10 years old on the leaderboard, so he’s definitely hooked.

Developer Response ,

Thanks so much for playing Stealth!! In an upcoming update, the Glider game will be embedded in the main app and will have its own dedicated scoreboard. Thanks!

Payment Structure Problem

This system received a 5 star from my husband and I right out of the box. It works great, haven’t had any issues with the app and entices is to get down and do the dreaded plank.
We’ve only had it a short while but I was interested in some of the other games. Upon researching - the app is available under family share but not the subscription.
????
What’s the point? Everyone in my family can download an app - especially a free app. I wanted to share the subscription, but it looks like each phone must subscribe.
Sorry. $100 is too much for my family to get down on the floor and hold a plank - especially when the exercise itself doesn’t require ANY equipment. Not to mention - we can just as easily put on a book or a Netflix show. I was willing to pay the $25/year for more games - which says a lot as I refuse to pay for games. Sorry you’ve lost a subscription customer.

We are glad to hear you are enjoying Stealth! Everyone in your family can use your account under the single phone challenge mode and share one phone. Our team is currently working on a family plan option that will allow you to add additional subscriptions at a discounted rate. We hope that you will take advantage of this plan once it's released.
