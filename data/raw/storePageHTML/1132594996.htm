Tara Janine , 04/02/2020

Very few mods - subpar in content

Got this app on a whim just to see what mods they have, and I’m a little disappointed tbh. The mods themselves seem boring to me, and add little interest to the game itself, though I have not downloaded them (they look boring and I’m broke).

There are only 13 TOTAL MODS, 5 of which (CompactStorage, Adventure Backpack, Secret Rooms, Malasis Doors, and Furniture) you HAVE TO PAY for. I’m not sure if it’s a one time payment, or if each mod must be bought individually, but it says $1.99. Another 5 (Moarsigns, Jump Pad++, Gany’s Surface, Deconstruction Table, and Decocraft) can only be used after you leave a review.

^^THESE MODS don’t tell you any details about what they do UNTIL you either pay or leave a review. That scares me, because I want to know EXACTLY what I’m paying for, even if it’s only $1.99. To me, I’d rather not even put money into a product if I can’t see what it is, that’s just shady to me. Not to say this developer isn’t trustworthy or whatever, it’s just that this app isn’t my cup of tea.
