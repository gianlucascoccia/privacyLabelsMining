Description

Fuel Monitor is your assistant to help keeping an eye on the cost for cars: fuels, charging, services, repairs and other fees. Just add the miles, gallons and cost, it could calculate the fuel economy for gasoline car, and energy consumption for electric car automatically, which would help you save your money and time.

Set the services interval, it would intelligently list the due date or odometer of each maintenance in schedule.

Key Features:

Vehicles
⁃ Supports multiple vehicles.
⁃ Manage basic info of your cars: VIN, Plate, Tire Size and engine type etc.
⁃ Track expense for each vehicle separately.

Fuels
⁃ Calculate fuel consumption automatically.
⁃ Calculate cost per mile/km.
⁃ Track fuel logs including fuel-up, price, total cost, date, odometer, location, etc.

Energies
⁃ Calculate energy consumption automatically.
⁃ Calculate cost per mile/km.
⁃ Track charging logs including reading, price, total cost, date, percentage after charging, location, etc.

Services
⁃ Track kinds of services’ logs including odometer, time, cost, labor cost and note.
⁃ Default and custom service types and maintenance interval.
⁃ Set time of in advance reminding by days or odometer.
⁃ List the next due date/odometer of each service type.

Repairs
⁃ Track kinds of repairs logs including odometer, time, cost, labor cost and note.
⁃ Default and Custom repair expense types.

Other fees
⁃ Track kinds of other fees, such as parking, washing, decorating, insurance, etc.
⁃ Default and Custom other expense types.

Powerful reports
⁃ Pie chart to show cost & percentage of fuel, service, repair and other expenses.
⁃ Bar chart to show the cost of fuels/energy, service, repair and other expenses by week, month, year or custom.
⁃ Pie chart to show the cost & percentage of each service/repair/other expense type by week, month, year or custom.
⁃Table to list fuel statistics, including average fuel economy, avg cost per mile/km, total miles and etc.
⁃ Line graph to show the trend of fuel economy and cost per mile/km.
⁃ Line graph to show recent fuel price trend.
⁃ Line graph to show recent fuel/energy cost.
⁃ Line graph to show recent service cost.
⁃ Line graph to show recent repair cost.
⁃ Line graph to show recent other cost.

Extra highlight
⁃ Clean and tidy UI.
⁃ Calendar overview of data.
⁃ Supports maps to track location info.
⁃ Supports both US and SI unit.
⁃ Supports backup & restore through iCloud and Dropbox.
⁃ Export data by email in format of PDF, CSV & HTML.
⁃ Supports Printing data directly by this app.
⁃ Supports importing data from a CSV file.
⁃ Supports customizing the digital decimal of cost, fuel economy, etc.
⁃ Supports App Watch.
⁃ Adds 3D Touch function.

Pat_Battle , 03/19/2019

Solid App

Converted from Fuelly (which was absolutely ruined after its original “Gas Cubby” version).

First, Fuel Monitor has a cool import feature that helped me import my vehicle’s data. It works well (assuming you know how to use Excel, FileMaker, or similar desktop application).

The app is easy to use, has some unique features, but it does have a slightly odd (and somewhat dated) interface. At first I didn’t see the need to split Services and Repairs, but it works and I suppose this approach makes sense.

I really don’t like that we’re not allowed to leave “months” blank when setting up service reminders. We need the ability to set reminders to miles / months / or both. Also, services are only grouped by service (oddly enough, Repairs are not grouped like this). We need the option to turn this forces grouping off, and additional sorting options would be great.

Overall I’m happy with this app - it does what I need, it’s fast, and it has multiple import / export / backup options. Looking forward to an interface overhaul, and some more flexibility with the above mentioned features.

Outside Engineer , 04/26/2020

Useful app

I’ve been using Fuel Monitor since I got my “new” car December of 2015. I track everything from fuel fill-ups, oil changes, tire rotations and other services or repairs. I love being able to track time and expenses, and it also calculates my cost per mile. When I’m at the repair shop, I have all my old history at my fingertips.

I just got a new phone and was worried that I might lose my old records or have to re-enter, but I was delighted to find the Backup & Restore option in the settings menu. It worked very smoothly. I used the WiFi backup with my computer. In the process, I discovered that I could also export my data to a spreadsheet. I’m delighted.

I recommend this app.
