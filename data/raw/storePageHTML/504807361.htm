*** TOPICS COVERED ***
StudyPad has the best math apps aligned to common core standards with virtually infinite number of questions. This app covers following topics:

1. Place Value - Generalize place value understanding to decimals to the thousandths place
2. Number Sense - Compare, Order and Round decimal numbers
3. Algebra - Write and evaluate number expressions, work with Number Patterns
4. Multiplication - Fluently multiply up to three digit numbers by two digit numbers
5. Division - Divide by two digit numbers
6. Fractions - Addition, Subtraction and Multiplication unlike fractions, division involving a fraction and a whole number
7. Decimal Arithmetic - Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication and Division involving decimals to the hundredths place
8. Measurements - Convert quantities in one measurement unit to another within the same system of measurement
9. Geometry - Coordinate planes and graphs, hierarchical classification of shapes, volumes of solids
