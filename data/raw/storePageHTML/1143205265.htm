Description

Take your violin skills from absolute beginner to advanced student with Trala. Trala combines video lessons from world-class violinists with instant pitch and rhythm feedback. It’s a learning tool in your pocket, allowing you to practice anytime, anywhere.

OUR MISSION
Trala’s mission is to make world-class music education available to every single person on Earth. Everything we make is designed with that goal in mind, and is developed alongside world-class artists to ensure that your experience is always educational, diverse, and fun. We’re helping real people create beautiful music.

INTERACTIVE LESSONS
Trala courses include:
• A professional music video of the song you'll learn
• History of the song
• Lessons on all the techniques you need to play the song
• Daily exercises to practice skills such as string crossings and intonation
• The "playboard", a video-game version of sheet music where you score points by hitting notes as they cross the screen

RENOWNED ARTIST INSTRUCTORS
Some of the most exciting and dynamic violinists in the world teach on Trala. Instructors include:
• Katie Jacoby, the electric violinist for The Who
• Dr. Nuné Melik, a classical violinist, musicologist, and humanitarian
• Grace Youn, a renowned expert in the fundamentals

INSTANT FEEDBACK AND TUNER
• Trala’s sophisticated audio processing software hears you play in real time and provides instant feedback on your pitch and rhythm
• In-app tuner provides high-precision tuning while also teaching you the skills to tune by ear

DAILY LESSON PLAN
• Benefit from a daily practice plan that aligns specifically to your goals
• Adjust to practice more or less frequently as needed

SHEET MUSIC LIBRARY
• We don’t believe the violin should be boxed into one genre. Access an expansive sheet music library of over 50 songs to learn, from Bach to Happy Birthday

PARTNERSHIPS
• We’ve partnered with several shops to help you find the best possible options for your instrument and necessary accessories
• Access an exclusive bundle deal with Kennedy Violins and Fiddlershop for a discounted year of Trala plus a new violin! For more info visit www.trala.com/bundle

What can I say!

I was told years ago I could never learn by my dad. I lost all passion for music and trying out after being told that. Still just in the trail and learning for ground zero but the lessons so far are easy and great to understand. Mattbklyn , 12/31/2018

Useful tool to add to your practice.

Cant say much about beginner lessons since I started at intermediate level. Anyway I have been playing around with this app for two days now. It is really fun and useful to add to your practice. The only reason I did not put five stars is because I think the description is a little misleading. It definitely does not replace a teacher even for those who use online teachers you definitely get more out of a live person on a video telling you how to do things properly. This app is great to keep intonation in check especially for those of us who practice on our own. I actually enjoyed practicing pieces already knew to check that I was playing them in tune then actually doing the lessons. Great boost of confidence. Some definite cons are the lessons are way too short, the sheet music for pieces does not have Bowing. I had to check with my regular sheet music for correct bowing. I also noticed that couple of the étude sheet music had wrong key and some of the half notes were not correct. So I had to play wrong notes in order to move on within the lesson.
