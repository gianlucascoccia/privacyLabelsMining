Description

Take notes easily with QuickNotes X. Organize your handwritten notes in notebooks with a selection of covers and paper templates and export them as PDF or PNG images.

Take notes easily with QuickNotes X. Organize your handwritten notes in folders and notebooks with a selection of covers and paper templates and export them as PDF or PNG images.

- ORGANIZE YOUR NOTES -
Create notebooks to organize your notes. Choose between a selection of paper templates and covers, and choose between vertical or horizontal layout to fit your note-taking needs. You can also rearrange your notebooks and create folders for them for maximum flexibility.

- ACCURATE NOTE-TAKING -
QuickNotes X has a new accurate drawing engine that never loses detail, even after zooming or moving around the page. It also has the tools necessary to take perfect professional notes, all in one place.

- DRAG & DROP SUPPORT -
With QuickNotes X, you can easily create multi-media notes: use an image as the background of your note, or import images from your library or from the web with an in-app tool or by using drag & drop from another window. In addition, these images are always resizable and movable, so that you can edit them whenever you want

- PROFESSIONAL TOOLS -
To create the perfect note, you need the right tools. This is why QuickNotes X has everything you need, from the simple Pen and Highlighter to the Eraser, so that no mistakes are irreversible. Select the perfect line width and opacity to further customize your style, and select the color you need from a hand-picked selection or by using the new HSV picker. You can even import a color from an image using the unique RealityPicker!

- LOCK YOUR PRIVATE NOTEBOOKS -
Want to restrict access to a specific notebook? Just set up a password when you create it or long press on an existing notebook to lock it. And when you need to add some pages to it, just enter the password again or use Touch ID or Face ID to unlock it.

- TYPE INSTEAD OF WRITING -
With the new Text Tool you can add Text Boxes to your notes, so that you are able to type instead of writing. Want a different text style? Choose the font, size and color you like, underline or set to bold important things: now typing and writing notes is easier with QuickNotes X.

- EXPORT TO EDIT ON OTHER DEVICES -
You can export individual notes or entire notebooks as a QuickNotes X Document, that you can then import on another device to continue editing your notes. If you export your notebooks they will retain all properties, such as background and aspects ratio, so that if you reimport them they will look exactly as you expect.

- SEAMLESS SHARING -
Export your note as a PNG image or PDF, with the option to export the whole notebook in a single PDF document. Then, open it in your favorite app or share it with Mail, all with a few taps.

- USE YOUR STYLUS -
Have a Bluetooth stylus? No problem! QuickNotes X support a variety of styluses, from the Apple Pencil (1st & 2nd Generation)* to Adonit Jot, so that you can take notes while resting your palm on the screen, just like on paper.

- OPTIMIZED FOR iOS 13 and 14 -
With iOS 13 and 14, QuickNotes X is better than ever! A new, more modern look of the interface and support for Dark Mode makes taking notes with QuickNotes X a new experience.

- BUT THERE IS MORE! -
⊱ Quickly change page while taking notes by double-tapping on the edge of the screen
⊱ See all your notebook pages at a glance while taking notes with the new "Notes List" feature
⊱ Create a new page while writing with the dedicated button or by double-tapping on the edge of the screen
⊱ Unlimited undo-redo while writing (before leaving the page)
⊱ Clear your page with a single tap
⊱ Change your notebook template while writing thanks to the dedicated button
⊱ Rapidly delete notes and notebooks with just a long press
⊱ Option to reset all content (Notebooks and Notes) and preferences
⊱ Lightweight: you can have the same features of a professional drawing app with less than 20MB of space
⊱ Localized in English, Italian and French.

Very User friendly, but lacking in some features

TLDR - Easy to use and very helpful for organization. Missing some features that are not essential for use, but would be a convenient and pleasant addition in the future.

The notebook organization system and the ease of changing writing utensil, page format, and incorporating images is noteworthy. It is also very easy to export notebooks in various formats (though the premium expansion is needed for some of the aforementioned features). It also works incredibly with the Apple Pencil.
However, there are some relatively common features that are currently missing, and could be helpful if added in the future.
-To my knowledge, there is no method to reorder pages, only add and remove.
- There is also no ruler tool, which has some niche cases where it would be helpful. While I understand it wouldn’t not be a significant priority for most users, I am surprised that even the paid tools do not compare with the included markup tools on Apple’s iBooks
-There is also no ability to copy and paste a portion of a page or a whole page, which can be helpful when weighting lengthy equations or statements
-there is no ability to separate or move specific selections of writing around the page, even though text and images can be moved as overlays

Overall, I like the app a lot, and I feel it has a lot of potential. However, there are many features I have seen in competitor’s apps that are missing.
