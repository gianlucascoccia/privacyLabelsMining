Inaccurate App Name

The only capability this app seems to have is heart rate (pulse) measurement and recording. While that seems accurate enough, one could do that manually.

There is no blood pressure measuring option in this app. While that might have been a lot to hope for without peripherals, that is what is advertised.

As others have said, you have to sign up for one of the paid subscription options to access the “free” trial. The trial is only 3 days long and you have to cancel at least 1 day before the billing date or you’ll be charged, so if you only want that trial you’d better cancel the subscription same-day.
