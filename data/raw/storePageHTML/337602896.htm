Description


WSOC-TV Channel 9 Eyewitness News is covering the Carolinas with our 24/7 news app. Dig deeper on the major news of the day with our live and on-demand videos to keep you plugged in to what’s happening now.

WSOC-TV Channel 9 News is your guide to:


Minute-by-minute weather using WSOC-TV Channel 9’s StormTracker Doppler Radar and 5-day Forecast so you know how the weather will impact you.
Getting where you need to go on time with live drive times in and out of the Charlotte Metro Area.
Direct sports coverage of the Carolina Panthers, Hornets, high school sports, and more.
Local news that matters for Ashe, Watauga, Avery, Caldwell, Alexander, Burke, Catawba, Lincoln, Cleveland, Iredell, Rowan, Cabarrus, Mecklenburg, Gaston, York, Union, Anson, Richmond, Chester, Lancaster, Chesterfield, and Stanly counties including Wadesboro, Monroe, Rockingham, Albermale, Newton, Boone, Newland, Jefferson, Taylorsville, Statesville and Shelby.
National and world news and what it means for you.
Engaging content that you can share easily from the app to Twitter and Facebook.

Get accessible coverage that keeps you up-to-date with WSOC-TV Channel 9 News.

Continued use of GPS running in the background can dramatically decrease battery life.
