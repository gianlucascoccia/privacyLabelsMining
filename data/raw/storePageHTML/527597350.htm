Description

The WPCU Mobile Banking App makes it easy and convenient to manage your finances – wherever you are!

Manage Your Accounts and Finances
• Check balances and activity quickly in your checking and savings accounts
• View and manage your mortgage, auto loan, or credit card accounts
• Set app alerts to get notified about balances and transactions

Security
• Log in securely using Face ID (iPhone X or higher) or Touch ID
• Mobile Banking uses a verification code so you can authenticate via text message or a voice call

Mobile Deposit
• No more waiting in line, or driving to a Member Center
• Deposit a check right from your mobile device, with just a photo

Move Money
• Transfer funds between WPCU accounts, or even to another WPCU member
• Set up payments to your WPCU loan directly from another financial institution
• Use Popmoney to pay people quickly and easily

Mobile Bill Pay
• Pay bills to existing payees, or add new payees.
• Edit or delete payees
• Sign up, view, pay, and manage your eBills
• Schedule automatic and recurring payments
• View payee history
• Add a memo

Get Cash Back (When Paying with Your Debit Card)
• View and activate your cash back offers from participating retailers
• Track the dollar amount of your rewards

Contact Us
• Find Member Centers, Shared Branches, and ATM locations
• Contact the Member Help Center (during normal business hours)


Notes:
The WPCU Mobile Banking App is free to download and use, but your phone carrier’s charges may apply. System availability and response time is subject to your carrier’s reliability.

Zackjmcgmm , 04/03/2019

Was happy. Now annoyed.

I used this app for many years when I had only my personal accounts. It worked great. We recently got married so now we have joint accounts. The problem is that WPCU only gives you one user name per account. This becomes an issue when I try to log in and it will send the two factor authentication text to my wife’s phone since it’s “her” user name. If you individual accounts and joint accounts each user should be able to log in independently and see accounts they are joint in and accounts they are separate on. At the very best this current system is inconvenient, at worst it is a giant security risk because it forces people to share log in information.

Zimukusa , 10/06/2019

Ads and no longer real-time

This app used to be one of my favorite for my financial institutions, but now I’m daily getting ads about money-back offers with no option to mark them as read or delete them. Tons of irrelevant “offers” (e.g. cash back on auto parts...I don’t own a car) that I tried to swipe to see if there was a delete option but somehow that activated it. Now I have these dang offers on my account and no idea what havoc it’s causing to my account history. And there’s no way to remove these from my account so I literally have to sit getting more notifications each day. Fed up.
Also getting extra annoyed that WPCU isn’t keeping real-time anymore. Funds in account but for some reason shows them three days later. Standing payments made but delaying issuing of my funds. Watching both this app and WPCU transactions carefully because if this keeps up I’m leaving WPCU at the end of the year.

ReRe0205 , 05/06/2018

User Friendly Banking App

I’ve had this app for at least two years now and I like it a lot! It’s very easy to use. But like another review stated, it would be nice to see the names to the linked accounts when transferring. I’ve basically had to try to memorize which person is which. Granted, before the transfer is complete it shows the name of the person you are transferring to, but there was a time where I thought I selected the right person and ended up transferring money into a wrong account.
