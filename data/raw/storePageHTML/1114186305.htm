Description

Dive Diary, DiDi, is the easiest, fast and comfortable way to have your logbook always at your fingertips.

DiDi is designed for quick access to your logbook, visualizing these nice and intuitive.

Browse among all your dives, dive sites, buddies, diving centers, boats...

Displays statistics about your dives (where you have dived more, total dive time, maximum depth...).

Add photos and enjoy specific galleries for each of your dives.

Sync your diary between all your devices using iCloud. No matter where you are or what device your are using, your dive diary will be always with you.

Generate beautiful PDF files for your dives to print or share it if you want.

Please, help us to improve. With your help we will be better and better!

rich2dive , 02/05/2018

Logbook and more

This app has the ability to store a lot of information and has many features. I was able to enter my log history by creating "summary" dives then using the attach photo to include scans of my paper log. The summary count is off but the hours total is right. There are couple quirks and opportunities for enhancements. Quirk: While adding several items(boats, locations, buddies,etc) the iPhone keyboard covers the field so you cannot see what you are typing. Enhancements: Allow "zoom" when picking pictures (otherwise must select extra then delete); Allow temperatures to be blank or NA; PDF nice feature, but one dive can be 4 pages.
