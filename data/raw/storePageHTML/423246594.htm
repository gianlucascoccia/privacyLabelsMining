Description

Get ready for NCAA March Madness 2021! Fill out your brackets for the Capital One NCAA March Madness Bracket Challenge and tune in for live games after Selection Sunday.

The official app of the 2021 NCAA® Division I Men's Basketball Tournament for iPhone, iPad, Apple TV, and Apple Watch is all new this year!



NCAA March Madness Live is the only app that lets you play the Capital One NCAA March Madness Bracket Challenge and simultaneously watch every game, all in one place. Explore on-demand videos of highlights, in-depth analysis, classic games and more to get ready for Selection Sunday. Lock in your brackets before Friday, 3/19 to play.



After Selection Sunday, NCAA March Madness Live will let you stream every game live and notify you with bracket updates, close games, overtime games and favorite teams. Stream games, follow the teams in your bracket and see how you rank in 2021. You can also view brackets from millions of other players, including celebrity players.



Download NCAA March Madness Live now to get ready to play the Capital One NCAA March Madness Bracket Challenge with millions of NCAA March Madness fans. LIVE games and Selection Sunday are coming soon!



= NCAA March Madness Live features =



Play The Capital One NCAA March Madness Bracket Challenge :
• Sign up and fill out your brackets for March Madness 2021
• Play with friends and family
• Compete with your bracket against celebrities
• Get personalized bracket alerts
• View your picks right from the Home Tab



Video On Demand (VOD)
• Replay highlights of past games
• Relive amazing March Madness moments
• View in-depth analysis, behind-the-scenes features, interviews and more


Stats & News
• View player and team stats for every game
• Top news sources and teams
* Get deeper insights into every game



After Selection Sunday: Watch Live Games
• Watch all 67 games across CBS, TBS, TNT and truTV
• Check your bracket standings while viewing live games
• Log in with your TV provider after the free 3 hour preview ends to continue watching
• After 3 hour preview, a paid TV subscription is required for games broadcast on TBS, TNT and truTV
• CBS games do not require a paid TV subscription
• Note: live video is only available in the US, its territories and Bermuda
