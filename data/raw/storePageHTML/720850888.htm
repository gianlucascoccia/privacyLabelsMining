b3rabera , 04/08/2020

Took a chance 🤷🏽‍♀️

After reading the most recent reviews, I was a bit skeptical but I decided to chance it.

I placed an order at 8:00 pm and the app said the *liquor store near me was open and the delivery would be to me in less than an hour*. Great. I placed my order. When I went to my email to check for my confirmation, to my surprise (and disappointment) Minibar said the supplier was closed and my order would be placed tomorrow, at 12 pm, when they opened. Naturally I was enraged bc I did not want the delivery tomorrow, I wanted it today. And I also felt that they misled me bc they should have told me the supplier was closed BEFORE I paid. And yes, they charged me! So I sent them an email telling them to cancel my order and refund me now, only to receive an automated email.
Less than an hour later I received a call from an unknown contact which I assumed was Minibar calling to address my ‘not so friendly’ email. To my surprise it was a delivery man telling me he was downstairs with my order! I was pleasantly surprised. All of this to say, if u receive that email don’t think all hope is lost. You may still receive your order. Thanks Minibar!

Developer Response ,

I'm sorry the order confirmation was a bit confusing! If you place an order when the store is open, the store should deliver the order within an hour. We will work to make it less confusing because the confirmation email does update to state when the store has closed.
