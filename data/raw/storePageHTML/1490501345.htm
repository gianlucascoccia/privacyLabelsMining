A modern oracle for a new generation of mystics, and fans of astrology and New Age.

The Starseed Oracle is a breathtaking 53-card oracle deck for starseeds, empaths, lightworkers and seekers. Starseeds are souls that first incarnated somewhere beyond this planet - many have a sort of 'knowing' from a young age.

Depicting energy portals, ancient sites, faraway planets and star systems, the oracle is otherworldly, literally opening a path to things that cannot be seen with our eyes. For those starseeds roaming the Earth with a pull towards the unknown within the stars, this deck will help them to:

• identify as a starseed, and understand what this word means
• feel less alone in the universe and understand their longing for 'home'
• activate imprinted memories of their soul history through beautiful cosmic images
• understand their mission on Earth and empower them to be a source of positive energy

About the Author:
Rebecca Campbell is a writer, devotional creative and visionary who leads activating workshops internationally, giving people an experience of their soul. She is the bestselling author of numerous creations such as Light Is the New Black, Rise Sister Rise and the Work Your Light Oracle, and the creator of the Rise Sister Rise Membership. Through all of Rebecca's creations she encourages people all over the world to spend more time with their soul.

About the Artist:
Danielle Noel is a visual artist and designer who creates intuitive wellness tools for spiritual practitioners and seekers. She is the creator of The Starchild Tarot and The Moonchild Tarot, and the illustrator for the Work Your Light Oracle cards. Often seen through a surrealist lens, most of her work is based in ancient lore and mysticism, woven through the magic of a Divine Feminine voice. Her aim is to share and expand new experiences for her readers and viewers.
