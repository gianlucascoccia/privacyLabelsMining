Description

NYC Bus Checker brings you live bus times, smart journey planning and detailed route maps for all of New York. Right to your iPhone, iPad and Apple Watch.

With official MTA-licensed support for bus and subway, NYC Bus Checker has you covered, no matter where you need to go in New York.

Main Features

• Live times at all of New York’s 15,000+ bus stops
• Use your Apple Watch to view an Instant Departure Board or track your planned journeys.
• Plan a journey anywhere in NYC by bus, subway, rail and more
• Get live times in your Notification Center with the Bus Checker widget
• Explore 3D route maps for all NYC Bus Routes - see exactly where your bus goes.
• Download official MTA bus and subway maps - get around underground
• Never miss your stop again with Stop Alerts
• Get real-time CitiBike dock info
• Be one step ahead with updates on detours, closures and cancellations
• Save your favorite stops, routes and places for easy access
