Description

SMASHY ROAD: WANTED

You are WANTED! Take your driving to new heights! Master and unlock all 90 vehicles. Race through the desert, grasslands and the city or encounter SECRET areas with great rewards! Escape the POLICE, the SWAT, the ARMY JEEPS and the TANKS! How long can you last?

Smash the road and see if you can beat your friends highscore in the online leaderboards!

FEATURES
- 90 unlockable vehicles!!
- Random generated environment with chance to encounter secret locations
- All vehicles have their own behaviour. Find your favourite!
- Great visuals and sound

Outstanding but...

This game is probably the best iPhone game I've ever tried yet! There's so many cars to get, so many things to do and so many possibilities! But the thing is that I gave a 4 star rating for a reason, and a good one too. There's a glitch that happens in the middle of a round where the bottom of your tires just fall into the ground preventing you from moving or doing anything! And it's not just me myself thats experiencing that glitch, because I noticed that when the glitch happens to you, it happens to every single other vehicle in that round it's self! So pretty much every single car and truck get disabled! And plus the only way to get out of that round to do another one where the glitch is fixed is COMPLETELY closing out the game itself and then opening it back up. Now this glitch isn't like a SUPER bad thing but it's just a bit annoying for me. But I'm pretty sure there's people that get really mad over this glitch so, developers, please. Do something about this. And then I'll give you a five star rating! Other than that though, I don't think there's anything else wrong with this game! Oh yeah and if you are one of the players of this game reading this, thanks for using your time. I highly appreciate it! Same goes for then devs!

Bigdaddyvolker , 09/05/2018

Buggy and too many ads

I have no idea how many times I’ve fallen thru the map or just gotten stuck (car drops down a bit on non-road areas and cannot move) but it’s far too many for such a simple game. It’s entertaining at first but once you get used to it, the game loses its value and the bugs just make it more frustrating.
I did purchase a legendary vehicle just to see what the big difference was but I still get ads that I have to sit thru. As if this wasn’t bad enough, after you sit thru one and get to close it, up comes an interactive one that you have to wait for the close button to appear. I know this game never said that buying something would get rid of the ads, but it’s fairly common for this to be the case with free games. So basically, another disappointment.
Also, the loot box chances I believe are rigged. I did pull 1 legendary vehicle (very early on) but I’ve done over a hundred since then, no more legendary. I think it’s rigged to give you 1 legendary vehicle and then make the chances zero in order to keep players paying. I have played a lot of games and earned a lot of money but I think if I had to purchase the rest of the available vehicles, the cost would still be over $100.
Still though, rating 3 stars because it did offer some entertainment and I’ll probably fire it up here and there when I get bored and have a few minutes to burn.
