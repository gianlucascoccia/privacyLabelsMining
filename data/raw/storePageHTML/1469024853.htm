Description

Create beautiful stories with Ash. Elegant and simple.

Combine photos and videos, customize the colors and fonts, using perfect eye-catching minimal templates, and share them on Instagram in one click!

● Get more followers with impressive stories
● Use professionally designed templates for every occasion
● Combine photos and videos in your stories from iCloud and your camera roll
● Add your own text
● Pick your favorite templates to use them again
● Create and post a perfect story in a few clicks
