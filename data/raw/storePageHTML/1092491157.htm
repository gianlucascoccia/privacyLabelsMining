1tap receipts’ receipt scanner EXTRACTS the supplier, date, amount and expense category from your receipts, bills and invoices with UNBELIEVABLE accuracy.

Email your digital invoices to 1tap receipts and we will turn those into tax deductions! 1tap receipts is the ultimate app to help self-employed individuals track their finances and expenses.

Manage your finances in a few taps! Scan your receipts and have full access to all of your documents for at least 7 years in case of an audit. Stop your accountant from chasing you down for missing receipts and invoices and keep all your finances and expenses.

Try 1tap receipts right now using the worst receipt you can find and see how it goes!

Use the receipt scanner to boost your income and never miss out on IRS tax deductions next tax season or any. Leave the accounting to us!

How it Works
================

1. TAKE A PHOTO:
Open 1tap receipts and use the scanner to snap your receipt or invoice.

2. AUTO EXTRACT:
Never struggle with inputting your data from receipts, expenses and finances again. Data from the receipt or invoice is automatically extracted and placed in the correct Schedule C category.

3. SAVE AND SHARE RECEIPTS:
Instantly save all of your receipts, invoices and expenses. Send your receipts and Schedule C categorization to your accountant or whoever else may need to see them this tax season.

Self-employed and annoyed with your business bookkeeping? Make accounting, invoicing and finance management easy for self-employed individuals with 1tap receipts. Snap, scan and let us take care of the rest.

Download 1tap receipts today and join the Self Employed individuals who are saving time and finding more IRS tax deductions than ever before.

Very good but needs some work

This app is very good...but not quite there yet.
It’s better than Shoebox. Shoebox does have mileage tracking which is a plus.
This app needs to have a user defined supplier field. Whoever enters the suppliers interprets the names differently each time from receipts.
This forces the user to make corrections. Thus a drop down pre populated supplier list would make it easier to make corrections. Also, small businesses that resell goods need an inventory category. This is not a business expense on schedule C tax form. The manual receipt entry is also week. It should allow multiple lines and multiple pics.
They need to get rid of the annoying tips that show up even on the paid version.
