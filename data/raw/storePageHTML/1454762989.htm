Description

Instories can help anyone who creates content in social networks: bloggers, small businesses, and SMM professionals. The app will help you every step of the way – from providing inspiration and source materials to video editing and saving it on Instagram, Facebook, Snapchat, TikTok, and VK in the right format.

Here is what you will find inside:
⋆ Ready-made templates for your stories and posts
⋆ Video editor
⋆ Photo and video library
⋆ Music library
⋆ Fonts that are not available in standard social media editors
⋆ Animated transitions between scenes
⋆ Possibility to add your own logo
⋆ High resolution file export
⋆ Formats for all social networks

All elements can be customized to match your account's style! The music and image files have been checked and approved for use on social media.

We keep an eye on current trends and expand our font, template, music, and animation collections 3-4 times a month, so that you can use the latest effects: neon, minimalistic, plastic, film, or the 90s vibe. Even if you create Stories every day, with Instories you will have enough ideas to create new content every time.

Send us your ideas and feedback to our email hello@ylee.io or on Instagram @instories.app. Use #instoriesapp to tag us in your Stories and posts, and we will share your Stories in our account!
