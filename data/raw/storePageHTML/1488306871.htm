Description

Welcome to Idle Used Car Dealer, in which you'll be running a used car business from the scratch.Keeping your used car business in operation means taking every step to ensure that you're an honest dealer. How to play:Acquire more vehicle inventory from used-car sellers.Hire assistants and salespeople to help talk with customers. Train your salesperson to assist customers and ask questions about what the customer needs.Calculate a "bottom dollar" for each of your used vehicles.Unlock various models of used cars and make more money.

Premium Membership offers a weekly subscription, you will have a 3-days FREE trail period, after this period you will be charged $4.99. After buying this subscription, you will unlock following features:
A batch of new models
A sales champion in each lot
All vehicles are accelerated with special effects
Automatic collection of sideline income
30 free gems per day

Fun game but some problems

I’ve played this game for about two weeks and I’ve enjoyed it so far. It’s a fun game similar to adventure capitalist/ communist but there are some problems. One, the game is really fast paced until you start renovating the super car and sports car lot. When you get them to silver and gold, it gets ridiculously expensive. Like a one day time jump can only get you one upgrade which is ridiculous and there’s a problem with earning money offline, you only get 2 hours worth of money each time. You could pay a weekly subscription to get 10 hours instead but it should be free either way. I stoped playing the game recently because well, i finished the game in less than 2 weeks so the developers need to add new lots of higher levels of lots. And lastly, you have to watch way to many ads. Like everyday, I had to watch a minimum of 20 ads to get gems and money to put towards upgrading my super car lot at most... 10 times. At one point I could only upgrade my lot 4 times. This game is fun to play, it just gets really tough and boring when you are a higher level, if you want a bette game to play, play adventure capitalist/ communists instead because at least in those games, you get money for every second you are offline.
