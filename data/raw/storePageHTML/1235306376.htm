Description

FASHIONPHILE
From Hermes to Chanel, Gucci, and Louis Vuitton, FASHIONPHILE is the premier destination for buying and selling the most covetable of pre-owned handbags and accessories at up to 70% off retail. With free shipping in both directions and 60-day Layaway at no cost to you, owning ultra-luxury designer accessories has never been more accessible… or sustainable! Our world-class Authentication team of brand-specific experts inspects and certifies every item we sell. We stand by our 100% lifetime guarantee to ensure your trust. Looking to sell? Get paid up front any way you want – by check, direct deposit, store credit (with a 10% bonus!), or PayPal – the very next day after your items are received and authenticated.

ANYTIME, ANYWHERE
The FASHIONPHILE app makes buying and selling easier than ever with customized alerts on your favorite items, enhanced product search and filtering, and quick tools for selling your luxury accessories.

Features include:
* Search, filter, shop, and share your favorites among our 9,000+ one-of-a-kind rare pieces
* Be the first to know when your favorite items are discounted, back in stock, or in other shoppers’ carts
* Request a quote on your luxury accessories in minutes with our quick selling tools
* Manage your account, shipping status, past orders, returns, and seller offers on-the-go
* Click-to-call and map your way to our showrooms in Beverly Hills, San Francisco, San Diego, and New York City for same day pay.

We want to hear from you! Email us at AppFeedback@Fashionphile.com to tell us what you love and what you’d like to see in future app updates.
