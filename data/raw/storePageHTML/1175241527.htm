Oh, hello there, mate! Welcome to this wonderful Pirate Life!

Z-Man NY , 05/22/2020

Fun addictive game but you need a strong alliance

This is a fun addictive game I have been playing for many months. Being part of an active alliance is critical, it’s also the most fun. They’d where you can do the extra tasks to earn in game rewards you will need. If you want to really make progress it needs to be a very strong alliance or at least have one or two of the strongest players on your server in it. If you are not in such an alliance, the big ones will always pounce on you any time you are not very careful. Some of the players seem to be spending loads on money to grow accounts so strong that no one can stand up to them, even so strong an entire alliance can’t stand up to them. That’s the worst part by far. You need to be prepared to spend many hours a day in the game to make any real progress.
The other major problem with the game is that they release app updates frequently and many times the updates are riddled with bugs. Numerous times I have been unable to connect to my account after installing an update. Most of the time the problem is on the iOS platform and/or connecting to my linked account on Facebook. These bugs often last for at least several days and they give little or no compensation for your resulting inconvenience and in game losses .
