Description

Your favorite Dominoes game is now even better with new visuals and UI. Play ONLINE LEAGUES & TOURNAMENTS and unlock new mastery levels every week.

"Do you need to take a break and play a good ‘ole classic from your childhood? Dominoes is the way to go!

With 3 exciting game modes & three difficulty levels, you can play Dominoes the way YOU like it! Choose your favorite, kick back and relax, playing this turned-based game at whatever pace suits you! Set your own winning score, pick your variant and start playing.

Draw Dominoes: Dominoes in their purest, simplest form. Just match the numbers on the tiles on each end and go for the win.

Block Dominoes: A very similar variant that will have you scrambling for solutions – there are no extra tries here – if you can’t figure out your next move, you will have to skip your turn.

All Fives Dominoes: If you want a challenge, All Fives is the variant you should go for. Requiring quick thinking and quick counting, this variant will have you looking to ensure that the tiles you place are a multiple of five.


You can play all these variants with the help of an intuitive user interface that allows you to play one-handed, giving you all the tools you need to learn this deceptively difficult classic board game. What are you waiting for? Take a break, install the game and start placing those tiles!"
