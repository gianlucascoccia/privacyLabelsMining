Description

Toko – Share Snappy Polls!
You can finally create anonymous polls on Snap - just like on any other social network.
Create amazing polls & quizzes and add them to your Snap Stories!
Ask your friends & followers any question, and see results in real time. Polls
are completely anonymous, whether you’re the poll creator or if you answer a
poll – it’s always anonymous.
Get friends’ advice and opinions on what to wear, where to go, and what they
love most.
Brands: Get market intelligence from Snap users on what they think about
your Stories, your brand or your products!
The #1 App for creating polls on Snap.
Toko is the most fun, cool & engaging way to easily create interactive polls &
share them with your Snap friends and followers. Creating a poll takes 10
seconds, after which you can see the results immediately & share them with
everyone back as a new Story.
Polls & quizzes have taken the Social Networking world by storm. Many social apps already allow their users to create and share polls with friends and followers. Now, with the new Toko app, you can do it in Snap! You can also add them to any
other social networking or instant messaging app.
