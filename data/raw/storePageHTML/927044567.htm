Description

Build a fantasy basketball team and compete all season with weekly matchups, live stats, player news and more and be crowned the 2021 H2H Fantasy Basketball Champion!

YOUR Team, YOUR Way!

You are the owner, general manager, and coach of your very own 2020-21 pro basketball franchise. YOU decide the starters each week. Using an imaginary $100 million bank account, pick your team of 10 pro basketball players (any position), who you think will rack up the most points each week using the unique Head2Head scoring system. All of the players listed have been assigned a salary based on past performance and expected performance this season. Your players will earn Head2Head Fantasy Points based on their actual on-court performance.

Head2Head Competition!

Your team is placed in a 28-team league made up of four 7-team divisions. During the 2020-21 H2H Regular Season, which runs from Wednesday, December 23rd through Sunday, May 16th, you will compete Head2Head each week against a new opponent. If during the week your fantasy basketball team earns more H2H points than your opponent's team, then you post a win for that week! At the end of the 2020-21 H2H Regular Season, the top 15 teams in each league will qualify for the H2H Playoffs.

The Playoffs!

At the end of the regular season the top three teams in each division will qualify for the 2021 H2H Playoffs. In addition, three teams from each league that did not automatically qualify for the 2021 H2H Playoffs will earn "wildcard" playoff berths. The 2021 H2H Playoffs will run concurrent with the pro basketball playoffs. Playoff information will be sent via email at the end of the 2020-21 H2H Regular Season. There is no Head2Head play and no elimination in the H2H Playoffs.

We'll Keep You Updated!

With the Complete Package you'll see your scores updated live during each game. Each week you will see updated news on the web site regarding the overall contest. Reports includes your head2head weekly boxscores and upcoming match-ups. Your opponents roster will appear after the final trading deadline each Wednesday at 10pm ET/7pm PT.

Talk some smack!

Use league messaging to talk some smack to your opponents or buddies in your league. You'll receive a message indicator on your team account and and email alert that you've been sent a message. This is a great way to talk up your game and get to know your opponents!

Important tips!

Have fun! H2H Fantasy Basketball is intended solely for entertainment purposes. But we all know that losing sucks. Try any combination of asking for our advice, throwing objects or even some 12 ounce curls to bring back your sanity.

Keep watch for "sleepers" (players who have low salaries and yield high H2H point totals). Take note that the salaries may change twice during the season. Once in the middle of the season some salaries will decrease only. Just prior to the playoffs all player salaries may go both up or down.

H2H Fantasy Basketball is not sponsored, endorsed or administered by, or associated with the National Basketball Association or any NBA team or player or Apple and its affiliates. No purchase necessary. Entries must be received by December 23, 2020 to play.
