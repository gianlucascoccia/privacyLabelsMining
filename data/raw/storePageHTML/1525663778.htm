Not as good as others make it out to be

I tired this game for about 10 minutes. I also tried Draw Duel. That game was more difficult compared to this game, but with Draw Duel, I realized how boring it was within the first two battles of the tutorial.

Draw Coliseum feels like you’re only playing against robots on the simplest easy mode ever. It’s kinda like playing with bots on CoD on “very easy”, only removing all of their weapons, and giving yourself a rocket launcher, only, sike you’re on the smallest floating island from Smash Bros and you have to knock your opponent off the edge.

I made it all the way to League 7 in 10 minutes, and I can assure you that all of the reviews about ads, are not lying. Literally, they just want you to watch ads so they can make money. The game is as easy as butter slices. It’s not even fun, and the only reason I downloaded it was the nostalgia of Beyblades. The idea of drawing your blades was cool but even that is limited. I drew the smallest dot possible multiple games and still won again those with detailed and extended blades. Don’t waste your time or phone storage on this junk. The developers won’t listen to their consumers. No point in playing, unless you need something for your child to do for a while. MAYBE they will think it’s fun.
