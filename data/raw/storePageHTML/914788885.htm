Description

Provides advanced Unusual Options Activities Monitor and Scanner, Live Scanning of Big Money Options, Options Price Alert, fast Live Portfolio tracking with in-depth options support, handy tools to draw powerful Payoff diagrams of your well planned options strategies and calculate the profit & loss, Live Options Chain, Options Greeks, IV, Put-Call Parity, etc calculated in Real-Time for Each options strike, Options Volume, OI, Max Pain Charts in various styles, and many more.

Options Pro is the BEST Options app and helps you trade options like an expert, e.g. helps you become fully aware of the Implied Volatility, watch the Open Interest, Daily Volume Strikes Patterns in different strikes, observe the subtle difference of the spreads between bid and ask to find out the best strike you want to trade on! Trade on the Options Expiration day with Max Pains; Track Options and Stocks in multiple Watch-lists, Check Real-Time Advanced Stock Quote; Study Advanced Stock Chart, Sync strategies and watch-lists among multiple devices; Share easily on Facebook / Twitter.

Watch the market wisely, Plan well, and Trade smart with Options Pro. Anytime, anywhere, even build the strategy & explore the Payoff Diagram without internet connection!
