JemSoso2 , 07/22/2019

One of the best!

This is one of the best games to have on the iPhone. Saying that alone means a lot as most movie tie-in games are never worth the players’ time. Not only does it look and play extremely well, it also does not force unnecessary micro transaction that plague most cellphone games in order to advance! There are plenty of robots to choose from and the upgrade mechanic can lead to some devastating blows. The online verses matches are also great as they are essential to achieving some of the better prizes the game has to offer and fighting opponents that are more skilled than the computer. I downloaded this app once before and enjoyed it immensely (why I gave it up is another story). However, I have it back and I can easily say that it still holds up!

Since someone else complained about the amount of energy given, I’ll only mention two other things that bother me. The first is the fact that the game will just kick me out at times when I start it; I don’t know if it’s the game’s fault or my iPhone’s but it does get annoying at times. The second is the fact that only one new robot was introduced after all the time it had to make updates. If you ask me, the developers should bring in some of the bots from the 360/PS3 version (such as Ramchip, Hylas, Seahawk, and Pallidius). Other than these complaints the game is perfect the way it and I plan on keeping it this time!
