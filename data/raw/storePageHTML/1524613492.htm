Description

Block Puzzle - Classic wood block puzzle game, is a combination of sudoku and woody block puzzles. Train your brain and Download now!

Block Puzzle - Classic wood block puzzle game is a classic addictive wooden style block puzzle game. Block Puzzle - Classic wood block puzzle game has 2 modes. One is the most CLASSIC mode: Match blocks to complete lines and squares to get them removed. Keep the board clean and beat your high score in a block puzzle game! The other mode is JIGSAW: Select different pieces of wood to board, until the block can be completely filled the grid. and you will unlock a beautiful painting after you finish game.

Block Puzzle CLASSIC mode challenges you to fit blocks of different shapes into a 10×10 grid. Match blocks and beat your high score. JIGSAW mode relaxing you to filled grid with different shape of wood block and you will get a beautiful painting when you complete it. Which is fun, relaxing and brain test.

Block Puzzle - Classic wood block puzzle game Features：
*Two modes: CLASSIC mode and JIGSAW mode make you fun game experience.
*CLASSIC mode: Fit Blocks of different shapes into a 10×10 grid. Game will be over if there is no room on the board for given blocks
*JIGSAW mode: Filled grid with different shape of wood block to unlock beautiful paintings.
*Easy, fun and challenging, no pressure and no time limit to play classic wood block puzzle game
*Free to play classic and woody puzzle game
*Master the block puzzle game by destroying several elements with just one move.
*Play it when you are bored or want to train your brain – Anytime, Anywhere.
