Description

Your fluffy loved one needs a little tender, love and care. It’s time to take them to the vet so they can get checked up! Are they scratching behind their ears too much? No problem - we can get those fleas for you. Are they licking at their paws too much? Maybe they stepped on something that needs to get pulled out! This Vet Doctor can cure any malady that is ailing your pet.

Whether you are a dog lover, a cat lover, or just love animals in general, then Paw Care! is the game for you. You can finally take care of pets all on your own. Brush them, pet them, love them all at your fingertips. It’s like you are a doctor for pets. It’s time to operate!


Game Features:
1. Simple but addicting mechanics
Pluck, Cut, groom, bandage - do whatever it takes to heal the pet.

2. Feel like a Doctor or Nurse
You are the best vet in town - everyone trusts you with their loved ones

3. Run your own Veterinary Clinic
Keep your clinic up to date. Complete operations and upgrade your business.

4. Relax and enjoy
Ever wonder what it was like to be a pet doctor? Now you can, with no stress or school!
