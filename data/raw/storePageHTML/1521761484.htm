Description

Meet, flirt, and fall in love with the gorgeous girls of Crush Crush!

Hey hot stuff! Want a fun and flirty game that's guaranteed to make you laugh out loud and have a good time? Welcome to Crush Crush - an idle dating sim!

Match with hot anime girls and have fun flirting your way to romance. Chat with your crush and go on dates with fun female friends who are looking for love. Earn cash with idle jobs and hobbies to improve your chances with these lovely ladies. Buy your lover gifts and outfits to boost their affection toward you. Unlock photos and dialogue prompts as your love grows!

Secretly my favorite idle game

I figured this would be a cheap generic idle game with anime girls but shockingly, it’s much better than that! It’s actually pretty unique, with funny writing and good progression. Instead of mindlessly tapping forever or not playing at all like other idle games, you use Time Blocks to set jobs and skills to progress and level up with the girls, and there’s a lot of switching them up and juggling. The texting side-game part is also great and a really smart use of timers and amusing writing, it’s a pretty fun gameplay loop unlocking new things back and forth. No mandatory ads either which is a big plus, although it does have optional ads for bonus rewards which is totally fine by me. Looking forward to new content!
