Description

Unbelievable photo books within 60 seconds. Automatic smart design!

With the new Phototales app, making a beautiful photo book is a breeze! Watch our artificial intelligence do the magic of automatically creating a custom photo book for you – for free! Order a print and enjoy re-living your beautiful memories!

________________________________________________________

SIMPLE AND EFFORTLESS!
Simply tell us your preferences, and watch the Magic Ai do everything for you – photo selection, cropping, grouping, layouts, decoration.

FAST AND BEAUTIFUL!
Phototales will automatically design a stunning photo book for you in seconds. Your book will look like it was elegantly designed by an experienced designer. We have beautiful themes for every occasion, and every style, complete with beautiful backgrounds, decoration & text stickers.

FREE DESIGN!
The design is free; you can purchase the printed photo book if you like it. Make & share as many digital versions for free – no limits, no strings attached.

USE ANYWHERE!
The Phototales app is optimized for use on the go! Start your book on any device and pick up where you left off on any other – a phone or your computer (www.phototales.com). It’s seamless.

________________________________________________________

UNDERSTAND MAGIC Ai
Our Magic Ai is the powerful Artificial Intelligence algorithm on which our photo book maker runs. Here’s a peak into the kind of things it can do, which is how it’s able to deliver the best results.

1. Photo Selection & Grouping: It is able to select the best amongst duplicate photos, and then group & order them to form a narrative.

2. Smart Cropping & Layout: It is able to determine the best crop for your photos, so that no important parts get cut out, and every photo is displayed in the most ideal way. It chooses from amongst millions of layouts to find something that works best.

3. Smart Design: It embellishes the space around your photos based upon the theme you selected. It is even able to automatically apply Smart Captions and Stickers, based upon the content of your photos!

LadyMelinda , 01/22/2021

Cool App

Nice app. Takes a little getting used to. The only problems I have with it are the limited amount of themes, backgrounds and objects that can be used/added to pages in my book and the limited choices for the sizes of pictures (as well as the shapes.) I prefer having a lot more choices for my work, as I make things as gifts for people and I have particular editing to my pictures that this app doesn’t accommodate. So I use what they have, which isn’t quite as elaborate as I like. But it does allow me to edit and rearrange pictures, so I’m happy about that. The app should really give the option to the person using it of being able to arrange and modify pictures to their liking. Some photos are in places I’d rather they not be in. They’re also not the shape I’d prefer. If I had a choice, I’d add extra choices for picture sizes and shapes, a wider range of objects, colors, borders and backgrounds to the app. In my opinion, (as a producer and editor) I feel those additions would make the app better.
