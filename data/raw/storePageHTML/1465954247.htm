Description

DoZ is an online simulator of survival in a post-apocalyptic world. Here, those who lived through Conflagration were left to survive against hunger, aberrations, fanatical mutants and zombies, diseases and radiation. And, of course, other survivors.

Game features:
- Build and craft on the ground and underground;
- Energy is restored for free by food and at the Shelter;
- Survive: sate your hunger and quench your thirst, recover from radiation and disease;
- Follow the story: dozens of characters, hundreds of quests and notes;
- Choose any vehicle to get around — from bicycle to UAZ;
- Realistic graphics and lighting — day and night change, volumetric fog;
- Aberration zones are filled with mysterious artifacts;
- Gloomy wastelands, dark forests, anomalous ruins full of zombies, bandits and wild animals;
- Trade and communicate with different factions — from scientists to military;
- Gain reputation with factions and characters to receive unique rewards;
- Advanced craft: 150+ blueprints for weapons, armor, vehicles;
- 60+ kinds of weapons: AK, M-16, Makarov pistol, and Mosin-Nagant;
- Stealth Mode: hide in the bushes, sneak up and kill without being noticed;
- Use a special workbench to repair weapons and armor;
- Complete events: protect allies from zombies or attack camps of fanatics;
- Locations are constantly changing: look for airdrops and hidden stashes, explore aberration zones;
- Go to raids on bosses in radioactive dungeons.

Coming soon:
- Multiplayer survival game mode with friends: free-for-all PvP;
- Big settlements where you can communicate with other players;
- Clan system: upgrade your own base and participate in clan wars;
- MMO raids on bosses and hunts for the walking dead;
- Co-op PvE quests;
- NPC allies and pets, including a dog and robotic dog;
- Build a spy drone and besiege other players’ shelters in PvP mode.

Gameplay:
You're one of the survivors, a Strider—the stalker of the wastelands. You explore the Last Territories, the aberration zone that mysteriously survived the war. Here, man is more dangerous than beast, here you can be killed for a can of tinned meat and worn shoes. Somewhere in the Territories, your last friend, a lone wolf aberration expert, has disappeared. Only he can help you regain your memory and survive…

Stjacks , 07/26/2020

Best Survivor Game on iPad

I have played a many of the survivor games and this is by far one of the best, if not the THE BEST, available. I love their energy using and replenishing system. It is so fair and friendly. I am single mom and I play this to decompress sometimes and I don’t have the extra money to buy energy or supplies but with this game I don’t EVER feel like i have to have fun and meet the goals. The developers have put a lot into helping you get rewards and resource boxes. I also LOVE that I don’t have to play with other people. I love playing these survivor games but I always feel penalized when forced to have to play multiplayer but I play to escape playing with folks and this game let’s me do it without penalties. The biggest challenge has been getting the supplies to build my bike. I have not been able to find the bicycle bars in the place designated as I result, i don’t get to visit the engineer trader or some of the other quests I cannot progress to because I don’t have a bike. Again, I don’t have a lot of money to buy items so when I finally did splurge to try to move things along, the bike materials gave me a lot of stuff it’s easy for me to collect and make, but not the hard to find bicycle parts. This is possibly the shortfall of the game. I’ve been playing during most of this Spring-Summer crisis and I still can’t get my bike done. Developers, I hope you help us along in that regard.

Linkz101 , 02/10/2020

Energy to gather resources???

This game is quality.... I’ve played every survival game in the App Store. This by far is the best. However, the one flaw with this game ( major flaw) is you are required to spend energy to gather resources ( ie chop trees, rock etc) no other game has this. I agree with energy to travel, that’s fine. The developers try and compensate with getting energy with watching ads and eating food. But all other survival games do the same thing and they do not charge energy to gather materials..... SUPER greedy tactic on the developers. Until they change this feature it’s a solid one star game. So much potential, but they need to remove energy cost to gather resources. Energy to travel is a good, just not to gather materials.

UPDATE: after months of playing this game, I have to change my review from 1 star to 5 stars. I fully take back the greed comment. This company actually rewards you and is NOT pay to win. The energy system is different but actually clever. Well done developers... I eat my words. Solid game and fun. Keep the events coming and please stay generous with the rewards.

Wow! Thanks a lot! We're happy now ^_^
