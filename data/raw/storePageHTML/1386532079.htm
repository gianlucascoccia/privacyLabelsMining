Description

Helium is a social dating app that allows you to communicate freely with potentials around you. Quick to join, and easy to use, we know that aesthetics are part of the equation, but when it comes to connection - conversation is where it counts.

Our tagging system encourages respectful behaviour and gives you the chance to put your best foot forward, empowering you to decide who you wish to communicate with.

Here at Helium, we believe that the fun of dating is one of the highest highs. So we created something fun with you in mind to add fuel to the float.

We’re not just another dating app, we’re bringing conversation back.


FEATURES

Everyone wants to know the perks, right? Well, here are ours:

* Make the first move. With helium, you don’t have to wait to be liked or matched. Impress with the flex of your flirting skills instead. Ready? Set? GO.

* Value your privacy? So do we. No integration with social media needed, no email address, no phone number.

* Pick your best pic with our built-in photo editing tools. We know just how much first impressions count.

* Long sign-ups make us yawn. You’ll be ready to go in less than 30 seconds.

* We are fully encrypted. Only the person you exchange messages and photos with can see your content.

* Rate your potential. Are they cheeky, wild, hot, funny, or…just… something else? Give them a tag or use the tags to filter your search. Hey, you never know when you might be seeking someone sleazy ;).

* Lost for words? We’ve got you. Our fully featured chat will allow you to use emoji’s, gifs, memes and photos when you need to fill in the gaps.


EXTRAS

Helium is free to download and use. If you are looking to get more attention on Helium, you can purchase our Boost feature, getting more eyes on you as your profile is pushed to the top for 24 hours. Our subscription service ‘Helium Plus’ will add additional benefits to your Helium experience with the following:

* Traveller - Change your location and make connections in different places.

* Free Weekly Boost - Stand out from the crowd. Your profile will be pushed to the top of the list.

* Hide your profile - You will only be visible to people you initiate contact with.

* More Potentials - Find 5x more people in your local area.

* Unlimited — Contact as many people as you want.

THE FINE PRINT

Helium is free to download and use. If you choose to subscribe to Helium Plus, it is a monthly subscription. The prices may vary per country and are subject to change without notice. Prices are clearly displayed in the app. Payment will be charged to your iTunes account at confirmation of purchase. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current payment period. Your subscription will automatically renew itself, unless auto-renewal is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current payment period. You can manage your subscriptions and turn off auto-renewal by going to your Account Settings in the iTunes Store. Your personal data is securely stored on Helium
