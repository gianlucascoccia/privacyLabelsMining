Description

Now available for iPad!

Make winning bets with advice and picks from top sports betting experts.

The BettingPros app puts consensus and expert-driven sports betting advice at your fingertips to help you pinpoint the best odds and make winning bets.

ODDS & LINES

• Browse consensus betting odds for point spreads, totals, moneyline, and futures across the NFL, NBA, MLB, NCAA, NHL, Soccer, UFC and more.

• Find the best NFL and NBA player prop bets for every game and keep up with player stats and covers live during the games.

• Target the best odds and lines aggregated from all of the top sportsbooks, including FanDuel Sportsbook, DraftKings Sportsbook, and BetMGM.

PICKS & BETS *

• Find winning bets with expert consensus picks from the most accurate experts. Over 150 of the top football betting experts were tracked during the NFL season.

• Track your bets and monitor your performance with our pick reporting tools and charts. Filter and track by sport, bet type, and more.

• Compete in an industry-first, pick accuracy competition and see how your betting instincts stack up against the community and experts on the accuracy leaderboards.

GAMES & SCORES

• Research team stats, follow player injuries, and analyze trends to help you make smarter bets before a game.

• Follow games and picks with live scores and plays. Once a game is over, easily see totals and line covers for NFL, College Football (NCAAF), College Basketball (NCAAB), NBA, MLB, NHL, Soccer, and more.

• Browse and search NFL player stats and prop bet lines simultaneously.

GUIDES & ANALYSIS

• Learn simple and advanced sports betting tips with our comprehensive Sports Betting Guide for beginners and sharps alike.

• Read daily articles and analysis, ranging from recommended picks to tips on how to become a more profitable sports bettor, across a multitude of sports: NFL, NCAAF, NBA, MLB, NCAAB, Soccer, Golf, Tennis, UFC, Boxing, Horse Racing, and more.

• Listen to episodes of the BettingPros Football Podcast and Daily Juice Podcast for quick sports betting picks every day, all year round.

BettingPros is a sports betting advice network created by FantasyPros, the #1 source for fantasy sports advice. Be sure to also visit us on web and social media for more sports betting and fantasy advice.

* BettingPros content is intended for entertainment purposes only and does not include real money gambling. Picks added in the app do not include real money wagers, payouts or prizes. Please be aware of and respect the laws regarding sports betting for your jurisdiction.

BettingPros is not affiliated with or endorsed by Apple, the NFL, MLB, NBA, NHL, PGA, or NCAA. BettingPros is not a gambling site and does not accept wagers.
