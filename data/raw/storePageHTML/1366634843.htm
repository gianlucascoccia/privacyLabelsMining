Description

If you are looking for a way to share your podcast episodes on social media, this is the right app for you!
Create a short video from a podcast or mp3 file!

If you are looking for a way to share your podcast episodes on social media, this is the right app for you!
Podcast Preview works simple, add the RSS feed of your podcast, select the episode, specify the time range and it's done. It will generate a short video displaying the soundwave that you can use to advertise the episode.
What you can do with the app:
- Add RSS feed to select episodes
- Use custom mp3 files to generate previews before you publish the episode
- Change background to a color or to an image
- Supports landscape (4:3), square (1:1) and portrait (9:16) aspect ratios
- Change the color of the title or the soundwave
- Reposition the soundwave or the title
- Much more coming in the future versions

If you have any suggestions, please let me know!
