Description

Moneycontrol App is Asia's #1 App for Business & Finance.

Track the latest updates on Indian and Global financial markets on your smartphone with the Moneycontrol App. It covers multiple assets from BSE, NSE, MCX and NCDEX exchanges, so you can track Indices (Sensex & Nifty), Stocks, Futures, Options, Mutual Funds, Commodities and Currencies with ease.

Monitor your investments with Portfolio and Watchlist. Stay updated with the wide range of news covered in our News and Personal Finance sections. Get expert’s views, in-depth coverage and analysis of the financial markets with live streaming of CNBC AWAAZ (Hindi), CNBC BAJAR (Gujarati) and CNBC PRIME HD.

Here’s what you can expect from the Moneycontrol App.

Seamless Navigation:
• Easily browse through Portfolio, Markets Data, Latest News, Watchlist, Message Board etc.
• Voice enabled search to help you quickly find stocks, indices, mutual funds, commodities, and news.
Latest Market Data:
• Latest quotes and in-depth market stats of stocks, F&O, mutual funds, commodities and currencies from BSE, NSE, MCX and NCDEX
• Latest prices of Sensex, NIFTY, India VIX and more
• Interactive and historical charts such as Line, Area, Candlestick and OHLC
News:
• All-day coverage of latest market, business and economy news; plus interviews of senior management
• 'Text to Speech' feature for news and articles to help you listen to content on the go
• Option to save your favourite news and articles to read later, even when offline

Portfolio:
• Monitor your portfolio across Stocks, Mutual Funds, ULIPs, Bullion and other asset classes
• Timely updates on your portfolio’s performance and news & alerts relating to stocks you hold

Personalized Watchlist:
• Track your favourite stocks, mutual funds, commodities, futures and currencies from Watchlist
• Stay updated with timely alerts whenever there is any news on the stocks you track
Forum Message Board:
• Stay a step ahead by following your favourite topics and top boarders
• Engage and participate in conversations relating to your portfolio or assets of your interest

For any queries or feedback regarding the App, you can write to us through the in-App Support section.

Moneycontrol Pro Offers:
