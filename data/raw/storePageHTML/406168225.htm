Description

US Lacrosse Mobile Coach
Welcome to the most versatile coaching resource ever developed for the dynamic sport of lacrosse: Mobile Coach! This new resource, developed by US Lacrosse and their Coaching Education Program, is your single source to take advantage of US Lacrosse’s coaching resources.

Mobile Coach allows you to:
• Browse a library of lacrosse videos and drills different ability levels
• Seamlessly build a practice plan for your team
• Share your practice plan with your team and others
• Show your team drills and videos while they are on the practice field

All US Lacrosse members have FREE access to Mobile Coach for the length of their membership. Not a member of US Lacrosse? Visit our website see the benefits that will connect you to the greater lacrosse community and allow you to take advantage of all our coaching and education tools.
