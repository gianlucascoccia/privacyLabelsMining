Description

Give it rythm to your life with Instant Buttons! Enjoy the best sound effects and surprise to your family and friends. Create funny situations!

Instant Buttons is an original soundboard, it's an unique collection of sounds. This application will offer to you many funny and quickly sounds that you might use anytime.

Up to 400 sounds! From the most famous memes of 4chan/internet to dialogues of your favorites series going through mitical videogames sounds. Instant Buttons includes all the sounds needed for a fun moment. Besides, we are open for suggestions, only send us an e-mail with the sound that you want.

And if that's not enough, Instant Buttons has the possibility to save the sounds as favorites, hide buttons, choose to repeat a sound, share buttons, add your own buttons...

It's nice?

I recently went back to the app, as I had it on my iPad years ago. It looks the same, although I noticed the new UI, But something is different. Sounds are randomly cutting off and some sound effects I try to favorite doesn't show in my favorites. Then when I go back to the same sound effect I tried to favorite, it says that I never favorited it. This app is great to a point. I love playing this through a party chat on PlayStation with my friends, some of the sound effects I want in my favorites so they are easy access to me. So I don't have to search up the sound effect leaving a awkward silence before switching to the other sound effect. But other than those complaints this app is a really good mobile soundboard, I love using it so much!
