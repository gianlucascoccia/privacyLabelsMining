Description

Advanced Concrete Construction-Math Calculator from Calculated Industries

The official ConcreteCalc Pro from Calculated Industries is now available for iPhone and iPad users. And while perhaps obvious, if you change from an iOS device to a non-iOS device, you WILL need to buy a new license as they are incompatible.

It helps concrete, masonry and paving professionals reduce material waste, improve design accuracy, prevent re-work and cut estimating time. Increase efficiency in the field or in the office with fast, accurate solutions for square-ups, loads, area, volume, rebar, drops and stairs. Even finds the number of bags required for small jobs.

Built with the same features and functions and the same core engine that powers the industry-standard handheld ConcreteCalc Pro from Calculated Industries.

Need to quickly and accurately solve a concrete problem for a job? Do it on your iPhone or iPad anytime, anywhere instantly.

Ideal for Concrete, Masonry and Paving Professionals

• Work directly in and convert between all building dimensional formats: yards, feet-inch-fractions, inch fraction, decimal feet (10ths, 100ths), decimal inches and metric (m, cm, mm)
• Easily calculate areas, volumes and weight per volume, truck loads
• Rebar quantity solutions
• Instant square-up and drop for perfect layouts

FEATURES:

• Created by Calculated Industries, the originator of the award-winning Construction Master® calculators
• Built-in help – just press and hold the desired key for full, clear definitions, explanations and examples of keystrokes

Dimensional Math and Conversions
• Yards
• Feet-Inch-Fractions

• Length, Width, Height (Depth) keys
