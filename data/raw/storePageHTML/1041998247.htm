Description

Transform your fitness with Gym Plan. Unlock workouts for the gym or at home with plans created by real personal trainers not computers.

We're an app that has deeply thought about designing the best gym workout experience. Such as the timing of when you're asked to log the weight you lift. The ability to adapt exercises on the go when gym equipment is in use. We know these small things are super important in real life to getting the most from your workout.

There's no substitute for real life experience and our plans are created by qualified trainers. Every workout is tested for effectiveness and our variety is second to none.

LOSE WEIGHT
---------------
High Intensity Interval Training (HiiT) is an incredible system to burn fat fast in only 10-20 minutes.

You will do fast paced movements with and without weights improve your fitness and activate your fat burning system. The major benefit of this style of workouts is that your metabolism will stay elevated for another 16 hours post workout, meaning you are still burning calories as you go about the rest of your day.

BUILD MUSCLE
----------------
Our customers love the muscle building benefits of hitting the gym armed with the right workout plan. You will unlock workouts with techniques such as supersets and trisets to stimulate your muscles to grow while burning unwanted fat!

Heavier low repetition weight training will help to recruit more muscle fibers ensuring you build strong muscles. Higher repetition weight training helps to achieve hypertrophy which will give you a toned, muscular look. You will use free weights, machines to target your muscles effectively. No matter what equipment you have access to there is a way to build muscle with Gym Plan.

What’s New

Mar 11, 2021

Version 7.0.2

Introducing Gym Plan Version 7.0.2 (with bug fixes for 7.0.0 & 7.0.1)

Hey everyone, we hope you’re doing well and staying strong. We’ve listened to your feedback and are happy to announce this BIG update to the app.

So what’s new?

--------------------------
New Home Screen Design
--------------------------
Many of you wanted to see what the progress of your plan easier. The new home screen shows you the name of your plans, how many workouts you’ve completed and what's left to do. It's much easier to stay organised and on track.

------------------------------
Follow Multiple Plans At Once
------------------------------
Another big request was the ability to follow multiple plans at the same time. So we’ve done away with the workout queue and you now have all your plans and custom plans in one screen. Quickly scroll to the next workout from any plan. Manage and change between multiple plans from your new home screen.

---------------------------------
Updated Discovery Tab (Search)
---------------------------------
With so many amazing plans to choose from we’ve made it easier to find what you want. You’re now able to swipe left and right through each section to quickly see a plan that catches your eye. Much like how you find a new film on Netflix.

----------------------
Filter Plans By Gender
----------------------
It's now easier to find plans for any gender whether you want something different, a challenge or to show Gym Plan to a friend. Choose your filter options at the top of the search screen.

---------------
New top menu
---------------
Your calories have been moved to top menu of the home tab to tidy things up and keep your focus on all things related to working out. You will also find your list of favourite workouts.

--------------------
New AMRAP Design
--------------------
We'll gradually rename AMRAP throughout the app to Max Rounds so it's easier to understand for new users. Max rounds got a fresh design making it easier to tap on the screen to completed a round. There's also a new round marker to show the pace you are completing rounds…this is a great way to measure your fitness levels.

----------------------
New 3-Tab Navigation
----------------------
We wanted to make things less confusing, so have done away with under used elements and whittled the bottom navigation bar down to just 3 tabs; discover, home and progress.

---------------------
Minor Improvements
---------------------
• Added animation when you select a filter
• Added haptic feedback when you tap a filter
• Added haptics to the Max rounds screen buttons and also when you tap for a new round

Bug Fixes
----------
• Discover plans loading issue is now fixed
• The missing Share Plan button after restarting a plan and the missing muscle groups is fixed on the Home screen
• Tapping on the Workout Card in the Home Screen opens the Workout Preview
• Finishing a workout takes you to your Home Screen
• Change Exercise screen not being shown
• Calories not being shown for some workouts in the Home Screen
• Favouriting/Unfavouriting a workout didn’t update the state in the Home Screen cards
• Fixed crashing when users were signing in with email

We hope you enjoy this huge update and as always there’s more to come. We know times have been really tough lately for all of us and we wanted to say thank you so much for your continued support.

Dave, Paul, Radu & Mark
