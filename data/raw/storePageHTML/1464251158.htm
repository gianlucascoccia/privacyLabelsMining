Description

Learn basketball from master trainer, Chris Johnson. Basketball training drills for shooting, ball handling, dribbling, coaching, and scoring. Practice drills at home.

Learn basketball from master trainer, Chris Johnson.

The app features 100’s of basketball training drills to improve your skills.

Basketball skills training for coaches, players, and trainers of all ages.
Basketball drills can be practiced on your home court, in your backyard, or even in your house.
Drills for shooting, ball handling & dribbling, agility, coaching tips, and a variety of other at-home basketball drills to improve your game.
TRAIN LIKE A PRO Practice the exact drills the top players in the league perform.
The basketball drills are for players of all ages.

Drills are great for beginner training, intermediate training, and advanced basketball training.
LIVE WORKOUTS Be on the lookout for exclusive live training sessions with Chris Johnson.
Get beamed into his exclusive and private training facility for special live virtual training sessions.
AT HOME BASKETBALL TRAINING Drills that can be performed right in your living room, outside in your driveway, or even on your home court allowing you to take the app anywhere you go.
Work on your dribbling up and down your driveway practicing right along side the pros.
PARENTS, this training app is for you to teach your kids how to train properly.
COACHES, this training app is for you to learn how to maximize your players in an efficient manner.

PLAYERS, this training app is for you to learn what drills to practice to elevate your game.
The basketball training focuses on: Shooting Scoring Ball Handling Dribbling Pick & Roll Post Ups Strength Training Conditioning Training Vertical Leap Explosiveness Mental Mindset Tips Coaching Tips Basketball Meditation BASKETBALL WORKOUTS Custom basketball workouts from Chris Johnson that you can practice on a daily basis.
