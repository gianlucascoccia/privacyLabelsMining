Description

Kala Ukulele App is the fastest way to tune your ukulele and learn new songs. Includes easy-to-play-along chords, lyrics and backing tracks, Color Chord system, and real feedback! It’s easy: The app breaks down songs into four simple ukulele chords that you will be able to master in an instant. No knowledge of tabs or sheet music required. Have fun with easy-to-play versions of over 100 traditional songs for free, or subscribe to get full access to over 2000 hit songs offered by Musopia. The perfect learning tool for beginners and an awesome songbook for more advanced players, our app also works perfectly with the Color Chords system.

Simply follow our easy-to-use play along system to learn how to play ukulele songs today! Kala Ukulele app displays the songs in a play-along style with easy chords, lyrics, and adjustable backing tracks. The app uses your device’s microphone to track your playing and offers you high-level feedback on how to improve your skills and speed up your progress. You can also enhance your learning experience with chords and strumming video tutorials, which can be accessed whenever you need.

Only 10 minutes of daily practice will have you playing your first song today and rocking with your friends in no time!

Why both beginner and advanced ukulele players LOVE the Kala Ukulele app:
- Forget the frustration with complicated notes and tabs.
- Four simple chords that will have you playing new songs in no time.
- Signature Color Chords song category that matches the Learn to Play Color Chord System.
- Get useful tips on how to improve your skills: Kala Ukulele tracks your performance and gives you feedback.
- Extra tools that will help you learn: tutorial videos, adjustable tempo, transposing to easier chords, chord charts, and a strum machine.
- Super accurate and easy-to-use tuner.
- Select your favorite songs from the music library to create your own songbook.
- Carry the best songs and lyrics in your pocket so you can throw a ukulele play-along party wherever you go!

After downloading Kala Ukulele app, you will receive a free 4-day trial of Musopia’s premium subscription which offers a full access to the interactive songbook with over 2000 hit songs. The use of Kala Song Selection, Color Chords Song Selection and the Tuner will always be free.

Awesome App but 2.2.1 update seems broken

I had never played an instrument before when I bought my first Kala uke 5 months ago. This app really helped me to learn all the chords and, more importantly, to smooth out my chord changes. I stopped going to paid uke lessons and just used this app almost every day. It’s a great app if you’re starting out whether you have a Kala uke or any other brand of uke. The annual subscription is well worth every penny. I’ve even recommended the app to friends who already play guitar in a band and want to learn the uke.

However, since installing the 2.2.1 update 3 days ago the app no longer opens on my iPhone. After uninstalling and reinstalling the app it will open the first time I use it, but if I close the app it won’t open again without completely removing and reinstalling. I reached out to support on their web site but have not had a response. Hopefully they have a resolution soon.

Awesome app

Over a year ago I got a ukulele. I had some books that showed how to play the chords and I started playing. Then this past Christmas I received an iTunes gift card and I got this app and I love it. It has tons of songs from old traditional to the Beatles to Adele and more and they are always adding new songs. When you pick a song the app plays the melody and you play the chords and as the melody is playing it shows you what chord to play next.
