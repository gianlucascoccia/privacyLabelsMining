Piglover999 , 05/24/2020

I don’t like this game

I was very excited when I saw this game. There are not many games where you get to free paint like this, but I was a bit disappointed when I started. First of all, when you mix the paint, it does not actually make the color you made. I mixed a nice light purple, and it said it was a ‘match’ to the color on the picture, then automatically changed it to the color of the picture they gave. Yes, this will help you get 3 stars, but I think it is more fun if you actually get to do it yourself. Second, I was in the middle of painting the first image, when I goy glitched out of the app. At first I thought this had something to do with my WiFi, but this has continued to happen for multiple days. I also asked my friends and it happened to them too. Over all, I think this app has a great idea but has some room for improvement in the technical area.
