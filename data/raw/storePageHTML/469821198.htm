Description

Now you don't even need to start the app to track silver prices and get notified on the silver moves. Packed with features, this makes the best real time silver price tracking system on the market. Welcome to the "Silver Price Watch".

This is not just an app, it is entire price tracking and notification system. Our app is backed by a powerful push notification server delivering user defined price alerts to your home screen, real-time prices to the badge and price information with the chart data directly to the widget.

FEATURES THAT WORK WITHOUT EVEN RUNNING THE APP:
● Price information and price chart using widget technology
● Price on the app badge trough push notifications
● Price change alerts on your home screen trough push notifications

FEATURES INSIDE THE APP:
● Real time price information
● Historical chats
● Custom alert editor
● Price conversion: currencies, ounces and kilograms (in progress)
● Market overview: commodities, currencies and stock indexes (in progress)
● Both orientations supported

REQUIREMENTS:
Supports all iOS Devices: iPhone, iPad and all iPod Touch devices!
