Greetify is an AI app for those who like to try new things and share them! We have prepared a lot of AI-based functions for you: from "what kind of celebrity do you look like" to predicting the future baby. Download it for free and share the result with your friends, family and colleagues!

What will you find with us?

• NEW! Time Machine. Try traveling to the past in a Time Machine - find out what they would have looked like in the past centuries and decades.

• Beauty analysis (Beauty Score) & Face Symmetry Tester. The app calculates the geometric proportions of facial features, such as eyes, nose, lips, etc., as well as the distance between them. When the face analysis is finished, you can see what features of your face determine your beauty. Find out how beautiful you are!

• Celebrity Look Alike. Face Matching determines which celebrity look alike you. We use advanced AI-technology based on a neural network to search through a database of celebrities. What celebrity do i look like? Find out now!

• Baby Maker. See what will your Future Daughter or Son looks like! Based on Exclusive AI Technology Baby Maker applies complex science to deliver hours of fun. We created a unique AI algorithm, based on the study of thousands of faces and on complex mathematical calculations and biometric analysis. Future Baby Maker simulates a photo of your possible common Baby with whoever you want in seconds. Let's check out! Hope you enjoy it!

• What animal do you look like? Which animal are you most like? Proud dog? Wild cat? Find out now! Our neural network will answer the questions: what animal am i ?; what animal are you?

• IQ Score. Have you ever wondered what your IQ is? Try our photo IQ test and find out your the level of intelligence right now!

• Fortune Teller. Use our AI algorithm to find out your future. It will tell you everything about your career, children, old age, etc.

• Character Analysis. Are you kind, suspicious, or...? Add your photo, and our algorithm will determine your character.
