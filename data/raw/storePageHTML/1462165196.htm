Description

* Develop Pro-Level Mental Skills with the #1 Sports Psychology Coaching App *

Created by Dr Jim Afremow (Arizona State, San Francisco Giants), Champion's Mind contains over 130 mental training sessions to help you master the mental component of your sport. Each session is built on the wisdom Jim has used as a mental skills coach and sports psychologist to 17 Olympic Gold Medalists, 7 Hall of Famers and the San Francisco Giants.

Champion's Mind...
- Is designed for competitive and amateur athletes
- Covers all the mental skills you need to excel at your sport
- Is suitable for athletes in all sports
- Includes 130+ sessions that will boost your mental strength when training and competing
- Will help you understand how top professional athletes really think
- Can be used daily to help you grow your mental muscle

The toughest battle in sport is always against your own mind.
Your mind can be your biggest weapon or your biggest weakness. Champion's mind gives you the mental strategies to achieve success in the face of adversity and the tightest of situations:
- Daily Sports Psychology tips and tricks, proven to boost your mental game
- Sports Psychology coaching sessions covering Goal Setting, Gratitude, Mindfulness, Visualization and Positive Self-Talk
- Mindfulness, relaxation and breathing exercises to help you focus on the moment, and then recover quickly after
- Visualization and coping techniques to ensure you perform best when it matters most

Champion's Mind is based on the latest sports psychology research and is the first mental skills training app to focus wholly on helping athletes master the mental component of their sport and achieve peak performance. Think Gold. Download today.
