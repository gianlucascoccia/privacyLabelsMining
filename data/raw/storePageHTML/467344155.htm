Description

OBD2 scanner — diagnostics, coding, maintenance for Audi, BMW, Ford, Lexus, Mercedes, Mini, Opel, Porsche, Renault, Seat, Skoda, Toyota, VW, and other car brands

Carly is the most versatile OBD2 scanner with diagnostic insights, coding, and maintenance functions.
It helps over a million car owners on average save $500 a year. Test it yourself now!

Get the Carly app and the Carly Universal Adapter that access data through the OBD2 port of your car.

It works for Audi, BMW, Ford, Lexus, Mercedes, Mini, Opel, Porsche, Renault, Seat, Skoda, Toyota, VW, and almost all car brands in the world with an OBD2 port.

>> Every car is unique — specific features will vary with every model, build year, hardware, and software.
————

BASIC FEATURES INCLUDED IN THE (FREE) LITE VERSION

Health (OBD), Live Data (OBD), and Emission Check (OBD) enable you to have control over the most important systems — engine and transmission. Check if they work for your car.

ADVANCED FEATURES INCLUDED IN THE FULL VERSION (SUBSCRIPTION)

These are supported for the following car brands: Audi, BMW, Lexus, Mercedes, Mini, Porsche, Renault, Seat, Skoda, Toyota, and VW. Every car is unique, so specific features will vary with every model, build year, hardware, and software.

• CHECK THE CAR’S HEALTH AND SAVE MONEY •
With Carly car diagnostics, you’ll have the right scanner tool to read from all built-in electronic control units (ECUs), including engine, transmission, ABS, airbag, & multimedia, keep track of your car’s health, gauge the severity of problems, clear fault codes, and more.

• MONITOR LIVE DATA •
Live parameters or live data can help you take the guesswork out of understanding what your car needs and detect the fault origins.

• CODE AND CUSTOMIZE FEATURES •
Access hidden features already set by the manufacturer and customize your car to fit your needs. This feature is only available on certain car brands/models, and the coding options vary.

• PERFORM SIMPLE CAR MAINTENANCE INDEPENDENT OF THE WORKSHOP •
Analyze emission and engine gauges, change and register your car battery, perform an oil reset, and much more, all with your smartphone.

• DETECT MILEAGE MANIPULATION •
Car mileage manipulation is on the rise. Don’t get tricked. Use Carly to perform a used car inspection before buying.

All of your reports are automatically saved into your account in the Digital Garage.

————

HOW IT WORKS

Step 1: Check which features are available for your car model
Step 2: Order your Carly Universal Adapter.
Step 3: Plug the adapter into the OBD2 port and log in on this app to check what works for your unique car.
Step 4: Upgrade to the full app version — if available for your car.

THE CARLY UNIVERSAL ADAPTER - MOST ADVANCED OBD DEVICE

The OBD2 device was designed to support almost all car brands with an OBD2 port (built 2001 or later) and to enable advanced Carly features. The adapter comes with:
• A lifetime warranty
• Premium customer support
• A 14-day money-back guarantee


THE CARLY APP

The Carly app offers a free lite version. For some brands with supported advanced features, you get a full one-time health check, together with one error report, and one used car check report.
The full version works for BMW, Mini, Toyota, Lexus, VW, Audi, Skoda, Seat, Mercedes, Renault, and Porsche.

You can purchase a subscription for an individual car brand or for all car brands.

————

This is a yearly subscription. Payment is handled via your iTunes Account. Subscription automatically renews for one year if not canceled 24 hours prior to the end of the current period. The subscription can be managed in your Account Settings and the automatic renewal can be deactivated there.

So worth it

I know a lot of hooplah has been made about the subscription fee, but honestly it's worth it for what the app allows. For me specifically, I have an R61 Paceman S All 4 Mini. One of the biggest pet peeves for me in the car came down to the gong and alert sounds the MINI makes, constantly interrupting my music in car with nagging reminders. In my case I changed my lights to LEDs and due to their low power consumption the car would notify of bulbs being out that weren't. I'd get at least 5-10 audible canbus errors on average as I drove and the Carly app allowed me to disable the system nag. As well as this, I shut off the seatbelt alert and set my car to auto lock as soon as the ignition went on. Additional touches include setting mirrors to autofold and window control from the fob; lows and fog lights coming on with unlock instead of just the parking lights. Well worth the price in comparison to the cost of an aftermarket audio system (which would remove the mini alerts completely) or purchasing an expensive set of *maybe* canbus free LEDs, on top of the other capabalities. Hoping for future coding to completely disable speed sensitive audio in the car but for now I'm cool with finally being able to drive without my car incessantly nagging me lol this thing is awesome I can't say it enough! Must buy for MINI owners fed up with their car's uppity shenanigans.
