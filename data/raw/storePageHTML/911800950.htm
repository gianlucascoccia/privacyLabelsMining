Description

CONQUER the Shattered Kingdoms as you CONTROL EPIC TITANS in STUNNING 3D WARS! COMMAND thousands of troops together with GOD TITANS such as THOR, ZEUS, LOKI and many more!

COLLECT them all, CAPTURE enemy territory, EXPAND your empire, DOMINATE the leaderboards, and BUILD your ARMY by taking part in the most engaging ACTION STRATEGY PVP WAR MMO RPG on mobile!

Join your friends in ALLIANCES and take on DAILY EVENTS, including Alliance War, Raid Boss, Conquest and Assault modes!

· Award-Winning, Console-Quality Graphics: Winner of IMGA Award for Best Technical Achievement and Game Artist of the Year.
· Non-stop Events & Live Campaigns: War never stops in Dawn of Titans, with Events starting daily, and new Titans and rewards available each week.
· Stunning Titans of Myth and Legend: Collect colossal Gods and Warriors and battle them out in epic fantasy PVP wars like you’ve never seen before.
· 100% Real-Time Battle Control: Directly control legions of troops alongside your Titans, with full command of strategy, spell casting, attack and defense.
· Form an Unbeatable Alliance: Unite with friends and take on other Alliances in the weekly Season leaderboard, and gather your total strength to take on new challenges each day.
· Build the Ultimate Kingdom: Capture lands and build your fantasy empire as you take on other players in the most captivating action strategy MMO RPG on mobile!
· Gather and Expand your Titan Collection: Collect Titans from real world myths and legends, such as the mighty Norse God Odin, or the Lord of Olympus himself - Zeus!
· Equip and Upgrade your Army: Gear up your Titans and troops with powerful Relics and artifacts, and conquer the meta!

Picture the Total War series, but with a killer twist—giants who can join our normal-size troops on the battlefield. Simple controls and seriously great graphics make every clash a spectacle we can’t turn away from. And sending dozens of enemies flying with a sweep of our titan’s axe thrills us every time.

Great idea ruined by greedy monetization and bad development

This game looks good and is a lot of fun to play for a few weeks. However, any progression quickly shows you that the entire game is designed around a greedy micro transaction model. The constant changes and update to event structure has increased effort but removed reward in an attempt to force players to spend real money to even be competitive. That coupled with constant crashes, server issues, and shoddy development means even when an exciting roll out takes place it is weeks before things work and often redesigns need to be done to create any semblance of game balance for players. Though the majority of events are simply cash grabs.

It’s also important to note that this developer is not responsive to player feedback. The in game relic forging system is dependent on random chance and the percentages shown are very misleading. Players have been begging for a change to this system since the model was introduced, yet developers remain silent. If you want to see the amount of community engagement look at any post on their facebook page. You will see numerous complaints about the same issue for months without a comment from the developers, but hey they will give you a shout out on their live stream if you say something good if that sort of thing matters at all.

Ghostrunner057 , 10/19/2019
