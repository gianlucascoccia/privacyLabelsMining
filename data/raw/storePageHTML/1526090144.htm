Great Game but needs adjusting

Let me start off by saying this game is great. i’ve watched literally hundreds of ads in this game and not deleted it because of one thing. THEY ARENT MANDATORY ADs!! if I choose to watch them in order to gain a bonus then that’s on me, I think ads should never be forced. but I do think some things need to be adjusted. the weekly reward is pretty weak to be honest. I think it should include at least a bare minimum of 100 gems. you’d have to complete the weekly challenge 20 times to get the legendary armor, and by week 20 most people will already have legendary gear. the battle pass idea is great but it should also include more diverse ideas like “get 100 kills with Dart Raid” or something along those lines. maybe more events or better scaling events. the mana one is great. gold one is eh bc at chapter 10 you’re getting the same amount of gold per run as you are for doing the gold event once a day. currently i’m top 850 for the leaderboards which isn’t great but this game can be p2w, which isn’t an issue bc it’s single player. overall though this game has a lot of potential and honestly is one of my favorite games right now. one added thing is the hit box. the purple dudes that punch, when they land their red ring is actually smaller than the hit box itself. you can get hit by them even if you’re outside the red ring

CryonicCord6 , 11/04/2020

Fun

I haven’t played this game for long but it’s very nice to play, especially since you choose the watch the ads rather than get them automatically. Plus there are rewards for watching ads so they make me feel better for watching them. I like how fast pace the fighting is, and I like how there are different ways to play and it even lets you try out some other things. I suppose the only things I would want is to customize the character I guess, and the ability to press and hold the buttons, or even just be able to spam click faster, even if it doesn’t really show on the screen.

sharkydaninja , 02/27/2021

Very Enjoyable In My Opinion

I’ve been playing this game all day and I’ve really gotten into it the gameplay is simple, but it challenges you on how to react against certain enemy types. It has a nice loot bring in my opinion, although that’s subjective. And honestly I just like how the game looks, though that could be me seeing similarities between it and hollow knight. So far the only real issue I’ve come across that makes me want to stop playing is that sometimes after a boss fight it starts an ad for no reason. Now this would be fine but I don’t if it’s just a bug or something but when it starts the ad it doesn’t pause the game, therefore you just die to a horde of enemies while you furiously wait for the skip ad button to pop up so you can revive yourself in time. But yeah over all a good game that just needs some polishing.
