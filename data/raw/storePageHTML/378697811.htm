Description

The most beautiful free compass for iPhone, iPhone X and iPad.

Simple beautiful rustic style compass for your iPhone and iPad.
Know your location, altitude and orientation instantly with this handy app.

The application accuracy relies on the device sensors. Works best if the device is away of any strong electromagnetic source.

Compass

Compass is a simple yet well designed and effective navigation application. Upon purchase if the free version users will get a classic compass interface that displays above the main design your location and the compass itself the coordinates steadily changing dependent on your movement. The app also has a paid version that will allow you to customize the compass interface using different themes and removes the ads as well. Compass works well as a guide for those who are using or are dependent on an iPhone or iPad device.
