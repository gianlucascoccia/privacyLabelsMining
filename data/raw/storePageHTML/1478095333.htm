Description

Night Owl Protect lets you view all of your devices in one easy to use App. Monitor your home or business, in real-time, on your Smart Phone or Tablet. Easily share images and recordings via your favorite social network or through email and text. Staying connected to your devices allows you to protect your world from potential threats. Secure, PROTECT, and connect your world!

Night Owl Protect Features:
Quick and easy mobile setup
Live video and audio from your Cameras on Wi-Fi or 3G/4G LTE networks
Easily configure push notifications from your Smart Device
Save recordings directly to your Smart Device
Save images directly to your Smart Device and share via text, email or social media
Share important MP4 formatted videos via email or social media
Video playback
Create Favorites and Groups
Multiple logins/users
Two-way audio compatible
Google Integration (Limited devices)
Human Detection, Facial Detection (Limited devices)

WARNING: This application uses data to connect your Cameras to your Smart Device. When connected to a 3G or 4G LTE network, the live video feed from your Camera will be considered streaming data by your phone service provider and will contribute to any data or download limit your phone data plan may have. If you exceed your data plan limit, this may result in additional usage charges. It is highly recommended to contact your phone service provider to know the limit of your data plan before using this video streaming application.

Misleading

I’ve had this unit for a little over a month now. Needless to say, I was thrilled about the idea of setting up security cameras around my new home. I purchased the Night Owl 10 Channel WI-FI NVR that came with 3 cameras. The selling point to me was it having the ability to add up to 10 WiFi devices. I just knew I’d be able to add up to 10 cameras. I hate wires so I didn’t want the wired option for that reason. Purchased an additional setup just for the cameras and found out I can ONLY have 4 cameras. How’s that expandable? Very disappointed with this purchase and even want to take the security system down and find another product that can provide what I want. I would be satisfied with this if I could add another 3-4 cameras. I guess we don’t always get what we want, no matter what the box says.
