Description

Welcome to the Gigi’s Cupcakes USA mobile app. Now you can find a bakery, earn points, and redeem available points for the rewards.

Download it today for free and you can:

1. Join Gigi’s Rewards Program and begin earning points.

2. Earn Points – Earn points with every purchase by scanning your QR code.

3. Redeem Rewards – Use your points to redeem available rewards.

4. Order Online - Use the Bakery Locator to order your favorite cupcakes online.

5. Bakery Locator-Find a local bakery, contact them via phone or get directions.
