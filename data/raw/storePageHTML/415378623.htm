Description

Get to know Dr. Harry Vanderspeigle in the Resident Alien comics. Now a SyFy series!

For more than 30 years Dark Horse Comics has published some of the best comics and graphic novels in the world. With the Dark Horse Comics app your favorites are at your fingertips, anytime, anywhere!

From celebrated creator-owned originals and award-winning manga, to breathtaking art books and ground-breaking series based on popular movies, games, and TV shows, Dark Horse has something for everyone.

There’s even more amazing content from Berger Books! Check out Anthony Bourdain's Hungry Ghosts, Invisible Kingdom, LaGuardia, The Seeds, and more. All brought to you by the legendary, award-winning editor, Karen Berger.

Discover and preview thousands of comics, graphic novels, and art books, with new releases each week! Save $$ with comic bundles! Personalize your reading experience with options like panel zoom mode! Get notified about sales and Dark Horse news! Share your favorite comic panels with your social media friends and foes! Nice, but One Recommendation

I love that I can purchase Mass Effect, Dragon Age and the Witcher comics amongst other comics as well. My only wish is that when purchasing the collection, I could break the series and the individual comics up instead of having one massive book. theodoubleto , 01/28/2021

Good eReader, a couple of points

Pros 👍
• Aesthetically pleasing
• Ease of navigation via dashboard

Cons 👎
• No “Home” page but instead a Featured tab and New tab.
• No general content browser option. Instead a limited “More” option.
Tip - Tap on More again to see additional options.
• No title stack in your bookshelf. You can sort all your comics by title which is fine.

Unable to preorder comics via app. When I attempt to preorder on the mobile website, the app will open to the most recent in a series instead of the desired preordered item.

Bugs 🐜
• When I archive a issue, I can’t always close the swiping view immediately. Sometimes swiping to another issue clears up the delay when I tap on Close.

All-in-all, it’s a good app and I enjoy reading Dark Horse’s products.
