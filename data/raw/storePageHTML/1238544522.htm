Wildfang95 , 06/26/2018

Ehhh...

Honestly I prefer the first pocket knights. I also believe that the hundreds of dollars I spent on that game should transfer over to the sequel to the game since you legitimately shut down the game and no one can access it anymore.

I have spent money on this game and I think the amount of money you need to spend to get to the next VIP level is crazy! From level 6 to 7 which is what I’m trying to get to would cost around $60. Just to get some wings and a little bonus exp... the level up reward per level is very miniscule as well as the peak level. The prices of each item and with the relatively new addition of the 2 times coupons become very expensive in diamonds. I understand that of course to get the bonus benefits of using the special items it’s going to probably cost money. I’m okay with that and so are a ton of other people. Maybe things should be quite cheaper than they are. There seems to be such a wall when it comes to getting mythical heroes if you spend hundreds of dollars to get them. Or the months I’ve spent only to get about 100 total pieces across almost every mythical hero. I think there should be a whole new section or addition added in to the game. You guys made the NPC city fairly large I’m sure you guys can figure something out to do with the space. Maybe add a pet system with pet battles. Or something brought straight from the original pocket knights everyone knows and loved.
Thanks I hope you take some into consideration.

Shifty Games , 03/28/2019

ARPG Gold

I have been playing these type of games (complete quests for currency, use currency to unlock and upgrade heroes to build a bigger and better team) for a long time, and this one is by far the best.

Unlike many of the other games in this genre, you can actually play this game all day without hitting limits of things to do, due to the large number of activities to do AND you can be competitive with little to no spending.

My only question would be how do I submit bugs/errors? I have found a few and documented them and would like to help improve this awesome game any way I can. As a fellow game designer, I feel invested and inspired to help this game. It’s clear the game was built not only to satisfy spenders and regulars of the genre but also to new and free players. If possible I’d like to help preserve that.
