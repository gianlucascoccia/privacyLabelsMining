INTRODUCING DAWN

Modern Calendars are complex and bloated with features.
Dawn is minimalistic and straightforward, yet even more powerful.

• Manage everything you need to do with a single app. Not two, not three. Dawn is your central hub for remembering and getting stuff done with coexisting events, todos, reminders, dues, and so on.
• Focus on today with Dawn’s Today page, an adaptive homepage that changes as your day goes by. Quickly add tasks to your day and check off tasks you’ve completed. Dawn is the perfect everyday companion.
• Sync your calendars and reminder lists to Dawn for a seamless experience. With Dawn's Spaces organizer, you'll be able to view your calendars as lists and further sort, categorize, and filter your events.
• Introducing Spaces: innovative custom lists for you to visually organize todos and events. Group your tasks, create itineraries, manage projects, plan vacations, and more: the possibilities are endless.


DESIGN

Dawn is a calendar app designed like no other. We sought to create the most beautiful and functional calendar ever.

Best calendar widget so far

I was looking for a calendar widget for the longest time, struggling to balance between an easy user interface and a clean looking design. Thankfully, Dawn managed to find a perfect balance between the two! Well, almost.

If I could rate Dawn 4.5 stars, I would, as I think this is an easy fix. While my favorite part of the app is its clean, minimalistic design, I think it could be improved with one slight change. Upcoming events are only displayed if they’re scheduled for the next day or the day of. I use this app for homework and personally, I always like to know the next assignment I have due, even if it’s a week out. I think this problem can be easily fixed, and would overall make this app the perfect calendar.
