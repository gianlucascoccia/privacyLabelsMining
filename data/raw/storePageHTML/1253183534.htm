Description

AT&T Photo Storage is just one of the premium benefits that comes with AT&T Protect Advantage. *

With the AT&T Photo Storage app, you can easily (and automatically) back up and secure your photos and videos, without sacrificing quality. Free up storage on your device with one touch to make space for capturing more memories. Fast speed and unlimited* storage space put your entire collection at your fingertips and ready to share.

• Original quality: AT&T Photo Storage backs up photos and videos in original quality
• Automatic: Seamless, automatic backup from your phone
• Storage: Includes unlimited storage**
• Speed: Upload speeds that won’t leave you lagging
• Restore: Simply tap ‘Download’ to restore backed up items to your device
• Share: Share your memories using SMS**, email, or social media of your choice
• No Data Charges: No need to wait for a Wi-Fi connection. You can back up photos and videos without using your AT&T data plan. **

Download the AT&T ProTech app to call or chat with a ProTech support expert for help with your phone, apps, and technology.

Learn more at att.com/protech.

Contact us at PhotoStorageAppSupport@att.com

*AT&T Protect Advantage subscription not required.
**Storage size depends on subscription in AT&T Protect Advantage. There may be limitations on the size of each video that can be backed up and secured.
**Messaging rates may apply. Data rates do not apply when on AT&T’s network. (Requires eligible AT&T data services. Excl. & restr. apply. Use subject to wireless service terms including those related to data speed.)

I really like having this back up for my photos as it allows me to keep everything separate from my phone manufacturer, Apple, & I also get more storage. In my Apple photos I just keep family photos, friends photos mostly. Once I know that the other things have been saved to ATT photos, then I delete them from phone memory. Not completely pleased , 04/29/2018

Low resolution

I downloaded this app because it was advised by the AT&T rep. I had previously been using the Ever app to store my pictures - only annoying thing with that app is that you have to then email them to yourself if you want to retrieve them and it can only be done one by one. Anyway, this AT&T Photo Storage app has been a hit and miss - after a while I had to uninstall it and reinstall it for my pictures to start uploading to it again. My biggest issue with the app is that even though the pics are taken with high-resolution cameras, once they upload to the app they are super fuzzy! Being that this is an AT&T app you would think that it would be much more advanced. Also, live pictures do not save as live pictures, they just save as a blurry mess. I am very disappointed. Especially because a lot of those pictures I cannot get back in full resolution now. Ridiculous.
