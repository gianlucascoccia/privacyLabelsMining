Description

Buddy helps you set up a budget and keep track of your expenses, either by yourself or together with your loved ones.
Here’s what is waiting for you inside:


BUDGETS
Buddy will help you not to overspend on your well-earned salary by setting up a budget plan that fits you.
Do you want a shared budget with your partner? Or keep the expenses between you and your mates fair on the vacation? No problem, just invite them and get a clear picture of who spent what.

OVERVIEW
We'll give you a beautiful & detailed overview of your spendings, income, and savings.

SPLIT
Split lets you know who paid what, and how to settle up.


And much more for you to find.
Take control of your finance today!

Buddy offers a $4.99 monthly renewing and a $34.99 yearly renewing subscription to unlock Premium functionality.

CourtCos , 01/12/2021

The Perfect Manual Budgeting App!!!!

I have been struggling to find a good budgeting method for a long time, as I have had my budget worked out in a spreadsheet, but previously had no convenient way to track it. Other account-linked budgeting apps like Mint just don’t process the transactions the way I would like and it ends up being more work to try to maintain it than it would be to do it manually — and even then it still wasn’t totally correct. With Buddy I have been able to exactly translate my budget into it and I am so pleased with all the customization options (even in the free version!). I love that I can schedule recurring payments and choose to account for them in advance so that I know how much I actually have left for discretionary spending. After using for 2 weeks this has already helped me to be more engaged with my day to day spending and stay on track! I could not have designed the features better myself. Thank you!!!!!

tugrockz99 , 11/13/2019

Awesome for a manual budget

I spent a week looking for a great budget app, but everyone app I downloaded wanted me to connect my bank account and they budgeted the transactions automatically. Buddy is a awesome app for people who want to manually control their budget without having to deal with automatic settings (automatic budgeting never let me take into account money that was sent to me to pay for something for someone else)
The interface of the app has a easily layout and has a perfect amount of personalization options. Buddy has plenty of preset options to choose from for your budget and for adding transactions, plus you can add your own transaction title with a icon and color choice.
I highly recommend this app for people who like to be in complete control of their budget and manually entering their transactions.

MLStobe , 11/19/2019

Hopeful

My search for a budget/spending app continues. The graphics on this one are about the best I’ve seen, easy to interpret. Seems it would keep just the right amount of data for those of us don’t want to link to bank accounts and credit lines. I know what’s in my checking account. What I want to know is where my money is going and where I could most comfortably save money. My problem with this app is with the user interface. I simply could not get it to let me add a new category to the budget section. And I am not sure why the categories in spending and budgeting are different. These should be the same and linked. Also I should be able to click through on a nonbudgeted item and move it to a budget category. And every time I set up a new category for spending, I should immediately be able to attach it to a budgeted category/amount.

I will keep an eye on upgrades to this app if I don’t find the right thing soon because I see the potential. Just not there for me yet.
