Description

Scanner App is a handy app that allows to scan documents, store your IDs and manage multi-page documents on the go.

- Scan any document, automaticaly detecting it borders
- Export it to PDF or JPEG
- Sign or add watermark to your document
- Share it anywhere

Scanner app is portable, easy-to-use but powerful mobile scanner. Forget cumbersome and big office scanners, scan docs at home or on the go. It will help you to manage all your workflow wherever you are. It will become your everyday tool to work, manage all your documents and keep them in hand.

Just place your document in front of a camera: app automaticaly detects borders, crops it and saves as a clean document in HD size. You can quickly scan a lot of pages or add pages to existing document. Then you can sign, edit, share it or store important docs on your device.

What you can do:

- Scan one or multi-page documents
- Add signature or watermarks
- Scan things, you need to keep records of: receipts, notes or sketches
- Export documents in different formats
- Keep your IDs with you at all times
- Share documents to email or clouds
- Turn handwritten notes into digital copies
- Store business cards
- Print any document via local Wi-Fi printers

Various powerful tools will allow your to scan, manage, store, organize and share all important documets anywhere you are.
