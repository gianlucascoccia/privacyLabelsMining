beekachu92 , 07/13/2020

Cute game but punishes you with ads.

I love the concept of this game. I love bubble tea and puzzle type games so this seemed like a perfect thing for me. It’s fun and just challenging enough. I don’t even mind watching the occasional ads. That’s how people who have worked on the game get paid for their work, I get it and am cool with that. What isn’t so cool is having the ads disrupt the game in a way that causes the players to fail the levels. Then to retry the level they have to watch ANOTHER ad only to have the same thing happen again. I never write reviews but this scenario is exactly what happened to me on the conveyer belt level. I watched the ad to play it but by the time the ad finished the cup had already passed the spout in the level and told me I failed. Weird. The game asked if I’d like to watch an ad to retry the level. Huh, well sure. Maybe it was just a strange error or something the first time. The same thing happened again. I did this FIVE more times watching ads each attempt with this same thing happening just to make sure that I wasn’t doing something wrong besides being an idiot and giving more time to the ads. Maybe try to fix that? It’s truly a cute game but that seems something that should really be fixed and there’s not really a good reason for that to be happening to the people who’d like to give your game a chance.

Too many adds!

I bought this game a couple days ago, and I’m in love! There is such a good mix of hard and easy levels. I found this game through my best friend and she also loves it. It’s good for practicing your art skills because you have to really think about how much/what colors you have to mix. Like all games, this game has one downside that NEEDS to be fixed. TOO MANY ADDS! This honestly brings anger in me. If you fail the level and it will bring you two options. Skip the level, or try again. Most of the time it says to skip the level, you have to watch a add. But, there isn’t actually a choice. If you hit the option to not watch an add, you will still have to watch an add. And there’s NO WAY I’m spending $2.99 on the no adds option. Overall, this game is so fun, and easy to do. Yes, it is annoying that every ten seconds you watch an add, but I recommend this game. It’s super fun, and easy to do. I really hope you change the price to $0.99 or something. The current price is to expensive in my opinion. I hope you see my review! This is an amazing game! Good job! :D
