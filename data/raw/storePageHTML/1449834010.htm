Description

Pottery.ly is a super relaxing pottery game that allows you to create your own unique ceramics. You can shape your ceramics anyway you like, and you can choose which texture you would want them to be in. But it’s not over yet, you can even draw on it to make it truly a one of a kind masterpiece. Creating an amazing looking ceramic is a very relaxing and satisfying process, and the result can be even more accomplishing. You can share your works with your friends, or you can even share it on the Pottery.ly community to inspire others!

AWESOME FEATURES:

- Simple but powerful tools: the tools are not only very easy to use, but also allow you to create pottery of any shape, texture, and pattern.

- Awesome community: you can share your works or view others on the built in Pottery.ly community. Creativity is limitless!

- Super relaxing gameplay: Pottery.ly offers a truly relaxing, therapeutic, and pleasant gameplay experience that you can share with your friends and family.

Let’s play Pottery.ly now and start making your first ceramic masterpiece!

star and thunder , 12/25/2019

BEST APP EVER!

I love this app so much and I’ve only had it for two days but it’s the perfect time waster and just the perfect game. Some people say that the subscription is a problem but to me the subscription does not matter at all. Of course, you need the subscription for no adds and glass pottery, but if you would just like to make regular pottery and don’t mind adds it won’t bother you. In fact, when you want to draw on your pottery it says you need the subscription first. But right next to the subscription button is a button that tells you instead of buying the subscription you could just watch a video. The video actually helps because while watching the video, you could get something done or started while waiting. The app also gives people the chance to let their creativity run wild. The adds are short and hands-on and are not always all that boring. Yes, the adds to pop up at random times but again, a chance to do something else. People can like and comment in your pottery and if they say something rude about it, go to the settings button or just click on the person’s name and you can block him/her. Also, if you are having trouble creating the shape you would like, there are default shapes were you can get closer to the shape you want, easier. If you are thinking of downloading this app, DO! There are no bugs and it’s very fun. So happy to have this app!
