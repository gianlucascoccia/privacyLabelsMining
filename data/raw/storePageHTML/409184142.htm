Description

JotNot Fax is the easiest, most reliable, and fastest way to send and receive faxes. Save time and money with JotNot by skipping the fax store.

JotNot Fax comes with a built-in scanner, so you don't need to find a scanner or fax machine if you need to fax a paper document. Just scan in the document with your phone's camera, enter the fax number, and hit send.

The app lets you get your own fax number that you can use for sending and receiving faxes. Pick a number in the app, and we'll set it up with the phone company for you in just a few seconds -- no hassle, and no service calls by a technician.

Create a free JotNot account to sync faxes and credits across multiple devices and other platforms, including iOS, the Web, and more.

JotNot Fax will send you a push notification to let you know when you've received a fax, or when a fax that you've sent has finished.

Other benefits include:
- Send faxes from your iPhone and monitor incoming faxes while out of the office
- Built-in, free cover pages give a polished first impression
- Stay organized and keep track of your faxes
- Get a proof of transmission for each successfully sent fax
- Fax any document (from email attachments, the cloud, or other apps)
- Supports all common file types, including PDF, docx, and images.

Fax Pricing: JotNot Fax charges a subscription for sending and receiving. There is also a pay-as-you-go option for sending only with credits. All purchases in the app are charged to the credit card or other payment method in your App Store account.

1. Sending and receiving with a subscription: We offer three subscription plan options. Each one comes with your choice of a fax number.

- Standard plan: includes 100 pages of sending and receiving each month
- Premium plan: includes 300 pages of sending and receiving each month
- Business plan: includes 1,000 pages of sending and receiving each month

Each plan can be purchased on either a monthly or annual basis, with a discount for annual plans. Subscriptions auto-renew unless you cancel more than 24 hours before the renewal date. Payment will be charged to your iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase. Any unused portion of a free trial period, if offered, will be forfeited when the user purchases a subscription to that publication, where applicable.

If you use more than the number of pages in a given month, you can add extra pages to that month by buying credits. Credits can be converted to pages at a rate of 5 pages per credit.

2. Sending with credits: Without a subscription, sending a fax costs 1 credit for every 5 pages. For example:
- a fax with 1-5 pages costs 1 credit
- a fax with 6-10 pages costs 2 credits
- a fax with 11-16 pages costs 3 credits, and so on

The fax cover page is free -- it does not count towards the page count for the purposes of calculating the credits needed for a fax.

You can purchase credits in packs within the app. We offer packs of 3, 10, 20, and 100 credits; larger packs offer cheaper pricing per credit. See the app for local pricing.

JotNot Fax supports sending faxes to these countries and regions:
United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Germany, Mexico, Japan, Italy, Australia, American Samoa, Argentina, Austria, Belgium, Brazil, Chile, China, Costa Rica, Croatia, Czech Republic, Denmark, Finland, France, Greece, Guam, Hong Kong, Hungary, India, Ireland, Israel, Malaysia, Netherlands, Norway, Pakistan, Peru, Poland, Portugal, Puerto Rico, Romania, Singapore, Slovakia, South Africa, South Korea, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, Taiwan, Thailand, Turkey, U.S. Virgin Islands, Venezuela, Vietnam.

JotNot Fax supports getting a phone number in the United States and Canada.

MommaD277 , 02/26/2018

Very good app especially for people who live in rural areas

I have used this app a few times & haven’t had any trouble. I believe it’s fairly priced for what it offers. I’d gladly pay it to avoid a 15 min drive to my town, tracking down a business that does faxing (which would charge me as well) then drive another 15 min back home. All can be achieved right from the comfort of my home & in conjunction with my WiFi printer & scanner is just as professional as a true fax. It can be used anywhere you have a good internet connection. This makes it almost priceless. The only suggestions I would like to make are being able to review the attached pages BEFORE sending, and being able to review the entire fax after it’s been sent. When you’re sending multiple pages, I’ve wanted to verify that I sent all of the documents needed as well as verify I didn’t accidentally duplicate any pages. Once you’ve moved to the cover page section, there’s no going back to review. You can only see how many pages, not the actual pages. Yes, you do get a “receipt” showing that the fax was delivered & the details, however, you can not look at the entire fax. It also needs to have a cancel or pause button if you hit “send” and immediately remember you forgot a page. Once you hit send, there’s no going back either to add anything and you have to send a whole new fax, which winds up using more credits for only one additional page. Overall, I consider this a great app.
