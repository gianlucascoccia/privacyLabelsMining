Description

Create stunning designs effortlessly with Over! Edit images and videos to create content that wows the world. The best part? No design degree necessary.

NEW VIDEO FEATURES

With Over’s latest video feature, you’ll find it easier than ever to integrate video into your designs and impress your audience. Layer videos with images, graphics and text, adjust the brightness, saturation or contrast of your video or blend a video seamlessly into your design to create content that is sure to catch the eye.


Over is everything you need to be a brand-building, social-media marketing guru. With beautiful, hand-curated and easy-to-edit photo and video templates, Over helps you create impactful content that will make your brand stand out.


Add text and graphic enhancements to your photos and videos. From an eye catching overlay, to stylish graphic add-ons - treat any video the same way you would a photo. It’s the professional, yet personal way to set up your brand.


3 reasons why Over is the most fun & powerful way to build your brand.


• Get inspired and showcase your brand with thousands of professionally designed and fully customizable templates.
• Customize your designs with colors and captions, videos and photos, graphics and fonts. On brand, every time.
• Create all your brand content right from your phone - with speed and ease. No computer needed.


Up your social media game, with eye-popping Instagram Story templates, Facebook posts, banners, logos, and more. Edit however you want and then post directly on Twitter, Facebook, and other social media channels. It’s the sure fire way for you to get your brand out there and noticed on social media.


How to build your next big marketing campaign with Over:


• Choose templates or fonts for social media posts, posters, ads, flyers, and more from our vast library.
• Edit and perfect your photo or video to your heart’s content.
• Add graphics, captions, or logos to make your design pop.
• Post directly to social media right from the app.


Go beyond customized templates and turn your side hustle into your main hustle. Our in-house team of designers, social media, and branding experts are on hand with tips, trends, and insights to help your brand shine. Just check out the Discover feed in the app.


Want your blog to make an impact? Building the next big business? Managing your company’s social media content? Whatever your brand or business may be, have full control over the content you create.


Download Over today and build your brand at the tap of a button.

Or around that number. Regardless, this app is still amazing and i will still be using it.
