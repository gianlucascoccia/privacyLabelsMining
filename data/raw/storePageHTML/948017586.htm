Description

With MSG GO, you can watch live New York Knicks, New York Rangers, New York Islanders, New Jersey Devils, Buffalo Sabres, New York Red Bulls games, & MSG Networks’ Emmy award-winning original programming!

Features:
- Live NY Knicks, NY Rangers, NY Islanders, NY Devils, Buffalo Sabres, & NY Red Bulls Games
- Video On-Demand: Catchup with Condensed Games, Full Game Encores, Game Highlights, Expert Analysis, and Original Programming
- Stats: Full statistics while you watch the game. Watch in portrait view!
- Game Alerts: Set alerts & reminders so you never miss the game!
- Game Schedules: View upcoming matchups
- Interactive Games: Have Fun! Challenge yourself during game breaks & compete to win prizes!

MSG GO is available at no additional cost to subscribers of participating providers who receive MSG Networks as part of their pay television subscription. To watch, subscribers must sign-in with their TV provider login and password.

Only Knicks, Rangers, Islanders, Devils, Sabres, & Red Bulls games broadcast on MSG Networks will be available on MSG GO.

League and programming restrictions require MSG GO mobile & tablet users to turn on location services. MSG GO will use location services to determine what content users are eligible to view. MSG GO is only available in the U.S.

Supported Devices: Smartphone, tablet, computer

Wireless access and/or data fees may apply to access MSG GO and MSG GO content. Consult with your wireless/data provider.

Get Started:
- Download now and explore everything MSG GO has to offer!
- Turn on Location Services: MSG GO uses location services to determine what content you’re eligible to view.
- Free of charge, just sign-in with your TV provider Login & Password
- Pick your favorite Teams & Set reminders
