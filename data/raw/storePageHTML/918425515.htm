Iloveamerica45654343578 , 01/27/2019

A few gripes but overall still great

I’m a lifetime subscriber since 2014, I listen to this app daily and love it with a few small gripes. 1) App seems to disconnect a lot. Updates never fix it. If you try to skip a track, it cuts off entirely about half the time. I have tried using different IPs, devices, etc and it still behaves the same way, year after year. 2) Skip track function isn’t great. It wobbles, often cuts off the feed and if you try to hit it a second time it takes you right back to the first song you skipped. Still, it comes in handy for bypassing those annoying Earl Klugh and Joe Pass songs. 3) Like/Dislike thumbs seemingly do nothing. The app does not operate like Pandora and this appears to be an empty function. Okay, enough griping. The music selection and themed channels are great. The app itself is beautiful. Despite my grumbles, it’s really a great service and I recommend it highly.
