Join a new era of war game, where your strategy matters as much as the size of your base and power of your Armada. The game challenges you to become the best navy commander. You must have a keen eye on the battlefield to dominate the action and bring order with your navy to spot, scout and sink enemy ships!

Become an admiral of invincible navy fleets in the best 3D interactive strategy game of naval warfare. Lead your powerful fleet through countless sea battles against pirates, sea monsters, and players from around the globe and rule the waves!

Enter a post-apocalyptic world engulfed by the ocean and thriving with pirate activity. Command the most iconic battleship, destroyer and aircraft to destroy your enemies in all out naval warfare! Why not join an alliance for true MMO glory and battle in the ultimate naval showdown – The Bermuda. Hunt the pirates and sea monsters wherever you may find them! The high seas are yours for the taking and it needs a ruler!

World war is only the beginning - Dive into the action packed sea battles now!

Crawl to a walk

In the beginning this game was plagued with all sorts of issues. Now it seems a lot of that has been handled and the game continues to get better. This is something to consider if your a pay to play type. Most of the apps out there are getting worse and this one seems to be getting better as tine goes on. One of the few that I have spent time and money on that the devs actually institute some of what the players are asking for into the game. When problems or glitches arise they tend to take care of the player which is also something hard to find in the many other options out there. Most of the server I’m in have been here for a couple years or a bit longer and there is a reason they have hung out that long. There is definitely strategy involved and all things have strengths and weaknesses that level the field even amongst the big beasts throughout. Many many options of setups can be used and you cant just buy your way to perfection. That’s huge for me, I want to play the game not pay to catch up and this game allows that. Prepare for a long experience because it will keep you interested!

RichGGG , 02/21/2020

Still hanging in there

I’ve been playing this game for a few years now. Started out from a small alliance of 10 or so people trying to go up in the ranks. Eventually got destroyed by higher alliances and quit the game for a year. Came back and got a chance to be a part of an awesome alliance and now everything is easier and I get to experience what the game has to offer. Really addicting and issues that come up are quickly dealt with. While I was gone the game kept giving me resources that really helped me get back on my feet and I believe that’s a pretty good way to support its players that go MIA for a while. Still love the game and hope to keep playing it for years to come!

The mechanics and buffing effects really make the game a challenge in a good way. The many different carriers and aircraft with a multitude of combinations or equipment to get the upper hand on your opponent are amazing. It’s a thinking game no doubt. Can’t wait to see what else the creators have in store for future updates.
