Description

FoodJets is the best way to get your favorite local restaurants delivered to your home & office.


FoodJets is the only locally-owned restaurant delivery service. With thousands of partner restaurants, we’ve got what you’re hungry for. Whether you need sandwiches for the office, or you’re surprising the family with their favorite pizzas, we’ve got what you need. You work hard enough, let us take care of the food.

We specialize in accommodating large orders for office meetings, work events, parties, family gatherings and any occasion where you need lots of good food. For catering orders, our uniformed drivers will bring the food to your dining area and set it up for you. First class service all the way!


Order from your favorite restaurants with a few taps on our website or iOS app to get the food you love, delivered fast. Online food ordering, simplified. Need help with a big catering-style order for the office? Give us a call! Need pizzas for your softball team? We’ve got you covered. Too tired to cook after working all day? That’s what we’re here for.


Simply enter your address, choose from the restaurants you love, then sit back and relax. With easy app and website ordering, live order tracking, and customer service via phone, app, or website chat, you always know where your food is.

FoodJets puts more than 3000 restaurants at your fingertips and offers service in 100+ cities in six US states. FoodJets was launched in 1993.


We partner with both the mom and pop restaurants you love and the national chains you count on. Whether it’s Italian, Chinese, Thai, or good old burgers and fries, look no further than FoodJets.


How does it work?
1. Visit our website or download our app.
2. Enter your address to see which restaurants are available for deliveries to your address.
3. Enter your payment info and place your order.
4. Track our driver in real time or just sit back and wait for our call. We’ll let you know when we’re around the corner.
5. Eat the food and pat yourself on the back for saving so much time and energy!

If you have any questions or concerns, we have the most accessible customer service in the industry with chat, email, and phone support!


What do we deliver?
Depending on your location and the restaurants that we partner with we offer Pasta, Pizzas, Calzones, Stir-fries, Sushi, Ramen, Teriyaki, Gyros, Fried Rice, French Fries, Burgers, Tacos, Burritos, Corndogs, Sandwiches, Hoagies, Grinders, Cheesesteaks, Fajitas, Quesadillas, Chili, Soups, Salads, cakes, pies, ice cream, gelato, and more!

What type of Cuisine available?
Indian, Mexican, Ethiopian, Asian, Thai, Mediterranean, American, Chinese, Japanese, Spanish, Fusion, and many, many more!


Where do we deliver?
FoodJets currently operates in 100+ cities in six US states and we are growing fast. Currently they include: California - Carlsbad, Berkeley, Windsor, Chico, Auburn, Livermore, Lincoln, Merced, Turlock, Monterey, Manteca, Tracy, Gilroy, Morgan hill, San Pablo, Fremont, San Ramon, Petaluma, Oakley, Brentwood, Antioch, Redwood city, Mountain view, Campbell, Napa, Fairfield, Benicia, Alameda, Martinez, San Jose, Pleasanton, Concord, Santa Rosa, Fresno, Stockton, Modesto, Vacaville, Folsom, Citrus heights, West, Sacramento, Elk grove, Roseville, Sacramento, Michigan - Southgate, Nevada, Reno, Utah, Salt lake city, Virginia - Virginia beach, and more!

WAYS TO PAY
All major Credit Cards and Debit Cards: Visa, American Express, MasterCard, Discover. All transactions are 100% digital and take place in the app or on the website. No cash? No Problem.
