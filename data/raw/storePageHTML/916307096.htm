Description

Create and share 500,000+ unique quote pictures and wallpapers with our beautiful gallery of high-resolution photographs. Email, text and share your creations with friends, family and social networks.

• GET INSPIRED WITH TODAY'S QUOTE
Get Today's Quote - our inspiring quote of the day - on your iPhone or iPad Notifications screen or your Home Screen. Use Settings to choose the time you prefer to receive the quote of the day and enjoy sharing our amazing picture quotes.

• CREATE AND SHARE BEAUTIFUL PICTURE QUOTES
Create and share inspiring picture quotes with our vibrant, awe-inspiring photographs that celebrate natural beauty, and feature stunning scenes of trees, waterfalls, canyons, deserts, icebergs, mountain lakes, ponds and more. Place quotes where you want them on the picture of your choice to create customized picture quotes to share with family, friends and social networks. Share or save the image to create a stunning wallpaper for your iPhone or iPad.

• EXPLORE 1,000+ AUTHORS
BrainyQuote's App features our most prized and popular quotations, topics and authors. Authors include famous people from the past and present: Albert Einstein, Walt Disney, Abraham Lincoln, Winston Churchill, John F. Kennedy, Steve Jobs and more. Browse or search over 1,000 authors quickly to find the perfect quotation.

• EXPLORE 90+ GREAT TOPICS
Choose from over 90 great topics to enjoy Inspirational quotes, Love quotes, Friendship quotes, Life quotes, Funny quotes, Motivational quotes, Success quotes and more. Browse or search our great topics to find the perfect quotation.

• CREATE STUNNING WALLPAPERS
Make stunning wallpaper for your Lock screen and Home screen. Save a beautiful picture quote to your Photos, go to iPhone Settings, select Wallpaper and choose the image you have created.

• ENJOY QUOTES ON APPLE WATCH
Enjoy an endless stream of quotes for inspiration throughout the day. Add the BrainyQuote modular watch face complication for a new quote each hour.

Experience the simple joy and inspiration of quotations.

With so much going on in this ever smaller world of ours, it is a pleasure to find a peaceful moment where one can truly reflect. No more and no less than that. Thank you for this App. Thank you for the small peace or thought provoking moments it brings to my days. Thank you for keeping it simple, when so much of life is already complicated.
