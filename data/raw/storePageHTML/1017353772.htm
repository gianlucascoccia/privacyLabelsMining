Description

The biggest fashion contest of the year is about to kick off in the bustling metropolis of Manehattan, and it’s Rarity’s chance to shine—an opportunity to take the fashion capital of Equestria by storm with her most fabulous designs and bring her best friends along for the show, and some sightseeing! But when a big-city pony takes advantage of Rarity’s big heart, her dreams could be in trouble, along with her friendships! In MY LITTLE PONY: RARITY TAKES MANEHATTAN, Rarity faces the conflict of high fashion vs. friendship, right on the runway!

Based on a popular episode from the hit animated series MY LITTLE PONY: FRIENDSHIP IS MAGIC, this whole new kind of interactive storybook app features 18 pages of story, iconic characters, television-quality animation, and interactive dress-up activities perfect for early and emerging readers AND your MY LITTLE PONY fan!

Features:

• Rich, interactive fashion show activity! Channel your inner fashionista—Mix, match, and create a chic look for your runway pony during Fashion Week.
• The world of Manehattan comes to life in a beautiful animated video introduction to the story
• Enriched by MY LITTLE PONY animations on every page
• Features everypony’s favorite fillies—Twilight Sparkle, Fluttershy, Applejack, Rarity, Pinkie Pie, and Rainbow Dash—with interactive action and voices on every page! Also featured: Prim Hemline, Suri Polomare, and Miss Pommel.
• New Interactive Features: Tap, swipe, and drag-and-drop your way through the story
• Read to Me and Read Along modes for all levels of early and emerging readers
• Auto Play feature to watch the story and animations come to life
• New and advanced vocabulary highlighted and sounded out on each page
• Includes a Sight Word practice section

Learning Goals:

• This app can provide an opportunity for preschoolers to learn the values of generosity, honesty, and friendship

Social Emotional Learning
• Social Skills – Learn how to maintain supportive and honest friendships
• Responsible Decision-Making – Understand how actions affect others

Problem-Solving
• Correcting and learning from mistakes
• Helping the ponies make responsible and healthy choices
