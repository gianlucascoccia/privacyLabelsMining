Description

Getting started is simple! You just head to stores, snap some photos, and answer a couple of questions. Every now and then, you’ll also speak to a store associate. That’s it! Observations pay between 4 and 20 dollars, and you’ll get paid via PayPal or Coinbase. This side hustle is the perfect way to earn money for dinner dates, movie nights, and other special occasions!

FAST FACTS

Observa has paid out more than $500,000 to Observers.
Observations take 20 minutes on average.
You’ll be notified when there are new opportunities near you.
There’s more than 100,000 secret shoppers.
Opportunities are available in the United States and Canada.
You’ll usually get paid within 72 hours. Please note: this process may take more than 72 hours on weekends and holidays.
Make $4-20 per observation.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is this a legit way to make PayPal money?

Yes! Observa has been in business since 2016. Today, we work with leading brands like Q Drinks and Morton & Bassett. Observers routinely perform observations at well-known retailers like Safeway, 7-Eleven, Walmart, and more!

Do you have opportunities outside of the United States and Canada?

Right now, the vast majority of our observations are currently in the United States and Canada. Observa is always working to get additional clients, in order to provide more opportunities for our international secret shoppers. If you turn your notifications on, you'll be notified when there are new opportunities near you. In the meantime, you should check out our paid photo challenges. We run these every few weeks, and they can be completed from anywhere.

If Observa rejects an observation, do you still send the data to the customer?

Never! Observa only wants to pass along high-quality data to our customers, so we discard data from rejected observations. We only reject an observation if the Observer didn’t follow the instructions correctly. Observa wants to accept observations, because we both lose money and time whenever an observation is rejected. Right now, more than 90% of observations are accepted.

Can I really use Observa to earn Bitcoin?

Totally! As a company, we think that crypto is cool! Observa uses Coinbase, so you can actually get paid in your preferred cryptocurrency. Coinbase supports Bitcoin, Bitcoin Cash, Ethereum, Litcoin, and more!

Make extra money

When I first downloaded it, there weren’t any Observa tasks in my area, but within a couple days time, there were 3-4 observations available. The only problem that I have see so far are that you only have 2-5 hours to complete the task after you accept it, and I don’t feel that it is enough time for most people. I accepted a task to go to Target store and observe a quip toothbrush section for $8, but there was a line of people waiting outside to enter the store because we are currently experiencing a pandemic and the store is only allowing a certain number of people inside the store at a time. Because of this, I had to decline the task because I would not have enough time. If the payment had been more than $8.00, possibly $15-20, it would’ve given me more motivation to get it completed, but with the given time frame, it still may not have been enough.
