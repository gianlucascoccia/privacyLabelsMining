Description

The most active community of outdoor enthusiasts. Period.

Connect with local hunters & anglers. Discuss gear. Learn tactics from outdoor headlines. Share tales. GoWild is like a digital campfire with your buddies.

• FIND LOCAL EXPERTS •
The Near Me feature connects you to local expert anglers, hunters and outdoor enthusiasts. Filter across 60+ topics.

• FIND BETTER GEAR •
GoWild Gearbox pulls gear from major retailers and manufacturers into one place, allowing you to cut through the marketing speak and have real conversations with real outdoor enthusiasts. Search hundreds of thousands of products, see reviews, get feedback on gear and more. The best part? 1% of our profits from Gearbox purchases are donated to outdoor nonprofits.

• TROPHY TRACKER •
Save your fishing and hunting memories with the GoWild trophy system. Load your animal or fish, capture the stats, share the details of the story, and we’ll save the story to your profile to share your best moments of the year.

• LOG OUTDOOR TIME • 
Share your outdoor adventures by importing your tracked activities from Garmin or Apple Healthkit. You can post an anonymized map of your activities along with photos and feel your heartrate with our haptics right when something amazing happened.

• OUTDOOR HEADLINES •
Tap Trailmix to find outdoor articles from all across the web. Keep up to date with tips, tactics and gear reviews. And share your favorites in the community!

• GREAT CONTENT, EASIER TO FIND •
Followers are not necessary on GoWild. As a forum, GoWild makes it incredibly easy to find content about the topics you love. The app’s content is fully customizable—see the kind of content you want, nothing you don’t.

• FIND WILD RECIPES •
We’ve partnered with some of the best chefs in the industry to create our recipe system. Log your own recipes, or find new ways to cook your favorite wild game.

Unrivaled outdoors social network app

Hands down the best app to allow outdoorsman/women to connect from all walks of life. You can even score your trophies (kills/creels) and earn points based on current scoring methods, and see how your trophy stacks up against others! It’s like Facebook and Instagram had a baby but the drama was removed. Instead, GoWild creates a place for you to connect with others that are just as passionate about the outdoors and gives you specific “trails” of interest to follow and really connect with those who share your specific interests! I’ve learned some valuable information and from other people’s shares experiences on the “trails” that I will take to my next hunt.

Download the app, choose your passion, score your trophies, make new friends, get educated, and be a better hunter, Fisher, trapper, outdoorsman because of it!

A Fortunate Accident

I literally stumbled upon this app while looking for something else (can’t even remember what it was). I am so grateful that I decided to give it a try. It is the first app I open every day and the last I check before shutting down the phone. It is a very “social” app but it goes way beyond that. You can post pictures, ask for advice, share recipes and literally start to feel as if you’ve known many of these people for a long time! All this with no judgement and a sense of true camaraderie that keeps you coming back to see what new adventure someone has had in the outdoors. There is truly something for everyone; hunters, fishermen, hikers and bikers, photographers and more. If you love the outdoors, you’ll find it. The people who created all this are as real as it gets and ready to help whenever needed. A really great app!
