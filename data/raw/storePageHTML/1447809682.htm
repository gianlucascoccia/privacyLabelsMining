Description

Free podcast app for iPhone to listen to all of the available podcasts as well as follow people/names to see all of their episodes. The easiest podcast player to use with a focus on discovery suitable for both new listeners as well as existing podcast lovers!

- Follow all of your favorite people to find and get updated on any podcasts they appear on.
- 1,000,000 names to search for, follow and listen to!
- Find new people to follow with our similar people recommendations.
- Similar podcasts to what you listened to, to help you find new podcasts to subscribe to.
- Most Popular Episodes of over 300,000 podcasts so you only listen to the best.
- Newest Episodes of your subscriptions, updated in real time.
- Hand picked episodes of over 15 different topics (e.g. Self Improvement, Science, Interesting stories, etc) so you can learn something new.
- Play and listen to over 500,000 podcasts for free. All the new podcasts, updated in real time.
- Downloads & play offline with a queue functionality
- Up to 32x speed (yes 32!), at the same quality!
- Sort episodes by date, length, popularity

What podcasts can you listen to in our podcast player?
- Keep up to date with the latest news. Hear people comment on Global Issues and Politics.
- Accelerate your personal development by listening to self improvement podcasts, education podcasts, history podcasts, philosophy podcasts, health and mental health podcasts and relationship podcasts.
- Get access to the best personal stories and interviews.
- Learn about Investing, Personal Finance, Startups, Management from the best business leaders.
- Listen to True Crime, fiction audio stories as well as documentaries.
- Learn a new language such as Chinese, Spanish or German.
- Find shows on religion, as well as meditation and spirituality.
- Find audio books and book summaries.

Jitsu101 , 10/06/2020

Major potential but needs critical bug fixed

I can’t find anywhere to submit feedback so I’m writing it here.

This is the first podcast app I’ve found that actually ranks podcast episodes, which for me is a killer feature. I love being able to find the “best of” when starting with any new podcast.

But there are major bugs that need fixing:
1. Frequently I open the app and the podcast I was last listening to has rewinded up to 10 minutes. I have to seek forward and try to find my place again. No idea why this happens but it is frequent. Perhaps the listener’s location is just not saved frequently enough.

2. At times the Play button at the bottom of the app doesn’t work. The play button at the top always appears to work

3. Player does not pause when using Siri, so I always have to rewind after that. Other podcast apps pause when using Siri.

Missing features:

4. Search podcast episodes. I’d like to just be able to view a podcast’s episode history and filter it by one or more search terms. This simple feature is available in overcast.

If the developers can follow up on these issues I’d be happy to update to 5 stars. I very rarely leave reviews but the “popularity” sorting of this app is so good I want this app to succeed!
