Love!

I love this app because you don’t have to pay to listen to specific stories like some other apps do and it really helps me fall asleep even though I’m not a child. Also because you only need to watch a 30 or 20 second ad but then what I love is that it dosent randomly interrupts the story.♥️

EvaMarieAngel , 11/01/2018

Great choice for adults as well

I love the storytelling. I’m 35, I prefer children’s stories to fall asleep to as they aren’t as mentally stimulating as audiobooks. The English accent is pleasant. Enough story choices to use the app for a long time before I will get bored with it.

I wish some stories were longer, sometimes a loud ad will play when you go to pick another story if you’re only half asleep. But it’s not a huge deal. I am usually asleep during the second story. The app is definitely worth a listen.

Julberg10 , 12/05/2020

The ads are not age appropriate

The content of this app is fine — the reader is good, and the length is good for before bed. But the loud ads that take over the interface are truly awful. They are for things like gambling and video games featuring guns, and feature creepy graphics that I have to hide from my kids. (Honestly having any busy graphics at all in a bedtime app is annoying, but I wish they were at least age appropriate.)
