Description

AntiStress anxiety relief game is here! Now you can play the most relaxing game to release stress or anger.

This is a pack of free antistress games including many fun games such as bubble popper, bubble wrap, and fidget spinner. The game includes a lot of bubble popping and diversion therapies, so you won't run out of options.

The features games in the app include:
• Fidget Spinner
Hands down the most relaxing game! Spin it as much as you want to relieve your stress.
• Metal Balls
Move your device to let the metal balls glide across the screen.
• Sand Hourglass
Enjoy watching the sand slip through the narrow midpoint of an hourglass.
• Bulb Smash
In this smash therapy, you have to smash as many bulbs as you can.
• Wood Smash
Release stress by smashing the maximum number of wooden blocks
• Brick Smash
Use your finger to smash as many bricks as you can.
• Pop It
Imagine a balloon filled with stress, pop it to reduce your anxiety.
• Bubble Burst
In this bubble popper, prick the bubbles while relaxing at the sight of the bubble popping.
• Ripple Effect
Tap your screen to enjoy relaxing water ripples.
• Window Mist
Move around your finger to clean a window covered with fog.
• Color Therapy
Positively use your energy to find stress relief by creating colorful art.
• Drawing Board
Create a piece of art to reduce your anger or stress level significantly.
• Water Flow
Connect the pipes to complete the flow of water from a start point to end point.
• Color Spiral
Minimize your anger by creating colorful particles.
• Wind Chimes
Relax yourself with the soothing effect of wind chimes.

All the games in AntiStress Anxiety Relief Game can help you to reduce stress and improve focus. Start your free antistress and relaxation therapy now!
