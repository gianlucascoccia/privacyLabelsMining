Description

Now including over 1,400 built-in bluegrass/old-time/etc. songs, plus an improved song creation/editing interface that's even easier to use!

Practice all your songs with customizable backing tracks generated with audio from real instruments: guitar, mandolin, and standup bass.

Strum Machine will play any chord progression, in any key, at any speed. It's the only backing-track app built specifically for players of bluegrass, old-time, and fiddle tunes.

• Hear rhythm backup generated in real time from real instrument audio.

• Instantly change the speed, or have the speed automatically increase.

• View chord charts as letters or numbers (i.e. "1-4-5") and change the key with two taps.

• Over 1,400 songs (mostly from bluegrass and old-time) ready to go.

• Add your own songs (or edit existing ones) with our simple song editor.

• Make lists of songs to practice from, or share with your friends.

• Syncs your custom songs, lists, and preferences with any device you use Strum Machine on: phone, tablet, or computer.

Strum Machine has totally transformed my guitar practice! There’s an incredibly vast collection of tunes in here - rarely have I searched for something that isn’t in here, but I’ve created my own custom songs a few times... so handy! Besides using this app for personal practice, I open it all the time when I’m in a bluegrass jam and someone calls out a song I don’t know. I just find the song, lay the app open on my lap, and I can play by watching the chords.
