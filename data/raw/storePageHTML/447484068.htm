Description

Eating well with Foodsmart by Zipongo has never been simpler with personalized meal plans, digital grocery lists, nutrition guidance, grocery discounts and more. Plus, get inspiration with recipe categories like "immunity boosting", ”in season”, “15-minutes or less” and “budget-friendly fare”.

EAT SMARTER with personalized nutrition guidance.
PLAN EASIER with instant meal plans optimized to you.
SHOP QUICKER with a digital grocery list integrated with online grocery ordering by Amazon Fresh and Instacart.
STAY INSPIRED by thousands of tasty & healthy recipes.

HOW IT WORKS:

1. SEE HOW YOUR DIET STACKS UP and how to improve your eating habits with the NutriQuiz, backed by research from the National Institute of Health.

2. DISCOVER YOUR CUSTOM MEAL PLAN based on your allergies, family food preferences, and cooking time available--and swap new options when you want.

3. GET AN AUTOMATIC GROCERY LIST and take it with you to the store, or order groceries online for home delivery with Amazon Fresh or Instacart.

4. CURATE YOUR OWN COOKBOOK by favoriting recipes.

5. SAVE MONEY with grocery discounts.

6. SAVE TIME by entering your ingredients on hand to find a healthy dish you can cook without going to the store.

*Accounts are supported by your employer or health plan - sign up today! If you don’t have access, ask your HR or benefits administrator to contact Foodsmart.*

Good but could be better

I’ve been liking Zipongo a lot more then I thought I would. I originally downloaded it through my employers health rewards app. The option to “favorite” and the “tap to add to grocery list” are great to help you organize. What I’d like to see in some upcoming updates is the ability to make “multiple cookbooks” where favorited recipes can be divided up e.g. already cooked cookbook, to try later cookbook, make in the upcoming week cookbook, so far and so forth. Another feature would be the option to filter by ingredients. I know you can set some dietary preferences but being able to choose which ingredients you want to use up in your fridge would be great. So you could filter ingredients based on having wheat bread and turkey and see which recipes come up that include 1 or both. Overall I’m really liking it and hoping you guys put out more updates in the future.

Developer Response ,

We're so glad that you're enjoying the experience and that it's helping you making cooking easier! We appreciate your feedback, and will certainly take it into account. I would recommend checking out the CookItNow feature to cook recipes with ingredients you already have on hand

So helpful!

So far I am loving this app. Yes, I had some issues getting logged in but once I got logged in I haven't had any issues since (knock on wood!). I've tried several of the meals and all so far have been very tasty, filling, and still somehow a low calorie count with high rewards in the nutrients department. Life changing!

I downloaded the Zipongo app a few months ago and can honestly say it has been life changing.
The easy access to quick, healthy recipes has made it so much easier to cook because I can find great dinner options when I’m on my way home from work and can pick up groceries on the way.

Highly recommend this app!
