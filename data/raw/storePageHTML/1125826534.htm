Description

JAMBL is a simple, yet amazingly powerful music creator tool that lets you compose tasty beats in seconds, and share your music in a music video.

A totally new way to become a master beatmaker – create and perform live music while staying in the flowwww. You won’t believe how easy it can be; you can even play with your eyes closed!

This is the ONLY music app that lets you turn those fresh songs into music and a music video!

Music making:
Our musical algorithm puts your taps in the groove, in the scale, on key, and on point. You can create a limitless variety of beats and melodies instantly. So, why not experiment and drop that bass?!

Video auto-edits:
After using the mixer to whisk up a fierce beat you love, record a short video – dance, throw signs, show your surroundings, take a video of whatever you want! The auto-edit feature will Jambl up your video to make it cut, reverse and loop with the beat. Perfect for sharing on social media stories.

- Super easy to use – just use your fingertips and your intuitive creativity
- No pre-made loops! – unlock your own musical creativity
- Everything you create is 100% your own – all rights reserved for you

With +100 high-quality samples by top sound designers and amazing musicians from all around the world.

The intuitive Jambl sequencer lets you create beats in any style imaginable:
- EDM
- Electrofunk
- Trap / Vaporwave
- Old school Hip-Hop
- Analog techno
- Classical music
- 8bit / Chiptune / VGM
- Traditional music from around the world
- New sounds added weekly!

How to get started?
- Choose a Jam-pack (music style with a collection of sounds)
- Tap and swipe your fingers on the Play screen to make loops
- Share your beats with a video edit
- Become instantly cool
- Develop your skills over time

JAMBL killer features:

What is our plan?
This is just the beginning! Expect many new features in the coming updates, including vocal
recording, online-collaboration, built-in community radio, and many more improvements.

We believe that music can create a better, more collaborative culture, and we want to use technology in a way that helps to create real-life human connections.

Our mission is to give the power of music creation to all people and create a world where everyone can make music together.

JM colorado , 03/21/2020

Fun,Easy, enjoyable

This app really does help make creating simple catchy and fun beats easy for anyone to catch on to. I really do enjoy this app. I have a few ideas though. When a creator makes a lot of beats, it can be frustrating to find something they have made a while ago, so I would appreciate filters for your profile. I was thinking we could also have different key signatures and octaves as well to really help our beats with their individuality. Of course, the app could use more beats and options. I also think that we should be able to create “songs” by accessing different tracks within a beat, for example putting multiple of these short beats together in sequence to create choruses, verses, and bridges. These are my thoughts for this app. It has serious potential!Edit: I have been on the app for a while, and noticed that the sounds update and change how my beats,that have already been published, sound. This has been bad for my sound and I’m sure many others. If a beat is already published, please find a way to refrain from changing the original sound if possible? I have a few recordings of the beats when I first made them and I know they’re different

Developer Response ,

Thank you! We are having the same thoughts! Lots of great things coming 2020. Happy holidays! Edit:
Hey im sorry that your beats have changed, the changes were to make packs sound better for new users and we imagined it would improve your existing beats, we will do our best to correct this and make sure it doesnt happen again.
🙏 thx

Best one out there, but yet to be the best ever.

Would drastically expand possible sounds if was able to use all different drum pads on any given template. For instance: only able to pair HIpHop94 with two different drum pads like Homework and Detroit limits sound possibilities. Also, if there was a record mode where we could play the loop for a given time and add effects and remove loops at certain parts would add depth and another level of creativity to come from this app. Not to mention that same record function could be used to actually save well made beats that could maybe someday be used on an actual track that gets published. There’s a lot of potential in this app, and paired with the right ideas it could change the way we make music forever.

Thank you for this review you gave us some cool ideas to think about. We will definitely give it a thought and it’s cool to have users like you ,telling us how to improve the app. Your feedback makes us better!

I would not say this is a great app. $8 a week to make music on a app that doesn’t even compete with garage band? Sounds ridicules! It’s not money that’s really the problem here. It’s the principle of having people pay money to even get an experience. What’s even funnier is that the experience is very limited.
