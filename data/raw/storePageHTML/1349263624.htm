Description

Elvie Pump is the world’s first silent wearable breast pump that fits in your bra, and your life. Elvie Pump makes it possible to pump on-the-go, completely hands-free, giving you more time and independence.

This app is for use exclusively with Elvie Pump. Download the app and connect it with your Elvie Pump to monitor milk volume in real time, track pumping history for each breast and control the pump remotely.

Simply place the small, lightweight pump in your nursing bra, turn it on and press Play on the pump or in the app to start your pumping session. Revolutionary technology eliminates noise so that you can pump silently, in peace.

Elvie Pump will automatically stop pumping when the bottle is full. Pumping has never been so easy - there are just five parts to clean and it takes seconds to assemble.

Find out more at elvie.com.

Freedom worth every penny

It would be impossible for me to provide breast milk if it wasn’t for Elvie. I had some issues that made breast feeding very difficult. The freedom to take the pumps anywhere and pump anywhere is indescribable. I can pump while hanging out with family , at a bbq, while driving , anywhere really. No one notices. Yes , there is a learning curve or do figuring out your shield size , suction, breast positioning , but once you figure it out it’s all a breeze. Elvie was worth every single penny!!! It’s an amazing product and the cost is not surprising now that I’ve used it and understand its value. I give it 5 stars for the freedom it’s given me and the ability to provide breast milk for my new born .

T. R. Watterson , 10/04/2019

Works well but...

First off I want to say that I absolutely LOVE my Elvie pumps & would've probably given up breastfeeding if I’d had to pump with a traditional pump. With these I can put them on in my bra and continue to live my life (mostly) without being plugged in & that’s amazing! A few minor issues I’ve had with it are that the app is not always very accurate with the amount it thinks has been expressed & I always have to tweak it before I save the session. It’s very easy to do so not a big deal for me. The main issue I’ve had is that sometimes the pump sounds like it’s on and doing its thing, but the suction isn’t right. Now I’ve used these enough to know how to line them up & put them on and it seems to happen randomly so I’m not sure what’s causing the issue. Customer service has been wonderful & sent me some replacement parts but still trying to get to the bottom of the issue. Otherwise, my experience has been great and it’s kept me on the breastfeeding wagon thus far. I would never have put up with the lack of mobility a traditional pump comes with, so these have been a GAME CHANGER! Keep improving your product & keeping up the great customer service because I tell every new mom I know about Elvie and I want them all to have a great experience.

Sophia's Jazzy Mom , 09/22/2020

Great except...

It changed my new motherhood experience greatly because I can take care of my baby and get around a lot easier which give me a lot more time to accomplish a lot more things. As a mother who wasn't able to direct breastfeed, it's helped me still provide the breastmilk to my baby with hopes to be able to continue for at least 6 months to a year. My only issue is that while I have it on I've found that anything I do that requires bending over at all, even picking my baby up out of the crib or really from anywhere, the cups leak. I am not pumping a whole lot so I have to actually supplement formula for a full days feeding, so every ounce is super important to me and it can be extremely frustrating for me when it feels like a full oz or 2 is on my shirt when I remove the pumps instead of in the cup. So that's my major issue with them: they are extremely expensive and would be worth it aside from the fact that they can take milk you pumped for 40 minutes and spill a good amount of it from any little movement. I still recommend it but I hope they can fix that
