Description

Using your Wodify account, stay connected with your box from anywhere, 24/7. Your CrossFit® box must use Wodify for performance tracking.

Featuring:

WODs - view current and past WODs
Class - register and sign-in to class
Coachboard - record your WOD results
Whiteboard - view current and past WOD results
Performance - add a result and see your history of gymnastics, weightlifting, metcons and totals
Journal - track your nutrition
WodiFind - want to drop-in to a box? Locate a box that uses Wodify
Notifications - real time alerts from your box and when you get likes and comments on the Whiteboard
Social Integration - share your accomplishments on Facebook and Twitter

Give us a shout if you have any issues at support@wodify.com.

Can’t Access Old Data

While I was paying for a box membership I didn’t mind this app, I had a few problems when it wasn’t available but other than that I liked it.
WELL, now that I am not a member of a box that uses Wodify, I can’t see ANY of my old PRs, workout times, or ANYTHING. My current box has Karen programmed for tomorrow, and I know I have done Karen before, I’d like to see what time I should aim to beat...but I can’t look at past info to see what my previous time was. It would also be nice to see my barbell PRs from when I first started CrossFit but this app makes that impossible. I should be able to access MY old data.

DDN2 , 10/29/2018

Such a Mess

This app is functional in that I can reserve and sign into class and then add my results...most of the time...unless the entire app is down again. Other than that this app is buggy, slow, crashes constantly, and isn’t especially good at organizing my performance history in a way that lets me recall it easily. Sure, I can look up a lift in a comically long drop down menu (forget just having a search function) but looking up a WOD based on a particular component of the workout? Forget it. Also, the whiteboard is frequently a jumble and ranks incorrectly. My personal favorite was the day that every woman athlete in my box took fourth place for the day. Bottom line, this app stinks and developer promises for improvement have yet to bear fruit. I have started charting my performance in a back up excel sheet for the day this app inevitably bites it and loses all my data.

Werdy , 08/01/2018

Web App Wrapper and It Shows

This app is barely functional, and never gets updates (no iPhone X update, for example). Basic features like viewing comments don’t work at all, and the performance is abysmal. It’s obviously just a wrapper of a web app, and even for a web app, it uses techniques that are laughably out of date (you can see the images they use for rounded corners load in after the the element is on-screen, for example). The servers are frequently down for maintenance, too, so you can’t enter results when you need to. Just a simple offline app would be way better. They still haven’t fixed the fact that if you don’t sign in within a narrow window (even if it’s because the servers were down), you can’t enter results. Terribly frustrating!
