Description

Mia is a passionate painter, who left her boring job to do what she really likes - draw pictures side by side with her boyfriend Leo. Join them in amazing colorful adventure: color a beautiful masterpieces, get stars to renovate the old house and abandoned Art Gallery.

Discover hundreds of unique, high quality paintings, made by the best modern illustrators exclusively for Gallery: Coloring book and Decor. Draw, renovate, decorate and follow the love story - this is the first artbook game where you can color by number and do home design in a one game.

Meet next special features:
- original gameplay: combination of coloring book and home design - creative game for real designers
- hundreds of beautiful pictures to colour
- different art styles of illustrations: geometric art, flat art, pixelart, low poly
- different choices of decor
- unique 3D graphics for the mansion and characters;
- relaxing pictures recoloring;
- regular updates

Great but a Suggestion

I love this game! It’s a great time killer and you can tell the developers put a lot of effort into the game. I love the pictures and how gorgeous they are. I love the graphics and how detailed everything is. I love the challenges that you can participate in. I am so tired of the bland match3 home design games because I love decor apps. This game has such a unique concept, and it combines my two favorite types of apps: home decor and coloring. This is a great game and I would definitely recommend this to a friend.

However, there is one major flaw with Gallery, and that is the fact that whenever I leave the app (not close the app but leave it to do something else for just 30 minutes), the app will restart, and that wouldn’t be so bad if I wasn’t in the middle of coloring a picture. One time, I had to color part of a picture 6 times before finally finishing it. This is a really big issue for me and super annoying. A way to improve this problem is for the developers to have a little section where you can see pictures that you haven’t finished and can just finish it whenever you want.

Overall, this app is amazing, but I do hope that the developers can fix this problem. Thank you for reading my review!
