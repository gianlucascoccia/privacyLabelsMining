Description

WebProtectMe promotes safe online spaces for mental health, content control, and privacy. Safe Browser allows you to the filter content you consider inappropriate. We're dedicated to supporting your mental health through removing triggering content.

Note: You will want to enable device restrictions and make Safe Browser your default browser. Follow the in-app instructions.

BLOCKS ADULT AND POTENTIALLY OFFENSIVE SITES
• Sexual / Pornography
• Image & Video Search
• Hate Speech
• Suicide Promotion
• Vulgar or Obscene Language
• Nudity
• Violence
• Drugs / Alcohol / Tobacco
• Gambling

FILTERING FEATURES
• Block websites by various categories like Pornography, Nudity, Social Networks,
Games and over 50 other categories.
• Create your own website whitelists and blacklists.
• Controls accessible from webprotectme.com
• Commercial strength anti-virus & anti-phishing technology protects you from sites looking to attack you!

MONITORING FEATURES w/ GUARDIAN APP
• Browse web activity for each WebProtectMe user on your account.
• Powerful search to quickly find specific sites and block them.

CUSTOM FILTERING FOR MULTIPLE USERS
Every user in your family can have their own custom protection, with a unique set of categories, whitelists and blacklists!

FILTERS POTENTIALLY MALICIOUS SITES
• Phishing & Malware

STOPS POTENTIALLY ILLEGAL SITES
• Hacking
• Fraud / Illegal Activities

STRICT SAFE SEARCH
You most often encounter inappropriate content by accident through search. WebProtectMe enforces strict safe search on all popular search engines.

YOUTUBE & VIMEO FILTER
Our advanced technology enforces controls on both Vimeo and Youtube video sites, blocking the videos you don't want to see.

PREVENTS ACCESS TO PARENT-CHILD DISCUSSION TOPICS
• Alternative Sexuality / Lifestyles
• Personals / Dating

UPDATES
Protection updates are constantly applied behind the scenes to stay ahead of threats.
