Good app with more pros than cons

This is a cool app that has it's pluses and minuses. One of the pluses is that someone else is able to manipulate aspects of a photo so that it looks better. This is by you choosing which elements you want to pay for them to do. The turnaround time is also fairly quick which is another plus. The negatives are that the file size of the photo that comes back to you is greatly diminished from the one you submitted to have them do the work on. The other negative is that the icons such as the hair for example are so small you can't really tell what you're buying. Then it comes back to you and you look at it and think this doesn't look right. And you've just wasted the purchase on it, so you have to purchase a different one and hope that works. And if the hair doesn't come in the right color, you have to then pay extra for the color. They should give the option of the color as part of the purchase. They should also re-submit the photo back to you in the same larger size you sent it to them with. They should also have the icons pop up bigger so you can see what you're buying. Some of the designers are better than others.
