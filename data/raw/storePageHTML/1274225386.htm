Description

Who unfollowed you or who deactivated his/her Instagram account?

Find all kind of features in the best Instagram analytics tool:

- Story Analytics
You can find out who is watching your stories without following you!

- Instant Stories
You can watch and repost the Instagram stories and images.

- Following Activity
You can see new and lost followers of the person you are wondering, also you can see who did they started to follow or unfollow from their followings.

- Relation
You can make one to one comparison of likes, comments and hours with your Instagram followers or followings.

- Compare Likes
You can compare two different posts and find out who did like the first one and didn't like the second one

- Users
New and Lost Instagram Followers

Other features:
- Users not following you back or vice a versa
- Instagram Followers most & least liked/commented your media
- Media
- Most & least liked/commented hours
- Most & least liked/commented medias
- Filters
- Most liked & commented Instagram filters
- Most powerful Instagram Analytics Tool
