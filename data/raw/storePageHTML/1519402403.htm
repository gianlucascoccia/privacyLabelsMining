Find the glitch and get billions in coins!

I beat the paltry 9 levels in less than 2 days. But most importantly, I found a glitch that literally paid me over a billion coins.

I’ve upgraded everything to level 200 (max) and now I just play it to kill time. The ads don’t much bother me (and there are plenty of those), so I just put the phone down for 30 seconds.

Reading reviews has taught me that they probably won’t ever come out with more levels.

Catawba Claw , 08/01/2020

Not a game at all.

You have no choice but to watch commercials. Each jump takes about 5 seconds and you get a good chunk of change to watch a single commercial but to use that money you have to watch more commercials. No ability to pass on that second commercial. I realize these games are not really game but click bait for the games that advertise in the game. But when game play lasts 5 seconds you watch a full minute of commercials it’s ridiculous. These games allow developers to make some quick cash to bring us more games but let player opt out of one of the commercials because that’s all it would take for me to continue playing.

HwongPong , 08/08/2020

Enjoyable fun

Addictive and fun to play. I went through the levels quickly though and wish there were more. I have been going back with my now upgraded bike and trying the early stages again. It’s super easy to overshoot and do adds a different challenge.

A controls guide would be a nice addition but I got on fine without it.
