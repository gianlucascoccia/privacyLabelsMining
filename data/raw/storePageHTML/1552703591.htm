Mhanimations , 02/25/2021

Honest review

Craft island is a great small indie game. It’s simple enough for kids to use and be entertained as well as adults to pass time.
Since it is an indie game on the market today there are ads, a lot of ads, but that can be bypassed with a $0.99 purchase or just using airplane mode. So far there are 3 power ups you can find in game that might prompt an ad watch: the golden axe, the big axe, and the pot of gold. The golden axe will give you unlimited damage allowing you to knock all trees down with one swipe, which is useful. The big axe allows you to reach further so you can chop more trees at once. And the pot of gold gives you extra gold with the ad watch giving you double the amount given. So far there are two worlds to explore and upgrade with increasing levels of trees so wood gathering isn’t too tedious. Though they’re fairly easy to beat, I’ve only had the game for 3 days and I’ve been able to get the maximum upgrades on all my shops, tools, and houses as well as unlock every piece of the island. I can see this game is very new but I’d love to see it grow into something even better, possibly add character customization or character upgrades other than just the axe. Over all a good game but the ads can also pop up even when you deny the power up so that’s why the one less star.
