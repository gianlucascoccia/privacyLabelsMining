Description

Measure your golf swing speed by turning your iPhone or iPad into a portable golf swing speed sensor.

- Load videos straight from your camera roll
- Calculate swing speed based on slo-mo videos
- Frame-by-frame video analysis tool
- App includes step-by-step tutorial

Note: For the most accurate results, you’ll need to use videos shot with 240 FPS (slo-mo) and filmed on the device in which you are using the app.

D. So Cal , 08/02/2020

Worth the $3

For me it works better as a very slow speed playback swing review/analysis tool. Recording and playing back every swing to determine each swing speed becomes tedious. But for $3 it works well at giving you an idea of your swing speed and much cheaper than instant feedback radar devises. Use the directional arrows, by tapping your finger on them, and you can analyze your swing frame by frame. I was initially frustrated by using the finger slide to find the exact frame to set the 90 degree and ball parameters to measure swing speed. Then figured out you don’t push on the double arrows, but tap them quickly to refine the exact frame you want or to see your swing in super slow motion.
