Description

WHAT WILL YOU FIND IN THE REAL MADRID APP?

Have you ever wondered if your football team has a mobile app? If you are a Real Madrid fan, you are in luck because you have the possibility to download the Real Madrid App.

Don't know if you can download it? Make yourself comfortable because we are going to tell you everything you can find in this app. Are you ready to immerse yourself in the world of Real Madrid?


DO YOU WANT TO WATCH REAL MADRID TV?

One of the functions of the App is that you can watch Real Madrid TV from your mobile phone. Take a look at the channel's programming and even set an alert for the program you would like to watch.

Relive more than one Madrid match, and enjoy programs like "Historias con alma" or specials like "Historia que tú hiciste. La Décima".

Have you always wanted to watch the Real Madrid channel in streaming? Now you can do it and not only in Spanish. The Madrid App allows you to change the language to English and watch the match in a different way.


ACCESS THE REAL MADRID STORE

Another option offered by the App is to go directly to the official Real Madrid store. Are you thinking of giving a gift or getting yourself a gift? We tell you what you will find in this section:
• Official Real Madrid kit: Your favorite players jerseys (Sergio Ramos, Hazard, Isco, Kroos and many more), socks and a wide range of products.
• Games and cuddly toys for the little ones: Give a Real Madrid teddy bear, an inflatable ball or a mini table football.

From the Real Madrid App you will also be able to see all the discounts available at the official store, so take advantage and get your Madrid shirt!


BUY REAL MADRID TICKETS WITH ONE CLICK

If you are a sports fan and a Real Madrid fan, you will know where to buy tickets for a football or basketball match. If you are not, you can access the Real Madrid ticket sale from the App.

Find out which matches are available on that date, find out the price of the Real Madrid tickets and pay directly from the App. Without having to move from your sofa!

DO YOU WANT TO GET A MADRIDISTA CARD?

It's not the first time you've heard of the " Madridista Card", but you don't know if it' s worth it. Here are some of the advantages that this card offers you:
• Discounts on tickets, store items and restaurants at the Bernabéu.
• Draws
• Official magazine
• Exclusive experiences

From the Real Madrid App you will be able to access the "Madridistas" section where you will be able to find out the price and get your card at that very moment.


GET TO KNOW THE REAL MADRID SQUADS

As a Real Madrid fan, we're sure that you know our squads inside out. Within the app, you'll find full information about our teams:
• Real Madrid men's football team
• Real Madrid women's football team
• Real Madrid basketball team
The Real Madrid app offers you profiles of every player, information about the matches played, points/goals scored and much more besides.


WHAT ELSE CAN I FIND ON THE REAL MADRID APP?

The Real Madrid App allows you to access a lot of information about the club. In addition to the above, we have summarised everything you can do:
• Get to know the team: visit your player's profile and social networks
• Leage table: Take a look at the league table, the Champions League and the Copa del Rey
• Virtual Bernabéu: Take a tour of your favorite team's stadium
• Virtual Valdebebas: If you have never been able to go to the Real Madrid City, you can see it from your mobile
