Description

Build wealth with private investing

Republic gives you power to invest in the future you believe in by providing access to startups, real estate, crypto, and video game investments. The future of investing will be defined by individual investors like you, not giant financial institutions. What will you give power to?

When you create a free account on Republic, you can:

INVEST REGARDLESS OF EXPERIENCE LEVEL
Anyone 18 years or older can invest.* Accessible to new and experienced investors. Option to invest directly with your credit card using Apple Pay.

ACCESS TOP CURATED DEALS, NO HIDDEN FEES
You can now invest in selected investment opportunities, previously available to only the wealthiest 3% of the U.S. population. All deals are curated according to transparent standards that you can see on our site.

DISCOVER DIVERSE INVESTMENT OPPORTUNITIES
Find deals to invest in across all industries. Empower diverse founders that are representative of a global investor base and community.

INVEST AT THE EARLIEST STAGES OF GROWTH
You’re not just another investor, you’re an active participant. When you invest in a company at the earliest stages, you become an integral part of the companies creating our future.

CREATE A DIVERSIFIED PORTFOLIO
Diversify your investments across numerous asset classes that are highly uncorrelated from volatile public markets.

JOIN A GLOBAL COMMUNITY OF INVESTORS AND CHANGEMAKERS
More than a million members have already invested over $250 million through the Republic ecosystem.
