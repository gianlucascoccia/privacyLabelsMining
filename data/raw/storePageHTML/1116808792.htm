Chikns , 12/01/2017

I’ve only found a few gems.

I’ve had about five different orders of 2-10 items of clothing that I bought from here and I only find about one, maybe zero, clothes that I like from those orders. I love my jeans, even though it’s hard to find my exact size since they never make jeans the same size anymore... That’s about it. The shirts are super thin and see through, they’re nothing like what it shows. I’d have to wear a tank top under all the shirts. I’m fat and I get hot easily. The shoes are not at all meant for wide feet so I’d avoid those. Even if they did fit my feet, a lot of them are really trashy... super cheep. Stuff you’d find at the dollar store.. I like this one parka jacket I found but still haven’t gotten a chance to actually wear it yet, but it’s a great fit.. only downside is that I had to order a size up... every since top runs small. If you’re a 1x, get a 2x. If you’re a 2x, get a 3x.. I’ve even had to order some 3x’s and they still run small. My regular size is usually 1x-2x depending on the shirt. Overall, it truly is a great store but just needs some tweaks. Love the variety of plus size clothing, shoes, and that they included a kids section. Especially that they have an app. But, I’m gonna have to stop shopping here, everything just either runs too small or way too big.
