Description

Need an effective way for your kids to learn math while they are staying at home during these hard times? Try Zapzapmath Home where your kids can learn math and have fun!

Learning math is easy and fun with 3rd Grade Math: Fun Kids Games! Enjoy hours of educational games and videos designed to change the way your 3rd grader thinks about learning common core math.

Children can use 3rd Grade Math: Fun Kids Games to learn about multiplication and division, the relationship between place values and arithmetic, fractions, measurement problems involving time, liquid volume, and mass, area and perimeter, and reason with shapes and their attributes.

Get a personalized learning experience built on adaptive learning. Each lesson is taught through entertaining math games and animated lessons, so children can learn while having fun.

Start making learning fun today!

I really love the graphics to the game. You get to pick your avatar. I love how it reminds me if I’m playing a video game that’s how good the graphics are. ferrelll , 06/15/2020

Confusing

I played this game myself, but struggled. There was no instruction regarding the skills and only a brief demonstration of the tasks. There were parts that were confusing, others that wouldn’t let me correct myself. Others, I had to guess what to do. When I made mistakes (purposefully), I was only given the right answer, no remediation. Traveling throughout the app was confusing. It was cute, I liked the idea, the music was good and I liked the characters. I wanted to give this a better rating, but if, as an educator, I became frustrated, I can only imagine how a child would feel when things didn’t make sense.
