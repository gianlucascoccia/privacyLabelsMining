Description

Build your own battle bot and unleash its power in this stylish PvP game!

From the creators of the hit games Cut the Rope and King of Thieves, comes the most ingenious and stylish battle bot constructor! Join more than 150 million players from all over the world and become the star of the Arena!

- City Kings: Fight against real gangs from around the globe to conquer the city in cooperative mode.

Recruits should not be able to use any of the tools to the city of kings only the tolls that is used on them self till the leader feels they have proven them selves a worthy member. Members should have more access to the tools in the city of kings. Officers should have control to demote players or kick players for breaking the rules. There should be a place to go that the leader can post the rules of the gang so the recruits can see them. As it is now we have to retype the rules every time some one joins the gang. Jk-Dubb , 04/11/2018

Long time player starting to get frustrated

This game has been my favorite game since I first played a year ago. One of the reasons I really loved it was because you did not have to purchase anything- you had to be willing to put in time and play lots and lots of quick fights but you could play without HAVING to pay. But now, playing quick fights isn’t nearly enough to be competitive. I have a ton of parts but no money/coins with which to fuse parts to build my health and attack. And with the latest update, I haven’t received money in the prize boxes- how am I supposed to build up coins to fuse????
Additionally, it is so much harder to GET machines to stay competitive (seen in the last couple updates). I have noticed that it is impossible to get a vehicle with 2 attack attachment points AND 1 defense attachment point. I used to be able to get those by stage 8.
I have loved this game so much and it has gotten me through some long chemo treatments, but if I have to keep buying gems to substitute for coins to fuse, I’ll have to stop playing.
Please, please bring back the ability to work hard and get rewards for doing so!
Thanks for allowing me to vent a bit...
