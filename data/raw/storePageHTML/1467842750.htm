Grimtown , 11/21/2020

Pay to win

This is the most money grubbing crap show I’ve seen. There is nothing there to keep a free to play player interested as you are hounded non stop to buy 20 to 30 dollar packs that have nothing worthwhile. I logged on and received six different offers non of which were affordable for the casual gamer. I’ve given this game the LOWEST rating I have ever given a mobile game and can’t see a reason to change it. You disappointed your player base with these overinflated price points and disappointing in-game loot. I made it to a player level of 420 before I was completely unable to proceed thanks to a high degree of monster stat boosts. Be ready to play this game in your pocket as that is the only way it’s worth playing. The creators should think hard about the business practices that they have incorporated and if someone buys a pack they are not gamers only people whose money is the only thing that is worth anything about them. Have a brain when playing these clear grease pits and steer toward a game that is actually worth it. Bad game.

FireNationFoxy , 07/30/2020

Could’ve been something good but...

The gameplay itself isn’t anything mind blowing but it’s fun for what it is, I enjoy getting on to do my dailies and chat with my guild mates. Drop rates are low per usual rpg law of grinding. The problem comes in where almost everything that raises your power score more than just a few hundred at a time is all locked behind a paywall, literally all of it so the players who can afford to throw away their wallets are quite literally millions ahead in gear score and free to play dedicated grinders have no way to catch up anytime soon with gear/dungeons being locked away and daily limits so they’re forced to just pray someone doesn’t run up on them in dungeons and can’t contribute to territory wars whatsoever. Obviously it’s a free to play game and they need money yeah, but this game is way too far into pay to win territory, it would be much better if they toned that aspect down a good bit.
