►NOT YOUR AVERAGE DAILY DEVOTIONS◄
The Practice Co App devotions dig deep into our sacred texts, they avoid cliché and sit with ambiguity, all while nurturing your curiosity and wonder. They're powerful, two minute reads that are simple but have helped change the script in thousands of people's lives.

There’s a new and meaningful, blog-style devotional each day, accompanied by beautifully designed images and lock screens. Join tens of thousands of daily readers as together we discover a daily practice of meaningful spirituality so that you can live a fulfilling and courageous life.

Love it!

This app is one of the best things that ever happened to me. I grew up in a religious family, going to church on Sunday and saying my nightly prayers with my parents.
