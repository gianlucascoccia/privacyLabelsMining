What’s New

Mar 10, 2021

Version 7.0.5

Ahoy Kptn!

Are you all set for spring already? We are really looking forward to fresh herbs, asparagus & more! Before we kick off the season, we did some cleaning up and fixed the following bugs:

-The filter in your favorites remains active with all selected tags when you click on a recipe and go back to the filtered overview.
-From the favorite list, you can select a recipe via "Longpress" and put it on your shopping list.
-When you are in your meal plan and add recipes to the shopping list, you will receive a little feedback pop-up that it worked.
-In the shopping list, the selected recipe with the associated ingredients is correctly highlighted.
-When changing the cooking steps from full-screen mode to scrolling view, a graphics error occurred that we eliminated.

Thank you for keeping your eyes open! As always, we look forward to your feedback at feedback@kptncook.com.

Beautiful Cooking App

I use the recipes in this app often, especially since the pandemic as we rarely order take out. The recipes are simple, healthy, easy to follow and beautifully presented.
The app has a fun side as well. Each day one receives three new recipes, so it’s like opening a small surprise package. If one likes the look of a recipe, one can save it, otherwise it disappears but might be reintroduced at a later date.
Additionally, the app takes advantage of the fact that many people already have a lot of basic cooking staples at home so there’s no need to run to the store for a exotic ingredient.
I find the recipes easy to modify as well. Thank you KptnCooks!!!!

Visual and Easy to Follow

I wish all recipes made each step as visual and easy to follow as this app. I can just scroll as I go through the recipe and follow along, using both the caption with ingredients and the photo to track my progress. It filters vegetarian dishes for me, and even though I’m vegan, I still use many of the recipes as I know how to adapt them to my diet with substitutes.
