Are you saving for a house deposit, new car, holiday, household bill etc or putting money aside just in case?

Buy 'Savings Goals' and track your progress when saving. You enter a target amount and an optional target date by which you want to save this amount and the app suggests a savings schedule and tracks your progress. You can have as many separate Savings Goals as you need.

◆ Use a Target Date and the App will :
√ Calculate the Amount you need to save per Month/Week to reach your Goal by the Target Date
√ Calculate when you will reach your Goal if you save a certain amount per Month/Week
√ Produce a Savings Schedule showing when to add to your savings to reach your Goal

◆ Features a graphical display to show :
√ How much you have saved as a proportion of your Target
√ How close you are to your Target Date
√ Whether you are behind or ahead of schedule

◆ Customisable to suit your needs :
√ Save for any purpose! Name your Goal yourself
√ Saving Time Periods (per week/month etc) are fully customisable
√ Create as many Savings Goals as you need
√ Turn on Secure Entry to lock the App with an Entry Code if required
√ Customise the look of the App with Themes


Buy Savings Goals, keep track of your savings and save more !

Solid App, Enjoy using it

Tried many savings goal apps in the past. Most of them haven't been updated for many months, to several years. This one is my favorite so far. Easy to use, intuitive interface, has all features that would be necessary to keep track of all your goals. App was well designed. Hopefully developers will keep evolving the app in the future and not abandon it like so many others. Definitely would recommend to anyone wanting to organize your goals.

Great App!

This is a great app it allows you to see how much your saving. The only suggestion I would make is to be able to make journal entries. Every time you add money you can enter the date and where money came from or if you have to take money out, the date and why you had to take it out.

Jeni17 , 06/02/2012

Better Functionality Needed

In theory, I like this app for planning savings needs. Two improvements would make it MUCH better-
1. Allow user to rearrange items in the list, so higher priority goals could be shown ahead of lower ones. Currently shows as order of entry. Since I entered "new couch" first, that shows as first on my list, when I really need "emergency fund" to show first.
2. Provide a monthly lump sum needed on the list page. If I am saving for 10 items, I do not want to open each one and add them up to see what I need to set aside from my June paycheck for items on my savings list.
