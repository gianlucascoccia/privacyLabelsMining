Description

ModeSens is the premier luxury fashion shopping assistant. Explore, search and find millions of the latest designer products in all one place.

Powered by technology, ModeSens is your free, digital shopping assistant helping you shop from 12,000+ premium brands and store partners like Gucci, Fendi, Net-A-Porter, Farfetch, Nordstrom, and Saks Fifth Avenue.

With full designer collections, powerful filter capabilities, built-in barcode scan functionality, link-based search and robust product pages, we make it easy to compare price and availability, set sale alerts, create curated collections and so much more. Real-time updates allow you to never miss an item in your size at the right price.

Plus, our community allows all fashion lovers to share their favorite pieces and best looks, while also finding inspiration from each other.


ONLY THE BEST OF FASHION

- Shop luxury brands like Gucci, Saint Laurent, Balmain, Tom Ford and thousands more


COMPARISON SHOPPING

- Compare prices and inventory across 300+ stores for every single product


EFFORTLESS SEARCH

- Filter by designer, category, size, color and more

- Scan product barcodes while shopping in store to view all the information before making a purchase

- Search for specific items using links directly from retailers websites


PERSONALIZED SHOPPING

- See only what you want by setting store and designer preferences

- Set alerts on products and designers for real-time updates on sales, new arrivals, back in stock, low in stock and more

- Build a digital closet to track what you own and create a wishlist for the future

- Earn ModeSens Rewards points and enjoy our Dedicated VIP personal shopper service


ENGAGED COMMUNITY

- Upload and share your own looks

- Follow other community members for style inspiration and advice

- Explore hundreds of trend and category topics
