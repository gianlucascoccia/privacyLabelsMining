Description

Cupcakes cooking games introduces the colorful world of cooking, baking and decorating cupcakes.
Enjoy the entire cupcake cooking and baking process by adding the ingredients, stirring the mixture, and choosing flavours. When the cupcakes are out of the oven it’s time to get creative with a variety of toppings, candies and decorations.
Use your imagination and create beautiful appetizing cupcakes at your own design and flavour.

This game is included in the Pazu subscription which offers all of Pazu’s games with full game versions, no ads, a child-friendly interface and up to 4 devices for each subscription.
