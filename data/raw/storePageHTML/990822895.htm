Great Weather App

I used to use WeatherUnderground but after IBM ruined that app and started charging exorbitant fees I needed to find something else. This is the best alternative I have found so far. Very reasonable price (3.99) and good interface. It’s not quite as good as the old WU (loved scrolling in landscape to see graph of temps, rain % and amount of rain) but provides most of the same info in a simple clear format. No stupid videos and other unnecessary stuff. Can’t customize layout or info much and radar loads a little slow, but overall I think this is a great weather app.
