Expectful is the #1 app for Fertility, Pregnancy and Parenting.Join thousands of other women who are TTC, pregnant, and new moms using Expectful to reduce stress, lower anxiety, improve sleep quality, and bondy with their baby.

No matter where you are on your reproductive journey, you’ve found us by searching ways to give your child the best start in life and create a happier, healthier path to parenthood for yourself.

At Expectful, we understand that every family’s journey is unique. Whether you’re looking for peace of mind while TTC or to soothe you and your baby to sleep we’ve created the world’s largest and most comprehensive library created specifically for fertility, pregnancy, postpartum, and parenting.

Popular content:

Fertility meditations for IVF and TTC
Weekly Pregnancy Meditations
Hypnobirthing Course
Emotional Self-Care
40 Daily Postpartum Meditations
Sleep Stories and Soundscapes
Newborn Lullabies
Partner & Parenting
… And many more!

Choose a 5, 10, or 20 minute meditation that fits your schedule. Never meditated before? No problem. Our meditations are guided.

All of the content we create is based on interviews with TTC, expecting and new parents, and with the help of licensed psychologists, meditation experts, and sound artists.

We're also integrated with Apple Health Kit to help track your minutes meditated over time to help you set your own goals for a regular practice.

kirmo , 11/13/2017

Love this app!!

I’ve been struggling to get pregnant for 15 months now. I had one miscarriage and am now on to fertility treatments. This app has helped me tremendously, especially during the more stressful times of TTC. They have a whole section of fertility meditations for women who are TTC (probably 20 or so). I can relate in some way to each one of them and I choose the one that best fits my needs/struggles that day, whether it be trusting my journey, connecting with gratitude, or just preparing for sleep. The woman’s voice is very soothing, and you can choose between 10 and 20 minute meditations. I try to listen to one every night before bed, which is when I find I feel most anxious. It always helps me calm my mind and prepare for a great nights sleep. I want to thank you so much for this unique service! I love it and highly recommend it!!

Developer Response ,

Hi there. Thank you for taking the time to share your review and we're so glad our meditations have helped you during your Preparing to Conceive journey. We really appreciate your support.

Tranquility

I started to develop anxiety when the pandemic hit close to home, mind you me and my family were taking all the steps necessary to stay safe. Anxiety hit so hard for me, more then I had ever imagined it to be. I didn't understand why I was going through it. I literally couldn't recommend this more.
