Description

Aromabyte is the first essential oil community for doTERRA™ oil users!

Learn everything about your doTERRA™ essential oils in one app. We are the ONLY app that provides REAL user reviews for every doTERRA™ Single Oil and doTERRA™ Blends. We combine our expansive usage guide of common ailments with hundreds of premium oil recipes to provide you with an all-in-one solution for your oil needs! Look up an ailment, find recommended oils and recipes to use, read reviews and other user feedback of those recipes and oils.

We are dedicated to keeping Aromabyte up to date with the latest products from doTERRA™! You can expect frequent updates with the latest oils and products as they are released.

OUR GREAT FEATURES

• REFERENCE GUIDE: Access to our expansive reference guide with detailed information about oil uses, benefits, safety information and more.
• RECIPES: Access to hundreds of our recipes with new ways for you to get the most out of your oils!
• RATINGS & REVIEWS: See star ratings and read real user reviews for every doTERRA™ oil and for hundreds of our oil recipes.
• INVENTORY: Keep track of your oils with our built-in inventory. Never run out of your essential oils again!
• NOTES: Create and save your own notes for any oil, recipe or ailment.
• FAVORITING: Save oils and recipes as your favorites for quick and easy access later.
• CHARTS: Quick reference to several useful charts with different application methods.

With 300+ recipes, 300+ ailments in our usage guide, EVERY doTERRA™ oil included and the ONLY app to offer real user ratings and reviews - our app is the best essential oil guide available!
