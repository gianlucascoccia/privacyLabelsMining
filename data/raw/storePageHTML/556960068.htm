Description

No one knows this city better than Indianapolis Monthly, and other apps can’t provide the depth on everything you want to know about enjoying Indy’s dining, entertainment, shopping, culture, and more. Drawing on the magazine’s deep commitment to authoritative reporting, this app will help you plan your next meal or outing, that next adventure or even the next stretch of your life. 
It’s all here, everything from Top Doctors to the Best New Restaurants, from the latest School/Education findings to the Best of Indy in many realms (food, fitness, and more). You’ll find features, reviews, contact information, and insider tips from our editors and experts.
This magazine app is designed for easy navigation and enjoyment. Colorful photography and crisp writing give you accurate, delightful pieces on what’s now and what’s next. We value your reviews, and rely on customer feedback to help us build the best possible experience, so please let us know your thoughts by emailing appsupport@indymonthly.emmis.com.

Indianapolis Monthly Magazine Subscription Information: 
Monthly subscription - $1.99, renews on a monthly basis, and during the monthly subscription, one issue will be delivered. 

Yearly subscription is $24.99, renews on a yearly basis, and during the yearly subscription, 14 issues will be delivered (12 regular issues plus the Menu and City Guide).

Payment will be charged to iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase. 

Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period. Account will be charged for renewal within 24-hours prior to the end of the current period, and identify the cost of the renewal. 

Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user's Account Settings after purchase. No cancellation of the current subscription is allowed during active subscription period. 

Please note that any unused portion of a free trial period, if offered, will be forfeited when the user purchases a subscription to that publication.
