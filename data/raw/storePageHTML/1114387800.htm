Description

Whether you are trying to lose weight, gain muscle mass, rounder buttocks or want to get 6-pack abs – our fitness app offers full body workout plans with many 3D workout exercises for free. Here you will find ready workouts with champions’ diets.
You can choose from a variety of exercise plans for muscle groups, such as chest workout or arm workout, or for a certain goal, such as fat burning workout. You can also choose between workout for women, workout for men and get ready workouts with diet.
This fitness application beats other fitness apps, because you can keep your personal workout diary and find the best personal coach from any city for online fitness workouts and bodybuilding sessions. Share your progress, ask questions about workouts, exercises and diet – you will get competent answers. Together you can keep fit at home!

Main advantages of Fitness Online:
- Ready workouts with a diet plan created by bodybuilding champions who act as coaches.
- Workout diary: Record your progress with our workout tracker, diet tracker and fitness tracker!
- In-App Encyclopaedia: Learn more about exercises and diet.
- Over 550 exercises with high-quality 3D animations.
- Diet plan: Over 4500 food products with nutrition facts, calorie counter and food tracker.
- Sports nutrition: Essential additives to your diet for fast muscle building (gain weight).
- Personal feed: Share workouts, ask questions, meet other users of our community.
- Direct message our coaches using the in-app chat. Send tips to your friends on how to lose weight, get perfect abs, workout arms and chest, maintain a healthy diet or get a rounder butt through the best buttock exercises.
How does it work?
As a fitness user you can work out by developing your own weight loss program from the exercise library, use our calorie tracker and nutrition guide to create a diet plan and start working out. You can track results in the workout diary. Or choose a ready training program with a diet for your goal, like lifting weights workout, fat loss workout, etc.
You want more? Hire and work out online with a personal coach. Choose your personal trainer by budget and feedback, contact them and set sports goals. Get an invoice, pay with your bank card and start your exercises.
Your coach will give you a workout plan with individual diet instructions, create a monthly course with a full set of exercises and meal plan to reach your goal, whether that is to get a bigger butt, lose weight, lose belly fat, build muscles, get six-pack abs, get a shredded body, get big arms or a wide chest or get fit after a pregnancy – all with fitness at home.
With a monthly course, such as a weight loss program, your coach will monitor your results for a monthly fee, give advice on your form or meal plan and adjust the exercise program if needed.
We only invite the best fitness coaches to Fitness Online with knowledge on all working out goals, like trying to get a rounder butt at home, lose weight fast, get six-pack abs, get big arms, get a wide back, build muscles or just define your body.
