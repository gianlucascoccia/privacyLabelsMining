Description

Stream music on-demand, download music for offline playback, and create playlists of your favorite songs.

Subscribe to Napster today and gain access to over 60 million* songs.

To support the creative, vibrant musician community, Napster charges a monthly fee. There’s no commitment—cancel anytime on web or mobile. We love artists!!

How does Napster work?

- Napster is Ad free. Listen to your favorite music without interruption.
- Download unlimited songs and playlists right to your iPhone or iPad to play offline; listen anywhere, anytime
- Stream music with your account to hundreds of compatible devices. And audio hardware including Sonos, Wii U, Chromecast, Denon, etc.
- Use the Apple Watch app to control and access your favorite music**
- Find new music you love through similar listening tastes of fellow Napster users around the world with our global Listener Network technology.

ilovelini , 07/14/2019

Hot or miss. A crappy app that could be much better

Although I’ve subscribed to this app for years, there is much that drives my bonkers about this app. The search engine is completely useless. Sometimes it requires investigating just to find certain albums from certain artists. It can not be assumed that all albums are listed under the “all albums “ tab. Also many albums by some artists are not available. Example, even though all Beatles mono mixes are available on CD doesn’t mean you’ll find them here. The most annoying part is the time it takes to load a new track when one is finished playing. You’d think with the speed offered to these music services, they’d be able to allow tracks to flow uninterrupted as is the case with any CD. Removing songs from a playlist is difficult and cumbersome. If I decide I want to remove a song from a playlist there should be an option while the track is playing to make a two click system that will remove it. There are plenty of other options listed while listening to a track, it should include an option for removing a song from a playlist. Lastly, would it kill them to include a small biography about the artist and album you are listening too? All in all an ok app that could be made great by these upgrades.
