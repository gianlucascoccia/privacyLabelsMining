Description

Explore nature as a wild animal and raise a family in the new RPG adventure Wildcraft! Play as your favorite wild animal and travel around a 3D world!

Start your adventure as a wolf, fox, lynx and more, and raise a family. Join online multiplayer games so you can play with friends and protect your family from dangerous enemies. Unlock new animal breeds as your family legacy grows!

Start living as different animals in the new online sim Wildcraft!

WILDCRAFT FEATURES:

Choose Animal Breeds
- Start your adventure as a:
- Wolf
- Fox
- Lynx
- And more!

Raise a Family
- Customize each family member by name, gender, fur color, bark, eyes, body size, and more!
- Raise a family and have up to six cubs per animal family.
- Want to start a new life? Leave your current family to start a new one.

Explore a 3D World
- Explore a huge 3D world full of unique locations.
- Battle the elements and live just as a wild animal would in summer, winter, spring and fall.

Battle Enemies
- As a wild animal, you’ll face dangerous enemies.
- Protect your family from predators and other enemies.
- Defeat specific enemies to unlock fighting achievements.

Online Adventure Games
- Play with friends, explore the world and battle enemies.
- Help protect your own family and your friend’s family by battling.

Friends & Leaderboards
- Play with other players so you can battle enemies together.
- See when other your friends are online and join their adventure.
- Make the leaderboard for winning battles and being a high level.

The game offers Wild Club recurring membership subscription which unlocks unique content to user as well as other additional bonuses.

If you choose to purchase Wild Club membership, the purchase will be applied to your iTunes account on confirmation. Subscription will automatically renew unless canceled within 24-hours before the end of the current period. You can cancel anytime with your iTunes account settings. The price of the subscription is 3.99 USD per month.

Good game!

This is a great game, with many cool features. Most of the people in multiplayer are actually really nice (which isn’t really part of the game but I would just like to say that), and I like making my families in single player. I can’t think of anything that I dislike about this game, but I did find a few glitches. Sometimes when I die I end up where I was before, just no health, on the ground, and the only movement I can do is like nodding a little bit instead of walking and changing which way I’m facing (I don’t really know how to describe it.) and also, whenever you run down the mountains in your den you fall underground (in some areas it looks really pretty though!)
Other than that, I love this game! I currently have a fox with a family of five. (The fox I play as, the mate, and three kits), and I’m happy there are foxes because those are my favorite animals! And I am quite fond of the other animals too. I also really like the landscape in the game, very pretty. I would like to see more animation things (the ones labeled “:).”) But I also think that you should be able to search for another player, Incase they leave (or you leave) before you can add them as a friend or report them or something like that. That’s all I have to say right now, once again, great game!

I forgot to mention that sometimes when I’m attacking I accidentally jump so try to make it that you can’t jump while attacking because I have died like that before so it could help a lot :).

K_Penguins , 06/28/2018

Love it but...

I've just begun playing this game and I absolutely love it! The graphics are great (and look better than some pc games I have played), the controls are easy to use, there are lots of fun collectibles, and there’s even a multiplayer option which I love. The game is overall amazing!
There are a few things, though, that i think the developers could improve upon or think about maybe changing.
First, my female wolf character (and the first one i made) runs incredibly slow compared to her mate. She is level 13 or 14 in speed and still seems to run just as slow as when her speed was level one. The male, on the other hand, is about level 5 in speed and runs much faster than the female. I have no idea if this was intentional or not but it is pretty annoying to level up the girl wolf when she is so slow.
Another thing i want to point out is the price of chests. I find that coins take a while to collect and chests are so expensive that it takes me forever to get elixir to level up my animals. Gifts do make it easier though, but it is just a little annoying to open a chest and find no elixir or something that i cant use because i dont have certain animals yet and then have to collect enough coins to open another chest.
Overall this game is amazing and I love it so much! Its also FREE and you can collect everything in the game without making purchases! ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️ 100% recommend trying this game out! (And sorry this review was so long haha)

Great Game but I have issues

I love this game and I have like 80 other friends that play. So the thing is that they all want to join my clan but there’s no more space. I’m also almost out of friend space. I think that there should be more space in for friends and clanmates. Also, my friends and I were thinking that it would be a good improvement if we could trade gems, coins, acorns, and skins. That way if we get a duplicate we can trade it or use it for acorns. My friend has already completed all the achievements and her parents don’t let her buy gems. She thinks that there should be another way to earn gems that way she doesn’t need to buy them. We also don’t think that there should be a limit to how many players can join a game because on electronics day all of us couldn’t play together and we had to play in separate games. One of my friends wanted to see if you could make tournaments to compete in to see who was the fastest or playing mini games in teams. Like Clan Tournaments, where clans can compete to see which clan was the best. And one more thing, the Mystic skins. It’s nearly impossible to get them. I’ve been playing this game for almost a year, and I only have three Mystic skins. Pls make them more accessible bc they’re the only skins that can’t be bought with acorns. Maybe sell them for 1,000 acorns or something. But other than that the game is an incredible simulation. Thank you for your time.
