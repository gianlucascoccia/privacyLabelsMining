Description

Boulder Trainer supports more than 70 Hangboards and is a very useful app for climbing and bouldering. Increase your strength and fitness for climbing and bouldering.

With Boulder Trainer you can create your own workouts which will make your training more efficient.

This app is very flexible, you can even use it for a freeletics exercises.

Easy to setup:
Boulder Trainer comes with more than 70 preset fingerboards. Boulder Trainer will help you to create your own exercises and getting better in climbing and bouldering.

Features:
+ Voice instructions for precise exercise timings
+ You can save your plans online an share them with friends
+ Training reminder: reminds you when it's time for your next training
+ Tips for healthy training execution
+ Manage multiple training plans

Developer Statement:
When I bought my first fingerboard, I was annoyed from the fact that there was no reasonable description for the workout or how to use the board, so I created the Boulder Trainer app, which supports the workout and motivates. Please note that Boulder Trainer is a small private project, which I started by my own and I'm trying to keeping it alive and professional which is not always as easy as it appears.

The Lucface , 08/20/2020

Great for the price

Please add notes. I would like to record where I had to come off the board.
Please add option to see board numbers from menu.
Please add explanations for which exercises help which abilities and a way to tailor an exercise based off of a personal goal assuming we know nothing of hang boarding. Also, maybe a warning before at least the first few exercises about correct body position and finger care.
Also, for some reason even though I set up two days alerts, it’s telling my my next one is 3 days from now.
Just a few things to iron out and improve but the way it counts and speaks aloud is super awesome. Maybe if you enabled an option to have her make announcements at any intervals as in when hanging for a minute or more it would be nice to here 30 or 20 seconds intervals.
Otherwise this app is freaking great. Stoked on it.

bobthebuilder256 , 03/02/2021

Excellent function, minor quirks

The function of the app is great. It helps focus the time on the hang board and it allows great creativity creating a workout on whatever hang board you own. There are a few small quirks that make the user experience a bit awkward. For instance, the inability to change the workout from the main workouts screen is a little awkward. You must go into manage workouts, pick the workout you want, then go back to actually do the workout. There’s also some strange quirks when you do anything other than hanging and pulling. It’d be great if there was some extra function for other workouts like sit-ups and push-ups. The app lets you build them, it just says weird things.

Feolaney , 03/27/2018

Off to a great start

Amazing app considering the alternatives.

I am seeing that although I have selected my board I am seeing plans for all the boards both in presets and shared boards. Kind of hard to find plans for your own board as there is no **filter** feature (even though I should only need plans for the selected board anyways). Would also be great to see the diagrams in the manage plans section where you see the moves on certain holds but without a reference to the area on the board where that hold is.

With a bit of work creating my own plans so they work with my board I can see me using this app more and more.
