Description

British Accent Learn is an educational guide that includes a plenty of British and American accent top tips.
 * Detailed instruction on how to achieve fluent British PRONUNCIATION *
- Get new knowledge about British and American accent and culture;
- Know tips on how to make your speech sound perfect.

* Each article is divided and illustrated what makes instructions clearer *
- Find out the UK & US differences in several word categories;

* Audio clips are read by speakers - listen, repeat and speak perfectly *
- Listen to the clear word pronunciation of the speakers;
- Train your speech repeating after them.

*Improve your vocabulary by passing the tests*
- Do exercises to remember new words

British Accent Learn is an application, that lets you carry your British English to the excellence!
