Description

Manage your F1® team and race in 1v1 challenges in this Official Formula 1® Race Management strategy game. Official 2020 circuits, teams and drivers. Get racing today!

Play NEW F1® Manager for FREE and be the boss! Take control, make the big calls and master the art of race strategy to become the best F1® team in the world. Will you tell your race drivers to risk it all and go for it, or play the long game and sneak victory on the final lap? Faster than ever on-track action with STUNNING graphics. Featuring all the OFFICIAL circuits, teams and drivers from the 2020 FIA Formula One World Championship™, including Lewis Hamilton, Sebastian Vettel, Max Verstappen and Daniel Ricciardo.

Depressed F1 Fan , 03/18/2020

Fun game, but matchmaking system ruins it

This game is actually very fun and enjoyable (at times) to play. It’s like a mix between clash royale and formula one. This is a game I could see myself pouring hours of my time into, except I won’t because of one thing: the matchmaking. Good god, the matchmaking is horrible. I’ve lost about 80 to 90 percent of the races I enter because the other person qualifies super far up the grid and I get stuck down around 20th, and from there I really can’t do anything. It also doesn’t help that the driving mechanics themselves are completely rng dependent. Basically, it’s completely random whether you win or lose a race, and the majority of the time you will lose because the matchmaker puts you up against someone with a much better car and drivers every single time. This is a problem because it costs money to enter races, and you don’t get any money back unless you win. Therefore I find myself playing for a few minutes until I run out of coins after losing four races in a row, then waiting four hours for a free crate just so I can get enough money to enter another couple of races, then I repeat that loop over and over again. It’s a shame, really, because this game has great potential. I really wish I could play it more, but I just can’t because the matchmaking is so terribly designed. If the developers were to fix the matchmaking, me and a lot more people would play the game a whole lot more.

Developer Response ,

Hi. Thank you for your constructive feedback. I understand your frustration with regards to the matchmakingwe look into the combined power level of the players’ equipped drivers and assets, not just by flag coun system. Our development team is always aware of the concerns about the functionality of the matchmaking mechanic and are trying to find ways to rebalance it, so that players such as yourself can enjoy more of F1 Manager. Throughout each update we push, we do everything we can to improve on the matchmaking in any way possible.

One thing to note when it comes to the matchmaking system, the way we match players with others is based on the combined power level of the players’ equipped drivers and assets, as well as the overall flag count earned throughout your time in F1 Manager. Unfortunately, you still may be pitted against players that are spenders, which can make things more difficult for non spenders. However. we have got a fair few non spenders that have ranked rather high on the leaderboard during their time on F1 Manager. I hope this helps. - Hutch
