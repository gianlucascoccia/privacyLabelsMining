Description

Selfie-A-Day is a history of you. Take a selfie every day and play them back in fun, life capturing movies.

Instantly play your selfie movies inside the app and save them directly to your Camera Roll or share to Instagram, Facebook and Twitter.

Already have a bunch of selfies? Easily import them into the app from your Camera Roll.

The Backup feature saves all of your photos to your Camera Roll for safe keeping.

Use our built-in camera grid or ghost image view to line up your face day after day. The longer you Selfie-A-Day the better your results will be!

Set reminders in the app to make sure that you never miss a selfie. Search through dates to easily remember how you looked on that special occasion.

NEW: Easily sync all of your selfies to your Dropbox account as you take them!

NEW: Password protect your Selfie Albums and unlock them with a scan of your fingerprint with Touch ID or using your face with Face ID!

NEW: Access your Selfie Albums quickly using 3D Touch and Siri Shortcuts!

Along with selfies, Selfie-A-Day is perfect for tracking:
- Weight loss transformation
- Your kids growing up
- Daily makeup
- Growing baby bump
- Fun facial hair
- Your pets
- Flipbook and stop motion
- All of life's moments

Almost to a year!

A week and a half more to hit a full year! App is easy to use. I wish it had better head guides, as it’s not easy to align yourself each time without a tripod, but the tools they have work well enough.

PRO TIP: if possible, make sure the lighting is consistent!! A lot of my photos are taken at different times of day, and it’s almost hard to watch, because the light flashing. I’ll have to pay to upgrade, export the photos, and edit them so they’re all similar. I’ve also noticed it looks better with a consistent plain background, rather than different ones each day.

An auto-fix for lighting/saturation/etc could be an interesting app feature idea.

CaptainRichman , 10/06/2019

Excellent

I had another photo a Day app that changed from free to an absurd subscription rate of something like $30/month. I guess they wanted less users. This app does it all even better. Multiple streams, ghostface aligning, convert to video, reminder notifications. It’s a simple concept, thanks for creating this.

I’d like to have it so there’s less clicks required to get to the photo taking. When I open the notification for a daily pic, I’d like it to go straight to that album’s photo taker, rather than open the app, select the album, then select take photo.
