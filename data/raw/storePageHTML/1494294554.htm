Description

Take your running game to the next level as you race against the crowd in this clash to earn first place!

Dash from rooftop to rooftop. Flip, jump and vault over obstacles to set all new records.
Join others and compete to win the daily race for special rewards!
Scale buildings around the city and land tricks to gain extra speed. Leave your opponents to rage as you take home the win!

Test your ultimate skills as your race in famous cities like New York, Paris, Tokyo and more!
Customize your character by earning epic outfits, unique back bling and special effects by winning races!

And the last thing is adding more things to the classic map like cannons to shoot you across the map or monkey bars to climb across and boost your speed and lastly skills that only take like 20 to 30 seconds to charge. One skill for ninja could be teleport forward or have super speed for 15 seconds, but overall this is one of the best games I have played and you don’t have to get all of this stuff but at least add a few new things to the game.
