Description

Use the Givelify mobile giving app for charity donations and giving to your place of worship in as few as three taps.

Donate during nonprofit fundraising events, make church tithes and offerings, or while traveling.

2014 TechPoint Mira Awards Nominee & Finalist, Mobile Technology Innovation. Featured in ChurchMag, Yahoo! Finance, Inside Indiana Business, The Boston Globe.

“Even our online giving doesn’t give the person the opportunity to give in-service or spontaneously.” - Pastor Matthew Ball, Faith Apostolic Church

“Givelify users can be at peace knowing their credit card information is not stored on their phones nor on Givelify’s servers. Information is safely transmitted and protected by the same level of security that protects customer payment information for nine of the top 50 financial institutions.” - TechPoint (http://techpoint.org)

“As a traveling minister, Givelify is my preferred choice whether I’m giving to my home church or any church I’m visiting.” - Bishop Walter S. Thomas, New Psalmist Baptist Church

Features:

- Give to any charity or place of worship in the U.S. in as few as three taps - All your donation records in one place for tax time

- No phone numbers, text message codes, or logins to remember

- Automatically pinpoints your location to identify the fundraiser or worship service you’re attending

- Mark home and favorites for quick one-tap access

- Place of worship or charity receives your donations next business day

Download the Givelify mobile giving app for the easiest, most pleasant way to make the world a better place one donation at a time. Learn more at https://givelify.com

tweenty 20 , 12/20/2020

✝️ Good App but Lacks Features ✝️

I’ve used the recurring feature to pay my tithes for the last few years. The app gets the job done but when compared to other money sending apps it lacks a few features...
1. When using the recurring feature there are no notifications when your credit card expires. Your gifts will just stop being sent.
2. You can’t update the expiration date of your credit card on file, you must add a new card.
3. Your wallet only shows the last 4 digits of your cards on file. If you have multiple cards on file and you add a new card to replace an expired card, you will see 2 cards ending with XXXX (i.e. 1234). No way to determine which card is the old card (so you can delete it) and which card is the new card.
4. For certain functions, the app seems to switch between the app and the website. Each time it does this I get a pop up message asking me if I want to open the page in the Givelify app. This can be annoying at times.
5. The wallet only shows 2 options; Make Preferred and Delete. There is no option to update/add your credit card from the wallet. The only way to update your credit card is when you’re in the process of making a donation.

On the plus side...
I like having a history of my gifts. This is great because some churches don’t provide annual gifting statements if you’re not a member.
The app is especially great for the tech savvy generation who are accustomed to managing their business affairs online.

Developer Response ,

Hello,
Thank you for taking the time to rate our app, these are all great ideas for the app. Our engineers are constantly working on adding features to improve the experience. As far as switching between the app and website, due to Apple terms of service, payment for organizations is not allowed to be processed within-app. Therefore, you are taken to safari to process your donation, and then back to the app for your confirmation. Please let us know if you have any questions at support@givelify.com. ^Miriam
