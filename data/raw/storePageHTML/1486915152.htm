Description

Train your brain and enrich your vocabulary while you stroll through the beautiful and soothing landscapes of this new and mesmerizing game.

An original gameplay provides a relaxing and contemplative experience that will help you improve your knowledge every time you play.

With thousands of levels, Word Lanes adds an extra layer of fun in the excitement of learning. Start your journey now!

Features

- A unique design with beautiful background art
- Enjoy the ride with a soothing soundtrack
- Having trouble finding a word? Our power-ups have you covered
- Follow the clues to find the right answers
- Breeze through thousands of levels (and they'll keep coming).
