Description

Simplify your holiday shopping and celebrate the holidays with friends and family! Easily create sharable wishlists and try out the Secret Santa name generator!

Elfster is the #1 holiday gift list, gift exchange and Secret Santa Generator for holidays and other social events! Christmas shopping season is here! Friends and family can create their wishlists to make Christmas shopping easy this year! Never worry about running out of gift ideas again and simplify your holiday shopping.

Host a Secret Santa gift exchange where everyone knows who wants what! Create wish lists, invite friends, draw names and exchange gifts.

Elfster is a fast, fun Secret Santa Generator and gift exchange planner that's perfect for any holiday celebration – Christmas, Hanukkah, Eid al-Fitr, or just for fun!

Simplify your wish list management and gift shopping. Make Secret Santa gift exchanges fun and easy!

Want to know what gifts are hot this holiday season? Browse wish list and shopping trends to see what people are asking for! Elfster even creates curated gift guides for your next gift ideas.

Easily create and update wish lists anytime, anyplace. Add gifts from your favorite websites, add photo wishes, or shop top stores in your favorite categories.

Make gift shopping for the holidays easy with gift lists for everyone you love! You can even add wishes to your kid’s wish lists so family members can get them that perfect gift.

Join millions of happy gift givers and Secret Santa party lovers using Elfster.
