A new year and a new anniversary. As we bid farewell to what's past and say hello to what's to come, dear onmyoji sama, a brand-new Onmyoji Arena awaits your expedition!

From NetEase's well known Onmyoji series, Onmyoji Arena is true to the original characters while bringing players a brand new MOBA experience. Replacing the conventional paid rune system with a completely free Onmyodo system, the game allows players to fully enjoy fair 5v5 battles. While retaining its delicate Japanese settings, Onmyoji Arena further diversify its art style by expanding with School, Fairytale, Steampunk and other skin series, appealing to a wider range of audience. There's also the addition of Mahjong chess, Shikigami Defense, and 3v3v3 Royal Battle. Download now to have fun in the various game modes!


— Beautiful Shikigami with original voice cast to replicate the Heian world with perfection.
Onmyoji Arena retains the elegant Japanese aesthetics from Onmyoji as well as its stellar Japanese voice cast to give players a more authentic gaming experience. Gorgeous 3D models. Awesome Shikigami ultimates. What are you waiting for? Summon your favorite Shikigami into battle now!


— A fair game with no amulets required. Prove your strategic prowess on the battlefield now!
Onmyoji Arena does away with the amulet system, and all players can now create their own combinations of Onmyoji's abilities (Onmyodo) for free. Based on their strategy, players can freely mix and match the abilities in Onmyoji, adding another variable to the game. Moreover, there is no paid system that affects combat stats. Now all is truly fair in battle and lore!


— Balance of power. Tons of skills. Battle to your heart's content!
Onmyoji Arena carefully evens out the distribution of power in the game, so that each Shikigami is equally strong yet possesses unique charm. Now there's a useful role for everyone. Shikigami have 4 abilities and 2 general spells, allowing for more combinations of abilities and tactics. Unique ability sets bring about greater possibilities. Innovative map designs and the Fog of War system make strategy even more critical, so that players can fully experience this new mobile MOBA. At the same time, the game is revolutionary in that it connects top players from all over the world to test their might.


— Innovative Battle Royale for a three-party showdown
The first ever 3v3v3 battle mode, Battle Royale, incorporates new features like multi-team battles, competition for resources, and fast-paced combat. Players are divided into 3 teams of 3 for an exciting new multi-team experience! In the Battle Royale mode, players will spawn from three locations on the map with the combat zone located in the center. The first team to seal 30 Shikigami shall reign supreme!

Derby Pets , 07/14/2018

Definitely Worth a Download

Experienced moba player here. I’ve been playing moba games on pc and mobile for many years now and I gave this game a try upon seeing its release. My expectations were not to high since the market has gotten over saturated with mediocre mobas. I’ve got to admit that this game was made for experienced players in mind as it lets you skip the tutorial and provides you with all of the summoner spells right from the beginning. That got me into the action right away without having to play through a time consuming tutorial or having to grind in order to unlock summoner spells such as flash, ignite, teleport, etc. This should not scare off new players as there is a tutorial option offered. Another aspect which stood out to me is the utilization of 3 hero abilities plus an ultimate. Or in some cases there are 2 hero abilities and a passive which you can level up throughout the match plus an ultimate. This provides an added element to the game which is not offered by the other top mobas. I have not played long enough to know if the game is balanced or how well he match making matches you up with players of your same skill level but I’d imagine that is a pretty standard necessity for a game in this genre. This game will not overtake my go to mobas on mobile but it will definitely see some play time when I’m looking to switch things up. What are you waiting for? Go download it! See you on the field!

Frookz , 10/30/2019

Amazing MOBA that deserves more attention and love!

I just recently got into mobile MOBA's and I am still jumping around between Mobile Legends, Arena of Valor and this one because I can not quite decide which one I want to fully dedicate my time to. But Onmyoji Arena is the one that I am playing way more frequently than the others. This game is absolutely beautiful in design and I love the different Shikigami (Champions, Heroes, whatever). Gameplay feels very balanced and I am having a blast trying out all the different characters with their unique playstyles. It has a very asian look to it with their heroes ranging from asian folklore like yokai/demons, monsters and deities, which I personally really dig so this MOBA is currently my favorite, just by design alone. The only problem, that also keeps my from fully dedicating myseld to this one, is that it doesn't have a big player base like it's competitors and that makes the waiting time to get into a match longer. I haven't had outrageous waiting times but compared to Mobile Legends or Arena of Valor, it is noticeably longer if it's not at peek hours. Which is why I writing this review here and I've never done that before. I don't want this game to die out and hopefully, more people will come across this gem of a game and make it more lively! Also, it has characters from "Inuyasha" so that is a plus.
