Description

Listen to podcasts how you want, when you want, with Mocast.

Mocast puts podcast junkies in command of their favorite podcasts by allowing them to personalize their queue for the ultimate listening experience. Share, manage, and enjoy podcasts with the style and ease of Mocast’s sleek design. Whether you enjoy Marc Maron, Radiolab, or classic NPR releases, Mocast has a streamlined UI and solid build so you can listen to the podcasts you want faster than ever.

With Mocast you can enjoy your podcasts with special features like:

- Full show notes search to quickly find episodes on any topic.
- Manual playlist queue so you can listen to what you want, in the order you want.
- No nested levels or “Now Playing” screens to slow down getting around in the app.
- Play history that keeps track of all previously listened to episodes.
- Stream podcasts fast! No downloading required.
- A fun disco theme!

Check out Mocast for yourself. Download now and redefine the way you listen to podcasts.
