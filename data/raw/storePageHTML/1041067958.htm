Description

Now with even more features to help you manage your money! New Walmart MoneyCards now get:

Bank transfers – Add money to your card from your existing bank account
Free cash reloads in the app at Walmart stores
Free accounts for family members*
Earn 2% annual interest on savings (APY) on up to $1,000 balance**
Online access and ID and mobile verification required to use and access features. See account agreements for fees, terms and conditions.

*Family members age 13 and older are eligible for Family Accounts. Limit 4 cards per account.

**2% Interest is paid on each enrollment anniversary based on the average daily balance of the prior 365 days, up to a maximum balance of $1,000, if the account is in good standing and has a positive balance. 2% Annual Percentage Yield may change at any time before or after account is opened. Annual Percentage Yields are accurate as of 3/27/20.

View balance and transaction history

Pay bills with online bill pay service
Get paid early with ASAP Direct Deposit™
Direct deposit timing depends on when we get payment instructions from your employer or benefits provider.


For Cardholders with the Walmart MoneyCard with Cash-Back Rewards, you can also:

View cash back rewards balance
Deposit checks using your smartphone
Order paper checks

Good for online tools & more!

This is the only prepaid card that is actually verifiable out of the green dot bank cards through the plaid system and pops up as a checking account. I love this because I was able to connect it to my budgeting apps allowing me to have a better financial picture. Over all good card. Direct deposit really is 2 days faster which was awesome ! I don’t recommend using this card as a main bank account. It’s more like a “allowance card” or cash advance on paycheck kind of card. Solid for every day use tho. Friendly support staff and you get bonuses for shopping at Walmart with it and saving money using the save feature they have. I like the card. I’ve read the people’s comments who rated this card negatively and honestly those are all so biased and situational. I think people should realize it’s a prepaid card. Not a debit card. If you want to bank then open up a bank account! This card is for shopping 😂! Anyway. I’d recommend this card to anyone. Beware of the monthly fee though.
