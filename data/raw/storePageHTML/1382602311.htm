Description

FaceFun - Make selfies more fun!
Cartoon avatar maker, aging time machine, oil painting filter and powerful editor tools...All-in-one selfie photo editor camera.

=========Advanced Features - The New Photography ========
[Cartoon Art Filter] - More Private Customization
You can create your own personal cartoon avatars that look just like you. Share with your friends on your favorite social platforms.

[Make Me Old] - Let Your Camera Show You the Future
Wondering how you would look like as an old man or an old woman? No longer need to do it! Just a few seconds, let you experience the strongest and most realistic AI face aging effects!

[Oil Painting Filter] - The Art Trend of Falling
Oil Effect is an easy-to-use photo editing filter that allows you to turn your photos into realistic oil works with just a few taps of your finger.

=========Professional Tools ============
[Stylish Filters]
Varieties of filters (LOMO, 90s, Art, and others) are ready for free! Including full coverage of daily life scenes, such as selfie time, food time, architecture, landscape and so on.

[Photo Editing]
Professional photo editing function: aperture, cut, rotate, sharpen and other adjustment functions.

[Body Reshape]
You can increase height and slim body with body editor tools. You can also add six-pack abs and muscles to your body.

[Beautify Face]
Adjust facial features: whiten teeth, plump up lips, grow a taller nose bridge, reshape eyebrows for a perfect shape, smooth skin...

[Hairstyles & Hair Color]
It offers you to edit any face with different hairstyles and hair colors on your pictures and make really trendy photos.

Hmm201929 , 09/26/2019

Cute app but has too many bugs

I think this is an app I could, in time, pay for. However, I just downloaded this app and have ran across a big issue. While in selfie mode trying on personalized makeup (not the pre-set filters it gives you), I kept experiencing a bug where after so long, it would erase my current look and I would have to start all over again. I also noticed it did this when i clicked a different mode (filters, etc.) As anyone with experience editing pictures knows, it takes time and effort to curate a specific look and have it look natural, and to have all of that effort erased and to have to start over is frustrating. I also wish the user could create their own makeup such as eyeshadow, lipstick, foundation (which isn’t even an option) but overall this app caught my attention and definitely has potential once the developers fix those problems.
