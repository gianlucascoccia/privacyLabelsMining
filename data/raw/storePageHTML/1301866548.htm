Description

RemoteNOW is the must-to-have assistant for your Hisense 4K Vidaa Smart TV and the latest Hisense 4K Smart TV! Here are the major benefits:
- A smart TV remote substitutes your traditional remote control and supports voice command (only available for some models).
- Browse installed TV apps and easily manage them on your phone
- View broadcast TV channels and program information on your phone
- Show photos and videos from your phone on Hisense TV with simple taps

Supported models:
- 43", 49”, 50”, 55”, 65” H6E Series
- 43”, 55”, 65” H60x0E Series
- 43”, 50”, 55”, 65”, 75” H8E Series (supports voice service)
- 43”, 50”, 55”, 65”, 75” H80x0E Series (supports voice service)
- 55”, 65” H9E Series (supports voice service)
- 55”, 65” H90x0E Series (supports voice service)

Zubry , 04/10/2020

Best ULED TV in it’s Class🏆 & Flawless App

Excellent Picture quality, Best all round in my personal opinion after being an avid Samsung fan my whole life, at least up UNTIL I reluctantly tried the all new ULED 4K HiSense TV and have to say it was the best decision I made and couldn’t expect more from a smart tv in 2020.
Unfortunately the ONLY thing letting this TV down is the built-in App Store it comes with from factory.
The VIDAA-U Smart is sadly anything but smart as 95% of the apps are useless as they are not English applications so besides Netflix, Showmax, DSTV-Now and You Tube the App Store is redundant.
🔌
Connecting successfully to this app was met with some challenges, however I found the problem was NOT the app itself but was from my Router that was found to be restricting a successful pairing of any of my devices to the TV.
Some quick changes to my router on an administrator level and I was able to connect all my devices to the TV seamlessly 🙌🏼
Thank you HiSense for this intuitive application and I look forward to some much needed added features in the not too distant future🏆
