Description

Quickly and easily make any picture talk with Talkr!

Your favorite stuffed animals can come to life to entertain children or read bedtime stories. Pets can now express their love in over a dozen languages. Babies can speak messages for grandma and grandpa. Your favorite cartoon characters can speak with your voice or a disguised version of your voice. Celebrities and politicians can interact in movies that you record and share with friends on social media.

Do your photos have something creative to say? Say it and share it with Talkr!

Your device must support ReplayKit in order to create and share videos. Have a different question? Check the FAQ! https://www.talkrapp.com/faq.html

Developer Response ,

Hi, you can totally make more than one face talk in the same scene. Check out our FAQ: https://www.talkrapp.com/faq.html On the FAQ you’ll see pictures and a step-by-step GIF showing you how to do it. Basically, just import the image into Talkr a second time to set up the second face (do this for every face in the picture). Next, you need another clip in your scene. To add a second clip, select the scene you want to edit, then hit the editor icon to open the clip editor. Hit the plus icon to create a new clip and select the second face. More detail is in the FAQ.
