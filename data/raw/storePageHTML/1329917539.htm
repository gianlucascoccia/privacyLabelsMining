myfirsnamewastaken , 02/25/2019

Good gameplay and sprites

The game is pretty fun, unit pull rates are mostly bad for free players because only the first pull is guaranteed a 5 star, paid pulls always have a guaranteed 5 star. Equipment 10x pulls always have a guaranteed 5 star, and for 20 crystals less than unit pulls. They have a lot of collaboration events, so far there has been Kill la Kill, One Punch Man, and That Time I Got Reincarnated As a Slime. Each collab comes with units and equipment from the series, and a special pull system with a currency you get from selling unwanted units called Alchemy pulls.

They often have giveaways with valuable in game items and crystals, and they had a really good anniversary event that gave away a free unit of your choice from a limited but good pool, along with a choice of 1 5 star and 1 4 star equip items. The developers have had AMAs on Reddit twice so far, it’s a nice bit of communication since most devs are often silent outside of a few community manager posts or posting to their own forums.

Overall I’d say it’s worth playing, but don’t go too hard spending for pulls because the guaranteed 5 star unit can often be a worthless 5 star that isn’t featured on the banner. The gameplay is good, grinding for materials/exp fodder/gold is easy and painless. Unit art is good, it’s an appealing style. I really love the sprites, there are a lot of great ones.

No Chat Mode , 09/25/2020

Wish it was better

Unless you're here for the story, which is alright, I don't recommend this game at all anymore.
Now, I will admit that there is a lot I like about this game. The RPG artstyle, the absolutely beautiful character designs (especially the ones for Roy, Iris, Mira, Celia, and Norn) great music, great voiceovers, mostly good art (except for the ones without mouths... those are quite awful), and very interesting story and characters. Those are at least the best parts of the game to me.
But the rates are horrible, they keep re-running events, they force you to pull your money at them... now, if you're only here for the story, you might as well enjoy this. But if you are here for the gameplay, crossovers and characters, then... I don't recommend to keep playing. I absolutely HATE the summoning system with a burning passion tbh, it takes about a thousand turns for you to get the character you want, unless it's one of the same 5 characters you are forced with while summoning...
Sigh, I honestly wish this game was better handled, and by a company that actually cares about their customers. Because it is such a interesting concept with a lot to love about it other than the ugly gameplay. And if you're all like "Well it's a mobile game duh" I recommend you check out Fire Emblem Heroes or maybe the Japanese servers of either Magia Record or Star Ocean Amnesis. They all are at least enjoyable in both story and gameplay and not as cash-grabby as this overwhelming app.
