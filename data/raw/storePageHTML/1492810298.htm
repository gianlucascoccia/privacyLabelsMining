JayR BEBEH , 05/01/2020

Decent...

So far, the game is pretty cool, adorable and somewhat entertaining...

But... it lacks customization options. The ads are a little too much, maybe once every 5 games would be a little more reasonable and weapon choices are kind of boring...

Another problem this game faces is... there is nothing to spend your money on once you’ve gotten to a certain level and bought all the items possible. The legendary items are behind a wall of ads meaning you have to watch 3 videos to get one item. Which just is not appealing at all. The subscription of $4.99/week doesn’t make sense, what is the point of getting 1,000 coins every day when there is nothing for them to be spent on? Also, character movement could be an option maybe? Moving from cover to cover? Maybe rocket launchers or grenade type weapons to hit multiple targets? Boss battles with damage phases maybe? Just some suggestions. Overall, pretty cool concept, definitely needs improvement though and a little more thought into when the ads should come up and how many of them need to be watched? Also, buying the removal of ads is not worth it, because once you remove ads, you still have to watch ads for items. You still have to watch ads to open gifts and you still have to watch ads to collect money bonus. Quite a bit of illogical problems so far... i would like to support the game more if it were to offer more bang for my buck.
