No interest notifications for 2/5/21

There are things I like about this new update, but things that I don’t care for, as well. I love the exchange feature. It adds a lot to the app. But I don’t like that it came at the expense of transaction records for staking interest. There is no longer a section devoted to it. Granted, we can get the amounts in the notifications drop down menu in the upper right corner, but it gives out less information than the old version did. Also, as of 2/5/21, I didn’t get any notifications for interest payments, so I can’t update my portfolio records. The best way to fix this is to bring back the Transactions tab at the bottom. Just make all of them a little bit smaller to allow another tab, and run it like you used to. Relying on notifications for interest payments just isn’t the most fool-proof method. Other than that, nice app.

Update (2/9/21):

The new update does not address the problem. It told me that there were 12 updates up top, but when I clicked on it, all I got was a blank list with a message “no new notifications”. All past notifications aren’t showing, along with any new notifications. Please just bring back the Transaction section in some form or another. I don’t think that notifications were ever intended to function as a permanent transaction history log.

Not as Advertised

I deposited the required amount in Stablecoin into the wallet for an “automated loan” I started seeing the compounded interest which is good. When I tried to request a loan, it was rejected because I did not have more than 50% of a different currency. Nowhere does their website indicate this. I have spent the last few days combing through their website and am unable to verify what their support staff indicated in their email. Their support staff emailed me stating that a wallet must have more than 50% in another asset in order to comply with Anti Money Laundering Laws. My poor little wallet is valued under $1000 and was flagged for Anti Money Laundering compliance. They have not been able to cite these laws for me and was more than happy to inform me that they are not required to state them in their terms of services. Very sketchy and false advertising. I will continue to play along and continue to paper trail my efforts because I want to believe in this platform. So far they have not delivered on their promises. In addition!
