Description

Now featuring a complete new flexible budgeting system for one-time or ongoing budgeting!

Wallet helps you flexibly plan your budget and track spending, so you stay in control and achieve your future goals. Actively plan and manage your finances, across multiple currencies, banks, and financial institutions. Get and keep full control of your finances.
Wallet is constantly developing its design and functionalities. We help ordinary people lead a richer life. This means that apart from providing new features and improvements on Wallet regularly, our blog and social media profiles are loaded with interesting and practical financial advice.

WHO SHOULD USE WALLET
Chuck your notebooks and spreadsheets, and budget for clear, tangible goals that are easy to track in real time. Wallet by BudgetBakers is not just an expense tracker or a budgeting app. It’s all about getting things under control now, so that you can make wise decisions and plan for the future - a month, a year, or 10 years down the line.
Wallet is designed to help you get your finances under control from day one, giving you ongoing insight into your financial situation, and helping you stay in control for the long term.

PREMIUM FEATURES:
Flexible Budgeting - set one-time or recurring budgets, combining any category you want, and monitor your results on a daily basis.
Automatic Bank Updates - Transactions are automatically and securely synced with your bank and smartly categorized. With 4,000 participating banks worldwide, you’ll save loads of time not having to track every penny.
Insightful reports - Easy-to-understand graphs and financial overviews give you actionable insights about the state of your finances, across bank accounts, credit and debit cards, debts, and cash.

Unlimited accounts - Create as many accounts as you like. Add your bank accounts, vouchers, club cards, money in your pocket and track your expenses based on each account.

the app that i like the best

i've been testing and trying several budget app, but this is the one that i like the best. it's easy to use, has a beautiful interface, it can handle multiple currency, and we can have several account (3 for the free version) which i found very useful when you have more than one card or when you use multiple currency regularly. the feature that i like the most? the statistic. i know the amount i spent for this and that, but when it's shown on a chart, i have a better vision about it.

but there are things that i wish they can do differently:

clickable categories. i hate it when i check my overall spending then when i want to check certain category, i need to go to the overall transaction.
calculator instead of regular number keyboard, so you can count as you enter the data.
i use three different currencies and i'd love it if i can change the exchange rate manually everytime i input the data because, well, it's always change.
it would be better if i can see the total from EACH currency i have instead of all but in the default currency.

Great unless you sync

This app was great for two years with manual input then I decided to sync with my banks/credit cards and that was a terrible idea. Half of them didn’t sync which was fine, I understand syncing doesn’t work perfectly all the time so I reached out to support to ask if there was a master list of connections with issues to see if my accounts were affected so I wouldn’t have to bother support every time there was an issue. This was the second terrible idea. Before I heard back from them I noticed another issue and asked about it. Neither of these questions were answered, ever. I suggested possible reasons for these things happening and it became really clear to me fast they weren’t actually reading the issues. They would give solutions to problems I didn’t have and generally, it turned me off to the whole app. I unsycned all my accounts and got rid of the subscription. Support might just be overworked but this app just isn’t worth it if response time is slow AND my issues get ignored.

ampatrick , 11/06/2019

Best app I’ve found so far

I’ve been looking for an app that works as a ledger, provides stats to help with budgeting and allows me to track weekly/monthly bills. This one does almost everything. I created an account for my credit card with a negative balance. I love how I can select transfer from my checking account to the cc account and only have to make one entry, as it will show the entry on both accounts accurately. I’ve been using this app in combination with BillOrganizer and it helps me tremendously keep up with payments and upcoming payments. Here are a few recommendations that I would love to see with WalletApp:
1. Keep navigation bar at the bottom of window on all screens so you can easily navigate to home
2. Allow editing or creation of new categories. Currently you can only add a sub category to one of the existing categories.
3. Add a field to the account types loans and credit cards for interest rate. It would be cool if the interest could be calculated automatically each month an added as a record for you.
4. For loans and credit card type accounts, add a field to collect payment due date and send notification reminders before and on due date.

Overall, really cool app and is by far the best one of found for keeping up with my account balances.
