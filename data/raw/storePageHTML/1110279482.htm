Description

Vi Trainer delivers on-demand running workouts that are fun, effective, and adapt to you in real-time while you work out. Vi also hosts community races where you can compete against others like you around the world in real-time. Running has never been this fun.

Every run counts: Vi's personal coaching gives you a sense of progress, every run.

Run with the community: Race virtually and get real-time updates for extra motivation.

Earn Achievements: Collect rewards as you become a more consistent runner.

How Vi Works
1. Download "the best running app of 2019" to your phone
2. Take a minute to set your goals with Vi and plan your first run
3. Lace-up, hit the road, and follow Vi's lead as she brings the fun!

Shenxhdoe , 11/24/2019

Going downhill

I’ve had these headphones since they first came out. With all the advertised features it seemed worth the $200 price tag. For the first couple of years it was very useful, it had a step to the beat coaching feature, an effort guide that used live coaching and gave you feedback on your speed and would offer tips on the fly like “pump your arms”, etc. Out of the blue those features just disappeared. When I emailed support I get an “it costs too much to support and develop the items that were the main selling points of the $200 headphones” well that’s basically what they said, you can read it for yourself below. Either way the headphones and app are just that... just another set of headphones with a subpar app. I’m forced to use MapMyFitness to get what I need. Here is the actual email response from “support”. Don’t waste your money on this product.

Unfortunately, the Voice command feature (which includes- Step to the Beat and Effort Guide) will not be coming back, I'm so sorry about that.

It may not seem obvious, but there's a real cost to maintaining features, especially as it relates to multiple different mobile platforms, headphones, and environmental conditions. As an early-stage startup with limited resources, we often have to make difficult decisions about what features we're able to build and maintain over time.

Developer Response ,

Hi there, thanks for sharing your feedback! Our team is always striving to find ways to improve the Vi experience, hence we really appreciate your insight! We see that you're speaking with a member of our Customer Experience team, please continue to work with us there.

Lozo69 , 04/26/2019

Doesn’t match up

I did what was supposed to be an intermediate run with 45 second sprints. The problem is those “sprints” were only 5.6 on the treadmill. No problem, I’ll just start over and kick my experience level up to advanced. When it gave me a new plan there were still no level 3 runs. At one point I did a workout that said it was going to be 3 miles in 30 minutes but the whole time it told me to run at a 4.7 pace. How does the math work out on that? If I stick this plan out I’ll actually be reversing the conditioning I’ve done on my own over the past few weeks and I’m no pro either. Oh, and the app doesn’t let you incorporate the heart rate monitoring native to the Apple Watch. If you want to do that they want you to purchase their proprietary heart rate monitoring headphones. No thanks! In fact, it does not even have an iwatch app. The best thing I can say about the app is the coaching is the best I’ve seen in an app. Having her say my name from time to time was a nice touch. I really want to like this app but I’m still deleting it.
