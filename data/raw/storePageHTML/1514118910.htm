Description

Non-stop, free karaoke! Carry thousands of songs in your pocket. Sing wherever, whenever and enjoy unlimited full-length tracks for free. With the very latest chart hits added daily, discover lyrics to your favourite new songs on Sing King before anywhere else. Join our 8 million strong karaoke community!

Whether you’re a Little Monster or a Belieber, part of the BeyHive or a member of the A.R.M.Y., grab your hairbrush mic and sing along acapella or duet with friends. Turn your bedroom, kitchen or living room into your own karaoke party with this app. Create your own playlists with nothing but your favourite tunes.

Key Features

Sing along to thousands of lyric videos
Unlimited full-length tracks for free
Discover the very latest chart hits. New songs added daily!
Create your own playlists
Enjoy studio standard recordings
Browse pop hits, rock, country and hip hop classics, K-pop tunes and more
Get ad-free playback with our Premium Plan

With over 8 million YouTube subscribers, Sing King is the world’s biggest karaoke community. Be Swift. Have Styles. Go Grande. Head West. And get ready to Sing It Your Way!

Hi! I love the Sing King channel, and the app is nothing less! It’s a great karaoke app that my friends and I bop to all day long, and definitely a downloadable app. My one critique is that it’s quite glitchy, and sometimes the karaoke continues even when I close out the tab. Dana;)Bee , 01/17/2021

So far somewhat disappointed

I downloaded this app to try out after being quite annoyed with the limit to what a person can do on a popular karaoke app (rhymes with mule) without paying a subscription. This may very well be a great app, but so far every song I have searched is not on this app. Couldn’t find Janis Joplin... come on, it’s Janis Joplin! I tried searching by song title and by artist... even tried the song writer for a song I love and all came up empty. I’ll try this app out again in a month or two to see if the songs I like to sing are available, but if not I guess I’ll have to sadly delete... kind of bummed

Developer Response ,

The ability to sing thousands of songs for free is definitely a huge plus point for our Sing King fans and we hide nothing behind a paywall, and we think any company that does is a bit of a mule... We're also disappointed to hear that you can't find your favourite songs to sing along too! We add new tracks every day so if there's anything you feel is missing please email hello@singking.com and we can try to get you and Bobby McGee singing all your favourites soon!
