Description

Experience the future of food delivery, today.

Save time with food, drinks and snacks delivered fast.

Our robots deliver from local restaurants to anywhere you choose.

Download the app and order your first robot delivery in minutes.
1) Discover your restaurant options and take your pick.
2) A human loads a robot with your order.
3) Track the robot in the app, as it travels to you.
4) Unlock the robot with your phone, when it arrives.

Discover the freedom of food delivered anywhere, in minutes.

Linda and Curtiss Petersen

We live on Hashem dr, so we were very close to save Mart, and had been thinking of using the Robot. Well, today was the day and so we set up a short order of a few things we needed. Then using the I-phone, the travel of the Robot was plotted on the map, plus a scale of how far along the order was. I received the order in about 25 minutes, almost exactly what the order said. There were only two items not received, two ice cream products, but in the check out, we were told of this. Other than that, the process was very interesting and exciting. The items were all correct, matching the photos on the order list. Many thanks for this most interesting technology. PS , it is fun to watch the Robots travel up and down the sidewalk all day long. Curtiss and Linda Petersen

There are many reviews about long delivery times or food arriving cold, but I’m guessing most of those occur during peak hours. Every time I’ve ordered from the robots, my order arrives in under 45 minutes and the right temperature as well! There are certainly improvements to be made, but I think they’re headed in the right direction and making lots of people’s lives a lot easier right now.
