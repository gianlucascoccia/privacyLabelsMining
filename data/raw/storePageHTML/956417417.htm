Description

Identify birds and track your sightings with this complete bird watching guide and bird checklist for the United States, Canada and Europe.

Add sightings of any of the world's 10,000 bird species. Get complete bird lists for your location with access to over 2000 scientific bird profiles, 5800 beautiful, full resolution photos and 3700 bird sounds including bird calls and bird songs.

------------------------------------------

FEATURES
■ Add bird sightings, completing lists, and tracking overtime in your life list
■ Designed for easy bird identification by appearance, call / song, and location
■ Use your location to see bird lists near you
■ Informative bird profiles with description and range maps
■ Enjoy beautiful high resolution bird photos
■ Listen to bird calls and sounds
■ Sort or search bird lists, and show or hide rare birds

------------------------------------------

BIRD PROFILES
■ Scientific names, common names and alias's
■ Photos of breeding, non-breeding and other variations
■ Bird sounds including bird calls and bird songs
■ Range maps that show location and movement
■ Written description of appearance including bird identification tips
■ Details on breeding, nesting, lifespan, habitat, diet, and more

INCREDIBLE!!

I don’t normally do ratings but I have to with this app. If you’re looking for a great bird app, look no further, this app is wonderful! I’ve downloaded a number of different bird apps and found they were limited in their information not to mention the amount of birds that were included in their apps. This app has everything. The initial free app has a wide selection of birds and they have a countless amount of additions you can purchase for your particular interest but unlike other bird apps, they don’t try to rake you over the coals, their prices are fair and they give you a lot for your money. I absolutely love it! On a scale of one to five stars, this is a ten!!

Best bird app in the App Store

It’s a comprehensive bird app packed with information and specified by region. It’s also the biggest iPhone repository for bird calls. For future updates, hopefully the developer will continue adding more of the missing bird songs and calls to the database—that would be my only suggestion (not criticism). But IMO, this app is already the most useful online bird resource available, and the convenience of having it through an app makes it even better.
