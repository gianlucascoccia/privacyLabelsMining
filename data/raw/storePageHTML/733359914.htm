Description

Get exclusive coupons and special rewards, earn points towards free pretzels, and place contactless mobile orders for delivery or pickup (curbside pickup where available). The Auntie Anne’s Pretzel Perks app lets you earn points toward free pretzels with every purchase – Oh how sweet (or salty) it is!


REWARDS:
---------
Enjoy a free pretzel after your first purchase (minimum $1)!
Free pretzel on your birthday.
Earn 10 points for every dollar spent.
Free pretzel every 250 points.
Exclusive offers and fun surprises.

Contactless Mobile Ordering

My App now works!!!

My app was previously stuck on the screen of “Ding your rewards are ready! Turn on your notifications...”. I could not find any fix online, so I wrote to Auntie Anne’s customer service. Adjust the text size lower and then app should work. I did exactly this and app now works great!!! They also told me after the app is working correctly, then I can readjust my text size to my original setting. THANK YOU, Auntie Anne Customer Service!!
