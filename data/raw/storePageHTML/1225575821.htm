Description

Plei is a pickup soccer app made by players for players

We give everyone access to play pick up soccer anytime, anywhere they want. Plei makes playing soccer as easy as ordering an Uber. With Plei you are only two clicks away from playing soccer near you

We host games everyday across three regions: Miami, FL, Orlando, FL, and Houston, TX, with many more regions to come in 2021

We organize every single game for you using a combination of automation and a human touch. All you have to do is show up, have fun, and score lots of goals!

Features:
- Join Pick up soccer games in your neighborhood
- Explore out our pickup game feed, where daily soccer games are hosted all throughout your city
- Easily pay for your soccer game. The best part is that there is we never charge you anything if the game does not confirm with enough players.
- Share soccer games and invite friends or add them as guests to your reservation
- Our technology takes care of making sure the game fills up with enough soccer players and that you have an enjoyable experience playing with us
- Communicate easily with us and receive instant feedback to any questions you may have through our Intercom messaging integration, as well as through text messages. This ensures you are always up to date with the status of your game
- View game details (Instructions, soccer facility address, where to park, where to find the other players)
- Find soccer facilities near you

Soccer should be an enjoyable experience for you and your friends, not a hassle. Enjoy Plei, the best pick up soccer app

Great App for soccer lovers

Definitely what I was looking for. Sometimes it turns difficult to get together and build a good amount of friends to play soccer and have fun. For those cases, we now have this app. It presents you available games around the city (in my case Miami) in which you can join, get to know new people, new places to play, but most importantly to enjoy of the beautiful game!
The app is pretty easy to understand and whoever is in charge of the IG page is pretty good with answers when you contact them.
This is my first time using it, and most likely will be one of my most frequent used apps! Thank you Plei! Only suggestion, please open up more games down south, it is a quite drive for me to go up to doral, Brickell, wynwood, etc. I don’t mind driving up there to have fun but it would be nice to have closer games 🤙🏼⚽️

Zafiriou86 , 05/20/2019

Best soccer app

Having lived in many cities over the last 10 years in Europe and the US, I’ve always had trouble finding games to join. Organising games is hard as commitment levels vary and can be expensive. If you’re new to a city, you also don’t always know enough people to create a game. Also going to the gym is boring!
This app eliminated all of that for me. I tentatively joined the group a few months ago and was surprised to find how many great facilities there are around Miami and how easy it was to join a game. Never seen this in any other city I have lived in. I now play 4-5 times a week in various locations but never more than 10 mins from home. The skill level is curated and full of good people from all different backgrounds.
The creators have done an incredible job, absolutely love this app.
