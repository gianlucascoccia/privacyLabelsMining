Description

Everbloom is a creative video editor that transforms your videos and photos into beautiful, professional-looking video edits and stories in seconds.


— Edit your videos and photos with style collections like Retro, Film, or Oats & Honey (by Bryant)
— Add effects, filters, and music to your video stories
— Customize your videos and photos with advanced editing tools
— Share your stories seamlessly to other platforms like Instagram, TikTok, or Snapchat.


Here is a list of some Filters and Effects:
· Oats & Honey
· Bahamas
· Fisheye
· For You Kira
· Prism
· VHS
· Film (8mm)
· Grain
· Bloom / Glow
· Vintage Camera Filters (1997)
· and many more…

Groovy App

Really do enjoying using this app for my BTS videos for my clients and my own creative projects.

It totally gives you so many groovy video filters that flows. The app leveled up recently with ways to creatively edit the videos to your liking. The only thing I will say about the app is with the audio for music could definitely be better. The tunes are out of sight, that’s not the problem. With the audio version on this app, it doesn’t let you really choose your own creative music that you would like to put on the video.

Trust, this app could totally get 5 stars but I had to give it 4 stars cause of the audio other then that it’s truly groovy!
