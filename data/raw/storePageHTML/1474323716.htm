Description

Free daily Christian meditation based on a Bible verse of the day to bring peace and calm to your day.

** Loving a short meditation on a scripture before bed or as I lead into a time of prayer. ** - Jessica B.

Be still. Stress less. With Soulspace, let calm be greater than stress and anxiety by listening to Soulspace's Christian meditations blended with an empathetic voice and original music.

Soulspace offers free daily meditations based on a Bible verse of the day which creates a feel of an audio devotional or bible study, but orchestrated with original music, a soothing and real voice, and original designed artwork. People love how Soulspace calms and prepares them to pray in the morning and at night. For those who are seeking, Soulspace's Christian meditations are open, loving, and inviting to everyone who needs peace.

The Soulspace app also includes Bedtime Bible Stories for better sleep, Meditation and Mindfulness Series for help in situational life events through the lens of Jesus, Original and Calming Christian Music for better sleep, Kids Meditations and Stories, and Themed Meditations for anxiety, purpose, healing, and much more. All available with our Soulspace premium subscription.
