Description

Watch full episodes of your favorite shows with the BET NOW app. Enjoy Tyler Perry shows like The Oval and Sistas, Lena Waithe's show Twenties and many more series, available right in the palm of your hand. You'll also get exclusive content like bonus clips and deleted scenes, and you can stream live events.

Watch full episodes from your favorite series the day after they air. Sign in with your TV or cable provider to unlock even more episodes and exclusive content, including:

• Tyler Perry's The Oval
• Tyler Perry's Sistas
• Twenties
• Boomerang
• Copwatch: America
• American Soul
• NAACP Image Awards
• 2020 BET Awards
• 2020 Hip Hop Awards
• 2020 Soul Train Awards
• Games People Play
• Being Mary Jane
• The Bobby Brown Story
• Real Husbands of Hollywood
• Sunday Best
• Super Bowl Gospel Celebration
• The New Edition Story
• The Rundown with Robin Thede

Signing in with your TV provider is simple: Just tap on Settings, select your provider, and log in with your provider user name and password. For more details about signing in with your TV provider, contact appsupport@BET.com.

APP FEATURES:

• We save your spot, so that you never miss a scene. Can't finish an episode? Continue watching right where you left off. Just tap on the series you were watching, and our video player does the rest.

• Stream Live TV. That's right -- you can even watch BET in real time when you sign in with your TV provider.

• Chromecast support lets you cast your favorite shows to your TV. Just hook up your TV and your Chromecast device.

The BET NOW app requires iOS 10 or later. Full episode streaming is available within the U.S. only. For help, contact appsupport@BET.com.
