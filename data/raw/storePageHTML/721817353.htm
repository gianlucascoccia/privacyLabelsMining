ROYALTON LUXURY RESORTS APP (FREE)

Traveling to one of our Royalton Luxury Resorts in the Caribbean or Mexico? Say ‘hello’ to your new favorite travel companion, the Royalton Luxury Resorts App which is part of the All-In Connectivity™ concept. When you download the Royalton app, you have everything you need, right at your fingertips.


APP FEATURES
Pre-Trip Features
• Register your booking
• Book your trip
• Online room selection
• Vacation countdown
• Local weather

On-Resort Features
• Interactive resort map
• Restaurant menus and hours
• Spa treatment menu and rates
• Book airport transfers (Nexus)
• Book excursions (NexusTours)
• Connect with social media

VALUE ADD-ONS

• Royalton’s App is 100% FREE. The app is fully functional while you are connected to the Wi-Fi at any Royalton Luxury Resorts hotel.
