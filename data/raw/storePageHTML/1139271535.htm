Description

Keep your health plan info right in your pocket
View your claims
Access detailed claims information for you and your dependents from anywhere. Know when Premera receives a claim, when we pay it, and what the provider may bill you.

Show your digital ID card
Forgot your card? We’ve got you covered—right here on your phone. Show it, save it to your photo gallery, or send it to your doctor.

Find care
Locate nearby in-network doctors, hospitals, urgent care facilities, and more. Use the app to request an in-person medical appointment or to find virtual care included in your plan.

Deductible met, or not yet?
Don’t guess. Know how much you have left to pay each plan year before Premera starts paying for certain expenses. View your individual and family deductibles for medical and dental at any time.

Prescriptions at a glance
Use the Medicine Cabinet feature in the app to view a list of your prescriptions, including dosage and pharmacy information.

Note: For 2021 plans, the Premera app supports only those who get coverage through their employer. The Premera app doesn’t currently support information for our Medicare Advantage or Medicare Supplement customers, or for those who bought an individual or family plan through Washington Healthplanfinder.org or HealthCare.gov.
