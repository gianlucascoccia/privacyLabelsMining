Description

Dictionaries now included:

▪ Oxford Advanced Learner's English-Chinese Dictionary *9th edition*
Understand what words mean and how to use them
▪ Oxford Learner's Thesaurus (English-Chinese edition)
Find the right word and know the differences
▪ Oxford Collocations Dictionary (English-Chinese 2nd edition)
Know how words combine and sound more natural
▪ Oxford Business English Dictionary (English-Chinese 2nd edition)
All the help you need to do business in English
▪ Oxford Idioms Dictionary (English-Chinese 2nd edition)
Helping you make light work of idioms
▪ Oxford Phrasal Verbs Dictionary (English-Chinese 2nd edition)
All the help you need to get plugged in to phrasal verbs
▪ Oxford American Dictionary (English-Chinese edition)
Learn to speak and write American English

**With quarterly new words update exclusively available for subscribers**

***Features***
▪ Integrated search - find words, phrases and examples in multiple dictionaries
▪ Real-voice audio for words and examples, British and American English both supported (available in Advanced Learner's Dictionary)
▪ Supports both traditional and simplified Chinese characters
▪ Tap any word, label or cross reference to look them up instantly
▪ Use Quick Access to jump to the different parts of an entry
▪ Use My View to choose how much information appears in the dictionary entry
▪ Add bookmarks and notes, set Learning Status, export your own word lists and notes
▪ No internet connection required for dictionary search (except for audio playback of example sentences and geographical names)
