Description

The Official Manchester City app, bringing you all the latest City news and video combined with an all new Matchday Centre and Cityzens experience.

Features:

- Stay up to date with latest City news from across all of our teams: Men's, Women's, EDS & Academy
- Offers the latest news and video in (Spanish/Indo/ Insert your own lang) language within our dedicated For You categories
- Choose which teams to follow. Personalise your homescreen to see the news & video you're interested in
- CityTV: Watch Match Highlights, Tunnel Cam, Inside City and Inside Training...plus lots more from CityTV
- Matchday Live: Never miss a kick with our immersive Matchday Centre with Line-ups, live text updates, audio commentary, match stats and more
- Cityzens : Log in or join Cityzens for FREE to access exclusive competitions and rewards just for you.
- Man City Fixtures for all of our City teams
- Allow Push notifications to stay up to date with the latest City news and never miss a kick on Matchday
