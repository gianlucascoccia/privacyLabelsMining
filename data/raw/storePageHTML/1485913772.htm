Description

The Wondery App is the best place to quickly find and listen to Wondery’s immersive podcasts.

Enjoy our newest shows - The Apology Line, Business Movers, Catfish, Death of a Starlet, Little Stories Everywhere, Jacked and Do No Harm!

For True Crime fans, we have classics such as The Shrink Next Door and The Dating Game Killer with new bingeable True Crime shows coming regularly.

We have every season of our award-winning Business and History shows, such as American Scandal and Business Wars.

And we have unique stories to keep you engaged, like inspiring interviews with Say Yes! with Carla Hall, friendship and fun on Even the Rich, and much more!

This is also where you can find our Premium Subscription service, Wondery+. Get early access to select podcasts, exclusives, and ad free listening of every episode. Enjoy the latest Wondery+ Exclusives including:
*The exclusive season of Business Wars: SAT vs. ACT, where host, David Brown, covers these tests, originally designed to help standardize the college application process, but which are now fraught with accusations of discrimination, inequality, and even fraud.
*Imagined Sleep, with immersive episodic journeys through soundscapes that will lead you into a restful sleep.
*Mediation, a new exclusive featuring 6 unique 10-minute meditations designed to enhance each part of your day.

The Wondery app is the best place to find and listen to Wondery shows:
*A home screen that’s personalized based on your preferences, including “Continue Listening”, which surfaces the shows and episodes you’re in the middle of, and “New Episodes from My Shows”, which highlights the latest episode of shows you’ve subscribed to
*Binge away with binge-mode! Episodes from the same series play back in order
*Browse shows with multiple seasons (there are more than 40 seasons of Business Wars!) easily by tapping on the season title on the show page
*Curated collections of recommended shows, seasons and episodes from across our catalog, including True Crime series, Daily shows, the Best Seasons of Business Wars and more
*Adjust playback speed, share episodes and set a sleep timer right from the audio player
*Download episodes for offline listening. Add a series to “My Shows” to get new episodes downloaded automatically to your device
*Manage your downloads in “My Library"
*Upgrade to Wondery+ right from the app by tapping any early access or exclusive episodes. Wondery+ episodes have a “W+” next to them
*Prefer to listen on a computer or in Apple Podcasts? Your Wondery+ subscription also unlocks private RSS feeds. Log into your Wondery+ account at wonderyplus.com/login to get feeds for shows and add them to other apps

The Wondery app and the majority of the shows in it are free and ad-supported. To listen ad free, get exclusives, early access and binges, join Wondery+ right in the app.

The free trial offer for Wondery+ is limited to new subscribers only. Cmcgary , 07/22/2020

Really want to love it

As many others have said, the content is excellent, but the app itself needs a lot of work. Episodes often take a very long time to load once you press play, once it starts playing often the buttons to pause, ff, and rewind don’t work or have a horrible lag. I have to restart the app entirely to try to get from the currently playing episode screen back to the library if I’m not into a particular episode because the down arrow doesn’t work at all. Listening to only a few episodes with no other apps open or in the background the other day somehow drained my entire phone battery. Also nearly impossible to get it to work with my apple carplay. I’m sure that improvements will come but not sure I want to pay to subscribe until the app is useable.

Robert White 74 , 08/31/2020

Great content, terrible app

I want to like this app. I really do. But it’s a hot mess! Several issues plague this app that don’t effect other podcast players. Here’s a few;

You’re listening to a podcast & you get a call, the app doesn’t pause & resume after the call. Oh no. It resets itself & you have to reload it from the start. If you’re lucky the show you were listening to held its place. Pause a show to look up something? Full reset. Stay paused for too long? Yep full reset. Here’s another issue. The fast forward & rewind buttons don’t work when playing. You can literally wind up anywhere in a shows timeline if you make the mistake of touching those buttons. The content gets scattered all over the home screen of the app with no sense of organization. It won’t save a playlist of shows you’re listening to. No notifications of new episodes so you have to keep checking. I could go on but it’s in several reviews already. Just read up. Bottom line... I got the subscriber feed from the website & just downloaded them from there in another player. I love the content. The shows are fantastic. I’m a subscriber. But this app isn’t worth the trouble.

Fix this Wondery!!!

Hi Robert - Thanks again for the feedback. We've made several improvements to the app to address the performance and stability bugs you mentioned. We're also actively working on additional improvements to continue making the Wondery app the best place to enjoy Wondery+. We truly value your feedback - if you have any additional suggestions or feedback, drop us a note at support@wondery.com and we'll dig into it right away. Happy listening!

WeAreOnePeople , 10/12/2020

Echoing Robert Whites review

I was prepped to pay for the content. I really enjoy some of their podcasts. I got so frustrated during my free trial that I cancelled early. Podcasts starting over after anything happens in iOS was already enough to put me over the top. But another super irritating thing is I listen at 1.5x most the time and that would reset and you’d have to cycle back through the speeds to get it fixed but if you do that too fast it wouldn’t pick it up right. It’s like the state management in this app is completely an afterthought. I’m usually pretty forgiving as I myself am a software developer but it’s like there was zero testing done on the features that exist.

I think the primary reason I ended up dropping the subscription and why I’m writing this review is because most people listen to podcasts while driving and this app effectively makes it unsafe to use unless you’re okay listening to the same episode repeatedly and slowly due to all the bugs.

I’m honestly stunned the app has over 4 stars in aggregate. It currently fails as a podcast player which is a shame because the content is excellent.

Developer Response ,

Hi WeAreOnePeople - thanks for the feedback. We've fixed the issues you've described, including preserving speed settings across sessions and across multiple episodes in the same listening session. We're also hard are work on many other performance and functional improvements. If you have tried the app again and have more feedback, we'd love to hear about it - you can drop us a line anytime at support@wondery.com and we'll dig in ASAP.
