Description

London’s world famous destination sneaker store, for over 15 years Footpatrol have been dedicated to globally supplying footwear from the likes of Nike, adidas, Jordan, Converse, Vans, Reebok, PUMA, New Balance, Y-3 & many more. Our app allows you to take Footpatrol with you wherever you go, shopping every single product available on footpatrol.com.

Key features include:

• Free UK delivery to over 500 locations
• Get hold of the latest sneaker releases from Nike Air Max, Nike VaporMax, Nike Air Max
97, adidas UltraBoost, adidas NMD, adidas Yeezy, Jordan launches & more
• Easily search, browse and shop hundreds of products
• Quick Buy feature to help you get what you want, faster, when it matters
• Save your favourite products to your personal FP wish list
• Read the Footpatrol blog about the latest sneaker launch with one click
• Pay using multiple options such as PayPal, MasterCard, Visa & more
• Exceptional quality imagery as well as high resolution zoom
• Share products via WhatsApp, iMessage, Facebook, Twitter, WeChat and email
• Scan functionality – scan product barcodes in store to find your size trainers online
