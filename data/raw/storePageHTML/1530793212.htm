Description

*Super Fun Salon Game for Girls*

Super trendy nail game with super cool and trendy nail polish, nail gel, acrylics and more!

Welcome to Beauty Girl Nail Salon! Hand nail design, nail polish and many accessories… - we have it all!
Every girl can take care of her hands and make herself a beautiful manicure here.

We provide 4 different nail styles for you. Summer, Cupcake, Galaxy, Flower… Start the nail design at your casual time now! Also, you can try our Special Nail Challenges! Come on, show your great artistic skills in Beauty Girl Nail Salon!

Features:
Game for all girls! Change your skin color freely!
Choose Casual or Special Style to play!
Large selection of nail shapes, colors, patterns.
Many modern jewelry and stylish rings are waiting for you!
Choose a background for your photo.

How to play:
Choose a game type and theme to start!
Create your own manicure designs!
Having fun from unlimited combinations of nail shapes, colors and patterns!
Decorate your fingers with glamorous rings.
Record your nail design and share it with your friends.
