Description

We’re launching Speech Blubs 2, a new update of the #1 speech therapy app that includes an engaging new design, more face filters, & a helpful parent’s section.

Speech Blubs 2 is an update of its highly-rated (4.6 rating on Apple Store) voice-controlled speech therapy app with 3 M+ downloads that helps kids learn 1st sounds, words, & sentences. This version’s new look, helpful parent’s section, new saveable face filters, & access to the reading app READING BLUBS are a “must-have” for late-talkers, kids with ADHD, Apraxia, Autism, and Down syndrome.

WHAT DOES SPEECH BLUBS 2 OFFER?

**EVERYTHING FEATURED IN** Thrive Magazine, Autism Parenting, Brisbane Kids, Speech Chick Therapy, Beautiful Speech Life, and The Speech Teacher. Awarded the Social Impact Award and supported by the Facebook Start Program, and winner of the “Safe for Children” certificate, the Mom’s Choice Award, the Parent’s Picks 2020 Award Winner, and techozens.com’s “Best and Trusted Speech Therapy App for Kids.

WHY LEARN WITH SPEECH BLUBS 2?
Our app was created with speech therapists, teachers, & parents, and uses video modeling for effective speech development. Our apps have zero ads and no flashy features, thus providing a safe and non-intrusive learning experience. We use ABA therapy-inspired positive reinforcement that rewards kids with fun games and stickers for trying. Having the app at your fingertips makes it easy to practice in a routine that suits you, and helps avoid stressful disruptions that can upset your child.
Our apps use multiple child and platform support, so with one sign-in ALL of your kids can use the app, no matter if it's on your device, a partner’s, or grandparents’. One sign-in also includes our new reading app READING BLUBS – designed with over 200 teachers and literacy experts.
You can now get access to language versions in US and UK English, Spanish, French, & (Brazilian) Portuguese.

KEY FEATURES:
Parents’ Academy:
A newly-added parent section provides access to an ever-growing content library of 350+ articles on parenting, homeschooling, education, speech therapy, and more. All written by 20+ speech and teaching professionals. Also, check out our practical video tips!
Kids’ area:
There are over 1500+ exercises, activities, videos, mini-games, and dozens of NEW face filters. We have lions, firefighters, monkeys, giraffes, hats, glasses, and more. Also, the app’s look and feel is even more child-friendly and engaging. There are some new animal characters to guide and motivate kids through exercises to achieve learning goals and make learning fun!
Choose from 28 fun sections: Early Sounds, When I Grow Up, Get into Shapes, Living Colors, Yummy Time, This Is My Body, Mouth Gym, Animal Kingdom, Ride Your Wheels, Sing-Along, and many more!

SCIENTIFICALLY-PROVEN LEARNING TECHNIQUES
Speech Blubs 2 uses video modeling for immersive learning, where children observe their peers as “teachers'' on video. When children watch their peers in real-time, their MIRROR NEURONS fire up! This scientifically proven and effective approach was confirmed in a UCLA study published by the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association and showed that kids learn faster and better when learning from other kids.

AshleySGW , 01/29/2021

Son enjoys the app

My son enjoys playing speech blubs (especially the games!). He has been slow to speak but speech blubs has kept him both highly entertained and engaged. He has made visible improvement watching it and actually repeats the kids with new words! It’s always a joy to find photos of him with the funny faces on my phone, too, they are an unexpected treat.

He has been using the app on and off for about a year and one thing we did notice originally was the “I am scared” section in the feelings section that we don’t love and actually wish we could restrict because not only has it scared the heck out of him (like drop the phone and run) but even after not seeing that module for months it has affected him and he refuses to go anywhere near a local museum here which looks like the scary castle that has the ghost come out in the module. I understand the developers are trying to get them to feel the feelings but that one causes us some issues we did not anticipate and we wish we could change because for our family exposing him to ghosts and scary growling monsters aren’t necessary, especially at this age. We overall enjoy the app and see progress in him and appreciate that but for this reason are now hesitant to let him use it and wish there was more parental control.

BS1222! , 01/29/2021

Toddler with speech delay LOVES THIS APP!

My son is 2 years old with a moderate speech delay. He LOVES his “Blubs” app! The app itself is very kid friendly & is filled with appealing colors/pictures/videos/songs! My son does not often like to imitate me when practicing words, however, he enjoys imitates the children in the videos on this app. I love the constant repetition of the words and that all of the words presented are words he has had real life experiences with (for example: apple, cow, bus, etc). I also love that aside from just practicing the words presented, they also talk a little bit more in depth about the word, typically with a video or animated photo expanding my son’s knowledge and understanding. It truly keeps his attention and makes learning new words fun, engaging, and unique. I highly suggest downloading this app if your little one needs some extra practice with speech/language or even if you are just looking for ways to enhance your child’s learning of a variety of concepts. We are big Speech Blubs fans!!!
