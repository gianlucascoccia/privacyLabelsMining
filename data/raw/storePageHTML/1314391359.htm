Lootcart , 08/19/2020

Good game but you have to make many changes

This is a great game. It’s a fun little combination of Overwatch and Clash Royale which I appreciate. All I ask is that you make some quality of life changes. For examples, the bots (other cards you’re not currently playing) are extremely stupid. Please make them smarter, wildcards especially, they don’t really have a meaningful role unless you play them. Balancing, some of the characters in this game are extremely OP and are just a hassle to go against, for example, Sogeki-Chan, every time I hop into a game with her on the opposing team, the game is always telling me I’m being targeted even though nothing actually happens to me. You could make a timer for when her ability goes off or make it easier to doge her attack just balance the characters please. Offline mode, I have been kicked out of games so many times because of bad server connection, could you make it so when this happens you loose less trophies or if the server detects that you’re lagging you go against a bot. Finally, take your time to add gun skins and better graphics and higher server FPS, these are changes everyone wants. So please slow down on the characters and make these changes so people can enjoy the game better. Keep up the good work BIBI team. (Also can you make a chest system like Brawl Star’s, the more cards you get that you already have the higher chance of you getting a card you don’t have). Thanks or everything BIBI.

Sansy Boi , 12/19/2020

Great game, but a few suggestions

I personally love FRAG, it’s really good, and not that needy ( you can probably play it on an iPhone 6 ) and overall generally fun. But here are a few suggestions. A team mode. Basically, not everyone might like always playing 1v1, I am not a fan of it myself. So there should be a Team Modes, where you choose one of the characters in your deck to play as and then it’s a 5v5. Or maybe a 2v2 mode, too, so you can get a friend and then demolish games without randoms, but just the two of you. You could also spice the team modes up with a Limited Time mode which appears every now and then, in which a character from a teammates’ ( and your ) deck gets picked randomly to form a Team Deck, from which every player on your team picks a character. This next one isn’t a suggestion ( sort of ), but don’t change the artstyle. I love it when games stick to one unique artstyle, and I like the one that FRAG has now, so don’t change it. Lastly, you could add a PC version like with some games like Raid Shadow Legends. It would be really cool to not have to download an emulator to play it on PC, but rather have a separate version for PC and for Mobile ( having cross-play is up to you, but I’d recommend it, if possible ). That’s all, sorry if the review is kinda long. But either way, I really love FRAG because it’s amazing as it is, but adding the suggestions above would make it better, and maybe attract more people, too.
