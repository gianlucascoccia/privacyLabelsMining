Fhfhgc , 04/22/2019

Love this app but would love to have additional functionalities

Don’t get me wrong, I’ve only used this app for less than a week and I’m already completely obsessed with it. I love singing so I’m very impressed with the app overall. But there’s just a few functionality improvement I’m hoping to see in the future for this app.

1. Functionality for singing with other users for duets and such
2. App Translation to English, I don’t have an issue reading but I want to recommend it to so many of my friends but many were born in the US so they can speak but cannot read Chinese so that is a little issue
3. A report button for instrumental tracks that are bad or incorrect or have the wrong lyrics. I’ve came across multiple songs that had that issue but can’t do anything about reporting it
4. Letting us import/save lyrics to the instrumentals we import in ourselves
5. Letting us play and listen to the instrumental first BEFORE I download it since there are bad files/incorrect instrumentals
6. Letting us just play the instrumental we downloaded without having to record it for if we just want to listen to it first and be able to scroll to parts of the instrumentals

That’s all I have for now after a little under a week of usage. Will come back and update this review if I have more comments to make or when you guys are able to implement any new functionalities. But AMAZING JOB to the DEV team that created this app! 👏🏻
