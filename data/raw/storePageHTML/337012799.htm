Description

Managing your finances on the go just got easier with Zions Bank Mobile Banking. Whether you need to check account balances, transfer money between eligible accounts, pay a bill, deposit a check, or find a Zions Bank branch or ATM, Zions Bank Mobile Banking has you covered.

     •  View your account balances and transaction history
     •  Transfer money between eligible accounts
     •  Pay bills to existing payees
     •  Deposit checks using our mobile Check Deposit service*
• Send, request, and receive money using Zelle®
     •  Search for Zions Bank branches and ATM locations

To use Zions Bank Mobile Banking, you must:

     •  Be a client of Zions Bank with a deposit, loan, line of credit, or credit card account
     •  Be a registered Zions Bank Online Banking customer
     •  Have a compatible mobile device and U.S. phone number
     •  Be connected to WiFi or a mobile internet data service**

Have a comment or question? Email our team directly at appstorefeedback@zionsbancorp.com

*Check Deposit service requires a rear facing, auto-focus camera.
**Message and data rates may apply. Please check with your carrier for details.

Must have a bank account in the U.S. to use Zelle®. Transactions typically occur in minutes when the recipient’s email address or U.S. mobile number is already enrolled in Zelle. See your Zelle Payment Service Agreement for more details. Zelle is intended for sending money to family, friends and people you know and trust. Neither Zions Bank, a division of Zions Bancorporation, N.A., nor Zelle offer a protection program for any authorized purchase made with Zelle.

Zelle and Zelle related marks are wholly owned by Early Warning Services, LLC. And are used herein under license.

Great app, annoying glitches

The application has really awesome features and takes care of all my needs. I am able to easily and proactively monitor and manage my accounts exactly as I prefer. However, it kicks me out often. It also never recognizes my thumbprint and will not save settings to unlock/open/log in. I have no trouble with this on other apps on my phone. Logging in is my biggest issue with the app because it glitches out and will not consistently let me into my accounts.

The app is easy to navigate. The app is accurate with my bank statement and account information. It always saved me a trip to the bank- easy to manage money with my phone.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for your feedback. Please try to uninstall and reinstall our app from the App Store. After doing that, check the 'Settings' page again to enable Touch ID. If you continue to have issues, please email us at appstorefeedback@zionsbank.com and we will contact you to further troubleshoot.

Levi Abbott , 12/14/2017

Good, stable full function bank app.

This app is for Zions Bank account holders. Instantly see your account balance, deposit checks (certain limitations and restrictions apply) with your camera, pay bills and make transfers between Zions internal bank accounts and even with external accounts.
I have been a Zions customer for more than fifteen years. It has been such a terrific bank that even though I moved east (for my job) and the nearest bank location is now more than 800 miles away I have continued banking with Zions. This app is almost like having a local bank branch in my pocket.
Every function simply works and very easy to use, with functions grouped intuitively. However, my only dislike is that some of the navigation labels leave a little to be desired. Once you have used the app a little bit navigating around inside the app is easy and natural. As I said using a function in the app is easy, finding the functionality in the app can be difficult until you have used the app a few times and become familiar with it.
