Description

The best novelas, like Vencer el desamor, news with Jorge Ramos, Despierta America, soccer from Liga Mx and UEFA, and your favorite realites and more.

THE BEST IN ENTERTAINMENT, NEWS AND SPORTS IN SPANISH
All the live programming of Univision and UniMás and more live and on demand at your fingertips. You can access the Univision app with most TV services, including Comcast Xfinity, DirecTV, Dish, Spectrum, AT&T, Verizon FIOS, and others. If you can't find your cable provider, come back soon as we are constantly adding new providers.

Watch your way
• ​ If you missed your favorite shows on Univision, Unimás, Galavisión and TUDN, now you can watch them on demand, one day after they are broadcast.

• Watch all the Univision channels already included in your cable package, including Univision, UniMás, TUDN, and Galavisión.

• You can watch live and on demand at home or on your laptops.

• With Conecta, your favorite shows have polls every night and prizes for you every week. Interact live and participate in all the activities that Conecta has for you.

• Log in with your TV subscription to get access to the entire catalog of on demand and live content.

• Use any device, including TV or mobile, at home, with Roku, Chromecast, Apple TV and Android TV

• Watch live programming from up to three days ago using the automatic DVR feature.

• Don't you have television service? Try a subscription to the Univision Now app.
