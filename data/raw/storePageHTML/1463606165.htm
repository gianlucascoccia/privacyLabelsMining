Description

Magisterapp: Over 30 million apps downloaded! Thank you !

A mum, a famous trapeze artist, has managed to bring her family back together again to remember the long-gone 1985, when she was working at the circus together with her 8 children.
We’re only missing a family picture, but there’s a problem… now they are all very different!

The clown is now a doctor…
The fortune teller is now a weather woman…
The trapeze artist with her elegance now works in a beauty salon…
The lion tamer uses her talents to handle a class of unruly kids, and is now a teacher…
The knife thrower, so as not to lose his skills, has become a butcher…

The illusionist is in a bit of a complicated situation, and has become a thief!

Before we take the picture, we have to get them ready, fix their hair, dress them up and maybe put on a bit of make-up.

So, let’s go to the dressing room to transform our characters, we’ve got everything we need for make-up and hair styles, just like in a beauty salon.

- Scissors to cut the hair
- Lotion to make it grow
- GLITTER for the hair
- Razor for saving
- Electric razor
- Lotion for dreadlocks
- Lotion for braids
- Curling tongs
- Hair straightener
- Hair dye of any color
- Tools to cut and style a mustache
- Colored lipstick
- Face powder
- Shampoo
- Hair drier and towel
- Loads of clothes to wear
- Loads of accessories to try
- Magic hats
- Comb the animals too: Bear, Lama, Lion and Rabbit!

Have fun and recreate all the characters of the circus or just have fun changing them however you want. A game for all the family!
Target: 3-99 years

Characteristics:

* Original Gypsy music
* Simulated hair in realistic way
* 8 human characters
* 4 animals
* Use of ARKit to move the characters (iPhone X,Xs,Xr and new iPad Pro)
* 100+ accessories and clothes
* 30 tools for hair
* NO advertising (like in all games by MagisterApp)
* Also suited for pre-school children
* Stimulates creativity both in children and adults

In the free version you can find a character to have fun with, and if you want to complete the whole family you can buy more characters and more accessories.
Have fun!
