Description

【微信读书】是基于微信关系链的官方阅读应用，同时支持iPhone和iPad两大终端平台。
在提供极致阅读体验的同时，为用户推荐合适的书籍，并可查看微信好友的读书动态、与好友讨论正在阅读的书籍等。

【精心打磨的阅读体验】
EPUB的精致书籍排版，你还可以随心个性化你的专属阅读风格

【和好友发现优质好书】
帮你发现下一本适合你的好书。书海茫茫，不妨让微信好友来帮你完成筛选，快速找到优质好书。

【和好友讨论交流碰撞】
在阅读中与好友的想法邂逅，跟好友交流你的阅读感想，碰撞出更多火花。这一次，让阅读不再孤单。

【和好友比拼阅读时长】
严肃的阅读游戏，让你轻松了解自己的阅读时长，在阅读中不仅收获知识，更收获与好友比拼的成就感。

katherinezyxy , 01/31/2021

Best reading app on the market

For a reading app associated with a social media network, it’s understandable that it has a lot of social media features and ads. I didn’t find this particularly annoying since you can turn off most of the notifications and the article recommendations. The reading features though, are so incredibly well designed. My biggest praise is for the endnotes feature. Every other reading app I’ve used has all the endnotes at the end of the chapter or the book like a physical book; however, it is not easy to flip through all the pages to the end of the chapters and back in an ebook as compared to a physical one. This app allows users to tap on the in-text references and directly read the endnotes in a pop up window. Absolutely love this feature. This app also has a great selection of books and even some English books as well. Highly recommended for Chinese readers.

Great reading app

This is the best reading app I have ever used. It is attached with WeChat so we could see other friends’ readings and share our point of views. It is kind of cool to have the reading rewards points and buy books with the points!

I also enjoyed reading with this app in three ways. First, it provides different background colors for your preferences. Second, it encourages you to build up your reading habits. Third, books are up to date and well organized.

Highly recommended for reading lovers!

End not end , 07/13/2018

Ridiculously censored books!

I’ve been using it for long time and had enjoyed reading and listening while driving. But with the latest few updates, books had gone through heavy censorship. Characters are replaced by *s for some ridiculous reasons. For example, in Frank Kafka’s Castle(Chinese version), one sentence was read like the following “眼睛眉**刻不停地看着发生的一切”. It’s easy for Chinese people to understand what’s going on. The first character marked by * is “毛”, together with the character ahead it, “眉毛” means eyebrows and the second is “片”，together with the next character ”片刻” means moment．This should be the way and the only way to read it. However if a dumb people just falls in love only with these two censored characters, their meaning is adult video. To eliminate such a ridiculous possibility they have to be sacrificed. I’m not sure if this is violation of copyright but definitely a shame to Tecent.
