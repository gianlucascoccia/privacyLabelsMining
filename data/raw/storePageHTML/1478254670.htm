Description

Welcome to Baby Maker!
You can make one cool & cute future baby with our app. Hope you enjoy all our features.

SEE YOUR FUTURE BABY:
Baby Maker uses your photos' facial characteristics to create a cute baby. Try it with your friend or lover now! Come to select your unique two photos and see magic happen!

GO BACK TO BABY TIME:
Everyone grows up from a child, but not everyone can remember the way he was when he was a child or not satisfied with the way he was.
Now Baby Time can help you with this situation! Through advanced AI technology, you can experience the novelty of childhood. You will love this feature, I'm sure.

WHAT'S MORE:
You can use your favorite pictures of you and your friends, celebrities, or even some crazier combinations! With Baby Maker, the baby you make is at YOUR command. You can even combine wacky, hilarious photos to create funny baby results!
Baby Maker provides the best and most accurate results when used with a front-facing and well-lit photo.
