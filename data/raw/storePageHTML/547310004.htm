Description

Armed Forces Bank’s Digital Banking app helps you do more on your smartphone or tablet. Deposit checks, transfer funds, check balances, receive alerts, view activity, and find ATMs or branches. To get started, download and launch the app. Then create a login ID and password. It’s that simple.

Free Mobile Deposits1
Make check deposits with your phone. View deposited checks history.

Login Improvements
New Touch ID and facial recognition access.
Additional passcode security and password reset capability

Monitor Your Account
See account balances and transaction history.

New Ways to Pay
Make fast, easy payments and transfers from internal accounts or to another bank account you own.
Send and receive money from people you trust

Mobile Banking Alerts2
Account Alerts: Lets you know when you have gone above or below your pre-selected amount.
Transaction Alerts: Notifies you when specific transaction activity occurs on your account.
Security Alerts: Receive notification when your account password is changed or your account is locked.
Alert History: See your alert history for the past 90 days.

Pay Bills, Send Wires, ACH, and Make Loan Payments3

Secure Messaging lets you speak directly with a bank associate about your account within the app.

Find Branch Locations and ATMs. Find the nearest Armed Forces Bank banking center locations & ATMs.

Safe and Secure
Armed Forces Bank is committed to your security and protecting the privacy of your financial information. We use state of the art technology to protect unauthorized access to your account, your wireless communication is securely encrypted, and your personal information is not stored on your phone.
