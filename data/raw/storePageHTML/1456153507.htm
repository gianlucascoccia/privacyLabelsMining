Description

• Take Apple Music to the next level by combining your favourite playlists into automatically updating mega-mixes. Simply tap the playlists you want to include (or exclude). Duplicate songs are removed automatically.

• Use rules to quickly filter out unwanted tracks (like sleep sounds).

• Combine rules to create interesting dynamic mixes, like a list of all your "loved" songs. Or songs you've never played before. Or "songs rated 4 stars and higher that haven't been played in the last 2 months." The only limit is your imagination.

• Easily navigate large playlists with advanced features: search for songs within lists, group songs by artist/album/etc, and sort lists 14 different ways.

• Works with your Apple Music automatically. Begin playback in Miximum and continue seamlessly in Music.app. Sync mixes across your devices. Completely ad-free.

justaBill , 07/09/2019

Best way to make smart playlists

There are really two apps that can make customized playlists from your Apple Music or iCloud Music Match Library: Miximum and SmartPlaylist. Miximum has a nicer interface. SmartPlaylist has more options but Miximum has the important ones and you’ll find the developer happy to add more filters if you can make a good case for one. There is one factor that really puts Miximum over the top between the two. By leveraging already existing playlists, you can make basic smart playlists back in iTunes on your computer and use Miximum to build the combinations you want while on the go. iTunes on your computer also has the option to check or uncheck songs which can be part of your iTunes smart playlist filters. The SmartPlaylist app only selects songs out of your entire library instead of from existing playlists like Miximum does. Apple does not offer remote access to the checked attribute which puts SmartPlaylist at a disadvantage since even unchecked songs could end up in their playlists. You won’t have the same problem with Miximum.

GitsCarried , 08/13/2020

My favorite app

I have a playlist with 1,200 songs I’ve worked on building and regularly refreshed over the last year. It seemed like no matter how much work was put into the playlist I was listening to the same songs repeatedly...
Enter Miximum the perfectly simple app with tons of complications. I can easily sort the playlist or make a mix from the playlist using least played as the rule and it constantly updates the mix so that the most played stuff is buried or in this case not shown in the mix. Instant play list bliss, the perfect companion!

After spending time just playing with their mix rules I’ve built several new mixes that I love and easily expand my listening library. It’s one of those things that the more you use it the more amazed you’ll get with just what can be achieved on a smart playlist from your phone. Tweaking these mixes gives satisfying results without ever feeling too complicated or burdensome.

I don’t want to ever live without the features this app provides. It gives the sort of options that honestly should have been provided by Apple Music on the iOS.
