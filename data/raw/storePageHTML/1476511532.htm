Description

Tapping has never been this spicy, fun, exciting and immersive!

Are you sick of reading Stories or Books and not getting any emotions while reading it? No worries, we got you covered on TapStory! With Tapstory, you will enjoy the exciting stories in many genres from romantic to horror, spicy to fantasy fiction while you're making decisions that will change the flow of the stories.
Become part of the stories with decisions, voice messages and video calls open the doors to the addictive world of Tapstory!

By tapping you unlock the next message, which means you’re just one tap away from the next exciting Story.

So what awaits you on Tapstory?

• "Should I go through the door or run quickly?" hundreds of decisions like this that will make you part of the story
• Spooky sounds and horror videos that you will hear inside a desolate house full of fear
• Exciting and spicy photos that come to your phone late in the night,
• Voice and video calls from unknown numbers when you least expect it, and many more!

Tap Story gets you in a mood where you always think ‘’One More Tap’’. But don't worry! you'll be hooked on Tapstory with brand new exciting episodes released every week and original stories which will make you feel more scared, naughty, lovely, shocked, suspicious or whatever you're looking for, we got a story for it!

With the reward coins given every day, you can enjoy the excitement of Tapstory and buy coins to get unlimited access to exciting contents.
