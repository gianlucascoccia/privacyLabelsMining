Description

Must Be Fast!

I love this APP & I’ve saved a lot of $$...Thanks to “Checkout 51”! For that reason, I would’ve easily given “Checkout 51” a five star rating, but the reason for my four star rating is...I’ve been having problems w/the availability of the rebates! Whenever I get excited because i see a offer for an item that I will be purchasing, I get ready to add the offer on my list, then I see that it says “0” (meaning I will not be able to get the extra savings that I could’ve, because the offer has reached the limit)! And what’s frustrating is...now w/more & more people finding out & using “Checkout 51”, I’ve noticed that majority of the offers on their APP, have reached the limit (especially if it’s a popular item)! The other thing I feels their APP needs work on is...when the offer is no longer available, it still shows up w/all the other available offers. I would prefer it if they would remove the offers that are no longer available! Other than that, this is a GREAT APP to save $$, & it’s REALLY SUPER EASY TO USE!!! One more important thing to remember...it’s not “Checkout 51’s” fault for running out of rebates. Every offer has a limit from the manufacturer, & when that limit is reached, then that offer is no longer available on “Checkout 51”. That’s why when a new week starts, you gotta be fast, before they run out!

Allison E F , 01/27/2021

Good for Gas but Slow Approval

This app is okay. I installed it years ago but almost never redeemed any offers, due to the very limited and random grocery offers available. They added cash back on gas about a year or so ago and I then started using it much more. I used to get my cash back on gas receipts after a day but they’ve since changed their process and it now takes a week or more. They’re also not always approved in the order they were redeemed. This can make it difficult to keep track of, because I’ll often redeem 2-3 gas receipts in a week and have to dig through emails to determine which ones were approved and which ones I’m still waiting on. Sometimes they also won’t approve and you’ll have to wait 10 business days just to be told to submit additional verification of purchase. The support team is helpful and I’ve pretty much always gotten any issues I’ve had resolved eventually but it would be nice if the process was a little quicker.

DCKJ84 , 03/19/2019

Could be better

The app is fairly easy to use. I’ve been disappointed with many of the offers though. Not a great number of deals available and not many new offers come available. I’ve had the app a month and not many of the offers have changed. Also, not a lot of products I’m all that interested in.
I also had an issue with a claim. There was an issue with the barcode on the product I bought so the product didn’t show up appropriately on the receipt. It showed up as “merchandise” as the cashier had to manually enter the product and so the claim did not go through and I did not get the cash back from the deal. There is a review process where I can circle the item on the receipt in case they miss it in their initial review, but of course all I could circle was the “merchandise” line and not something that matched the product name. In the review process, I’d enjoy an option where I could explain what happened and take a picture of the product or its barcode. The only reason I bought the product was because after the rebate, it would have been cheaper than a competing product... I just ended up paying more than planned and would have just bought the cheaper competition instead if I knew the claim wouldn’t go through.
Just hasn’t been what I had hoped for but tough to complain too much about a free app.
