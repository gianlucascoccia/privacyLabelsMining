Description

Clean, simple and FAST!

Get live scores, stats, news and alerts for MLB, NFL, NBA, NHL, CFL, soccer, tennis, golf, NCAA football & basketball and more.

Add our Live Scores widget to your Today view to get exactly the scores you want without having to open an app or even unlock your device.

Install our Apple Watch app to get live scores for all games and frequently updated complications on your watch face.

Watch post-game highlight videos for MLB, NFL, NBA and NHL games.

All your received alerts are saved with your app's badge # showing the total # of new alerts.

Get standard game alerts and player alerts you won't find anywhere else, all easily configurable.

Get live stat updates in your icon badge. You can track Brady's passing yards or LeBron's points without launching an app.

Import your fantasy teams or create custom teams to get stats & alerts for any group of players you like. Get notified instantly after every touchdown, goal, home run and much more.

Full team schedules, league leaders, season and game stats for every player.

Choose from a dozen custom sports-related alert sounds.

Great All-Around

Hey, the main reason I’m leaving this review is because of the widget you guys have. I would give it 6/5 stars if i could. It’s amazing to have all the scores in one place and so organized. It’s how i stay up to date most of the time. Makes me automatically love this app. The actual app is great as well, and has many amazing features. The one thing I think could use a tiny improvement is the presentation, the app can feel a but cluttered at times and the horizontal scrolling can be less than ideal at points. Sometimes it just feels like there’s too much on the screen at one time and i think a new menu system or organization would help. Also (this is just a want, not necessary), I think a change (or addition in some cases) to the visual updates to a game’s play-by-play could use a visual improvement (similar to the way ESPN does theirs for football and basketball, with visuals for shots missing/going in, a visual of the court, and overall just a visual appeal for drives/field in football). This would help me to want to use the app for score updates instead of just the widget; I wouldnt be just staring at a blank play-by-play and refreshing. Thanks so much!

Spam ads say you have a virus

For the past 2 months, when using this app, I've been getting spam ads that say I have a virus on my phone. I took a screenshots of the spam ads and googled them. Sure enough, they were spam ads, and I don't have a virus.

Over the past 2 months, I've noticed new spam ads come with each update. There used to be no ads. Now there are ads. I understand maybe they need to make $. I have no problem with a little spam in a free app. Buy these shady spam ads saying you have a virus is bothersome. I know better, meaning I know I don't have a virus. But what about naive people, what happens with them?...they can be taken advantage of.

The thing is, I've been using this app for over 2 years. I love it. Very simple, lightweight. But these new spam ads have me questioning the integrity of the company. For that, I give 2 stars.
