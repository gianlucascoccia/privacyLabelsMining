Description

A calculator customized to assist consultants with totaling up Arbonne orders.

You’ve been there before… You are at a presentation with numerous clients and order forms full of products. Quickly and accurately adding up all of those products, calculating complex discounts, sales tax rates and shipping can be daunting. All while you could be building deeper client relationships and growing your business. The Order Calculator is here to help!

• Easy to read interface
• Interactive product calculator
• Multiple product type support
• Custom discounts
• Full host reward support
• ASVP & Success Package support
• Automated location-specific shipping & surcharges
• Export order summary by email or by printing.
• Built-in help guide
• Ability to easily customize calculations.
• Ability to override default package and membership fee prices.

Note:
This app is designed to primarily functions as a calculator and optimized specifically for the Arbonne business plan. It does not contain any comprehensive product listings, with the exception of the current lineup of ASVPs. Orders cannot be placed through this app.
