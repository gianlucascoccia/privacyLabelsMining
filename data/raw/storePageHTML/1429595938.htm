Description

Experience the magic of Disney in the official Disney Story Realms app and explore enchanting worlds we all know and love!

An adventure and storybook app loved by children aged 4-9 and their families. There’s bundles of free content including explorable realms and iconic storybooks. To access and enjoy the full content you need to become a member. Get started with our 7 day free trial.

*EXPLORE 3D REALMS*

• 10 magical worlds to explore, featuring Frozen, Toy Story 4, Moana, the Lion King and many more!

• Exciting new Disney and Pixar realms added regularly.

• FREE: Hang out in Mickey’s House, step into Belle’s world, and collect prizes in Rafiki’s ancient tree!

*FULL MOVIE RETELLING STORYBOOKS*

• Rediscover the greatest tales of all time, complete with every twist, turn, challenge and triumph!

• Feature length story books including the Incredibles 2, Wreck it Ralph, Frozen 2

• FREE: Beauty and the Beast full movie storybook.


*MAGICAL SHORT STORIES*

• Over 50 exclusive short stories featuring Disney and Pixar family favourites.

• A forever-growing library full of books!

• FREE: Short stories from Mickey & Friends, Winnie the Pooh, Lilo & Stitch


*ACTIVITIES AND PUZZLES*

• Stimulate learning with word searches, dot-to-dot and hidden object activities.

• Get creative with painting, dress up games and sticker albums.

• Solve jigsaw puzzles and find matching pairs.

• FREE: Mickey’s House is bursting with fun to discover, as well as Beauty and the Beast and Lion King activities

MINIMUM DEVICE REQUIREMENTS:

Disney Story Realms requires an iPhone 5 or newer, an iPad 4th Gen or newer, an iPad mini 2 or newer and any iPad Pro*, and 1.95GB of free space to install. You will need iOS 9.0 or newer.

*iPod Touch devices, iPhones released before September 2012, the iPad mini 1 and any iPads released before November 2012 are NOT currently supported.

ADVICE TO PARENTS

Disney Story Realms has been tested by children and teachers.

The app features a passcode-protected Grown-Ups’ Corner where you can:

Give permission to save media to view images of your child’s activity progress

Choose the length of time your child uses the app

Get help and advice about playing the game with your child
