Description

Take the KITV 4 News app with you everywhere you go and be the first to know of breaking news happening in Honolulu and the surrounding area. Always stay up to date with the latest local news, national, sports, traffic, political, entertainment stories and much more. Download the KITV 4 News app for free today.

Our Local News, Weather and Sports App Features Include:
• Honolulu breaking news alerts with push notifications
• Live streaming breaking news updates from our KITV 4 News reporters when it happens, where it happens
• Local news submission area for breaking news, news tips and the ability to email your news photos and videos right to our newsroom to be featured on air
• Ability to share stories via email, text message, Facebook, Twitter, or any of your other social media pages
• Up to date, current local weather conditions, hourly Honolulu weather updates, and 7 day forecasts
• An interactive radar that lets you zoom in and out to street level and watch storms as they approach. Move the map around to see major weather activity
• Honolulu weather alerts and updates, weather videocasts and more
