KEELO IS FOR YOU, IF YOU WANT:
• Strength to lift & carry heavy objects in real life without strain
• Stamina to climb flights of stairs without feeling winded
• Endurance to hike long distances without trouble
• Physical independence throughout your life

MORE ABOUT KEELO

FULL BODY WORKOUT
We combine strength, cardio, and conditioning into one program. You'll work and develop your body's full musculoskeletal, central nervous, cardiovascular, and metabolic systems.

PERSONALIZED PROGRAM
Keelo's AI-based algorithm examines your entire workout history to ensure you're working all parts of the body and getting the most out of your workouts. We keep the muscle confusion high.

DON’T THINK, JUST DO
We show you exactly what to do. No more guessing: "what workout should I do today?" Keelo keeps track of your scores and progress.

DO ANYWHERE
At home or gym. Bodyweight-only workouts included. Some gym equipment, e.g. barbell, dumbbells, kettlebells, recommended. But don’t worry. If you don’t have equipment, let us know. We’d be happy to suggest alternatives!

LEADERBOARDS
See how well you've done compared to others in your peer group!

FREE COACHING BY EMAIL
Email your coach at coach@keelo.com at any time to ask questions about workouts, alternative movements, weight selection, and more. We want you to succeed.

COACHING BY CHAT (PREMIUM)
Chat with your coach to get faster responses to your workout questions.

APPLE HEALTH
With your permission, we sync your calories burned and workout time with Apple Health. If completed through an Apple Watch, we also record your heart rate during the workout.

More Features:
• HD instructional videos for all movements.
• All workouts scalable to beginners and experienced alike.
• Works with most Bluetooth heart rate monitors.
• No login required. Just open the app and go.
• New workouts added regularly.

Perfect balance of functional and rehab because it’s scalable!

First off, I want to say this is exactly what I have always wanted. I have been an avid exerciser since I was in high school. I used to do it for vain glory, but as I got older and I believe wiser, I realized it was all about practical, and healthy living as an everyday lifestyle. Between youthful confidence (I.e. arrogance) and ignorance my physical abilities went downhill fast. I started exercising again in my 30’s but the most difficult part was Kevin that focus and balance between work, and play. I took a course, and practiced everyday healthy lifestyles and that was awesome, but there was always one thing missing, an ability to log and keep my training regimen diverse enough to not go complacent. Keeping things on pen and paper only went so far and therefore teaching the people I trained to do the same was of low quality on my part. But with this program It keeps a record of my workouts, with enough diversity that i don’t have keep a mental bank of exercises on the ready. I also love that the cooldown is literally stretches that allow me to relax into a steady heart rate. I forgot to mention I have had a very bad back injury when I worked for a distribution company, so having a core focus workout has always been hard. But now with functional training, a good warm up and cooldown, along with healthy eating practices my confidence I believe will be better than ever, Praise God!

Best Fitness App

I have consistently used Keelo and followed their recommendations for several months now. I am getting significant results. I am noticeably stronger and fitter, not only in my home gym, but in my everyday activities. Even with things like trimming the yard, carrying the bag of dog food, or moving a piece of furniture upstairs. The workouts have also helped me improve my distant running. I have tried so many fitness apps, but this one is unique. It is simple and right to the point and keeps me focused. Sometimes I tell myself there is no way I can complete the workout, but what an incredible feeling when you realize how much more you had in you when it’s all over. If you listen to your body and scale back where you need to, as they recommend, the workouts are very effective. I have never done CrossFit or anything similar. I mention this because I truly feel this app is for anyone. I don’t use the social media part or post the workouts I have done, and I don’t pay attention to leader boards. I just enjoy seeing myself get stronger. The videos of each lift/movement are extremely helpful and well done. You can customize the app to your specific needs/wants, for example if equipment is unavailable to you, or there are movements you don’t want to perform, you can indicate this in the app. The workouts will push you and that’s what I really love about the app. Thank you Keelo!

Jizzie , 11/04/2018

You really don’t have to think

This is probably the best workout buddy I’ve ever had. I used to be part of a great Crossfit gym a couple of years ago and this app reminds me of that gym. At the gym (I call it gym when most call it a box) you walk in, sign in, mingle, then do what they say and wait for great results. The only thing I would have to do extra was write down lifts, times and reps for workouts, and calculate percentages for 1 rep max lifts. It was awesome. Well I moved away and was not able to find a home box in my new location. I needed to fitness so I am able to work.

Two years later, I find this app. With Keelo, you put in a little information about your and poof, a personalized program just for you. The program scheme is just like my old gym/box; warm-up, lift, then metcon. I was nice and tight. Keelo also breaks down your 1 rep max on lifts so you wouldn't have to. One fun option this app gives you is your fitness balance. I’m not really sure if it matters to me, but it’s fun to look at after workouts.

I love this app. Hats off to the creator. You really did a great job with providing an app that meets all needs a person looking to better their fitness is looking for. Thank you thank you thank you!!!
