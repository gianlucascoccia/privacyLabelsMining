Description

Join millions of players in one of the most competitive multiplayer soccer games ever created. Experience the thrill of the fast-paced multiplayer online matches!

Compete against other players in real-time in your league and play to claim your place at the top of the ladder.

Challenge your friends in real-time!

Get social by connecting your Facebook account and play the most exciting soccer game with your friends, show them who is the best! You can also establish your own team and gain different rewards as you win matches!

Take Control of Your Soccer Career

Progress through the unique career mode to unlock special bonuses, characters, and accessories. As you progress, rewards become increasingly difficult to obtain, do you have what it takes?

Upgrade Your Character to Become a Champion!

Stand out from the crowd by choosing from hundreds of accessories to find your unique combination. Improve your characters’ skills to dominate the field. Progress to unlock new characters and cosmetic rewards and upgrade your favorite characters.

Spice Things Up with Different Superpowers!

Unlock 18 different superpowers and use them at the right moment to catch your opponent off guard. Your opponents can counter you with superpowers as well!

Gather Fans and Unlock New Stadiums

Increase your fanbase by gaining supporters after each win. Unlock new stadiums to fit your ever-growing supporters. Journey through 10 different stadiums as you walk towards glory!

It’s more fun with Daily Missions!

Come back and fulfill different missions to get the big reward every day.


Features;

-Online soccer games against real players all across the world.
-5 competitive leagues with 15 brackets to play through.
-Dynamic & exciting online gameplay with dashy graphics.
-Upgrade your character, equip them with cool accessories and empower them with special powers.
-New characters, items, and events added constantly!
-Card packs to collect items and characters.
-Facebook connection to play and team up with friends.
-Daily Missions to get more fun and rewards!

Download Head Ball 2 to experience the thrill of the match and compete against millions!

IMPORTANT!

Head Ball 2 is free to download and play, however, there are some items in the game that can be purchased for real money. You can disable in-app purchases in your device’s settings if you don’t want this feature.

A network connection is required to play.

feverghoul , 09/27/2020

A game of lag.

This game is pretty fun and I enjoy it, but it has a major issue when it comes to connection. When you play a game and there’s a connection issue the scoreboard will display which player it is that has the bad connection which is how I know it isn’t me because it’s always my opponents who are shown to have the connection issue. I would say on average, if I play 5 games, 3 of them will have connection issues that results in some serious lag, but if your opponent has the bad connection it’s you that actually suffers. It will completely stop you from being able to touch the ball in any capacity. You’ll go to kick it and it goes right through, you’ll go to block and the ball will go right through you. This results in your opponent scoring goal after goal until the connection finally stabilizes, but by that time you’ll be so far behind you can’t win. Since the game is based on energy being used to play matches this issue completely wastes and decimates your supply of energy and if you try to leave a game because of the connection issues your penalized by losing fans (which is how you earn gold, player packs, new stadiums, etc). On top of that, pretty consistently the game just shuts down while you’re in the middle of matches or loading a match. These issues are serious enough that it completely kills any fun you’re having in the game. If these issues are addressed and fixed I would be very happy to update my rating, but until then it’s a 2 star game.
