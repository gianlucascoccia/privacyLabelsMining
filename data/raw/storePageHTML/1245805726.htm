Description

This revolutionary app, “Awakenings with Iyanla Vanzant,” promises an extremely personal spiritual coaching session that’s customized for you every single day. Daily guided spiritual principles uplift you to live your best life with Iyanla’s powerful coaching and soothing voice…anytime, any day.

 Host and executive producer of the No.1 reality show on OWN: Oprah Winfrey Network, Iyanla Vanzant now offers her new Daily Spiritual Coaching App. Known for her award-winning breakout hit “Iyanla: Fix My Life,” where she goes behind closed doors and deep inside people’s lives revealing emotionally riveting experiences, Iyanla applies spiritual principles to help people gain inner peace and guidance on a path to healing.

Now you can get your own personalized pep-talk from Iyanla Vanzant every single day. With our Alarm feature you can wake up to a custom spiritual coaching lesson from Iyanla instead of that annoying alarm in the morning. With the reminder feature, you can choose the time each day you’d like to be reminded for your custom spiritual coaching lesson from Iyanla. For example, listen after waking up, during lunch or just before bed as Iyanla’s magical soothing voice leads you through your own daily principles and affirmations toward gaining your own guidance and inner peace.

Awakenings with Iyanla is completely free and will guide you through three weekly principles. After that, by subscription, gain access to the full Awakenings with Iyanla Vanzant app features including 3 personal daily guiding principles, alarm feature and exclusive access to Iyanla’s Spiritual Feast audio. Each day, spend just three minutes listening as Iyanla delivers three personalized principles she has selected just for you. 

FEATURES:
▶ BEGIN your day with Iyanla’s guided spiritual principles
▶ RECEIVE three new daily principles & affirmations selected by Iyanla
▶ LISTEN to Iyanla explain the deeper meaning of your declarations
▶ DIRECTION on completing the actions associated with each principle
▶ REPLAY your messages any time before the day's end
▶ REMINDER feature allows you to set a daily reminder
▶ SHARE your personal principles with others
▶ WAKE up to Iyanla’s soothing voice with the Audio Wake Up Alarm feature
▶ Exclusive access to Spiritual Feast personal audio messages from Iyanla.

This is not just an app with positive affirmations. It’s small teaching moments with each of the three principles that you hear each day with the paid subscription being much value. As you go through your journal for the previous week, you will be amazed as to how on point the principles were that you got in time for what you needed as a reminder or to get through something in your life.
