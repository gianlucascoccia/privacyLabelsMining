Description

Beat Swiper is a unique mobile rhythm game where your goal is to slash the beats (cubes) as they come flying towards you.

Beat Swiper is a unique mobile rhythm game where your goal is to slash the beats (cubes) as they come flying towards you.

SIMPLE CONTROLS:
Your fingers are your sabers. Swipe your fingers on the screen to cut the beats in the corresponding direction of the cubes.
Are you a beat master? Prove yourself by taking the highest score.

Football iz life , 10/19/2020

One of the best mobile games out there

See, I don’t have a VR headset, ergo I don’t have beatsaber. I was scrolling though the app store to find a similar game like beatsaber. And well, I found it. This game is fantastic, like really, the swiping is super responsive, the song library is decent, and in general the game is put together. Some negative reviews say that there is too many ads. Um hello? No there isn’t, even though yes there are some, all I get are 5-10 second long ads. Plus, most of them you can skip. I don’t understand why people complain about this.

I have a couple things I’d like to request. It would be AWESOME if we could use songs we had bought on ITunes, so then we could play some songs that we really liked and were familiar with. I’d also think it would be cool if in say a later update when you add more songs. Instead of scrolling really far to find the song you want, you could pick from a certain music genre so it would be quick and easy. Last thing I wanna talk about, it would be pretty neat to have a shop. Every day you play a level, and log in to get coins. Then in the shop you could spend them on powerups like, slowing down the song, or points multiplier. Heck you could even get songs or cube colors. I think these things would be super cool and can add just that little taste of awesomeness. Overall though i love this game and think it’s cool!

Very good! Had only some problems

This game is very good the swiping is clean and works very well (Most of the time I’ll explain in a second) and it’s over all very good and has actually entertained me for something like 6 hrs+ in the past 2 days. The hit detection on some songs (For example Dance Monkey) when you know you actually hitting the note perfectly it sometimes won’t register the hit. Not much of a problem as it only happens sometimes to me. Only annoying when going for a FC (Full Combo/100% notes/cuts Hit) as you don’t actually miss one but you still did. Another thing I do not understand is the points. For some songs (All the ones with only expert difficulty still) I feel like even with the FC and hitting the notes practically perfect. When I look at the leaderboards I’m in like 1000’s for position and people have 1000+ more points then me (but with expert Plus songs I’m always near top 10 on leaderboards for most songs) so I don’t know if it was in a different version of the game they got the score or something but kinda annoying to me at least who likes to be going for the highest score of fastest time in things. The soundtrack for the game is very good. I’m what we would call a “Metal Head” and I still can enjoy the songs that I don’t know (Uprising being the only song I actually have ever listened too fully out of them all) over all a great game can recommend 10/10

Mobile Beat Saber! But...

This game is obviously supposed to be like the VR Game “Beat Saber.” This game feels like it is the mobile version of it. I 100% recommend it! It is awesome and, for me, feels like the most accurate game to Beat Saber you can get on this App Store so far. It’s awesome, but there are a few things I have to say.
