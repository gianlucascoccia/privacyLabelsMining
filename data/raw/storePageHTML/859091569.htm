Description

This app selects for you, each day, people to connect with. Whether you’re seeking friends, recruiters, mentors or employees, each swipe is a new opportunity and a new discovery.

This app is ideal for entrepreneurs seeking cofounders and ideas, professionals exploring career changes or job opportunities, building contacts within their industry, people new to a city seeking friends, and anyone who wants to break up their day by chatting with like-minded and inspiring people!

*CUSTOMIZE YOUR PROFILE*
- Create your account with your status (entrepreneur, freelancer, student..), place of work and professional goals.
- Select the career fields you’re most interested in (communication, marketing, sales, finance, engineering …)
- Don't forget to mention how you want to meet people: having coffee, aperitif, for lunch...
- Add you LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram profiles

*SWIPE*
- Have a look to the personalized contacts list that we make for you every day
- For each profile, you have 2 options: pass or meet
- When you are interested in the profile, just swipe and start talking with inspiring people
- To have a match, you need to have both swiped right
- Then, you can chat, discover your mutual interest and build a durable relationship

*MEET & ENJOY*
- Each person is unique by its personality, career and aspirations
- Discover entrepreneurs, founders, professionals, recruiters, mentors, artists, among other countless talented and inspiring individuals throughout the world
- Meet-ups are business connections, career opportunities and path

By using this networking app, share aspirations, bond with people, and embark on new journeys. You can read some of our members’ stories in our website (www.shapr.co).

*Don’t forget that big ideas come from small encounters. Start shaping your life today!*

More then I dreamed of!!

This app is full of people looking for all sorts of networking connections. And the great part is you never know if you are going to find a mentor, a peer or a potential investor that will hire you and work with you! So many great connections for the digital networking world! It’s a great approach to networking on demand. My ONLY desire is that i wish there was a web page so i could type on a keyboard my responses instead of my phone. I don’t want to quickly transfer the convo out of the app and I’m sure y’all don’t want that either bc churn is bad. Other than that, i just have a slight pet peeve that pop ups about a “new message” from a different person pops up while I’m replying to another and it’s just bothersome and unnecessary. If i am already in the app I’m going to check for any new messages before i leave it, plus the badge icon with numbers of unopened messages can tell you if you have new messages. Other than that keep it up! This is way more personable than LinkedIn as a relationship builder and I love that! The timer for limiting connections a day is GREAT and truly does what it’s intended to do! Hopefully i have other great updates from my connections soon!

Ash1237654! , 05/28/2018

Experience probably based on area.

I have been using Shapr for two months now and it has been a strange experience to say the least. Most of the profiles that I see in a day are incomprehensible. Sometimes I’m pretty sure I’m viewing fake profiles (when I see the same profile back to back with the same interests and same weirdly worded bio but with different names and different pics). I also get the impression that some profiles are fake because there are no links and a bio cuts off mid-word as though the folks at Shapr have been pulling data from LinkedIn.

I am in Memphis, TN and most days there isn’t any Memphis resident whose profile I can check out. Usually I’m seeing Georgia or Chicago or somewhere in Canada. If the primary purpose was to actually meet people beyond the internet, that’s not helpful to me. I need to see my community first and then increasingly further out.

I also don’t understand the logic of matching either. Maybe some days there just aren’t enough profiles to match my key words against, but there are many days where most of the profiles I’m given have no matching interests at all.

I’ve matched with several folks in the past month. Most aren’t anywhere close to me. So it makes it very hard to get myself to use the app on a daily basis when it’s clear that my likelihood of interacting with someone in person is in the single digits.
