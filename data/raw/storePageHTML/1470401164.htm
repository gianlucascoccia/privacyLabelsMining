Description

The Official Thermomix® Cookidoo® App gives you access to the growing universe of mouth-watering Thermomix® Guided Cooking recipes. You pick and choose which recipes go with you wherever you are. Create an account and get cooking!


SMART @ HEART
Connect to Cookidoo® and access over 50,000 recipes directly from your app and through the screen of the Thermomix® TM6. Also be sure to check out the new exclusive step-by-step photos, making cooking easier than ever.

YOUR THERMOMIX® COOKIDOO® ACCOUNT
To use the app, you need to have your Thermomix® Cookidoo® username and password. Your local Thermomix® Cookidoo® website will tell you how to create your account for the app.

GET INSPIRED
Wondering what you are cooking today? Discover hundreds of ideas for every taste, season and occasion! With Cookidoo® Membership you get instant access to every recipe on Cookidoo®. Everything’s on the menu! Our weekly suggestions, thematic pages and editor picks will point you in the right direction.


PLAN AND COOK
Planning is easy and fun, add recipes to your planner and find the recipes ready for cooking when you need them. The Cook Today button on each recipe lets you schedule a recipe with a single click.


MAKE IT YOUR OWN
Create your own recipe lists to manage your recipes the way you want. Bookmark any recipe you find appealing so you can find it later. You can also view recipes you scheduled in the past.


COOK-KEY® BRINGS GUIDED COOKING TO YOUR FINGER TIPS
Cook-Key® connects your Thermomix® TM5 to Cookidoo®. Enjoy sending your recipe favourites, weekly planning and recipe collections from the iOS App to your Thermomix®.

Very convenient

This app is extremely useful for planning, shopping, organizing and making recipes on TM6.
It also allows me to decide whether I can do a recipe with the ingredients, skill level and time (or thyme) that I have.
It’s a learning tool in and of itself. It’s truly a global cooking class as you can see recipes intended for other markets. Just from looking at the proportion of ingredients one can decipher a TM “palate.”
I imagine the TM team will be releasing further iterations and can’t wait to see future incarnations where they apply machine learning based on user interactions.

Lindsinic , 07/27/2020

Needs So Many Improvements

Thermomix is such an expensive machine, and this Cookidoo app is so disappointing as its only integrated cookbook. Some issues that need addressed: 1) Platform needs an internal translator built-in so that, say, USA customers can choose recipes from other countries and actually be able to read them and cook them. Recipes in English are only 7,800, whereas recipes across entire platform are 60,000+. I want the ability to choose from all of them! 2) Should have the ability to adjust serving size to 2, 4, 6, 8 and all of the ingredient amounts automatically adjust. 3) App should have a way to save filter preferences and not reset every time the app is closed. 4) Recipe quality needs improved. Seems heavily geared toward sweets. I would love to see more healthy, whole-food recipes that are packed with flavor. Healthy food doesn’t have to be just plain, steamed and bland. 5) Recipe variation needs improved. Searched United States for “Steel Cut Oats”. Nothing. I would love to see twenty different steel cut variations to choose from. “Grouper”. Nothing. I want Cookidoo to work as a true online cookbook-quality compilation, but it is lacking SO MUCH. These things are just leaving me very disappointed.

Techadopter , 10/19/2020

Major shortcomings

Thermomix brags about the variety of recipes from around the world but doesn’t provide a mechanism to translate ingredients or directions from their native language. (When asked about this, the Thermomix rep said, “that would be a good benefit”. The rep went on to say one could translate through the web, BUT THE APP IS DESIGNED TO RESTRICT COPYING CONTENT. So copying isn’t a solution. The product is web connected so there has to be a solution to integrate translation capabilities into the cookidoo phone app and the tm6 interface thus truly making the final recipe catalog accessible to all languages. I like how the product developers are constantly improving the interface but not truly being able to access the global variety of recipes is a major miss. I have communicated this to Thermomix directly but after a year I feel like their response was simply platitudes.

Another grievance I have is the new candy making functionality. Cookidoo recipes tap into features that are not accessible to users outside of preprogrammed recipes. This is the equivalent of buying a race car but only being able to drive 30 mph. Thermomix needs to open this functionality up so users can experiment and make candy that is not part of the cookidoo recipe catalog.
