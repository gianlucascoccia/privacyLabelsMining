2amlullaby , 10/12/2017

Thus far unimpressed

I would love this app if it would save my general information, instead of having to enter the same 5 or 6 questions at the beginning of every survey. I downloaded it yesterday and have yet to "qualify" for a survey, which is a little frustrating when I spend 5 minutes answering the same questions I answer every other time. I've updated my notification settings, and hopefully if I get to the surveys quickly I'll see better results. On the upside, they do give 5 points for going through the hassle, even if you can't do the survey. Would also be great if you could complete surveys in the app. The app is very well designed and pleasant to use on mobile devices, the website a little less so, and so far each survey has opened in their website.

With a few improvements, this app could be great...that being said, I'm still sticking with it.

Hi! Thank you for your valuable feedback and interest in our surveys!

Tickleheads , 01/02/2020

Good app and fun

I really enjoy this app and earning points. The only downsides I have found are that you consistently have to enter the same information, and having 4 kids makes this process time consuming. This information is already saved to my profile so I am not sure why it just does not automatically send that as opposed to having to fill it out. The other problem I have had of late is that I complete a good portion of a survey and it will kick me out and say that they no longer need my results- in the past, I used to get 5 points when that happened but now, often I get nothing. I also have surveys on my board that when I try to do them, they say they have already been done or that they are no longer available, yet they stay showing as available surveys to complete. One other thing is that I completed well over my 200 surveys last year and did not receive my loyalty points for it. I have found that this may just be because they are behind as the loyalty rewards often were late arriving. I still love this app and do it daily- it is an easy way to earn some money and I will continue to do surveys.

Hi! Thanks for your message! We are continuously trying to improve the user experience and we value every feedback we receive on using the app. . Please write to questions@i-say.com with more details about the issues you encountered, and a member of the support team will try to help you.
