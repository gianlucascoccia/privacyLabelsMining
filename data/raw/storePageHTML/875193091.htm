Description

Let your little ones learn the Alphabet in the fun way!
ABC Phonics is full of interactive and dynamic learning games just right for your first ABCs!

Great app, but just FYI....

We love Pink Fong apps! They really are great, even if they can be a bit pricey. I gave it three stars for this reason: Purchases do not transfer from one device to another! Even if you are using the same account and have family sharing activated (on iPads), it has been very frustrating that I have to purchase the same packages multiple times for each device. That is not fair to the customers! I have not found a fix for this, so just be aware.

Multiple devices

My husband and I share an iCloud account. I bought this app on my son’s iPad, and was able to download from the cloud on to all our devices and phones with that iCloud account.
