Description

Do you already use WellRight as your wellness program? Then download the WellRight mobile app to log your activity and check the progress of your challenges! Take full advantage of WellRight and ensure your Health Risk Assessment is complete so you can find out where to put your focus. Manage your account, monitor your ranking among other challengers, and make sure you don’t miss a day of tracking!

You can use the app to:
- See the status of all your current challenges and view completed challenges
- Track progress for current challenges
- Join a new challenge
- Check out the challenge leaderboard
- Make updates to your profile
- Take your Health Risk Assessment
- Integrates with the Health App (HealthKit) to track your steps, calories, distance and duration.

New features!
- Improved overall design in order to enhance flow with quick access to product features
- Includes menu revamp, which allows quick access to product features

(This app is only available to current WellRight members who are given access to the program through their employer. To find out more information visit wellright.com.)

Set goals, track them, and see results

This app is filled with hundreds of overall wellness challenges. So it’s more then just physical fitness challenges. It helps you stay accountable to yourself by giving you a place to track your success. You can step up text reminders to track your goal while at the same time they serve as great reminders of the goal(s) you are working on. You can even create your own goals and track those as well. I have been using this app for 3+ years and find that as I strive to set goals or do community goals I can see a difference in my life. Give it a try. Do it for you and if not for you do it for someone you love. After all there are challenges that you can pick that are designed to serve others! I would love to see the app be easier ans fast to track goals.
