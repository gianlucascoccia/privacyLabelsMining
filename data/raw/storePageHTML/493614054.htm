Description

Download the only iPad app that will give you an exact replica of the Kalamazoo Gazette newspaper on your tablet. Access in-depth, high-quality news and analysis of MLive’s Kalamazoo Gazette 24/7 from the convenience of your iPad. Get local coverage of West Michigan including news, events, politics, opinions, sports, and much more.

The app is free to download and comes with a preview edition of the newspaper.

Subscribers to the Kalamazoo Gazette get full access at no extra charge. Simply sign in with your existing eReplica login and password.

If you are not a current home delivery subscriber, we offer three app-only subscription options through iTunes:

1 Month Auto-renewing Subscription: $20.99

Your subscription will automatically renew at the end of each subscription period and your credit card will be charged through your iTunes account. You may turn off auto-renew through your iTunes account settings.

Any unused portion of a free trial period, if offered, will be forfeited when the user purchases a subscription to that publication, where applicable

interface no longer awful

The latest update of this app includes a long-overdue UI update which drastically improves the user experience. Prior to this update, the Gazette app was easily the worst app I still used daily. I value the local news this paper provides, and now it’s so much easier to see past the interface and see the content. Plus: no crashes since installing, uninstalling, reinstalling, and logging back in.

Thanks to the coders who found a way to get the medium out of the way of the message.
