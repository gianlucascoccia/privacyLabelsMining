Description

The ClubReady member app is designed to easily manage your gym experience.

- Viewing your class schedule and signing up for classes
- Booking and managing personal training sessions
- Checking into your club using a membership barcode loaded directly to your phone
- Updating your personal information quickly and easily, directly from your phone
- Syncing your classes or services to your personal calendar
- Adding credits directly through the app which you can use for classes, or other sessions your club offers

“filtering” is a nightmare

When filtering activities to book, the default (including with reset filters) is ALL selected. EVERY TIME you search you have to DESELECT - unclick- every one of 20 other activities to just get, say, ‘lap swim’. Even when booking another swim in the very same session, or returning to the class list after booking a session! Wow. Unusable!

This is backwards compared to how filtering works on almost every other shopping or sign-up site encountered. The default (and reset) should be nothing selected — but that means no filtering is going on; you get everything. Then if you click just, say, swimming and tennis, you narrow it down to just those items by clicking those — not by unclicking the other 20!

LTD Diamond , 12/04/2018

Great idea. Lazy implementation

The idea of having virtual access to your membership card is nice. Also being able to look up and register for classes is a cool idea too. Couple things though show this app developer is not taking this app seriously and just cashing in on the gyms that have decided to use it.

The iPhone X came out OVER A YEAR AGO and the app still ain’t optimized for the notch area of the screen.

You should be able to load your membership card from the app to the wallet that way you can double click the home button/sleep button and have quick access to your card.

There is no area of the app for feedback. So bugs can go completely unreported. That’s because this app developer is cashing in on a casually implemented good idea and the gyms that are paying them to use this app. Hopefully someone will compete with them and cause them to lose some gyms so they will take their app more seriously. I’m going to be talking to our gym management team to encourage them to look for an alternative.
