Description

Find a charging station nearby, charge your electric vehicle, manage your charging session, pay, and even sign up for a membership.

Charge in as little as 30 minutes on the nation’s largest public DC fast charging network. The Electrify America app lets you find a charging station nearby, charge your EV, and manage your charging session. (Level 2 chargers also available at some locations).

You can use this app to:
• Find an Electrify America charging station
• Get directions from your current location to a selected charging station location
• Become an Electrify America Pass or Pass+ memberWhen you sign up for a free Electrify

America Pass membership, you can use this app to:
• Check the availability of a charger in real-time and get notified when a charger becomes available
• See charger details and save your favorite charging stations
• Start and stop your charging session from your phone
• Track your charging status easily while you shop or dine nearby
• Get notified when your compatible EV reaches your desired state of charge
• Pay contact-free from your phone
• See your charge history and email a receipt

Visit a charging station often? Consider our Pass+ plan, which gives you all the benefits above, plus reduced costs for charging. ($4 monthly fee applies.)

Electrify America’s powerful DC fast charging stations support all CCS (SAE Combo) and CHAdeMO-compatible EVs. You can also use the app to find Electrify America’s Level 2 chargers (with J1772 connectors).

That means you can charge just about any electric vehicle on the road with us, including:
• Audi e-tron

What’s New

Mar 3, 2021

Version 5.8.1

We thought it was time for a fresh new look. And we think you’ll like what we’ve done. Check out our fully redesigned app for all of your charging needs. Updates include:
- Innovative & user-centric design enhancements to existing features and interactions
- New intuitive iconography
- Map pins with new active-state labels, providing additional information for each charging station and charger availability
- Biometric authentication
- Effortless search experience with search bar now within range of your thumb and prioritized for one-hand use
- More prominent charger states within map navigation
- Glanceable in-session charging overviews
- Consolidated setting options
- In-app visual cues for charge session payment methods
- Transparent and inclusive account and transaction history.
- Increased options for users to check eligibility for available charging offers
- Faster Account Creation (credit card no longer required)
- Streamlined plan and membership enrollment
- A Single Charging Ecosystem to manage public and HomeStation charging.

Navigate to the Home charging tab or visit https://www.electrifyhome.com/ to sign up for updates on HomeStation availability.
