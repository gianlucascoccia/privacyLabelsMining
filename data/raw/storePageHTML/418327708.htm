Description

Business finances made easy with Dext.

Dext is the business expense tracker that tracks, reads and stores your receipts and invoices on the go, so you can focus less on finance and more of your time on what really matters: growing your business.

Whether you’re a small-to-medium sized business, a sole trader or self-employed, Dext can transform the way you do business making your accounting admin easy, quick and painless.

Capture, store and keep track of your business receipts, bills, invoices and bank statements with Dext’s award-winning technology. Start expensing more, paying less tax and saving vital hours in your week.

* Snap It
Use your phone to snap and extract all the important data and then scrap the physical copy. Simple and paperless!

* Send It
Upload bills, receipts, invoices and bank statements on-the-go from your phone, laptop or email account, before connecting your accounting software via our seamless integration.

* Sorted
The time you’d usually spend on your finances? Using Dext, you get it all back so you’re free to focus on what really matters — growing your business.

Effortlessly update your reports, Dext uses machine learning to tailor bookkeeping to your business needs. Connect with leading cloud accounting platforms – such as Xero, Sage and QuickBooks – to unlock automated publishing and automated transaction matching for quick-fire reconciling.

No data entry and no paperwork, for cloud or desktop systems, we update the accounting software in real time, so your business and your clients are always up to date with your finances.

Dext makes life easier; clients’ receipts, invoices, bank and credit card statements are arranged in an organised, easy to access reports. It’s an uncomplicated expense tracker, just a few taps and you can pay an invoice and record it on your books and the archive option will keep your items on file for several years, perfect when going paperless!

Features:
- Receipt Scanner: Snap your receipt on your mobile, forward via email, or upload through the web
- Accounting without data entry - just snap your receipt with our app and you're done
- On a business trip abroad? We automatically record and convert transactions to your chosen currency
- Manage invoices and capture additional notes when adding a receipt, saving time later
- Bills and expense tracker with 100% secure storage
- Receipt images and data are stored securely in the cloud
- Unlimited digital storage for all your finance reports
- Receipt tracker with easy access to your expenses whenever you need them - on your phone or the web
- Bookkeeping for the Digital Age: We digitise and securely store your paperwork online, so you can throw those receipts and bills away yet stay compliant
- Small Business Accounting: Integrate with your accounting software including Xero and QBO, save time by automating your bookkeeping
