Description

• Turn your own photos into coloring pages
• Create beautiful HD images from pixel art

Colorscape takes any image and creates an outline that you can color in – on your screen, on paper or both.

Take a picture of anything, anyone or anywhere and instantly you can create a blank canvas to add any color or shade you wish.

Or take a picture from the web and put your own style on it.

If you're looking to relax, try our color by numbers feature and let Colorscape turn pixel art into gorgeous HD images. It'll even generate a time-lapse video you can share with your friends!

Features:
• Turn your photos into beautiful outlines you can color in
• Turn pixel art into gorgeous HD images
• Share your art with your friends
• Or print it out with AirPrint and stick it on your wall
• We provide a range of color palettes, including one generated from the colors in the original image

So if you want to color it – just Colorscape it!
