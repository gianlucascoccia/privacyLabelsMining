Description

­Looking for a fast and easy-to-use invoice generator? Invoice Maker by Saldo Apps is a simple billing app that will take care of your invoices and estimates.

If you're a small business owner, self-employed contractor, entrepreneur, or freelancer, this simple invoice app is designed perfectly for you. Save time with Invoice Maker and get paid faster!

SIMPLE BILLS ON THE GO
Create invoices and make estimates for your clients in minutes with preset templates. Easily convert estimates into invoices with one tap. When everything is ready share, email, or print invoices, quotes, and estimates with one tap.

PROFESSIONAL CUSTOM INVOICING
First, add your billing information and company logo. Then, create a list of products and services for quicker invoice creation. Also, include discounts, tax, and shipping fees. Finally, add photos, signatures, and notes.

DETAILED INVOICE SUMMARY AND REPORTS
Quickly sort invoices by paid, unpaid, or by date to help you keep track of your accounting balance.

OTHER OUTSTANDING FEATURES
• Get paid via PayPal, bank transfer, or check.
• Create invoice templates with a simple invoice template maker.
• Create a list of clients or import from your existing contacts.
• Receive notifications when invoices are opened by your clients.
• Preview invoices and estimates before sending them.
• Set invoice payment terms: 1 day, 7 days, etc.
• Work with any currency.
• Enjoy light and dark themes.

SUBSCRIPTION DETAILS
Invoice creator is free for your first three invoices or estimates during a free trial period. Get started with free invoices, then upgrade in-app to Pro account for unlimited invoicing and estimating. Choose monthly, annual, or lifetime subscription that suits you the best.
