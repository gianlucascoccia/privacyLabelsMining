Description

World's first location-based dating app and still the best and getting better every day.

At SinglesAroundMe (SAM) our focus has always been connection. Since our launch as the world's first location-based GPS dating app in 2010, we have endeavoured to make you happy by making it easy for you to connect with like-minded singles around you, anywhere in the world. During this period of unprecedented anxiety and social upheaval, we want to help in every way we can. We are concerned not only for the people who we work with, who remain fully-employed, but also about social isolation and the mental health of isolated people globally, as social distancing stretches into the future. 

To this end, we have included access to our featured "Destination" search service as part of our basic free membership to allow you to meet people beyond 100 miles (160KM) and all around the world, even if you can't travel there right now. We are also providing free access to "My Likes” so you can circle back to people you have "Liked" and would like to connect with again. With SinglesAroundMe you always get a second chance to make a first impression.

Offer one another the gift of human connection at a time when we could all benefit from a hug. With millions of matches, millions of dates, we are a dating app that actually works.

- 100% Free to join and discover local singles. Easy registration with Facebook or quickly by form.

- AroundMe: Swipe through profiles of nearby singles and engage with people that interest you.

- GPS verified users are displayed as “heart pins” on our patent pending privacy protected “Check In Location” interactive map.

- byChance: Meet people you crossed paths with throughout your day.

- Mingle Room: Presents the newest and most active members in your area to one another.

- Like" or “Dislike" photos and keep a fresh list of all the users you “Like”.

- See who has already “Liked” your profile.

- Users that match (mutually "Like" each other) can talk for free.

- Travelling for work or pleasure beyond your current location? Use "Destination Search" even if you can't currently travel.

- Message anyone that interests you and let your personality shine through.

- Keep track of all conversations in the message section.

- Check "New Users" that have recently joined in your area.

- 100% location-based, 100% GPS dating - verifies that users are real.

- Location Privacy: Choose when to "show", "hide" or "shift" your location on the Last Check-In map.

Nudity is strictly prohibited and users can monitor and report any bad behaviour.

On Valentine's Day, SinglesAroundMe was featured on the cover of the USA Today and on ABC World News Tonight as one of the world's leading dating apps.
