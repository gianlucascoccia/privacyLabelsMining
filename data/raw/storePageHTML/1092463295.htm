Editors’ Notes

It’s funny, it’s polished, and it lets you capture monsters. What’s not to love? While Monster Super League feels unique in its style, fans of Pokémon and Final Fantasy are gonna go crazy for it. Our favorite touch? Filling the airship with all the creatures we’ve caught.

NanDanMan , 03/16/2019

Most generous f2p game out there imo

People complain that the game is p2w which is understandable but that’s only true if you want to be the top players. Overall the game is really generous to players who don’t spend money. You can pull really good and strong monsters without paying anything. I haven’t spent any money on the game and I have gotten many five star monsters. I also hear a lot of people complaining about grinding which is bs because the game literally has a feature that grinds for you. Whenever I’m at work I just let the game play and my monster lvl up without me doing anything. The game also gives you so much energy to battle and other thing like gold and fruit. The final thing that I want to address, which is something that I think a lot of people find annoying is getting an evolution 3 monster. Basically you need 4 of a evo 1 monster to upgrade to evo 2 and you need 16 of a evo 1 monster to upgrade to evo 3. Considering that 5 and 4 star monster are hard to get , it will take time to get 16 of one 5 or 4 star monster. However, there is a monster in the game that acts as a sort of wildcard. This means that you can use it to evolve any monster. Because of this, you don’t need 16 of a monster, you only need 4 to get a 3rd evolution monster. The wildcard monster are not really easy to obtain but they aren’t impossible to get either if you want to play the game for free. Overall this game is great if you don’t mind spending some time on it

Rebel21otaku , 10/14/2018

Quality game

This game is fun, been playing it for about 4 months now I think lol. some say it’s pay to win but honestly it only gets to that point if you’re really competitive, if you’re tying to collect like I am then it’s very enjoyable! and has plenty of monsters to collect. Downsides is that for each monster requires three tributes of the same monster after the first that you’ve gotten. If you evolve it to evo 2 you can’t use previous evolutions of that monster as tributes, you have to evolve each individual tribute to make it a material tribute for the second evo.. it’s tough but it takes time.. it’s a mobile game, what do you expect. cool thing is every day you get awesome prizes in bingo, my advice would be is to play a level in a certain mission, pick whatever monster for team and then click on 20x and it will auto play for 20 levels ina particular level your playing on, so not only are you leveling up as you sleep but feeling up time, when you wake up all you have to do is collect in bingo in one feel swoop without having to wait :)

UPDATE. It’s been almost a full year ongoing in this game and I sill love it. You never run out of things to do. you got the Hero fest to prep for and the down fest to prep for. Get a nat5 mon, then you get a festival egg that carries another nat5 mon during Hero’s fest. join a clan and get many perks of being in a clan! I’ll leave it all the rest to u to experience new things!
