Description

Download The Daily Wire to access the web’s fastest-growing conservative commentary from Ben Shapiro, Andrew Klavan, Michael Knowles, Matt Walsh, and more.

You’ll get the latest headlines and podcasts from The Ben Shapiro Show, The Andrew Klavan Show, The Michael Knowles Show, The Matt Walsh Show. Plus, All-Access insiders get access to exclusive extra content, live discussions with hosts, an ad-free experience, and more.

Daily Wire Podcasts:

Tired of the lies? Tired of the spin? Are you ready to hear the hard-hitting truth in comprehensive, conservative, principled fashion? The Ben Shapiro Show brings you all the news you need to know in the most fast moving daily program in America. Ben brutally breaks down the culture and never gives an inch! Monday through Friday.

End of Western Civilization got you down? All is tickety-boo on The Andrew Klavan Show as Andrew laughs his way though Armageddon with political satire, cultural commentary, interviews and relentless mockery of racial pieties, sexual perversities, and feminist absurdities. Monday through Thursday.

Come for the conversation. Stay for the covfefe. The Michael Knowles Show goes beyond the headline, analyzing the top cultural and political issues of the day. Monday through Thursday.

Fed up with the talking heads? Matt gives you a no-holds-barred take on today’s cultural, religious, and political issues. Catch The Matt Walsh Show Monday through Friday.

Join Ben Shapiro as he sits down with a different thought leader every Sunday for an in-depth one-hour interview. The Ben Shapiro Show: Sunday Special is where you can hear the best and brightest minds on politics, news, culture, and everything in between.

Join the guys for Daily Wire Backstage, featuring Ben Shapiro, Andrew Klavan, Michael Knowles, and Daily Wire god-king Jeremy Boreing, as they host longform roundtable discussions during key political and cultural events.

Want to ask Ben Shapiro, Andrew Klavan, or Michael Knowles questions…and get instant answers? Then tune in to The Conversation, a monthly live-streamed Q&A where you get to ask the guys whatever’s on your mind.

Love the content! However, the iPad app is unusable!

How has the iPad app not been updated to support landscape mode yet? The content is great, but the iPad app is unusable. I use the magic keyboard with my iPad so I consume everything in landscape mode, but for whatever reason this app doesn’t support it. Also, like many others have stated...you can’t lock your device without it stopping the content, and it doesn’t start back up where you previously left off. If these three issues were fixed then it would without a doubt deserve a 5 star rating, but without them it severely hampers your experience and due to that I’ve found myself coming here less and less. I would be much more likely to renew my subscription when it ends if these issues were fixed.

BTW...this is the first review I’ve ever written on any app or product. I just felt compelled to leave one because this would be the best app in the AppStore if these issues were fixed.
