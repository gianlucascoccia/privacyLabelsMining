Description

DayLight shows the time of sunrise and sunset anytime and anywhere on Earth in a neat visual way. Just a quick look at the display will tell you how long the sun will shine.
Whenever you need to know the details of the sunrise and sunset, you have all of them at your disposal.



Properties

- Simple and neat graphics, stylish animation and modern design
- 24 hour clock dial
- Portrait and Landscape modes on any device
- Countdown timer for sunset
- Astronomical, Nautical and Civil twilight times
- Detection of current location via GPS
- Universal app for iPhone and iPad
- Automatic time-zone adjustment
- iMessage
