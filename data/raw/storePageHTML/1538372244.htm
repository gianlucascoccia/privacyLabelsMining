Bre🗿 , 01/26/2021

(:

I’m writing this for anyone reading to understand how it works. YES it is the idols messaging you- but it’s not JUST you. See it as you’re in a big group chat with everyone blocked BUT the idol- but they don’t have anyone blocked. They can see all the messages, but you only see theirs because they’re the only one you don’t have blocked. So, if they’re sending “weird” messages they’re probably responding to other fans! I have Chan, Felix, and Changbin on bubble and it’s honestly so much fun. The members have stolen Chan’s phone and sent voice messages, Changbin always sends us food pics, and Felix is SUPER active and sings to us a lot. They ARE the real members, it’s not a bot, but they aren’t necessarily responding to YOU specifically. It’s a lot of fun, the only thing I’d rate low is the character/message limit but it’s understandable so there isn’t a crazy amount of inappropriate, long messages coming in to the artists. I definitely recommend if you wanna see what your idols are up to, and maybe have them answer one of your questions one day!
