As bad as scalpers, unethical price gouging

A retail PlayStation 5 is roughly 2,000 AED. The retail version of FIFA is roughly 350 AED. However, when they are sold as a bundle (which is the only way that noon sells them), the price jumps to 3,800 AED. So noon is inflating the price by 1,500 AED. This is noon the seller not a third party by the way. In fact, they go even further to say this 3,800 AED price represents a 32% discount!!!! Taking advantage that a product is in short supply and raising the price by over 50% and claiming it represents a 32% discount ,while not illegal, is definitely unethical and a very poor business practice. Most other retailers have taken measures to prevent price gouging, but noon apparently sees it as a core business practice.
