Description

Never forget who you borrowed money or who you owe money. Who Owes Me - Debt Manager is a simple App to remember debts. You can mark debts as paid and always have an overview of money you lent to friends.

You can also use your contacts to quickly add new debts.

Easy to use interface to quickly manage debts.

Features
- Cloud Sync between multiple iOS devices
- Touch/Face ID
- Multiple folders
- Notifications
- Use contacts for faster inputs
- Over 100 Currencies
- Part payments
- Mark as paid
- Export data
- Automatic backups
- Remind people via SMS/WhatsApp/E-Mail


All pro features, unlimited entries and Cloud Sync between multiple iOS devices can be used after a one time payment or an active auto-renewal subscription. This three in app purchases can be bought in the app.


Subscription informations
- You can choose between a monthly and a yearly auto-renewal subscription.
- Subscriptions include all pro features, unlimited entries and Cloud Sync between multiple iOS devices.
- The subscription will renew automatically 24 hours before the end of the current period.
- The subscription price depends on the subscription period you choose.
- You can manage your subscription and turn off auto-renewal in your iTunes Account Settings.

Free trial
- Both subscriptions include a free trial period.
- You will not be charged until your trial has ended.
- Your iTunes Account will only be charged once your purchase is confirmed.
- Any unused portion of the free trial period will be forfeited after purchase.
