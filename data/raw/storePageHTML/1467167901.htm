Description

Welcome to the best stickers app for all your messaging apps!
Create your own stickers or download Sticker for free and access one of the largest sticker collections available on the App Store. Add photos from your gallery and create your own amazing stickers.

Upload your photos and create personalized stickers easily.

Use different tool to cut your sticker the way you want to:
The SMART CUT cut feature auto detects people on a picture and removes background in only one click!
The MANUAL ERASER let you choose the eraser size to manually remove background
Use FREE CUT with a zoom feature to precisely cut your sticker with your fingers.
Select predefined SHAPES to cut your stickers

Customize your created stickers:
Choose the stickers border size and color to complete your sticker


In the catalogue, you will find hundreds of stickers organized in different categories. You will be able to browse easily and find the sticker that fits you best.


This huge catalogue will keep increasing for the benefit of our premium users.


Import directly your sticker packs in iMessage & Whatsapp!
