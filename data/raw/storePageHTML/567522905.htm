Description

Drive Box aggregates all of your favorite tools and services, getting you from a to b in the safest possible way.

Drive Box is an intelligent copilot app, built for drivers and all types of vehicle lovers, and daily users. It brings navigation, music, phone, radio, browser, video and in-car entertainment under one roof for your ultimate driving experience. Drive Box takes your driving experience to the next level.
Drive safe and intelligent with Drive Box.

Main features:

Dashboard
• Display navigation, time and date data
• Access all features with one click

Music
• A smooth Spotify integration
• Drive Box EyesFree® gesture controls (simple gestures to browse and listen to your music) without ever taking your eyes off the road
• Drive Box MusicFx® presents your music in a beautiful and minimal effects panel which also feature our Eyes Free gesture controls
• Filter between Songs, Albums, Artists, Playlists, and Audiobooks
• Experience full graphics and visual richness with all your music artwork in full color
• Repeat or shuffle your tracks for pleasure

Navigation
• Get directions to your chosen destinations
• Drive Box StreetView presents a 360 degrees full-color view of your desired destination point (available as in-app purchase)
• Drive Box StreetView displays desired destination weather information

Phone
• Make calls directly inside the Drive Box app
• Access all your address book contacts directly in the app
• Use of large buttons and typography for ease of calling
• Connect your device via Bluetooth for hands-free conversations

Youtube
• Search for Youtube videos directly in the Drive Box app
• View your video in full-screen mode directly inside Drive Box app

Web Browser
• Use our browser directly inside the DriveBox app
• Access, save and edit your favorites for ease of use
• Features forward and back buttons for a smooth and fluid interaction

Video
• View your videos (non-DRM)
• Access DRM (digital rights managed)
• Experience full graphics and visual richness with all your video artwork in full color

Great app but needs some tweaks

Very nice and clean UI. However, when looking at it from 2-3 ft away, it’s hard to see and tap the app icons. It would be nice to increase the size of the icons and UI elements like the text when a song is playing.

The home bar on the iPhone X is always active and doesn’t go away. This affects the middle 2 icons on the bottom row since the home bar overlaps them. The home bar needs to be moved up a couple pixels.

Overall. This app is great.
