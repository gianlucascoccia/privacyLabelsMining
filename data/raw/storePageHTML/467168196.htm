Description

Enjoy DStv as a DStv customer and always have the shows, movies and sport you love with you wherever you go. Download now and get watching!

Never miss your favourite TV shows, sports team or breaking news with the DStv mobile app, watch live on your iPhone or iPad anytime, anywhere*; catch up on the latest episodes and movies or download to watch offline later.

The DStv mobile app gives you the freedom to watch your best-loved DStv entertainment when you’re on the move.

As a DStv customer you can enjoy all of these features on the DStv mobile app.
- Get it as it’s being broadcast, tune in and start live streaming selected TV channels via 3G/4G or WIFI.
- Missed it live, catch it later by watching a selection of the most popular shows, sport highlights, and movies on Catch Up.
- Don’t want to use mobile data to watch shows or movies, download up to 25 videos on Catch Up while on WIFI to enjoy offline later across 4 devices.
- Want your own experience based on the sport, shows and movies you like to watch, setup up to 6 different profiles to personalise each family members viewing experience.
- Want to plan your viewing in advance, add Catch Up items to your watchlist to come back to later or access the full 8-day TV Guide and set reminders to arrange your viewing.

- Need to keep the kids entertained, our Kids section is a safe and fun viewing environment with PIN controlled access so your kids can enjoy the coolest shows and movies unattended.

If you don’t have DStv, no problem, just download the mobile app, sign-up, and start watching our selection of free live streaming channels and video on your iPhone or iPad (available in South Africa only). As long as you’ve got the data, you’re good to go.

*Available in country of subscription. Viewing requires data / internet.

The Live TV and Catch Up content on DStv will be as per your DStv package and country. Due to geo-blocking this content can only be played within the countries in the DStv territories.
