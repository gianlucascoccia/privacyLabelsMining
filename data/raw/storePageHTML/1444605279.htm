Not a fan of the building system

I am only level 20 as of this review but I already dislike the building system. The fact that the fire station, police station, and all other municipal/services building have a specific grid that they cover makes it restrictive to build. Ever time I unlock a new building, I have to move things around to make them fit within the predefined building area.

It would be much better if there was a capacity requirement (10 building per police station for example) and the distance from the station only affects citizen moods and amount of taxes they pay. Maybe even have a timer before events like fire is resolved and during that time the fire fighters can address other issues. This way we can decide how many fire stations to build and where to build them.

Embewalker , 11/28/2020

Could be great.... Could be....

First off in order to not be waiting for several days to progress in this game, you need real $$$. You are expected to pour tons of real $$$ into your game. Then into the gameplay... You have now purchased diamonds and gold bars. Great now you can expand and grow! But watch out you won’t be able to move items you already have in your inventory without population restrictions or time to rebuild in the new spot sometimes quick but while waiting for these restrictions some of your buildings fall subject to plague or natural disasters. And If you already put some diamonds in to accelerate time for production within certain buildings that are now under attack from plague or natural disasters, too bad those are now gone and most likely the building will be decimated and will have to be rebuilt from scratch. Thus furthering you from the finish point on so many invested production times on buildings and resources. Again could be great. But too many obstacles to open up a fun enjoyable gaming experience.

Nostalgia

A fun throwback to the Caesar, Pharaoh, and Emperor games. It’s a good start with a lot of potential. Hopefully they’ll keep developing it. For now, I’d just like to know why industrial and agricultural buildings require coverage by a clinic; it’s a huge waste of workers.
