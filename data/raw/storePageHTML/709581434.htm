Description

Freddi Fish 1: The Case of the Missing Kelp Seeds

Join detective Freddi Fish and her best friend Luther as they take on their latest case: finding Grandma Grouper’s kelp seeds that feed the fish under the sea. Explore underwater caves, deep canyons and colorful reefs, and discover fascinating places like King Crab’s Castle and A Sunken Ship Out of Time, in an effort to save the reef.

Freddi Fish and the missing Kelp Seeds entertains children for hours as they explore the wonders of the sea, use logical thinking and reasoning skills to solve the mystery, and make unforgettable new friends along the way!

Most Nostalgic

This game was wonderfully done to work on my iPad Pro. It was just like back when I was 10 playing it on our old desktop box. Yes, the game only took 20 minutes to complete but to experience childhood again for $5, I would say it’s worth it. Now I’ll have it for my future kids too. 😂
