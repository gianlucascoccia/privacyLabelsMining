Not bad, but not great

I just took my NCLEX yesterday and I passed. But I only give partial credit to this app. The pretest, mini simulation, and posttest were nice features but the rest were garbage. Whenever I would try and update my graduation date and test date, it would blank out and get the dates all messed up. When taking the three included exams, I got between an 80-90% passing probability but when I did practice questions, their little “probability meter” was all the way down on the lowest probability and it seriously stressed me tf out. Let me tell you this right now, these questions are much more difficult than the NCLEX! I was in tears on Sunday while trying to study because I was getting so many questions wrong on this app. Questions that included things I’ve never even HEARD of while in nursing school. Do not let yourself get stressed out if you aren’t doing well on questions because the NCLEX questions are NOT this difficult. I switched to a different program and felt SOOOO much better about testing yesterday and went in to take my exam with much more confidence. All in all, the only reason I would recommend this app is for the pretest, mini simulation, and posttest.

We are glad to hear that the simulated NCLEX exams worked well for you. Thank you for taking the time to leave thorough feedback. We appreciate that. Congratulations!
