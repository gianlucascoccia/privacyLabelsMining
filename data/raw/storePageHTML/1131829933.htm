Description

Jungo is the only student-athlete college placement application and software platform focused on connecting the student athlete with the appropriate schools of interest. Simply put, we match players with programs that are an appropriate fit based on ability. Jungo provides the clarity so desperately needed in the college placement process. Jungo also supports youth development, player identification, and advancement to elite playing platforms:

A smarter way for players to receive timely ratings/evaluations from trusted coaches. Crowd-sourced coaches’ ratings create an accurate player profile that automatically matches and recommends level-appropriate college programs.

Supportive and time saving communication method for club/team coaches. Reduce the amount of time required to provide meaningful feedback to players and families, while minimizing the ongoing challenges associated with recalibrating expectations and recommendations to players for schools they should pursue, and simplifying communications with college coaches.

Focused recruiting and identification for college programs. A streamlined method to filter, identify, and focus recruitment on players that are an appropriate match for their program.

Player subscription upgrade provides full access to Jungo recommended schools based on coaches' ratings. Subscriptions are charged to the iTunes account at confirmation of purchase. Your account will be automatically charged for renewal at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. You can manage your subscription and auto-renewal by visiting your iTunes Account Settings. If you turn off auto-renew during the current subscription period, your subscription will be canceled for the subsequent period.

The Youth player subscription is $.99 per month and provides access to detail coach feedback, snap evaluations, Jungo's rating system, and development tools. The High School player subscription, for those players 14 and older, is $1.99 per month. It provides additional features to help players identify and research colleges & universities based on their level of play. Additional features include academic checklists, college recommendations based on overall rating, and college filtering/search functions. This is an auto-renewing plan. Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user's Account Settings after purchase. Any unused portion of a free trial period, if offered, will be forfeited when the user purchases a subscription to that publication, where applicable.
