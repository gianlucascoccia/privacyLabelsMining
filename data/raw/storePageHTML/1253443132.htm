Description

*Featured app of the day*

Astound helps you speak your best, so that you don't ever lose an opportunity because of **how** you said something.

Each day Astound provides you with a set of personalized warmups, including breathing, and vocal exercises, that you can watch where ever you are. Simply select your experience level, and one of the following goals:
- speak at a comfortable speed
- speak with clarity
- increase your speaking volume
- modulate your pitch to express emotion

Many users report feeling more confident speaking in front of others after just the third day of practice.

Astound helps you track your progress. Record your voice daily, and get helpful feedback and guidance on what to listen to.

-----------------------

*Content*
- Astound offers an extensive library of 100+ easy-to-follow video tutorials
- A 30 Day Challenge, which gradually strengthens your voice over time
- Byte-sized practice packs for specific goals, like preparing for an interview or presentation on stage, an important meeting or call, talking in front of a group, or even your next date.

*Features*
- Audio Voice Recorder
- Audio feedback and analytics on your speaking tempo, pitch modulation, and volume
- Create a playlist with your very own warmup exercises
- Reminders to help you build a healthy habit.

carsen b , 01/15/2021

Looking for this for years

I am a high energy, fast talking, at times lazy mumbling, think out loud person but let’s get to next topic.
I have been speaking fast all my life and dealing with clarity/articulation issues. Frustrated by the advice “just talk slower” (most frequently by mom).
This wasn’t working for my brain and is impractical advice, I looked for books and exercises to practice to improve my speech. Where are the exercises King George VI used?? 😂 How costly is a speech pathologist?
Thankful this app was invented for a common and overlooked challenge many have!! People have commented on how I am talking more clearly and definitely getting less “what?”. And I realized SO much more goes into clear communication besides “talk slower” e.g. pitch, lazy tongue, opening mouth more, etc.

Some of these exercises I have come across in books but having an app to track and demonstrate is invaluable. Many books/podcasts focus on public speaking or other focus. This breaks down where our speech is garbled in day to day communication and why certain problems occur. And something I think everyone would benefit from trying once.
Only minor inconvenience — It is a new app so crashes every once in awhile but it is very user friendly and continually updated, so not a deal breaker — especially when nothing like this exists or that I have found to help.

alicia728 , 12/03/2019

I do love the app itself but...

I wanted to train my voice to be deeper and more resonating so I’ve been using this app as a part of my morning routine. I do love the contents and could tell the difference almost immediately, as I got more conscious of the impact my body and mind can have on my voice. I also like how the app eases you into various techniques.

That said, the app needs to be updated more often. Due to some bugs, it keeps shutting down as soon as I click on the icon. There’s a bit of buffering for, like 3 seconds, and then it shuts down. This has happened before and also this last 4,5 days. I also don’t know why I get logged out so often as well as the routine I choose (e.g. “speaking in front of a group” or “articulation”) doesn’t come up as the very first thing on top. Last time I was on the app, the category I had downloaded didn’t play Day 2 for some reason and I was stuck in Day, even though I already finished that routine twice.

Given that it’s a training app that requires one patience and consistency to keep up, it’s so frustrating that technical glitches like these occur this often and in so many different ways to sabotage my desire to develop a good habit. Plus, I have a 6-month deal and it feels like I’m not getting the most out of what I paid for. I really hope they’re able to fix these issues so that it can become the greatest app that it truly deserves to be.
