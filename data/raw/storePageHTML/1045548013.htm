Description

"The Roomster App makes finding roommates and rooms to share easy." Search Roommates, Flat shares, a roomate, rooms for rent, spare rooms with a roommate or roomates.

More people around the world are choosing to live together and we think sharing a place can be a brilliant living experience and a great way to save (or make) money so we can all live better.

Founded in 2003, Roomster has made the experience of sharing a home easy & safe. Roomster is a trusted name for people to post & search Roommates and Rooms for rent around the world with other like-minded individuals. Roomster connects people at all price points in 192 countries.

-Choose from multiple social logins to create your profile and update roommate preferences
-Search for roommates based on personal interests and living preferences
-Roomster’s in-app messaging makes it safe and easy to chat with potential roommates
-Roomster serves up recent rooms posted by people searching for roommates
-Post your apartments, rooms & sublets within the app so matches can connect with you

With 24/7 customer service and an always expanding community of users, Roomster is the best way for people to monetize their extra space and make it available to a membership of authentic individuals. Roomster is like air bnb, but for longer stay.
