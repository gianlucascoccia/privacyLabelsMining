Description

This is car rental you will actually enjoy – experience completely hassle-free, flexible car hire with no ambiguous paperwork! Virtuo is the new generation vehicle rental service – book a car 24/7 for up to 28 days with our revolutionary all-in-one car hire app and drive away any time of day or night!

1. BOOK
Book and start your car rental with just a tap, whenever you need it

2. UNLOCK
No more searching for keys – simply open the app and unlock your premium ride with a tap

3. DRIVE
Hit the start stop button in the car and hit the road!

AIRPORT CAR RENTAL EASIER THAN BOOKING A TAXI
Skip the taxi and car reservation queues. Choose, book and drive away a pristine, best in class car all from the comfort of the app. Drive away hassle-free from the airport, train stations and key city locations throughout France! Conveniently save time with Virtuo’s service now in London, Manchester and Edinburgh, as well as across France, Spain and Belgium! More cities and countries coming soon!

LEADING TECHNOLOGY
Virtuo’s next generation technology allows you to complete the entire car rental process through the app! From searching through premium cars all the way through to unlocking and starting the engine – the whole process is done with just a few taps! Don’t forget all Virtuo cars also come fully equipped with Apple CarPlay, GPS, automatic transmission, Bluetooth, reversing camera, cruise control and everything else you need for a great trip

TRAVEL WITH FRIENDS AND FAMILY - SHARE THE ADVENTURE
Add up to four friends as co-drivers completely free! Trips out of the city have never been so convenient

BUSINESS TRIP COMING UP?
We put our customers first, ensuring every step of your business travel plan goes smoothly with Virtuo. Prices are all inclusive with no hidden charges and can be seen in just a couple of taps. Enjoy a seamless, convenient, premium service with Virtuo.

NO WORRIES
All Virtuo car rentals are fully insured with Personal Liability Cover; Theft, Fire and Damage Cover and Personal Cover car insurance! Our world-class customer service team is available 24/7 to help you have an amazing and completely hassle-free experience

PERFECT CAR FOR EVERY MOOD
Reserve a car in seconds via the app for any occasion – having the flexibility to leave and travel the route you want (and even extend in seconds), Virtuo is your partner for any adventure.

- Every day driving or carpooling
- Weekend road trips and adventures with friends
- Family holidays and vacations
- Attending a wedding with friends
- Business trips

CAR RENTAL IN JUST A TAP
Virtuo is the easiest and quickest way to get on the road. Download Virtuo today and get on the road in moments!

CHOOSE A BEST IN CLASS CAR – With built in GPS, Apple entertainment system, automatic transmission, free additional drivers and much more as standard, our range of best in class cars will wow you every moment behind the wheel

URBAN PICKUP – Find your car waiting for you at convenient, easy to get to, pick up points in city centres, railway stations and airports. Easier than booking a taxi!

DRIVE AWAY WITH A TAP - Our revolutionary 100% mobile technology puts your key conveniently in the app, allowing you to unlock and start the car in moments without worrying about where your keys are!
