Description

Pro features let you...
• Play engaging games and track data in them
• Use time saving tools for the monotonous tasks of therapy
• Sync content and data between devices & view/print data online
(Note: This is only available in Word Vault Essential)
• View 2400+ HD images and use Interactive activities
• Track Data for Multiple Clients
• Listen to and record audio
• Add Words, Pictures, & Audio to Any List
• Create Custom Lists

Developed by two ASHA certified Speech Language-Pathologists, Word Vault makes teaching all areas of speech and language easier.

It is the ultimate tool for SLPs, parents, teachers, and professionals to teach articulation, language, and social skills. It has 8100 words, 1800 phrases & sentences, and 115 stories of targeted practice for articulation.

Word Vault reduces wasted therapy time and is designed for both children and adults to quickly find functional words, phrases, sentences, and stories by sound, blend, place, difficulty, or concept through easy navigation.

For rapid drill, content can be used in list view. For more focused practice content can be used in single stimuli view to see one word, phrase, sentence, or concept at a time.

120+ categories of language building material, 3800+ minimal pairs and 295+ multi-syllabic words, and over 25 categories of social language content are also at your fingertips.

Need to send homework or share a list of words or concepts with someone? 1-touch email makes it a snap. Simply tap the email button to share a list.

Angelscribble , 02/05/2018

Great app but needs work!

This app is great for many things including articulation, language, phonology and pragmatics. I gave it 3 stars because I feel like for children some things in the app are too hard.

I do like the articulation part and that it has one word syllables, unlike Articulation Station sometimes has 2-word syllables. Maybe a feature can be used to have 1, 2 or even 3 syllable words for articulation, especially for more severe students.

When I do antonyms with my special needs students, I get frustrated that the pictures are not accurately representing the word (i.e. for open/close - they have someone closing a suitcase and for open, they have a hippo opening his mouth). I think that for an accurate and more clear understanding of the concepts being shown you should stick to one thing (opening/closing the suitcase) not two different pics that have no relation whatsoever.

I also use the minimal pairs and idioms.

Keep up the good work! It has a lot of potential.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for your feedback Angelscribble. So you know, most of the sounds in the Articulation Vault have 1-6 syllables. We are working on a solution to allow our users to flag us to let us know of discrepancies like the one you mentioned. We have a lot of work to do and appreciate your feedback. If you have any more, please email me at lukeb (at) home-speech-home (dot) com
