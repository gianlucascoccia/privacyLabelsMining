Description

Download the app, setup your profile and get your first raid done in less than 10 minutes. It's that simple.

The ability to raid remotely from anywhere in the world might be the biggest thing since the release of Pokémon GO. Now it doesn't matter where you live, you can join a party to take down that Tier 5 boss you've been dreaming about.

With GO Raid Party, we match people who need help with people who want to help, with a simple and easy to understand interface. This app is perfect for people who:

- Has plenty of raids around but nobody to raid with. Go host a room!
- Has a strong team but no raid around to join. Go find a room to help out!
- Wants to raid regional stuff. It used to sucks when you can't have Azelf/Mesprit/Uxie just because you can't travel. Now you can (when it comes back to raids, of course).
- Want to raid 24/7 to grind that hundo or shiny or dare we suggest, SHUNDO.

Download the app, setup your profile and get your first raid done in less than 10 minutes. It's that simple.

yogibare88 , 02/18/2021

Works as advertised

This app is great. I’ve been using it to do 5+ legendary raids per day. I host during local raid hours as well as join other raid groups during 9:30pm-7:00am while they’re aren’t raids locally. If you use the free version (like me) you’re competing with many people for 1 of 5 spots available. Premium users only can join during the first 10 seconds a raid becomes available, after that free users can join. Since there are so many free users competing for a spot within a second or two the raid is full. If you click “join” as soon as the timer goes from 9:50s to 9:49s, you shouldn’t have any issues joining after 1-2 attempts. With multiple legendary raids per minute there’s not an issue. Im not sure where the negative reviews are coming from? Just be quick on the trigger so to speak, if you don’t want to pay $...

(The only issues I have are; that “unless u pay”, u can’t filter out lower lvls, so occasionally you run into a situation where you join a legendary raid & the other players are all beginners. & premium service should be way cheaper.. I already have to buy remote raid passes, & the game itself is free, IMO this should be a one time fee of $5 or 1$-2$ monthly).

Developer Response ,

Thank you and good luck with the raids!

Frizzle Face , 08/27/2020

Donation

Thought I was using the app wrongly because I couldn’t find a raid for a couple of minutes - was about to give up, but a raid popped up, and we ended up winning it. I was so excited for the fact that my patience paid off, and that the app worked. The only thing I saw that made me uncomfortable was that the text is hard to read whilst using Dark Mode on your phone. Also, when I tap my profile, I can’t exit out, you have to press “submit” and for first time users, that induces heavy paranoia. Especially for an app where people depend on each other.

Other than that, I just hope a donation button gets added. I’d easily give up a minimum of $5 dollars just because he’s providing a free service. EZ pocket change for the developer.

Hi there,

I’m glad you were able to raid with our app. We’ll see what we can do to make text easier to read in dark mode.
