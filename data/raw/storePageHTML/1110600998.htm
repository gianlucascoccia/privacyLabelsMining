Description

Our brand new hero system is just around the corner! In the ever turbulent land of Nyria, war is here, there... everywhere. The people suffer constantly at war. In these, the most crucial of times, a group of heroes must rise up! This is a group of the most gifted people and the most magical of creatures! With their tremendous talent, they will turn the tide of battle or play a pivotal role in the development of your castle. Choose the heroes you yearn for in the new update, and have them join the fight against your enemies!

The Great Dragon casts his shadow across the land...
In Rise of the Kings, players from all over the world battle to rule in this epic real-time strategy game. Utilizing everything from the RTS, RPG, and MMO gaming genres, take your place in this epic game of war as one of many Lords fighting for survival in a hostile fantasy world. In this struggle between titanic armies, you hold the fate of your kingdom, your people, and yourself firmly in your hands. Tap and install this mobile game to build an army to clash with evil in a titanic online struggle of empires!

Rally your friends and engage in epic clashes against fearsome foes. Rely on your wits, utilize every bit of strategic genius you have, superior leadership, and cunning diplomacy, to rise to the top and claim the crown and the throne.

Download this sprawling fantasy RTS MMO today on your phone or tablet and begin playing online with players from around the world!

Amass Your Army

- Gather friends, old and new, and form a top Alliance to conquer the best new war strategy game available.

- Build your empire and train an army, then lead them in PVP war against friends, new and old!

- Train your army, lead them into battle, and crush your enemies.

Explore the Wild Lands!

- Stalk the monster clans roaming the lands and dungeons, and kill them to sharpen your skills and claim their treasure.

- Explore the dark dungeons inside the Ruins of the old Kings, and face down the Dark Guards to uncover the riches of the ancients.

A King will Rise

- If you love PvP challenges, then prove your mettle against the best Lords in the Realm.

- Grow faster, build faster, train faster, and then put it all on the line against the terrifying Orcs, the ghastly Grimm Raider Clan, and more as you clash with other ambitious Lords.

- Do you have what it takes to Rise and become King?

Great game! Here’s a suggestion to make it even better

Absolutely love this game! I find myself always on this game whenever I get the chance to play. I’ll literally wake up 5am to let my dog outside and play this game to kill time which usually ends up becoming a longer task. I think this is the best strategy games in the App Store. The gameplay is nice and I’m always looking forward to new things being added. However I think if they added a building in the castle where you can play a bunch of mini games would make a nice edition. This option would be a great way to kill time when your waiting on all your resources/troops etc to build up. Another thing I think you guys should consider adding is a prison in your castle. This option should be added so that every siege victory you go and do you can also take their troops prisoner and have them become yours instead of just killing off half your troops. The last thing that would make this game better is that if you guys added a guardian dragon in your castle. For this option there should also be Dragon Training in the institute so you can upgrade your dragon and whatnot. The concept of the dragon is basically to help with defense and attack whenever your in a raid. I design apps and I’m really hoping you guys consider my ideas thank you all for making such a wonderful game!

Ange7989 , 01/25/2018

Enjoyable

It’s got a massive amount of players that are active. I’ve progressed quite far, in game purchases are completely optional to play. The way the game is though, you want to spend a couple bucks (nothing major) but again, completely unnecessary to progress. There’s a built in translator so it makes it possible to communicate literally with people from all areas of the world which is AWESOME.

***Suggestion box***

I know they do read these, in the event that you read mine. I’d suggest one or two more events. How about something where for a couple of days specific monsters or rebel camps pop up throughout the map. I mean all over the map, not rare. They can be killed a certain amount of times before they level up (lv1 camp\monster zeroed teleports and changes to Lv 2 and so on) and they should drop decent rewards, of course some more rare than others. Each time they level up they increase in difficulty significantly. Again, JUST an idea. More events then the norm, it’s fun and engaging.

Charmingpoodle , 05/07/2020

The game itself it great.

I’ve played ROK for over 2 years. It was my first game like this. I loved the game at first. It was so much fun. The graphics are good, they do update a lot, add new things regularly, I rarely had trouble with bugs. Now for the downside. This game is all about money sadly. I’m not talking about a few hundred dollars. If you want to have any chance of competing you’ll need to spend 10s of thousands of dollars. I know lots of players that have spent more then 100k on their castles. If you have the money good for you. The problem lies in with so many people from different parts of the world in your kingdom, problems arise, civil wars, non spenders losing interest. A year or so into the game you realize there are only a few dozen die hard players left in your kingdom. So your kingdom can’t compete in KVK, you start to lose KVKs and more quit. ROK absolutely refuses to allow you to move your castle to a new kingdom. They promise kingdom merges but in 2 years they have had maybe 3 of them thats 18 kingdoms merged into 9. Out of over 400 kingdoms. Their solution is start over in a new kingdom and spend more money. It’s sad that ROK has run this game into the ground with their greed. If you play check out some of the older kingdoms and see for yourself. Many are dead or dying
