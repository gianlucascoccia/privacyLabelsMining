Description

Fittr is a community-based health & fitness app with everything you need to start your transformation journey; It’s like having your own fitness coach with a huge community supporting you wherever you go!

Personal Training
Our latest offering providing personal training for Strength & Conditioning,Yoga, Zumba, Martial Arts & more. Get trained by professional trainers with personal attention and workout from home.

Fittr Live
Workout sessions covering strength, bodyweight exercises, home workouts, yoga and interactive sessions with our coaches on queries regarding quantified nutritiion & exercise

Free Tools
Choose from a suite of free fitness tools including:
• Diet Tool
• Training Tool
• BMR Calculator
• Macro Calculator
• Body Fat Calculator
This one app provides you with all the tools you need to get fit and stay healthy.

Community
Join an online community of fitness enthusiasts & experts from all over the world. On Fittr, you can browse through fitness & nutrition articles, seek guidance from experts and peers and check out amazing transformation stories. Share your journey, get inspired, be a part of the Social Fitness revolution.

Track Your Progress
Good health is built over time. Keep tabs on the below & track your fitness journey!
• Daily Steps
• Sleep Tracker
• Water Intake
• Workout Volume
• Weight & Body Measurements

450+ Expert Coaches
To take your journey to the next level, enrol with one of our certified Coaches. Fittr Coaches will create a customized diet and training plan to suit your fitness goals and mentor you throughout your journey.

Healthy Recipes
Think going on a diet is boring? We have you covered! Fittr has 1000s of detailed recipes with step-by-step guides and complete macros and calories. Whether it’s breakfast, lunch, dinner (& yes even dessert!) we have recipes for every lifestyle and taste. Plus, new recipes are added every day.

Fitness Challenges
Have fun while you get fit! Participate in community fitness challenges, show off your skills and win rewards

Reward Points
Earn Fitcoins on the app and redeem them for exciting goodies on Fitshop.

Fittr app uses the Apple Health app to track sleep and steps data for sharing the Insights with coaches and you also can see it insights tab inside "My Plan" section.

Best app so far to get fit n for nutrition

I have been following diet n workout plans for five months. Fittr is a very unique app started by Squat team wherein you get a diet and workout plan as per your inputs while signing up. Most importantly it gives a document to understand the logic behind everything you need to know about your body. Anyone can access the app n start a healthy life by reading the default doc, following diet n performing workouts.

You will find numerous articles on fitness which not only clears your doubts but also enrich your existing knowledge again all free of cost. Anytime post a question and not only coaches will reply you ASAP but others members are also ready to answer.

You can enroll under any mentor but it is by choice. Without any mentor also you can make your own diet and workout plan which can be reviewed by mentors absolutely free. Such an amazing app with this freedom.
