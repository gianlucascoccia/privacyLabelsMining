Description

This is the simple, thoughtful app pushing the boundaries of what you expect from your calendar:

- Intuitive, beautiful views of days and months
- A single, unified view of events and reminders
- Powerful natural language entry to quickly add events
- History and context with any contact you're meeting
- Supports all major calendars: Google, Exchange, iCloud, and more

Berkthefirst , 09/10/2019

Great for organizing your life XD

I love this app, I went from a Samsung phone to an IPhone recently and was very disappointed with the default calendar app. It was mainly because there’s a very limited choice for calendar colors (for labeling different types of calendars: work, home, family, etc.) and this calendar app, having the choice of more colors, brings the potential of a whole lot more organization. I also love the ease of turning off other calendars within the app to view just one at a time. This feature then gives you the option of having “sub-categories” if you will, having the sub-categories of the larger category all in the same color, but on different calendars (hopefully that makes sense). It also syncs with Apple Calendar which is really cool, so everything still integrates with each other in Apple’s ecosystem (is that what you would call it? :/ ). I use this app for everything in my life from my schedule, to expenses, to even simple reminders. This app is all about organization, so if that’s what you need, your in the right place :)

Jpp6677425996 , 01/12/2021

Can you please update to fix reminder glitch?

I am very happy with how this app puts both my events and reminders on the same calendar, however I keep running into a bug when checking off reminders, and I’m very disappointed to see no updates in over a year given that I paid for premium features.

The problem I’m running into is when I get a reminder notification, I go into the app to check off the task and it gives me no visual feedback that the task is completed. The check box does not check. It stays empty. But the reminder gets completed. Since I get no visual feedback that checking off the task worked, I will often try to click it again. This means for repeating reminders, I have now accidentally checked off the next reminder and will not be reminded. This is a pretty glaring bug and I’m disappointed that it hasn’t been fixed in the year I’ve been using the app. I’m giving three stars for now because this really is functionally the app that I need for time management. But if this bug is not addressed soon, I’ll be deleting the app and updating with a 1-star review. Reminders are the thing that makes this app superior over the default calendar, so it’s unacceptable for them to be so buggy for this long without a fix.

MGO1963 , 04/11/2019

So Close to Being Great!

I love “pod” quite a bit. It’s simplicity, ease of use and intelligent design makes this app quite nice. It is really visually intuitive and appealing.
However- there are glitches. The “keep in touch with the people you meet” part of the app doesn’t work. You can link contacts and add participants but you cannot do what the preview slide suggests. Pressing on a linked or added contact does nothing.
I also don’t understand why there are two calendar symbols in agenda view. One is top right the other is bottom left. They both take you to “Today”.
Finally, it would be nice if we were able to link not just contacts with email addresses but those with phone numbers to events. And, in the contacts section it would make sense if we were able to access all contact information. Also- linking event address/location search to our contact list would be a great addition as well.
