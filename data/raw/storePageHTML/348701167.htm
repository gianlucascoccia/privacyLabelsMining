Lots of options

Good value. There are lots of options because you can type in anything and can use different messages and voices on all your alerts, messaging features, alarms & individual phone ringtones. I'd like more music options and the voices are a bit too computer sounding still but they are continually improving on that and they regularly update voices. I sent an email question and they responded within an hour and solved my issue.

JimWilson.BocaRaton , 09/02/2010

This App delivers exactly as Promised

Basically a 7 step process, 1) type your message you desired to be played over your phone's speakers, then 2) go online to the App's website and retrieve the audio speech file downloading it, 3) Open up iTunes, then play the file which places it into the ringtone directory, then 4) Sync iTunes with your iPhone, 5) then go to your Contacts select the contact name, edit it, 6) go to ringtone, select it, then choose your voice to text file, 7) and save the contact, and viola your done... Works great, and once you create a number of messages, and bring them all over together, it will be lots easier to edit the contacts with each ringtone voice to text file... JimW. Boca Raton, Florida.

TR911 , 01/11/2010

Ultimate Flexability

I don't write many reviews but the flexabilty of this app separates it from your run of the mill ringtones. I got this app and after taking some time to make the ringtones and assigning them to contacts I never have to guess who it is again. The other thing that I liked is that every ringtones does not need to be obnoxious - it can simply announce the callers name with as little fanfare as you'd like. What took the longest was deciding what ringtones to use for the various people in my contacts.

Overall, if you are looking for a very flexible app for ringtones, then I would highly recommend this ringtone app, you will quickly see how fun AND useful it is to use.
