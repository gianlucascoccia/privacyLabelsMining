Description

Booksy Biz is a smart scheduling and business management platform for solo entrepreneurs and shop owners with appointment-based businesses.

Take appointments online 24/7, manage your calendar, and grow your business - all in one place. Booksy Biz is a smart scheduling and business management platform for solo entrepreneurs and shop owners with appointment-based businesses.

What you get with Booksy:

- All-in-one Calendar Management: Manage your calendar, staff, and client list efficiently and effortlessly from anywhere. No more keeping track of availability or jotting down appointments on paper.
- More Work-Life Balance: Eliminate interruptions, phone tag, and after-hours calls and texts. Clients have 24/7 online access to your calendar, so you can actually clock off and recharge.
- Built-in Marketing and Promotions: Connect with existing clients and reach new ones. Send out Message Blasts with special offers and promotions, or rely on automated reminders to keep customers coming back.
- Custom Profile Link: Show off your unique style by uploading photos of your work. Build your reputation through verified client reviews. Share your profile link wherever you want clients to find and book you.
- Mobile Payments: Accept credit card payments directly from your phone. It’s easier, faster, and safer. Plus, track your cash flow and know exactly when you’re getting paid.
- No-Show Protection: Protect your bottom line and guarantee your income. Let clients Prepay for services and set up a Cancellation Policy to discourage no-shows and protect your time.
- Increased Visibility: Clients can book directly from their Booksy client app or the Booksy website, or they can book wherever they find you, including Facebook, Instagram, Google, and Yelp.
- A Full Calendar: Stay as busy as you want to be. Use the Waitlist feature or offer last-minute discounts and promotions to keep your calendar full.
- Booksy Boost: Grow your business and reach new clients with Boost. Unlock premier marketing tools, increase your exposure in the Booksy Marketplace, and drive potential clients to your business.
- Gift Cards, Packages, and Memberships: Encourage customer loyalty and increase your upfront cash flow with eGift cards. Drive additional revenue by offering bundled services and memberships.

Booksy also helps you follow public health regulations and safety precautions to protect your clients, staff, and yourself.

- Health and Safety Rules: Provide your clients with a fully customizable checklist of health and safety procedures, so they know their safety is your top priority.
- Mobile Services: Meet your clients where they are. Set a travel fee and service area. Get more flexibility and make your business more resilient.
- Online Services: When connecting in-person is not an option, supplement your income by providing online services from home. Show off your creativity with tutorials, classes, or workshops.
- Communication and Custom Forms: Let your clients know about the precautions you are taking. Set up a disclaimer and consent form for your clients to sign off on before their visit.

Booksy Biz has helped thousands of salon owners, barbers, stylists, nail techs, and more transform their businesses. But don’t take our word for it. Here’s a sample of what our providers have said after they started using Booksy Biz:

- They see 20% more bookings per customer
- They cut interruptions in half
- They are less stressed about appointment scheduling
- Booksy Boost providers see a 60% increase in bookings
