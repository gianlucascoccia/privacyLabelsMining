meaper222 , 01/24/2021

Worth playing

It’s a very fun game, and having Connor as the face of the game makes it all the more enjoyable when he starts talking about dream chasing in that immediately recognizable voice. However, the AI of troops and heroes is probably the dumbest I’ve ever seen in a game like this. So many times I’ve thrown my phone across the room because the hero or my troops will literally go out of their way to go attack a building that they should not be targeting because there are more valuable buildings right beside them. I watched my Volta walk around a core to attack a blacksmith and it cost me the 2 star. I was at a loss for words. Also , the UI in the alliance and how the alliance description is displayed is pretty pathetic and really shows a lack of care for the little things by the development team. And my last problem is definitely not my last problem, but it’s ahead of the rest of them on the list of game breaking problems. I have changed my base, and upgraded buildings and after I close out of the app, the last 30 minutes of what ever I was doing is lost when I get back on. This game is fun, as all games are that follow this scheme, but it’s got so many bugs and inconveniences. The development team definitely should of spent more time tweaking the game for quality of life reasons and to make sure it’s even playable. The game is literally unplayable very rarely but more frequently than I’ve seen in any other mobile game that I’ve played the last few months.
