Description

The Traeger App puts a world of flavor at your fingertips. Control your grill anytime, anywhere with WiFIRE®, access over 1500 wood-fired recipes, learn from the pros, and receive curated content that helps you become a better cook, all right from your phone.

Featuring:
• WiFIRE® Control: Adjust grill temperature, monitor internal food temps, set alerts and timers, & more directly through the App.
• Learn From the Pros: Detailed videos featuring recipes and tips & tricks put you in the kitchen with your favorite pitmasters and chefs.
• Over 1500 Recipes: Access amazing wood-fired recipes that cover everything from BBQ to baked goods.
• Personalized Content: Set up a profile with your skill level and personal preferences to receive curated content that suits your tastes.
• Follow Your Favorites: Never miss a new recipe from your favorite pitmasters & chefs.
• Shop: Order pellets, sauces, rubs, accessories, and everything else the Traeger website has to offer right from the App.
• Dealer Locator: Find dealers near you for all of your Traeger needs.

Skip the essays

You ever try and google a recipe and have to sit there and scroll through a 10 page essay on the history of the authors grandma and the passage of her secret baked ziti recipe just to find out you don’t have an emulsifier (what even is that) and you can’t do it. So it’s on to the next essay just to try and find an ingredients list.
Well those days are behind you now. Just a picture of the food and a name saying “hey, does this look bomb to you? Here’s how you make it.” The app is extremely intuitive and user friendly. Hide what you don’t want to see, open what you do. Save recipes that you want to come back to later. I save my recipes and go to the store with nothing but the Traeger app. Just open up the recipes I want to make as I shop and look at the ingredients. Easy. I went from cooking almost nothing to doing ALL the cooking in my household. Every night before bed my wife prays and thanks Jesus for inventing the Traeger because now she doesn’t have to cook anymore. 10/10 would recommend.

Do not buy a Traeger!

I hope this review saves someone time and money. Buy a Green Mountain Grill, you get more for your money and the WiFi actually works!

I have a Pro 575 with “WiFire”. The grill by itself work fine, but the additional $$$ for the WiFire (which never works) is a complete waste. I believed Traeger’s advertising about the WiFire capabilities and was I miss led. The WiFire has been nothing but disappointments since the day I assembled the grill. Traeger customer care has not been helpful. I have over 30 emails back and forth trying to figure out the issues, they say “oh, you just need to update your app/grill”, “your signal strength is to low”, or “can you move the grill closer to your router?” The app/grill is up to date and the grill is less that 10’ from my router and is separated by a window! The customer care rep that I last spoke to on the phone didn’t even know what their app or grill menu looked like or the options for either of them!! How can you help someone with a product you don’t know anything about?

If you want to pay to much for a pellet grill buy a Traeger, if you want to pay even more for a WiFi pellet grill (that doesn’t work) buy a Traeger. But if you want to save money, get more, and have a WiFi grill that works, don’t buy a Traeger.
