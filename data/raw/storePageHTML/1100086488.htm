Description

Currently, Youtube is not available.
And sorry that it's not sure when the fix can be ready.

Youtube video. 100K radio stations. mp3,, flac, opus, ogg, ape file play. 10 bands/30 bands EQ, Bass, Treble. Music alarm, Sleep timer.

Music is memories.
Reminds of the old memories, and creates new memories.
HighStereo will lead you to the beautiful memories.

HighStereo looks like a classical audio components.
It gives you the high quality sound as well as the beautiful interface.
Enjoy the luxury high-end audio in your hands.

- Support most of all audio formats, such as mp3,flac,ape,opus,ogg ...
- Support internet streaming radio including Icecast and SHOUTcast
- Support m3u and cue playlist files.
- Support m3u, xspf, asx, wpl and pls playlist for Radio.
- 10 bands equalizer
- Bass
- Treble
- Sleep (Sleep with music)
- Alarm (Wake up with music)
- Mood sound mix (at the beach, in front of the fireplace, with birds singing, in the rain... )
- 0.3x ~ 4.0x play speed
- Folder management for files and Youtube videos.
- Add, move and delete songs in real time, while you're playing them.

*****************************************
In the case of YouTube playback and Apple Music,
Bass, Treble and the equalizer do not work.
*****************************************

ilmwahp , 10/06/2018

Great app

I’ve tried at least a dozen internet radio and DNLA apps and this is the one I use the most. Pros: 1) ability to manually enter stations by IP address. 2) Equalizer!!! Free!!! Not only can this be applied to internet stations, but also to the music on your device, and music streamed over a home network; AND it works when sending to Bluetooth devices. I don’t know of any other app that does that. Plus, multiple memories for equalizer settings. I have separate settings for my wired headphones, wireless headphones, earbuds, and Bluetooth speaker. Cons: 1) limited search function for internet stations. 2) No online account to save your list of favorite stations. When installing the app on my iPad, I had to painstakingly manually re-enter a long list of IP addresses. This was really aggravating in a previous version of the app that would lock up on certain stations found on the built in search function, requiring force closing then re-installing the app, loosing all the stations. Fortunately the current version has been stable. My one request is for a way to export your station list to use when installing on another device
ps. On and off the app asks you to write a comment, blocking you from accessing your station list. Even though I upgraded to the paid version, and sent a comment, it is still doing this. So downgrade 1 Star.

Developer Response ,

Thank you so much for your sincere advice. In the next version, I will add an option that you can export and import the favorite radio station list. And about asking review, I'll also study the way to avoid harassing users, I can't give up itself though.... Thank you so much!

Promising but not quite there

Love the look of the app and the music sounds great but getting to the music on my iPad is clunky and unintuitive, I really had to dabble with the app to get there.
