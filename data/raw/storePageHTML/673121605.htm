Description

Incredible on-the-water travel experiences start with GetMyBoat. Find boat rentals, charters, tours, lessons and adventures in any worldwide waterway. Discover the beauty of the marine environment in your next vacation destination, book yachts for events, paddle your local rivers, or organize a sailing getaway. Browse faraway destinations or places nearby and get out on the water, or start earning money as a captain, guide or host.

BOOK WATER EXPERIENCES

GetMyBoat makes water experiences accessible to everyone. Whether you want to snorkel with sea turtles in Sardinia, kayak in California, catamaran in Croatia or bachelorette party in Barcelona, we make it simple to find and organize your next adventure on the water.

POPULAR DESTINATIONS

USA & Canada - Wakeboard the Great Lakes or cruise the coastline, from Seattle to Miami, San Diego to NYC and everything in between.

France & Italy - Rent a RIB on the French Riviera or tour the Amalfi coast in a Motor Yacht.
Greece & Croatia - Sail the Sea of Crete or kayak on River Korana.
Caribbean & Mexico - Go sports fishing in St. Vincent or host a dock party in Cancun.

FOR TRAVELLERS

Getting on the water is easy:

• 130,000 water experiences in 184 countries.
• Search any destination worldwide or browse nearby.
• Message the owner directly to plan and price your experience.
• Compare quotes and options.
• Book and pay securely online.

FOR CAPTAINS, GUIDES, AND HOSTS

Start making more money for your business or offset costs of your privately owned boat.

• Easily create a free listing. Describe the experience with photos, pricing, and features.
• Travellers will find and contact you directly with dates, group size, and other essential information.
• Customize the pricing and details for each booking on the fly.
• Use built-in messaging to chat and manage your bookings.
• Quick online payments with built in fraud protection straight to your bank account.

POPULAR ACTIVITIES

Every type of water experience for every occasion.
