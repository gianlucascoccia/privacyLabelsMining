Description

Match! Tennis Tournament Manager is a mobile version of the Personal Tennis Tournament Organizer from matchtennisapp.com. 

If you are like us your life is very busy. Short on time? Turn hours a month into minutes a month managing your players tournament life and college recruiting process, all directly from one place! Want your player to be recruited by U.S. College Coaches? Match U is your answer!

Tournament Management: Match Tennis App turn hours into minutes each month managing your tournament life!
• View all tournament (UTR, USTA, ITF, ITA) applicant and competitor lists and sort players by rankings state, sectional, national, UTR rankings… (Saves Hours)
• Take scouting notes on every player you ever play for the next umpteen years (Yes your little black book…invaluable for post-match reflection and pre-match reference before you play your opponent!)
• Search for tournaments, view their details, and add them to your personal tournament calendar for the entire year (UTR, USTA, ITF, ITA)
• Track your players monthly ranking progress with visual graphs (UTR, USTA, ITF)
• Receive automated ranking alerts to your email every time your players ranking changes (so fun to see that ranking move up…not as fun when it goes the other way)
• Never miss a tournament deadline again with automated email notification reminders (We haven’t missed a deadline since)
• Find doubles partners for tournaments using our doubles pairing service (at all MTA sponsored tournaments and growing…is completely privatized)
• View your complete player record…
• And MUCH more….


College Recruiting: Who knows where to start? We didn’t! So, we took it upon ourselves to help by holding your hand all along the way helping to make sure you give yourself the best chance to be recruited for college tennis…it’s easy to get lost in the process… so Match U provides a pathway and helps keep you on track!

• Search all U.S. colleges that have tennis teams
• Search and view which teams you could possibly play for based upon your academic metrics and UTR rating
• Create your dream top 10 school list that compares your current metrics and needed metrics to play for these teams! (Match U keeps you motivated and on track working towards your dream schools)
• View each schools details, such as contact info, enrollment, tuition cost (in state and out of state), website links and more…
• Our auto email alerts for the following individual college deadlines (Admissions Applications, Early admissions, Financial Aid Applications, SAT and ACT Tests, International Applications, Housing and more) keep you on track helping ensure you stay on track to getting into college!
• View academic admission standard requirements (SAT, ACT, avg GPA) for each individual school
• Contact tennis coach directly through Match U (logs entire email history between you and coach and great to review when making your college decision)
• Keep journal notes for each individual school (From 9th-12 grade you might take dozens of notes on your schools of interest…you can review them all to help you make your final decision)
• Use our starter email templates to help you send college coaches the info they need and want about you!
• Match U is a tennis parents college scholarship recruiting management tool!

Be careful with set-up

When I first set this up it was during my kid’s tennis tournament. I had some issues and called them and they helped. Somehow during this 2 accounts were setup. This app is awesome! I'm so excited about it ...and so thankful it is now available. It has saved me tons of time already.
