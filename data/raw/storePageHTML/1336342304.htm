Mincci983 , 12/24/2019

M

I’ve been playing this game for more than a year bow and I can actually say this game makes me want to spend money on the game without needing to. The main thing I have to say is that the graphics are great and the gameplay is really fun. There are a few issues I have with the game, such as the game crashing on specific story stages and unbalanced matchmaking, but those are issues I feel can be fixed or at least balanced in the future. The way some of the valkyries’ fragments are time-gated feels really slow, but it’s not that bad. I highly recommend this game for those who are just looking for a game to enjoy for a long time. I’d also recommend that the developers make a way for you to choose a focused supply list, because if you don’t get the list you want, you have to wait a week or two for it to reset and the process can repeat. It’s not that bad if you take constant breaks from the game, but it’s something I wanted to point out. I’d also reduce the cost of supply crates because the actual rate that you earn crystals is pretty slow. The energy system is pretty inefficient for starting players that barely have a stamina pool to begin with, as the rate at which you gain stamina is way too slow, so I would increase the rate at which you earn stamina or just remove the stamina system all together. Theses issues don’t necessarily ruin the game in any way, they’re just minor inconveniences that I’ve noticed at I played the game.

CosmicEngine , 11/23/2018

First review...

I’ve never felt the urge to review a game before, but this one has frankly AMAZED me. I didn’t know that these kinds of things were possible in a mobile game, but... yeah. The graphics are awesome, the voice acting (although it is often in a language other than English, it is excellently subtitled) performances are great, there’s so much to do here. An excellent single-player story. Co-op play. Open-world exploration. Anime-style FMV cutscenes. Engaging story with episodes still coming out and many more on the horizon.

I can understand why some might spend a lot of money on this game to get things, but I don’t think it’s necessary to do so in order to enjoy it. I’m going to spend money on this game, although I haven’t yet and I’ve been playing for over two months. I’m not going to spend that money because I need to buy something to “win” or have fun, but because I just got an “achievement” that told me I’d spent over 500 hours “in adventures” - and I know I’ve been doing stuff in the game that didn’t contribute to that counter. Anything that can give me over 500 hours of enjoyment is well worth my money. It’s well worth a LOT of my money, and I’m really just amazed that they’ve been able to set it up so that I could reach this conclusion on my own, and I want to reward them for that.

This is a really good game. I think you’ll enjoy it - but you don’t have to take my word for it, it’s free! Give it a try and see for yourself.

Wow, at first this didnt look that amazing. But when i started playing it it really was amazing! The graphics are great, its not a mainly pvp based game (which i love!), and the gameplay is awesome! What i dont like is co-op. I like team games, and this one is awesome, but i would like if you could play with people closer to your level.
