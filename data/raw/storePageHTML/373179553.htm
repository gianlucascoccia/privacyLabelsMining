Description

OVERVIEW
Shopping your faves just got faster and easier. Not a Rue La La Member? No problem. It’s free and you can join quickly right from our award-winning app. With exclusive sales, delivery alerts, order tracking, and Boutique reminders, you’ll never miss a stylish beat.

TOP FEATURES
+ Exclusive sales: Get special access to app-only sales. (Make sure you have push notifications enabled to stay up to date. For information on how to turn push on, visit https://now.ruelala.com/turn-push-notifications/.)
+ Shipping perks: Get 30 days of unlimited shipping after you make your first purchase.
+ Delivery alerts: Track all of your orders and get real-time notifications when they arrive.
+ Easy checkout: Check out in just one tap. Also, add new credit cards with our easy-to-use credit card scanning functionality.
+ Boutique reminders: Add upcoming sales to your device calendar so you don’t miss out. (App-only feature.)
+ Shop by category: Filter sales to shop by brand, color, and size. Sort them to shop by best sellers or price.
+ Syncing across devices: Add a product to your bag on one device and your cart stays with you on all of your other devices. (Perfect for shopping in bed on your iPad or on your iPhone while on your way to a meeting.)
+ Social sharing: Show your friends those sought-after heels you just bought via Pinterest, Facebook, Twitter, email, or SMS. They’ll thank you for it.
