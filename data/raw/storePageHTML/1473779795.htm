Description

Amazing Keyboard Fonts for iPhone! Over 100 Fonts that work in Instagram, TikTok,YouTube and ALL text app. No no copy & pasting required, type directly to other apps.

Fonts
Fonts provides the most popular fonts which you can simply select in the keyboard: cursive, gothic, bold, italic, upside down, bubble, and so much more! You can use it in all social media apps and texting apps to make great content and be unique when chatting with friends.

Symbols
Over 300+ symbols, characters and shapes including animals, arrows, stars, geometric shapes, constellations, etc to choose from. No more copy and paste from different apps. All the editing can be easily done in the keyboard.

Emoji & Text emoji
use emoji as fonts, make chat more fun with creative emoji pattern and cute text emoji!

Quote
Fonts has over hundreds of quotes templates to help you express yourself and create text stories in the easiest way ever!
