Description

Calling all eagle-eyed fans of Hidden Object Games! Play Pearl's Peril, the stunningly beautiful Hidden Object Adventure Game set in the golden age of mystery and adventure!

Join heiress and ace pilot Pearl Wallace on a round-the-world hunt for clues as she unravels the mystery of her father's apparent suicide. From New York City to the heart of Africa, follow Pearl into deadly danger as she strives to unmask the real killer and reveal their villainous plans! Plus...

Renovate and redecorate the Wallace family's exotic estate on the private Polynesian island of Artemis!

Uncover a diabolical plot that touches everyone in Pearl's life, and could even topple nations!

If you love Hidden Object Games, Pearl's Peril is the mystery you've been waiting for. Stand side by side with trailblazing 1930s heroine Pearl as you explore hundreds of exquisite hand-drawn scenes and exotic locations. Meet and make friends, and test your skills against a huge international community of Pearl's Peril Adventurers.

Your adventure begins here!

A NEW WAY TO PLAY
Pearl’s Peril is growing! Play Iris's Eyes to match objects to their silhouettes and unlock exclusive island decorations!

HUNDREDS OF BEAUTIFUL SCENES
No other Hidden Object Mystery can match Pearl's stunning hand-drawn scenes. From the streets of 1920s New York to Paris, Polynesia and beyond, an exotic world of glamour, mystery, adventure, and romance comes lavishly to life.

A GRIPPING STORY
Epic adventure, mind-bending mystery, and heart-stopping romance are woven into the fabric of Pearl's Peril, for a rich, rewarding story you won't forget.

UNRAVEL THE MYSTERY
You'll need a keen eye and a sharp mind to unlock the secrets of Pearl's Peril and uncover the mastermind behind her father's untimely fate!

YOUR PERSONAL PARADISE
Welcome to Artemis Island, the Wallace family's private estate. Build and decorate to create the island getaway of your dreams!

If you love Hidden Object Games, Pearl's Peril is the mystery you've been waiting for.

neversit88 , 08/24/2018

Don’t spend a dime. Fun, but has its problems

It’s a very fun game. Based on that alone I would give 5 stars. However, started playing this game when I was sick as something to do and for anxiety. I noticed when looking for objects, at times they just weren’t there, until you click on the help button. I took a screen shot and sure enough object was not there, then I hit the hint button and all of the sudden it appeared. It’s a lot of fun, but if you don’t spend money, you can’t play. 1 energy every 20 minutes. At least give enough energy to clear a scene. It’s easy to get wrapped in and just hitting a button to buy the daily deal or cash, but it adds up. Over 6 months, its embarrassing to see how much I spent on this game. If you have anxiety, you could buy a low end treadmill that still gets the jog done, or join a luxury gym and pay in full the first year, or ( if you are into gaming) you could buy the newest xbox or ps for the amount of money spent over the course of a 4-6 months. I don’t like video games, I did like this app, but felt cheated a lot. When I bought a pack of 30 energy, I found that that I had to use more energy to play the game. So now I am back to running and feeling good. If you love it and don’t mind spending the money or mind you are being taken advantage of at times, then it’s for you. Personally, I flushed hundreds of dollars over the past 6 months. Approaching a thousand. I deleted the app just consider that money wasted “stupid tax”.
