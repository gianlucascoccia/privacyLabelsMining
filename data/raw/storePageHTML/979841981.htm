Description

Gstring——The most professional chromatic tuner & guitar learning app. Excepting for the traditional teaching, this guitar lessons bring the guitar beginners the best experience on how to learn guitar well on base training, playing skill, tabs exercises and guitar chords library, is an effective shortcuts for guitar beginners to become masters.

Gstring Core Functions_
【Guitar Tuner】
#Instruments#: Suitable for several instruments like guitar, piano, ukulele,bass, violin and so on
1.Guitar Tuning Modes: General Tuning、Lower Tuning、Higher Tuning、Dropped Tuning......
2.Piano Tuning Modes: all transposition
3.Ukulele Tuning Modes: C Tuning、C Tuning (Linear/Low G/Tenor C)......
4.Bass Tuning Modes: Standard、Whole Step Down、Drop D、Drop C、Drop D/G......
5.Violin Tuning Modes: 4.Standard Tuning(Violin)、Tenor Violin(Violin)、Cajun tuning(Violin)......
#High Accuracy#: Professional chromatic tuner that can accurate to 0.01 cents
#Tuning modes#: Auto mode & manual mode
#Left-handed mode#: Meet different requirements of guitar players
#Other parameter#: Pitch(A4), frequency(Hz), temperament, notation, etc.

【Guitar Tabs】
#Professional tools#: Metronome, speed and melody
1.Metronome: To master the sense of rhythm and speed
2.Speed: Adjust the difficulty of guitar tabs and so does the learning’s
3. Melody: To grasp the position of each scale as well as the tempo
#Hit songs#: All from the most popular song list, including every songs you want to learn
#Smart tabs-reading#: Appropriate tabs-reading interface that especially designed for iPhone and iPad, suitable for every guitar players to read
#Classification#: Elementary, advanced and hard levels that specially customize for all players

【Guitar Lessons】
#Course system#: To lead you from a guitar beginners to a master
#Learning modes#: Tabs mode & game mode, always follow you as a “guitar teacher”
#Playing skills#: Quick-learn tutorials on guitar playing skill
#Pro tools#: Met. & speed
#Accuracy analysis#: To make a great progress via the accurate system calculation

【Guitar Chords】
#Comprehensive chords#: over 5000 chords can be searched in one click
#Transposition#: One-click transposition, the fastest chords calculation
#Strumming pattern#: Unlimited patterns that easy to learn and play
#Chords memory#: To grasp chords through funny chord games

【Base Training】
#Scale memory#: To grasp scale positions and improve fingers’ flexibility
#Finger independence exercises#: To improve the fingers’ independence, coordination and strength
#Classification#: Easy, advanced and hard levels that help you to do well in guitar playing.
#Professional tools#: Met. & speed
#Learning modes#: Tabs & game modes
