Description

Prizensta is the all in one solution for Instagram Giveaways!

Find participants and winners for your Instagram Raffles fairly.  
Gain Followers! Boost your Likes!

1. Rules :
- Following
- Like contest photo
- Like extra photo
- Comment
- Tag friends in comment
- Remove unfollowers and much more...  
- You can also make partnerships with another Instagram users to run a raffle. 

Prizensta finds everyday your participants automatically and notifies you.
Set your winner count, find winners and give your rewards!

2 . See who have unfollowed you as long as you have Prizensta Pro (even when you do not make raffle).

3. You can create unlimited contests as long as you have Prizensta Pro.

4. You can customize your own caption easily before you publish a giveaway post or any post! Prizensta offers you caption template for giveaways and shows you best hashtags to add to your caption!

5. New Feature: You can now easily define extra entry count for a specific rule and give extra entry to participants you want.

6. New Feature: You can see the counts of your gained and and lost followers everyday in a graph!

---------------
HOW CAN I MAKE A GIVEAWAY?

- Prepare first your giveaway post and publish it on Instagram. You can publish photo, video or caroussel photos.

- Select that post on the first screen of Prizensta. Select the same rules as you have written on your Instagram post and click 'Create Contest' button.

- Prizensta will send you everyday your participants count via notification.

- When the raffle time comes go to the second screen on Prizensta, click to 'Start Raffle' button and go to participants screen.

- Select how many winners you want and click to 'Find Winner' button.

- You can select recording feature to save the screen cast in your device.
