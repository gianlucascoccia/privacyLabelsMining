Description

If the app does not show on your Watch after purchase please go to App Store on your watch (not phone) - scroll to bottom - Account - Purchased - and download from here.

Create custom cryptocurrency complications for Apple Watch.

- Support for 150+ cryptocurrencies including all of top 20 (as of last update, by market cap)
- Add multiple complication configurations, and set any of them as a complication, support for multiple different complications at the same time
- Select what you want to see on complication: price, market cap, 24h change, 24h volume, currency name (select 2)
- Support for all watch faces and complication slots
- Data can be displayed in 35 currencies ($, €, £ and many more + BTC, ETH, gold)
- Summary of all configured cryptocurrencies when you open the app
- Set colors, separately for both lines (only for multicolor watch faces)
- Option to hide currency symbol
- Complications update every 30 minutes, if it's not enough you can open the app any time to see the most up-to-date data
- Standalone watch app means there is no iPhone app, you can configure everything on your watch
- Market data powered by CoinGecko API
