Description

With this App at your fingertips, Europe is at your feet.

We’re Ryanair, so of course you’ll find the lowest fares in Europe on our app. But you’ll also get handy extras like being able to check in while you’re on the move, a mobile boarding pass delivered straight to your phone and a choice of in-flight extras in one quick click.

So why are you still reading? Download it now and get flying!

Update 3.75.1 has rendered the app unusable (iPhone 6 iOS 12.4.4). It stalls and then crashes within 1 minute. I have contacted Ryanair support but nothing seems to have been done. Old review:
I have been flying Ryanair for many years.
There have been rough times with them in the past (as it is with most airlines), but I have been most impressed with their new direction over the past year or so.
This app shows that they are serious about keeping their customers and keeping them happy.
It is easy to search, choose, and book a flight using only the app. You can get your boarding passes and keep them on the app or onto Apple's wallet.
Checking flight status is quite good, but I'd prefer to have push notifications of when a flight lands or takes off.
Otherwise, I have been impressed and content with this app. It doesn't tax the memory of my phone, it is simple, functional, and quite easy to use.
Thank you all for keeping this app a top notch app.

Upset Customer

Absolutely horrible airline/service. I tried checking in on my phone two days before and then 1 day before and it gave me the same message saying that I had to print the boarding pass at the airport. So I went to the airport to print it and they said it cost €55 to print it. That makes no sense because they didn’t say anything about a fee online. Therefore, I explained this to the manager at Nice Airport and he was so rude to me and said I had 1 minute to pay because he was shutting down the boarding process. So I ran through security to my gate, only to find out that the boarding process has not even began and our flight (without prior knowledge) was delayed. I am sitting here waiting for the plane (FR 1959) to leave Nice and arrive in Dublin. However it is 9:35 (the time the plane is supposed to leave) and we haven’t gotten any word when we might leave. I am disappointed in the flight, the airline, and the staff. The manager at Nice was so rude to me and I never want to fly RyanAir again.
