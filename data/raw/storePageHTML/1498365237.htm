Description

Built specifically for Apple Watch. App brings you tons of beautiful, fun, unique, easy-to-install Watch Faces.

* AR Preview Watch Face on your Apple Watch
* Scroll through collections of beautiful Watch Face particle design
* Add particles Settings, you can select Color and Texture
* Add your Photos
* Add Gallery of beautiful wallpapers
* Add Gradients background
* Add background Color
* AR Preview Watch Face on your Apple Watch
* Easily install images on your Apple Watch
* Install an animated Watch Face and see your Apple Watch display come to life every time you look at it
* Update every month

Create your own unique combination of particles and background colors!

Watch Face cannot be repeated can only be shared.

Animated designs are sent to your Camera Roll & can be installed in seconds, bringing a new level of life to your Apple Watch display.

If your iPhone is already connected to your Apple Watch - Watch Faces can be up and running in seconds. Create particle design, choose a Watch Face, click and add (or customize if you want).
*** Animation limited to 2 seconds on Apple Watch!
