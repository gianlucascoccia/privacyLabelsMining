Description

Read the latest issue of Swipe for a look at the best web apps out there – plus our view on what the future holds for iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

Get more from your iPhone, today. Swipe is a unique digital magazine designed to help you discover the best new apps, the most helpful iPhone and iPad tips, and the latest iOS news.

Our aim is to help you get the most from your Apple device. They’re capable of so much more than you could imagine – if you only know where to look. That’s why we pack every issue with reviews, features, and tutorials from our friendly team of experts.

To read full issues of Swipe, you’ll need a subscription. But even non-paying users can browse the contents of every issue and even preview articles to get a feel for the magazine before committing.

Breaking news stories posted to our regularly updated news feed are available to everyone, ad-free. It’s a great way to keep tabs on the latest iPhone and iPad news, and it’s completely free – even if you’re not a subscriber.

But if you do subscribe, you’ll get new issues of Swipe fortnightly, plus unlimited access to our extensive back catalog. All for less than the cost of a nice coffee per month.

There’s a free trial available, and you're free to cancel any time if you change your mind.

SUBSCRIPTION PRICING AND TERMS

・A Swipe Subscription provides access to all issues of the magazine, past and present.
・New issues of Swipe are published every two weeks, with the exception of the Christmas period where we take an extra week off between issues.
・Newer issues (March 2020 onward) are accessed in the app, while older content from the archives is available in PDF format.
・A monthly subscription to Swipe is priced at $1.99 USD / £1.99 GBP.
・A yearly subscription to Swipe is priced at $19.99 USD / £19.99 GBP. (Around 16% cheaper).
・Note that pricing may vary based on your country of residence based on fluctuating exchange rates.
・Subscription payments are charged to your iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase.
・Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user's iTunes Account Settings after purchase.
・Subscriptions automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period.
・ iTunes Account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period, at the monthly rate at the time of renewal.
・No cancellation of the current subscription is allowed during the active subscription period
・Any unused portion of a free trial period, if offered, will be forfeited when you purchase a subscription.
