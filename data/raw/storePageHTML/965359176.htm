Description

In 2015, we started Minder with the intention of helping Muslims all over the world get married. Since then, we’ve helped match tens of thousands of people with their spouses. We loved being Minder, but we needed a name that better reflects our mission and values. Today, we are happy to welcome you to Salams! Along with our name change, we’ve added a suite of new features like voice notes, Islamic priorities, and other enhanced profile features to further enrich the experience. We are excited to start this new journey with you.

-------------------------------------

THE MUSLIM MARRIAGE APP
Join almost 2,000,000 (yes, really) other single Muslims looking for their spouse on Salams (formerly Minder)! Featured on the New York Times, Vice, Washington Post, Daily Beast, Der Spiegel, and more! Salams is the popular approach to finding a great Muslim spouse.

EFFICIENT
Who has time to go and meet everyone your auntie recommends for you? With Salams, you only spend time on the people you like.

SAFE
We only display your first name, and the pictures and profile information you provide. You can block any user from seeing or chatting with you at any time. All profiles are verified by a selfie and GPS location.

FUN
Why does finding a compatible spouse have to be such a negative experience? Minder lightens the mood and makes it fun.

COMPREHENSIVE PROFILES
Profiles on Salams include everything you want to know about someone including their sect, education, career, height, prayer levels, and more!

HALAL DATES? PHONE CALL WITH HER DAD?
Muslims are diverse and everyone has their ideal marriage timeline and process. Whatever it is, you’ll find users on Salams that meet fit your process and what you’re looking for in a partner.

HOW IT WORKS
Finding someone is as easy as swiping! Swipe right if you are interested, swipe left if you are not. Your swipes are anonymous and you are only matched with someone if they swipe right on you too. When you match, we’ll notify you both and you can start chatting!

LIKE, MATCH, AND CHAT ALL FOR FREE
Salams (formerly Minder) is free to download and use for all of our main features. However, if you’re looking to get more out of Salams (formerly Minder), you can subscribe to the optional Salams Premium. Prices and features for Salams Premium are clearly displayed in the app. Some things to keep in mind if you decide to subscribe to Salams Premium:

* Payment will be charged to your iTunes account at confirmation of purchase.
* Your subscription will automatically renew itself, unless auto-renewal is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
* Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.
* You can manage your subscriptions and turn off auto-renewal by going to your Account Settings in the iTunes Store.

PRIVACY
We take your privacy VERY seriously. We don't post to your Facebook page and we don't share your personal information with other parties. You may delete your Salams account at any time and all of your user information and conversations are instantly deleted.
