Description

Breaking down barriers to make Maps for Every Hunter.

Uncomplicated offline GPS maps geared for the western hunt and encrypted for your eyes only. Potential is yours to create. We’re here to Elevate Your Instincts™.


WHY FREE?

With the Scout To Hunt App the Essential Map Features you depend on are Free Forever. We find these features to be just that, essential. We believe every hunter should have easy and free access to them. We have taken meaningful steps to provide you with Free full-access to the Essential Map Features that you expect.


TRUST IS ENCRYPTED

We trust the tools we depend on and believe you should too. We ensure that the GPS location data of your Markers, Smart Tracks, and other personal map data are encrypted. In other words, your location data becomes hidden from anyone who does not have access to it – including Scout To Hunt. It's your information, and we keep it that way.


WESTERN HUNT SPECIALIZED

Scout To Hunt has taken a specialized approach in solely focusing on the western big game states to provide phenomenal detail, insightful data, and enhanced performance. We currently specialize in Arizona, Colorado, Idaho, Montana, Nevada, New Mexico, Wyoming, and Utah.


▶ HuntReady Offline GPS Maps™

• Living Map Automation - HuntReady offline maps autonomously stay up-to-date and maintain reliability and accuracy, even after they’ve been saved. You’ll only need to download it once, forever.

• Tip: Download an entire Hunt Unit (GMU) while online and you’ll never have to worry about forgetting to save a map when you go offline or happen to lose service. Don’t ever go unprepared, get HuntReady from the start.



FREE FULL-ACCESS ESSENTIAL MAP FEATURES


▶ GPS Tracking

• Tracks your duration

▶ Visually Optimized Layers

What’s New

Feb 5, 2021

Version 1.7.46

New offline map Layer: Elevation Bands

(Just in time for shed season!)

Scout To Hunt's Elevation Bands layer brings science and simplicity together to make your decision-making a breeze.

The Elevation Bands simplify how you determine your target areas to scout and hunt. More importantly, the Elevation Bands organize your own data. Adding a powerfully visual perspective, patterns and trends of your own Markers and observations from the field stand out with eye-opening insights.

Elevation Bands are filterable elevation ranges segmented by color-coded elevations. This layer allows you to take control of your map to see the big picture like the animals do.

Ranges:

» 10,500 ft
» 9,000 - 10,500 ft
» 7,500 - 9,000 ft
» 6,000 - 7,500 ft
» 4,500 - 6,000 ft
» 3,000 - 4,500 ft


"It helps me know what elevation the animals are at that time of year and what elevations I do best at." - Architect, Scout To Hunt's Insider Build Program

If Elevation Bands are making a difference for you out in the field, let us know with a review!

Good but not great

I had no idea about this company until I visited the hunting expo in feb 2019, after speaking with the scout to hunt team these people really enjoy the company and the product they create. All that being said here comes the novel of a review. The desktop version is way better than the app, the app is nice as it is in the early stages, but there are a few bugs to work out. I’ve only played with the app for a few minutes but I already ran into a snag, some of the labeling on your trail cam photos is wrong, I want to see bull elk not a cow moose and vice versa. The zones don’t have terrain view or 360 view of the area. You have every single picture of the same animal which is nice as more info is better, but it seems your wasting space keeping 20 slightly different photos of the same animal in the same position seconds apart. I would like to see these things changed. I’ll let you know if I find anything else as well.
