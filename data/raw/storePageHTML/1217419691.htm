Description

Play & learn! SKIDOS is a collection of safe & fun learning games without ads for 2-11 years olds. Over 2 million kids use SKIDOS to learn math by playing games they love

Welcome to SKIDOS! Let your 2-11 year olds enjoy our wide range of fun learning games.
SKIDOS games including Brainy City help kids master their mathematics skills and develop their interest in coding. You may subscribe to SKIDOS from within this app or you can log in to an existing account to get started.
SKIDOS subscriptions are auto-renewable with monthly & annual plans for 6 users. So, 6 kids of different age (2-11 years olds) can learn and progress in their own individual learning style, adapted to what grade they are in (1st,2nd,3rd,4th,5th grade).

BRAINY CITY:
Get ready for the smartest adventure of your life, play Brainy City Rush now. Hundreds of wild animals escape the zoo and bring chaos to a big and bustling city. Only you can help them survive new adventures and get through the city safely.

RUN as far as you can and DODGE all obstacles. The animals in Brainy City need your help to sharpen their math and coding skills.

Once you jump into our crazy, colourful 3D world they'll collect coins by avoiding moving cars, alligators, bulls and many crazy obstacles. Then test and develop your coding or math skills to unlock new characters and power-ups.

Every right answer will win you the coins you need to complete over 1000 challenging levels!

DEAR PARENTS & TEACHERS,

SKIDOS apps are designed first as super fun games that children already LOVE playing. Then, we integrate the interactive learning content into them. That’s why kids ACTUALLY love our games.

In this updated version, we added much more playfulness and even more interactive ways for your kids to strengthen their math skills! Our improved math questions are now more visual and dynamic, for a more engaging and fun experience.
Kids love playing games. Why not teach them the logic behind their favorite games? With SKIDOS games, they can get inspired and interested in programming from a young age.

LEARNING THROUGH PLAY
Kids LOVE mobile games. We LOVE building games. All SKIDOS games are predominantly designed as fun engaging games and then integrated with interactive learning content, specially made for the age of 4-11 years olds.

CUSTOMIZED LEARNING AND PRACTICE
Kids have different learning styles. All SKIDOS games give children the option to pick their subject (mathematics or coding), grade (1st,2nd,3rd,4th,5th grade) and topic, and progress with problem-solving.

START CRACKING CODING AND LOVING MATH
All learning content in SKIDOS games is aligned to the national standards for Mathematics and covers a comprehensive list of topics.

These include number sense, addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division as well as fractions, decimals, geometry and so much more!

For kids who are interested in coding, SKIDOS has developed a unique curriculum that teaches them concepts around basic programming and computational thinking at a young age.

All SKIDOS games are COPPA and GDPR compliant and have no third-party advertising.
