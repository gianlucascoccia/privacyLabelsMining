# Introduction

Simple and cute weather forecast application with pixel style :)


# Features

- Display current location weather, including temperature, relative humidity, probability of precipitation, precipitation, wind direction & speed, UV index, pressure;
- Display future 12 and 24 hours weather forecast;
- Display future 10 days weather forecast;
- Display current location pollutant, including AQI (Air Quality Index) / PM2.5 / PM10 / NO2 / SO2 / Ozone / CO;
- Display and change Celsius temperature and Fahrenheit temperature;
- Display current location detailed street or city information;
- Support global cities and POI weather;
- Support widget;
- Support iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch;
- Support Arduino Uno and Nano devices as Personal Weather Station.
