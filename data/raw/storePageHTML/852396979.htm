Description

The only official FIA WEC® App.

The only official FIA WEC® App.

The FIA World Endurance Championship: A global competition played out in nine different countries on four continents, including the world famous 24 Hours of Le Mans and a further eight 6-hour races in Europe, North and Central America, Asia and the Middle East. The cream of top motoring manufacturers – Aston Martin, Audi, Ferrari, Ford, Porsche and Toyota – as well as a host of legendary motorsport names, competing in the ultimate test of man and machine to become world endurance champion.

So, welcome to the 2019 WEC season! The official WEC App, launched three years ago, is designed to bring what you, our fans, want direct to your iPhone, iPad and iPod Touch. Download this App for free and keep up-to-date with all the news from fiawec.com and more: results, circuit information, team and driver information, timetables, calendar, pictures, interviews and videos from behind-the-scenes, highlights, live timing and social network links. The whole Championship at your fingertips.
Use your dedicated Crossplateform account to connect on other devices or other plateform.
To immerse yourself even further into the FIA WEC® experience get the Premium Packs available as an In-App purchase. It will allow you to follow FULL LIVE streaming of each race and replays* as well as FULL DATA TIMING, exclusive content with on-board cameras and the possibility to personalize and follow your favorite drivers and/or teams.

(*) Replay are only available on the APP (not on the website) for 3 weeks or until the next event qualifying practice.
