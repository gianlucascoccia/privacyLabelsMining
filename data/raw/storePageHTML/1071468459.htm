Description

Speak with confidence. Public speaking practice and instant feedback.

Have you dreamed of giving your own "I Have a Dream" speech? Ever feel like anxiety has gotten the best of you when you were on stage? Speeko will teach you the core speaking techniques to achieve effective delivery of your message when it matters most. Use the voice recorder to practice for an interview, a debate team event, or speaking off-the-cuff (like giving a wedding toast). After that, you' get instant, personalized feedback on how well you did, including how quickly you speak, how dynamic you sound, and a filler word counter (your um's and ah's). The more you use Speeko, the better it gets. Speeko intelligently recommends lessons and exercises based on your performance. Our coaching library is full of tips to help you speak your best.

Speeko is the world's smartest personal speech coach to help you improve your professional speaking style.

Holt Hanley , 12/04/2019

If you want to improve your speaking... get this app!

This is probably my favorite app I have ever downloaded! I literally use it every day, and I love going back and listening to old recordings to hear the progress I have made. My favorite part of the app is the Random Topic generator because it forces you to think fast, and allows you to review your results every two minutes, so that you can instantly apply what you should be doing better in the next speech.

To the developers:
First off... well done.
Second, it would be cool if the performance review metrics had a number attached to them. I like to really dive into the reviews to figure out what I can be doing better, and it would be nice if they had an exact score. Then with those scores, you could have a number tracking your overall growth. Just a thought, but at the end of the day, great app.
