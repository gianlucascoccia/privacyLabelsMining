Description

Connect your iPhone to the Blue&Me and play your favorite music directly from the car’s speakers without wires or other adapters.
And with the "FullStream*" addOn you can listen the audio from Spotify, Apple Music, Youtube, Netflix and more...

Adjust the volume directly from the car radio, it is compatible with most of the volume controls on the steering wheel.

Easy to use.
User interface simple and fast, excellent to use in the car.
Impeccable integration with the iPhone’s media library and with the Blue&Me Infotainment System.
"FullStream*" addOn compatible with +90% of the apps available in the AppStore

At this link an example of use:
http://youtu.be/ZCscDQYU0Os

Because some technical limitations the audio will be mono. The Blue&Me System doesn’t implement the ad2p protocol and it effects the audio’s quality.

* FullStream addOn is available as in-app purchase
