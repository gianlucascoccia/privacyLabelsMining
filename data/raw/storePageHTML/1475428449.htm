Description

94FEETOFGAME Basketball Training App offers an inside look into the exact training drills and workouts Phil Handy has done with the likes of Steve Nash, Harrison Barnes, Jewell Loyd and a variety of other stars.

Phil Handy is known as the “OG” in basketball for a reason when it comes to basketball training. His training techniques are imprinted on the game of basketball that we all know, watch, and love today. The app was designed for basketball coaches, trainers, parents, and players of all ages to train just like a pro athlete anytime, anywhere, at home.

Phil Handy has always promoted the fundamentals for the game of basketball and strives to pave the way for the next generation of players, trainers, and coaches.

BALL HANDLING
The app offers over 60 unique ball handling drills and workouts used by the pros that can be practiced at home to elevate your game to the next level.

SHOOTING
Learn the expert advice Phil Handy has shared with his players to make them the most unguardable athletes on the planet. Shooting drills, shooting workouts, and shooting tips shared directly from Phil Handy.

DRILL OF THE DAY
Receive 1 unique new basketball drill to practice each day on the app. The drills will focus on ball handling, shooting, footwork, finishing, post moves, breakdown handles, and more.

STRENGTH & CONDITIONING
Access a library of over 50 unique strength and conditioning drills and workouts to help take your game to the next level. To become a complete athlete, one must also focus on at-home workouts. These drills focus on agility, balance, core strength, durability, flexibility, and explosiveness. Learn the proper way to train to increase your vertical leap and speed on the basketball court.

DEFENSE
Basketball is a two-way sport. Defense is crucial in the game. Phil Handy teaches you a variety of defensive basketball drills that will help you become the best defender possible.

AT HOME WORKOUT LADDER SERIES
At home basketball cardio workouts. Phil Handy challenges you to complete the first basketball fitness ball handling workout. Follow along and complete the entire 17 drill basketball circuit. This is a great way to improve your ball handling, but also your cardio at home.

COACHING TIPS
Phil Handy gives basketball coaches and trainers personal tips and advice on how to become a better coach on and off the court. Learn how to develop the proper pregame mindset going into a game, in game preparation, and more directly from Phil Handy. As a coach, take a look at the drills inside the app to create basketball workouts for your players.

94FEETOFGAME basketball skills training is for use by any basketball player who wants to improve and become complete. The focus is on instructing basketball players on how to be skilled, crafty and FUNDAMENTALLY SOUND. It is the mission of Phil Handy to provide the best basketball training workout program to fully meet the instructional needs of all coaches, trainers and players. In addition to providing an excellent value, the basketball training program has an unmatched basketball atmosphere that will add enjoyment for you in using the app.

Here’s your chance to train and practice with Phil Handy who has trained/coached some of the greatest players to ever play basketball. The 94FEETOFGAME app is for any player, trainer, coach, or parent who wants to gain detailed knowledge on the game of basketball.
