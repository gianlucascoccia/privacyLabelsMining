Description

Keep track of your unpaid and overdue invoices, bank account balances, profit and loss, cashflow, bank reconciliation, bills to pay and more.
Run your business from anywhere with confidence. Choose when you work, not where.

** Great Features**
- Invoices
- Quotes
- Contacts
- Bills
- Bank reconciliation
- Spending
- Bank account balances
- Profit and loss
- Cashflow

Manage Invoices - Avoid cash flow problems for your business, by staying ahead of unpaid and overdue invoices, view outstanding invoices and payment history at a glance

Automate your CIS Calculations (UK only) - Xero calculates the right amount of CIS deductions on your invoices the moment you create them. It's all automated, so you can get back to business.

Reconcile Bank accounts in a flash - Start the day by knowing exactly where your business stands. Reconcile and categorise bank transactions within seconds

Get paid faster - Create professional looking invoices with ease. Edit, preview and send via email or a messaging apps of your choice

Get more clients - Convert quotes into invoices at a tap of a button, without needing to re-enter information

Manage contacts - Add individual details to personalise contacts and view useful insights, including average days to pay, along with invoice and bill activity

Stay on top of your bills - Quickly and easily create a record of money you owe and attach source documents, as a reminder of what needs actioning

Monitor spending - view a record of what your business is spending instantly in one place and make informed decisions about your expenditure

Reporting on the go - get visibility of all your bank account balances, profit and loss, cashflow, outstanding invoices, bills to pay and much more right on the dashboard

Everyone has a better half, and in the app’s case, it’s the desktop version. If you need to do the more complicated stuff, take it to the desktop.

Xero ​is a beautiful, easy-to-use global online platform for small businesses and their professional advisors. Helpful App

As a Xero Advisor, I’ve found this app quite helpful. It’s handy to be able to sit in a waiting room and still reconcile clients accounts. It’s also very helpful to be able to generate invoices and access important files on my phone. The app does occasionally stumble. As a Xero Advisor, my clients contact me with questions and I’ve never had to contact support about the App.

Developer Response ,

Hi Taylored Books - thanks for your feedback and explaining how the app is adding value to your business. Regarding app stability - we are constantly monitoring and addressing performance issues to ensure our customers get the best mobile experience with Xero.

Dustin949 , 06/10/2018

Finally

I’m a CPA with 20 years experience and specialize in financial systems applications. Xero is fantastic and this App does what it is supposed to do and it does it very well. With my clients and myself, I use this App to enter receipts, reconcile Bank and credit card accounts automatically fed from the bank, and issue invoices to clients. I also use it to upload files to my Xero file storage. I don’t use this App for financial reports, adjustments, or other things it does not allow (of course) but I do everything else in Xero cloud/browser. Xero is very intentional in its design. Learn it and you’ll love it.

LSTNJ , 05/24/2019

US Payroll shutdown - Time to move on

Their payroll was what kept us using Xero, it was really awesome. Now that Xero has shutdown payroll, quickbooks may just be where we need to go. I totally lost confidence on the company at the moment our payroll was pushed aside somewhere else. Besides rearranging top menu UI on the desktop, no new things being developed or meaningful improvements in years, same old, same old. Users feature request have been seating for years. This app particularly hasn’t seen any breakthrough update since it was launched.

Developer Response ,

Hello, we retired our built-in US payroll in favour of a tight integration with GustoHQ for full service payroll and HR, and of course we understand some users are upset by this - we did it because we had feedback that our US Payroll solution was not full-featured enough.
Our most recent releases for the app have contained some features that users are saying they love - like adding bills to pay (and attachments), converting quotes into invoices and showing outstanding invoice totals in the Contacts view, to name just a few. Make sure you're using the latest app version and then check them out and let us know what you think.
