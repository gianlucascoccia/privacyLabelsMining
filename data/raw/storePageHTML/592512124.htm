Description

What would you do with 2 extra hours in every day?

ZZTasks is is committed to helping you build the 26 hour day. The earth's rotation around the sun doesn't allow a literal 26 hour day, but what if you became so productive that you felt like you had an extra two hours per day? This is the ultimate goal of ZZTasks. With this robust technology you’ll be spending minutes instead of hours on your task list, making each second count as the clock ticks away each day. With your daily to-do’s being plotted out, no time is wasted. Once you've finished all you need to do, you may find yourself with a couple extra hours to do as you wish. Maybe even catch a few extra ZZ’s.



  ***** Creating Tasks is Easy! *****

  You start by creating and customizing your life’s categories— Business Catalyst, Fantastic Parent, Patient Advocate, Healthy Eater, whatever works for you. Then you can:
1. Add new tasks to your categories
 2. Prioritize tasks using customizable priority systems
 3. Manage tasks with all of the features you expect:
* Due dates
* Reminders
* Notes 
* Repeats
* Delegate and email
* Private tasks 

 ***** Sync in Real-Time with GMail or Exchange! *****

When you purchase a Pro subscription, you’ll see where ZZTasks really stands out from other, more limited “to do” apps; it syncs with GMail, Exchange, and ZZTasks.com--one planning system, one master task list, everything in one place wherever you go.


Purchase a yearly subscription to ZZTasks Pro Services via In-App Purchase for $19.99 (US) with automatic yearly renewal and receive real-time sync plus other features, like delegating tasks and the ability to have private tasks and notes. Purchase a subscription to ZZTasks Premium Services for $39.99 (US) with automatic yearly renewal and receive all the benefits of a Pro subscription plus longer storage of previous tasks, and COMING SOON, the most engaging delegation feature of any app on the market.

No Support. Lost all of my data

I am a long term Franklin Planner user and was hoping this was a good application to manage my tasks. I’ve been using it for a year and like others have had numerous occasions that the website is down and you have no access to your planner.

Despite the average performance, I decided to renew since I had all my data (300+ tasks) in the system. I renewed through iTunes and the website did not recognize the renewal. When I was finally able to log into the website….all my tasks were gone…even though they were on my iPhone app. I’ve been trying for three days to get support to address the problem. Franklin Covey support refers this to the third party developer who has been non-responsive.

UPDATED: January 16. After two years of lost data, down websites, no customer service, and quite frankly no FRANKLIN COVEY ownership of the app, I downloaded SWIPES.

Do yourself a favor and try SWIPES. Hands down a much better product.
