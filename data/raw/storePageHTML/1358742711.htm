Description

Match pieces and solve puzzles to design your dream restaurant mansion and garden! Manor Cafe is the home for tasty recipes and interesting characters!

- Swap and match items to beat uniquely designed match-3 levels!
- Show your design skills and renovate the restaurant with awesome decors!
- Uncover and be a part of an exciting story with many interesting characters!
- Unlock tasty foods and learn new recipes!
- You can play Manor Cafe offline!
- All this fun-filled adventure is FREE!

You had one dream when you bought this old and outdated restaurant mansion: bringing its full glory back by decorating and re-opening all the beautiful parts like the bar, dining hall and the garden. Meet Meg the restaurant manager and Bruno the angry chef and be a part of their gripping life! Play and beat match-3 levels and earn stars to decorate your cafe. Uncover the intriguing story as you move, meet exciting characters and unlock tasty foods! If you’re a fan of puzzle games, house and kitchen design games, Manor Cafe is just the game for you!

Matching items has never been more fun! Among all the match-3 games, Manor Cafe has a special place with tens of powerful boosters, super power ups and unique storyline, funny characters and the lively atmosphere of an open restaurant! You’ll never live a dull moment in the crazy world of Manor Cafe! What makes it different than other cafe games and match 3 games is not only the exceptionally gorgeous decor items or the delicious food menu, but also the unique gameplay that merges match three and cafe makeover!

Nymomnfla , 09/18/2020

Best Game Ever

I’ve been playing this game for a month before I wrote my review on Manor Cafe. I cannot express enough the joy in playing this game and decorating the manner. Let me begin by saying first thing you need to do is join a team to reap the rewards of playing as a team member. You can share lives at no cost and receive lives from teammates. You can play the side games along with regular playing and receive rewards and when teammates also play the side games and win the entire side game you get a prize as well. You get a chance to play for daily coins that’s in addition to all the other levels. There are more than plenty of chances for rewards such as coins, flags for the yacht race and decoration stars. The levels range from easy to super hard but can all be completed and great rewards are offered along the way of playing and winning. I have never played a more enjoyable game that is free to play. I’ve decorated 3 different rooms in the manor so far and I’m offered several choices of decor items to pick from. It’s fun and relaxing. I never liked playing a match game but this is a must play. I’m glad I’m playing it and have made several friends along the way. Enjoy playing, relax, have fun and say hi to your teammates. A+ game!

Major Diva # 9 , 05/28/2019

Love It; but please make changes

I love the game and I enjoy playing it. I wish that there were more ways to earn coins and boosters. If I’m playing to replace wallpaper and it calls for 2 stars, I would love to play a level where I will actually earn 2 stars if I win. Also, some of the levels are harder than others and I run out of moves and boosters too soon; I wish more boosters were available on harder levels. I don’t like the fact that the bottles are so difficult to get to. It takes too much effort and not enough coins and boosters to win. I have already been spending my money to purchase more; but this is too much money to spend and still run out of moves, coins and still lose. Lastly, to play on or add more coins is too high.... 900 coins to play on and if you lose with the five moves; the cost to play on increases. Please consider giving free coins on a daily basis, providing more options to receive stars, increasing boosters and lowering the coin cost to play on and adding boosters. I love it but I’m spending too much money to not reap the benefits of spending it. I’m considering deleting it.
