Description

Monitor your home from anywhere in the world with the Lorex Home app. Watch live HD videos and get easy access to recordings on your compatible security camera. With Lorex Home, receive instant motion alerts and notifications, so you can see and respond to anyone on your property.

With Lorex Home, you can:

• Enjoy easy setup and control of your camera
• View live video from multiple cameras remotely
• Playback recorded video to review events
• Listen and talk using compatible security cameras
• Program warning lights and activate siren on compatible cameras
• Record videos or take snapshots directly from your iOS device
• Configure advanced motion detection functions
• Configure camera or system settings and push notifications
• Enjoy professional-grade features

Compatible with select Lorex HD Active Deterrence security cameras and solutions.

Overall good and responsive

I’m not sure why there are so many bad reviews on this App. I recently purchased the smart NVR with 8 IP cameras and it loads all the cameras very quickly and in HD quality with no lag. Coming from Swann where cameras would take long to load and had to watch in low quality stream, Lorex is way better. I’ve only had this for about 3 days so will update my review as I go. I also like that I can export video and clips from app to my phone where I can email or share.

UPDATE
Everything I mentioned still holds true after couple weeks. I do have to lower my rating to 2 stars because the Smart Motion detection is useless. I’ve done everything correctly, and after selecting my area/parameters to be alerted as an alarm and get notification, I’m getting over 100 false alarm notifications when nothing is even moving or it detects a small squirrel outside my selected area as a vehicle... being alerted when someone is near my property is my biggest concern. Lorex please adress this issue in a firmware update, I love the system and picture. P.S I have 8 cameras set up correctly and have the issue of false alarms on all of them. Selecting your area for motion is completely ignored as of now in the lorex system.

unitedappblows , 03/04/2021

Lots of lag and frozen screens

I purposely waited to write this review because I wanted to give the app a chance. I have two systems in two different households so I think I’m ready to do this.

What most need to know is the playback function (speed and fluidity) is based upon your internet connection. Whereas, the faster your connection, the better the viewing and playback speed.

The app is full of features however much of it relies upon curiosity of “how it works and why it does this or that”. One thing that seems to be reoccurring is when you change the order of of cameras, the app does not hold that order, it changes back to whatever it had previously (before you closed the app).

Lastly, the “lock ups”’are driving me crazy. Often when just viewing a camera, it locks the screen AND my iPhone. This happens on 3 different phones so I am quite sure the issue is not with Apple.

Overall, you have no other choices with it comes to lorex and the app. Will it work to show young playback? Yes. Will it show you live video when you receive an alert? Yes...kinda. By the time you receive the alert, start the app, what ever caused the alert is probably long gone.
