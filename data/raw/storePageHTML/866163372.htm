Editors’ Notes

The best thing we can say about Resy is that while we were checking it out, we ended up with four separate dinner reservations. Maybe it’s the high-quality photos, the local review snippets, or the curated nature of the restaurants showcased—but something’s definitely working.

jillybean1966 , 06/25/2020

New menu items, great service and beautiful outdoor seating!

Cadence has done an outstanding job at navigating their business through the pandemic. We had dinner last night in their outdoor space, all socially distanced but still feeling normal, almost like an European outdoor cafe. What what also nice is the new and updated menu items. First, Robert's updated red sangria mix is DELICIOUS. It's not the typical watered down wine with fruit juice. He soaks his fruit in the wine and adds brandy, like real sangria recipes. I really enjoyed my drink. The scallops appetizer has been updated and has a nice summer fresh taste with sprouts and grains. We tried the pork pillows and was that a fun dish - a lighter version of pork rinds with a delicious dipping sauce. We hope Cadence continues this expansive outdoor seating for years beyond the pandemic. It was a perfect evening of outdoors, great service and superb food.

MtnMamaL , 02/23/2020

Great Waitlist Notification Feature in Otherwise Crappy App

The “Notify” feature is awesome and I was happy to find that I was automatically booked - didn’t have to worry about missing the notification.

But then I needed to reduce the number of people (no option for that in the “Change” option, only allows you to change date or time.) Then I went to call the restaurant to tell them, there’s no “call” button or prominent phone number. Then I clicked on the picture/name of the restaurant in my reservation and it only showed me options to invite guests or navigate to it. Then I went to my favorites to click on the restaurant there but there are no hyperlinks, just a list of the names of the restaurants and the option to “Un-favorite” (what good is that?) I then went to the app home to search for the restaurant name and click on that. Buried in the restaurant description is the phone number, but the text is rendered such that my iPhone doesn’t recognize it either as a phone number to call, OR EVEN TEXT THAT I CAN COPY. I had to find a piece of paper to write it down. What a crappy app! Writing this all out I’m going back and taking off another star.
