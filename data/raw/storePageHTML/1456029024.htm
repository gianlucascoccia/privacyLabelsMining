Over 1,000,000+ Poshmark items have been shared through ClosetMate! Start sharing smarter, not harder.

Make more sales on Poshmark by staying active without staying in front of your computer! ClosetMate helps you share your closet to your followers or to parties while you're on-the-go so that you can focus on other things that matter to your customers.

While you're thrift shopping for new clothes to add to your closet, with one tap you can share your entire closet. While you're taking pictures and listing your items, with one tap you can share your entire closet. When you're on your way to the post office to ship orders, with one tap you can share your entire closet. You get the picture! With ClosetMate, you can easily incorporate the tedious act of closet sharing into your already busy schedule.

Still not sure if sharing your closet actually helps increase your Poshmark sales? You can also use ClosetMate to see trends in your news feed! Easily compare your news feed activity to previous timeframes to see the change in your community shares, likes and followers. Use the charts and data to make more informative decisions on what works for you.

Features:
- Easily filter your closet and select the items you want to share (or select them all) with a single click
- Sort and share you closet in reverse to keep your current closet order
- Customize the amount of time between shares and the number of times to share
- Share items in smaller groups with a customizable delay to avoid automatic post bundling
- Automatic party closet filters to easily see and share the items that fit the party guidelines
- Analyze trends (community shares, likes, follows) from your newsfeed
