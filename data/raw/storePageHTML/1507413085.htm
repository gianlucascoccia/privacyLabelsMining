Description

Watch Together at home. Watch shows, concerts, movies, and any other video channels together with your friends and start to video chat with them!

CuddleTube is a platform where you can enjoy your watching time with friends together. Except for the great shows, movies, and any other video channels, you would also be able to see your friends' reactions to the videos you love or hate.

Enjoy face-to-face time and your favorite videos with your friends and families. You don't have to go to their house and watch shows, movies, and any other video channels and be physically together.

Use your special word for the room name, enter the room name and share it with your guys, invite them to join the room and start to watch shows, movies, and any other video channels. Laugh or have a roast for the show, whatever you do during your Friday video night with friends, do it online on CuddleTube!

Don't like the shows on the play? Paste the video links into the box and start to watch your favorite shows! Ask your audience about how they feel about the videos you are playing. You can select from different topics including MTV, Fun Video, Movies, Game, Food, Workout……anything on YouTube, you could watch it on CuddleTube with your friends!


Highlights:
- Watch videos with your friends together
- Customizable name of your room
- Enter a YouTube link and watch the video right now
- Push the button and talk to your friends
- Video or voice chat with up to 16 friends at once in your room
- Share your room on Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter and invite your friends
- Beauty mode during the video chat

Share your room name with friends, be yourself with your favorite people. Let‘s watch together on CuddleTube!
