Description

MediaFire lets you easily upload all your files - like your photos, videos, music, and documents - and access them from anywhere with your iPad, iPhone, or on the web.

MediaFire makes it easy to share your files too! You can share with email, text, iMessage, Facebook, Twitter, or just copy a link to share anywhere!

MediaFire Features:
• 10 GB of space - free!
• Earn up to 50 GB of free space
• Keep all your important files at your fingertips.
• Create and manage folders of files.
• Share files on the go via email, SMS, Facebook, Twitter, Weibo, or copy and paste links.
• Quickly search all your files and folders.

Securely store, organize, and share all your media through your iPhone, or iPad. MediaFire keeps all your photos, videos, reading material, work docs, spreadsheets, presentations, and even your homework in your pocket, ready for you to view or share anytime.

Apps needs the option to share files from other apps

I simply love the simplicity and intuitiveness of the MediaFire app on iOS but one feature that is seriously lacking is the ability to share files and content from other apps to the MediaFire app. I have to go to other apps like google drive and Dropbox for this functionality. mr3j , 06/07/2020

Several issues to point out...

Cons: Not all image/video previews are visible within the app compared to Dropbox. Also, when uploading, the status count of uploads does not change until the very end. It will say failed and offer the option to “Retry,” which it will just speed through them all quickly - indicating they are all actually uploaded already. Another thing I’ve noticed is if I create a folder it will not appear sometimes, so I’ll create multiples and then they will appear randomly after a refresh and I delete duplicates.

Pros: Free 50GB of storage for new users compared to much lower storage space offered by other top competitors.

Jbgood75 , 07/19/2017

MediaFire owns my videos

I only have videos on this app so I can't speak for photos, games, documents, or music. I have too many photo and video apps and wanted to condense them to one app but I am not able get my videos from MediaFire to download back to my phone. The only thing it will let you do is download the link so that you can view your videos on MediaFire. This is BS, they are my videos and I should be able to get them back on my phone and not just the link to view them on their app! So I have personal videos of my children and my pets that I can't get back now and I deeply regret using this app to store them there. I wrote to the company to see if there was any way for me to get my videos to upload back to my phone and I heard nothing back from them. Crap app, crap customer service!
