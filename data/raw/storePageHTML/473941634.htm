Description

Paytm (पेटीएम), India’s #1 Payment App, trusted by 45 Crore+ Indians. Download Paytm app for bank to bank money transfer via BHIM UPI & instantly pay at shops & at online websites/apps like IRCTC, Flipkart, Uber, Zomato and Swiggy. Use Paytm for mobile recharge, Fastag recharge, DTH recharge, Movie Tickets, Postpaid bill payment, Utility bill payment such as Water, Gas & Electricity bill payment as well as Landline, Broadband & insurance payments. Invest in Mutual Funds, Digital gold & NPS, avail insurance, check free CIBIL credit score & get instant cash via Paytm postpaid. Make IRCTC train booking, book flights, & buses in just a few taps.
