What’s New

Feb 17, 2021

Version 2.1.1

How is the community doing?

2020 was a tough year for group studying. Our goal in 2021 is to add features to our platform that help members study more effectively together, regardless of their location. We don’t want to say too much more, but in the short term, this update provides the ‘community score’ for each question when you are reviewing it. This means you can see on a per question basis just how hard questions are and how other people are answering. To view a breakdown of how the community answered and the percentage of people who answered correctly just press the community icon, 2 people, next to the flag icon in the lower tray area. This functionality will stay on as you review questions until you press the icon again.

Keep studying hard and we’ll keep the app working hard for you.

Passed my CMP exam!!!

Paid the $20 for the premium version about a month out from my test date to upgrade to 600 practice questions to continue testing my knowledge. Along with reading the material EIC suggested I took at least one quick 10 quiz daily. I really like how an explanation was given for each answer along with the page number for reference. The formatting of the questions helped me prepare to make sure I read the question CAREFULLY and pick the BEST answer not just the right answer. Also loved being able to test myself on the go! Simply opening the app is so much easier than lugging 50 million flash cards with me everywhere I go. I’d highly recommend!

chdleoj , 06/08/2020

Great App, Slight Typos

PocketPrep (PMP, Professional) has been a staple in my studying habits for my PMP exam. Hands down, it is indeed the best iOS studying app available for the PMP. Their recent update to their platform overall was hard to initially accept, but after working in it, it’s way cleaner and more intuitive! My favorite feature is seeing where each topic (PMBOK chapter) ranks from strongest to weakest. I especially like the ability to create a quiz that allows me to pick certain subjects, questions I’ve not answered yet, flagged questions, or even just questions I’ve gotten wrong. Minus one star because I’ve had to tweet PocketPrep several times regarding the accuracy of their questions or answers. Pedagogically, these issues aren’t huge, it’s just the PMP exam plays on words heavily and by misrepresenting certain acronyms or having typographical errors, this inflames test anxiety like mine!

Jon Acevedo , 09/27/2020

Convenient way to study for the PMP

App has really useful reminders. Turn on notifications so you get a daily reminder to study and take the question of the day. The 10 question quizzes are easy to take anywhere and everywhere. You can even build you own customized quizzes with your weakest topics. I purchased the $20 version which gives you access to over 800 questions. The only issue with the questions, is that they’re not situational questions like you see in the exam. They’re more knowledge and memorization questions. Even still these knowledge questions will help you answer situational questions on the exam so I highly recommend it.
