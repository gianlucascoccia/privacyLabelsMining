Ringwarrior , 04/12/2019

Game started great then went downhill

I dont understand how matchups are made. The person you are up against is always many levels higher with much higher upgraded equipment. I dont get how the opponent always gets at least one extra pitch to beat you whenever you are ahead. This game started out great then turned for the worse. It doesnt matter what cards you have as you never can win enough coins to upgrade them and you get stuck with inferior equipment and skills. Its not a level playing field for the average player and seems to not be possible to be competitive and have fun playing if the same thing keeps happening again and again. How is it possible in mission mode to hit the ball at the target every time only to have it die before it gets there or have something that can only go to one field go in the complete opposite direction. Also why is every batter righthanded? The gamemakers must know that people dont all bat righthanded. The pitchers are right and left handed why not the batters? I hope the game gets better as i like it but i want to be able to enjoy it and be competitive not just get beat by people who overmatch you at every turn. I dont understand how i can close out a higher stadium and not be able to do anything in a lower one as it seems anytime i play in it i get put against people who are so far ahead of me that they beat me in 4 pitches. If things keep up like this i will just have to walk away from a game i like because its just not worth it.

Developer Response ,

[HAEGIN] Hi Ringwarrior,
We'd like to offer you an apology for not addressing your previous review properly.
Although the frustrations you felt with the matchmaking and time limit in the game were not errors, we understand that there is a lot of room for improvement.
Since your original review, we've made some adjustments to the matchmaking system, so we hope you check us out again when you have the chance!
Additionally, please know that the game system does not allow an opponent to hit one more turn than another, but it may seem that way sometimes due to time differences between each hit, and delays.
We'll also consider your feedback about adding left-handed batters too!

King Leos Roar , 02/18/2021

Great game, for those who know how to play

I’ve been playing this game for a year now and after the update I was reading the comments about how “unfair it is” and how “hard it is to upgrade, which is almost laughable. I think a lot of what these players are facing is a perceptive bias as you don’t hear anyone complaining when the game goes in their favor. Do you get beat by incredibly slim margins in PvP sometimes due to the flight path of the ball? Absolutely! However, do you also beat your opponents by the same margins?? Of course you do! It’s a challenge to make sure you hit correctly, giving the ball an optimal and quick flight path to the target, and this my friends, is on the player, not the programmer.
As for upgrading your character, the game is well rounded IF you use all of the features! PLAY the home-run challenge everyday to compete for free chests, and make sure to open the FREE chests that are available every day, 3x a day, and PARTICIPATE in the events! I’ve gotten almost all of the available items by doing these three things, and am quite pleased that the devs have been generous enough to even allow Legendary loot drops out of the free chests- understand they don’t have to do this!
Give this game a dedicated effort and you will be rewarded!
