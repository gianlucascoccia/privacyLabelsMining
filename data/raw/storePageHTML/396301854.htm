Fastest flashlight ever!

Wanted to make sure this review was read so i gave it 5 stars! I find that this app does what all the other flashlight apps do...it lights up and illuminates what I need to see. As far as being the brightest...ummmm....there is no way that the app magically makes the light bulb a brighter stronger light bulb. Every phone has the same standard bulb when manufactured at the factory...smh. So if you really buy into the hype, I have the worlds hardest set of rock hammers cut from the same materials as any other rock hammer. I also have the worlds fastest snails ever raised in captivity.

Zoe in Tennessee , 09/30/2019

Reliable and bright

I’ve had the app installed for several years and it’s never failed me. More than bright enough to find keys you dropped at night or light your way to the door. Great for finding things that find their way under a car seat or furniture...including a skittish new cat, which I couldn’t find for more than a week before remembering this great light. Better than any mini light I’ve used, ever. I do think it’s a battery hog, but that’s easily addressed by remembering to close the app after use.

BraveJediMaster , 03/25/2017

Dumb

Yes I rated 5 stars because the app does what it's supposed to. But I find the concept of Flashlight apps dumb. Let me explain: every iOS device has control center. You access it by swiping up at the bottom of the screen. It lets you go to 3 apps, enable the flash of your device, turn airplane mode, wifi, Bluetooth, do not disturb, lock rotation, night shift (iOS 9.3 and up), enable airplay and airdrop and adjust brightness and volume. TL:DR, swipe up from bottom, tap Flashlight icon, profit.
