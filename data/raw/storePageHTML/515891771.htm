Description

Introduction:
We know that you love doing everything on your mobile phone right from food ordering to fixing plans with friends to get latest news update to shopping for your favourite brand. So when you live the mobile life, why should banking be any different?

What’s New with HDFC Bank?
Presenting the all-new Mobilebanking App, with the same security you are familiar with, for your everyday banking needs. Bringing all the services of a branch to you mobile, so that you can truly #BankTheWayYouLive

The HDFC Bank MobileBanking App is designed for a generation that gets everything done on their mobile phone, from sending money via UPI to paying and managing your accounts, bills and investments. Even making investments like Fixed Deposits is now available at your fingertips.

The HDFC Bank MobileBanking App provides services under three broad categories—Pay, Save and Invest. These features and more make it the one App for all your everyday financial needs.

Here are some features you will love: 

1) Quick and Secure Log-In: Access your HDFC Bank MobileBanking App via face recognition (for iOS 10 & above) or fingerprint sensor mapped only to you. No more hassle of remembering passwords or PIN. Moreover, with multi-level authentication for every transaction this app is extremely secure.

2) All-in-One View: Single view dashboard of all your accounts/product holdings with HDFC Bank i.e. available balance in your A/c, Credit Card. Fixed Deposits and utility bills due.

3) Instant Funds Transfer: Need to make an instant payment? HDFC MobileBanking App auto selects between IMPS / UPI / NEFT depending upon which part of the day you have initiate the funds transfer request.

4) Easy Share: Once you have made a payment, you can share your payment receipts just like you share the latest memes.

5) Adding Favourites: One of the best features of the App is that it lets you create a favourite playlist of your frequent transactions under ‘Favourites’.

6) Organised Bill-Pay: Pay all your utility bills in an instant with our new HDFC Bank MobileBanking App. Clear your mobile, cable TV, electricity, gas or any other utility bills in a flash.

7) Automatic Payments: Forgetting to keep track of all your monthly bills? No problem, the App will do it for you. You can also set up automatic payments and never miss due dates.

8) Credit Card Linking: Having trouble managing your HDFC Bank Credit Card? No problem, this one-app-for-all-needs lets you view all your Credit Card transactions, make Credit Card payments and get account statements along with Reward point status.

9) Swipe to Block: Lost your Credit or Debit Card? Just go to the HDFC Bank MobileBanking App to hotlist the card in a jiffy.

10) Invest: Want to invest in the stock market? Check your Demat Account balances on the HDFC Bank MobileBanking App and quickly navigate to your HDFC Securities App.

Apart from these, there are a host of other features that helps you #BankTheWayYouLive. For example, updating your Aadhaar details, downloading your e-TDS certificate, applying for new Savings Accounts or Credit Cards, applying for loans, Buying or renewing insurance and buying e-Gift and Forex Cards.
Need to get in touch with your Relationship Manager but can’t remember the number? Go to the HDFC Bank MobileBanking App and get in touch with one tap!
So now you know what an awesome and easy-to-understand MobileBanking app HDFC Bank has. Download it now and #BankTheWayYouLive!
