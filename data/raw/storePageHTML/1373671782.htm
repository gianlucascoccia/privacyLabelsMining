Description

Browse the IMSLP / Petrucci Music Library and view scores from the comfort of your iOS device.

IMSLP Membership Subscription
Length of subscription: 1 month.
Benefits include:
• Full access to commercial recordings
• Access all features in this app
• No waiting for downloads
• Access new uploads instantly
• Knowing that you helped keep IMSLP running and serving the music community

Payment will be charged to iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase. Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period. Account will be charged for renewal within 24-hours prior to the end of the current period, and identify the cost of the renewal. Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user’s Account Settings after purchase. Any unused portion of a free trial period, if offered, will be forfeited when the user purchases a subscription to that publication, where applicable.

Gone too far

I love using imslp to keep all of my music, but the new updates have gone too far. First, you made it so you have to wait 15 seconds to search. I get that. But now you are literally removing the search function unless the user has a subscription. If you want to make money, I understand, but you have to keep in mind that the purpose of this is to benefit everyone, not just to make money.

Edit: Thanks for clearing that up, I’m truly sorry. I realized that you could also search for the piece you want if you know the composer. Sorry again for the misunderstanding.

Developer Response ,

We have to pay Google for each in-app search, so it is now limited to members. Browser search should have the exact same functionality (but doesn’t look as good of course), if you are having trouble please email eguo@imslp.org

We don’t want to make money off search, just not lose money, which we were doing in significant amounts due to the search. You now get the same search feature as on the website.

Edit - no worries, glad it worked out.

Pyco85 , 12/21/2020

Amazing, Must Have App for Classical Musicians

Wow! Hats off to IMSLP for a truly great app. Gone are the days of downloading PDFs from my laptop and sending them to a third party app on a tablet. Now it’s just the tablet and whatever piece of music I want to read next. Navigation is easy and straight forward, and the marking functions are far superior to any other app. It’s tailored with musicians in mind looking to make quick markings during rehearsals. And as a bonus, it happened to work great with my foot pedal page turner. A must have. Keep up the good work!
