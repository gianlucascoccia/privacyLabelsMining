Description

The BTES Gratitude journal is live in your mindset section of the app. Fill in your daily gratitude, affirmations and wins for the day. There's even a place to keep notes

We all need a bestie who will hold us accountable and be there to lift us up when we need that extra push. B.T.E.S has been created by its members, for its members so we totally GET YOU and what you need!

Get access to an entire gym, kitchen and mindset library all in one place. B.T.E.S gives you a vast library of workouts, meals and mindset programs that will get you to the next level you desire while rewarding you for it.

Earn B.T.E.S points by doing your daily tasks, eating healthy & burning calories. Then spend your points in our shop, why not treat yourself for doing a great job!? You deserve it.

The fact is by joining today, you take control of your life the transformation begins and you become an official B.T.E.S girl!

Download your app, become a B.T.E.S girl & let’s start working on your goals.

p.s The App is FREE to download so go and check it out and see what we are all about!
