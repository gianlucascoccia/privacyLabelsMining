Description

Ermine is a monthly calendar and Widgets for your home screen. This app makes it easy to keep track of your events. It has a simple, clean design and is packed with many useful details.

.........................
WIDGETS

・Four different Widgets to display a monthly calendar.
・A dot will appear on days when you have events.
・You can change the background color. (except for the illustration calendar).
・You can change the calendar language.
・Pro Features
Upgrading to Pro will allow you to customize the widgets more to your liking.

.........................
DISPLAY OF HOLIDAYS

・You can set up calendars for holidays.
・The date of holiday calendars will be in red.

.........................
PRIVACY

This app reads and writes calendar data set on your device. We don't collect or send any data to an external server.
You don't need to create an account or log in.
It's easy and safe to use.
