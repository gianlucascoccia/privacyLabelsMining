Description

The KRONon app offers 24-7 live from KRON4 News. It includes streaming video, exclusive breaking news, in-depth coverage, clips of the day's top videos, and a searchable archive of all available content.

KRON4 News is now available on your AppleTV! Watch 24-7 local news, weather, sports and traffic programming streaming live from the KRON4 team as we cover the San Francisco Bay Area, San Jose, Oakland and surrounding communities.
