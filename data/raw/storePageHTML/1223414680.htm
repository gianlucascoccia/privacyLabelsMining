Description

The adventure continues! Get ready for all-new areas, Blast levels, mysteries, and renovations. Stay tuned for more of the very best the genre has to offer.

Welcome to your very own island! Step onto its shores and solve puzzles together with lovable characters to build a beautiful tropical paradise!


But this is no ordinary island. Legends tell of ancient spirits that haunt its sandy beaches and lush jungles.

Design, build and decorate to transform it into the home and garden of your dreams. Solve challenging match Blast puzzles and a treasure hunt to investigate and uncover the secrets of the past.

Combining the best of match games and adventure games, Lost Island takes you on a fun-filled journey through a mystery paradise.

Travel to the heart of the island and unearth its mysteries!

Game Features:

● Play exciting Blast puzzles with explosive boosters and tons of levels
● Construct and customize buildings, beaches, gardens, jungles, and more
● Go on a magical quest through the tropical island and uncover the fun story behind its ancient culture
● Repair and decorate the island to restore its beauty and magic
● Experience a fun story set in an exotic, magical environment
● Investigate mysteries and discover secrets while combing through ancient ruins
● Explore the island and find gold, treasure, and historical artifacts
● Meet interesting new friends and help them fulfill their dreams
● Partner up with Ellie, the adventurous archaeologist
● Play and hunt in the quest for treasure with your cute pet seal

NOTE: Lost Island is FREE to play and download, but you can make in-game purchases with real money. If you don’t want this feature, please disable it in your device settings.

Just the right amount of challenge

Puzzles are easy enough to get through, but they’re not so easy that i find myself gettin bored
And seeing the island get cleaned up is nice
My only complaint is when you have obstacles on the board that block rainbow pearls. I just attempted to do a puzzle and got enough explosions for a rainbow pearl, but it spawned between three coral pieces and couldn’t use it. What’s the point of a mechanic if you can’t use it? Other pop block games like this allow you to place their own version of the rainbow pearl anywhere on the board, but your rainbow pearls spawn randomly. This isn’t the first time I’ve been unable to use a pearl and lost all 7 lives in the span of a few minutes. Just infuriating sometimes.

EDIT: Ya know what. Even though I only gave four stars and my review was just a complaint on something annoying that only really happens every 50 levels or so (now that I’m up in the 600s) the developer reached out to me. I’ve left reviews for other games that have had real major problems (like buying something from their game stores and then the game taking away what I bought so I’m essentially being robbed) and gotten no replies. But these developers still reached out even though my complaint was superficial, so I’m changing my review to five stars because THATS how you handle reviews.

Developer Response ,

Hi! We do care about the game and what our players think about it. This is why we leave no review unanswered. I hope that you're still enjoy the game but should any difficulties arise, you can always contact us directly using the lostisland.support@plarium.com email. Have a great day!

PerfectMischief , 03/02/2019

Great game but not for everyone

Love the design, building, options, the storyline is fantastic so far. Unfortunately, if you don’t have a lot of time and patience, it just takes too long to get past some of the puzzle levels to progress. It takes a very long time to earn lives, and it takes only a few minutes to blow threw them all at once when you get in the 400 level. If you’re lucky, you might get a star or two and may even be able to add one of a four part piece to your island, or a rail to a walkway - and then you wait, and wait, and wait some more. The story makes you want to keep going, but the frustrating pace is just too much. I don’t have that kind of free time and just want to relax and enjoy a game when I can steal a few minutes.

I continue to see the response from the developers that every level is beatable without additional purchases, and I suspect that is true. IF you have ten hours to earn one star toward progressing your island, like I just spent on level 476.

Update: level 1119 for six days, no stars, no progress, no bonus awards, just try and try and try until you’re bored to tears and frustrated beyond belief. If you haven’t started playing - don’t. If you have, save yourself a lot of wasted time. Nothing YOU do is relevant, the game decides when you get to win and it becomes almost never after a while. You don’t get to know why the giant octopus is hugging the ship and it isn’t worth it.
