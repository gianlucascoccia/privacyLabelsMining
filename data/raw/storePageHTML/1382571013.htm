Description

Track NYSE and Nasdaq listed stocks. Make smarter trading decisions with your stock and crypto portfolios using BullsEye's market tracking and analytics.

Track and analyze your stocks and crypto holdings on one app across your iPhone/iPad. Packed with useful features, making BullsEye your go to stock and crypto portfolio tracker.

► STOCK MARKET & CRYPTO PRICES & TRADING CHARTS
Track thousands of stock tickers from major stock markets - Nasdaq, NYSE LSE and TSX. Near real-time and historic price quotes, with >7,000 tickers covered. Track your favourite crypto too - Bitcoin, XRP, Ethereum, Link and hundreds more altcoins.

► PORTFOLIO TRACKER & ANALYTICS
Track your stocks and crypto portfolios in one place. Robinhood, Fidelity, TD Ameritrade, WeBull, eToro, Coinbase - it matters not! Record and edit trades with ease. Monitor portfolio value and track performance of individual shares and cryptocurrencies vs. book cost. Your transaction history is only a few taps away.

► STOCK MARKET & CRYPTO SCREENER
Screen global stock and crypto markets by ticker, filter for top 24-hour risers/fallers, or by market cap.

► WATCHLIST
Track your favourite shares and crypto tickers in near real-time. Quickly add, delete or re-order your watchlist.

► MULTIPLE CURRENCIES & LANGUAGES
Choose from a range of base currencies – USD, EUR, GBP, HKD, SGD, JPY, KRW, CAD and INR. Tailor the language to your mother tongue. English, Spanish, Korean, Japanese, Chinese and Hindi are available.

-- TAKE YOUR GAME TO THE NEXT LEVEL WITH BULLSEYE PRO --

► FLEXIBLE SUBSCRIPTIONS
1 and 12-month subscriptions. Unlock superior functionality to turbo-charge your investment research, portfolio tracking and analysis, incl.:

► TRADING STRATEGY & RISK
Personalize to your own trading strategy and risk tolerance. Make better informed investments. Monitor investment risks such as portfolio concentration and market liquidity. Improve your research and evaluate the risk vs. reward and short/long-term prospects for your investments.

► PRICE PREDICTIONS
Put your reputation on the line; set price predictions for your favourite shares and cryptos and grow your BERP score. Compare your stock and crypto picking powers against the BullsEye Community.

► STOCK & CRYPTO PRICE ALERTS
Set market price alerts directly to your device as push notifications. Configure alerts to trigger at your target trade price. Update existing alerts with ease.

► COMPARE PORTFOLIO PERFORMANCE VS. MARKET
Track performance vs. established market indices, such as the Dow Jones and S&P 500. For cryptos, track against our BullsEye Crypto10 cap-weighted index. Just like the professionals, track alpha over multiple time horizons.

► TECHNICAL ANALYSIS & CHARTS
Analyze stock or crypto charts using technical indicators, such as candlesticks or 50/100/200 day SMAs.

► CORPORATE PROFILES & FUNDAMENTALS
For stocks research key fundamentals, including 12M revenue, 12M gross profit, P/E ratios and dividend yields.

► CHAT
Share news, rumours and analysis using the chat boards for your chosen stocks and cryptos. Save your favourites for easy access.
