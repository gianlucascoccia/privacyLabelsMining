Description

Stay connected using Warframe companion app and get the latest news. Login using your PC, PlayStation Network, Xbox Live, or Nintendo account to receive the best experience.


ALERTS, INVASIONS, AND MORE
Get notifications of new Alerts and view active Invasions, Sorties, and Void Fissures

NIGHTWAVE
View your completion of daily and weekly Acts as you rank up through the current Season! Also, take a peek at the rewards for the later tiers.

DEPLOY DRONES
Deploy Extractor Drones with ease right from the Warframe App. Gather resources such as Orokin cells, Plastids, and Neurodes.

BUILD IN YOUR FOUNDRY
Your Foundry is hard at work — even when you're offline. Build the latest Warframes, Weapons and gear right from your phone.

MANAGE COMPANIONS
Shower your Kubrows and Kavats with affection wherever you go. Purchase DNA Stabilizers to keep them healthy or manage Companions in Stasis to make sure they're always ready for action!

I love this app it has helped me so much since in my time playing warframe, I mostly use it for the foundry but being able to look through the available Warframes and weapons is super cool as well. Eko Foxx , 10/21/2019

Extractor/ Foundry App Only

This app has a lot of missed potential. Clean design but very limited in what you can do. I hope DE improves this app.
It’s a great app if your aim is to specifically run extractors throughout the day or to start and claim foundry items. Even care for your companions or move them in and out of stasis.
Everything else is heavily lacking in this app. You cannot change loadout - only view current setup. You cannot customize your mod slots or enhance their abilities. Ranks for items/weapons often do not matchup from arsenal to inventory (taxon rank 30 in inventory, true rank 27 in Arsenal). This slip up caused me to open my stasis companion early.
There is also no ability to view your profile to see how much exp is needed for next ranking or syndicate rankings. No access to clan functions at all. No access to orbiter market. Can’t even view your own personalized warframe/s.
Essentially what I would like to see is the ability to do everything you can do in game in orbiter with access to in game markets.

T A M 17 , 04/07/2020

Could do more

I have been playing the Warframe since 2014. I have been using this app on and off for about two years. It’s a simple and solid app.

The good.
I am able to build things I need in the foundry while I’m at work I’m able to send and claim extractors as well. And honestly that helps out a little bit. I like how I can check and news updates and stay up to date with any updates before I get home. I enjoy that I can see what my mod load outs are.

The bad.
But I don’t like that I can’t see how each load out effects my stats. From time to time I watch videos on better builds and I would like to see how the numbers compare. Sometimes I want to change stuff but I can’t due to the limitations of this app. With mods being such a huge part of the overall game you would think they would want us to be able to change them on the fly so then we play more vs sitting around changing mods.

Things I want to see.
I have a clan in the game, so I would love for there to be an option to change the message of the day (MOTD). Or chat with them if they are in game. The Xbox app allows me and my friends to see when we are on Xbox or the app and it allows us to message each other with no issue. It would make it easier to stay connected with the homies when I’m away from my Xbox. Even more so that I pick up new players from all over the place.
