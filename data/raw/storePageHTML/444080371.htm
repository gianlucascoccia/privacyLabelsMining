Description

Learn Korean is a free and easy to use mobile Korean phrasebook that will give visitors to Korea and those who are interested in learning Korean a good start in the language. Learn Korean is recorded using a native speaker and we have tried our best to be authentic in the pronunciation whilst ensuring it is easy to understand. Recommended app for tourists and business people visiting Korea.

Features:

• Universal app works on iPhone, iPod Touch, & iPad
• Carefully translated list of essential phrases
• 200+ common words and phrases included for FREE
• Upgrade and unlock over 1000+ phrases in 18 categories
• Audio recorded by native speakers
• Option to playback audio at slower speed
• No internet connection required
• Highlight important words with tips
• Store frequently used phrases in favourites
• Search by words inside the app
• No adverts or annoying popups
