Description

The Subscription Manager app is a convenient way to track your subscriptions and expenses. Totals are calculated for you, so you'll always know what you owe and when you owe it. You can even get reminders when bills are due!

Subscriptions
Keep track of your subscriptions and recurring payments, capturing data such as the name of the subscription, what it is for, the amount of the subscription, due date, currency, and even special notes like when you want to cancel it. You can even archive a subscription if you want to pause your subscription and pick it up at a later time.

Categories
Group subscriptions by category so you can see the amount you spend in each category.

Totals
Automatically see the total amount of money you spend on all subscription on a yearly, monthly or weekly basis supporting multiple currencies. You can select the option to see the total, remaining, or average amount due.

Widgets & Siri
View your subscriptions and expenses right on your home screen or just ask Siri about your remaining expenses and more!

Alerts
Get notifications (Opt-in only) when subscription bills are due.

Customization
Sort subscriptions in a number of ways, pick from tons of currencies in the app, create custom categories to group subscriptions, change the theme or icon of the app, and more.

Privacy
We believe privacy is a human right and do not collect any user data.
