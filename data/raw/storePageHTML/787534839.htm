Description

The Universal Kids app is the best place to catch your favorite Universal Kids shows, stream episodes live, and watch on the go! Download the Universal Kids app now to watch Top Chef Junior, American Ninja Warrior Junior, Floogals, Get Out of My Room, Super Wings, AND MANY MORE!

Whether it's food and friendship on Top Chef Junior or tiny alien adventures on Floogals, Universal Kids has safe and trusted programming for kids of all ages that we know you’re going to love!

PARENTS - Get your kids started by signing in with your TV subscription to unlock all of the awesome, kid-friendly content! The Universal Kids app supports most TV providers, including Xfinity, DirecTV, Verizon, Charter, and AT&T.

APP FEATURES:
• Chromecast and AirPlay compatible! Start episodes in the app and stream to the big screen.
• Busy family always on the go? The app saves your place so your kids can resume watching later!
• Save your child’s favorite shows to the Watchlist for quick access.
