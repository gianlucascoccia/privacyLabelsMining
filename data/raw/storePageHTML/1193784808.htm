Description

Clubhouse is collaborative project management without all the management. We streamline and refine your existing software development workflow so that managing your tasks (and your team's tasks) is no longer a task.

Features of Clubhouse for iOS:

• Create new Stories quickly with a few taps
• Search for, track progress on, update, and add comments to all Stories in your workspace
• View details and progress on all Epics in your workspace
• Quickly see what you’re working on in the Dashboard
• See all activity on the projects that you’re working on in the Activity Feed

Thousands of technology-driven organizations – from start-ups to large enterprises – enjoy using Clubhouse to develop software more efficiently:

“Clubhouse provides the ability to work on a task list at the smallest level where I can check things off, all the way up to tracking my entire company's engineering velocity at the milestone level.”
- John Kodumal, CTO/Co-Founder, LaunchDarkly

I love CB but the iOS app super buggy

Five star to show love and support. Our team switched from Asana to CB and loves it. However, CB iOS app is quite buggy (I totally understand mobile is not your priority). I’ve been tolerating until this moment it became quite annoying.

For one, I usually fail to upload images and videos to my tickets. To solve this, I’ve been slacking images and videos onto my Mac, and then uploading them onto CB web version. Total hassle.

For two, when I tried to make a comment on a ticket, the input field on keyboard disappears as keyboard slides up, which means I cannot see what I’m typing. Now I’ve got to turn on my computer again in order to make a comment.

For three, whenever I tried to create a new ticket, there’s a chance the “create” button doesn’t activate even though I’ve filled in title, description and others. I’ve had to cancel the ticket, click the new ticket icon, copy and paste the same content again to activate that create button.

For four, really hate that I cannot drag and drop on mobile...

For five, share and archive options on tickets are not working. Share brings out the iOS standard drawer for literally a split second and it disappears. Archive brings the popup which also disappears instantly.

Please invest some time to fix these! Thank you :) Awesome product!

T-Middy , 07/19/2020

Lacks features on mobile

I really love the desktop (browser) app and use it almost everyday, but I almost never try to use the mobile app on my phone because I find it frustrating to work with.

The options for views are pretty limited, I’ve never learned how to access iterations on the app - if that is an ability that exists. When creating stories I’ve noticed bugs where I can’t seem to change the estimated point value on them after creating (it just stays stuck on unestimated)

Overall it’s not just a single thing about the app but when I use it it just doesn’t feel intuitive or easy to work with. Moving cards to different swim lanes can be tricky and despite the nice visuals - I’d settle for rock solid functionality.

Again, I really like the desktop (browser) app and I don’t doubt that the team is capable of improving the mobile experience overtime. But for now I don’t recommend this app. It all comes down to the fact that given the choice, I will get up and go to my computer to use clubhouse instead of doing it on my phone because the difference in experience is that apparent to me.

Maybe I’ll check back in a year and the app will exceed expectations! The company certainly seems capable of it.
