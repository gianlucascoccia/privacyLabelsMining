Why switch to IndiaMART App ?

For Buyers :

1. Search for Products & Services 24x7, Send Enquiries & Post your Requirements. Get instant alerts & stay updated no matter where you are.
2. Post Requirements & Send Enquiries in Offline Mode as well.
3. Compare Quotes from multiple suppliers & get best prices.
4. Pay instantly and do business securely using Pay with IndiaMART.
5. Buy from sellers near you - Search in your area with the help of Geo-Location.
6. Get detailed information about products & chat with sellers on the go. Discuss your requirement in detail, negotiate the price & buy from India's most trusted suppliers.
7. Mark your preferred products as "My Favourites" to check them out later.


For Sellers :

1. Purchase Buy Leads on the go - Connect to potential customers instantly & discover new opportunities to sell online.
2. Review & Respond to enquiries anytime, anywhere, categorise them into multiple folders. Sell to interested customers easily.
3. Receive Payments hassle-free through Pay with IndiaMART.
4. Add reminders & notes to increase lead conversion. Add prices to your catalog easily.
5. Search Buy Leads by Product Name & increase your chances of conversion.
6. Never miss an opportunity to grow sales. Receive instant Buyleads & Enquiry notifications.
7. Upload & Download PDF, Product Images, Documents in Enquiry chat for better communication.

The IndiaMART App also supports exclusive features for individual accounts, as well as detailed catalog listing view, as per categories & sub-categories, thus facilitating on-the-go deals for them. Whether you are an importer or exporter, seller or reseller, manufacturer, wholesaler or a SME, with the help of IndiaMART App, you can buy & sell across the globe with utmost ease. Get instant buy leads & sell with high rates of conversion. Join over 47 lakh+ suppliers who are already a part of IndiaMART.

Pay with IndiaMART is a payment gateway that enables IndiaMART's sellers & buyers to accept & make instant online payments through Credit/Debit card, Net Banking, Wallet, ATM-cum-debit card. The service is available even on holidays. Pay with IndiaMART provides 100% Buyer Protection to all Indian Buyers.
