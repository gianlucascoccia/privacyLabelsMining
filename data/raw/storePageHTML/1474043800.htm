Very poor bible app

Worst I've had quite honestly.
For one the search doesn't work properly. Oftentimes to narrow my search I'll select the new testament only option, and I'll still get old testament passages popping up. Same goes for the old testament, but new testament passages still pop up.
Secondly, the controls are terrible. I'll scroll down to get to a certain place, and when I put my thumb back on the screen to stop the scroll it doesn't register. That's right. It just keeps scrolling. The next page arrows will not register my touch, and I'll sit there pressing it around ten times to get it to work.
Another inconvenience is the fact that you can't have this app online. Unless you want to be interrupted by tons and tons and TONS of ads. What's worse is that many of the ads have an "x" so small that instead of exiting the app you click it. Now have fun as you leave your bible app on an ad's pop-up journey.
Lastly, you cannot highlight multiple verses at a time. Yep, you can only highlight one verse at a time. So sharing scripture with your friends can now take twice as long! Woo hoo!
So yeah to whoever made this app, congrats! You have successfully made the worst bible app of all time! I never thought that someone could take a great translation and make it unreadable. To everyone else, disregard the person before me who gave this five stars. Either it was a nice bait, or they were this apps creator.
