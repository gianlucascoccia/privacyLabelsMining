Description

Save Stories, Highlights & Posts

SAVING and EXPLORING has never been easy

Browse and save profiles, stories, highlights and posts as it in instagram. Create lists from them with FULL HD resolution.


NO REQUIRED SIGN IN

We don't require you to type your username and password to explore and save stories.


VIEW & EXPLORE STORIES ANONYMOUSLY

You are not seen by the user whose story you watched. You are absolutely hidden!


NO EXTRAS

There are no ads, watermarks, unnecessary in-app purchases and things that make you angry.


Basic features;

- Save and watch story & post
- Create list from stories and posts
- Share stories & posts with your friends
- Explore and watch contents as it in Instagram
- No required your instagram username and password
- Anonymously explore.



Made with SwiftUI
