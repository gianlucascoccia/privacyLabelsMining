Description

The Demon Prince Moro is about to descend upon the Land of Thel, and it is now up to the 6 chosen Astral Warriors to restore the power of the stars to the Light Realm.
Are you up for an intense new challenge? All new game modes? Stella Arcana is now calling upon the bravest of adventurers!

Brand New Content
1. Invite to Duel
Whether friend or foe, you can now challenge anyone to duke it out and prove who is truly the strongest warrior - Invite to Duel, an intense and merciless 1V1 PVP Mode.

2. Battle Pet Formation
Arrange your Battle Pets in specific formations in order to further improve your stats and attributes, while bringing even more depth to the class and character customization system.

3. Mystery Chapters
As you grow in level, more and more of the Mystery Chapters will be unlocked, bringing adventurers bountiful treasures and rewards.

4. Experience Recovery Function
Forgot to log into Stella Arcana for a certain period of time? No worries, you can retrieve missing experience here!

5. Champion's Tower
A fierce cross-server 5V5 team PVP battle mode - Prove who is the strongest team in each server.

6. Starlight Weapons
Unleash the power of the Stars - Yield the legendary Starlight Weapons.

7. Sage Artifacts
Relics left behind by the Sages of Thel, containing ancient secrets of power and wisdom. Collect these artifacts to unlock all new character upgrade mechanisms!

8. The Lost Land
In the Lost Land, you can play as one of your favorite mercenaries and explore this land for ancient blessings. The Lost Land is split into two areas - the Lost Ruins and the Lost Tower. You may only venture the Lost Tower as a solo mercenary - not for the faint of heart!

9. Sage Battlefield
A battlefield holding the knowledge, secrets and treasures of the ancients of Thel. In order to advance through each floor, you must find the keys of the ancients.

Anyfreenicknames? , 02/03/2021

Best, but still a LOT of clutter

This is probably the best of these free ios MMOs I’ve played. It’s got cute, cartoony graphics and a storyline that doesn’t take itself too seriously. The quests are varied. Unlike some of the others, you’re not just running around fighting everything and picking things up. There are stealth quests, maps that have to be rubbed to find hidden details, cannons that shoot you around, etc.

But, at the end of the day it’s still got a huge mess of different menus to obtain and claim rewards of various types of currencies. It starts off small and lures you in. Within a few hours of play you’ll log in and go hunting through the various tagged menus to find this or that claim for a daily or quest or achievement or whatever.

It’s disappointing, because I absolutely hate these types of mechanics, but the base game is legitimately decent (something I’m not sure I’d say for the other games I’ve tried in this genre). I know why they do it, but all of the claims just leave me with a bad taste in my mouth.
