Description

Become a Hollywood superstar — climb the career ladder to the very top in this real life simulator game! Make your career and write your own story in Life Sim 2.

Do you dream of becoming popular? Then LifeSim is the game for you! Just one tap separates you from the life of a real superstar!

You will star in the most famous TV series, become the highest grossing actor on the planet and record a platinum album! You will become the face of a famous brand, an ambassador to the UN, stage dive into a crowd of your loyal fans and even get to direct a musical.

Commute to work in your private helicopter, live in a castle and become the owner of your very own private island. Build your career: from getting hired as an extra to becoming a superstar. Find a life partner that you will be comfortable with and overcome all of life’s obstacles together.

CHOOSE YOUR CHARACTER

Choose the character most similar to you from a huge variety. And choose wisely because you will live a whole second life for him.

START YOUR CAREER FROM THE VERY BOTTOM AND BECOME A FAMOUS HOLLYWOOD CELEBRITY

You will start with a temporary job like a janitor or barman in this simulator. The most important thing is to grab fortune by the tail at the right time and take a first step on the road to success. Whether you will be able to complete it to the end depends only on you.

CHOOSE CAR AND HOUSE YOUR LIKE

What do you prefer to drive - a sports car or a limousine? Or maybe you could buy something eco-friendly like an electric supercar. For those who like to travel fast, we have a personal helicopter. Want to get a bird's eye view of the city? Check in to the penthouse on the top floor of a skyscraper. Afraid of heights? Pick a mansion and never go higher than the second floor. Want to get away from the hustle and bustle of the city and the paparazzi's attention? Move to a private island where you will be the sole full owner. All of this is possible when you are a Hollywood superstar.

WATCH YOUR HEALTH AND MOOD

As in real life, in our game you must always be in good shape. Only a healthy and happy character can fulfill all his dreams. Lead a healthy lifestyle, do fitness and meditation, attend concerts and museums with you friends - do everything to keep your character healthy and happy. Just like in other life simulation games.

Fulfill your most cherished dreams in LifeSim. In LifeSim, you can be anyone!
Remember, that your decisions affect your fate and only you can choose the right path to success!

Now you know what to do! Download LifeSim right now for free!
