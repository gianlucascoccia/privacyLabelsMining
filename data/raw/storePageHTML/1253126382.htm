Description

We're proud to announce our new Messenger function! On-site communication just got even easier.

SINC Workforce brings employee data collected from the field, back to your home base. We provide a digital audit trail of where your workforce was at any given time, what jobs they were working on, what cost codes they were working, who was responsible for marking project tasks complete and every note and image uploaded from the field or the office.

Choose from either the free plan which includes the time clock and employee tracker features but limits you to a maximum of three users or go for one of our paid plans that include our project management and staff scheduling.

All plans include access to both the mobile apps and the web version of SINC for true cross-platform, cloud-based access and our offline-first approach means that if you find yourself without an internet connection, your device will store data locally until a connection is established.

Employee Time Clock (free and paid plans)


◆ Supports Weekly, Fortnightly, Monthly & Semi-Monthly pay periods
◆ Write paychecks knowing your timesheet data is accurate
◆ Timesheets are backed up with GPS location data during the shift
◆ Input pay rates for your workforce and see gross payroll reports at a glance
◆ Visual graphs showing total hours worked that pay period
◆ Pay period attendance summaries of employee hours at a glance
◆ Export detailed reports to pdf and excel to easily calculate overtime
◆ Employees can add notes to their time card that are included in the reports
◆ Push notifications of any timesheet edit mean transparent paychecks
◆ Push notification for staff notes added to shifts
◆ Option to be notified when specific members of your workforce clock in
◆ Easily sync you time clock data to Xero UK, Australia and New Zealand.

Employee Tracker (free and paid plans)


◆ See what staff are currently on shift at your home base and in the field
◆ Staff tracker records GPS points during the shift only
◆ Filter employee GPS points by clock-in and clock-out

Project Management & Job Costing (paid plans only)


◆ Track employee hours at both the project and cost code level
◆ Easily gather data from fieldworkers for accurate cost accounting
◆ Preloaded CSI MasterFormat cost codes for easy construction accounting
◆ Manage job-specific tasks and punch lists
◆ Assign specific cost code groups to your projects
◆ Use task groups to share common task lists across projects
◆ Quickly generate driving directions to jobs directly from the app
◆ Generate project reports and export them to excel
◆ Upload project notes that can include images

Staff Scheduling (paid plans only)


◆ SINC is the perfect staff planner for fieldworkers
◆ No more SMS back and forth to confirm times
◆ Changes to the schedule are sent to the affected staff in real-time
◆ Employee shift reminders reduce no shows on the job site
◆ Add instructions through shift notes
◆ Remind staff to clock out through automatic push notifications

If you choose to purchase the Starter, Growth or Enterprise plans, after the 1-month free trial the subscriptions will automatically renew for $24.99 USD/mth(Starter), $49.99 USD/mth (Growth) and $99.99 USD/mth (Enterprise) respectively unless the plan is cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the trial period.
