Description

NFL PLAYERS CLASH IN REAL-TIME ONLINE MULTIPLAYER!

The first real-time online football game featuring NFL Players is finally here! Jump online and experience the most action-packed football game in the universe.

Control all the action as you fight your way to the end zone using skill, strategy and powerful moves. Collect and upgrade your favorite NFL stars to build the ultimate team. Take your team online or into single player to level up and earn amazing rewards.

Your NFL season starts… NOW!

2017 - 2018 NFL PLAYER ROSTERS
> From first-year rookies to seasoned veterans, every active NFL Player is available to collect and upgrade!

ONLINE MULTIPLAYER
> Battle against players around the world in real-time multiplayer and win trophies to climb through Online Arenas.
> Earn cases based on your Arena ranking for special rewards like trick plays and rare players.

TOTAL CONTROL
> Control all the action on the field: run, pass, push, tackle and spin!
> Over 50 special moves including Fumble Tackle, Fire Pass, Perfect Pass, Power Push, Distraction and more!

MANAGE AND UPGRADE
> Build the ultimate team with your favorite NFL Players.
> Level up your players to increase their stats, unlock new abilities and dominate the competition!

ONLINE LEAGUES
> Challenge other players in turn-based multiplayer to climb the ranks and earn daily rewards.

PLAY ANYWHERE
> Battle your friends head-to-head over local Wi-Fi.
> Hone your skills against the AI in single player mode.

Please note - Football Heroes Pro Online is free to install and play, however, certain in-game items are also purchasable using real money. If you want to restrict this feature, please disable in-app purchases through your device settings.

Just meh.

Fun but could be way better. Too many times I run into a problem with online games which I am usually penalized for. Must put in time to have a good team without payment which even then isn’t really that great because you will play 30-40 million dollar teams online and lose... the online matchup/lobby could be wayyy better. mptn1973 , 01/10/2018

Are you freaking kidding me (edit after update)

Loved the game at first, but it's extremely unfair. First, when you play online, if you win you may get 15 trophies. But when you lose (and I mean every time you lose) you lose between 25-40. If you disconnect from a game (on purpose or accident) you lose 25-30 trophies. Not to mention there seems to be ZERO matching system. I was matched up against a guy with an entire team of gold level 20 maxed players. How the f am I supposed to have a chance with bronze and silver? I'd rate this a 5, but there is no desire to upgrade the game to make it playable. I've been stuck in the lowest level of arena because I keep getting matched with teams I have zero chance of beating and I lose 30 trophies each time I lose to a top 3 player (I know he's top 3 because he's listed...his name is MVP)

Fix this and I'll change my review. Until then no. Treat all your customers better, not just those who buy maxed level 20 gold players.

Edit: So yesterday I couldn't access the game because of server maintenance. Absolutely no problem there. But after the server maintenance I was unable to find a match for sometime, and there were a couple times I matched but the game froze. When I checked my trophies, it dropped the 70 after 2 games found and frozen!!!!!!! I'm at 11 trophies now. Can I at least get the game designers to comment on this? This is unacceptable. Fix it!
