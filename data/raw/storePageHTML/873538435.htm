Description

GoCamera is a powerful camera app that gives you remote control over your Sony PlayMemories Mobile camera. Use GoCamera to take stunning photos with custom exposure settings. Once shot, easily edit and share your pictures with your friends.


FEATURES 

- Remote control Sony PlayMemories Mobile cameras
- Zoom in and zoom out of your scenes
- Use different shooting modes like Program Auto, Aperture Priority, Shutter Priority and Manual (depending on camera and firmware)
- Control exposure settings such as exposure compensation (EV), ISO speed, aperture and shutter speed (depending on exposure mode)
- Adjust white balance pre-sets
- Edit your photos using features like cropping, filter effects or blur
- Share your favorite moments with your friends via iMessage, Facebook, Twitter, and more

Please note that GoCamera does not support capturing videos.


RELIABILITY & USER EXPERIENCE

With our latest updates we were able to significantly improve reliability and user experience. GoCamera is now faster than ever. Furthermore, we tested GoCamera with a large number of different Sony camera models such as the QX10, QX100, Alpha 5100, Alpha 6000, HX400V, and more.


DEVICE SUPPORT

List of Sony PlayMemories Mobile cameras: Sony a7, Sony a7S II, Sony a7R, Sony a7R II, Sony a7S, Sony a5000, Sony a5100, Sony av6000, Sony a6300, Sony a6500, Sony NEX-5R, Sony NEX-5T, Sony NEX-6, Sony HX60, Sony HX80, Sony HX90, Sony HX400, Sony WX500, Sony RX10 II, Sony RX10 III, Sony RX100 III, Sony RX100 IV, Sony RX100 V, Sony DSC-QX10, Sony DSC-QX30, Sony DSC-QX100, Sony ILCE-QX1.

Sony discontinued the PlayMemories Mobile interface in March 2017. Cameras released after this date such as the Sony a7R III may not be compatible to GoCamera. Please read your camera’s user manual or visit Sony’s support website for further information on device support.

Please note that some devices need to install the ‘Smart Remote Embedded’ service from the camera's built-in app store to enable WiFi connectivity. Please adhere to your camera's manual. Furthermore, being a third party developer, we cannot guarantee device support for all PlayMemories Mobile cameras as Sony can restrict third party usage and change interface settings at its own discretion.
