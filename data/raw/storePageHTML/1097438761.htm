Description

Introducing Homework App: The most simple yet elegant solution to track and organize your school life. The only app made by students; for students!

CREATE COURSES:
- Create your own courses and complete them with colors and periods

TRACK ASSIGNMENTS:
- Create highly customizable dates and attach images and notes
- Add custom reminders to your dates so you never miss an assignment
- View all of your dates by day, week, and month
- Quickly view, edit, and edit your dates using a slick date preview interface

DEEP SEARCHING & ANALYSIS:
- Use query view to search by:
- Name
- Course
- Type
- Due date
- And more!

EXTENSIVE CUSTOMIZATION:
- Access a wide array of settings to personalize your experience, including:
- Course display customizations
- Default reminder times
- Custom date types
- Add images and notes to dates
- And more!

I love the layout and the simplicity of this app! One problem I have noticed is that it crashes at two points. When I add a piece of homework and at 6pm, when it’s supposed to notify me (which it doesn’t btw), the app seems to “crash” (my phone has a black loading screen, then asks me to unlock it again). I added all of my math homework for the semester, and I think it started about a third of the way through, so maybe the issue is that it can’t handle too much homework at once (and really, who can)? I actually forgot to turn in some homework because I’d expected this to tell me (not a big deal, this is a class I’m great at), but it’d be nice if you could work out that bug. I have an iPhone 7, iOS 11.4.1

It was good until....

This app for the last couple of months was an absolute lifesaver. In my high school we could buy our own planners which were $35 each they were made out cheap material and etc.. This saved me the $35 however my school blocks out cell and had “WiFi” by this I mean it’s a connection that you can make to just text not really do anything. Even on WiFi or not the app worked until a couple of weeks ago I updated my apps (as usual) and noticed that I would get a pop up add every time for premium there was nothing I could do about I ignored it and moved on. Then I tried writing my math homework when the app said network error tap to retry I tapped and it didn’t do anything after practically Hulk smashing the phone it worked I put in my assignment and went on. I later went to my locker which is a total dead zone of cell before the app worked perfectly now it didn’t let me see my assignments thankfully I memorized them. However when I got home when I got home my assignments on the app were gone just gone! All it said was Untitled assignment I was so so mad! I emailed and got my homework pages and etc. Back thank fully. I just went with the fact that this app is not going to work anymore and deleted. Unless you will have good WiFi or a very strong cell connection constantly at your school use this app otherwise be ware!
