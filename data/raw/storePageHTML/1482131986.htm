Description

Millions of people all over the world are in love with Numberzilla - the TOP numbers game!

The best brain teaser from our childhood with good old familiar rules now has come back with a brand new witty look. People say this super addictive and relaxing puzzle game helps their mental health as well as lets them relax especially after a work day. Enjoy daily achievements and unlock cool badges which will definitely cheer you up!

You will meet our cute mascot Numberzilla and friends - Smart Croissant, Sassy Avocado, Watermelon Warrior and others. They will be happy to guide you through the rules and will keep track of your results and achievements.

How do you play the game? The logic is simple!

• Cross out the pairs of the same numbers (4-4, 2-2, 9-9) or those which add up to 10 (2-8, 3-7 etc). Two numbers can be removed by tapping on them one by one.
• In the Numberzilla puzzle game, the pairs must be located side by side, and you can cross out them vertically, horizontally and also, if one number stands on the last cell in the line and another stands on the first cell in the following line of the grid. There can also be empty cells between the 2 numbers.
• The goal is to cross out all the numbers and empty the board.
• When there are no more numbers to remove, the remaining numbers can be added to the end.

We offer 3 modes:
1) Classic
This is an "endless" mode: you have unlimited time and field to set a score record! If you run out of moves - just add extra lines to the bottom.
2) Survival
The field is limited, and numbers are falling down similar to "Tetris" game. Once the board is full - you lose!
3) Dynamic
Once you reach the maximum amount of numbers - you will be able to shuffle them. Go for it and you will make it to the next level!

This game is also known as Numberama, Take Ten or 10 Seeds. You can play it either online or offline by using a sheet of paper, but in 2019, we definitely prefer mobile versions of number puzzle games, which you can play on the go:)

AmericanMa , 08/09/2020

Pretty good

I got this game a few days ago and at first it seemed very hard and annoying to play, but then I got the hang of it more. I have been winning sometimes (getting all of the numbers eliminated). It is hard to win and thus all the more satisfying when you do. Sometimes I get “too far” into a game and realize I will not be able to win and just start a new one, it’s kinda like you need to know that once you get to a certain point (too many numbers on the board) the game is nearly impossible to win or it would take super super long- if it’s even possible. In a review someone else wrote they complained that sometimes the game adds numbers that make it impossible to win.. but that’s the thing, it only adds the numbers that you have left, so you are in total control of when to add numbers and thus of which numbers it adds. When adding numbers you have to think ahead, never wait until you have only three numbers left to press add bc you won’t be able to win then. There is actually quite a bit of strategy to this game along with luck, which is why it is challenging. My complaint is that the “dynamic” mode doesn’t seem to be any different then “classic” and I don’t know why.. it seems like a glitch. I came to the reviews hoping for an answer on how to do something different in dynamic mode but nothing was here. Maybe I’ll figure it out someday but for now I tend to prefer “survival” mode, which is actually much easier to win than classic. Happy playing!

User10895 , 12/10/2019

Impossible loop? Good game otherwise!!

I have been playing this over a few days, and it’s very addictive and fun, and and I have been enjoying it immensely. However, there are some circumstances in the game that I will press the “add” button to add more numbers, and it will only add two or three numbers that can’t be combined, that alternate (such as 2, 4, and 7, and it will keep repeating in different combinations of these three), forcing me to either keep pressing add, in which case I get the same numbers continually popping up, and not allowing me to actually make any moves because none of the numbers actually appear consecutively. This has occurred 4 times within the past few days, and it is frustrating for the game to put me in a position when I cannot make any moves. This seems like a bug, and I hope it can be dealt with.
Also, I also agree with a previous review that there should be something added where it will tell you whether or not there are pairs left. As there does not seem to be a way I have found to easily “finish” the game or win, I feel that there is no need to be competitive with hints and such, and just as plenty of other games do, they bring in their audience by listening to them, and feeling stuck and not knowing whether there are pairs or not leaves me feeling frustrated.

End3r_mage9 , 04/30/2020

One BIG downside

This app is great if you’re looking for a game to stretch your brain. It makes you think with puzzles that are challenging but not impossible. However there are loops that you can get stuck in if you eliminate all but a few numbers. This is manageable if you just make your moves carefully. Undos and hints help a lot, as such getting unlimited would be something I would love to do. The one thing that is stopping me is the price point. Rather than being a flat price it is a weekly subscription. Any subscription based payment is deterring as it’s an upkeep you have to mentally and financially manage. However that’s not the worst part. It is $10 every week. This price point is astronomical, even if it were monthly it would still be pushing the limit. I would love to see the prices adjusted to a more reasonable price point. Another complaint I have about the subscription is the lack of options for duration. The only option is weekly, this does give the customer more control however it also prevents any opportunity for bulk discounts. I throughly enjoy this game and would love to see an attainable price for a premium subscription. It plays smooth but the one aspect holding me back is the lack of available undos and hints unless you subscribe at a ridiculous price point or watch a video every two minutes. The game is wonderfully made but that one aspect is the only major downside I see.
