Great Game, Some Bugs

This is easily my favorite game on the internet and I have been playing for years. I love that it has allowed us to keep our same team from year to year but update the players to keep it competitive. I also love that you can play other people in live battles in real time without having to wait for them to take turns. It also has the best gameplay around. By making it harder to hit than most apps, especially in the live battles it really captures what baseball is about unlike in some games where you can hit a home run essentially every time. My only problem with the app is that there are some bugs that need to be fixed. For example since the new 2020 update the app has just randomly force closed while I am in the play modes and today it got so bad that I couldn’t even complete the daily missions because it wouldn’t even let me in the play modes. I am not sure if this is a problem with the game, the high traffic from the start of the new season, or heaven forbid something with my phone but it has been extremely annoying and has cost me multiple wins and in game rewards. Another smaller problem that I have with the game is when I receive notifications from my phone such as the battery level the game logs me out of live battle. This also occurs if I try to check the time at the top of my screen. Overall though it is a great game and I hope that you get back to me to help me understand the issues that I have been having.

S. Cochran , 12/16/2018

This game COULD be amazing if...

This game could be amazing if the developers would fix a few things. Like for instance, I was playing an opponent today who’s overall team rating was CLEARLY better than min, by far. And we were tied 1-1 in the 10th inning. I had a man on 1st and a man on 3rd and only one out. My batter (Acuña Jr.) popped up to the left fielder, at the warning track. So my runner on 3rd tagged up and scored (or so I thought). He clearly beat the throw so the run should have counted. However, when the play was over, it put my runner back on third base and obviously didn’t count the run. And this isn’t the first time this has happened to me. And secondly, there have been multiple times I’ve been playing someone who has a much better team (ratings-wise) and I’d be leading and all of a sudden I get knocked off the game completely. Then I have to wait for the game to load so I can log back into my account and then the game. And my the time I log back in, my opponent has taken advantage of the computer pitching for my team, and he’s scored a few runs. It seems as though the game protects the ones who spend a lot of money on this game so therefore it cheats for those players. I’ve been playing this game for quite a while now, but I’m starting to get to the point to where I just say screw it and never play it again. These issues NEED TO BE FIXED, especially if I’m going to be spending my hard earned money on it. But I’m sure they won’t!
