Description

Poker Analytics 5 is the brand new version of the poker tracker used by tens of thousands of players!

Poker Analytics follows you in your poker life - whether you're a casual player or a pro - and lets you track and analyze your results, record your hands and manage your bankroll.


* Statistics:
The app shows you all the key statistics from your cash games or tournament. You can use the unlimited capabilities of filters to get all the answers you are looking for.

* Reports:
Get a full understanding of your performance with a whole range of reports. See how your game evolves, compare your results by stakes, by game or any parameter you want to learn about your strengths and weaknesses.

* Cash game stop notifications:
Based on your game history, the app notifies you when you’re getting tired and tells you to stop! The app keeps you fresh and away from tilt for the long run.

* Hands recording:
Record all your hands for later review with the super fast hand recorder. Share your hand histories in GIF, video or text to discuss your moves!

* Bankroll management:
Precisely track all your expenses, bonuses and tips, or create your own types of transaction to match your own needs. Define bankrolls with specific currencies and rates for your sessions abroad. Get your risk of ruin and never get broke again!

* Player management:
Manage the result of all the players for your home games with dedicated graphs and take notes on any opponent you play with.

* There is more:
Poker Analytics lets you create custom fields, comes with an M-factor calculator, uses iCloud to backup your data, and the list goes on!

The app offers three different yearly subscriptions to track your sessions.
- 1 session per month: free
- 1 session per week: $14.99 per year (USA)
- Unlimited sessions: $29.99 per year (USA)

Before choosing a plan, you get 25 sessions to test the app. All other features are free of charge.

The app works on your iPhone and iPad, and comes with an Apple Watch extension that allows you to simply manage your sessions on your wrist.

You can learn more on our website. If you have any questions, please contact us!
