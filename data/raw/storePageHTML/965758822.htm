Description

Hello Pal provides an international social platform with instant messaging and livestreaming features. Our users come from more than 200 countries all around the world.

You can chat with people globally whenever you want, seek a relationship, and watch livestreams.

APP HIGHLIGHTS
*****************
Global Coverage

We have photos from more than 200 countries around the world, from the natural scenery of the United States to the historic buildings of Europe, to help you easily understand different cultures.

Instant Friendship

Met an interesting friend from another country? Follow him or her and start a conversation through chat or video calls!

Get Popular

Get popular on our livestream platform and receive gifts from your followers.

Look for a Travel Companion

Enter a travel plan or the country you want to travel to, and you can receive information and travel advice from local friends. Maybe you can find a foreign friend who wants to travel together!

Break the Language Barrier

Chat with people from all over the world ​​with Hello Pal. Together, we can bring the world closer together.

ABOUT HELLO PAL
*****************
Hello Pal's mission is to bring the world closer together by helping eliminate one of the key obstacles to mutual understanding between people: the language barrier. By creating a platform where it is easy to instantly interact with others around the world, and giving them the tools to communicate with each other in a joyful and fun way, we hope to do our part (however small) in fostering understanding and tolerance between all citizens of the world.

For more information, visit www.HelloPal.com.

Eh

I like what this app intended to do...expose people do various cultures, languages, and new friends globally. In reality, I just get spammed with dozens of inappropriately sexual messages or people getting mad because I won’t send pics, even though all I did was post a question asking a cultural or language related question. Yes, I know I can click on “ignore”, but it’s annoying when over 95% of the message you get are attempts to turn this into a dating/hookup app. HiNative is a similar app that manages to accomplish the intents of this app, minus the spam. Maybe if Hello Pal were to incorporate more language related question templates and learning materials, it would help attract a different community of users. In the meantime, it resembles Tinder more than anything else.

Developer Response ,

We're sorry to hear about your negative experience. Hello Pal is meant to be half social app, half language learning app, although there are many users who are more interested in socializing. We do have Community Guidelines in place, so if you met someone or saw a post that violated our rules, please report the person or post (how to report: http://goo.gl/M5KRm7).

If you prefer to see more language-learning content, we highly recommend our other app, Language Pal. Please check that out!
