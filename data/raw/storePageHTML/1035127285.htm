Description

Monitor your dog or puppy while away from home. Get alerted with noise and motion notifications to know when your dog is barking, howling, or suffering from separation anxiety. Record videos of your furry friend owning the house. Talk to your pet remotely to say hello or continue with the training.

Leave an old iPhone or iPad running the app at home as a pet camera. Use your iPhone to receive noise and motion alert notifications from the pet camera. Connect back home and watch live, record videos or take pictures of your pet's funny moments. Soothe him remotely when he misses you.


HOW CAN PET MONITOR VIGI HELP YOU

Want to know what is going on at home, while at work? Use sound and motion alert notifications with automated photo snapshots from the pet camera to get notified about any unexpected activity.

Do you have a dog with separation anxiety? Is your dog barking or howling? Talk to him and soothe him remotely. He can see and hear you on the pet camera unit left at home.

Do not miss any special pet moments. Record videos, and snap photos while remotely watching your pet, share them with friends directly from within the app.

Going out with friends and family has never been easier for pet parents. Switch on Pet Monitor VIGI and enjoy your dinner with a mind free of pet worries.


FEATURES

- Motion and sound alerts with automated photo snapshots from the pet camera
- Two way audio & video chat
- Record videos and snap photos of your pet during live monitoring
- Zoom in on live video
- Activity log recording past behavior of your pet
- Remotely controllable night light
- High quality live video and super sensitive audio
- Unlimited range
- Secure, reliable, easy to use
- Support for Apple Watch notifications

MOTION AND SOUND ALERTS
Get instantly notified of any disturbance in pet activity by the app's sound and motion alerts. Enjoy the sense of security, because if your dog is barking or has a separation anxiety, you will know it right away. Sound and motion alerts are automatically triggered whenever any dog or cat activity is detected by the pet camera. Photos from the camera are saved in the Activity Log, sent to your iPhone, and Apple Watch.

TWO WAY AUDIO AND VIDEO CHAT
Chatting with your furry friend is easy. Watch live video and talk to your dog or puppy remotely from your iPhone or iPad. Keep your pet happy even if you are not at home. Drop in every now and then to say hello, to soothe your dog from separation anxiety, or to continue with the puppy training.

RECORD VIDEOS, SNAP PHOTOS
Do not miss any important moments from your pet friend‘s life. Record videos or take photos of your dog, cat, puppy, or kitty’s special moments remotely with the pet monitor and share them with friends or family directly from within the app to your favorite social media (Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest, Twitter, Snapchat,…).

ACTIVITY LOG, PHOTO LIBRARY
Play back recorded videos, browse photos, examine activity alerts to learn more about how your pet behaves when you are away. Send recorded video clips and photos to your friends, family, or social network.

REMOTE NIGHT LIGHT
Turn on night light when you want to check your puppies at night.

UNLIMITED RANGE
Keep checking on your pet when away from home, watch live video from anywhere. Pet Monitor app works on any Wi-Fi network, over 3G or LTE. Requires no special configuration of your home Wi-Fi router. Protected by industry standard encryption.

REQUIREMENTS
iOS version works on iPhone 4s or newer, iPod touch 5th generation or newer, iPad mini and iPad 2 or newer. Single purchase can be installed on up to 5 devices using your Apple ID. You can use iOS Family Sharing to share the app with other family members for free.

Life Changing

We recently moved to Washington DC. Our poodle mix has severe separation anxiety and was having a difficult time adjusting. Each time we would leave our apartment she would bark incessantly. We were beside ourselves. We were so afraid our neighbors were going to complain. I read the reviews Then purchased and installed Pet Monitor on my iPad and phone. We left the apartment and waited for the first bark. As soon as she started barking I reassured her over the voice command to stop barking and lay down. She immediately stopped!! A few minutes later another notification - same thing. After three or four times she finally got the feeling that someone was with her. Now we have peace of mind that when we leave if she barks I will get a notification and I can calm her down with my voice through the iPad. It has been so liberating for us to be able to explore a new city and know that our dog is not stressed and is calm at home. Thank you, thank you, thank you for this awesome life-changing app.
