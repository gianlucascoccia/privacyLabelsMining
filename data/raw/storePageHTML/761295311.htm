Description

Photo shooting, house lighting, gardening, and sunset watching is better when you know where's Sun gonna be. Enjoy the sun compass!

SunCalc.net on your iPhone! Know the sunset time. Simple tool for everyone who wants to know when and where sun rises or goes down. Combined with compass and time, and weather.

Knows time zones and shows time word wide - probably nicest tool to know the time at some place at the Earth, so far :)

What’s New

Version 1.12

Winter is coming. Going through the dark times, it's always good to know where's the light.
This update has the invisible changes, but brings a bit more light. The battery consumption is reduced (although it was ok, but still!). Also, refined UI integration with dark and light themes, improved location permission detection, and a bunch of other invisible nerdy things. Hope you'll like it!
Follow the light, keep the path, and don't forget to take your camera with you!
