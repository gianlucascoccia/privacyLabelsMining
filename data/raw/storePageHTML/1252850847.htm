Description

Stay in the game with NBA 2K Mobile and experience authentic NBA 2K action on your phone or tablet in a brand new Season. Season 3 offers deep card customization, an updated 2021 roster with more NBA stars and legends, new themes, new MyPLAYER items, and a new event type.

Requires an Internet connection and iPhone 6s, iPad Air 2, iPad Pro, Mini 4, iPod 7th gen or newer.

Experience console quality graphics and lifelike NBA 2K action on the go. Collect your favorite NBA players, build your dream team, and then step onto the court in 5v5 matchups or real-time PVP. Use Drills to train up your players, compete in Seasons to earn rewards and new crafting materials, compete in limited time events for exclusive cards, and rise up the leaderboards on your way to becoming a legend.

NBA 2K Mobile is the newest game from the NBA 2K franchise.

Changes necessary, please look into!!

Please please fix!!!! I love the game but there are some things that I would love to be considered. First of is the gold issue. It is impossible to get gold. I have to grind for days to get any gold, and that is through daily objectives. If you could create another way to get gold or make it so that you get more gold (like 20 gold per objective and 50 gold for completion) that would be awesome! Another thing is the events (Domination, king of the court etc...), they are all really fun but can be really unfair at times. I think of dominations as an example. In amethyst dominations I was at 2857 pwr and the best guy was somewhere in the 3000 pwr range. When I got the 3400 pwr I thought, sweet, I will be the best guy in the event, but no, I was put with players way better than me and I was back at square one. So ANNOYING!!! One more thing, draft board. I like it but it gives issues. It is all about luck, and it can be impossible to get the best reward at times. I would love to see the draft boards have no reset. That way you can always get the best prize and not be disappointed at the end when I resets on something bad. Also maybe announce Redeem Codes on the actual game instead of on a different place (like Twitter) because guys like me don’t have Twitter and can’t get the redeem codes. I still think that the game is really good and that people should totally play it! Please consider making changes though, PLEASE!

fguerra2999 , 06/14/2020

Developers please read

Overall I think this is a great game. The gameplay for mobile is amazing and it’s entertaining. The only thing I ask from developers is to give us a little more control of in game control and rules. Give us the option to turn off auto pass because I’ll be driving down the court ready to pull up and I let off the directional stick for one second and my player automatically fires a pass that I didn’t press for that often leads to a turnover, which is really annoying. Give us options as to allow which calls to be made in game, for example the three second violation I feel glitches a lot because my player will be called for three seconds but he’s not even in the paint which again results in an annoying unnecessary turnover. Lastly give us a few more ways to earn coins, I get y’all trying to make money and I respect but maybe adding coins as a prize for completing a season and winning the finals would be good. That way we’re still earning the coins by playing the game and it gives people like me who really can’t spare money because it’s so tight now a days a chance to get coins to buy packs and level up my teams/players. Anyway like I said overall this is a great game and I love playing it it’s really fun and worth the download. 10/10 would recommend to anyone looking for a new game.
