Description

Bitmo is a mobile gift card app that allows you to earn points and rewards on every purchase you make with our 140+ brands.

How it Works:
- Join: Download the Bitmo app and securely connect your favorite credit or debit card
- Shop: Start shopping! Whether online or in store, you’ll earn points for every purchase you make at Bitmo brands. These points are in addition to any other point or rewards programs you are a part of, so you are just earning more!
- Earn: We’ll curate free gifts and bonus perks just for you based on your purchases and the brands you love.
- Redeem: Cash in your earned points anytime for gift cards to your favorite stores

Download now and we'll give you 4,500 points to get you started!
