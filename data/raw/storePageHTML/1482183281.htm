Description

GeoFamily is a GPS tracker that allows you to know the exact location of your family and friends and does not drain the phone’s battery.

GeoFamily provides:
• Smart location tracking algorithm that saves battery consumption.
• Convenient display of travel history to keep you informed of all locations.
• Unlimited groups for your contacts (Family, Friends, Colleagues)


Energy Saving GPS Tracking
• The application uses the mode of change of location when moving (SLC - Significant Location Change).
• In SLC mode, the location is determined by the system using a mobile signal from the communication tower. Most of the time, GPS does not work, which helps extend battery life.

You can get full functionality with the free version, but our subscription can offer you even more features!
