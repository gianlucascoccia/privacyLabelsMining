Description

A portable recording studio with 17 different sounding studio microphones. Great for podcasters, musicians, voiceover artists & more. As seen on TechCrunch, MacLife, etc.

A mobile recording studio. Record or import audio, swap microphones for different sounds, change studios, edit, color label, sort, share and store your recordings anywhere. Use standalone or connect an external microphone or interface and get the hifi sound of 17 different studio microphones with a swap of the screen.

MicSwap Pro works with any external mic or interface compatible with your iDevice to deliver high fidelity professional sounding recordings. You can also use your Bluetooth mic to record. Great for podcasting, voiceover, music creation and more.

MicSwap Pro comes with all microphones, studio environments, unlimited recording and can be used as a plugin with other apps like Garageband, etc (Audiobus, AUv3, IAA).

MicSwap can also transform your iPhone or iPad into a microphone when you connect it to external speakers. If you're using a computer without a microphone, MicSwap is am easy way to add a mic to your setup so you can add voiceover to videos, record podcasts, add commentary to games and more

• Use standalone or with an external mic or interface

• Edit or trim recordings
• Enable / disable live monitoring

• AudioCopy enabled
• Change backgrounds by swapping studio environments

Real flexibility. Solid. Stable.

First glance: looks great. Half of its value is the "recorder" end of it; which isn't emphasized so much in the details.
So the ability to record and index things - and be able to retrieve them later with whatever mic voice you want creates for tons of possibilities. Especially when used with other production and routing apps.
One immediate concern: the mic is super hot. If your trying to grab, say, a Plexi 6" from the grill, you have to just barely turn on the gain. Even at its lowest setting, it still clips when working with anything "loud".
Can't wait for the new update!
