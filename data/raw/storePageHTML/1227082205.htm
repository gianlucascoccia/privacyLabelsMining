Description

○ App Store "Spring Cleaning" feature 2019
○ App Store "App of the Day" July 1, 2018
○ App Store "New Apps We Love" February 2018
○ #1 on Product Hunt Labor Day 2017

Merge helps couples stay on top of the small, day-to-day responsibilities of living together. It's a private place for just the two of you. Whether you've just moved in together, or you've been married for years, Merge is guaranteed to immediately improve household communication.


Create tasks among these four categories:

- CHORES is meant for household chores
- SHOP is meant for groceries and anything else you may need
- VOTE is for collaborating and coordinating on decisions big and small
- PAY allows you to organize lists of payments, such as rent.


For each kind of task you can:

- Set due dates
- Create reminders
- Sync reminders with Apple Reminders
- Make tasks recurring
- Set task urgency
- Reassign tasks
- Edit tasks
- Add #hashtags to create sublists, e.g., add #camping to all tasks related to your next camping trip to be able to view them in one combined list

Additional features includes:
- Merge Status: a quick and easy way to check in with your partner throughout the day. Choose from several standard statuses to update your partner with just one tap, or create your own! Statuses are cleared after each day.
- Comment on new and completed tasks
- Add a profile picture
- Seamlessly sync across iPhone and iPad

A simple addition

Edit: After using the app for a while, we found that list services like AnyList got the job done much better.
