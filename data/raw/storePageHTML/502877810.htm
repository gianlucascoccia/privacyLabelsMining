Description

You made it! We’re glad you’ve found the official mobile app of the Minnesota Vikings. In our opinion, this is the best app if you’re a diehard Vikings fan… or just want to constantly root against the green and yellow team in Wisconsin.

You’ll be able to turn your phone into a unique part of Vikings games at U.S. Bank Stadium and be the first to get breaking news about your favorite team. Featuring all the best content from Vikings Entertainment Network and Vikings.com, you’ll never miss any videos, photos and articles about the Minnesota Vikings.
And don’t forget to sign up for push notifications so you never miss an important alert.

Features include:
- News: Real-time breaking news from the Vikings, previews of upcoming matchups, post-game
recaps and inside access
- Video: Live shows, press conferences, players shows and behind-the-scenes segments
- Photos: See all the images captured by our team photographers… the best in the biz!
- Audio: Listen to the Minnesota Vikings Podcast Network, including Under Center with Kirk
Cousins
- Stats: Real-time stats and scores from the official NFL stats engine, head-to-head stats of the
matchup, player stats, drive-by-drive stats, box score, out-of-town scores around the league
- Standings: Division and conference standings
- Depth chart: Shown by offense, defense and special teams
- Schedule: See who’s next up for the Vikings and purchase tickets to watch in person
- Games: Luck and skill are combined in Pass or Play, presented by Mystic Lake. Set your lineup
each week, earn points and compete for prizes. You can also try to predict game outcomes with
Stat Showdown for a chance to earn gear from the Vikings Locker Room store.
In-Stadium Features:
• Download and manage your Vikings tickets
• Get directions to the stadium whether you’re driving or taking mass transit
• Explore the stadium and its many points of interest
• Order food via the app for a safe, contactless experience
• Gain Vikings Rewards points for using your Vikings app
