HOA President , 02/20/2021

Best game

I play a lot of games, and this one is the best. It has lots of different ways to keep you engaged in the game. There is always something to do and lots of ways to improve your characters without paying. I typically dislike games that force you to join a group because they usually force you to participate in events at specific times rather than when you want to. This game does not do that, instead the group is more of a way to improve your own characters and statistics, while occasionally joining a raid or an alliance event. You can choose not to participate, but the rewards are totally worth it. I even ended up become the leader just so that I could see what it entails. So far as leader, I mostly make sure that the base is improved as abilities are upgraded and to put stuff in the shop for other members. As leader, I was able to assign 4 people to leadership roles to help with that. One caveat, when building forts and hospitals you have to assign a group to build it. This build takes days to complete. While others can help you, that may not occur so wait until you have the ability to send out two groups so that you can build and still play.

Game is nice

I wasn't sure about this game at first but when i saw how the character leveling and how important it is to upgrade your people on the nostalgic menu i was hooked. Weird way to stay but this game is not as bad as the other builders when it comes to your base and you can research build speed almost right away. Im already headquarters level 5 and i gotta say i cant wait to progress and get as far as i can. The puzzle gameplay is as shows. Each character has one and i have to say Doc is my favorite because no matter how much of a beating my team takes. As long as i make a few matches with the same element as him or whatever it is he heals my entire party for a chunk of their health.
