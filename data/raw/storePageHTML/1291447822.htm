Achieve the fitness results you want – inside and outside of the gym – with 24GO, the Official App of 24 Hour Fitness.

Designed for members, friends or anyone who wants to live fitter, 24GO coaches you on what to do to achieve results, in the ways that work best for you.

Based on the goals and preferences you enter, 24GO will recommend workouts – and personalize them to fit your needs, no matter where you are. Custom plans and easy scheduling help keep fitness on your calendar, so you always have what you need to succeed.


KNOW WHAT TO DO AND WHEN with Custom Workout Recommendations

- 24GO custom picks workouts for you that fit your goals, equipment and location in that moment, so you can go straight to working out.

- Adapt your workouts on the fly: skip or swap out exercises, change the duration, or add or remove equipment for the perfect fit.

ACHIEVE YOUR RESULTS FASTER with Thousands of Workouts and Plans

- Browse over 1,500 in-club and at home workouts from trusted partners like 24 Hour Fitness, Your Trainer, Yoga52, LES MILLS, Zumba, and more..

- Work out at home in virtual group training sessions led by 24 Hour Fitness coaches with over 50 sessions available per week including cardio, strength, yoga and more.

- Create personalized workout routines set to you and your goals with Smart Workouts.

- Get expert coaching in your ear with audio-guided cardio workouts.

- Find live in-club studio classes and on-demand group exercise videos.



- Get a game plan for success with multi-week, goal-oriented fitness plans you can customize to fit your schedule.


STAY ON TRACK TO RESULTS with Scheduling and Reminders

- Allow 24GO access to your mobile calendar, so you can plan out your week and add workouts and classes.

- Receive alerts and reminders about your scheduled classes, workouts and training sessions, so you never miss the best part of your day.

- Allow 24GO access to your location to find nearby 24 Hour Fitness clubs and classes.

MEASURE YOUR PROGRESS with Performance and Goal Tracking

- Fitbit integration - Connect your Fitbit account and your 24GO workouts will count towards your goals in Fitbit.

- Heart Rate Monitor integration - Add your Bluetooth 4.0 capable device and view HR inside your workout.

- Apple Health integration - 24GO can send your workout data (Active Energy, Heart Rate, and Workouts) to Apple Health, so they count towards your Activity Rings. 24GO can also read data from Apple Health (Date of Birth, Height, Weight and Sex) and use this data to recommend 24GO workouts.

GET THE MOST FROM YOUR 24 HOUR FITNESS MEMBERSHIP

- Get a free fitness appointment with a personal trainer, to help you build and customize your 24 Hour Fitness Fit Plan.

- Add your 24 Hour Fitness personal trainer to your account for extra accountability and personalized support.

- Find your favorite classes, add them to your mobile calendar, set reminders and get real-time updates.

- Invite friends to join you for classes and workouts by sending them a free guest pass to 24 Hour Fitness.

reinalove , 08/08/2018

Ok as a guide to follow loosely

I signed up for the weight loss program. All the workouts start out with the use of a band but apparently my gym in Fairfield, CA does not have them for use unless I buy one. I asked the front counter as I have never seen those type of bands there. The front desk did not seem to know anything about what the app has, which was disappointing. I think the staff should be familiar with it. The app only allows me to change the type of band workout but doesn’t allow me to delete it all together. The app should not suggest gym workouts with equipment that are not available at EVERY gym. Also the timer is kind of annoying in that it only times the ENTIRE workout, rather than breaking up each portion although it does allow you to pause. So when if part 3 of my workout is to do jumps for one minute, I have to remember if I started that minute at 15:15 and stop myself at 16:15 or what have you. I did the day 2 workout on day 3 instead but there is no option to move stuff around. Also it would be VERY nice if we could track our progress in terms of weights on different machines or exercises. Overall I use the app as a guide but I do my own stuff as well. It’s nice to get out of my comfort zone and same old routine and do something different than what I normally do at the gym.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for the feedback Reinalove! We've added your request to our list of future enhancements. If you have further suggestions/feedback please let us know by contacting us at support@24GO.zendesk.com, thank you!

sunnysideupsss , 10/17/2018

I think it’s great but....

I think this app is great so far! My only complaint would be that there isn’t a page/tab to show your progress. I want to be able to log my weight/measurements and have it show on a graph. I hate to compare but the FitnessPal app lets you log your weight and you can see your fitness journey on a graph. There should also be an option to be able to choose which progress measurements you’d like to see on your graph. For an example, an option to be able to see my weight loss progress over the month, week or even year. Another option would be able to see your body measurements change over the course of a week, month or year. It’s way more satisfying and motivating to see your weight loss journey that way. Maybe the home screen (GO) can feature your progress. Maybe have the first thing you see be more motivating and encouraging. I would love to input my fitness data on your app and have it all in one place.
Something else that I thought could improve was your classes tab. Maybe I haven’t figured it out yet but I’d love to be able to just select my gym for the classes schedule instead of constantly resetting it. This app would be way more helpful if it was customizable to the user. Would love to see these changes! 💪🏻🔥

Developer Response ,

Hi sunnysideupsss! Thank you for using 24GO and sharing your feedback with us. We hear you and we absolutely agree on the importance of tracking your progress. That's why we're working behind the scenes on some awesome new features like an enhanced dashboard that can help you track your progress and stay motivated. Keep an eye out for these updates in the future! Regarding Classes, the latest version of the app 1.3.1 allows you to set any filter one time and it will apply every time you come back to the app, unless you do a force quit or logout. If you're still having an issue with the filters resetting and you're not force quitting, please feel free to email our Support Team at support@24go.zendesk.com and we'll assist you with troubleshooting the issue. Thanks again for sharing your thoughtful feedback with us. We really appreciate it! Keep up the awesome work!
