Pete Kelly , 01/07/2018

Not compatible with iOS 11

I’m giving this app 3 stars because the content selection is 5 stars, but the app itself is 1 star. Many of the live action videos are streamed at a poor resolution and the video occasionally stutters even while playing on a very fast 100 Mbps WiFi network.

The biggest problem is that it is not compatible with iOS 11, which no longer has a system-wide airplay option separate from screen mirroring. As a result many video apps have an airplay option incorporated into the app, but LS does not. This leaves screen mirroring as the only option to airplay LS content. Screen mirroring has many setbacks for this type of content, including not adapting to the proper resolution for the TV (creating vertical and horizontal letter-boxing), continuing to show the status bar on top at all times, and the inability to use different apps or allow the screen to sleep while mirroring. The last app update was 5 months ago, prior to the release of iOS 11. This app sorely needs an update to catch up.
