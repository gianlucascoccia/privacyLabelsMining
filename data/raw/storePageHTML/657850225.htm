Description

Facebook Social Network & Online Dating Lock - secures your online social life and online dating life by requiring a passcode with TouchID every time accessing your social networks.

Support Social Networks:

◆ Facebook
◆ Twitter
◆ Google+
◆ LinkedIn
◆ My Space
◆ Youtube
◆ Pinterest
◆ Tumblr
◆ Weibo
◆ Tagged
◆ Match.com
◆ OkCupid
◆ Plenty of Fish
◆ Instagram
◆ Flickr
◆ Blogger
◆ Reddit
◆ Secured and Private web browser to access any mobile web apps.

The app uses the mobile website of the social networks so you get the same high level of security provided by the social networks. We do not log or store any of your credentials or personal information.
