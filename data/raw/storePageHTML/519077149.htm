Description

Loved and trusted by over 3 Million users - Cleaner Pro is the best free duplicate contacts remover out there!

• Merge duplicate contacts and clean up your phone book
• Easily find and remove duplicate contacts
• Safely undo changes with a single tap


Your contacts address book is one of your biggest assets in day-to-day life. That’s why it’s important to keep it in order and constantly updated. Our great contacts manager app will help you with this task.


Cleaners Pro app’s mission is to prevent your contacts from becoming a mess. It will help you with the following functions:



Merge Duplicate Contacts:

Having the same address book for many years, switching phones, importing contacts from other sources – can all cause the appearance of duplicate contacts.


Cleaner Pro will help you to find and merge these duplications, by allocating contacts with similar names or identical phone numbers and email addresses.



Contacts Cleanup:

Simple mistakes and lack of attention can cause the creation of “bad” contacts with missing data. Locate and get rid of these "worthless" contacts, with missing names or phone numbers, by simply removing them for good.



Create restore points:

Avoid the chance of losing your precious contacts by creating a restore point before each address book changes.



Apply Smart Filters:

Quickly find the contacts you need by applying filters, such as - Creation Date, Company Name, Job Title and Upcoming Birthday

TimothyC , 11/19/2019

Quite Good, Then Rather Disappointing

The app works well the first time it scans your contacts — especially if you have a relatively large database and have not used any similar product previously. It catches most everything and makes it relatively easy to clean up duplicates, delete useless records, etc. That said, I have two major pet peeves. First, the app counts any record that has the same phone number as another record as a duplicate record. The suggested solution— to combine the two records into one or delete one of the records — is not helpful. In most cases, it’s a situation where a couple shares a phone number so it’s in each of their records. I want to keep both records as they are. I don’t see an option to do that so the same records keep showing up as duplicates, and keep wanting me to choose between merge or delete. I think there is something I’m missing because there just has to be a way to deal with this issue in the app. But I can’t find it and, my second pet peeve, Support has never responded to either of my requests for help with this issue. So there may be an easy way to deal with it in the app, but if there is, Support is not interested in sharing that information with me.

KevinReza82 , 01/25/2020

Deletes Notes Section of All Contacts & can’t Restore

This app seemed great. Except when I merged the duplicate contacts it does not keep all the fields- I know for sure that the “Notes” section gets deleted, and there could be other fields as well who knows. The Notes section gets deleted even if both contacts had notes. So it seems that the app is creating a new contact instead of keeping the most complete contact and updating it with info that does not match from the other contact being deleted- which would be a much better approach and help to reduce this problem. I write important notes in the contact field so this is really bad, I would rather have duplicate contacts than lose important info. Also, before it merges the app backs up your contacts. And there is a section in the app that allows you to Restore from this backup. BUT the back ups do NOT have the Notes section either so the back up for this issue is useless and gives you a false sense of comfort to do the merge. If you had this same problem and sync to Exchange then go to your Deleted Items (or Online your Deleted Contacts) and restore from there. Took a lot of wasted time but I got my contacts back, but with more duplicates now!
