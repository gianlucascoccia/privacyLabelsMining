Description

Smart TV Cast allows you to mirror phone screen to your Smart TV. It also allows you to stream media content (Photos or Videos) directly from your Camera Roll.

[Features]
- Cast photos & videos from your Camera Roll
- Mirror you phone screen directly to Smart TV
- Supports any Smart TV with DLNA protocol, such as Samsung, LG, Sony, Vizio, Phillips, Panasonic etc.
- No wires and no additional hardware needed!

Make sure that your iPhone or iPad is connected to the same local network as your Smart TV. Don't use multiple VLANs or subnets.
