Description

Take advantage of the unique mobility application Free2Move to meet your travel needs: car sharing, car rental, parking and driver service.

With a presence in 170 countries around the world and 400,000 vehicles available for rent, Free2Move simplifies your daily mobility.


Car sharing in Paris :

Need to get around town at any time? Locate a car around you and drive in one click with over 500 self-service electric cars.

Take advantage of electric car-sharing for a few minutes or a day, anywhere in Paris and the suburbs. Thanks to our application, locate a car downstairs from your home or office, unlock it, drive and park in the service area on a paid parking lot: it's free with Free2Move!

During each refill, the vehicles are cleaned with disinfectants applied to the contact areas.

- 100% mobile: location, reservation, car opening, payment... You control everything with one app.

- Clean cars: our teams maintain the fleet regularly to guarantee you a quality service.

- Charged cars: we recharge the cars daily to ensure optimal autonomy.


Car Rental :

Need to move or go on vacation? Choose the vehicle and duration, which adapts to your needs. Free2Move offers a flexible car rental solution from one day to one month with a wide range of recent vehicles available at Peugeot, Citroën, DS, Opel outlets and airports!


Free2Move Rent is :

3 drivers at no extra charge

24-hour roadside assistance

100% digital experience with the 24/7 service: book, open, start the vehicle and end the rental anytime from your smartphone.


As a complement for companies, Free2Move guarantees employee mobility and fleet management with :

A car available in less than 24 hours

An adapted price offer and centralized billing

A national framework contract with an all-inclusive formula (insurance, assistance, maintenance) and centralized invoicing.

The Free2Move rental service is already available in France, Italy and Spain, with more than 30,000 private and commercial vehicles in 3,000 branches.

Parking :

Need to park in train stations, airports and city centres? Our application allows you to find a parking space anywhere in the world. And that at the best price!

Find your ideal parking space among 500,000 available spaces in 65 countries.

Car parks wherever you want: airports, ports, city centres and train stations.

24/7 secure car parks, including official airport car parks

Easily reserve your parking space in advance or at the last minute

Our car parks are available at almost every airports: London, Manchester, Luton, Birmingham, Liverpool, Glasgow etc...

Driver & Transfer :

Need a taxi, driver or Transfer around your position or at the airport? Free2Move allows you to instantly book among the best offers:

Compare and choose your driver

Easily book your transfer at the time and place of your choice

More than 21,000 destinations and 150 countries covered

Immediate booking or up to 30 days in advance

JCinDC , 11/26/2020

Useful service, so-so app

I originally left a one star review because I couldn’t get past the initial first step of reserving a vehicle for the next day. Once I tried booking nearby available vehicles on demand though, I realized the Free2Move service is actually fairly seamless. The *app* is still buggy and exasperating at times. For instance, I booked a car yesterday and when I arrived to start the car it had a completely flat tire? Like with no warning. Also at the end of a ride the “rate your ride” review prompt pops up twice for no reason. And on a couple of occasions I’ve accidentally left something in the car or forgotten to roll up a window, something people do all the time — the app really needs to account for contingencies like this, instead of forcing us to call the service line or rebooking just to roll up a window. But overall, if you’re a car-free individual who needs to get around fairly quickly and cheaply (and safely during this pandemic), Free2Move is a good choice.

Noonc , 09/30/2020

Great service, horrible new app, terrible customer support

This new app is the worst. I’ve tried to come to terms with it. I really have. But it just doesn’t doesn’t work at all on one phone—it gives me weird errors about my credit card or already having a reservation. Then I try it with the same account, credit card, etc on another phone and it reserves fine. But even then the new app has fewer features and is harder to use. Try viewing a list of your previous charges. All you’ll get is a list of giant photos of a car/SUV and you have to open each of them separately to see the cost and time. Why not have a list with times and costs? The cars all look the same; I don’t need photos of them. Also there’s no timer showing how long I’ve had a car rental anymore, which I suspect is to avoid us arguing when they bill us for more time than we used it (I used to take screenshots of the timer to show when I actually ended the trip versus when Free2Move registered the end of the trip. Last, God help you if you have any problems because when you call they won’t be able to do anything. They will just tell you they’ll refer it to the billing or tech departments who will get back to you in 24 hours. Plot spoiler: they won’t. Can we go back to the old app and get some phone support where they can actually help with issues?

Developer Response ,

Hi Noonc, we apologize for the inconvenience and we will make sure that the concerned service take care of this matter and keep the app more practical. Free2Move Team

Love the service, hate the app

I love Free2Move cars - they’re really how I get around in my home city. However, I’ve had so many issues with the new app upgrade that occurred within the past couple of months. I’ve had multiple instances of me ending a trip but the app doesn’t register it properly - I’ll reopen it later and it will look as though the trip ended, but when I try to reserve a new car it doesn’t allow me. I’ve resorted to taking screenshots of my phone each time I end a trip to have proof that I’ve ended it. Fix these bugs, they’re horrible!
