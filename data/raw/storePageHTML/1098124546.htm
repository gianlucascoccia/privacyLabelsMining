Description

Add Music to Video is the most advanced video editing app on the App Store. Make your everyday videos more exciting by adding background music or voice-overs to your video. Create music videos for Facebook, TikTok, Messenger, Instagram, Snapchat, or YouTube. Join the community of millions of people who trust and use our creative services for fast, powerful, and easy video editing on the go.

KEY FEATURES:
• 60,000+ royalty free music from 80+ different music genres.
• Add multiple background music and voice overs to your photos and videos.
• Import music directly from Apple Music.
• Precise Trimming: Remove any unwanted part of a photo or video.
• Stunning Filters: Multiple filters for your project.
• Add fonts, emojis, stickers, etc from multiple categories.
• Combine Videos: Edit, reposition & merge multiple videos.
• Track Positioning: Precisely reposition your audio for a perfect result.

CherryMadison , 06/01/2020

Excellent selection of music but...

I was so eager and excited to subscribe upon sampling the wide variety of amazing music. However, when it came time to edit my video I was sourly disappointed with the poor efficiency in the very few editing tools they do have.

The music audio provided in this app is far too loud. I’m not able to hear the voiceover in my pre-made video. There is a tool to adjust the volume of each, but for whatever reason whenever I do adjust the volume it doesn’t register or “save”. So there’s really no point in having a non-functioning volume adjusting tool.

Also, there’s no option to add pre-made voice over. Given, you’d like a voiceover in your project the voice-over has to be added live during editing which is wack because this app doesn’t have the capability to edit voice-overs in the way I’m looking for.

All in all the quality of this app is extremely unbalanced. Again, the music is amazing (*****) but the editing capabilities are extremely basic and limited. (* ) The programmers should either upgrade the editing capabilities or allow access to download the songs provided on this app.

That being said, I will have to cancel my subscription and look elsewhere being that this app is virtually of no use to me.
