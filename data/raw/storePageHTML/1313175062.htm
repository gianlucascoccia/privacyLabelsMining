Description

Amazing camera filters & effects with Disco's Aesthetic video editor. Vintage filters, VHS, Retro, Glitch, Light leaks, endless trendy effects to create stunning videos for social media

With Disco Videos you can:
- Record live trippy videos and clips with a variety of filters and effects including chromatic effects, glitch effects, distortions, mirror effects, VHS effects, beautify, blends, collages, duo-tone and pixel effects.
- Add music to your videos from our free music library or add your own.
- Mix, edit and blend your existing videos into new music videos.
- Add text to videos with beautiful text effects.
- Make intro videos using dancing text, video footage, music and effects
- Take photos using beautiful and surreal filters.
- Express yourself live with video and audio effects and joysticks that move with the beat.
- Add your voice or beatbox to videos.
- Record live video selfies or add music to your existing videos.
- Share your music videos to Instagram, Snapchat, Facebook, Musical.ly, Youtube and Twitter as stories or posts.

With Disco Videos Pro you get:
* Unlimited videos.
* Unlimited access to all filters.
* Unlimited access to all text effects.
• No watermark on exported videos.

Amazing video filters

I love this app! It’s a wonderful app for creating unique beautiful videos and stories for social media. I use it very often to make cool videos and am always getting asked by my followers how I made them. The app offers over 60 different cool filters and effects such as glitches, mirrors, vintage, vhs, distortions and more, all that move together with the beat of the music. There is also an amazing collections of songs to pick from. You can also add text which dances to the beat, mix several videos together, slow down videos and more. The results are stunning! The free version offers a bunch of free filters and gives you access to all of the capabilities, but you might want to upgrade to get access to all of the filters. But try it for free and see if you like it first. Have fun!!

Moderately interesting

So here is the problem with subscriptions… When I do work on a video, I use multiple apps, often up to six at a time in order to achieve the effects that I am looking for. I’m sorry, your app is not the only one out there and although it seems interesting, I’m afraid it is not the very best and thus, It’s not worth the almost $30 buy in rate or the monthly subscription. I pay for moderately priced apps, I never, ever, go for subscriptions. If your app was the only interesting one out there, it might be a different story. Every developer thinks their own app is the end all be all… but there is a cornucopia of interesting apps out there. You, could be one piece of the puzzle, but at that price, probably not.
