UltravioletBlue , 12/22/2020

Awesome, just a few suggestions.

This is an awesome app. Like, I would not prefer any other widget app at all. But I have a few suggestions. First, would it be possible to add on a widget making tutorial? Like, I really want to make a widget but I don’t understand half of what I’m supposed to do when I go to make the widget. Another thing, would it be possible to add live gif widgets? I mean, it might cash some people’s phones, but I still think it would be a fun thing to add. One problem I found was that whenever I go to click on the little icons in the widget that is supposed to take me to the app it is representing, it just sends me to the widgetopia app instead. Otherwise, this is a really awesome app that I would share with all of my friends (if they had iPhones).

Developer Response ,

We are glad you are enjoying widgetopia! If you would like to help us, please share widgetopia on social media and with your friends. Right now we are a very small app and not many people have heard of us! Let us also know what features you would like to see in the next update?

sageypants , 02/28/2021

Almost there

I just bought a premium version of this app and it’s a little expensive for what’s on offer so far at a pretty heft $6 price point but this widget app has by far the most options of any I’ve ever tried and I’m happy to support its creators for how ambitious they were.

My only gripes as of now for only having the app one night is that for some reason it seems to destroy the quality of my images when I use the photo widget option. It will look very high quality in the preview however when I add it to my home page it’s suddenly pixelated and ugly. I know it doesn’t have to do with the dimensions because the preview is flawless so I’m not sure what’s going on there but that could use some optimizing.

Additionally there is an advertised option to add animated gifs to your Home Screen but I have yet to successfully do it. Either that part of the app doesn’t function as intended or it’s too complicated to figure out without a little guidance—and I did check the FAQ beforehand to no avail.

In the future I’d love to see some widget renaming options. Developers may not be able to hide app names but if we had some kind of edit option, I use the empty character “ “ to hide the names of apps via the shortcut app. It would be great if I could do this with my widgetopia photos as well.

Keep up the great work!

Developer Response ,

Thanks for your feedback. You can adjust graphics quality in options. We will look into your other suggestions.
