PurplePenguin355 , 12/02/2018

Ads + Glitch

First of all, great game to play, super addictive and fun! Unfortunately, there were a couple things that prevented it from a 5 star rating. For one, there are way too many advertisements, and they would sometimes affect gameplay. For example, you would see an ad when you enter the game. You close it out, and you start a race. A few seconds later, an ad pops up and forces you to stop racing, resulting in a loss. This can be very frustrating and I believe it should be fixed. Secondly, the glitch I mentioned isn’t actually a glitch, just a mistake in programming. Sometimes, when you earn enough coins to advance to another thousand, it will give you another thousand coins you didn’t earn. In one instance I experienced, I started off with 1,400 coins. I then earned 800 more, which should have resulted in 2,200 coins. However, the total came to 3,200. (1,000 more than expected) I hope that these two issues get fixed in the future. Despite the problems, it is a great game!
