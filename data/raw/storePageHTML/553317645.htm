Description

The first release of the redesigned MySCE app allows you to quickly and easily pay your bill online, or see your billing history. You can also report an outage, whether it’s your home or office, or if you see a streetlight that’s out.

Features include:
• Pay your bill online.
• Let the app notify you when your bill is ready.
• View 37 months of your billing history.
• Report an outage.
• Sign up for outage notifications.
