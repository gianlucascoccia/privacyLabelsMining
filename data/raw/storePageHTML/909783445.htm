Description

When Super-sleuths Freddi Fish and Luther arrive at school they are just in time to witness what they believe to be a ghost, and to make matters worse the ghost is also taking toys that belong to the students. Freddi and Luther quickly take on the case and devise a plan to catch the ghost. All they need is a little help from you!
