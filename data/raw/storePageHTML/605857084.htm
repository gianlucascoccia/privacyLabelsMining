Description

Welcome to California Pizza Kitchen! The CPK Rewards™ app is your go to hub for tracking rewards, online ordering, surprise offers and seasonal menu updates!

Features include:
+ View, track and apply your available CPK Rewards to your check
+ Pay by mobile right at your table
+ Order your CPK favorites for take-out
+ Search your nearby locations
+ View seasonal menu features
+ Receive notifications about local promotions and special offers

CPK Rewards member enjoy:
+ Free Small Plate when you register (added 24 hours after registration)
+ Earn 1 point for every $1 you spend (excluding alcohol, tax, gratuity and retail)
+ 75 points = $5 Reward
+ Free Dessert on your birthday
+ Exclusive members-only offers throughout the year
+ View other special CPK Rewards benefits like Free Birthday Entrée, Skip the Line (for up to 4 people), Sneak Peek Access to New Menu Items and more at cpk.com/newcpkrewards

Online ordering is badly broken

I love the convenience of online ordering, especially these days. Unfortunately, I have very little use for the app if I’m actually in the store. The app logs you out of your account as soon as you select online ordering and takes you to the website in the app browser window. You must then login again there or you are ordering as a guest. After logging in should you try to go back to a previous screen you are taken back to the app main screen. This closes your online ordering and deletes your cart. If you go back to online ordering then you will have to login again. This is repeated easily over and over.
It’s actually easier to call the store and place your order than ordering via the app.

Please fix it, QA of the app should have caught the issue 100 out of 100 times.
