Description

Your letter has finally arrived! What kind of witch or wizard will you be? A heroic Gryffindor? A cunning Slytherin? A clever Ravenclaw? A loyal Hufflepuff? YOU decide! With countless choices, you’ll be able to carve out your own unique path in Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery.

This is YOUR Hogwarts journey. Whether you’re mastering powerful spells with Dumbledore, brewing potions with Snape, discovering never-before-seen rooms at Hogwarts, forging alliances with new friends, or duelling your rivals, there’s always something new to explore! As part of WBIE’s Portkey Games label, this groundbreaking mobile game puts you at the centre of an all-new story in the Wizarding World.

WITCHCRAFT & WIZARDRY:
• Learn magical spells and brew powerful potions!
• Unlock spells, potions, and locations as you advance through the years of Hogwarts!
• Immerse yourself in the world of Harry Potter!
• Take your place among witches and wizards!

MYSTERY & ADVENTURE:
• Use your skills to investigate mysteries at Hogwarts!
• Discover the puzzling truth behind the Cursed Vaults and your brother's disappearance in an all-new story!

FRIENDSHIPS THAT MATTER:

Airfield munch , 11/13/2018

Almost* Perfect 🧙🏻‍♀️

This game deserves a five star rating just because the creators did such an amazing job at making Hogwarts, the entire app, look so amazing and on point with how the school actually looks.

I was immediately addicted to this game as soon as I downloaded it months ago. I love that you can make your own custom witch/wizard. If you’re a Harry Potter fan, being able to choose your House is a big deal. The story starts and you’re hooked.

I’ve read the negative reviews and most people just don’t want to pay for anything extra. That’s fine. This game does not require you to buy anything for you to be able to move forward. But if you love the game, want to buy some extra gems on a sale for some extra energy, then don’t feel guilty about spending $5-$20! It supports the game, helps bring updates faster, and helps the creators expand the game further. All for FREE! People need to stop complaining about getting an amazing game for free and that they don’t want to spend money or wait for the energy to refill. Just support the game! If you love it, support it! If it was a popular Xbox game, you would be spending $65, plus whatever the season pass was, PLUS a monthly fee to play online. I’d rather spend $20 on Harry Potter and hope they expand the game or make a second game. Our money really does help bring us amazing entertainment with this particular team. They’re awesome!

Truthful Game reveiwer , 06/04/2020

It’s Great but...

This game for many Harry Potter fans wanting more of the magic world of witch craft and wizardry is amazing and fun, it’s also good for people looking for a little thrill. This game doesn’t have ads popping up every few seconds and I had this game a while ago and was easily sucked in being a Harry Potter fan myself. I later forgot the game and ended up deleting it when just recently I got it back and it has honestly been pretty fun but I can see why I had come to forget it in the first place, the energy. Firstly, you don’t even start off with that much but on top of that, all of the things you need to do require a lot of energy and when your out you either have to wait a few minutes for them to give you like one (which won’t do you any good) or you have to watch a video which really only gets you three which isn’t much better. I usually wait till the next day to get full energy but there’s also a time limit that you have to follow in many quests and classes which is usually up by the next day so I honestly can’t find a good solution to this problem, maybe you can try to fix some things. The final thing is I don’t think you can restart the game, even when deleting it for a while. I honestly want to start a new house with a new character since I can’t remember what was going on last time but I really don’t think that’s an option, maybe you can also do something about that to? Besides that this game is pretty fun and one that I won’t forget for a while.

Kaye Moon , 10/23/2020

I love this game but.....

I’ve been obsessed for the longest time, and I was super excited when I finished the first year. However I couldn’t even finish the welcoming feast in year two before the game crashed. So I said “Alright, I’ll just restart the game.” So I did. It started the feast over so I went through it again but in the same spot it crashed again. I tried several more times and again and again it crashed and would start me back over. This made me so so upset because this happened on my previous phone too, which was an iPhone 5 and I thought that this new iPhone 6 was going to be better. And it was! Until the second year feast. I just really wish that I could play the game all the way through without getting pushed back when it crashes. I can’t continue the game anymore and it saddens me because I really looked forward to playing the rest of it. I can’t even finish events anymore because the game will crash while I’m playing at random. I hope maybe the developers and people who work on the game can fix this, because I love this game. It’s so fun and whimsical and makes me laugh. It takes me back to being a kid and loving HP and watching it during Christmas time and Halloween. I adore the characters, the story is so compelling, and the visuals are really cool and often funny.
I really just want to know what’s going on, and why it keeps crashing, and I hope it gets fixed.
Thank you for a great game while it lasted, I hope that one day I may play again.
