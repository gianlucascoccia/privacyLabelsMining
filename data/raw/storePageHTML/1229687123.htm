Description

Get the best spam protection for your phone with Sprint Call Screener. Identify unknown callers by name and get warnings on Spam calls. Block individual unwanted callers by number or use the advanced block filter to automatically block all Spam calls by risk level. You can also customize your own Spam list and get contextual information on Spam numbers.

Sprint Call Screener is built with patented technology to deliver identity information before, during, and after an incoming call. We continuously analyze call events, over 25 billion per month, to provide the most accurate and up-to-date Spam protection

Update fixed an issue

Really lacking in features. I’m paying three dollars for the service a month and it doesn’t tell me the calls that it has blocked automatically. I’d like a history of all the calls that it has automatically blocked on my phone.

As for the latest update that’s about 30 hours old as of the time of this review, it fixed a huge problem with the app that it basically was not allowing me to search numbers directly from my call list. However, there was nothing in the App Store on my iPhone that said there was a pending update. It could be that the update was throttled because it was so new. On a hunch, I went into the app in the App Store and I was just lucky to have seen that there was an update to the app. This is not the same as updating the call block list, which appears to work every time I open the app. It would be great if they call block list could be updated off-line without the app having to be open.

Might be nice to allow us to use the T-Mobile name ID app instead.

Andyboypj , 09/11/2020

Does not stop calls

I download this app because it was free with Sprint. If you are looking to stop unwanted calls from getting through this is not the app for you. Although I’m sure it does block a few numbers for big companies or registered corporations. The main problem I have and what most people have are robocalls and cloned numbers. Thus app will give you a name to an unwanted number but that doesn’t mean that’s who the call in coming from. So these type of calls still get through. I still get about 5 per day on average. So if you’re looking for something to stop the madness of unsolicited phone calls, this is not the app to do so.
