Description

Night Owl Connect helps you keep an eye on your world, from anywhere in the world. Monitor your home or business, in real time, on your Smart Phone or Tablet. Easily share images and recordings via your favorite social network or through email and text. Be smart, be safe, be connected!

Night Owl Connect Features:
Quick and easy mobile setup
Live video and audio from your Cameras on Wi-Fi or 3G/4G LTE networks
Easily configure push notifications from your Smart Device
Save recordings directly to your Smart Device
Save images directly to your Smart Device and share via text, email or social media
Share important MP4 formatted videos via email or social media
Video playback
Multiple logins/users
Two-way audio
Google Integration (Limited devices)
Fish Eye Camera
Human Detection, Familiar Face, and Unfamiliar Face Alerts

WARNING: This application uses data to connect your Cameras to your Smart Device. When connected to a 3G or 4G LTE network, the live video feed from your Camera will be considered streaming data by your phone service provider and will contribute to any data or download limit your phone data plan may have. If you exceed your data plan limit, this may result in additional usage charges. It is highly recommended to contact your phone service provider to know the limit of your data plan before using this video streaming application.

I knew it was going to be different...

Coming from the Arlo cameras I knew this system was going to be different, but not this much... cameras work great, definition is there but the app LACKS so many good essential features. I wanted an “upgrade” but can’t believe this app is missing so many good features, even from the gen 1 Arlo cameras which is what I’m coming from, YES! gen 1... Why not have only one screen with all 6,8,10 cameras in it? Can’t put cameras in the order I want, I have to swipe to next screen to see the camera I want to see. Can’t adjust camera brightness from the app, landscape mode? Nope not supported... you can just play and pause recorded clips and select live view from the app, anything else forget about it... the technology is there is just not put to work for some reason. This camera system/app needs a total revamp. I think i’m returning this system and getting a new Arlo... I really want to like this camera system and app, cameras are wireless, easy to mount, easy to install and super easy to sync and range is pretty good but I just can’t keep it with all these simple features missing right now. Hope Night Owl listens to customers concerns and try to deal with them. Thanks in advance if you do.

Looks like Night Owl is listening! We’ll look for future updates, 😎 thank you!

Does not work consistently

First of all, the cameras work great. But the app is so bad, half the time the cameras don’t do me any good. I deleted the basement camera off the app months ago because the only thing it ever alerted me to was the wind blowing! I tried adjusting the sensitivity but that did not help. One day, my alarm started going off, but alas, I had deleted my basement camera and could not see who had set off my alarm. Fortunately it was my housekeeper but that could have been bad! The front porch one is not as bad, I’m assuming because the wind is more blocked by the porch roof. But on the Fourth of July, the alerts were incessant. It was just my flag blowing in the wind. However, a lot of times, the app will not load at all. So, if an actual person was on my porch, ringing my doorbell, I would not be able to get it to load fast enough to see who it is. And in the last couple of weeks, the app stopped working all together. Now it just spins. My cameras are now totally useless. A bunch of people worked really hard installing these cameras for me, not to mention, I paid a lot for them. If this does not get resolved soon, I will be forced to shell out another couple hundred bucks to buy more cameras that are a different brand and hope if those nice people will come back and install them for me.
