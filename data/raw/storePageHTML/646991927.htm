Description

Get unlimited access to 3,400+ streaming and downloadable high-quality, fun, and effective Pilates workouts you can do anywhere, anytime. 195+ of the industry's best teachers will guide you every step of your practice, whether you are just beginning, or you are an advanced practitioner. Customize your Pilates experience for any duration, level, equipment, prop, or special need. We add new classes every week, so you will always have access to fresh, new workouts.

For Pilates teachers, enrich your practice and your teaching with online workshops, Pilates history, talks, tutorials, and more.

Start your free 14-day trial and try one of our challenges to get started!

With the Pilates Anytime iOS app, easily stream our videos on your iPhone, iPad, or Apple TV. Your subscription includes access to our website PilatesAnytime.com. Plus, download up to 30 videos for offline viewing.

You will not be charged during your free trial. After your free trial, you will automatically be billed $17.99 USD per month (price varies by currency) to your iTunes Account. You can turn off your auto-renew in your iTunes account settings. You will not be automatically billed if you turn auto-renew off at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.

You can cancel your subscription anytime in your iTunes account settings.

Whether you are a beginner or advanced practitioner, whether you are looking for Mat Pilates or have a Reformer or other Pilates equipment, we welcome you to our community.

I feel like a kid again on my birthday whenever I log on and see a new class that I know I’m going to love! Lastly, ( I could go on for days), I love how Pilates feels as I’m getting older. Too difficult to filter given price

I live how many videos there are. I love that I can input my preferences and have the app recommend videos for me. I do t live
That I can’t exclude videos that are completely irrelevant to me. For instance, after inputting all preferences, the first recommendation I am given is part 3 of a 4 part series on a Pilates Birth, geared towards pregnant women. I’m 43 so I’m largely past childbearing age. Plus I’M A MAN, and I can’t exclude videos that are intended for a specific subset of women.

It would also be lovely if on the AppleTv version of the app i could configure multiple users, as my wife and I both would like to easily use the app, switching between our preferences without having to reconfigure each time.
