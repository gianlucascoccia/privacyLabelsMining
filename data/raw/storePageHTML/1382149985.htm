Description

MyFridgeFood is an interesting and definitely quite useful tool. It presents you with two options – quick find and detailed kitchen. Both these pages have check boxes representing various food items. Just check the ones which you’ve got in your kitchen and it’ll show a list of recipes you can make with some or all of them. Click on the recipe and get the cooking details. Hence a nice tool to quickly check what food you can prepare with the items you’ve have at hand.
