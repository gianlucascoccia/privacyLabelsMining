Description

HiHello is a free digital business card and address book app. With HiHello you can make and share your own digital business cards, scan paper cards, and manage your contacts, all for free.

***HiHello has been featured in The Wall Street Journal, TechCrunch, Slate, and Axios***

HiHello is the best free end-to-end business card and contact manager app that’s designed to help you curate and grow your professional relationships.

CREATE CONTACTLESS DIGITAL BUSINESS CARDS
• Going digital is better for both networking and the environment. Digital business cards are always available on your phone or desktop when you need them, so you’ll never need to order more.
• Put as much or as little information as you’d like on your virtual business cards—they’re completely customizable and your information will always be up-to-date.
• HiHello Tip: We highly recommend adding a live photo or video to your vcards! A video business card will help your card stand out even more, and can be a great conversation starter.

SHARE YOUR BUSINESS CARDS WITH ANYONE
• You can share your digital business card with anyone, even if they don’t have the HiHello app.
• Share your virtual card using your unique QR code, or send the link to your card via email, SMS, Slack, and more.

MANAGE YOUR CONTACTS WITH A SELF-HEALING ADDRESS BOOK
• HiHello’s self-healing address book is your free personal and professional CRM. Your address book will heal itself, so you’ll always have your contacts’ latest information.
• Easily keep track of your contacts with a chronological timeline of who you met and when—we’ll even send you a reminder to follow up.
• Add notes and tags and search within your contacts.
• Sync HiHello with Google Contacts, so you can access your contacts across both platforms.

SCAN BUSINESS CARDS
• We get it—some people still love paper business cards. HiHello has a human-verified business card scanner so you can scan any paper cards you receive.

VIRTUAL BACKGROUNDS
• Take your video meetings to the next level with a free, personalized virtual background. These backgrounds are linked to your business card, so anyone on your video call will have access to your information.

EMAIL SIGNATURES
• Use your digital business card to create professional email signatures. With multiple templates to choose from, HiHello’s signature generator will allow you to easily create an email signature that links to your business card.

PRIVACY
• Our Privacy Policy ensures that your information is safe and secure.

ABOUT US
HiHello’s Digital Business Card & Address Book is made with love in Palo Alto, California. We welcome your feedback! Send us your comments from the HiHello app Settings page or email us at feedback@hihello.me.

We hope you love your digital business cards!
