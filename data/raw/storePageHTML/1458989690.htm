Description

The U.S. Soccer app is officially the easiest way to follow the USWNT and USMNT. Stay up to date with the latest USWNT & USMNT match announcements, roster announcements, starting XIs, behind-the-scenes content, ticket presales, results, and more.

Never miss a goal with video highlights and real-time updates. Track every moment with one-of-a-kind visualizations and play-by-play commentary. Dig into team stats and compare player leaderboards as the match heats up.

Insiders decide who gets crowned as the official "Player of the Match" – sign up is free!

*Master the schedule and mobile ticketing*
• USWNT & USMNT match announcements
• Easy-access ticket purchase
• Access, scan, transfer your Ticketmaster mobile tickets*

*Be the first to know and follow every match*
• Get notified when news breaks
* First look at Starting XIs, roster announcements, match announcements, and more.
• Live scores, lineups, commentary, stats, and pitch visualizations to track the action

**

*Behind the Scenes Content*
* All the best stuff from the only content creators embedded with the teams
* Articles, Videos, and Podcasts centralized in one spot
* Get the latest episodes of "Behind the Crest", "The Journey", "5 Things 2 Know"

*Sign up to be a U.S. Soccer Insider*
• Exclusive ticket presale access
• Vote on the official "Player of the Match"
• Track and earn caps for every match you attend

*Mobile tickets only accessible for matches controlled by U.S. Soccer and sold via Ticketmaster

It still needs work

Love the app and glad I now have somewhere to go for updates about the team and games. But the app definitely still needs some major work. Right now there is a notification problem that causes it to not disappear. There’s no way to get it off. And also wish we could choose which team we want to get notifications about. Baseballfanatic9 , 06/07/2019

Terrible just ads and data collection

This app just pushes advertising and collecting data. So many new fans like me who want to learn about players, where they are from, how they arrived, read what inspired them.. their stories and see their stats and watch matches. I had to hunt around at first to just find the women’s players bios. I enjoyed reading about each player. Now I can’t find even the list of players. Poor navigation. The menu disappeared. All I see is the same advertising push when I come back for viewing a video. Please take me back to where I was previously. This scan thing does not work. Please don’t block all content forcing me to give email address, verify email, forcing to give location and keep pushing ticket sales when I can only watch the matches. Please just allow me to read about the players, follow my player and matches and stats on matches and players. Just keep all simple so a fan can read, follow and enjoy.

Rey Pygsterio , 07/30/2019

Accurately reflective of U.S. Soccer operations

I don’t usually write reviews of apps, but this one is really frustrating. First, why would anyone assume I want an app to launch into loud music when I open it? That also seems to cause it to take a little too long to load. Second, not only does it not give me the information I am looking for such as a extensive schedule for all teams, it doesn’t seem to be professionally updated with game results and next matches like you would find in ESPN, for example. Third, I seem to always have an indication there is a notification but can’t figure out what it is and the indicator won’t go away. I will say one thing, I guess its shortcomings are accurately reflective of the entire U.S. Soccer operation.
