Always love apple , 06/05/2020

Surprisingly great so far. Ignore some of the negative reviews

I have been using the app so far for a few weeks now. With that being said, I have not come across any issues with this app whatsoever. I was hesitant to download and try because of a wide variance of reviews(either you liked it or hated it(some people calling it a scam). I’ve had a great experience so far wagering games through Madden 20. Almost all of my opponents were good sports and the process itself for match making and submitting results is fairly smooth. I haven’t tried out NBA2K yet, but I assume that the experience is the same. I was able to add funds and withdrawal funds fairly easily. It does take a few days to withdrawal to PayPal but that doesn’t bother me too much. All in all, I would advise you to ignore the negative reviews, but do understand this is a wagering app, meaning you’re risking the loss/gain of money. I would advise using this app only if you’re an experienced player in one or more of the games they offer, because your opponents will most likely be well-experienced and will give you a run for you money.
