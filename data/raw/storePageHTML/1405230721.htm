Description

Master your Spanish verbs conjugation with Ella Verbs! Work your way through 30 levels of bite-sized lessons & quizzes, learning 16 tenses and covering over 1,300 verbs.

Hola! We are an Irish couple (Jane and Brian) living in Spain and learning Spanish. We started Ella Verbs as we were looking for a way to improve our Spanish verbs and make the jump from intermediate to advanced Spanish speakers. We tried many ways to improve our Spanish grammar (books, apps, tutors – you name it, we tried it!), but never found the one that clicked. So we built it!

We aren’t your typical Spanish flashcards or verb trainer app. Our philosophy is “learn, quiz, repeat”. We teach you, then quiz you and then surface content again when you need it most. It’s been proven that quizzing helps with concentration, identifying gaps in knowledge and long term retention (Roediger & Karpicke, 2006). You’ll enjoy our Smart Quizzes to drill verbs into your memory!

Because it's just the two of us, we personally read and respond to every feedback email and review. So if you have an idea, issue, question or whatever, please get in touch! We really want and listen to your feedback, and regularly release new features and improve existing ones (to date we've released 30+ updates per year!). Together we want to help everyone learning Spanish to master their verbs :)

You can reach us in-app, or by emailing us at jane@ellaverbs.com or brian@ellaverbs.com. Looking forward to hearing from you!

Features:

* 30 levels (6 for free) with bite-sized lessons and quizzes guiding you through 16 tenses.
* Verb tables for 1,300+ verbs (101 for free), with *full* English translation, synonyms and highlighting of irregulars.
* Track your progress across multiple devices and stay motivated with a daily streak.
* Step-by-step conjugation guide when you get a conjugation incorrect.
* Estimates your CEFR level (e.g. A1, A2, B1, B2, C1, C2) so you have an idea of where you stand.
* Get daily smart quizzes personalized to your level and progress (Pro only).
* Speaks the answers aloud to help you with pronunciation (on supported devices only – uses built in voice library)
* Build your own quizzes with our highly customizable quiz builder.
* Build your own custom list of verbs to study (Pro only).
* Access all content offline so you can study anywhere!
* Supports both European (including "vosotros") and Latin American Spanish.
* Dark mode and many configuration options.

We cover 16 Spanish verb tenses across 3 moods:

* Indicative present
* Indicative present continuous
* Indicative present perfect
* Indicative preterite
* Indicative imperfect
* Indicative past perfect
* Indicative informal future
* Indicative future
* Indicative future perfect
* Indicative conditional
* Indicative conditional perfect
* Subjunctive present
* Subjunctive imperfect
* Subjunctive present perfect
* Subjunctive past perfect
* Imperative (affirmative & negative)

Best Conjugation Trainer

Update:
App has changed to subscription model, so not all below applies...

I was hesitant to buy the app without trying it, but am so glad I did! It is easy to use and flexible, no nonsense but still feels fun. The way it divides learning verbs into categories like, stem-changing e to ie verbs or irregulars makes learning the verbs so much easier. I’ve heard this approach recommended by teachers (like the Spanish Dude) and have found it very helpful. Also the explanations of how to conjugate are concise but informative. The app feels like it was written by a good teacher / Spanish enthusiast not just a developer trying to make a buck. Also I like the fact that you don’t have to go through a huge instructional phase before quizzing, you can dive right in or choose to read the tutorials.
One piece of info I couldn’t find before buying was what verb forms it includes. As of this review it has: Present, preterite, imperfect, past perfect, future, future perfect, conditional, conditional perfect, subjunctive, subjunctive imperative, subjunctive present perfect, subjunctive past perfect, and imperative. Each section divides up into regulars and irregulars, with irregulars divided up into logical sections as mentioned. There are also options for taking quizzes mixing verb forms as well as a quiz on the ones you’ve missed.
Very well thought out. Highly recommend.
