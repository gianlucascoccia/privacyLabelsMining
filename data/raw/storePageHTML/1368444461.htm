Description

Pure offroad driving experience. Drive pickups, SUVs, quads, ATVs, SxS, rock crawlers through mud, water, mountains, rocks, etc. Customize and tune cars the way you like.

Offroad Kings is the most realistic offroad game for mobile phones. The vehicles in the game are based on real physics, no arcade simulations.

You can choose various vehicles from 6 different vehicle classes now: Trucks (SUVs and pickups), ATVs (quads), SxS, Rock Crawlers, Muscle Cars and Semi Trucks. There are 5 different maps where you can drive your vehicles - mountain woods, desert, snow mountains, rock park and stunt park.


Vehicle Customization Features
-----
* Body mods - front & rear bumpers, cages, roof lights, running boards, horns, winch, fenders etc.
* Paint customizations + Wrap decals
* Various rim style & tire style selections
* Tinted windows


Vehicle Suspension Features
-----
* Change axle types on your vehicles
* 5 different suspensions - Solid standard, Solid master, Independent standard, Champion independenet and I-Beam
* Tune suspensions - choose your axle width, damping, travel, stiffness, rear steering, axle offset, shock size
* Tune tires and rims - set tire width, tire radius and rim size
* Test your rigs - test vehicles on ramps and terrains


Upgrade Features
-----
* Upgrade your vehicles
* Power upgrades
* Tire grip
* Reduce vehicle weight
* Install snow tracks on all vehicles - ATVs, Trucks, Crawlers, SxS - you can switch between tracks and wheels
* Install diesel to your trucks - you can switch between diesel and petrol


Trailers
-----
* Choose different trailers
* Choose different cargo for each trailer


Different playing modes
-----
* Day & night modes - do you prefer driving during the night? No problem, just select your mode
* Play singleplayer modes
* Or play multiplayer modes


Are you ready to start exploring huge maps and drive around with your tuned and customized trucks? Enjoy driving through mud and water, drift on snow and ice. There are no limits in this game. No gas meters, no damage. Just pure offroad driving experience. Climb the hills, climb the rocks and conquer all maps.

Coins and some other complaints

When I downloaded the app I really liked it because it offers more mods and trucks (and even some trailers with real loads!) than offroad outlaws. FreshieJohnson , 10/20/2018

Better than it looks...mostly

At first glance this game when opened looks to kiddish. But that’s mostly the garage screen. Actual play is pretty cool. Love the physics. Love the courses. The vehicles, mods and trailers do pretty well.
But the earnings/cost ratio is way off from other games. I wouldn’t change anything else really. But there isn’t a whole lot to do. When you complete a trail you get extremely small amounts of coin compared to what things cost in the garage. It took me 2 days of random play to trick out a Jeep. I played the 2 first maps through and through. After that, you’d have to play the same trails hundreds of times just to buy/collect different vehicles. Then do them again and again to be able to beef em up. Plus There’s only 4 maps. 3 different modes. (Which are all pretty much the same) so this game got boring. QUICK!
