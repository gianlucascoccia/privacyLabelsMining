Description

Explore the world and travel with Barbie!

Journey with Barbie as she travels across the world to different locations such as the African Savanna or the jungles of Costa Rica in this brand-new AR adventure game!

Help Barbie find secret Hot Spots that uncover fun facts about new places and the critters that live there. Finding all the hidden spots allows you to collect and unlock items useful in your trek like binoculars and a travel guide.

Using AR, walk alongside Barbie and explore the virtual world through your device as if you’re actually there! Walk through a virtual door and into Africa or Costa Rica within seconds. Don't worry if space is limited because you can also view the surroundings as a 3D model.

Travel the World
• Play with meerkats, giraffes, elephants, and many other animals in the African Savanna
• Discover fun facts about the ecosystem!
• Swim with whales, and hang-out with monkeys in Costa Rica

Acquire New Tools
• Track all the hidden Hot Spots and collect new tools for your journey as you play
• Use your Binoculars to get a closer view when looking from a distance
• Dust off some ostrich eggs with your helpful brush

Capture the Memories
• Take pictures of your amazing finds or fun facts
• Style Barbie in different outfits and capture the looks you like the best
• Play with your surroundings in the virtual world and create wonderful pictures

Join Barbie and become a world explorer!
