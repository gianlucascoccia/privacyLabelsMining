Description

We’ve rebuilt Stitcher to make it easier and quicker to listen to the podcasts you love. Discover new features made for and only podcasts. Explore shows from NPR, Wondery, WNYC, the New York Times, Earwolf, Exactly Right, and more. Stitcher is home to all your favorite podcasts, from politics, true crime, sports, and comedy, there’s a podcast for everyone on Stitcher.


ORGANIZE YOUR PODCASTS
Use Stitcher to follow all the podcasts you listen to so you never miss a new episode or update. Access your latest and unfinished episodes, downloads, and favorite shows all in one convenient spot.

STREAM PODCASTS ON DEMAND
Stream instantly or download podcasts to easily listen offline

EPISODE SEARCH, FILTERING, AND SORTING
Search for specific episodes on show pages, filter episodes based on Unplayed/Played, Downloaded, and Liked on show pages. Sort episodes by Oldest to Newest/ Newest to Oldest or by duration on show pages

MORE CONTROL
Customize download settings on a per-show basis and determine how many downloads you want saved before they are removed. And listen at virtually any speed.

EXCLUSIVE PREMIUM PODCASTS AND AD FREE SHOWS
Subscribe to Stitcher Premium for exclusive content from My Favorite Murder, WTF with Marc Maron, Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend and many more Earwolf shows - plus ad-free, original content on Stitcher Premium, like exclusive episodes of Office Ladies and True Crime Garage, and ad-free versions of podcasts including Comedy Bang Bang, How Did This Get Made, and most Wondery and WNYC shows.

SMART SPEAKER INTEGRATIONS
Now features an Amazon Alexa skill and connects to Sonos speaker systems to play podcasts easily throughout your home or office.

HOW TO GET STARTED
1. Install the free Stitcher app
2. Sign in with Google, Facebook, or your email
3. Start listening!

What’s New

Feb 22, 2021

Version 10.7

Thanks for using Stitcher! We are listening to all the feedback and feature requests, and rapidly making changes. 

Version 10.7 includes some super nice tweaks and improvements:
• Playlists! Tap the name of a Show Group to open the Playlist view, then you can sort however you want (and filter and search). 
• Wanna listen to your episodes in a particular order? Sort by 'Custom' and then drag to rearrange (finally, amirite?)
• Swipe to open and close modal views like the player and episode info view (slick!)
• Audio warbling fix (no more robot-music)
• A bunch of UI improvements and bug fixes

From 10.6:
• Auto Play Toggle (aka, the artist formerly known as Continuous Playback)
• Filter By Year on show pages (great for listening to those archives)
• New Episode badges on the Shows grid (see what shows have new episodes)
• Filter on Likes and Downloads (clean out that Likes list)
• Improved Episode Cells with 2 line titles (no more ellipses!)
• Improved Play Queue UI
• CarPlay improvements

Anantsi , 01/05/2021

Updates always ruin my experience

I seem to be in the habit of resisting technology. Then accepting technology. Finally figuring it out. Using and enjoying said technology, and then the update comes. And it’s like I’m an infant again.
I have been using stitcher since my kids were babies. So that would be 13 years. It was always a nice easy platform for me to use. Even if I didn’t venture into the more special features. I was able to navigate the app. Find what I wanted and enjoy listening to all my favorite voices. I felt confident with stitcher.
And then the update came. Nothing is the same anymore. I don’t know what any of the symbols mean. I barely know how to back out of what I was attempting to press to do whatever feature I wanted but it never seems to be what I’m looking for anymore. Stitcher used to continuously play (unless sleep mode was activated!) a series for me. No it only seems to play one episode and I’m constantly having to scroll down to where I was to continue on. Very annoying. It doesn’t feel like stitcher anymore. It feels foreign and frankly it looks like it’s for toddlers. The graphics got all bubbly and round like some two year olds front page. I’m not sure I will continue to use it.

Developer Response ,

Thanks for your feedback - we hear you and are working hard to make Stitcher the best podcast app it can be!

Shannabanna08 , 10/17/2019

Overcast is way better

The only reason I keep this app is to access premium content from earwolf. I otherwise find it extremely frustrating to use and not intuitive. The playlists are not very customizable in my experience. I can’t erase episodes from the list only mark them as played so the lists end up being very long and unwieldy. If I listen to one second of a podcast, it then shows up below any unplayed episodes so I have to scroll down the list to see it. You cannot manually reorder episodes to your preferred play order or set priorities so your favorite shows always show up first in the list. I also downloaded old episodes of a show since the archives are only on stitcher but then had to manually scroll back through years of podcast episodes to find the ones I downloaded since they only show up by order of release and don’t prioritize downloaded episodes. Either that or manually search for them or take the time to mark hundreds of episodes as played in order for the episodes to show up on the top of the list. I don’t want to have to go to a separate downloaded episodes list when I’ve taken the time to make a playlist for a specific podcast. The downloaded episodes of that podcast should show up first. These are just a few reasons why I strongly prefer Overcast as my go to podcast app. If stitcher made some of these changes I at least wouldn’t be actively angry anytime I tried to listen to threedom.

Version 10 stinks!

While Ive been using and I guess enjoying Stitcher as a podcast player for many years, this recent “upgrade” to Stitcher 10 stinks! The layout is different, the default settings seem to be different (no longer plays podcasts in a row, I have to open the app after one ends to find the next one I want to play and manually start it), I found my “up next” list once but I cant find it again to get a selection of podcasts lined up to play (not tjat auto play away), and to add insult to injury, the icons are now huge and take up so much space in any list that I cant see episode titles, Im just seeing the podcast icon, and the name of the podcast (which is stupid, because I already have the icon so I know which is which) and then I only get a few letters into the actual episode title... trying to listen to “episode 12, part 1” and then “episode 12, part 2” is a disaster if you have more than one podcast you listen to because the “part 1/2” gets cut off.
