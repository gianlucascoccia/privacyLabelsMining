Description

** 2020 Apps We Love ** - Apple

Get fit & lose weight at home with the world's smartest personal trainer! No equipment needed.

With just a camera Onyx counts your reps, corrects your form, and motivates you at the perfect time.

It's everything you want in a trainer, without the crazy cost, and on your schedule.

Want to know if you're doing it right? Your coach tells you to bend lower, straighten your back, and jump higher in real time.

Want to understand how you're improving? Onyx shows you stats that were never before possible.

We'll help you stay accountable with weekly plans built for any goal or fitness level.

For extra motivation, turn on the leaderboard and compete with your friends!

Onyx also connects to Apple Health so you can log all your workouts and close your rings.

There is no fitness app more motivating or more life-like. Check it out and see for yourself!

* Exercise tracking is only supported by TrueDepth devices (iPhone X, Xr, Xs, Xs Max, 11, 11 Pro, 11 Pro Max), if you have an older device you can still enjoy our catalog of workouts and videos!


How it works:

Onyx uses advanced artificial intelligence and computer vision to bring you the first AI personal trainer.

Everything runs on-device to give you real-time tracking, feedback, and motivation.

Stef_M_M , 03/08/2020

So cool!

As someone who hates working out, I actually love this app and really cannot recommend it enough. I especially like how the trainer corrects your stance/form while you’re working out. I find the leaderboard motivating, and love seeing my improvements over time. Onyx is so smart and helpful, and I actually find myself looking forward to using it (which is weird, for me).

My only suggestion is to create a way to “favorite” certain workouts so that users can remember which ones they really liked (or ones that they want to try in the future)! Would mainly allow me to plan my upcoming workouts more effectively.

i love downdog , 08/24/2020

I really wanted to love this app

I really wanted to love this app! While we’re isolating in the midst of COVID, it seems like a dream to have a trainer give you real-time feedback about your form. I did a couple of workouts during the one-week trial and then signed up for the annual subscription. Unfortunately as I started using the app more, I found the tracking functionality to be poor. It would simply not track my movements and when you are physically pushing yourself to the limits, it’s beyond frustrating to not have your exercises tracked. I contacted customer service, who replied promptly and was very nice, but he gave me like six different things I had to do to make the tracking feature work (be inside, not too close, not too far, pull up your hair, don’t let other people in and out of the frame, etc), all of which are not super easy to do when you are trapped in a house with your family and pets. I tried again with all the guidance and still, app wasn’t tracking my movements. Sooo frustrating.
I was less than a month into my subscription and requested to cancel my subscription and get a pro-rated discount for the remaining 11 months. Sadly for me, the very nice customer service rep says that’s an apple thing and out of his control. Bummer, I’m out the money for an entire year.
I love the concept of this app, but am super disappointed that I have paid a full year for essentially a beta product.

Bunnycc , 06/01/2020

Updated Review after more use...

I am currently using the free trial and initially enjoyed my experience. At first it seemed to be a good replacement for boot camp classes during COVID-19. The tracking has been accurate except for one time is was off my three push ups. I just wish the workouts were longer and offered more variety. Tonight I wanted to do an arm workout.
As I scrolled through the various options, I realized each instructor was basically using the same 3-5 exercises. This is what made me decide to cancel the week trial. It’s fine for a week trial, but I’m not going to keep repeating these same exercises over the course of a year. I want more variety and longer workouts. There is potential with this app, but it still has a way to go. I hope improvements will be made. I would reconsider down the road.
