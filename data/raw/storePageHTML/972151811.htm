Description

ShipStation Mobile gives you the power of ShipStation wherever you go.

MANAGE ORDERS
• Import all your orders
• Scan barcodes to find orders fast
• Edit & verify addresses
• Hold or cancel orders
• Apply bulk actions and presets

CREATE LABELS
• Create & print shipping labels
• View & print pick lists & packing slips
• Manage & process fulfillment orders

TRACK SHIPMENTS
• Track shipments
• Re-send tracking emails
• Create & send return labels
• Generate SCAN forms for EOD pickup

VIEW INVENTORY
• Keep track of on hand quantities
• Filter by low stock
• View empty locations
• Filter by warehouse

STAY CONNECTED
• View insights & analytics
• Calculate shipping rates
• Get alerts (+ on Apple Watch)

Wherever you sell, however you ship, ShipStation makes order fulfillment exceptionally efficient.

Shipping made Easy!

I love the shipstation mobile app with the built in barcode scanner so I can scan my packing slips once I’ve packed my orders and make sure I accurately print the right shipping label at my workstation. VBFA , 11/29/2018

If everything worked..

This app would be my most used app if it worked smoothly. Anytime I create a label and have to add the dimensions in the package field I have to exit out after each number I enter as the pop up box does not let you scroll up or down or move to the next field. A couple of updates ago, the app finally let you add additional funds but now that function is no longer working. Even after all updated this app causes more of a headache than making it a smooth transition while on the go. Even printing is a hassle as I have to email myself a pdf to print. Please fix these issues and then it would be one amazing app!

Developer Response ,

Hi, thanks for leaving us a review! We'll look into the keyboard getting in the way. For the other issues, can you please reach out to us using the Feedback tab in the left-hand menu? We can take a deeper look once we have a little more information from you.

Krissycoll , 01/19/2021

Love shipstation

I love shipstation. The only thing I wish this app could do would be to *buy labels, *add funds to the shipping account, and assign orders to employees. Sometimes my employees are working without me and need more money for labels. I don’t want to grant employee access to buy labels because it comes off my debit card but I don’t want to have to login through my computer every time either. The website for shipstation isn’t mobile friendly so it can be a tedious process. I still do it the long way because it HAS to be done sometimes, but I do wish it was easier or could be done through the app.

*items most important to me for app functionality.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for your review! This is great feedback! We have let our Product & Development team know about your issues. We will work on improving these pain points you mentioned.
