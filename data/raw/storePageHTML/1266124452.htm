Description

Have a magic unicorn party! Make rainbow & unicorn ice cream with friends!

Features:
- Super fun ice cream cooking game with TONS of CLASSIC & TRENDY ICE CREAM！
- Keep updated with new ice cream cooking games!
- Make the various ice cream: classic ice cream, unicorn bubble tea, birthday ice cream sundae, galaxy popsicle, skittles rainbow ice cream, galaxy Frappucino, and so much more!
- Tons of realistic cooking tools to play: bowls, spatulas, spoons, plates, ice cream dispensers, piping bags, slushy dispensers, freezer, food processor, hand mixer, and so much more!
- Tons of food materials and decorations to try: fondant, chocolate, candies, sprinkles, fruits, syrup, and so much more!

How to play:
- Use interactive controls to play the game
- Try different tools to make your ice cream
- Mix various ingredients to create the unique flavor and color of your ice cream
- Decorate your ice cream with beautiful fondant, chocolate, candies, sprinkles, fruits, and syrup
- Eat ice cream to trigger the dreamy frozen princess magic
