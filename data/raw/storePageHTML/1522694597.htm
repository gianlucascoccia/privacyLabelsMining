Description

ProCam is a picture editing app with unique filters and effects. Incredible sections help you to edit images and change your style. Have fun with cartoon effect and apply magnificent filter, adorn your selfies and photos! You will be generating the most lovely picture!

* Creative Cartoon Filter
- Get special cartoon effect with one click.
- After the magic process, imagine yourself being a cartoon character.
- Surprise your friends with the magical effects.

* Funny Cosplay
- Do you want to know which role suits you best? ProCam can realize your dream.
- See your face in artistic painting like Picasso and Monet, entering the famous art painting world.

* Stylish Hairstyle
- Stylish hairstyles have come! Try short haircut, long haircut or layered haircut.
- We use the smart face recognition technology to help you suit these haircuts.
- Don’t you want to see your look?

* Fancy Hair Color
- One tap to change your hair color.
- With the trendy, gradient and multiple hair color, let your photo become more special.

* Swap Gender
- Want to have the fun of seeing yourself at the opposite sex? Will that make me more charming?
- Come and have a try.

* Advanced Filters
- Over 30 different fashionable filters available for you to switch around.
- Apply your selfies into stunning pictures, even the same sceneries will be shot with different touches.

* Photo Editor
- Powerful editing tools to meet your need: saturation, contrast, exposure, brightness and more.
- Easily edit your selfie and photos the way you want.

* Facial Beauty
- Smooth, firm, tone and adjust the hue of your skin the way you like.
- Slim down your face if you wish. Make your nose smaller and eyes bigger.
- Whitening your teeth takes your smile to the next level!

* Time Travel
- Will you still charming when you getting older?
- Time travel to 30 years later to see how you look, combined with advanced facial scanning technology to provide the most accurate results.
