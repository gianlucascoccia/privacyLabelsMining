Setsuu_0 , 12/29/2020

Issue as VIP member

I downloaded this app so I can read a certain BL comic. I had to pay to read most of the chapters, which wasn’t a problem at all. But after finishing the story in less than 24 hours, I find myself not using the app after that for the rest of the year. It’s been 7 months since then and I literally found out TODAY that the membership required RECURRING payments. That means I’ve been billed almost 10 bucks for the past 7 months despite me never going on the app after using it in May.

Upon reading their FAQ and TOS, it has come to my attention that I won’t ever get my money back simply because I completely read through the same comic in less than 24 hours. I like to think I’m legally supporting the content creators with my contribution but the fact that I barely went on the app for the majority of the year is a little... displeasing? I admit that I am to blame for being unaware of my blunder but at the time I signed up for the membership, my only option was to go for the one month plan since that seemed the cheapest. But it seems I misinterpreted it. Maybe to prevent this mistake from happening, the company ought to offer more flexible options like one-time payments to earning a fixed amount of coins like what other companies do...

Developer Response ,

Hello and thank you for your review. We apologize for the inconvenience you have been experiencing with our service. Please contact us at our 1:1 Customer Service, or at en.help@toomicsglobal.com, we will gladly assist you.

Horrible customer service

I had forgotten and entered in my password incorrectly a couple of times. I am not even able to recover or reset it, due to my account being locked. I contact their help center with no help. It’s frustrating especially when I don’t do anything wrong. I hope this flawed system gets fixed.
If people want to know how to cancel their subscriptions, either contact your bank to cancel recurring payments, or go to your PayPal account and do the same.
