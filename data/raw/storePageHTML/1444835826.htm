AbraCadaverXIV , 10/28/2019

Very good!

Great job here! I really can’t find anything wrong with this game at all so far; but I’m sure some things could be improved. Love beat em ups and love KoF and this is a beautiful mashup. The animations are super clean, the music and sound effects also really add an arcade experience feel to this game as well. The only issues I have encountered so far are live PvP matches lagging a bit; but I’m playing on an iPhone 6s so I’m DEFINITELY NOT running optimal hardware plus I am playing on the highest resolutions/graphic options available to me so I’m honestly not surprised I get some lag! I’m still new to the game so I have plenty of quests to complete for free gems (or rubies? Idk what the premium currency is called) so I can’t really talk about the long term; but the summon system seems decent enough so far although I wish there were pools for separate years to avoid pulling 3 different versions of goro but that is just my own personal preference. The game is very generous with new players; allowing a FREE re-rollable 10x summon until you are happy with your free 5 star. Awesome game and I’m super excited to see where it goes.

I demand substance , 10/30/2019

Grind fest battery drain

To stay even remotely competitive, you need to grind and spend all the time. The price of rubies (in-game currency) is absurd. Sure you get a guaranteed top tier unit after enough summons (random chance draws of characters) but that’s after spending hundreds of dollars’ worth of rubies. Never play this game if you won’t be able to charge it soon, as your battery will drop precipitously, even on the lowest graphics settings.

The gameplay itself is alright, but gets repetitive. It’s a side scroller beat-em-up with good graphics but sloppy controls. There have been numerous times where the virtual joystick wouldn’t let me move at crucial moments. The move priority is broken (in fighting games, that is where if two characters attack each other, which one causes damage). You can attack and have your opponent shrug it off, walk around, and attack through your attacks if they outlevel you. This eliminates the ability to use skill and emphasizes stat checks over player ability. There are several great ports of KOF on here, some are free. Play this if you want, but keep in mind some places can’t be beaten without top tier characters which are expensive to obtain.

Also keep in mind this game is very... very... time demanding if you want to finish daily quests and keep a moderate pace.
