Description

~~> BREAKING NEWS: There’s a new stylist to the stars in town, and it just so happens to be you!
~~> In this business, it’s all about word of mouth. Make sure your styling is on point so you can build up a big client base.
~~> Be seen! Go to fancy schmancy events with your clients and take pics for their Stylagram.

You’ve just realized that you could turn your mad shopping skills into money… by being a super stylist! Now, anybody who’s anybody will come to you, because you know just how to style them. Create perfect looks for your customers. Join them at their VIP events and photograph them so the world can see how awesomely talented you are.

Features:
> Open a styling business and build up an impressive client base.
> Dress up your clients in fab outfits they can wear proudly to specific events, from casual date nights to red carpet premieres.
> Use your stylist bank to buy the clothes, and make a profit when you sell the items to your clients.
> Listen carefully to what your clients are looking for so you can choose their clothes wisely from different collections.
> The better you do, the more fashion collections you can unlock.
> No look is complete without the right accessories and killer shoes.
> If you want your clients to recommend you to their friends, they’ve got to look great from head to toe! Give them a hairstyle to remember.
> Give your clients makeup looks that will make them the envy of all the girls.
> Accompany them to celeb-filled events and snap pics for their Stylagram. Make sure they tag you in their pics!

Highly addictive

I love this game ! I literally started playing last week and I’m already on level 26. I have spent quite a bit of money on it. A little $10-$20 here and there, they really start to add up. You need diamonds to get different color variants and styles. Which is fine but it can be expensive. Sometimes the ads I watch don’t work it’s happened about 5x or so & on one occasion I got on the game and 500 of my diamonds were missing. I still don’t know what happened to them.

The clothes are so cute and realistic. This is like the game you played as a child but it’s like times 1,000. Mind you I’m 29 and have completely fallen in love with the game. It’s competitive and filled with different things to gain currency and items you will need to be successful in the game. There are mini challenges and coloring book pages you can do to earn other rewards. I’ve unlocked almost all the designer items.

Some things I wish were there we more silver jewelry to choose from like necklaces and rings and larger selection of make up. Maybe some black girl hairstyles. And shortening the time for delivery waiting literally 5hrs is crazy, spending a large part of your profit is hard too especially if you’ve played without paying.
