Are you a  Lyft driver? Drive smart with Lyft app for drivers and Skeddy — make more!

Skeddy is a ridesharing app for Lyft drivers that help you schedule trips near you.

Features:

• Skeddy discovers Lyft rides in advance and combines them for you into a profitable chain trip. 

• With Skeddy, you choose from multiple available rides in advance, and build a driving schedule, which is good for you! You just need to make rides using  Lyft driver app.

• Skeddy allows you to plan your Lyft pickups for up to a week ahead!

• Skeddy maximizes your daily pay while cutting off your behind-the-wheel time! 

How does Skeddy work:

1. Skeddy discovers the rides scheduled by passengers in a 10-miles zone from the arbitrary location (Search Pin) defined by the driver. The driver can save the search pins as favorites, and re-use them in the future.

2. Discovered rides are displayed on the map (day and night mode), as well as in a list with the corresponding time, length, and estimated earnings.

3. Skeddy automatically accepts the ride by using the time frame and distance criteria previously entered by the driver.

4. The driver is able to drop any previously accepted ride, or to manually accept the ride, which was filtered out by the previously entered filter criteria.
