Description

Divtracker presents.. unlimited portfolios, unlimited stock alerts...

The "original" Divtracker... we offer free push notifications:
* "Notify me when Verizons yield goes above 5%".
* "Notify me when Verizons ex div is 4 days away".
* "Notify me when Verizons dividend increases or decreases".

Platinum members can enjoy Calendar EXPORT functionality:
* Export Pay, Ex and Earn dates to your iOS device
* and.. We will keep them up to date in the background!

Being able to live off of your portfolio's dividend income is a dream many investors have. Tracking your dividends and portfolio performance is one of the most challenging aspects of dividend investing. Common excel formulas work for few months before having issues with data providers that leave you struggling. Most existing brokerages have a boring line-by-line accounting of your dividends, but do not focus on VISUALIZATION. FORECASTING.

Currency conversion has never been easier: do you have EUR, USD, CAN holdings, and want to know what all the payouts convert into EUR? Divtracker handles this:
-Input your holdings as the currency you bought the stock in
-Select your local currency within Settings (platinum only--- Free version converts all foreign currencies into USD )
-All screens throughout the app will convert into your chosen currency (so if you have EUR, GBP, USD stocks but want it into CAD, it will convert all to CAD for display)

Best way to track dividends

I have a number of equities across several different financial institutions, and despite subscribing to some of the big portfolio trackers, like SeekingAlpha, MorningStar, Yahoo Finance, and Mad Dividends, I still wasn’t getting what I needed for tracking my dividend stocks and planning my future investments. Dividend Tracker admittedly has a “different” UI, and it takes a bit of practice to get to know the many features, but I find myself referring back to it again and again now. It has truly become indispensable to me as a dividend-focused investor.

And at least one other reviewer mentioned the amazing support, and it’s true. If the app does crash, there’s an option to send a report to the developer. Well, after a few crashes on my device (for which I chose to send the report to the Dev), they emailed me, telling me they identified the issue and had a fix in the works. It hasn’t crashed for me since then. I also sent them an email requesting a couple of features and they wrote back within an hour or so, telling me how to find one (what I wanted was already there - I just didn’t know how to do it), and telling me they’d look into the other.

Aside from maybe Mail and Messages, this may very well be my most used app now.

Krahnin , 04/03/2020

App Seems Impressive, but...

At a glance, I think this app will do just about everything I am looking for. Tracks my owned stocks, lets me set up multiple portfolios for my brokerage account and my Roth IRA, estimates dividend income, etc. I’d love to give this 5 stars. I’d also probably love to purchase the premium lifetime platinum (though a trial first might be nice).

Where things get dicey is when the App Store listing shows premium platinum as $50 for life but then in the app itself it is double that. Then, poking around the app to try and find a contact form to ask support about it, the ads are so bad that navigation is unbearable. When I finally got to the support section, there is a physical mailing address, but that’s it. Then the “contact us” section says something about a phone number, but nothing is listed....last, but not least, when I open the support page in a web browser, Safari alerts me that the page is not secure.

Edit: I did find an email option in the app; might be prudent to remove the other contact us section if that is the intended method of contact.

We will try to streamline our contact us within the app based on your feedback. Hope you are enjoying the app, and please don't hesitate to reach out to us for any questions or feedback.
