trammmq , 10/21/2020

Not good

The app has 5% free effects and the rest you have to get a premium subscription for. I hate when apps are like that. Obviously having a premium version of the app is good for business and completely necessary, but it won’t kill you to offer just a few more free options. Also, I think you should be able to preview the premium filters. When you click on something that only premium users can use, it brings up an option for you to get premium but won’t let you see what the effect would look like.

ALSO, THE MOST IMPORTANT PART OF THIS REVIEW; this is one of those absolutely scummy apps that offers free filters and other benefits to its users in exchange for them giving it a five stars rating. I HATE apps that do this. Bribing your users to give you five stars rather than making an app that’s legitimately, actually worthy of five stars is trash. Anyways, if they didn’t bribe people into give them a high rating, they’d probably have 2-3 stars (and I’m being generous In saying 3)

Don’t know why this is marketed as a free app when you have to pay for everything.
