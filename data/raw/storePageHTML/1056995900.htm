It's Good, But Not Perfect.

I've been looking for a good app for slow motion videos. This is the best one I've tried, even though it's not perfect.
I think it's wrong that you said in the description that the app could also speed up your videos, but you can only do that if you rate the app and write a review. But that's the only thing I disapprove of.
I think it's cool how the app can make every single video slow motion in only a few seconds, and most of the other apps I tried took about five minutes.
This is a really fun app even though it's not perfect. But if you're looking for a good slow motion video app, this is definitely one of the bests.

Sam👑🍉 , 11/01/2019

I like it!

To be honest I’ve deleted it off my phone before but today I thought the speed that my camera provides is too fast. I remember from previous times that it has a speed up option. I guess I didn’t realize how well it worked! It was fast uploading my video then other apps. It took a little longer for one of my videos but I believe that’s only because it was ten minutes. It took my video and shortened it to five minutes! I saved it and I was surprised to see that I had to press it only once and it immediately saved to my camera roll without me having to wait! I saved it and shortened it again because I wanted it a little shorter. It went all the way from a ten minute video to a two minute video! I am very happy with my results! It doesn’t even have a watermark! Although it says you need to rate it you can immediately exit without having to rate! I really like the app! I’m so happy I decided to download it again!

The charge popped up and I clicked my home button to get back to the home screen so I could delete this app. However it seems that I held my finger on it for just enough time for it to charge me.
