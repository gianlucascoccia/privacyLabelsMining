Description

All you need for the High School sports you love the most! The Varsity Bound app gives High School sports fans a pro experience, giving you everything you need to follow your school, size up the competition and see how your teams and athletes stack up all season long.

Stop hunting around the web searching for schedules, rosters, standings and stat leaders. With the free Varsity Bound App, you can track the action all season long. At the game, whether you're in the stands or not, the app is your game day program. With game previews, rosters, box scores, and recaps you'll get everything you need to follow the big game.

- Season-long schedules
- Scores and standings
- Find driving directions
- Game previews, recaps and box scores for every game
- Stat leaderboards
- Stay on top of all the activities at your favorite schools
