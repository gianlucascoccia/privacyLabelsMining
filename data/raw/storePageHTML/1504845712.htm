Description

Create your customized daily shop for your favorite game and share it with your friends.

Create your daily shop, customizing it however you want.

Do you want to create a daily shop and share it with your friends and family? Thanks to this application, now you can!

Main features:
- Customize your shop, and add items based on your preferences: skins, gliders, pickaxes and emotes.
- Choose between skins of every type of rarity: uncommon, rare, epic, and legendary.
- Customize each item adding the price you want.
- Download your custom shop creation and share it with friends and family in high resolution.
- Configure the settings to your liking by adding your creator code.
- Save all of your creations and keep editing them later on.
