Alarm Clock Radio is a simple & beautiful alarm clock that can be customized to your needs with gorgeous themes. You can listen radio & podcast, display weather temperature, guaranteed to wake you up with a smile everyday!

We know that most people prefer to be woken with a different alarm on a weekend to that of a working day. Alarm Clock Radio offers you this functionality and much more. A good wake up can have an impact on the rest of your day.

Change the way you wake up every morning! Sense the difference of waking up with Alarm Clock Radio!

* Original and Elegant Lockscreen Clock Themes
