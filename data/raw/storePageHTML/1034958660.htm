Description

+++ Try now for free! +++

Utiful is the #1 photo sorter for busy people and neat freaks. It lets you move photos out of the camera roll and sort them into a folder structure of your own.

PHOTO ORGANIZATION THE WAY IT SHOULD BE
Why make things hard, when they can be so easy? Utiful lets you move (not just copy!) photos from the camera roll into a folder structure of your own, so that your pictures are neatly organized and easy to find, and your camera roll is clean and tidy. It's simple and fast, backs up safely and lets you sync to all your iOS devices.

YOUR BENEFITS
Busy people use their camera phones a lot and keep tons of photos of different types. With Utiful, you can clearly separate the different categories of pictures you keep, so you can find things quickly when you need them, enjoy a tidy camera roll and keep everything under control.

Think of it: The iPhone enabled us to keep not only personal photos like holiday snaps but also non-personal pictures for reference like bills, recipes, books to buy, screenshots etc. etc. Freelancers and small business owners keep also pictures they need for their work. However, all these images get saved in the same place and your camera roll becomes a cluttered mess. Not anymore! Utiful lets you categorize and separate your work pictures from your family photographs, your home remodelling project from your holiday snapshots, and also keep your private pics really private, away from the camera roll and from prying eyes.

You can also easily export all your Utiful folders and subfolders to your computer and seamlessly sync them between your iPhone and iPad! Plus, you get a world-class customer support!

HOW IT WORKS
It's easy as a charm! Utiful enables you to easily create a folder and move to it photos and videos from the camera roll so you can then delete them from the camera roll but keep them in the new folder. And, Utiful is the only photo organizer that allows you to do this also straight from the standard Photos app! A must-have! Manage your work, hobby, and private pictures in Utiful to clean up the camera roll and get your memories clutter-free. Sort pictures into Utiful folders to find things quickly when you need them and put an end to the endless scrolling through a messy camera roll.
