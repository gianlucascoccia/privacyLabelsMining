Description

Your handheld guide and planning tool for all of the Walt Disney World® Resort, Universal Orlando® Resort and SeaWorld® Orlando theme parks. Do more and wait less with preset and customized touring plans, wait times and easy access to all the details of your favorite attractions. Use the directional compass and interactive maps to navigate each park, view wait times and execute your touring plan.

Key Features:
▶ Interactive map with directional compass for easy park navigation
▶ See your location in the park
▶ Accurate Wait Times
▶ Recommended Park Plans to experience the most popular rides with the lowest waits
▶ My Park plans customized by you
▶ Share plans with friends and family even if they don't have the app
▶ When to Visit Recommendations
▶ Daily Park Hours
▶ Extra Magic Hours
▶ Today’s Wait Time Curves
▶ Height Restrictions
▶ View Favorites
▶ Restaurant Recommendations

****Named “Best App for Taking on Walt Disney World” by Fox News!****

How will Undercover Tourist’s iPhone app help me BEFORE my trip?

Recommended Plans
▶ Save up to 4 hours a day by following our recommended plans for each park.
▶ See more and wait less with our recommended order of rides and attractions.
▶ View current wait times for each attraction on your plan in list.
▶ Share your plan with others right from the app!

Customized Plans
▶ Select any Disney, Universal or SeaWorld parks to create a new plan or modify our recommended plans to fit your needs.
▶ Add a personalized note or reminder throughout the day.
▶ Share your plan with others right from the app!

Favorites
▶ Create one account and sync between the app and UndercoverTourist.com.
▶ Tap the heart icon to save an attraction to your favorites list for quick viewing.

What about DURING my trip?

Interactive Park Maps
▶ Use the directional compass to know exactly where you are in the park and what attractions are close by
▶ Sort by “Near Me” to find the closest attractions
▶ View current wait times on map to see the park at a glance
▶ View and execute your touring plan in map view as you navigate the park

Park Plans
▶ Keep your group in sync by following along with our preset touring plans or your own custom plan
▶ Check off attractions as you ride them.
▶ Navigate to the next attraction using the directional compass in map view.

Planning Tools
▶ Be right on time with park opening hours
▶ Know which park has Extra Magic Hours
▶ Use our recommended “When to Visit” guide within Wait Times for more flexibility (great for late arrivers and families who sleep in!)

Bugman14 , 01/30/2021

Good app

I have never been to Orlando, but if I do end up going sometime in my life this app will always be on my phone screen. I always love staring at this app throughout my day, just to see the wait times as they change during the exciting day at the park. My only complaint is that the parks close at night, so the times no longer show up. I understand that the park is closed so it is not really the fault of the app, but still I would enjoy to see the wait times even if they are just flat out made up. Cannot recommend enough for non Orlando use, like I said earlier have not actually been to Orlando.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for leaving a review. While it's true that we don't show wait times when the park is closed we do still show red/yellow/green on the left border of each item in the wait times list. These colors correspond to rides with long/medium/short wait times.

Daisydancer7 , 01/23/2019

Inaccurate wait times led to much frustration

I decided to rely on this app for wait times and it led to much frustration. On the most popular rides, it was way off. I tried to do my part and submit accurate wait times to help the cause but it never updated to that. Also, I had used the park attendance tracker to pick our day to go to the parks. We went on a “green” day, but it was actually exceptionally busy according to a couple of Disney employees with whom we spoke. We didn’t get to do much of what we wanted to do. The app and website are great tools but unfortunately the most pertinent information was misleading and inaccurate.
