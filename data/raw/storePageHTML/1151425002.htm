Description

Millions of users, hundreds of thousands of reviews, simply the best app for Chromecast and Chromecast TV. It is "the app" for anyone with a Chromecast.

With Streamer for Chromecast you can use your Chromecast to it s full potential. Streamer for Chromecast lets you do a lot. Here is how:

Setup Chromecast :
You can setup your Chromecast dongle by following the a few easy steps. There are clear instructions and even a video tutorial to guide you through it.

Cast Photos :
With Streamer for Chromecast you can cast your Photos to your TV. Be it you trip pictures, still photos, live pictures or even Slo-mo vidoes you can cast then with the Streamer for Chromecast app. You can cast photos, cast vidoes and cast recordings.

Cast Music :
You can cast music from you Streamer for Chromecast app to your TV. Browse through your music and cast. It is simple as that.

Camera Cast :
Cast your camera live to your TV screen. Apply filters and have lots of fun.

Cast Screen :
Cast your phone screen to your TV. You can use it to cast presentations or displays. It is also best for playing Chromecast games.
Note: Some apps have copyrighted content and do not allow screen casting. These apps usually recommend alternate ways to cast. Please contact our support team if you face any problem casting from an app.

And much more... :
There are many more features waiting for you to explore. We keep adding new stuff. Just like you, the app never gets old.

We are excited to see that you can do with your Chromecast. Download now.

So far so good

I started streaming a few months ago because of the increasing fees for cable. I live in a condo, and can’t have a dish. I started with ROKU and use Tablo to record local content. You Tube TV was brought to my area and I like it the best so far for streaming internet stations. I have had some issues with it locking up, but in those cases, the program is always fine. I just loose my ability to pause or fast forward from my iphone7. I have had to reboot my phone, and sometimes I disconnect the TV and reconnect. I had Direct TV now, and it was always buffering. No issues ever with chromecast & You Tube TV in that regard. I still use ROKU to stream my other channels like Netflix. I just purchased a second chromecast for another tv in the house. I if did not lose control at times, it would be 5 stars.

Finally !!!

I’m so happy with this app now. I’ve had a ChromeCast since the beginning, and was pretty disappointed with all the limitations ,inabilities, incompatibilities, bugs, disconnects, resolution quality and how painfully long it would take to connect. I had given up on the ChromeCast and it sat in a drawer for 3 years, replaced by FireSticks & Smart TVs.
Recently I bought a new Tv and it had ChromeCast built into it..... it was still rather unimpressive and had some of the same (slightly improved) issues. It had me connecting via Google Home app. Which also fell short, but then I noticed an update on Streaming for ChromeCast app, and gave it a shot...WOW, I could not believe it, finally!!
Finally nailed it, totally redesigned the app, and it includes everything that I know it has been capable of all along. Screencast, browser cast, photo roll cast, camera cast, app cast, music cast. Etc. and it all just simply works. Amazing quality and speed, all in one app. So relieved that I can show a video from my iphone on my tv now in seconds rather waiting and hoping it would connect and buffer before everyone in the room lost interest..
And I dug out that old ChromeCast and hooked it back up to the bedroom tv, it is like having a brand new device now. Thank you!!
