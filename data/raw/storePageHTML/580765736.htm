Description

Ladies and gentlemen,
Welcome to Megapolis - a long standing city building strategy game. Megapolis is not only a true economic game based on the rules of the market, but also a perfect chance to build the metropolis of your dreams.
You'll never be bored in this game, Megapolis has a lot of opportunities to grow: build a perfect urban infrastruсture, advance science by forming a research center, expand the mining industry for natural resources or become a true oil tycoon. You can play however you want.

LINE UP REALISTIC BUILDINGS AND MONUMENTS
Ever wanted to combine Stonehenge, Eiffel Tower and Statue of liberty all in the same town? Now it's possible! Megapolis gives you an opportunity to build a huge number of famous buildings and monuments which look exactly the same their real-life counterparts. Build houses, skyscrapers, parks and choose the monuments you'd like to add to your city to enjoy the view. Place buildings strategically to keep the taxes flowing and your city growing. There’s always something new and different to make your town unique.

BUILD PERFECT URBAN INFRASTRUCTURE
Megapolis is constantly growing! Create one of the busiest transport hubs ever and provide your citizens with all the blessings of civilization . Construct infrastructure such as a Ring Road for vehicular traffic, an extensive underground network, railway stations for cargo and passenger trains, airports with fleets of airplanes to send flights all over the world, and much more!

DEVELOP AN INDUSTRIAL COMPLEX
Form your own manufacturing system strategy. Develop deposits, collect and process resources, build factories, extract and refine oil, and more. Build up a military base, develop new weapons and enter the arms race. Choose your path and become a true industrial tycoon.

ADVANCE SCIENCE
Mayor! To progress faster and conquer the space, your town definitely needs a research center. Discover new materials, advance science and build a true spaceport to fire the rockets into space. Don't forget to invest in high-tech equipment such as survey boats, atmospheric sounders, deep-submergence research vehicles and much more!

WIN STATE CONTESTS
Cooperate with other Mayors and compete in real-time state contests. Once the weekly contest is on, earn as many points as you can to get rewards and climb the ranks to advance through Leagues. Compete in seasonal contests to get even more valuable rewards. Become a top State and get a unique state emblem and rewards that can upgrade and beautify your city.


MAIN FEATURES:
- Realistic buildings and monuments
- State contests - Develop your own state and join state contests
- Amazing opportunities for infrastructure upgrades: railway, airport, ring road, underground, ships etc.
- Industrial complex - collect and process resources
- Military base - develop new weapons and enter the arms race
- Research center - advance science to progress faster

Is your Megapolis going to be your personal source of pride? It doesn’t matter what type of player you are or what your age is. Every decision is yours as your city grows into Megapolis. Nothing will be in your way once you start planning a strategy to progress. Make smart choices to keep your citizens happy and your skyline growing. It’s all there for you to enjoy! Become the most creative tycoon the world has ever seen and the best builder, too! Build, expand, plan your strategy - the decision is in your hands!

Gianna_v , 07/27/2020

Good game but a few ideas

I love this game but there is a few things I want to recommend which could make the game better. One of the things that annoys me is placing roads. If you guys can, you should make it so we can put a road down and drag it to make the road longer. It’s kind of annoying to place each one where you want it to be. Another thing that I think would be cool would be the state house. When you’re getting points and building stuff for your state, you should make more options for the material. I feel like all the coin ones you have, are really high. There’s a lot of housing ones, which I’m fine with but I think there should be more coin options too. This one isn’t that important but when you have like special events happening. You should make better prizes, I’m not going to spend a lot of time doing one just to get like 1000 coins. If it were to be more than that and maybe some building stuff, I think that would be kinda cool. Also, you should make new things available as you level up. Once you reach level 50 or 60 (I don’t remember) you don’t get anymore places unlocked. Like you can still build more city areas but nothing that new. But overall, it’s a really fun game to play, I’m level 70 something and I’m still not bored of it. You don’t gain levels too fast nor too slow. If you’re looking for something to play and pass time, I recommend this game.

Developer Response ,

Thanks a lot for the constructive feedback and the great suggestions. We'll take them into account when designing the upcoming updates for the game.

Absolutely Awesome Game, but a few ideas and suggestions

So I’ve been playing this game for a while, and I like it a lot, but when there aren’t any events going on, there really isn’t any motive to do stuff like launch probes or rocket ships, or military operations. This brings on the first idea/suggestion, host events/contests more frequently. It would really encourage a lot more people to try and progress with military, or research. Another idea/suggestion is to add more building variety. In other words, if you place a building down, the wooden house, for example, you could edit the color so that when it is built, it will be different from the original color, and now for my final idea. You should add more things past level 52 I think it was, because you don’t really unlock anything new around the map past that level. So I’m thinking maybe add a heliport, just like the new airport, but for helicopters, then maybe a big racing thing, but you focus on holding all of the tracks, and you can sponsor teams, stuff like that. I also think adding a bit better detail, (graphics and stuff) would be pretty nice. Well that’s basically all I have. Other than that. It’s a great game that I would suggest to friends. Keep up the good work!

Coolgamer24 , 07/19/2020

Good game, but could use some minor changes

So most things about this game are good. You can kinda do what you want, and build whatever, I mean you still have to worry about money, but there is a lot of cool stuff you can get for cheap. But the one thing that kind of turns me off of this game is the big projects. They require materials that can’t be produced with factories or stores, and you have to rely on friends donating them to you, or your own stash of megabucks. And sometimes these big projects are mandatory for you to move on in the game. Now I agree that it should take time and effort to do these projects, but I think you should be able to complete them yourself without relying on friends or megabucks. I think maybe you should be able to make these goods by maybe stores or something, or maybe other missions, or anything, I just honk you should be able to complete them yourself without asking friends or splurging megabucks. Again, I think that these projects should take time and effort, but from you, you should be able to do them independently, and without megabucks. I think that megabucks should be more optional of when you spend them, because like I said, a lot of times these projects are mandatory to move on in the game.

Anyway, besides my rant, this is a good game and you are pretty free to do whatever, the only reason I gave this 4 starts is because the problem above, otherwise it would be a definite 5.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for your feedback. There are many other ways to get the required materials. You can receive materials as rewards for completing tasks, unlocking achievements and reaching new levels; win materials at the Stock Exchange; receive them as a daily bonus, and as a reward for sending airplanes with goods; ask for some materials in chat.
