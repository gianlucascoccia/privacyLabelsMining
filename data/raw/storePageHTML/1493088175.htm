Description

Welcome to HMU, the best LFG platform to find your next crewmates, I mean teammates! HMU has created the ultimate voice chat matchmaking platform for gamers around the world to find teammates for any mobile game, including Among Us, Roblox, Minecraft, Fortnite Mobile, Call of Duty "COD" Mobile, PUBG Mobile, Hearthstone, Brawl Stars, Just Chatting and many more!

How many times have you played a game of Among us, found a crewmate or two you liked, but then lost them and never matched with them again? With HMU, that problem is a thing of the past!

How it works:
Join any of the real-time voice chat rooms of your favorite games to be matched up with other players instantly.
Copy the game code provided by the host.
Paste it into the game’s app!
Enjoy the game with HMU audio chat in the background! No need to jump from one app to the other.

That’s it! Social gaming has never been so easy, and finding the imposter has never been so fun! HMU is the best place for LFG, playing mobile games, and making friends.

Other features that make HMU the best place for voice chat LFG:

Add Friends!
Don’t want to match up with random gamers? Add friends and invite them to a private game so only they can join!

Leave school, but never leave your friends!
It’s always better being together. Jump on HMU and meet new teammates with your friends! Watch out for traitors!

Games, Games, and More Games!
HMU has the largest selection of games to join and meet new friends. They are, but are not limited to: Among Us, Fortnite, Roblox & Minecraft!

Watch the game while waiting!
Joined late and your squad left you in the lobby? No worries! The host has the ability to quickly screenshare their screen so everyone left in the lobby can watch the game live!

Filter Your Location!
Having connection problems because you’re playing on the EU (Europe) servers in NA (North America) or NA servers in the EU? Just simply filter your location so you are only put in a lobby with gamers in your region!

Safety is a Priority!
Someone acting suspicious? We know how important it is for our gamers to feel safe while playing. That is why safety is a top priority for us. We have staff on 24/7 to make sure there is as little vulgarity as possible in the lobby. We also offer volume control of other matched teammates, as well as the ability to mute and block gaming traitors you do not match well with.
