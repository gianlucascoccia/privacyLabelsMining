Description

Ready to become a better plant parent? Steward makes happy plants easy. Combining thoughtful technology with friendly guidance, we’re here to take the guesswork out of plant parenting.

Steward helps you match plants based on your home’s unique environment. We show you where to place plants so they will thrive, send you customized watering and care reminders, and help ensure you and your plants are happy — looking to buy plants? We help you find the healthiest plants local to you that are right for your budget, values, and lifestyle.

Scan your room with the app, and we have everything we need to create your custom Plant Map that shows you where each plant will thrive in your home. Simply a few feet can mean the difference between struggling and thriving.

Here’s what you can do with Steward:

- Fill your home with happy, healthy plants

- Care for each of your plants without doubts, worry questions. Each plant has care customized to your home and in-depth care guides.

- Get thoughtful watering and care reminders

- Discover plants that will thrive in your home

- Identify and learn more about your plants

- Ask Steward: Get unlimited support and messaging from our team of botanists, horticulturalists, and Plant Doctors.

- Because we understand your space, we’ll quickly diagnose any plant ailments and help you solve them.

- Plant Concierge: We don't sell plants directly, instead we match you with the best local and online plant shops. From classic to rare plants, we’ve got you. We love local! Say hello to only happy plants in your future when you shop based on your Plant Map.

- Keep on top of your plant’s health with our quarterly plant physicals and your own plant journal

- Discover Plant Playlists: Filter for pet-safe, edible, flowering, patterns, hanging, care level, and more.

Download Steward today, and let’s grow together.
