Amazing

This app is truly the best. I am abroad learning arabic in a markaz, and when i tell you i use this app everyday, i use it everyday! You can find, plurals, opposites, definitions, example sentences and related words, all under one search. Granite the app is in arabic, so you will need some previous arabic exposure to navigate and get the most out of it. The dissect word for word translation is in English and that can obviously benefit anyone at any level. I truly suggest this app. The interface is not beautiful but boy, oh boy, it gets the job done!!
