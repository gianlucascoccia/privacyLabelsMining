Description

Note: this app keeps transactions locally on the device only, it does not sync or back up to the cloud automatically. Manual backups must be used.

Have a Bank Account? Are you single, married, or share a bank account with another person? This app is perfect for you!

Perfect for reconciliation as well as anyone that has a Bank Account. Simply keep track of each of your purchases directly with the app. If you share an account, then this app allows you to reconcile with the other person to transfer all their purchases and update your remaining balance in your account!

You can now backup your transactions via email to .csv or .txt file and restore from a back up!

---How it Works---
Please read instructions on the app before starting! (Both devices must have a beginning Deposit and at least one withdrawal)

One user is set to a 'primary' user. This user will have the total remaining balance as well as all of their transactions. The other person set to 'secondary'.

When reconciling, the app connects the devices via bluetooth to move all 'secondary's' transactions to the 'primary' user. This will update the remaining balance on the 'primary' user, and then send the new balance back to the 'secondary' user.

----Highlights-------
-Manage multiple accounts! NEW!!!
-Shows a 'Total' Balance of all accounts NEW!!!
-Select the currency for each account
-Backup your transactions to .csv or .txt file!
-Restore from a backup!

-Simple to use

-Wonderful for people sharing the same bank account.
-Transfers all purchases to the 'primary' account and updates each remaining balance when reconciling.

(Use for personal tracking of Bank Account balance only. Check monthly with real bank statement to verify transactions)

Cheap Purchase Price, It’s Flaws Can Cost $100’s When You Overdaft

Trying to get organized and avoid overdraft fees and this app looked good with all of the features... unfortunately there is too much goin in that it becomes a hinderance. Should you be in a busy shopping line at checkout and distracted while entering in your ledger you may forget you forgot to select “save” any one of the three times you have to select that option during a standard ledger entry. One would think that selecting “back” would equate to saving as in other apps I’ve tried, but not here. It doesn’t even so much as warm you your about to back out without saving your changes and carries in business as usual. Next thing you know your ledger is off and you have 2-3 overdrafts since you last crosschecked. Overdrafts are certainly users liability in most cases, but that’s what I am trying to fix with this app and it just misses the mark.

Simplify the entry process and don’t make so many steps for user to get distracted. I would MUCH rather have a duplicate entry saved during the exit process than I would have an item missing and no notification there was a user error. THIS APP OFTEN GETS USED ON THE GLY AND DISTRACTIONS CAN BE COSTLY.

Please redesign so user does not have to condition an inefficient workflow into their routines and it’d help greatly. I’m willing to give it another chance and revised review if the “save” issue is addressed.
