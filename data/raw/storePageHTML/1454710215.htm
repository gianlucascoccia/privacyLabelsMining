Description

Video dance battles and challenges. VOTE FOR YOUR FAVORITES!

Dance it out!

VOTE on your favorite dance videos and challenge your friends (or anyone) to a dance battle!

YOU DECIDE THE WINNER OF EACH DANCE BATTLE
The power is yours! You help decide the outcome of each dance battle by swiping (or double tapping) to cast your vote. Support your favorite dancers as they climb the leaderboards. Get notified who wins each dance battle. It’s that simple!

WHO KNOWS, YOU MIGHT CREATE THE NEXT VIRAL DANCE CHALLENGE...
It takes less than a minute to create a dance fight.
1 - Choose your favorite dance off music (choose from MILLIONS OF SONGS - hip hop, fdm, battle anthems, pop, throwback, edm, trap - all your favorites!)
2 - Record your dance video
3 - Challenge friends to a dance battle or take on the dance world!

The app does the rest: It sends out the dance challenges and lets your friends know to come watch your dance battles and support you.

THE DANCE APP FOR VIRTUAL DANCE BATTLES
All Styles Dance Battles! Krumping, Hip Hop, Tutting, Popping, Breakdance, Litefeet, Waaking, Flex… Bring your best and inspire the best in others.

THE BEST DANCE BATTLE MUSIC
Millions of songs to choose from including Battle Anthems by Shash’U, fdm from Uninamise and Davincii & all the most popular dance off music available!

RACK UP THE Ws
Compete and dance your way to the top of the Weekly and All-Time leaderboards for bragging rights. Challenge anyone to a dance off & show off your best dance moves for friends and fans.

CONNECT WITH OTHER INSPIRING DANCERS
We make it easy for you to find dancers--from amazing to beginners--and challenge others to a dance fight. The DanceFight community is all about taking risks, walking through fear, and inspiring others to do the same. At the end of the day, DanceFight is a safe space to create, and most importantly, to have fun! We’re all about creating and sharing joy.

SHARE WITH YOUR CREW
Like something you're watching? DanceFight allows you to follow your favorite dance battlers and share their best dance videos on all your socials.

JUST DANCE!
A tidal wave in the sea of sameness, DanceFight is a chance to celebrate the weird and honor the unique! A space where people aren't tearing each other down and where you can be as “you” as you want to be.

DanceFight is about having fun and contributing to a creative global community. We choose to be inspired by others, not intimidated, because shared success makes us feel liberated. Bring us your best dance moves and craziest ideas. We can’t wait to see what you create!

If it moves you, move it! And just dance!

Follow us on IG to get served up our weekly dance tutorial, new dance challenges and some of the best dancers and dance videos anywhere.

Entertaining and fun, even for non-dancers

I am not much of a dancer, but this app makes it fun, and I admire the much better dancers I see throughout the app. The music is great as well. The layout is very confusing and not very attractive in my opinion. There doesn’t seem to be a way to check everyone’s dance under a select song? Developer Response ,

Hi, "warmsucculent", and THANKS for the suggestions. It's not everyday that we get great ideas like this flowing in through App Store Reviews, and we're totally grateful you took the time to share your suggestions. Can we ask that you send any other ideas to feedback@dancefightapp.com (or just give any additional details about the ideas you already shared)? We say, bring it on! In terms of what you already shared, we're improving the experience on a weekly release basis, so things are evolving for the better rapidly. Many of your recommendations are definitely consistent with our team's views on how we can make the DanceFight experience even better. In just a short few days, for example, you'll be able to record your dance and immediately post "open" challenges onto the main feed for any and all to join! This is our first foray into "random" functionality, with more to come on that front. Also, we're constantly improving our search functionality. Currently, if you tap the Search icon and enter a song name, all dance fights using that song should show for you. Tap around and let us know if not. And regarding comments, our top priority is the safety and positivity of our community. We figured, comments are where there's so much insensitivity, so like you said, we removed that feature. WE ARE THRILLED that you are excited about the concept, and hope that you can be active as a dancer, voter and advocate for our budding community of awesome people!
