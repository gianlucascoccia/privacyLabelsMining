Description

IntellectoKids Super Parents offers interactive tutorials on various parenting issues, such as children developmental crises, how to deal with jealousy toward a little brother or sister, how to discuss difficult issues, such as death or sexual abuse, etc.

IntellectoKids’ mission is to make parenting easier and less stressful on the life of a parent.
