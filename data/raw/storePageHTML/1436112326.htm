Description

A new client for power saving intelligent video device
Main Features:
- Support user register and binding device to user account
- Support power-saving mode and remote wakeup
- Support TF recording and cloud recording video
- Support notification with a snapshot
- Support notification with AI face recogization
- Live view H.264 720P/1080P up to 30FPS
- Dual way audio
- Remote Parameter setting
- Remote query alert and playback
- Support WiFi doorbell
- Support WiFi battery camera
- Support 4G wireless battery camera
- Support Solar WiFi/4G wireless camera
- Support Panoramic camera

Walt131 , 10/21/2020

App is not ready for users

So many problems with this app. I’ll try to list them here. I use it with their solar wifi camera.

1. Even though the camera has a good SD card, video from motion detection does not save.
2. When you open the camera to live view, even though cloud storage is OFF and memory card is ON, the app tells you that there is no video on cloud. Does not show you what’s on SD card. The app is trying to force you to pay them to use cloud storage.
3. The camera supports two way audio, but if you send voice to the camera speaker, the cam microphone remains ON. So the speaker audio is picked up by the microphone creating a horrible feedback loop. Loud screeching and massive distortion. Two way audio is unusable.
4. There is no speaker volume control for the camera. It is permanently set to maximum loudness, so the sound output is extremely distorted and barely intelligible.
5. The app will not allow uploading pics or video to your mobile device. You can’t even copy photos or video files in the app. If you need a file, say to give a copy to the cops, get a screwdriver, go to the camera and physically remove the SD card. What if the camera is not accessible, maybe in another state? As a security device it’s useless.
6. You can’t turn off the IR illumination. If your camera is located where there is other lighting, such as a porch light or streetlight, the camera forces you to use night mode, which is poor at best.
