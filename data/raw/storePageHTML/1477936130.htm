Description

No Sign Up Required! Helponymous allows users to anonymously share feelings, advice, & engage in conversations with a like-minded community to find support.

Helponymous provides the user with the ability to start self-care by privately sharing personal experiences with real people. These experiences will be divided into different categories displaying specific examples of an issue. You can also check out the main feed to see a little bit of everything. Our slogan is “No shame, no blame, we’re all the same.”

How to start? Post a message in 1 of our 100+ topics, check random users’ compassionate feedback. Don't stress about receiving millions of likes because the like counter was disabled since we don’t believe in fulfillment through egotistical means ;). After scrolling through the feeds, a sense of relief usually resonates with a user because we all notice that “I know I’m not the only one.” Not ready to share a thought? Scroll through all the different posts and find relatable topics to get your toes wet with this caring community. Or click the “plus” sign to experience a chatroom of privacy with a complete stranger who will never come back to judge! Fourth wall break: “Sorry if your parents said don’t talk to strangers haha.”

Morning, during the day, in between classes, night, It does not matter what time of day, the Helponymous community is always here to help you realize that you’re not alone in what you are going through whether good or bad. Remember the app is completely anonymous so no one will ever know who you are so good luck and enjoy the judgment-free community.

Participate without fear of shame. You are always anonymous without the need to blame. Find daily support to realize we’re all the same.

Anxiety, Depression, Cancer, Academic Support, Sports, and everything in between, you will soon realize the similarities every lifeform shares.

PHENOMENAL COMFORTING APP!

This app right here is a must. I don’t usually rate apps because I don’t want to take time out of my day. However, this app is just so great I have to rant about it. To begin with everything is anonymous and you don’t even have to enter an email or name when you first open the app. In addition there is a feature which allows you to create a passcode. This way, regardless of who has your phone they’ll never be able to open that app to see any of your posts. Then on top of all that you can track your moods daily. I like to journal but sometimes I don’t feel in the mood. With this app you simply add what you’re feeling that day along with some notes so you don’t miss out on journaling. It’s a phenomenal app that encourages and comforts others. I’d like to mention that they have more than 20 topics to choose from. So if you want to specifically see posts on depression or anxiety you can do that as well. Everything about this app is wonderful and I highly suggest you use it. Every now and then when I don’t feel well I can just hop on the app and post about it and have other people reply to me. God’s work I tell you. Please do yourself a favor and download it you won’t regret it.

A truly wonderful anonymous app

If you are looking for an app where you can speak about your life or problems or illnesses without strings/name attached, this is the app for you. This is a great app for self-care without identity. There is no word limit for your posts, so you can either quietly whisper or loudly vent to the whole world without any concerns or issue. There is no sign up required, and you stay behind a generic name and avatar (Arlo, Moki, Paskel, etc). There are a lot of active members as well, so you’ll always receive feedback & reactions. You can hide posts if you feel uncomfortable or report if it’s bad. There are always people working on this app. I highly recommend this! Especially when you just want to shout out something to the world without identity/personal consequence :) This app has topics such as Depression, Anxiety, Whisper, PCOS, ASL, DBT, CBT, and much more to come. Honestly, this app has everything you need. You can rent and vent about anything. Seriously, the topics are amazing! I 10/10 recommend this app :)

fortuneravine , 07/02/2020

Generally wholesome

I really like this app! It’s nice to be completely anonymous sometimes when venting about an issue.
The community is the kindest I think I’ve ever seen. Every reply I’ve seen has been loving and supportive or at least not outright mean. Love it! The avatars that you can choose from to represent you are adorable too. They all have their own names, so you go by whatever their name is. This is another way you are anonymous.
I completely understand about the paywall on some features, but as someone who isn’t willing to pay (yet) it’s a little annoying. If you don’t pay for a subscription, you can’t search posts by topic (all you have is the main feed with all topics together) and you can only make a new post once every 24 hours. It’s still very useable and not a big deal though.
It’s a cute, simple app to find friendly people and help each other!
