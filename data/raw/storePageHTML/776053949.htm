Description

Complete Chants D'Esperance with Tunes provides you access to all the lyrics of the songbook Chants D'Esperance. Some Tunes are also included with the Lyrics.

Complete Chants D'Esperance with Tunes provides you access to all the lyrics of the songbook Chants D'Esperance. Some Tunes are also included with the Lyrics so you can learn and help you worship. We add music to the app with each update, so keep your Chants D'Esperance with Tunes app up to date.
Other Features include: Add to Favorites, You can create your own favorite list of songs/lyrics.
Send Lyrics to Email.
Play Tunes many of the Lyrics.

We respect your privacy.
