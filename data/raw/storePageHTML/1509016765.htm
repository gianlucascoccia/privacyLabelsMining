Description

Our app will turn your devices into a full-fledged baby monitor in a matter of minutes.

Now you do not need to buy expensive and complex equipment, our app will save your money and another trip to the store.

Look after your children while they sleep, left alone to play in the room, and in other necessary cases.

The app works regardless of your internet connection, it can be used even when your devices are connected to other internet connections (WIFI, 3G, LTE , etc.)

Functionality of the app allows you to always keep a close eye on your children.

The app has an sound and motion alert function. This helps to be completely sure that your child is safe even if you are not there.

If the child is awakened, you can turn on the built-in melody for him/her, or send a soothing voice message.

The night vision function delivers a clean video feed with a low night-light on in the room.

Also, if necessary, you can activate the flashlight on the broadcast device for illumination.

If you want to record some moments, the video recording and screenshot functions are available for you. Note that the video recording can be configured both to get activated by sound and by motion.

It has also an option of HD quality that converts the video stream to the maximum image resolution.

The app also has a brightness adjustment option of the broadcasting device, which allows you to adjust the lighting next to your child, which will eventually work as a baby lamp and will not let your child get scared when he wakes up.

The switchover function between rear and front cameras allows you to view other compass areas or spaces you want to record.

Hla Libbey , 05/31/2020

Excellent for travel

I’ve used this everywhere on my journeys with my children- airport lounges, on board trains, hotels, rental houses etc and it has been consistently good. The option to run off mobile data as well as Wi-fi also means it’s suitable for going further afield (though this could get expensive in the long run its great for short stints). The battery consumption is a little high on both iPad and iPhone so I tend to keep the device set to child mode plugged in and then swap devices around. On the whole a very useful piece of kit that other parents seem envious of!

Artavius Braidon , 05/31/2020

Our every day baby monitor

When our original radio style baby monitor started to fail we got this app as a ‘temporary’ replacement. The app is so good that we have never got another baby monitor. Works over Wi-Fi in the house but also over cellular if we’re out at friends and baby is sleeping in his buggy etc.


I would love for one small feature to be added and that would be a (perhaps optional?) sleep timer. Often the baby wakes up and I say to myself “how long was he sleeping for?”
