Description

KeyHero is a new app that saves your keys the smart way, so you can share, organize and copy keys like never before. With all your keys conveniently and securely stored in a virtual vault, you can access them at the touch of your fingertips. So no more lockouts. No more "oh no" moments. Just one Key Hero, always ready to come to the rescue. It's a seamless service that unlocks possibilities and provides infinite peace of mind.

Simply scan a digital copy of your key at a KeyKrafter key cutting machine and upload it to the KeyHero app. Lose your key? No problem. Visit any KeyKrafter machine at Home Depots nationwide to get a new copy made by simply sending your digital key from the app to the machine. Need to share a key with a friend or family member? Also no problem. Give a “spare key” to a family member or friend simply and easily. Just upload, store and share via email or text.

KeyHero is brought to you by two leaders in technology and key cutting: The Hillman Group and Unikey. The Hillman Group is the leader in key duplication technology with over 50 years in the hardware industry and a heritage of unsurpassed key expertise, quality, service and user satisfaction. Unikey is a leader in application and technology development. Military-grade encryption technology is used to secure your digital key so you can rest assured that your key will be in good hands in our virtual vault.

For more information on our app please visit our website at www.mykeyhero.com.

Great idea but a bit problematic

MyKeyHero, although is a good idea, it has numerous limitations and it isn’t updating account data when new keys are made. Twice I’ve cut keys and neither was added to my account. The app offers no way to manually add the missing transactions either. Another problem is that neither the app or web site offer a way to communicate with the company (e.g. an email address) if there are technical issues. Social media like Twitter is fine for general comments or simple questions, but compl inappropriate for complex technical issues, questions, or comments.

So in short I recommend the following app improvements:

1. Add a way to manually add transactions that don’t automatically get added to a user’s account (for whatever reason)

2. Add a way to contact the company regarding technical issues

3. Add a way to change email address in app. This likely should be in Settings

Great idea, terrible execution

The idea of backing up keys seems great, but after wasting several days with both the kiosks and customer service I am still without a backup. I needed a backup as my roommate needed a mail key and I was out of state, henceforth this service would have been great since I can’t mail a mail key. Although after visiting these several stores and attempting to get any aid I can, I still am without a key as they tell me everyday to go back to the machines and try again, then once I go In I am told to wait 24 hours and after receiving nothing I call back in and they tell me to return to the kiosk restarting the process, which I did several times before giving up. If you need a key, I would not trust this company to help you, or they have a long way to go.
