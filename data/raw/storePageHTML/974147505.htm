Description

A literary and artistic tour-de-force, bringing to life such classics as The Cask of Amontillado, Alone, The Facts in the Case of M. Valdemar, and El Dorado.

iClassics: Beyond Engrossing

iClassics unites the old and the new to create immersive experiences that inspire and move everyone.

In this innovative Immersive Entertainment app, a fine selection of macabre tales is lovingly and artistically enhanced with interaction, illustration, animation, FX, and OST.

Following on from the previous two installments of the iPoe Collection (Vol.1 and 2), the iPoe Collection Vol.3 is a literary and artistic tour-de-force, bringing to life such classics as The Cask of Amontillado, Alone, The Facts in the Case of M. Valdemar, and El Dorado.

By casting a modern glare on a fine selection of Edgar Allan Poe's stories, the result is a spectacular and wholly original experience that utilizes every feature of modern mobile devices (gyro-perspective, flash, vibration).

TILT to reveal what's in the shadows

TOUCH to uncover obscured details

TURN to discover what lurks off the screen

Main features:

* Available in 4 languages: English, Spanish, Portuguese and French.
* Poe Stickers (iMessage iOS 10)
* Outstanding artwork by David G. Forés and a breathtaking original soundtrack by Miquel Tejada.

The iPoe Collection (Three Volumes) marked the beginning of iClassics Production's journey, and has now become a classic in its own right, along with a cult following.

Be part of it.

apptappin , 01/11/2020

Suspicious

These books are entertaining and well done, but unfortunately I have to give them a low rating due to the apps suspicious behavior. I have purchased all of the iclassics and found that they all immediately default to the 'on' position in cellular data and immediately use nearly a megabyte of data. Why a book would need to use any data at all is curious and that they use that much data is both odd and suspicious. I wrote to the company at the email posted on their website but received no response. I cannot recommend purchasing these books unless they stop this practice of sending whatever data they're gathering and using customers cellular data without permission.

Developer Response ,

Hi, we just released an update. Now the app works correctly. Regarding the data, we do not use data, we do not even ask for the email or name. Payment apps do not use any type of advertising. We are a small startup and we are trying to monetize the apps with just the payment per download. Our apologies for the delay to reply.
