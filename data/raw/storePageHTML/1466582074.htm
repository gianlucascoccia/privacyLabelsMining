Description

Game of Skateboard.
Enjoy battles and chats in multiplayer mode.
One park per person is given. It is freely customizable.
Parks created by someone continue to be released one after another.

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Space for playing Skateboard.
It is a game where you can experience the various attractions of skateboarding. You can enjoy freely without any rules or restrictions.
Please wear the clothes you want to wear and go to the place you want to go, make your favorite tricks.

You can
・Customize your avatar and fashion.
・Customize your own park.
・Configure trick list.
・Skate in the parks of others.
・Skate together while chatting.
・Try to Score Mission.
・Multiplayer online battle with up to 10 skaters.

By use the rich features in the game,

rush357753 , 11/12/2020

Good time waster, subpar game

Fun game to waste time, good enough to try. There are sooo many problems though. Skating feels so clunky and unnatural. The trick stick is a good idea but falls very short of success, sometimes unresponsive and half the time unpredictable. Physics are inconsistent on what will happen while trying to land on a rail to grind, it might work, you might stop, you might fly off into space... who knows. On the flying into space aspect, holy cow the skate comps are terrible and based off of luck and what tricks you’ve been lucky enough to get out of their loot box style system, no skill needed. Randomly drop into the map in various spots where you may lose 15 secs on setup,meaning, you lose. Another skater bumps you... off to space with you! 20 secs lost.... you lose. Tricks ranging from useless 50 points to literally easily chain thousands of points. It’s 100% vicious pay to win since the only way to get tricks is to overcome those horrendously broken competitions over and over in a progressively growing goal to have a loot box chance at a decent trick, with limited try’s allowed per day of course. Or pay $20 for 11 trick chances, which may also just turn out to be duplicates of garbage tricks that will do nothing for you.

Fantastic Game but.....

I really like the game but I really wish that there could be an offline mode where you could like play offline by yourself sort of like a practice mode. Also maybe add a little more customization. I have my ideas below.

Ideas for Practice Mode
In this mode you could like ride around on the regular default park without other players or CPU. Also during offline mode you won’t be able to do most things like customize levels or the park but you could still customize the character and pick out different boards to ride on.

Customization
The customization is nearly perfect. Customization for the character is great but for the board... well there needs to be more. Also I don’t really get how the “X” currency works and how you get it so maybe just get rid of it and make everything in the game free but like you have to buy the game to play it (maybe like $0.99). Or be extra kind and make everything free including the game. But you to actually make something off the game maybe have some items locked behind an amount of ads you had to watch, for example to get a golden skateboard you would need to watch 30 ads or something. You could do this with outfits too.

In conclusion great game worth playing. Hopefully the devs have read this review and maybe consider adding the changes I’ve talked about. Have a great rest of your day and thanks for easing this review!
