Description

The BevMo! app will make your shopping much easier! Browse and shop online, find your nearest store, get special notifications on discounts and fun events, create or login to your account and view your personalized available offers and purchase history.

CLUBBEV! CARD: Login or create an account to access your ClubBev! Card, then simply scan the barcode at the register to get the discounted ClubBev! pricing on your purchase.

OFFERS: 1st time app downloaders get $10 off your 1st purchase of $50 or more! (coupon available in the Offers section once you add your ClubBev! card to the app). Plus, view all of your personalized available offers.

BROWSE AND SHOP: Shop best-selling items by category, or search for your product by name.

MY ACCOUNT: Create an Account of login with a previously created bevmo.com account

FAVORITES: View the products you tagged and easily add them to your cart for purchase.

ORDER HISTORY: Loved a previous purchase but can’t remember what the name of it was? You can easily re-order previously placed orders.

Too Much Work

I downloaded this app during the quarantine since curbside was the only option. I would like to continue to use it for connivence, but it is not very intuitive. My biggest complaint is reordering items should be much simpler than it is. When you select the “reorder” tab, the first thing that comes up are items I’ve never ordered. The second tab is past orders, but instead of pictures of items I’ve ordered, I see an order number and status. Once you hunt through that and find the items ordered, the only option is to add the entire order to the cart. So I did that figuring I’d edit the items in the cart, but that wasn’t an option either. If you want me to buy from you, instead of the grocery store, or Target, you’ve got to make it easy.

Nairbjc , 10/01/2019

Wishing for better selection

The sales and discounts BevMo! gives are fabulous
Their prices are high though generally. Like $6 , $10 , sometimes $22 dollars more than other stores. Also their selection is not much. And I wish that their app would show you other stores if the one you shop at doesn’t have what you’re looking for. For instance I was looking for the new Booker’s Batch and my store didn’t have it but the app couldn’t show me other nearby stores that do. My store was getting the Booker’s Batch but now they don’t get the newest Batch. What gives?

Shells-bells , 12/18/2020

Overall layout good but payment process terrible

So the overall navigation of the app is pretty good, it’s pretty easy to navigate and it’s pretty responsive and it’s pretty well-laid out but the process of paying is absolutely terrible they use an outside source that requires you to put all of your credit card information every time and there is no possible way to use PayPal, it doesn’t even respond to automatic input of credit card information which is already saved in my phone. To be honest, there have been many many times where I just didn’t order from BevMo because I just didn’t have the time to put in the credit card information or I didn’t have my credit card with me so I just close out of the app and got my liquor from somewhere else.
