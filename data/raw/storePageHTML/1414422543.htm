The latest iOS 12 compatible is a fast and free PDF converter to Word that works on Apple mobile devices, including iPhone Xs Max and 3rd generation iPad Pro.

The interface is very convenient, user-friendly and clear to understand and use. The application perfectly performs its service of changing formats and it's not overloaded with unnecessary or complex functions.

We realize the occasional disappointment when it comes to handling Portable Document Format and the fact that the appropriate software to view or convert PDF to Word DOC is either hard to find or it only works with a monthly paid subscription. So we designed the Alto App to make the process easy for all iPhone and iPad users.

All of the best features were gathered in a single solution for you:

◇ It gives you the fastest conversion speed.
◇ Doesn’t require the internal space of your device since you are using a cloud-based platform.
◇ It’s totally free of charge with no trial periods or registration.
◇ You can easily get textual information from the document within seconds.
◇ High exporting quality due to enhanced recognition technology.
◇ Top-grade security protection of your sensitive information.
◇ Import documents from the internal memory of a smartphone or tablet and from cloud-storage: DropBox, Google Drive, OneDrive.
◇ A purposeful tool that provides users with everything needed to convert PDF to Word document.

The main advantage of this software is that it’s distributed completely with no payments or any restrictions on the functionality.
