Description

Welcome to TETRIS®, the official mobile app for the world’s favorite puzzle game. Hang out and chat with friends in private TETRIS TOGETHER matches, battle to be the last one standing in 100-player TETRIS ROYALE mode, play a quick round to beat your own score, or play infinite rounds to master your skills in the TETRIS Single Player modes. TETRIS forever!

TETRIS ROYALE
• Battle against 99 other players in last-one-standing matches.
• Clear lines and use boosters to send attacks or defend your Matrix from Garbage.
• Earn Tournament Points to climb limited-time leaderboards and win rewards.

TETRIS TOGETHER
• Invite friends to compete in your own private matches.
• Customize the game to your group’s skill level.
• Talk to each other while you play with real-time voice chat!

TETRIS SINGLE PLAYER
• Master your skills with the iconic Tetris gameplay you know and love.
* Pick between the Traditional mode for endless rounds or the new Quick mode for when you want to play Tetris but only have a few minutes to play.
• Rotate Tetriminos, clear lines and crush your high score.
• Available offline - enjoy Tetris anytime, anywhere.

PUZZLE YOUR WAY
• Enjoy intuitive touch controls or choose on-screen controls.
• Personalize your game with custom themes and avatars.
• Show off your Tetris style in case you’re featured in in-game videos.

The world’s favorite block puzzle game is now a game for everyone to play together, anywhere. Tetris forever!

Too many ads

Good game but way too many ads. If you made a one time purchase to remove ads forever I would purchase it I recommend you make a one time purchase feature to remove ads forever. Also you should add more game modes. Add the old Tetris blitz game as a mode that would be cool. Add a level mode too where it’s just a bunch of levels that you have to complete with an objective like “reach _ score in _ minutes” Or make it so that when you clear more rows you have to defeat something or remove something and it makes the “something” take damage or be removed kind of like matching games. Add friend modes like 1v1s and 2v2s. It would be cool to do a season mode where it’s 1v1 against bots or even a public 1v1 mode. You could add a season pass or battle pass to the game as well and add challenges so that you can do them to earn thing on the passes. You could also add a mode that is like all of these block puzzle games. Make a square board and make it so the game gives you certain shaped blocks and you place them anywhere to make a row vertical or horizontal. Add modes to that too like levels, endless high score mode, etc. It would be cool to make a block puzzle mode as well where you get all the pieces and have to put them together and it makes something. One addition to this game I enjoy is the local leaderboards I really enjoy that. I also think that the themes are cool as well.

malicemethods , 01/29/2021

Almost as fun as it used to be

I remember playing Tetris for hours on my game boy classic back in the day. I even had the attachable magnifying glass and light. And when your friend brought his gameboy with the cable to connect your gameboys together and play 1v1. Man... those were the good old days. When my wife and I found this game we were hooked immediately. This game brings back great memories especially with the ability to play 1v1. The one thing that kept me from giving this game a 5 star rating was because in the 1v1 game it does not send over your cleared blocks to your opponent. This was a complaint my wife and I both had. The 1v1 mode should leverage how many points you have and how fast you can clear the blocks rather than just being a game of how long you can last against your opponent. It would be a more interactive 1v1 if I could send my cleared blocks to my opponent just like in Tetris Royal or how it used to be in Tetris classic. I want to be able to hear my wife grow frustrated at how blocks keep piling up on her screen and immediately feel the sense of competitive satisfaction. I’d give the game a 5 star rating if this were added to the 1v1 mode.

COOLLBEENZZ , 09/12/2020

Too many ads, play area is very small, players should be able to play using a remote

Every single time I’ve lost a game was due to thinking I’m pressing one of the onscreen buttons, but in all reality turns out I’ve been missing because the UI and the rest of the play area are both unnecessarily small. As of now I am using an iPhone 11 Pro Max, but the play area is smaller than my little brother’s iPhone 5c. I don’t have the greatest vision which makes this problem a bit more frustrating, but I think there should be an option to make everything larger. I currently struggle and feel very uneasy with the given controls and would very much appreciate if players were allowed to use a remote as I’m sure the game could be played with fewer mistakes. I would also like to ask for the option to have a different menu background rather than hearing the guy talk as soon as I open the game. The last thing this game needs is to cut down a little on the ads. There’s usually somewhere you would go to remove ads, and I’m not sure if there’s a spot I missed, but I’d definitely buy that in a hurry. Other than that, I think this game could be a lot better with these changes. I really love this game, but I will unfortunately be taking a break until at least one of these features are implemented. I hope this game grows and I look forward to rating higher soon.
