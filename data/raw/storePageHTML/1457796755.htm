Description

Smart Resume Builder is the most advanced resume builder that quickly creates professional resumes just by entering your information which makes your job search easier. Just fill your relevant career and education information only once using our user-friendly design and apply variations on the go.

GET HIRED BY TOP RECRUITERS!

Smart Resume Builder helps job seekers create a stunning resume in a matter of minutes. It’s never been easier to stand out from the competition without the professional and modern resume. Well, don’t worry, this app will provide you with professionally tried-and-tested resume templates that make your resume stands out from the competition and help you in getting a job easily. Our creative templates excellently manage your information to make your carrier at the top. 

GET READY FOR THE INTERVIEW!

• Free industry-tested templates for freshers, and
• Premium templates are available for professionals.
 
KEY FEATURES:

• Simple, professional and user-friendly interface.
• Pre-made professionally tested resume templates.
• Add, edit, and re-arrange resume sections easily.
• Easy to use customization wizard for templates.
• Easily adjust the margins of your resume template.
• Preview your resume progress at any time.
• Smart CV Builder saves your info automatically.
• Send your resume directly to your email as a PDF.
• Export to iCloud, Dropbox, OneDrive & Google Drive.
• Get your resume printed using AirPrinter.
• Works on iPhone, iPad, and iPod.
