Description

Try the MathEdge system FREE with this app!
Practice multiplying two-digit numbers together, and study flash cards from 0-5 at no cost, or purchase the full version for a larger set of numbers and even more of a challenge.

To learn multiplication with a step-by-step guide, there's no other app like MathEdge!

Features:
- Multiplication step-by-step guide
- Multiplication flash cards
- Single number focus: practice your times table for 3's, 5's, or the number of your choice
- Audio instructions
- Caters to both left and right handed children
- Great for classrooms or at home

• Test your child with a set of multiplication flash cards to exercise and improve their math smarts.
• Choose between a right-handed or left-handed layout for ease of use.
• Show your child how to properly arrive at each answer with a guided step-by-step experience, improving their skills and understanding
