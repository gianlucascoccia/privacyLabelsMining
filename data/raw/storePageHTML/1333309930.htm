Description

Welcome to the updated Shop, Play, Win!® MONOPOLY App. With the Shop, Play, Win!® MONOPOLY app, participants may use the app to (1) register for the MONOPOLY online game; (2) enter and track their MONOPOLY online codes; (3) scan game ticket codes they receive; and (4) find Rules & Odds Charts, Winners, Frequently Asked Questions, Prize Submission Forms, Privacy Policies, Customer Service, etc.

The Shop, Play, Win!® MONOPOLY app may be used only in association with the MONOPOLY Collect & Win Game at participating Albertsons Companies, LLC banners (Acme, Albertsons, Albertsons Market, Amigos, CARRS, Jewel-Osco, Lucky – Southern CA only, Market Street, Pak ’n Save, Pavilions, Randalls, Safeway, Shaw’s, Star Market, Super Saver, Tom Thumb, United Express, United Supermarkets, VONS, Haggens).

demcanulty , 03/01/2020

More fun than the paper version

I remember when I moved to the Bay Area my housemate and I played monopoly that year and we thought it was amusing but grew frustrated with the great effort involved in figuring it out and keeping track of the little pieces of paper. So I’ve ignored it for years. But this year I opened some and realized there was an app. I had a little trouble with the login, but I made an account on their website, and then I was able to scan in my tickets, and it was quite painless. I could imagine kids and parents having a lot of fun with it, getting to scan bar codes like a checkout person, which honestly is still like a little magic trick that happens everyday at the supermarket. Thanks for making this app, and I hope you keep working on the bugs, but it has real promise and is now a fun little addition to shopping.
