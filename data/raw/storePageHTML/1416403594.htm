Description

Hello Dating is the fastest growing dating app of 2021!

Join millions of active singles to find love or expand your social network. View other users’ behaviour towards a match; if they misbehave or break any rules, you’ll be the first to know! While Safety Filters are enabled, users who send abusive or unwanted messages will not be tolerated.

Our goal with Hello Dating is to allow everyone the right to feel safe and comfortable, all while keeping the negative users’ behaviour transparent for everyone to see! Up to 60% of our users report finding someone to connect with after only a few hours of signing up and creating a profile. What’s more, up to 95% of users find a potential partner or relationship after only a few days on the Hello Dating app!

** NAUGHTY REPORTS **
Before you chat, you should know how a match behaves before meeting, right? Each user on Hello Dating has a public Naughty Report that displays what and how many rules they’ve broken, such as sending abusive or suggestive messages to a match.

** SAFETY FILTERS **
Hello Dating’s Safety Filters allow you to prevent other users from sending abusive or unwanted messages to you. When it comes to meeting friends, hookups, or a potential partner, it’s best to know what kind of language they use before taking things further, right?

** INCOGNITO MODE **
Privacy is the hot topic when it comes to the world of online dating; that’s why we created Incognito Mode. This essentially hides your profile from anybody on your phone’s contact list on Hello Dating. If you don’t want to match with any friends, family, or singles that you know, or just want to keep your dating life a secret, this feature will do it.

** SUPER MESSAGES **
Stand out by sending a Super Message to your potential love interest before a match. They’ll see this message and decide if you’re somebody they’d like to match with. Sending a Super Message increases the odds of receiving a match by up to 90%.

** PRIORITY LIKES **
Getting noticed can be hard - that’s why Hello Dating has created Priority Likes. When liking someone, your profile will pop up first for them before somebody that hasn’t liked them! This will help you find a match or potential partner dramatically!

** BOOSTS **
Not getting noticed enough in the world of online dating? Don’t worry, we have just the thing! Boost your Hello Dating profile and get seen by everyone close by for the next 30 minutes! This increases your chance of getting a match, message, or date by up to 80%!

** SECRET CRUSHES **
Secret Crushes is the newest way to connect with potential friends, hookups, relationships, and more without having to send a single message. Easily view a list of people that you choose to match, connect, and chat with right away.

What’s New

Feb 17, 2021

Version 4.0.1

We’re pleased to submit our brand new version of Hello Dating.

We have entirely redesigned Hello Dating from the ground up; in addition, we’ve added heaps of features you’ve been asking for and more!

Here are some of the features we’ve built for you:

FREE FEATURES:
* Safety Filters - Safety Filters allow you to select specific kinds of messages to not be received from other users. You can choose Explicit, Sexual, or Personal messages to get blocked by using Safety Filters to cancel out types of language you do not approve of.
* Naughty Reports - The Naughty Report is attached to every user’s public profile. It gives you the opportunity to view how a potential match treats other users, reflected in what they have been reported for while using Hello Dating.
* Incognito Mode - Incognito Mode allows you to prevent matching or being seen by any user that is in your phone’s contact list - this is for privacy concerns, keeping your dating life away from friends, family, and acquaintances.
* Search Filters - Search Filters allow the user to search for specific traits in a partner, such as gender, age, and distance.
* My Favorites - This feature allows you to ‘favorite’ particular matches that stand out from other users.
* Local Matches - Match users with other users based on their geographical location. The distance between users’ locations is set by the user themselves.

PREMIUM FEATURES:
* Priority Likes - Priority Likes allows you to appear first on the list of every person you like on Hello Dating - this helps the user stand out more and increases the chances of creating a match.
* More Swipes - The number of times you can swipe in a day has been increased from XX to 600, giving you more people to potentially match with.
* Super Messages - You can send a pre-emptive message to another user without matching, increasing your chance of making a strong first impression rather than getting swiped left with a Super Message.
* Boosts - Boosts will show your profile to everybody within the specified distance selected in your settings.
* Travel Mode - The Travel Mode allows you to change your geographical location to any location in the world to speak with locals/foreigners in that region - particularly good for meeting people abroad, especially for travelling and holidaying.
* Read Receipts - Read Receipts lets you to see when a user you’re chatting with has read your sent messages - because nobody likes being left on ‘read’.
* Undo Swipe - An Undo Swipe allows the user to go back if they accidentally swipe left to another user in their swipe queue.
* Secret Crushes - This feature allows you to see who has liked your profile, allowing you to instantly match with those users for an instant connection.
* Who’s Viewed Me? - This allows you to see a list of all users that have personally viewed your dating profile.
* Reset Swipes - Resetting your swipes undoes all the swipe lefts and swipe rights you’ve used in your profile’s lifetime - this can help you find new matches if you decide to do things differently with your profile.

ADDITIONAL NOTES:
* LGBTQIA+ Inclusivity - Hello Dating is a platform built on and dedicated to freedom of speech and expression. We support peoples of all colour, race, religion, culture, and gender expression and identity. Users can choose to identify as 50+ different genders to fit their preferred gender identity.
* Free & Unlimited Messaging - Sending more than two messages to matches no longer requires a 12-hour time restriction in-between sending messages. Users can now send as many messages as they please.
