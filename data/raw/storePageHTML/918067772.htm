Description

Design your dream interior with Webby Award Winner Amikasa. Recreate and style your room using furniture and decor from real brands. Then use augmented reality to see how furniture really looks in your room before you buy.

AWARD WINNING APP

Love the design....but needs some work

Although I’m new to home design, I gotta say this app is pleasing to the eye however as a new designer I’d like to have the possibility to explore my many ideas and see them come to life but this app is more or less limited. Being an open minded person I stand to be corrected. Ex...I tried to create a sunken living room and the possibility doesn’t exist. I also tried to create a raised kitchen that was possible but I couldn’t furnish it because I couldn’t elevate the appliances and counters above floor level. These are just some amongst the many features I think should be added. The user should have the possibility to create. Nevertheless, I still love the app but make love it even more. Get creative.

LiL6186 , 05/31/2017

Truly fantastic app!

I LOVE this app!
First of all, it's FREE. Totally and completely free - no ads, no push to have an account where they could send tons of junk, no "limited" access. It's full access for all!
I am by no means an interior designer, but this app has allowed me to visualize my whole house and make decisions on what to buy/where to place items. It's a life saver!
The app is pretty easy to use. It's different from other apps in that it has a very realistic feel by using 3D and augmented reality. I actually feel like I'm walking through my own house!
There is a good selection of furniture/decor options. Obviously there could always be more, but there is enough to find something that will work.

Mrs. Mami Chula , 04/23/2017

Great start, but needs improvement!

This app is the easiest to use and most intuitive of all the other room/home designers out there, but here is what it is lacking that is stopping me from using it more:
1) Lack of size/scaling of objects!! This is a MUST! I can't scale most items, so the room is all off. If you want me to even consider buying the products you offer, then at the very least let me make the room look somewhat familiar to my own!
2) Lack of generic objects. I understand you're trying to sell your specific/sponsored items, but at least let us add what we Already have that isn't from your vendors so that we can feel comfortable seeing what we might be missing!
3) Being able to then color those generic objects.

Thank you for such a great app and keep up the work! Will only recommend/continue using the app if you change these things. Also, will increase rating too. Thanks!
