Description

Color by number Oil Painting - your new favorite coloring book for adults. 1000+ free images from the very beginning of the game. Every day – new free pictures.

13 categories of pictures: Holidays, Food, Still Life, Vehicle, Children, Flowers, Butterflies, Animals, Marine, Travel, Colorful, Landscapes, Birds.

Two features to make the process of coloring easier:
• Next shape for the color – the game finds an uncolored shape for the current color.
• Fill the shapes with the same color – the game finds all the shapes with the same color and fills them automatically.

Share colored images on social media!

My fave coloring app until...

Update: The developers replied and said I should update the app, but I had already updated to the latest version when I wrote my review. All my completed images are still gone. Especially don’t like that the colors don’t advance once you finish one, but it’s obvious they want you tapping in the color area more often because the ad is right there. More tapping means more errant taps on the ads. Still like the end results, but the app is not as enjoyable as it was and I’m using it less.

This is my favorite coloring app. Just challenging enough to take a while, but not too hard, and a pretty pic at the end. (Although I never liked the obnoxious logo obscuring part of the picture when saving.) But the last update had the app constantly crashing, so I reinstalled and, like others, discovered all my completed pictures lost. Looking through the gallery, some that I had completed before are now premium, some are totally gone. Also, the find and fill hints dropped to 1 each. Hardly ever used those, so whatever. Honestly though, updates that take things away are just frustrating. The premium price is much too high and there are a couple of ads that give you a “close” button but nothing happens when you press it. Still my fave for the end result picture, but disappointed with recent changes. (Also, please stop the pop-up hints! No one needs those after the first two minutes.)
