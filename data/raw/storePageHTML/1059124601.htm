Description

Let's face it. There's a lot of loan calculators out there. Why choose this one? Well it's easy to use, the font is fairly large and it's got lots of colors. If you're still interested here's more:

• Early payoff calculations.
• Break down of payments for each period (amortization).
• Compare multiple loans and payment schedules.
• Easy export to HTML and CSV for view on a computer.
• Portrait and landscape mode.
• Currency based on the region.
• Graphical payment representation.
• 6 bonus calculators *NEW*
• Apple Watch simple loan calc *NEW*
• Did I mention it has lots of colors? =)

Thanks for reading this far, I hope you give this calculator a try.

Great tool at your disposal

It is great for a couple reasons. First, it is always good to be able to have an idea of how much you need to borrow, what the monthly payments would be, and how it changes based on varying the down payment amount. Perfect for what I need

Fastest way to approximate a loan payment and has the nice feature of showing how additional payments will affect the remaining term.

It was within pennies of my car loan payment. My HP calculator used to be off by a few cents too.

WordBossMe , 04/24/2019

Deleted app due to excessive ads

I could see how this could be useful but honestly there are a significant amount of ads. I understand ads maybe necessary and for that I typically am okay with tolerating them.

However, I have to watch an ad everytime I want to save a new loan so as a new user of the app if I want to put in all 15 of my student loans in at once I have to watch 5 mins of just ads (each ad is 20 secs). It is either this or enter in the same numbers 15 times every month when I want decide how much to overpay my loans on their due date which may change month to month...maybe I am missing something.

Unfortunately, I deleted the app for this reason.
