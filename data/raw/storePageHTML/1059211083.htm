Description

The easiest way to safely store and organize all of your recipes! The OrganizEat Recipe organizer eliminates the need for stacks of cookbooks, long lists of saved bookmarks, notes, or pictures/screenshots scattered across your phone’s photo gallery. Quickly turn your mess of recipes into an organized and easily retrievable collection. You simply snap a photo of your recipe - whether it’s in a book, a magazine, or on the web - and it’s instantly cataloged and searchable. Whether storing Grandma’s secret recipes for posterity, creating your own custom recipes, or importing new recipes from the web, you can do it all quickly and easily - right from your iPhone. Categorize your recipes and adds tags for easy organization and simple searching. Share your recipes with friends and family via email, text messages, or social media. Finally, you can have all of your recipes with you, all in one place, wherever you go!

App Features:

• Three easy ways to capture your recipes (Photos, Web, or Custom)
• Snap ‘n Store: Snap a photo of your recipe and store it in one easy step. No typing necessary!
• Web Import: See a recipe you like on the web? Import it in one tap from Delish, FoodNetwork, AllRecipes, and more than 200 other recipe sites.
• Custom Entries: Type or copy and paste your recipe directly into the app. Sometimes simple is the best way to go.
• Backup & Sync. Backup your recipes to the cloud and sync across all your devices.
• One App. The same one app across all iPhone and iPad devices.
• Grocery List. Create a shopping list by adding ingredients with a single tap!
• Meal Planner. Plan your meals for the entire month and generate a shopping list.
• Folders. Organize recipes in fully customizable category folders.
• Tags. Add as many tags as you like to each recipe.
• Quick Search. Easily find recipes! Search by keywords or tags (one or more!).
• Recipe Inspiration. Need a new idea? See a daily list of recipes imported from the web by other OrganizEat users.
• Share the Yum. One-tap sharing so you can quickly send recipes to friends and family, or email it to them directly - all from within the app.
• Cooking Mode. Presents your recipe in full screen and prevents your device’s screen from going black. No more struggling with greasy fingers on screens!
• View your recipes full screen while cooking - and the screen will not turn off - so there is no need to touch the screen with greasy hands!
• Beautiful Interface. Choose from 8 “skins” designed to cozily house your precious recipes.

If you like to cook and collect recipes get this app

When you are serious about cooking, you collect plenty of recipes from many sources. OrganizEat is a well thought-out tool that let us get our recipes organized and it gets used everyday at our house. We had hundreds of printed recipes that we scanned and uploaded into the program over a few hours. This is sort of a brute force approach, but we had computer print outs, hand written notes, index cards and random scraps of paper that we are now able to find quickly. There are better ways to get recipes into OrganizEat that we also use, you can import recipes directly from many sites like Serious Eats, Bon Appétit, New Your Times and others. Other sites that are not directly supported by the app can be used by snapping pictures within the app. Of course you can also type your recipes and add photos too. The developers are working to add other ways to import and additional features to the app, I had contacted them with a question and they were very responsive and open to future improvements. This is not the cheapest app out there, but you get what you are paying for.

Developer Response ,

Hi, and thank you for this warm feedback, and the suggestions for improvement! I am so glad to hear that you love the app :) About your request for additional photos: the reason we limit the number of photos is storage space. We are offering a cloud backup and sync service as part of the app, and additional photos storage has substantial cost for us. Therefore, the Extra Photos are available as an additional premium feature in the app (which is not part of the regular "Full Version" upgrade that covers 3 photos per recipe.) It's available in the app's Settings -> Premium Features. The small package allows adding 2 more photos per recipe (making it a total of 5), and the large package allows adding 5 more photos per recipe (making it a total of 8). Also, we would like to use the opportunity and explain our pricing policy better here. The Full Version app's price is not $25, it is $5.99/year :-) The reason we charge annual subscription is storage costs. We offer a sync & backup account for the recipes, as part of the app. The recipes are mostly photos (or screenshots), and long term storage of so many high resolution images, has substantial costs for us. This is why we must charge for it repeatedly every year. However, since we received many emails from people who strongly dislike subscription: we decided to give it a try and release a fully pre-paid alternative as well. It's price was set to be equal to 4-5 years of subscription, this is our current initial estimate for covering the average image hosting costs of a user - and this is price that you are mentioning in your review. This relatively "high" price, is actually for many years of the subscription in adnvance. But here is no need really to purchase the whole 5 years of in advance. You can always pay the yearly packeage that costs 5 time less than the lifetime price you quoted, without any long term commitments. If you don't like the app: you cancel your subscription, and you will not be charged anything more.
About the additional websites: we add more and more websites to our supported list all the time. When we first started we had only 30 websites that we could support, now it's almost 200. We are gathering users request for more websites in this form: http://bit.ly/2TlZpJF
I hope this helps, and if you have more questions then please write us to "support@organizeat.com" and we'll be happy to assist! (by Rachel)
