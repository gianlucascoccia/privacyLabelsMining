Description

Welcome to My Home, a place that you can make your dream come true! Play this fun addictive match 3 game and make your dream home-design FOR FREE! From a condo to a mansion or even a villa, you can design and decorate everything on your device by your fingertips!

Enjoy the game that fuses colorful matching puzzle levels with a home you restore, makeover and decorate. Many free styles and furniture combinations await you in-game! Don't forget to stop by your landlord or best friend's house to catch up and talk about your dream! Come and download to design your home for free now!

Game features:

* House Design: Open-ended addictive puzzle gameplay. Various styles at your disposal! Makeover and restore your houses all by yourself!

* Exciting match 3 levels: Hundreds of challenging match-3 levels with many awesome boosters. More matching challenging means more fun!

* Personalized storyline: All stories focus on YOU! Follow along with the adventure while designing your dream homes!

* Vivid characters: Fun and unique characters! Your best friend, your sweet landlord and many more surprises!

* Multiple houses: Not satisfied with designing and restoring only one house? We have plenty of houses for you to design and decorate!

Challenge the match 3 puzzle levels Now! Time to show your talent for home-design in-game! Take the reins and bring your dream house to life now for free!

Best Decorating Game Ever!

Only day 2 of playing this and I’m in love! Yeah, the boosters are needed a lot but I don’t see why this game gets bad reviews on it. You should just think about everything good about the game. All the bad reviews are on boosters and honestly the game is fine and it gives you hints too! I don’t see what the deal is with the boosters. But the design of the game is adorable and every house looks different. You get to know all the characters along the way but at first, you just don’t think much about them.

Something I wish the game had is character customization. At first I was like “Where is the part where I customize my character?”. But I’m fine with Dorah and her love for designing! Also, I wish there was more customization options for your house. To explain what I mean, when you click on something to change it, maybe it shows a list of everything you could get. Because when I started I was like “Okay so I got the light blue couch but nothing is feeling the light blue couch when I’m trying to buy the arm chair. (But I fixed it up and my house looks better.)

In summary, the game is super fun! The challenges are easy and the booster madness needs to calm down.

jenn865 , 11/27/2018

Impossible to play without spending money

I really liked this game at first but now it’s just frustrating. The game is really designed so that in order to pass levels and continue playing, you HAVE to spend real money. In the beginning, the levels are relatively easy and the renovations in your home are pretty cheap; if you pass one or maybe 2 levels, you can afford to improve something in your house at the beginning. However, as you progress, renovations become increasingly expensive. Now I must pass about 6 levels in order to renovate just 1 thing. And this can take a week, as the levels are so difficult that it can take days to pass. The only way to pass levels is to spend money to buy boosters. Not only that, many renovation items in the game can only be bought using real money. I really enjoyed this game at first but now it’s to the point where it is no longer fun because of how impossible it is. I understand apps wanting to make money, but the amount of money this app would require you to spend in order to keep playing is just ridiculous. You would have to spend $5-$10 just to update a single item in your home, it’s crazy. And games should not be designed so that playing it is only enjoyable if you’re willing to spend a ton of money. Last complaint is the amount of ads this game has!!!! So many ads! I really enjoyed this game but now it’s just frustrating. I really don’t recommend downloading this unless you want to be frustrated.

Paigie waigey , 12/21/2020

I really do have ups and downs for this game...

OMG! This game is such a fun thing to get into! It is so fun to play, and the styles for the houses are so cute! This game has super smooth animation, and I have not ever experienced any technical delays, such as bugs, viruses, or any WiFi issues. This game is super fun if you are into designing and decorating structures with your own creativity! This game allows you to design each room and have your own house! It is also fun to follow along with the story! I like that for each item of design or furniture, you only have three options to keep it simple. In my mind, this is necessary, and helpful, because with too many options , I can get stressed out and my designing does not look as well.
There are many positives to this game, however I have found one important issue and problem for me.
As I started out playing this game, I had a lot of fun completing the games. They were nice and easy, and enjoyable but as I moved on in this app and into more rooms, I noticed that the games became way more harder. Too hard. I understand that games are supposed to have an amount of difficulty the more you progress, but I can not seem to accomplish anything anymore. I don’t if anyone else is having this issue. I really don’t know, I just might really be bad at this!!
That is my only complaint, otherwise I love this game!
