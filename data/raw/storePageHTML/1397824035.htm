DRRosen3 , 01/31/2020

Money Grabber

The game was fun at first. The first restaurant you run is a breakfast joint. I’ve been great at cooking games for YEARS, so needless to say I raised a brow each time I failed by a hair and it asked to spend gems to keep going. I shrugged it off and spent the gems since they’re the ones they give you in the beginning. However, the cat came out of the bag in the second restaurant, the burger joint. I went into the restaurant with a lot of spare money so I upgraded early. On level 15 I continuously failed, however. I went back to the breakfast place and farmed enough coin to completely max our all of the burger places upgrades and still kept failing level 15. That’s when I noticed the scheme. The level requires you to serve 23 dishes (get the food out before the guest leaves). It doesn’t matter if the guest gets angry just as long as you serve the food before they leave. That’s when I noticed it...they only give you enough guests to order 21 dishes. Doesn’t matter how good you are you can’t win. Not without spending the gems to get more customers...and of course...gems cost real world money. If you just want to enjoy one day of this game go ahead and waste your time on it. Otherwise it’s going to cost you money to advance to higher levels. Unless you’re okay with that, DON’T BOTHER WITH THIS MONEY GRABBER!
