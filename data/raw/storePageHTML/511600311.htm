10K Trainer is the easiest program to get beginners from couch potatoes to 10K distance runners in just 14 weeks. By the same experts who created the #1 C25K® (Couch to 5K) training app!

Don’t worry, if you are already a 5K graduate, you can skip ahead and pick up where C25K® app leaves off!

This proven 14 week plan will allow users to slowly build strength and stamina by alternating between walking and running. The blend of walking and running together reduces the overall impact and risk of injury, keeps your body guessing, and prepares you to go the distance. By the end of the program, you will be stronger and healthier than you have ever been. You'll be a 10K runner!

Great app. Very easy to use, and almost identical to the C25K app. After I completed the C25K running app, it gave me the option to download this 10K trainer, the next app in the series. Once I did, instead of starting me at the beginning, the program started me at week 6, because it recognized I was already capable of the 5K. That was a great feature...it's a huge perk how all these training apps are from the same company, so they're all connected and easy to use. It still gives you the "one minutes remaining" audible announcement, which is handy. I like all the voice notifications (when to begin running, stop running, and when I'm half-way) because then I can just go and listen to music, without having to look at my phone. Thanks for the easy-to-use, beginning runner-friendly app! Combined with healthy eating (and minimal extra sugar and bread), I am in the best shape I've been in, in 5 years.

Nay1012 , 05/17/2019

This app changed my life.

I have always felt like a runner in my head, but once my shoes hit the pavement it was proven otherwise. I would be winded after 1 minute of running and wouldn’t try again for a long time. I downloaded the C25K app August 2018 and really struggled the first two weeks. Fortunately the reminders and breaks in between running is what kept me pushing through the program. I was running 30 minutes non stop and enjoying the program in no time (2 months later). I took some time off because I had a hard time running in the winter time, but when spring came around I knew I was ready to jump back in. Naturally, I downloaded the 10K app. If you have completed the 5k program, it will start you off at week 9 which was great because I needed to ease back into running. Honestly, this is the best program. I feel like a runner, I have more stamina, I have lost 30lbs, and it’s all because of this app.

SKirtonBear2 , 08/09/2018

My audible Coach

I’ve been using this for a few years and it is better than ever with or without an Apple Watch.

The watch app is now easy to see with a large logo that pops up to let me decide if I need to read what I already heard in my ears (sometimes I’m distracted and missed the verbal cue), but the haptic buzz helps.

I’ve had to restart my training after two months off and 10K let me choose where to start. I backed up to several weeks earlier very easily.

I use RockMyRun’s music, and 10K’s verbal instructions weren’t lost- worked great together though I had to choose “None” in the app for the music.

I have used the app to play my own music and that works great, too.

You can set the screen to dark or light backgrounds depending on what you need.

The update makes the music and light controls easier to access and it has always given a short, and useful description of the time breakdown for warmups, walking, jogging, and cool down.

I recommend this app for getting your body ready to jog a 10K. It focuses on time spent, not actual distance. By the end of the training you can do a full 10K. It gets you there. Afterwards, you’ll need to work on upping your pace.
