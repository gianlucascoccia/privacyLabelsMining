Description

The Fitness app is here to guide you through your fitness journey, have you enjoy working out and help you become the best version of yourself. Getting fit and gaining muscles has never been so easy! Work on any body part and stick to your fitness goals by choosing exercises from the vast library with descriptions and videos to avoid injuries.

WORKOUT VARIETY
Stay motivated and don’t get bored with the variety of workout programs. Switch it up with specially created strength training programs and HIIT workouts to reach your fitness goals faster.


• Strength training to work out different muscle groups.
• High-intensity interval training (HIIT).

EXPERT GUIDANCE
Our team is made up of world-class sports professionals in bodybuilding or powerlifting. They designed all exercises and programs for all levels to take the guesswork out of your fitness routine and lead you through to the specific results:


• Workouts for building biceps, getting wide back, shoulders, chest, strong legs, and more.
• Library of exercises for men for different muscle groups.


COMPLEMENT YOUR ROUTINE
The app has a useful set of features to make your Fitness journey easy and fun:


• Personal coaching: voice guidance in HIIT workouts lets you stay focused to ensure that you perform exercises correctly.
• Create your own exercises and add descriptions, notes, and images.
• Create a personal training plan using the large library of exercises for different muscle groups.
• Enter weights, sets, and reps in strength workouts to keep the statistics updated.
• Use a photo diary to track your progress.
• Enter your body measurements to display the dynamics of any changes in reports.
