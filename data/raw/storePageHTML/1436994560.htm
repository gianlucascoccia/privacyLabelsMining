Description

Imagine being able to transport yourself to some of the most peaceful and awe-inspiring places on the planet, whenever you need to focus, sleep or unwind.

That’s the experience we’ve set out to create with Portal, combining immersive 3D sounds, ultra-high definition motion visuals and Philips Hue integration to create an ambient experience unlike any other.

* Wake up to the dawn chorus at Lake Bled, Slovenia
* Maintain your focus and flow surrounded by the rumble of an Amazon thunderstorm
* Meditate beside the Pacific Ocean in Hawaii
* Draw inspiration from high up in the Himalayan mountains of Nepal
* Unwind to the sound of lapping waves and the sea breeze rustling the palm leaves overhead on Thailand’s Laguna Beach
* Drift off in the shadow of Mount Fuji to the tranquillity of Japan’s Lake Kawaguchi,

Bedtime Stories Told By Nature.

No voices – just the sounds of nature to help you unwind and drift off around the world. Portal’s sleep timer allows you to put down your device for the night and drift off without a care in the world.

Here Comes The Sun.

Love being gently woken by the sounds of nature and sunrise’s warm glow. Portal’s wakeup alarm with its Philips Hue integration combine for the perfect start to any day.

Truly Remote Working.

Work productively from anywhere by replacing noise and distraction with nature. Portal’s focus timer (a simplified pomodoro timer) will help you stay in flow throughout your day.

Think Somewhere Different.

Escape the confines of your four walls and be inspired by a growing library of over 44 different beautiful locations from around the world. In the words of J.R.R. Tolkien, “not all those who wander are lost”.

Discover A Haven For Your Mind.

Escape to your own quiet corner of the world to relax, meditate and unwind. Portal’s breathing exercises help you control stress, lower blood pressure and reduce anxiety in even the craziest of times.

Perfect Harmony.

Reach for the stars…or your favourite audiobook, podcast, playlist or guided meditation wherever you portal. Use Portal alongside other apps, to combine nature’s ambience with what you love.

Just Say The Word.

If you’re all tapped out, Portal’s deep integration with Siri Shortcuts means your next portal is only a word or two away.

Absolute Privacy.

We’re committed to delivering experiences which are liberating and make you feel truly immersed and connected with nature. Peace of mind that there’s no third-party tracking, no targeted advertising and that we don’t collect your personal data is crucial. It’s just you and nature – and we’re proud of it.

Freedom.

We’re a small completely independent team of creators. For us, it means we can take a stance on things we and our customers feel deeply passionate about and in doing so continue to build something that we’re truly proud of.

QuantumElectron , 08/22/2020

Incredible for Sleep and Getting Work Done

This app was a game changer for me. I’ve always struggled to go to sleep. I have tried music, sleeping masks, white noise, muscle relaxation, etc. This app was really helpful in transporting me to beautiful, serene locations, putting my mind at ease, and finally allowing me to sink into the depths of unconsciousness at night, It’s soundscapes are beautifully realized visually, with varied, 3D immersive sound. I discovered that some of the soundscapes, especially the barley field and the cloud rainforest, were great aids to helping me write and work without getting distracted. Overall, the app has been a great improvement to my quality of life, which I did not expect initially.

I initially got the free version and was really satisfied with the included features, so I upgraded. I have to say the monetization model was really wonderful, encouraging me to support developers without making me feel like I had to pay for a good experience. Recently the app was updated with tons of new sounds from Scotland and Slovenia. It now includes a subscription model, but for the quantity and quality of content, it seems well worth it, especially because it will help the developers flesh out the app even more. On top of that, they gave everyone who had purchased the app free access to everything, which is incredibly generous! I look forward to great new content in the future. Thank you so much!
