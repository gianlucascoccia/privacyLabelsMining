Description

Painting is a way to relax. Color Number is a stress-free drawing art game for everyone. It is also a coloring book. All pictures are digitally marked. Color and recolor your favorite photos. Rediscover the joy of simple relaxation and coloring. When your coloring is complete, you can produce an animated video to resume the coloring process and share it with friends.


### Game Features
-Color with numbers everywhere-No pencil, paper required
-Draw beautiful flowers, animals, mandalas, plants, people, landscapes, cartoons, etc.
-Easy to operate, just tap it. Open and close with two fingers to zoom in and out.
-Updated daily, new paintings are waiting for you to discover every day.
-Share your work and display your fine art.
-Draw colorful pictures, relax and feel positive emotions


Drawing pictures with this picture book will help you release stress and be happy!
