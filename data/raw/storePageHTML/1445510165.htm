Description

Over 600 healthy, plant-based recipes, meal planner, shopping lists and step-by-step images to make cooking easier. You’ll also find a wide selection of exercise content so you can get moving at home, plus guided meditations for a moment of mindfulness.

Our newest feature is My Tracker: your wellness tool serving up personalised weekly wellness plans based on the 5 Pillars that anchor Deliciously Ella and everything we've learnt from the past 8 years of exploring plant-based living. Our founder Ella builds the 5 Pillars of plant-based food, exercise, mindfulness, sleep and hydration, into her every week and together they have had a huge impact on her own physical and mental health. My Tracker is also fully integrated with Apple Health.

The Deliciously Ella app is easy to use and updated weekly with inspirational new recipes, videos and content, helping you feel better both mentally and physically.

- 600 healthy, easy to make, plant-based recipes
- Over 200 yoga, meditation and exercise videos ranging from 10-40 minutes and from beginner to advanced
- 30 recipe videos, showing you exactly how to make the recipes
- Step-by-step images of a recipe breaking down what each step should look like
- Personalised wellness plans and daily goal tracking with My Tracker (Works with Apple Health)
- Meal-planning feature
- Shopping list feature
- In-app podcast on health and wellness
- Advanced search functionality
- Updated weekly with new content
- Ability to change between metric and imperial measurements
- Favourite button to save your go-to recipes

*Please note our video content uses Vimeo which means users in Indonesia and China will not be able to access this.
