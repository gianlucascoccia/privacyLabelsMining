Description

FATUMSCOPE is a multi-feature astrology app helping you to understand your past, make a decision in the present and gain insight into your future events

Our application consists of:

HOROSCOPE

Zodiac horoscope reading helps you discover what stars have in store for you. Our astrology app creates a horoscope for today, tomorrow and the whole year for all the 12 zodiac signs. Want to get your horoscope today? Based on the location of the sun, stars and planets, your personal astrological assistant is ready to make accurate future predictions for your zodiac sign.

OMEN TAROT PREDICTION

All-embracing 6 Card Tarot Spread from the Major Arcana
Collect one of the 6 Tarot cards every day to put them together and gain insight into how things are in your life at the moment.

TAROT

Tarot cards reading is a powerful form of divination using an ancient deck of cards which helps you to find answers to your most important questions about health, love and relationships, career and finances and many more.
Psychics and fortune tellers have used Tarot cards for hundreds of years considering this divination to be among the most accurate ones. Every card has a different meaning depending on its position, so you will get a unique personalized and detailed perspective on your current situation.
Just pick and click!


MAGIC BALL

Still can't make up your mind? Hesitate whether to do or not? No clue about an important life issue? Our Magic Ball will provide you with a prompt and easy answer whenever you need it. Just shake and watch!

LOVE COMPATIBILITY
Find out if you and your sweetheart or partner are true soul mates, good friends or just a time bomb.
But have no fear, even opposites attract! Let’s see your chances!


Learn all about your past, present and future with the help of FATUMSCOPE!
