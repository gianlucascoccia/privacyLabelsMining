Description

The MySubaru app makes owning your Subaru easy by connecting you to your vehicle with features like remote start*, remote unlock/lock, service scheduler, digital service history, vehicle locator, and 24-hour roadside assistance.

Whether it's starting your vehicle from the comfort of your home, scheduling a service appointment, or locating your vehicle, the MySubaru app puts the power of your Subaru at your fingertips.

Features:
- Remote Start*
- Remote Lock, Unlock, and Vehicle Locator**
- 24-Hour Roadside Assistance***
- Schedule Service Appointments
- Track Your Vehicle’s Service History
- Access Owner’s Manuals and How-To Videos
- Find Nearby Subaru Service Centers and Subaru Retailers
- Contact Subaru Customer Support
- Get Vehicle Service Reminders and recall notices
- Real-time Vehicle Health Notifications**
- Plan and save trips, then send directions to your STARLINK navigation system**
- Access STARLINK Safety and Security features**
- Remote Climate Control settings from your smartphone**
- Remote Horn and Lights
- Automatic Collision Notifications**
- Exclusive special offers and coupons
- Manage the drivers in your household with Boundary, Speed, and Curfew Alerts****
- Touch and Face ID for Remote Services

*Remote Engine Start available on keyless access with push button start vehicles. Remote Engine Start with Climate Control available on vehicles with keyless access with push button start and automatic climate control. Not available on vehicles with manual transmission.
**Subaru owners must have an active STARLINK subscription to access remote services.
***24-hour roadside is included in standard 3-year warranty and after 3 years with a gold package.
****Available on select 2019 models and later.

Good, not great

The app offers some good features, but is not as comprehensive as other vehicle companion apps I have used. That being said, the subscription fee for the remote features in this app is lower than the other vehicle companion apps I have used. The remote start and destinations have worked fine for me thus far but, like others, I would also like more options for the remote start duration. I would also like if the status of the cars locks were easily visible or, even better, that the app would send you a notification that your car is unlocked if it remains in that state for a certain amount of time. The tire pressure data has never populated for me, but that’s not a huge deal. Overall, a decent app but with definite opportunities for improvement.

MrsStrendin , 02/12/2021

Good Luck if you live where it’s freezing!

I live in Minnesota and it is imperative that my autostart actually work when it is below zero degrees out. Several times I have gone to open the app to start my vehicle only to receive an error message that tells me the app is unavailable and to try again. Unacceptable when it is FREEZING! Not only that, why can’t you make it so we can adjust the time to either run for a full 20 minutes or longer? Why limit it to a 5 of 10 minute run time? That is pretty much pointless. Had I known this I would have purchased an after market autostart and NOT this one.

Seems may eventually work but...not now.

2 stars because the app looks ok and because I love our Subarus but really should be 1 for lack of functionality.

First, when asked for my VIN to find my account, app would absolutely not allow me to paste my VIN from my note keeping app. So I had to switch back and forth to type 17 digits into the lookup tool. And, no, “security” is not a reason, it’s just bad UI design, period.

Oh, not only can you not paste into the app, you can’t copy from the app, as in I tried to copy a dealership address to look up driving directions and, yeah, app would absolutely not allow me to copy the address. Just bad.

So, I finally pressed the “call“ button and the app connected me to the voicemail of the dealership warranty manager. Really?

I sure hope I can get my service done though. I left a message, I think they’ll call back. I’ll let you know.
