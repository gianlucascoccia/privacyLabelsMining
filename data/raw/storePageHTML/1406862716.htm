Description

Anyone can create a video game with Bloxels.

CREATE CHARACTERS Build your own heroes and baddies- and give them superpowers in Character Lab.


MAKE ART and ANIMATIONS Create pixel art and animations to bring your worlds to life.

BUILD AND SHARE GAMES Build and configure everything about your games, tell stories, craft puzzles, and share it with the world. No code required.

REMIX Use themed asset packs in your own games, including pirates, ninjas, pigeons and everything in between.

PLAY Bloxels games for free. Purchase a Bloxels account to start building and publishing your own games. A Bloxels account code is included in the Official Bloxels Build Your Own Video Game Kit. For more information go to playbloxels.com.

Bloxels & Education: Are you an educator? Bloxels EDU plans include added features and resources specifically for education. Educator plans include access to the EDU Hub, where you can see and showcase student work, and access resources, including standards-aligned activities across subjects and grade levels K-12. Learn more at edu.bloxelsbuilder.com

What’s New

Mar 6, 2021

Version 2.4.0

New Feature Unlocked: Keys and Doors

We've been hinting for a bit, and now it's official: Keys and Doors are here. It's just like it sounds: you're now able to make collectibles (keys) that have the power to remove other blocks (doors.)

But not all keys are shiny, and doors don't have to have hinges or handles. We're expecting to see all kinds of new takes on what makes a "door" and a "key."

Also in this update:

- Fixed a weather FX bug in Home Screen Featured Games
- 3 new music tracks

Natamarsh , 10/28/2020

Updates please respond

The first thing I think would make Bloxels better is adding attacks like a slice attack which would damage an enemy in the block in front. Then you could have a long range attack like when you fire an arrow or something and if a enemy gets in its way, they are damaged. Second, it would be more challenging to have a boss option. For example, if the hero is within a ten block radius, the boss turns and starts attacking. It would be nice to have an option that allows you to kill the boss and end the level. Third, it would be nice to make art that is more than one block. That way if you are trying to make a moving platform within twenty blocks, you can make your levels more interesting and more fun.

MonkeyMaster31 , 12/29/2020

Very fun, some update suggestions

Bloxels is very fun! I like making pixel art (even if I don’t use it), and making levels is very fun! Just so you know, I’m MnkyMstr31 (MonkeyMaster31), a creator of Bloxel’s Awesome Glitches. I do have some update suggestions, like a hitbox editor where you can make the hitbox fit the character (I don’t like how the hitbox is so much bigger than it needs to be), also since the devs made weather and song switches, a switch that when you hit creates or destroys a block. Also PLEASE make the enemy fireballs and electric shots disappear after they hit you once! It’s so annoying when they hit you twice! And one last thing: if you search “Mnky” you get all of our levels. There’s car simulators, 3D levels, and a level about Bloxel’s glitches (I still think the devs should keep the glitches! They make the game more fun!). You should get Bloxels. It’s very fun! Lastly, no one could find our levels on the Infinity Wall on Bloxels Builder (old Bloxels), but we found a way to get people to play our levels on the arcade! I think the arcade is WAY better than the I-Wall!

superultraboomnerd , 10/19/2020

This is why it costs so much

LISTEN UP YALL, STOP COMPLAINING BOUT HOW MUCH IT COST TO MAKE A ACCOUNT AND I WILL TELL YOU WHY IT COST $20. So I have seen that there is a lot of complaints about how much it costs to make a account but the creators are just trying to make it fair, here is why. So back in 2016 it was free for people who did not have the Bloxels board set, which was unfair to the people who paid $20 to buy the set. So they are just making it fair so the people that bought the set are not getting ripped off. I think this is a very fun game but it would be cool if you could customize a bit more things, like projectiles, winning messages, and start menus. Over all Bloxels is a great game to spark kids creativity. Also you can have hours of fun. If you are a parent and are concerned that people will post inappropriate games don’t worry, the game has moderates that check the games before they are posted to make sure they are kid friendly.

Developer Response ,

If your games are not showing up after being published, they are likely being moderated. If they do not show up after an extended period, it is likely the games were denied because they contained inappropriate or offensive language or content. Once they are approved by a moderator, you will receive a message and they will appear in the "My Games" tab. You can edit and update your games to republish if they are not approved.
