Very real support for making success a daily reality

This app has been surprisingly effective in helping me not only plan my day but understand the motivation behind my goals. It reminds you to get things done and even gently asks if you have time so that you don’t feel pressured. I particularly enjoy this function of the ‘nudging.’ It’s as if I have a personal coach there for me at all times. I am a psychologist myself, so I tend to easily see through a lot of similar techniques which make it hard for me to respect them. However, this app was so well developed that it’s not insulting or critical. The developers thought it through so well and I hope it is not only successful but that a lot of people will use it. You will NOT be disappointed unless you’re not truly serious about achieving goals and only want to ‘pretend’ success. Thank you for such a wonderfully thought-out program I can trust. Dr. Gregg A. Pizzi, Miami, Florida USA

Roznation , 11/04/2018

Success wiz rocks!

I’m loving success wiz. I’ve only been using it for a couple weeks, but it helps me to identify the 5 or 6 main items to get done each day so I can focus on these. Also, it’s easy to reschedule these items by swiping left or marking them complete by swiping right.

Also like the morning checking for preparing for the day, but even more the night check in for reviewing how the day went and planning for tomorrow. So right when I wake up I know what the most important tasks to complete are.

I am having some trouble with the habit feature though. I try to set an activity for Monday at 6 pm and it always pops up on Sunday, not sure why this is, must be a bug. Other than that this is a really helpful tool, considering upgrading, thanks for developing this app!
