Description

Saucey is alcohol delivery at the push of a button. No delivery fees. No order minimums. We'll deliver beer, wine, liquor, mixers, ice and party supplies in under 30mins. So why use Saucey? Picture this:

It's Tuesday at 5:59pm. You think about it, and you realize you went a whole workday without watching that Netflix show. You'd better get home soon. Going to the store takes too much time and you need your wine to meet you at home. Order now, get home... we'll press play together without a second to spare.

It's Friday. Time to go make some stories for Monday. However, you've also had a long week. You are ready to rage - as long as it doesn't involve a loud club or $12 vodka sodas. You hit the group text, offer up your place for a night of What Do You Meme? and then have Saucey deliver the Tito's, Club Soda, and enough limes for drinks tonight and Bloody Marys tomorrow. #NightSolved

You wake up on a Sunday morning ready for mimosas but not for that long line at the brunch place down the street, so you say “Hey Siri, it’s brunch time”. That’s right, max laziness reached. Use Siri to place your order and in less than 30 minutes, you're brunching in bed, like a boss.

Get the picture yet?

--HOW IT WORKS--
Saucey is the fastest, easiest and most reliable way to order beer, wine and spirits. Simply pick what you want, checkout, and your Saucey courier will be there in 30 minutes - it's that easy!

--DELIVERY ANYWHERE--
We’ll deliver to your apartment, house, office, the beach, the park, the birthday party, even your wedding reception (yes, we have done weddings!)—wherever you need us.

--30 MINUTE DELIVERY IN MANY U.S. CITIES--
Los Angeles, San Francisco, Brooklyn, Dallas, Oakland, Sacramento, San Diego, & Chicago. More locations coming soon!

--NO DELIVERY FEES--
Honestly, we hate them as much as you do. That's why we have free delivery on 30-min orders.

--NO ORDER MINIMUMS--
Sometimes you just want a six pack of beer or bottle of wine, we get it. We won't peer pressure you, that's a party foul.

--REAL-TIME TRACKING--
Keep tabs on your delivery progress on a live map. Track your courier walking up to your door!

--FASTEST ALCOHOL DELIVERY IN TOWN--
Our team works tirelessly to make sure an ice-cold beer can be in your hands in under 30mins. Because, that's how delivery should work, right?

--CUSTOMER SUPPORT--
How can we help? Our awesome customer support team is ready to help whenever we're delivering. Text/call us at (213) 204-9608, email us at support@saucey.com, visit our help center over at help.sauceyapp.com, or tag us on social @sauceyapp

--YOUR FAVORITE BRANDS--
Golden Road, The Prisoner, Tito’s Handmade Vodka, Hennessy, Bulleit, Budweiser, Patrón, Goose Island, Stone Brewing, Grey Goose, Magic Hat, Johnnie Walker, Fireball, Corona, Dark Horse, Casamigos, Blue Moon, and many more!

--WAYS TO PAY--
Apple Pay, Credit Card, Debit Card. Major Credit Cards: Visa, American Express, MasterCard, Discover. All transactions are 100% digital, because who uses cash anymore?

Awesome And fast

My first time using app. They sent a notification that my order was picked up. They sent reminders of when the delivery will take place. He was very professional and the whole thing took less than 1 minute. I didn’t realize that tips are given through app after the exchange, so I gave him cash tip. I am so glad there are people brave and committed to their work to help those who can not or choose not to risk exposure.
