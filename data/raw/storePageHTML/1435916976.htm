Login In Issues

Let me begin by saying that I have and iPhone and the accompanying iPhone accessories (i.e. iPad, Apple Watch, etc.) I first downloaded the app onto my phone (iPhone XR) and it worked fine signing in and setting up passwords. Once the app was downloaded onto my iPad i tried the same setup (passwords/Face ID/ etc.) and noticed it kept telling me the password was incorrect. Now knowing I used the same password for my phone as I did my iPad on the app i could not understand why it wouldn’t work but ignored it because I really didn’t need it on my iPad anyway. Now that I have upgraded my phone (iPhone 12) I am having the same problem I had with my iPad. I tried changing passwords and it still will not let me log on to the app. I have to go to my safari to check my account.

Thank you for your feedback. We're sorry to hear that you're having issues with multiple devices logging into the Mobile App. Please email AppFeedback@ncsecu.org so that we can troubleshoot with you.

cnn795 , 10/06/2019

Continual setup issues with updates saying resolved but they are not

I am unable to setup the NCSECU Mobile App, version 2.0.4, for iPhone on iOS version 13.1.2 build 17A860, current public build.

Attempting initial setup following all prompts, I enter a 5 digit passcode after receiving my verification text message. I then get an error stating the passcode I just created and re entered is incorrect. This error occurs whether Face ID is enabled or not. I have tried different passcodes, quitting the app after setup and then attempting to login. All attempts fail and present a passcode incorrect prompt. When attempting to authenticate via Face ID if enabled during setup, it will load indefinitely and present a spinning authentication icon but never authenticate.

It is very frustrating to have this app not work on iOS for the last two to three months after multiple versions of the app stating they have resolved issues. Given the long development time of the app and the long lead times for develop with beta versions of iOS availed for developers it is unprofessional and downright beginner level from a top tier bank in NC. I update iOS upon new version release solely for security improvements provided. Not having this app work shows how little development time and resources NCSECU is willing to devote to a safe and secure way to manage and view financial resources when competitors had these features and benefits enabled, developed, and available for years.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for your feedback submission. We have reached out to you via Secure Message to discuss this issue. Please take a moment to review and respond. Thank you.

Heaven Sent!

I’ve been with SECU since I started banking at 18. 9 years later this is still my primary bank! For years I’ve been waiting for them to develop an app. That one faithfully morning when I was attempting to log onto the mobile site to view my DD and that notification popped up that said they had an app was one of the happiest days of my life. The app is so easy to use, convenient, the layout is great and it makes transfers and advances a breeze. What really sold me, I emailed customer service and thanked them for finally creating the app and let them know all it was missing was Face ID. They finally added that feature this week but what really sold me, customer service actually took the time to personally email me and tell me it was now available for use on the app. I was so touched. My children’s children’s children will bank with SECU when I tell them how much this bank has done for me. 5 stars!
