Description

Homeplus Light can improve how you feel when using the light. A variety of different function modes can be perfect for all kinds of environment. Make you feel more fun during the process of use.

Choose your favorite light
Homeplus Light App has 6 common light color and 4 preset DIY color to help you find your favorite color in the shortest time.

Create the perfect atmosphere
Adjust the brightness or change the light color and light temperature in the Homeplus Light APP.

Cinema light mode
4 automatic flashing modes that can adjust the speed automatically. Make yourself immerse in the movie world.

Music mode
Installed with an inner music player that can let the light flash with your favorite songs.

Microphone mode
Make sure you and your friends have fun at the dance party.

Smart timing
With Homeplus Light APP, you can sleep and wake up in a comfortable lighting environment everyday. Let the light remind you of the daily plans.

Can be controlled within the distance of 30 feet by Homeplus Light APP.
