Description

Kaspersky VPN Secure Connection is an easy-to-use app that lets you explore the Internet privately and securely on your phone or tablet at super-fast speeds. Join us now to start enjoying a better, safer Internet experience – and discover why we’re trusted by 150 million users worldwide.

Enjoy lightning fast internet
Get online through the fastest VPN servers. Simply select “The fastest server”* in the list of virtual servers and you’re ready to surf like a pro.

Broaden your Netflix horizons
Netflix’s hottest shows and movies* can often premiere in regions outside your own**. Explore the possibilities of bringing global entertainment to your devices with our borderless VPN.

Enhance your gaming experience
Looking for a way to improve ping and FPS so you can enjoy better, faster gaming? Simply choose to connect through a server that matches the location of the game server.

Stay safe on public Wi-Fi
The app launches automatically when it detects public Wi-Fi hotspots. Which means it’s simple for you to stay safe in cafes, airports and other public places.

Gain the power of invisibility
Because our VPN service hides your IP address, everything you do online is completely invisible – in fact, you’re so well disguised, not even your ISP can see you.

Access unlimited sites & apps
With no-limit* traffic at super-fast speeds, you can stream all the music you want. Or watch an endless supply of online movies, TV shows or live sports – even in HD.

Encrypt your online data
Protect all the data and communications you send and receive online, including files, emails, chats and video calls.

I had Kaspersky for our virus protection and after learning about VPNs decided to go with their VPN all on both of our iPhones and iPads. The VPN fails to secure the connection on my iPad 80% of the time and when it does manage to secure it, it’ll loose it 90% of the time within 2-3 minutes. This is even while sitting at home on my own wifi. It will also interfere with sending emails on a regular occasion which is very aggravating. After contacting Kaspersky about the email issue I was advised to change the setting for where the VPN is being set up, i.e. another country. I find it extremely unreliable.
