Description

GodMod Craft is a powerful tool for creating Mobs and Addons for MCPE.
The application will give you a unique functional and content so the game will become much more attractive.
With our application you will not only create new Mob but you will also be able to edit their Skins, thus making your world unique.
Be unique, be the best only with our application it will still be possible to be God in your world.

Everything is very easy, just open the app and tap on the "Create Addon" button and add your entity.
And when your Addon is ready and you don't want to make any changes to it, press the "Save" button and after press the "Install" button to install the changes made directly to Minecraft PE.


The possibilities offered by our app:
- Modify Mobs/Animals
- Edit textures (Skin)
- Change Characteristics (Health, Speed, Size, Damage)
- Change behavior (Float, Fly, Run, Teleport)
- And much more!
