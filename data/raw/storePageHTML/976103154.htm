Description

What is more annoying than losing things? Find My Stuff is the ultimate object-finder that makes it easy to safely keep track of your valuables. The Find My Stuff app can locate any object that has a beacon attached. All popular beacon models are supported.

WHAT YOU'LL GET

NO STRESS IN DAILY LIFE

• Know exactly where your keys, car, wallet or other belongings are, at all times.

• Get a quick overview of all your stuff locations on the map. Please permit access to location services to determine the stuff location.


NOTIFICATIONS MADE FOR YOU

• Receive an immediate alert if an object is lost, stolen or accidentally left somewhere.

• Check the proximity range of the distance from you to your stuff: far, near, or immediate.

• See the location change history for each object.


SAFE ZONES

• Disable all notifications when you are connected to your home or office WiFi.

• Use Silent Mode to switch off all notifications quickly.
