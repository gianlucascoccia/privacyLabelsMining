Description

You are connected to the world around you with e-mail, Facebook, Twitter, Instant Messaging, and more. Are you connected to God?

If you are like most Catholics, it's difficult to find time to pray during your busy day. If you would like to change that, we made iRosary for you.

Got a few minutes waiting in line? iRosary turns this into time with God. Built for your busy lifestyle, iRosary works like a traditional Rosary, but tells you the prayers, remembers your place, and is always with you when you need it.

NATIONAL MEDIA:
The Catholic News Service says: "Need to Pray? Try iRosary"

National Public Radio (NPR) featured iRosary on All Things Considered.

The United States Council of Catholic Bishops featured iRosary on Catholic Radio Weekly.

But don't take their word for how wonderful iRosary is - see the user reviews below!


DESCRIPTION:
iRosary is an innovative new Rosary for the iPhone and iPod Touch which displays a fully-animated set of Rosary beads that flows across your hand as you move it with your finger, like a standard Rosary.

For those new to the Rosary, there is no easier way to learn this perfect prayer which engages the body, mind, and soul, while meditating on the life of Jesus Christ. Those who pray the Rosary regularly will appreciate how closely iRosary resembles a traditional Catholic Rosary.


HOW IT WORKS:
Just like a classic Rosary. Simply recite the prayer and pull the beads down to advance to the next prayer. It's that easy!


FEATURES:

•Beads which MOVE to the touch

•Create your own PERFECT DESIGN from 243 cross, chain, and bead combinations

•Two sets of FAMOUS ARTWORK

•Text can be RESIZED directly with two fingers for easy reading

•Portrait and LANDSCAPE modes

•English, Spanish, French, and Latin prayers

•Correctly recommends the Mystery to pray based on the LITURGICAL YEAR

•PLEASING sound cues and vibration

•Use with EITHER HAND by moving the beads to the left or the right

•Always REMEMBERS your place

•Uses the same set of prayers listed on the VATICAN website

•The same number of beads as a STANDARD Rosary (between bead prayers fold out)

•Push beads back to return to a PREVIOUS prayer

•The LUMINOUS mysteries can be turned on or off

•Includes the CHAPLET of Divine Mercy and Loreto LITANIES

•A DYNAMIC interface which moves, slides, and rotates to your gestures

•We use iRosary every day ourselves.

iRosary is a wonderful compliment to the Holy Bible!

Why I Love This Rosary

There are many reasons why I love this Rosary. For one, no matter what distractions may take place while I am praying or whether I doze or fall asleep during the rosary, this app will save my place for me so I will know where I left off snd can finish it. Otherwise, I often get discouraged or frustrated with not knowing where I left off, or I feel as I must have prayed 15 Hail Marys instead of the perfect 10. This becomes especially handy when I am praying during my cardio exercise on the elliptical. Second, it is always with me because my iPhone is always with me due my very busy life and the need to keep my calendar handy along with a way for so many people to be able to stay in touch with me. Third, no matter how many times I pray the rosary, it seems that I still cannot burn to memory all of the mysteries and fruits of the mysteries which make contemplation when praying the rosary so blessed, but with this app there is no need to memorize; the mysteries and fruits of the mysteries appear with each touch of a bead on this app. Fourth, the pictures and beads are so beautiful that it forms as another spiritual aid in meditation and contemplation during prayer. Fifth, this is the first time that I read why this rosary app was created, and now I am drawn even more to using this app to pray the rosary as often as I am able.
