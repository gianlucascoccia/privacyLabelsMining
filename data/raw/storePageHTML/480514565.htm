Description

Enjoy more than thousands of ebooks, audiobooks, comics, newspapers and magazines in a single application for the monthly price of a paperback (throughout all our offers). A limitless library offering all literary genres, for the whole family, on all your devices.

For polyglots and international readers, the catalog has been enriched with titles in English, German, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch... and to be continued!

The Youboox reading experience:
• Are you commuting? Resume your ongoing reading instantly thanks to the offline mode.
• Flying away for vacation? Travel light and download as many ebooks and magazines as you want before departure.
• Need more comfort? Change the font type and font size, switch to night mode, leave a bookmark, listen to books instead of reading them.
• Short of reading inspiration? Try out our customized suggestions.
• Spotted a book for later? Create a playlist or add it to an existing one.

Youboox proposes a monthly subscription the price of which may vary according to the country, currency and taxes.
Youboox may offer a free trial period. If you unsubscribe at least 24 hours before the end of this period, you will not be charged. After the free-trial period, you will be charged for the first month subscription amount.
The purchase will be charged on the user’s iTunes account as of the purchase confirmation.
The subscription will be automatically renewed at the end of every month, unless the user has unsubscribed 24 hours at least before the renewal date.
Any subscription is commitment-free and can be cancelled at any time from the iTunes store or Apple device (Settings > ID > iTunes & App Store > Apple ID > View Apple ID > Subscriptions). The subscription will be interrupted at the end of the ongoing billing period.
Our privacy policy is available at the following address : http://youboox.fr/confidentialite/
Terms and Conditions are available at the following address : http://youboox.fr/cgu/
