Description

Effortless time tracking & invoicing!

Ranked as "the best time tracking & invoicing app" by its developers, Orbit makes keeping track of clients, projects, worked hours, and invoices simple.

Orbit's goal is for you to focus on the tasks you want while the app takes care of the rest. Are you a designer? Welcome! A small team of developers? Cool, so are we. A consultant? Perfect! A project manager? We're not judging, welcome to you as well!

Ever wondered how much time you worked last week? We've got you covered. Ever forgot to stop your work timer when you’re done for the day (or when just taking a break)? We've got you covered. Dreading to manually create invoices for all those hours you've worked? Guess what, we've got that covered as well!

Obligatory feature bulleted list:
• Easy setup
• Time tracking across projects & automatic idle detection
• Automatic invoice generation from all your logged time entries
• Ability to create custom invoices for expenses or material cost
• A nice overview of how you spend your time
• Crew Plans for small teams to track time & invoice together
• A strong focus on privacy. No ads, no tracking of metadata, and no selling of personal data.

You can try Orbit using our tourist mode, before creating an account or subscribing to any of our premium plans (we also offer free trials for those). If you cancel your account, you'll still be able to see all of your data in our read-only mode (unless you ask us to remove it of course).

New in 1.13.1:
This time we've improved the overall app performance and fixed some rare issues that caused projects to load slower than they should have.

New in 1.13:
Our invoice builder improvements are continuing!

On top of rounding time when generating line items, you can now also manually tweak the rate and hours on the invoice.
