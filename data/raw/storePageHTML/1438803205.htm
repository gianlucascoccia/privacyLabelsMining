Description

Join our inspiring community and get ready to live your happiest and healthiest life. Keep it Cleaner is a world-class wellness app offering a fitness program, wellness hub, run tracker, daily meal plans, hundreds of recipes, playlists, interval timer, goal setting and more. Think of us as all your favourite fitness and mindfulness apps together in one inspiring place.

Get sweaty with powerful HIIT, Boxing, Strength, Yoga or Pilates sessions and focus on your mental wellbeing too with our guided meditations. You can set running goals and track your run with our 12-week running program. Or master a certain discipline like boxing with our elite Master Trainers in the workout bank. No matter your fitness level or preferred style of training, we’ve got options to take you to the next level.

Keep it Cleaner is your home for all things wellness including:

• Hundreds of 20-30 minute workout sessions to help you get fit and strong
• Equipment Free workouts you can complete in the comfort of your own home
• Warm-ups and cool-downs to help your body get warm and recover
• Delicious meal plans catering to Pescatarian, Vegetarian, Vegan and Regular preferences with hundreds of recipes and modifications to suit your individual needs
• Your weekly shopping list, plus remove items as you go. Meal prep was never so easy!
• 12 week running program by our expert trainers, with 3 levels, 0-5 km, 5-10 km and 10-21 km.
• Blissful guided meditations to help you find balance in all areas of your life.
• Our KIC LOG allows you to track and monitor your goals and progress.
• Extra fitness challenges for when you want to push yourself
• Inspiring blog posts and candid advice from our team of professionals
• Access to our private Facebook Community, which is your place for sharing empowering advice and life hacks.
• Integration with Apple's HealthKit to help keep track of your workouts all in one spot

Who says you need a heap of expensive equipment to get fit? We can change your life from your lounge room.

Keep it Cleaner isn’t about quick fixes or fad diets, it’s about toning up and building strength in a sustainable way. Doing something for you to lead a healthier life each day.

You no longer need multiple health apps - the Keep It Cleaner app will have everything you need, all in one place. Stick with us and you’ll feel energised. Balanced. Fulfilled. Calm. Confident. Lean. Sweaty. HAPPY!

Unforunately not what I need , 12/27/2019

Needs Improvement in the Exercises

Overall it’s a great app that includes lots of different features. I really enjoyed the recipes and different workouts throughout the week, however, it seems as though every workout is full body. For someone that has been working out for many years, I prefer to have days where I’m doing just legs or just arms. Unfortunately, I found myself always having to adjust the workouts into something that I wanted to target and it just wasn’t worth paying that much when there are other apps that charge the same and give targeted workouts. I also think that an added option for difficulty could be added. So for those people that are not beginners, they can adjust the level to where they are at. I think what they’re doing is great, but the exercises can definitely be improved.
