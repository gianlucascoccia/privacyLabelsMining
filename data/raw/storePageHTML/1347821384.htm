Terrible App

The app is crude and terrible. It doesn’t act like an app, it acts exactly like a website. In fact, there is absolutely no different between their website and their app. An app is 1) supposed to run smoother (than a website); faster transition between pages, not having to wait for your selection to load as long as clicking on a link on a website. 2) Not supposed to require you sign in EVERY time you use the app. A website asks you to sign in each time, and app is supposed to retain your login credentials and not ask you to sign in again.
Furthermore, the app has so many glitches and errors (one that comes to mind right off the top of my head for example, is adding things to Wishlist; I select the size, I select the quantity, and I press the add-to-wishlist selection, but I get an error message saying “you must select quantity before adding to wishlist”).
It’s just very crudely designed and frankly I don’t see a point in this app, when it functions the same as the website and I can just use that. In fact, app functions so terribly, that I usually just give up on ordering something and look for something similar elsewhere . I’m probably going to delete it since I’m just not using it.

Venus Product Awesome; App Needs Fixes

Always love the Venus products. Everything I have ever bought is great quality with reasonable prices. Venus is my 1st choice when shopping for swim wear and dresses for my wife. The size information has been very helpful and spot on. I downloaded the iPhone app today 1st time and placed an order. I noticed it can use some improvements: While logged in with my account that I’ve had for years, the checkout did not load my name and address info. I did not let these things deter me from purchasing the items I wanted, and always recommend the Venus product line to all my friends.
