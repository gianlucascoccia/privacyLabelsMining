Description

Discover enchanted tales and quests in the mysterious world of Merge Magic! where you can combine everything into better and more powerful items for your journey.

Merge eggs to hatch magical creatures, then evolve them to uncover more powerful ones! Encounter and solve challenging puzzle levels: match the items to win, then bring rewards back to your Garden to collect and grow.

The only hope to lift the curse from the bewitched land rests in YOUR extraordinary power to MERGE ANYTHING -- eggs, trees, treasures, stars, magical flowers, and even mythical creatures.

Reveal wonders as you merge your garden to perfection and nurture your amazing creatures!

MERGE MAGIC! FEATURES:

• Discover over 500 fantastic objects to match, merge and interact with through 81 challenges!
• Unearth fairies, unicorns, minotaurs and never-before-seen hybrid creatures like Butterphants (butterfly & elephant), Peacats (peacock & cats) and many more.
•An evil curse has been placed on the garden, fight off the fog and lift the curse to restore, and take back the creatures’ home!
• On your puzzle journey, you may cross paths with evil witches. You will need to watch out and be careful!
• Participate in frequent events, win more advanced creatures that you can take back to your garden.

Merge Magic! is brought to you by Gram Games, the studio behind the top hit titles Merge Dragons!, 1010!, Merged!, Six!, Bounzy!, Merge Town!, Merge Farm!, Paint Tower!, and Merge Gems!

fishstickz86 , 07/10/2020

Suggestions

Alright, before I go into suggestions, let me just say; this game is so much fun. I truly enjoy every aspect of it. I'm fifteen and unemployed, but I'd practically consider this game my job 😂. I've got a lot of medical stuff going on in my life and I find that it's great to pass time in waiting rooms and whatnot as you can play for an hour without even feeling like you've played for five minutes. I highly suggest this game to anyone who likes to have fun!

So now onto suggestions;
I love that you're able to view other peoples' worlds, but I feel that part would be more enjoyable if perhaps you could interact with them in some way. In addition to this, I'd also like it if you could choose something from your own garden to send as a gift. Whether it be a creature or an egg or a bush. Maybe there could still be an option to send a random gift, too, if you're not keen on sending something of your own. I'd also like to say, the magic gems drive me insane! Like I mentioned, I'm fifteen and unemployed. I can't afford to be spending my money on the magic gems constantly! Maybe there should be an option to watch an ad and get five gems, or something similar. I know it'd make my life easier 😂. Alright, well that's about it. This is a really cool game and I can't wait to see what else y'all do with it.
