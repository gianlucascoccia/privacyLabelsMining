Description

Precise calculations are performed using the exact moment you were born, together with detailed interpretations by professional astrologers, to provide you with accurate, personalized horoscopes and astrology charts. Find your Sun Sign, Moon Sign, Rising Sign and see how the positions of the planets influence your unique personality and life path.

Interested in reading horoscopes for other zodiac signs? You can quickly and easily access any of the 12 signs from within the app - Aries, Taurus, Gemini, Cancer, Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpio, Sagittarius, Capricorn, Aquarius and Pisces.

Tarot Card Readings
Each day you will have access to a new three-card tarot reading. Shuffle the deck and then select your own three cards, one each for the past, present and future. Tarot cards can help to explain your current situation and anticipate future events.

Zodiac Compatibility
Wondering who you are most compatible with each day? Your daily forecast tells you which zodiac sign you are being drawn towards. You can also check the compatibility ratings for any two signs.

Chinese Horoscopes
Find your Chinese zodiac sign and element based on the lunar calendar and get your weekly and yearly horoscopes.

Daily Outlook
Looking for a quick summary of some key issues in your life? With our simple 5 star system, you get individual ratings for money, energy, love and mood.
