Description

Crop Video is a powerful and handy video maker that lets you create amazing videos to share on social media.
Looking for a video editor to create outstanding videos for Instagram, Snapchat or Facebook? You found the right one!

Even if you know nothing about video editing, these easy-to-use features will make your videos stand out from the crowd!
CROP, SPLIT, TRIM, ADD BACKGROUND and MUSIC to share your adventures with the entire world!

FEATURES:

• USER FRIENDLY UI that lets you use all the features with no effort
• Select your video with QUICK PREVIEW
• Pick among different TEMPLATES to kickstart your project with the right format and frame
• CROP, MIRROR and ROTATE your video to size it perfectly for your social media platform
• Color up your video with BACKGROUNDS
• TRIM and SPLIT to combine the best parts of your video
• ADD MUSIC from our library or your iTunes collection to turn your video into a masterpiece
• SHARE EASILY on Instagram, Instagram Story, Instagram TV, Snapchat or Facebook with just one click
