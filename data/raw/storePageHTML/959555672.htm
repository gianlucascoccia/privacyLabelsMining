Nooowayjose , 06/07/2019

Almost there!

I’ve been using this for a couple of months and it’s exactly what I was looking for. It’s interesting how this simple, but effective app replaces most of my budget/goal saving apps. I even bought pro because I use it really often.

Now the bad. I’m still unsure what the savings account option does/the purpose of it. I haven’t really found a purpose for it other than tracking what account I’m using to save the goal for. But other than that it’s just a placeholder.

Another issue I found is the lagging issues when inputting an entry into your savings goal. For example, let’s say I want to put in $5 (arbitrary number) into a goal. When I hit the check button to confirm my input amount, there’s a slight roughly 4-5 second delay. This is really common on goals that have more than +10 entry inputs. There’s also a glitch where if you hit the check button more than once (let’s say you’re impatient and hit the button for a bit because you think the screen is frozen) while it’s waiting to save changes, it will replicate the entry and have an incorrect amount displayed. In this case, my input will be $10 rather than $5.

I would personally love to have a “funding account” feature that displays my current checking balance (offline of course) where I can distribute funds manually/automatically to my goals so I don’t have to manually go into every goal and subtracting/adding the balance one by one. This is more of a wishlist feature.
