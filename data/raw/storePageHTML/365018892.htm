Description

Let Sun Life help you discover the true benefits of benefits —anytime, anywhere.
With the Benefit Tools app you can easily view your dental and vision coverage, find a dentist, access your electronic Dental ID card and more. Use this app to quickly access:
• My Benefits – An overview of dental and vision plan details.
• Dental ID Card – Your electronic dental ID card
• Find A Dentist – Uses your location to find a dentist nearby
• Find An Eye Doctor - Uses your location to find an eye doctor nearby
• Contact Us – Connect with us to ask questions
• Individual Dental Plans – Learn about the products we sell in your area
