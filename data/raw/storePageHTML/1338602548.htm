Worked very well until I got a new phone

The green light comes on when I plug it into the data link connector so I know it is getting power. For some reason it just will not connect. But on my iPhone 8 plus, this program was amazing. I could do anything from recalibrating tea PMS, programming remotes and immobilizer keys to my car, and even just looking through all of the live doubt it to make sure everything was all right. Please make this work on my iPhone 11 and I will be happy again. This is a great professional level tool and piece of software for those do it yourself mechanics that like to have a nice system without having to purchase a $1200 or more scan tool. The only complaint I have, it hasn’t worked since I got my new phone. I switched from an iPhone 8 plus, on which it worked from flawlessly. To an iPhone 11. It shows up in my Bluetooth manager like it is going to connect. But then it always says there is an error connecting.

Almost 5 Stars

I’m not sure why this isn’t getting better reviews. It looks like people are having issues after an update but I was able to connect and scan my card with no problems.
