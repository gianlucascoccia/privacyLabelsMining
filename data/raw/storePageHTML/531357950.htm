They ruined this app!

I've been using this app for years & loved it. It was so easy to use. With the latest "improvements," it no longer works at all on my iPhone 7. Just keeps freezing up (nothing happens when I tap "discard"), and difficult to find the grids that I'm used to using. I doubt that I'll be using this app anymore. Just spent 15 mins trying to figure out how to make a 2-photo collage, and couldn't do it. I'm really disappointed because I liked this app a lot!
