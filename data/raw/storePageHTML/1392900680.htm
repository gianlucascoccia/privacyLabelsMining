Description

Get your hands on the wheel, step on the gas and be ready to jump! The cutest cartoon crew is waiting for you - Hello Kitty, Chococat, Pompompurin, Kerokerokeroppi, Gudetama, Little Twin Stars, Cinnamoroll, Kuromi, Tuxedosam, Bad Badtz-Maru and My Melody. A car racing game based on our famous Beepzz series, is fun for the entire family and challenging for young children.

Join Hello Kitty and her friends for an unforgettable journey through 80+ levels in 9 different countries all over the World - including Hello Kitty’s hometown London, entertaining U.S.A., adventurous Brazil, mesmerizing Egypt, challenging Austria, fascinating India, mysterious China, surprising Russia and enchanting Japan.

This is a uniquely creative racing game for everyone that loves Hello Kitty and other Sanrio Characters. It is a simple game to start and as you progress to different countries or even advanced levels within each theme, many challenges are presented which will test your skills.

11 Sanrio characters are on a mission to explore the World and along the way collect as many hearts as possible. Each one of them are with their unique vehicles - Chococat with a boat on the wheels, Pompompurin with a tuk-tuk, Kerokerokeroppi with an airplane, Little Twin Stars with a moon-mobil, Cinnamoroll with a red sports car, Bad Badtz-Maru with a scooter and My Melody with a train engine.

From melodious sounds to unique animations and from creative terrains to challenging obstacles, this racing game is filled with Surprises and will keep you thrilled till the end!
