Description

Curious to find a BFF, an FWB, or an LTR? Connect, chat & explore with sex-positive Solo (singles) and Partner (couples) profiles for CNM, ENM & ethical poly #opendating

#open, the polyamorous #opendating app for couples & singles who embrace ethical non-monogamy from polyamory, threesomes, kinky dates & more. With tools to identify your desired relationship styles, #open has space to meet polyam, kinky or open minded people you want. #open is Dating, Updated.
SIGN UP FOR FREE
Join a growing poly #opendating community for free! Match & start dating polyam singles & couples near or anywhere in the world! Connection is more important than proximity, so the distance you can search is open- play the field near & far to find couples & singles sharing your passions. Match & chat with polyamorous folx looking for threesomes, kink partners, swingers and more! Looking for relationships? Meet members in our polyamorous dating app looking to make ENM connections.
BE AUTHENTIC
With over 60 options to describe your gender, orientation, relationship type & status, there’s a field for all your labels. Transgender, Non Binary, Gender Queer? Straight, bisexual, bi curious, pansexual or queer? Want a polyam relationship? Engage in solo polyamory or relationship anarchy? Looking for couples & singles dating? We’ve got labels to express your identity & preferred relationships. Don’t see the perfect word? Add in your own in our open field!
USE HASHTAGS TO SEARCH
Are you DTF or searching for NRE? Curious to find a BFF, an FWB, or an LTR? Interested in BDSM/kink? In search of poly dating? Looking to date and explore with other swingers, couples and singles? Our hashtag search field allows you to share interests, preferences, desires & boundaries - and find people with hashtags that pique your curiosity! Not sure where to start? Connect using a hashtag like # swingers, # 3some, # kink, # FWB, # enm or # polyamorous!
DATE SOLO OR PARTNERED
Wanna date solo? Create a profile & start swiping. #opendating with a partner or poly dating? Join solo profiles into a confirmed partner (couples) profile to swipe, match & chat together. Confirmed partner accounts set #open apart from other polyamory #opendating apps in the field & verify you’re chatting with a real couple. Seamlessly switch between profiles with a tap! Ethical non monogamy, polyamorous & kinky dating, made easy!
YOUR DATA IS YOURS
Concerned about privacy & security? We are too! We created a privacy policy that’s easy to read & feel good about. We don’t sell or share your personal data ever! We want you to feel more secure, whether you’re poly dating, swinging or just checking out the playing field.
KEEP IT REAL
Our community guidelines prioritize respect, consent, curiosity, & honesty; chat with open minded, polyam and kinky people in our sex-positive community. Our team is here to field questions, help navigate the app, build a profile, & ensure that #open remains a safer space for alternative and poly dating.
CONNECT WITH COMMUNITY
#open is created and run by people who are passionate about community. In fact, we’re all #open members, too! Connect & chat with us in app, through our blog, at our #open virtual sex, kink, and relationship education workshops and events featuring leaders in the field of sexuality & polyamory. Check out our virtual members-only nights!
