Description

Apply artistic cartoon effects to your selfie, try the latest fashion hairstyles, animate your screen with special live wallpapers etc. No need to search for materials or tools, you can achieve whatever you want with Artisy. The app was designed with you in mind. Making it fun and effortless to edit your pictures is our goal.

【Cartoon Effect】
An innovative and creative effect that converts your selfie into a cartoon style.

【Fashion Hairstyle】
All kinds of hairstyles you can imagine is here. We use smart face recognition technology to help you suit these haircuts.

【Trending Hair Color】
Experiment with multiple hair colors in seconds to complete your looks virtually before taking a photo.

【Future Face】
What’s your future look? Uploading your current photo, it will let you see yourself in 20 years, 40 years, or even 60 years, and instantly takes you through time and space.

【Live Wallpapers】
An unique opportunity to bring your screen to life with fabulous Live wallpapers.

【Custom Widgets】
Customize the Home Screen: Tired of the usual style? Make your home screen unique with our brand-new themes! Every theme contains a set of icons, wallpapers and widgets, you can freely edit the themes.
Easy Setup: Create widgets quickly in 5 steps with our step-by-step guide.
Icon Packs: With icon packs, you can choose the icon design as you like.
