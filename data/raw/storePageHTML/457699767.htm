Description

1800Flowers: Flowers Delivery, Plants, Gifts+

Send flowers, plants, gift baskets, gourmet food, and wide variety of other gifts in just a few easy clicks with the 1-800-Flowers.com App!

Whether at home or on-the-go, gift and flower delivery makes it easy to let friends and family know you’re thinking about them whether near or far.

Join our Celebrations Passport program with FREE shipping flowers and gifts for the year, for only $19.99 and send gifts from our Family of Brands all year long to the special people in your life.

Gift & Flower Delivery Facts:
* Whether sending flowers in the U.S. or around the world, we deliver gifts and smiles to 195 countries!
* Our blooms are the best quality and always arrive fresh.
* Send flowers as soon as today by a local florist, or order in advance.
* From classic to unique, we offer many different flower types such as roses, carnations, tulips, sunflowers, orchids, lilies, daisies, mixed bouquets, and many more.
* Trust us to deliver a smile while experiencing streamlined browsing and a secure checkout while shopping on our app.
* With reminders and reliable order tracking, you will never miss another important notification or occasion!

Every day is a chance to connect for all gifting occasions including, but not limited to:
*Birthday: No matter their age, birthday flowers are sure to brighten their day! Send timeless birthday flower arrangements from our most popular floral designs or even one of our truly unique arrangements to make their day even more special.

* Love and Romance: Whether your sweetie lives with you or across the world, send romantic flowers to shower them with love.

* Sympathy: Convey your deepest condolences during a difficult time by using our “White Glove” service to send funeral flowers or sympathy gifts to the home.

* Congratulations: Whether you’re celebrating graduation, a new job, promotion, first home or retirement, our fresh flowers and gifts are the perfect way to say, “congrats!”

* Just Because: Sometimes you don’t need a reason to send a smile, send one just because they’re awesome. Our same day, “just because” flowers and gifts are sure to brighten anyone’s day!

* Thank You: When words are just not enough, show your appreciation and gratitude with a beautiful bouquet they will never forget.

* Wedding Flowers: Wedding flowers are easy to order online, especially for virtual weddings. Get ready for your special day with breathtaking bridal bouquets, centerpieces, corsages and boutonnieres.

So, whether you are saying, “Happy Birthday,” “Congratulations,” “I Love You,” “Happy Mother’s Day” or anything else in between, we have the perfect gift!

Every flower delivery includes our 100% smile guarantee. Our caring service team is ready to help you with your order and welcomes your feedback!

We love hearing your stories, share your flowers and gifts on social with #MadeMeSmile!
