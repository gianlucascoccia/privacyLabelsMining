Description

The most popular app of the Holiday Season 14 years running is back with great new dances and features!

This holiday tradition lets you "Elf Yourself" and star in dozens of personalized videos with your face on dancing elves. Add up to 5 faces, then select a dance and the app automatically creates your video! Share your dancing skills on social media - or message a super fun holiday greeting to your friends and family. It's as easy as pie!

ElfYourself is FREE to download and enjoy, some dances are free, but most require purchase to unlock. You can purchase dances individually or get our Season Pass to access all content and remove ads for 12 months!

Doesn’t work

I used to love Elf Yourself. It was super fun and funny to edit people’s faces onto the elves, and my friends and I would spend hours doing it.

I recently tried to download Elf Yourself again, only to find that it doesn’t work. I opened the app, and it asked me to allow notifications. I clicked no, and then the pop up disappeared. The screen then stayed blank and I was left sitting with a blank screen. I assumed the notifications were the problem, so I went into settings and allowed them. I refreshed the app and went back in, and was again forced to wait with a blank black screen. I’d really like to use this game again, but I’ve tried everything I can think of and I can’t get the app to load.

I’m adding on to this review after the developers responded and I reinstalled the update.
Elf yourself still does not work. I have been greeted with a blank screen once more. Don’t waste your time getting this app, you’ll just get your hopes up for nothing. I really want to play, but the app still doesn’t work.

Hello there! Thank you for the feedback and we are very sorry you are having trouble with the ElfYourself app. We are quickly working to fix this issue and we will have an update soon so this does not occur anymore. Once again, we apologize for the inconvenience! Look out for update v9.1.1 and higher versions to fix this issue.
