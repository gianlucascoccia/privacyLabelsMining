Description

Cooking Frenzy is a kitchen game for cooking mama. In our event, only the cuisine tile masters could drive food truck to reach tasty town or cooking city. Let's cook now!

Welcome to hug the cooking joy and feel the collecting fever! Cooking Frenzy is not only an Easy, Relaxing, Fast Paced cooking game. We tailored and fused the popular collecting and current cooking gameplays together, only to offer you more and different fun from those outdated machine-made cooking games!

A valuable album of worldwide cuisines are divided into pieces and lost in different countries for a long time… All the world class collectors, top gourmets and crazy chefs are looking for the lost cards and pieces. It is rumored that some famous restaurants are hiding the pieces in their kitchens, and use them as rewards for people who pass their cooking challenges.

As a cooking fever, you are invited to those challenges. Now you have the advantaged chance to make the album complete again. Accept this challenge! And start the treasure hunting around the cooking world! On this cooking dash adventure, you will:

- Travel across different countries!
- Cook and Serve in various restaurants!
- Beat the creative and addictive levels!
- Feel the exotic taste of the fantastic cuisines!
- Collect the precious cards of distinctive cultures!
- Expand your collections and achieve great fame!
- Master the secret recipes of the authentic food!
- Claim generous rewards and gifts for your victory!
- Enjoy the great graphics all about cooking!
- Have a fast paced but relaxing time!
- Get endless fun from the engaging progression!

Cooking cultures may be different, but the cooking craze are all the same! From North America to South America, from Europe to Asia, come and dive in the franticness in the kitchens of these worldwide restaurants! Achieve great fame and fortune, become a world class cooking master!

COOK
- Tap to put right pieces of ingredients to make the target cuisines!
- Remove the food from the cooker on time to avoid burn!

SERVE
- Tap to serve the correct dishes the customers ordered!
- Serve in time to keep the customers happy!
- Complete serving task before the time or customers run out!

COLLECT
- Open treasure boxes to collect the unique cards!
- Redeem rare cards to complete the fancy sets!
- Claim valuable rewards of signature collections!
- Open the gourmet feast for all the restaurants!

LEVEL UP
- Get culinary EXP to upgrade the restaurant by beating levels!
- Unlock new challenges with new dishes and special kitchenwares!
- Open new restaurants in the engaging progression!
- Improve your cooking skill and cook worldwide food in real life!

Cooking Frenzy is made by the professionals who have been making cooking games for more than 10 years. Therefore, Cooking Frenzy is a high quality product full of sincerity, and has the most stable and consistent game experience.

JaidedRH , 01/14/2020

Fantastic Game

Kudos and Thank You to the developers of this game! Finally a game that is fun and challenging with lots of opportunity for boost packs via “Frenzy Cards”, completing collections and general rewards. You don’t spend your coins or gems on upgrading the equipment or dishes, that automatically happens by beating levels which is a huge help! Sure there’s ads, but they’re not as annoying as they are in other games I’ve played. Watching a 30 second add to get 10 more seconds or 1 more customer helps a lot. The higher you go the more challenging it gets. Yeah, some levels are really, really hard unless you have boosts, but they’re not that hard to come by. I think these developers found the right balance between earning boosts/rewards and having to spend money on boosts. I’ll still buy boost packs, but I don’t feel like I have no choice like other games.

Dejasmiles , 10/27/2020

Annoying pop ups!!!

I really want to give this game five stars, but there’s one major annoying thing this game does. Even though I pay to have ads removed, I still get very annoying pop ups after every single level. When I am on a roll I do not want to be stopped everytime to be told “like us on Facebook!” “Try our other game!” “Buy your piggy bank!” “Complete your daily tasks!” “Rate us in the App Store!” I could play 10 levels in a row and still get pop ups. All these things could just be icons on the side of the main screen with a notification dot and I will get to them when I want, especially if I paid for the game. There needs to be more fluidity into the next level because of the nature of this game.

Other than that I love this game. I love the graphics, the sounds are satisfying, it’s fast and keeps up with my taps. I don’t too much care for the mini games like collecting cards and spinning the wheel, but I see the appeal to other players. Another suggestion I have is to see more diversity in the customers. Black people aren’t just football players! We are cute kids and college students and happy business people and fitness junkies too. Would like to see other races included as well.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for playing the game and active suggestion. We value your feedback, and will investigate the feasibility of your suggestion and keep on working hard to improve the game. If you have any ideas, please feel free to contact us in the game.

ajryan9582 , 09/11/2020

Fun and challenging but...

This game is challenging, it is fun, but they don’t have enough ways for you to earn boosters as rewards. Some levels can’t be passed without a booster. As far as I know there are only three ways to get more boosters: 1. Buy them with diamonds (you earn minimal diamonds when you pass a level and boosters are expensive so this is not a great option), 2. Get them gifted by friends (in order to add friends in the game you have to add friends on Facebook, sorry not adding a bunch of random people to my FB to get boosters), 3. Buy boosters through in app purchases (this is not a good strategy either as most people do this as a last resort, and it makes it seem like the game company really just wants your money, the ridiculous amount of ads they force you to watch after playing each level should cover the cost of rewarding you with boosters on occasion like pretty much every other major game on the market).

Although the game is fun the company that produces it has some serious work to do in order to retain players without making you buy the items you need in order to play the game. I’d be more likely to spend money if I knew there were other non-paying ways to earn boosters.

Thank you for playing the game. Selling ads is a key profit way for a free game, which allows us to keep on developing and improving the game. We balance ads and game experience very carefully. And you can remove the ADs by a very cheap price :) We will be very appreciated to your understandings.
