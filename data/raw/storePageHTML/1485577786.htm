Description

Slowing down aging and giving your skin the love it deserves with our brand new app, offering the best from the world of beauty:

Accurate evaluation of your skin needs
Personalized recommendations
Guided face yoga and massage
Progress tracking and skincare routine calendars

With a few simple swipes of your phone you'll discover hundreds of ways to enhance your daily routine and lifestyle for a positive effect on your whole body.

All it takes is just to:

Open the DIARY, elaborated by PROFESSIONAL COSMETOLOGISTS.
Input all INDICATORS OF YOUR SKIN HEALTH, like daily skin state, diet, regular skin care you apply, as well as your age.
Receive your COMPREHENSIVE and PERSONALIZED skincare routine.

The app will become your dedicated aesthetician, that will guide you to better products, healthy habits and will let you track all your improvements.

WHAT YOU'LL LOVE:

FACE YOGA
Revive your facial beauty with 13 best face yoga programs to suit your every need: de-stress after a workday, achieve sculpting or zone-focused anti-aging effect.
Enjoy detailed and clear video explanations that will help you gain all of the face slimming and tightening benefits of exercising the muscles.
FACE MASSAGES
Lift and tighten skin with the help of 2 tried face massage routines.
Learn the right techniques from professional estheticians to easily use them as a part of your at-home skin care routine.
MORNING & EVENING ROUTINES
Achieve consistency in your skincare routine and follow a personalized scheme of applying products.
Learn what, when and in which order to apply, and check every morning and evening the products and procedures off your list to make sure you're not missing anything out.
FACE & NECK EXERCISES
Enjoy 50 varied and effective face fitness exercises developed by professional cosmetologists.
The app will carefully guide you through each of them, making the exercising process safe and pleasant.
FACE-TRACKING DIARY
Make selfies daily, add them to the diary and watch your skin condition progress with the before/after pictures.
Track how cosmetics, food, lifestyle & face yoga improve your skin condition and overall appearance.
LIFESTYLE ARTICLES
Try something new with numerous fun skincare tips and insights on everything that will help you keep and enhance your beauty.
The advice you'll find here is regularly updated and fact-checked for accuracy and clarity by professionals and experts.
BEAUTY ROUTINES & LIFESTYLE STATISTICS
See the way your lifestyle, food and skincare habits change over time
Assess the effectiveness of your regime by viewing graphs and dynamics of your skin condition.
