Yay! Party cannons!

I didn't want to wait for this app to ask for my review in exchange for it to be free. I already love how this app works. Easy to use - means easy to meet your water intake goals. And the tiny celebration with each glass is endearing. I've been won over.

Some folks are whining about making a 5 star review in order to continue use. Really? If you love it, rate that you love it. This is the app I decided was most user-friendly and deleted the others.

Now if only I can change my woman icon to different expressions. There's room for creativity. All in all....great app.

Scottso , 06/15/2016

Like fIlling a bucket with a hole in it

This review is for the FREE version of this app, which is the problem with this app. No where in the description did I see that the app would stop working unless I either paid $1.99 or gave a 5-star review. And this is the reason I believe the app is rated so high.

To be fair, the app does what it says - it is easy to use with a simply slide to record water intake. It has a simple, attractive design and makes it simple to know exactly where you stand against your daily intake goal. The constant cheering and confetti rewards quickly grew old but could have been overlooked.

I simply can't recommend an app that allows me to use for a few days and then informs me to access my data and continue using I must either purchase or rate with 5 stars. If I had known up front that this was the case and paid the Pro fee, I beleive I would have rated this app much higher as it does do what it claims.

RealmReal2013 , 01/28/2018

Excellent App

I absolutely love this app. Before downloading it, it was hit or miss with the water intake but I sincerely wanted to be good to and express gratitude for my body and give it what it needs and this app, with its built in incentives, assists and applauds me as I attempt to reach my goal each day.

On the one hand, I was a bit surprised when I opened the app this morning and learned that continued use was conditional but on the other, I understand and will not give it a lesser rating because the app does deliver. Perhaps the conditions could be stated before one downloads it.

Thank you kindly for such a helpful tool. I have recommended it to my family, friends and colleagues.
