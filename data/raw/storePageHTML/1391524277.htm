Description

JotForm Mobile Forms is an online form builder that lets you fill forms and surveys — even offline! Use JotForm's form maker to build forms from 10,000+ free form templates, or create forms from scratch.

Place the full power of JotForm at your fingertips. Download the JotForm Mobile Forms app to collect data, access offline forms and surveys, and stay connected on the go.

**** Exclusive Features for JotForm Mobile Forms ****

** Digitize your forms & surveys **
- Save time and money with paperless forms.
- Generate, view, and edit forms.
- Collect and manage data instantaneously.

** Collect data anytime & anywhere, even offline **
- Fill out your forms and review submissions on the go without having to wait until you have access to a computer.
- Once you are back online, JotForm syncs your data automatically.

No internet connection, Wi-Fi, or LTE data usage needed!

** Advanced Form Fields**
- GPS location capture
- QR code and barcode scanner
- Voice recorder
- Signature capture
- File upload
- Take photo

** Run your forms and surveys in kiosk mode **
- Enter kiosk mode to collect multiple submissions from a public or personal device.
- Lock down your app and turn your device into an online or offline survey station.
- Continue without locking to look at your other forms.
- Go from a completed survey to the start page automatically and securely.
- Perfect for trade shows, conferences, and fundraisers
- Provide a touchless form-filling experience for your patients with QR codes

Surveys display full screen — no toolbars or menus will be available.

** Take action quickly with notifications **
- Receive instant push notifications for each response
- Enable or disable notifications for specific forms

** Collaboration with your team **
- Share forms via email, text, and other mobile apps (Facebook, Slack, LinkedIn, etc.)
- Assign forms to members of your team to send and view responses
- Team members can access their forms without a JotForm account
- Take an action according to your team responses.

With JotForm Mobile Forms, you can quickly access every JotForm feature from your mobile device. New to JotForm? Here’s what you can do with the world’s easiest form builder:

** Build Any Form in Seconds
- No coding skills required
- Drag-and-drop Form Builder
- 10,000+ customizable form templates
- If you need, login or sign up with your Google account
- Create registration forms, application forms, work order forms, contact forms, sign-up forms, online or offline surveys, and more!

** Automate Your Workflow
- Add conditional logic, calculations, and widgets
- Set up autoresponders for confirmation emails and reminders
- Generate analytics reports for your data

** Publish Your Form Anywhere
- Copy and paste a short embed code into your web page HTML
- Embed on any web page, such as WordPress, Facebook, Blogger, Weebly, Squarespace, and Wix

** Protect Your Data
- 256-bit SSL encryption
- GDPR and HIPAA compliance

Our 24-7 support team is always available to help with any issue you might encounter with JotForm Mobile Forms.

catwriter , 07/02/2020

Best app for tracking form submissions

I work in marketing and have tried most of the major form tools. Highly recommend this app if you need mobile tracking, or to gather submissions on mobile. Editing forms also works fine. Clean and easy to use.

Because most of us need web as well as mobile tools I will mention some pro/cons for the overall service beyond how the app works.

Cons: JotForm’s form templates could be better. Most of the available designs seem to be for a freestanding form rather than to embed in a site, with way too much body padding and a background color so it doesn’t look integrated. This has to be over-ridden using the “Advanced Designer” that some users would find challenging. You’re not going to be able to make it truly responsive either. I wish they had a “naked” or unstyled option.

The Wordpress plugin is also basically useless.

Pros: It’s otherwise excellent. Pricing, ease of use, customer service and the variety of form fields available are all equal to or superior to peers. You can easily make a ton of different kinds of forms, like quizzes, uploaders and social contests.

Developer Response ,

Thanks for your valuable feedback. That's greatly appreciated. Just a few clarifications with regards to the cons side, the default theme has 60px spacing around the form but there are themes that you can find on theme store (jotform.com/theme-store/) with less spacing e.g. jotform.com/theme-store/theme/simplicity so you do not need to adjust them on the Advanced Designer. In addition to that, these spacing will also automatically adjust depending on the device it is viewed. Example, from 60px top spacing on desktop to zero spacing when viewed on mobile. The forms are also already mobile responsive so there's no further configuration needed but you can still inject custom CSS codes to customize it according to your preference. With regards to the Wordpress Plugin, it should be working just fine. If you have experienced any problem with the WP Plugin, please let us know by opening a support ticket here http://www.jotform.com/contact/ so we can further help. Thank you so much and have a good one!
