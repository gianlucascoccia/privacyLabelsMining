The Ultimate Guitar Ukulele Tuner & Tabs In The World
No matter you are a beginner or advanced, or just require a quick-learn course, you will find the course that best for you. Besides, no matter you are a newbie or a master, almost all of what you need is all-in-one this app: tuner, chords, courses, practices, tabs, metronome, etc.

【The Most Accurate & Fastest Guitar / Ukulele Tuner】

"Multiple Tuning Modes": C Tuning、D Tuning、G Tuning......
"Two Patterns": Auto & Manual
"High Accuracy": Professional uke tuner that accurate to 0.01 cents
"Left-handed Version": Specifically for ukelele players in need

Good- But mostly as a concept

Loved the old app! It was basic and didn’t thrust too much at me all at once. This however? Oh boy. It’s got a very modern and appealing aesthetic but that only gets it so far. Unresponsive is a light way to put it- over half the time it doesn’t register touch OR sound. Which is incredibly frustrating considering it’s supposed to be a hub of sorts that has a tuner, lessons and so on. All of this content, yet accessing it is where the issues lie. I mostly use it as a tuner however when I did try a lesson it worked at the beginning, but died on the second lesson and wouldn’t accept ANY input, sound or touch. Which only makes it much more annoying to have a pop up asking for you to have a subscription when it actually DOES work.

It’s got a ways to go but as a concept? Not bad. In practice it’s meeting some issues though.
