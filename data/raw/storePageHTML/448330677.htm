Description

Gain access to a world of books! 24symbols is a service to read digital books in English, German Spanish, and in other several languages: you will find novels, essays, stories, poetry and management books. Read wherever you are: from your computer, smartphone or tablet.

24symbols is a service without limits. In exchange for a small monthly fee you can download and read on any device (mobile, tablet, ereader with web browser or computer) all the books you want from a catalog with more than one million titles, in several languages. This subscription does not imply any commitment to stay, at any time you can easily cancel it from you iTunes store.

Enjoy a catalog of more than 1 million ebooks. Search and discover new readings among endless literary genres: classics, best sellers, cook-books, new releases, business books, and much more, so you can read everything you want. You'll also find that unexpected book you were longing for!

No downloads required, incompatibility problems or format issues. You can find the titles you want in a single click. Make your dream library come true by creating bookshelves to organize and manage your ebooks with absolute freedom. Build a social network and connect to readers with tastes similar to yours by updating your reader profile and sharing what you have read, what you are reading and what you want to read.

What are you waiting for to sign-up? Follow other readers and look-out for new discoveries: you never know what your next favorite book will be, what new authors you can come across... or what’s the next story that will make you catch your breath!"

Horrible app but has tons of books

The app itself is horrible. Doesn’t let you change the rotation of the iPad, takes a long time to load the book, books stay in the currently reading even after showing 99% read and the search function is horrible and you can’t search for a book from the author from a books info (ie looking for the others in the series).

The app needs a lot of work and help but the thing is I continue to use it because the datebase of books is large and has a lot of the books I want to read that other online libraries don’t have so I suffer using the app.

Have it 2 stars because of the large book selection but the app need major overhaul.

Just awful

Literally the only positive of this app is the selection. Beyond that it crashes every few pages/minutes.
