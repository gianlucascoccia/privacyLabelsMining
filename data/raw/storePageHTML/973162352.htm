  Led by world-renowned investor Emmet Savage, MyWallSt is the award-winning app investors of all levels rely on for extraordinary stock picks and unparalleled analysis. • Highly-vetted stock picks that crush the market
With over 7,000 stocks listed on the U.S. stock market, do you know how to pick winners? You don’t need to when you have the MyWallSt app. Every stock in our portfolio has been thoroughly researched and analyzed based on a long-term investing philosophy used by some of history’s greatest investors - including our very own Emmet Savage. Most of our high-growth picks are lesser-known companies we believe have the greatest potential for explosive, long-term growth.

We can help you build a portfolio of high-growth stocks primed to outlive any downturn. Start now before this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to buy low is gone.

• The Journey to Confident Investing
Our highly-trained, industry-recognized customer service team works around the clock to help you invest with confidence. We’re there for you every step of the way - from setting up a broker, to making your first investment, to building a diversified, market-beating portfolio you can retire on.

• Start Investing
Start by reading our analysis and opinion on our handpicked stocks to discover why we believe they’re primed to beat the market year over year. Once you're ready, you can link your brokerage account to our app or you can set up a new investment account with our trusted, fully-integrated partners at DriveWealth.

MyWallSt’s stock research and insights come from world-renowned investor and our CEO, Emmet Savage. Emmet’s personal investing track record has been independently audited by KPMG. Over the past 10 years, his average annual return is 30.08%. Over the past twenty years, his personal stock picks have beaten the market nearly four-to-one every single year.
