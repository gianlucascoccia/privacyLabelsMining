LANGMATE CONNECTS JAPANESE PEOPLE WITH THE WORLD

Langmate is an International language & cultural exchange matching app made in Japan, ranked in the TOP 10 in more than 80 countries (Education category).

At Langmate, we think we can learn better when we have fun! 

Simply chat and have fun learning new languages and cultures directly with real Japanese people.

KEY FEATURES
• Improve your language skills while talking with real local people all around the world directly from your phone!
• Simply search, swipe, and start making Japanese friends! 
• 92% of users matched within one hour!
• A huge Japanese community! More than 65% of Langmate users are Japanese!
• Perfect your pronunciation. Chat with native speakers using voice and video messages!
• Learn new cultures. Ask local people about anything you want to know!
• Practice your writing. Using the drawing tool in the chat, you can exchange hand writings and improve your kanji and kana skills! (But you can also only send cute drawings if you prefer ;-)
• Make friends before your next trip in Japan! 

FUNCTIONALITIES 
- Filter user profiles by age, nationality, and gender.
- Swipe and Match -  Send likes to the users you like. A match happens when two users send a like to each others.
- Around Me - You can see all the users based on your filters and location.
- Rewind (New in version 4!) - If you accidentally passed on someone, you can now swipe back and undo an accidental dislike (VIP only).
- Likes List - See all the people who sent you a Like.
- Matches List - See all the people with whom you have matched. Click on their avatar to start chatting.
- Messages - List of your chats.
- My Page - Update your profile, settings, current status and more!
- Boost - Be on the top of the list for one hour and get more views and matches! (In-App Purchase).
- Coins - Coins are used for swiping cards, discovering Around Me user profiles, and sending messages.
- Free and Bonus Coins - Get free coins by logging in everyday or by watching video ads.
- Rating - The Star Rating help you to recognize popular users. Rate and be rated after exchanging your first few messages.
- VIP - As a VIP user you can use all the premium Langmate features for free and get even more bonus coins!

ABOUT VIP
- Unlimited messages.
- Unlimited matches.
- Free bonus coins.
- VIP exclusive functions (unlimited Rewind, video messages, voice messages, drawing, location sharing).
- VIP badge on your profile and Card.
- Available VIP plan durations: 1 week, 1 month, 3 months, and 12 months.
- VIP subscription will continue until cancelled (at least 24h before the renewal date).
- Subscription will be renewed within 24 hours of VIP ending.
- Subscription will be renewed automatically unless it is cancelled at least 24 hours before VIP ends.
- To manage your Langmate VIP subscription, in iOS go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.
- You can cancel your VIP subscription at any time.
- If you delete your account, your VIP access will be removed. Please stop your VIP subscription manually before deleting your VIP account.

SAFETY AND SECURITY
- If you log in and create your account using your social account, your social network access will only be used for logging into Langmate, and we will never post anything on your behalf. Your friends and followers won’t be informed that you are using Langmate.
- You can delete your account anytime.
- Your personal informations will never be used for purposes other than the Langmate services.
- Any inappropriate usage will be moderated and the offending user may be reported or banned.
- Visit www.langmate.jp to see our FAQ or read on our future plans.
