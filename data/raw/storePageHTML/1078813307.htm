[GAME FEATURES]

■Strategic Party Creation & Quick Battles!
Create strategic party combinations and run quick, easy-to-control battles perfect for mobile devices!

■Intense Co-Op Connect Battles!
Battle alongside other players in real time to take down massive bosses in Connect Battles!

■Amazing Graphics & Limit Burst Attacks!
Activate Limit Burst attacks and watch your enemies turn to ash with epic screen-filling attack animations!

After powering up your tank, will you give him the gear he needs to become an unstoppable wall? Or maybe you'd prefer to boost his speed and attack to make him a well-rounded warrior. Make your party uniquely yours!

■Collect Unique Characters -- Each with Their Own Story!
Unlock a huge cast of characters, each with strengths and weaknesses, and power up the ones that suit your playstyle! Immerse yourself in the fantasy world of Valkyrie Connect by getting to know each character through their individual stories!

Titan_levi_freak , 05/26/2020

I love this game but..

I literally am in love with this game, I got it around June of last year because they were having an AoT (attack on titan) event, and I’ve loved it ever since, but the game does have some problems. After a certain level it’s pretty hard to level up not including the hero because those are pretty hard to level up as well. You really have to hustle and grind on this app to get anywhere significant. I love that hustle and grind fact, but I wish they just made it a tad bit easier to do things. Like sometimes I couldn’t level up my heros to make them powerful because during the quests I was stuck with my heros power too low to beat the boss’. It was quite aggravating as well has how the do kind of scam you on the summons. The new event that’s out I decided I wanted to spend my diamonds to get a specific character. I saved up 24,000 diamonds (which is pretty hard in this game) and now I’m down to 3,000 without the character I was going for. I am quite bitter about that, but I do still enjoy the game. I don’t know it’s all up to the player if you’re willing then I recommend if you’re very impatient and non-willing this game isn’t for you.
