Description

All your breeder records in one place. The new way for responsible breeders to manage weight tracking, litters, heat cycles and everything else. Spend less time on paperwork, more time with your dogs. That's PuppyFat.

Over 5000 downloads so far!

#1 rated by responsible, registered hobby and professional breeders

"Awesome App!! Any breeders dream come true for easy on hand record-keeping" -- Mason Farms Labradors

"I love having their information at my fingertips" -- AJ Ranch Australian Shepherds

BEAUTIFUL & SIMPLE

* Record daily weights of puppies
* Produce stunning litter weight charts
* Easy conversions between kg, g, lb & oz.
* Log every feed
* Log every treatment, vaccination or worming
* Log daily notes on the health of your dogs and puppies.
* Log owner details for easy waiting lists

MORE THAN JUST WEIGHT TRACKING

* Heat cycles
* Matings
* Gestation countdown
* Feeds
* Medical treatments
* Pre-whelp temperatures

UNLIMITED, SECURE AND SAFELY BACKED-UP

* Cloud backup
* Sync data across multiple devices (such as iPhone & iPad)
* Unlimited dogs
* Unlimited bitches
* Unlimited litters
* Unlimited puppies
* Unlimited heats and mating records
* Unlimited temperature records
* Unlimited weights and feeding records
* Unlimited vaccination, flea and worming records.
* Unlimited Contacts (inc. Waitlist, Owners, Breeders, Guardians & more)

CREATING THE BEST, MOST ACCESSIBLE SOLUTION FOR BREEDERS.

Love it but wish it had more

I had been looking for this type of app and stumbled across this one. It is amazing but is in need of a few things:
1. In the puppy records it would be great to be able to indicate when a puppy is sold and when it is delivered. Two different steps because a pup can be sold but still hasn’t left for their new home. Then when they are delivered, if we can “swipe” a delivered tab and the record goes to the bottom of the litter record and become lightly grayed out.
2. A print option for paper records. It would be wonderful to be able to generate a report with all information of the puppies from a litter including buyer info, treatments, etc. To be able to specify each printing what details you would like to print in the report.
Sorry for so much in the review. I LOVE the app though and am glad I purchased it!!
