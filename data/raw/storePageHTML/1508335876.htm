Awesome wellness platform

This app is amazing. Extremely well organized with all of their amazing content, which is top notch. I have followed Juliana and Mark for years now and have always loved their incredible work on the mat and off. This app puts everything together in one convenient location and is very easy to operate.
Whether you are looking for mediation, lifestyle posts and recommendations, vicarious traveling, yoga of all varieties and levels, Pilates or some mixture of these, boho beautiful has created it.... and its in this app. As mentioned earlier, all of this content is well organized and you can find the category or type of wellness content you are looking for very easily and quickly.
Lastly, I love the calendar features with recommended classes for different results but also the ability to pick and choose my own selection of videos in my personal calendar within the app. This calendar comes complete with a daily journal function that makes self reflection along your wellness journey much easier.
I obviously recommend this app to all .... Love this app and Boho Beautiful! Juliana and Mark are amazing and I look forward to everything to come including their little light warrior!!

Wellness as an Artform

The quality of the content on this app is unparalleled. Julianna and Mark are such authentic artists sharing their gifts with the world. You will find a variety of practices in this platform - yoga (multiple types), cardio and HIIT, Pilates...ranging from 5 minutes to over an hour. The app is very intelligently organized. It is so intuitive to find what you are looking for. You can schedule your practices in advance using the calendar feature (helpful for competing challenges!) or you can follow the monthly member calendars for motivation. Love my roadtrips , 12/01/2020

Best app!!!

For the past couple years, I have been a huge fan of the Boho Beautiful videos—the tone, tempo, variety, pace... and bonus that there is almost always a gorgeous mountaintop / beach / forest in the background. As much as I loved BB offerings, I was skeptical (but hopeful) that the app would be easy to navigate and would offer an organized approach... this app far exceeds what I had hoped to find! Easy to navigate, well organized, items like journal and calendar that I hadn’t thought would be in there... can sort through by length of class (important with my crazy schedule!), can browse by style or focus... varying lengths of meditation, yoga blends with strength or Pilates... absolutely the easiest way to get up and get my day started. Not just the best yoga app I have found—one of the best designed apps I have come across!
