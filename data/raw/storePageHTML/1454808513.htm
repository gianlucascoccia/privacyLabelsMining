Deleting, with NO REGRETS

How cool would it be to accomplish 1 million steps! I mean I know I’ve walked a million steps, but to actually SEE this accomplishment AND make $10... would be AWESOME! But then they “updated “ this app, and now with regrets, I am deleting it. Why? Let’s say you walk 10,000 steps in a day. This app will give you a base of 1,000 steps. In order to increase the base to your 10,000 daily step-count, you need to watch an ad that will increase your base by 200 steps. So in order to have your base = your daily step count, you will need to watch 45 ads. (200 x 5 = 1,000 x 9 = 9,000)... EVERYDAY! In other words, if you walk 10,000 steps a day, it will take you 100 days and you will need to watch 4,500 ads. To me, the real challenge is watching all of these ads, not walking these steps. And because if this, this is one challenge that I will not accomplish, with no regrets!
