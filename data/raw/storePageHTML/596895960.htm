Alternative to Apple’s Notes

I have used Apple’s Notes app for as long as I’ve had my phone to give myself reminders and notes for my daily life. Awhile ago though every single time I use the app it started to spam me with the message of “Your iCloud Storage is Full...” I looked for a way to disable this, but there was no way as far as I know, besides upgrading an already expensive iCloud plan. So thank you developers for creating an alternative to Apple’s Notes app. Your loss Apple.

ND CountryGirlAt Heart , 02/02/2016

Good but could be better!

I do love most things about this app; however the thing that is keeping me from getting the paid version is that there is no quick way to jump to a certain letter. Other apps have the alphabet along the right side and you touch the letter to take you to words starting with that letter. That saves a lot of scrolling as I have many notes. If that feature is added, I will give 5 stars and get the paid version!
2/1/16- Took rating down from 4 to 2 stars. Since one of the updates to my phone, the lines are all screwed up and I waited months for you to fix it. Finally did and it's some better but still not like it was. It is quite disconcerting when the writing is through the lines and all over the place. Also since the last update, ads are now full page whenever you go out of a note; I always switch between numerous notes and having to wait while an unwanted ad plays is REALLY ANNOYING. I didn't mind the banner ads near as much. I would buy the paid version if these things were fixed but as it stands, I am looking for a better notepad.

Micwriter , 10/12/2019

Not Ready For Release

I downloaded the app yesterday and liked the interface so much I decided to pay to remove the ads. Unfortunately, while trying to do so after hitting their ‘remove ads’ button, all I got was a screen saying ‘$3.99’ and a spinning loading indicator that never finished loading.
I contacted the company to ask for assistance, and to their credit they did get back to me within 24 hours. But, after acknowledging some users were having this problem, they gave me a ‘fix’ that involved hitting the ‘remove ads’ button multiple times. How can that be a solution when, after the first hit, it immediately goes to the ‘$3.99’ screen again and freezes on the loading indicator? I’ve since deleted the app in frustration and have moved on to another. The two stars were given because I liked your interface and sleek, no nonsense appearance.
