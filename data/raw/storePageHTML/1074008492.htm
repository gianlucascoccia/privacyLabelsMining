Description

The My Blueprint Mobile app from Arkansas Blue Cross and Blue Shield helps you manage your healthcare and insurance.

Helpful features on My Blueprint Mobile include:
- Electronic member IDs and the ability to email or fax (where applicable)
- "Find Care & Costs" features with cost estimates for procedures, mapping and doctor reviews
- Access to claims and policy information
- Personal Health Record access to help you fill out medical history forms
- Easily reach customer service by phone or email
- Pharmacy information with prescription history and refill orders (where applicable)
- Bill payment (where applicable)

My Blueprint Mobile is available to Arkansas Blue Cross and Blue Shield, BlueAdvantage, and Health Advantage members.

Demo of the mobile app available on our website: https://www.arkansasbluecross.com/ResourcePackages/ArkBlueCross/demo-app/home-medical.html
Note: Your plan may not include all of the features in the demo.
