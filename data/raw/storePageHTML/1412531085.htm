Description

“It’s an app designed, literally, to help you have conversations that are more meaningful, and not just chit chat.” Oprah Winfrey

Meaningful conversations bring you closer - it’s just that simple! Our daily prompts & guided conversations are a fun and motivational way to strengthen relationships, improve your emotional wellbeing, support each other, & stay up to date.

DAILY PROMPTS:
More than just a daily journal app, Longwalks has free journal prompts to form deep connections as friends, families, & couples. Each day of the week has an intentional theme so you can build a habit of reflecting, connecting, & growing closer:
Monday: practice mindfulness, focus, & peace
Tuesday: speak your truth, offer advice, & learn from each other
Wednesday: practice vulnerability through deep questions
Thursday: share stories, memories, & your personal identity
Friday: share your favorite things together & grow closer
Saturday: talk about joy, happiness, & your emotional wellbeing.
Sunday: use Longwalks as a gratitude journal & share your thanks.

Every week, you receive a personally curated summary of your responses. Keep this digital keepsake of your thoughts & stay in touch with your mental health.

CLUBS FOR THE COMMUNITY:
Besides the daily online journal, have a meaningful conversation within one of our club topics. Answer prompts on specific interests or conversation topics with other members of the Longwalks community. Talk about topics like parenting, affirmations, optimism, poetry, & more. Discuss diversity, celebrate differences, embrace empathy, & find understanding. Practice wellness together by exploring self care, acceptance, gratitude, healthy habits, body positivity, & motivation. Our thoughtful & inspirational content sparks deep reflection.

MADE FOR VULNERABILITY:
Everyone answers the same prompt, & we all have to share our own answer first to see what others are sharing. This way, there is no social anxiety or competition - just self worth, self care, & support. Longwalks is your community journal where you reflect, set goals, share joy, practice wellness & motivate each other. Unlike social media, you can truly focus on your relationships & build a lifestyle that supports your mental health. You can even send thoughtful letters & digital cards!

PRAISED BY EXPERTS:
Oprah Winfrey uses “this wonderful app called Longwalks” as a wellness tool to have more meaningful conversations. Experts like Brene Brown share the importance of vulnerability, daily reflection, expressing gratitude, & journaling for your relationships & mental health. Longwalks prompts & questions are rooted in insights from focus groups, communication theory, media psychology, & mindfulness practices. Longwalks is the perfect morning routine for a focused lifestyle where you can keep family & friends close, even when you can’t see them in person.

MEANT FOR RELATIONSHIPS:
Longwalks is not your typical journaling app. It is a safe space for meaningful connections. Unlike social media, Longwalks is a community based on friendship, rather than followers. We don’t publicly display statistics such as likes or friend counts & we don’t show you ads.

Longwalks helps you build deeper relationships, inspire others, keep track of your mental health, & practice self care. Invite your family, friends, team, spouse, & more to build healthy habits together, grow closer, & stay close! Focus on your relationships and mental health, without relying on social media.
