Description

Drive for Free your favorite car now! Over 150 cars available!

Do you love driving cars? Driving School Sim 2020, the latest driving simulator will allow you to feel what driving a real car is! Driving School Sim allows you to get behind the wheel of the newest cars on the market!

Drive sport cars, SUVs, Sedans, Supercars, Hypercars and Hatchbacks!
It will teach you to drive a manual transmission with clutch and stick shift or keep it simple with the automatic gearbox.

Play with the virtual steering wheel or many other control options that suit your needs. Improve your road rules knowledge with this amazing driving simulator.

Huge maps are waiting to be explored: famous cities, mountain roads, desert landscapes and icy and snowy streets, etc...

Play with your friends in new free ride Online Multiplayer mode or Race against them in the new multiplayer system!

Career levels, Learning Modes, Events, Races and much more!

Show off your driving skills, get your driving license now! Play Driving School Sim!

Ethan Spence , 10/16/2020

Very fun and addicting

The moment I got this game I was immediately hooked! (Had to turn down the resolution due to lag though) The graphics were stunning, this struck me clearly as the best driving simulator on the App Store! The controls work very well (Still can’t understand what ‘Sports Plus’ is) The steering sensitivity works very well too.
But, every game has its bugs, this game though, fortunately doesn’t have many of them, I’ll name the one issue I have a problem with:

Stop Signs - The stop signs are very buggy in my opinion, in career mode, I have to have the whole front of my car in behind the stop sign to get points, but after a while you get used to it.

Car Seizures - The AI in this game works well until the AI has a stroke. My first experience with this was driving in career mode in Paris, when a car somehow got stuck on a curve and shook violently. Then again, in Las Vegas, a van turned horizontal on a road somehow and started shaking as well, holding up traffic. I also saw a truck in the distance have the same thing.

Despite these bugs, I would definitely recommend this game to a friend! Amazing game!

-Thanks, HeartAttack3 [GD]
