Description

If you like Sailing, SUP, Kite, Windsurf, Foil, Kayaking etc, you'll love Waterspeed. Use Apple Watch alone or iPhone to track Speed, Distance, Heart, Pace and more stats

Waterspeed helps you to track and improve your Water Sports performances. Waterspeed is explicitly designed for iOS devices and fully optimized for the Apple Watch.

Allowing you to gain statistics such as instant speed, distance, direction, max speed, best speed, weather, wind, tides and saves the track for off-line review and stats. Waterspeed also includes a countdown so you can accurately track race starts and regattas.

* Designed for Sailing, Windsurf, Kite, Paddle SUP, Kayak, Powerboat, Jet Ski, Surf, Outrigging, Windfoil, Waterskiing, Wakeboard, Rowing, Kitefoil, Hydrofoil, Foil, Wing, Canoe, Dragon, Pedal, Jet Surfing

* You can check the weather and tides forecast 6 days/1 hour in your spot with temperature (°C/°F) and wind speed/gust.

* You can review your performances within the single track, point by point, or with Statistics and the new Graph tool.

* Waterspeed works on iPad too, with automatic sync between devices.

More than 100,000 among Sailors, Kitesurfers, Windsurfers, Paddlers, Kayakers, Wakeboarders use Waterspeed every day. Give it a try!

Everything you need for FREE:
• Real-time Speed, Heading, Trajectories, Max speed, Distance, Pace
• Full view with big numbers
• Logbook of all activities with Stats, Totals, and Graphs

• On iPad: use the internal GPS, if available, or any external GPS compatible
• Alarm after 60 seconds of poor GPS signal during navigation
• Save the number of maneuvers, wind speed and angle, comment
• Force touch menu on Apple Watch for an easy start
• Activity list on Watch with "Re-sync" button
• Social sharing
• Open a specific point in Maps/Google Maps

Make the most with the PRO version:

mk1727 , 06/01/2018

Great app

I purchased the pro version which allows you to set a count down timer for the start of a race. When I race I-20’s we use a 5 minute starting sequence. When I race windsurfers we sometimes use a 3 minute sequence. You can choose any length you like. It then starts tracking you while the time is counting down for the start while tapping you on the wrist when the start is getting close. It drops a pin on the map when you start the starting sequence and then drops a pin on the map when the flag go up “the actual start of the race” then drops a pin when you finish, which you have to press the stop buy when you finish. It lays the tracks down on a map with color indicating your speed. FYI, it shows the map stuff on my iPhone when I get back from sailing. This was all tracked with the Apple Watch 3. I can also easily send all the info to my iPad using airdrop. For me, it’s a great app for racing sail boats and windsurfing. Really easy to use. I Highly recommend it. I don’t usually leave reviews, but I felt I had to because I like this app so much. Can’t wait to try it out this winter when I go Iceboating and achieve speeds near 70 mph.

Jeff N - CBI , 07/06/2018

Essential for Sailers

Wow!! Do I like it and then some!! I sail on the Charles River and started to track my sailing late 2017 and found an app that did it pretty well. I record about 28 trips and then I found Waterspeed. I am instantly impressed as I ONLY need my Series 3 Apple Watch to record my sails. Previously I had to take my phone and watch to record trips. With Waterspeed I can see my trips played back with a visualization of my HR - Speed - Time - Heading. So cool! Waterspeed also uses colors to indicate your speed on a map (green, yellow, orange, red). I have 4 sails with Waterspeed and each time I’m more eager to look at the results and see where I had speed on the sail and how I performed. Awesome!! The data that is gathered is complete. Each sail tracks duration, distance, wind speed, avg speed, top speed, best speed 10 sec, best speed 20 sec, best speed 500m, best speed 1km, best speed 1nm, HR min, HR max, HR avg, calories, heading, and location. Also, I did purchase the Pro membership. BTW there is start timer too. Wow!

Flickerstop , 11/12/2019

Best tracker I’ve used for kayaking

Absolutely amazing app!

One of the problems I had with a lot of “tracker” apps for kayaking is that they required you to use your phone, which causes problems when you keep it in a dry bag in a hatch on your kayak. This app solves that problem! I can easily start/pause/stop my trips from my apple watch which means I no longer need to take my phone out.

The data that the app collects is perfect and works very well with the apple health app. You can even import/export your data and full cloud sync’ing. A lot of times developers just stop working on apps, but I don’t think there’s been a week without an update on this app and so much stuff gets added!

Another amazing feature is the trip editing. A couple times I would forget to stop my trip before packing up and driving away, then it completely screws up the data since it thought I was going 60mph in a kayak. But I can easily cut out the section of the trip that’s in a vehicle and the data works perfectly fine!

Lastly I wont forget to mention the amazing developer(s). I had an issue about 2 years ago with something on the app and within a day he messaged me back with a solution and even went as far as making sure it worked perfectly. Easily earned a life long user of this app because how good the support is!
