TOP GEAR is the world’s greatest MOTORING MAGAZINE, bringing you unrivalled access to the very best cars on the planet. There’s everything from family SUVs to mind-blowing supercar exclusives, as well as driving adventures from across the globe, honest appraisals and reviews of every new car that matters. Plus, TopGear TV presenter Chris Harris features every month alongside other opinionated columnists and of course, The Stig.

Our AWARD-WINNING digital edition on iPhone and iPad lets you download the latest issue direct to your mobile device. Every issue has stunning HD photography, epic features on just about anything from the world of cars, the world’s best motoring journalists, group-test reviews and comprehensive buying guides from the TopGear experts, a brand-new retro section, first-drives of the most important new cars, all the latest motoring news, insights into global car culture and motorsport updates.

You’ll also get the latest from TopGear TV and plenty of entertainment in the way only TopGear knows how…

Content is designed to fit your specific device and you can download each issue to read offline.

To restore any previous purchases after updating the app, go to your TopGear magazine UK library, click the menu button in the top right of the app, then tap the cog icon button in the bottom right of the screen and select ‘Restore Purchases’. Then select ‘Restore’ on the pop up, and your previous purchases will be restored.
