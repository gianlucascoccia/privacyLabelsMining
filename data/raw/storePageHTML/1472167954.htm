Description

VSBL - video editor with various editing features for perfect results (no watermark):


3D EFFECT

Create “moving” 3D pictures in seconds: select a subject to shoot and move your phone, capturing the subject from differing angles in the process.

Once you’ve got a 3D picture that you’re happy with, you can add effects and filters over the top to complete the look.


BLUR BACKGROUND

Add a background blur effect so that it looks like you’re using an expensive camera:

- custom blur intensity & direction
- try different blur effects: Default, Radius, Motion, Zoom


EFFECTS & SOUND EFFECTS

Turn your videos into VHS films or simulate glitches of old videotapes with trendy effects & sound effects: VHS, Glitch, Super 8, Light Leaks, Film, Triple and many more.

And we 100% in love with our NEW start effects!


PHOTO FILTERS

When Instagram filters are not enough - over 100 popular filters, including Retro, Vintage, Grain, Street Style and more.


ADVANCED EDITING

Discover a wide range of editing tools: Brightness, Exposure, Contrast, Temperature, Tint, Saturation, Sharpen, Vignette.


MUSIC & VOICE-OVER

Add music & sounds to your photos and videos:

- choose from a huge variety of free music and sounds effects, or use licensed audio tracks from your own iTunes collection
- add voice-over


Enjoy using VSBL and follow us on Instagram @vsblapp.
We can't wait to see what you come up with!
