Description

Zenly is a live map of your friends and family. It’s the most fun way to meet up — or just see what’s up! — so even when you’re apart, you can feel together. Zenly is an app from Snap Inc. that millions of people around the world use. Here’s what it can do:

MEET UP WITH FRIENDS
*See exactly where your friends are and what they’re up to
*Know which friends are awake and ready to hang out
*Get to your friends without asking for the address
*Know when your friends are on the move and how fast they’re moving
*Know when they’re at home, at school, at work and when they’re on their way to any of those places

FIND THE PARTY
*Know when your friends are together with fire on the map and create a group chat in a tap
*When you do meet up, Bump phones with friends to let others know you’re hanging out

FEEL THE LOVE
*Blast your friends with your favorite emojis (hint: sound on)
*Know when your friends run out of battery when you can’t reach them
*See how much of the map you’ve explored with Footprints and compete with friends
*Get notified when your friends travel to different countries

USE WITH LOVE
*Blur or freeze yourself to get off the grid for as long as you need
*Zenly is for best friends only. We have a number of ways you can add friends on Zenly, but remember that your friend will need to accept your request before you can see them on your map!

Zenly is free to use and without ads (yay!). We’ve also spent 10 years developing a very special algorithm that allows for continuous sharing without draining your battery (also yay!).

Got a question? Fancy a chat? Need an ice cream recommendation? Send us a message on @zenlyapp on social and love@zen.ly on email.

Zenly loves you.
