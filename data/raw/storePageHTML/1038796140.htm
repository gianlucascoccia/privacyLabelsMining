Description

Explore Shopville and play cute and fun mini games with all of your favorite Shopkins friends! Help decorate the Shoppies Café, get creative in the Cupcake bake shop, go bowling with the Shopkins, visit the spooky haunted house, help Donatina at her Donut Cart and more! Once you shop, you can't stop!

FEATURES
- Collect and play with all your favorite Shopkins from Seasons 1 to 10 and add them to your My Shopkins Collection
- Explore the Shopville streets and interact with your favorite Shopkins!
- Play games, earn coins and unlock Shopkins!
- 24 fun-filled shops to play in!
- Meet the Shoppies and decorate your own café!
