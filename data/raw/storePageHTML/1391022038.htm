jbbic , 04/04/2020

Fun, but Limiting

It’s a fun game. Great ideas and good questions. I like most of the format and the way the game is played except there is too much waiting in between turns. I think it would be more interactive if you could take turns answering questions and seeing each other’s answer for each question, instead of answer 15 questions, then waiting, then answering 15 again, then waiting. It’s seems like it’s set up more for couples who are away from each other for an extended time, but not really ideal for couples who want to play a live interactive game on a date night. Also, it only has 3 options for free games. Once you’ve played the three free games, then you have to pay 1.99 or $10 for a whole pack. I’d rather save the money and make up my own questions, or play the games for free and see a few advertisements and let the advertisers pay for the game. Overall fun game, but wouldn’t pay!
