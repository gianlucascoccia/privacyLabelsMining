Description

Crop Video is the most popular photo & video editor on the app store. Crop Video with Music is an easy video editor with fast editing tools, filters and many other effects for personalizing your videos & pictures.

New Features from Dec-2020:
#1 - Add Background to Portrait / Landscape videos or photos to make it Instagram ready and Square.
#2 - Add Blur Background with No Crop Videos and Pictures.
#3 - New Sharing Screen.
#4 - Access your videos/pictures everywhere from different devices on the "My Videos" screen.
#5 - Get the Music and Backgrounds Updates Periodically.


FEATURES
1. Trim: Cut out any unwanted moments.
2. Music: Add from your iPod library
3. Adjust Display: Change Brightness, Blur etc.
4. Filters: Select from several filters to enhance your videos.
5. Crop: Rescale your video within the video frame.
6. Share on Instagram, Facebook, YouTube or email

+ Best support in iPhone 12 & 12 Pro Max, and also other iPhone Devices.
+ Also Compatible in the latest iOS 14.

Download 'Crop Video' App Today and Enjoy!
