Description

The ultimate 3D Coloring Hub for realistic anime characters, fantasy miniatures, cyberpunk statues, popular DressDolls. Kawaii kids toys, and custom color 3d prints.

If you like character design, painting, minis, or love visual design and mandala coloring, then ColorMinis is for you.
Coloring apps are relaxing & creative, and with ColorMinis we bring this to a new dimension coloring real 3D models and shapes with a huge color palette, real materials, 3D painting layered techniques, and advanced lighting and filters. There's nothing else like it and it's easy, relaxing, and fun to color real 3D models and shapes using a huge color palette and real materials.

Free Models
We've unlocked all our Classic ColorMinis models, including our new Chibi collection! This gives you over 100 models to color FREE! with ads
Try the ColorMinis coloring experience and discover what millions are already calling their favorite creative app.

New Membership
We're showcasing the most talented figure creators in the 3d print / tabletop gaming hobby by offering you a new Premium ColorMinis membership. Join now to preview color layering directly on your favorite hero figures, and enjoy a realistic preview of your painting project for easy iteration and an unprecedented level of experimentation and coloring fun.

New Features
- New! Touch model parts to select
- New! Flexible preview tab lets you show/hide parts of the color box
- New members feature - Advanced painting mode allows multi-part coloring and ghost preview tool with paint list
- New! Universal login for online cross-device and cross-platform saves
- New! Music and HD settings easily accessed in coloring box
- New! 3D color print-on-demand for custom color Classic ColorMinis figures
- New! Model streaming for faster loading
- New! Intermix both rainbow and palette coloring modes on the same model
- Color and design FREE anime figures, animals, sci-fi robots, mature manga, fantasy knights, elves, and more
- Members Premium access an ever-expanding library of figure kits and projects by Artisan Guild, Arte Creator, Epics “n” Stuff, Titan Forge, RN Estudio, Sephon, Walades, Ritual Casting, and more!

Classic ColorMinis Features:
- Test your paint and color combinations with 2 coloring modes
- Combine color with Realistic 3D resin, gloss, shine, metal, wood, glass, glitter and more
- Easy controls. Simply tap the part and then add color and materials. Pinch to zoom on the HD details, and Swipe the figure to view from different angles and study for drawing and painting reference.
- Add custom name tags and fill your collection shelf
