Description

Give yourself a natural, youthful boost with Facetory!

Facetory is an anti-aging beautification face fitness app that helps you sculpt the perfect visage using specially tailored facial yoga techniques.

Facetory’s AR Coach* is your personal face yoga guide, right on your smartphone. We use the front camera and scan your face. Our technology recognizes your facial movements and through voice gives gentle corrections that help you do exercises efficiently and safely and get better results.

Skin Care Diary lets you monitor your skin condition. Your skin changes day by day, so keeping close track of your progress can help you better care for it.

Imagine your skin looking younger, more toned, with those tell-tale wrinkles reduced. Facetory engages key muscles with face exercises so that you can unlock the wellness within to look more serene than beautiful than ever before.

The app’s natural facelift techniques allow access to a variety of programs. Choose from:

- Anti-aging – target and eliminate the signs of aging and replace them with a younger, fresher you.

- Anti-stress – when life is stressing you out, take some time to yourself and make sure those lines don’t leave a permanent mark with unique face massage exercises.

- Lifting – tone your main attraction with this face workout program dedicated to putting you back the way you should be – gorgeous!

- Skin healing – put your wellbeing ahead and treat your skin to a well-earned, natural facial massage.

- Skin firmness – boost elasticity and re-engage with your youth with this skin firmness facial yoga program.

And much more…

But that’s not all. Facetory also provides you with all the necessary support you’ll need on your journey to a fresher face. Including skincare recommendations, pop science articles on aging, and the hidden secrets that you simply need to know.

Download Facetory and meet a younger, healthier-looking you!

Want to give 5 stars

I really like this app and would love to be able to give it 5 stars. Unfortunately I’ve been experiencing some issues with possible bugs. The one exercise that requires you to stick out your tongue to try to touch your nose never picks up my tongue movement no matter how bright of light I’m in. So any time I have that in my workout I automatically skip it now. There is also an issue with the videos. I usually can get through two or three workouts before they stop loading and I’m stuck on a load screen. It’s not my internet because all my other apps work fine when I check. I signed up for a 3 month subscription and have only been able to use the extra videos for a week of it due to loading problems. If those things get fixed, this 4 star review would easily be a 5 star review! Thanks!

Vocal instructions

The app is nice, and you feel the exercises working on your face. It’s just difficult to know how long to hold the position before switching to the left or right as you turn, or as you look up and down when you can’t see your phone screen.

It would be nice to have a few more vocal directions as you are doing the exercise such as “now left.. hold...3...2..1 now right,” etc. They did a little bit but it was more like, “now the last one”😕 and I was like oh shoot I’m on 7 out of 10. 😂

Especially when they tell you to close your eyes and don’t tell you when to do the next set for the exercise... I mean really 😂😂. If my eyes are closed how can I see when she’s doing the next step I just learned 3 secs ago if she never speaks up and guides me a bit more. 😇 Just some helpful advice to improve the experience.
