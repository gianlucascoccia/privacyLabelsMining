Description

Work out at home or at the gym! The Fit Body app is designed to get YOU results that LAST. With four expert trainers and eight unique programs that you can switch between any time, the Fit Body app is the app that has it all.


Get 7 Days FREE! No payment information required.


Join millions of women who are transforming their lives with the help of our expert female personal trainers, 250+ weeks of workouts, 300+ recipes, community support, and more!


Results-driven workout programs and delicious recipes by our expert trainers.


Anna Victoria:
GROW + GLOW: Pregnancy-safe, trimester specific, low-impact workout program.
SHRED: At home HIIT program designed to help you burn fat and lean down. No equipment required.
TONE: HIIT strength training program designed to help you gain strength and tone up. Incorporates dumbbells and bodyweight exercises that can be done from home or the gym.
SCULPT: Gym-based program designed to help you build muscle and sculpt your body. Gym equipment required.


Brittany Lupton:
LIFT: A strength training program designed to help you gain strength and burn fat. Perfect for women wanting to lift weights and improve their fitness.
REVIVE: A postpartum program designed to help women regain strength and restore their core. Can be done from home or the gym.


Nicci Robinson:
STRONG: A strength and conditioning program guaranteed to make you sweat, improve your athletic ability, and get you results.


Martina Sergi:
MOVE: A yoga program designed to help improve strength and flexibility as well as get you lean and toned results. No gym required.


Fit Body app features


Step by step workouts
• Targeted workouts designed to get you results and help you feel and look your best
• 250+ weeks of workouts across 8 programs that you can switch between any time
• Workouts laid out in sessions so you can make the workouts fit according to your schedule. The goal is to complete all workouts by the end of the week
• Work out for as little as 30 minutes from anywhere
• Weight recommendations for each weighted move, as well as a timer to help keep you on pace
• Daily 5 minute workouts that can be done as a warm-up or a burn-out after your workout session for that extra burn
• Stretching and foam rolling videos for rehabilitation
• Cardio workouts with speed and duration directives for LISS, MISS, LIMIT, HIIT, and sprints cardio workouts
Food Tracker + Meal Plans
• 72 weeks of meals plans with custom portions according to your daily caloric needs
• 300+ recipes to mix and match and make your own meal plan
• 7 eating preferences: Regular, Vegan, Vegetarian, Pescatarian, Gluten-Free, Dairy-Free, and Keto
• Daily food tracker to enter your meals and track your intake
• Easy add features to enter your own macros from meals or ingredients
• Barcode scanner for US and Canadian food products
• Nutrition database with caloric and macro information to assist you in reaching your goals
Workout Calendar + Wellness Journal
• Track your workouts in our weekly and monthly workout calendars
• Track your mental and emotional well-being in our wellness journal
• Look back on your workout performance to track your improvements
Guidance and Education
• Educational videos with guidance and dozens of fitness and food topics intended to help you reach your goals of achieving physical, mental, and emotional well-being

Love everything about this program

I’ve never felt compelled to write a review before, but this app and Anna Victoria and her team have changed my life (literally). I had been yo-yo dieting and flip-flopping with different ineffective workout plans for years until I got this app last June (2018). I have lost 0 (ZERO) pounds. And I’m thrilled about it!!! I realized through her message and all the guidance she gives through her app and social media that fitness is not about weight, it’s about nourishing yourself (body and mind) and moving your body. This app gives you every tool you need to face fitness head on. It provides you with an accurate macro breakdown (hard to find one that doesn’t give you unrealistically low or high numbers), easy to follow meal plan, convenient grocery list including all the ingredients needed for that weeks meals, three different exercise program options with different goals for each of them, and video guidance on all things fitness, mental wellbeing, how to work through struggles with your fitness journey, and food guidance. Such a reasonably priced app for all that you get. Amazing, kind, and prompt team that has fixed every tiny glitch I’ve had within 24 hours of me letting them know about it (shoutout to Sara). I could go on and on. If you’re looking for something that spells it out for you, with a community that holds you accountable without guilting you when you slip up, this is it.
