Humorous Owl of Rhodium Vale , 04/10/2020

Restore Poseidon’s Wave Cleaving Cooldown

Hi Developers,

So recently Poseidon has not gotten any love from Art of War: Legions’ player and the reason for that is due to him not being viable in the current meta. After you guys decreased the damage from Poseidon’s passive skill and increased the cooldowns for his active skill, his damage per second has been trimmed tremendously. I suggest that you guys restore (bring back) Poseidon’s old cooldowns for his Wave Cleaving to compensate for the damage lost on Surging Tide. I know that restoring Poseidon’s old cooldowns can make him become overpower again, but you have to take into consideration that there are other heroes out there that is more overpowered than the previous version of Poseidon. A good example of that would be Selene and Wukong. Selene just blocks the majority of the damage for her troops when the battle start which makes her troop extremely hard to kill and Wukong can one-man army because of the ridiculous cooldowns and damage on his active skill. Also when combined with his passive skill Wukong just become unstoppable. What I want you guys to see is that there are more heroes out there that is more overpowered than Poseidon, so if you're going to nerf Poseidon please do so with the other or don't nerf Poseidon at all because he can no longer compete with the other overpowered heroes.

Developer Response ,

We will consider it and try to make it more democratic, however, understand the whole idea of legendary heroes it should be hard to get. Thank you for the suggestion, that's why we have a Flip Card event where you chose yourself the Hero you want to use.
