Description

Spark ideas to create a better life at home.

IKEA Place lets you virtually place true-to-scale 3D models in your very own space. Combining the latest AR technology and IKEA’s smart home solutions you can experience IKEA like never before.

Get inspired through camera first experiences and our specially curated collections. With continual product highlights and groups there is always something new to try at home.

Furnish a whole room in just one tap or find the perfect product by searching with your camera. IKEA Place has new and exciting ways to enjoy IKEA. Plus with more coming soon - keep checking IKEA Place to see what you can place next.

KyleDaley , 11/22/2020

Great concept, needs work

This is a great concept, and when it works it is a dead useful way to plan out how you will use space in a room. However, it seems to work only intermittently even on a brand new iPhone 12 Pro with Lidar capabilities. About half the items you pick for the AR experience will show the placement indicator on the floor but as soon as you select where you want to put the furniture, nothing happens. At least half the items I selected to see in the AR view wouldn’t appear. When an item does appear, the app works fine and you can manipulate the piece of furniture to see how it will fill the space. Just needs some work/stability improvements to get a higher rating.

Developer Response ,

Hej Kyle Thank you for reaching out and apologies for the inconvenience. We’re aware of the issue and we’re looking into solving it. Look out for updates as we’re always trying to fix issues for all our users.
