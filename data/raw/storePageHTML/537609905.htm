Ads are far too frequent!

You get not one but two lengthy ads throughout usage of this app. At first I thought it was after every conversion, which is still obnoxious if you're trying to compare two prices and have to wait over a minute for the ads to be over, but the ads are also activated at random number presses and not just after hitting the conversion calculation button. This app accomplishes what a search with Google can do on the fly (and I can only assume Google is much more accurate as conversion rate can change any minute), so I'd rather deal with going to the search bar than waiting minutes for what can get instantaneously.
