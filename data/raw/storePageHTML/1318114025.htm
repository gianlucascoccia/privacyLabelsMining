Description

New Summonses App for iOS 12. This app contains most of the summonses in New York. If you want to learn more about the laws and summonses in New York this app will help.

What’s New

Oct 4, 2019

Version 2.8

-Fixed bug for iOS 13 DarkMode. Text will now automatically change to white font with black background in the reference section.
-Changed the arrest statements to 61 Statements.
-Added new sample narrative for Burglary, Grand Larceny, Larceny, and DOA
-Added more 61 statements (Robbery, Lost Property, Sex Offense, DWI, Criminal Mischief, Assault, Harassment)
