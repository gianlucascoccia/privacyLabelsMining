Description

New rigs and map! Roll coal or go green with an electric truck!

Awesome 4x4 trucks that you can upgrade and customize into the trail rig of your dreams. Mud bogging, rock crawling, bombing around the dunes, off-road racing and even demolition derbies - there's an activity for every four-wheeler lover. Get together with your friends and go wheeling in an online session!

Customize your rims, tires, bullbars, bumpers, snorkels, racks, cages, colors, and more. Install that lift kit, disconnect your sway bar, engage the lockers, air down the tires, and get on that trail! Don't forget to take a pic with Photo Mode once you get your rig to an impossible place!


Huge and tough off road levels, Diverse environments: muddy forest, scorching desert, freezing ice lake, bumpy hills and a demolition derby arena stadium.

Complete challenging missions, trails, races and derbies to earn in-game points.

23 off roaders - trucks and jeeps, to choose from as a base for your 4x4 rig.
Get behind the wheel of a precision-modded wheelin' rig and show'em how it's done!

Megapeyt , 08/23/2020

Very fun, but.....

The initial grind is tough. There isn’t much in the way of online walkthrough’s, help, or guides. I don’t know if that’s because it’s a new game or not enough people have played it to warrant that level of community?

That being said, I REALLY enjoy it. I started out with the awful starter jeep and played derbies because I didn’t stand a chance in racing and the missions just didn’t do it for me. Got to where I could beat the hard level derbies and make some decent money. Saved up, bought the APC, got the plow, sold the Jeep and rolled the money over into the APC. Now I can cash grind the hard level derby with ease.

Most frustrating for me is trying to complete the special missions for the project car. I have no problem doing the missions to get it. I really enjoy completing them to actually earn it and not buy my way through using gold, but there is no way to locate them. If I have to drive every square inch of all the maps to find those missions I’m going to have to pay gold to unlock the project car, and I feel like it’s a cheap shot to get someone frustrated enough to spend cash on the game.

Overall I REALLY enjoy it, I just wish there was more help in the initial grind and finding the special missions.

Keep up the great work guys! This one is one I deeply enjoy playing!

Developer Response ,

Hi, thank you for the detailed review. There are currently 4 special missions for one vehicle, the first 2 are on Desert, the second 2 are on Forest. You can find them by looking for a vehicle icon on the map in single player free roam. Bear in mind they do need rare parts to unlock (except the initial one), those can be found in crates and earned in other ways (you can read more about it in the game's 'Game Info' section, the 'Currencies' page).
