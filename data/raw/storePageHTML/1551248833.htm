Description

The Alabama Alcoholic Beverage Control Board’s mobile application improves customer access to available products in its stores. The mobile application will allow customers (ages 21 and older) to quickly find products in the store(s) most convenient to them. App users can search for products by category, name, price, bottle size, user ratings, and availability by store. Additionally, users may opt to create a user profile where they can save their favorite items and stores, as well as to make private notes on each individual product.

The app displays directions to ABC store(s) from their current location through the primary map application installed on their device.
The app also provides a news area where upcoming events such as barrel releases and other current information can be found.
