Description

Upload photo to use as widget background. Many weather icon sets with various and more to come. Track multiple locations simultaneously on home screen.

Weather+ Widget is easy-to-use widget app for you to fully customize widgets background to fit your phone stylish home screen.
Having unique weather widgets on your home screen to keep updating weather status while completely fit with your stylish wallpaper, other widgets and app icons.
Upload your own photo to use as widget background. Change from Weather+ icons.

Each weather widget on your home screen can show weather condition and forecast for a different location. Enjoy full of weather+ info of your current place and any friends, family members or you next destination on your home screen.

Weather Widget features are:
º Current weather and conditions of your location or any locations you want you check
º Current condition info including: temperature, realfeal temperature, humidity, wind info, sunrise, sunset, UV index, rain info and cloud cover.
º Add many locations to quickly check weather data of them
º Multiple different locations show in different widgets on home screen
º Select from many Weather icon sets
º Hourly forecast up to the next 72 hours
º Daily forecast up to the next 15 days
º Many premium background colors and images.
º Upload your own photos or images to make unique widget background.
º Able to activate many widgets at the same time on home screen.
º Support all three widget sizes to lets you take full advantage control the whole space of your home screen.
º Many themes available and more will be added

*Please enable location services so our app can can get correct weather data for your widgets.
