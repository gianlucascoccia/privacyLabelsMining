Description

Milady’s best-selling state board licensing Preparation is now available for your iPhone, iPod Touch and iPad! Use the app that helps more Cosmetology students prepare for their licensing exams than any other on the market.

•Relates directly to your Milady Standard Cosmetology 13th Edition Exam Review lessons.
•Choose which chapter to study from so that you can focus on weak areas.
•Rationale is provided for each question making this a perfect on-the-go study solution.
•All questions are in multiple-choice format.
•Randomized questions keep you on your toes and provide a more realistic examination environment.
•“Question of the Day” pulled randomly each day when you open the app.
•Over 1200 questions covering all aspects of your cosmetology curriculum will get your prepared so there are no surprise topics on test day.

For more information on Milady test preparation solutions, go to www.milady.cengage.com or call 800-998-7498 X. 2700.

Pretty good actually

I’m a terrible test taker, I don’t study well, I hardly ever retain information and I finished Cosmetology school a year ago. I went through every chapter, wrote down every answer that I got wrong, redid every chapter until I got an 80% or higher and just studied most of the answers I didn’t get right. All two days before my exam and I passed both portions! Yes, some questions I never encountered in this app but for the most part, I think it really helped refresh me on the stuff I don’t do in my everyday Salon setting. I know it’s pricey but I think it could be worth it! Ask for Apple gift cards for the Apple Store for Christmas and use those!!
