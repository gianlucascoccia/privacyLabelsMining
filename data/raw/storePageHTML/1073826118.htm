Description

Make your visit more magical!
Get Shanghai Disneyland tickets, make Disney Standby Pass and Disney Premier Access selections, find your way around the resort and see estimated wait times for attractions and show schedules.
-Buy tickets and Annual Passes.  
-Check ride wait times. 
-Find schedules for parades, shows, special events and entertainment.  
-Get a Disney Standby Pass for select attractions. 
-Purchase Disney Premier Access to reduce your wait time. Subject to availability. 
-GPS-enabled map to find rides, restaurants, shops near you.
-Get official, up-to-date information on Shanghai Disneyland, Disneytown, Shanghai Disneyland Hotel and Toy Story Hotel.  This app is available in English and Simplified Chinese.
This app uses location services to display Guests’ position on the map.  
These permissions only apply when the Shanghai Disney Resort app is open.
