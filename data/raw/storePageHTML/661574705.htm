Worth the price tag

I first balked at the cost for the full app for my kids, but when comparing it to the cost of paying for an after school tutor for the same subject, and realizing we had some Christmas money from grandma that would more than cover an annual subscription, it seemed like a worthwhile investment as they are on the app everyday (...in order to earn screen time elsewhere. 😄)
