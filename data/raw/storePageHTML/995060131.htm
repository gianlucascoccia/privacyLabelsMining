Description

Cornershop by Uber is the easy way to order fresh food and more online and get it delivered in the same day. Simply tap to order and your Shopper will carefully select, pack, and deliver your products at a time that fits your schedule.

Cornershop by Uber, which became part of the Uber family in 2019, has been helping people do their shopping for over five years. You can use the app to order produce, meats, packaged goods, toiletries, electronics, drinks, and more. Download now for a helping hand and groceries delivered to your door.

To order, simply:

Choose your favorite local store, including grocery, drug stores, and specialty stores.
Select the products you need. You can also add special instructions, like how ripe you want your avocados.
Choose a convenient delivery window.

That’s it.

Your Shopper carefully fills your order and will contact you before they leave the store - just to make sure you didn’t forget anything. It’s that easy.
