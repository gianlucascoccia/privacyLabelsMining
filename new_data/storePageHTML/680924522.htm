Description

Why should I get KANDY? KANDY is for the man who enjoys the virtual company of beautiful women, their personal stories and conversational topics free of politics.

KANDY magazine is a men's magazine featuring photos and interviews of the world's most beautiful and articles covering sports, health and fitness, sex and dating, cars, gadgets, music, movies, television, ... all things men love.

Monthly digital editions consist of interviews and photos of the planet's most beautiful women, the hottest cars, sex and dating advice from swimsuit, runway and lingerie models, opinionated sports columnists, and helpful tech and gadget articles.

Kandy Magazine features:

*The World’s Sexiest Celebrities and Social Influencers

*KANDY Dating – The Kandy girls are here to cure reader dating ills and will share with you tips on how to go from being the hunter to the hunted!

*The Geek Experience - Yeah, we all have a little geek inside of us no matter how hard we try to portray a cool exterior. So, for your inner geek, we provide the latest must-have gadgets for home, auto and office.

*The Buddy Experience - Firsthand, unbiased "test drive" knowledge from "your boy"; guidance and recommendations on vacation spots, cars, watches, cooking, fine dining, wines and spirits.

*KANDY Concierge – As a KANDY reader you are immediately a member of an exclusive inner circle. The Concierge brings you a network and knowledge only 1% of the population can access.

ABOUT IN-APP PURCHASES
In App, you can purchase subscriptions & single issues through your iTunes account.

When purchasing through iTunes, your account will be charged at confirmation of purchase.

Magazine Subscriptions may be managed by you and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to your Account Settings after purchase

Your magazine subscription will auto-renew unless you turn this off 24-hours before the subscription ends.

No cancellation of the current mag subscription is allowed during an active subscription period.

Subscriptions managed by app user from the computer's iTunes Account & auto renewal may be turned off by going to the Account Settings anytime after purchase.

Any unused portion of a free trial period, if offered, will be forfeited when the app user purchases a magazine subscription to that publication.
