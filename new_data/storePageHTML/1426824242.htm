Description

Get 100+ TV channels, live local events and breaking news, movies, TV shows, and more. All for free.

STIRR is available for free on your Apple device. Watch over 100+ channels featuring the latest in entertainment, live music performances from today's top artists, live local events, breaking news, election coverage, national and local sports, movies, documentaries, classic series, cult favorites, and network programming you know and love.

STIRR has you covered with:

• Monster mayhem and sci-fi cult classics
• High-impact action movies
• Thought-provoking documentaries and investigative news specials
• Music concerts featuring today's hottest stars from LiveXLive and more
• Stand-up specials, romcom and comedy flicks

STIRR gives you access to live local entertainment. Watch the ball drop in Times Square. Sit in on a presidential town hall. Cheer on your local high school football team. Don't just watch TV. Experience it. LIVE with STIRR. It’s is the new free TV.

With new channels and a growing on-demand library, you won’t want to miss out. Download STIRR and watch free TV today.

Why was Search function removed?

I’ve been enjoying this app for a year or so, but changes were made in the last update that are unacceptable. First, the Search function was removed, and secondly, my favorite show is gone. Tay B. TH , 01/21/2019

Love it, but not Chromecast compatible

I actually miss having local news and channels, really the only thing I miss about having cable, but very disappointed I cannot cast this app to my tv and have to watch it from my phone (since I tend to be using my phone for other things whilst watching tv). Nonetheless I’ll be using this app everyday in the morning to catch up on weather and news and I’m very happy it’s free! The trending videos/important clips is a nice feature also. The layout is very clean and easy to navigate, haven’t had any buffering issues yet (whereas on my local news app it wouldn’t connect after commercials many times unless I restarted app). Please make this Chromecast compatible!
