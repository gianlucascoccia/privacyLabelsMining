Description

Live, with last-minute updates, from LibriVox database. 7000+ human-read audiobooks. Free! No other apps have more Librivox audio books, and no other apps have more accurate information on librivox audio books.

Read-along: Audio and Text are synchronized. You can read, listen, or read-Along, and switch between them at will.

Have Favorite Readers?
Browse by reader's name. Find all books narrated by your favorite readers.

Relax to enjoy a story:
Full remote control through headset. Finish an entire book with your device safely in your pocket.


In the mood to read a bit?
Text is presented by a sophisticated book reader: beautiful presentation, graceful page turning.
