Description

Eden is the first international dating app for Christians

Eden app offers millions Christians around the world a way to meet new people, find friends or even create a Christian family.

Try Eden and find your soulmate easily!

Our app is absolutely free, and you can find your significant other today!

Eden abilities:
- Swipe left or right to see new people
- Give likes and check the likes you’ve got
- Chat

There are two ways to block a user:
1. By the button in the list
2. By the button in the bottom of the user’s profile
After tapping the “delete” button the user must confirm the wish to delete that user forever.
After you delete an unwanted contact, you will never see each other in the app again.

How to start?

1. Download the app
2. Fill in your profile
3. Indicate your gender, age, height and confession.
