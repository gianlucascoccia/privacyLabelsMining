Description

Try all of Thumsters Parenting App features free for 7 days!

Your Ideal Parenting Partner - Thumsters is the agile and fun incentive based behavioral tracking app, designed to encourage desired behaviors in your children and develop great lifelong behavioral habits. Partnering with you and your child will be our privilege.

"As a busy family of 6, tracking my kids behavior in real time has been hugely beneficial. My kids love getting involved with setting their goals, which they get a huge sense of achievement reaching. It's been so helpful in helping shift hard to budge undesirable behaviors!"

STANDARD FEATURES:
* Set up multiple children
* Thumbs Up Streak tracking
* Change the value of Thumbs Up 'Reasons'
* Access Protection via Pin or Fingerprint
* Provide your child multiple goals / incentives
* Basic Goal Reporting
* Kid friendly design

PREMIUM SUBSCRIPTION FEATURES:
* Multi Device Account Sharing / Magic Code connection
* Advanced Reports
* Custom Behavior Reasons

MULTI-CHILD SETUP
Thumsters allows you to set up and manage multiple family members for free.

STREAK TRACKING
Set up 'streaks' as a fun challenge, where kids can try and beat their previous best 'streak' of Thumbs Up.

THUMB VALUE ALLOCATION
Deal with/encourage a certain behavior your child is currently struggling with by changing the value of each Thumbs Up or Thumbs Down for different behaviors in the app settings.

ACCESS PROTECTION
To prevent kids sneaking their own Thumbs Up, access can be protected via a fingerprint, Face ID or unique Thumsters passcode.

MULTI GOAL SETUP
Setup as many goals / incentives for each child as you like. Once a goal is achieved an interactive fun screen pops up for your child, with the ability for parents to 'set again' that particular goal. This feature is great for recurring goals such as daily screen time.

BASIC REPORTING
* How many thumbs they have been given for each day/period.
* Tracking of their current goals / incentives.
* Which rewards are available/claimed.

KID FRIENDLY DESIGN
With interactive screens, fun graphics and sounds, Thumsters was designed with kids in mind. We engaged an up and coming young talented Australian designer Caleb Gregory (15 years) to help us make the overall 'look and feel' of Thumsters both appealing and relevant.

THUMSTERS SUBSCRIPTION FEATURES
The following features are available on both our monthly and yearly subscriptions.

Multi Device Account Sharing/Family Cloud
(only one subscription required)
The ability to sync the app across multiple devices and family users can be beneficial. This feature may come in handy particularly with Grandparents/Nanny's/in-home-carers, or families with shared custody agreements where children may live in multiple homes. Goals, Thumbs Up and Downs and reports are shared between all devices in real time.

Advanced Reporting
The advanced reports were designed to give a more comprehensive breakdown of your child's behavior. WHAT they are getting the bulk of their thumbs for; this may help you formulate a plan of action for helping them with certain behaviors. WHEN these behaviors are taking place; helps parents discover potential 'patterns' leading to certain behaviors.
