Description

ONLY Tool in the World with Hitting Launch Angle & Exit Velocity and Pitch Speed using standard smartphones, hands free, from anywhere you Manage or Watch the game.

SmartPitch® turns your smartphone into a Hands Free PRECISION SPEED GUN FOR PITCHING & HITTING!!!

ONLY Tool in the World with Hitting Launch Angle & Exit Velocity and Pitch Speed using standard smartphones.

Hands-free, automatic, Use LIVE during the game. Not a toy stop-watch app. As accurate as $1,200 radar guns at less than 1% the cost.

Charts and history of all pitches and hits.

Measures LAUNCH ANGLE, EXIT VELOCITY and DISTANCE for Hitting, with a Heat Map display of results showing which hits are in the Barrels Zone.

Major QuickStart New Super Simple & Quick User Interface gets New Users measuring speeds in seconds. See Tutorial at: https://youtu.be/AuTQUMcdn9s

See Tutorial Videos at: https://www.smartpitchbaseball.com/tutorial-videos
See How-To Blog Posts at: https://www.smartpitchbaseball.com/blog-posts
Contact Us at: info_and_help@smartpitchbaseball.com

Only SmartPitch® offers "FREEDOM OF LOCATION" -- Better than radar or other apps, use SmartPitch from where you watch or manage the game.

* From the dugout.
* From your folding chair behind the foul line.
* From the stands

Use by attaching your phone to a dugout post, or fence or railing, or on a tripod.

Frees you from having to line up exactly behind the catcher to get speeds.

SmartPitch has a Practice Mode for use close to the pitcher in Bullpens, or near the hitter in Batting Practice.

Share SmartPitch® pitching and hitting results and charts with friends on Facebook, Twitter or by email.
