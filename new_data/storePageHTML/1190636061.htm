Description

Nosey is the FREE TV video app with full episodes of the best of Maury Povich, Jerry Springer, Steve Wilkos, Sally Jessy Raphael, Blind Date, Joan Rivers, Surreal Life and much, much more!

Reality, Court and Talk TV shows have never been easier to watch online. Nosey lets you watch wherever, whenever and for as long as you want.

Who cheated? Who’s the father? Who’s to blame? Who’s better off single? Who’ll win big!?

Whether you’ve been watching for decades and wished you could see your favorite episodes again, whether you fell in love with these great shows while staying home sick or staying up late, you can finally watch these tv shows for free online with your phone or tablet!

Wondering “who’s the father”? Maury Povich will answer that question all day long on Nosey.

Relationship Problems? Watch Blind Date and 5th Wheel anytime you want on Nosey.

Remember Sally Jesse Raphael? Remember Joan Rivers? They’re back…on Nosey.

Best of all, Nosey is absolutely FREE!

Unwatchable…now fixed.

OK, the commercials are crap, but, again, they are on a par with the daytime TV stuff shown with OTA programming and I can deal with that. What makes the streaming video unwatchable is an extremely annoying and LOUD 10 second loop played when there aren’t enough commercials to fill the break time (at the time of this review 8-10 repititions). If I am absolutely determined to watch, I kill the sound until the program begins again. This means I’m not listening to any of the paid advertisements at all. Usually, though, I just exit the app and try to understand why I haven’t deleted it yet.

update: the @#$%! commercials and the annoying chanting have been eliminated, MUCH better. Still a few minor bugs, but all in all a distinct improvement.

ProGinix , 01/17/2019

I liked it more before than now

I understand that media needs sponsored advertising, but There are a few things wrong with this app besides the ads. There are a few things I like, which will also be mentioned.
1. If you scroll by mistake, the commercial on the next thumbnail forces you to watch that accident so you can’t scroll back to what you were originally watched.
2. Everything is in a scroll format! I liked exiting and entering each video on an individual basis where it can be seen without any accidental scrolling.
3. The advertisements repeat over an over. When you go BACK to show and try to begin where you left off, the advertisement comes on as soon as you find your place back in the video and starts over as if you didn’t even forward the video ahead
What I do like is the accessibility of Steve Wilkos who is my favorite person and watching him has always given me fresh perspective on society and the world we live in today. This is the only reason I have the Nosey app!
I also like that’s it’s much crisper in the video and the audio is nice too.
I just get turned off watching Nosey due to the other problems above.
