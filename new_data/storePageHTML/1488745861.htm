Description

Do you want to explore the underwater world? Have you ever wanted to be a Mermaid Princess?

Enter the world of mermaids and explore the underwater universe. Pretend to be a beautiful mermaid in the depths of the ocean. Take the mesmerizing experience of living underwater just like Mermaids and many other beautiful sea animals.

This game is made for kids who want to explore the marine world with lots of exciting water games. Enjoy sea adventures like scuba diving and other diving games. Kids can also play different sea games along with sea animals. They can also get ocean experience with various ocean games.

EXCITING STUFF FOR OUR LITTLE UNDERWATER MERMAIDS

Submarine Ride
Take a trip to the vast blue ocean in your own submarine. Wear pretty mermaid dresses and become a part of the sea animal family in the ocean.

Underwater Home
In the Mermaid home, you can check out the wonderfully designed room filled with lots of items. Be a princess stylist and show off your skills. Help your character get ready like a Mermaid Princess.

Supermarket
Whenever your princess is hungry, just head over to the supermarket and get some munchies. Get delicious food items for the characters! There's always something yummy available in this underwater supermarket.

Hospital
It's time to become a doctor and cure patients in your own hospital! This isn't an ordinary hospital, it's totally unique! Play doctor games in this pretend hospital and have tons of fun curing sea animals.

...And so much more!
Along with the other features, our little mermaids will also get the experience of an Underwater Salon, Police station, Restaurant, Daycare, and an Accessories Shop. Play with cute characters at these locations and have lots of fun.

Features of the app are:
* 10 unique rooms to explore.
* Play with fun new characters.
* Touch, drag and explore every object and see what happens!
* Kids friendly content without violence or scary treatments
* Made for kids of ages 6-8, but everyone will enjoy playing this game.

Start exploring the deep blue sea right away. Play exciting water games with fun characters. Download now!
