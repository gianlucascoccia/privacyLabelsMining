Description

Stock Screener offers a free stock & penny stocks market screener to search and screen stocks by as many selection criteria parameters as you wish to define , including share data, technical & analysis and more. SSS is a AI enhanced stock screener using cutting-edge NLP and big data technology to provide stock information by asking a simple query.

Main Features:

Stock Screener: We provide many scanner criteria for active stock picker and new investors
Stock Watch: We use morningstar as our market data vendor to provide nasdaq real time market quote for stock market tracker and stock picker
Stock Alert: Real time stock alerter provides price alert in real time, so our users can get instant alert to check their ideal stock trading and day trade set up
Technical Analysis: Fancy stock charts , technical indicators, drawing tools and our customized Magic 9 indicator for blue chip stocks and penny stocks
Stock News : We are free news apps, provide global News & blog and individual stock news
Fundamental Analysis: stock advisor and retail investors can use our fundamental data for stock analysis. They can check earning report filings, financial data, company summary, shareholders and so on in order for buying stocks and learn stocks

Please send us feedback in the App if you need more features or find any issues.

Amazing potential

I really like this app, from its UI to its great list of features. There are some functions that I think could be added that would greatly add to user functionality, ordered by implementing difficulty. First, being able to screen for volume UNDER a % and not just over. Second, to allow the combining of different screening inputs to bound a screen. For example, volume over 5% but under 10%. This can be accomplished by allowing interaction between inputs or putting bounds as an option for a single input. 3rd, allowing the columns shown to be chosen by users, and to use screening inputs as column options, so if I want to organize the results by the stocks that had the lowest price movement over a month period I could do it. These are things that I believe users of this app would greatly appreciate and would cause it to be many more peoples go-to screening app! I hope you keep up the great work, this app has great potential!

Great Screener

I’ve used quiet a few screeners, and nearly every single one had a paywall, and asking me to spend money to get more than 2 or 3 criteria. That alone makes this one of the way better ones I’ve used (because all are essentially the same, and will obviously give the same results when you enter identical information into the search.)
