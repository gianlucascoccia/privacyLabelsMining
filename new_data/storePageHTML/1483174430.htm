Description

Welcome to the World of Hubble Connected …. Where we empower parents, home owners and expectant mothers to stay connected to what matters most, no matter where they are.

The Hubble Connected app provides one centralized place to manage all your smart prenatal, baby, nursery and home products with the touch of a button, from anywhere in the world.


- SAFE & SECURE
With advanced end-to-end encryption technology and AES 128-bit streaming, you can rest assured knowing that your camera stream and other data is totally private and secure.


- RECORD, STORE & SHARE*
Record videos and image snapshots and save them using Hubble Connected’s secure cloud storage. Or easily share them with family and friends.


- DAILY VIDEO SUMMARY*
Get a quick overview of what went on when you weren’t home. Rewind with a time-lapse Daily Video Summary of the key motion-triggered events from the past 24 hours.


- TWO-WAY TALK
Speak to and hear your loved ones, callers, or intruders from anywhere. Sing your little one to sleep with a lullaby, chat to the delivery guy about where to leave your parcel, or scare off unwelcome visitors in crystal clear sound.


- CREATE VIRTUAL BOUNDARIES**
Only get alerted about the important things with SmartZone. Select specific areas to receive motion detection notifications for, such as doors, gates, and windows.


- VOICE ASSISTANCE
Control your monitor or other voice assistant compatible products hands-free using Amazon Alexa or Google Assistant. Use your voice while multi-tasking to turn on your camera’s recording, ask for the temperature, play a lullaby, and more.


- BABY TRACKER
Hubble Connected’s baby tracker lets you log and track your baby’s development and routines over time, from feeding and sleeping to growth and diaper changes. And when your hands are full, just update the tracker using Amazon Alexa.


- SLEEP & PARENTING TIPS
Access articles and videos with sleep and parenting tips from leading childcare organizations.



HUBBLE CONNECTED PRENATAL: The Hubble Connected app lets Roo users discover a new way to experience the miracle of pregnancy.



- LISTEN, TRACK & SHARE BABY’S HEARTBEAT
Listen to the magical sound of your baby’s heartbeat from the comfort of your home. Save the recordings to cherish the memories forever, and share them with family and friends.


- PRENATAL TRACKER
Conveniently track important prenatal information such as your water consumption, weight, bump, and baby’s kicks, all in one place.


- VOICE ASSISTANCE
Update your prenatal tracker hands-free with Amazon Alexa or Google Assistant. “Alexa, ask Roo to record a kick.”


- EXPERT PREGNANCY ADVICE
Access prenatal tips and advice based on your pregnancy stage. Explore the range of articles and videos, sourced from leading experts.


- PREGNANCY FAQs
Find answers to the most commonly asked FAQs, from morning sickness and back pain to nutrition and exercise.

Camera works fine but app is bad

Not sure why there’s an option for settings if you can’t change any of the settings.

1. There’s a switch on the app to stop the blinking light that WOULD be helpful since my baby is distracted by it when he’s supposed to be trying to go to sleep.
2. The option to turn off notifications doesn’t work either (since I can’t change the camera sensitivity) I had to go to my phone settings and block them from the app manually.
3. Supposedly the times are synced to match my phone, but that isn’t the case since it’s way off.
4. It would be nice if you could have the sound on the camera off and then turn it on when you open the app if you choose to, sometimes I need to open the app near my sleeping baby in the dark and it is very loud.

*UPDATE* from 2 star review to 4 star

After hearing back from customer support, problems 1-3 were solved, as well as one I forgot to mention, the ability to control music/white noise from the app.

All I had to do was delete and reinstall the app. Just be sure to save all of the photos and videos from the gallery because those will be erased!

Number 4 is still an issue and I hope someone takes it into consideration.
Thanks!
