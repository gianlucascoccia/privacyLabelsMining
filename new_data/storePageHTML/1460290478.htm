Description

First 10 minutes of transcription are free! Get your audio transcribed with our AI-driven technology. We offer speaker recognition, direct exporting to PDF, TXT, or DOCX.

Meet your new personal Transcriber app, crafted to transcribe your voice notes into text. Transcriber saves you precious time and transcribes your audio in no time. Whether it’s interviews, lectures, meetings, or simply your quick voice notes, Transcriber can do it all.

Record your audio directly in the app, or import an already existing file and let Transcriber do the work, while you work on what is really important.

Save yourself some time and money. Thanks to the latest AI technology we will deliver a quick, cheap and precise transcription of your audio files in a few taps, while providing you with maximum privacy.

FEATURES:
- 10 minutes of transcription for free to test if Transcriber is the right choice for you
- Cutting-edge AI technology
- Speaker recognition
- Unique in-app editor to edit the transcript in the app
- Import or export from/to - Slack, Mail, Messages, WhatsApp, Google Drive…
- All transcripts can be exported in PDF, TXT or DOCX.
- Smooth and easy-to-use design
- No Ads

FREE CHECK BEFORE PURCHASE
Have the 1st minute of each of your recordings transcribed for free before spending money, to see if the quality of the recording is good enough for Transcriber to provide a meaningful transcription.

SUBSCRIPTION:
Our standard Subscription Plans are:
* Monthly Subscription (Lite, Basic, Pro or Ultimate Plan).

All plans cover all transcriber features + additional transcription time.

If you choose to get one of our plans, your payment will be charged to your iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase.
Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period. The cost of the renewal depends on your Subscription Plan.

Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user's Account Settings after purchase.
When cancelling a subscription, your subscription will remain active until the end of the period. Auto-renewal will be disabled, but the current subscription will not be refunded.
