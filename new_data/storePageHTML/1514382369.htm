Description

Canvas: Pocket 3D Room Scanner has been renamed Canvas for Homeowners to distinguish it from our pro-focused app, Canvas: LiDAR 3D Measurements.

Canvas for Homeowners is the only 3D scanning app designed for homeowners working remotely with home improvement professionals. Simply scan a room, upload your scan, and a complete set of 3D measurements will be sent to your professional, in the format they use every day. You will need a code from your professional to ensure your scan is sent to them.

If you are a professional looking to use Canvas and have a LiDAR-enabled iPad or iPhone, we strongly recommend using our other app for professionals, Canvas: LiDAR 3D Measurements. If you don't have a LiDAR-enabled iPad or iPhone, you may also use this app to test out the experience, but LiDAR will make scanning 5x more accurate.

Canvas for Homeowners requires an iPhone 8 or above and delivers the best scan quality and accuracy with the LiDAR-enabled iPhone 12 Pro/Pro Max or iPhone 13 Pro/Pro Max.

Smooth1784 , 06/20/2021

Absolute garbage

Literally any room scanning app can do what this one does. Most of the time better. The usage of LiDAR is a gimmick. You only get more resolution in a scan using it which allows for subjectively more accuracy. That doesn’t mean anything when room scanning other than a boost in processing speed. That boost is ignored by this company forcing users to upload their scans for processing. Don’t buy into this greedy company. There are plenty of alternatives that offer the exact same functionality. Most don’t even need the LiDAR Scanner but simply benefit from having it. They also don’t require you to upload a customers private information to this company’s servers to “process”. The amount of information that can be harvested from scans like these is sickening. GPS data, network information, any photos or family pictures hanging on the wall, a full blueprint of a space along with the location of all the valuables. You are forced to upload all of it and give it over to be stored and processed remotely for anything usable. I’m guessing the steel sack it takes to then charge folks $0.15/ sq ft for these scans is so that the information can be adequately safeguarded and encrypted but I highly doubt that the case. Even if the info is properly handled, what stops this company from just selling that info to advertisers for a nice amount? Don’t be fooled by a gimmick.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for taking the time to review our app, even if you didn't have a good experience. It sounds like you may be looking for a general 3D room scanning app, and you're right that there are many apps that can generate a 3D scan, especially with LiDAR. What Canvas specializes in is making that 3D scan data useful for professionals in the home improvement industries - people who need to capture field conditions and measurements and use that data in CAD or BIM programs. We charge $0.15 per sqft for that conversion to various CAD and BIM formats, but make the ability to scan (as well as export) free. We'd also like to add that we in no way sell uploaded data to advertisers, and because we charge for Scan To CAD processing, we really have no incentive to do so. We would welcome an opportunity to discuss further - please contact us at support@canvas.io and reference your App Store review.
