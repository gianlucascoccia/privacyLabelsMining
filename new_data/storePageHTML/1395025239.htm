Description

America's free TV guide for iPhone & iPad for all your TV needs including Free, cable, satellite, NBC, ABC, CBS & more TV listings. Never Miss A TV Show Again!

Introducing America's free TV guide app!

Download the latest TV times to your device and get all your viewing needs in one place!

Simply enter your local ZIP code to find out what's on TV! You can also search the guide to find out when your favorite TV show is on this week and add your most watched shows to a favorites list.

Viewers can browse the schedule of every channel, listed in every region of the United States, including Free Over-The-Air antenna channels, satellite and on cable TV. If a TV channel is not often watched, it can be removed from the schedule instantly and put back on if desired.

- Clear and easy-to-use interface
- All features are 100% free to use
- Look up instantly what's on TV right now, tonight or later this week
- Includes USA TV listings for every channel on every service including Free Over-The-Air antenna TV and cable!
- Save favorites to your list and get notified before it airs
- Direct links to IMDB & Rotten Tomatoes!
- Sync with ontvtonight.com or create a brand new account!
- Never miss a TV show again!

Great when it doesn’t freeze

I have this app on an iPad. It’s laid out in manner that’s easy to read. I can fine tune it for the channels that I’m interested in. I can read the content for each show. I can mark my favorites so I’m alerted when they are scheduled. But what’s driving me crazy is that I cannot slide the screen sideways to view the entire selection for that range request. I have tried moving to favorites or channels and back again, and sometimes this works. But like today, sometimes it Doesn’t. So I know what’s coming on at 6pm ( when I Don’t watch tv) but I have no clue what’s coming on later in the evening when I normally view the idiot box. 🙁 I have even deleted this app and reinstalled in an effort to correct this. That wasn’t a fix. I have deleted this app in frustration and searched for another app but couldn’t find anything else that I liked as well. So here I am with my favorite tv app that may or may not show me if anything worthwhile is lighting up the screen tonight. Life is so much more than a tv box but isn’t it nice when the small things can bring us joy?!

hrdillard , 11/01/2019

The best I’ve used for OTA programming

It’s been hard to find a guide that’s been accurate, streamlined, customizable, and easy to use for cord cutters. It lets me save favorite shows and sends alerts before those shows start. It lets me hide the channels I don’t watch. It lets me look up shows a week in advance. Before I found this app, I was using 2 different apps to get what I wanted. This one combines all the things I liked about each of those plus adds some more and doesn’t crash like they did. I don’t know if this is useful for those with cable, but if you only have an antenna, give this app a try.
