Description

The official app of GamerDVR.com, the #1 platform for Xbox clips and screenshots.

It has never been easier to view, download, edit, and share your Xbox clips and screenshots with all compatible social media apps! Discord, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, WhatsApp and many more.

- Edit your clips (and soon screenshots) with professional effects, then download or share the final masterpiece
- Download your clips for offline viewing and backup
- Easily view all your friends Xbox clips and screenshots as well

As the official app of GamerDVR.com, you can expect many continuous new features, updates, and more to give you the absolute best Xbox clip and screenshot sharing experience.

For great features such as Albums, Playlists, Twitch integration, YouTube uploads, Challenges, Comments, Achievements and more, checkout GamerDVR.com from your phone, desktop or laptop computer!

Amazing, but...

If your a gamer or use social media, this app is great! You can easily upload clips to your camera roll or instantly share them to twitter or instagram. The only problem I have had with the app is that whenever I share my fortnite clips to Instagram, it cuts out the sides of the screen and the clip only shows the middle. It doesn’t do that with other videos and it’s irritating because some of my clips focus on the side of the screen and I can’t upload them. Other then that, I would highly recommend this app and I hope you fix my issue!!!

Developer Response ,

Hi, this is an Instagram issue as Instagram only allows square videos and pictures so it crops them. Appreciate the review either way.

Error

For as long as I’ve used the app, it’s been pretty much my go to for watching my clips and downloading them. But lately I’ve been getting tons of error codes or the app won’t be able to get any my clips or screenshots. This isn’t just for me. I can’t view my friends clips as well without getting any error about not being able to get the clips. Everything else still works fine like it has in the past but these error codes are getting more and more common than they used to

Update: there are no more errors but I can definitely say that app has a mind of its own. Some days it shows me what I want and there are no problems. Other times I gives me error codes, won’t bring up my clips but will bring up my friends clips, or won’t recognize any of my friends clips just to name a few. The app works but there will be days of frustration with this app
