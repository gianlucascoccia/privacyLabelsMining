Description

7News WHDH-TV is Boston's News Station and with this app all our resources are at your fingertips. You can live stream all our newscasts along with breaking news and weather coverage and watch other important events as they unfold. You'll get up-to-the-minute developments on the stories you care about, your weather forecast, warnings about traffic or safety issues in your area, coverage of our local sports teams and the stories everyone is talking about. You can receive push notifications for breaking news, live video streams, and weather alerts that matter to you.

FEATURES :
VIDEO : Live stream 7News newscasts and breaking news, watch the latest forecast from the 7Weather team, and play hundreds of news videos.
SHARE : Send in your videos and photos with our Send it to 7 feature and see some of them on air. You can also tip us off to a story you think we should be covering.
WEATHER : Track storms live with our interactive radar and view a list of the latest closings and delays. Check out current weather conditions, your 7-Day forecast, special weather maps, and blogs from Pete Bouchard, Jeremy Reiner, Chris Lambert and Bri Eggers.

Download our 7News app today so you can get caught up whenever you want, wherever you are.
