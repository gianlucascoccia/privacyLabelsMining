Description

Stage|Tracks Mobile is the exclusive app to play music from Right On Cue Services. This app only downloads and plays music provided by Right On Cue Services. With Stage|Tracks Mobile, you gain more control than a CD player provides, with the convenience and simplicity of an iOS device. Stage|Tracks Mobile is designed to be the best solution to control, automate, and customize your show, both in rehearsal and performance.

Right On Cue Services provides music under Grand Rights for Musical Theater. This means we provide music only for entire musicals and you must secure the rights to perform the musical from its respective licensing company. Before Right On Cue Services can send you music you must provide proof of rights from the licensing company.

Stage Tracks is only meant to play music from ROCS and does not play music from other sources.
