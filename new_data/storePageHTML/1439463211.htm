Happymedia , 07/07/2020

Good service, but too many fees

I enjoy having the service and payment into the account is prompt. It’s very convenient on the road and I’ve now started using the card for more every day purchases we’re not on the road. The downside or the numerous fees are tacked on to nearly every transaction and the fact that if I transfer to my personal account it takes days to reach that account. This was a company issued card that I only have access to and how payments to me are made. With that being said, it takes days to transfer into my personal account that my spouse has access to for her and my children’s every day needs while I am away.

Developer Response ,

Hi there, there is zero fee (free) using your card anywhere mastercard is accepted for personal purchases. That includes paying your personal bills, shopping in store and online etc. If you feel there are fees being charged, please reach out to comdataonroad@comdata.com and we would be happy to walk through transaction history for you and validate that all is as expected. Thanks so much for being apart of the Comdata family!
