Description

Summons Partner is an All-In-One Solution for law enforcement officers in New York State. The app provides you with references to summonses, overtime calculator, RDO calendar and much more.

APP FEATURES
• Reference guide to Summonses
• RDO Calendar
• Overtime Calculator
• NYPD Patrol Guide
• Most common TPO and 61 samples
• Reference guide

SUMMONSES
• A, B, C, TAB, ECB and OATH summonses
• Traffic court testimonies
• Accusatory text
• Favorites list or so called hammer list

RDO CALENDAR
• Supports Patrol, SRG, steady RDO and custom RDO calendar
• Keep track of Vacation’s & IVD
• iCloud sync
• iOS calendar sync
• Today’s view widget with Dark Mode support

OVERTIME CALCULATOR
• My Tour option with automatic date and time entry
• Detailed breakdown by month, quarter, year
• Option to track Paid Detail
• Easily edit and account for any entry
• Time or cash split and travel time
• Overtime and paid detail rate
