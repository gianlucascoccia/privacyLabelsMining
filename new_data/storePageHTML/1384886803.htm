Description

Miogym application gives the great opportunity to transform training (develop facial muscles, logopedic treatment, face yoga) into an ​interesting game

SPEECH THERAPY APP
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
This speech app is designed to transform “Speech therapy into an interesting game”. Each speech therapy exercise has a special game card with fascinating animation that ensures a child’s interaction with an app.
Take for instance, “Eating pizza” game card for language therapy. When a child starts repeating the speech therapy exercise (opening the mouth – eating a slice of pizza), the speech app recognizes this movement and accelerates animation.

The first 2 game levels are free, while subsequent levels are available with a subscription: weekly, monthly, permanent.
First game cards are available for free in each of the 11 categories. The app is free for the first three days of the trial period.

COLLECTION OF EXERCISE IN THE SPEECH THERAPY APPLICATION:

¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
• Exercises “First Sounds”. This is a collection of exercises that helps children begin to make the first sounds. These are sounds of the outside world, sounds of animals or sounds of transport. Also, these exercises allow you to develop the pronunciation of sounds s, r, l, t and many others with which the child has difficulty in pronunciation.

• Exercises "Speech exercise". These exercises are designed to develop articulation in children. The constant practice of speech therapy will allow you to quickly begin to pronounce sounds and words.


Children are reluctant to perform speech therapy because they are too bored. Faced with this problem, many children have difficulty developing speech and even disorders such as Apraxia and Dyslexia. We decided to use mobile apps as a fun tool for interactive children's speech learning. The application also prevents the development of problems such as Apraxia and Dyslexia.

The Miogym speech application also helps children with autism and promotes child development. The Autism application is one of the areas we are developing. Autism is hard, but we try to make it easier for children to learn.

After consulting with a speech therapist, together we made the best plan of speech exercises and training sets for children.

The exercise plan was implemented in the Miogym application in the speech therapy game format. Through interactive exercises, the child receives rewards and opens new levels.
All kids love smartphones - that's a fact, so we want kids to spend time in front of the phone with the benefit of themselves, learning to speak. Parents also reduce the difficulty of achieving good pronunciation in their children. And any speech therapist can give interesting tasks as homework in the format of the game with the help of a speech therapy app. This approach will save the speech therapist's time and that of the parents.

The speech therapy application is for toddlers and children 2,3,4,5,6,7 years old.

Now Miogym is actively used by speech therapists and parents around the world to teach children. Download the app and enjoy your kid's development.

From a speech therapist: DO NOT DOWNLOAD

I am a licensed speech language pathologist and YOU SHOULD NOT GET THIS APP!!!! This app uses non speech oral motor exercise (i.e., a technique that does not require a child to produce a sound) which are unethical and SHOULD NOT BE DONE. There is countless articles and literature that state explicitly why these exercise are counterproductive and not an appropriate treatment for speech sound errors! Just google “non speech oral motor exercises” and the top results will be articles discussing why these strategies are not recommended. Do not fool yourself into thinking moving your jaw around, blowing, or sticking out your tongue will improve your child’s articulation or speech production. If you’re working on imitation skills, fine go ahead, BUT DO NOT USE THESE EXERCISES FOR ARTICULATION OR PHONOLOGICAL THERAPY!!! Furthermore, the app is highly inaccurate! It wasn’t even directed at my face and it gave me credit. This could help your child practice things INACCURATELY and could further impede your practice. Go to a licensed speech therapist that does their research and stay away from apps like this!

Developer Response ,

Thank you for your re­view!
Our application has not only non speech oral motor exercises but also speech exercises.
The article notes that «Between 71.5% and 85% of practicing clinicians use some type of NSOMEs in therapy to change children's speech productions». Miogym app is an additional tool to improve the result of speech therapy and it is not positioned as the main therapy.

We will be happy to discuss your opinion in more detail. Please email us at info@miogym.com

Many factors affect the quality of recognition of an exercise. We had 2 versions of the application on the developing stage with different recognition configs. We gave them to the kids and watched them use different versions. One version of the application was completely controlled by recognition and the other was partially controlled. Most children could not perform the exercises correctly. And because of this, they were very upset and quickly lost interest in these exercises when the game was fully dependent on recognition.  Therefore, we have developed an algorithm that helps children do exercises without losing interest in doing them.
