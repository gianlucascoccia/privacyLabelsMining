Description

The HOTWORX application is indented for members of our HOTWORX Studio facilities. The purpose of this application is to encourage users to track their total amount of calories burned during a HOTWORX workout session, and to track their "1-hour after-burn." A HOTWORX workout is 40 minutes, and the after-burn is 1 hour. The total session time will capture the calories burned for a total of 100 minutes.

This app integrates with the Health app.

live4hotworx , 01/31/2021

Amateur design

I love hotworx. I live for it, honestly. I go every single day. However, the app is very poorly designed. I hate how I have to log in and go through several screens every time I open the app. Also, there is no back or refresh buttons, which means you have to go back and log in again if you are trying to book an appointment and the app has been idle for several minutes.

The activity feature is a mess! If you want to record your hotworx calories from your Apple watch, you cannot simultaneously record a workout in the watch workout app. I hate this because I love tracking my workout data, and it’s not the same if I can’t do that on my watch. So, to get around this, I started manually recording the calories. It’s fine I guess, but you HAVE to input an after burn or else it doesn’t record your session calories. The after burn never works, it always significantly decreases the amount of calories I put in, and sometimes it even records negative calories!!! Also, you can’t do anything else within the app while you have a workout or after burn running.

Overall, I do hope hotworx will find some advanced developers because this app needs major improvements.

AngelF09 , 01/11/2021

Love the facility, hate the app

I love the hotworx facility, but their app could use a lot of improvements. It’s annoying that I have to log in every single time in order to make appointments or to view my current appointments. The app is so slow (especially since I upgraded to sweat everywhere). It takes five minutes or longer to load the location. I honestly thought it was not working the first couple times I tried, so I closed the app out and went back in, but the same thing just kept happening. Then, once I am able to log in, I can’t even click to make an appointment from the next screen that comes up. I have to hit “view appointments” and then click on the “book by session/time” on the next screen. Its like the two other buttons don’t work on the initial page. It’s also extremely frustrating that my garmin fitness tracker does not sync with this app. Garmin is extremely popular, but it isn’t integrated into this app? I don’t understand that. More improvements absolutely need to be made.
