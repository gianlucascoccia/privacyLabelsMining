SilverSpider2103 , 05/27/2021

Love the game, but a teensy bit dark in subject matter

This game is great, I love checking in a couple times a day on my island of cats and making the place better for them.
However, I noticed that although the game is all cute and fun on the surface, some details and lines of dialogue in the game hint at the nature of the game being a lot darker and more depressing than it looks. Granted, it took me a few days of playing to notice the signs and maybe it’s obvious, but it seems like this island that the cats are traveling to is their version of the afterlife. I don’t know if it was intended to be this way, but there are a couple of cats whose character descriptions mention that they are wondering where their humans are, and that they haven’t seen them in a long time. That coupled with the fantastical nature of the game leads me to believe that you are creating a utopia of an afterlife for all of the cats you invite. This makes me want to play it more, I want to make my island as comfortable as possible for these cats, even if they are just clumps of pixels on a screen. If the game was intended to be this way, then I applaud the developers in leaving the lore in character descriptions and small details, for those who are curious to piece them together.
Overall, this is a phenomenal game, and I hope it just gets bigger as time goes on, or more games like it are developed by the same team.

zubkeon , 03/23/2021

Cured my depression

This game cured my depression when three different antidepressants and therapy couldn’t so that should tell you something. In the beginning I was skeptical because I wasn’t sure I’d like the game, but I stuck with it for only like a half hour or so and fell in love with it. It’s so cute and entertaining and I could play for hours. There are enough goals and achievements and new unlocks, that you never get bored and there’s always something cute that comes with it. Like for every new location you get, you also get new animations from the characters because they can do new stuff with each place. This game is so well thought out and has the player in mind; there aren’t too many ads and the ones that exist are either optional or not obnoxious and actually helpful or interesting (there’s a guide that is in place of some ads so you learn some stuff from that). I cannot recommend this game enough, I never write reviews but I have never found a game that isn’t solely created for monetary gain. You can buy stuff with money in this game but you never feel pressured to nor is it necessary for fun gameplay. If you’re on the fence about this game, get it. It’s so worth it and such a fun time consumer. It’s also not draining of battery which is insane seeing how beautiful and thought out it is.

If you love cats and are looking for a game that is both peaceful, pleasant, and enjoyable I would definitely recommend! I have been playing this game for a couple months, and it is my favorite game I’ve played on my phone. I’ve struggled to find a game that i can put a lot of time in and one that i can enjoy at this same time. I’ve put hours into it!
