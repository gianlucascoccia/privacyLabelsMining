Description

Welcome to Dragon Tamer - a turn-based strategy game that tests your skills in taming and mastering the art of dragonery.

Are you ready to prove yourself as the ultimate Dragon Tamer? Then let's find out! This world is full to the brim with rare & powerful dragons. It's up to you to tame them all! Collect dragon eggs, raise & breed new dragons, and train them to form your own unstopable league. Show off your skills and fight against Tamers from around the world.
Join us in the Dragon Tamers!

Features:
- Build, expand, and decorate your Dragon Island paradise.
- Side with your Dragon in new events and daily quests.
- Over 100+ Dragons to Collect.
- Breed new species, train your dragons, and unlock new abilities.
- Lead your unique lineup of dragons to victory.
- Challenge Dragon Tamers from around the world, participate in Alliance events, battle in the Arenas, and rise to the top of the leaderboards.

The minimum specs needed to play Dragon Tamer:
- 2 GB of Ram

What’s New

Oct 27, 2021

Version 1.0.27

1.New Dragons
- Titania, Legendary, Nature/Dark, can be obtained from the Breeding Season.
- Glaucus, Legendary, Water/Light, can be obtained from the Time-Limited Summon event.
- Emerald, Epic, Nature/Earth, can be obtained from the Monthly Sign-in Event.

2.Limited-Time Benefits
These offers last only for 3 hours. You can see a maximum of 3 packs one time.

3.Balance Changes
1)Magics
- Origin Magic
-> Each attack made against an ally has an 18%/21%/24%/27%/30% chance to bless the ally under attack, reducing one of their random Skill cooldowns by 1 turn. It will also increase your Speed Bar by 10%.
- Protection Magic:
-> Increases DEF and Effect RES by 16%/19%/22%/25%/28% (can be dispelled) and erects a Shield that absorbs DMG equal to 15%/16%/18%/19%/20% of Max HP for 2 turns.
- Health Magic
-> Restores 4%/5%/6%/7%/8% of Max HP before turn begins.
- Counter Magic
-> When your Dragon takes DMG, there is an 18% chance they’ll fight back against the attacker. This will also increase their ATK by 5% for 10 turns.
- Curse Magic
-> Deals 20%/22%/24%/26%/28% of the highest ATK among alive allies as Curse DMG to the enemy after it acts.
- Revenge Magic
-> Buff provided by the Revenge Magic can be dispelled.

2)Nerfed the 4-pieces Baldr Set Bonus
-> When you are critically hit, there is a 100% chance that you and a 30% chance that your allies will gain a Shield lasting 1 turn that absorbs DMG equal to 5% of your Max HP.

3)Island Brawl
- Lowered the AI bot’s HP by 25% and their DEF by 30%
- Reduced the number of mines from 100 to 50
- Adjusted the required player level for searching mines:
Lv.1 Mine: From Lv.45-69 to Lv.45-74
Lv.2 Mine: From Lv.60-99 to Lv.65-99
Lv.3 Mine: From Lv.85-150 to Lv.90-150

4) Skills
- Venom Bomb(Max): Deals 177% of ATK as Dark DMG to an enemy, and 64% of ATK as additional DMG for 10 turns. It will also decrease their DEF by 30% for 3 turns.
- Divine Impact(Max): Deals 112% of ATK as Light DMG to an enemy, and restores 14% of MAX HP to the ally with the lowest HP percent.
- Parasite Attack(Max): Deals 15% of caster's Max HP as Nature DMG to an enemy, and restores 20% of MAX HP to the ally with the lowest HP percent.

5) Increased Nymph's HP Growth from B to A

4.Other Improvements
- Gold and food obtained from limited-time packs and watching ads are scaled with the level
- Improved some daily requests’ rewards
- Now, the Forest of Sprites resets every Monday and lasts for 7 days.
- Reduced the required number of Advanced Farms for repairing Canoe from 6 to 3.
- Added a sound effect for forging Artifacts.
- Improved UI/UX

5.Bug Fixes
