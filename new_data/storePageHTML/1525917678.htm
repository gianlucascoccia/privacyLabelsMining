Description

Learn to dance, have fun, and sweat away the stress with your favorite champion and celebrity dancers! Featuring pre-recorded and live videos with Dancing With the Stars and So You Think You Can Dance professionals, Dance & Co is here to make you happier & healthier through dance education, fitness, and entertainment so you never stop moving, grooving, and improving!

Enjoy exclusive interviews, performances, and dance content that you can't find anywhere else! Learn various dance styles including ballroom & latin, hip hop, lyrical, contemporary, jazz, Tik Tok, and more! Treat your body with special warm up, stretching, yoga, and dance fitness classes.

Access videos any time, any place, and on any device with one log in! Become a member today and have fun with the Dance & Co crew!

So much more than a "dance" app, this is a comprehensive body movement encyclopedia.

I hesitated initially to purchase this app, as I already follow some online trainers and attend dance classes. I'm also a former personal trainer and coach, so I was a bit skeptical at first.

This program is so comprehensive and offers so many fun, interesting and challenging different exercises that I just want to use it all day long. Any kind of dance you could want or imagine, as well as stretching routines, bodyweight exercise routines, tap, jazz, ballroom, ballet...is right here at your fingertips. You can pick a short program or a longer one. Work through a series, try different teachers. (They are all really great, by the way!).

This app does it all and is worth every penny. They've thought of everything, constantly adding new material...these guys will work as hard as you do!! Get this app, it will make you smile every time you use it. And you'll want to use it a lot!

LadyByrum , 10/03/2020

So thankful for this app!!

I am LOVING this app!! With two small kids (and a COVID pandemic, of course), it’s so hard to get to a gym. The only form of exercise I enjoy is dance, but social dancing is not an option right now, and this app is a perfect alternative to both of those things! I feel like I’m taking lessons from my friends (because I’ve been following DWTS for years). The lessons are so much fun—I am actually motivated to work out every day. They get my heart racing, it’s convenient to access from anywhere you are, there’s constantly new content, they cater to every skill level, and the teachers are all so encouraging and talented. Thank you thank you thank you for such a fun, easy way to get active and learn new skills during a really depressing year! Everyone download it, it’s worth the cost of admission, I promise.
