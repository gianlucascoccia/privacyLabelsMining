Description

Version 6 is here! Modernized UI, smart event search, simplified invitations and Pro subscription Family Sharing and Kid Mode. Happy Carpooling!

Carpool-Kids is the best way to organize family carpooling with your friends. Finally an easy way to know who is driving and riding on any given day. Carpool-Kids is the top rated family carpooling app on the App Store for good reason – accept no imitations!

Features:
• Organize carpools with any number of drivers and riders
• Instant customized updates via push notification and/or e-mail
• One or two-way carpool events
• Event Reminders
• Mapping for Event and Friend Locations
• Sync events to your iCloud Calendar (Pro)
• Graphical Driving stats (Pro)

Organization Made Easy

I love this app. We started a carpool with 4 family’s to and from school this year and this app saved the day! It makes it so easy to see who is driving and which kids will be participating each trip. With the swipe of a finger kids can be in or out for any specific trip. Our 5th graders are able to look st the app to see who is driving each way. Makes carpooling what it is supposed to be, helpful! I highly recommended it!

bradrogersau , 08/15/2016

Great Start! Really, Really Needs a...

I love this app. But it's solution to our carpool problem is very clunky. We have 11 kids, so they obviously can't get into one car. So we have to create multiple events for the same event. The problem is that sometimes we can fit them all in 3 cars, but other times it takes 5. We don't know how many cars are needed ahead of time. So we still have to discuss back and forth until we gone in on a solution...even tho we still use the app. Our lives would be much simpler if multiple drivers could sign up for an event and each creates a number of available seat slots. Please please please add this feature soon. As it stands the app simplifies already simple carpools. Please help simplify our complicated carpool!!

Dance mom on the go , 10/19/2021

This has been helpful

I use this app to organize a carpool for my daughter’s dance classes along with a couple other parents. It has definitely been helpful.
But how can I view all events/drives that have been entered? I am only seeing about a month at a time even though you can create events/drives farther into the future. I upgraded to the pro version thinking it would let me access them but it doesn’t.
This is an issue for us parents that need to plan farther in advance and we have had the issue of inputting the same event/drive multiple times because we can’t see that it has already been done.

Developer Response ,

Hello!

As a Pro user you can create & view up to 12 months in the future or past. Use the “…” button on the upper left of the Events list and change the “Time Period” to any month option from 1-12.

Hopefully this info will earn the app that ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️!

Thanks and Happy Carpooling!
