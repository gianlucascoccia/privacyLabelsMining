gratefulrag , 10/24/2020

A Daily Necessity

I’ve been using this app daily for ~6 or 7 years and I absolutely cannot live without it! The app originally provided a daily bible verse only with an applicable prayer. This alone was wonderful because you could read the verse, then open your bible, go to that passage to get more context, and learn/gain insight everyday. Over the years they’ve added features that have improved the experience even more - e.g. reflectional questions with the option to record in a journal, and inspirational anecdotes from various followers of Christ. They often include stories about historical figures whom most people have never heard of (e.g. writers of hymns who had incredibly difficult lives). I’ve learned so much! And just now they’ve added an archive of all their prior bible verses and reflections/stories from the past. You can even browse by topic! More than being thrilled to experience this app and write a review, I’m also incredibly grateful to its creators and contributors. To those involved with this app, you have made a difference in this man’s life and have undoubtedly contributed to my improved relationship with and closeness to Christ. Thank you! God Bless.
