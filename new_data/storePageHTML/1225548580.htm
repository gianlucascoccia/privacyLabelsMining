Bohemian Kimi , 07/11/2020

Highly recommended

This game is one I have played on and off for a year or two now..maybe a bit longer. Every time I have had any type of issue, the support team is always pretty quick to get back with me and very helpful in resolving whatever the issue is; and they’re always really nice on top of it. You can play solo or join in PVP competitions. Every day there are small missions helping to earn money, gems, and other helpful items. Playing in endless mode earns gems with every 1,000 enemies you destroy. Due to these little helpful details, you don’t *have* to spend money to enjoy the game by any means-but every time I have spent money on a ship or package-I have not come to regret it, or felt they misled me to believe it was more worthwhile than it was. Spending the 3 or 4 dollars every month for the daily sign in bonus is worth it-even if you do not log in for a week, you start back up right where you were at-you do not miss any of your bonuses. This a great game with hours of enjoyment, and highly recommendable.

Great support

I lost track how long I’ve been playing this game, about 3 years. I love this game. I have downloaded several other games to give them a try and I always eventually delete them in short time and return to my favorite action play. While there’s a lot of hand eye coordination the play is quite simple. Kill everything and avoid getting hit by dropped bombs. Not at all a pay to play which I love. However I like this game so much I feel it’s important to support the makers for entertaining me. Some of the hardcore ships absolutely cost money, but success is easily earned on other paths without paying for them. I had 2 of my ships mistakenly locked. I contacted the command post and after displaying proof of ownership they promptly unlocked them. It’s like getting a girlfriend back. I felt very well supported. Thank You Command Post.
