Description

Email questions and concerns to support@favoreatsapp.com

Favoreats is a social meal planning and recipe sharing app that connects you with your favorite foodie friends to inspire and simplify your meal planning. Favoreats will show you which recipes your friends like and are using in their meal plans, which gives you a steady flow of ideas for your own plans.

Connect:
By connecting with your trusted friends and foodies on Favoreats, you can see their favorite recipes as well as the recipes they are using in their meal plans so that you can be inspired with ideas for your own meal plans.

Collect:
Easily import your favorite recipes from your Pinterest boards and trusted recipe websites so that they can be added to your meal plan and shopping list with a single tap. And any recipes you pin to Favoreats connected Pinterest boards going forward will automatically be added to your Favoreats collections.

Create:
Add any recipe on Favoreats to your shopping list and meal plan with a single tap—simple. You can also scale the servings of any recipe on Favoreats up and down to meet your needs.

Favoreats key features:

See your friends' meal plans as well as the recipes they are trying and recommending

1-tap add any recipe in Favoreats to your meal plan

Shopping list is automatically created for you

Pinned recipes are automatically added to your mapped Favoreats collections

Save recipes from your favorite recipe blogs and websites with a single tap

Explore friends' recipe collections and/or add your own

Feeling like you need some recipe inspiration? Want to know what your friends and neighbors are making for dinner or what new recipes they have tried and would recommend? Favoreats (your “favorite eats”) is the app that lets you browse those recipes! In addition to connecting with others about meal ideas, you can store your own recipes, curate collections from the internet, make a weekly meal plan, and then use the shopping list generated from your meal plan to pick up everything you need at the store.

Join Favoreats, the free app that will simplify and inspire your meal planning from now on!

Additionally, Premium subscriptions are available for $11.99 per month or $69.99 per year and include the ad free experience.

You can turn off auto-renewal by going to your Account Settings after purchase. No cancellation of your subscription is allowed during the active subscription period. Any unused portion of a free trial or introductory period, if offered, will be forfeited when you purchase a subscription, where applicable.

RickyLyn48 , 06/29/2019

Not happy about the pop-ups

Was loving this app and how quick and convenient meal planning was. Favorite features are the shopping list and ability to move around scheduled meals to different days as needed.

Would love to see the ability to consolidate recipes into new or multiple categories. Also would love a way to add my personal recipes.

Recently I’ve had to wait several seconds to even use the app once I open it due to all the advertising pop ups. Additionally, I’ve been asked to pay an EXTRA $1/month to get rid of the ads. Pretty bummed about this. Would have preferred to pay $20 for the app than be locked into paying $1 a month for a lifetime when I go in waves of using it depending on my current life schedule.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for the feedback. Like you, we don't love the ads. For that reason we offer the ad free subscription. Unfortunately if we move to a paid download model like you suggest, many fewer people will ever give us a look. It's a tough balancing act. We hope you find much more than $1/month value from Favoreats such that paying the $1/month subscription rate is overwhelmingly worth it!

In terms of the features you suggest, we already offer both saving recipes to multiple collections and adding your own recipes. Swipe on any recipe tile in the app and choose "Move Collections" to manage which collections you would like to assign to the recipe. The add your own recipe feature is accessible from the "New Recipe" menu on the profile screen. Please email support@favoreatsapp.com for additional help with these or any other feature. We are always happy to help.

Subscription Baiting

I used to love this app and all it had to offer. However, when it wouldn’t let me save meals to my plan I was a bit frustrated and confused after years of being able to do so (pre-the decision for developers to start charging for services). So I decided to buy the basic monthly subscription, as I understood that would limit ads and allow me to save meals as before. Unfortunately after only a few days I was blocked from adding meals to my plan again and still received ads to buy the higher subscription... what they term unlimited? Not sure if it is because I tried to add more then 5 or so meals at a time but that seems a bit ridiculous for something you are paying for. Monetary value aside, the biggest problem I had with this app was communication and transparency up front. Canceled basic subscription due to these issues and deleting from phone. I suggest you search for a different meal prepping app, with a more transparent and user-friendly interface.

Developer Response ,

We completely understand how the transition from free to a subscription based approach is a difficult one and sincerely wish there were a way to continue to offer our full feature set at no cost to you. We regret any confusion you feel relative to the difference between our Basic and Premium subscription tiers. It is not our intent to be anything other than transparent, and we try to enumerate the included features clearly but will review our descriptions in an effort to remedy any ambiguity. We are sorry to see you go and wish you the best.
