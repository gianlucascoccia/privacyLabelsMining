Description

Fun to play, easy to get in hand! Collect all the balls to reach the next level, dig in the sand to guide ALL of them and use them to breack locks.

Benefit from a lot of original courses and enjoy the best puzzle game!

Main features:
- Hundreds of amazing levels
- Dig the sand
- Huge varieties of obstacles
- Tons of different colors and skins for the balls
- Simple control: dig where your finger is
