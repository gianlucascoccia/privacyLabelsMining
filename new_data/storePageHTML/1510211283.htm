Great game!

Like others, I don’t usually review games. This one was so great though, I had to! The only thing I didn’t like is that I beat all the levels in only a few days. So, on the off chance that convincing more people to play YOLO will spur the developers on to adding more levels quickly, I am doing a review! The visuals on the game are so much fun, and the music and sound effects are perfect. I find myself leaving the volume on while playing just to hear the screams at the end of a level when you kill all the people on screen. 😂 The puzzles are challenging, but definitely not too hard. And when you upgrade the workshop it is really easy to earn enough bulbs for as many hints as you need. That is such a great feature! On behalf of game players everywhere...thank you so much for thinking of the players enjoyment instead of only earning extra money by making us pay for hints. Overall, very well done!!!! Please update with more levels soon!
