Here’s to good health & taste

When I want a healthy and filling meal Zoe's is my go to. The food is always fresh, tasty, and hot. Some of my favorites are the steak and chicken kabobs. The steak is so juicy and flavorful! The baked falafel has also become a fav. Until Zoe's I'd never had a baked falafel so I was skeptical, but to my surprise I don't miss the extra calories from the grease at all. I'm not sure what I like better the food or the excellent staff. I've been here several times and the staff has always made you feel welcome with a greeting and a smile One employee who is especially wonderful is Troi. On a recent visit, my friend and I were the only ones sitting outside. Trioi came out and made sure we were good and that the other eating areas were clean and ready. We were there for a while and she came out again to make sure we were good. I was so impressed with her enthusiasm to give us quality service that I had to thank her. On tonight's visit the staff was no different. As usual they were friendly and accommodating. I had to let the manager Danny know how impressed I was with his staff. He said that during the interview process customer service is emphasized. He said that the "customer has a thousand food options, and good food is good food, but we have to set ourselves apart from the rest and make our service outstanding too" Welp! That made me love this place even more You can't go wrong with this formula!

LGA4083 , 02/04/2021

Was great, but now has issues.

I love Zoe’s and began using this app in 2019 and loved it. I entered my orders easily with no issues, paid thru app and my food was ready when I arrived, saving me 30 minutes of my lunch hour (my location is very busy). I used it 3-4 times per week. Biggest fan ever.!
Early in 2020 I noticed there were issues with the app. First, all my login info was cleared and reentering it was filled with errors which prevented me from logging in. After a month of trying, I got in again, only to find that I spend time inputting my order, but it wont complete the order and take me to checkout. After hitting “add to order” 25 times in every way possible it finally worked, only to have the same issue when completing the order.
I am not at the Zoe’s as often during Covid, but look forward to my 2-3 visits per week when my office reopens. I really hope the issues are fixed or I won’t be eating there as often.
To developers, I use this app on an iphone 11. Please test the app and fix the issues introduced in 2020.

pljkate , 01/09/2021

Always fresh and high-quality

I continue to be thrilled that I get delicious foods in perfect portions at a fair price. I used to go to Rubio’s, Chipotle, and Flower Child a lot for healthy fast options but portions and food quality has dropped dramatically at Rubio’s and Chipotle, although still good quality, the service is TOO FAST. They churn people through the line teams many times I’ve encountered mistakes on pick up orders. Employees at both places come off like they are exhausted and unenthused and tough to talk to during a rush and you need a problem fixed. Flower Child is beautiful food but portions are tiny and way over-priced. Folks at Zoe’s are always friendly and available to help. Last, Zoe’s salad dressing is simplistic but just the right amounts of oil, lemon, and vinegar. I’ve tried replicating it but can’t get it right so I just buy a bottle. Try it. It’s so good on everything!
