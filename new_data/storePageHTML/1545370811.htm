Description

Dinero app is a convenient way to track your subscriptions and expenses. Totals are calculated for you. You can even get reminders when bills are due!

Dinero makes it extremely easy to -

• Add existing subscriptions in one go
• View all your subscriptions in an organised manner
• Keep track of all your subscriptions

Features -

• Cloud sync and BackUp.
• Available on iPhone, iPad and Mac at no extra cost.
• Customise app with multiple tint options available (coming soon).

Dinero Pro features -

• Track unlimited subscriptions.
• Available on iPhone, iPad and Mac at no extra cost.
• Multiple app icons.
• Customise alert timings.
• Ask Siri to add a new subscription for you.

Best in class platform features:

• Designed for iPhone, iPad, mac
• Trackpad and mouse support
• Multiple windows on iPadOS
• Dark mode
and much more…

Available for all your devices -
Dinero is available across all of your Apple devices.

What’s New

Jun 22, 2021

Version 1.1.3

Introducing two new monthly, quarterly Dinero Pro subscriptions with this release. However, lifetime Dinero Pro is still there.

Hi all—Hope you are having a good weekend, a lot of the work we've been doing these past few weeks has been under the hood and the new user interface with great user experience. Thank you for your help in finding bugs, and apologies for not detecting them sooner...

Features -

• More performance improvements. We've continued to invest heavily in performance improvements this month. Most of this is under the hood and hopefully invisible to you, as you add new subscriptions on the dashboard. 
• iCloud support. When you create a new subscription the data will now be saved in your iCloud (if enabled) which will be automatically synced across your all other Apple devices. 
• Sort subscriptions. You can now sort your existing subscriptions by upcoming first, upcoming last, price low to high, price high to low, alphabetical directly from the dashboard or from the settings page as Settings > Sort subscriptions. 
• In-app notifications. We've noticed sometimes even after receiving notifications, users were struggling to find out which of your subscription(s) is due. So, we've decided to add a new screen to manage your in-app notifications right with a click of button from the dashboard. 
• Total expenses tracking and subscription categories. With this update we've categorised all subscriptions in a total of 7 categories as follows: Entertainment, Creativity, Utilities, Productivity, Education, Social, Others. Also, now when user click on the total expenses banner on the dashboard, he'll see a new screen where he'll see his expenses as per the categories represented in a pie chart. 
• Archive subscriptions. Now you've two options to remove your current subscriptions form the dashboard. You can now archive (just in case you want to keep track of your old subscriptions) your subscriptions once they end and you'll be able to see them at Settings > Archived subscriptions. 
• Haptic feedback. One of our favourite UX change for this update is haptic feedback, by default haptic feedback will be turned on in the device but in case you want to turn it off you can do this by the switch at Settings > Haptics. 

Improvements -

• New shades of blue. With a new brightening design palette comes a brighter, more saturated Blue with #3C1DFF for light and #5865F2 for dark mode. ( More information available in PressKit )
• We realised in a-lot of countries in the world , is used as a decimal separator and at a-lot of places currency symbol is placed after the price's numeric value. So, we're introducing new Default value in the currency selection view which will display you currency in correct format according to you location. ( P.S. - We don't have access to your location, your device will set is up for you. )
• Now we've made it way too easy for you to keep track of your subscriptions on the dashboard, you will see a new circular progress bar beside you list of existing/new subscriptions with remaining days in the centre of it. So that you never lose your track :)
• Dinero Pro users will now see a new Dinero Pro badge on the dashboard and Pro banner in the settings page.
• Go back to previous screen. Now you can easily fo back to previous screen from any screen by just swiping from left edge towards right.
and much more ...

Bug fixes -

• Limited maximum amount of subscription which was causing app crash earlier, it should work perfectly now.
• You can change default currency and see the changes without quitting and restating the app.
and killed a few more minor bugs...

That's it for now. As always, thank you for being part of Dinero App Community !
