Description

Compete against the pros. Discover unique drills to develop your physical skills and become the best athlete you can be. Volleyball, baseball, football or fastpitch: you can now train and RISE.

Need lightning-fast reflexes in volleyball? No problem. Whether you specialize in defense or in hitting, you will gain those precious extra seconds and smash your game.

Want to improve your batting skills in baseball? Learn to recognize different pitches in a fraction of a second.

Do you need an edge in football? With many drills for all positions, from QB to LB, you will find what makes a difference on the field.

And what about fastpitch softball? Improve your pitch recognition to the next level. Never be surprised by a rise ball again.

4 reasons to join the RISE:
Dozens of drills: coverage recognition, pitch recognition, ball placement & trajectory
Train your pitch recognition skills
Determine your current skill level and progress to your own rhythm
Find a random opponent, and compare your skills to theirs


Set up goals, train with efficient drills and improve your skills.

Whether you compete for fun or aspire to get in pro circles, RISE will help you achieve your goals and your dreams.
