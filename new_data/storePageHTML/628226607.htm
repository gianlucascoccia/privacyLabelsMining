Description

Your taco loyalty has been rewarded. Join Fuzzy’s Taco Shop Rewards and earn 10 points for every $1 spent, then redeem ‘em for your Fuzzy’s favorites.


Features:


Mobile order and pay – Grab your favorites from our crazy good lineup. Customize and place your orders through the app, then pick up from a Fuzzy’s near you. Save and repeat your favorites to make ordering faster.


Rack up points – Earn tacos by lifting one finger. You will earn 10 points for every $1 spend ($1 = 10 points; gift card purchases not included). Scan your profile QR code or give your phone number at the register, order through the app, or scan the barcode on your receipt.


Dish out – When reward time rolls around, select your reward and press “Redeem.” Enjoy. Earn some more. Repeat. Choose from a selection of free Fuzzy’s food based on the number of points you’ve earned. Keep earning points until you have reached your desired menu item!


Find nearby stores – Find the Fuzzy’s Taco Shop nearest you, get directions, and view store details. Favorite your local Fuzzy’s to stay up-to-date with the latest offerings.


Earn more – Earn even more with friend referrals and special promotions. Share your invite code with a friend and we’ll dish out even more rewards after they make their first purchase. Plus, you’ll receive exclusive offers and bonus points just for being a member.


Stay connected to exciting things happening by following @FuzzysTacoShop on Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter.


Note: Location and Notifications may be required to receive news and exclusive offers.

Scan your receipt

I went to Fuzzy’s On Saturday and had my receipts to scan after we received our food. I scanned one receipt and it gave me credit. However, the other two receipts required that I waited 4 hours before I could scan my additional receipts. After waiting over six hours I try to scan my additional receipts with the same error message. The next morning I tried to scan the receipts within the three day window and still received an error message regarding the four hours between scans. I have sent three emails to Tech Support to no avail. I went to their website and clicked on contact us to let them know the problem. I have never heard back from using that method either. It would appear that Fuzzy’s no longer supports their app or the customers. It’s a shame.
