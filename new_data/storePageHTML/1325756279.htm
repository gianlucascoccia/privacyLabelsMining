Description

Aesthetic effects and filters for photos and videos: VHS, Stardust, Disco, Monroe. Retro, vintage, or Indie Kid – find your own aesthetic!

Create aesthetic edits with Prequel and such iconic effects as DISCO, DIAMOND, BLING, TEAL, MIAMI, PRISM, VHS, D3D. There’s Prequel for every story – start telling it now.

FREE FEATURES

I installed and deleted it immediately... I’ve paid for my favourite mobile editing app (MuseCam) in the past, and enjoy the purchase.
