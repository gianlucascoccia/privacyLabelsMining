Description

Welcome to Buti® TV. Life is too short for punishing workouts. Subscribe now & meet movement you won’t dread. This is Vinyasa. REMIXED. Music guides our classes like an internal GPS; no more 8 counts or reps. This beat blended movement takes you out of your head and INTO YOUR BODY.

Buti® TV features all 5 signature formats: Buti, Deep, HotCore®, Buti Sculpt, and Buti Bands. Stream 300+ classes and receive new workouts weekly. Join LIVE classes with top instructors. Plus, guided programs for all skill levels. Primal movement, cardio dance + conditioning seamlessly weave through yoga sequencing to transform the entire body while burning up to 900 calories per class.
