Description

Life Lapse simplifies the creation of stop motion videos. Use the app's ghosted image feature when taking your clips to align your subject from clip to clip. Life Lapse will save you time, money and the headache of learning photoshop & other expensive editing software! No video editing skills? No problem! Life Lapse stitches all the clips together for you. All you have to do is select a filter, song, video ratio & you're good to go!

FEATURES:
 -Ghosted Image / Opacity Slider- The previous photo in the projects is overlaid in camera mode so you can align your subjects from clip to clip. No tripods needed.
-Multiple Projects
- Filters
-Camera Timer
-Interval Timer
- Speed - Adjust the speed of how fast your clips play
-4K Camera for mini video clips
-In-Camera Crop Guidelines
-Custom Grids
-Re-arrange clips in a project by holding down the clip and dragging to a new spot
-Delete select clips
-Copy Select Clips
- Import photos into a project
-Back Camera & Selfie Mode
 -Customize Sizing - Whether you want to up your Instagram story game, share on Facebook or upload to youtube, we have you covered with each size dimension. (9x16, 1x1, 4x5, 10x16, 5x4, 16x10, 16x9)
-Re-Align an image after you take it - You can go into each photo and align it with either the first photo in the project or the previous photo, for the perfectionists out there ;)
 Music - Have you ever tried uploading a video with a Beyoncé song in it to social media? Life Lapse has you covered with a library of 15 songs.  *Pro Feature to access full Library
Tap and hold on the screen and the ghosted image will disappear.
- Guideline Grid - To help you center your subjects
- Reminders - Never miss a moment with reminders. Hourly, daily, weekly.
