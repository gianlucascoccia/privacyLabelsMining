Description

Create beautiful stories with Ash. Elegant and simple.

Combine photos and videos, customize the colors and fonts, using perfect eye-catching minimal templates, and share them on Instagram in one click!

● Get more followers with impressive stories
● Use professionally designed templates for every occasion
● Combine photos and videos in your stories from iCloud and your camera roll
● Add your own text
● Pick your favorite templates to use them again
● Create and post a perfect story in a few clicks


Ash Premium:

Ash is completely free to download and try free for 7 days. Ash Premium is a $29.99 USD/year subscription that provides access to our entire collection of templates updated monthly. Payment will be charged to iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase. The subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period. This subscription auto-renews at the end of each year term at $29.99. You may manage your subscription and turn off auto-renewal by going to your Account Settings after purchase. Any unused portion of a free trial period will be forfeited if you purchase a subscription, where applicable.
