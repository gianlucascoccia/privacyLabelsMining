Description

FEATURES. the pūrvelo studio experience is now available to you, anytime, anywhere with the on-demand pūr ride: our signature 45-minute cardio and strength-based total body workout. high intensity and low impact, our streamlined choreography and epic beat drops create the ultimate dance party on a bike. ride to the rhythm in and out of the saddle, and utilize your resistance to build a personalized experience complete with a warm-up, cool-down, and arm track. grab water and a towel, and get ready to clip in, crush your goals, and have FUN doing it.

INSTRUCTORS. meet the pūr demand team: our world-class instructors representing all pūrvelo locations, now available at your fingertips, here to inspire and motivate you.

DANCE PARTY. count on uniquely fresh playlists and wildly fun choreography in every ride.

YOUR RIDE. no metrics, no comparison - just you, showing up for yourself. our workout is designed for riders of all levels, no experience required.

COMMUNITY. ride for yourself, but never ride alone. discover a powerful community, network, and support system of riders and instructors alike, committed to bringing out the best in one another on and off the bike.

CONNECTION. use your favorite streaming device at home, your favorite mobile device on the go, or stream online. connect to your largest home screens with Roku, Apple TV, Android TV, or Amazon Fire.

LIFESTYLE. not just a workout, but a fitness lifestyle, complete with our own athletic apparel, loungewear, streetwear, and accessories. in-app link to our latest collection.

SUBSCRIPTION. pūr demand all access: stream anytime, anywhere, and as much as you want with pūr demand all access. receive unlimited access to our pūr demand streaming library, ride to the beat with our leading instructors, replay your favorite classes, and be the first to access our newest content. $59.99/month, cancel anytime.
