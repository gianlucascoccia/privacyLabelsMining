Description

Listen to Live Police & Fire Radio

Scanner Radio is the #1 police scanner and alerts you of breaking news so you can listen to the action live with millions of others.

Stay up to date with your local news, traffic, emergency and public safety or listen in during major national live events in real time.

Features:
• Breaking news alerts to get the scoop before anyone else.
• View scanners located nearest you.
• View the most popular scanners.
• View the newest scanners being added all the time.
• Star your favorite scanners for quick access.
• Browse thousands of scanners from all over the world by location or genre (public safety, air traffic, railroad, marine, etc).
• CarPlay support

Receive an alert anytime:
• A scanner in the directory has more than a certain amount of listeners
• A specific scanner has more than a certain number of listeners
• A Broadcastify alert is posted for one of your favorites
• A Broadcastify alert is posted for any scanner
• A Broadcastify alert is posted for any scanner near you
• A new scanner comes online near you

Turn on notifications to receive alerts anytime a scanner has more than 2000 listeners (find out about major events as they're breaking).

The audio you're able to hear is provided by volunteers (and, in many cases, police and fire departments themselves) for Broadcastify.com and a few other sites using real scanner radios.

For assistance, please go to http://support.gordonedwards.net

Jakeconsig , 02/02/2019

Used to be great

I just wrote a long review on my other device a few weeks ago about how this was the greatest scanner app on the market. That has changed.

The ads used to be simple and subtle, but now they take up the whole screen. Often times when you’re trying to load a scanner app time is of the essence. Waiting for a full screen ad to load is a huge inconvenience and delays our ability to monitor a current unfolding public safety situation.

I just bought a paid version of a different app to replace this app after years of being a loyal user. Either get rid of the full screen ads (banner ads are totally fine) or give us a paid option. Until then, I’ll use a different app to get the exact same data streams.

Developer Response ,

Yes, I fixed the app's ad delivery functionality (which broke a few years ago) in order to fund the development of improvements and new functionality (some of which you've probably seen added recently with much more to come).

Note that there *is* a paid option, the "Remove Ads" button that's in the upper right corner of the player screen.
