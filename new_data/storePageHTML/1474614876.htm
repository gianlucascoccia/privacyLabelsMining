Description

Welcome to Tuscany Villa! The fun begins in your very own Tuscan guesthouse.

Tuscany Villa is a free casual puzzle and adventure game for fans of home and garden design with a twist! Run your own hotel, a charming Italian villa set in the beautiful Mediterranean, and welcome guests from around the world. Make them feel right at home with stunning interior design and decoration or jaw-dropping natural creations in the house’s garden and grounds.

Enjoy a gripping story as you make decisions to design the best hotel and win the loyalty of your guests. The adventure is yours to shape!

Features:
- Endlessly immersive gameplay: Renovate your hotel by playing a series of free and engaging puzzle games, challenging match 3 mini-games, and other casual games set to test your skills. Create powerful combos and make awesome improvements to your rustic guesthouse.
- Make your name in the village by enlisting the help of the locals in making your hotel the best in all of Tuscany.
- Protect your hotel from the greedy property developers by working your way through many adventure-filled levels.

Play, solve, win, enjoy!
- Match and solve puzzles
- Earn stars to upgrade the house’s interior and garden design
- Play again to keep the adventure going

Free adventure puzzle fun for casual gamers with an unforgettable story!

PrincessAudrii , 07/08/2020

Really Great Game!

My daughter enjoys the storyline and the decor choices for each chapter, and I am finding the match 3 to be very relaxing. Currently I am almost at level 300 and I haven’t really had to spend my coins that often. With 5 lives you can easily pass several levels at a time for stars. I also am loving the events as you get infinite lives for a set time when participating.

The only thing that I would think could be improved is that if you get infinite life timer you have to play at the moment for the next 30 / 45 min or longer. I would love it if you had the ability to either start and stop the timer or choose to activate it at a different time or if it only ran while you are in the level because I also find that you get a timer when you complete a chapter of the story, but then reading the story choices you can lose about 3 minutes if you dont skip and then there is also loading screens etc. So if I get infinite lives for 30 minutes I want to have the whole of the time.

Anyway its a great game and I highly recommend it.

Developer Response ,

Thanks for your comment! We love to hear from our users on what we can improve on. We are glad you're enjoying this adventure. We hope that you can stay with us for a long time!

OregonReign , 09/17/2021

Excellent Game Play But Still Deleting

I am current on level 954. It has been a good little time killer, so far. I have spent some money but not because it was necessary to win any games. Unfortunately, the game has gotten to the point where it is no longer rewarding to play. Almost every level now takes way more than 5 lives to complete. Unless you are willing to sink days worth of play time on one level, I imagine you'll either spend money or quit the game. I am quitting the game.

That being said, it was my favorite of this company's games, so far. They've removed a lot of the annoying stuff but much still remains. You almost NEVER get in game special items (although they are generous with pre-game items). This is a common money-making technique but even when they do pop up for sale, the deals are rarely worth the money.

There are many ways to get more time but mostly you cannot control WHEN you accept the time. I'm still waiting on a game that will let you save time until you can actually sit and play. When I play, I'm killing time waiting for a meeting to start. I'm not going to be able to commit to 45 minutes, but later in the day I might be. This is just a missed opportunity for sales as far as I can see, but I'm not an expert in the psychology of micro transactions and mobile games. All I know is it doesn't work on me. I just stop playing. If none of these things bother you, download away! You'll probably love it.
