Description

Find your OM at home!

YogiApproved Classes features premium on-demand yoga and fitness classes with world-class instructors. Follow along with our knowledgeable and approachable instructors as they guide you through the yoga or fitness class of your choice. With hundreds of unique classes that range from complete beginner to advanced, you're sure to find your soulmate practice with us. We also plant a tree for every class you complete as a premium member! Grab your mat and start your practice today!

FEATURES:
• Hundreds of classes for all ability levels
• Yoga, fitness, barre, wellness classes and more
• New programs and classes added regularly
• We plant a food-producing tree for every class a member takes
• Beautiful HD streaming videos
• Members can download classes for offline use
• Watch classes on your TV with Apple AirPlay or Chromecast
• Free programs and classes available
• Premium membership with free trial offered

Premium memberships to access all classes and content require a monthly or yearly auto-renewing subscription that can be purchased within the app. Prices vary by region and are displayed in the app before purchase. All payments will be charged to your iTunes account at confirmation of purchase. Subscriptions automatically renew unless turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current billing cycle. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24-hours prior to the end of the current billing cycle. Your subscriptions may be managed and auto-renewal turned off by going to your Account Settings after purchase. Any unused portion of a free trial will be forfeited upon purchase.

yogedi , 10/18/2019

Love YogiApproved

I’ve practiced yoga for several years and tried many different apps. YogiApproved is my favorite and the only one I use now. There is variety in classes and ability. What I like most though is that the majority of the shorter classes include a warmup section. With other apps, if a class is 20-30 minutes long, there is little to no warmup before beginning major movements. My body needs a bit of warmup and stretching first. I highly recommend YogiApproved!

Nomad0321 , 10/08/2019

In desperate need of a good developer

This is a review of the app itself. The content on here is great! The problem is there is no way to search for content. There is no way to filter content. There is no way to favorite content. There is no way to save content to view later. I also cannot find an easy way to provide feedback to the developer. There are no settings option in the app - probably because this app is just a video repository.

To the developer: This is a paid app which has been out for ~ 1 year now? If you are going to charge people to use your app, at the very least it should have, or should have been updated with by now, the very basic features I have described above. I am glad you are focused on getting new content out the door, but it’s time to fucus on the app. If you don’t have someone in IT building this app for you, please invest. If you do, I can only imagine they are very bored at this point.

Developer Response ,

Thanks for the feedback! As of Oct 26, 2019, the search and filter functionality has been added to the app. As for saving content, any program you start as a member is automatically added to your "My Classes" list on the profile tab.
