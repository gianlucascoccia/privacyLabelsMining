Description

Photo Recolor is the best and latest color splash effects app out there!
--> Gray out other people in a group photo except you
--> Change part of a photo color to any color you want, for instance you can change your hair color to pink, lipstick to green, jacket to blue etc.

Tools :
[+] Color/Gray: Apply original color or gray on any part of the photo.
[+] Recolor : Use any color to apply anywhere on the photo.
[+] Pan / Zoom : Move, Zoom, Rotate your photo for detailed view and easy coloring.
[+] Undo : Undo the errors easily, and use the Gray button to redo the gray color.
[+] Brush Size : Use any brush size you would like based on the requirement.
[+] Share : Share your creation to friends easily!
