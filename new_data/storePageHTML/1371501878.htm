-Zhur- , 03/17/2021

An amazing game that can possibly pull something great in the future

This game blew me away, I remember the first smashy road and how that game was one of the only games I found addicting on mobile. This game is amazing, from the map that you are able to explore, to the objectives, challenges, drivers, and vehicles. While I was playing this game, I thought of an idea, what if you gave the character weapons? What if you added a story? What if you added a store where you can buy weapons? What if you started with no stars but had the choice to do crime? Well, that’s what GTA is, and this game reminded me of the old GTA’s, especially when the camera moved from top to bottom view when your in the city. I have a feeling that the people behind Smashy Road could possibly execute a GTA type game, if they tried that is. Think about it, the police system is there, the free roam aspect is there, with a little bit of tuning and adding it could be a mobile game staple if you ask me. Years ago Smashy Road was a simple game of driving away from cops in a car, now look at it, why stop now, why not take it to another level. The game is an amazing game and experience, and in the mean time of waiting for their next game, hopefully a GTA type game, I’ll just sit here patiently and play Smashy Road 2 and 1.

mrb731 , 08/04/2020

Amazing!

This game is all I have ever wanted for a Smashy Road Game. This game is just like GTA but smashy road edition. For this game you basically take smashy road wanted, update it and you can now get out of the car and run around. You also have a whole world map that you drive around. It is as if you took all of the location (and added some more) form smashy road arena and put them into one map! Sure, this game does not have weapons for the person or the car but I think that this game does not need it, but it would be cool if there was a power up that gave you a weapon for a little bit of time. In this game, the slot machine makes a return. This time you will ether get a costume for your character, a new car, or a upgrade for one of your cars. You now don’t get colors from the slot machine and they are all available from the start. This is amazing because I hated when you would get a color in smashy road arena. The upgrades for the cars make sense as when you upgrade the car it seems like it gets more Heath and might get a little bit faster but I can’t tell yet. Also, the costumes are all amazing and are fun to get. I really love this game and I hope that it takes off just like the other smashy road games.
