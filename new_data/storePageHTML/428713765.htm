Description

Be the master of your money with the Bank of the West app. Enjoy secure access to your accounts and manage your finances from virtually anywhere.

BEST-IN-CLASS BANKING

Sign in and bank securely with TouchID, FaceID, Mobile PIN or password.

Send money, make payments, transfer and deposit funds on the go:
• Quickly send money to almost anyone you know in the U.S. via their email or U.S. mobile number with Zelle®.
• View or modify scheduled payments and more with Bill Pay. Take a photo of your bill, and we’ll do the rest.
• Easily move money between Bank of the West accounts and accounts at other banks.
• Deposit checks through the app and email yourself a receipt that has an image of the check. Also verify and view images of cleared checks.

View and manage your accounts wherever you are:
• Manage your credit and debit cards: set travel notifications, activate new cards, change your PIN or order a new card.
• Access up to 7 years’ worth of checking, savings, CD and credit card account statements online. Manage your paperless preferences.
• View account balances and recent transactions without having to sign in using Quick Balance.
• Quickly look up a transaction without endless scrolling using our search bar. Just type in first few letter or numbers to activate search.
• Find Bank of the West locations near you with our ATM and Branch Locator.

OUR SECURITY COMMITMENT

We put the safety and security of your personal and financial information first. We:
• Never store confidential information or account numbers on your mobile device.
• Use industry-standard data encryption to protect your transactions.
• Prompt you to verify your sign-in information after 5 minutes of inactivity.
• Block your mobile banking access if your device is lost or stolen.

DISCLAIMERS

The Bank of the West App is available to download at no cost. Your mobile carrier may impose data charges depending on your individual plan. Check with your carrier on specific fees and charges.

The Bank of the West app isn’t optimized for the iPad.
