Description

Welcome to the official Hatchimals CollEGGtibles app! Here, you can hatch new friends from all over Hatchtopia! The more you hatch, the more fun you can have!

Explore every corner of Hatchtopia with your Hatchimals. Fly up to Cloud Cove! Visit the sandy shores of Breezy Beach! Plant beautiful flowers in Glittering Garden! Dance at the Light-Up Disco on Illuma Island and so much more! You can even keep track of your real collection with the Collector’s Book. Hatch a Whole World™!

FEATURES

- Hatch your favorite Hatchimals CollEGGtibles from Seasons 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and!
- Now over 300 characters to collect!
- Play 6 mini-games to earn items and hatch new Hatchimals!
- Care for your Hatchimals by feeding them, washing them, and petting them!
- Keep track of all of your Hatchimals with the Collector’s List!
- Learn about their personalities and their Hatchtopia hangouts!
- Play the Daily Spin Wheel to get free rewards everyday!
- More to come!

Good but…

This game is real good and cool and you can collect Hatchimals! But the bad problem is… You can’t keep track of Seasons 6.5, 8, 9, and 10! I can’t wait to see them on the checklist! It is a little strange. You need to add more mini games, for example the Crystal Canyon mini game has your Hatchimal riding on a mine cart and you jump over certain obstacles! You should also add a way to scan your Hatchimals. Step 1: Go to Settings and tap on the antenna button. This activates a scanning capability. Step 2: Aim the camera at your Hatchimal’s bottom and wait till the “H” egg begins glowing. Step 3: After the egg finishes glowing, it begins to crack as a voice says “Awesome! You hatched a rare Hatchimal!” Step 4: Down, down, down goes your new Hatchimal. It will look down nervously as it speeds through a rainbow colored screen. Step 5: Your Hatchimal lands face first into a nest! The other Hatchimals will even ask “Is ba hurt?” That’s Hatchinese for “Are you hurt?” Your new Hatchimal will respond with “Ba is ok!” That’s Hatchinese for “I’m ok!” Step 6: You can now go on and care for your Hatchimal! This game is still very good.

~breathe~ , 10/16/2019

Several cute things,several flaws.

The creatures themselves are very cute. Time for growing flowers is REAL time-until you step out of the game. Then, the timer stops. Really? I have to be actively playing this game for, depending on the type of flower, up to 24 hours? That’s a bit unrealistic don’tcha think? Also, for a kids game the games are pretty hard. The habitats they live in are very pretty. Would like the map destinations to have more to do other than getting fun facts that have no purpose. There are 6 playable games. Cloud Cove, The Glittering Garden-where you grow flowers, Sunshine Surf, Polar Paradise,and Hatchy Matchy. Plus, there is a daily spin wheel, and collecting pearls once a day in the whirlpool in the Mermal ocean. There are no guides other than the small bits that tell you which food your pets prefer, their nest type and their rarity. The HELP symbol provides you with 2 of 3 pages of text. There are some pets that require 2 certain types of goods to obtain, but I have not encountered either of the symbols’ origins. Lots of ways to improve the game for the developers. Lots of things to address. Most of all a comprehensive game instruction manual.

Good Game, but faulty

I have many Hatchimal collectibles at in real life. This game was perfect when I wanted to care for digital Hatchimals. It was great-until it began to shut down.
One time I was playing Sunshine Surf when my hatchimal stopped moving. I thou it was the lag so I went to my wifi source. But at the time I was up there, it had been off the game and on the main screen.
Also, the Hatchimals do creepy things. Once, my golden Lynx was jumping in Cloud Cove, but every time she jumped her body disappeared.
Also, sometimes I feed a hatchimal and she jumps and twirls. But after that, her head would turn 360.
Overall, this is a fun game and I hope the creators fix the flaws. Make more games and put more Hatchimals in the Hatchy basket!
