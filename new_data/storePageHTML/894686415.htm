Description

DragonFly makes sports and activities more organized with digital forms, health records, payments, credentials and communication tools. We simplify the administrative side of sports so more focus can be on the game. Our platform is a one-stop-shop for anyone involved in sports and extracurricular activities.

Features:

· Parents and students use DragonFly to fill out forms, pay fees, communicate with the team, see the team schedule and get injury and rehab updates from their care team.

· Coaches and administrators use DragonFly to manage eligibility, collect fees, manage rosters and schedules, communicate with teams, pay officials and document injuries.

· Associations use DragonFly for eligibility, transfers, sanctioning, fee collection, assigning of officials and credentialing.

· Officials use DragonFly for registration, assessments, assignments and payments.

Poorly Fleshed Out App

If you are a sports official switching from any other assigning system to dragonfly.. prepare to be disappointed.

1. The app doesn’t allow you to view your schedule in a calendar format. This should be an easy feature to implement, as both Arbiter Sports and RefTown have this.

2. Block Dates: if you set several blocks, they do not display in chronological order or on a calendar format.

3. Notifications come in for new assignments.. sometimes. Other times I receive no notifications at all.

4. Assignors do not receive notifications when you reject games. Make sure you call them.

All in all, there are several areas that dragonfly can improve in. The app works, but that’s about all it does.
