Description

Golf BPM is the #1 music app for perfect swing tempo. For less than the cost of a lesson, you can have perfect tempo in 3 minutes.

GolfBPM was created so golfers can develop the same consistent tempo by timing their golf swing to music.

HOW IT WORKS

- Download and Register for Free
- Choose a Station
- Select a Club type & swing speed. If you are at home, start with putting
- Start your swing on the “One” count
- Start your down swing on the “Two” count
- Make impact on the “Three” count
- Boom! You now have perfect tempo

Featuring original music from hip hop, to electronic, to rock and pop, each song contains subtly timed verbal queues, so a golfer knows exactly where to hit their swing marks.

Thoroughly tested on PGA tour players, amateurs, juniors, and senior golfers. Golf BPM founder Doug Timmons (PGA Class A member) created GolfBPM to make your practice fun and more effective.

No two golf swings are alike. But regardless of variables like grip, speed, or stance, every PGA Tour golfer in the world moves at an almost identically even tempo, on every swing. When you are watching PGA players at the US Open or The Master you will see perfect swing tempos.

Now you too can have that perfect muscle-memorized cadence by timing your swing to music.
