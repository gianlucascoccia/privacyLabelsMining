KeithInDallas , 09/22/2018

New to Vero

I’m trying Vero because I am looking for a replacement for Path, which is shutting down. Our extended family had used Path for 5 years now to have a “private” social network, and it looks like Vero has similar possibilities. Compared to 4-5 other social networking apps I have tested, Vero’s level of polish, functionality, and stability is far ahead of any of the others!
One thing I liked about Path that seems to be missing on Vero is the ability to create customized locations, such as “naming” our home so we can check in there, and so family can check in as well when visiting.
The other glaring issue is that the app will NOT run on my iPad Pro. I understand that you might not create a separate iPad app, but I would still like to be able to use the phone app on the iPad!
Meanwhile, I am getting all my extended family to download the Vero app and set up accounts. I hope that Vero will consider the two issues that I mentioned as they continue to make updates and improvements.

The man of a million passwords , 06/09/2018

See the potential

First and foremost I definitely see the potential in this app! But there are definitely some kinks that need to be worked out here and there as well. I feel like it the UI isn’t bad, but it’s not attention grabbing. Just very functional. I’m not getting “hooked” on scrolling through my timeline like I would if I was scrolling through IG or Twitter.

Also the hashtag suggestions always get wonky on me. Half the time I start typing in a hashtag, the suggestions that it gives me are misspelled versions which is kinda strange. If it isn’t the misspelled version being suggested then it’s not showing a suggestion at all for the most part.

With all of that being said, I still give it 4 Stars. I really how they streamlined the sharing is (from books you’re reading, to music you’re listening to, to links for other content, etc). I also really like the content being put out on Vero now too. Definitely some talented content creators out there doing their thing!
