◆ Main Features


▶ Bible text and audio synchronized with autoscrolling

Underline the portion of the Bible audio that is currently read
Can be found at the top of the screen
Set the auto scroll function on / off


▶ Bible dictionary linked to the text

The Bible dictionary is linked to the text word so you can check it without searching.


▶ Screen black-and-white conversion

Bible text, bookmark list wallpaper in black and white


▶ Smart Bible Search, remain Search History

▶ 5 different coloring pencils

▶ Section bookmark + memo that can be seen at once

▶ Hymnbook Favorites

▶ Search for new / old hymns

▶ Support high quality audio contents


◆ Other advantages
1. All functions "landscape view" possible
2. Bible text search settings (by book, by translation)
3. Easy to set audio sequence, repeat, and listen to one song
4. Bibles and hymns are included, including Christian, Apostles, Lord's Prayer, and Ten Commandments.
