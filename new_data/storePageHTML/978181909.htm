Description

Download iScore Central today to view live Scorecasts of your favorite young sports stars' games. Whether it’s soccer, baseball, softball, football or basketball - you’ll never miss a game – ever again!

Perfect for working moms and dads who are stuck in the office, grandparents at the other end of the country, or any fan who wants to experience all the play-by-play excitement of their favorite young sports stars games.

Also get access to the team’s website to view schedules, player stats, and more.

All users get access to:
• Watch live scorecasts for FREE
• Watch unlimited replays of live scorecasts
• Push notifications for live score updates

Want more? iScore Central Premium gives you access to:
• Boxscores
• Detailed player stats
• Live play-by-play
• Scorecast radio

New subscribers can try iScore Central Premium, free for 7 days, then just $1.99/month or $19.99/year thereafter.

SUBSCRIPTION DETAILS

This app has an optional recurring subscription option as described above (iScore Central Premium) that has a subscription price of $1.99/month or $19.99/year.

iScore Central Premium gives you access to:

• Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user's Account Settings after purchase

Dalkebj , 07/07/2019

Very inferior product to the web based viewing

If i was starting over again I’d use game changer.

I’ve been using iScore for over 6 years for my youth team. When parents could watch the game for free on the website it was an excellent experience. Then they moved to the paid service only available thru the app. I get the paid part of it. They have a right to make money off their product. My concern is that the app is a far inferior product to the website.

The website offered season long stats and you could view details on pitching easily. The app does not offer any way to see either. Thus its impossible to k ow how a player is doing this season and how the pitcher is throwing over the year or that day.
