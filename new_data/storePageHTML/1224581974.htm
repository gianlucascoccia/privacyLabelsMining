Description

This app integrates a variety of professional features such as professional camera, photo editor and video editor to help users create outstanding photography.

Specific features of professional camera:

[Cameras Module]
· Professional Camera:
6 shooting modes
-Manual: Can manually adjust the camera focus, shutter, ISO, white balance and other parameters
-Slow shutter: Support up to 30 seconds slow shutter shooting
-Video: Recording HD video
-3D: It can take 3D images
-Depth: It can take pictures first then focusing, control aperture size and select various blur shapes
-Timer: Support 3 seconds / 5 seconds / 10 seconds timer shooting

Support wide-angle lens,telephoto lens, ultra wide-angle lens, and can display lens aperture parameters
Support focus lock, exposure lock, white balance lock
Built in 6 white balance presets
Support JPEG, RAW, RAW + JPEG, HEIF format shooting

· Camera Lens:
9 camera lenses are available and can be used with more than 150 filters
-Starshine lens
-Tilt-shift lens
-Chromatic lens
-Snow lens
-Fisheye lens
-Neon lens
-Glitch lens
-Zoom lens
-Split screen lens

· VLOG:
-We have designed 15 exclusive vlog filters for vlogers, which can capture vlog or import local video to process
-Support multiple vlog resolution size scale
-More than 150 filters can also be used for vlog short video capture or video processing

· Film Camera:
-It can take an imaging picture. Different time watermarks can be added, with negative effect, and one-time imaging frame can be added

[Filters Module]
· Filter: A total of 12 categories, 150 + filters, can be used for real-time camera shooting, import video processing (support 4K video add filter), import image processing
