Description

HeyWeather provides the new widget feature introduced in iOS 14.
you can have stylish widgets on your home screen to check your desired locations weather.

The HeyWeather App is not just another weather app with widgets, it's a handy, simple but accurate weather app with lots of customization on widgets! With HeyWeather you can customize any aspect of iOS 14 widgets on your home screen!

•Do you want to see the next hours weather on your home screen? There is a widget for that!

•Do you want to check tomorrow's weather on your widget? There is a widget for that!

•Do you want a stylish widget to match your blue icons? There is a widget for that!

•Or maybe you want to check your hometown weather on your home screen alongside your next destination weather? There is a widget for that!

There are tons of icons, colours and styles available for you to customize widgets, and more coming soon!

Choose up to 10 cities to monitor your desired location forecast. You can also show different cities on any widget on any size!

What’s New

Oct 7, 2021

Version 1.6.0

We are really happy that the HeyWeather app is now accessible to more people, including new languages and new accessibility features!

Latest features and changes in HeyWeather Version 1.6.0

- HeyWeather is now available in the following languages:
• French, Italian, Spanish, German, Chinese, Portuguese, Russian, Turkish and Korean
• Just update the app and enjoy HeyWeather in your language. You can always select a different language for HeyWeather from the setting page.
And if you found any mistake please report us to fix it.

- Hey Weather app now supports all accessibility features in iOS:
• Voice over is customized and can reads texts in 10 languages.
• Enabaling Reduce motion in iOS setting now disable animations in the app.
• All clickable content now can be shaped as Button.
• Application's font size is now dependant on iOS font size.
• Increase Contrast and Reduce Transparency is now supported in HeyWeather.

- Precipitation charts for the next hour and next 8 hours added to the app

- Lots of bugs were fixed, including:
• A bug that caused AQI to be zero in some conditions.
• New Moon and Full Moon images were fixed.
• Wind direction image bug was fixed.
• Pressure in inHg is now shown in 2 more digits.

Amazing weather widget!

Since the release of iOS 14, I know everyone’s favorite topic right now is widgets. This weather app allows you to customize the color, icon designs, and shapes of your small, medium and large widget. I still have not paid for the premium version but the developer was kind enough to provide free options to get you try and test the widgets out. Honestly, I have been impressed so far. If I had one quirk, the hourly forecast (11am, 12pm, etc) the text gets cut off at times. Also if you have a long location name, the text tends to cut off at the bottom of the widget. I know this is version 1 so I’m excited to see where this ends up in the future. I will continue to update my review as the week progresses. Thank you for making this and hopefully they will continue to add an enhance to the experience, widget and app.
