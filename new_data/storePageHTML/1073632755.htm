Description

Eat well, cook never. With Snap Kitchen, get healthy, ready-to-eat meals. We're committed to delicious chef-prepared, dietitian-curated meal plans that flex to help you reach your ever-changing goals while satisfying your cravings.


NUTRITIOUS FOOD, HIGH STANDARDS.

Our chefs & registered dietitians work tirelessly to curate delicious meals with thoughtfully-sourced ingredients so you can have healthy food delivery at your fingertips.


YOUR LIFESTYLE, YOUR WAY.

Whether you're whole30, vegetarian, paleo, low-carb, or anything in between, we have a healthy meal plan that suits your lifestyle.


CONVENIENCE & FLEXIBILITY, MADE FRESH DAILY.

Desk lunch, school snacks, anywhere meals. All of our items are made fresh by a team of expert chefs, so you can stop chopping, cooking, and cleaning.


COUNTING CALORIES OR MACROS.

Let us solve your nutrition and cravings with more than 65+ options, including meals, snacks and juices that are entirely gluten-free, antibiotic-free, artificial-preservative free, and more.


• No more meal prepping. Set your nutrition on auto with our meal prep delivery.
• Get nutrition facts and key ingredients for every item.
• Dietary lifestyles accommodated for keto-friendly, high protein, low carb, Whole30, paleo, vegan, vegetarian, pescatarian, or build-your-own; and it's all gluten free!
• Refer your friends and track your SnapFunds rewards.
• Tag your favorite meals.
• Rate your meals to help us innovate and improve our menu selection.
• Receive alerts for new items, SnapFunds rewards, and order status.

CuppycakeT83 , 04/02/2020

App is fine, food is basic, shipping it the worst.

The app itself is easy enough to navigate, but could use tweaking - I wasn’t able to track my shipping through the app or even copy the tracking number to pop it into the web browser on my phone. It’s a small annoyance I’d normally ignore but once my order arrived it was so subpar and just an awful experience. It also would not save my apartment number - thankfully our packages go to a mailroom and you get your own, so I was able to get it. Of 12 meals, 7 arrived with busted containers, snapped lids, plastic in the food. I was refunded for them, but immediately cancelled my subscription - such a hassle to wait for food and think you’ve made life easier, and it isn’t. I wish I’d asked for a full refund for the trouble but I didn’t. They apologized and my agent was very friendly and helpful, but there is no wonder why they didn’t arrive safely. The plastic tops are paper thin and they are stacked with heavy ice packs and need more substantial containers. The taste is meh at best of what we’ve been able to salvage.

TLDR: if you like a Lean Cuisine but want half the flavor at three times the price, with better macros but higher calories, and a headache when it arrives, this might just be the food service plan for you. I was frankly shocked to log in and find such high reviews.

We’re sorry to hear that your first shipment experience was subpar! This is definitely not the experience we want our customers to have. We can assure you that your feedback has been forwarded to both our distribution and procurement team in order to reevaluate and improve our shipment methods. Thank you for choosing snap, and we hope to see you again soon.
