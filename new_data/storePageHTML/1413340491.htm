Description

With the Faire mobile app, retailers are invited to shop wholesale whenever, wherever. Use the app to easily shop the Faire marketplace on the go, view your wholesale orders and shipping info, and discover thousands of new brands your customers will love.

Retailers, download the Faire app today and shop unique lines for your store! Plus, enjoy free shipping on your first app order.

App features:
- Easy online shopping, designed for your phone
- An inspiration feed of new products and brands for your store
- Simple order management and shipment tracking
- All the same great benefits of shopping on Faire, including net 60 terms for brick and mortar retailers and free returns on opening orders

Sydney's Secret , 03/30/2021

Incompatible on iPads

I appreciate Faire retailers, but this app on iPads is very inconvenient. For those using the iPad with a keyboard, you will lose a lot of time. It is made to use on cellphones so you must hold the tablet vertically. This completely slows down work days, as you must disconnect your iPad and use your finger or Apple Pencil to navigate everything. Becomes tedious very fast. Unless you work from a cellphone, this app is not compatible with updated technology in business. That is the only downfall, but it is inconvenient enough to set you back twice the time. The developers might take this into account with updates. The app is very cool, would be much cooler if it was easier to navigate on iPads.

Developer Response ,

Hello Sydney's Secret, we are working on improving the app experience and your feedback is truly appreciated! I will be sure to pass your message along to our developers. If you have any further suggestions or concerns, feel free to reach out to support@faire.com. We would love to hear from you!

YummyTreatDiva , 09/24/2021

Ghost and Non-Responsive

Contacted multiple times regarding an unknown issue that will not process purchases (paying instantly and not using credit terms). I also requested refund of two months Insider Access paid for but not used because purchases will not process and the company will not return communication emailed to support. The website and app do not provide a phone number and email/message communication is the only way to reach anyone.

No communication of any nature is returned, purchases are not processed and refunds are not made. Vendors are missing sales from probably more than myself. Skip the site and go directly to vendors viewed in the site. It is easier, expedient and you know you will not be ignored if there is an issue.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for posting about this - we absolutely acknowledge that our support team has been delayed in responding to customer inquiries in our regular prompt manner. This retail season we've seen an unprecedented amount of outreach that we are still catching up to.

That said, we very much want to help get this resolved for you. If you do not receive a reply by end of day, please reach out once more to support@faire.com again, this will help us get your inquiry escalated more quickly.
