Description

Study the Bible together, from anywhere! Group up, create custom studies & stay connected through the week.

Biblegroups makes it easy to connect over the Bible with your study groups from anywhere! Start a Bible study group with your community to stay connected & engaged, every day of the week. Daily Bible study, questions & group chat make it easier than every to build an engaged & thriving Bible group.

Join thousands of leaders around the world engaging their Bible communities with Biblegroups, every day!

FIND A READING COMMUNITY & MAKE FRIENDS
Join thousands of young people around the world reading God's word together. Search for reading communities nearby, your age, reading a book you're interested in or create your own!

BUILD A DAILY BIBLE HABIT WITH YOUR FRIENDS

• Choose a book of the Bible to read through with your friends
• Read a daily Bible Chapter together each day
• Chat & Discuss daily Bible Study questions
• See where each other are up to each day & stay accountable


READ THROUGH BOOKS OF THE BIBLE TOGETHER

• Choose a book of the Bible you want to read through together.
• Choose a pace, from 1 chapter a day to 5 a day!
• Customise your daily discussion questions.
• Get a smart daily reading plan for your group!


STAY CONNECTED WITH GROUP CHAT

• Lightning fast group chat for daily discussion .
• Chat about the passages you read with daily questions.
• Share Bible verses directly to your group chat.


KNOW WHAT TO READ & SEE YOUR PROGRESS

• Know, every day, what chapter the group is reading together.
• Know exactly where your friends are up to.
• Track how far through your goal you are!


BEAUTIFUL BIBLE READING:

When it comes to reading the Bible, less is more. Carry is designed to remove all unnecessary distractions and help to make reading through the Bible with your friends beautiful.

• Dark mode for easy Bible reading at night
• Free ESV & Free NIV Bible Versions
• & much more!

Ajstarpack , 03/11/2020

Bright Potential (EDITED)

THIS IS a really neat app with bright potential. The most remarkable thing so far is the easy note taking/sharing. I look forward to how the “Bible progress” page will develop and be used.

MY PREVIOUS review gave the app FOUR stars because it seemed to me that you couldn’t remove or revise your notes (**it turns out I was just doing something wrong**) and I took notice that there are no bible versions currently available which include the Roman Catholic and Orthodox Christian greater canons. I suggested adding the following versions: The Douay-Rheims (Richard Challoner revision), the RSV/ESVCE, the WEB with the Deuterocanon, The 1611 KJV, and/or the Orthodox Study Bible.

A DEVELOPER of the app kindly responded to me, pointing out my error regarding removing/revising notes, and assured me that they plan on adding more bible versions as soon as possible. For these reasons, I am giving the app *FIVE* stars. This is a very promising bible app and stands out for its intentions. I look forward to seeing how it continues to grow, develop, and cultivate a community. Thanks for everything.
-Austin

Developer Response ,

Hi Ajstar! Regarding the notes, these can be edited and deleted by taping on the text in your note to edit & tapping the bin icon in the top left to delete 🙏 We plan on adding additional versions as soon as possible. Thanks again for your support.
