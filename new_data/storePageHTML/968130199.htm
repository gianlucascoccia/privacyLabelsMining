Description

Connect to doctors 24x7 for diagnosis and treatment, compare procedure prices, shop for prescriptions, find and research doctors and more. Use your membership to the full potential by taking advantage of our connectivity, savings and geo sensitive reminders for the best and most affordable care choices. Stay on top of your insurance plan and make sure you're making the right healthcare choices. All from a single app, all free as part of your HealthiestYou membership.

Use of this app requires an existing HealthiestYou membership, provided by your employer or group administrator. If you have not activated mobile access to your current membership before, you will be asked to provide Primary Member's information to verify eligibility for service.

Continued use of GPS running in the background can dramatically decrease battery life.

Gold3nlily , 03/10/2020

I thought this app would be great

This is the 4th time I am using this app. I used it three times for rashes and once for a bad cold. I was actually helped with the cold but had to go to an actual doctor because the help I received on HY did nothing for the rashes and moreover prolonged my problems with them.

My first few experience with HY were decent in terms of wait time and customer service, but my last two experiences with either were very bad. I had serious technical issues with the process that wasted a lot of my time. First of all, the video waiting room never shows up in a dashboard, so if I make a video appointment an hour away, I can’t let my phone sleep or use any other apps during the wait or I will loose my appointment. So now I do phone calls only and last night I revived 3 phone calls that hung up as soon as I answered them. Then the customer support argued with me / invalidated me about whether that happened! It was ridiculous and unnecessary because all I wanted to do was be added back to the beginning of this list because that was a technical issue and not my fault. So I’m trying that appointment again this morning but worry that the same thing will happen and that I wont receive any help from customer support.

I like the concept of the app but have not had good results whether or not they can successfully connect me with a doctor. After today I might not think it’s worth it to ever try again.

20202001LT , 03/16/2020

Excellent!

A chest cold has been morphing into something a little more serious. With COVID-19 taking root, I didn’t want to go to the ER, doctor, or med express for a condition that is serious, but not an emergency. I also needed answers in the middle of the night, instead of having to wait until my primary care physician opened. I remembered last minute that I had access to this app. It did take a couple of hours to get a call back, but this was not such an emergency that I thought I would be harmed much by the short wait. I received a call back, talked to a doctor who walked me through my symptoms and diagnosis. She also screened me for COVID-19 to rule it out and sent prescriptions off right away. Before the call ended, she emphasized certain health procedures related to my diagnosis and keeping healthy at this trying time.

This was such a wonderful process and I appreciate the care offered.
