Description

A budget app needs to be simple and intuitive, otherwise you’ll quickly stop using it and lose track of your spendings. This is MoneyControl. Not more. Not less

A budget app needs to be simple and intuitive, otherwise you’ll quickly stop using it and lose track of your spendings. MoneyControl does not bother you with unimportant information on cluttered screens. The interface is kept simple while providing you with all the features you need to plan and manage your budget, spendings and savings. Nothing more, nothing less.

► Your current budget and spendings at a glance on the start screen
► Shortcuts to enter new expenses with just a view clicks
► Recurring payments are created automatically for your income and expense entries
► Preview upcoming months and spendings
► Savings goals

► FREE DOWNLOAD & TESTING
We believe in "try before you buy": You can download MoneyControl free of charge. The free version can be used for an unlimited time and is fully functional; however, the maximum number of entries is limited to 20 per month. Making a simple In-App Purchase, you can permanently remove this restriction. Put MoneyControl to the test and you’ll soon want to buy it.

ThatBuff , 01/11/2021

Very easy to use which makes you use it.

Money control is an excellent little program to let you keep track of the amount of money you’re spending every day and give you a running total of how much you have spent this month. It also allows you to notate what you spent the money for and one really nice feature is that if you find that you spend the money primarily on one expense you can set a default so that it automatically will credit that spending to that default unless you change it. I very much like to feature that I can go in and check what I have written on various notations and go in and change the date the amount and what it was for. Very happy with this app.

Dkac , 04/10/2020

Great app for budget

I wanted to make suggestions but they are a German company. I have no problems with that but wish they would have a English version of the support website. I was really looking for a simple accounting app that can help balance checking account, other accounts, and track money. Having a budget is secondary, for me personally. Unfortunately this was the best app I could find. If they add the ability to split payments into several categories, and check off when payment cleared the bank I would give 5 stars. As a side note not really important I would like the option to hide accounts on every screen unless you click a box saying include this account. I know they they give opportunity to hide account and it works but not convenient to use. I also understand that this is a budget app so should not complain when I'm using it for something different. I did pay for it. If that helps you decide to get it or not.
