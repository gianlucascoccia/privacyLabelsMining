Description

Zoho Inventory is a powerful, free to download, and easy to use inventory app. It is designed for growing businesses to meet their end-to-end inventory management needs. If you want to keep a check on your sales and track stock even when you're on the move, this is a much-needed app.

With this inventory app, you can manage your daily sales and purchase transactions, invoices and bills, item SKUs, and also warehouses.

Zoho Inventory belongs to the family of Zoho apps, which are trusted by more than 50 million users globally. You can check out our Home page to learn more about Zoho.

Here's a complete walkthrough of how Zoho Inventory is the best fit for handling your inventory management operations:

Dashboard - This is the main screen of the app. It gives you an overview of your sales and shipping activity, and also a quick inventory summary.

SKU management - Add items, SKU, images and also create categories and bundles for efficient SKU management.

Item tracking - While adding items, you can also enter the serial or batch tracking number to track the movement of items and expiration date.

Barcode scanning - To enter all the above information, you can also use your phone camera and scan the barcode of the items. This makes your job even more easy.

Warehouses - You can create and manage different warehouses or stores in the same app without worrying about losing the track of the stock count

Online business - If you run an online business, your online orders will get synced in the app, and your stock can be easily uploaded in your online store

Contacts - Save the contacts of your vendors and customers so that you don't have to fill the information every single time.

Order management - Create sales and purchase orders quickly by just selecting the contacts and the right items.

Payment solution - Keep a record of all your receipts and payments with invoices and bills. You can also share them invoice link via iMessage.

Reminders - To make sure that your payments don't get caught in the loop, you can send reminders to your customers.

Order fulfillment - Create package slips, shipping labels and also send real-time shipment updates to your customers.

Transaction approval - You can add users, give them access to specific modules and also check and approve the transactions created by them.

Reports - To keep an eye on your business and inventory performance, you can generate reports and re-visit your growth strategies

Sync - If you use the web app of Zoho Inventory, all your inventory data gets synced between the apps automatically

We are here to help business owners make their inventory management easy and effortless. If you have any questions or suggestions related to our mobile or web app, then reach out to us at support@zoho-inventory.com.
