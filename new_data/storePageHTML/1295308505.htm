Description

Become A Favor Runner - Sign Up & Start Making Money

We believe in giving our Runners what they deserve, starting with 100% of their tips. Want to elevate your earning potential? Schedule your hours to take advantage of minimum payout guarantees (fun fact: most Runners average between $10–$18 per hour).

HUSTLE HOW YOU WANT

Full time or in your spare time, we make it easy for you to run on your schedule. Set your own hours in advance or pick up a few Favors on demand—it’s totally up to you.

GET RUNNING IN NO TIME

Ready to hit the road? Our application and onboarding process is quick and easy so you can get on your way. No experience necessary—to get started, you’ll just need to be at least 18 years old and have reliable transportation (car, motorcycle, or bicycle) with proper licensing, and a clean record.

DEPOSIT EARNINGS INSTANTLY

Whether you spend it or save it, you’ve got it on demand. Never wait for a payout again!

ACCESS EXCLUSIVE PERKS

Being your own boss has never been this simple (or fun). From supplemental auto insurance on every Favor to discounts at AAA, Jiffy Lube, Discount Tire, and more, we make it easy to keep running.

RUN MORE FAVORS, GET MORE $

Earn enough to turn your side gig into your main gig by unlocking weekly promos, incentives, and guarantees.

GET PAID TO MAKE SOMEONE’S DAY

No matter what our customers need—tacos, flowers, cold medicine, dog food, or their favorite wine—we’re on it. We’re personal assistants, and we’re making lives better one Favor at a time.

JOIN A COMMUNITY AS BIG AS TEXAS

From unmatched live support to local Facebook groups, we’re serious about setting our Runner community up for success.


25 MILLION FAVORS AND COUNTING

Favor is the highest rated on-demand delivery app in Texas. Our customers love us and show it.

PROUDLY SERVING FAVOR, TX

We’re currently delivering in over 100+ cities across Texas, including Abilene, Amarillo, Arlington, Austin, Baytown, Beaumont, Brownsville, College Station, Corpus Christi, Dallas, Denton, El Paso, Fort Worth, Georgetown, Houston, Killeen, Lubbock, Midland, New Braunfels, Odessa, Port Arthur, the Rio Grande Valley, San Antonio, San Marcos, Temple, and Waco. If we’re not in your city yet, hang tight—we’re expanding every week!

SIGN UP TODAY, RUN TOMORROW

Visit apply.favordelivery.com to get started!

Not Worth Your Time!

I was a favor runner for two months. It started off ok but the company does not seem to care about the runners. If a customer has a large fleet order and the store they ordered from screws up the order, the runner is penalized and receives the bare minimum of $4.10 for your service. Also, they advertise bonuses and top offs throughout the week but don’t initially inform you that you can only receive those payouts if you scheduled yourself in certain areas. If those areas are busy, you can get locked out from receiving favors because there are too many runners and miss the opportunity for the bonus. There is not a working number to contact customer if there are issues. You can only message through the app for concerns. I have been trying to get someone to respond to me since I never received my $25 for completing 50 favors. It may not seem like much but it is the principle. The fact that I reached out to support on 5 different occasions regarding my bonus and they gave the excuse that they were backed up with a lot of emails. Favor has customer service problems that they must rectify before I would recommend anyone to use their services (employee or customer).
