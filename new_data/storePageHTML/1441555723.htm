Description

A fan-made map for RDR2. We've collected hundreds of important RDR2 locations into one simple map, so you can quickly find what you're looking for!


FEATURES:
• Over 1500 locations - treasure, collectibles, easter eggs, rare weapons, animals & more!
• 50 different categories including Legendary Animals, Cigarette Cards and Strangers
• Quicksearch - just type the name of a location to quickly find what you're looking for.
• Progress Tracker - mark locations as found and track the progress of your collectables.
• Sync progress with the website: https://rdr2map.com
• Take Notes - mark places of interest by adding notes to the map.

If you find a bug, or have any suggestions for the app, please use the 'Send Feedback' option below to let us know!

Evan Buckley , 01/03/2019

Functional

It does what it’s supposed to. Unfortunately you can’t zoom in very close, and the markers get bunched up and are hard to click on without clicking something that’s right on top of it, it also makes it hard to pinpoint the exact locations of things without a close zoom. Certain items and locations, like things in chimneys or Valarian Root/Ginseng Elixers and Event Areas aren’t on the map. You can only mark up to 200 things, unless you pay $4.99 for Pro, and mind that it does not increase the zoom.

Plus, I’m guessing since this map is powered by Google Maps, if you click the Google in the bottom corner, it will take you to Pakistan.

jchnnystorm , 05/28/2021

Pretty good, needs an update

I love this app! It’s super helpful. For some reason when I close the app, my selections go away and reverts back to default. It’s kind of annoying to have to go back in and change it back to how I like it (I like to only have the categories I’m looking for at that moment turned on, because the map doesn’t zoom in very far and it gets overwhelming to have everything turned on.) the exotics map is also not very accurate, but I get that it might be a work in progress kind of like the animal heatmaps. I’d love if the app would save my selections with my account so it would stay how I left it even if I close the app/go to my computer instead of my phone. It would also be great if we could zoom in further. Overall really useful, and I bought pro because I want to support the development of this app! I hope it keeps getting better.
