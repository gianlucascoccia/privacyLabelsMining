Description

Photo Background Changer, an app where you can replace your Photo Background with around 100+ beautiful hd backgrounds and make it look like you are in places you have never been but you want or change ugly background into something awesome. This photo background changer is a easy to use photo editor tool where you can easily and perfectly cut any part of your photo and add different backgrounds with different categories . Just imaging your photo could be edited and changed with a background of beaches, gradient background, mountain background, plain, road and 3D background or erasing a boring background with something more colourful or textured. All this is now possible on your mobile phone with this amazing real Photo Background Changer app.
Features of photo background changer:
- Different categories to choose background from like:
Beaches city, gradient, mountain, plain, road,and 3d backgrounds.
- 100+ hd backgrounds for decorate your image.
- Add multiple photo on single background.
- Adjustment tool for background and foreground images.
- Connected to cloud.
- Cut the selected picture as per your need.
- Import pic from gallery, take photo from camera.
- Share photo via the most popular social networks such as facebook, twitter, instagram, Flickr and others.
- Easy to use and much more.
So friends change photo background or replace photo background with this easy to use app.
