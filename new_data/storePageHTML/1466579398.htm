Description

Cuddle - a group voice chat room app for you, to live chat with strangers & find friends. Have Fun with Friends online!

- Cuddle is NOT a dating app, even though lots of Cuddle users chat with strangers, find friends, participate in online marriage ceremonies, become real couples, and even find a soulmate.

- Cuddle is NOT a radio app, even though we have lots of DJs that host live shows, play music and sing every day, all of whom engage and interact with their listeners and earn money!

- Cuddle is NOT a walkie talkie, even though people call in and talk with each other and join the party online about all kinds of topics while having fun in the community.

- Cuddle is a group chat room app, where people can enjoy anything they want to kill time and to kill boredom together. Stay home and enjoy!

Join Cuddle, the leading group voice chat app with over 100k+ Cuddlers online. Listen to live streams, chat with strangers, and find friends all around the world. Best of all it’s anonymous and free to chat and call. Share your thoughts, play music, join trivia, find someone to match with and love, connect to people, find friends, host games, and earn money. Everything is available 24/7. It’s more fun than walkie talkies and radio. No matter where you are, you’ll never be lonely again.

【What do Cuddlers love about Cuddle?】
- Join a free random chat room, where you can send messages/emojis, call in to talk and interact directly with a DJ, and send them virtual gifts to show your love and appreciation.
- Climb the leaderboards, with daily, weekly, and monthly DJ/Fan rankings, and you'll turn into a famous superstar in Cuddle.
- Follow your favorite DJ. Stay connected through direct messages or whisper (private message) your secrets in live rooms.
- Select single-host live show, multi-host chat room, Dating Game Theme, or password-protected Private Rooms, to just talk to random strangers, find friends, play dating games to find your romantic date and meetup.
- Choose topics such as Social Dating, Just Chatting, Relationships, Music, Comedy, Movies/TV, Anime, ASMR/Sleep, Video Game, Horoscope/Astrology, Trivia Games, and Just Talk.
- Add voice labels to your favorite DJ: Sexy, Cute, Romantic, Friendly, Sweet, Funny, Warm
- DJs can invite other DJs to BATTLE using 1V1 PK function.

No matter where you are, with Cuddle, you'll never be lonely! Enjoy the new era of radio and walkie talkie!
