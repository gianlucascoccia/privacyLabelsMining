Description

Straight from the world’s greatest bass fishing minds to your phone, BassForce® puts the expertise of the greatest bass anglers of all time right at your fingertips. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned tournament veteran, BassForce® will help you elevate your skill to the next level!

Meet the BassForce®: Christie, Evers, Jones, Martens, Palaniuk, Reese, Swindle, VanDam, and Wheeler.

Just input the conditions for your fishing day and our pros will show you which specific baits have worked for them under those exact same conditions. They’ll give you the details all the way down to lure names, colors and sizes to help you get dialed-in to the best bite quickly.

Video tips from the pros will help you make every cast count. Our instructional videos are exclusive and available only to BassForce subscribers. Many lures require a specific retrieve or cadence to entice big bass, and our pros reveal their secrets to you.

Real time on-the-water conditions always dictate which fishing techniques succeed and fail, and those conditions are constantly changing. A cold front came through – no problem, rain is moving in – we’ve got you covered, the water is getting muddy – we’ve seen that before.

BassForce® even lets you save custom Condition Presets to account for times when you visit the same bodies of water frequently. Just load the preset, import the current weather conditions, and get your lure picks from the pros.

With BassForce® you can see exact lure choices for any of our 9 pros, or you can use the Premium Match feature to cross-reference and see which lures the pros agree on for your fishing conditions.

Lure picks from every pro are highly accurate and come directly from the pros. Each BassForce® pro spends countless hours putting their personal bass fishing know-how into the BassForce® app to account for any possible scenario.

Our pros have collectively amassed over 200 years of bass fishing experience, and for the first time ever it’s available to you on demand!

To view our Terms of Use, visit bassforcefishing.com/terms-of-service/
