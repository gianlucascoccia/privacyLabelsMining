Description

The most unique cyber fantasy mobile game, a completely new hardcore gameplay, which will immerse you in a modern 21st century underworld.

It is the year 2098, the end of the century is near, the great alliances of the world are in a great cold war with each other, while neon lights flash around the borders city under the thick fog, but the people of the city are still unaware of the situation. This city is one that lives outside the law, and is far from all countries, and has gradually become a battleground for all camps. Conflict between advanced technology and the decadent civilizations has become irreversible, and Cyberpunk has finally opened his curtains after long nights.

It was around this time on one of the street corners in a little humble detective agency, a sharp and keen detective sensed a change in the air, he quickly needed to round up a team to go and search out the hidden scheme that was in the making...

DaHKkid , 12/14/2020

F2P friendly idle cyberpunk RPG

This game is quite generous with what it provides for advancement through the various game modes. As an idle game you don’t really have to spend money to be able to get quite far. Of course you’ll hit some walls where you just need to give he game time to generate enough exp for you to level up to advance, but that is the nature of idle games. The game could use some extra explanations of certain mechanics and the developers should update the halidoms provided in higher stage mines ( blue halidoms are pretty useless past a certain point of progression but are rewards for beating mine levels that require high level gold or at least purple halidoms). However overall the game is well done and you feels pretty fair to all players. Definitely worth a try if you like this art style and idle game mechanics.

Zerohero0087 , 10/27/2020

Hard wall stopped at level 60

So I found this game though a Facebook ad, and I’ve never done a review before but I felt I needed to for those that may download this. It’s a fun game for certain with plenty to do and it’s not really pay to win, the paying part just makes it easier I’m sure. My issue with the game is once I hit level 60, and I got caught up with all side stuff that you can do, I am no longer able to proceed without waiting days and days to get enough money/exp to level up. I also outranked myself in arena and now just get beat over and over and over and makes me not want to play it.

Overall the game is nice and plays well but it makes me not even want to open it anymore because I can’t enjoy it like I used to, really big downfall in a game that you want people to like and keep playing.

Also a small pet peeve of mine is that some of the messages and dialog are English but you can tell someone that isn’t English speaking wrote them and it makes the game feel cheaper.

Noxkit , 10/31/2020

Great game!

It’s a lot of fun, and can keep you going back multiple times over the day. The only thing I’d like to see improved maybe is the AI for combat. I’ve been in fights where it never feels like it’ll end just because there’s a tank and healer n my characters will stay solely focused on the tank rather than the healer who is constantly keeping the tank alive, resulting in a draw because of the 30 round limit, lol. It’s a minor issue that can be solved by me having better dps of course, but yeah that’s it. I love the game regardless and fully recommend it. :)
