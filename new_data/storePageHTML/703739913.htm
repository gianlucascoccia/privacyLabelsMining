Very difficult to use app when purchase digital online

I purchased the digital bundle online from my desktop computer, and everything works great there. However, the app was unusable to start without technical support. With WDW support and a few tries, I am now able to use the app. My recommendation is this- if you get the subscription, it is great. If you want to view online, plan to use your computer and not an Apple app until you’ve gone back and forth with support. That said, if you buy the package through the app, apparently it works better that way, but the print editions are not included. I’m looking forward to my subscription. WDW support was great. They were really responsive and got it to work reasonably quickly. Just wish the app worked with less hassle. Good luck.

The best gift you will ever give yourself

This magazine checks all the boxes. It is well written, the pictures are frame worthy, and there are always some fun facts to learn that you never knew before. For any Disney Lover, it is a dream come true. And very helpful when planning a trip. You learn about all the great menus and dining experiences, what is new at the parks, and what us coming in the near future. You get helpful hints about booking a spot on the new Star Wars rides and how to better immerse yourself in the whole Galaxy’s Edge experience. This magazine truly is a must have. I wait excitedly for it to arrive every month. And the digital version is fantastic as you can click onto certain pictures and a world opens up before your eyes. I get both the digital and printed copies and am glad I have both formats. The magazine is truly a gift to be enjoyed and cherished.
