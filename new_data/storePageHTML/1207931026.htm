Description

Request late plate meals and view the menu of your sorority or fraternity.

Upper Crust Food Service makes your life easier through our kitchen management program. We handle all aspects of your kitchen including staffing, ordering and food preparations. We take the burden of running a busy kitchen off your shoulders, leaving you with fresh meals and great service. Outside the kitchen, Upper Crust helps you operate the house by providing household chemicals, cleaning supplies, and paper goods.
