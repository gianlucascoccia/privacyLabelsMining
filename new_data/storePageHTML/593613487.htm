Description

Got a hankerin’ for them there Burgushi? A restaurant this creative deserves a creative app, and now we’ve rolled everything you love about The Cowfish into one easy-to-use mobile app!!!

We’ve made it easier than ever for you to find "an unusual place to enjoy a great dish"! Use The Cowfish app to find us, put your name on our wait list with the push of a button, browse our menu selections, or even give real time feedback to our team while dining at your table… all from your mobile device. With the help of some of our friends such as Eel Eel Cool J and Mackerel Jordan, this app offers a fun, interactive twist on keeping track of your place on our wait list, even if you step away to enjoy some nearby shopping! You will also enjoy one-touch phone number access for easy calling, plus an interactive map that will direct you straight to The Cowfish!

So whether you’re near or far, The Cowfish experience is now in the palm of your hands. Use it wisely, and use it daily!

Useless bogus app

We joined the waitlist when we were within 3 miles, and immediately received a text saying we were in. I reserved a booth. The app showed us that there were two parties ahead of us, and as we neared the restaurant, it showed zero, so we were next—woohoo! When we walked in, the hostess said there were no booths available, but we could sit on the patio. I said I had reserved a booth and that we were next. She said she texted me 18 minutes ago and I had ten minutes to respond. The text said nothing about responding and the app kept me in the waitlist. The staff obviously pays no attention whatsoever to the app, so it was pointless—don’t waste your time.

loganwolf113 , 05/04/2019

Improvements

I guess they've made some changes since these negative reviews were written. The radius is ten miles now. Once you're ten miles or closer to the restaurant, you can be on the list. I checked with the hostess one we got there, and we were definitely on the list. The app does show you how many parties are in front of you, but the number doesn't update unless you close out of the app. All in all, it saved us about fifteen minutes, so it was useful.
