Description

Go Live with friends! Create dance music videos with special effects, filters, and text! Create your own mashup music! We highlight upcoming dancers so come join the fam!

RenRico is the social network that supports dancers, creators, and artists! Create short form mobile videos, lip sync dubs, and showcase your incredible talent. Live stream and dance to music together with your friends over video chat with our Link Up feature. Or make your own song mashups with our beat pad drum machine! Fans build closer relationships with the creators and influencers they love and creators can earn additional income. Follow others in the community and discover new #challenges trending near you or around the world!

■ CREATE COOL VIDEOS:
* Add music to your videos with your favorite sound clips
* Create professional looking music videos with our AI-Smart auto-editor - REMIX!
* Edit your videos with fun filters, special effects, GIF & sticker overlays, and custom text captions - all for free with RenRicoFX
* Instantly collab or duet with other user videos
* Create up to 8 frame video collages

■ GET DISCOVERED:
* Mix multiple songs together to create the next viral mashup music
* See what's trending and who's on fire on the RenRico tab
* Discover new viral content, sounds, and challenges on the Explore tab
* Search for the most popular dancers, challenges, dubs, or other creators in your area

■ GET CLOSE:
* Go LIVE and chat with your fans in a live stream broadcast
* Video chat with others and even record your duet videos live together
* Let your followers know how you're feeling with Vibes that disappear after 24 hours
* Private chat (DM) your favorite creators and friends

■ GET PAID:
* Use coins to send Super Likes to others and show some creator appreciation!
* Creators can receive Super Likes that can turn into Props
* Props help you earn compensation as a creator

■ GET CLOUT:
* Add hashtags and a location to your posts to help you get discovered
* If you’re the most popular user of a hashtag or #challenge, you get to add the tag to your profile – you’re now the head Honcho!
* Download and easily share your RenRico videos to other social apps like TikTok, Triller, Snapchat, Lomotif, Zynn, Musical.ly, Funimate, Dubsmash, Firework, Flipagram, Mustar, One Challenge, Clash, Byte, Likee, Tik Tok and more!
