Description

Make faster and smarter personalized decisions in the worlds largest wholesale automotive marketplace directly from your phone.
New features include:
- New Native home screen with clean, modern design
- Digital Runlists for all Manheim locations
- Powerful VIN scanner that knows the status of your vehicle and where it’s located
- Personalized experience based on where you buy/sell
- Valuation tools to help you get the right price for a vehicle
- Recommended Vehicles based on your buying history

Note: This app is only for Manheim customers with an existing username and password. You must be a licensed automobile dealer with a valid AuctionACCESSS membership to register. To become a Manheim customer or for further assistance please call Manheim Customer Care at 1-866-MANHEIM.

Improved layout but way Too slow!

The layout is much improved over the original Manheim app, but the load times are way too slow! It makes the app practically unusable!

It doesn’t matter if I’m on 5G or my home WiFi with very high speed. I’m also on iPhone 12 Pro Max, and I close all my other background apps, doesn’t make a difference. Doesn’t make a difference if I use my girlfriends phone on different carrier (she has at&t,
I have Verizon) or iPad. The app is just way too slow, takes 10 minutes to load work book (no exaggeration). Inventory run list takes 10-15 min to load too. Scanning a vin takes 4-5 minutes. Condition reports take forever to load. I don’t think it’s a problem with the server side either, because the Manheim website loads at least 10x faster on the same device (iPhone 12 pro max).

Again, nice layout but load times are crippling.

Please address the loading time issue because the layout is nicer on the app vs website when using a mobile device.
