Used to be good but has gone downhill fast

War game turned I don’t know what. The updates are constant now. Hardly any kingdom vs kingdom events these days. All kinds of crazy events like alliance carnivals every month rather than kvk. Now the newest update is some magic thing that will allow a player to attack you bubble or not and you loose t4 troops that don’t go to alter. I don’t know about many of you, but I work and don’t play this game 24 hours a day. Not spending money like they want only to have the developers turn this game into something more than what it should be. It’s no longer a war game where you’re able to strategically plan attacks unless you are planning on strategically planning the next carnival and how to rebuild your colossus after the next AVA. No thank you. I’m moving on. Money down the drain. Too bad because it was good first two years. Third year not so much.

Harry myers , 06/19/2018

Amazing Game!

I feel like at the start it wasn’t too much fun. Really had no one to help me out or anything. I wanted to play though to get items in another game. I’ve been playing this game for over half a year now and I’ve got nothing but positive about this. The only thing I feel like is dull is the start when you are new. I did not have too much fun when I was new and trying to understand everything. This game isn’t enjoyable if you do not have friends or people that you met inside of the game. If you know how the game works, you will most likely enjoy the game a lot. All of my friends say that it’s just a knockoff of other war games but I always tell them it’s much more than that and it’s not like other war games. There’s strategy and different mechanics. There’s dragons to attack monsters, kingdoms, thrones, farming and more. I’ve actually created a second account to help my main account because of how much I play. So for all of you people deciding to play or try the game, find a friend or two to play with or find other people who might take you in so you can understand better.

LivJ90 , 04/08/2021

BoB Addiction Is Real!

I’ve been playing this game for years now and it’s seriously the best escape. The community of people have become like family and many complain about having to pay to play but that’s just not true. Play to win? Absolutely. I mean you have to understand that some people have put thousands and thousands in to the game so of course they have the best stuff and are farther ahead than people who don’t but you CAN grow with little to no money involved...especially if you’re in the right alliance and active. Most likely you’ll end up putting some money in to it if you play for any amount of time though. It’s a great escape from the real world and very fun...especially during any fighting events. Big rivalries which make the events 100x better and a big enough game to have many kingdoms and a wide diversity of players but small enough that you begin to know most of the top players in every kingdom after awhile. I quit for a few months when I had personal things come up in life but friends took care of my account and now I’m back and feel like I never left.
