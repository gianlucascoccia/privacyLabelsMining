Description

Make new social friends with Wave. If you want to request someone's profile swipe right, or swipe left if you are not interested. Links to your social media are kept private until you approve a user.

Wave Features:
- Fun swiping to make new friends.
- Add them on social media or get to know them directly in Wave.
- Connect with new people on your favorite social media apps [Snapchat, Instagram, Tik Tok, Twitter].
- Let other people discover you on your favorite social media apps.
- Make new friends from around the world.
- Send disappearing text messages.
- Send animated GIFs by Giphy.
- Take 10 seconds videos that can only be viewed once and send them to your friends.
- Take photos that can only be viewed once and send them to your friends.
- Chat directly with your matches directly on Wave.
- Chat and Ephemeral messages include screenshot protection. If someone takes a screenshot of a chat, photo or video you send them their account will be automatically suspended.
- Screen Recording Protection: If Wave detects that the screen is being recorded the app will refuse to open up chat.

Go make new friends today!

DISCLAIMER: Wave is NOT a dating app. It is a fun way to connect with new people on Snapchat.

Unlimited gems and boosted profile views are available through a VIP Subscription

A purchase amount of $1.99 will be applied to your iTunes account on a weekly basis if you subscribe to a VIP membership. You can cancel at anytime in your iTunes account settings.

VIP Subscription for Wave is offered in 1 week periods. 1 week subscription price is $1.99 per week.
