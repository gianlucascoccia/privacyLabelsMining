Low radio review

I thoroughly enjoyed this app. As someone who is visually impaired, it enables me to listen to the live feed of the pro football teams that I follow. It is extremely accessible using the speech feature on iPhones and other eye devices called voiceover. This app cannot go away! A couple of years ago, while I was on vacation in Europe, I was able to use this app to listen to some of the football games. I was not about to miss a game simply because I was halfway across the world, and pro radio enabled me to do just that.
