Description

Get a daily shot of motivation that will prime you to be your absolute best every day!

Peptalk provides life-changing curated playlists of videos, audios and quotes that are perfect for your morning routine - or whenever you need a boost in motivation or inspiration to get things done.

It’s no secret that better mental habits yield better results! Our goal is to help motivate and inspire you in different ways ranging from crushing your fitness goals or improving your business skills to fighting anxiety, depression and addiction.

The Peptalk app offers many features and curated topics to help you create the daily habit of positive thinking and encouragement.

Was great until...

App was decent until the most recent update. IMHO the old app had a much better look, this new white/pink layout looks pretty weak and not very ‘pep-ish’. But more importantly, as others have mentioned, this new update is just randomly missing important features that the old app had...one of the things I really looked forward to each morning, in addition to Peptalk audio, was the daily motivational quotes (usually overlaying great pictures of the people who said them) that showed up as the app was loading. You were able to choose other things like custom motivational texts instead as well. I’m really not sure what the point of this update was, but don’t see myself staying long at all if this is the way the app’s going to be.
