Description

Chessable is the best place to learn and improve at chess online! Chessable is built for chess lovers and uses spaced repetition to make sure you never forget a move!

Why Chessable?

Companion app to Chessable, the best place to learn chess online from the world’s top players. Brought to you by world champion GM Magnus Carlsen, Chessable’s MoveTrainer technology prompts you to learn different moves, and then finds the optimal time for you to review them so you remember every detail. Enjoy free courses on openings, the middle game, endgame, tactics and strategy to improve your chess skills with hundreds of free lessons.

You have the ability in the app to:


LEARN:

-Learn new content from some of the best chess players in the world (including Magnus Carlsen).


REVIEW:

-Review course content and video using our exciting MoveTrainer technology which prompts you to repeat moves and aids in memorisation.


DISCOVER COURSES:

-Find new FREE courses and start learning them immediately.


VIDEO CONTENT

- View hundreds of videos and interactive lessons from top Grandmasters and coaches.


PROFILE

-View your profile where you can track your progress, XP points, streak, badges and more!


SETTINGS

-Be able to change your course, chapter, and global settings all within the app in order to be able to use Chessable to its fullest potential.

I know it’ll get better

First off, I love Chessable. Been using it almost a year now and it’s content is excellent. Such an awesome platform. The content and Chessable as a whole are 5/5 easily.

The app is great at the things it does, I just wish it did more. As others have eluded to, I definitely miss the analysis board and opening explorer. Very often while going through a book the author will make a comment about something being obvious or bad etc and in the website when it isn’t clear to my peon brain why, I like to click over to the analysis board to see. I also use it when in a course I know the players I will be playing will make some non-theory move and want to see what my response should be.

There are other examples of the benefits of the website compared to the app and I have no doubt those features will be added (as well as dark mode...) but until then, I have to give only 4 stars because I know how good it can (hopefully will) be!

HeyBinet! , 01/20/2021

What you’ve been missing ...

Want to work on an opening repertoire? Check. Want to improve your endgame? Check. Want to dominate in the middle game? Check. Chessable has a ton of free training sessions that will introduce you to any number of ways to improve your game, with scheduled repetitions to train your memory muscle. The trainers are high grade masters, the sessions are thorough—some if not most include videos—paid courses are worth the money, too. I’m currently working through 100 endgames you must know, an anti-Sicilian repertoire for black, middle game strategies from 2020 Legends of Chess contenders ... there is a PRO membership, but the only advantage I see with that is it identifies the moves that prove difficult concepts for you to grasp and allows you to work on. There is also an option, if you have the points in the bank, to work through all the a particular training session to prepare for a tournament. I could on. Lots of perks in this app. If you are serious about your chess this app should be on your phone. You won’t be disappointed

Turtle7373 , 10/25/2021

Very Close

I think this mobile app is very good, and I really appreciated the addition of Dark Mode. I think there are two things that would instantly make the app much better however. Firstly, there needs to be an option to watch the videos in full screen in the app. If people are spending a lot on video components for courses then you should make that experience as good as possible and the fact that you can’t watch in full screen makes no sense. Secondly, moving between chapters in courses feels clunky. It would be a lot better if we could swipe to change chapters rather than having to use the navigation or arrow buttons. Other than that the app is really good!
