Description

Now you can sound like Daft Punk, Stevie Wonder, Frampton and Zapp!

HOW DOES IT WORK?!? Point the speaker directly into your mouth, but DO NOT SING. The SHAPE OF YOUR MOUTH makes the notes 'talk' (not your voice). Start with a simple wah-wah effect before attempting full lyrics and tap HELP to see the TUTORIAL if you're confused.

PRO TIP: Point the SPEAKER (not the mic) into your mouth while you play the notes. Don't say anything- just shape your mouth around the sound. All instruments, especially talkboxes require a certain amount of practice, so spend some time with it and have fun! For additional info, full tutorials and videos, please visit http://vbiliti.com/pockettalkbox
