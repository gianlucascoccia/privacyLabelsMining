Description

Alex Crockford, the creator of #CrockFit is a personal trainer & fitness model from Surrey, UK. Alex‘s passion is to help people around the world become healthier, fitter & stronger. He believes fitness is for us all, no matter what our current fitness level or experience is! The workouts in the #CrockFit App are accessible & fun, but will also challenge you to become the best version of yourself!

The #CrockFit App functions as your complete training guide, providing workout programmes suitable for gym or home training, designed to increase your physical fitness, burn fat, gain muscle and transform your body. You are guided through your workout plan with a step-by-step journey, tutorial videos and the ability to track the weights you use during workouts.

Additional features include the ability to upload monthly progress photos and body weight measurements. The #CrockFit App will also provide access to the #CrockFitter’s (the user) additional information such as the #CrockFit Food Plan and the training fundamentals from the original PDFs.

The FREE version includes the ability to try the first week of any of the programmes for home or gym training to experience what it’s like to be a #CrockFitter!

You can learn more about the varied #CrockFit Plans (GymFit SheFit HomeFit etc.)

#Crockfit

When I first decided to start working out, I wasn’t sure how to go about it so I researched various plans. I came across #Crockfit and I couldn’t be more happier with my decision.

As mentioned, I am a beginner when it comes to working out. This app takes this into consideration. It’s easy to use. Not only does the app list exercises but if you are unsure of how to perform the exercise you can watch a video demonstration. Also it allows you to enter in weight information so you don’t have to remember what you did the last time. It also times your rests between exercises and sets.

Overall my experience with the app has been positive and I can not wait for the plans 3 and 4 plus the abs routine to be added. Also that is another perk each plan is 12 weeks so you feel like you making small accomplishments and the routines are changed up within the plan every couple weeks so you are not constantly repeating the same routine for 12 weeks.
