Description

It’s finally here for iOS, an app that simulates a boxing coach and calls out realistic, fast paced combinations.

The boxing coach simulator calls out boxing techniques for you to execute, there are 4 modes and 3 intensity levels. This includes a Southpaw Mode, Counter Punching Mode, Virtual Padwork and Combo Creator Mode.

Choose as many rounds as you like, adjust the round length, the rest between rounds, and set a warning bell to tell you how many seconds are left in the round. Press ‘Start’ and get to work!

Love the app, could be better

This is a great boxing trainer app. Worth the investment if you like training on your own and would like guided combos round by round. Some improvements I recommend are allowing Apple Music (or any other music app) to play while using the app. I also think it would be great if the app would still work while my phone screen is locked. Keep up the good work!

goflow9 , 05/09/2019

Disappointed

This app used to work well in the background while having the Hykso app (with the boxing sensors) in the foreground counting the punches/speed/intensity. It was also working even with the screen off (just like any other audio app). Now none of these things function anymore and this is very annoying. The idea itself is brilliant and the app would be really useful if these issues were solved. How can I use this when I’m in a gym, hitting the Heavy Bag and having to look at the screen of my phone to prevent it from going off during the resting periods? Once it goes off the rest timer stops and the next round doesn’t start. Why not just force the screen to stay always on or make the app work with the screen off? Is anyone testing the app before release? Currently it’s almost useless.
