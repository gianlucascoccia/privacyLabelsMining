Really cool, but..

I just downloaded it and the available selection of wallpapers are great, but I wish there was a search bar that I could use to look for specific wallpapers, like backgrounds for the different seasons. Also, the live wallpapers do not have that much of a smooth transition when they loop. I tried out a live wallpapers and it pauses briefly to restart the loop. I know it's just me being picky, but I do like the smooth transitions, otherwise it bothers me and I won't use it. Something else I am not a fan of is that I do have to give a rating and review to unlock some of the wallpapers. It is better than having to pay, but it'd be nice if the wallpapers could just be accessed without having to write anything.

Other than the things I said just previously, the wallpapers are wonderful and are of great quality. I will keep using them and I do recommend since a lot of pictures you find online can be blurry or won't fit your screen right.

The 4 star rating is because of the things I listed. Otherwise I would have given 5 stars since the wallpapers are really nice.
