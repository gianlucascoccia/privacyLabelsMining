Description

Easy to follow guided meditations for mindfulness, sleep and relaxation! Install now and unlock your potential.

Serenity: Guided Meditations allows you to learn a variety of meditation and mindfulness techniques that aims to bring a sense of peace, calm and happiness to your life.

Learn the basics with the free, easy to follow 7 day audio course. Gain the skills required to make mindful meditation a part of the rest of your life.

Foundations: Expand your skills and learn about mindfulness, self awareness, values and goals

Sleep guides: A selection of guided mediations designed to guide you to sleep using relaxation techniques, peaceful music and peaceful sounds

Stress relief: soothe anxiety with relaxation and mindfulness techniques to calm the mind and body

Quick meditations: short sessions to practice your skills or provide a sense of peace and calm from the stresses of your day

Kids meditations - simplified and shorter audio sessions designed specifically for children.

Plus more being added to the app all the time

Other features:

No sign up/log in required - start meditating straight away

Ideal for beginners with over 2 hrs of free audio.

No subscriptions necessary - extra content is available either through one time permanent purchases or unlock everything with a subscription

Easy to follow - ideal for beginners and more experienced mindful meditators alike

Silent mind - learn a technique to quieten the mind of thoughts

Meditation and mindfulness skills - Learn to separate thoughts from feelings to improve your emotional well being

Gratitude - Change your perspective on life by understanding gratitude and gain a new appreciation of the world around you

Plus

Set up daily reminders to stay on track

Stay motivated by completing challenges and unlocking more free sessions as you progress

Monitor your progress with stats and graphs

Apple Health - Serenity works with Apple Health app to track all your meditation session if you require

Airplay - in built support for Airplay to play your sessions through AirPlay speakers

Over 2 hrs of free content:
7 Day beginner course
Basic sessions for regular practice
Plus a different mini meditations each day

See if mindful meditation can improve your life - install now!

ocdiva34 , 01/06/2020

Simple and easy to use

I’ve tried so many meditation apps and this is the first I feel like I can stick with. I love the simplicity of the app. It’s very straightforward with a clean UI. With other apps I felt overwhelmed by the amount of content or the way it was organized in the app to get to the content I was interested in. Because I’m new at meditation I really like that the guided mediations are helpful and also 10 min. Any more than 10 and I’d be too overwhelmed to try. They also offer mini mediations that are less than 5 min. I also love that they give you actual free content that’s useful. And for those of you who are tired of paying a subscription you’ll love (like i do) that you can choose to unlock everything with a one time payment. In the end I actually chose the very reasonably priced 6 month subscription to try out more of the sleep sections and stress relief sections. But if I stick with it I will definitely upgrade to the one time payment. This app made it easy for me to want to support by upgrading. Keep up the good work!

Laura'cska , 12/25/2018

Foundation - Level 1: Great overview

I just completed Level 1 - Foundation. It provides a very nice overview of basic meditation techniques, including focusing on breath, and body scan. I am not new to meditation, but I am struggling to incorporate it into my daily routine, which I have recently realized is very important to me.

The short time (10-12 min) that each lesson takes is perfect for helping me to commit to a daily meditation practice. It’s just enough time to provide me with a centering experience and almost guarantee that I can complete a session in the morning before anyone in my family wakes, which is my preferred timing for meditation (and I don’t have to wake uncomfortably early to accomplish my goal).

Thank you for this free level! It helps me to start my day in a positive way, despite the various challenges I am currently navigating in my life. I encourage anyone to try out Serenity, no matter where you are on the spectrum of meditation experience.

spongefan , 09/14/2018

Great app for all levels

Definitely one of the better meditation apps that I’ve tried. The background music as well as the voice of the narrator are very calming and help you relax easily. As someone who has always had a rough time meditating, I found these guided practices to be very helpful as they break things down into smaller steps and help you to ease into the practice of meditation slowly. This app offers various different levels and types of meditation, making it ideal for both those who are new to meditation and those who are more experienced.
