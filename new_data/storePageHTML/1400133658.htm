tmike94 , 03/11/2020

I like it but there are issues

I’ve had this app for 2 months, and bought the monthly subscription. The browser is a little glitchy. The videos I get are really good. Nothing wrong on that front. The main problem I have is that when you save videos, after you delete them, the videos stay on the phone. You can’t access them or you can but only view them continuously. I’ve had to delete the app and reinstall it. Other minor issues are the crashes, and sometimes the app will have you download an ad. Those problems happen once in a great while. Another issue is the bookmarks or saved websites don’t load.

My main purpose is to save videos that I have access to, and this apps does that. There has been no issues with the videos. And I would recommend but proceed with caution. These issues do pile up especially the use I put the app through.
