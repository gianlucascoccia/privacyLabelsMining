Description

Imagine the possbility to press and mash buttons, the possibility to hear incredible sounds in front of thousands or on your! With DJ Soundboard we have collated some of the best sound effects to make sure your DJ set will hipe up your audience to the extreme. Effects include sirents, airhorns, vocals, alarms and many more. Effects can even be looped to increase or decrease temp ensuring every effect matches your track nicely, and with an incredibly design that scales and looks perfect on any device you'll be able to focus on what really matters - the music!.
