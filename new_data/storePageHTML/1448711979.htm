Description

THE GLOBAL SNEAKER DESTINATION

The best app to get hold of limited edition sneakers and streetwear, as well as keep up to date with the latest SNS content and projects.

SNS (Sneakersnstuff) is one of the leading sneakers stores in the world.

Started in 1999 it has expanded with retail stores in New York, Los Angeles, London, Paris, Berlin, Tokyo and Stockholm.

ONE TOUCH SIGN UP TO LIMITED SNEAKERS AND STREETWEAR
• SNS continues to develop the fairest way to get hold of limited sneakers
• Sign up and verify your phone number and payment details
• Add your preferred size, pick up or delivery location
• Enter all future draws with one touch

BE INFORMED OF RANDOM DROPS
• Make sure you have notifications switched on
• Be prepared for random drops exclusive to verified app users

STAY UP TO DATE WITH RELEASES
• The first place to check for what is officially releasing
• Check back daily for release updates
• Be the first to get access through dedicated links

GET INSPIRED
• Check out SNS projects and editorials
• Get the story behind your favourite pairs

Upgrade with room to grow

Sooooo...oooooooooooo much better than before for raffles. Huge upgrade over having to enter all info every time you entered. That being said, the rest of the app is ok. Actual sales items are kind of here and there, product details/pics are tough to navigate and really get a feel for. But.. just the improvement in the raffle system makes this a full win. Now fine time some other aspects and this is a 5star engine.

EasySnipes , 12/16/2019

App won’t send me verification text (can’t enter raffles)

Giving this app a one star until they can’t fix these technical issues I’m having. I have been trying for a week now to get a verification text sent to me so that I can use the app as intended. Right now, the app has been able to verify my payment method and billing address but for some reason they won’t send me a verification text for my phone number. I’ve tweeted then and haven’t heard anything back. I made sure to enter a ‘1’ before my phone number and that isn’t fixing anything. I ever created an account for my wife and her phone number wasn’t sent a verification text either. Not sure what to do to fix this so hopefully someone at SNS will read this and can throw me a bone. I’m about to miss out on this raffle opportunity for these shoes I’ve been eyeing for a minute. Please SNS, help a playa out with this technical issue!

R Anthony , 06/26/2019

Can’t add payment info...?!?

Of the 3 family members that have the app, I am the only one that it actually works for. Every time they have tried to add payment info for their accounts, so they are ready for raffles or releases, they get error messages. There’s zero issue with the cards or the data entry. They’ve each tried 2-3 different cards just to make sure. They contacted SNS via social media, and the response given was it was their fault for trying to enter the info more than once after the error message. They were told to wait several days and try again. That didn’t change the outcome at all, and SNS has yet to reply to their last communication.
