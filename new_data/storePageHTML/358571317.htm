Description

The Newsday app provides extensive Long Island local news coverage 24/7.

Newsday subscribers have unlimited access to the app. Or, you can subscribe via iTunes for an all-access digital subscription.

App features

Customizable alerts
 Personalize your experience and enable notifications you want to receive, choosing from breaking news, weather, traffic, sports, business, opinion and more.

Newsday on your TV
Watch our award-winning videos and documentaries from the app on your flat screen from Feed Me TV to breaking news with our redesigned video player, complete with playlist functionality and casting capability.

The Digital Crossword
Enjoy Newsday’s daily interactive crossword puzzles presented by Stan Newman. You can start and save puzzles for later, check for errors as you play and reveal answers if you’re stuck.

The Digital Paper
Enjoy an interactive version of Newsday print edition with exclusive photos and videos that come alive off the page. Open articles in easy-to-read story windows, download the paper to read offline and bookmark content to read later. Plus, check out current and past editions of Feed Me magazine, Newsday Classic editions, the Fun Book and other special sections.

Offline reading
 When in airplane mode or not connected to the Internet, the text of all articles loaded when you were connected last will be available for you to read. You can also save any article, video or photo for viewing later.

Section swipe and customized navigation
 Easily swipe left and right from section to section rather than having to open them individually through the navigation menu. Our redesigned navigation also lets you customize which sections you want to see and the order you see them in. You can also customize sections for news and information in your community.

Manage your newsletters
 Subscribe to all of Newsday’s newsletters delivered to your inbox, from breaking news, the morning update, afternoon update and more in the app.

Manage My Account
 Update your information and personalize your subscription. You can schedule a vacation hold on your print subscription, resolve a delivery issue, view and pay your bill, enroll in eBilling or EZ-Pay, update your billing address and more.

 Interactive weather map
 Our weather map can be easily customized to show you a variety of weather conditions on Long Island and around the world. Take a look at the future cast, which shows you what the next six hours of weather could bring and lets you easily track rain, snow and storms. You can save up to 10 locations for quick reference.   

Sports scores
 You can view full stats from all of your favorite pro sports teams on the scores pages and see all upcoming games.

Online Subscription Troubles

Three times I subscribed to Newsday digital services, the count is not over a lifetime, years or several months. The 3 attempts were actual agreements I accepted to pay within a few months.
It was more times I tried logging in after creating passwords that were saved. It seems like Newsday soon reject my subscriptions and state they have no record of my accounts.
The Long Island newspaper was a part of my life growing up and residing in Suffolk County. They have an excellent staff with talented reporters, photographers, and writers. Newsday investigated and produced prize-winning reports, series, articles, and videos. The establishment won major, editorial awards.
Nonetheless, I am much discouraged by the experience as a subscriber. Many thousands people might be having a great time with Newsday. But, I had enough; the only task for me is to make sure they don’t charge my credit card.
