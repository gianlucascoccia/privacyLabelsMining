Smerzyy , 04/01/2021

Wow

Let me say, I was stuck on baseball super stars 2012. But it ended up crashing all the time recently. And I finally gave in and got the new one. (I tried 2013 and really didn’t enjoy it). And it’s definitely a different experience. Feels like a Nintendo type baseball game all on ur phone. Truly addicting. I wish there was just a story mode that you could play only like the old versions. Because when you play the “my player” you only play one season consisting of 7 weeks (6 season games and a championship week) and then your done with that player. They then can be assigned to your main team you use for season or online play. That is truly my only complaint. Wish I could just have a my player mode where I can make my person the video game version of Barry bonds and not have to worry about his storyline ending. But, the benefits greatly outweigh that negative. The new features are crazy fun, and I am heavily invested in this game as well as Dokkan battle. If you have free time I certainly suggest downloading it. If you don’t have free time, then you may procrastinate and fall behind on work or college haha.

Shredfearn , 08/27/2020

Changes are preventing game from shining

I used to be obsessed with Baseball Superstars 2013. The changes they made to 2020 are just not as enjoyable. The “Myplayer” feature offers a short 6 game season along with a 1-game playoff match. It’s very underwhelming. Additionally, once your done with the season your player is just transferred to your online team and you can’t continue on to new seasons with them. The thrill you got from playing 2013’s baseball superstars is what made the game fun and developing your character season by season was what made it for me. I also think the art style of it was overall more enjoyable to the eye. The fact that there is an auto mode offers no challenge, and the trainers/players aspect is not straightforward and kind of confusing. This reminds me of every other game on the app store when it comes to drawing characters/heroes to enhance your team. It has a lot of p2w aspects.

Unfortunately, I doubt the 2013 version will ever be updated to be optimized and updated and I doubt that the devs will implement the features that made 2013 version fun into the 2020 version. I will continue to give this game a chance, but overall a lot of the features just aren’t up to par with what I expected out of Gamevil. All they had to do was make a better 2013 Baseball Superstars, but instead they are trying to introduce new, confusing features that just don’t gel well with what the game is supposed to be.

Maybe user error

I love the game, I used to play the older versions back in highschool and saw this on the App Store and downloaded it. So far it’s been a ton of fun, and I’ve almost built myself a complete lineup of custom dudes. The problem is, when I try to use my team in pvp the game won’t let me control all my players. It auto plays most of my batters and only occasionally asks me if I want to hit with two outs and a runner on first.. great. Well my team is decent enough that I can score runs even when the game won’t let me bat, but then it gets worse when it won’t let me pitch and the opposing team is scoring runs willy nilly. Whatever not every pitcher is perfect for every at bat, but when the game gives me challenges like don’t give up more than 2 runs max, then the first three auto batters against me get three base hits in a row it feels a little unfair. Even when I do choose to use my pitcher, all of my pitchers are average joes because the game won’t tell me where I can create my own pitcher. The create a player only lets me make position players so I’m left out here with a pitcher with a fastball and a cutter and nothing else. Don’t get me wrong Mariano had some great stuff but these guys aren’t wearing 42 for a reason. Google hasn’t helped so maybe submitting a review here might
