What is Dream Zone ?

· Gorgeous love interests!
· Breathtaking stories!
· Sensual scenes!
· Unique cards to collect!

Dream Zone is a dating simulator and a series of exciting interactive stories for guys. Create your character and choose your story. Flirt, search for your true love, start a slow fire in a long term relationship or dive right into fast-paced passion - it’s totally up to you! In this thrilling game for men you are free to live your dreams and unleash your passion.

What do you do IF:

· Your female boss craves your company?
· The girl next door is too scared to be alone and asks to spend the night with you?

Who will you choose?

· A former A-Grade student of a respectable family
· OR a spoiled Insta-diva with an explosive personality?

In Dream Zone you will become the youngest billionaire, a vlogging star on the rise, a world champion, or a renowned scientist. You choose your path in Dream Zone: The Dating Simulator & Interactive Story App!

If you like life simulation games, dating games, and choices games / episode games - Dream Zone is something to check out.

Twisted storylines, romance and drama in our original stories:

The Impossible Game

A mysterious voice message from the past has got you caught up in the game of billionaires. Can you make it to the finale and find out what is going on? Will you stay your former self or change your life once and for all? You decide! The game is on.

You are an ordinary guy, you and your geeky friends go to an ordinary school called Cleveland High. But what if the students around you aren’t human? What if everyone you’ve known all your life is a robot? How do you survive in a world that has become foreign to you?

Devil's Deal

Life just keeps getting worse every hour, but then a stranger offers you a once-in-a-lifetime deal. Will you accept it?

cheeseburgerchuck21 , 12/07/2020

Best interactive story game there is

Dream Zone is my favorite of all of the interactive story games. If you enjoy these games I highly recommend this one. Every story I have played through so far has been amazing with the perfect combination of mystery, adventure, and love. My favorite part about this game is the amount of free diamonds you get. You get 4 per episode for watching an ad with one energy every hour and a half until you have 2 energy. This equates to 8 diamonds every hour and a half which is way more than the typical 1 or 2 given by other games. You can also receive 20 free diamonds every day for watching ads as well as a sign in bonus of up to 60 depending on which day it is. This leads to almost 300 free diamonds a week without even counting ones you get from playing episodes. I recommend having a story you want to spend diamonds on and then one you read through without spending just to use your energy to get the end of episode diamonds. The stories are still enjoyable without spending but you just miss out on a few scenes. I would not recommend buying any of the cards as they are priced way too high for what they are. (And you can find them online) Overall I highly recommend this game over all other interactive story games.

There's a lot to like but with one major drawback

Just downloaded the app. I am enjoying the Half Human story and really digging music in it as well. The writing is decent enough and as for the stories although lacking in content at the moment I am looking forward to more story content. When it comes to the gems I have always had a problem with the cost of gems to make a choice and the amount of gems you get for finishing a chapter/episode. Obviously the cost of gems vary when it comes to choices and I find it to be demoralizing especially when you aren’t earning enough gems. You get 1 gem per episode and you get 4 ad videos daily to get 4 gems and it still isn’t enough to buy one good dialogue choice. There should be something other than ad videos to earn gems. For now, the app gets 3 stars from me. UPDATE!!! So every Thursday there is a new chapter released and that's all well and good except it's only a single chapter... A single chapter a week is insulting. While I understand that the game studio may only have 3-5 on staff but c’mon man. It's pretty ridiculous to only have two titles none of which are completed and no other titles to occupy your time before the next chapter release. The devs clearly had an idea in mind but it was half-cocked and poorly executed. Maybe take more time and release multiple chapters because I would much rather wait longer for at minimum 3-4 (Longer) chapters.
