Description

Grain is a revolving credit line you can use from your debit card. Your offer is based on the cash flow in your linked checking account.

• Get credit in minutes - link your primary checking account and get access to credit in minutes. No credit check.

• Use credit - transfer credit to your linked checking account and use it as you usually do from that account.

• Credit reporting - every month, we report your account activity to all three credit bureaus.

• No unnecessary fees - only 15% APR and 1% finance charge on credit advances.

• Smarter auto-pay - monthly minimum payments are made for you at the best time during your billing cycle to avoid late and overdraft fees.

• Personalized credit tips - adjust your habits and improve your credit score through actionable recommendations.

• Security taken seriously - we protect your data with 256 bit bank-level encryption and we are FDIC insured.

* Credit offer dependent on cash flow in primary linked account. Offer may require a security deposit. See your Credit Agreement for further details
