**Best Japanese learning app on the go! Ranks above 100 place in Travel section in the App Store in over 125 countries**

Looking for a simple app to learn Japanese? Try Learn Japanese by Codegent and you'll understand why we receive so many good reviews from our customers. Over 900 essential words and phrases spoken in native voice, useful for travellers and new learners.

• Store frequently used phrases in favourites
• Search by words inside the app
