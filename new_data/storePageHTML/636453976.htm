Description

Download FXStreet to get the relevant, real-time market updates. Get rid off all the noise and stay tuned to the markets.

FXSTREET, THE APP WITH THE BEST FOREX AND CRYPTO NEWS AND ANALYSIS IN REAL-TIME

With the FXStreet app, you can stay informed about market news and currency changes. Do not miss the latest news about the forex, crypto and stock markets to help your Trading with the best economic calendar and trading insights and signals.

WHY SHOULD I USE FXSTREET TO TRADE THE MARKETS?

FOREX AND TRADING NEWS
FXStreet has all the news about the currency and commodity markets in real-time. Download our app and get the most relevant live news that have an impact on the currency markets.

Forex and trading news 24/7, including EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, USD/CAD, USD/CHF, AUD/USD, Gold price and more.
Don’t miss a single detail about spot technical analysis patterns and trends in our forex charts and get relevant asset forecasts to help your trading.

Besides, you can read, filter and digest every forex news piece and receive notifications in real-time to be informed when new macroeconomic data and events are released.

CRYPTOCURRENCY NEWS, ANALYSIS AND PRICE PREDICTION
FXStreet also covers the cryptocurrency markets on a 24/7 basis. On our app you will get the most important crypto news and the most relevant technical and on-chain analysis for the top cryptos, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Cardano, Ripple, Dogecoin or Shiba Inu, among plenty of other coins.

You can also follow live cryptocurrency prices measured against different currencies and other cryptos on our live rates tab.

STOCK MARKET NEWS & TECHNICAL ANALYSIS
FXStreet is now covering a selection of important stocks. Get news and our trademark technical analysis on a big bunch of stock market tickers, with real-time updates. Get to know which tickers will be trending and get market trading opportunities from our stocks coverage.

MARKET RATES
Market movements, currency rates or stock price changes. We show you the best graphs so you can have a complete analysis of all the markets.
Follow currency pair price changes in real-time for more than 1,600 different active markets.

- Real-time contribution and change percentage
- Filter by pre-established lists or personalize your own ones
- Live charts by clicking on the asset name

ECONOMIC CALENDAR
Check our economic calendar in real-time and get all details on how macroeconomic data releases and other important fundamental events impact the Forex markets.

- 1,000 economic events in 42 countries
- Includes the current, estimated and previous data as well as a volatility indicator
- Time zone: you can choose between GMT or local time
- Real-time notifications that will let you know anytime new economic data is published
- Custom alerts for the Economic Calendar

FOREX & TRADING ALERTS
Receive custom Forex and Trading alerts.
Stay informed in real-time about the rise and fall of stocks, market movements and currency changes.

CHARTS IN REAL-TIME
FXStreet has the best real-time graphs to follow stocks and the best Forex analysis.
You will be provided with reliable currency change prices and a Trade View about the most essential information of the market, including EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, XAU/USD, BTC/USD, ETH/USD, XRP/USD and much more.

Guide your trading operations! Go in-depth and find forex, crypto and stocks chart patterns and trends.
