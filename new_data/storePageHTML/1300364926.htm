Description

Want to puppeteer a 3D animated emoji on an older device? Now you can! Using your face as the controller, you can make a 3D emoji mimic anything you do! Express yourself with brand new Supermojis every week! These are live emoji face filters and masks, better now as animated emojis. Make sure to check back often for new Supermojis.

In order to use the app, make sure you have an Internet connection, select a 3D Emoji, press and hold the record button. Videos will automatically replay after they are rendered. You can then share the video with others or privately.

Mikemdp , 05/04/2021

Absolute trash

Most of the overwhelmingly negative reviews of this terrible app focus on the insane $5/week subscription price, or that the free version limits you to choosing one character, and that becomes your Supermoji forever, unless you subscribe (I got stuck with Frankenstein).

Yes, those are two very good reasons this app is hot garbage. But other reasons are the facial recognition is terrible and the character’s expressions are stiff and limited. When you talk, the mouth just moves up and down awkwardly, literally as if you have a Halloween mask over your head. What good is an emoji that can’t express anything? That’s literally the role of an emoji.

This might be a fun impulse buy if it was a one-time price, and if you’re drunk. But at $5/month, Supermoji is one of the most foolish purchases anyone could make in the App Store.
