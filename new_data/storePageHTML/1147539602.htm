Description

Loke is an app for skaters, by skaters.

Finally, a skateboard app that helps you find the best skate spots, watch to-the-minute skate videos and industry news, and offer endless challenges to win skate product.

Loke is the largest skate spot finder ever.
With 25,000+ skate spots & 75,000+ skaters worldwide, we help you session the best spots near you, and connect with the skateboarding community in your city.

Loke has the best skate videos in one place.
Stay up to date with the latest videos shared by skaters worldwide, and browse our archive of the best skateboarding videos of all time.

Loke is more than a skate spot app.
With insight into skate sessions, to-the-minute skate videos and industry news, and endless challenges to win skateboard swag, we take your ideas to build a better world for skaters.

Loke is the social skateboarding experience.
Follow @getloke and tag #getloke to be featured in the fastest growing skateboarding community, where skate spots, skate videos, and industry news all come together.

We have been skating for 20+ years, and come from a design and technology background, positioning the app to offer an authentic skate experience and a wealth of future features. You can learn more about Loke at http://getloke.com

While Loke is a skateboarding app, it can be used for all urban action sports like inline skating, scootering, BMX, and free-running.

Could be better

Lots of technical issues. It’s one of those app ideas that every skater thinks about how cool it would be, but they skate too much to want to go through with it.
Always want to use my location even when I set it to only when I’m using it. I really think it would be cool if it got big but at the moment it needs work and more users before it could really get going. Not sure why there’s a friend list? Is it a social media app? Seems like they spent more money/time on the whole social media side of it. Should just be trimmed down to only skate spots. Why should I post how long my skate session was? Why is it like Instagram? Why would I ever want to find new skaters through this? I would rather just find new skate spots.
I love the idea. I love that you can add new spots with descriptions and categories of the them. It’s great.
It also runs pretty smoothly and looks and feels like everything is deliberate. Color scheme feels good.
3 stars only for the fact it’s got technical problems and the crappy instagram feel to it.
