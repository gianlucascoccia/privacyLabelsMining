Description

The updated Tim Hortons app is everything you love about Tim Hortons — now easier, faster, and more personal. Enjoy the convenience of Order & Pay, earn and spend Tims® Rewards, quickly customize your order in the app and find the latest targeted offers -- all through your phone!


Mobile Order & Pay
Select and customize your favourite food and drinks, choose your preferred Tim Hortons location, and pay from the app. It’s now that easy to order your favourite Tim Hortons items from your phone.

Personalized Menu
Add recently ordered items with one tap. Customized orders are saved on your menu so you can get your order just the way you like it.

Tims® Rewards
After just seven eligible purchases, receive your choice of a FREE coffee, tea or baked good. Keep checking for more special offers to come. It’s time to reward your routine!

Scan for Tims® Rewards
A digital version of your loyalty card that you can scan easily when ordering in the restaurant – never miss an opportunity to earn rewards.

Scan to Pay
Save time and pay for your order right from the app -- no need to carry cash or a credit card!

Take Out, Dine In or Drive Thru
Choose your pick-up method. Payment is completed in-app, so you can grab your order to go, or dine in with us. Your choice.

Preferences

I like the convenience of being able to order through the app. A couple of things I would like to see improved are in the app there is no option to have certain things such as a plain croissant toasted and add butter to it, there’s is no option to add sauce/dressings to breakfast sandwiches, no option to really customize your order to your liking by being able to add/drop condiments or other things to your order. So when I place my order and go into the store to pick it up I have to tell them at that time that I want the croissant toasted with butter or can I have chipotle sauce for my breakfast wrap which by that time they have already made the wrap so they give me little cups of sauce and the I have to unwrap the wrap and add the sauce. Also the store doesn’t have any control over the app so when it comes to their inventory that is an issue as well....sometimes I’ll order something from the app and get to the store and they say oh we don’t have such and such right now or we don’t have that at this store. Just a few things that would make ordering through an app even more convenient and add to good customer service.
