Description

Waiter.com is the premier on-line restaurant ordering service for the hungry web surfer. Today, over 1300 restaurants nationwide are available. We offer delivery for restaurants that do not have their own delivery service. Tired of just Pizza and Chinese? Ordering from Waiter.com can help you discover new cuisines ranging from Japanese and Mexican to Thai and Caribbean for a family dinner or simplify the task of ordering lunch for a large office. Featured restaurants include Amici's East Coast Pizzeria, California Pizza Kitchen, TOGO'S Eatery, Max's Opera Cafe, Akane Japanese Cuisine, and BJ's Restaurant. All restaurants are searchable by location or cuisine.

Waiter.com also offers a WaiterPoints rewards system. Earn prizes like movie tickets, gift certificates and even iPads just by ordering food. Waiter.com offers premier customer service in addition to easy specification of special requests and paperless menus for local restaurants right at your finger tips.

Waiter.com also helps you save and manage time. Save favorite orders for future use and you can replay them in less than a minute. If you don't have time to order during the work day, order ahead and specify a later preparation time... Ordering food for takeout can also rescue you from long lines at popular restaurants.
