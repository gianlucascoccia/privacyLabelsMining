Description

Viv is an ultimate guide uniquely created for your body’s changing needs to help you look and feel your best at every age. It’s a collection of bite-sized simple sets of exercises to get real results in the comfort of your own home.

Here’s what you get with Viv:

TARGETED HOME EXERCISE PROGRAMS
- Evidence-based right kind of exercises designed for results
- Easy and safe
- Customizable length of exercises to fit into your lifestyle. You’ll be surprised how little time it takes when you know what to do.

PRACTICAL NUTRITION AND WELLNESS GUIDANCE
- Evidence-based
- Targeted
- Easy-to-understand
- Right to the point

NO UNNECESSARY EXPENSES
Stop wasting time and money on useless health trends. Viv has already distilled all the useful health information for you — the stuff that actually works. Viv aims to target health promotion and health prevention activities. That means this is an opportunity to reduce unnecessary expenses.


Targeted Programs include:
* Toned Arms
* Firm Butt
* Younger Face
* Toned Chin and Neck
* Lower Back Pain / Sciatica
* Vaginal Dryness
* Urinary Incontinence
* And so much more...


On these easy and effective guided programs, you’ll:
* Become healthier, happier, slimmer and stronger
* Gain confidence
* Get that glow back
* Be your best self at any age


Viv programs are designed in collaboration with the leading experts in fitness, biochemistry, physiology, healthy aging, and nutritional health. Viv's scientific medical board includes Chong He, PhD in Biochemistry, Postdoc researcher at the Buck Institute for Research on Aging.


Viv is integrated with the Apple Health and Fitbit app, so you can sync activity data with Viv.
