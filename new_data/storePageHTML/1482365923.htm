Description

Pixel art coloring for kids offers a wonderful set of pixel art coloring pages for boys and girls 6,7,8,9 and 10 years old. With great pixel artworks, it is designed especially for children, providing an educational and creative experience.
Pixel art coloring for kids is brought to you by Pazu Games Ltd, the publisher of popular kids games like Girls Hair Salon, Makeup Girls, Animal Doctor and others, that are trusted by millions of parents worldwide.
Pixel art coloring book provides an opportunity for your kids to express their creativity and artistic skills, spark their imagination and enjoy the many colors and designs available.

Pazu games for kids are designed especially for boys and girls under the age of 10. It offers fun educational games for kids to enjoy and experience.
We invite you to try out Pazu games for kids and toddlers for free and discover a wonderful brand for kids games, with a large arsenal of educational and learning games for both boys and girls. Our games offer a variety of game mechanics adapted to the kids’ age and capabilities.
Pazu games have no ads so the kids have no distractions while playing, no accidental ad clicks, and no external interferences.
