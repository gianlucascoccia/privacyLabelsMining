Description

The Shell app is designed to help you make the most of the stop you need to make.

SHELL PAY & SAVE
This mobile payment service provides you improved speed, convenience, value and security when you use your mobile device to pay for fuel and convenience store items at participating Shell stations in the United States. Shell Pay & Save eliminates the need to carry multiple cards, reduces the number of prompts you have to answer, and is integrated with the Fuel Rewards® program so you can save automatically on every fill up, every day!
• Pay your way. You can link to Shell branded credit cards, your checking account (via Shell S Pay), Chase Pay®, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or Samsung Pay.
• Ready. Pay. Go. To pay for fuel outdoors, simply unlock the pump from the comfort of your car using the app. Or to pay inside the c-store, present the app QR code to the cashier. Either way, you are back on the road in no time. Everything you need is in the app, including receipts.

FUEL REWARDS® PROGRAM
It's easy to save on every fill up by redeeming your Fuel Rewards savings through Shell Pay & Save.
• New to the Shell app? You can earn a one-time 25¢/gal savings by downloading the app and completing your first Shell Pay & Save fuels purchase.
• All Shell app users earn additional savings for continued use of Shell Pay & Save.
Participation in the Fuel Rewards program through the Shell app requires you to link a new or existing Fuel Rewards account in the app. See Terms & Conditions for complete details.

STATION LOCATOR
Find your nearest Shell station and a list of services quickly and easily, including which stations accept Shell Pay & Save mobile payment.

Card Issue

I actually genuinely enjoyed the app at first. Quick payments, quick to work, linked to fuel rewards, and impressed many friends when I initially used it. However, they took off the payment options for credit cards and debit cards and has been a couple of weeks since they took it off with the only option being a shell branded card (typical) and limited paying options like Apple Pay (which doesn’t work) or chase pay specifically (being the only bank option I’ve seen on the app so far). Then, I read the reviews and I’ve noticed that the shell branded cards aren’t even connecting to the app. There is also a promise of there being more Payment options in the future with the words “coming soon”, but there is virtually no communication on the updates, a date, or why they took off the payment options. Not even the website on the app has been updated. Very disappointing and left with a useless app with the only hope of any updated or progression being “coming soon”.
