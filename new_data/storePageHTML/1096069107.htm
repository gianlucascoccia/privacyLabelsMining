Description

Available now: Extended 2 month free trial

Golf Magazine Golf Product of the Year and PGA Recommended product.

Turn your iPhone into the ultimate golf performance tracking tool!
Golf Magazine Golf Product of the Year and PGA Recommended product.


Fore™ lets you:

1. Track every round through your iPhone or Apple Watch
With built-in Auto Shot Detection (ASD) technology,
Fore™ captures every shot in your golf round automatically.

2. Edit & review every shot via your personal Fore™ dashboard
See where you hit every shot, edit or add notes,
and sign the round for a comprehensive performance overview.

3. View every stat for unprecedented game insight
Fore™ delivers game-changing statistics to analyze your game
and powerful analysis tools including Professor Broadies
Strokes Gained system used by the PGA Tour.


Additional features include:

• Precise GPS rangefinder for stroke planning

• Electronic scorecard

• Group or team statistics ranking & sharing

• Social media sharing

• Compatible with over 40,000 courses worldwide
