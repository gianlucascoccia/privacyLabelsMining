Description

The underwater world is crawling with sharks of different kinds. And you are lucky to meet the new ones:

Swordshark. Famous for its "sword", the ultimate weapon with sharp spikes.
Roboshark. Rather rare and extremely dangerous monster.
Zombieshark. It is impossible to destroy what is already dead. But try to be careful anyway.

Also there are two new style items. Make your shark really fashionable!

Spike bracelet: add more danger to your look.
Mohawk: Sharks are Punk-rock fans too.

Be ready to explore all under the sea innovations.

HUNTING MISSIONS
Swim and eat fish and other sea inhabitants

UNEXPECTED DANGERS
Avoid depth charges, they damage sharks' tough skin

EXPLORING DEPTHS
Start from the water surface and reach the deepest places.

REMEMBER: the lower you want to go, the more deep water shark you need.

Start the underwater adventure to the ocean depths together with sharks right now!
