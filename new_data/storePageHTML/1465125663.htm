Description

Get ready to play one of the spookiest games ever! Join creepy Addams family in decorating their mystery mansion and giving their creepy home a renaissance!
Collect characters like Morticia, Gomez, Wednesday, Pugsley, Uncle Fester, Grandma and many more while you design a house so spooky it will crash your creepiest dreams! Make room in the Addams home to fit the whole family (not that Thing and It will need it, LOL!) and play puzzle games in our events to dig deeper into the mansion’s story.
Main features at Addams Family Mystery Mansion
DESIGN and decorate with unique decos Gomez and Morticia’s home to give them a fresh start.
COLLECT the creepy characters that inhabit the mystery mansion.
CRAFT spooky items to help the beloved Addams fulfill their needs.
EXPAND the house and discover a creepier room with each step.
UNLOCK design costumes to dress your characters.
ENJOY a thrilling story with sassy dialogues that will make “lol” fall short.
EXPERIENCE seasonal events and play match 3 puzzles until you crash!
Place the kookiest decos in the creepiest rooms while getting to know each family member a bit more. Help Wednesday with her science projects and Morticia with her gardening, but pray Pugsley isn’t there to crash everything afterwards, LOL!
Ready to experience the game that makes all other spooky games seem bland? Set out to decorate the kookiest house and make all daily activities more fun. Gardening doesn’t seem like much of an adventure until you see the carnivorous plants!
Puzzle yourself with a story based on the animated film “The Addams Family'' by Metro Goldwyn Meyer while making the Addams’ home yours! What are you waiting for? Those creepy decos aren’t going to spring to life and place themselves on their own —unless Morticia quits gardening and decides to do some magic to make Grandma proud!

Enjoyable but …

I pre-ordered it what feels like forever ago and I've been playing ever since. I enjoy the game. Sure, there's a few things that might irritate some like that it takes a while to get gems or supplies to unlock new rooms but I'm not the kind of person that gives up and spends real money and it does keep me busy. The big thing that bugs me though is how it's entirely too easy to click wrong while checking the time left on an action and suddenly a huge chunk of those gems you've worked so hard to get are gone simply to speed up an action that was only a few minutes from complete!!! CAN WE PLEASEEEEE GET A CONFIRMATION??? A simple pop up that asks if we're sure we want to buy this action? Something? Anything?!? This is literally the only thing that made me come write a review and the only reason it's not 5 stars. I just accidentally spent 20+ gems on a fairly useless action and I am devastated. I was soooo close to unlocking a room and now that's likely another day or more of watching videos to get my gem count back up. I'm sure it's part of the scheme to get people to buy gems but please help a girl out here?!?!?

Love the graphics

I absolutely love the graphics. It’s really a cute game, watching the characters do their actions is very funny, like uncle fester taking a bath or sleeping on his bed of nails. Kitty scratching the couch and Wednesday doing witch craft on her dolls or Lurch hanging himself with rope. It’s creepy in a fun way! For the current event they brought a character name Patunia that allows you to play a Match 3 game for up to 30 levels. I’m obsessed with this game and really hope they bring her character back for the additional add on Match 3 game that they didn’t have before. I tried looking up a similar Match 3 game in the app store and nothing compares to how fun it was. The game in general definitely gets a bit frustrating having to wait hours for characters to complete task but if that wasn’t the case, players would go through the levels way too quickly, so you definitely need patience to strategically have your characters complete their longer task over night or when you’re not going to log on for a while. This game is super fun and the graphics won’t disappoint!!

Hepyaddakat , 11/26/2019

Excellent - it is NOT pay-to-play!

I love this game. I am a huge fan of city-builders and this one is refreshing after some of the overly complex mega-cash-grabs the others have turned into. To the complainers: You have to complete quests to earn gems. Completing quests is THE WHOLE POINT. It took me three days to unlock Wednesday (100). I’ve been playing for two weeks now and I’ve nearly got enough gems for the greenhouse (250) and I haven’t paid a cent (clearly none of you have played The Simpsons). People who cry “boring too fast!” or “<this item> costs gems!” need another game; the pace of the gameplay is perfectly fine if you are used to crafting/decorating games.

I’m giving it five stars because it deserves it. Although, as others have pointed out, beyond “let’s decorate!!” it does lack any sort of plot or storyline to engage the people who care about that sort of thing. That being said, I could care less about plot.
