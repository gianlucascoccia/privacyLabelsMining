Description

Important: Please read before downloading!

Pre-requisites for use:

- This app is currently optimized only for Male workouts
- Gym Membership: this workout is designed to be performed at the gym
- Basic Fitness knowledge. Experience with basic Compound Exercises such as Bench Press, Squat, Deadlift, Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press, etc.
- Must be 18 years of age or older

BAU5 Workout gets you Strong and Lean, quick! The workout is provided through Digital Personal Training. Just press "Start" and be on your way to the beach body you've always wanted.

This app provides a hassle-free way of following the BAU5 Workout. No reading or researching. Just sign-up and press start. Like a Personal Trainer, The BAU5 Workout will be adapted to you and your ability.

Features:

- Complete Workout Guidance (Exercises, Weight, Reps, Rest)
- Personalized to you and your preferences
- Video, description and muscle target diagram for each Exercise
- Displays exact equipment setup
- No data entry
- Optimized for 3 workouts per week and plenty of rest
- Hits all main muscle groups
- Progress Photos
- Apple Health Sync

PRO Subscription Features:

- Smart Adapt: adapts to your progress and performance
- Rep Speaker
- Full Workout History and Charts
- Heart Rate monitoring

Coming Soon:

- More Workouts and Exercises
- Gym setup
- Bodyweight logging
- Apple Watch Extension
- And much more!
