Description

Wine.com is the better way to discover and buy wine online. We are the #1 Online Wine Store, with the largest selection of wine available to order and deliver across the US. We now offer a wide assortment of Spirits (Liquor) for shipping to select states. Whiskey, Vodka, Gin, Rum, Tequila, Mezcal, Brandy, Cognac, and more.

+ Browse our extensive assortment using powerful search, filter, and sort features, or let our friendly, non-commissioned wine and spirits experts help you buy wine online using our Chat feature.

+ Scan a wine or liquor label to view pricing, professional ratings, and tasting notes.

+ Enjoy free access to critical scores – from Wine Spectator, Robert Parker’s The Wine Advocate, Wine & Spirits Magazine, Connoisseurs’ Guide, Wine Enthusiast, Whiskey Advocate, and more.

+ Wine delivery is fast and convenient. Get free shipping on every order all year long with Wine.com StewardShip for just $49. Most wine deliveries arrive in 1-2 business days – or set your own arrival date. Pick up on your schedule from more than 17,000 local pickup locations. We use sturdy, recyclable packaging.

+ Our Customer Support team is top-notch, and ready to assist with any questions you may have. Chat with us seven days a week.

+ Track all your wine orders in MyWine. Easily rate bottles, make a note, and re-order favorites. The more wines you rate, the more personalized recommendations you’ll receive.

+ Try Picked -- our new personalized wine subscription service! Tell us your wine preferences, budget, and level of adventurousness. We’ll match you with a personal somm, who will select six great wines just for you, at a frequency you choose. There are no fees or markups, and your Picks always ship free.

+ Customers love Wine.com, honoring us with a 9.2 out of 10 score on Bizrate. Browse some of our exceptional reviews at https://bizratesurveys.com/reviews/www.wine.com/21001

The app has great options and a good selection to choose from for gifts as well as other occasions. It seems there was a price change as I was in the process of checking out, which then kicked back my order.
