Description

Create & Cut Out your own stickers from your photos! Easy to use, you can create your own personal stickers within one minute. You can export and use your stickers in WhatsApp, iMessage, and other messengers.

Features:
- Create stickers from your own or other photos
- Create sticker packs with up to 30 stickers in each
- Cut out any part of the image, do stickers of any size of a form you want
- Add text and funny elements to stickers
- Export stickers to WhatsApp, iMessage other messengers.

The files you create/cut-out can be exported and used on any other app.
