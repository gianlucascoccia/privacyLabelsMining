Description

World-leading cryptocurrency exchange

Founded in 2017, CoinEx consists of a team from world leading internet and finance companies, boasting rich R&D experience with the proprietary trade matching system, creating a professional and compliant blockchain environment.

With multiple languages supported, CoinEx provides highly secure, stable and efficient cryptocurrency trading services with millions of users in over 100 countries/regions.

Features:

1. Fast crypto purchase
Supporting purchase of BTC, ETH, BCH and more cryptocurrencies with credit cards. Obtain your first cryptocurrency assets easily.

2. Real-time market info
One click to keep an eye set for most mainstream crypto assets, professional K-line trading view, price rise and fall at a glance, grasping the market trend in time.

3. Massive trading market options
Supporting Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Litecoin (LTC), Dogecoin (DOGE) and over 200 quality cryptocurrencies, bulk trading anytime and anywhere.

4. A variety of derivatives
Supporting a variety of digital currency derivatives, e.g. leveraged trading, perpetual contracts, financing services, automated market making, meeting the diversified investment needs of investors.

5. Rapid and stable deposit and withdrawal
100% reserve and self-developed blockchain technology ensure rapid and stable deposit and withdrawal. Instant inter-user transfer, no more waiting, 0 confirmation or fees required.

6. Users first
Serving global users with 24/7 exchange services, multilingual support, professional customer service and simplified page design.
