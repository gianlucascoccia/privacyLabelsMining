Azninvasion86 , 07/30/2020

Pay to Win

What a fun concept hampered by terrible execution and what essentially amounts to a “pay to win” scheme. Sure enough, you’ll start out and win some, lose some, you’ll collect normal power ups, but get into it enough, and you’ll realize that the ones who spend money on the “premium” power ups are winning at significantly higher rates, and FAR quicker. You may be 5-6 calls in and someone will already have won. If you wanna break into the top 25, where bonuses and winnings are increased, good luck.

The most useless power ups are the ones they most frequently dole out: the coin “power up” doesn’t help you win, it gives you a few extra coins. The single marker (or “daub” as they refer to it) powerup is another useless powerup, but those two are the most common for players who don’t put money into the game.

The live hosts are about the only positive aspect of this game, although, it feels more like an attempt to keep people hooked by creating a feeling of community. Don’t fall for it. Don’t waste your time. Skip this one. You’ll be better off not playing this game. It’ll let you win enough until it doesn’t, then if you wanna keep playing and winning, you’ll have to spend money. Two thumbs down.
