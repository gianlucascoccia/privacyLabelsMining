Description

With the ActiveBuilding app, everything in your community is at your fingertips. You can pay rent, communicate with staff and neighbors, reserve amenities, manage service requests, track packages and more—anytime, anywhere.
Find all of your community's important information and receive messages from management on events, specials and alerts via text, voice or email.
Plus, you can safely and securely connect with your fellow residents to share information, find people with similar interests and even meet new friends.
With all of its helpful and convenient features, the ActiveBuilding app can make apartment living more convenient and enjoyable at the same time.
(Some features may not be available at your property. Contact your community management if you have questions.)
KEY FEATURES
• Pay rent right from the app with your credit card, debit card or e-check and set up scheduled payments to avoid late fees
• Submit maintenance requests with photos and videos and automatically receive status updates
• Connect with your community in the Activity Stream-get updates, swap news, coordinate neighborhood events, share pictures and create social profiles with specific interests
• Renew your lease completely online
• Sign up for events, reserve amenities and (where applicable) pay for them right from the app
• Receive package delivery notifications in-app, through email or via text
• Shop your community's marketplace for affiliate services including cleaning, child care, dog walkers, maintenance and more
• Gain easy, secure mobile access using Biometric Login
