Must tune in! , 02/15/2020

Too... Many... Ads!!!!!!!

I downloaded this game after seeing an ad for it and after seeing decent reviews. This game is pretty good I just have a few problems with it. Too many ads! As I started playing I was receiving these “new icons”. I was about the press the button that said “collect” because it was the only button to press on the screen, but then I saw a little sign next to it that took me a couple seconds to realize what it was. To collect the icon you had to watch a video. After a few seconds another button appeared and it was to “lose it”. I didn’t want to sit and watch a video just for a little new icon, so I pressed the button to select to lose it. After the next level, another video appeared, and then another. The amount of ads in this game is incredible! I’m not that mad about how many ads there are (because I’m pretty sure you can pay to get them removed), but it’s the fact that the video icon is hidden and that for a little prize you can’t just collect it you have to watch a video for it. It’s basically like you are getting scammed of your time. If you are reading this and you are a creator please do something about it, even though it probably benefiting you, it’s a pain for the player and that’s why I am no longer playing this game.

LilBelDy , 02/25/2020

Great Concept, Too Many Ads

I appreciate this game for being creative, fun, relaxing, and also a little challenging for certain levels. I hate to say this because I usually power through ads very well, but after every single level an ad appears. I fully understand this is how the game makers profit - no shame in making a living, but, after 3 mere seconds spent on drawing a line(s) to park, the waiting 10 plus seconds becomes draining and completely ruins the gaming experience for me. I wish it wasn’t a big deal, but the back to back ads are frustrating. They usually take about 5 to 6 seconds before being able to fast forward, but then additional time is spent waiting for the “x” to then appear. It’s a great game but the ads appearing one after another after a few seconds is spent completing a single level is too much. It just makes the experience fall flat and takes away the joy making it no longer worth my time. An ad once every 2 to 3 games would be more than tolerable. I uninstalled but wish I didn’t need to. If you can sit through the ads after every single game then my hats off to you. Thank you for this game but my friendly feedback is to space out your ads, please. Thank you for spending time to read this.
