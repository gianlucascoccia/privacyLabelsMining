Demoman takes skill , 06/24/2017

How this game is (mostly) a ripoff

Now, this isn't a complete rant that blatantly says that this game is a ripoff and that's it. Here are reasons why this game is "heavily inspired" by Shooty Skies. At first glance it's just, basically a pixelated llama you can play as, however, once you start a game, it becomes clear that this game has almost the EXACT same style of that of Shooty Skies, pixelated player flying around defeating enemies as he flies around, power ups giving you companions to help fight, and bosses that will come to attempt to defeat you. There are differences between them, that's obvious in some points in the game. But still, Nickelodeon, have some originality! Do you really have to stoop so low by almost plagiarizing another game? I get it, some people are going to disagree with me and, well, do what people on the internet do best, which is blatantly say your opinion is better than mine. But still, at least here me out on this: if you love this game so much why don't you just play the original thing that actually wasn't a failed attempt to promote a TV show? It's a lot more fun and has content for it that wasn't rushed simply for a two-part special. I rest my case.
