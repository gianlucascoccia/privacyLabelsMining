Description

Stable Secretary is an easy-to-use, secure, customizable web and mobile software application that enables horse trainers and barn managers to record, view, and manipulate information about their horses and their services – anytime, anywhere. Stable Secretary makes it easy to manage information about your horses, their health care, your customers, and your billing.

This mobile application is available as a standalone product or as an add-on.

Great App. Struggling to add Photos!

I just wanted to take a moment and say Thank You for development of such a quality product. The ever expanding data points are truly on point for our Thoroughbred operations and I look forward to many years ahead using the app and web versions. While the web version is a breeze to add photos, the app not so much. At this point the only complaint I have is the ability to quickly open the app during a Vet visit and take a picture of an x-ray or wound and upload it immediately. We don’t always have our iPads stall side, but inevitably there is a phone handy. While we can navigate to the website, I feel it would be much easier to open app icon click horse and upload or even take photo in the app under the designated horse. I apologize if I am understating the ease or missing the proper steps to complete this task, but I have tried 3 ways to Sunday with no luck. Anyway, keep up the great work and I look forward to future updates!!!

Melaniemarie121 , 12/16/2020

I REALLY want to like it, but needs some work

As a person who manages a large breeding and training farm I have hopes that this app will be a god send.... and it is on that brink...however it needs some work. The mobile version is far to limited and doesn’t show me the same information that the web version does. For example if I enter in a milk test recons, in the app it doesn’t show the same format (ph, calcium, type, etc.) that it does online. I also cannot add a new milk or other breeding related record with my phone, which with today’s technology age is crucial.

I also wish there was a way to set a default setting on each horse to be able to see all of the medical history without have to keep hitting the (next month) button. Some of my horses have been with me for years and this is incredibly time consuming to do. I just wish there was a button to “view all”.

I also would love an option to customize the reminders that show on the horses main page. Most of us plan our spring vaccinations months in advance. Also with traveling and competing around the country it would be helpful to be able to have the Reminders show up to 12 months out. This gives us time to see them daily and plan ahead accordingly.

Like I said I have looked at other apps for this and this one is the most promising, I just wish it had these few tweaks to truly make it the most efficient equine management app on that market!
