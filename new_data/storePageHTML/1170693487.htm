Description

Give your players PS4/PS5/Xbox/MFi game controller so they can move on their own while presenting on TV!

EncounterPlus VTT is a powerful virtual tabletop app designed to help Dungeon Masters to run D&D 5E combat encounters more easily. You can track initiative, health, and conditions for various monsters and players and position them as tokens on the map.

Basic Features:
• Initiative, health, experience, and condition tracker
• Random encounter generator, difficulty calculator
• Bestiary, Monster Manual, Magic items and Spells
• Bookmarks, a quick search, shortcuts
• Player and Non-Player characters
• D&D 5E SRD content included
• Campaigns, Modules, and Asset packs
• Page content editor
• Dice Roller
• iCloud sync, manual import/export
• iPad Multitasking, Split screen, Drag&Drop
• External Screen with AirPlay Mirroring
• No Ads

Battle Map Features (as In-App purchase) :
• Tokens, Auras, Tiles, Spell Area of Effects,
• Square grid, Hex grid, Custom grid size and units
• Fog of War, Dynamic Line of Sight with multiple wall types
• Custom drawings with Apple Pencil
• Animated video backgrounds

Premium Features (Subscription ) :
• 3D Dice Roller with multiple themes and styles
• Animated Tiles, Auras, Area of Effects
• Weather Effects (rain, snow, fog)
• Web server + Remote Play (early access)

Bahalock , 09/23/2018

A MUST have app for DMs

This is an amazing app. It is my go to for just about all of my DM planning, and while running the games. It took me a little bit to learn how the app worked when I first got it, but when I figured it out I ran with it. Other reviewers cover all details as to what features the app has, so I won’t waste time repeating what they say. I will say this though, this app makes planning and running my games massively easier. Before I did everything with a notepad and a calculator, and it slowed the process down. Now the sessions and encounters move quicker, and run much smoother.
There are a couple of ways that I can see the app being improved upon though. There being some sort of guide to tell you how to work the basic functions of the app, the list of creatures to be updated instead of having to manually enter them in yourself, and be able to fill out PC stats like you would do with creatures.
This all being said I absolutely LOVE this app and I can’t stop recommending it to others who also run games.
