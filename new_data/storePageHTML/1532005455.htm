Description

Let your home screen tell you when’s the next sunrise, with Sunlight.
Whether you simply want to watch the magnificent view of the sunrise, or to capture the best landscape shots during the beautiful period of the Golden Hour, Sunlight is the perfect app for you.

Sunlight app combines beautifully crafted widgets and app design to enable you to monitor the sunrise and sunset timings of different places anywhere on Earth, along with the moon phases, illumination percentages, and more.

Sunlight also includes the following features:

◆ NO INTERNET CONNECTION NEEDED ◆
- This app uses algorithms on device to calculate everything that it has to provide. Once configured of locations for which you would like to monitor the timings of, no internet connection is needed!

◆ LIVE WIDGETS THAT UPDATE THROUGHOUT THE DAY ◆
- Widgets that shows the approximate time left of the day through a sun that moves up and down throughout the day, based on the remaining daylight hours.
- There is a total of 3 kinds of widgets for you to choose from, all of which, are customisable.

◆ LIVE CALCULATIONS ◆
- Need to know the current azimuth and altitude angle of the Sun or the Moon? Choose a location in the app, and you will be greeted with the information you need along with a beautifully crafted live planetary movements based approximately on the time of day.

◆ SUN TIME CALCULATIONS ◆
- Sunlight supports viewing of timings of different geographical regions with support of viewing with either the device's timezone, or the region's timezone, through toggling in app. Below are some examples of what you can expect:
- Sunrise, solar noon, sunset times
- Twilights times such as civil, nautical and astronomical dawn and dusk times
- Golden Hour, Blue Hour

◆ MOON TIME CALCULATIONS ◆
- Moon related statistics and time is also calculated in Sunlight! Here are some things you may expect:
- Moonrise, Moonset, meridian passing times
- Current moon phase and illumination
- Next full moon or new moon dates

◆ PRIVACY FOCUSED LOCATION USE ◆
- You can select the locations you want manually, or use location services, in an opt-in basis. Location is only used to compute accurate timings, and refreshed only when you are using the app, or seeing the widget.

- Alternatively, you may also add your locations in manually.

◆ WIDGET CUSTOMIZABILITY ◆
- Enter the edit menu of Sunlight’s widgets and you may choose to show the moon in the widget, use 12 or 24 hour formats, to show the region or not!

◆ MULTI PLATFORM ◆
- Sunlight works on both iPhones and iPads as a universal app.

◆ NO TRACKING PRIVACY POLICY ◆
- Sunlight does not, and will not track you. It’s that simple.
