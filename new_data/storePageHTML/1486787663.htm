Description

The app contains information about more than 1200 characters of Warriors by Erin Hunter: names, positions, appearance, kin and more.

• Search by current and all the other names of a character, navigate easily through clans and other groups.

• Read about both major and minor characters even when you are offline: use it as your handbook.

• Add liked cats to Favorites and access them in the future.

The app will be useful for both experienced fans and beginners in the world of Warriors. Meet the cats that rule the wild!
