The Comments

The app is good, and overall, the update has some very good improvements. I like that Smack and the team is listening to the subscribers feedback... in which I hope they continue to do that... with that said, I would like the ability to stop the auto scroll feature of the live comments section. If I want to actually read them, I need the screen to stop taking me to the bottom of a new comment... because ultimately what you want is to create a platform - besides twitter, where BR fans can engage with each other. I’ve been a subscriber since day one and haven’t deleted it and reinstalled... I’ve been here for the culture, and having that comments section get more fine tuned would be a game changer.
I would also like to see some sort of volume normalization between the actual battle volume and the 🤘🏾 button... too often the difference is too great. The battle is low and the horn/Don Demarco is loud as hell! Yikes. Please add this to the next update as well. Thanks. Y’all headed in the right direction. *PS. Don’t make us wait forever to see the December 8, 2019 event with Hollow and Danny, everybody caint afford $60 around the holidays. Real Talk. 😬🤘🏾
