Description

Thousands of FAIRY TALES and CHILDREN BOOKS are in one app!

All parents keep asking: “How to get a baby ready for bed?”, “How to keep a child busy in a car?”, “How to help a kid make familiar himself with the world around?”, “How to widen his horizons?”, “How to prepare children for starting school?”. And many other questions confront moms and dads.

Our answer: listen to FAIRY TALES and AUDIOBOOKS with your child!

In our app, you will find THOUSANDS of fairy tales and audiobooks from the world's BEST authors and publishers for kids of ALL ages.

We regularly update our catalog with the new engaging books and TOP bestsellers.

Only in OUR app:
- THOUSANDS of fairy tales and children audiobooks without limitation;
- fables, poems, short stories, LULLABIES;
- user-friendly interface that even a CHILD can understand;
- offline mode: books are available everywhere WITHOUT access to the Internet;
- audiobook section on PARENTING and child development;
- narrated by professional ACTORS and voice talents;
- subject selections: what to LISTEN to on the drive or before going to BED;
- opportunity to create your LIBRARY;
- and MUCH more.

Install our app and give your children joyful moments!
