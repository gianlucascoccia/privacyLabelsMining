Description

Trace is the all-in-one soccer game film system. The Trace Teams iOS app lets you control the TraceCam and the Trace Case, update team rosters, start and stop games, and manage game uploads. (Looking for game results? No app needed. Results will be emailed to you after each game.)

Recording games and editing film can be complicated, time-consuming, and expensive. Trace captures your game footage, generates tactical playlists, and delivers each player's moments directly to their inboxes after each game. Trace’s AI-powered system is the most affordable all-in-one solution for game footage, analysis, and personalized video.

Use Trace Teams to:

• Manage teams and update player rosters and Tracer assignments
• Create games and manage game recordings
• Connect your Trace Case to Wifi and upload Tracer data
• Monitor the status of your game uploads

A 5-star Program in the making.

The Trace app is very simple to use and keeps the user on task with step by step instructions. The equipment set-up, viewing functions and Trace a game set-up have been very well thought out for the user. I would give the app 5 stars but have noticed one error in the roster settings. Between games one of the rosters for our club keeps adding a player to an empty Trace slot after we switch the player to a different number. We switched the player because a Trace cube is not working but when the operator opens the roster to hand out tracers it keeps putting a player in the bad slot. This does not happen on all rosters so we are in the troubleshooting phase to figure out if it is operator error or a trace app error. Once we solve this issue I would give the whole program 5 stars. The customer support from the app and staff have been amazing. Thank you

Bree3318 , 05/22/2021

app and video system are great!

the app allows on-the-field set up of the Trace video camera. it provides instructions with pictures to guide you through the process, which makes it so easy that anyone could record a game on their first try. they also include a way to get urgent ‘on-field support’ if something goes wrong, and their customer support is the best out there. a very helpful FAQ section is also accessible through the app.
Trace automatically separates out several playlists which gives the option to easily watch individual player clips (the first thing we watch after filming a game are all of my daughter’s plays), or tactical moments from the game (the second thing we watch are all the goals scored, then our ‘touch chains’). you also have the option to watch the full game and the players can save and easily share their favorite soccer clips with coaches, family members and friends or to social media.
we have used Trace for the entire spring season and our families and players love it!
