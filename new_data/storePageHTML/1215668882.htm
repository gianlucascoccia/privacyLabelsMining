Description

Big Farm: Mobile Harvest is a free farm simulator game that you can play online with friends, family, and farmers around the world. Build your own community and have fun creating the farm life of your dreams.

*Farm with friends: Big Farm: Mobile Harvest is a free farming simulator online game that lets you join up with your friends and create a beautiful farm village of your very own.

*Farming simulator experience: Build your farm and plant, grow and harvest your favorite fruits and vegetables.

*All done with your farm chores? Time to look after your animal friends: A good farmer keeps their four-legged friends happy. Have fun caring for cows, goats, chickens, horses, pigs, and many other cuddly companions.

*Farm, harvest, and trade: Did you harvest extra corn but need some strawberries? In the market, you can make trades to help your farming village prosper.

*More than a farming simulator game - it's a community: Meet, chat, discuss, and complete joint quests with farmers worldwide.

*Build the number one farm: Starting with nothing but raw seeds, you'll use all your farming skills to grow your crops until they're ready to be sold at the market.

*Farm your way: Grow hay on your farm. Harvest organic foods and farm-fresh goods, all from your farm village.

*Build your dream farm: Add vintage buildings, windmills, and decorations to create the farm of your dreams.

*Plenty of options: Choose what you want to grow! From tropical fruits to organic vegetables, your farm village is sure to set new trends in the market.

*Manage your farm village: Distribute your crops after each planting cycle, sow seeds, water your plants, feed your animals, make clever deals in the farm marketplace, and collect rewards.

*Farming adventures: Participate in events and farming quests to find missing items that will improve your farm.

*Relax on your farm: Escape the hustle and bustle of the city and enjoy life on your very own farm! Take a break and enjoy some sun and relaxation.

*Farming with your family: Invite your family and enjoy farming together in the peaceful countryside.

*Learn from farmers worldwide: Join the Big Farm community and meet farmers from all over the world. Learn about their farming methods to keep your farm village prosperous.

Big Farm: Mobile Harvest game is completely free to play with optional in-app purchases. You may disable in-app purchasing using your device settings. This game requires an internet connection. Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, Imprint: https://www.goodgamestudios.com/terms_en/

sparkle1047 , 08/20/2021

Awesome & Addictive

Love this game and have since 2015 when I played it on my laptop. Love the tournament’s. There’s always something new and interesting going on. Love the bond you create with your co-op family. The only thing that really makes me sad about this game is that when they created the Mobile Harvest version they excluded it from Big Farm desktop version rather than just make an app for the same game that exists on your pc. This leaves you with a decision to try and play through the GG website on your mobile device as a workaround but it’s barely functional and only leads to frustration. The other decision you have is start all over from the ground up losing your rank, farm, money gold and extremely hard work. I think that if you do want to change over to the mobile version then you should be able to transfer the gold you purchased and the farm money you worked for to the mobile version to buffer having to start from the ground up again. It’s a mobile world and pc games are just not as popular as they used to be. The workaround is a flop. I will say it again that I’m just very sad that it was done this way but what can I say other than A Farmers Life is Full of Strife. : )

512BlueJay , 09/10/2019

Fantastic

I’ve played a lot of games, and this one is by far the most generous and balanced. Spending is not required to grow in and enjoy the game. The Devs have designed a game, that with strategy and patience, will provide everything the player needs. Yes, you progress faster if you spend, as it should be. Updates bring bugs, but that is the case with every game I’ve played. The Devs respond to resolve them. That is all that can expected. Also, each update has incorporated changes to address player feedback on game modes that don’t work well. All in all, it is a fairly well designed game, that continues to evolve to be better, and is fun to play. Side bar: I think trying to “win” this game is like trying to “win” at yoga. This is more of a “it’s the journey, not the destination” kinda game.

You’ve gotten too greedy Big Farm

The game was fun, updates have always been an issue (mostly due to bugs added in with each one), but the latest is just too far over the top. You can play alone or in a group, but now the contracts all want things you need to spend real cash on if you plan on getting anything done. Big Farm claims it is to keep the game challenging for the long term, but they have gone too far. It is no fun to ditch all your contracts and have to wait hours to maybe have the chance of getting a fish that you can turn into the latest golden ticket (mallets) so you can upgrade layers of items to finally make the newest addition to the game, honey. I don’t know at what level you need to be to have the honey forced down your throat, but once you get there, it isn’t fun. Great job at making everyone mad at you Big Farm! And it continues! Now you need the glorious water buckets to get a sheep pen to make hats at the tailor shop. Plus you need those mallets to upgrade your manufactory to get the dye to make colored items in the tailor shop. The honey requests became outrageous and this last update seems to have reduced that at least. They just can’t leave well enough alone! Or maybe they know they are making it outrageous to play without spending real money and add something they plan to fix so it looks like they listened and fixed something??
