Needs a few improvements

I think this app is awesome and I’m enjoying it. The thing is, is that it has downsides. I notice you have to have the app up and going and your phone plugged in for it to work correctly. The other thing I’ve noticed is that it vibrates when it starts to come on, which is kinda pointless when my phone is sitting on a desk because the first thing I hear is vibration instead of music. I also noticed that when the music come on it comes on properly, but about 10 seconds into the music turning up and getting going it jumps to a louder sound, instead of coming up to a louder sound smoothly. Good app and idea but it needs some improvements.

Developer Response ,

Hi!! Got you! Indeed, to avoid having the app in operation, we need to be able to work in the background with the app closed (we do it only open) and According to iOS system they only allows it for its products. This is the reason basically. You only have to leave it in the multitasking or block your phone when we indicate it to you that it is the most recommended. Thanks for sharing that detail of the vibration, we will take it into account for future updates. Regarding the sound, I think you mean the gradual volume, you can adjust it to your liking, we include it to prevent you from preparing for the sound of the alarm little by little. Thanks a lot for supporting 💚. Alarmify team ⏰
