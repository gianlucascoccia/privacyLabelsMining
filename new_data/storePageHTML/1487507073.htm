Description

In-Form is excited to provide you the next-level training app that we’ve been dreaming about for years.
- Breakthrough for developing mechanics and rhythm like the best for any athletic activity: golf swing, basketball shot, baseball pitch, tennis serve, gymnastics, volleyball, martial arts, etc...
- User driven content: users like you who share short high-quality (usually slow-motion) videos of experts and professionals with exceptional mechanics.
- Cutting-edge AI technology allows users to then create “Target” motions where the athlete is extracted from the video (think background removal or green screen). This is incredibly powerful as the Target joins YOUR camera screen and YOUR video recordings as an on-screen guide.
- With clear goals and great feedback, you can develop mechanics and rhythm like the best. With your focused efforts, In-form helps you achieve your best performance, get there quicker, and stay there.

iPhone OR iPad IS ALL YOU NEED
- Just record a video for incredible feedback: with a stable camera (hold steady, use a tripod, or use the empty shoe as holder trick) from a similar angle as your selected Target guide and get an awesome comparison.
- Train anywhere to build muscle memory to match the best.

BETTER TOGETHER – In-Form IS A COMMUNITY
- If you have great videos for an existing sports category or new category for In-form, please upload so you and others can benefit!
- Ask or provide answers to the community, share “I Fixed It” videos or instruction, and learn from others. Rate and comment on videos.
- Post to your social media accounts and help build the community.

LOCK-IN YOUR BEST AND TRACK PROGRESS
- Save videos to “My Library” where you can track progress and easily take notes. Never lose track of your key learnings.
- Upload your video and create a Target of your progress or your best and use as your guide to help you stay at your best.

OPTIMIZED TRAINING CAMERA & PROCESS
Optimized record, trim, analyze, ... process for repetitive training for mechanics development. 3 steps instead of 7 using native camera for training.

GREAT FOR COACHES
- Coaches can drive better results faster and increase connection through online engagement.
- Our screen recorder allows you to deliver premiere digital lessons using Target and/or drawing tools with a trim, crop and compress for easy delivery of optimized lesson videos.

LINK TO ORIGINAL VIDEOS TO DRIVE TRAFFIC
If your video is on another site, you can create a link then create and attach a Target motion to provide new value to your video with a Target motion for training and increase traffic to your original video.

PRICING
Free: Free for almost all features. To use My Library, store Favorites, upload videos/create Targets, comment, rate, and post to Ask/Answer, you must create an account. You can post public videos and store up to 30 entries in My Library.

Premium: Post up to 50 private videos if you prefer not to share and unlimited posts to My Library. More premium features in development. If you love the app, we also welcome premium subscriptions to help accelerate introduction of new features.

In-Form premium is available as an auto renewal monthly subscription after a 7 day trial. Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account at the end of your trial. Subscription automatically renews unless it is canceled at least 24 hours prior to the end of the current period. You can manage and cancel your subscriptions by going to your account setting on the App Store or through Settings and selecting Account then Subscriptions.

Please contact us with any questions, suggestions, or ideas on how we can be better. support@in-form.ai

Best golf swing app yet!

This app is highly useful, so happy it’s out. No other golf swing app can let me record my swing then find a pro swing that’s similar and overlay (not just put side by side) the two swings and place markers so that both swings move together, for super fine-tuning analysis. A very valuable tool for fixing your swing! The app’s technology overlays the pro swing while erasing its background, an ingenious idea! I can even re-size the pro swing to match mine.
There are hundreds of pro swings for comparison, searchable, and if one isn’t there I can upload it.
I have discovered key subtle but harmful flaws in my swing that I could not see with the other standard swing apps. Finally I can see how pros build their swings each little step and where I can improve mine. Simply awesome way to improve! What I’ve been needing!
