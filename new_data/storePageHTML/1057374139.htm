Description

Welcome to Novel Effect — an award-winning app that follows your voice as you read out loud from kids story books and responds at just the right moment with music, sounds, and character voices. Bring the story to life, promote literacy, imagination, and fun for kids 12 & under!

There are 3 versions of the Novel Effect service, all accessible through this app. New books are added weekly — including classics, bestsellers, hidden gems, and more.

FREE
Free Novel Effect offers hundreds of soundscapes for a growing library of children’s story books curated for teachers, librarians, kids and families. Enjoy limited access to soundscapes for free when you bring your own copy of the print book or ebook!

PREMIUM
For families, Novel Effect Premium offers unlimited access to a growing digital library of kid friendly content. Enjoy soundscapes for popular story books when you bring your own copy, or read aloud from hundreds of in-app books, including non-fiction and early reader chapter books, as well as exclusive members-only content.

PREMIUM CLASSROOM
For educators, Novel Effect Premium Classroom offers unlimited access to Premium features and content for one teacher and up to 30 students in a safe and secure environment. Overwhelmingly, educators say reading with Novel Effect is more engaging, creating motivated, confident, and empowered readers.

Download for free and become an epic storyteller! Start your free trial today to access hundreds of in-app story books and save with an annual subscription.

HOW IT WORKS
Novel Effect’s service follows along as you read out loud, playing music, sounds, and character voices in response to your voice. Our library includes a wide variety of high-quality soundscapes for carefully selected kids story books to engage and promote imagination and learning, updated weekly with new titles! Hundreds of titles are available as in-app books, some titles may require you to read aloud from your own copy.

FIND - Use search or browse our collections to find the book you want to read out loud.
PLAY - Tap the cover and then choose how you’ll be reading — with print or an ebook.
READ - When you hear the chime, start reading aloud!
LISTEN - Hear the music and sounds respond to your voice and change with the story.

Add a little magic to story time with Novel Effect.

- Novel Effect requires access to the device's microphone to perform voice-recognition.
- The privacy of kids and their grown-ups is our top priority. Voice-recognition is performed on your device, no voice data is saved without express consent.
