Dan Weckerly

Took the wife up to Manitowish Waters and had to hear about how she wished I had would take her somewhere warm instead the whole way up. She has always ridden on beat down trails back home and was not excited to go on this trip. After the first day of riding on freshly groomed trails I didn’t hear her complain at all. In fact she said she would go again on our next trip up. Thank you groomer tracker for the awesome ride and a happy wife. Will never go another season without it. Thanks GT
