Description

"There is no cloud. It's just someone else's computer"

Cloud music streaming services are great except when their catalogs don't contain most of your favorite music.
Why should you pay a monthly fee for songs you already own in your local music library on your computer?
Or why should you pay extra for an unlimited data plan?

If you have a well managed music library stored on your computer My Music Library is your favorite player.

Key-Features:
- Sync your audio files wirelessly by using Music Server app for Mac or iTunes file sharing
- Seamless integration of purchased music and files from your music library
- Hide unwanted albums ("Songs of Innocence")
- Listen to the top 100 songs in iTunes
- Explore and expand your music library via media browser (YouTube, Vimeo, Dropbox or Google Search)
- Support for music video files (mp4, m4v)
- Full control center and Apple Watch Glance and EarPods remote control support
- Modern audio player UI with circular indicator with seeking support and wave form visualization
- Sleek mini player
- Intuitive gesture based navigation
- ID3 Tag and artwork support
- Classic and reliable file and folder based music library management experience
- Background music playback
- Spotlight integration
