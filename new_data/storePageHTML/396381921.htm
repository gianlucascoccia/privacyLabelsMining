Description

Daily Expenses has received the following accolades & reviews:

"What's Hot" section on the App Store.

"A handy financial management application for the common man out there." From BestApps.com
http://bestapps.com/daily-expenses-app-for-iphone-review/

Daily Expenses app exports your daily expenses and income data into spreadsheet program via email or WiFi. It is a personal incomes and expenses tracking application that allows you to manage your expenses and income while on the go.

Features:
- Exports your daily expenses and income data into spreadsheet program via email or WiFi.
- Tracks spending for multiple payment types/accounts (eg. Cash/Credit Card/Checking/Saving).
- Secure your private financial data with Passcode protection.
- Using device camera to captures images for Item Photo and Receipt Photo as proof of expense, with function to export to Photos Album or HTML file.
- User customisable Auto Repeat /Recurring Expenses or Income.
- Backup/Restore via Wi-Fi or Email. You can backup the app's database into Mac/PC/USB thumb drive/external HDD and restore when needed.

- Search bar for searching and filtering data including category, description, payment method, note, specific year month data.
- Summary reports based on categories, months, payment types/accounts.
- Specific dates selection for summary report.
- Easier month to month expenses and income comparison in one page, as well as the net balance.
- Multi-Currency support for database & report, with built-in currency converter. Useful for traveling oversea.

- User customisable payment types/accounts (eg. Cash/Credit Card/Checking/Saving) .
- User customisable categories (Add New/Edit/Delete Categories).
- Summary with pie chart and percentage.
- Customizable color themes.
- Itemized category in the Summary Report for both monthly report and specific date report.

- Simple and intuitive interface.
- Quickly enter transactions.
- 74 types of currency symbol for selection.
- Supports iOS11.
