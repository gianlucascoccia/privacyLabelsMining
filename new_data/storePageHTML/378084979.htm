Description

News doesn't wait, why should you? Take FOX 5 Atlanta everywhere you go! Our app connects you with top stories in and around the ATL— complete with breaking news alerts, live video, and real-time weather forecasts.

We cover topics that matter most to you including local & national headlines, weather, sports, traffic, politics, entertainment, food, education, crime and so much more.

NEWS & VIDEO
- Breaking news delivered as fast as it breaks
- Live stream newscasts daily
- Streamlined content display for a user-friendly experience

WEATHER
- Hourly conditions and 7-day forecasts so you're always prepared for your day
- Interactive radar 24 hours a day
- Video updates from FOX 5 meteorologists

GOOD DAY ATLANTA
- Catch up every morning and watch what you missed
- Browse delicious recipes for dinner, lunch and breakfast
- Pet parent? Visit our pets page for our Pet of the Day feature

SPORTS
- The best source for news on the Falcons, Braves, United, and Hawks

FOX5 App for good local news

I use the Fox 5 app to quickly get me informed on my local news and the top national events. I especially like the fact that it is not politically biased. My only very small complaint is that sometimes the written reports have glaring grammatical structural errors which make it a little hard to understand exactly the meaning.
