Description

Cost & time trade-off based FREE trip calculator. Toll & gas for CHEAPEST & FASTEST route on Apple, Google Maps with toll tag ez pass sunpass cash - car truck HOS mileage

TollGuru Toll & Fuel (gas, petrol, diesel, electricity) trip calculator computes toll & cost for car, carpool, EVs, taxi, rideshare, truck, trailer, bus, RV and motorcycle. Travel on the cheapest, the fastest, and compromise routes post time and cost tradeoff.

For toll road, bridge, tunnel, turnpike, express lane trips in the United States, Canada, Mexico, Australia, India, UK, France, Spain, Portugal, Ireland, Netherlands, Denmark, Norway, Sweden, Italy, Germany, Peru, Colombia, Argentina, Chile.

Absolutely FREE. Save thousands in dollars, euro, pound, krone, peso, rupee!

Looking to integrate TollGuru Decision Engine into your app or product? Join 3,000+ rideshares, mapping services, OEM, mobility apps, carshares, last mile delivery, TMS, fleets. Free integration: https://tollguru.com/toll-api-docs

Already using Google, HERE, Bing, MapBox, Apple Map, TomTom, ESRI, MapQuest, JawgMaps, PTV, MapmyIndia? Adds tolls to the maps above: https://github.com/mapup

Wish I had this app years ago

This app is more than just a toll calculator. It is truly a one-stop shop for all traveling needs.
We often go on family road trips and this app is so useful in helping us plan out our trip including duration, best route, cost of gas, traffic congestion and most importantly cost of tolls. A great way to save money!

Were from the UK and so the toll system was not easy for us to understand. This app made the stress of toll charges much easier to avoid. Our car hire company wanted $500 to “hire” and easypass system for the six week hire.... we were told without it we would get a $15 fine for each violation, this app told us the charges and if we could pay in cash or not. Together with Google maps (avoid tolls option) we paid for only one toll in Rhode Island, all others were clearly identified. Apple do offer a similar app “Toll calc”, but I think Toll Guru was a much stronger app.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for leaving us a 5 star review. The idea behind TollGuru is simple! When you fly you get to compare and pick your flight based on flight time and ticket cost. But when you drive, the common mapping apps such as Google or Apple only show you travel time. You do not get to decide between trip time and trip cost. You can either "avoid tolls" on Google maps or Apple maps or go on the fastest route suggested by the map app.

There was no website or app that let you choose your route based on cost and time. There are a few websites that show gas expenses and a few others that show you tolls. Tollguru Trip Calculator calculates tolls & gas costs for different route options to your destination and lets you choose from the cheapest, the fastest, and the compromise routes. Pick the route that suits you the most.

We are glad you found the toll functionality helpful. Our goal is to be a route decision engine for our users. Thank you!
