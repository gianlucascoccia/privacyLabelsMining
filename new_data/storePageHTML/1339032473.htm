Description

The smarter bank account

Douugh is not a traditional bank, we're a financial technology company that helps you live financially healthier, by managing and growing your money on autopilot.

One app that manages it all! Douugh's intelligent bank account takes the stress and hassle out of managing your money - helping you spend, save and invest smarter to reach your goals.

We’re changing your relationship with money for the better while helping you create healthier financial habits. The Douugh bank account and debit card are issued by Choice Financial Group; Member FDIC.

Douugh helps you pay off debt, spend smarter, save more, and actively build wealth.

Create your Douugh account in 2 minutes and have a full bank account from your phone without having to leave whatever comfortable position you’re in!

With Douugh, you can:

Spend
All you need to spend smarter and take control of your spending!
* Manage your everyday spending and bills like a pro.
* With a dedicated Spending Jar to manage your checking account
* A separate Bills Jar to manage and pay your bills and subscriptions
* Have confidence knowing your money is organized and you’re on track to reach your financial goals


Save
Easily achieve your saving goals.
* Organize your savings with Stash Jars - to budget for your short-term goals.
* We make savings a priority every payday - with automated savings to help you reach your goals.
* We'll make sure you’re prepared for life emergencies with your Rainy Day Jar.

Invest
Effortless investing for the long-term.
* Reach your future goals with our smart investment portfolios.
* Managed by experts and tailored to your goal and risk appetite.
* Create as many goals and portfolios as you need.
* Grow your money to reach your goals.

Automate
Put your money on Autopilot to fast-track your financial goals
* Every payday our autopilot technology organizes and manages your money for you.
* Autopilot is at the epicenter of living a financially healthier and stress-free life!
* Automatically manages and grows your money around the clock.

We’re building the future of banking, and our journey has only just begun.

Still needs improvement

App needs an atm finder! Also, percentages for salary sweeper only make sense if you have a steady income. Some people do not, especially gig-workers. I know how much I need for bills, but with varying checks, it’s near impossible to get the right amount into the jar unless you just blatantly overfund it, which defeats purpose of the tool. It will cause more work and stress by guessing and/or moving the money later, only to do it all again next paycheck. Why even have a bills jar if you just have to dump most of the money in and use it in a single transaction list. And there seems to be another issue, as there is nowhere to put varying expenses, such as food or gas. You can’t create a jar for them, or you’ll just have to come back later and move the money manually when it’s time to spend. Fellow Simple users beware, until they fix some of these things. Maybe Douugh will read this and take some suggestions. I won’t be funding my account for now. Will check back in time.
*update*
Thanks for responding. Maybe your Douugh just doesn’t fit in with my style of money management. It’s definitely a great tool and a major improvement over a traditional bank. Because the company is still new and responded to me, willing to take feedback to improve, maybe two stars was a bit harsh. Updating to 4 stars. With some tweaks, this could be a 5-star app.

Looking forward to hearing back from you.
