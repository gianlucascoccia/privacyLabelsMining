Frequently Asked Questions
-------
How does this app record calls?

When you're on a call and you want to record it, open Call Recorder app and press the record button. We will put your call on hold and dial our recording line. As soon as our line answers tap the merge button on your screen to create a 3-way​ call between your other caller and our recording line.

If you want to record an outgoing call, just press the record button. The app will dial our recording line and will begin recording as soon as our line answers. Once that happens, tap the add call button on your screen, call the person you want to record, then press the merge button when they answer.

Great app / easy to use

This app is so easy to use. You just dial out using the app, then dial the number you wish to call, and merge the two (as if it’s on 3 way). If you would like to record an incoming call, you accept the call, then dial the app, and merge the calls. There is a slight delay in the phone conversation, but the person on the other end has no idea, and you can pretend the phone cut out if you don’t hear what they said! I used this for a conference call at work, and it was perfect. My call lasted about 20 mins, on a conference line with 3 other people, and the quality of the call was perfect. The only thing I noticed that might be a concern to some, is that the company can delete your recordings after 30 days, so if you need it, back it up onto another device. You can also rename your recordings, so you don’t have to listen to all of them, while your looking for the one you want or need.
