Highrizer , 09/28/2017

Best data transfer App ever

I literally ran to this feedback section to give this review. I’ve gone a week waiting for my old contact sync app to provide an update to make it compatible with iOS 11. In that time my contacts have been in limbo and I’ve my text messages have been name and faceless.

Enter this beauty of an App. In under 2 minutes I downloaded the App and officially transferred all my contacts just like that. Not only that, I’ve had issues transferring my old pics and videos because I have an antiquated windows computer that required a a days worth of windows updates in order to properly back up my old phone. That being said, all my info was still on my old phone waiting for the smoke to clear before I could transfer to the new one. But just like that, the AT&T Mobile Transfer App has brought balance back to the Force. Thank you for restoring my world.

Go Pats!!! , 05/25/2018

Doesn’t work...

My phone was an iPhone 5S and I’m upgrading to an iPhone 8. For whatever reason when I backed up my old iPhone to update my new iPhone not everything transferred over. I was using iCloud to transfer everything but there were some things that didn’t make it such as my contacts and calendar appointments; ironically all my photos and apps did. So I tried this ATT Mobile Transfer app. Upon setting up it took forever to “get connected” or whatever term it uses. After that it said it was “finding content”. The issue is that it never stopped loading... I kept both of the phones connected for about 45 minutes to an hour and the entire time the app said “finding content”. I decided to close the app down on the phone to see if anything happened and it didn’t. Other people are saying theirs took a few minutes....I was not so lucky.

2nd time around: I went through my old phone and I deleted apps and photos that were transferred with iCloud. I think this made connecting to the ATT Mobile Transfer much easier! However, there still 200 or so contacts that will not transfer over. I go through the prompts and select contacts, it sees 378 of them, but when I hit transfer only 178 actually transfer... I’ve tried it like 4 or 5 times and it still doesn’t work.

MemphisBelleTenn , 11/21/2017

Does what it’s supposed to do!

This was one of the rare times where I’ve downloaded an app, used it and it worked flawlessly, not to mention quickly. This app was so easy to use, transferring all of my info from an iPhone 6 to iPhone 8, that I came back to the App Store immediately to leave my review.

This app gave me an estimated transfer time of one minute...it was under two, which was perfectly fine by me. For reference, this was for 626 contacts (man, I need to figure out how to keep business emails from saving to my account), 34 pics (you know, because the other 3,000 are already on laptop and iCloud) and 8 videos. I would be confident that this app could handle much larger amounts quickly, depending on network speeds. Thank you for making a great utility app!
