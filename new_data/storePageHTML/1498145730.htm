Description

Simplify your day-to-day!

Featured in App Store's "Hot This Week" for multiple weeks.

Welcome to Smart Tasks: a simple, yet powerful way to get things done!

See your whole week in a minimalistic and interactive design. Write down everything from your time-sensitive work to things you just don’t want to forget about. Keep yourself from worrying about what you have to do and start getting things done.

Simple. Fast. Intuitive. Built for you!


FEATURES
• Simple and highly interactive design
• Drag and drop to quickly add, reorder, delete, and import tasks
• Swipe on any tasks, category, or date to access quick actions to get things done quicker
• Choose between 28 aesthetically pleasing colors for categories
• Home screen quick actions
• Export/import tasks and categories

APPLE SPECIFIC FEATURES
• Widgets
• Dark mode
• Notification quick actions
• Add, edit, delete, and view Apple Calendar events
• Import reminders from the Apple Reminders app
• Device Handoff when adding tasks and categories
• Full VoiceOver support
• Smart Notifications

PREMIUM FEATURES
• iCloud sync
• Reminders in tasks
• Daily, weekly, and after completion routines
• Notes in tasks
• Week layout
• Unlimited categories
• Change app’s icon color
• Special configuration options
• Duplicating tasks
• Prioritize today
• Link, email, and phone number detection in titles and notes

MAC SPECIFIC FEATURES
• Multiple window support for categories and tasks
• Keyboard Shortcuts
• Right-click for quick actions
• Touch Bar support
• Drag and drop task files for quick importing
• Drag and drop multiple task files

SUBSCRIPTION
Smart Tasks has a subscription service for premium features. No sign-up needed. New users who purchase an annual subscription will automatically get another 7-day free trial. Upgrade anytime to Smart Tasks +.

Without Smart Tasks +, users will only have access to a limited amount of features.

Subscriptions from Smart Tasks + will be charged to your card connected to your iTunes account. Your subscription will automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage your subscription in your account settings after purchase. Any unused time left in a given free trial will be forfeited when purchasing a subscription.

You Want - No NEED - This App

Well let me be the first to say that I’m a very compulsive organizer & obsessive over time management. We’re talking 2 planners, daily reminders, & lists on lists of more lists. 🥴
I’m taking a pretty heavy course load this semester & I work full time, like most of us do, & the crazy organization is the only way that I can keep up with it all without falling behind.

This app is a D R E A M if you’re someone like me (hopefully not as extreme because I’m not having a good time) or someone wanting to get organized.
It’s very customizable & has multiple options per task to make it what you need it to be.
For example:
I have each class broken up into a Task & within it are all of my assignments, due dates, exam days, etc..
I also made a Task full of “routines” to help keep me on track with weekly studying & reading.

Super helpful is also the ability to look ahead at the next 7 days.
It gives a good look at what to expect in the coming days.

I could really go on & on, but I’ll finish up with saying that there’s something super satisfying about marking a task as complete & watching it disappear off the list. Just as satisfying is seeing the completed list.

Honestly just do it. Download it. You won’t regret it.
