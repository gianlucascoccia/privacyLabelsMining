Description

Enjoy PianoStream the piano app that’s compatible with all player pianos and keyboards! Your Yamaha Enspire, PianoDisc Prodigy and or QRS are all in for a treat!

*Please join our PianoStream Facebook page for continual updates regarding everything PianoStream!

Welcome to PIANOSTREAM featuring MauColi The Pianoman and many other incredible artists from around the world. PianoStream is like Pandora for your player piano! It works incredibly well on PianoDisc iQ, Prodigy and QRS and on ALL Disklaviers!

We have multiple streaming stations for your piano! We cover BEAUTIFUL music that no one else would EVEN think of covering from the 60's, 70's 80,s and 90's. Also Modern hits, Classical, Jazz and every other genre. And our Christmas station is the best!

We also have a "Requested Songs" station where users around the world submit their requests and also a "Recently Added" station.

PianoStream has two interfaces: PDisc for PianoDisc systems namely PDS128+, PDS228, CD Only, iQ and Prodigy. And Disklvr for all Disklaviers and for all pianos with MIDI in/out.

The Disklvr interface is compatible with the Wagon Grand all the way up to Enspire and everything in between! This includes DKC850 and DKC900 and E3. The Disklvr interface will also play on most QRS systems, PianoDisc Prodigy, PDS128+ and the PDS228 because all of them have midi ports. Most PianoDisc players will work with both PianoStream interfaces. The PDisc interface sends an analog signal to the piano and the Disklvr interface sends midi data to the piano.

Disklvr interface uses wireless midi. The best wireless midi receiver out there is the Yamaha MD-BT01. Use the B06 bluetooth audio receiver on Amazon to send an analog signal to PianoDisc systems.

PianoDisc Prodigy has a built in Analog Bluetooth receiver and Bluetooth MIDI receiver therefore it can work with both PianoStream interfaces.

PLEASE join our Facebook PianoStream page where you can garner a better understanding of PianoStream. Long story short however, this app is INCREDIBLE! You will love your piano now more than EVER. Please email Richie at hayesiq@gmail.com with any technical questions or post them on our PianoStream Facebook page.

With PianoStream you can stream your own copyrighted music library. As in you can upload your legally acquired PianoDisc mp3's or Yamaha MIDI files and other third party content to your iCloud account. Then you can create playlists or "Stations" in PianoStream to stream your music which only you can see and access.

For $2.99 a week, $9.99 a month, or a flat fee of $99.99 a year, you'll have full access to all of our content which is constantly growing! All of our music is HIGH quality. Most of it is not quantized or produced by a computer. You'll feel like someone is actually there performing a concert just for you and your family. That's how good we sound! Currently we have about 40+ stations which include almost every genre of music.

*Much of our material was produced in-house by some really incredible pianists. A lot of it was purchased. We also stream some public domain content. We are fully licensed with ASCAP so as to legally stream all kinds of beautiful music. We do not wish to violate any copyrights. If a concern is ever raised over a song we stream, we will resolve the concern or remove it immediately.
