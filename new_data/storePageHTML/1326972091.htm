Description

What do the hottest bodies in Hollywood have in common? The secret is in this at-home fitness app. Get the body you want and deserve with celebrity trainer Simone De La Rue and celebrity nutritionist Kelly LeVeque. The Body By Simone (BBS) app is going to redefine your lifestyle and the way you workout.


EXCLUSIVE AT-HOME FITNESS PROGRAM

Get access to the exclusive workout regime of Body By Simone, called “Hollywood’s Hottest Workout Empire” by Forbes and “The Girlfriend Workout” by the New York Times. The BBS app utilizes advanced artificial intelligence and decades of experience in fitness for you to become the best version of yourself, while keeping a smile on your face. 


PERSONALIZED WORKOUTS, DAILY NEW LIVE CLASSES, & FULL NUTRITION PROGRAM

The BBS app provides you with a personalized, AI generated workout program, as well as access to daily new live classes at BBS studios and on-demand customizable workouts. You’ll also receive a 4-meal per day nutrition plan, either plant or protein based, for you to achieve your ultimate lifestyle goal.


KEEP TRACK OF YOUR PROGRESS

Keep track of your measurements and take selfies to see your progress. Enjoy exercising at home and have your own online personal trainer right there in the palm of your hands.


This app is the one-stop shop for living a healthy life. Instead of going to the gym, experience BBS from the comfort of your own home. Change the way you exercise at home and start living a more fulfilling life.

Start using the best home workout app and enjoy!
