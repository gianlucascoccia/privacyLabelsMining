Description

NEW UPDATE!

Flow Sports is revolutionizing how you watch sports in the Caribbean. We are the #1 app in the region for all the latest live streams and on demand content from your favourite leagues. Watch the Premier League on RUSH, Bundesliga, Indian Premier League, Champions League, Europa League, Diamond League, CONCACAF Football, International Cricket and much much more.

The Flow Sports app gives you access to:

• Stream every match from every league.
• Select the game you want to watch – Your Choice
• Live streaming on the go
• Stream the Flow Sports channel 24/7
• Search for your favorite videos and events
• Easy access to event schedules so you never miss the action
• Re-watch full key matches on demand
• Watch highlights, features, and magazine shows from your favourite leagues
• Live regional coverage from around the Caribbean including the Caribbean Club Championship
• Next generation experience bringing exactly what you want

Flow Sports is the premier sports brand in the Caribbean servicing hundreds of thousands daily and now digitally with the Flow Sports App. With a commitment to high quality premium content including EPL/RUSH, UEFA Champions League, UEFA Europa League, Bundesliga, International Cricket Tours, Diamond League and more. Flow Sports is delivering substantial content to the sports fanatic like never seen before in our region.

*Geographic restrictions apply

Outside of the Caribbean, enjoy hyper local content that will connect you back home to your favourite teams like Jamaica's Waterhouse FC and Portmore United.
