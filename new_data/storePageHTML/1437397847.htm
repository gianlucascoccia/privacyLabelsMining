Description

GET TO KNOW YOUR FRIENDS OF FRIENDS
Quack is the friendship app, designed to make it easy to meet new friends through the people you trust - your friends.

DISCOVER WHO AND WHAT'S NEAR YOU
New friends are all around you with Local Groups or create your own Channel, free of likes or comments.

BUILD BETTER FRIENDSHIPS
We're firm believers that the best way to make new best friends is through your mutual friends! All you have to do is invite your friends to meet their friends.

FIND YOUR PEOPLE
Start chats with new friends when you mutually approve, keep in touch with old ones, or grow your squad with our popular local group chat feature. We never share phone numbers, yours, or your Contacts.

OPEN, HONEST, ANONYMOUS
Share your true self anonymously. No likes, no comments, no judgments.

LEARN MORE ABOUT THEM
Meet online friends with real-life connections. Discover who and what you have in common. Find your next roomie, study buddy, or cycling sidekick - you choose.
