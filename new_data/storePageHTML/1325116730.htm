Description

Save time tracking your self-employment income! Giggy Bank is the simple way to keep track of your work and payments. Stop using clunky spreadsheets, notebooks, or trying to "remember" your important business. Download Giggy Bank today and track your first ten clients for free!*

EASY TO USE
Giggy Bank tracks a single balance for each client. Enter "gigs" to track work done and increase the balance and payments to bring the balance down. It's as simple as that.

GET INSIGHTS
Who has an outstanding balance?
How much money have you been making?
Who are your best clients?
What was your best month last year?

LOOK PROFESSIONAL
Text or email professional statements and receipts to your clients in seconds. You can even include your logo!

FOR SELF-EMPLOYED AND SIDE GIGS
Dog Walker, Pet Sitter, Housekeeper, Personal Trainer, Tutor, Musician, Landscaper, Snow Removal, Photographer, Handyman, Plumber, Electrician, and so many more!

SAVE TIME WITH RECURRING GIGS
Schedule gigs to repeat on a weekly or monthly basis with just a couple taps.

GET PAID ON TIME
Payment Reminders highlight gigs that have gone unpaid for too long.

TRACK BILLABLE EXPENSES
Bought something you need to bill to a client? No problem! Track the cost of the item, add markup, and bill your client for it using Giggy Bank.

NO WIFI NEEDED
Track your self-employment income anytime, anywhere. Giggy Bank is always in your pocket and doesn't need an internet connection. It literally takes seconds!

YOUR DATA IS PRIVATE
- No accounts and no cloud storage. Your data lives in your phone and nobody else can access it.
- Lock Giggy Bank with a PIN for added security from prying eyes.

PRICING
- FREE: You can use Giggy Bank for as long as you want for free to track unlimited gigs and payments for up to ten clients.*
- SUBSCRIPTION: Track more clients with a subscription. Increase the maximum number of clients in the app with the "30 Clients" or "Unlimited" monthly plans.

Pro Tip: Giggy Bank's subscription costs are usually a deductible business expense!

* Start with three free clients and earn up to ten by sharing Giggy Bank with your friends.
