Description

You are a Baller, right? Then you've found the right app.

Basketball Backgrounds is an easy way to design cool, beautiful, custom basketball wallpapers with a few steps.

Check it now!!!

========== Features:
● 30+ beautiful wallpapers.
● Add shelves, icon skins or bars to make your wallpapers cool.
● Blur your wallpapers to make it fantastic.
● Instantly preview your custom wallpaper.

========== How to DIY:
1. Pick an exquisite wallpaper from Basketball Backgrounds or photo album.
2. Add shelves, icon skins or bars on the selected wallpaper.
3. Save it and set it as Home Screen or Lock Screen.
