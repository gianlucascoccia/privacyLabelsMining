Description

Meet the Pocket Ponies! They’re cute, collectible and the latest craze in this new arcade classic, featuring MY LITTLE PONY! Your favorite bubble shooter games remixed!

In this ridiculously fun arcade game, join TWILIGHT SPARKLE and your favorite MY LITTLE PONY™ characters at the School of Friendship! As a new student, you are swept away in the first, awesome Pocket Pony Championship! Face-off against masters, students and teachers in thrilling arcade challenges using the unique Pocket Ponies figurines. Collect them all to make your perfect team!

NON-STOP ARCADE ACTION
• AIM at barriers and walls to get balls bouncing everywhere!
• RELEASE your Pocket Ponies’ magic to defeat enemies in fast-paced arcade challenges!
• LEVEL UP to get multiple shots going at once for crazy popping action!
• COLLECT unique Pocket Ponies, such as the Cutie Mark Crusaders or the Mane Six. No two are the same!
• RANK high enough to take on our legendary Pocket Pony Masters: RAINBOW DASH, APPLEJACK, PINKIE PIE or FLUTTERSHY!
• EXPLORE the School of Friendship and meet a whole host of characters!
• UNLOCK new areas on campus as you complete challenges!
• PLAY as a student character from MY LITTLE PONY: Friendship is Magic!
• BONUS: Come back every day for sweet rewards!

In this unique game, the angle of your shot is key to your offensive strategy! Gamers of all ages will find this game crazy challenging to move up the ranks of this fast-paced entertaining action!

Guardian Blade , 04/09/2019

Actually very friendly to free to play players! If you don’t waist your gems.

So I noticed someone said this game was to hard. But here’s the thing, if you save your gems up you can get a legendary (most powerful) character with a 100% chance of success! How many games do that!
Most games have a “chance”, this game throws that system out and just has a set gem price for each class. Awesomeness abounds!

If you get a character you already have then they level up, as well as giving you bits and gems. And the free gifts you can get every 4 hours are actually good! Yes you read that right, this game dose make you wait 24 hours or even 12, it’s every 4.

One piece of advice for players, when doing a bit of grinding to level up your characters, go for the levels the most chests, they can sometimes give you gems. . . Yes, this game let’s you get gems by just playing it. You don’t get them fast, but it’s noticeable and extremely nice for people who can’t afford to buy them. Basically, the game rewards you for for playing it. Which is something I really like to see!

One suggestion I have would be to let people spend gems to rank up a character. Some people are big fans of certain characters and would love to be able to use them though the whole game.

Also I love the idea of adding a nemesis group as suggested by a different user.

Thank you for reading! And have a wonderful day/night!
