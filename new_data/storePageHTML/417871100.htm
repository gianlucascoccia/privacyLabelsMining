TwoPERCENT14 , 01/01/2020

Has a problem or two, but still a good app

First off I want to say I grew up enjoying Adult swim. I’ve probably seen every episode of family guy and American dad while watching the quirky and original channel. Times have changed but I still enjoy Adult Swim, as I try not to miss Toonami on Saturday’s (I especially enjoy Jojos Bizzare Adventure and One Punch Man). I happened upon the app and found it very reliable in delivering the shows I grew up with, needed to catch up on, or wanted to start watching in the first place.

The only reason there’s a star missing is because of a problem that’s recently happened, every Time I try to watch an episode, only audio will play, leaving me with a black screen or a still frame of an ad. It could be my internet, I live in the state of Alaska where our servers are made out of frozen potatoes and copper wires, but when I figure out the problem or it gets fixed, I won’t hesitate to give it 5 stars

Thank you Adult swim for making my teenage years that much better, as well as being the chill vibe of a T.V. channel the world needs.

Yeah that for sure sounds like a connection issue, which is out of our hands. (Maybe Sarah Palin could look into it?) But we thank you for the thoughtful review (We don't get many of these.)

Dylan Kirchthurn , 01/24/2020

Great Content, but Casting doesn’t work

This app offers a really great pool of shows, most of them are full seasons too which is really nice. Almost all of the Adult Swim programming you’d expect is present, even shows that are brand new like Primal. Also having all of Samurai Jack is a huge plus. I think they should add some more of their old syndicated shows like Mission Hill, Clerks, or the Oblongs if they still have the rights for those. My main issue with the app is the casting, it’s really annoying. Every time I use it the app loads for a really long time before failing. It doesn’t pause the episode so while I was trying to figure out the issue it kept playing ads while I retried the casting. This may just be an issue my Tv has so I won’t lower the rating too far, but it really annoyed me after trying to get it to work for over 30 minutes with no results.
