Passed my 65 on first try

This app is well thought out and slick to use however the questions in the all are very high level. The acru test is very in depth. I used Kaplan as my primary source and this as a back up. The questions were more similar to Kaplan’s than this. This will at least make sure you have a high level understanding of the test material but don’t go into it thinking that doing well on this will give you a pass on the real test.
