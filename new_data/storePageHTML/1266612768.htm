Description

Lose Weight in 30 Days is designed for you to lose weight in a fast and safe way. Not only does it have systematic workouts, but it also provides hundreds of diets at your disposal. It is scientifically proven to help improve your health and fitness. Your workout and calorie data can be synchronized on Apple Health. Stick with the program, and your body will be more beautiful than ever before you know it.

The workout plan contains arm, butt, abdominal and leg workouts to help you lose your extra weight and shape your body. With animations and video guidance, you can make sure you use the right form during every exercise. There's no equipment needed, so you can easily do your workouts at home or anywhere at any time.

You can track your weight loss progress in graphs and clearly count your calories. You can also set targets for self-encouragement. Since exercise intensity increases step-by-step, don’t forget to take a break every three days so your body can adjust.

alysonskye , 11/20/2018

Amazing - but needs more rest time

This app was really great for the first two weeks or so. I could commit pretty well to the schedule, and it became just one more thing to do before bed. My core became significantly harder during that time too.

But with the schedule prescribed by the app, muscle soreness just kept accumulating over time since there was never enough time to recuperate. I wanted to keep up the habit so I started doing it despite the muscle soreness, but it just kept getting worse and it was just too painful after a few days. I decided I’d pick it up again once it went away, but by the time it did go away I almost forgot about the app and had no desire to put myself in that much pain again.

Maybe the app could be improved by allowing the user to set the rest dates based on when the muscle soreness is at its worst? The rest days would sometimes occur on days I felt fine, but then the soreness would start the next day, and I’d have 3 days straight of working through the soreness with it getting worse everyday. Allowing the user to control their rest days, or even just allow for more rest days, would make this app much more practical.
