Description

Control your North Connected Home Bulb from your smartphone or tablet with this simple app.

Features:
• The timer feature allows you to turn the bulb on or off.
• Control the brightness and color of the bulb using the app.
• Create groups of lights when you want to create special lighting options.
• Sync your bulb with the beat of the music by using the microphone on your device.
• Use the shake feature to turn the bulb on or off and to randomly change the color of the bulb.

Bluetooth 4.1 connectivity connects to nearly any smart phone.
