Description

Design your own kitchen from the palm of your hand in augmented reality under few minutes. Simply select, place, and transform your dream kitchen to fit into your room.

Give your kitchen a new, refreshing look by using this interior renovation 3D home design app.

Please enable camera access for Augment Reality (AR) experience. Also ARKit might not work with older IOS devices (requires Iphone 6s or later; Ipad/ipad mini 5 generation or later; iPad Air 3rd generation or later).

Are you bored of your old kitchen? Do you want to change the interior design colours without wasting time? Would you like to check out how your kitchen will look like before spending thousands of dollars on kitchen re-modeling?

Time to take the reins of your interior designing under your control! Build or customize your kitchen in a matter of seconds by using augmented reality. Try out different renovation ideas and give your space a modern look by showing you designer skills. From replacing the cabinets and shelf types to adding new kitchen ware and changing the colour of styling elements, the ARKitchen app is all about enjoying the freedom of creativity while you renovate your kitchen.

Try it for FREE now!

Get Creative with Your Kitchen. Save your time before going to kitchen stores such as Home Depot, Lowes or IKEA.

Unleash your interior designing creative potential in a free designing AR designing space. Use your camera to view your kitchen space in AR and try out the left and right panels to find exciting kitchen design and customization options. You can easily determine the measurements of kitchenware, cabinets, shelves, slabs and other kitchen design elements easily by using this creative app.

Easily Design and Customize

No need to trouble yourself with complicated design tools! Now, you can customize your kitchen design from the palm of your hands. Impress your family and friends with your new design ideas once you have successfully determined what type of customizations you need by using this app. Build your dream kitchen by trying our different wooden textures, cabinet types, kitchenware options and much more without wasting any time or money. 

Maneuver you camera to place items in AR!

Once you have scanned the kitchen space in AR app, you can use the left design tab to choose and place different types of cabinets, shelves, slabs, etc. in different spots. Whereas, you can expand the right tab to change the object colours and try out new textures for various kitchen design elements. Change tiles, replace floors, pick a new kitchen design or try different wooden objects colours/textures to have fun while designing. You can select and place items on the floor and even take measurements by using the AR app.
Find Suggestions, Share Design Explore product inventory to find possible kitchen products, equipment and item suggestions. Once your design is complete, you can also share it with your friends or family members.

How to use ARKitchen
- Download and launch the app
- Allow camera access to launch the AR kitchen designer
- Scan a section and customize it in real time using smartphone
- Expand left and right panels to choose cabinets, shelves and change colors/textures respectively
- Take dimensions and find product suggestions from inventory
- Save and share design with anyone!

Features of ARKitchen
- Simple and easy AR kitchen designing app UI/UX
- Highly intuitive user-interface, smooth scanning and accurate predictions
- Dual panels featuring element customization and colour/texture customization options
- Option to select and place items on floor to replace the items later
- Cool item suggestions available in the kitchen designer inventory
- Try your own textures and see it in real size

Are you ready to give your kitchen a new look? Download and use ARKitchen today!
