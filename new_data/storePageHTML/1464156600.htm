Description

UEFA.tv Always Football. Always On.
Sign-up today for free to enjoy live match streaming of UEFA’s Youth, Women’s and Futsal competitions*, match re-runs, magazine shows, live UEFA draw coverage and much more.
Throughout the regular season, UEFA.tv will also feature post-match highlights from UEFA’s flagship competitions including the UEFA Champions League, UEFA Europa League, UEFA Nations League, and European Qualifiers.
In addition, content from the very best of UEFA’s historic video archive will be featured in the form of classic matches, legendary players and compelling football storytelling.
Existing UEFA registered users can simply sign in to UEFA.tv with their current credentials.
Download the app today and enjoy the best of European football on UEFA.tv.
*Live matches subject to restrictions in certain territories.
