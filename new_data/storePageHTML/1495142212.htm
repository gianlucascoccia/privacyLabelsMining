We are sorry to hear about your experience and appreciate you bringing this issue to our attention. The delivery of the ad in our apps is a process that includes a chain of partners. What exact ad is shown is determined by the advertising network that “gets” the slot in our app in every single moment of the time. That’s why when a kid starts playing on an adult’s gadget, the advertising algorithm may show an ad that he thinks could be appropriate for an adult.

However, our commitment is that all the ads that you may see in our apps should be appropriate for children. We set these requirements for all the advertising partners we work with. If you see any advertising that you think is not appropriate - we can ask our advertising partners to block this ad for all users. We need to work with the advertising network that showed this ad, manually tracking it. To make it we will need some data about the ad you saw in our app: a screenshot/preview with the time and name of the advertiser, and the name of our game that you played.
