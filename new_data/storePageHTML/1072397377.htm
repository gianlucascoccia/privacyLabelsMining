AmyB8484 , 08/09/2021

This app is changing my life

I’ve always been a naturally negative-minded person. I also have type 2 bipolar disorder (meaning I’m depressed the majority of the time and rarely have mania). So I wasn’t expecting this app to do much for me because a lot of my negative thinking was coming from a severe mental illness that therapy hadn’t helped much and medication barely kept at bay.

The writing prompts challenged me to think in a new way which was actually foreign to me at first. I wasn’t used to putting so much thought into things I was grateful for and what was good about life. I was only used to focusing on problems and things I wasn’t happy with. And the writing prompts are really well-written. You can tell a lot goes into writing them. Each one is like a short blog post. It’s not like just a couple sentences. So they really go in-depth and make you think.

But the thing that helps me the most is the social feature. It’s kind of like Facebook, only you share things you’re thankful for and read what others have posted, and you can post pictures as well and comment on posts. Going down the feed and reading others’ entries just puts you in a very positive frame of mind. There is a really positive and supportive community.

Since I started using this app, I feel happier in my daily life. It’s like it’s rewiring my brain to focus on good things instead of the negative. I can’t recommend it enough, and it’s definitely worth the price.
