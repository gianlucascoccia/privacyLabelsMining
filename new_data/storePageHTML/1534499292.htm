Description

Monitor the sleep data you care the most and improve your sleep quality over time.
Rem is an autosleep tracker that uses the AI-Machine Learning algorithm to provide you with the most accurate sleep analysis.

=== Key Features ===
— Completely Automatic and Easy to use
— Sleep Pattern Diagram
— Sleep Score
— Score Breakdown
— Heart Rate Analysis (Dip and Detail Diagram)
— Sleep Regularity
— Time to Sleep
— Trends
— Nap Detection
— Sleep History
— Sleep Assessment
— Smart Alarm Clock
— Low Battery Usage
— Compatible with All Available Apple Watch Versions
=================


We have made it easier for you to track your sleep cycle and improve your sleep score by developing a smart sleep tracking algorithm. Wake up to your sleep analysis in the morning and learn if you have sleep apnea, insomnia or sleep debt.


HOW REM WORKS:
• All you need to do is wear your Apple Watch to bed.
• An iwatch sleep tracker, facilitates the AI-Machine Learning algorithm to analyze sleep cycle.
• Moving more or less than others, Rem adjusts automatically to your personal data
• Fully integrated with the Health App, Rem syncs automatically.
• We ignore sleep sessions that are less than 1 hour.


— EASY TO USE —
Just wear your Apple watch before bedtime and check your sleep log after you wake up. Your shut eye report is displayed in a clear and understandable way.


— SLEEP PATTERN DIAGRAM —
Understand your sleep cycles with simple graphs. Discover how to get the restful and restorative sleep you deserve! You can find out the exact times you’ve been disrupted, as well as easily learn the total amount of deep and light phases.


— SLEEP SCORE —
Your Sleep Score is displayed clearly with a number 0-100 which is an accurate interpretation of your rest quality.


— SCORE BREAKDOWN —
REM App highlights the most relevant data of your sleep in 6 items:
• Time Asleep »» The total time you’ve spent in the light and deep phases of sleep
• Depth »» The total minutes of your deep phase, the most restorative phase of your sleep
• Heart Rate »» Your heart rate analysis is key for measuring your shut eye quality. You can view your sleeping heart rate graph, your average sleeping heart rate, and the dip percentage of your heart rate during sleep
• Disruption »» Get an accurate overview of your sleep disruptions with help of REM sleep tracker’s algorithm.
• Regularity »» REM app records your sleep rhythm automatically and will demonstrate your sleep consistency in a user-friendly diagram.
• Time To Sleep »» “How long it takes to fall asleep” is an important metric for many users. REM App algorithm detects your “Time to sleep” with high accuracy.


— TRENDS —
Follow the impact of your routines on your sleep quality with help of trend diagrams. Easily see your reports to discover your progress.


— SLEEP HISTORY —
Monitor your previous sleep diagrams and compare them to your latest data.


— NAP DETECTION —
REM is an auto nap tracker and will show the nap sessions in a separate field.


— SLEEP ASSESSMENT —
Learn more about healthy sleep practices by taking the assessment and set yourself up for better sleep.


— SMART ALARM —
Wake up refreshed and ready for the day. Our smart alarm clock gently eases you out of deep sleep. To use this feature, Watch OS 6 or higher is required.


— LOW BATTERY USAGE —
Don’t worry about draining your battery because it’s low battery usage, even less than your usual usage of Watch.
Charge your watch during the day or before sleep. Make sure it has at least a %30 charge.

Rem is for anybody who struggles with insomnia or sleep apnea. With the power of its smart sleep tracking algorithm, you can easily find accurate and valuable insight about your sleep. Set the smart alarm before bed time and rise refreshed and on-time. It’s a simple-to-use sleep recorder, no need to put it under your pillow. You need to wear your Apple Watch and go to bed.

I didn’t originally love this app.

Unsolicited review. I bought this after reading several reviews about it. Wasnt crazy about paying for a sleep app when there were free one’s available (including apple’s health app). Most of the apps I used prior to this either were really not accurate or provided a million details I didn’t care about. When I downloaded this for a trial, it didn’t seem to track my sleep accurately. I provided feedback to the developer and it has become very good. It provides exactly the level of detail I want, and is very accurate now. Definitely worth the few bucks a month in my book if you want to track your sleep patterns. Ive always struggled with sleep regularity so I love it now.

Brian Buie , 01/10/2021

Most accurate sleep tracker I've found

I've tested several sleep trackers now and this one has been the most accurate. I love that I don’t have to explicitly tell it every night that I’m going to bed, it figures it out, and it doesn’t record false positives during the day.

It also does a good job of accurately reporting data to the Health app. A few of the previous trackers I used really messed up my sleep data.

My only gripe is I wish there was a way to schedule daily smart alarms. Currently, I have to go in and set the alarm every night, which kind of ruins the whole “automatic” aspect of this.

AidanHeartsBread , 11/22/2020

Almost

New sleep deprived mom here. I’ve been trying to see how much sleep I’m getting with it all broken up.

Such a minimal to the point app which is why I feel it will be so effective for me. Not a lot of information I don’t need to truly sleep better,and distract myself from the core of it.

I like the attention to detail. The colors of light, deep and disruptive in the graph are the same to identify the score of the items below with circles next to them. It helps to easily remember them.

I’d like naps to be included in the grand picture of sleep not a separate tab. Perhaps underneath night sleep. So you can look at both together and with ease.

I like the smart alarm and it truly woke me during my light stage. When I stop the wake up alarm however it stops collecting sleep data, for some this might not be a problem but I am the type to fall back asleep most mornings for another hour or two, and the watch completely misses these period of time giving me inaccurate results.

I’d also like their to be the option of am/pm not only military time.

Other then that it looks like the sleep tracker i’ll be sticking with. 👌
