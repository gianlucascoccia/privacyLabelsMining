Description

eBay Kleinanzeigen - you can buy and sell on Germany's No. 1 online marketplace, easily and conveniently with the app for your smartphone!

Find great deals, from household goods to clothing, garden tools, electronics and second-hand items, to apartments and houses, and even jobs. eBay Kleinanzeigen is the flea market app with the best deals in your neighborhood.

Your advantages:

Perfect for searching and posting free classified ads in your neighborhood
Find cheap offers - favorite things at a fair price
Place an ad quickly and easily: take a photo, write a description, add a price - your classified ad is ready!
Over 30 million monthly visitors and 40 million offers in over 280 categories
Communicate with sellers and buyers directly via the app
Over 280 categories are waiting for you, for example:

FASHION & ACCESSORIES

Find your favorite second hand fashion by brand, size, color, style and other filters
HOUSE & GARDEN

Find great offers and facilities for home and garden
Get inspired by offers for furniture and garden accessories
ELECTRONICS

Find electronics for your household
Buy new or used TV or multimedia center at a good price
Find iPhones and other smartphones in very good condition or look forward to your next game console, such as Playstation or Xbox
REAL ESTATE

Rent a house, buy a house, rent an apartment or buy an apartment: Your new home is waiting for you on eBay Kleinanzeigen
CAR, BIKE & BOAT

Car, bike, motorcycle, caravan, mobile home or boat - find everything for your next trip and the right accessories for it
Find your new used bike, whether it's a BMX, city bike, mountain bike or racing bike
FAMILY, CHILD & BABY

Everything for children and everything that makes family life easier
Choose between different categories, search for baby clothes, children's fashion, toys, children's vehicles or baby items
