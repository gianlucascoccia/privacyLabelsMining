Description

With one single login you are accessing the world of Helbiz - Zoom around your city on electric scooters, bikes and mopeds, order food from top restaurants delivered directly to your door, and stream live sports while on the go or at home - all connected to your HelbizWallet allowing you to pay-as-you-go or enjoy our unlimited subscription across all our offerings.

The following services are waiting for you:

Ride
Instantly rent fleets of electric vehicles with a tap, from e-scooters, e-bikes and e-mopeds from Helbiz, Skip and Mimoto.

Food Delivery
HelbizKitchen: Experience restaurant quality food delivered directly to your doorstep. Order food directly from a curated selection of top restaurants carefully selected and optimized for a home dining experience and track your delivery in real time. Access HelbizKitchen directly from inside the Helbiz app.

Live Sports (coming soon)
HelbizLive: From August 2021, all Helbiz users will have access to live sports streaming

HelbizUnlimited ∞
Unlimited use of the entire Helbiz Universe - a monthly subscription that allows you to, take unlimited rides on our fleet of electric vehicles, enjoy unlimited free deliveries with HelbizKitchen and stream unlimited live sport through HelbizLive.

Download Helbiz today and experience l freedom at your fingertips. 

For more information see www.helbiz.com

BasedLyfe247 , 10/14/2021

Used to be great, now they’re terrible

I’ve used this scooter service for over two years in DC. It used to be great under Skip until Helbiz acquired them. Now it’s absolutely terrible. I’ve been on their monthly subscription plan for a while and now only 20% of the scooters I unlock actually work. Most are completely broken but they still list them as available on their app. On top of that, there have been several instances where I lock a scooter (one that, a majority of the time, doesn’t even work) and I get confirmation from the app and via email that the closing of the ride was successful. But then 1.5 hours later I’m charged a small fortune because the app suddenly claims I never locked it. Customer support is a joke so of course none of these issues are never resolved. It’s a real bummer because this used to be the best scooter service in DC but now I’m dropping it. Not a surprise when you do some research and realize Helbiz is a sketchy firm that’s been sued by the DoJ for running a crypto pump and dump scam, among other things.

GSM2003 , 02/08/2021

Used to be great

I use e scooters almost everyday as my go to mode of transport around the town and have used Helbiz a few months back then it disappeared and I saw it all around the place today again in Arlington VA.
I am so disappointed!! This used to be my go to scooter. The county I live in has a speed limit of 15mph allowed for e scooters. The other e scooters allowed in my area are Link, Bird and lime. I stopped using lime almost a year ago cause to limited their speed to 10mph!! They were the only ones and not like they made rent cheaper since it’s time based. Bird had reduced acceleration so that was bad as well. Helbiz was fast and quick acceleration!
Today this scooter was limited 9 mph!! Why?? I almost bought the monthly subscription for unlimited rides and now I feel glad I didn’t.
I had to write this review cause I was excited to see it again and then quickly very disappointed!!

Update after response from developer:

Hello there! We are really sorry to hear that you are not as satisfied with our service as you used to be. Best, Helbiz Team
