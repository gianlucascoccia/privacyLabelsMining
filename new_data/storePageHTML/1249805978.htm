Description

Join thousands of people who are reaching their health goals with MealPrepPro.

Get a plan that adapts to your schedule, your portions, your goals and your preferences. Choose from 1,000s of delicious, healthy and easy recipes. Order groceries at the touch of a button. Add a partner and set their goals.

- Reach your goals
No more counting calories. Meal plans are automatically adapted to your calorie and macro needs.

- Meal Prep is the new fast food
Prep easy meals in advance to save time and money. Get ahead and make healthy meals your default option.

- Your plan, your way
From high-protein to vegan, Mediterranean to keto we have over 15 plans to choose from. Add your allergies and dislikes to get the recipes you can't wait to eat.

- Cook together
Add a partner to your breakfast, lunch, dinner or entire meal plan. Set their goals and stay on track while enjoying meals together.

- Smart grocery shopping
Our smart grocery list consolidates exactly what you need and organizes everything by aisle. Whether you're ordering online or in-store, make shopping a breeze.

- Dietitian approved
We work alongside registered dietitians to ensure your body gets the fuel and nutrition it needs.

- Track your progress
Track your meals each day and sync with Apple Health to see your progress.

MsRoRodone , 07/27/2018

They have thought of EVERYTHING to help a person be successful at meal planning!!

To start: The app creates a menu for you & if you don’t like what it chooses, you can swap out individual meals for something different; it offers suggestions to swap out with. You can also add your own recipes as well. It also has a shopping list. All you do is push a button on the recipe you’ve chosen & it adds the ingredients to the shopping list. You can cross off items you don’t need & cross things off your list while you shop. All the ingredients on the list are listed in the sections you find them in the store. ie: produce, frozen, spices, etc. The recipes only take 10 to 15mins to make, & the ones I chose, were sooooo delicious that I’m completely sold on this app!! I’m a horrible binge eater & I’m horrible about planning meals & snacks on my own. I have a feeling this is my key to weight loss success! Had to share this find for anyone else interested in meal prep & needs help with their portion control. The only downside to this app, for me, is that it measures by ounces instead of teaspoons, tablespoons, & cups, so I have to look up the conversions every time bc I don’t want to weigh things. So far, that is the only thing I don’t like about the app... They’ve really thought of everything - they even have step by step instructions to scroll through as you make the recipe... Sorry such a long post, but I’m blown away by this app!! 😊

MylesPerAura , 01/05/2021

Wow!!!

About 3 months ago, I was diagnosed with Type 2 diabetes. I was pretty active ( a brown belt in Martial Arts and an avid cyclist), so the news hit me like a freight train. With all the exercise and “somewhat” watching what I ate, the reality was, I could and should be doing more. Thankfully, my A1C was borderline, so the diagnoses came with periodic blood sugar checks and a nutritionist instead of medication(s).

That’s when I found this app. I had gone through a couple of others, but none of them were helpful and a ton of them provided me with information overload, and eventually, deletion of the app. But this app is sooooo on point. I have a clear calendar of what to eat and when, a platform that allows me to click one button to chow I have eaten the meal and stats that are meaningful and easy to review. Most importantly, the food is absolutely scrumptious (I can’t tell you how many times I made spaghetti without whole grain noodles because I thought it would taste lifeless - until I just had the Turkey and Garlic Meatball recipe!!) I just wanted to give a huge kudos to you and your team for putting together a well thought out and user friendly system that will ultimately keep me on track with my fitness and health goals.

Wrightykattie , 01/31/2020

Life changing

So this is my first time ever writing a review but this app deserves it. I have struggled with anorexia in the past and had to stop using MyFitnessPal because of it. But I still wanted a way to track my macros and eat healthy foods without it controlling my life. This app does it. I love how you can swap meals out (over time you really start to find your favorite recipes) and you can trust that each meal is balanced and hits all your macros. The shopping list makes things super easy and most of the recipes I’ve had turned out amazing with very little prep work. There are a couple minors things that need to be fixed. I have had some recipes that leave out one ingredient from the list and you don’t notice it until you’re in cooking mode and it tells you to add an ingredient that wasn’t listed! There are a couple other minor bugs that pop up here and there but honestly the pros outweigh the cons and this app still deserves 5 stars. I have one suggestion though, Kevin Curry if you are reading this, could you add in a search bar or a way to favorite certain recipes?
If you are debating about this app, do it!
