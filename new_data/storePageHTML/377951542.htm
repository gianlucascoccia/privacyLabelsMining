Description

Stream free Hollywood movies, TV series and originals on your favorite devices. Wait, free? Yes, free, always and forever. Watch premium TV shows and movies from a full library of new and iconic hits. All on-demand, without a subscription.
 
Set up a free account to see fewer ads and to pick up where you left off on any device.
 
Filter genres like comedy, drama, action, thriller and fandom to find fresh hits and all-time classics. Fill your ‘Watch Later’ list with all your favorite titles.
 
Check out our Spotlight Channel for handpicked recommendations from the Crackle team.

Ustro , 01/26/2020

Frustration

Let me start with the good. I was able to watch a movie that was not streamable elsewhere. Now the bad. I understand that this is ad supported. It’s actually good, because that also means it’s free. What’s not good is that the ads start out at a reasonable frequency, then seem to be every few scenes in the movie. Even that wouldn’t be as bad if it wasn’t the same ad over and over again. A bait and switch for online gambling that promises a money back guarantee that ends up being only redeemable for store credit. Also, as the movie progressed it became laggier than 90’s online video using a dial up connection. Yet no problems with other streaming before or after it using other services. For this to be useable the lagginess needs to be fixed and the ads need to be more diverse. I’ll likely try again, but only for something not streamable elsewhere.

I like this app a lot

I use this app a lot, I have a boring job so I watch movies on it when I’m at work. I like the movie selection, I like how it has a lot of good movies from back around the 90s and early 2000s since that was the best era of film in my opinion. I don’t mind the advertising breaks, it’s just like watching tv. But a lot of times after the ads are finished, the X, “Learn More” link at the bottom and Ad Length time in the top right corner gets stuck on the screen and it’s annoying. You have to close out of the app, reopen it and find your movie again. It’s the only way to get everything off the screen. It saves your point in the movie though so it picks right back up where you left off which is a plus. I wish there was a fix you guys could do for that. Then it would be totally fine.
