Description

First, the award-winning app by BabyFirst TV is the COMPLETE EDUCATIONAL EXPERIENCE for your little ones!

Make learning fun with 1000+ LEARNING GAMES, TV SHOWS AND VIDEOS, SONGS, and WORKSHEETS in a 100% safe environment.

First has an educational 24/7 TV LIVE BABYFIRST TV STREAM to encourage independent play learning, and exclusive school and classroom games created by education experts!

First can develop strong school foundations and assist babies, infants, toddlers and parents - anytime and anywhere.

We suggest educational videos and learning games for your routines - including features such as FIRST SONGS with nursery rhymes and original babyfirst songs, Sleepy time to assist in your baby and child sleep, and a Book library for reading.

What Makes First So Great?
- Learning Games, TV episodes, and Songs by child education experts
