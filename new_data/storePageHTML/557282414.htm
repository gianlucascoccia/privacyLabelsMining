Description

Welcome to Downpour, your one-stop shop for audiobooks on the go. Discover the wonderful world of audiobooks and enjoy our everyday low prices on downloads, as well as our budget-friendly digital rental option.

Downpour boasts a catalog of over 80,000 unabridged titles with hundreds more being added each month. Offering the finest in contemporary fiction and nonfiction, as well as the world’s great classics, our title collection includes Pulitzer, Nobel, Man Booker, PEN/Faulkner, Hugo, and National Book Award winners, as well as countless New York Times bestsellers. Authors include James Patterson, Karin Slaughter, Don Winslow, George Orwell, Dan Brown and many more.

App Features
• Chaptered tracks
• Offline listening
• Speed through with variable speed playback (1.25x, 1.5x, 2x)
• Fall asleep to your favorite book with the integrated sleep timer
• Browse our huge selection of titles right in the app
• Purchase books in the app with your Downpour credits or in-app purchase
• Use the wishlist to save titles for later
• Remember important parts of the book with bookmarks and notes
• . Review your favorite books
• Stay in sync across all your devices

Curucahm , 11/29/2017

Observations

While I like that the new app updates my library almost immediately. Since I'm not a subscriber I can't purchase books through the app as it only seems to want to use credits. I'm not fond of the new interface, you've shrunken my sorting options to title and most recent and replaced everything with a Hyde icon. Really it doesn't need to be flashy it just data and columns I just be able to list it in text and sort by author/title or author/series, date purchased, whatever. I'm also not liking the management. I don't like the idea of having to mark something as finished. Really what's the point in that. I want to be able to delete a book whether it's finished or not. I'm not sure that it is actually deleting the book locally even when I mark it as finished. Downloading seems to be funkier and less reliable with the new app. It says that it's downloaded things completely but there are way too many times I've received an invalid file error, and had to re download the next 10 tracks. It's really annoying when this happens and I'm driving. Also the addition of DRM books is disturbing. I would prefer it that you keep downpour DRM free.

Developer Response ,

We appreciate the feedback. You'll be happy to know in-app purchases is in the works and will hopefully be available in the coming weeks. You're right sorting has changed but library searching has also been greatly improved; you can now search by author and series, in addition to title, which should help you quickly find your titles. Marking titles as "finished" is just way to let you hide books you're done with. Finally, a large portion of our titles on Downpour remain DRM free but some titles have restrictions from third-party publishers. Happy listening!

GungaDan , 05/17/2020

Problems

I would like to point out three problems with your app, all of which make me more inclined to use Audible instead, even though I would prefer to buy titles directly from you, whenever possible... if only you would fix the app.

First, the time display shows progress and time remaining only in the current chapter. I would like to be able to switch that display, but could not find an option for doing so.

Second, the volume level drops whenever I get an email or other alert tone, but does not pause playback, and so I miss several words of the book, every time this happens. It is quite annoying, and mostly, I would like a setting to choose how I want this behavior to exhibit.

Third, is that the playback will pause when I remove an AirPod, as does Audible, but if I have it out for more than a very few seconds, it will not resume without my taking out my phone, opening the app or pull-down access, and hitting the play button again. This is a lot of extra effort every time someone makes a comment to me or demands my attention for a moment, while I am listening. This also should either be changed, or be made a selectable preference option.

I hope that you will fix these problems, so that I may use your service as much or more than I use Audible. If not, I will likely return completely to Audible, even though I am terribly annoyed with how poorly they treat authors.

Sincerely,
- Prof. Cascaddan
