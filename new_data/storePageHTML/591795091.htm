Description

Go Talk – learn any language when you're on the move - available across your iOS devices.
This travel-ready, easy to access language learning tool is simple and fun with immediate results.
You can start for FREE and then learn to speak more by upgrading to Go Talk Essentials or Premium.

Go Talk is powered by the award winning language company EuroTalk with more than 30 million customers worldwide.
And our customers love Go Talk.

“It is without doubt the most thorough and easy to use language learning tool I have ever seen and once I’ve mastered Japanese I plan on exploring the basic of other languages too.” Codie, UK

“The Go Talk app is a whole load of fun, filled with useful words and phrases. It works offline which is great for learning on planes.” Patricia, Canada

“Go Talk is, in my opinion, the future of language development whilst on the road.” – Will Hatton, The Broke Backpacker

“I have found the app quite addictive - as soon as I have a good score in one topic I want to start on another one.” Katherine, UK

Go Talk is:
● Free to start
● Fun – with games to fill a spare few minutes or many hours when you get hooked
● An Instant Phrasebook - instant search allows you to find the words or phrases you need
● Authentic – we only use native speakers so all words and phrases are spoken correctly
● Smart – our clever software knows the words you struggle with so focuses on them more
● Perfect for Pronunciation – you can record and listen back to your own voice
● Visual – our beautiful pictures help link words with images that help you remember what you’ve learnt
● Soothing – you can listen to background music that helps you learn even better
● Tailored for you - purchase additional topics like golf, driving, camping and more as you need them.

2LaZ2ThinkOf1 , 02/04/2020

Four stars but one critique

Overall I felt like my little test run with the app went smoothly. The fact that so many languages are offered makes this an absolute gold mine especially for someone wanting to learn more African languages like myself. Keep up the good work and expand on the offerings and make things more robust.

My one critique, is that the Easy Game of Tigrinya that I played went well but I didn’t have an English translation of what the words were. So aside from very obvious things like numbers or a glass of water I didn’t know what I was saying exactly. If all of the languages had an English (or whatever the learners language is) translation of what is being said it would really make this a top choice Language app overall. I do however love that words are written out phonetically in English.

Of course, I only did the free trial game so there’s no telling what the paid versions have. Btw, making this a Duolingo style app with the choice of subscription would also be great. I just wanted to relay that critique. Love the app overall. Keep up the good work.

Developer Response ,

Thanks so much for saying you like our app and its choice of languages - you’ve given us all a boost! 😊 But please speak to support@utalk.com because the translation should be available in your own language in the Practice section. The app is designed to automatically default to the language of your device but you can change it to any of 100 others by pressing the cog on the top right of the screen. We really appreciate you taking the trouble to post this review and love the fact you clearly love languages as much as we do! 😊😊 Looking forward to you speaking to Support so they can get your learning back on track asap. Best wishes, Sarah
