Description

Tashi Coloring Games for Kids encourage your child’s creativity. This Coloring Book helps them to develop drawing & painting & learn about colors. Finger painting develops fine motor skills. Thanks to coloring activity your child feel like a little artist. It is beautiful coloring book with lovely coloring pages to color with princesses, unicorns, animals, dinosaurs, cars and more.

Let your child enjoy hours of fun. Painting & coloring book is effective learning tool for kids with undoubtedly funny, bright and creative artwork. Created along with experts in children’s education so this app is adapted for every child. Tashi Coloring Games for Kids is the best way to spend time with your family.

Overall features

You will find there hundreds of bright memorable coloring pages, simple and intuitive menu and navigation easy for all ages. Choose your coloring tools, select from different brushes. Our coloring app is the easiest way to teach them how to do that. Let your creativity run wild with us. Download and install this game now. Enjoy drawing for kids and the best activity for kids.
