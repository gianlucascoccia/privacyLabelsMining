Description

Set your money goals and track your personal savings with this app. Take control of your saving goals and spend cash wisely.
No matter your paycheck’s size, this piggy bank helps you to set aside a portion of it. Use this smart piggy bank to bring your financial goals to life!
This app is completely FREE to use, so why not install it now and start saving money?
Here is why many savers and planners love Money Box and why you will love it too:
· MOTIVATIONAL: You can add a photo of your dream house, holiday destination or any other purchase goal and see a progress in a visual way.
· EASY: Our intuitive user interface makes using this money manager easy.

* REWARDING: It’s easy to set goals, but many find it difficult to follow through. Money Box guides you from desire to success so you can enjoy the fruits of your money.
* SMART: Money Box takes away the frustration of saving money for a specific goal. It gives you the power and confidence to succeed every time, and that’s just plain smart!
* EFFECTIVE: By providing you a simple interface and valuable features to organize and manage your saving targets, you’ll love how effective Money Box is at helping you save money.
Join the growing community of responsible adults who know how to get what they want from life.
=== HOW Money Box WORKS ===
Easily manage your goals from the dashboard:
1. Title
2. Amount and currency
3. Target date
4. Icon/picture
5. Savings account name
6. Planned savings frequency – weekly, monthly etc.
7. Reminders
Make contributions toward your goals.
Just like a major savor card, this app lets you reach your goal and get exactly what you wanted!
