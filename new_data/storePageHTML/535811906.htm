Description

In need of a logo maker? A speedy photo editor? A flyer maker? Or a poster creator that is easy to use?

Over is now GoDaddy Studio, the world’s easiest photo editor and story maker–no design skills needed. Create stunning marketing materials and design graphics, professional ads, branded content, business cards, and stunning stories in minutes.

The new Remove Background tool in GoDaddy Studio is a game changer for everyday entrepreneurs. With a single tap, the subject in your photo is cut out from the background, giving you clean, professional-looking product shots for your online showroom.

Best features:
- Add videos from your own on-device video library or from our exclusive Stock Video Library.
- Logo maker with over 67,000 ready-to-use graphics
- Poster and flyer maker with a selection of over 500 fonts
- Unlimited access to a large selection of stock images
- New design templates for your creative canvas available daily
- Flyer, business card, poster and logo maker, and graphic design and photo editor app all in one

Story
GoDaddy Studio (formerly Over) is a great tool for all influencers and social media managers. Get your storytelling and brand image aligned and reach your followers!

Discover a large selection of templates, stock images, canvases & wallpapers with incredible layouts. We offer logo creator tools and a large variety of fonts to best suit your graphic design team.
Make your designs pop. Add text to photos, blend, add layers and use the masking tool like a pro.

Get access to a huge stock image library and create stunning Instagram stories, Facebook and Tiktok posts, Snapchat stories, banners and flyers.

Photo and Video editing
Easily import your photos and edit them like a pro. Retouch your pictures with a large selection of filters and find the right preset to best suit your pictures.

You can add videos to your designs, add filters to videos, edit them to adjust brightness, saturation, or contrast, layer them with images, text, and graphics, and blend them with images to create amazing, unique video effects.

GoDaddy Studio (formerly Over) is a complete photo editor tool. Adjust and edit the settings of your photos. Play with your photo perspectives & grids and get automatic crops to share on your favorite social media channels.

Create memes and collages
Are you a meme fan? Add text to photos and create the best memes to share on Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat, and TikTok.

GoDaddy Studio (formerly Over) offers many layouts to combine your best pictures and create beautiful collages.

Design your logo
GoDaddy Studio (formerly Over) is also a logo creator tool: draw or select a logo template and visually share the value of your company.

Use the logo creator to make a design and apply it to all of your marketing materials and design graphics, then post to Instagram, Facebook, Tiktok, Pinterest, or make a Snapchat story. Let your logo and brand appear everywhere.

Download GoDaddy Studio today and build your brand at the tap of a button.

Best design editing app, but not perfect

Words cannot explain how much i love using over, I’ve used it for so so long i dont know how many years it’s been. I do hate how you often change the layout of the app though, it’s often confusing and makes me don’t want to update at all because what i have currently is already great, doesn’t make me feel like i need an update. I really do love and want to support this app, however the prices of subscription are honestly so high. This isn’t even a very complicated app/program like, for example, clip studio paint which a way more reasonable subscription price considering how much the app can do rather than over. I understand it might be because of all the available clip art licensed on the app, but we don’t even really... use even half of it. Maybe change the system to pay the clipart’s artists based on how much their cliparts are used? And slash the subscription price. I would definitely subscribe if the yearly price were like, 25$? 30$? Or around that number. Just a more reasonable price than the current one because the current price would only be reasonable for a professional designer, which mostly doesn’t use apps like over professionally. Regardless, this app is still amazing and i will still be using it.
