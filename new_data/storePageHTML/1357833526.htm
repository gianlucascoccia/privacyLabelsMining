Description

Saildrone builds and manages a global fleet of wind and solar-powered autonomous ocean vehicles designed to collect data critical weather forecasting and climate modeling.

Featured in: New York Times, Outside, Popular Mechanics, Bloomberg, Forbes & more!

Saildrone Forecast is a beautiful and accurate weather app that uses the same data expertise we use to manage our fleet of ocean drones.

Get hyper-local current and future conditions presented on an easy-to-use weather map, glanceable tables, and scrollable graphs. Take advantage of powerful design customizations to visualize map conditions the way you like. Weather anything with high-definition forecasts powered by cutting-edge technology. And—NO ADS EVER!

Saildrone Forecast Top Features – Free to All
- Check current conditions and minute-by-minute changes on a fully interactive global weather map
- Actual and feels-like temperature
- Rain, snow, and mixed accumulation
- Wind speed and direction across land and sea
- Tide and current speed and direction for oceans, seas, and bays
- Search for locations or landmarks around the world
- Set weather alerts—where and when you want to receive them
- Add the widget to your device’s widget screen for quick access to conditions
- Data security—your personal data will never be sold to 3rd-party companies 

Get the Most Out of Saildrone Forecast
- Customize layers with transparency, units, color range, particles, vector arrows, or barbs
- Long tap any spot on the map to reveal conditions for a precise location
- Save and name favorite locations by tapping the star
- Tap the color wheel to enable Local Colors for a more precise range of color values
- Get a 5K map view of your local weather with a Premium subscription

Sailors Love Saildrone Forecast!

Saildrone Forecast is designed for use on both land and water, but with wind-powered water activities at our core, sailors (windsurfers and kite surfers) love the hyper-local wind speed and direction and current forecasts.

“What makes Saildrone Forecast superior to other available sailing weather apps is that it’s updated on a day-to-day basis with fresh high-definition forecasts available each morning,” said Will Paxton, a San Francisco-based pro sailor.

Better Data Means Better Forecasts

Saildrone’s mission is to collect and share more data about the planet. The oceans cover more than 70% of Earth’s surface and drive weather, regulate temperature, and ultimately support life on Earth. Much of the weather we experience on land starts in the oceans, so we believe weather forecasting should start in the oceans, too.

Our global fleet of saildrones continuously sails the world’s oceans collecting atmospheric and oceanographic data. These expeditions generate unmatched, proprietary high-resolution data sets that are used by scientists at the world’s leading science organizations to better understand air-sea interactions and improve long-range weather forecasting and prediction of extreme weather events.

We invite you to join our mission! Download Saildrone Forecast now.

lucky gander , 03/24/2019

High hopes

I am pulling for this app, seriously! It has great information on very specific locations, the design is particularly beautiful for a weather app, it is easy to use, easy to search/favorite locations, and the features look to be very promising if they mature (I hope you add the surf feature!). I see the potential and this app could really be amazing.

My one critique/skepticism is on temperature accuracy. Upon first use in LA, it seemed to me like the temperatures reported at all locations were too cold. When comparing to other resources, Saildrones was always the coldest by anywhere from 1-10 degrees. I have a hard time believing everyone else is that far off. I spent the day outside with most other apps reporting around 68 degrees while Saildrones reported 64, and 37 years of life experience tells me that 68 was more accurate. Looking out in the forecast, the coldness trend continues for all 7 days compared to other forecasts.

I’ll be using this app, hoping that my skepticism will disappear and that the temperatures will prove to be spot on. Good luck and keep up the good work!

Developer Response ,

We're on it! Thanks for such detailed feedback. We've shared it this with our forecast modeling team to take a closer look at. Los Angeles is definitely a priority area for us. If you have any other thoughts or feedback to share, please email us at forecastsupport@saildrone.com

Interesting and pretty accurate

Saildrone Forecast is an interesting endeavor using autonomous marine vehicles to collect weather data. This allows them data that others don’t have, making weather forecasts pretty accurate, especially on the coast.

The UI is nice and has a richness that makes it nice and easy to use.

So why not 5 stars? Mainly two items. The first is that every time I open the app it shows winds, and I cannot seem to change that. I would like either a default setting, or that it remembered what layer o had active last. The second is that when you click the “Help & FAQ” link in the app, it takes you to a Zendesk page that says the following “Oops, this help center no longer exists
The company you're looking for is no longer using our help center. We suggest reaching out to them directly via their official website.”

To me that is very lacking in care of customers. Especially for a paying customer.
