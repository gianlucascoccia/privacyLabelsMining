Description

Making food has never been more fun! Monster Chef is all about cooking unique and surprising monster food. Enjoy the entire food-making process - choose the ingredients, mix them, cook them, fry them, bake them and create extraordinary monster dishes.
Feed the creatures and see what they like, try different dishes to figure out their taste.
Does the monster love what you made? Great! It doesn’t? Never mind, try something else...

Monster Chef is brought to you by Pazu Games Ltd, the publisher of popular kids' games like Girls Hair Salon, Girls Makeup Salon, Animal Doctor, and others, which are trusted by millions of parents worldwide.
Pazu games for kids are designed especially for children under the age of 10. It offers fun educational games for girls and boys to enjoy and experience.
We invite you to try out Pazu games for kids and toddlers for free and discover a wonderful brand for kids games, with a large arsenal of educational and learning games for girls and boys. Our games offer a variety of game mechanics adapted to the kids’ age and capabilities.
Pazu games have no ads so the kids have no distractions while playing, no accidental ad clicks, and no external interferences.
