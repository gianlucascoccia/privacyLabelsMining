Description

Looking for a new hairstyle?
Just not sure what will suit you?
Upload your picture – try on a new hairstyle, see if suits you?

• Over 600 hairstyles to try on.
• More than 50 different hair color options for every style.
• Short, medium, long, straight, wavy, and curly hairstyles to choose from.
• Take a photo, or use your photo album.
• Share results with your family, friends or hairstylist.
• Receive hairstyle information and important tips.
• Understand what suits your face shape.
• Risk free!

So try on a new hairstyle and avoid a hairstyle disaster!

Love this...very funny!

I haven't laughed this hard in a long time. I made my husband look like Rod Stewart from 1978 and my 14 year old son look like a punk rocker with purple hair. I also put long hair on my cat and my dachsund and have used it on some of my co-workers. It is hysterically funny. I guess if you buy it thinking you are going to use is seriously you may be a bit disappointed, although it works well for giving you an idea of hair shape, the texture definitely looks fake. I love that you can really adjust your photo to fit correctly. This is a way fun app. Only suggestion would be to add some crazy colors to the hair, like lime green, hot pink and blue, oh, and my son has requested a mohawk.

Reezree , 10/02/2018

Waste of Money

I just downloaded this app about 5 minutes ago and I can already tell you it’s a waste. There are several ways this app could be better. The first is the way in which you take your picture to use. This is an import part of the utilization of the app because of your face doesn’t fit, then NONE of the hairstyles will work, making the app look and feel cheep. I have a high hair line, and long oval face and could not get any of my pictures to line up properly. This is because you have to snap a pic first then put in in the guides.... it doesn’t work. I also agree with the other user that most of these style are not ones any female would use. Overall, the app had a very cartoon feel to it. DON’T waste your time or money on this. Your imagination is better I promise.

Lil' Ang! , 10/07/2019

NOT REALISTIC!

I’m really looking for something new & was excited to see see this app & the bundle with the color options for $3.99, unfortunately tho, the hairstyles are awful! The few “normal” ones are very similar to each other & basically what I have currently. The others all remind me of the “Korean Club Scene” with their un-even angles & waxed pieces. They are not realistic hairstyles for the average woman looking for a change. Thought I was getting a deal by bundling the 2 apps, instead I was just wasting more money!
