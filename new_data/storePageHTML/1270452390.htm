Description

Tully was created to allow recording artists and songwriters to write and record ideas, store creative content and distribute their music all within one application.

It's the only app available with tools for everything the Artist needs, from songwriting, secure storage, file sharing, music industry management tools right through to music distribution.

Tully has a rich feature set with support on mobile and desktop for subscribed Users:

- Write lyrics while listening to your audio
- Free premium beats for your song writing projects
- Find rhyming suggestions while you write lyrics
- Record ideas for your song and create mixes
- Store your Masters and music in a secure cloud server
- Share and collaborate with others
- Track and secure your audio and media files
- Distribute your music to all major streaming platforms
- Create contracts and split sheet agreements
- Manage your metadata for your audio files

Create. Manage. Distribute.

All from one app!

Needs more features in my opinion

For the past few days I’ve been using this app, because I was introduced by it, by Joyner, so I was intrigued to think that he made a song with an App! So out of curiosity I checked it out, I started writing some lyrics off a free beat provided by the app. As far as I was concerned the app was pretty decent so far, and I actually believed that Joyner really made a song only using this app. I took upon myself to check out the free beats provided, and they didn’t cater towards the Hip-Hop Genre, so I tried importing a beat online, but I’m confused if you could import beats!(Make it more simple) So I tried to attempt on looking for a feature to produce my own beat, but that doesn’t exist though! In my opinion, it’s like everything was just advertised to be “100% made by the Tully App”. If it isn’t obvious enough, this is more of a personal issue to be honest, but it puts me in distress, because this app would just be insanely unique if you could make beats and record lyrics.
