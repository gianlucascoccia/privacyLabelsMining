Description

The EagleView App lets contractors order and access EagleView property measurements to help generate estimates, plan for jobs, and show homeowners what they can expect.

Features:
- Easy to use interface
- Auto login
- Quick onboarding experience
- Streamlined ordering process that can be completed anytime, anywhere
- Dashboard containing report history
- Automatic quoting
- A Suggested Waste Factor, unique to every residential asphalt roof
- 3D Visualizer to view measurements and proposed improvements
- Augmented reality (available on AR compatible devices)
- Access to EagleView property imagery and ability to upload your own imagery
- Ability to annotate images
