Description

◆◆◆ To do List Widget for iOS 14 is here ◆◆◆
Create unlimited To Do lists and add to your Home Screen.
You can open and edit to list directly.

Follow these 3 simple steps to get started:
1. Create a list in the app.
2. Add a widget in your preferred size.
3. Long press to configure the widget.

FEATURES:
- Create unlimited To Do lists.
- Add and Edit lists directly from Home Screen.
- Change colors & font size.
- Support 3 different color styles.
- Support 2 different layouts
