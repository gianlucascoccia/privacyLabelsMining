warongerms , 06/20/2019

Like the podcast version of a novel!

I first learned about this app from a local author whose work I really enjoy (Curtis Chen). He did a project in collaboration with a couple other authors and it was published here (Ninth Step Station). I listened to the audio series and it was great! I love that it’s episodic like a TV show, but that it’s still a book. It’s like the podcast version of a novel!

Then I tried out a few more series and I have loved them too! The writing is enjoyable, the stories are interesting and well put together, and the narrators are wonderful.

Also, the app works nicely. It’s easy to search for things or just brows, easy to buy a season (after listening to the first episode for free!), and easy to move from one episode to the next. I am such a fan, this app is great!

lv186000 , 10/20/2021

Great content, wonky ui

Update: thanks to support for being so responsive! The bookmark bug is being worked on, and the chapter numbers are missing only in Marvel titles, which they are now aware of.

Original comments: Love the realm content. For the few I've listened to, the production values are quite high and the voice acting has been superb. I do have some problems with the app, however. First, a strange design choice to me is that when viewing the list of all chapters there are no chapter numbers provided. This means that I have to remember the chapter title so I can remember my place. I *do* see a chapter number when the app is open and the player is running minimized (for browsing other realm titles), so I know the numbers are there somewhere. Another issue is that there is no means to bookmark one's location while listening. This is supposed to be automatic but it doesn't always work. When listening to the Marvel's Jessica Jones title (which was great), the app returned me to the same early chapter over and over again, so I had to find my spot by hunting through the unnumbered chapter titles and basically guessing. I didn't find a means to email support with these issues so I'm posting here. I'll continue to listen to content and use the app.

Developer Response ,

Hi there, thank you for taking the time to leave these kind words about our content, and this honest review of your experience! Our team is looking into this, since both of these features should actually be available to you. If you wouldn't mind, can you please go to the "More" menu in the app and click on "Report a problem with the app"? That will give us a bit more diagnostic information about the issues (and you can include screenshots or recordings of what you're seeing in the app), which should help us figure out what's going on here.
