Unstable Apple TV support

December 13, 2020. The Apple TV app now starts locked either as Kids profile or it shows all profiles but when clicking the adult profile the screen stays put (nothing happens). The app suddenly forgets who I am (a VIP account through Sling account). I have deleted the app and reinstalled it for at least three times to get it working again and I’m getting tired of doing so.

When the app is working, there is no closed captioning or subtitles offered through a menu to make adjustments. Subtitles have been implemented in western countries for many decades but they are still stubbornly missing from video production in the middle east. I’m surprised that Apple even CERTIRED this app for use without it, since it is a requirement to have closed captioning here in the US!

Let’s not fool ourselves, this app is a weak and sketchy copy of a very popular worldwide app whose name starts with an N. Are people in the middle east no longer able to put on their own face or at least attempt to look original 😏.
