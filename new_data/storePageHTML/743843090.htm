Description

Known as the best mobile app for Athan and prayer times, our app is used by millions of Muslims around the world. Athan brings you the most accurate prayer times and many other features such as Quran, Tasbeeh, Islamic Calendar, Compass to locate the Qiblah, Ramadan 2022.

+ You can now visualize the Qibla using Augmented Reality. We are hamdolilah the first Islamic application to offer this feature to our users

+ Full Athan: Athan Pro is the only app on the store to offer this important and unique feature

Use Athan Pro to:

- View your daily dashboard for Ramadan 2021: Contains all the prayer times for the day in addition to the remaining time for each of them.

- Accurate time such as Suhur, Fajr, Zuhr, Asr, Iftar, Maghrib and Isha.

- Listen to the Holy Quran

- Qibla: Either you’re at home or traveling this feature will help you locating the Qibla direction with less effort. Thanks to the integrated compass. See the distance between your location and the Kaaba. You can also now use Qibla with ARKit and a choice of different prayer mats.

- Dynamic wallpapers that change according to the time of the next prayer.

- Wallpapers: We selected stunning backgrounds (Mosque, Minaret, Gradient colors, Sunset etc.) to apply to the app or to save to your photo album. Each picture has been selected to deliver a high-quality res to fit all your devices.

- Azan sounds: Listen to the most beautiful azan from all around the world.

- Today’s Extension (Widget): Designed to give you an overall view of your daily prayers without having to open the app constantly.

- Apple Watch: Install the Apple watch version & enjoy having the prayer times on your wrist​.

- Use 3D touch shortcuts to quickly accomplish tasks such as accessing ​your daily prayers dashboard or locating the Qibla direction

- Iftar and tarawih time (Iqama)

- Hadith & Picture of the Day

- Calendar with of religious holidays

- Islamic greeting cards

- Monthly schedule (imsakiye) for prayer time

- Send funny Halal Muslim Emojis (Hijab etc.) to your friends & family.

- Tasbeeh with phonetic support, French & English

- Azkar before, during & after the prayer

- Display of 99 names of Allah.

- Mosque and Halal places

Please send us your feedback/remarks by email to our support. You will find the email in the app.

Good. Excessive costs, questionable design choices.

This is a very good app. It’s the most beautifully designed Islamic app and well maintained. The different theme options, dark mode, Hadith of the day, picture of the day are all lovely options.

The one thing that kind of ruins the app for me is the pricing. Every few months there seems to be some new feature with a new higher pricing, I’ve already paid almost twenty dollars for this app. That’s just too much. Oddly, I’d prefer that the people developing this app just do a monthly $1 or $2 per month charge and provide all the features instead of adding some new feature package that is for a larger amount.

The other issue I have is the app ringtones. There isn’t a simple ringtone like the iPhones default Tri-Tone. Everything is either long Athans or ringtones that just go on too long. Both are not helpful for the brothers and sisters who are in non-Muslim countries and don’t want the Athan playing loudly as their notification tone.

Unacceptably Inaccurate

I’ve seen many people complain that the timings were inaccurate before installing this app. I decided to give it the benefit of the doubt because I really like your Quran Pro app. However, when I first installed the app, I noticed that the most crucial prayers for fasting, fajir and maghrib, were off by 1-2 minutes. The other day I noticed that asr prayer was off by a total of 4 minutes. And now, fajir prayer is off by TEN minutes. This is just unacceptable. It’s a shame because the app’s design and interface is really good. However, looks aren’t everything. The core function of the app is completely off. This is why I’m giving this one star. I regret buying premium, because I will be uninstalling this app. Please use the money I paid, and that many others have probably paid, to fix this problem. If this issue is fixed, I’d gladly reinstall the app again.

UPDATE: Just reopened the app before uninstalling, and it updated the prayer times so fajir prayer is now off by one minute instead of ten. However, still incorrect and I find it unreliable. It shouldn’t go off by 10 minutes because I haven’t opened the app in a couple of weeks. And it’s not correct in the first place.
