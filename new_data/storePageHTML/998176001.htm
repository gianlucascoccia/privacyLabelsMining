Description

Do you remember growing up with a dollhouse and all the hours you’d pretend play with dolls & their home? Kids love to play games & create fun life stories. The My Town Home Dollhouse game is the perfect pretend role play game for kids & children. With a digital house they can call home kids can interact & play with just about anything in it. This is a digital dollhouse with lots of rooms & fun characters just waiting for kids to role play with.

My Town : Home Dollhouse is a kids pretend role play game! It is your NEW digital dollhouse where everything is interactive & fun to play. In My Town, one of the best role play games, hours of digital adventures are ahead every time you open up the My Town app & enter the world. Kids can interact & role play with almost every digital item they see in the My Town home world & create their life story. Live through the day in the miga city town with your different characters. In the My Town game, they can then go into the kitchen & treat themselves to their favorite breakfast! Children can use their creativity & imagination in so many different ways as they pretend role play in the family game & explore the 6 digital rooms of the miga city town dollhouse.

The only limit in My Town is your imagination! Pretend role play any life story in the digital house with the amazing dollhouse game! The game is perfect for 6 year olds.

FEATURES
*6 detailed digital rooms in the house including a living room, kitchen, parents room, little kids room with fun outfits to dress up in, bathroom & back yard - a complete play home & house for a family!
*Many family characters to play with in the house, including a mom, pepi dad & six kids aged 6 to 12.
*Pure open ended play & fun in the house of the My Town game world without stress. Create your own little life story in the city!

What Type of role play can kids have in the dollhouse home game world?
The My Town : Home Dollhouse game has all the rooms a real world house in any city has for hours of fun role play! Little Kids & children can Role Play, write their life story & have fun dress up games where they role play. Kids could also have cooking games where they pretend to have a family breakfast or dinner. Very much like a real doll house, kids role play their life story at home.

RECOMMENDED AGE GROUP
Kids 4-12: My Town games are safe to play for hours even when parents are out of the room, not at home, not in the house or city.

ABOUT MY TOWN
The My Town Games studio designs digital dollhouse games that promote creativity & open ended play for your children all over the world. Loved by children & parents alike, My Town games introduce environments & experiences for hours of imaginative play. The company has offices all around the world where games like My Town are created for children to play & create their own life story. For more information, please visit www.my-town.com
