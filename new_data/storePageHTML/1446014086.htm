Description

Send animated emojis with gestures and entertain yourself and your friends! Over 100 cute emojis for iMessage, Messenger, WhatsApp, Signal, WeChat, Mail and other apps.

Choose your emoji face and send thumbs up or thumbs down, okay, kiss, heart, victory, middle finger and many and many other fun gestures. Use animated emoticons in your messages to easily express your feelings and moods.

Try the app for free and have a lot of fun.

Features:
- over 100 original stickers expressing different emotions
- eleven emoji faces for each sticker
- all high quality animated stickers
- quick search using keywords or emoji
- designed for all applications that support animated GIF or video
- direct support of animated stickers for iMessage
- direct support of animated stickers for WhatsApp
- possibility to save stickers in Photos
- keyboard for stickers copying into clipboard
- ten animated stickers in all styles for free
- purchase of all stickers for a one-off payment
- no monthly fees
- no advertising
