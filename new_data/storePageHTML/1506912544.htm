Description

Everbreed is the easiest way to keep track of your rabbit records. All your rabbitry information is now available in your hands, and accessible on your phone, tablet, or computer.


*** MAIN FEATURES ***

SCHEDULE BREED PLANS
Create chains of events in your Everbreed schedule to reflect your rabbitry’s procedure for breeding, palpating, nest boxes, and more!

REPORT BIRTHS
When a doe kindles, you can report that birth in Everbreed to create the Litter, and schedule Litter events. If the doe doesn’t kindle, report the birth as missed to track doe and buck misses in Reports.

TRACK WEIGHTS
Enter weights for kits and track their performance as they grow. Track which breeders have the best kits.

CREATE AND SHARE PEDIGREES
Offer your customers customized printed or online pedigrees. Easily share them online with a verifiable link.

REMINDERS AND NOTIFICATIONS
Never miss a kindle date again! Everbreed keeps track of your important events and notifies you on your phone or email of upcoming tasks in your rabbitry.

RABBIT SALES AND TRANSFERS
Easily Sell or Transfer rabbits to other users.

PRINT CAGE CARDS
Design and print Cage Cards for all of your rabbits. Scan them with your phone to access records right at the cage.

FINANCIAL LEDGER
Record income and expenses for your rabbitry to make sure no money slips through the cracks.

OVER A DOZEN REPORTS
See reports on breeder performance, litter weights, meat production, and much more!

YOUR DATA IS SECURE
No more worrying about computer crashes or a broken or stolen phone. Your data is stored securely and backed up in the cloud for you to access at any time from anywhere.

USE ON MULTIPLE DEVICES
Everbreed automatically syncs with your calendar, desktop browser, and other devices.

NO WIFI? NO PROBLEM. With the Everbreed app, access your rabbit records even when you’re offline or away of from wifi, after you’ve synced your data (currently read-only).
