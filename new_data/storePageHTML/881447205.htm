Description

Biz2Credit is the country’s leading online small business lending marketplace. Our easy-to-use mobile application provides entrepreneurs an instant access to a multitude of Small Business Loan options for establishing and expanding their business. It enables users to apply directly for Business Loans using their mobile device or tablet within 15 minutes and receive up to $1 million in less than 72 hours.

Using Biz2Credit App you can:

1-Securely, register or access your Biz2Credit account on the go.
2-Submit financing requests to lenders and manage important notifications.
3-Use DocVault™ to upload and share up to 10GB of business documents.
4-Consult any of our Loan Specialist for various small business loan options, completely FREE of cost.

If you prefer to use your computer you can continue from where you left off on the mobile app. We would love to hear from our customers, so please call us at 800-220-5678 or email us at info@biz2credit.com if you have any questions or concerns.
