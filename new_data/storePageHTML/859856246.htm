Description

Get fully featured, disposable phone numbers instantly.
Keep your personal phone number private and secure.

• Need a throwaway number for Craigslist?
• Need a second phone number to use for work?
• Wary about someone who just asked you out on a date?
• Want a number to post safely on social and dating networks?
• Need a temporary number for short term projects?

With Numflix, you can add extra phone numbers to your devices. They are real phone numbers with full capabilities including voice calls, texts, voicemails, call recordings, and more. It is perfect for occasions when you need to give your contact to someone, but would rather not reveal the personal phone number.

Here is why you will love Numflix:

FAST AND EASY: Numbers are created instantly on your demand with few taps. Straight forward to use with familiar interface.
FULLY FEATURED: Voice call, text messaging, voicemail, call recordings, reverse caller ID lookup, and more.
RELIABLE: Uses infrastructure of top tier carriers.
WORKS ON ALL DEVICES: Works not just on iPhone, but iPod Touch and iPad as well, turning them into fully featured mobile phones!

Download the app and start using the service with the included free number.

Please Note

1. Currently you can create numbers with US and Canada country code only. Support for more country codes will be constantly added in the future.

2. In addition to the on demand packages, the app also has monthly subscription options that include heavy discounts, prices ranging from $1.99/month to $4.99/month. Note that with all subscription options,
- Payment will be charged to iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase.

- Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user’s Account Settings after purchase.
