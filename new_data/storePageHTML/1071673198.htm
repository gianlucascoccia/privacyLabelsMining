Description

Be on the lookout, king warriors!
The weave of magic is on turmoil. Soon the ancient Rune Continent will open for a short period of time and allow you all to enter it and face challenges like you never had, fight like you never had, rally with your alliance like you never had, get rewards like you never dreamed of.
The Rune Continent event will open after 00:00 on Dec. 14th (UTC) until Jan. 3rd (UTC).
The Rune Continent is not for the faint of heart. It will challenge your spirit, your willpower and your resilience. Your alliance brothers will be the beacon of light in that mayhem, hold tight to them, fight with them, shoulder to shoulder, back to back and conquer the Rune Wonder together.

Game of Kings: The Blood Throne is completely FREE to play and it features:
- Players vs. Players Epic Wars.
- Explore the world map, seek and attack different Monsters and get precious prizes.
- Gather 6 different kinds of resources (food, iron, stone, wood, silver and gold) to use to build and upgrade buildings, train armies and research new technologies.
- Gather hundreds of thousands of soldiers among Swordsman, Archers, Knights and Siege Machines, in 4 increasingly powerful tiers! Attack other players and defend your Empire!
- Create or join alliances and wage wars with other coalitions. Win them all and dominate the Kingdom! Make friends with other players, develop together and fight your common enemies like brothers!
- Forge Epic and Legendary weapons to become immensely powerful!
- Power up your Hero with 2 different skill trees system.
- Develop 142 different technologies and unlock new buildings and armies.
- No language barriers! Talk to anyone freely with our real-time in chat translation in 34 different languages.
- Frequent events with FREE gifts and engaging activities.

lAmesl , 03/10/2021

Chess Not Checkers

Honestly this game can be a lot of fun if you focus on the right things. Never compare your base to the next. We all work/play at out own pace.

Some helpful tips....

Shield Maintenance is the biggest issue I see with new and old members. CHECK your Shield!!!!

Do endless events..... Helps keep you afloat

Save hero energy. It is the most valued thing in the game. Everything is based upon monsters.

Try joining a Hunting alliance. This will help you grow without spending. Gives speed ups for training troops, building and research. Also Gold.
Save gold you will need it later.

Starting out work on hero/trap/economy research. This will help you in the long run. Don’t waste your scripts. Save them as you grow and spend on the research needed.

Figure out what kind of account you want. Trap, filler or lead. Leads cost a lot and so can bigger traps. So many ways to play. Just find one that suits you and have fun. Its a GAME!! Please have fun.

Playing small accounts under SH15 can be fun too. They make great hospital traps. Again have fun and build your account the way you want.

peas in my corn , 10/13/2019

Confessions of an addict

Just don’t do it. It starts out as a fun strategy game. You’re building up your base under the illusion you can compete and win on tactics. You join or start an alliance, get to know people and are having a great time. It’s consuming your days now, so much fun you can’t set it down. Every chance you get, you’re checking your timers..hmm I think I’ll buy this $1.99 pack and finish the base so I can whoop some A! Then you realize everyone else is waaaaaaay past that $2 pack and buying packs at $100 a pop. Most “good” players easily have $1,000+ into it. Some players have even spent in the 10’s of thousands. NOT KIDDING. I easily had 2 grand in this game by the time I quit. Now, at $2,000 I was good. And was also good at the strategy of the game where I could compete with the guys that had 5+grand in it...but who’s really winning here. We’re talking about silliness in the amount of money you’ll blow. BTW-same review goes for Mobile Strike and Game of War. This game is where MS and GoW players came when those got too expensive. Then it turned into the same thing. Steer clear.
