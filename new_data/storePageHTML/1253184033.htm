Description

Help and support for your technology is just a tap away!



The AT&T ProTech app provides quick access to your AT&T Protect Advantage and/or AT&T HomeTech Protection benefits. If you’re having tech issues, you can chat with our team of trained support experts. Plus, you can access digital support tools, manage repairs, and even file a claim with Asurion straight from the app. We make it easy to get the tech help you need, right when you need it.



Here are the benefits you’ll have at your fingertips:

• Connect with a ProTech expert in seconds

• Easily find your protection plan benefits

• Track device repairs and replacements

• Troubleshoot issues with your mobile device and home technology

• Enjoy periodic checkups to improve your mobile device’s signal strength, speed, and battery life (available only to Protect Advantage customers)

• Get the most out of your technology with proactive tips for device setup, OS upgrades, and other relevant topics.



For continuous improvement of the ProTech app, real-time user analytics and crash reports may be collected.
