Description

The easiest way to pause your camera while shooting video. Edit your videos and save or share it with friends! Looking forward to your reviews!

Features‌
+ 'Pause' button for video recording
+ Video Editor: Trim and Cut your videos
+ 4K support
+ Black&white, sepia, hipster and other filters
+ Filters are updated from the cloud
+ Add filters to your iCloud videos
+ Social networks support: YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, VK, Flickr, Tumblr
+ Cloud services support: Evernote, Dropbox, One
+ Classic: E-mail, WhatsApp or SMS!
+ Video length without limits

Pay for everything

I needed to use this app for my university project. It worked good while recording but after recording you notice that you have to pay for <Literally> everything. It has a watermark near the center of the screen that cost $2 to remove, then if you want to edit the video you have to pay another $2, and to save the video to your phone you have to pay another $2 for medium, high, and original quality. The lowest quality is pretty blurry. Also when I paid to remove the watermark it gave me an option to unlock all features for 50% off (3.99) but after I paid the $2 removing the water mark the cost to unlock everything went to 4.99. Thankfully I only had to use this app once so congratulations on the 2 dollars you made off me
