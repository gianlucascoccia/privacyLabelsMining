Description

ECOVACS HOME debuts! With awesome connected features, our latest App allows you to control your DEEBOT anytime, anywhere, and takes your cleaning experience to a new level.

By connecting to your DEEBOT, you can:
• Start, pause, or stop cleaning
• Set a regular cleaning schedule
• Set voice report, suction power, and Do-Not-Disturb time*
• Receive notifications from your Wi-Fi enabled robot*
• Share DEEBOT with your friends through multiple accounts*
• Receive software and firmware updates*
• Access instruction manuals, video tutorials and FAQs, and contact customer service

And you can do much more with your advanced mapping DEEBOT (powered by Smart Navi™ Technology):
• Set up Virtual Boundary™ to create no-go zones*
• Use Custom Cleaning to customize any cleaning area you want*
• View real-time stats from the visual map of your home, areas cleaned, and cleaning time*
• Adjust water flow level when DEEBOT is mopping (Robots with mopping function only)*
*Features vary with models. Go to ecovacs.com to see detailed features of your model.

Plus, you can control your DEEBOT with simple commands through Amazon Alexa and Google Home**.
**Smart Home commands are available only in some countries/regions.

Requirements:
Wi-Fi with 2.4 GHz or 2.4/5 GHz mixed band support only
Mobile device with Apple iOS 10.0 or later

Need help? Visit ecovacs.com for more information or to contact our customer service team.

IWA TULIP , 03/01/2021

Good kit slightly marred by poor software maintenance

I have had my two DEEBOT 900s for almost a year. Initial installation was a headache partly because of poor documentation but the enthusiastic support techs more than made up for that.
To be honest I am not sure whether the software is entirely the problem that still plagues my installation. It seems to be a compatibility issue caused by one bot being a little older than the other. This means I cannot use the same OS for both. I must use Home for one and 2017 for the other. The Home installation is much more buggy and freezes all the time. On average I have to reinstall the entire system every month if I want to use it regularly. Irritating.
Conclusion? Great value for money - they work well cleaning a mostly stone floor around 185 sq metres. The dust box needs regular emptying and you have to check it manually because the “box full” function does not work. So if you don’t mind the challenge of the software it is a good buy. By the way they updated the manual so it is easier to use now.

Hhinkll , 06/09/2021

Excellent vacuum/mop

Have been overall satisfied with the evac. Does the same job as competitive bots i have used but mop is very cool and convenient in one bot.
Sometimes a little finicky when moving the charger to another floor. Doesn’t seem to recognize the saved map, but not a big deal as it maps out new or adjusts when finally recognizing saved map. Small dust bin but hasn’t presented a problem, easy to clean. Smart design with cleaning tool storage on the bot. Wish the charger had a mount for the mopping plate when not in use.
I wet the mop pad as the water spray is light even on the highest setting which seems to help the mopping quality but it is still very streaky. More water spray is needed. Larger water cartridge would be ideal. I do wish it had a sensor to stop the vac and alert you to when the water is depleted so you can refill or choose to continue. Right now it keeps going so if you’re doing an entire floor you may not know where it ran out. Tracking that on the map would be cool.
App is good, room maps are great. Run the vac without mop for initial mapping run as rugs and carpets drag the mop plate. Ability to customize room features is key to avoid rugs and carpets when mopping plate is installed. We put chairs on top of counters so job is unobstructed but the vac does a good job of recognizing obstacles and avoiding, like my dog.
Would recommend the evovac and will most likely upgrade when new features improve the bot.
