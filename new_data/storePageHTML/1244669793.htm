Pyk , 12/27/2019

Neat

But it feels poorly designed and cheaply made. You might want to consider not putting the most important button in the entire app behind the bar at the bottom of the screen so it’s impossible to press. Makes it feel like you haven’t even tested your own app. The entire social part is pointless and I would pay to turn it off. If you can’t create your own good looking visual style, just stick with the built in ui elements. They look fine and you don’t have to spent extra effort “designing” your own. And for the love of god stop changing the orientation of my display. I’m sitting still, the iPad is still and parallel to the ground and no other apps have this issue. It happens when the iPad is perfectly stationary on a stand on a table. If you are going to allow an orientation, make the entire app support those orientations. So many of the issues I have with this app are iOS programming 101 (and common sense). It is beyond me why anyone would program something this way. It feels like malware. The only reason I find the app interesting at all is the unique motion you can add to photos that I haven’t seen in any other apps. Maybe create a separate simple app that does that and nothing else.
