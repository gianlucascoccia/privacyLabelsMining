Description

Simple and easy to use, professional video editor to Crop, Cut & Trim , Flip & Rotate Videos.

Crop Video is an advanced video editor with professional cropper, cutter, and add filters, backgrounds, effects and musics, and many other adjustments to create fantastic videos.

FEATURES
1. Crop the interesting area of videos to many aspect ratios
2. Background: filled with Gaussian & mosaic blur effects, color backgrounds, and 30+ popular stylish background images
3. No crop: post best size of videos to Instagram and Facebook without cropping
4. Trim&Cut: Cut out any unwanted part of videos
5. Adjust: Change temperature, brightness, contrast of videos
6. Filter: 30+ awesome filter to enhance your videos
7. Add ambient music from our library to videos
8. Add iTunes music to videos and extract audio from other videos
9. Share videos to Instagram, Facebook & Messengers, TikTok, or Email

With Crop Video - Video Cropper App, use awesome filters, blur backgrounds, stunning images, pattern borders to create and share high resolution output to Instagram and Facebook without cropping.

+ Best support in iPhone 12 & 12 Pro Max, And also other iPhone Devices.
+ Also Compatible in latest iOS 14.

The snap is 3 pixels, but sometimes 2. You can’t set the margin to 625 for instance. It also doesn't move unless you move at a certain speed, so if you're trying to carefully crop something precisely, then you can’t because you have to quickly move your finger ON TOP of the snap feature. This makes trying to crop both margins carefully if you have to do both even harder.
