Description

Send Fax in 3 Easy Steps:
1. Open App
2. Select Images or Take Pictures
3. Press Send Fax

Receive Fax in 3 Easy Steps:
1. Open App
2. Select Receive Fax Number
3. Waiting for Receive Fax

KEY FEATURES:

Send FAX:
- Fax Photos and Images: Use images from Camera Roll or shoot a photo from scratch
- Fax any paper documents: contracts, invoices, notes and so on
- Send Fax App in 100+ international countries;
- Preview and edit fax before send it
- Export any fax to PDF and JPEG files

Receive FAX:
- Select Fax Number in the US, CA
- Share Received faxes via Email, Gmail ...
- Each fax received is stored as a PDF that you can email wherever you want.

WT Kite , 06/29/2020

This app has to be a joke

In the first sentence in the description of this app is the question: “Have to work from home?” I bought this app because I do have to work from home and I do not have a landline installed, so I thought this application would give me the tools to receive and print faxes. Receiving the fax worked well, I was even notified quickly of the received fax, but I cannot print the fax. I have an iPad Pro with the latest iPadOS installed. When I click on the box with an up arrow that should give me multiple options, including print, nothing happens. I wrote the support team before 2:00 pm and it is now almost 8:00 pm and I still have not received a reply to my request for help. I really wish I had chosen one of the many other fax programs rather than this one. Almost any program can have issues, but there needs to be a support staff that will respond within a couple hours to your needs.
