Description

Roomie - College Roommate Finder

Roomie is the easiest way to find a roommate, whether it is at your university or nearby to you! It is free to download and use, but you might find it beneficial to upgrade to Roomie+ for some pro features. We give you a feed of people to scroll through so you can easily pick your roommate and start chatting with them. Download Roomie for iPhone to find the perfect college roommate & best friend!

Tap the heart to like someone, or tap the red x if you're not interested in talking to them. Two mutual right swipes will result in a match! It is at this point that you can start chatting with someone. You can also tap the blue and white message button to instantly send someone a message after watching a short advertisement. Don't worry! If you accidentally dislike someone, you can tap the yellow undo button to undo your selection.

If you don't want to wait for a match you can upgrade to Roomie+ to enjoy unlimited Instant Chats and other pro features.
Roomie plus also gives you access to an ad free experience and boosts your profile to the top of the feed.

Want to appear first in the feed? Use a boost which is valid for 24 hours.

Easily filter by gender to prevent people of another gender from talking to you.

If somebody is spamming you, feel free to report or block them.

Have a suggestion on how we could improve the app? Contact via email (support@amplesftwr.com) or through the app!
