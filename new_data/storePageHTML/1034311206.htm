** THE DARE APP **

The DARE app is an evidence based training program to help people overcome anxiety, panic attacks, worry, and insomnia. You can track your progress daily with the mood journal. Based on the best selling book ‘DARE’ that helps people overcome anxiety and panic attacks fast.

Take the app with you wherever you go. Almost every anxious situation is addressed including driving, flying, eating out, health anxiety, intrusive thoughts, public speaking, at the gym, at the doctors, going on holidays and many more.

Download the free audio guides to help you feel like your old confident self again. Included are also a collection of meditations and sleep guides to tackle insomnia.

If you suffer from anxiety or panic attacks, DARE will teach you an effective evidence based approach to get you back living life to the fullest.
