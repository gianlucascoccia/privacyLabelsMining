Great game, recycles too much

This is a really fun game. Bite size levels, simple yet involves strategy, fun cosmetic upgrade system, and in my case, easy to compete with my wife to see who’s in the higher level.
Unfortunately, around level 75 the level designs start getting recycled. Not similar, exactly the same. After level 100 you won’t see a new level design.
To put this in perspective, each level generally takes about 90 seconds, sometimes less.
In one case, I beat a level and then played the exact same level design 3 levels later. Also, the cosmetic upgrade system, while fun, runs out too quickly. By level 200 I had everything bought except the 3 most expensive heads.
Similarly, they have a landscape upgrade that randomly changes the cosmetics every time it’s purchased, but after about the 10th time it repeats and recycles all of the older designs. Fun, but it’s best just to find one you like and stick with it.
Finally, it’s worth saying that there’s a lot of ads. Clearly this game was meant to be downloaded by a large amount of people and only be played for 75 or so levels. However, they’re only 5 second or so ads, and you can just go on airplane mode if you want to skip them, so not a big deal to me.

TL;DR: super fun, perfectly fits the niche “on the toilet” game, but suffers from recycled levels later on and not enough upgrades to keep you invested after about level 75.
That said, I’m on level 375 and clearly still enjoying it :)

Despite the repetitiveness of the game, I love it! I usually hate the type of games that have the same goal with slightly different results but this is one that has me hooked for sure.
