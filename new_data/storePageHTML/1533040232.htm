Description

The only karaoke app with 3D sound effects! Duet any song, enjoy endless effects and much more!

Love to sing? Get ready to experience Karaoke in a whole new dimension!
MiXiT offers first in the world music effects for you to customize and share a unique singing performance. Explore singing in 3D audio, editing duets with your favorite artists and mixing it up with audio and visual effects.
Select your favorite hits from MixiT’s huge catalogue or upload your own songs and let MixiT turn them into Karaoke. Use a vocal guide and scrolling lyrics to make your performances shine!
Download MiXiT’s special promotion now to sing for free!

FEATURES:
Sing any song from your genre of choice. Love songs, pop, rock, musicals, k-pop - we have it all!
Record video or audio. Use a cappella effects, sing solo or create a duet with your favorite stars.
Use rolling lyrics and a vocal guide to make sure you nail your performance!
Create and customize duets with top artists.
Take your voice to a new musical dimension with our first-in-the-world 3D sound filters (also known as 8D)! Make your voice move around as if you’re in a concert. Control the movement of the drums, bass and any other instrument. Music is in the palm of your hand!
Edit your audio with cool effects such as: reverb, speaker, echo, a capella, chipmunk, yodel etc.
Use cool looks and filters to give your music video that extra special touch.
Use our state of the art vocal remover/ karaoke maker to turn any song in your library to quality Karaoke. All other main features will still work, so you can explore making duets and 3D magic with your own songs!
Share your amazing singing performances on social media - youtube, tiktok, facebook, snapchat, instagram, twitter, whatsApp etc.
