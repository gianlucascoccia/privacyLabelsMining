Description

Will guide you how to solve your Calculus homework and textbook problems, anytime, anywhere.

FX Calculus Solver is a comprehensive math software, based on an automatic mathematical problem solving engine,
and ideal for students preparing term math exams, ACT, SAT, and GRE:

- Over 1,000 sample math problems and fully animated solution steps
- Scientific calculator supported
- Graphing calculator supported
- Automatic problem solving and generation of fully animated step-by-step procedures for problems typed in by users
- User friendly math problem expression editor (WYSIWYG mode)

Math Solver covers problems at the level of College Algebra, Pre-Calculus and Calculus courses:

- Matrix
- Equations, system of equations
- Inequalities

- Function

- Graphing

- Limit

- Differentiation

- Integration
