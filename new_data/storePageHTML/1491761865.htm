Description

Get yourself a new private phone number for texting and 20 FREE CREDITS right here and now. No contract or provider needed.

Texter is a private phone number app specifically designed to protect the sender's privacy. It is a simple and inexpensive way for those who need a disposable number to send text messages around the world. No contracts, transparent fees, absolute privacy.

NO HASSLES
Our numbers come without a contract. You don't need a cell phone plan or another device to use your second phone number.

SIMPLE INSTRUCTIONS
Using your second phone number with Texter is super-simple. Purchase a number, recharge credits, and start messaging.

TRANSPARENT FEES
Texter messaging fees are as transparent as possible. By using the in-app Tariff calculator, you can see how much it costs to send a message to other countries.

UNRESTRICTED OWNERSHIP
Purchase and keep your number for as long as you need or dispose of it anytime.

GLOBAL COVERAGE
Send messages to anywhere in the world for a fraction of the price.

NO ADS
The app doesn't bother you with any advertisement and never shares any of your data. No exceptions!

Use Texter in everyday situations:

• Buying or selling things on Craigslist, eBay, or other e-commerce platforms
• Dating
• Hosting apartments on Airbnb
• Using Uber
• Applying for a job
• Working (e.g. as a second work number)
...and much more

Phone numbers available in: USA, Canada, United Kingdom.

PRICING
Texter is free to download and comes with 20 free credits to send messages. Phone numbers and additional credits are available through subscriptions and in-app purchases.

Our standard Subscription Options for phone numbers are weekly, monthly and yearly.

If you choose to get one of our plans then your payment will be charged to iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase.
Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period. The cost of the renewal depends on your Subscription Plan.

Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user's Account Settings after purchase.
When canceling a subscription, your subscription will remain active until the end of the period. Auto-renewal will be disabled but the current subscription will not be refunded.

IMPORTANT: Messages use prepaid credits. Area code availability varies. May not work with SMS shortcode services. Not for 911 or other emergency services.
