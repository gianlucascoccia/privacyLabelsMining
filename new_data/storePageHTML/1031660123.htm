Description

Results start with Beachbody® On Demand, the only app that gives you access to world-class workouts, simple nutrition guidance, and buddies to support you—all in one place.

In just a few taps, you can stream over a thousand workouts of every style and for every fitness goal. You’ll also find easy-to-follow eating plans and nutrition programs, plus tools to track your progress. And to make sure you reach your goals, BODgroups will connect you to a Coach and your very own workout community who can help keep you accountable and motivated.

FIND THE RIGHT PROGRAM FOR YOU
● Many workouts take 30 mins or less and require minimal or no equipment
● Select from 1,400+ strength-training, cardio, yoga, dance, mixed martial arts, and other workouts designed to fit your goals and preferences
● Modifiers demonstrate easier versions of the moves in real time
● Access complete fitness programs or mix and match your favorite workouts to make a program all your own

WORKOUT ANYWHERE, ANYTIME
● Stream workouts via iPhone, iPad, Apple TV, Roku and many other devices
● Select the streaming and download qualities that fit your needs
● Download videos and workouts even when you’re offline

GET BETTER RESULTS, TOGETHER USING BODGROUPS
● Join a group, where you can connect with others looking to get fit and healthy too
● Get positive support from your Coach and workout buddies to help you stay on track
● Log your daily workouts, nutrition, Shakeology, water intake, as well as weight and measurements with built-in tracking tools

ACCESS PREMIUM NUTRITION PROGRAMS
● Discover proven strategies to help support sustainable weight loss in the 2B Mindset and Ultimate Portion Fix program videos
● Complement your fitness routine with customized nutrition plans, the latest recipes, meal prep tips and more
● Note that some enhanced premium nutrition content and functionality is available with additional purchase
