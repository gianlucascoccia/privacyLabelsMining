Description

Followers for Instagram Followers - Powerful, fast, accurate followers tracker, analytics, insight for Instagram!

Track your followers, unfollowers, ghost followers, stalkers,... Find out WHO not following you back.


 FEATURES:

- Followers tracker lost/gained
- Track following users that don't follow you back
- Track followers that you don't follow back
- Track post’s performance and engagement
- Get likes, post, media reports & charts
- Track all your accounts in a single app - support of multiple accounts
- Insta followers cleaner, mass unfollow instagram unfollowers

Who is ghost follower?
Who do you like most?
Find your top followers, stalker

Quick, easy and reliable, get all analytics in one click and keep track of your profile anywhere and anytime. Gain insights on your followers helps you know how to optimize a post (best and worst time to post, the topic your followers like most…). Analyze, optimize and get on top!

For complete access to all Followers+ features, you will:
+ You must have an Instagram account to use this app
+ Allow the app access to Instagram account – so that you can have a comprehensive analysis of your Instagram account and post's performance.
