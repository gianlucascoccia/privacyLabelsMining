Dash Catch'm , 08/12/2021

Fun game, small problem

I’ve only had this game for about a week now and it’s a lot of fun! I love the puzzles and how you find stuff. However, I’ve had this problem where objects I need, for example the key in the owl train level, are too far down on the screen and, whenever I try to get them or move them, I just end up tapping or moving the circles that show what I need to collect. I’d suggest lowering the list of items to collect or making a way to temporarily minimize the list so that you can reach objects lower on the screen, however, I’m not a game developer so if there’s a better or easier solution, I’d be fine with whatever you come up with as long as it fixes the problem. Other than that, this is an excellent game and I definitely suggest trying it if you’re into solving puzzles and finding things hidden in the environment.

67sadypants , 09/04/2020

Not updated frequently enough

I enjoy finding the hidden items, in fact this is one of the best games of its type and I love that it’s not timed so I can take my time finding things and I don’t have to stress that I’m running out of time.
However, releasing one new game a day is ridiculous.
I went through Detective and story mode in about a week then I got one new game per day unless I wanted to watch an ad.
I definitely do NOT want to watch an ad to get another game when I paid to have the adverts removed.
I do not enjoy the Discover portion of this because it’s clearly designed so you buy guesses which would be fine if they were guesses you would have a hope of finding yourself, but these are INSANE. Who would guess that you have to plug in your phone or hold your phone upside down whilst dancing a jig and enhancing the picture with 3 fingers?!
It’s that ridiculous.
I’m not a fan of the Just Find it portion of this game either but mostly because it’s timed and the pictures can sometimes look nothing like what they asked you to find.
This would still be an awesome app if they took the time to update all of the games more than once a day and didn’t try to bait me into watching adverts that I paid to have removed in order to get another game, but as it is now, I’m feeling pretty meh about it and will not be recommending this to friends.
