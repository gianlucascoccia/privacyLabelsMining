Description

Banking done right. Enjoy zero fees, high-yield savings options, and affordable credit, all within your One account. Save, spend, borrow and share money with the One app.

At One, you can easily spend, save, borrow, and share from a single account—for free! We’ve simplified banking with:
• One card
• One app
• One account

Banking services provided by Coastal Community Bank; Members FDIC. One is a financial technology company, not a bank.

Every One account can...
• Manage money with no fees and no minimums.
• Earn 1.00% APY* – $5,000 in Save Pocket.
• Earn unlimited 3.00% APY* on One card round ups.
• Create and share free Pockets.
• Access 55,000 fee-free Allpoint ATMs.
• Pay anyone with free ACH bank transfers.
• Activate overdraft protection. (subject to approval)
• Access budgeting tools.

Add direct deposit to unlock…
• Early Paycheck**
• 3.00% APY* on up to 10% of your direct deposit (up to $1,000/month).
• Increased savings potential in Save Pocket - up to $25,000.
• Increased Credit Line. (subject to approval)

smittybaby13 , 04/30/2021

Unique banking experience

I was a skeptic at first because I’m not someone who feels completely comfortable with online banking systems. However, one finance proved me wrong 100%, everything they advertise is completely true and I wasn’t sure how the non traditional joint account would function with my husband and I, but it works perfectly. We just share each other’s pockets and we have about 4-5 different pockets and I look forward to the budgeting tools they have to offer. They give a credit line of $10 which allows you to go under by that much for emergencies and they charge 12% interest on that if it’s not paid by the end of the month which they give you a count down on your account to remind you, love it! After a few paychecks you can ask for a higher limit but be aware of 12%, although they won’t charge the standard $35/6 fees in one day for overdraft, it can be pricey if you ask for too high of a credit limit with interest. They text you when you have transactions or get paid (opt in), I was surprised to get paid 2 days early, it was around 3pm but it was still processed to count as 2 days early! I was nervous too about switching the pockets related to the main card, but I always do it right after and switch it back, love love love. Lastly, they tell you it could take 14 business days (no weekends/holidays) for the card to arrive, we did this 4/22 & got it 4/30. Thank you one finance.

Very Good Mobile Banking App

This is my first review on the App Store & felt like this was needed. One Finance deserves some more recognition as the UI & Customer Service is very top tier. K Echols , 07/14/2021

I'll say 5/5

I'm going to give One 5 stars because of their ability and willingness to quickly implement customer feedback.

I love having each bill/merchant assigned its own pocket. It's perfect for fraud protection and to stop possible overdrafts if I'm double charged. The addition of virtual cards for every pocket is awesome. This set to allows me to budget down to the exact penny (a virtual cash envelope system).

I adore my credit line. It is now higher than any of my credit card debt. Why is that awesome? As I pay off my credit card debt (woo!) I know I'll have a 0% loan and then 1% every month after (12% APR). That's HALF my credit card interest rates.

No worries on the credit card debt. I'm on a debt free journey and my pocket set up with One has eliminated me from making extra swipes just because. I have a "Frivolous" pocket that gets funded after all my bill pockets are filled.

What I'd love to see? More automation in the budget. I'd love to say these are my fixed expenses and One do the calculation for each payday contribution. I'd really love a Goals feature. "I need $15,000 by Dec 2023" and One calculate the amount per paycheck and set aside the funds.

I'd also love more built in budget features like: how much am I spending on categories (pet, restaurant, automotive)

Hopefully those things will come. That would truly make One the only bank, only ap, & only card in my wallet/phone.
