Description

Check out the #1 Rated Yoga & Meditation App with over 1,000 Guided Yoga, Meditation and Wellness Classes in taught by Top Yoga Instructors from around the world.


Download our app for Free and join the community of over 100,000 yogis who have chosen The Yoga Collective as their home yoga studio. The Yoga Collective provides you with the highest quality HD Yoga, Meditation and Wellness classes from the palm of your hand. Practice Yoga daily from the comfort of your own home or while on the go!


The Yoga Collective has guided yoga classes ranging in style, duration, focus and level (from beginner yoga to advanced yoga). Our yoga and meditation classes are perfect for everyone from athletes to workers stuck at a desk.


If you are a Beginner we have great resources to start your yoga journey. Check out our Yoga for Beginners section and take the Beginner Yoga series. We will walk you step-by-step from your first Down Dog to your Warrior Flows with clear and thoughtful instruction.


Customize your class to your mood and needs using our advanced search filters:

• Level: Beginner to Advanced

• Times: 5, 10, 20, 30, 40 & 60 minutes

• Style: Power, Vinyasa, Yin, Restorative, etc.

• Focus: Core, Strength, Stretch, Basics, Shoulders and more


We also have custom Programs and Plans built just for you to help you reach your goals!


Check out our Collection section with Programs such as:
Mindful Meditation, Yoga For Athletes, Office Series, Journey To Handstand, 10-Minute Yoga Tone, 6 Day Core Challenge and many more...


Advanced Features Include:
• Tracking your progress on your Yoga Collections and Challenges
• Favorites: Save your favorite yoga classes to take again and again
• Recently Viewed: All of your recently viewed yoga classes in one spot
• Downloads: Download yoga classes and stay connected even when your phone is not
• Music Player: Choose background music to play with your yoga class and set the tone
• Meditation Timer: Set the timer and get your meditation on
• Daily Reminders: Customize your yoga calendar and get notified when it’s time for your yoga practice
• Meditation, Life Coaching, Yoga Philosophy, and Breathing classes
• Yoga & Wellness Blog
• Full integration with website

And more…

BDThrom , 03/15/2021

Perfect for whatever my mood

I love Yoga. But sometimes I want an easy stretchy feel good yoga and sometimes I want a no holds barred, sweaty, muscle building yoga. This app lets me pick whatever I am in the mood for. I can download my favorites and do them anywhere. It’s so easy to pick a body part to focus on or the time that I have to practice. There are so many videos that I have a wishlist of those I can’t wait to try.

The only thing I would like to see with this app is a place where I could add my notes about a workout. Some would be great for a slow wake up to full workout In the morning. Some start fast and stay that way. It would be brilliant to be able to write that out for future reference or group them into categoroes that I choose.

Overall this app is worth every penny!

Rubina65 , 06/03/2020

This app changed my life. Highly recommend

I have had this app for about a year now. I love The Yoga Collective because it’s not just classes of 1 hour flows, but they also provide videos with deep detail of every stretch. I love that you can choose a class time between 5 minutes to 2 hours. The teachers in the videos make me feel like I am at an actual class.

I also love the meditation classes. It really helps me stay focused. The fact that I have it on my phone truly helps me in times of stress, so I can just quickly find a 5-10 minute class and realign myself.

I started this app as a beginner, and I truly have learned so much from the teachers at The Yoga Collective. The Yoga Philosophy classes has given me a deeper look into what Yoga means and motivated me to take online classes!!

I am currently taking a Yoga Teaching Course to become a certified Yoga Instructor! I have always had a connection to yoga but my life has truly changed since I started taking classes on The Yoga Collective App. I truly thank all of the teachers at the Yoga Collective.

Thank you so much for guiding me. I wish there was an in person Yoga Collective school in NYC!! Xoxo Sat Nam 💫✨🌟
