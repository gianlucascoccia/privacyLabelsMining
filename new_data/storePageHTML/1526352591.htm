Description

Shotbud is a full shot timer that uses the sensors in your watch to detect and isolate your shots.


What makes Shotbud work?

• We combine audio and motion data to remove false‑positives, so Shotbud works even around other shooters!

• Run different drills by setting a par time.

• See your results and split times.

• Quickly adjust between modes using our built-in presets, including Live Fire, Dry Fire, CO2 Dry Fire, and Airsoft.

• Optionally adjust microphone, echo, and motion sensitivity.


Shotbud is free for a limited time. We will be adding more features. During this time we would especially appreciate any feedback from users.


Software Requirements:

• Shotbud is made for devices running iOS 13+ and watchOS 6+.

• Shotbud uses your watch's microphone to detect the audio of your shots.

• Shotbud integrates with the Health app on your watch to detect your heart rate and to run on older devices while the screen is off.

What’s New

Sep 1, 2021

Version 1.3

Shotbud now uses your watch’s microphone to detect and process audio – you are no longer required to have your phone with you!

Also in this version:

• Enhanced shot detection across all presets: Live Fire, Dry Fire, CO2 Dry Fire, and Airsoft
• Fixed a bug where the par time buzzer would sometimes be detected as a shot
• Improved split time detection to 0.14 seconds (from 0.22s)
• Fixed various other bugs

Works great for dry fire

This works great for dry fire practice. I haven’t spent much time with it in live fire so I can’t say how it performs on that level.

My only concern is that, when I start the delayed timer, if the watch face goes dark the audible beep and physical buzz are slightly delayed to when the timer starts counting. My times when the watch face goes dark vs when it’s on are longer by about 0.25 seconds. I’m not sure if there is a way to force the watch face to stay on in that moment (I’m running a series 3 fully updated for reference), but if there is a way to keep me from having to keep my hand raised while I wait for the beep it would greatly improve my experience.
