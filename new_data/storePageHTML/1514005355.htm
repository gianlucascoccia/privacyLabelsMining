Description

Eliminate 95% of spam text messages with our A.I powered Smart Blocking. TextKiller is the text-blocking app created by humans and driven by AI that's powered by RoboKiller. Our blocking technology uses machine learning to filter out unwanted spam text messages. You can finally live spam-free and never miss a legitimate text message again.

Get automatic protection included out of the box. No complicated setup or configuration needed. You can customize your protection with powerful block and allow lists for both numbers and keywords. You can also filter all messages outside of your contacts.

Being able to receive legitimate text messages is just as important as blocking unwanted ones. TextKiller will never block iMessages (blue bubbles). TextKiller will never block text messages (green bubbles) from a contact. If the text message isn’t from a contact, TextKiller’s advanced protection will filter out any spam messages to your Messages “Junk” folder and leave the legitimate ones in the inbox. Basically, it works like you expect and need it to.

PREDICTIVE TEXT-BLOCKING POWER
Using Machine Learning (ML) TextKiller filters all spam text messages with unbelievable accuracy.

24/7 UP-TO-DATE PROTECTION
New spammers and spam messages are continuously added to our global spam radar so you aren’t bothered by unwanted text messages.

TextKiller is powered by RoboKiller which has been featured on The New York Times, NBC, Wired, Engadget, Vice, and other publications.

TextKiller is built by people who hate robocalls, spam texts, and respect your privacy. We promise to never sell your data, spam you, or waste your time. Our mission is to stop spam text messages forever, or die trying!

FEATURING:
• Automatic spam text protection - eliminate spam and scam text messages automatically
• Take control of who can and can’t text - choose the phone numbers and keywords you wish to block, and allow the ones you want to hear from
