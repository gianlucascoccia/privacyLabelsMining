Description

iPersia Calendar is the best Persian calendar on the app store with every feature imaginable for a calendar. Our team continues to work hard to give you the best user experience. Below are some of the amazing features of this application,

- Supports both English and Farsi
- 2020 International Holidays (USA, UK, Canada, and more)
- 1399 Persian Events and Holidays
- Daily View
- Monthly View
- Annual Glance
- Dates in Persian, Islamic, and Gregorian Calendars
- Date Conversion between Persian, Islamic, and Gregorian Calendars
- Add one-time or recurring personal events with two alerts
- Display personal events in their corresponding calendar colors
- Option to select the calendar type for personal events
- Birthdays recorder and reminder
- Search birthdays
- Work schedule recorder
- Search for personal events, personal notes, and national events
- Option to display national holidays and Fridays in red
- Pray times based on your last observed location

- Option to go to any future or past month in the monthly view page
- Local sunrise and sunset times
- Daily personal note or diary recorder

- Option to auto change the calendar design monthly

- Option to select the launch page of the app

- Setting page to fully customize the calendar to your liking

Good App, but....

Overall very good and useful app. I particularly like the option of 'tabdil Tarikh'. However, I have two requests:
1- when I'm on monthly screen, and want to go back to previous months to look for any events I noted on my iPersia calendar, the screen on that particular month only stays on for 2-3 seconds and then go back to current month!! It's very annoying. It should allow user to stay on that month, and have option to go back to current month after that.
2- also. In your app, under settings, it should give user an option to contact your administration for any help and suggestions.
