Description

AeroVision is the App for the latest generation Marshall Radio GPS tracking system. This App allows you to track up to 5 remote transmitters live, in real-time simultaneously. In addition to live tracks, other metrics such as speed, distance to target, altitude, and distance traveled are also offered. Flights may also be recorded to later replay or share with friends.


AeroVision users may also share live flights to others using the new Broadcast feature, even to users without the Marshall GPS system.

AeroVision does require the Marshall PocketLink data receiver as well as RT or Turbo GPS transmitters for full operation.

AeroVision works on Pocketlink and transmitters purchased 2018 onwards and it requires 64bit Apple Hardware

iPhone 6S or newer
iPad Mini 2 or newer (cellular enabled)
iPad Air or newer (cellular enabled)
