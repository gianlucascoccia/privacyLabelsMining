Description

Stream your favorite KSL-TV shows and sporting events for FREE, anywhere at any time! No cable subscription required.

With the newly designed KSL-TV app, you'll experience:
• More of your favorite On Demand videos and multiple live stream options
• Exclusive, app-only shows and content, including the binge-worth podcast COLD: The Susan Powell Case Files
• Extensive feature content from Real Salt Lake, Utah Valley University and KSL-TV’s most popular shows
• Easier navigation puts thousands of videos and shows at your fingertips
• Up-to-the-minute forecasts for cities across Utah

Get real-time alerts and extended coverage of breaking news happening in your neighborhood.

Download the KSL-TV app now to get started!

This app is...

Love that I can watch me some RSL games on this app!! It is awesome that we can watch games that have been missed but it would be delicious if there was some sort of schedule of when you are playing previous games so that I can get on a watch when you are playing missed games and not hoping your playing the game I missed, or jumping into the middle of the missed game and have everything spoiled. Blind and buggy

Blind: For the RSL Streaming one has no idea what will be currently playing or what is up next. If you do not watch live. You then have to guess and hope you find it at the right time. Many times if you do not accidentally see the score for the game you want to watch you can calculate when the next replay will start, but which game is not known.

Icjeeps , 05/21/2018

Not working as advertised

I have an iPad Air 2. I have several issues with the app. First, every time I open the app, it will rotate from portrait mode to landscape mode, but will not stay on landscape mode. It is very touchy, and drives me crazy. Another thing that doesn’t work very well is the ability to airplay to an Apple TV. With that I have a third generation Apple TV (I think, its the one before the first Siri remote), and the screen is not a wide screen. It is only doing mirror play. While in mirror play, it will not load very fast, and slows to a crawl or just buffers.

If this is how it was designed, without these old devices then I’m sure it is working correctly. But for me and my devices we will hope for the best, and hope for some improvements in the future. I have tried it the last 3-4 RSL Games and nothing has worked.
