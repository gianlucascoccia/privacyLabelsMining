Description

Fun yoga & mindfulness videos for kids aged 3 to 9, presented by Jaime. Try free for 14 days, no ads, playlists, downloads, our full catalogue & more. Kids love to follow Jaime as she goes on yoga adventures and explains mindfulness in ways that they really get.

Now with 50+ yoga adventures including the Frozen, Star Wars, Minecraft and Moana specials, plus 17 Zen Den mindfulness sessions, 14 Peace Out guided relaxations as well as Yoga Pose Universe clips, stories and songs. Popular in schools and homes all over the world, this is healthy screen time that kids simply love. Includes every Cosmic Kids yoga and mindfulness video in a safe space, with no commercials or links. Stream our entire library - and get every new video first.

Recommended for schools! Try it free for two weeks!

Impressed

After using Cosmic Kids in my preschool classroom every day for 2 years, I was sad that they were switching platforms and to view new content, I had to pay. I spend a lot of my own money on my classroom already, so I was bummed, but I read their reasons for doing so and understood the reasoning. After thinking about it, I decided to purchase the app because we just love CCY so much. Jamie does such an amazing job, and my students are so engaged every time. It’s cold where I live, so we can’t go out and play as much as we would like—CCY is an asset for my classroom to help my students with gross motor skills, motor planning skills, listening skills, body awareness skills, and mindfulness skills. My students are focused and since starting using CCY daily, I have noticed behavior changes, increased coordination, increased stamina, and increased fun and giggles. I love that some of the new videos have the alphabet and stories we read in class-I love all the videos, but I hope CCY moves forward with these types of videos so I can make deeper connections to my teaching. Purchasing this app has been an invaluable investment for my classroom. 3, 2, 1...Namaste ❤️🧘🏻‍♀️
