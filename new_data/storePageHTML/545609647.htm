Description

The new Calendar of Spanish football is here! Download the La Liga app now and don't miss any news about the new season of Spanish soccer.

Have fun with the Official LaLiga App!

Experience all the thrills of the 2020-2021 LaLiga and Europa soccer leagues in the Official La Liga App.

Download now the "LaLiga" app to enjoy the latest soccer news and current content and always be up-to-date with all the latest information about LaLiga Santander. All the news about the Spanish League competition collected in one place so you don't miss a thing. In addition, you will be able to enjoy the highlights of each matchday once the matches are over so as not to miss the best plays and the goals of your favorite team.

Get the news, goals, highlights and reports on all the matches in the La Liga 2020 calendar. You can find news about the Santander League (First Division), LaLiga SmartBank, Copa del Rey Cup, UEFA Champions League, UEFA Europa League, and Iberdrola League (women's soccer league).

Latest Spanish and international soccer news and live goals, schedules, live results and highlights.

Enjoy the best news content about your favorite Spanish soccer teams and players: Real Madrid, FC Barcelona, Atlético Madrid, Sevilla, Messi, Bale, Griezmann, Luis Suárez... Experience the king of sports as never before using the official soccer league app.

Main features of the Official Soccer League App:

◉ Highlights of soccer matches: Find the highlights of FC Barcelona, Real Madrid, Atlético, etc. both for domestic competitions and for European competitions.

◉ FansLaLiga: Enter our fan zone and discover all the advantages of being a fan of LaLiga Santander.

◉ Schedule, sports results, standings, reports and live goals: LaLiga Santander, LaLiga SmartBank (Second Division), Copa del Rey, UEFA Champions League, UEFA Europa League, Iberdrola Women's League, and more.

◉ Live soccer info: Watch each second-to-second highlight live scores for all the soccer matches. You can follow user discussions and add comments every day on Twitter as well.

◉ “My Favorite Team” section: Personalize the app with your team's colors and details to get fast, easy access to coming and played matches, players, stats, goal scorers, social networks, soccer highlights videos, soccer reports, pre-match info, and all the news about your favorite teams.

◉ Notifications: Thanks to the Official Spanish Soccer App's push notifications, you can set up alerts about anything that happens in your favorite teams' live soccer matches, and stay up-to-date on soccer fixtures: the day's schedule, line ups, match kick-off and end times, goals scored, live results, match highlights video, red cards, substitutions, and more.
You'll also be able to configure them just the way you want, according to your favorite team and your preferences. You decide which alerts you want to receive for the best coverage of live matches.

◉ Soccer News: Receive the latest info on Spanish teams, live sports scores, players, the top scorer, national leagues, European championships, and 2020 League press releases. Watch the best pre-match info and match highlight videos and other news from the world of soccer.

◉ “Teams” section: With LaLiga app you can access everything about your favorite team. Watch up-to-date multimedia content with photos and videos; find schedules and sports scores; check the most important team and stadium info; look up the squads and team and player stats.

Get soccer on your cell phone!

Download the only official soccer league app now for free, enjoy all the best from your favorite soccer teams, and see all the soccer scores live.
