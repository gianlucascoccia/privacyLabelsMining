Description

Download and join over 7.5 million users who trust Noseeyou to protect their privacy.

Tired of trying to hide your photos? Worried your privacy might be leaked? Take control of your privacy and lock up your private photos with Noseeyou’s photo vault, secured with military-grade encryption. Keep your personal photos, files and search history secure with a PIN protection, and private browser. Noseeyou is the securest virtual safe you can find.

Enjoy these top-notch features to ensure your privacy is protected and safe:

● Secret Photo Vault
Preserve your memories easily and import images with one quick click. Enjoy unlimited storage and import more than 1,000 pictures. Our photo vault supports multiple file types and allows Wi-fi media imports enabling you to upload all your downloads: pics, videos, files, notes & recordings. Protect copies of your driver’s license, ID card, and credit card. Explore our features like blurred photos for extra privacy.

● Protection Against Intruders
Always know when someone is trying to log into your private photo vault. When someone tries to log in with a wrong pin, a screenshot is immediately taken and sent for review. An email notification is also set up if this occurs. We keep your pictures and albums safe and are committed to block all intruders.

● Fake Pin & Turn to Switch
Facing an emergency situation and being asked to share your account against your will? Enter a fake password to a fake Noseeyou account that doesn’t have your private data. Need to hand over your phone? With Noseeyou, your phone automatically switches from the secret photo vault to a Safari page when you turn it over. When it comes to extra measures to keep your personal content safe, we’ve got you covered.

● Save Space with Cloud Storage & Compression
Don’t worry about storage with our unlimited cloud storage: upload all your images, movies and files easily. Save space with our customized data compression and enjoy our reliable backup for all your files.

Just Two Problems

I really love the app, but I’ve noticed just two problems so far. First of all, when you go to enter the passcode for your account, the first button you press will sometime not register, causing you to get the passcode wrong and potentially lock yourself out of the app. This is probably an easy fix. The second problem I have with the app is the amount of features that are locked behind a paywall. I mean, I understand that you need to make a profit, but it’s a little annoying when I can’t upload more than 5 or 6 images without paying a subscription fee. How about you raise the limitation to 20, or maybe even 25? Other than that, great app.

(Edit: after writing this review, I discovered that uploading images is now a premium feature. Either that, or the picture of a cat I want to upload is too large of a file to fit in the app. It’s just a little stupid, considering that I got the app so I could have a cheap and easy way to hide my photos.)
