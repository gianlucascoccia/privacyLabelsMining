Description

My Robi – One App for All Lifestyle Needs.

Our newly updated My Robi App helps you take care of your all Robi Prepaid & Postpaid account; also connect with your all lifestyle needs - conveniently in one place, on the go and with no data charges! 
The brand new seamless interface makes for easier navigation and fuss-free management of your account, ranging from the most basic buying of internet plans and topping up your account balance, to giving you the scoop on the latest release of Movie, Music, Sports & many lifestyle deals from Robi Axiata Ltd. My Robi is not just another app, it’s an experience that connects with all your lifestyle needs under one umbrella. So, why you’re waiting for? Just Install My Robi App and bring your all favorite lifestyle apps at your fingertips.

Use the My Robi App to:
* Login with your Robi number - seamlessly without any password while you’re in our network
* One Time Password (OTP) authentication while you are in WiFi or in roaming
* Check your Robi account balance
* View simplified latest bill for Postpaid, as well as previous bills including roaming bills
* Quick Recharge with simple, easy steps
* Buy Internet Packs or Social Packs within clicks
* Create your own plan using MyPlan App
* Buy Bundle, Voice Pack & Goon Goon
* Manage your FnF & VAS seamlessly
* Balance Transfer as a gift to your dearest one
* Recharge & Bill History
* Usage History -Track your Call, SMS and Internet Charges
* Send us your complaint & Connect with Robi Customer Experience Team
* Locate your nearest Robi Shop
* Join Robi Communities
* Robi World – a appSuite for all Lifestyle App like Music, Movie, Sports, Utility Bill Payment, TV
* And many more!

Standard data charges will apply for using other available Robi Apps/portal from My Robi
