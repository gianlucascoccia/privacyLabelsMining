Description

Enter Lightgliders to experience a multiplayer online game with exciting missions, virtual community, and activities that encourage faith and values! Play fun games and watch videos to level up your Lightglider and unlock more to do in the Academy. Attend in-game events to meet new friends and to get exclusive clothing, hats, and other items to customize your character. Learn how you were made to shine with Bible-based activities updated each week. Ask your parents to get Lightgliders for your computer, tablet, or smartphone today!

PARENTS: Lightgliders is a game world of Christian faith and fun for kids and families. A subscription to Lightgliders gives your kids a safe, fun, faith-based alternative for their screentime with biblical content updated each week. But that’s not all! Lightgliders also sends you weekly resources to encourage meaningful conversations, helping you connect more deeply with your kids.

OUR MISSION: to encourage faith, character, and service into kids before their teenage years and to be a strategic partner for parents and churches worldwide.

OUR PROMISE: No negative content. No ads. No in-game purchases or micro-transactions.

NEW TO LIGHTGLIDERS? Discover a world of adventure by becoming a Lightglider. Choose an individual (1 child) or family (up to 5 kids) plan and start your 7-day free trial today! Create your character(s) and begin exploring! Your subscription will begin automatically at the conclusion of the free trial. Cancel trial anytime to avoid charges.

RETURNING TO LIGHTGLIDERS? Welcome back! We saved all your progress! Simply log in and follow the steps to reactivate your account, and you’ll pick up right where you left off!

Lightgliders was created by Kingdom Scene Endeavors. To learn more, visit www.lightgliders.com or contact us at info@lightgliders.com - we’d love to help!

FEATURES:
- Thousands of character creation and customization options

The Lightgliders experience for all mobile devices has received a major overhaul! Play fun games and explore the virtual community inside Lightglider Academy like never before. Exciting updates include:
