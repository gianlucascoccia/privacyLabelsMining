GamingGuineaPig , 07/12/2019

Wow! The only sticker maker I could find that actually let you make stickers!

This works very well and I’ve been having a ton of fun making funny and disgusting stickers to annoy my friends and it is awesome. Of course it isn’t just for people like me who use it for bad reasons. You could make some really awesome stickers with it if you wanted to and I can’t express enough how cool it is to do. The only thing though is a lack of options while actually making the sticker. I think their should be a few toggles available while making the sticker that would be very convenient to have such as the ability to temporarily make whatever is cut out transparent just so you could better see what exactly is in and out of the sticker or to find something you missed. Also I want to be able to move stickers from one pack to another if I want to. But aside from those things, I am really enjoying this app!
