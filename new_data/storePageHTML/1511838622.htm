Description

Boost your birth-to-five year old's speech and language development! Built for parents and caregivers of early language learners, from preverbal through full sentences.

SpeakEasy is trusted by thousands of Speech Therapists as the leading app for early language development.

With SpeakEasy, we empower YOU to boost the speech and language development of your early language learner (birth-to-five).

You'll get fun at-home activities and effective teaching techniques you can use to help move your child from preverbal to their first words, onto combining words, and into speaking full sentences. With SpeakEasy, you and your child get language, delivered daily.


For parents and caregivers of early language learners with or without speech delay

SpeakEasy is built for early childhood language development. We like to think in stage, rather than age: this app is for you if your child is preverbal, using first words, combining words, or using full sentences.

We're here for all caregivers who want to focus on the speech and language development of their child, whether or not they have a speech delay or are currently receiving therapy.

If you're an SLP or other early childhood professional, we've got a special version of the app for you too.


At home language learning

Young children spend most of their time at home with their family. That's why all speech language pathologists (SLPs) know the best place to learn language is in the comfort of your home.

We're founded by an SLP, and more than 2000 SLPs trust our content. That's why we're working with SLPs as a complement to their services, to help teach you exactly how to boost your own child's language development.


Customized to your child: start your Personalized Language Journey

Language learning is a journey. Our guided experience will take you step by step through your child's own Personalized Language Journey based on the needs of you and your early talker.


Caregiver focused: you become the speech and language expert

While screens have a time and place in every home, we believe when caregivers like you interact directly with your child, you have the biggest impact on their education and development. That's why our app is focused on empowering the caregiver, and why we don't have an app where you hand your phone over to your child.


Skills Tracker, Learning Labs, Routines, and more

Beyond fun activities, effective teaching techniques, and a Personalized Language Journey, we have many more features to help you and your child communicate more often and more effectively.

-Know exactly what you should be working on with your child at their language development stage with personalized goals.
-Track your child's development with the SpeakEasy Skills Tracker.
-Do activities as part of your existing routines, including playtime, mealtime, bathtime, bedtime, and seasonal specials.
-Read hot speech and language development topics in Learning Labs: "Does being bilingual cause speech delay?" - "How much screen time is too much?"
-And more!


How does the app work exactly?

1. Receive daily activities that teach you language learning techniques
2. Do activities during your existing routines
3. Earn points, level up, and unlock new stuff
4. Advanced on your child's Language Journey


SpeakEasy Basic is FREE! Then, unlock even more with SpeakEasy Unlimited

We have plenty of free content for you, including new activities available every month. If you love the app (and we know you will), then take it to the next level by subscribing to SpeakEasy Unlimited.
