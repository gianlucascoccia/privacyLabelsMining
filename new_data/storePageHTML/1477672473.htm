Description

Tongan hymns from the Free Wesleyan Church
• select hymns by number, by first line, or search for keywords
• all hymns with variations, including some special additions; 858 total
• view as in the book (Tongan hymns, © Friendly Islands bookshop, 2019)
• view tu`ungafasi music notation + lyrics
• view tu`ungafasi music notation + lyrics on tiny iphone screens
• view lyrics only
• play the music with optional karaoke lyrics
• play in silence for when the congregation is singing
• play selected notes in some voices for choir practice
• no in-app purchases, no advertisements
• available languages (interface): Tonga, English
• can force use of Tongan language only, whatever settings
• dark mode on macos 14
