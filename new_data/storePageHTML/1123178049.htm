Description

Cheddar gives you access to all the quick updates on trending news stories in business, politics, tech, markets and culture you could want.

With the Cheddar application, you will receive:

• Daily weekday updates from the stock exchange floor
• Exclusive interviews with top tier CEOs like: John Legere of T-Mobile, Mark Fields of Ford, Spencer Rascoff of Zillow, and John Oringer of Shutterstock
• The company and startup profiles of companies you care about like: Facebook, Uber, Airbnb, and Snapchat
• Business advice straight from the experts and founders
