Description

Boutiqaat is the Largest Social E-Commerce Platform in the Middle East for Men & Women, connecting them with premier Luxury Fashion & Beauty Brands. It is also the sole platform with a unique concept of linking Middle East’s most influential celebrities to their fan base.

Shop a hand curated collection of Fashion, Makeup, Perfumes, Footwear, Sportswear, Accessories and Sunglasses that includes 700+ top international brands, local and exclusive in-house brands. That’s not all…you can discover personal recommendations from Middle East’s most loved celebrities & Fashionistas and shop their selections at a click of a button!

In addition to this, we create & curate specialized content to provide a seamless shopping experience across mobile, tablet and desktop, through our Boutiqaat App, website, YouTube, Instagram & Snapchat.

Why we love Boutiqaat Mobile App:
• Discover latest & trendiest styles within Fashion, Beauty & Accessories and, shop a wide variety of 30,000+ products from 700+ premium international brands including ANASTASIA, LIMECRIME, BENEFIT, MAKE UP FOR EVER, SHISEIDO, THE BALM, HUDA BEAUTY, NIKE, ASICS & REEBOK.
• Explore 400+ exclusive partnerships with the region’s most powerful celebrities and influencers.
• Choose from the widest spread of products ranging from Apparel, Sports, Footwear, Bags & Leather Goods, Makeup, Bath & Body, Hair Care, Skincare, Contact Lenses, Phone Acessories, Arabic Fashion, Arabic Fragrances, Oriental perfumes, Bakhour, Sunglasses, Watches, Fashion Tech, Lifestyle and more.
• Catch up on 1500+ Makeup tutorials, product reviews, and Fashion & Beauty tips by celebrities on Boutiqaat TV.
• Guaranteed 100% genuine products, secure payments, cash on delivery and delivery across GCC including Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, Qatar, UAE and Oman.

Never miss out on any of our updates, sign up and enable notifications to catch up on the latest trends, exclusive new launches and new arrivals! Make sure to check out Boutiqaat’s social media handles on Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, Twitter & Snapchat for fun behind-the-scene shooting sessions!
