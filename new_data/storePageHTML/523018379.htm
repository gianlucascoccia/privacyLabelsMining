Description

As seen on Bar Rescue and the official age verification app of MillerCoors!

Use the camera of your iPhone, iPod or iPad to scan barcode IDs.

Protect your bar, nightclub, event, casino, dispensary or retail establishment from fake IDs and underage patrons while getting smarter about your customers. BCS created the best age verification solution on the market that reads drivers licenses from all 50 states and all Canadian provinces.

BCS is not your standard ID scanning software. We help you analyze customer information and let you access it on our website. You can see patron flow over the night, week or month; compare different time periods; view gender splits on different nights; the most popular age ranges and average age; most popular zip codes, etc. Figure out if your marketing and advertising campaigns are working.

An additional option allows you to add photos to each scan, after all a picture is worth a thousand words. Newly added it the ability to add notes to each scan, you can any additional information that may be helpful to your business.

Using our Questionnaire option you can gather additional information on your customers at the time of scan. From our website you can design a wide range of custom forms/surveys to ask each customer after you scan their id.
