Description

Keep track of your watersport performance! Waterspeed helps you collect key stats such as speed, angle, pace, distance, heart rate while providing wind and tide forecasts

Whether you Sail, SUP, Windsurf, Kitesurf, Kayak, Foil, or any other watersport, Waterspeed turns your iPhone or Apple Watch into a performance tracking device by using your GPS. Stats and activities are shown in real-time and collected within a diary after each session, ready to be analyzed.

Designed for iOS devices, Waterspeed presents an independent app for Apple Watch, allowing you to keep your phone out of the water.

Waterspeed also includes a countdown so you can accurately track race starts and regattas.

* Designed for Sailing, Windsurf, Kite, Paddle SUP, Kayak, Powerboat, Jet Ski, Surf, Outrigging, Windfoil, Waterskiing, Wakeboard, Rowing, Kitefoil, Hydrofoil, Foil, Wing, Canoe, Dragon, Pedal, Jet Surfing, Surfski, Strapless, Tiki

* You can check the weather and tides forecast 6 days/1 hour in your spot with temperature (°C/°F) and wind speed/gust.

* You can review your performances within the single track, point by point, or with Statistics and the new Graph tool.

* Waterspeed works on iPad too, with automatic sync between devices.

More than 150,000 Sailors, Kitesurfers, Windsurfers, Paddlers, Kayakers, Wakeboarders use Waterspeed every day. Give it a try!

mk1727 , 06/01/2018

Great app

I purchased the pro version which allows you to set a count down timer for the start of a race. When I race I-20’s we use a 5 minute starting sequence. When I race windsurfers we sometimes use a 3 minute sequence. You can choose any length you like. It then starts tracking you while the time is counting down for the start while tapping you on the wrist when the start is getting close. It drops a pin on the map when you start the starting sequence and then drops a pin on the map when the flag go up “the actual start of the race” then drops a pin when you finish, which you have to press the stop buy when you finish. It lays the tracks down on a map with color indicating your speed. FYI, it shows the map stuff on my iPhone when I get back from sailing. This was all tracked with the Apple Watch 3. I can also easily send all the info to my iPad using airdrop. For me, it’s a great app for racing sail boats and windsurfing. Really easy to use. I Highly recommend it. I don’t usually leave reviews, but I felt I had to because I like this app so much. Can’t wait to try it out this winter when I go Iceboating and achieve speeds near 70 mph.

nuebar , 05/20/2021

Excellent app for watersports: kayaking, rowing, supping, sailing etc.

This is a fantastic app to keep track of your performance when doing water-based sports, regardless if it is sailing, windsurfing, kitesurfing or paddle sports like kayaking, rowing or SUP’ing.

I use it mostly for kayaking and surf skiing. The app on the Apple Watch can be used stand-alone to track speed, heartrate, distance etc. The app then syncs to the iPhone app automatically when in reach again. This means that I do not have to take my phone on the water. On my iPhone and iPad I have access to the exact GPS route on a map, speed and other statistics I collected while paddling. The PRO version is also well worth it, it gives you access to detailed weather, wind and tide forecasts and provides seamless syncing with Strava (your route and stats get uploaded to Strava with the correct activity classification automatically). It works very well; the support is outstanding, with frequent app updates and feature additions.

This is a must app for anyone serious about water-based sports!

Jeff N - CBI , 07/06/2018

Essential for Sailers

Wow!! Do I like it and then some!! I sail on the Charles River and started to track my sailing late 2017 and found an app that did it pretty well. I record about 28 trips and then I found Waterspeed. I am instantly impressed as I ONLY need my Series 3 Apple Watch to record my sails. Previously I had to take my phone and watch to record trips. With Waterspeed I can see my trips played back with a visualization of my HR - Speed - Time - Heading. So cool! Waterspeed also uses colors to indicate your speed on a map (green, yellow, orange, red). I have 4 sails with Waterspeed and each time I’m more eager to look at the results and see where I had speed on the sail and how I performed. Awesome!! The data that is gathered is complete. Each sail tracks duration, distance, wind speed, avg speed, top speed, best speed 10 sec, best speed 20 sec, best speed 500m, best speed 1km, best speed 1nm, HR min, HR max, HR avg, calories, heading, and location. Also, I did purchase the Pro membership. BTW there is start timer too. Wow!
