Description

Christmas is coming again! Play this Christmas game with 2400 levels of match 3 fun, filled with lots of new challenges! Light the candles, clear the snow to find the gifts, catch the jumping snowmen, fill the gift bags. Drop Santa down the chimney, ignite the firecrackers, fill the big sock and look what's in the fridge. All while taking care of the snow guns!

Christmas Sweeper 3 will keep you engaged until long after Christmas!
There are no lives to lose, and you don't have to annoy your friends to progress. Play as long as you want. This game will fill the long winter evenings with lots of pleasure!

Swap, drag and sweep the cute Christmas pieces, like the christmas tree, santa's hat, Christmas bells and baubles, gingerbread man and ice crystals. Make matches of 4 or 5 to create powerful bombs and power-ups. And use Santa's mitten to collect a single piece. You'll be rewarded with cookies and soda.

Be sure to share the fun with your facebook friends. Invite them and compete with them in the leaderboards and on the level map!

If you like holiday themed match 3 puzzle games, click and match, swap and match or any type of casual match 3 puzzle game then Christmas Sweeper 3 is just for you!

Love this game!

I’ve been playing this game since December 2017. Every day I log in and play a few levels. The new animation is cute and I am enjoying replaying the Worlds. I used to be a big fan of Two Dots but I found the levels are too frustrating without freemiums so I stopped. What’s the fun in needing a crutch for every level? This game is challenging but a good way to relax.

Timing61 , 06/16/2021

Not happy with new power ups

I’ve been playing the game off and on for a couple of years. And mostly I enjoy it. I am on level 2147 so you can tell I play fairly regularly. Each game is done in just a couple of minutes. And I don’t have to wait for new lives to generate. It’s a diverting way to spend time in a waiting room or other place where one must be still and quiet. The fact that the ads blast so loudly even when the setting is on silent is a major headache. I can’t hear if my phone rings or I get a message if I turn the sound all the way down.I would very much appreciate your fixing that ghastly glitch. I don’t mind so much watching ads but I really HATE listening to them.

One of the recent updates introduced power ups. The new power ups are to be selected before the game is started. You can’t direct where to put them or use them within the game, just at the start up. They are useless. I would much rather have the regular Weapons. I always feel ripped off when I have to settle for one of the power ups instead of useful weapons after getting through a turn of the wheel. Please come up with something that is actually beneficial to play instead of those horrible power ups things!

Thanks for making what is, on the whole, a pretty good game and sharing with us. 😀

CucumberWatermelon14 , 11/10/2019

Don’t like the change....

The last time I played this game was during the winter of last year/beginning of this year. I recently downloaded the game again, as it is getting close to winter and i enjoyed this game a lot the last time I played it. But, when I came back and played a level, I noticed that the music is different. This really upsets me because I loved the old music, it’s what made the game so fun. Now it’s just 3 songs on loop and they don’t even go all the way through. I dislike this new music and I prefer the old music. I don’t know if there can be anything done about this, maybe an option where you can choose which songs you can listen to, that play all the way through instead of cutting off half way, because I really enjoyed the old songs but now it’s just cut off early and I don’t get the full feel of the song. Besides this one flaw, it’s a pretty great game. But, I think something should be done about the music, just my opinion, but the old music sounded better.

Edit: Let me rephrase what I said. I like the music, it just sounds 100x better listening to the full songs. If there could be a change made that lets you listen to the full songs, I’d appreciate it.
