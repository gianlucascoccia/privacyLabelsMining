Description

“Keyboard Fonts & Emoji Maker” is a fonts & emoji keyboard for iPhone & iPad.
All fonts work on all apps, and you can customise your emojis and use on iMessage.

[FEATURES]
- 150+ keyboard fonts work on Instagram, message, snapchat and other all apps.
- Emoji Maker
- GIF Maker (Animoji)
- Text Maker
- Decorative texts
