Description

PerfectMotion®. Groove repeatable, efficient body motion with every club.

Just take swings and let our unique continuous expert feedback translate instruction into action and improve your game.

On every swing, our AI captures your body motion and detects your key issue - instantly. A trace of your motion and a video fix from a top instructor help you adjust and improve.

Every session generates two scores: PPI - quality of your motion, and Groove - repeatability of your swings. Use PPI (the PerfectMotion® Performance Index) and Groove to track your progress and motivate your efforts.

Like elite golfers, use your best shots as a model to dial-in consistent results. Regardless of handicap, matching sessions will elevate your game to the next level.

Train with or without a ball all year. All you need is your phone. Improve anywhere, anytime.

Nick63k , 01/15/2021

Pretty interesting

So this is an app that will take a look at your body positions front on and give you feedback on what the app considers its model. It tells you things like body position too forward at impact of laying back at impact and even if you’re losing posture (surprising given it’s only face on but head popping up is a key signature so it makes sense as to how.) I don’t always believe the feedback 100% but it’s mostly right and will get you consistently moving towards a North Star so to speak in terms of getting consistent impact. It also will give specific advise on the issues it detects.

What it doesn’t really do is get things such as your face-path orientation correct so your struggles with a big slice still will require coaching but it seems like what the app is doing is creating better parameters in our swings for consistent contact (other reviews point out it fixing a slice so perhaps it does create conditions for better club face and path) I’m a fan this far.

I play to a 7.1 Index and have only had the chance to hit into a net, but I feel like ball striking is more solid more often. Will let everyone know what improvements I make if any when it’s time to get out on the course.

John the "Weatherman" , 05/15/2020

Showing Improvement

This app is helping me address my swing issues. As a 22 handicapper, I have lots of swing issues. The app is flexible in that I can use a golf ball, BirdieBalls, or no ball to work on improving my swing. You can enable the app to view your swing and have your swing analyzed by a professional. The app is easy to use and the video tips are based the motion of your swing. I started off falling back in my backswing and then started going to far forward after impact. The graphs help track my swing improvement or lack there of. I do not practice without PerfectMotion. It gives my time grinding off the course a purpose and goals to meet. It’s a blended learning- AI- game improvement tool. Maybe an Apple Watch companion app with vibrations to help with putting and swing temp and a pause button and audio speaker will be the in version 2.0 or an upgrade soon.
