Sir_Quincent , 06/27/2021

Please fix Ourwin!

Hi, I’ve been playing for a month or so, and really enjoy this game. The merge and bullet h3ll elements are used in a very interesting way. However, I recently got Ourwin up to 5 stars and an equal level/enhancement to my weakest team dragon (so I could substitute him in without losing power), and immediately discovered that his attack (a big fast-rotating, flashing ring) gives me motion sickness and triggers my photosensitivity. It’s very difficult to look at, and after a few minutes of playing with him, I get a headache/nausea/vertigo. Please make his ring not spin so fast in the next update, he’s supposedly one of the best non-chronicle dragons but I’ll have to remove him from my team at this point. I don’t have seizures myself, but I’m sure that Ourwin’s attack could trigger one.
