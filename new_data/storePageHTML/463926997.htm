Description

magicJack customers can take their number on the go so that you never miss a call. Download now and login to magicApp with your existing magicJack email and password to make and receive calls.

magicApp Features:

* magicJack customers can enjoy texting to any U.S. mobile number using magicApp - your friends & family don't have to download the app to text!
* Calls to your magicJack number rings on your smartphone at the same time so you never miss a call
* Make calls over Wi-Fi so you never have to worry about roaming charges when traveling
* Unlimited calling to the U.S and Canada
* Unlimited calling to other magicJack customer worldwide
* Caller ID, voicemail and call forwarding
* Caller ID pulled from your mobile phone contact list so you always know who is calling

Call family and friends on magicApp worldwide and save on international calling at the lowest rates.

Use magicApp on devices running iOS10 or higher.

Follow us on Twitter (@magicJack) or on Facebook (Facebook.com/magicjackApp).

*Texting to international numbers is not available and texts to some US mobile numbers may not be delivered. Unlimited calling to numbers within the United States and Canada, and unlimited texting and other services provided by magicJack®, are based on normal, non-excessive use. A combination of factors is used to determine excessive use, including but not limited to the relative use when compared to the average magicJack® user, the number of unique numbers called, calls forwarded, minutes used, texts sent/received and other factors. Unlimited calling does not include calls to Alaska or the Yukon and Northwest Territories of Canada or calls to non-8YY calling card, platform, conference or chat lines, for which additional fees will apply. Subject to our Subscriber Agreement located here: http://www.magicjack.com/action/saps/.

Dralexrom , 06/11/2020

Love the service; not the app

Since the updated app has come along, my love for MJ has dropped faster than some of my calls. I understand the quality of my calls depends on my internet speed, and since I get very slow speeds, calls are not always great but that’s ok.

Sadly, the updated app has made things worse for me. Now the app interrupts anything I’m doing on my phone and doesn’t let me dismiss the call. Sometimes it shows a “decline” button, (sometimes not), but what I really want is to keep using my phone and dismiss the MJ screen all together. It also constantly makes me log in again, it switched buttons around and still doesn’t show caller ID. And it does a super annoying thing: I get a text, I’m in the middle of answering it, same person texts another thing before I finish typing and when the new text comes thru, it erases all MY text I had just done!!! Makes me so frustrated that people will text one liners and as I’m trying to finish, my words get erased to let another, one-liner, text in.

These things didn’t happen with the old app, so I hope a new update is coming ASAP, and I will return to my 5 star review.
