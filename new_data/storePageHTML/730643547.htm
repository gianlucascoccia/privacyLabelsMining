Excellent resume app with a great payment method

I loved this app. It was so easy to use, very user friendly and efficient. I was able to make a great resume in probably a quarter of the time that It normally would take. You have freedom to add and delete categories and sections as you see fit, and it also has options for distributing your resume. There are options for turning your cover letter on and off, using bullet points, etc. If you choose to email the resume, the email that is brought up even has a preloaded message for the hiring manager, if you choose to leave it. This app has tons of options to customize your resume, but they still made it very simple and quick to do, which is amazing. It's not over crowded with options that end up taking longer than just typing an actual resume. This app is thoughtful and well made, I will definitely be using it again. I also love the "honor system" method of payment. They took a risk but I have no doubt that it's paid off. This alone makes me want to give them my money, on top of how well the app performed.

Well Made and Efficient

It's exactly what it says on the tin. Not only is it free, it doesn't have the clutter of ads. You only pay if you find the app useful.

I most certainly found it useful. It was well designed. I had no trouble navigating the menus. The app itself as well as the resume it creates has a very clean and efficient appeal.
