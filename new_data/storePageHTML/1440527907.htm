Description

Could your living space use a makeover or does it need a fresh addition? Fun as home decor is, committing to that area rug you've been eyeing, or putting a new love seat in the living room without seeing it there first can overwhelm even the most seasoned home decorators.

Luckily, I’ve solved the age-old question of ‘How will this furniture look in my house?’ with my Bob's Discount Furniture app. Now, you can view a wide variety of pieces from my online furniture inventory in your home before you buy, using my 3D View in room augmented reality (AR). The AR capabilities let you enjoy a futuristic shopping experience from the comfort of your home. My app lets you view furniture room by room, so you can calculate sizing, colors, lighting, and compare multiple pieces to achieve the look that makes your house feel more like home. Just scan your room, select your furniture, and let your inner interior designer take over with AR capabilities, to create the perfect look!

When you download my Bob's Furniture shopping app, you can:
- Select from a wide variety of living room, dining room, and office furniture, as well as bedroom sets and home decor products
- View furniture to scale with incredible quality and detail
- Choose your furniture color
- Choose your finish
- Place anywhere in your space using my AR furniture technology
- Share your image with family and friends

When you're ready to make a purchase, I offer four convenient delivery service options that ensure safe arrival of your order, thanks to the customer care Bob's Discount Furniture is famous for.

Visit www.mybobs.com to see my latest products and don't forget to follow Bob's Discount Furniture on Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and YouTube.
