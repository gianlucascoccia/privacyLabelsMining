Description

Wondrium is a place where you can let your mind go. Wondrium provides an expansive collection of educational videos to help you learn about new topics. Learning new topics has never been easier.


Wondrium is what happened when the folks behind The Great Courses Plus stepped out from behind the podium and offered thousands of new online learning experiences, providing videos, documentaries, lessons, series, and more. With topics spanning across math, science, history, biology, crafts, art, music, personal development, chemistry, foreign language, English, French, Spanish, German, and other categories, you’ll have vast opportunities to learn what you love and love learning about it.


All our educational streaming videos are academically comprehensive, relentlessly entertaining, and led by engaging experts. If you’ve ever wondered about anything, Wondrium will be your new favorite place to go.


Whether you’re a beginner in a subject or advanced in your knowledge and skills, whether you want to begin binge-learning deeply on one topic or sample a few select categories, there’s something here for you. Whether your interest is watching documentaries; discovering the latest breakthroughs in science, math, biology, or chemistry; or learning photography, piano, knitting, painting, French, Spanish, German, or anything in between, there’s something here for you.

If you don't already have an account, you can register and subscribe right in the app. With a subscription, you can access thousands of educational videos online and offline for one low price.

This app works best on iOS 12.0 and higher.

Powerful Tool to Eradicate Ignorance!

The GCP is a powerful and effective tool that has provided my entire family members ranging from ages 6-80 with wealth of information from multiple fascinating lectures.
I have enjoyed many courses from History to Astrophysics to cooking and more. The lectures are engaging and easy to follow (English is my second language).
I appreciate the fact that I can log in from any device (10 in my account) and from anywhere in the wold! I can execute multiple daily tasks while learning. Getting stuck in traffic is no longer a waste of my life as long as I can listen to any of The Great Courses Plus lectures.
The only thing I wish can be improve, is the accessibility of the lessons in each Course. It takes too many steps of I want to start from the beginning or play a previously studied lesson. In any case, the fact that one can have access to such incredible amount of knowledge at his/her fingertips is powerful!

olamk , 07/13/2021

Great content and features, despite usability impairments

I love this app and service. I like that new content is being added and I particularly like the audio-only mode. I will be a subscriber for long time, but have three more (necessary) usability requests and two suggestions. (1) allow users to browse the app while audio programs are playing. Right now, the only way to browse for other content within the app is to stop the program. This does not follow the iOS HIG. Also, please change the player to the native iOS media players. (2) performance improvements, particularly background battery drain. I’m unsure why this app is such a battery hog, even when content is downloaded in the background. (3) for browsing, a better system is needed. I’d suggest filtering options (including one for “percent seen.” The current “Netflix” style of browsing is painful and slow. These are just ideas, but I think they would be awesome to add after the usability problems have been addressed! (4) note-taking during content and live closed captioning should be added and integrated for future reference, given the service’s educational focus, this would be a boon for studying or looking back on past materials. (5) ratings and reviews for content, simple thumbs up, thumbs down. Would help in finding quality content when there is overlap in available programming.

Npfxo , 11/12/2017

Good content but app seems buggy

Sometimes it doesn’t sync properly across devices, starting further back in a lecture than where you left off - and sometimes you can’t even fast forward to where you were; it just keeps jumping back, forcing you to re-watch parts you’ve already heard. You can’t download just the audio, only video, which is not too convenient if you are listening while driving unless you want to get a ticket for watching video while driving, because you can’t switch the downloaded video to audio only. Exit the app and when you go back you have to navigate back manually to where you left off, it does not automatically show the lecture you were on and offer to continue. It doesn’t even have the course you are on on the front page when the app opens. If you exit the app while watching a video to, say, read a message or google something, it stops playing; you have to manually switch to audio only then exit the app to keep listening. So, several frustrating clunky inconveniences or bugs, but I’m enjoying the content of my first course.
