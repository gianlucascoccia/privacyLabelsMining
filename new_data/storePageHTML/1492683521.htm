Description

The official app of Tucker Budzyn, the cute and floofy doggo you all know and love from social media!

FEATURES

ヅ More than 150 emojis, animated emojis and Tucker GIFs!
ヅ All the Tucker reactions including "Da hekk" "hekk you linda" or "tree of doom"
ヅ Regular updates (new emojis monthly, new features...)
ヅTuckerMoji Maker! Build custom tucker emojis. Billions of combinations!
ヅ Tucker's Wall - Super secret fan group with exclusive content of Tucker
ヅ High quality, hand-drawn illustrations and converted into a adorable emoji
ヅ Download all the Tucker emojis to your device, so you can use anywhere
ヅ Possibility to be "Pet-Mojized": (We Turn your pet as emoji - upon request).
ヅ Integrated on WhatsApp Sticker
ヅ Quick & simple installation -> as keyboard / Messages App
ヅ Works in Facebook, Instagram & other social media apps

HOW THE EMOJIS WORKS

ヅ Open the app, select the Tucker emoji and share it wherever you like
ヅ Install the Keyboard (tutorial in the app) and open any messaging app /facebook comment and tap one emoji.
ヅ Export them to WhatsApp and use them as stickers
ヅ Download all the Tucker emojis & stickers to your device, so you can use anywhere
