Description

MICO is an exquisite app to meet new people, have a chat, explore something fun, and experience exciting live streaming together.

Do you want to meet new friends? Chat with someone cool? Watch live streams performed by tons of talented broadcasters worldwide?

Download the perfect app for finding new friends to chat with :)

Excellent Features of MICO:

Stranger Chat: Make new friends via Match game and moments
- Random match feature gives you a unique experience of swiping left or right to match strangers accurately and fast.
- Moments provide you a place to interact with people, share your memorable stories, or explore something cool posted by popular people around you.

Group Video Chat: Voice chat, video chat with more people
- Want to chat with more strangers and meet more friends at the same time? Join a group live chat room or create a multi-guest room, invite at most 8 people to join your group video room and live video chat with them, meet more interesting people.

Live Streaming: Go live and watch live stream whenever you want.
- MICO live videos are classified by the countries, tons of talented broadcasters from more than 100 different countries are gathered together in MICO, choose different places to watch live videos according to your favor.
- Live streaming is not only a way to relax, but also it is a big stage for everyone's dream come true. Broadcast your talent, meet fans, and follows, to be next star.

World Travel: Meet fresh faces in popular cities
- You are so busy watching out for what is ahead of you that you forget about the present, why don't you slow down, take a rest and enjoy the journey? MICO takes you to meet new people from every corner of the world, pick a famous city to make new friends.

Real-time Translation: No Barrier in Worldwide Chat
- In MICO, you will have an opportunity for the random match & meet strangers from different countries, you never miss a chance to chat with strangers because of the language barrier anymore. Click the translation button to break the language barrier in random stranger chat easily. It is easy to chat or makes friends from other countries without language pressure!
