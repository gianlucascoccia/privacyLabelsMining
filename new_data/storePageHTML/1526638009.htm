Great Game

So to start out I would like to say that if I could I would give the game a 4.5 out of five. The game has a lot of ads and I know they need them to make money since the game is free but do they need them while you are in the middle of doing a nail? The game is really fun and I love that you get to pick the design of the nail. One of the problems is a lot of the ads are inappropriate which stinks because this would be a great game for younger kids. The game is to easy, I wish there was like a challenge mode so that you could try to compete with others and do a themed nail. This game is not a scam but in order to get new stickers and stuff you need to watch an ad. The VIP clients also require watching an ad. I wish there were more nail polish or stencil options or a freehand option so that you could just paint. Sometimes the screen freezes and it chooses a color for you which is annoying. The marble option requires watching an ad but it is my favorite. This game is really fun and the ads aren’t that annoying. If the developers would please read this and try my ideas this game would 100% be a five star game,

🐱Cat Lady🐱 , 11/03/2021

Littered With Ads, Lags, And Makes My Device Overheat

This game isn’t bad. It seems like a good game to play when you’re bored as bricks and no other apps on your device seem appealing, however there’s a few problems. The first one is the ads. Oh my god, the ads. It seems like every three clicks there’s a popup ad, and on top of that there’s a constant banner ad at the bottom of the screen. The ads aren’t even regular popup ads, but video popup ads that play for 30 seconds each. This causes you to get more ad time than play time, and your only option to get rid of them without using airplane mode is to subscribe for $4.99 PER WEEK. The second problem is the lagging. The sheer amount of ads causes the entire app to lag horribly, which defeats the purpose of the app—to be a calming/relaxation game. This brings me to the third problem. All the lagging causes my device to overheat. After playing this game on my phone and having that overheat, I figured it was because the game size was too large for it to handle. As of writing this review, I am currently on my iPad where I just downloaded the app. Lo and behold, my iPad *also* overheated within 2 minutes of playing this game. In concept, the app is great, but the execution is absolutely garbage and feels like a game that was released mid-production and wasn’t even fully finished. If you really want a nail art game, I highly suggest you go elsewhere. Save your device from overheating and yourself the headache.
