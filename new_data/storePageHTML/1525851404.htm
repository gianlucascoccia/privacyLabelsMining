Description

Secret Photo is a photo safer that keeps all your private pictures hidden by keeping them in a password protected photo safe.

Have any photos you don’t want someone to see? Hide pictures and videos in your personal secret photo vault.

Our app keeps your photo safe by requiring a password to view them. Secret Photo allows you to password protect the entire app as well as individual photo albums, keep them away from nosy folks when they are using your device. Moreover, this application will issue a break-in alert when your personal data is invaded.

Secret Photo will keep safe pic in the encrypted storage area protected by a PIN code or your fingerprint. Now you can be sure that your personal album will be kept safe and secure. You can access your private photo albums from different devices by using private cloud storage. Your friends and family will only see what’s left in public photo gallery, not in your private photo vault.

All your photos stored in our app are 100% private. Your photos are only stored on your device and are never uploaded to our servers. Enjoy our photo vault right now.
