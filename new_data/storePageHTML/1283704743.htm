Description

Harmony Helper is like having a 24/7 digital rehearsal room in your back pocket. It’s a singing app [more…] that allows you--and singers of all levels--to get real-time feedback on any song, anytime, anywhere. Add your sheet music by scanning or uploading it, and then practice your custom vocal part without the pressure and constraints of a live setting.

Harmony Helper is made by singers for singers: Our patented technology makes it possible to learn any song, giving accurate feedback on pitch and timing as you sing. Whether you’re practicing to perform with a choir, in musical theatre, with a band, for a singing competition, or even for fun, Harmony Helper is here to help.

Key Features:

- Sheet Music Scanner: Subscribe to Harmony Helper and you can add any song by taking photos or uploading a PDF, which is then converted to a simple, easy-to-practice format.
- Custom Vocal Part Creation
- Real-Time Feedback: Powered by our patented pitch-tracking algorithm, you’ll see exactly where you need to focus and improve.
- Vocal Part Volume Controls makes it easy to isolate your vocal part as you learn.
- Try our 5 Steps to Learning and Holding Harmonies, which guides you through an expert-backed approach to your practice.

Download the app and practice songs from the Harmony Helper Songbook for free, and then subscribe when you're ready to practice your own songs. Every subscription starts with a 7-day free trial period.

Pretty good

This app is pretty good. I used it for a simple two part musical theatre song. It doesn’t quite understand what part is the focus. Very limited in that regard. Had the music not been fairly easy to decipher it would have confided the heck out of me. It needs a major overhaul or upgrade. Especially for complicated parts or lots of parts in a song. I think it’s an excellent simplistic tool used for very simplistic songs. If this app handled multiple parts and more complex songs it would be worth the money. I hope they keep improving it.

Developer Response ,

Hello! Thank you so much for the review and feedback. We are constantly working on improving our music algorithms inside of the app to work flawlessly with all music from simple to complex. In the meantime, if there are any specific pieces of music that you would like to discuss, feel free to send us an email at support@harmonyhelper.com. We will be happy to help you with them!

WalterLevi , 12/19/2019

Almost Great

I’m not sure about others, but I’ve had issues with not all the notes on music I import (either via camera or uploaded file) being correct. 90 plus percent is great, but wrong notes are jarring and don’t help with practice. If we had an interface for being able to review and correct the translated notes, this app would be phenomenal. But for now I still find myself going back to a piano to plunk out my line so that all the notes can be correct.

I’ll be thrilled to see this functionality in an update, or to be informed it’s an option I’ve somehow overlooked. Getting my music to 100% correct so I can practice it all would be worth five stars, a renewed subscription, and more!

Developer Response ,

Hi! Thank you for your feedback and review! Your suggestion about having more control over the song editing/importing functionality is a feature that we have on our radar. If you'd like to vote for that on our productboard, it will help us prioritize which features our users consider the most useful. If you haven't already done so, please shoot us an email at support@harmonyhelper.com so that we can take a look at the issue that you've been having with your music not being imported correctly and continue this conversation. :)
