Welcome to Camsea - Live Video Chat with Strangers!

Wanna make new friends around the world instantly?
Camsea, who is similar to Joi, livU, Omegle, DODO, Monkey, Monkey Run and Lamour, allows you to have 1-on-1 live video chats with cool people around the world randomly at any time. You can simply select the region and find your perfect match with a single tap! Start your friendship journey on Camsea!

Awesome Features:
1/ Instant video call
2/ Check one’s video profile
3/ 100% real profiles
4/ Select the region you like
5/ Add Friends and Send Messages to stay connected
6/ Chats are translated in real time

Live Video Chat and Meet New People Instantly

If you are looking to expand your social world and meet new friends while having the most fun in live video chats, Camsea is actually one of the funniest live video chat apps out here, where you can meet other like-minded strangers. Every connection you make here matters, we can assure our community and features are ideal for you to enjoy random video chats and meet friends worldwide.

Meet the World Through Live Chats

Wanna know her/him better?
Open your Dream Club, where a lot of people show up, and go through the recording videos to pick your favorite. You can check her/his profiles with video stories where people show themselves in real life and photos to get to know her/him better so you can make more like-minded friends on Camsea！
Match with people from all corners of the world, and find out what they are up to with live video chats! Video chatting with strangers from faraway places is truly a magical experience, especially when you have a chance to meet your soulmates. Our live video chat experience is like no other, and people you meet on Camsea are just so amazing!
