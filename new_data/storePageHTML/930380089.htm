Description

Key Features：
• Professional Video Editing:
VideoShow makes it easy to cut, merge, trim, split, reverse, duplicate, rotate, convert, flip, dub and compress videos. You can collage videos and images from the gallery, erase and edit video backgrounds and use fast motion or slow motion. Advanced features like video overlay, PIP, custom cover, mosaic, and scroll text help you to create a super interesting piece of art. Support widget.

Make trendy videos by using our video template. All you need to do is upload images and choose the template you like. With those templates, even beginners can produce videos with simple steps and become popular influencers or vloggers.

• Popular Music:
Hundreds of fully licensed music is available. You can also add local songs from your device. Extensive online catalogues and local music make your video fun and popular. You can even record and use your own voice to make the video cooler.

• Trendy Themes & Filters:
Themes, filters, after effects and beauty camera, make you look fantastic in every photo and vlog. They will help you grab attention and gain more followers and likes on social media.

• Subtitles & Graffiti:
There is a variety of text styles, outline colour, fonts and opacity for you to choose from. You can change your voice in the video into robot or minions, or extract the audio from your own video. You can also doodle on the video and become a video star.

• Share:
We provide high quality 720P/1080P HD export with no duration limits. Square theme and multiple aspect ratios are supported. Record special moments like weddings, birthdays, Valentine's Day, Halloween, Thanksgiving and Christmas.
