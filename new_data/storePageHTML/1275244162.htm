Description

ZooPix, or “The Zoo" for short, is where every kind of pet goes to be social, have fun and be seen. And since pets run the accounts (not humans) things can get pretty wild around here. So whether you bark, purr, chirp, hop, gallop, swim or slither (you get the idea) we welcome you to the neighborhood. Find friends, share pictures and - if you're feeling a little competitive - enter the weekly Pet Show for a chance to collect awards, win gift cards and snag a trophy! Download ZooPix today and join the crew.

Key Features Include:

* SHARE pictures & videos with your Zoo Friends and leave comments

* LICK posts you like

* JOIN the weekly Pet Show for a chance to COLLECT AWARDS & WIN GIFT CARDS

* ADD all your pets under one account

* SEND AND RECEIVE direct messages with ZooChat

* CREATE a custom profile to share fun facts

* WEAR a RESCUE BADGE to show you were rescued and support Animal Rescue & Adoption

* FOLLOW the Zooperstars (celebrity Pets on the Zoo)

* FIND AND SELL great products through our Zoopermart feature

* MEMORIALIZE a beloved pet that has passed away with a rainbow badge

Twinklinmamaof5 , 06/30/2020

Use Instagram instead

Honestly the idea behind the app is good, I like the idea of weekly contests and all that, but the execution is off, the app is pretty slow/glitchy, it’s a little confusing to use, and for the few contests I participated in it was like the same few people were always on the leaderboard and on top of that there is so much downvoting that it’s not even really worth it to join contests unless you are a massive account. The Zoochat is another great idea that was poorly executed you can’t send pictures from your camera roll which makes it hard for people who do edits and things like that. Going back to the contests, they have themes that are announced, but half the time I don’t get the announcement, and I even ask other ZooPix members and because of the badly constructed Zoochat they don’t get back to me or they didn’t get the notification either, so I don’t know the theme until the day of, which doesn’t work for me because I plan out my photo shoots days in advance. All the work that I put into the contests was completely trampled on by downvoters and it’s just not worth it. If you want to have a profile for your pet, use Instagram, their app works WAY better, less glitches, way more user friendly, there is a HUGE amazingly supportive pet community in there, there are pages that do pet contests, it’s easier to get ahold of people and it’s just one hundred times better
