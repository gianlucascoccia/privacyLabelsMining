Description

The best-known architecture, interiors and design magazine at international level.

Published in two languages (Italian and English) and aimed at a cosmopolitan readership interested acquiring information on the sector as seen from the perspective of Italian taste.

Each month it presents reports on the latest architectural designs, and provides news on interesting design and production developments; it analyses phenomena that are more broadly linked to the world of architecture and design with content of high quality from an editorial, graphics and pictorial point of view.
