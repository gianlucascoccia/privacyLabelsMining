Description

Take control of your motherhood fitness journey with the Bloom Method. Inside our online studio you’ll find hundreds of challenging and effective workouts on demand, that will have you safely working up a sweat at home. Rooted in evidence-based methodologies, Bloom’s programs are designed for fertility, pregnancy, and postpartum mamas. Our goal is to help you thrive during every stage of motherhood.

Bloom’s techniques are proven to help you get stronger through pregnancy, recover faster after birth, and heal past pregnancy injuries like Diastasis Recti. Join thousands of happy Bloom mamas and discover the power of our method. Inside Studio Bloom you’ll find:

WORKOUT VARIETY: Foundational techniques, our signature BirthPrep class for pregnant mamas, postnatal routines, core, cardio, HIIT, Boxing, Cycle, and strength classes that will have you working up a sweat!

EXPERT INSTRUCTION: Designed by a Prenatal and Postnatal Exercise Specialist (and mama herself!) and guided by experts, Bloom’s workouts will motivate, inspire, and guide you through one of the most powerful transformations of your life. You got this, mama!

INJURY PREVENTION & HEALING: Step into your power. Learn how to activate and move from your core, strengthen your pelvic floor, and breathe diaphragmatically to prevent and heal injuries during pregnancy and postpartum.

GUIDED TRAINING PROGRAMS: Exercise plans designed for fertility, pregnancy, and postpartum. Specifically tailored to remove the guesswork and help you reach your goals.

JOIN THE COMMUNITY: Become a part of our thriving community of thousands of mamas. Get access to the wisdom of our experts, plus fellow mamas who have experienced the struggles and joys of the motherhood fitness journey.

MEDITATION & YOGA: Flow through your asanas with integrated pregnancy modifications and added awareness to your core and pelvic floor. Reset your “mom brain” with a few minutes of mama-specific mindfulness.

NUTRITION: Custom recipes created by a fertility and pre + postnatal nutritionist curated for your motherhood needs.

STREAM ON YOUR FAVORITE DEVICES: Sweat at home or on the go: watch on your computer, phone, tablet, or cast to the big screen using Airplay or Chromecast.

NO EQUIPMENT? NO PROBLEM! Get sweaty with one of our bodyweight-only classes. You can also modify many of our classes and get a good workout without weights.

spark.leigh , 04/23/2020

All mamas need this!

I am NOT a fan of working out and very easily get discouraged when I finally give it a try. That being said, I knew how important training for my birth was and I turned to the Bloom Method to help me learn about my changing body. I admit I didn't do many of the actual cardio-type workouts, but the foundational principles of how to functionally move and support my body were so so so helpful. Belly-pumping throughout the day was a simple way for me to keep checking in on my core and I could really feel the difference. The concepts taught not only helped me go about my daily activities safely while pregnant, but they absolutely prepared me for my daughter's unmedicated birth. I better knew how to breathe (diaphragmatically) and was in touch with the muscles needed in my core to deliver my baby. I also enjoyed the stretching videos on days I didn't feel like working out, yet needed some movement to relax my body. Their coaches' approach with gentle guidance and encouragement to listen to your own body and what it needs at that time is SO refreshing, and it really does allow me to stick with exercises I would otherwise give up on in other programs. I do have a goal to get back into exercising regularly and utilize the growing library of workouts they have available through their app/website. I really do recommend the Bloom Method and Studio Bloom to all my pregnant and mom friends. Thanks for being a wonderful resource!

kbkorzekwa , 07/15/2020

Prenatal 1st time momma

I started studio bloom around 20weeks pregnant feeling a desire to learn more about my inner core. I have been extremely active my entire life playing sports into the college level. During all of those years training, I was never once taught how to engage your core during exercise. I knew pregnancy was a different ball game and I needed to change my mindset on how I trained to be safe during pregnancy and make the 4th trimester a smoother ride. I had to change my mindset. It’s not a “work as hard as you can or lift as much as you can or push your body as much as it can go” mindset but rather a listen to your body, push yourself when things are challenging, but give yourself time and grace to learn how to do things properly. I love the prenatal calendars, the introduction to core, and the wide variety the Bloom Method provides you during your entire journey. I plan to continue to use the Bloom Method during my pregnancy and during the 4th trimester to help me get back to my former exercise routine safely. The workouts are challenging in physical and mental ways and have made me rethink the way I exercise and move daily. I’m more conscious of my breathing and connection during daily activities. Would definitely recommend this app to anyone who is wanting to learn how to connect to their core ( bc I DIDNT).

jabiles11 , 05/01/2020

Wonderful birth prep tool

I looked at a few pregnancy safe workout apps before choosing this one. The free trial definitely sold me. I love that the focus of each video is on your breath and pelvic floor, no matter if the class is stretching or HIIT. The birth prep classes are by far my favorite. They focus on the same muscle group and burn out that muscle so that you mentally reach your edge, simulating what you’ll feel in labor. You then get to practice how you mentally and physically respond to this so you can go into labor knowing what works for you and what doesn’t. This has made me feel more confident about my birth. With COVID19 and not being allowed more than your partner in the room, I’m focusing on being more self-reliant and this is a huge part of that. Im a very active person - I run, hike, and do workout videos multiple times a week - and these classes always feel like a good workout. I also like that the strength and express classes are shorter so I usually do a few videos in a row but it’s also very convenient for those days you only have a few minutes. I’m also looking forward to using this same app pp. Highly recommend for any mama!

The only downside I’ve found is that if I try to use the app on Roku the sound and video don’t match up but I’ve had no problems on my phone.
