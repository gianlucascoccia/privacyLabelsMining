Allset: Food Pickup & Takeout 4+

Order ahead at restaurants

Allset Technologies Inc

Designed for iPhone

    • Free

iPhone Screenshots

Description

Order ahead for takeout or dine-in at restaurants and coffee shops in your area. Choose from your favorite local foods - salads, burgers, sushi, pizza, and more - and pick-up easy and fast. Get personalized suggestions based on your dietary preferences. Enjoy exclusive restaurant deals, menu specials, and daily rewards.

EXPLORE LOCAL
From trending cuisines to healthy eats, see the best food spots around you and access them more quickly. Moreover, you can easily swipe between the map view and restaurant cards to check the business hours, pictures, menu, and more.

NO-CONTACT PICKUP
Order ahead and safely pick up your order in a dedicated area in-store without contacting others. Look for a special tag 'No-Contact Pickup' in-app to find participating restaurants near you.

CURBSIDE PICKUP
Now you can get your food without going inside the restaurant. The restaurant team member will bring your order out to you. Check out participating restaurants with the tag 'Curbside Pickup’ and follow additional instructions if any.

PERSONALIZED EXPERIENCE
Add your allergies, diet, and food preferences to get the best meal options tailored to your taste and needs. Your allergies and special notes will apply to every order automatically. You can update your preferences at any time.

ENHANCED FILTERING
Filters and dietary tags (vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, kosher, etc.) make it easy to find the food that’s right for you.

ORDER TRACKING
Now you can track your order from the second you send it to the moment it's ready. Plus, to keep the whole process more transparent, we'll send you status updates so you know when the restaurant starts preparing the order, with an accurate order ready time. You’ll know exactly when to arrive for a quick pickup or dine-in, so you never wait for your order.

FAST REORDERING
Repeat your previous order to get your faves in just one tap. Previously ordered foods & drinks are now featured in a special section of the restaurant’s menu. Plus, you’ll be able to repeat your recent orders right from the main screen.

HEALTHY AT YOUR FINGERTIPS
Our Healthy options and hand-curated menus make it easy to eat well every day without spending much time. Based on nutrition expert recommendations, we feature wellness-minded and high-quality meals like vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, and other healthy offerings.

MENU SPECIALS
Enjoy affordable and delicious everyday eats from your favorite places on Allset. Find restaurants with the tag ‘Special Offer’ to see all the tasty options for less near you.

IMPROVED MENUS
Allset allows you to find what you crave easy and fast with clear and on-point item descriptions and thoughtfully selected, highly customizable meal options and add-ons.

Allset always has the foods you’re looking for: salads, sandwiches, burgers, sushi, pizza, coffee, burritos, bowls, vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, and more.

LOW FEES FOR PARTNERS
We offer our restaurant partners one of the lowest fees on the market, along with the commission free option. You can view the restaurant’s fee per each order at the checkout. Be sure that what you pay through Allset is going directly to restaurants.

Works exactly as advertised. A text is sent as to when one’s order is confirmed and what time it’s ready to be picked up. An added bonus is when you pick up the order you can check to confirm it’s what you actually requested, unlike services that deliver it to your door, i.e. if there’s a mistake or something’s left out you can ask it be corrected then and there — so far no errors. If restaurants you like are convenient to walk or drive to, this app is for you!
