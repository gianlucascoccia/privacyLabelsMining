Description

Learn and look up guitar chords and scales. Play electric and acoustic guitar, right on your iPhone or iPad. Record riffs, and share them with friends.

Full fingerings show you which fingers to put where, with barres clearly labeled to get you up and down the fretboard.

Built-in guitar video lessons help you go from zero to guitar hero, taught by professional musicians.

Know how to play it but not what it's called? Reverse ChordFinder mode lets you tap the fretboard and identify any of the thousands of chords inside of ChordBank®.

Clean, clear interface makes choosing chords a snap.

Full left handed support for all fingerings.

Strum to hear any chord played from gorgeous, crisp audio samples. Sounds great from the iPhone speaker, or headphones to brush up on chords in private.

Many of ChordBank®'s features are free but, for just $4.49 per month, less than even a single set of guitar strings, a ChordBank® All Access subscription provides full access to thousands of chords in all keys, scales and scale patterns, backing tracks, a library of video lessons recorded by professional musicians, and more.

If you decide to purchase a subscription to ChordBank® All Access, an initial payment will be charged to your iTunes account. Your subscription will automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of each subscription period, at a rate of $4.49 per month.

You can turn off auto-renewal at any time via your iTunes Account Settings.

You can find our privacy policy at https://chordbank.com/privacy, and our terms of service at https://chordbank.com/terms-of-service.

We hope ChordBank® helps you, as it has helped millions of other guitarists since 2010, to get the most out of your playing. If you enjoy the app, or have suggestions to make it better, please let us know at chordbank.com/support. Thanks!

Mr. Johnny 24 , 11/08/2018

Great Learning Tool

I had this app in my iPhone and really enjoyed it. In order to get more use out of it I downloaded it for my iPad. The bigger screen of the iPad allows me to see the whole fretboard of notes for each of the scales. There are enough scales offered to help a beginner to learn to play with some good soloing skills or to help an advanced player increase their skill set. An advanced player will probably find the backing tracks only to be OK. However for a novice player he/she will find them quite useful. The chord directory and metronome is quite useful as is the tuner. A players skill level will determine the usefulness of these straight forward lessons.

Myrkskog , 07/25/2018

Great app, but could fit the screen better for portrait

iPad 9.7. This app has tons of uses and is well worth the price for the full version. My only pet peeve is the way scales don’t display properly and some of the notes are cut off until you scroll down in portrait. This may be because the same amount of notes display on the landscape mode. Take some hints from how the interface from rock smith works for scale patterns, display it so I can zoom in and out on different sections of the pattern, or view the entire fretboard. The reverse chord finder and genius are helpful for writing. I also like being able to hear every chord and note if I want. Anyways great app and hopefully they can fix the notes disappearing thing.
