Description

Truth and Dare app is now a multiplayer & free party game for adults to enjoy, especially on Friday / Saturday night.

Here in the game, you can play the following levels:

• Easy : Truth and Dare.
• Hard : Some difficult challenges for friends.
• Extreme & Hot : Enjoy some exciting questions with your close friends.
• Clothing : Play exciting dares with the help of clothes.
• Dirty & Messy : Dirty challenges to make the atmosphere crazy.
• Awkward : Know the embarrassing Truths and Dares of your friends.
• Couples : Play exciting questions with partners.

Enjoy the WILD and CRAZY Night Party!

Easy to Use! Yes – here's how:

#1. Open Truth and Dare game app
#2. Select a level of party game from the '7' levels.
#3. Here you can use the game to display the cards of Truth or dare or can play the complete game by assigning up to 30 boys & girls internally.
#4. Join all the boys & girls.
#5. Shake the phone to stop a player randomly. And then send a Truth or Dare to that player.
#6. Each Game level provides more than 700+ Truth and Dare questions as per the category.
#7. Enjoy the game.
