I really like tastyworks as a brokerage, but stick with E*TRADE right now just because of the app

In my opinion, tastyworks is the best brokerage out there. Good desktop platform, great service, good fills, etc. The sole reason I do not use them as my primary is the mobile app. The iPad app does not truly support landscape, which alone is a huge issue for me using the iPad Pro with keyboard. But in my opinion, the mobile app needs a full rewrite. It’s convoluted and seems antiquated in comparison to E*TRADE and TD. It’s kind of surprising to me, given that TW is so great in every other way. Again, this isn’t a knock on them as a brokerage - I really hope their mobile devs see this and take a look at TD and E*TRADE mobile apps. If they do this, I’ll move TW to my primary immediately.

Sroc88 , 06/29/2021

👎🏽Poor Customer Service

I opened my account during a promotional period and deposited the required cash to qualify for it but was never given the promotional options. I emailed Tastyworks and they said they would put the options in my account and that it should take a few days. I waited 2 weeks but nothing happened so I called them and they apologized and said they would put it into my account and to give it a week. I waited 2 weeks again, still no promotional options, and called Tastyworks customer service back. This time I was told they never entered my choice (you could choose between options or stocks) and that’s why it didn’t go through to my account. I was told again to give it one week and the options would show up in my account. It’s now been over 2 weeks since my last phone call and still no promotional options. All in all it’s been 3 months since I first contacted them regarding this issue and each time I was told they would fix it but never did. Thank goodness this wasn’t an issue with my own money or trades because their incompetence in resolving this one issue is concerning. Now, I don’t have any faith that they would resolve any more serious issues that could arise. I’m closing my account with them.

BigOtrader , 03/30/2019

Major issues

I was going to transfer to TW after all the hype about low commissions and great technology. Of course I had to check out the app first before making the move. Well, unfortunately, I am hugely disappointed! It is completely unusable. At first glance, the app looked great and easy to use. However, my enthusiasm quickly soured when it would not let me set my strikes in a simulated trade. The navigation on the trade page has to work flawlessly and efficiently. Ugh! It was extremely frustrating! I realize when an app is upgraded there may be a few bugs. That being said, it looks to me this upgrade was released without much testing beforehand. You might get away with this on a game app, but for serious option traders it should not be tolerated. I am sure TW will fix these bugs in due time, but I have to wonder about next upgrade after this one and the one after that. So for now, I will stay with my current broker and continue to watch TW to see if they establish a prolonged culture of excellence before I transfer my account.
