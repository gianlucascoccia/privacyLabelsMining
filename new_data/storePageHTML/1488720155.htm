Description

Track earnings, dividiends and crypto, manage your stock portfolio, view performance over time and much more.

Track earnings, dividiends and crypto, manage your stock portfolio, view performance over time and much more.

Dividends Calendar
View all upcoming and past dividends in your personal dividends calendar view.

Portfolio Tracker
Add transactions with price and date to your stocks and see how your portfolio value changes over time. It also includes profit and loss calculations per intraday and overall.

Earnings Calendar
View all upcoming and past earnings in your personal earnings calendar view. Get notified on earnings calls and updates as soon as earnings are published.

Price Alerts
Create Price alerts for your favorite stocks and get notified as soon as your target price is reached.

Price Change Calendar
View all significant price changes in the past and get related infos for each of them.

IPO Calendar
The latest information on initial public offerings (IPOs), including latest IPOs, expected IPOs, recent filings, and IPO Performance.

Stocks, ETFs, Commodities, Bonds, Indices, Crypto
With Stock Events, you can follow stocks, ETFs, commodities, bonds, indices, crypto and more. You can also track them in your portofolio to keep up with your earnings and dividends.

Your privacy is important to us
You don't required an account to use Stock Events. We also don't aquire any personal information from you and we don't store any information about your subscribed stocks on our servers. All the data remains on your device only.

Simple and intuitive
Our services, and information are built to be intuitive and accessible for everyone — newcomers and experts alike.

Kristin Dilly , 07/16/2021

Beautiful User Interface & Easy to Use

4.5/5 Stars (needs 3 things to be 5 stars)

This is a stunningly beautiful app that’s easy to use, intuitive, accurate and gives all the necessary info without adding a bunch of extra junk we don’t need to watch, track, see, etc.

I tried several others but they were so overly complicated and filled with spam, ads, etc that I could barely use the app and didn’t need 90% of what they offered.

It’s so much better when an app focuses on one thing and provides amazing value vs trying to cram everything in.

There are only three things I’d like to see added so far:

1) I’d love to see the ability to link to other platforms (TD Ameriprise, M1, etc) so new buys are automatically updated and reflected in the current shares held.

2) I’d like the ability to add government sponsored plans like TSP funds/holdings (C Fund, I Fund, lifecycle funds)

3) It would be great to offer a la carte options to buy 1, 3, 5, 7, 10, etc Watchlist spots. Then, if someone wants to track only an additional few stocks they can easily do that.

Otherwise, I absolutely adore this app and will definitely be recommending it to friends and family. Already have in fact!

Dr Pinckard , 06/20/2020

Almost perfect!

I’ve long been looking for a simple yet intuitive dividend tracker for my aggressive growth portfolio. This app has everything I’ve been looking for and then some! I especially like being able to see earnings, yearly dividend income projections, upcoming dividends and alerts! The only downside is the price. A yearly subscription model is pretty strange for an app like this, and the large investment price of $30/yr makes it a pass for me. I like the simplicity, but having dark mode restricted along with a small amount of stock picks available to free users gives this app a 4/5 star rating overall.
