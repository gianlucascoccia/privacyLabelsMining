Description

People like to make simple things complex. Don't make this simple thing complex.

In Out is a simple little app with an EASIER WAY to record and track your incomes and expenses WITHOUT ALL THOSE STUPID STEPS and un­nec­es­sary clutter in other money tracking apps you loathe to use.


Fea­tures of this app:

• Sim­ple in­ter­face.
• Eas­i­er way to quick­ly enter any in­come or ex­pense.
• App will adapt to your requirement.
• Works offline.
• See your in­come/ex­pense his­to­ry.
• With iCloud sync, your data will automatically save in your iCloud account. So you won't lose your data.

Things this app doesn't have:

• No un­nec­es­sary clut­ter.
• No sign-up / lo­gin.
• No dis­tract­ing pop­ups.
• No stu­pid no­ti­fi­ca­tions beg­ging to use the app.
• No need of in­ter­net con­nec­tiv­i­ty.
• No ads.
• No sub­scrip­tions.
• Just no non­sense.

If you want a sim­ple app to track your incomes and expenses, This is THE app for you.
