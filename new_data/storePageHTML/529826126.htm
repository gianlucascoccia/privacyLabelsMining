Editors’ Notes

What’s made OLO game one of our multiplayer favorites since 2012? The way it perfectly captures the friendly fun of real-world shuffleboard and air hockey. Virtually anyone can jump into a match (locally or online) and have an amazing time flicking discs into the target zone for points—and slyly knocking an opponent’s discs away to lower their score. Elegant and irresistible, Sennep’s all-ages masterpiece lets up to four players enjoy games that can be as casual or hardcore as everyone wants them to be.
