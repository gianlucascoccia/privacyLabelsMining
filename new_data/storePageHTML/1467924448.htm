Description

Your kids will have hours of fun arranging music, learning about instruments and exploring dozens of colorful musical characters who live on Studio Island.  

Welcome to Studio Island!

Designed by Music Teachers and Early Childhood Educators! No in-app purchases and EVERYTHING is unlocked!! Studio Island is your all-in-one music studio for kids!

Your kids will have hours of fun arranging music and exploring dozens of colorful musical characters who live on Studio Island.  While exploring the island, they will learn about many instruments and different kinds of music!

Simple Music Studio for Kids! No prior musical ability necessary. Kids of all ages will enjoy!

Record hit songs with DJ RiDonkeylous by arranging musicians in this open-ended, musical puzzle.

Explore Studio Island to learn about over 50 instruments and meet the musical bands and musicians who live there.

Play at your own pace.

GREAT EDUCATIONAL TOOL

In this children's music game you can:
- arrange music clips
- Add fun sound effects,
- sing along on the microphone,
- play on the piano,
- add record scratches like a DJ
- create endless combinations and patterns for your song.
- You'll be jamming in no time!!

FEATURES SECTION

- Over 50 characters and growing!
- Lots of bands to choose from!
- Over 150 different sound clips to choose from
- Explore musical styles (Jazz, Hip-Hop, Rock, Country, etc.)
- Learn about instruments and multitrack recording
- Easy to use, learn through play!
- Frequent updates with new sound packs
- Tutorial videos available in the help section
- Be sure your device is not on mute/silence and volume is up.

Studio Island is music software, a game, and an audio sandbox to explore.

Tess_lexus , 08/31/2019

Fun but confusing

I recently downloaded studio island and I immediately got creative and I made a song. I have to say though, the song creating process is a little confusing. The app doesn’t tell you exactly what to do. For example, I wanted to put in a few notes from the piano and I recorded something but it didn’t transfer to the actual song. Same with the sound effects. Also, while picking instruments and such, I found it hard to remember what I already put Down and that made the process not as fun. After the song building is finished, you get a very satisfying concert with the band you made playing your song. However, if you add more than one musician in a instrument category, you have to manually switch them. I put two so they could play at the same time. I eventually learned that it is easier to just have one animal per instrument. You can also look around the island to see other bands. Doing that gave me more ideas for my songs. This game is very fun when you get past the challenges that I had!!! I would definitely use this app again!!!

Developer Response ,

Thank you so much for using our app and creating your own song! We appreciate your wonderful feedback which helps us as we continue to make adjustments to improve the experience. Currently, the piano will only be recorded when the microphone is on. The sound effects should record when you press the record button and then play them over your song.

We have included tutorial videos located in the app. Access them by pressing the purple question mark button on the bottom right of the Music Studio. Several question marks will pop up to teach about the various features of the Studio. Tap any question mark to watch a video.

Thanks again and please feel free to send detailed comments and feedback to support@Mus-EDLearning.com We would love to hear more from you!
