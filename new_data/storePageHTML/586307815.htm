Description

Hilton's Realtime-Navigator Mobile Navigation Application.

Navigate on almost real-time imageries such as sea surface temperatures, chlorophyll, surface currents, altimetry, and salinity using your iPad, iPhone, and/or iPod device(s) as your chartplotter!

Features include:

-Ability to create waypoints/routes "On the fly"
-Waypoint creation and editing
-Map zooming
-Point to point navigation
-Real-time GPS location
-Ability to change chart selection "On the fly"

The RT-Nav app interfaces with the RT-Nav.com website to provide a platform to allow subscribers the ability to navigate on desired RT-Nav.com images using youriPad, iPhone, and/or iPod device(s) as a chartplotter. View your real-time position on dynamic maps outside of cell/internet range navigating to temp breaks (probable weedlines), color changes, or default/custom waypoints by using our touch-screen technology!
