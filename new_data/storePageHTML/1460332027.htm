Description

Find out why thousands of casual and hardcore bettors trust WagerLab as their #1 APP FOR FRIENDLY BETS!

Bet with friends or bet for fun against The House! You can even win one of several weekly cash prizes. It’s all 100% free - no credit card required.

*NEW* WEEKLY CASH PRIZES
While using the app, you’ll earn tickets which you can submit towards multiple weekly cash prize drawings. You can even bet with your tickets to earn more and increase your chances of winning. It’s a fun way to have something riding on your bets without actually risking anything!

OVER 40 LEAGUES & CATEGORIES
All major sports leagues are supported including NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, NCAA Football, NCAA Basketball, PGA Golf, UFC, XFL, KBO, and tons of international soccer leagues including the English Premier League, La Liga, Champions League, MLS, Liga MX, Serie A, and much more! We’re constantly adding new sports and we will always have bets for every major sporting event.

PROP BETS ON FUN SOCIAL TOPICS
Bet on tons of fun prop bets updated daily. These bets are based around social topics like Politics, Weather, TV Shows, Celebrities, News, Events, Markets, and more. Some examples of these types of bets include: “How will the price of Bitcoin move tomorrow?”, “Who will get kicked off of The Bachelor this week?”, and “Who will win the Presidential election?”.

MULTIPLE BETTING OPTIONS
Bet the spread, over-under, moneyline, or pick from a variety of prop bets. Our goal is to ensure you always have tons of options!

SIMPLE TO USE FOR BEGINNERS
We make it easy for beginners to understand the lines. Browse simple betting odds like “49ers +6 points vs Raiders” or “Lakers to Win vs Heat”. Still have questions, we’re available any time to walk you through it all. Betting is supposed to be fun - not intimidating!

ZERO JUICE
Unlike other betting apps, there is no juice when you bet on WagerLab. All bets have fair odds!

BET WITH FRIENDS
Browse odds across over 40 leagues/categories and tap on your bet!
Propose the bet to a friend (or bet solo against The House) along with an amount
If you bet against a friend, they must accept the bet for it to become active
Keep track of your bet in the app. Once the game/event ends, your balance gets updated based on whether or not you won the bet. You can reset your balances at any time.

WagerLab is strictly for social gambling. No real money is transferred through the app. The app merely tracks “units” and it’s up to you and your friend to decide how to settle the balance.

Questions, feedback or problems? Email app@wagerlab.app for a quick response! We’re always looking to make the app better and we promise to go the extra mile to fix all your issues.

Don’t miss out on the fastest growing social betting app. Download WagerLab, the #1 app for friendly bets, for free today!
