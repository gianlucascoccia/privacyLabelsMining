A couple things before I hand over $7.99

This looks like it has potential, but I see a few issues. 1) On an iPhone XS screen, the brown resistor band appears as dark red. I deal with color every day, and my phone display is fine. Perhaps find a more neutral brown color to use. 2) Browsing through the free features, I managed to activate a musical note in the function generator that sent my speaker into a high pitch.
