Description

Workout videos created by top American trainer Charlee Atkins, Le Sweat TV is having an elite personal trainer right at your fingertips. Get smart and practical training in follow-along video form using the most straight-forward approaches and techniques for working out on your own.

Select full workout videos by body part or participate in one of the Le Sweat challenges. Videos are sorted by a body part (abs, booty, arms, HIIT, cardio, total body) and range from 5 to 30 minutes. Le Sweat challenges can help jumpstart your fitness routine, get you ready for an upcoming event, help destress or prepare you for bikini season.

Inside the app, you’ll also find videos organized by the equipment. From bands to dumbbells, to bodyweight only, Le Sweat has you covered with every type of workout that will leave you feeling healthy and accomplished.

See why Men’s Health, Women's Health, the New York Times, PopSugar, Cosmopolitan, Well + Good, and many more have featured Le Sweat workouts and Charlee Atkins as their go-to trainer! Join today for 7 days of training FREE!

Features:
-Stream videos from anywhere in the world there is WIFI. Perfect for travel, when you are on-the-go, or when there is little time to hit the gym.
-No internet? No problem. Ability to download any video for offline viewing.
-Just like private one-on-one sessions, train alongside Charlee every step of the way.
-Engage with the global Le Sweat community right in the app! Ask questions, give feedback, leave comments, or even find a friend to sweat with.

All you need to get started is yourself. Other equipment used in workout videos range from dumbbells, kettlebells, bands to yoga blocks, and balls. Don't have any equipment? It's okay, check out our library of bodyweight workouts.

Are you ready to Le Sweat? Yes, or yes! Take control of your fitness and download Le Sweat TV!

Fantastic Workout App - great moves and positivity!

I have been following Charlee since she was “Trainer of the Month” for Well + Good and then also on playbook. I love how enthusiastic she is and the tips she gives as she powers through the exercises with you. Her candid nature is refreshing, and her enthusiasm is contagious! When she started live workouts on IG I saved as many as were available and joined the live whenever I could. So excited about the app! Charlee makes every workout fun! Even if I am just not in the mood to work out, (we’ve all been there!) once I start a session with her I forget about that 5 minutes in as I am too busy trying to keep up with her and feeling her positive energy through the screen. I never thought I’d love workouts with bands, dumbbells, etc as I’m a yogi that always preferred body weight, but now I find myself gravitating towards those workouts more, and they are making me strong 💪🏻 I highly recommend the app to anyone looking for challenging fun workouts! The app is easy to use, I can save all my favorite workouts to a list and download them for on the go workouts when I don’t have wifi. Charlee also has 28 day challenges that really motivate with weekly 7 min sessions for abs and arms (so far, I am sure there are more to come!) that brings our community together as we check in with each other to motivate and work towards common goals😃

Liz Gil , 06/22/2020

4-week training guide is what I never knew I needed

When I first downloaded the app I had started doing random workouts that seemed to target the areas I wanted to focus on. I was doing pretty good with it, a workout once a day maybe trickled to a workout every other day, and so on so I decided I needed more discipline. My boyfriend and I started the 4-week training guide together and now we’re two weeks in. I already feel so much stronger than just a couple of weeks ago and after seeing my mom for the first time after a month, the first thing she said was how she noticed how much more toned my legs were since the last time she saw me. This training guide holds us accountable, but also keeps us doing workouts we wouldn’t normally do (stretches or arms because we want to be focusing a lot of core and lower body). Don’t let happy, energetic Charlee in a hot pink outfit fool you, we drip sweat to our mat every single workout-they’re no joke even as easy as she makes it look! I’ve never been able to even do a downward dog without feeling so strained, and now I do it with ease into plank position. Amazing what two weeks of consistency can do. Thanks for this amazing guide that’s keeping us in check! Highly recommend if you’re someone that needs a feeling of a personal trainer behind you telling you what to do, when.
