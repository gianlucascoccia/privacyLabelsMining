Description

Find a home that’s perfect for you with the award-winning, easy-to-use Realtor.com® Real Estate app.

Whether you’re renting, buying, or looking for a home’s estimated value, Realtor.com makes home search fun and easy with a robust set of tools and a guiding hand throughout the process, to help you browse millions of homes and zero in on the one that fits your needs.

Customize your home search
• Use a large variety of filters to tailor your home search around your wants and needs
• Search by price range, lot size, number of bedrooms and bathrooms, commute time, and more
• Search by school name to see home listings near your preferred schools boundaries

Find the right home in the right neighborhood with the interactive map
• Define your own search area with Draw on Map
• Use the points of interest map layer to see nearby restaurants, shops, cafes, and more
• View neighborhood noise levels to find a quiet home
• Use the crime heat map to find a home in a safe neighborhood
• Assess a home’s flood risk with the flood map layer

View homes in stunning detail
• Browse through big beautiful photos
• Get to the pics you really want to see faster with photos categorized by room
• Take a 3D home tour from the comfort of your couch
More 3D tours added all the time

Collaborate on your home search
• Renting or buying with someone else? Link your Realtor.com accounts to share your favorite homes and comments with each other
• Request feedback on property listings from those you trust quickly and easily via text, email or social media

Never miss out with timely home alerts
• Save your searches to know when new homes hit the market that match your search criteria
• Act fast with alerts when home prices increase or decrease

Stay on budget
• Use the Affordability Calculator to figure out your home price range
• Use the Mortgage Calculator to fine tune your estimated monthly payment
• Search homes by price or estimated monthly payment

Have feedback? Contact us at realappfeedback@move.com

Addictive!

I really enjoy this app. I frequently spend my sleepless nights looking up properties all over the country. It’s an obsession. I live in Florida and I am glad they added the ability to remove 55 plus communities. Helps me sort through local listings a lot faster. There are a few features on the web version that are not on the app. I also wish you could search multiple counties at once like you can with some of the competitors. I wish they would also add additional sorting options to the app. Filtering by price per square feet or lot size to name a few. I also wish they would add a pet friendly feature because there are a lot of HOAs in Florida that restrict the size dog and number of dogs someone can own. Even though both my dogs are registered ESAs and one is starting his Therapy Animal training in the next few weeks (it had been delayed due to COVID), I still get harassed by my neighbors who don’t understand that the law protects people with a medical condition and they are exempt from HOA regulations regarding pets. I would like to be able to filter those communities out all together.
