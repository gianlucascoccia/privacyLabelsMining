Description

KnitSimple, the only magazine for newbies--and knitters with a busy lifestyle --is now available on your tablet and phone! Get the latest issue here and keep it right at your fingertips so you can learn new skills, keep up with knitting trends, and follow the easy patterns for everyone in your family right here on your device.

Download the FREE app now! Purchase issues and store them in the app.

This is infuriating since I had a few projects in the works and I can’t get to the patterns to be able to finish. Needless to say I stopped subscribing when renewal time came up this last fall. I love the magazine, but this is ridiculous.

Update, I was finally able to get into one of the magazines that I needed. The one magazine that I needed most is forever lost to me (spring/summer 2017) it seems, and I refuse to pay for something twice. I do still have hard copies of most of these magazines from 2014 through part of 2018, however there were a few that were lost in a move, which was the whole reason why I downloaded the app to begin with. This is absolutely ridiculous, and reinforces my thinking that buying anything to store on line is a waste of money, because one cannot depend on being able to access their purchases. I did love this magazine, but I now feel that the magazine admin is nothing more than thieves. They do not respond to emails ever. DO NOT WASTE YOUR MONEY ON PURCHASING MAGAZINES FROM THIS APP.
