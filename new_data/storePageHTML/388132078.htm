Description

Thanks for using the Workhuman mobile app (formerly known as Globoforce Mobile).

Recognize colleagues, manage performance, redeem awards, and approve nominations while on the go.

• Stay up to date on the latest recognition activity
• Recognize individuals & groups anywhere, anytime
• Request or give Feedback
• Record a Check-in with your manager
• Add Priorities aligned to tasks and goals
• Approve award nominations
• Redeem for gift cards of your choice

We’re always making improvements to our mobile app, so keep your updates turned on to be sure you don’t miss any upgrades.

To use the Workuman app, you must participate in your organization's integrated recognition and performance management program.
