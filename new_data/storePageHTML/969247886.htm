Description

Bounce through the sky collecting donuts to keep your narwhal up in the air! Use clouds and crazy objects to bounce higher and farther. Insane combos will take your narwhal into outer space. And scuba gear will send him on an underwater adventure. Earn coins to unlock awesome items, blasting your narwhal farther than he’s ever gone before!

· Play the REAL game from Nickelodeon's new show: GAME SHAKERS!
· The more donuts you collect, the longer you bounce through the sky!
· Insane item combos add boosts to your bounce like: DOUBLE MONEY MONKEY TOILET!
· Pick up the special rainbow donut to trigger the ultimate SUGAR RUSH!
· The more you unlock the farther you go!

New AR Mode
Bring the fun of Sky Whale into your real world, 3D space! Now, you can play Sky Whale in AR mode and collect treasure in the bedroom, the backyard, or just about anywhere.

Editors’ Notes

In Nickelodeon’s TV series Game Shakers, Sky Whale plays the part of a hit game created by two 13-year-old girls who headline the show. You don’t have to be a Game Shakers fan to enjoy the charmingly fun Sky Whale, which Nickelodeon released as a tie-in game. As you send a cute purple narwhal spiraling through the air by feeding it donuts or bouncing it off toilets, the game’s slapstick, kid-friendly energy is irresistible. We were quickly engrossed by the challenge of keeping that whale airborne as long as possible—and laughed more than a few times along the way.
