Description

Currently, Youtube is not available.
And sorry that it's not sure when the fix can be ready.

Here's HighAmp for you who want everything about Music in one place.

- Better sounds quality with Bass, Treble and Equalizer
- All music collections including Youtube, Apple Music, iPod library, files in your PC and files in Clouds.
- Supports various audio formats such as mp3, flac, ape, opus, ogg etc.
- 100K radio stations.
- Sleeping with music.
- Waking up with music.

With all your music collections and better sound quality, you can easily start a playing queue and add, delete songs, and change play order whenever, while you're listening.

[Usage Help]

- swipe left in the play view : play next song
- swipe right in the play view : play previous song

- long press on 'Alarm' button : Alarm on/off toggle
- long press on 'Sleep' button : Sleep on/off toggle

- long press on a directory/playlist : shuffle play for the directory/playlist selected

[Note]

- YTPlayer is used for Youtube playback.
- Bass, Treble and Equalizer do not work while Youtube and Apple Music playback.
