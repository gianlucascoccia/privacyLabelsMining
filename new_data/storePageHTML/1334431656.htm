Description

Anxiety, self-doubt and negative thinking can stop you from being the person you want to be. Mindset will help you take control & overcome these unhelpful thought patterns through science-based hypnotherapy.

Anxiety, self-doubt and negative thinking are caused by your mindset. Your mindset is made up of the automatic thoughts and beliefs that drive how you think, feel and act. Using Mindset, you'll take control of these unconscious thought patterns and create new, positive mental behaviours that will help you become the person you want to be. Day-by-day, you'll progress through each course, listening to short, relaxing and effective hypnosis sessions that focus on & change different aspects of your mindset.

“Hypnosis is a very powerful means of changing the way we use our minds to control our perception and our bodies" - Dr. David Spiegel, Stanford School of Medicine.

Programs teach skills known to help you:
• Focus on feeling good
• Achieve your goals
• Increase your self-awareness
• Feel less anxious
• Understand cognitive distortions

Single sessions to help you:
• Fall asleep in minutes
• Sleep deeply & wake up refreshed
• Increase energy
• Become relaxed
• Decrease stress
• Relieve pain
