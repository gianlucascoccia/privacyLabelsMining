Killer Kreeper , 09/28/2021

Great app, but used to be better

This is, without a doubt, one of the most user-friendly beat makers I’ve ever seen. Everything is nearly organized and easy to find. But being the broke bloke that I am, I only use the free sounds that you start with. However, things used to be better. Up until around July of this year, I was able to go back and add/change tracks on old songs that I previously made. But since then, I’ve been locked out of those old tracks, and an ad for the premium subscription pops up. Now, I can no longer edit the old tracks themselves, or even new tracks once I click out of them for the first time, but I still can edit any effects I put on them. Was this merely a bug left unchecked or an attempt to get more people signed up to the subscription? I hope that its the former, but the latter seems to be the more likely answer. It’s still a good beat maker IMO. Better than GarageBand at least.
