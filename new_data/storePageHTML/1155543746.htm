Description

Corkulous© for macOS and iOS is the incredible way to collect, organize, and share your ideas that feels completely natural. If you have a Mac, iPhone. or iPad, you need this app!

Corkulous is a multi-purpose app containing boards where you can place notes, labels, photos, contacts, shapes, strings/lines connections, pdf's, link previews and tasks. There are also powerful sketch tools, that can create shapes and lines based on gestures. Group your ideas visually on one board or spread ideas out across multiple boards (sub-boards also supported). Each board has plenty of real estate and resolutions to capture your best ideas and plans.

WHAT IS CORKULOUS?

Cork Boards, Flow Charts, Idea Board, Photo Albums, Reminder Board, Mind Map, Goal Manager, Planners, Vision Board, To-do list, Exam Study Aid, Lesson Planner, Photo gallery... and more!

FEATURES

- PDF's, Link Previews, Emoji, Strings/Lines, Notes, Labels, Photos, Contacts, Tasks, Index Cards, Arrow Text, and over 40 shapes
- Sketch tools with shape/line gesture support
- Export and share PDF's, PNG, Text, and Corkulous files
- iCloud and Dropbox syncing of boards. Share boards with other users on dropbox
- The best searching capabilities of any iOS/Mac OS app
- Awesome Drag/Drop support.
- Dark and Light mode support
- Multi window support on iOS/MacOS
- Markdown for text formatting
- Multiple board resolutions
- Pan and zoom boards
- Create templates of boards for future use
- Nested boards! Go as deep as you can
- Biometrics support for locking content
- On the Mac, quick access Touch Bar and menu support
- On iOS/Mac keyboard shortcuts to fill many tasks


SUBSCRIPTION

Corkulous subscription is cross platform! You can work on iOS or macOS with the same subscription. Just tap Restore Purchase on any other device.

The Corkulous subscription will be charged to your credit card through your iTunes account. Your subscription will automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24-hours before the end of the current period. You can cancel the subscription renewal at any time, and the subscription you purchased will remain active until the end of the current period. Manage your subscriptions on the App Store's Account Settings after purchase (http://support.apple.com/kb/ht4098).

Nice little app, but...

I want to love this app not just like it. It has lots of cool little features except for one I consider the most important...being able to handwrite anywhere on anything. It does have one note-sticky that lets you write or draw, but it would be nice if that worked on all the stickies and tags. When I'm using sticky notes, I'm usually moving really fast and loose which means jotting with a pen or pencil, not typing. Also? Could we make it a little easier to change the board names? Maybe my device is malfunctioning but it's only by sheer luck that I manage to get the board to go into "edit mode" on the front screen so I can change its name. A little frustrating. Otherwise, I really love the concept of this app!

sharpening , 07/26/2017

Glad It's Back!

When you buy a real cork board and put stickies on it, eventually it becomes old, worn out, the stickies fade, and over time you get tired of it and move on. Corkulous changes that and gives you plenty of cool useful options to work with corkboards! I understand the pain of using an app that the developers stopped supporting - all of my corkboard apps have ended up in the app grave yard after a few years. I'm glad someone thought enough of Corkulous to bring it back and update it, and cared enough about the user community to change the pricing model so it could be profitable for them to keep it up to date and add new features. Let's be totally 100 - the price for this app even with an annual subscription is more than fair compared to the price of an actual cork board - and you probably won't be carrying many of those around with you every where you go! This app is really useful in a lot of ways, and the new developers deserve some appreciation for resurrecting this app from the big bit bucket and planning so they can keep supporting it in the future. Let's do our part and give them the support they need to update this app since Apple is going to keep changing iOS. If we get some new features along the way, that will be even better!
