Jumpy Horse is:
- One of the first iPad and Game Center apps,
- A MUST HAVE if you are a horse lover!

BREEDING!
Choose from many cool horses and breed new sweet foals. It’s amazing!

GROW UP!
Now your foals can grow up. Not all foals, just the ones that were born in the Breeding game and the ones you painted yourself and are part of your herd. Tap the haystacks to feed the foals. Wait to see your foals grow up!

COMPETITIONS! Jumping! Racing!
New: Multiplayer chat is on - talk to friends and go compete with them riding your own horse. Great gifts! Send some of your horses as gifts to friends and receive horses from their herds in the Horse Exchange.

CROSS COUNTRY!
Design your own Place! You can arrange obstacles to make your own place for jumping. Leave distance between the obstacles. Group some obstacles to make combinations. Save your new place and go jumping with your favorite horse.

BARREL RACING
Be fast and finish first!

LET'S JUMP!
The horse gallops and jumps to throw the cowboy as fast as it can. You can throw the cowboy in a mud pool and make fun of him. Want to clean and saddle Your Horse? Choose English or Western style tack.

60 SECONDS!
Find out how many riders you can throw in 60 seconds.

LET'S PAINT!
Scroll the palettes to find and mix your favorite colors. You can paint the horses, foals, riders, bears and even the wolves.

LET'S GRAZE!
Apples, pears and carrots carry the same points as the grass chunk, but the horse eats them much faster.

RUN WITH THE FOAL!
Protect the cute little foal from the wolfs and bears.

HERD LEADER!
Lead your herd to food and water.

RUN WITH THE HERD!
Gallop free and happy!

CATCH ME!
Do you like Catch Wild Horse mini-game? Now you can play as wild horse and escape from lasso. Stay free and wild!

SAVE YOUR FOREST
The forest is burning! Only you can save your forest!

AWESOME IN-GAME CAMERA
Make super cool custom wallpapers! Share with friends your cool photos in Facebook, blogs and photo galleries!

FEATURES:
- FREE UPDATES. Keep playing! New games are coming! Tell your friends!
- IMPROVE hand eye coordination.
- BONUS 200 amazing horse and nature wallpapers!

Have an idea? Write us your suggestions at support@jumpyhorse.com We love high rating comments!
