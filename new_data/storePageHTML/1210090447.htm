Description

Are you interested of who unfollowed you or hiding stories? Want to know who is your secret fans and who blocked you? Watch stories anonymously? Make repost stories, photos, videos and IGTV? With Social Scan app you can do this and more!

- Who followed or unfollowed you
- Who hiding their stories from you
- Your secret admirers and ghost followers
- Who blocked you
- Who from your following doesn’t follow you and whom from your followers you don’t follow
- Who is the most active user
- Which posts of yours are the most popular
- Most popular filters and hashtags
- Detailed story analytics
- Watch videos and stories in full size with the video player
- Repost Stories/Photos/Videos/IGTV
- Watching of stories anonymously
- Adding media to favorites
- Handy filter for favorites media
- Creating videos from stories
- Profile photo in HD quality
- Search history and recommendations
- Support for multiple accounts

and much more…

NOTE:
Your Instagram account is safe. We do not see or store your password. Authorization takes place on the official Instagram website and is protected by encryption protocol (https) in order to improve security.
