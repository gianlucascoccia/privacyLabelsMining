Love it!

One of the best rpg games i have ever played, with it having a great amount of heroes to collect and enemies to fight. The story is extremely interesting and the bosses very difficult. Pvp is also a challenge and quite fun with all the rewards you can earn besides when it comes world arena- only if you have the best of the best max gear and everything with a functional team you can win… or just might as well have the enemy take a turn with all their heroes 5 times in a row before you can start. Anyways Theres a lot to do but sometimes … there isnt.
You can have a moment where you feel stuck in the game. cant get past a mission or story mode, nor even spend gold or have enough diamonds or summons and honestly its hard to get yourself back up to it at times. Doesn’t help that summons are actually a scam in a way because you can go for months summoning more then a hundred times and get either nothing or the same hero you already had. I even once summoned a hero, earned a 5 star book that taken forever and summoned the same hero in one day and even summoned that same hero once again the next day. Happened 3 times with other heroes getting dupes from long waited summons and at some point it just makes me not want to play if its going to pull that. Theres so many heroes yet the system chooses to give me something i already have. Other then that the game is perfect.
