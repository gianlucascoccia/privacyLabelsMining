Description

BUY AND SELL CRYPTO
Trade over 40 of the most popular cryptocurrencies directly from the app. Just link your bank, add cash to your account, and start trading. All cash you add or receive is held in an FDIC-insured bank account in our partner bank.

LOWEST FEES
Buy Bitcoin, TUSD, Metal (MTL), and Proton (XPR) for 0% fees - the lowest price across the industry. Metal Pay users can also send cryptocurrency to other users for 0% fees as well, making it the most affordable way to move crypto.

NO WAITING
Metal Pay users can buy crypto immediately while their cash deposit is pending, which means no more lengthy wait times as your bank clears the transaction. Get the crypto you want at the price you want it and never miss an opportunity again.

Blueboome , 03/16/2021

Super user friendly, low fees

I’ve used Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, Gemini, and Metal Pay to buy crypto. Metal‘s clean, beautiful design, easy UX, and low fees make it my favorite. If you just want to buy and send cryptocurrencies, this is the app. Registration and connecting my bank account was super fast and easy, sending and receiving crypto is a breeze. If you have questions, their support team is very responsive. Their Proton wallet is great too. I send crypto between Metal Pay and Proton with no fees, while the same transaction between Binance and their official wallet, Trust Wallet, costs an arm and a leg - worse than a traditional bank. I’m looking forward to Metal Pay adding more trading pairs. Then I can just stay within its ecosystem and forget about the crazy fees and clunky payment processes of other platforms.

Torrast , 03/30/2021

Cannot use Metal Pay cash

A new issue. I sold some ETH in the Metal Pay app, cashing it out for Metal Cash. I am able to withdraw these funds by transferring them to my bank if I choose, but when I try to purchase any crypto product, my balance displays as $0. I cannot see any place within the app that shows my balance as not being settled, and I would think I could not withdraw the funds to my bank if they were not settled. What is the point of Metal Pay cash if it is treated differently depending on the transaction type you are attempting to complete? If there is a difference in the way these funds are treated by Metal Pay based on the way the user is attempting to utilize them, the documentation of such handling is poor enough that I have not been able to locate it.

Update: I contacted Metal Pay support and received a response that my funds cannot be used due to a delay with MTL sell orders. This issue does not relate to a MTL sale and I am able to transfer the funds out to my bank if I want to, so this issue does not appear to be relevant to me. I am disappointed that Support would try to assign blame on an issue that does not, on its face, have anything to do with my issue. If somehow it does relate to my issue, the Sell Order outage notice does not indicate such, nor did the feedback from Support. Extremely troubling for a company that wants me to trust them with my money and crypto.

Developer Response ,

Hi Torrast, Sorry to hear about your recent Debit Card replacement experience. Kindly reach out to us at support@metalpay.com, and we'll be more than happy to resolve any outstanding setbacks you may have with the Debit Card feature.
