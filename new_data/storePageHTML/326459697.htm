Description

With the new Air Canada + Aeroplan app, you can now enjoy the advantages of the Aeroplan loyalty program with the combined ease of managing your Air Canada trips in a single convenient place.


EVEN BETTER TOGETHER
Sign in with Aeroplan to unlock access to loyalty program benefits. Dive in to see your points balance, track Elite status progress, view recent transactions, and access popular program features like Family Sharing, eStore, car rental, and hotels.

BOOK YOUR WAY
Book your next trip using cash, or by redeeming your Aeroplan points. You can now also book with a combination of Points + Cash, or even pay 100% of your fare using points, including taxes, fees, and charges.

TIMING IS EVERYTHING
By presenting timely updates on your home screen, you'll always be up to date. Action cards prompt you when it's time to check-in, show your upcoming flight status, and even offers your flight's menu when it's almost time to eat on flights offering meal service.

NOW BOARDING
As your flight approaches, your boarding pass will appear throughout the app for quick access. Your boarding passes are saved for offline use in the app but can also be added to Apple Wallet.

KNOW AS YOU GO
Stay up to date with notifications about flight and gate changes, and cancellations.

FOLLOW THE LEADER
We added turn-by-turn airport navigation, a first for a Canadian airline, available in Toronto (YYZ), Montreal (YUL), and Vancouver (YVR), with more coming soon.

IMPORTANT DISCLOSURES
These functions apply when enabled:
• Location: your location data is used to show the closest airport(s) for booking, and flight status. Location data is also used to present the correct boarding passes at connecting airports, and to provide the current location when using airport maps.
• Wi-Fi connection: used to determine if internet access or a connection is available for onboard Wi-Fi and the wireless entertainment system on Air Canada Rouge flights.
• Calendar: access to your calendar is used to sync flights from your upcoming bookings to your device's calendar.
• Notifications: push notifications are used to send you service messages related to your upcoming travel.
• Camera: add images to the feedback you send to Air Canada.
• Your device and app information (phone model, language, system and app version) is attached to comments you send when an issue is reported via the app.

Developer Response ,

RESPONSE TO AN EARLIER REVIEW (Sep 8, 2020):

Thank you for your feedback regarding check-in and boarding pass.

After you've checked-in (using the app), the boarding pass becomes available in the app and can be found at the bottom of every screen for quick and easy access. Boarding passes stored in the app are also available offline. You shouldn't need to retrieve them through the check-in window.

The app will also automatically attempt to retrieve boarding passes if your booking is saved in the app (in Trips), but you can manually add your boarding pass to the app by following these steps: From 'Home' tab, press the menu button in the top left corner, then press 'Boarding passes'. From the 'Boarding passes' screen, press 'Add boarding pass' and provide your booking reference number and last name, then press 'Add'.

We'll be making some improvements in this area over the next few releases, but in the meantime - let us know if the steps listed above worked for you.

Questions? Write us at mobile@aircanada.ca, and please include "App Store Review from jsonlinter" in the subject line, so that we can follow up.

FOLLOW UP (Oct 5, 2020)

Thank you for your updated feedback regarding the app.

At every app start, the app automatically pulls/fetches the latest information for all bookings in the app, as well as retrieves updates for flight status, and Aeroplan profile if you're logged in. This means that a few seconds after the app starts, it is always showing the latest and correct information for your booking, flights, and profile.

To let you know that everything updated correctly (or not), we display the last updated time on all screens which pull information from these backend systems. Look for the "Updated less than a minute ago" messaging, which usually appears near the top of the screen. If this information cannot be updated, then you'll see a different message, such as "Updated more than an hour ago".

Even with the tips we provided earlier, it sounds like the app isn't working as well as expected for you. We're sorry to hear that. In our earlier reply, we asked that you kindly reach out by email - but we haven't received anything from you yet.
