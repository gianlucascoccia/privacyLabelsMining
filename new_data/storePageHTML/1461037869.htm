Description

Explore a magic castle full of jewels, treasure, and match 3 games! This intriguing puzzle adventure will satisfy your thirst for fun at once!

Discover magical combos by transforming jewels into exploding gems. The gems will help you to pass different levels of the quest and walk you through your mystical path! Thrilling surprises and breathtaking mysteries are waiting for you in this magical world of puzzles!
A bejeweled adventure is about to start! Unlock different levels, practice your skills and become a jewel master!
Begin your jewel journey for FREE and play these addictive match 3 games anywhere & anytime!

HOW TO PLAY
• Match 3 or more jewels in a line to crush them.
• Match 4 jewels to create a Lightning Jewel that can blast all the jewels in a row or column.
• Match 5 jewels in an L shape to create a Jewel Bomb that can blast all the surrounding jewels.
• Match 5 jewels in a T shape to create an X Jewel Bomb that can blast all the jewels on a diagonal.
• Combine 5 jewels in a line to create a Rainbow Jewel that can blast all the jewels of the same color.
• Combine 2 special jewels together to create stunning effects.

SPECIAL FEATURES
- Match 3 games that have been downloaded by over a million users!
- Create all kinds of powerful special boosters and gorgeous magic jewel combinations.
- Hundreds of magic jewel quest levels with weekly updates.
- No WiFi required. Enjoy anytime, even offline!
- No time limit! Play at your own pace.
- Earn daily bonuses and rewards.
- Engage with millions of other players around the world.

It’s very good and hopefully stays that way.

It’s the least frustrating match 3 games I’ve played. I’ve played for 3 months now only bought the intro $2 coin pack, which I usually do for all half decent games. I haven’t really needed to buy anything at lvl 1,000.
There is a 3x daily optional ad for coin+booster, then a daily ad free treasure box, a daily challenge for a treasure box, and if you loose a match then you can watch an ad for a free booster.
I would say the difficulty is average compared to others. There are 2 slightly hard challenges: one is to beat 3 boards on the first attempt and the other is to beat 3 boards scoring 3 stars. I quit a different game that at high lvls all the boards would be impossible to beat without several boosters and it was cumulative so if you lost once you’d start all over. This game has no pressure like that, just watch an optional ad if you want or don’t. It may take a few attempts to beat a hard board but I don’t mind.
My $2 was not wasted. If they had a $4-$9 monthly subscription for daily coins etc, I would pay for that too.
