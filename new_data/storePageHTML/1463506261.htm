Description

TeamLink is one of the world most advanced solutions for video and web conference that enables anyone to work together with teams and partners from anywhere at any time. TeamLink is better than Zoom for free, with no time limit and up to 300 participants.

- The world's most advanced real-time video technology for ultra-low latency and crystal clear video and audio.
- Designed for Mobile and unreliable IP networks with high packet loss resiliency.
- Cross-platform support.
- Ultra high-definition screen sharing and real-time interactions to get work done effectively.
- Global coverage, connect with anyone, from anywhere at anytime.
- Large Scale meetings (Up to 300 participants).
- Meeting Recording and Playback.
- Free download and a free to use.
- Easy to use and you are just a couple of clicks away to start your meeting.

This App Deserves a 5 Star Review

I got a recommendation from a friend by using this app. I hosted an online conference with around 100 people. It turned out an awesome online meeting. forcotton , 03/23/2020

Can we have a silence mode please

First of all, this is a good quality app, video works fine and multiple parties can talk without problem.

I have a use case that I think is popular now with the pandemic situation. We use it for remote piano class and our piano teacher needs to see both the student’s face for conversation and the hand action. We can use 2 devices, but TeamLink does not allow me to turn the volume down to 0 (on one of the device), and this creates an unwanted echo effect.

Developer Response ,

Thank you so much for your feedback. Sorry for the late response. We have added the suggested "Turn sound off" option couple months ago. You can find it under the more icon ( three dots) on the toolbar when you are in a meeting. Please feel free to contact us at support@teamlink.co if there's anything else we can help.
