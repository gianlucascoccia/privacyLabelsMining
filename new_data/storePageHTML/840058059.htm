Description

High-performance portfolio tracker that supports stock, options, mutual fund and cryptocurrency. Super Fast and Easy-to-Setup portfolios. Allow management of multiple portfolios and tracking of the value, performance, profit, loss, and changes in real time with tons of powerful tools and charts. Pre/After market hour included.

Plenty support for trading options. Fast loading options chain, current implied volatility, and advanced options strategies setup with various built-in strategy templates.

It comes with stock price alerts, various portfolio alerts based on portfolio's value, and alerts of news, earnings, dividend, portfolio summary, top mover, SEC filings, insider trading, options expiration of holding positions in portfolio.

You can get very personal with this app: take your own research notes, set password and touch ID for your portfolio access, customized background image for each portfolio, easy to adjust the contents and display order for the holding positions, cloud-based platform to sync your portfolios among multiple devices, and much more!

Plus, this app provides you rich stock research information, e.g. Advanced stock charts with dozens of Technical Indicators, Analyst Rating, Insider Trading, SEC Filings, Extensive Key Financial data, Company Profile, Competitor List, Institutional Holdings, ETF Holding, Short Interest, & many more.

360 degree tracking and research on your existing and future picks, all at your fingertip! This app is a must!

LuckyInvestor , 07/16/2017

Good but could be better

This app has basic functionality and I am impressed that the developer seems to actually read review comments so I am recommending the following improvements:
- The circle/pie charts need to show all holdings in the portfolio and not just show 100% stock. As they are now, they are not helpful to show distribution of holdings.
- The Summary roll-up needs to show the percent holding of each of the portfolios. This is critical for tracking distribution and checking to see when re-balancing might be needed. A circle/pie chart at the summary level would be a nice feature to illustrate the percentage of each portfolio.
- If the stock being tracked is a mutual fund, the dollar amount listed under "Today's profit" should not reflect yesterday's profit. The amount should be zero or a comment saying "waiting for market close."
- News alerts are hard to decipher. This may be a formatting issue. Also many of the alerts seem to not be applicable to any of the holding in my portfolios.
- Need charts for mutual funds
- Kind of cool that stock charts show continuous (after hour) trades but please format the 5 day chart to make it easier to see the start and end of trading for each day.
- Would be nice if the summary page also listed the major stock indices so one can gauge the market and/or make comparisons between portfolios and market performance.
