Description

Well done Amigo, you found us! Welcome to the perks of Cafe Rio loyalty.
Earn free meals, special offers, and the bragging rights that go along with being a Cafe Rio fan!

In addition to earning points for free meals, the Cafe Rio app gives you fast and easy access to our mobile ordering, special offers, location information and more.


Loyalty Benefits:
• Earn 1 point for every $1 you spend. Reach 100 points and unlock $10 toward a free meal.
• Refer a friend and receive 20 points!
• Enable push notifications to receive offers that could earn you free food faster, hear about sneak peek events, and stay connected to new things happening at Cafe Rio.

App Features:
• Rewards: Sign up for our rewards program and get free stuff! Track your rewards and see how close you are to getting your “Free Meal.” Your points are useable with online ordering. Earn points and redeem rewards without having to scan at the register. Simply complete your entire purchase online and pick up at the restaurant.
• Menu: Check out our entire menu complete with pictures and product information. Enjoy speedy, one-click reorder! When you place your order through the app, simply mark your meal as a favorite. Then, when you come back to place another order, just click on your favorite to order it again.
• Location Finder: Easily find Cafe Rio locations with turn-by-turn directions or call us with “one touch” dialing.
• Exclusive Offers: Enjoy special offers only available through our mobile app. Make sure to enable push notifications.

Clint Arthemis Golub , 02/08/2019

Fast-casual perfection! Subpar app :(

Cafe Rio has really been wooing us with the app’s promotional offers like 3x and 2x point days in recent years for what I consider, fantastic food for the price (it’s not organic though). These specials add up and you get those free $10 credits fairly quickly for the free meals, and that gamification totally got us to try most of their meals. My literal only complaint however is how buggy this app is for ordering. If the order is large (multiple meals) or god forbid, you get a phone call or have to leave the app momentarily to check your map or bank account, this app completely resets causing you to have to reorder everyone’s meals from scratch. It’s been like this for some time and I really hope the general reliability, and the ability to “hold” your order in place are addressed. All-in-all I’d give CF an A+ for their excellent customer service (at least here in North Idaho/Eastern WA) and they’ve come a long way with the accuracy of their completion times. There were a few occasions early last year where the meals took an additional 15-20 min past their pick-up times until they were ready. I’d definitely encourage anyone to try them out, and app devs, PLEASE REFINE THIS APP! Hasta Luego 🇲🇽
