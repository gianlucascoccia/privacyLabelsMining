Description

TTop can help you master TikTok with real advice and tips, ranked by a vibrant community of TikTokers. The app shows tips ordered by popularity, allowing you to improve your videos, artistic effects, posting patterns and the way you engage with your followers. The app allows you to submit your own tips, provide feedback for other people's tips, mark if they have worked for you, and build your own list of most liked ideas and tips. To support the development of the app and maintenance of the platform, the app also includes a handy AI-based video hashtag generator that can help you quickly prepare hashtags for your video posts.

Features:
* submit your own tips and tricks
* browse through a list of user-provided tips
* curate your own favorites list
* provide feedback for tips that worked for you
* handy AI-based video hashtag generator
