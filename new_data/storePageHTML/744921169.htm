Description

The free, *official* Radio Free Asia (RFA) mobile and tablet application serves news to your iPad, iPod or iPhone in 10 languages.

The RFA mobile and tablet app features include:
* Customization for 10 international languages and dialects (English, Mandarin, Cantonese, Burmese, Khmer, Korean, Lao, Tibetan, Uyghur and Vietnamese)
* Enjoy photos, video, audio and text content
* Read stories while listening to on-demand and live audio
* Save download time and data costs with the Low-Bandwidth Mode in the settings
* Save content in your favorites for offline reading, watching, and listening
* Customizable navigation items and order
* Social media sharing of content on Facebook, Twitter, email and other platforms

Having problems with the App crashing?
------------------------------------
Please make sure you always update the app to have the most recent, stable version of the application. Turning on automatic updates helps us deliver the new releases automatically to you every time!

Having problems with the App loading slowly?
--------------------------------------
1- Check to make sure you are on a fast, stable connection (WiFi, ideally).
2- If that doesn't work, open the application settings and check the box for "Low Bandwidth Support."

Still having problems?
