Description

Riff is a new social media platform that combines voice and video group chat with music. Join rooms and make new friends who share your interests. Tune in to events like release day parties, virtual concerts, meet and greets, and more. Talk with artists and influencers live and meet other fans in their rooms!

For artists, every stream counts on Riff. If there are 1,000 people in the room, that’s 1,000 streams for every track.

Start a room with friends and Riff on your favorite topics. Customize your room to show your style and add your favorite music. Create group playlists and listen to ad free music together. Livestream your interests in a public room, gain followers and become the next big content creator!


Riff Features:
- Make new friends and connect over shared passions.
- Talk on voice and video chat with friends, followers and artists.
- Add and share your favorite music and listen with friends.
- Meet artists and influencers in live events.

Create:
- Create a public room to meet new people and chat over voice and video.
- Share your passions with friends and followers in custom themed rooms.
- Start a private room and invite your friends to talk about the latest gossip.
- Customize your room with a cool cover and add all your favorite songs.

Discover:
- Attend daily events like concerts and release parties hosted by artists, influencers and other users.
- Find your community and explore different topic rooms to find users with similar interests and passions.
- Connect with users from all over. You never know who will be your next friend!
- Meet “The One” with Music Match and find users with similar music tastes.

Listen:
- Listen with friends to all the latest music for free with no ads, on the day songs are released!
- All group members can add songs when in a room together. Riff makes sure everyone's songs get played.
- Experience synchronized listening as if you were sharing your earbuds with your friends.

Start Riffing today to find your community and become the next big content creator!
