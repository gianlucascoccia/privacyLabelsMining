Description

Braille Skateboarding, the #1 YouTube skateboarding channel in the world, has launched the Braille Skate community app. Find friends and fellow skaters, post your latest tricks, stay up to date with Braille news and more importantly LEARN TO SKATE!!!

What’s New

Nov 7, 2021

Version 2.0

The new and improved Braille Skate app is here and we can't wait to introduce it to you! The new and streamlined look puts the content first and foremost while still including all the aspects of the app that people love. We really hope you love it as much as we do. Enjoy!

Features Added:
- New "Home" screen layout allows you to see the full Braille Army+ library
- A new sidebar and bottom tab bar allows for easy navigating around the app
- A sleek new "dark" theme is easier on the eyes and better for consuming video content
- Added ability to store video progress so you can pick up right where you left off
- New artwork and video descriptions to better organize shows
