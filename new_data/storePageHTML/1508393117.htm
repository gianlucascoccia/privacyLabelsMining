Description

Fitter offers personalized at home workout programs for various goals – weight loss, muscle gain, getting fit, and more. Choose the perfect plan for you and join Fitter challenges to compete with other users!

Challenging workouts

Up for a challenge? Choose a workout and compete with thousands of Fitter users. The faster you complete the workout, the higher you’ll be on the leaderboard!

Personalized workout plans

Explore workout plans to find the perfect routine for your goal. Whether you want to lose weight, get fit, or build muscle – all you need is a 4-week plan of highly effective exercises.

Weight tracker

Track your weight loss progress and celebrate achievements!

Step tracker

Every step counts! Set daily walking goals and see how many calories you burned throughout the day.

Water tracker

Proper hydration is essential for weight loss, overall health, and energy levels. To form this incredibly beneficial habit, start setting goals and track your daily water intake.

Our app also integrates with HealthKit to track your steps data and calories burned.

App needs work

The exercise plan is good for me at 52 and having not been doing much in the way of exercise for the last 2 years. Need to lose about 20#.
The app is ok. You have to constantly touch the phone which is a pain. There is zero transition time between exercises. Finish 30 seconds of squats and the clock immediately starts for a 15 second side plank. Takes me 5 seconds to get to the position. I assume you are supposed to pause it after each exercise until you get in position but what a pain that is. Plus half the time the app is paused the gif to doesn’t restart just the clock.
I think with a few simple changes to the app I’d give it 5 stars instead of 3. I’d like to touch the app at the beginning and not touch it until the end unless I want to pause it. Give me a few seconds between exercises to transition.

Sassystarrr , 06/03/2021

Don’t get this app

Purchased the work out plan and the fitness coach program. I received an email within a couple of days of payment telling me I would have a coach call me within 2 to 3 days. After 2 1/2 to 3 weeks, I still had not received a coach call so I emailed customer service. I told them I wanted my coach call within the end of the month or I wanted a refund. I still didn’t hear from anybody until two weeks later. The month had already ended. The email was from somebody new telling me they couldn’t find my profile and asking me for my information again. Once I provided the information they needed, they issued me a refund. Their reason for the horrible customer service was the overwhelming response that they were receiving. My review on the app itself, the work out plan, is not good. The videos have no sound or notification bell of when the exercise is ending and when to transition to a new exercise. The order of the exercises is difficult, too, because they’ll have you do two or three standing exercises then do a floor exercise then standing exercise then back to the floor. My whole experience with this app is just completely underwhelming.
