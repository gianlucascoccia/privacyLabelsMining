Description

Step into the Next Dimension of Islamic Learning - With Ali and Sumaya School...Now in 3D! Your child can truly enjoy learning Islam while playing a Game :)

What is The Ali and Sumaya School?
It's the Worlds First Interactive Online Islamic Learning platform for Primary aged Children

The School utilises the same proven methods of our 3D animated Videos which have helped to educate over 200 thousand children around the world!

Now we aim to take learning to the next level…

With over 100 lessons and learning activities awaiting your child. It's the most interactive, Islamic learning programme online!

Our School features:
Animated Content and teachers - Learn with our adorable characters
Flexible study schedules - Study when and want you want
Individual Tracking - Monitor unto 5 Student's Progress
Badges and Certificates - Amazing Rewards
Global leaderboard - Compete with students around the world

To make learning fun and easy we’ve created Bitesize Interactive lessons, that help children to learn by themselves and at their own pace!

Learning becomes Super Simple...
Step 1- Watch video
Step 2 – Practice Exercise
Step 3 - Take Quiz to test knowledge.

Our Courses
*Reading Arabic - From Alphabets to reading the Holy Qur’an
*Wudu - Follow and learn how to make Wudu perfectly
*Salah - Learn the full prayer step-by-step
*Daily Duas - Learn all the Duas a muslim needs to read on a daily basis
*Juzz Amma - Memorise all 36 Surah in the last chapter of the Holy Qur’an with the world famous reciter Sheikh Muhammad Jibril
*99 Names of Allah - Learn all about Allah and get closer to him!

You see education, your child see’s fun.

With NEW COURSES and lessons UPDATED regularly, your child will never run out of things to learn insha’Allah.

And you can access your account from any smartphone or tablet, anytime anywhere in the world...and pick up right where you left off.

And with numerous recommendations by Islamic scholars, you can be confident you are giving your child the most authentic and fun online learning experience available.
