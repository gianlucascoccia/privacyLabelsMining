Description

The Speed Queen app makes it easy to pay for a washer or dryer right from your phone. No more last-minute searching for quarters or wondering if machines are available.

Enter a location, view machine availability, pay from your phone and you are ready to go. We’ll even let you know when your laundry is almost finished. Your best laundry day starts now.

LittleKA , 03/15/2019

Be Wary

This app is great when it works, but as of late, it’s been glitchy. If you’re going to use it, I would keep a laundry card on hand just in case. A week or two ago, it would not connect to any of the machines; they came and reset the connection and it was fine. But now it just took me 3 attempts to start a washer, and it charged me for 2 of those attempts instead of the last one (which worked). Their customer service was good the last time, so I am hoping they can credit my account. I really like being able to see if machines are open before going to the laundry room and the alerts when your wash is done, so I’d recommend trying it out...just don’t throw away your laundry card.

Millyjazz , 09/24/2021

Good luck if you need a refund

I thought this would be a good tool to wash clothes at the laundromat around the corner from my home but good luck if you need a refund. After my first wash I accidentally hit the pay now button again which I didn’t want to do. It’s confusing because it doesn’t go right back to your account screen after a wash is done. This makes it very confusing to you. After going back-and-forth with the app representative they can’t do anything on there and if you need a refund. You would have to contact the owner and nine times out of 10 sometimes the owners are not at the laundromat. I just chucked it up and someone is going to have a free wash on me. In this economy when people are trying to save this app is doing no good if you cannot cancel a wash via the app. Which I find very weird if you can start a wash but can’t cancel a wash via the app. They need to work on this .I’m deleting for now

RickyDaNerd , 09/29/2019

Steals Your Money From Time to Time

I’ve used this app because I don’t really have any other choice since I live on a college campus and this is the only way to pay for laundry. Every now and then, I receive errors that a payment didn’t go through and that “I wasn’t charged” but when I check my recent transactions, it shows I was still charged for that load and it can happen multiple times in a row. What’s even worse is that whenever I try to click on the support button that is in the app, it says “no email is registered with this account”... but you have to have an email to log in to the app in the first place, so what exactly is it talking about not having an email registered??? It’s been frustrating and I’ve lost a good amount of money due to this issue. It has been an ongoing issue for quite some time now and it’s unfortunate that there hasn’t been a fix about this.
