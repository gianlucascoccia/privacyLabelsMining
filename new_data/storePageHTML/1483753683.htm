It’s okay...

It does have a very good selection of books and most are rather interesting, HOWEVER they are obviously translated through a machine and without proofreading. The grammar is off wrong pronouns are frequently used, words have the wrong translation making them extremely out of place and makes it hard to understand some sentences as well things like nicknames are occasionally translated as words instead of names. The wrong tense is also occasionally used however that is much less difficult to understand. I sincerely hope that the app fixes these problems as I generally do enjoy reading on this app and I find many of its stories interesting. Other than that it has a very similar system of payment and collection to other apps like this, and it’s pretty reasonable. The app also has different brightness color and other similar settings for the pages. I do wish it had a flip through option though I don’t think that is heartbreaking. It’s also a little more difficult to browse than I would like but I have seen worse. The interface overall is great again my only problem is the translations but it really does interfere to an annoying degree.
