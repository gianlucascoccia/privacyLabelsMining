Description

Driving for Uber, Lyft, Juno, or delivery services, such as DoorDash, Instacart or Uber Eats? Optimize your work with the help of Rydar.

Rydar 2.0 is an AI-based assistant app for rideshare and delivery drivers. Driving for multiple services? Rydar will solve all your pain points: assist you with finding high-demand areas, suggest big events and simplify mileage tracking for tax deduction.

MAXIMIZE EARNINGS
Our unique algorithm analyzes multiple factors and uses Artificial Intelligence to show you high-demand areas to drive at the moment and send alerts about the hottest places.

OPTIMIZE DRIVING SCHEDULE
Rydar keeps you informed about all significant events going on around you. Never miss a chance to drive all those people home after a show or a big game.

TRACK BUSINESS MILEAGE
Stay aware of how much you earn driving for different ride-hailing and delivery services, compare your hourly rates. Easily keep track of your earnings and expenses and automatically log mileage.

FOR ALL SERVICES
Rydar works for rideshare (Uber, Lyft, Juno, Curb, Via, Bounce, etc.) and delivery services (Uber Eats, GrubHub, Instacart, Postmates, Doordash, etc.)

Features
• Hotspots: Areas of high demand based on surge/Prime Time, history of trips in your area, drivers and popular places nearby
• High-demand alerts: Notifications about the hottest places to drive at the moment
• Surge/Prime Time: Info and alerts about increased Uber surge and Lyft Prime Time rates for hotspots
• Status control: Easy switching between services for mileage tracking and dynamic pricing alerts
• Calendar: Upcoming concerts and festivals, sports and theatrical events for the next 30 days
• Schedule: Your customized schedule of popular events, alerts when it’s time to drive
• Mileage tracker: 100% automatic tracking, precise capture and breakdown by service
• Expense tracker: Easy logging of all rideshare-specific expenses
• Earnings tracker. Add your earnings, tips and bonuses to control finances and compare your hourly earnings
• Services comparison: Up-to-date info about your earnings and hourly rates by rideshare/delivery services in Dashboard.
