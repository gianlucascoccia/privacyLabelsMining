Description

Ever wanted to know more about the boats and ships you see? Boat Watch Pro lets you spot boats and ships and follow them on a real-time map wherever you are...

If you are interested in a particular vessel, Boat Watch Pro can send you alerts when a boat you track sets sail or arrives - perfect for people with loved ones at sea, for tracking ferries and rescue vessels - or following cargo across the world!

If you're in port or at the marine, point your camera at a boat and Boat Watch Pro's amazing augmented reality mode will instantly tell you what it is and where its headed!


Boat Watch Pro offers...

* Spot any Boat or Ship via real-time AIS
* See the Name, Type, Photo, Origin, Destination and track of vessels
* Set any boat as your favorite, and automatically follow it on the map
* Get alerts when your favorite boat arrives or departs
* Search and find boats and places
* Easily identify vessels with in-built Augmented Reality (AR) display
* Shows ActiveCaptain points of interest markers
* Includes Apple Watch app
* Share boats on Facebook, Twitter or by email
* OpenSeaMap charts showing buoys, lighthouses and shipping lanes

Fleet upgrade ( subscription):
* Fleet - set and receive arrival and departure alerts for multiple boats

Love this app!

We live in central Minnesota and vacation in Duluth MN on Lake Superior several times a year. We love watching the ships enter and depart the canal under the lift bridge. This app lets us see which ships are in the area and the alert feature lets me know when a certain ship has pulled up its anchor and will be moving through. We use it every day to sneak peaks at the action on the webcams set up in Canal Park while we are at home. I also like checking out the busy ports around the Great Lakes to see our favorite ships and where they are. App works great and the one time I had a problem I emailed them and they fixed it within a day. Worth every penny and more!
