Description

Hope is a guided Christian meditation app that combines the techniques of mindfulness meditation with Christian principles!

Hope is the best way to find peace and happiness right where you are! Our fun and inviting style makes meditation simple. And if meditation isn't your thing, we have story-telling podcast-style content, nature sleep sounds, and relaxing music! We also offer free daily Bible verse push notifications, daily motivational quotes, and mini devotional notifications!

Learn to live a life of intention, not just reaction, and experience God’s love in your life. If you are new to meditation, that's okay! Our free Foundation package will teach you the basics of Christian meditation. Whether you are struggling to sleep at night or wanting to start your day off on the right foot, Hope can help!

Our Premium Meditation Topics Include:
Rain sounds, relaxing music, daily morning & bedtime meditations, podcasts, guided sleep exercises, positive affirmation meditations, and emotion-specific meditations on topics such as stress, anxiety, disappointment, finding your purpose, loneliness, self-esteem, happiness, depression, and many more.

Why Hope?
- Get the physical benefits of meditation, with the added benefit of growing your faith.
- It is easier to fit into a schedule than reading a daily devotional.
- It makes connecting daily with God a fun, easy, & accessible experience.
- We offer a different mediation style than any other Christian or meditation app on the market.

I finished the Foundations and as soon as my next paycheck came, bought the year subscription. Not going to lie, that dollar amount hurt. But it’s worth it.
