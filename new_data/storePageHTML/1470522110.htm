Description

Order a famous made-from-scratch Casey’s pizza, for carryout or delivery, just the way you like it. Browse our menu, see all the best deals, customize your order, and get it fast!


Join Casey’s Rewards to start earning points for the things you buy at Casey’s – in-store, online, at the pump, and over the phone. Just scan your QR code at the register or use your phone number to get credit.

Earn 10 points for each $1 you spend on food and drinks (some exclusions apply). Each gallon at the pump is worth 5 points. Then you can decide how you want to use your points: for Casey’s Cash, fuel discounts, or as a donation to a school of your choice.

Download the Casey's app and sign up for Casey’s Rewards today! You’ll be glad you did.

App doesn’t recognize address for new orders

When I enter my address manually to place a new order, the app tells me there is no store that delivers to this address. So, I have called my orders in & they have been delivered to that same address without issue of location (I am in town by the way).
If I want to order from the app, I am able to select “reorder” from any number of previous orders, with no issues with my address, as it is saved under previous orders. However, if I want to change my order up & have something different, I have to select a reorder, order the old item(s), then order the new item(s) I want, & then delete the reordered item(s) I actually don’t want to reorder. What is up with this???
I am also able to use a certain card to pay when in store but not over the phone or online with that particular card. (To be fair, this MAY be on the financial institution’s end too. I am unsure but, have been told others have had the same issue. I just use a different card & have not looked further into that issue) I have heard that others complain of the same issue too though, just FYI.
Other than these minor, more annoying than upsetting issues, the app is just fine.
