Description

CalenDo: Your Life, Organized Seamlessly. CalenDo is a simple planner with To-Do lists, Calendar, Reminders and Task Manager that helps you de-cluttered your daily life.

You can plan and review your day with "Today View" and with “Calendar View” you can view and plan an upcoming day, week or month in advance.

TO-DO LISTS FEATURES:
• Organize your tasks and event in different categories (e.g. Home, Work, Chores etc.).
• Quickly add a task or schedule an event from right inside the list.
• Share the list with your family members, friends or co-workers.
• Assign a task to a list member.
• Never forget anything with due date and multiple reminders.
• Add subtasks, notes, and attachments to your tasks.

CALENDAR FEATURES:
• Automatically syncs with all your calendars.
• Every list can act as a calendar in which you can add your tasks and schedule your events.
• Today view to help you plan and review your day.
• Easily view tasks and events for the upcoming day, week and months.
• Schedule events with other list members, great for family and small teams.
• Add notes and attachments to your events.

MORE FEATURES:
• Get repeating reminders for tasks and events.
• Custom repeat options e.g., every 2 days, weeks, months or years.
• Search across your tasks and events.
• Communicate with others using comment (e.g. Running late notice).
• Automatically sync your work across all your devices.

CalenDo is free but you can always upgrade to premium to access all premium features for $2.99/month or $29.99/year using auto-renewing subscription.

Payment will be charged to iTunes account as soon as you confirm the purchase. The subscription will be automatically renewed by App Store after 1 month or 1 year at least 24 hours before the end of the subscription period unless auto-renew is turned off. Subscription may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to user's account settings after purchase.

2 small things

Alright. So let’s start off with the good. It is a really good app. It’s very simple to use, you can customize it in many different ways, and it sends you reminders. The only two problems that I have with it are very small. The first is that the app crashes. If you so to “list” (third button on the bottom from the left) in the app, there’s 3 dots in the top right corner. I have never been able to press on these dots. I still have no idea what they do. I think it’s just my curiosity getting the better of me because the lack of use of the three dots has not seemed to prohibit my use of the app in any way. The other problem I have is when you create an event, you choose what category the event goes into and it color codes it. Well once you’ve added a category, you can’t delete it (to my knowledge). I haven’t figured out how to delete it. Maybe it’s those three dots. I guess we’ll never know but it would be nice to limit my categories just to make it neater and nicer.
