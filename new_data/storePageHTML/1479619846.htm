Description

Replace your sign-in sheets, customer surveys, client intake forms, and more with Form Builder Pro. Create custom forms that you, your employees, clients, prospective customers, and sales leads can fill out right on your iPad or iPhone. No internet connection required. Open your form with an attention catching slideshow. Capture responses to questions as well as signatures and camera photos. Form Builder Pro is great for:

- Capturing leads at trade shows and career fairs
- Registering guests for events
- Creating open house sign in sheets for real estate agents
- Taking attendance at meetings and trainings
- Logging visitors at office reception desks
- Collecting field information for mobile professionals like photographers, inspectors, and salespeople.

View responses on your device or export your data in CSV format to use in other apps and online services. With an internet connection, configure your form to email you when a new response is submitted. Form Builder Pro also integrates with Contactually allowing you to have form results sent directly to your CRM.

Start creating great-looking forms and capturing valuable data with Form Builder Pro.
