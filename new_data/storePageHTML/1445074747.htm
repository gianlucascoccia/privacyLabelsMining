Description

Now enter the furniture store and help craft items. Can you find the rainbow lights or the bunkbed? Kids use the 100s chart but must watch out for the moving robots!

Teachley: Market Bay is a unique 3D world where children are immersed in doing math everywhere they go! They can buy and sell objects at the Marketplace, visit local shops to purchase or craft new items, collect and spend coins, customize their own character and home while completing daily quests. This fun and engaging world will have kids spending hours deeply engaged in math!

We can’t wait for you to come visit us in Market Bay! We’re confident children are going to love it and become even better mathematicians. See why 2nd grade teacher, Laura D. in Maine says,

"Wow, Market Bay is wildly popular with my second grade students. Thank you so much! They are just loving it!"

As with all of Teachley's games, Market Bay is designed by teachers and informed by Learning Sciences research to ensure our instructional approach and rich scaffolds align with how to best support math learning. Our visual and auditory feedback help children master concepts and be confident in math. Teachley's games are intrinsically rewarding and motivating for kids.

ABOUT TEACHLEY:
Teachley, LLC is an award-winning edtech company founded by expert teachers with PhDs in cognitive science and supported by the U.S. Department of Education, National Science Foundation, National Institutes of Health, and non-profit organizations. Teachley is transforming teaching and learning through research-based math games with formative assessment data to help schools and districts drive instruction and target interventions. Based on cognitive science research, Teachley’s apps focus on teaching important strategies shown to improve learning within fun and engaging games. www.teachley.com
