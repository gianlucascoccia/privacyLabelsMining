Not A Fake Review :) , 04/11/2019

This one is better than any of the other private photo album apps on the AppStore.

This App works great on the iPhone. It does not work well on the iPod Touch. When going to upload photos into the App, nothing shows. It’s just blank. When I do upload the photos, the photos are too zoomed out. I think the app needs to be resized for the iPod Touch. I’d also suggest changing the name of the app since InCalc does not really make sense, unless of course the app icon was a calculator or it had a calculator function. Other than that, all the features are great. I’d also suggest changing the color layout to look more modern or more like the Apple photo albums than the plain blue colors. Overall, the app is awesome. This is just my criticism. Please fix the layout though, so it works for the iPod Touch. I gave five stars cause it actually deserves it. It’s not like the others that require payment, In-App purchases for basic features, or have very limited functionality. This one truly is better than any of the others. Great job developers.
