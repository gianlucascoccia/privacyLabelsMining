Description

Resultina covers ATP, WTA, ITF, ITF juniors and Tennis Europe tennis results. All info in one app.

Select your favourite players and Resultina does the rest. It delivers results that YOU are interested in right to YOUR phone.

You will get a notification:
- every time one of your favourite players finishes a match,
- when the draw with your players has been published,
- when a match of your favourite player that has started.

But there's much more
- LIVESCORE ATP, WTA, ITF
- See matches of your players (completed and scheduled)
- Browse results, draws, player activities and even head to head stats
- See interesting stats highlights
- See ATP, WTA, ITF, Tennis Europe rankings and tournaments calendars
