Description

With this fun math game, get your SKIDOS Pass to 40+ learning apps without ads for 2-11-year-olds. 4 million kids use SKIDOS to learn math by playing games they love.

Welcome to SKIDOS!

With our unique learning games, make learning fun for your 2-11-year-olds. SKIDOS games help kids practice their math skills & develop an interest in coding logic. You can get a new SKIDOS Pass or log in with an existing one from within this app. All SKIDOS Passes are auto-renewable with monthly, quarterly, bi-annual & annual Passes for up to six kids. So with one SKIDOS Pass, you can add up to 6 accounts for different ages (2-11-year-olds) & measure their progress individually. Each account can be customized according to their grade (Preschool, Kindergarten, 1st-5th grade) & the learning style of your kids.

SKIDOS Doctor is a fun role-playing game that will let your child play a doctor and treat patients. Your child can try out different medical tools and help 3 cute characters to get healthy again. Whether they have a running nose, a hurting ear, a broken bone or toothache - we can easily cure them.

The Game has 5 scenes:
• Treat the flu
• Cure ears
• Take care of teeth
• Get an X-ray
• Clean the wounds

SKIDOS Doctor game is a great way to teach kids how and when to use a thermometer, nose drops & ear drops, plasters & disinfectants, how to brush teeth & much more.

SKIDOS Doctor game features:
- Learn how to treat common diseases
- Get familiar with doctor tools and medicine
- Play with 3 cute characters
- Learn numbers, counting & coding
