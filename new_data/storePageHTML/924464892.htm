Description

The Retro Fitness app provides class schedules, social media platforms, fitness goals, and in-club challenges. Our app will also allow you to link many of the popular fitness tracking devices and fitness apps on the market. It uses HealthKit to save your workouts, so they can contribute to your fitness goals and challenges progress.

Have a comment or question? Email our team directly at digitalsupport@egym.com.

Easy to use Great App

I’ve been using this app for about 4 months since I started and I love racking up points! Easy to track workouts and set goals. Every month there’s new challenges like # of check-ins or Calories burned to get entered in gift cards or months free. Might as well because you’re going to the gym in the first place! Also deals every month like $1 off smoothies.
Definitely get this if you’re a member!

Great App / Gym

Love this app. I’m able to keep track of work outs and record them easily. They have challenges that allow you to earn points to use at their pro store. You can also earn points by creating goals and achieving them. The app is easy to navigate and user friendly. It also allows you to check in to the gym without having to carry your card.
