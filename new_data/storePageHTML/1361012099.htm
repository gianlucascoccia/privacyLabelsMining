Description

Create your perfect look with Facelab! Smooth skin, remove blemishes, reshape face, whiten teeth, change your hair color, and more. The best touch-up tools for selfies!

EASY-TO-USE & SMART WITH EVERY SELFIE
Facelab knows how to hide your flaws and play up your beauty. It recognizes your face, scans it to detect your issues, and takes care of them. It’s your go-to set of powerful tools for any edits!

SKIN
– Smooth and even out your skin tone and texture.
– Get rid of unwanted wrinkles and laugh lines.
– Remove pimples, spots, acne marks and scars.
– Reduce oily shine and make your skin matte.
– Freshen your face with a healthy glow.
– Use Patch to hide any flaws and imperfections.

LIPS & SMILE
– Whiten your teeth for a pearly bright smile.
– Get fuller, bigger, plumper lips.
– Adjust and lift the corners of your mouth.
– Make your natural lip color more saturated or try a new fun color.

EYES
– Tweak the size and shape of your eyes.
– Remove puffy eyebags and dark circles.
– Make the whites of your eyes whiter, brighter and more sparkly.
– Make your gaze more enticing by deepening your eye color.

NOSE
– Tweak the size and shape of your nose.
– Edit any part of your nose from top to bottom.
– Make your nose slimmer and smaller.
– Adjust the tip of your nose.

FACE SHAPE
– Sculpt, firm up and reshape your face.
– Make your face more oval or round by adjusting its height.
– Slim your face and jaw to bring out your cheekbones.
– Sharp your chin and refine your jawline.
– Change the pose of your face.

MAKEUP
– All popular lipstick shades: glossy/matte red, pink, burgundy, peach, etc.
– Contour palettes: get higher cheekbones, slimmer nose and chin, etc.
– Breathe life into pale skin by adding a rosy glow with blush.
– Eyeliners and shadows for a drop-dead gaze.

HAIR COLOR
– Try on different hairstyles and shades of black, brown, red, and blonde.
– Experiment with vibrant, bold colors and color mixes.

FILTERS & EFFECTS
– Set the right mood with a variety of atmospheric filters.
– Try portrait filters to get the most charming look.
– Adjust your skin tone with special skin filters.
– Add gleams, flashes, and bokeh effects with Light FX.
– Liven up still photos and make them 3D.

FINE-TUNE
– Crop with pre-set sizes.
– Rotate and skew your photo the way you like.
– Adjust the color, light, tone of an image.
– Bring yourself into focus with Vignette.
– Make your photos crisp and clear.
