Description

Izneo BD Comics Manga Webtoon is the best app for comics on your smartphone, with thousands of digital comic books. Welcome in the largest virtual library!

• Comics, graphic novels, manga, webtoon for free or at crazy prices

Enjoy thousands of comics, chapter and preview for free. You won't find better prices for your comics than at izneo (feel free to compare!). We also have special offers every week with rock-bottom prices.

• Enjoy your HD comics on all screens

Smart TV, laptop, tablet, smartphone: your comics are synchronized in your library and available at any time, anywhere, making it easy to pick up where you left off.
A convenient and pleasurable reading experience to enjoy comics as you always have done: on the train or on the bus, in your couch or in bed, and even offline within our free apps!

• Discover the richest catalog of digital comics!

Do you like to explore worlds, discover new stories and share the emotions of your favorite heroes? We do too!

Access the most comprehensive range of European comics, US comics, Webtoon, Mangas, Graphic novels and children's comics, available in digital format. Discover new comics and rare reprints in the Highest Definition possible… And because you shouldn't judge a book by its cover, you can browse the first few pages of all our comics for free. You don't even have to create an account to do so!!

• Explore all kind of comic book genres like never before; Adventure, Romance, Thriller, Heroic Fantasy, Sci-Fi, Comedy…

• Discover a new way to enjoy reading

With a unique and customizable player, you can adapt your reading experience to suit your taste and situations. Enjoy your comics with the panel by panel mode called eazycomics, for the most comfortable reading experience possible! You will be amazed by how good your comics look on the big screen so don't forget to launch izneo in dock mode!

Needs option to archive books purchased

Nice app to read french-language comics but (1) the app lacks an option to archive books purchased, which is a major flaw. We need that option. Right now it is impossible to archive (or delete) a book purchased, meaning books one does not care about keep cluttering one’s library, which is a hassle. Comixology has the option to archive books purchased; (2) more than once I have telecharged a book only for that book to not load correctly when I want to read it; (3) the March 15 update took away the “forthcoming” listings, which is unfortunate; and (4) in the “my series” tab, when I get to the last book listed and keep pulling down on my screen the last book listed repeats multiple times; this should be fixed.
Note: The developer is acknowledging feedback. Hence I am adding one star. I look forward to future updates addressing the aforementioned issues.

Hello, thank you for comment, we noted your feeedback and we will investigate, we are sorry for problem on wish list and "My series" our teams are informed, do not hesitate to contact our teams at support@izneo.com, thank you for support and we stay at your diposal. Regards.

Khodadadr , 03/24/2020

Patchy quality

It’s a great app and the selection is phenomenal. Living in a non-BD country, it is a lifesaver for accessing and discovering all the comics I want. The quality of the material is undeniable. But the app is clunky and too heavy. It tajes too long to load sometimes, frequent errors when trying to check out the catalogue, and moving between pages often sends you to the top of the page where you have to start from the beginning. Also, occasional download errors, slow speed of albums loading up. Last is the recent move toward being monolingual, the understanding being that if you read English, you cannot manage French and vice versa. So now every time I want to see the French titles, I have to go and change the app language. In general, excellent material, could be more user friendly!

Developer Response ,

Hello and thank you for your opinion, we have taken your comments into account and we thank you, we are doing our best to improve the application and the comfort of the readers, our teams are at your service at support@izneo.com, good read and see you soon.

mynameisgroot , 05/24/2021

Simply unusable

I’ve been a customer of Izneo for years and must have purchased hundreds of books through their services. They have a great catalog available across a wide variety of genre.
Most unfortunately, the quality of their app and website has always been poor on iOS, yet manageable with a bit of patience. However, their latest update finally ended up breaking everything.
I use an iPad Pro and am running the latest version of iPadOS. I can no longer read most of the comics I’ve purchased on the app. A black screen with a loading spinner appears when starting a book or after a couple of pages, freezing the application, whether the book has been previously downloaded or not. Best case scenario, you’ll be able to read normally the first 10 pages until an obvious memory management issue causes the entire app to slow down, causing scrolling and changing pages to no longer work.
So ultimately you end up purchasing books that you cannot read. And these are only the major bugs, many common simple features like sorting your library are awfully buggy.

Attempting to use the website is also useless on iPad, give it a shot and judge for yourself before you purchase anything.

I’ve sent a number of emails to the customer support email address and haven’t heard back from them a single time.

This app should be banned from the app store until they hire a proper developer and QA team that can fix these issues. Selling goods that cannot be consumed is illegal...

Finally, the Izneo website does not seem to present a problem and we would be very interested to hear from you with examples and information. We will do all we can to help you and provide you with solutions. Thank you in advance. Regards
