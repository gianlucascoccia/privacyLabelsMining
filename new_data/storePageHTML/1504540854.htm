Description

All the resources a stepmom needs, right at her fingertips. Choose either monthly or annual subscription plan. 7-day free trial. Cancel any time.

The first mobile app for stepmoms, by stepmoms. The Stepmomz app connects you with a private community of stepmoms, just like you. Staffed by professional and experienced stepfamily coaches, you'll get the answers to all your questions. Learn from self-paced courses, or join a group and take a course together, led by a certified coach. The app includes a library of exclusive content and self-care resources to help you build confidence in your role and strength in your relationships.

* Community groups and forums
* Direct messaging
* Questions and Answers database
* Self-care resources
* Professional coaching
* Exclusive library of content
* Desktop access

Choose from either monthly or yearly auto-renewal subscription plan. 7-day free trial. Cancel any time.
