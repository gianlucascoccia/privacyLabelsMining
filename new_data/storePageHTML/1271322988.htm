Description

Sortifyd is an all-in-one organizer app - create tasks, assign chores, share lists, access important documents on-the-go, and record journal entries and photo memories!

Sortifyd helps you organize your life and home on-the-go! Create lists, assign to dos, share files with your family, save health information, and more!

Organize your life or add your family to create a command center for everything your family relies on daily. Use shared lists to assign chores or to dos, share files and passwords, or create a record of your family’s adventures with the journal and memory timeline!

Password protect your health and other documents, modify access for each user on your account and keep certain things private for yourself. Access documents easily on the go. Organize your loved one’s medications, medical history, documents, appointments, caregiver information and more, anytime, anywhere.

Families of all sizes will love using Sortifyd to organize, from young professionals on the go, to busy families, to couples, and even empty nesters traveling the world!

Download now and start organizing your life!

Lists & Planner:
• Checklists keep you organized day-to-day, weekly, monthly and more!
• The calendar will help you organize and create events that work for the whole family. You can even import events from your other calendars into your shared calendar in Sortifyd.
• Get event, task and expiring document reminders (for driver’s license, passport, etc.)
• Chores can be assigned to specific family members
• List templates will help you organize quickly and easily

Organize Documents:
• Your account is password protected to keep documents, receipts and your other personal and private information secure
• Organization is easy with predefined folders and categories (like a virtual filing cabinet)
• Information is at the ready for any emergency: insurance cards, automobile records, health records, etc.
• Pull up documents easily on the go with keyword search

Passwords & Security:
• Keep your data secure in this vault where it is always fully encrypted and can be decrypted only on your authorized devices
• Organize and customize user access and control who has access to what
• Touch/Face ID allows secure and convenient access to the app
• Be notified of any changes your family makes to shared information
• Records and files are securely backed up on the cloud
• Password sharing lets you share password information with family members

Family Health & Wellness:
• Family organizer keeps everyone’s documents safe, secure and easy to access
• Organize your family’s health information from eyeglass prescriptions to medical conditions
• Save vaccination records, allergies, doctors, medical conditions, visits and more
• Keep track of your pet’s vet visits and vaccination records by adding your pet to your account

Journal & Memory Timeline:
• Add your favorite photos to create an organized timeline of your family’s memories
• Journal and record your favorite memories and moments, all in one place.
• Set some memories and journal entries private if you want to keep others in your account from viewing them

Travel:
• List and share your vacation or travel itinerary with you family
• Add notes on important hotel and flight details
• Add reward cards, passports, and account information

Download Sortifyd today and start organizing your family life!
