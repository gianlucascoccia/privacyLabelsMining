Description

Make precious moments last forever with SlidePic! An unforgettable trip to the mountains, a warm cozy family dinner, or a fun crazy night with friends... Liven up your memories and turn them into lovely slideshow stories!

FEATURES

- Create a perfect slideshow video in minutes
- Mix photos and videos, as many as you want
- Trim, split, cut and edit the length of your slides
- Make your slideshow smooth with creative transitions
- Choose the right music to fit your video
- Templates for any mood and occasion
- Edit easily: rotate, zoom, crop, flip, enhance
- Add some magic with amazing filters and overlays
- Share your slideshows or save them to your gallery
- Super quick & easy, no fuss
