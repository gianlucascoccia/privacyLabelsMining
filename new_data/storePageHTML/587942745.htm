Description

DFW’s official mobile app is the perfect addition for your next trip. With the app, you can:

· View flight information and receive notifications on flight updates

· Prepay for parking and save up to 50% off

· Know before you go by viewing security checkpoint wait times

· Browse through more than 200 shops, dining options, and services

· Pre-order your next meal from participating restaurants inside the terminals

· Use our interactive map with turn-by-turn, detailed directions

Great Ap

I don’t normally leave an airport if I have connecting flights. I had a 6 hour layover at DFW and my Daughter-in-law and Granddaughter came to hang out with me. I was a little nervous not knowing which terminal I would have to return to. I asked this wonderful Airport worker to help me. Her name was Maureen and she was at the exit door concourse C. She told me about the app& made sure I was confident to make it back to the right gate.
When I came back and made it to my gate, a passenger came off the plane, she didn’t speak English very well. She didn’t know what gate she had to go to. I offered to look it up for her and get her headed in the right direction.
I will use this AP every time I come to DFW.
Thank you,
