QUICK, EASY, AND ACCURATE DRAWINGS:

ArcSite is an intuitive CAD design tool that's easy enough for anyone to START SKETCHING FLOOR PLANS RIGHT AWAY and powerful enough to take on advanced CAD projects.

Contractors love ArcSite for home addition, remodeling, audit, cabinetry, site surveys, and flooring projects.

STAY ORGANIZED:

Add enhanced visual information to your drawings by embedding on-site photos from the exact perspective they were taken from in real life. Easily add markups, notes, and annotations to any photo or drawing. Keep all of your drawings in a cloud folder that your whole team can access from anywhere!

PRESENT AND CLOSE:

With ArcSite, YOUR DRAWINGS LITERALLY PRICE THEMSELVES. Once you’re finished drawing, ArcSite provides you with a professional estimate to share with your clients.

BENEFITS OF ARCSITE

AVOID CONFUSION - ArcSite's unmatched ease of use allows you to add more detail to your drawings and helps you avoid common miscommunications with your clients and installation teams regarding your scope of work

STAND OUT FROM THE COMPETITION - Look professional by showing your teammates and customers impressive CAD-drawn floor plans, estimates, and detailed proposals all from within ArcSite.

GO PAPERLESS - ArcSite stores all of your drawings and proposals on the cloud where they can be organized and viewed from any device across your team.

FINISH YOUR DRAWINGS FROM ANYWHERE - Say goodbye to needing desktop CAD software to complete a drawing.

WHAT'S INCLUDED?
* Scaled drawings can be exported to PNG/PDF/DXF/DWG
* Compatible with desktop CAD software such as AutoCAD & Revit.
* Instant access to over 1,500 Shapes
* Create your own custom shapes
* Import and markup PDFs
* Embed photos within your drawing
* Upload to the cloud. Share and co-edit with your coworkers
* Takeoff (quantities of materials)
* Proposal Generation (based off of your drawing)
* Leica Disto Laser distance meter support

After years of drawing via the office drafting table and Mutoh Drafting Machine, Arcsite has given me the freedom to create in the field so I am on the jobsite to direct my crews and still knock out the drawings that keep us working. Having the ability to draw scaled details while meeting with a client at the project or being able to text or email them immediately so decisions are expedited is alone worth the cost. Try it yourself and you'll be sold.

Kinda Pricey...

So I looked at all the other reviews and I thought that this would be a good app. I got the app and I was a bit disappointed. this app costs quite a bit of money to use. I understand that you need money to improve the app and for personal uses, but it is very expensive even for the lowest plan. I am giving you three stars because the app is pretty good, but some of us can’t get it because of the cost.
Maybe you could make a graph of profits per price. The less the price the more people buy it. The higher the price the less people buy it but people will charge more. I’d say that with apps and games, a good price is the most affordable price. Affordable but profitable. If it costs too much you get bad reviews, plus it won’t become as popular because some people can’t get it. The lower the price the more people will get the app and cause it to become popular. It will also get you good reviews, too.
I think that the best price would be free but with in-app purchases. This way people have a CHOICE to get features. It gets you money, good reviews, popularity, and more. This is probably the most beneficial way of improving your apps and games. I’m not saying I don’t like the app, I’m saying I think it could be improved.

solarsitetech , 07/14/2020

I use this for writing up solar site visits. It's excellent.

I'm a site visit specialist for a solar company and I have been using Arcsite for the last year to prepare site visit reports. I make site plans, diagram roof planes (which are later copied into a desktop CAD program), mark up photos, and fill out forms to provide designers, engineers, and installers with vital information to be able to design, engineer, and build high-quality photovoltaic systems.

Arcsite has been absolutely fantastic for this. I've used other methods as well and it is by far the best I've tried. It's the only system I've found that offers a one-stop shop for all my needs, and it does it in a way that is fast, effective, and highly mobile. I found it easy to learn, easy to customize, and my coworkers appreciate the quality of the reports I am able to create.

I have also found Patrick and the rest of the Arcsite team really excellent to work with. On numerous occasions, I've made feature requests or pointed out minor bugs, and then seen them addressed in the very next update. Updates are frequent and the program has improved substantially since I started using it, even though it was already good enough for my needs at the beginning.

I really like this program, and the people behind it. It's not even expensive. Highly recommended.
