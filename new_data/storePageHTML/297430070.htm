Description

Now the Target app can help you have a more rewarding Target run! Introducing Target Circle, which gives you access to hundreds of deals, a birthday gift and the chance to support your community.

Plan your next Target run by checking out Target Circle offers, the Weekly Ad and item availability all in one place. Then streamline your run with store maps and aisle locations, and scan item barcodes for prices, reviews and available deals.

Wallet is the fastest, easiest way to pay and save in store. With one scan, you can pay with your credit or debit RedCard and redeem Target Circle offers, gift cards and Target Circle earnings.

With improved iPad support, the app makes it easy to shop from anywhere, allowing you to search our entire selection, complete with online promotions and flexible ordering options. Order quickly and securely every time, then choose to pick it up at your local store or have it delivered to your home.

My favorite app to date !!

This app is truly life changing if you shop at target on a frequent basis. I heard about this from one of my friends and it is crazy how easy it is to navigate, return items, and earn free money. It is especially helpful with returns, you just put in the phone number or scan barcode when checking out and the items will immediately show up on the app. If you need to return an item, just click on the purchase date and then there will be a barcode - then you don’t have to keep track of receipts, a very useful tool! It will show what you regularly buy and you can add it to “favorites” - a very conviene to say to shop. I have used the drive up method and it has worked wonderfully with no issues, if they are out of the item - you will not get charged and it will be cancelled out of the order. You can add gift cards, red cards, and every time you use your account in the store - you will earn money and if you shop a lot, you will earn money just from using the app.

I love this app and it is truly a life saver !!
