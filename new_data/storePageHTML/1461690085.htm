Description

Take cinematic videos same as iPhone 13 for all dual / triple / LiDAR camera iPhones and iPads.
Focos Live offers master class video editing capability, amplified flexibility, and more functionality than any App that you may have tried before. With keyframe and adjustment layers for all options and easily navigable options for both beginners and experts.
With the functionality of taking cinematic videos with wide aperture and bokeh effect for dual camera & triple camera iPhone, Focos Live is the next big thing when it comes to computational photography. With Focos Live, you can make limitless customization of any cinematic video that you create, and you can even change the aperture as many times as you wish.

MAIN FEATURES
- Cinematic video-capable, with depth data on dual camera, triple camera, LiDAR and front Face ID camera.
- Taking videos with the aperture up to f/1.4.
- Focus point continuous change functionality for tracking object and aperture size.
- Multi-choice simulated aperture diaphragms for generating different bokeh spot effects
- Multi-layer feature for fitting at least 4 different videos on a single screen.
- Adjustment layer for each option.
- Speed ramping and video reverse capability for creating custom-made videos.
- Industrial-grade chroma key can perfectly knockout the hair & feather for the green/blue screen video.
- Video stabilizer can remove the shaking issue for all existing videos.
- Mask, Blur, Bokeh and Tilt-shift effects.
- Text animations can make your videos look cooler.
- Standalone audio editor for managing music and sounds.
- Video export feature; for exporting videos as screenshot, animated GIF, or 4K&60fps.
- Eligible for importing 3DL and CUBE files as custom files.
- Essential tool for iPhone 12 Pro, iPhone 13 Pro & iPad Pro with LiDAR.

Focos Live is an App that should be on everyone's iPhone and iPad.

What’s New

Nov 2, 2021

Version 1.4.2

- Support added for cinematic videos on iPhone 13 series. Now you can edit cinematic videos which are taken by iPhone 13、13 Pro & 13 Pro Max for adding bokeh effect & adjusting the focus object. The difference from the built-in functions of cinematic mode is that there is now more functions for cinematic effects such as adding swirly & reflex bokeh, or continuously changing the different aperture size on the timeline.
- The portrait video has been replaced by cinematic format. Currently, there is no public interface for developers to add the tag of cinematic for the videos taken by Focos Live and then add them to the cinematic album but this will be fixed if I find the path.
- New option for saving GPS location while taking videos.
- Fixed some issues and crashes on version 1.4.

Luke McQuillan , 01/09/2021

Bokeh, fantastic editor, and great customer service!

I’m using this on both an iPhone and iPad 12.9 (with LIDAR). The bokeh effect is greatly improved with LIDAR and the editing suite it comes with is very impressive. The ability to add key frames, chroma key, stabilizer, etc is something that Adobe doesn’t even have on Premier Rush yet (and this is a one time payment).

I had an issue with Bluetooth remote shutters not working with the iPad version (which I prefer to shoot in because of the LIDAR and extra processing power - which does make a difference). I emailed the developer, and within the week, they had a fix pushed out on the App Store - that is amazing customer service. In my ten+ years on the iOS ecosystem, I’ve reached out to many developers over the years, and I don’t think I’ve ever seen such a quick response. Thank you!!

If I had any suggestions, it would be to add mouse/keyboard shortcuts. A mouse works with the app, but you cannot scrub through the timeline while editing, and there are no right-click functions. I use my iPad Pro as one of my main computers, so adding this functionality would take this already amazing app over the top. It’s not a big deal as the touch controls are good, but it would make it feel just that much more powerful. All in all - great app, highly recommend!

N8H2O , 10/03/2020

$15 to achieve bokeh (blurry background effect)

Bought the app just for the “bokeh” effect (the blurry background effect), super legit, not dslr quality but I prefer filming with my phone for the convenience factor and I’m a beginner with shooting video so this does a fantastic job achieving that effect without needing the bulky camera or an expensive lens if you already have a newer iPhone. I have 2 feature requests I really wish they would add however:
1) being able to use the size/autofocus bokeh feature from a video you recorded with native camera app instead of having to record from the Focos app, you can manually edit it with imported video, but it just works so much better and smoother if shot initially in app and I really wish that feature would carry that over to imported videos
2) and this is by farrrr my biggest feature request, please please please add bokeh effect to the wide angle lens, not manually editing the blur, but with the size/autofocus like with the other 2 lenses. That would make this app absolutely amazing.

Overall this app is absolutely fire if you’re looking to achieve a more professional looking video in combination with the ease of using your phone
