Description

Identify birds and track your sightings with this complete bird watching guide and bird checklist for the United States, Canada and Europe.

Add sightings of any of the world's 10,000 bird species. Get complete bird lists for your location with access to over 2000 scientific bird profiles, 5800 beautiful, full resolution photos and 3700 bird sounds including bird calls and bird songs.

------------------------------------------

FEATURES
■ Add bird sightings, completing lists, and tracking overtime in your life list
■ Designed for easy bird identification by appearance, call / song, and location
■ Use your location to see bird lists near you
■ Informative bird profiles with description and range maps
■ Enjoy beautiful high resolution bird photos
■ Listen to bird calls and sounds
■ Sort or search bird lists, and show or hide rare birds

------------------------------------------

BIRD PROFILES
■ Scientific names, common names and alias's
■ Photos of breeding, non-breeding and other variations
■ Bird sounds including bird calls and bird songs
■ Range maps that show location and movement
■ Written description of appearance including bird identification tips
■ Details on breeding, nesting, lifespan, habitat, diet, and more

Greatest Bird App!

I absolutely love this app. You get a fair amount of bird identifications to start with and you can choose to purchase additional packages for the areas and types of birds your interested in. The identifications, pictures and bird calls/songs are well done without being over done. Best of all the additional packages are fairly priced, they don’t price gouge you for additional packages. I couldn’t recommend this app enough!

Great app for nature lovers!

As a nature lover and hiking enthusiast, I found this app to be very cool. Bird watching is something that I’ve been interested in but never really knew how to pursue, so this app has helped me learn more about birds and be more engaged with my surroundings when I go on hikes. Easy to navigate and would definitely recommend to anyone even remotely interested in learning more about the natural world!

Good info and ability to keep track

The app is a little buggy. Have to reset the app to get out of the add screen. Plus there’s no way to input info about the birds to narrow the search. I use Merlin for that. You also can’t click the birds you’ve found to look at pictures and info. It would be cool if the app could show what other people have seen in an area.
The pictures, info and sounds are really nice though.
