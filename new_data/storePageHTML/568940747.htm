Description

The Bump is the #1 baby tracker app for first-time moms, providing features you won’t find anywhere else. Our interactive tools help you navigate pregnancy and parenting with ease.

+ Planner+: Prepares mom-to-be for each doctor visit
+ EXCLUSIVE: Inside The Bump: an interactive 3D visualization of baby's growth during pregnancy
+ Daily, award-winning pregnancy and parenting editorial articles
+ The largest catalog of baby products and reviews across all major retailers
+ Exclusive offers, savings, and giveaways from our top tier partners

The Bump gives you trusted information backed by experts and science that you can understand. Decode pregnancy symptoms and get updates about the baby’s development. Stay ahead of the curve with The Bump app.

** FEATURES **

HOW BIG IS BABY
Prepare to smile and look forward to each week during your pregnancy: The Bump compares your baby’s growth in utero to beautifully illustrated fruit (“Baby Is As Big As a Peach”) that’ll delight you and make you excited to share your baby’s development with friends and family.

Plus, learn how to prepare for the changes, developments, and symptoms taking place with your body and baby.

INSIDE THE BUMP
Explore an exciting and exclusive 3D interactive visualization of baby's growth week-by-week with BioDigital Inc.'s proprietary technology. Learn new biological facts about the baby and the changes inside your body during pregnancy.

DAILY CONTENT
Dive into fresh and relevant content specific to your week of pregnancy. The award-winning editorial staff of The Bump delivers timely articles to help you navigate the journey ahead. Want to find out how to ward off morning sickness or learn the best things to pack in your hospital bag? We’ve got you covered!

PLANNER+
Stay informed about the all-important prenatal doctor visits with this game-changing feature. Planner+ suggests questions you may want to ask your doctor and integrates appointments seamlessly with your iOS calendar.


REGISTRY
The Bump is the #1 Baby Registry destination in the US, hosting over 1 million registries every year! The Bump has collected over 15,000 of the top registry products across Target, Amazon, buy buy Baby, and Walmart, WITH TONS OF REVIEWS to explore. The Bump will automatically track all your registries across each retailer so you can keep tabs on what you’ve registered for, what’s been purchased, and what still remains.

BABY NAMES
Trying to find the perfect baby name? Use our search tools to find both baby boy and baby girl names, sort by origin, or discover the most popular or uncommon baby names.

BEST of BABY
Browse The Bump editors’ top picks of must-have pregnancy and baby products for new parents! Review hand selected baby products from categories such as Baby On-the-Go, Nursery, Baby Eats, Diapering, Healthy Baby, Play & Learn, Maternity Basics, and more.

THE BUMP PERKS
Gain access to exclusive offers, savings, and giveaways from The Bump’s top tier partners. Get everything you need for the baby with a few perks on the side!

BELLY AND BABY PHOTOS
Document your pregnancy by creating a weekly album of your happily growing belly. After your arrival makes their debut, the album expands to track the first amazing year of baby’s life.

CUSTOMER SERVICE
The Bump team reads every email, answers each phone call, and takes to heart all of your reviews. This is your pregnancy. This is your baby. This is your app. Have a problem, don’t hesitate to let us know.

Kikians3! , 08/06/2019

Very informative and helpful

I used this app while pregnant with my first born. He was born this March. I had very little knowledge on pregnancy and found lots of information that was useful. My son had a few problems on the womb and I stressed over it bc I do not do well when o don’t understand an issue and I want all the details. The bump calmed me down having unbiased straight facts and I was able to talk to moms with similar issues with this app. There are so many articles to read and it is so easy to search for a specific topic and ask questions if needed. My son is over 4 months and the app still gives me information that I need and has great suggestion articles. My all time favorite feature with The Bump App or website is the weekly information and 3D visual of the baby and his progress with them and the mother’s body as well. I would recommend this app to any expecting mother or even fathers to learn how to help their wives and themselves through the 40 week process and also to anyone with a newborn!

Hgdgiyg , 11/24/2017

Good overall!

This app is great for answering the big and small questions that come along with pregnancy and becoming a new parent. I’m on my third child and in my 30s, so I would like to see more diverse articles for moms as well as Dads and families with older kids.
I love how there’s 3 to 5 new articles per day, and getting notifications about how far along I am. It’s a fun little reminder. It feels a little bit like someone is right there excited about your pregnancy alongside you.
I like the 3-D example each week of what my baby looks like, as well as the little tidbits of information that are specific to help my babies growth. I also love how every week right when I open the app there’s a comparison to a familiar fruit, so it’s easy for me to visualize how big my baby is. I can also easily share information about our new family member with my older kids, their ages six and eight. They like the pictures and easy-to-understand information about babies progress.
I have no big complaints about this app, just improving the demographic it’s geared towards and broadening the article information would be a positive improvement.

I love this app! I’ve been using it since I found out I was pregnant around 6 or 7 weeks pregnant and it’s been amazing so far.
