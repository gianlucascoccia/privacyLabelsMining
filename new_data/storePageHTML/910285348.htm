Description

A mobile app for Pokemon TCG players, and collectors!

Collector for Pokemon TCG allows you to easily search the database for cards from various expansions. Collector's database includes all Pokemon Trading Card Game Expansions from Fossil up to the latest release, including the original Base set.

All Pokemon cards are organized based on series, allowing you to navigate quickly. Information such as HP, evolutions, attacks and stage can be found once you tap on a Pokemon card. Easily view the next card in the set by swiping the card when in the fullscreen view.



Additional Premium Features include:

- Collection (keep track of the cards you own, import & export collections to view your friends' as well)

- Stats (view completion % and a lot more from all expansions)

- Wishlist (add the cards you wish to own)

- Deck Manager (create and manage your own custom decks, and even e-mail your deck list to yourself for use in Leagues & Tournaments)

- Game Helper (a tool used to keep track of damage counters & special conditions. It includes a coin flipper, turn counter and much more)

- QR Code Scanner (scan and group your PTCGO Code Cards)

- Earn points to unlock new coins to use in the Game Helper!

- View the lowest price of a card off eBay, Amazon and PokeShop96!

- Keep track of your Pokemon Pin Collection! (requires separate iap)




Access www.PokeShop96.ca from within the app to purchase Pokemon card singles, booster packs, booster boxes, collection boxes, coins, pins and much more!



**When Importing/Exporting Collections, be sure they belong to the same version of the Collector App you are running**

Chaos Hawk , 06/13/2020

The best PTCG app now

Does everything pretty well. The card database is basically complete, which I can’t say for the official app by Nintendo. Build deck lists, read card info, track collected cards, and can even use the battle feature for matches where you don’t have your counters/dice on you. Some features require the one-time pay but that’s fair.

The interface can be buggy sometimes and unintuitive but the amount of work they put into this is impressive none the less. Still better then the official one in my opinion, though I do use that for scanning foreign cards to get translations easily. That’s an exploit though, and this app does the main things you’d expect it to do better. If they clean up the UI, add card scanning, different pictures for card translations, and maybe a nice dark theme option it would be perfect. As is, still hands down the most useful app available for PTCG.

freddie12345 , 03/24/2020

Best PTCG app out there!

I’ve been using this app for a while now. I have tried others in the past, but this one is far superior. They’ve recently been very good with keeping up to date on new expansions too. If you’ve ever had a hard time finding comprehensive set lists for all of the Pokémon expansions, look no further. Every single set in existence from base to swsh, including pop series and McDonald’s promos, you name it. And to top it all off, it’s free! There’s a paid version which, frankly, I forgot about because they don’t push it on you like a lot of other free apps, and they don’t force adds on you either. I’m definitely going to upgrade to the paid version to support the team that brought us this. It is so easy to search for a set and look at all the cards and their rarity and number and whatever other piece of info you need. There’s a wishlist for any cards you want and set lists for cards you have. There are even more options that I haven’t gotten around to exploring yet too. I’m constantly recommending this app to people in the community and you should get it too.

Hiphopllama , 12/22/2020

Great info, but terrible bugs makes it hard to actually use

I love the app for keeping track of my collection, and it’s certainly worlds better than the official Pokémon company app. Great database of all available cards. However, major bugs/issues brings it down from a 5 star to barely a 3.

The UI is definitely not user friendly, even counterintuitive in places. An example of a necessary fix is making it easier to remove cards from your collection. Also, for whatever reason, adding cards to your collection doesn’t always save - I’ll add a few cards, and get the pop up that they’ve “been added to your collection”, then later I’ll look them up and they won’t be there. This is the worst problem: very frustrating to spend hours inputting cards, and the next day, they’re gone.

All in all, well worth the small purchase amount if you want to start to keep track of your collection. Great work by the developers getting started. However, needs several improvements before being a 5 star app...notably, adding cards to your collection not saving.
