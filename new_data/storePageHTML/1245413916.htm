Description

TrackMan Golf is the one-stop-shop for all your TrackMan activities. Better golf starts here.

The app gives you comprehensive access to all data registered during your indoor and outdoor TrackMan activities, allowing you to analyze your practice and play performance and track your improvements, regardless of your golf skill level.

Enjoy the power of the TrackMan tracking technology while using TrackMan range, and access detailed data reports summarizing and providing insights on all your TrackMan range, simulator and practice sessions.

Features include:

• Live ball-data tracking for TrackMan range sessions (carry, total distance, ball speed, height, launch angle and more)

• Activity overview with insightful reports for all TrackMan range, indoor and practice activities

• Games that will make you stay longer on the range and compete with friends

• Your personal TrackMan account with lifetime statistics including your TrackMan handicap

• Updated leaderboards across competitions

• Quick login to easily connect your TrackMan personal profile and immediately start tracking your golf performance

• Available in different languages (currently English, Spanish, German, French, Chinese, and Japanese)

Download TrackMan Golf to unleash your potential and make practicing or playing golf a more rewarding experience, whenever you want and wherever you are.

Works well but improvements needed

This app works well pulling your data and allowing you to view some of the shot stats that you’ve chosen and course scores, but stats cannot be exported which would be useful and most
Importantly you cannot remove rounds that are affecting your trackman handicap. Someone else played a round under my account and it highly affected my handicap. Was able to remove the round from recent activity but it still shows in 18 hole averages. Besides that it’s great to have quick insight on club averages.

Developer Response ,

Hi,

Thank you for your feedback.

We have in the pipeline the ability to let users edit their stats by removing bad rounds that influence their stats negatively.

The ability to export data directly from the app is not possible at the moment. If we see an increase in demand for this feature, we will definately consider implementing it. Detailed reports of all activites that have been performed with a coach is available on www.mytrackman.com.

All the best,
TrackMan

dzfun , 06/23/2018

Love it! Thanks Carl’s Golfland!

I absolutely love it. I finally got to use Trackman Range for the first time on a trip to Detroit. I was so impressed that I will actively look for more locations. I’d be willing to pay significantly more for range sessions to have this type of data regularly available.

There are a few features I’d love to see in future releases:

1. The ability to see more detailed stats like dispersion AFTER the session. This is probably Trackman’s most valuable feature. Maybe I should say “no” to my ego and put that hybrid or higher lofted fairway wood in the bag after all.

2. Ability to add custom clubs. Like many players, I carry more than just a pitch, sand, and lob wedge. I have a PW, 50, 54, 58. I also hit those clubs differently. It would be very valuable to be able to create separate categories on the reports for those “half wedge” shots so I can start dialing in the carry, roll, and dispersion.

3. The ability to merge data with Trackman accounts. I know Range doesn’t give all the data points, but it would be nice to see both data sets together to track improvement over time.

pajama1218 , 04/20/2019

Good, Could Be Great

TrackMan Range works very well most of the time. There have been instances where only half or so of my shots were picked up, which is frustrating. The app is pretty good, but I would offer a few improvements. First, I wish your desired units from settings carried over to the Activity tab instead of having to change it every time. Second, I would like the ability to tag or name different versions of the same club. For example, when testing 3 different drivers, I would like to be able to tell them apart easier for easy side by side comparison.
