Description

FutuBull is a one-stop digital financial service platform from NASDAQ-listed Futu Holdings Limited (FUTU). Professional service and the aids in technical analysis is available at all devices. Investment community, latest 24/7 Financial News, real-time quotes, all-rounded data and technical analysis are all available.

【Special Features】
1. [Global Markets] Access HK/US/CN/SG stock markets via 1 account.
2. [Real-Time Quotes] No more delays, get instant updates from 4 major stock markets.
3. [Multi Functions] Quotes, AI analysis, 24/7 financial news and other functions to help you preserve more profits.
4. [Online Interaction] Share your trading experience and find companions. Interact online with investment experts and be inspired by them!
5. [Excellent User Experience] 24/7 customer services support. We continuously optimize the product experience to ensure reliability.

【POWERFUL DATA AND RESEARCH】
- Speedy real-times quotes, refreshing only every 0.3 seconds. Gives you a detailed insight into the price action.
- Exclusive AI support. Create customized screener to find market spotlights. Stay informed 24/7 with how the market moves by pushed AI-driven alerts.
- Save your time with over 50 FREE technical indicators in our smart charts and more than 22 drawing tools. VWAP, multiple charts, pre-market and post-market data are available. Discover the best timing for trading.
- Gain market insight from big data at anytime. Data analysis reports such as fear & greed index, capital flow, financial report etc will help you to maximize your profit.

【INTERNATIONAL STOCKS TRADING AVAILABLE】
- You can make any investment decisions in any stock market of HK/US/CN/SG and other opportunities with one app.
- Access US stock trading and quotes is up to 16 hours including, in pre-market hours and post-market hours. Never miss any opportunities.
- Set your own earnings calendar for announcements reminders, helping you to make better investing decisions.
- 14 conditional alerts are available. Notice dynamic changes with no delay.
- 24/7 global financial news is available. Monitor the marketing via the latest on stocks, commodities, and the global economy.

【ACTIVE COMMUNITY WITH THOUSANDS OF USERS】
- Never miss Futu promotions and events!
- Share your trading ideas and investment journey in our community. Get inspiration from over thousands of users worldwide.
- Know more about prevailing investment trends such as meme stocks.
- Learn investment skills that help you make a better decision from experts and lectures.
- Enjoy mock trading functions as practice. Become well-equipped in investment.

All investments involve risks, so please be careful！
