Description

The My Little Pony Comics app has been discontinued and all comics have been consolidated into the IDW Digital Comics Experience app.

To continue reading your My Little Pony comics within the IDW Digital Comics Experience app, please run the My Little Pony Comics app and follow the steps to make the transition.

What’s New

May 19, 2021

Version 5.0.2

The My Little Pony Comics app has been discontinued and consolidated into the into the new IDW Digital Comics Experience app.

This update converts the app into a transition app to help move your content over to the new IDW Digital Comics Experience app, to allow you to continue reading your comics. Please follow the steps within the apps to make the transition.

For more details please visit the IDW App Transition FAQ: http://www.idwpublishing.com/app-faq/
