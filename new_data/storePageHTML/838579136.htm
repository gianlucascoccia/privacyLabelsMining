Description

SamRemote is a virtual remote control that lets you control your smart TV.
To use the remote control, you must have your mobile / tablet is on the same wifi network as your TV and you accept the message that appears on your TV. Because the application runs through the wireless network , it is not necessary to be close to the TV .
If by mistake you have refused the confirmation message of your TV ( Message for establishing communication ) , it is possible to change your selection by going to :
/ Menu / Network / AllShare Settings
In addition to a careful representation of the remote control , you can use all the functionality of the real remote.

_TruthSerum_ , 05/01/2019

Ads and more ads

To start off, when I first downloaded the app it connected to my Tv just fine... the only reason I need this is because I haven’t seen the actual controller in like a year.

And because it was easy to connect it gets an extra star, because from the reviews I’ve seen on other controllers, weren’t happy about connecting it.

This app would be perfect... if it didn’t have ads. I get it that you need to make money and it’s free to download, but $6?! You can get rid of ads on Tetris for $5.

This is a TV controller.
Tetris is a free video game and it has less ads than this.

I believe that a TV controller should have little to no ads because who needs them. As I said, it’s a controller. You’re supposed to be turning up the volume not clicking out of ads.

Another complaint is how slow the buttons click, not too much of a bother for me but it still is annoying. I’ll tap the volume button 5 times and it’ll turn it up by 2 (then pops up an ad).

My recommendation, if you want a decent controller, honestly, go buy an actual one.

But if you don’t really care about advertisements that have nothing to do with their $6 to have freedom from a random app. Then download this.

It’s not that bad, without ads, my only complaint is that if you want to turn it up higher you have to wait about 2 seconds for every tap. Which is easy to get used to.
