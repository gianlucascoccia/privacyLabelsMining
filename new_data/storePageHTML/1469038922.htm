Description

Unlock your true potential by giving yourself every opportunity to succeed. Lanevision provides you the feedback only 1% of swimmers currently have access to.

Develop data-driven race strategies, set long-term training goals, and let Lanevision help you track your progress over time.

kete05 , 06/07/2020

So disappointing

This app sounds amazing. From the limited amount I was able to use it, the tools look wonderful. The layout was good. The tool tips were helpful. However, there are three big issues.

1- I had to enable 2-factor authentication to use it. It’s just annoying but I did it because I really wanted to try the app.
2- I had to pay to try it. Which isn’t terrible if the app functions. But there is no way to try before you buy.
3- The biggest issue is that the app crashes before it even analyzes a video. I tried to upload a video, indicated the start, laps, and race finish. When I tried to analyze it, the whole app crashed with no reason. Spinning wheel of death on a black screen and then my phone locked.

So basically I paid for an app, set up additional security on my phone to access it, then I couldn’t even use it. Huge waste of time for an app that sounds incredible. Too good to be true.

Developer Response ,

Sorry to hear that you had trouble using the lanevision app. Our support team will be pleased to help you with this via email support. Pls share your email ID or reach out to support@aspiricx.com.
