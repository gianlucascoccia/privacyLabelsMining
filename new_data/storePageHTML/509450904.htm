Needs another bug fix

I’ve had this app for a couple years now and I’m still in love. I’ve made so many amazing friends and memories and I have AA to thank for it. My only issue is that it needs another update. Nothing major, just to fix some bugs. There’s this bug where when you’re scrolling through the conversations in your inbox, you don’t always see the most recent message sent. You’ll often see a message sent weeks ago even though you text that person daily. Another bug is one where at random moments while you’re in a back and forth conversation, the messages will suddenly appear out of order. This makes it so that when you scroll up, you see like 12 of your own messages as if they were all sent in a row and if you’d scroll above that, there will be 12 different responses sent by the other person in a row. It can be very frustrating. I have to keep refreshing and restarting the app every time I encounter a bug which doesn’t always fix the problem. So please please please give this app another update! Thank you <3
