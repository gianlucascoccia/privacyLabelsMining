Description

Enjoy movement again and get balanced! Moms Into Fitness is designed to help you achieve optimal health without going to extremes - loving yourself form the inside out one day at a time. Access the best online fitness, nutritional guidance, and recipes for moms.

We build confidence inside and out starting with over 600 scientifically-backed streaming workouts for moms and kids. Our fitness programs are for beginners to advanced done in as little as 15 minutes, 3-4 times a week. No experience necessary, just show up for as little as $1.90 per week streaming from your computer (PC/Mac) or favorite mobile device or TV.

Moms Into Fitness shows you how to nurture your body without stripping away the fun. You are a mom after all and celebrations and birthday cake are a part of life. We offer over 200 kid-tested recipes plus meal plans created by a registered dietitian.

Most importantly, we know we can’t do this alone. Our communities and “villages” are critical to our success. Here at Moms Into Fitness, we are about serving moms. Whether you just found out you’re pregnant or you’ve got six kids, we got you!

Moms Into Fitness also offers:
- Targeted fitness programs creating cardio endurance, burning calories, building strength, helping you lose weight, finding your balance and safely keeping you motivated no matter your fitness level. 
- Beginner and advanced programs for all fitness levels plus workouts specifically for women that are pregnant, postnatal, or have an ab separation/diastasis recti.
- Workout calendars with every program to help you plan and stay on track. Just show up!
- Nutritional guidance and recipes from our registered dietitian following simple nutritional guidelines versus fad diets. We believe in integration not elimination.
- Guides that address abdominal rehab and pregnancy and postnatal exercise and nutrition.

Grounded by her faith, midwest roots, husband and three kids, Lindsay is passionate about helping moms, and dedicated to serving women and their families.

Lindsay Brin, C.P.T. & B.S.E. Exercise Science, is an international author, contributor to Fit Pregnancy magazine, Parents magazine, Iron Woman, Mom & Baby magazine, Fitness, Conceive and many more. She has choreographed over 600 video workouts designed just for moms.

Specializing in prenatal and postnatal fitness programs, her research and programs have been used by fitness organizations to not only teach, but certify other fitness professionals.  This Prenatal and Postnatal Fitness Specialist course is rooted in research, science and helping women find confidence & strength in themselves.

Functional Fitness

I started using Moms into Fitness 5 years ago and haven’t looked back. I was an athlete in college and an avid runner, swimmer, and cycler pre-motherhood. I would easily spend 2+ hours a day working out and I loved it. But then, I had children, I was working, my life was a fast moving train that never seemed to slow down and I couldn’t figure out how to work out. Enter MIF.
I was feeling pretty down after baby #2. I felt like I had no time and there was no way I was ever going to lose any of the weight. A friend recommended MIF and I lost 30lbs in 12 weeks with (what was then called) Postnatal Slimdown. But even more than that, I learned I could get a killer workout in 30 minutes or less. Even better than that? I learned proper form for the exercises and learned how to work out in a way that would reduce risk for injury and maximize benefits. Five years later and another baby later and MIF is still go-to workout source.

Honestly, I feel like I have my own personal trainer when I work out with MIF. If you want to get a great workout into your day in under an hour, from the comfort of your own home, with someone who will actually teach you how to properly perform the exercise and why you do it- MIF is for you and this app makes it easy and convenient to access from anywhere.

Su$123456 , 05/07/2020

The perfect workout videos for busy women!

There are plenty of workout program options based on your ability level and the amount of time you have to workout. If you are someone who likes to have a plan, Lindsay has calendars you can use, but if you want videos based off the amount of time you have or the type of workout you want, her videos are well organized. I am on my third program and really enjoying Lindsay’s workouts. She is constantly correcting form and encouraging you throughout the video. There are modifications as well to help with certain conditions or to take it up a notch. I have needed minimal equipment - a chair, towel, several sets of weights, a yoga mat, and a pilates ball. Instead of the pilates ball, I have used one of my children’s kickballs and even substituted some canned goods for light weights. I am feeling better and more in shape than I have in years. My children also enjoy watching the videos and attempt to workout along with me. The workouts are all “G” rated in my opinion and all about you physically becoming a better version of yourself. I highly recommend!

Perfect for Postpartum fitness and beyond!

I was pretty active prior to my pregnancy and during, but I had no idea where to start postpartum. I wanted to ease into fitness but also go a little bit harder than just the occasional stretch or walk, once I was cleared to exercise, of course!). I found Moms Into Fitness, tried the free first week trial and I was hooked! Lindsay is so encouraging and kind while also helping you strengthen, tone and feel amazing.
