Description

FightCamp is the most enjoyable and effective at-home boxing workout you can do whenever you want. We offer a growing library of more than 1K on-demand boxing and kickboxing workouts. You’ll see punch goals in each workout so you know exactly how hard to push yourself. Paired with our wearable punch trackers, you’ll see your workout stats on screen, in real-time, including your punch count, punch speed and total output. At the end of each workout, you’ll see a summary that displays your data and progress.

After each workout, check the leaderboard to see how you stack up against the community. Compete against your past performances or others with the versus mode feature designed to fuel your competitive side.

FEATURES:
∙ Interactive punch goals set for every round
∙ Real-time workout stats when paired with FightCamp punch trackers
∙ Community-wide Leaderboard
∙ Efficient HIIT-style workouts
∙ World-class trainers with real fighting experience
∙ Syncs to Health App
∙ Versus Mode to compete head-to-head with other members, or to compete against your previous best score
∙ Stack multiple workouts together to enhance your workout session
∙ Instructional paths teaching the basics of boxing and kickboxing
∙ Advanced paths for experienced boxers & kickboxers
∙ Total output score to show workout intensity
∙ Achievements & badges earned by working harder
∙ Ever-growing selection of on-demand home workouts that challenge your body & mind
∙ New boxing & kickboxing workouts added to the app each week
∙ Connect with your favorite streaming platform to listen to your own music

Fight Camp and Aaron Swenson (One of Fight Camp’s trainers) has made it so convenient to get all the benefits of boxing with a trainer at a gym but at a fraction of the price. I workout at my local gym (for the heavy bag and sauna after my workout) for 20 bucks a month. I work out 6 days a week with Aaron Swenson, rotating his beginner 15 minutes burnout class one day and his advanced 45 minute class the next. I’ve yet to try any of the other trainers because Aaron’s workouts are just perfect for me. He gives advice on form, techniques, and even challenges you to switch stance. With a 30 day free trail, you have nothing to lose. Take it from me. I do not benefit at all from suggesting this app or this trainer. I’m just someone that’s loves boxing and understand it is expensive to get this kind of training and want to spread the word. Hopes this helps.

RaginAsian55 , 04/13/2020

One of a kind!

Love this app! It’s one of its kind. I looked for something like this for a few months and finally stumbled upon FightCamp. My husband and I work out together and feel great. I have 10+ years of formal martial arts training, and my husband never threw a single punch or kick before starting. So as a couple we can certainly attest that FightCamp is great for all levels of skill! For me it’s fun to get a good workout with trainers who I know are technical and proficient. (I cringe when I see cardio people trying to throw punches or kicks) My husband loves the distraction of an activity with punch goals and getting mental exercise from the punch & kick combos. We sweat like crazy!

Our biggest hurdle is we live in an apt. So only one of us works on the bag and has to pull our punches, while the other does shadow boxing. It’s a great workout either way.

I’d love to see:
1) More kickboxing (maybe Muay Thai)
2) more 6 round workouts
3) skills drills to improve form vs just a demo video
4) plans for 1 hour (12 round) workouts?

The other biggest boon is the upfront cost. We only purchased the wraps and trackers which set us back $500. There is a money-back guarantee for 30day trial. But obviously this is the biggest barrier for most people to start. $39/mo for membership is still better than any other boxing gym or fitness center, and this is a specialized and technical workout.
