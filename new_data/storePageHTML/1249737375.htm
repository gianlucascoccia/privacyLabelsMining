Description

24/7 cloud synchronized access to the golf world’s most powerful scientific instructional putting technology, with comprehensive statistics and reports across all your iOS devices.

Player or Instructor Subscriptions are available as monthly and annual in-app purchase, auto-renewing subscriptions.

** The BioMech Golf Putting Sensor is sold separately and is required to use this Apps functionality. See the BioMech Golf website (www.biomechgolf.com) for purchase options.

POWERFUL TECHNOLOGY. Available to all subscriptions:
PRACTICE:
Real-Time Aim™ - use the Aim screen to show where your putter is aiming in real-time
Capture putts even while offline - putts are synchronized with the cloud when you get back online
Measure and analyze all aspects of the putting stroke in real-time and receive instantaneous feedback
2D & 3D synchronized graphical playback of any and all putts
Automatic cloud-based record keeping, analyses & reporting
Optional video capture and playback
Link to Instructors to share practice session activity

REPORT:
My Golf Bio - Your personal putting dashboard. Review 30/60/90 day putting metrics with interactive charts and period reports available for further analysis

REVIEW:
History give you a chronological view of your session history providing a Timeline View with summary metrics and a detailed List View that drills down by day, to session, to putt.

TARGET LINE LENGTH™
BioMech’s exclusive display of the length, duration, and accuracy of the proper putter orientation

YOUR PUTTING SCORE™
BioMech’s comprehensive metric that objectively measures your progress

PLAYER SUBSCRIPTION
For $14.99 USD per month, or $149.99 USD per year, the player subscription includes the features mentioned above in the section "Available to all subscriptions".

INSTRUCTOR SUBSCRIPTION
For $49.99 USD per month, or $249.99 USD per year, the instructor subscription includes the Player Subscription features plus these additional features:

PRACTICE:
Create new and review existing student practice sessions on the Practice screen
Set the Putt Map for a session, compare a Putt Map against another session. Quick compare Putt Maps by filtering for sessions by player, break/slope, putter, distance. (iPad only)

REPORT:
Instructor Console - View students Golf Bios, Review sessions, Start Sessions, Run Reports
Putt Metrics Report. Review key metrics from selected sessions compare sessions across player, break/slope, putter, distance. Expand metrics to view a heat maps of each putt in the 2 sessions overlaid (iPad only)

REVIEW:
Instructor session history provides a Timeline View with summary metrics and a detailed List View across their students that drills down by day to session to putt. Filtering provided to focus on specific students or other criteria.
Run reports against student sessions or by time periods
Run a detailed analysis of a students session and print or share with them via email
Use the Putt Map & Putt Metrics tools to visually and quantitatively compare a students putting results across sessions (iPad only)


PURCHASE INFORMATION:
- Payment will be charged to iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase
- Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period
- Account will be charged for renewal within 24-hours prior to the end of the current period for the cost of the subscription you chose
- Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user's iTunes Account Settings after purchase
