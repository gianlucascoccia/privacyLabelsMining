Description

RFD-TV Now – use our app on your iPhone or iPad to get family-friendly programming that includes programing related to Agriculture, Equine, Entertainment, Rural Lifestyle, and Western Sports.

Includes RFD-TV's 24/7 live stream of the broadcast and well as the full library of video on demand episodes.

Agriculture - Get the latest farming news and agriculture insights with a range of shows created just for farmers and ranchers. No matter the size of your operation, these shows give you the information you need to see. Some of the shows featured include: “AgPhD” with the Hefty Brothers “This Week in AgriBusiness” “U.S. Farm Report” “Ag Day” and many more!

Equine - Whether you own horses or just love them, we have programs that celebrate the beauty, strength and intelligence of these magnificent animals. Look for “How-To’s” from renowned experts, watch beautiful trail rides and learn about the special relationship between people and their horses. Some of the shows include: “Best of America by Horseback” “Chris Cox Horsemanship” and many more!

Entertainment - If you’re looking for family-friendly entertainment and a variety of music, this is the app for you! See performances from country music’s icons including, Dolly Parton, Porter Wagoner, Marty Stuart to name a few. See bluegrass greats like Alison Krauss and enjoy polka shows featuring bands from around the world! Some of the shows include: “Opry Encore” “The Joey + Rory Show” “Gaither Gospel Hour” and many more!

Western Sports - Whether it’s roping or riding or racing around barrels, our Western Sports programs are filled with exciting rodeo action. Celebrate the next generation of rodeo greats and get insights into the sports from the trusted experts. Some of the shows include: “The American” “Racer’s Edge” “Western Sports Weekly” “Cinch NHSRA Rodeo” and many more!

Rural Lifestyle - Explore America with a variety of shows from every corner of the country. These programs include cooking segments, profiles on small town life, hidden gems off the interstate and so much more. It’s like taking a road trip on the backroads without the need to stop for gas. Some of the shows include: “Positively Paula” “Small Town, Big Deal” “FarmHer” “Classic Tractor Fever” and many more!

Like what you see? Then subscribe to the Full Access package for $9.99/month or $89.99/year and see all this great programming.
