Description

Teleport is your style studio. Everyone can share their fits. We believe creativity comes from safety to experiment.

You can share no filter videos quickly with your favorite music. Feel free to tag products and build your closet. Our app feels more raw and authentic thanks to (a) no camera uploads and (b) no filters.

We have an incredibly positive tight-knit community that celebrates playfulness and a raw aesthetic. We believe that having a safe and playful environment makes the Internet feel like a shared cozy place.

We’re still early in our journey, so you have a huge impact on our community and the app. Write to us any time, and have fun. Let’s build something beautiful together.

Charliebd818 , 07/26/2020

Tik Tok... wait for it... couture

Occasionally, if you’re lucky, you’ll come across an app that just makes sense. You open it for the first time and you immediately get it. Teleport did just that. It took shopping and made it hyper personal. So personal that these recommendations aren’t shrouded by “influencers” and “brand deals”. People just recommend the products they love, use, and wear everyday. If I buy something from one of their videos, they make a small commission. That seems like a fair and honest way to incentivize authenticity. For example, there’s this watch I’m obsessed with called the Withings. Tracks my heart rate and steps but looks like a luxurious (non techy) watch. I can make a video flashing it, talking about it, running with it, etc. I grab a link for it from somewhere on the web, I add it to Teleport, and like magic, people can now buy the watch through my video and I make a small cut.

I know this app is super young (i haven’t purchased anything), but for it to be this polished already is astonishing, and the community is growing pretty quickly too. Pretty fun to just peruse through videos.

jaylaaax , 01/15/2021

Amazing!!

Teleport is one of those apps that you really haven’t seen before and I’m being completely honest. One look into the app and you may feel that the vibe is similar to TikTok however, the atmosphere and the app itself is completely different.

Through Teleport, you aren’t crowded by “influencers” who are profiting off of posting as well as shoving brand deals down your throat. The users are all genuine people who just love fashion. The users are also very friendly and supportive unlike TikTok. Teleport also emphasizes “no filter outfits” which is really refreshing. This is because you don’t have to go through your feed and see people editing their outfits and videos to put pressure on the user that they have to look and edit like that. Teleport really is a raw and genuine app.

Teleport also made the option of online shopping easily accessible. If you see an article of clothing that you like on someone’s page, you can easily click the link and buy it straight through the app. This also benefits the user because it allows for the user to make a small cut of profit. The “profit” though, is credits that allow you to purchase straight from the app as well for a discounted price.

You wont regret using Teleport, download it and try for yourself!
