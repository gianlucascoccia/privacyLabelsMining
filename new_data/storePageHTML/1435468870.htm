Description

Preflop+ is THE best app to improve as a poker player. GTO preflop charts pre-loaded (no internet connection required) to make +EV shove, calls and rejams based on Nash equilibrium.

Matt Berkey, High Stakes Cash Game Player and Founder of Solve For Why Academy says "Preflop+ is a great tool. It takes all the Nash Charts and distills them at your fingertips. It is far better than other apps as it displays EV of what each hand will be earning."

Lara Eisenberg, WSOP Circuit Ring Winner 2019, says "Preflop+ is a fantastic app for poker training. I find it particularly useful for drilling pot odds and equity which is something I haven’t found with any other app on the market. I use the app every single day to improve my ability to quickly calculate pot odds/equity situations which is invaluable to be able to do quickly, especially in the live setting."

Unlike many popular solutions, we present you with the exact EV of all the hole cards as a chart so you are equipped with making your own decision. This will help you make better decisions such as avoiding marginal spots when you have clear edge over the field. And also help you take any +EV spots when the field is pretty tough knowing the exact EV of your holdings.

Now you can create your ranges and view them on the go. Create ranges OPEN, CALL, 3-BET, 4-BET and more! Beautiful Chart overlays lets you see relevant actions in one chart.

The app also has the fastest and most accurate equity calculator for all streets - Preflop, flop, turn and river.

We have training modules to train different aspects of Poker. Pot Odds, Equity Drills, Shove/Fold Charts Trainer, Combinatorics are some of the training modules available in app for you to train. Train regularly and become the best poker player, ever!

Some of the features include:

- Beautiful charts, color coded so you can make your decisions quickly
- EV of every single shove and/or call is shown so that you can base your decisions based on EV and avoid those marginal spots as an exploit!
- Training Mode lets you train to improve on all situations.
- Equity Calculator and Equity Drills lets you study equity calculations - hand vs hand, hand vs range, range vs hand and range vs range.
- Training can be targeted for specific spots so you can choose what to train and what not to.
- Easy to navigate and use.
- Eye-friendly color schemes to use the app for long period of times.
- No internet connection required and results are shown instantly as soon as you choose the spots!
- 100% satisfaction guaranteed!

We are continously working on the app to keep it up-to-date. If you do have any suggestions or feature request, please email us on info@craftywheel.com

Lots of potential, still buggy

App has great potential. What is designed is supposed to help greatly even advanced poker players.

However, bugs limit usability to a point where app ends up becoming shallow. For example, when defining and customizing ranges in iOS, it is not possible to add different actions (open, open shove, limp, etc...). It is also not possible to add frequencies to different combos.I tried to contact the company by email to see how to sort this but got no answer...

Hope they will correct the bugs and the app will become all that it can be.

Update: after contacts with the developer, I can indeed add frequencies, but different actions are limited to the paid version.

All in all, this seems like a great app, not super user friendly and lacking better instructions, but super powerful. As it is, the free version is a sample of what the app is, like a trailer to a movie, but not very useful.

Paid version is probably worth depending on your buy in levels, for me at this time it is too expensive to justify the returns I would get.

Wish the developer luck and updated to 4 star which is fair for the free version, in my view.
