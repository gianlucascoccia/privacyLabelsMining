Description

Never show up unprepared on Sunday again! Worship Online is used by over 20,000 worship musicians and vocalists to prepare for their worship sets each week! Cut your preparation time in half. Guaranteed!

Get instant access to the highest quality worship tutorials taught exactly how it's played/sung on the album! Including:
• Electric Guitar
• Acoustic Guitar
• Keyboard
• Drums
• And Vocal Tutorials!

Whether you’re an individual musician, or a worship leader seeking to equip your team, we have the tools to excellently prepare you for worship while giving you back hours of your time.

With tutorials, lessons, chord/number charts, and
transposable guitar/bass tabs, you can master your setlist with ease!

Our instructors currently play for and have previously played for, some of the most popular worship artists in the world including; Kari Jobe, Lauren Daigle, Elevation Worship, Bethel, Chris Tomlin, Matt Redman, Vertical Worship Band, Passion Band, and many more!
