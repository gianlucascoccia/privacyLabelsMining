Description

Effectively track your time at work with Clock In!

Clock In helps you keep track of your hours, pay, tips, and notes for all of your jobs. You can clock in and see your time and earnings as you work or add shifts after your work. Each shift includes when you started, stopped, any breaks you may have taken, the name of the job, your pay rate, tips, and notes. You may quickly export/share a timesheet with any number of shifts in a variety of configurations and formats. Add jobs to make tracking your work even quicker. Jobs are color coded so that you can clearly see when and where you worked in the calendar. Jobs can also have a pay period which allows you to see pay period by pay period time worked and pay.

Features
- Clock in to have a time card that tracks your current time worked and pay.
- Add jobs with pay rate and pay period, each color coded.
- Add shifts with a job or no job, pay rate, start time, end time, breaks, tips, and notes.
- View all your shifts in the calendar and list with a sum of time worked and pay by the month or pay period.
- Share a timesheet/invoice with your selected date range and the fields you would like to include as a CSV, styled/HTML, or plain text.
- Dark mode
- Minutes and decimal format

Paid Features
- Unlimited shifts and jobs.
- Sync between your devices.
- Store everything in iCloud.
- Remove Ads.
- Export timesheet/invoice as PDF.

Excellent, logical layout and reasonable price

Clock in has a very easy to use layout and very logical app mechanics. It gives you the ability to program in a specific time range or just press and hold to start a shift right when you press the button. You can enter in break time, different pay rates, and things like overtime and different jobs. You can also edit any of these after the fact. One last good feature is the ability to export shift data via email. I really liked how the app gives you access to all features for a limited quantity of recorded shifts. In conclusion, if you are working as a handyman or anything where you are recording your own hours, this is the app you want. This is not a sponsored review.
