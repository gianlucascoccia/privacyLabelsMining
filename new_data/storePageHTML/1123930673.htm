Description

Find the hidden words with your fingers in Word Search PRO for FREE now!

If you're looking for the most trendy word search game, Word Search PRO is the game for you! You will enjoy a fun challenge as you identify the hidden words and then swipe up, down, left, right or diagonally to mark them. It's a fun way to hone your vocabulary, spelling and puzzle skills. The game has 3 difficulty levels from easy to hard, you can choose them freely. Need more challenges? Try the Blitz mode and Marathon mode and enjoy time pressure.

abcs300 , 04/17/2019

Fun time waster

I didn’t really know what the game was when I downloaded it to my phone. Finally after months of it being on here I decided to give it a try. I really thought it was a more of a traditional “word search” in which the words themselves are already listed and then you find and circle the words as they’re found. This game starts you off at the lowest/first level (I think anyways, it didn’t seem to allow me to just choose anything, there are little lock symbols next to every level except for the first one). Anyways, it differs from a traditional word find in that it gives you clues to let you know what kind of words you should be looking for. It gives you points for each word found, does not let you move on to the next set of words without finishing the words you’re currently searching for, and from what I can tell you can call on other players in the game to help you out if you really can’t find your words. I’m still very early in the game, so some of this info may be incorrect (Sorry if so 😬), I am providing a review because the game brought up a prompt asking if I would do one.. hope this helps someone! 😊😅

Flyingheartclub , 08/12/2021

Fun and challenging but….

I am really enjoying playing the game where you have a theme & you look for the amount of words with the amount of letters indicated. I enjoy the fact that you’re allowed to gain points by finding & selecting other words outside the theme, HOWEVER, I never have any idea why a word like car will be accepted in one puzzle but rejected in another, or why over half the words I find aren’t accepted, it is extremely frustrating. I find “sea” not a word, “adamant” not a word, but nasty is. Then I find “twinkle” it’s a word, but parts of it are not. “Twin” was, but “wink” was not nor was “win”. Words like “err” or “rye” & other 3 letter words are rarely a word that gets points. I get so frustrated cuz I’ll find large words & they’re not accepted like “waking” or “former”. I really want to know the rules concerning the extra words as it can really drive me nuts, And I know it’s not giving me a certain number of words per puzzle as it will reject then accept in same puzzle. This app is a win overall but I feel like I’m going to go cray if someone doesn’t give me the criteria for the extra words. It’s so illogical seeming, like I said some words are accepted sometimes but not others like “run”. Okay happy playing! Sorry for rant. 🤪

What's the use? , 07/03/2018

Fun game but...

Very enjoyable way to pass the time. However, the ads that pop up between games are extremely annoying. I wouldn’t even mind paying a little bit to get rid of them (although it would be irritating), but I don’t see any way to do that. Also, sometimes when you finish a game, a blank white screen appears, and there’s no way to get rid of it except to exit the game and start again. That happened to me today, and even when I tried to start the game again, the white screen was still there.

Update: I just went in to play the game, and all I get is the blank white screen. Very frustrating. I am probably going to delete this app even though I really like it, and look for a word search that works all the time. Or maybe I’ll just read a book.

Update: The game froze, and all I could get was a blank, white screen. I wound up deleting it and downloading it again. So far, so good. It’s very enjoyable, and I spend far too much time playing it. This time I paid to get rid of the ads. Big improvement.
