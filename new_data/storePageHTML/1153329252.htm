Description

March your way through the evil lands, crush the gigantic bosses and free the princess in the epic idle role-playing game, Crush Them All.

Tap with one finger, collect and upgrade hundreds of heroes, find powerful artifacts and progress in this never-ending journey even while taking a break!


Features:
● COLLECT and UPGRADE heroes to crush stronger enemies along your journey
● LAUNCH visually impressive skills
● EXPLORE 1000+ stages
● ASCEND to new worlds to earn prisms and make your army stronger.
● CRAFT 50+ powerful artifacts to give your heroes more powers
● PROGRESS while being away, earning gold and battling enemies.


PLEASE NOTE! Crush Them All is free to download and play, however, some game items can also be purchased for real money. If you don't want to use this feature, please disable in-app purchases in your device's settings. Also, under our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, you must be at least 13 years of age to play or download Crush Them All.


A network connection is also required.

Best idle play to date

Sasha303 , 07/19/2021

Fun game

It’s a fun game, and impressively mmo like for being idle - there are resources, dungeons, various guild bosses and different combat options, pvp, then the idle aspect of gold collection.
There are a lot of ads. I have played other idle games, and the game boost ad is usually 2-4 hours. Here it is 20 minutes base. That said, all the ads are optional even though they’re helpful enough you probably want to do them. There is no direct way to purchase no ads; (in that you get the same boosts without watching the ad, called skip ads in this game) instead you have to get to the maximum tier of their VIP system, for which it doesn’t show the price for at the beginning, but given my experience with that sort of thing I imagine it’s far too expensive for a normal person to buy one-off. Although you get lots of other useful things, it would be nice to have a way to purchase just no ads immediately.
My other annoyance is the gold collection. It is set up so each new thing makes substantially more than the old one. However, it takes a few manual collections of the new one to reach auto collection level. The result is, by the time you reach auto collect, that particular revenue source is pretty worthless. Considering this is an idle game, it would be nice if we could actually make some significant amount of progress idle in that area. Currently, without using items, you can only really progress offline through the stages.
