Description

Have you ever been tired of spam calls, telemarketers or scammy messages?
Call Protect is the app that helps you look up the unknown phone numbers, identify spammers and block unwanted calls and messages.

With Call Protect, you will feel in control of your phone and be confident again to pick up any phone call without guessing who's calling. You will be protected from spammers, scammers and annoying telemarketers.

Main Features:
1. LOOK UP PHONE NUMBER - CALLER ID
Enter a phone number and look up who they are. Caller ID helps you always know who's calling

2. IDENTIFY SPAMMERS, TELEMARKETERS, DEBT COLLECTORS AND EVEN SCAMMERS
Our huge database of blacklisted numbers shields and protects you from harm.

3. AUTOMATICALLY BLOCK UNWANTED CALLS AND MESSSAGES
The app helps you to detect, get warning or automatically block spam calls, unwanted messages or inappropriate advertisements.

4. JUNK SMS FILTER

5. BUILD YOUR OWN BLACKLISTS

6. REPORT SPAMMERS AND HELP US TO STRENGTHEN OUR EXISTING DATABASE

Download the app, protect yourself and fight against the spammers and scammers !
