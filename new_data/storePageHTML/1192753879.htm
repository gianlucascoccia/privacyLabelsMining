Description

Official app for FanFiction.Net is now on MacOS!

* Read - Access millions of stories for free
* Write - Edit and Publish your stories on the go
* Translation: Read stories translated into your native language via our AI engine
* Syncing - Library/Downloaded syncing between devices
* Dictionary - Multi-language dictionary lookup
* Forums - Join writing groups or make friends with millions of other readers and writers.
* Push - Push notifications for Followed/Downloaded stories
* Private Messaging - Enjoying private communication with other users. Send text, image or voice messages.
* Community Archives - Start or browse story archives .
* Beta Readers - Browse and search for registered beta readers.
* Story Management - Browse and manage your favorites and follows.
* User Management - Browse and manage your favorites, follows, and others.

Please note Private Messaging (PM) feature is currently app to app only. PM site integration is coming soon in a future release.

Please email all feedback and bug reports to dev@fictionpress.com.

Nice, buggy, needs interface work

UPDATE: truly incredible. You’ve broken it again. Story flips to the last Page and the font size changes and clips the ends of lines when you shift from the program. So much for conveniently staying where I put it.

I truly enjoy this app and the FanFiction it gives me access to, BUT it could use a bit of work and flexibility

People have touched on the bugs, so I’ll give my wish list instead.

Configuability:
I would like the ability to force the upper/lower status bands to remain constant. I get lost in a good story and currently have no convenient way to monitor the time and the state of my battery without performing the status band mambo of tapping just right and then the top (important because it has the info I need) band disappears the moment I scroll without so much as a by your leave.

Screen functionality. I would love to be able to turn off the screen navigation in whole or in part. Nothing more frustrating than to be in the middle of a chapter only to slip and have the app decide you meant to flip to the top or bottom of the chapter.

Sorting. Sometimes I want my list sorted in most recently updated order, sometimes in most recently read and sometimes alphabetically by either author or title.

Other nice to have: history of the last n stories I’ve opened, not just current or last read as I have now.

987456512 , 07/14/2021

Newest update resolved issue

Up till recently over the past couple weeks I have been unable to access this app. It would crash instantly upon opening, however after deleting and reinstalling the app the issue was resolved. Note I did have to delete and reinstall, simply downloading the new update didn’t resolve the issue.

I mostly use this app simply to check recently released chapters. This app has several features that make it useful to have in conjunction with the website. For example it is easier to pull up favorited and followed stories on the app. Plus this app has the ability to allow you to search by filters not listed online. Though my favorite quality of the app is that you can search both crossovers and normal stories at once whereas on the site you can only search crossovers OR non crossovers.
