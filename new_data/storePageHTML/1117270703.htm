Description

Player Packs for L. MESSI, C. RONALDO, and M. RASHFORD added to the SHOP!

■ The Thrill of Console Soccer in the Palm of Your Hand
With PES 2021, we've taken the same critically acclaimed console gameplay that won E3 2019's "Best Sports Game" award, and distilled its essence to bring you the most authentic soccer experience on mobile to date. Featuring an exclusive partnership with AS Roma, new Matchday mode events, updates to players, clubs and leagues, and much much more!

■ Featuring Europe's Finest
Play with a veritable smorgasbord of officially licensed clubs from the upper echelons of European soccer; including FC Barcelona, Manchester United, Juventus, FC Bayern München, as well as new exclusive partner, AS Roma!

■ New Iconic Moment Series Players
New additions are coming to the 'Iconic Moment Series', allowing you to relive and recreate even more magical moments from the careers of current and former soccer superstars

■ Sign Star Players Through New Player Packs
Player Packs are now available in the SHOP, allowing you to sign Iconic Moment versions of superstar players like L. MESSI, C. RONALDO, and M. RASHFORD.
Each pack comes with an Iconic Moment Series player, myClub Coins, an original menu theme, and 2 Black Ball Special Agents.
*Please check each Player Pack's details before purchasing.

■ Real-Time, Online Matches
Face off against friends near and far with both local and online multiplayer functionality. Then, once you've sharpened your skills, head over to eFootball mode to take on the world in Matchday and various other competitive esports events.

■ Live Among Legends
Live out your soccer fantasy by signing various Legends to your squad, including D. BECKHAM, F. TOTTI, D. MARADONA, S. GERRARD, G. BATISTUTA, FERNANDO TORRES, and K. RUMMENIGGE.

■ Rare Featured Players
Players who performed well during weekend matches will appear in the game as Featured Players. These special versions have increased ratings, unique card designs, and in some instances, additional skills.

■ Weekly Live Updates
Data from real matches being played around the world is collated on a weekly basis and implemented in-game through the Live Update feature to create a more authentic experience. These updates affect various aspects of the game, including player Condition Ratings and team rosters.


*Downloading the Game*
Approximately 1.7 GB of free storage space is required to download and install eFootball PES 2021.
Please make sure you have enough free space before beginning the download.
We also recommend that you use a Wi-Fi connection to download the base game and any of its updates.

*Online Connectivity*
An internet connection is required to play eFootball PES 2021. We also strongly recommend playing with a stable connection to ensure you get the most out of the game.

*Title Display*
If you select Japanese in the language settings of the game application, "eFootball Winning Eleven 2021" will be displayed. If you select a language other than Japanese, "eFootball PES 2021" will be displayed.

Great game, TERRIBLE referee system!

I love this game, and it's by far the best mobile football game, but the referee is just terrible! For one, there are no actual linesmen or referees on or around the pitch, so when you get a card or a talking-to, it makes no sense why you see the referee walk up to your player. I know there is a referee figure running around on the pitch for the console version, but not on mobile.

But it's not just the absence of a physical referee: the fouling system is ridiculous. The referee calls fouls on every little thing. Even when the OTHER TEAM'S PLAYER is charging me, I often get called back for committing the foul! Not to mention that you basically get a yellow card no matter what if you do a slide tackle without making contact with the ball first. Even if your foot barely touches the other player—BANG, yellow card.

Another small thing I want to mention is that yellow card cautions from the previous season still stay with your player after each season. What I mean by this is that you still get post-match "cautionary" suspensions even if you only get one yellow card that season, as you got a yellow card in a previous season. This is not at all realistic and yellow card cautions should be removed after each season.
