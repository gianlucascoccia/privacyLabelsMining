Description

The SwingTracker app requires a SwingTracker sensor for data collection. It includes a demo mode with examples of softball swing data to see how the app works.

The SwingTracker system (sensor + app + online site) uses motion analytics to unlock never before seen swing data.
- COLLECT: Faster than the blink of the eye, SwingTracker captures thousands of data points per second from the softball swing and converts them to swing metrics.
- ANALYZE: Review the most comprehensive softball swing analysis available, including 11 metrics and captured video and 3D views that are linked to key data.
- IMPROVE: Track your progress over time. Practice using SwingTrackers interactive 3D drills to improve and analyze your swing.

After your one month trial, become a PREMIUM HITTER for only $4.99 per month or $54.99 annually to gain access to premium features.
- Sync data to Video Views
- Track your swing progress
- Connect with your coaches & teams
- Dig deeper with web analysis tools
- Access all of your swing data

The SwingTracker app will still collect a limited amount of data without a subscription. See more PREMIUM HITTER iTunes payment details further below.

MEASURE PROVEN SWING METRICS
The SwingTracker sensor accurately captures data throughout the softball swing using thousands of data points per second. It provides insight and feedback, including one overall score; and scores for four major swing components - Speed, Power, Quickness, and Control. And, it compares your swing within a skill level.

VISUALIZES COMPLETE SWING INFORMATION
Watch the bat’s path using the unique 3D Viewer that shows bat and hand speed throughout the swing, impact and bat distance in the hitting zone. Using Video & Data Sync, pinpoint what every part of the body is doing during each step of the swing

ACCESS SWING DATA ON THE FLY
View hitting metrics using graphics and charts and combine them with real-time visuals to better understand the complex dynamics of the swing. Improve your Swing Plane and Hand Cast Distance using interactive 3D drills customized for you.

CREATES POWERFUL COACHING & INSTRUCTION AID
The SwingTracker system is engineered to help coaches and trainers to maximize their effectiveness with each of their players.
· Use one SwingTracker sensor to work with multiple players
· Connect with players remotely to easily track their practice and progress between training sessions.
