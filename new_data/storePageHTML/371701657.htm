Description

Discover now the new version of Corriere della Sera Digital Edition, the best app in Italy for tablets and smartphones that allows you to read your favorite newspaper wherever you are!

Membership types:
- Monthly - € 9.99
- Annual - € 99.99

All forms of subscription are recurrent, recurrence can be deactivated at any time from your profile setting iTunes.
If recurrence is active renewal it will be charged within 24 hours prior to the expiration of your current subscription.

For more information about disabling the anniversary go to the FAQ section of the application.
Please note that payment is managed by iTunes and the purchase will be charged to your account.
