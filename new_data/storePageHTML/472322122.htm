Description

Feeling amazing after your workout? Share your progress to Instagram or Facebook stories, and inspire your friends to join you in getting fitter & stronger than ever!

At Home Workouts by Daily Burn — Get Fit, Have Fun, Repeat!

Fitness training for real people with real goals. Whether you’re looking to lose weight, gain strength, tone your body, or build muscle, Daily Burn has a program and a personal trainer for you.

The best part? You never have to step foot in a gym.

ANY WORKOUT, ANYWHERE, ANYTIME
With our expert-led audio and video workouts, the trainer and the class come to you. Whether you have 10 minutes or 60, there’s a program to fit your busy schedule. And with thousands of workouts to choose from — including yoga, cardio, HIIT, dance, dumbbell strength training, pilates, meditation, postnatal exercises, and more — you’ll never be bored. Don’t feel like choosing a workout? Tune in to Daily Burn 365. It features a different group workout class at 9 am EST every weekday, and you can chat with friends and trainers in the community during and afterward.

NOT SURE WHERE TO START?
We’ve got you. Beginner or advanced, all levels are welcome! Answer a few quick questions about your goals and fitness level — and we’ll help you set goals, create a personalized workout plan, and help you track those goals as you crush them.

FIND YOUR FITFAM AND STAY CONNECTED
Communicate with other Daily Burn members and our roster of expert trainers through live chat to share your fitness journey, feel connected, and stay motivated. Sync with Apple Health (iPhone and iPod Touch only) and share your workout activity with Apple Watch, Fitbit, Jawbone, and more.

STREAM ON YOUR DEVICES + TV
Access Daily Burn on your iPhone, iPad, desktop, or laptop. Cast onto your TV using Apple TV, Roku, and Amazon Fire, or stream your workout from your iOS device to your TV via AirPlay.

Six Years and Counting

I love DailyBurn. I’ve been a member since I had my daughter in 2015 and I truly feel like the trainers are my own, personal friends. I’m an RN and can sometimes have an erratic schedule, so working out can be difficult. I had been using the app for the days when I couldn’t make it to the gym, but after the lockdowns last year and we were stuck in the house it became my primary means of exercise. When our gym started accepting members again in the fall we ended up canceling our membership and now I just use DailyBurn. The variety of workouts is fantastic— I especially love the bodyweight, no equipment classes— and the mobility and yoga series are also amazing. I know I’m going to get a great workout no matter what I choose. I don’t own a scale so I don’t know how my weight has responded to DailyBurn, but I do know that I fit my clothes more comfortably, I feel more confident, and I actually look forward to working out. And because I work out in my garage my 5-year-old is able to participate instead of hiding away in a gym child care center, and hopefully I can be a good role model for positive self-image. Anja, Phoenix, Cody, Dara, Cece, Briohny— Thank you for keeping me going and being so excellent at what you do!
