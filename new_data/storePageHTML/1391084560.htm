Description

Get your dōTERRA® oils game on—anytime, anywhere—with the #1 app for Wellness Advocates!
Modern Essentials® Plus delivers all you need to know for using, sharing, and succeeding with essential oils. It is the most comprehensive app for all dōTERRA® oils, blends, and products!

Two ways to get going:

• “Basic” app—thousands of uses for dōTERRA® oils and blends for a low, one-time fee. (Plus a free, 30-day trial to the “Full Access” subscription service.)
• “Full Access” monthly or yearly subscription—features noted below with an asterisk (*).

Here’s the in-depth information included for each dōTERRA® oil and blend:
• Primary uses
• Secondary and tertiary uses (single oils)*
• "Simple Solutions" protocols*
• Application methods
• Body systems*
• Safety data
• Detailed usage and application charts*
• Chemical constituents*
• Extraction methods
• Aromatic influences
• Blend classifications
• Blending compatibilities
• Historical uses
• Scientific and medical research references (coming soon)*

Here’s what’s in "My Usage Guide"—the working core of the app:
• Instantly search through hundreds of conditions and symptoms to find primary recommended oils, blends, supplements, and personal care products.
• Find secondary and tertiary recommendations of oils and blends.*
• Get "Simple Solutions" protocols.*
• Access recipes for personal care, health, cooking, cleaning, and home care.*

Here’s how to organize and personalize your app:*
• Mark your favorite products.
• Bookmark conditions for quick-and-easy reference.
• Track your current essential oil inventory to see which recommended oils you already have for any condition.
• Make a wish list for easy ordering.
• Write personal notes for any product or condition.

Disappointed

This is my second download of this application. The first on a Samsung galaxy, the second on the iPhone XR. When downloaded to the iPhone XR I now notice that I cannot enter my inventory anymore because I now have to pay an extra fee to do that. This was an utter disappointment I am so sorry that I ever decided to download it the first time much less the second. Shame on you for continually trying to nickel and dime the public. Lil Bit's mom , 01/13/2019

So far, not bad... but not great either

I think this is the beginning of a great app... but it’s not there yet. My main problem is the inventory/wish list section. While it’s great to know what oils are in my inventory, I’d like to be able to add how many are in my inventory, what size of bottles and if they are roll ons, mists, etc. I’d also like to be able to put their locations, and other details in them that could be quickly accessed. I’d also like to be able to add my own notes to each oil, as part of the oil information, not in a separate area. So these are just a few of my issues with the app. I think it’s a great beginning but it still has a ways to go to be a great app.
