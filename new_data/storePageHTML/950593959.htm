Description

Dr. Panda Restaurant Asia is open for business! Explore a beautiful new Asian-themed restaurant while making sushi, stir fry and more!

Kids can take charge in their own kitchen in the brand new Dr. Panda Restaurant Asia! Future fine chefs have the freedom to choose what they want to prepare and exactly how they’d like to prepare it! Chop, grate, blend, fry and more with over 20 ingredients and create the perfect dish!

Key features:

• Choose from over 20 ingredients: Eggplants, tomatoes, peppers, fish, and more!
• Chop, bake, boil, fry and more to make the perfect dish!
• Experiment and have fun! No time limits or scoring.
• Check out how customers react to what you use and how you cook! Dozens of different reactions to see!
• New Asian-themed setting!
• Safe for kids! Absolutely no third-party ads or in-app purchases!

Fun for certain people , 08/30/2017

Very good- please add more!

I like this game a lot. I've heard other reviewers say "what's the objective of the game" and so on, but I like that it doesn't have an objective. It's not something where it makes you come back every day for rewards, and you don't have to collect money to be able to do certain things. This game is perfect for when you don't really want to have to think about anything or get stressed out about anything in the game (which is useful to me in many scenarios). 😌

Although, the reason I didn't give 5 stars is because there are only three meals you can cook. Yes, you can make the meal different every time by adding different foods and so on, but I would just love this game if there were more possible things that the customers asked for.😐

Overall I really like the game, but if you're looking for a strategic cooking game, or a game that has rewards for accomplishing things in the game, I doubt you would like this game. But if you want something to relax with or have a relaxing fun with, you would probably like it.🙂👍🏾

cutiewigg1ns , 07/29/2020

Some changes

I love this game but what I don’t like is the fact that when you something, you can’t go back. So I was just playing and grated some ginger and tried to put it in the dish but accidentally put it in the blender and had to restart because there was no go back button. Also don’t like how rare stir fry is, I want everything to have an equal possibility to be made. I also don’t like the limit on cutting, this is in most of your cooking games and I don’t like it. Last thing I have a problem with is that you can’t cut up the stuff you’ve grated. That’s it😁

A Sims Player , 02/27/2016

Ideas!

Dr. Panda is pretty fun, but I think the game could use a few ideas to give the players some more options to do! For an example, allow us to wash the dishes! We can go to the kitchen, click soap, scrub it around, rinse it off, and put them away! It would teach kids responsibility. It would also be nice if we could have more than one customer at once. We could have groups come in and enjoy themselves as friends! In the dining room, add some fidgets like objects you can press that will customize the room. But instead of changing back to the original every time the player comes in, keep what they changed! Maybe a few more mini stuff like cleaning the tables and kitchen will spice it up too. It's entertaining to have multiple things to do. Again, the game is pretty fun, but some more options would make it perfect!
