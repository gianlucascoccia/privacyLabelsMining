Description

MAKE LASTING CHANGES TO YOUR HEALTH & HABITS

Welcome to the FitTrack health ecosystem. If you’re here, it’s probably because you’ve just bought our Smart Scale. MyHealth is the health app that shows you the results from your scale, what they mean, then tells you how to take action to change them—and it’s currently helping over 1 million people worldwide reach their health goals!

CAN I USE MYHEALTH WITHOUT THE FITTRACK SCALE?

Absolutely! You’ll still tap into workout & nutrition content tailored for your body. But to get the most out of MyHealth & see your 17+ body metrics, we recommend ordering the Dara Smart Scale!

UNDERSTAND YOUR BODY WITH MYHEALTH

You need to know more than just your weight to reach your goals. Every time you step onto your scale, MyHealth will give you insights into your health as a whole. From BMI to body fat, hydration, and more, find out where you stand & how to change your habits to live your healthiest life.

• Get 17+ body metric readings
• Visualize your data
• Tap into progress and history charts
• Log your calories & nutrients
• Sync with Apple Health, Google Fit, FitBit & Apple Watch
• Register up to 8 profiles and bring your family along!

TAKE ACTION TO CHANGE WITH MYHEALTH+

Connect your health measurements to personalized plans and finally achieve your goals. Subscribe to MyHealth+ for workouts, nutrition & lifestyle guidance based on your metrics and goals. You’ll get everything that’s in the free version, plus:

• Science-backed, personalized nutrition and workout plans based on your metrics & designed for your goals
• Daily calorie & nutrient recommendation based on your BMR, food preferences & health goals
• Nutrition tracking that connects to the body metrics on your scale
• Calorie & nutrient logging - automatically by scanning your food with your phone
• Daily workouts based on your metrics & goals
• Deeper insights into what your metrics mean—and how to change them
• Accountability & motivation
• History & progress reports you can share with your doctor, trainer or nutritionist
• Guidance on nutrition, exercise & more

NUTRITION: MAKE EVERY BITE COUNT

• Get personalized calorie & nutrient recommendations based on your BMR & health goals (weight loss, weight gain, weight maintenance)
• Track your food manually by entering calories & nutrients
• Log your food using our database of over 300k foods
• MyHealth+: Track your food with a tap using FoodScan
• MyHealth+: Make tracking even easier with our barcode & packaging scanner

WORKOUTS: SWEAT WITH THE BEST WITH MYHEALTH+

• Connect daily workouts to your health goals
• Track your progress in real time with your FitTrack device & MyHealth+.
• Receive workout recommendations for your health—from weight loss to heart health, holistic health, and body recomposition
• Exercise comfortably with adaptations made for your fitness level
• Learn the habits that will make your body thrive
• Hit your goals, from weight loss to body composition & more!

ABOUT THE MYHEALTH+ PREMIUM SUBSCRIPTION

To use the FitTrack MyHealth+ app with no limitations, you need a subscription, charged on a monthly or yearly basis.

MyHealth+ subscriptions will automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period. There is no increase in price when renewing. Payment will be charged through your Apple account at confirmation of purchase. Once purchased, refunds will not be provided for any unused portion of the term.

Your subscription may be managed and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to your Apple Account Settings after purchase.
