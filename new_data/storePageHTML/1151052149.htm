Description

The first months of having a baby are rough and there isn’t any technology to put your baby to sleep instantly, Baby Tracker is a great app that will help you during that foggy and fantastic first year.

Having an easy-to-use interface, Baby Tracker makes it simple to log all aspects of your newborn's care and view entries from current and prior days. You can track daily information about your baby: feeding, diapers, sleep, activities, pumping, symptoms, temperature, medication. Baby Tracker also offers a timer to track nursing duration, naps, activities, feeding sessions.

Babies go through many firsts, which are exciting events that you'd most likely want to remember. Baby Tracker lets you take a picture of your newborn to preserve memories from your baby's everyday life and fill up a book of moments from your mobile device that you can share with family and friends.

Baby Tracker is also a great app to fill the silence with specially selected music to enhance creativity, engage attention, inspire confidence and even help parents put their baby to sleep or calm them down when they are endlessly crying.

Glitchy and unreliable

I started using this app since the day my son was born. For the first week it worked fine but once our subscription kicked in ($23 for a year with all benefits unlocked) it either wouldn’t save entries, the time stamps for naps and feeding would be wildly different, and the app never updates so you have to close it down and reopen just to see how long it’s really been since your child ate or slept etc. with the time, say I started the timer when my son went to sleep, when I’d go to turn off the time, maybe an hour or so later, to save the amount of time he’d slept itd say a ridiculous amount. 2000 minutes plus. For $23 this is a waste of money and only causes frustration. Only reason I gave it two stars is because for the first week out of three I used it, it was fantastic.

cstobs120 , 12/01/2019

Needs improvements

My baby was born this week and the doctor recommended that I download an app to track feedings, pees, and poops. I wanted something simple and easy to read/input. Unfortunately, the input was easy (though you NEED an edit feature), but the history is terrible. I recommend showing time for each event instead of just “x hours ago”. Also, the history needs to be able to go back more than a day. Im bummed to have diligently tracked everything over the last day and have nothing to speak to at the doctors today. Another recommendation is that Insights should include diaper changes.

Otherwise, very buggy and saw some spelling mistakes. (See insights page). Didn’t personally need the music or additional features
