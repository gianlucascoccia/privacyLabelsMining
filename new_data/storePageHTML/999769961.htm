Description

Get closer to Luke with the official Luke Bryan app! Receive updates from Luke and his team about everything that goes on in Luke’s world - directly to your phone, instantly! The App is an ultimate mobile destination for any Luke Bryan fan.

The app features:

- Exclusive live streams from Luke and his team, on and off the stage, from the road and the studio
- Access to all of Luke’s music to listen via In-App music player
- Tour dates and app-only opportunities including ticket pre-sales for Farm Tour and Crash My Playa
- Exclusive videos, photos and updates directly from Luke and his team documenting his life on and off road
- Access to Luke’s thriving fan community with ability to post and share your own content, fan to fan friending and direct messaging
- Limited edition Luke merchandise - only available in the app
- Regular competitions and ticket give-aways for live shows

Stick With Instagram

I’ve subscribed to this app a few months after Luke launched it in 2015. The first 18 months were fantastic- constant livestreams directly from Luke, exclusive content and a Fan Wall that was packed with Luke fans and beyond fun. Since mid 2017, Luke’s interest in his own app has waned (to put it mildly). He hasn’t posted much of anything other than promoting something to buy or something to watch on tv. The content is So disappointing compared to what it once was. Having to pay for it is ridiculous. I love Luke, but he sure as hell doesn’t need my money for what’s offered in return. Nuthouse membership is worth the money if you go to his concerts. But ‘exclusive content’ on the app won’t be found there as promised. Stick with Instagram- he and his wife post more on there and it’s free.
