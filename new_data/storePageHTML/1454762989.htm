Description

InStories is a unique video editing tool for social media content creators: bloggers, influencers, small
businesses and SMM professionals. The app is trully multifunctional and offers various options of working with content
– from providing inspiration and source materials to video editing. Save your creations in right formats for
Instagram, Facebook, Snapchat and TikTok.

Choose from hundreds of animated text tools - fonts and styles are carefully curated.
Edit photos and videos with InStories intuitive editing tool.
Add cool tunes to make your videos stand out from the crowd.

Here is a recap of numerous InStories functions:
⋆ 300+ ready-made stylish templates for your stories and posts
⋆ Video editor
⋆ Photo and video library
⋆ Music library
⋆ Fonts that are not available in standard social media editors
⋆ Animated transitions between scenes
⋆ Possibility to add your own logo
⋆ High resolution file export
⋆ Formats for all social networks
and more!

You will find that evrything is customizable. Use templates or create your own layouts. Play with varied options,
mix&match, come up with unique and awesome art. Develop new ideas for your account or personalize elements
to match your current style.

And here is more: music and image files fron InStories library have been checked and approved for use on
social media. We took care of this important aspect, so you don't have to worry!

We love high-quality, authentic and original content. And we love it when our users come up with amazing solutions.
That's why we keep an eye on current trends and latest developments in design and art. The result: always improving
and growing library of fonts, templates, music and animation collections. In fact, we update our catalogue 3-4 times a
month! Among our high-popular effects and filters are: neon, minimalistic, plastic, film and the 90s vibe.

Even if you have to create Stories every day, with InStories you will have enough ideas for fresh content. We offer
flexibility, variety of choice and simplicity of usage. Unleash your creativity and don't worry about trivial things.
We've got you covered!

InStories team is always looking for ways to improve our app. So don't hesitate to send us your ideas and feedback:
email hello@ylee.io or Instagram @instories.app. Use #instoriesapp to tag us in your Stories and posts, and we will
share your Stories in our account.

Bring your ideas & business to life!
