Description

More than a simple password manager, My Eyes Only helps organize all your critical digital information perfectly designed data categories. It supports multi-device syncing and data backup of all your encrypted data.

Have fast, convenient access to all your essential personal data. My Eyes Only® provides a secure, easy way to store and keep the critical information you need with you at all times.

Using powerful encryption, it secures your critical information and utilizes Software Ops' secure cloud service to back up and sync to all your devices.

Store website passwords, membership and credit card images and data, bank account information, insurance and membership account information, and much more in predefined categories. If a category doesn't exist, create your own!

Sync To All Your iOS Devices
Add your data to one device and have it show up on your other iOS devices in just seconds. Sync all your devices for one price (other competitors charge per device). Plus, you can share your data with a family member or loved one on their device.

Secure Cloud Backup of your Encrypted Data

Our secure cloud backup makes it effortless to back up your important information no matter where you are. It's never convenient to have a device lost, stolen, or damaged, but we make retrieving your data easy. If you're on vacation, on a business trip, anywhere you go, your personal information is secure and retrievable by you.

Extra Security Means Extra Peace of Mind

My Eyes Only® utilizes layers of protected encryption. This advanced encryption technology includes password protection, powerful 256 bit AES encryption, and an additional layer of keychain security that other apps don't have. This ensures that your important data will be safe if your phone is lost or stolen. Even we can't see or access your data.

Try Before You Buy
My Eyes Only® is free to download and a try-before-you-buy app. This gives you the chance to try it before you decide to purchase. You can add 20 items to the app before you are asked to purchase a subscription. Pay once for all your devices.

Mike'sMental , 05/17/2018

There is a niche for MEO

I have been using this application for years and it has yet to fail me. It’s a solid app with a straight forward user interface. Notwithstanding these observations, the world of cyber security is growing more complex and there are now other password managers that are more robust than MEO, such as Dashlane and LastPass. Personally, I have opted for the former over the later, but both are pretty much at the cutting edge. This however does not imply that I have given up on MEO. I still have the application on all my desktop Macs as well as the app on my iPhone and I use both quite regularly. Why do I continue to use MEO after adopting Dashlane? Because it’s user interface is more efficient. If I need automated sign-on or a strong password generator I will go to Dashlane, but if I just need to get to a password quickly, I can authenticate by finger print and I am there in a matter of seconds. This is where MEO earns it’s righteous place in the world of password management. Software Ops has found their niche in the complex world of password management and they’ve nailed it. For the convenience it offers, I am happy to pay for my subscription.

Is it the be all end all of password management? Probably not, but in my opinion it has found it’s niche...
