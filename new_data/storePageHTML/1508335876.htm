This App Changed My Life

More appropriately, Juliana and Mark helped me to change my lifestyle and make yoga a practice in my daily life to relieve stress, tension and to feel good. I started following Boho Beautiful in the beginning and through pregnancy and recovering from having children I was able to tone and strengthen my body and regain my core. I recommend them to everyone and I can’t recommend this app enough.
