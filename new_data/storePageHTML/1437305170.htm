Description

Ellevest was built by women, for women. The financial industry wasn’t. Designed to get more money in the hands of women.

Ellevest is NerdWallet's 2021 winner for Best Robo-Advisor for IRA Investing

Membership at Ellevest

As a member, you get access to digital investing, cash back with the Elevest debit card(2), retirement planning, learning tools, career coaching, and financial planning starting at just $1/month(3). There are no minimums, no barriers, no hidden fees.

Start Investing

There’s no minimum deposit(4) to get started. Our investment algorithm factors in important realities for women, such as pay gaps, career breaks, and longer average lifespans.

Learn Something New

Access quick tips, interactive quizzes, weekly newsletters, in-depth guides, and free workshops to discover what you need to know.

Banking to Earn Rewards

You can earn cash back when you shop select brands with the Ellevest debit card. Round up the extra change every time you spend(5). There are no hidden fees with the Ellevest debit card(6) plus get access to your money at any ATM with unlimited withdrawals — we’ll reimburse the fees.

Retirement Planning(7)

A personalized investment plan for your retirement. We recommend how much you should save for retirement — down to the dollar. We’ll also help you roll over a 401(k) or transfer a traditional, Roth, or SEP IRA.

Work With a Coach

Get more out of your membership with discounted coaching. Work 1:1 with a financial planner or career coach to create a plan for your money and career.

Mbderby68 , 02/08/2021

More Data & Education About YOUR Accounts

I love Ellevest and everything it stands for, and as a woman in my late-twenties who had yet to build a dedicated investment account outside of my 401K, I was really excited to branch into investing with Ellevest! The articles and courses for learning are great. I think Ellevest still has a lot of improvements they can make on sharing data, insights and learnings for your accounts. Finding the accounts that my money is invested in and learning more about them isn’t super user-friendly or intuitive and honestly doesn’t make much sense. It would be great if there were articles or an easy way to learn more about what they’ve invested my money in and why. There’s a chart that shows my overall projections based on what I invest each month, but I’d also love to see a chart with my overall account holdings over time and see how it’s risen and grown. I feel like their education offerings are very generic and would appreciate if they could build those features out to be more specific or at least link to education articles that could teach me more when I’m in my account looking at my portfolio. Overall, I really believe in this company and am glad to be supporting it, but I don’t feel like I’m learning as much as I could and don’t have as much visibility and education on my portfolio as I’d like. Hoping that’s in their improvement plans in the coming year!

CarJasPa2013 , 08/25/2020

Fan of the brand not the app

I love Ellevest as a brand and have followed their newsletters for months before downloading the app and starting an investment account. I am shocked at how buggy the app experience is - 1) logging in was a pain because Face ID and any saved passwords were not working at all; the login boxes remained blank. 2) I reset my password and after getting an in-app confirmation, something was running in the backend that forced the app to run without dimming or locking, so by the time I got back to my phone it was raging hot. 3) The text input was wonky and every time I tried inputting my login info or reset password info the CTA under it would block the text somehow so it was hard to see what I was typing? 4) Basic things like changing linked bank accounts or upgrading/downgrading/canceling memberships are difficult, and I get it, you need to make money but it’s a treasure hunt which is an unpleasant user experience.

Things I do love: the brand, Sallie, and the aesthetic of the app. The content available is also very interesting and I’ll give the app some more of my time!
