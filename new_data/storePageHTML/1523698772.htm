Description

Coach USA offers safe, reliable, affordable bus service. No more waiting in long lines! Use our app to view our bus schedules or seamlessly purchase your bus tickets. Simply show your ticket to the driver to scan when boarding for a truly contactless experience.

Whether you’re a commuter, a leisure traveler or a college student, Coach USA has your transportation needs covered. We make it easy for you to purchase a single ticket for a day trip or a multi-ride ticket for regular travelers. Create an account to help us remember your preferences and help you to get your tickets as quickly and easily as possible.
