m99712 , 12/13/2019

This worked for me!

I have had this app for a couple months. I downloaded it shortly after signing up for the TEAS. The thing is, the last class I have taken was over two decades ago, so suffice it to say, my knowledge on these topics was fairly rusty (especially science).
I took the TEAS today and passed with an 85. I went back to look at this app and how I did compared to the real thing. I answered around 600 questions on the app, and my scores on the actual TEAS are about the same or better as they were with each subject on this app (overall here is 78% vs actual 85%).

Over the course of the last month or two, I have used a TEAS book, this app, and recently, a few practice tests the faculty gave us to practice with, and the scope of the questions were so different on each platform that I had no idea what to expect from the real thing. In my opinion, the difficulty of the questions on this app most closely mirrored the difficulty of the actual test, and I think my results exemplify that.

I am very very pleased and so happy I spent the money on this.

3rd attempt

So I’ve had this app before my first attempt at the teas. I never really used it nor did I take it seriously. Now I’m using it more and I do see a lot of similar test questions to what I remember being on the exam. Think of it this way. There’s only so many ways they can rephrase anatomy test questions. The only thing I don’t like about this app is the English section. It is in no way similar to the TEAS English testing style. Other than that, I do appreciate this app, it’s a great way to study on the go if you don’t want to lug around a large book!
