Description

Forget typing in captions for your videos by hand every time you’re ready to post. “Captions” will automatically create captions for your videos and give you editing power to adjust content before you’re done. Simply export your finished captioned video and share it wherever you want.

djspookyty , 02/24/2021

Great overall especially if you have auditory processing issues!!!

I myself have auditory processing issues and so for the longest time couldn't caption my own Tiktoks which made me feel like crap. I want my content to be as accessible as possible and for the longest time couldn't. This however is an amazing app and I couldn't recommend it more.
I did a video using it and asked some of my friends to review it to make sure the captions weren't going too fast and the general consensus was this is a good app! While it does lag a little it follows your words really good. This surprised me honestly because I do talk a little fast but it picked up pretty well. Also its almost 100% accurate! I only had to correct a couple of words.
Editing captions is a bit of a pain if I'm honest because it gets a little slow and laggy when typing but overall pretty good.
If I could recommend any changes its less lag when fixing captions and maybe a way to make sure the words match up 100% when speaking.
Otherwise I love this app and I've already begun to recommend it to everyone I know!
