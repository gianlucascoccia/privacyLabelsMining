Description

Recruit Justice League Legends like Batman, Superman, The Flash, Wonder Woman, The Joker, Harley Quinn and many more in the ultimate DC superhero fighting game!

Download now to experience the ultimate strategy RPG, packed with all of your favorite Justice League superheroes and villains. Join Batman, Superman, The Flash, Wonder Woman, The Joker and many more in a battle against Nekron and the Manhunters. Collect and level up your unlikely team of Super Heroes & Super Villains to save the world in epic 4v4 combat!

Editors’ Notes

Boy, do we love having the DC Universe as our personal fight club. In this team-based battler, classic powers and weapons can be upgraded in so many ways we’d need a Batcomputer to figure out them all. Beyond the fun of having Superman fight beside Lex Luthor, Legends’ tactical challenges—should we use Flash’s Speed Punch or Red Streak ability?—keep us coming back.

Amazing game

I’ve been playing this game for well over a year and that’s saying something as I prefer console over mobile and scarcely download or play mobile games. There are so many amazing features of this game and it really captures the amazing dc experience. You can also make you wildest dc fanboy (or fangirl) dreams come true by making doomsday fight along Superman Batman beyond and brainiac or other combinations. I can play as all my all-time favorite characters such as Batman Beyond. Furthermore, I love the strategic approach you all take which makes the game very dependent on you yourself, but also give you the thrill of getting a random character’s fragments from a mystery pack. There are near infinite possibilities and other features I haven’t even began to talk about, but case in point I love This game a lot and I hope you guys keep up the good work. (Also I’m super hyped for The Batman Who Laughs and King Shazam to join the roster)

However, I wish it was just a bit easier to acquire the in-game currency and easier to get character fragments. A way to solve the currency problem is just to increase the amount you get from R.E.D. Alerts, rewards from the story mode, and so on and so forth. And as for the character fragment problem, an idea I had was do divide the mystery packs into sections, those being things like hero or villain, energy physical or mystic, earthing or alien, or some kind of classification like that.

The Elven Minstril , 01/18/2018

Fun game with major Balance issues

I have played this game every day for about a year, give or take a few months. I enjoy it for the most part. It is fun to fight with my favorite super heroes and slowly build them in strength and power. The game stands well as a free to play game. You can of course pay money to acquire things quicker, but it isn’t mandatory. And there is plenty to do without having to spend a lot of cash for time-earned energy.

But in the past few months, one character, in combination with a handful of others, has been so brokenly overpowered that a team with him (Deathstroke) cannot be beaten in auto play and generally beats teams several hundred or thousand power points stronger. Ninety percent of the top ranked players are exploiting Deathstroke teams. The makers of the game have done little to fix this and have even gone so far as to delete any forum post making this complaint. I waste many PVP resources each week trying to reset my opponent list until I am not burdened with facing a half dozen of them at once.

If this balance issue was fixed, I would give this game a 5 star rating.
