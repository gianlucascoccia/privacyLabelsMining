Description

Wherever, whenever …get the Ecobank Mobile app and make everyday banking as easy as ABC!

The Ecobank Mobile app allows you to enjoy our banking services instantly and conveniently on your mobile device. The app provides you with an easy channel to manage your account and perform financial transactions in a simple and secured manner. The app gives you access to affordable banking services and more wherever you are on your mobile 24/7 ,365 days a year. The app also gives you access to the Ecobank Xpress Account.

It is one unified application for all the 33 where Ecobank Operates.

Features on the Ecobank Mobile App include: 

- Send Money to 33 African Countries instantly
- Open an Ecobank Xpress Account
- Check Account balances & transaction history
- Airtime TopUp for Self / Other
- Pay Billers & Merchants
- Account Statement Request
- Block Card / Cheque Request
- Locate the nearest Ecobank Branch / ATM / Xpress Point
- Attach Other Bank cards
- Cardless withdrawal

Go digital! Get the Ecobank Mobile App.
