Description

Now in public beta! Una is the one place for all your travel plans. Discover trips, collaborate on plans with your friends, book your hotel and get smart personalized assistance so that you can spend less time researching and more time enjoying.

DISCOVER TRIPS
Una offers many different flavors and durations of itineraries to suit your interests. New trips are added weekly to Una’s Public Trips feed from our user community and cool sources like NYT 36 Hours, Lonely Planet and YouTube.

COLLABORATE
- Invite your friends and fam to join your trip to start sharing and building itineraries.

BOOK YOUR HOTEL
- Awesome deals on 1000s of hotels!
- Reservation support provided by Una and Hotelbeds.
- Book securely with Stripe checkout.
- Hotels that meet health and safety standards are tagged as “Safe 2 Stay”.
- Track all your reservations in one place by tapping on “Reservations” in the menu.

ONE PLACE TO ORGANIZE IT ALL
- Plan, book and share your trips in Una - from spontaneous weekend getaways to bucket list treks. No more jumping between tools that aren’t suited for trip planning like notes, maps, docs or spreadsheets!

MAPS, MAPS, MAPS
- See your plans on a map with estimated driving times
- Let Una optimize your route for you, based on a starting point of your choice
- Let Una know which stops are important and get personalized recommendations for what to do, see and eat nearby

BUILT FOR YOU
- The “For You” carousel offers personalized suggestions based on your likes, past selections, and important trip stops

NO PROMOS or ADS
- Una is ad and promo free. The recommendations you see are purely driven by what Una thinks you’ll enjoy.

ADD A CUSTOM STOP
- Want to add a stop at a friend’s house or a trailhead? Adding your own custom stop is easy!

NATURAL LANGUAGE HIKE SEARCH
- Search for hikes in your own words and find the perfect trail for your group.
- Coming soon: Una will offer natural language search for hotels.

TRAVEL RESPONSIBLY
Una encourages responsible travel by offering information about:
- Outdoor activity (hiking, sightseeing, cycling, beaches and more!)
- Restaurants with outdoor seating
- Cleaning and safety information for accommodations

PRIVACY
- Una doesn’t sell or share your data. Your preferences remain private.

COMING SOON:
More booking types!
More trip content!
More smart, personalized assistance!

Our mission at XOKind is to let AI help you travel better. We use cutting-edge machine-learning technology to simplify everyday decision making, eliminating hassle and confusion.
