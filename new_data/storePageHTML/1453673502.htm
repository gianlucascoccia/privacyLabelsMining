Welcome to Winner, the best tournament manager and tournament organizer app.
With Winner, creating your dream tournament is just a few clicks away!
Try our tournament manager app now, it's free!

- Main Features -

- Fast tournament creation -
With Winner creating a tournament is fast and easy.
You can create multiple teams, multiple players or an entire match schedule with just a single click!

- Multiple sports types -
With Winner you can create a tournament for any sports type:
football, soccer, basketball, tennis, cricket, ice hockey and many more.

- Multiple tournament formats -
With Winner setting your tournament format is easy.
You can choose from a predefined set of formats: league, cup, group stage, round robin, playoffs, series, knockout, single elimination bracket, double elimination bracket or create your own custom format.

- Match schedule -
With Winner you can create a match schedule for your tournament manually or randomly, and edit the match teams or players, date, time and location.

- Tournament sharing -
With Winner sharing your tournament is easy.
You can make your tournament public and let friends and family follow your tournament matches.

- Match events -
With Winner you can add events to your matches.
Goals, assists, penalty kicks, yellow cards, red cards and substitutions.

- Match venues -
With Winner you can set up your own match venues to host your tournament matches.

- Offline and online mode -
With Winner you can update your tournament from any place and at any time, even when you're offline!

- Customized logos for tournaments, teams and players (PRO) -
With Winner you can further customize your tournaments by setting custom logos and pictures for tournaments, teams, players and venues.

- Multiple admins -
With Winner you can set your tournament followers as admins. Admins can also update match results and help you manage your tournament easily.

Great App (Suggestions)

This is an all around great app for people who would want to track their own leagues, tournaments, and playoffs. I mainly use it for the hockey league I’m in charge in. For the hockey template I recommend adding these things that’ll make the app easier to use.
1) When your scoring a game and the home team scores a goal it would be nice if there was a goal horn.
2) When I press the penalty button when a penalty is committed it’ll be nice if I was able to state what the penalty was for (Hooking, Charging, Delay of Game, etc)
3) I don’t like how instead of having periods it has the soccer way of time.
4) Instead of the overall players in the main screen being placed in alphabetical order, they should be placed in the time they were added (It gets hard looking for players for different teams if they’re alphabetically ordered)
5) There should be a shot on goal option when scoring a game.
6) It’ll be cool if we could add the team’s goalie and how much shots they saved or let in. Then we can compare goalies by their save percentage, wins, and losses.
7) When a player scores the app should ask how they scored (Wrist Shot, Slap Shot, Snap Shot, Tip In, etc) and the type of goal (Power Play (PP), Short Handed (SH) or Empty Net (EN)).

Like I said it’s a great app, but adding these things will make it better for people who love hockey.
