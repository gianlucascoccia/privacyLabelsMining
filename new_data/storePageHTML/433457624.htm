Description

The Florida Blue app gives you access to your account information and convenient tools on the go.

· Find care quickly based on your location
· See your deductible and benefits at a glance
· Flash your virtual ID card for proof of insurance
· Connect with us for help
· And more!

Download the Florida Blue app and experience the simplicity. Making the most of your health plan has never been easier!
