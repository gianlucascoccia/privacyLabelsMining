Finally, business cards for the new gen!

First, let me start by saying that before using wingman, as a millennial in the workforce the thought of carrying and exchanging business cards in a professional setting felt like more of an antiquated formality than anything of real value to drive a conversation forward. Most times, I’d hesitantly accept a card from someone at a conference only to connect on LinkedIn/twitter as soon as I walked away, quickly stuffing their card into my backpack, never to be seen again outside of quarterly cleaning. Now, when handed a card, I can scan it with wingman, store all their contact info in my phone AND connect with them on social right there in the moment. Not only do I get points for looking cool, but I also get to politely hand them back their card afterwards. #savethetrees

It would be a crime for major conferences, especially software focused, to not use wingman as a streamlined means of attendee networking. Overall, super sleek app with a surprising wow factor!
