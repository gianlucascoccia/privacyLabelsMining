Description

Note: This app is for trip and rental request management only. You can sign up, book new trips, and list new motorcycles through our website.

Available anywhere in the United States, Twisted Road allows motorcycle riders to rent motorcycles from fellow riders. Creating a profile is fast, easy, and secure. You can list a bike for rent or find one to ride in under 5 minutes. Thousands of motorcycles are either unused or underused across the country and Twisted Road has created a platform for people to earn extra cash by renting out their bikes to local and traveling motorcyclists. We want to match owners with riders so anyone who wants to ride, can ride.

Awful, horrible, potentially a scam

My goodness. Where to start with this service. I booked a bike rental with this app, and after going through all the steps and being approved to ride, i get a text from owner of the bike the day before the rental is supposed to happen telling me that the bike isnt road worthy....really dude?? You didn't think to check that before you listed your bike on a rental service? He told me the tires are bald. Ok whatever, just a dumb guy. Well the owner of Twisted road texted me and apologized and said the bike was unable to be rented because of “damage from the recent storms”. So which one is it? Storm damage or bald tires. Very fishy. Money was refunded so i tried to book another bike, eventually the app told me this rental was cancelled because they couldnt reach the owner of the bike. What is going on. There is absolutely no communication between the people with the bikes and the people who run the app. Now im sitting here with 1000 dollar deposit missing from my account plus the amount of the rental. Im assuming this will be refunded, unless this truly is a shameless scam. In that case i will go to the ends of the earth to get my money back. But still, the service doesnt work at all, do not waste your money, avoid this potential scam like the plague.
