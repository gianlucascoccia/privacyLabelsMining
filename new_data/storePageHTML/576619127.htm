Description

Travel companion for the New Jersey Transit Rail System.
* No Internet connection required!
* Live Track and Train Status
* Find station near you.
* Clickable New Jersey Transit Map
* Advisories.
* Fares
* Notifications
* Alarms
* Transfer Time Option
* All New Jersey Transit train lines supported.
* Path included.
* Supports One Transfer
* Today Widget that shows the status of a selected train # from a certain station

OnlyDavid , 09/17/2018

My go to app for train schedules.

I have the NJTransit official app for buying tickets that includes live train schedules. A have two more apps with schedules. I can go to the NJTransit page and get them, so why this?

This app is the fastest way to see what train (and which track) is coming. Plus, I can follow a train once it leaves the station. Perfect if I know a friend left New York on the 4:57 and I am picking them up from the train station. I can see if the train is on time (and make sure I’m at the station) or is delayed for the innumerable reasons trains get delayed (and not waste my time at the station).

Thanks for this convenient app. It would be great if i could reorder the entries on the main screen. Also, I’d like to be able to name the entries.
