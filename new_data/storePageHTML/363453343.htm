Description

This is the official Pittsburgh Penguins Mobile app. Make your iPhone or iPod Touch a unique part of your game-day experience for Penguins games. Want to catch breaking news of the team? See real-time statistics? Watch video-on-demand clips of press conferences and player interviews? Follow post-game recaps and pre-game previews of the matchups?

Now, you can stay in touch with the Penguins anytime, anywhere, on your iPhone or iPod Touch. And, with one click, you can spread the word--tweet or post to Facebook any media item from the app!

Anytime-anywhere features:

- NEWS: Real-time breaking news from the Pens, previews of upcoming matchups, recaps of previous games
- MEDIA: News, photos, video-on-demand clips of press conferences and player interviews, audio podcasts, organized by date
- HD RADIO: Live streaming 24x7x365 Penguins HD Radio
- STATS: Real-time statistics and scores from the official NHL stats engine, head-to-head live stats of game, player stats, box scores
- STANDINGS: Division and conference standings
- ICE TRACKER: Real-time visualization of the ice rink, viewable by shots, penalties, goals and saves
- SOCIAL MEDIA: Aggregated twitter of all of your favorite Pens tweeters
- DIGITAL KEEPSAKE: Your game-time moment superimposed on the Consol Energy Center jumbotron in the form of a unique digital keepsake
- SCHEDULE: Schedule of upcoming games, scores/stats of previous games, ticket purchases
- CLOSED-CAPTIONING: Text feed of the game commentary

Follow us @yinzcam on Twitter for updates or to ask questions and provide feedback.

Easy Way to Keep in Touch

This is, by far, one of my favorite ways to keep in touch with the Pittsburgh Penguins. I can listen to games, see photos, and watch the feed when I’m free. I’m from the Pittsburgh area but I’m in Montana for school, so the time difference and distance makes it difficult for me to keep up with games. Keeping notifications on is an easy way to make sure I’m still able to follow the games. I’d recommend this app to anyone looking to keep up with the Pens.
