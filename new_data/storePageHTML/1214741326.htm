Lookscope: Closet/Outfit Maker 4+

Virtual clothes organizer

Lookscope, Inc.

Designed for iPhone

    • Free

iPhone Screenshots

Description

Featured in Who What Wear | THE/THIRTY: https://thethirty.whowhatwear.com/best-organization-tips

Lookscope is a revolutionary solution for your wardrobe hassles. Using the app to manage your closet and outfits will feel like cycling to a destination instead of walking.

The app allows you to import photos of clothes and accessories in seconds and makes it very easy and quick to explore possible outfits based on the existing and wished for items in your digital closet, just with swipes. Make sure you use this feature to simulate possible outfits and discover stylish ways of wearing before you buy new items!

You can also save your favorite outfits to build your own outfit portfolio. You will never need to re-think what to wear for work, dates, weddings, interviews, and other special occasions. :)


WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT FROM OTHER CLOSET APPS
1. Simple and basic aesthetics
2. Unlimited closet and lookbook spaces
3. Automatic outfit generation and filters optimized for speed (and saving time!)
4. Automatic backup of closet and favorite outfits data in your iCloud account
5. No need for any extra login (an iCloud login with your phone is sufficient)


SUMMARY OF FEATURES IN DETAIL
1. Closet
- See the collection of your existing and wished for clothes and accessories
- Edit profiles of clothes and accessories easily with intuitive, button-based UI elements
- Use the WISH button to indicate wished for items after importing photos of clothes you are interested in buying. If you star any outfits that are based on any of these items, the outfits will be highlighted with a WISH tag in the Star Looks menu
- Use the 'Try On' feature to get an instant outfit suggestion based on the item you selected
- Check today's weather (just the most relevant stuff)
- Pull down the grid to request a data sync/update if needed

2. Add Item
- Download sample items to quickly fill your closet with essential items (Available for only men at the moment, but we are working on doing something similar for women clothes as well!)
- Import photos of your existing or wished for items from your phone's Photo Library
- Resize and crop imported photos
- Edit colors of photos with filter buttons and sliders
- Edit backgrounds of photos with automatic or intuitive background removal tools
- Create a profile for your new item easily with intuitive, button-based UI elements. If you can't find a brand, you can request us to add it (support@lookscopeapp.com).

3. Change Room
- Swipe the screen or use buttons to explore outfit options based on the items in your digital closet
- Tap on the Star button to save the outfit currently on display
- Explore outfits completely randomly for fun or systematically with a goal while progressively tapping on (holding) items you want to be included in the final outfit
- Change the composition of outfit to display. You can add or remove the following six "body parts" to/from the display during outfit exploration: Head, Top-Inner, Top-Outer, Bottom, Feet and Other (for accessories)
- Explore outfits even faster by choosing color(s), season(s) of items, style(s) of items and a brand of item that you want to be included in outfit options

4. Star Looks
- Check the collection of all the favorite outfits you've starred in the Dress Room
- Tap on one of the favorite outfits to see it in a bigger size (better for sharing with your friends for feedback!)
- Tap on the PICK button to remember your choice for the day or an upcoming occasion

5. iCloud backup (automatically on)
- Keep your closet and lookbook data backed up in your personal iCloud space
- Exercise full control of your device and cloud data (e.g., download/upload/delete/sync)

Great app with greater potential

So I am using this app to organize life and make choosing my outfits fast and efficient each day. I waste a lot of time just staring at my wardrobe feeling overwhelmed and confused—even though it is organized for outfit-pairing! Haha! So enter this app, which I trust will be a lifesaver for creating outfits.

I only have two issues with the functionality, which is (1) we are very restricted on what tags we may put on the clothes. Why not give us ability to make our own tags? Heck, I would purchase that in-app upgrade!! Also, (2) you can tag individual items, but not outfits. Tagging an individual item, say a camisole, as summer is useless if my outfit layers it with a cardigan and outer coat. Now I’ve got a sweatsuit popping up in my Summer outfits because of that darn cami. 🤣 So, if you want to perfect this app, please give your clients full reign on tags. It would be a huge burden to those who struggle with focus and overwhelm when faced with too many options and no way to properly eliminate or categorize them.

Thanks!!
