Description

Lotus Lantern BLE 4.0 is an intelligent APP to control RGB(W) LED strip. It can adjust the brightness and color of LED strip, also has the music and microphone rhythm function, and build in many transition patterns of color and brightness.

AwesomeMazda04 , 08/13/2020

Awesome

This application may not be convenient or satisfying to everyone, but it is for anyone. This app is perfect for me personally. I can use this app to change RGB lights through Bluetooth. I use this on a product named GUSODOR LED strip lights. The best part, I can still use other Bluetooth device while using the app to change the RGB strips. All you have to do is leave Bluetooth on your phone and it’s that simple. Go back to the app and it automatically connects. It’s very convenient. You can simply change colors or use light effects through the app with just a tap on your mobile device(s). ALERT, it DOES NOT support any other music media or any equalizer applications except musics that were bought from iTunes, which like I said, it’s not convenient or satisfying for everyone. Fortunately for me, I buy my music through iTunes, so it’s not a problem to me or for anyone that purchase their music through iTunes. (The music has to be downloaded through your phone in order for it to show up in the application’s playlist through Music lighting effect mode). However, I just want them to fix a bug where when you leave the lights off through the app, the switch is shown off then closing the application. But, after opening the application, you see that the switch icon is turned back on even though the lights are appeared to be off. It does throw me off because I can’t tell whether I left the lights on or off when I’m away from my room. Other than that, it’s an amazing app to use.

rwhprism , 02/22/2021

App works great - even separate devices

I bought 3 of these to put in cabinets around the kitchen. I was only nervous that my kids would break or lose the IR remotes. The app uses Bluetooth and connected to all 3 devices so I can change lighting everywhere I put it. It has some built in color patterns. Also has some ability to follow music if you have downloaded music to add. Can’t use music you haven’t downloaded. I have all power lines on one switch so when you turn on the power, the controller defaults back to the last color setting. Very nice effect. Took some practice to learn to make splices and cuts but It’s basically 1 @ 12v power line with separate RGB grounds . 4 wire and diodes so they can’t be connected reverse biased.
