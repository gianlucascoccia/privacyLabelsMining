Mike A Born , 03/14/2021

Incredible addition to the Adventure Series

I’ve been a huge fan of Adventure Capitalist, and have beaten it 100% after years! I also just found this game and love it all the same.

The game feels incredibly balanced, and rewards you thoughtful investments and strategies to help your progression, with or without paying money.

My ONLY feedback is having some kind of carry over in events each week. Adventure Capitalist did this by gaining unique rewards in the main game, that could be applied to small multipliers in the events. The better you are at the main game, the more you could compete in the events. I would LOVE to see this implemented in Adventure Ages. Each week the events are fun but they start to get repetitive as you are fighting against the clock to receive the same rewards and goals, and it would be fun to have a more competitive system for players that have invested their time, not just their money in the main game. The bonuses or unique rewards you could gain in the main game or events that would apply to events would be a game changer. They don’t have to be major multipliers to the events, just something small to keep players coming back to see if they could achieve higher and higher ranks in the events. Hope this is considered!

Developer Response ,

Thanks for your feedback! We appreciate you sharing your thoughts 😀 If you have any other suggestions (or questions) please let us know. Just tap your Settings Cog, then ‘Get Help’ to chat with us!

Mr. Emoji 😀 , 03/15/2021

Very solid and entertaining idle game

I have genuinely enjoyed every moment I’ve played this game. I have AdVenture Capitalist and Communist as well and I’ve gotta say this is my favorite of the three.

As usual, it’s not pay to win and they don’t force ads onto you, which is something I’ve always liked. The layout is pleasing to the eye and, while it’s reminiscent of Communist, it has a different mood of being not only more futuristic, but professional. The leveling up is also a bit faster and the historical basis is very fun to see who exactly boosts what. The Egypt event actually made me do some research into what those historical figures actually did.

The only glitch I’ve experienced an audio bug in the Japan event where the katana menu opening is replaced with a loud high pitched beep, which can be annoying when swapping back through tabs, but otherwise there have been no terrible technical issues. I’m excited to see what gets added to the game!

Cancan0912 , 02/25/2021

Adventure Communist Reskin

This game is basically just a reskin of their previous game. That alone isn’t so much of an issue, the issue is that they still included a major flaw that Adventure Communist had as well... and that is the inclusion of the tasks that require you to “upgrade x number of cards”. This can cause the player to be caught in a rut where the only way to progress is to either wait multiple days by opening the free daily packages which contain the Xp/cards required to level up, or to (of course) spend real money on the xp or cards needed. The worst part of this is that these tasks are not always visible at the start of a rank, so you can spend all of your resources, just to immediately be met with a task that requires you to spend those same resources that you are now out of.

The devs need to do one of 2 things. Either remove these tasks, or make it so that they are retroactive in the current rank in some way. The latter option can still leave the player stuck, but at least it’s better than how it is now. The player shouldn't be forced to horde limited resources just to meet the requirements of these arbitrary tasks that appear randomly, and are required for game progression.

I mentioned this exact issue back in my review of AC, and nothing was ever done, so my hopes aren’t high. It’s almost feel like these games aren’t tested. Yet another cash grab... I’d hold off on this game until these obvious and glaring issues are fixed.

Developer Response ,

Thanks for your feedback! This aspect of the game is not likely to change in the future. Resource management is part of the strategy of the game. If you have any other feedback, don't hesitate to let us know. 😊
