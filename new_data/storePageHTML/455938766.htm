Description

The Kontinental hockey league official application.

The app is convenient and user-friendly, with a new interface, swift info updates, and live broadcasts of key moments from games.

The new tradition of Mastercard and KHL gives hockey fans an opportunity to share a trophy with the player of their favorite team. You can vote for the best players of the game in the KHL app and collect points.

We make all these improvements to KHL apps just for you, so stay tuned for the latest news
You can send any requests and comments to app@khl.ru.

Terms of use: https://video.khl.ru/page/agreement?locale=en
