MattScott9 , 01/14/2021

Good for both F2P and P2W

I have been playing this game for about 5 months and I’m still having fun! If you log in 2-3 times per day to collect idling rewards and watch ads, and you have a little patience, you can be competitive while never spending a penny. If you are less patient, and don’t mind spending a little, there are plenty of deals and events to micro-transact your way to glory.

On the down side, new servers open every week and die off within a couple months and no efforts are made to consolidate dead servers. Also, the devs have started releasing new features and angels more quickly than they used to and the events are starting to focus more on spending money so I am concerned about where this game is headed.

NicknameAlreadyTaken183646 , 02/21/2021

Crash Simulator

I’ve never written a review for a game but I’m motivated to do so with this one because I loved it enough to put money into it, another thing I never do. BUT IT CONSTANTLY CRASHES. Every 3 minutes it crashes, and it didn’t used to do this. I’ve stopped spending money on it because of the crashes. My device is updated and I run it with zero other apps in the background and it still crashes every few minutes. It’s unenjoyable and I wish I could get my money back because at this rate, I can’t even play it. Super frustrating. Not only that, you can feel it stutter and lag during certain parts of the game (upgrading your angels in any way).

Other than that, it’s cute and fun and pretty straight forward. Feels very rewarding. I would say you could play it F2P if you don’t want to be competitive. If you do though, prepare to drop serious dough. The only other complaint I have besides the obvious CRASHES, is the English translations are terrible. I can usually ignore a couple small mistakes, but the frequency of terrible English syntax irks me and makes me wonder if they care enough about their game to get the language polished up.

If the devs see this, please fix the crashes!!!

mikerhoads , 07/10/2020

Purchasing problems.

This game has had a lot of work put in to it. The characters are beautiful and have a lot of different powers and abilities. Leveling them up is not too easy, but hey! You don’t want a game that’s too easy.

The mechanics of the game are simple with their drop down menus. You aren’t wasting your time trying to find where you have to go to collect rewards. The parts you complete and collect rewards from will be marked with a red dot. Just click on the menu with the red dot, collect your rewards.

The auto battle is handy for when your at work and can’t be on your phone to play. Which is a good feature to have and continue to build your equipment and level up.

Only problem I have, is much like all of these games. I find that once I get over level 50 and I have spent real money on these games. The games always crash and I can not open the app.

Just like this game 😢. I have spent real money on it a number of times. Reached level 72 and now can not open the app to play. When I start the game it shows the white back ground with the gold letters that spell out “JOYPORT GAMES”, then the app force closes.

I may have to delete and reinstall. But will probably lose all my data and characters I have paid to unlock. If that happens, I will not keep the game or spend any more money on it. Disappointing, cause I really had a lot of fun playing this well made game.
