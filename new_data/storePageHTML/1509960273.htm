About the game
----------------------
This game will provide your kids hours of fun and calming creative activity.

The game includes 35 beautifully illustrated coloring pages for boys and girls, designed for the pre-kindergarten toddlers.

Game features:
--------------------
- Selection of colorful crayons \ markers\ sparkling brush.
- Color with animated elements
- Innovative interface that helps children color areas
- Easy and intuitive: Toddlers can play this game independently.
- Calming background music while working.
- Safe environment for your kids (girls & boys). Ads free and No pop-ups.
- Enjoyable and educational.

Educational targets:
----------------------------
- Practice visual perception concepts.
- Establish Motor skills and Hand-eye coordination.
