Description

MyWeather is a beautifully crafted weather app providing you with realtime temperature, AQI, 10-day forecast and accurate time-trend. Powerful weather forecast, covering 50000+ cities. Specially for iPhone & iPad, multiple well-designed themes.

· Multi-platform: Supports iPhone & iPad, specially optimized for differernt screen sizes

· Apple Watch: Clear and concise structure, suitable and effienct for wrist equipment

Joaan Nguyen , 01/03/2018

Pleasantly Surprised

I was surprised to find that this app has so many customizable features that do NOT require purchase. The many options for customization make the app very user friendly. The widget settings are equally sweet and simple.
The design of this app is charmingly minimal; The themes and graphics are modern and attractive without being too flashy.
The app does lose one star because the ‘hourly’ view isn’t very prominent in the widget and in the app itself and gets lost among the rest of the information. A clear and accurate ‘hourly’ forecast is one of the most important parts of a weather app to me (in addition to current/daily and weekly).
Despite this one issue, I think all of the features that this app offers is enough to make me keep it and look forward to future updates and improvements.

lovinmymac , 12/27/2018

Been through many, found one

After trying at least 10 weather apps, paid and free, I’ve decided this app the one. It’s free, has a clean interface and works with the Apple Watch. The depth of information, however, is limited. No maps or radar information. While this can be helpful during times of unpredictable weather patterns or localized weather activity, it is not the purpose of this app. This app appears to be a substitute for the Apple weather app. In this regard, it offers slightly more information along with a beautiful interface and ad free experience. If you need maps and hate ads, pull out the $ and pay for a competing app, otherwise, this app is the free, fast loading, multiregional, ad-free, nice-looking alternative you may have been looking for...

sunmornrain , 06/22/2018

Cute weather app

Very cute weather app. Very pleasing to look at. I can’t stand all those “clean” designs out there that are so devoid in personality. This weather app comes with several themes to choose from, including an old school pixel theme. Weathers about as accurate as every other app I’ve tried. As some one who’s watched the weather for years, I can tell you on that front...yeah, they’re all about the same. Not even apps like Dark Sky will be much better than any other app. But I’m aware of this already, so when looking for a weather app, I’m looking at aesthetics. This app is very easy on the eyes and I don’t have to look at boring standard icons half the apps use with the same old colors. This app is more like little sets of art.
