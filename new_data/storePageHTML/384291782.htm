Description

We believe that being organized is easy and everyone can do it.

Our philosophy is clear: simple *stays* organized. If you want an organization system that will last, you need something easy for you to implement and continue using for years to come.

For simplicity, nothing beats a piece of paper – except a piece of paper that can be magically accessed by you from anywhere.

So that’s what TeuxDeux is: simple, syncing paper.

We know people probably love the meditative nature of throwing away a part of a tree, but we sync everywhere so you can't lose us.

Keeping organization simple will bring you:

**GREATER PRODUCTIVITY**

*We're the most refreshing task manager in a sea of Red Bull and Monster Drink cans.*

- A single, streamlined view of your day (just like a paper to-do list!) so you can clearly see all of your items

- Procrastinate with our intuitive 'Swipe to Tomorrow' gesture

- Re-arrange your lists with simple drag and drop for tasks

- Custom colors and a beautiful, clean design so that every time you open your to-do list, you like what you see

*(There are also flying cats hiding in TeuxDeux but – similar to the gravity-locked ones in real life – they don't help with anything.)*

**PEACE AND CALM**

*Greet each day with an uncluttered mind.*

- Automatic task rollover so you can try again tomorrow to finish what you left undone today

- Recurring to-dos for those things you need to do every day, every week, every month, and so on...

- With Markdown and emoji support, TeuxDeux is your handmade to-do list. A beautiful, elegant way to get stuff done.

**MORE FOCUS**

*A simple tool for making and keeping your commitments—to yourself, your work, and your loved ones.*

- Stash ideas, bucket list items, and plans for later with our unlimited Someday lists.

- Add a new task with one click, tap, or pull – whatever works best for you at the moment so you can add a to-do and forget about it (for now)

- Our clean and simple interface is free from distraction – so you can get it all done without interruption

Best of all? We're free to use on iOS. And free beats paper, every time.

Rachelk29 , 06/24/2021

Great but could use some small changes

I love the set up of this app and I find it so beautiful and simple to use. I’m a big fan of the flying cats and the customization as well. I also like that it is free for iOS, as I never use my computer to plan anything. I’m just not near it enough but I always have my phone on me. I also really like the lists category, as I can just brain dump everything I need to remember and shop for.

While I love the app and the simplicity of it, there are a few small things I would change.

1. I am not sure how to stop reoccurring tasks (besides cancelling them via the website version of TeuxDeux. Maybe this is a feature but I can’t seem to find it.
There were a few reoccurring tasks I created by accident and I was scared they would just be there permanently and bother me for eternity.

2. This is very small and nit-picky, but the ability to delete something without crossing it off first. Being new to the app, I struggled trying to delete something for 5 minutes before realizing I needed to cross it off and then delete it.

3. Creating the # headers seem to be a tiny bit glitchy.

Overall the app is great! But I do think it could be a little more user-friendly for iPhone users who rarely go on their laptops. The reoccurring tasks thing is a big one specifically for me, as I’m prone to accidentally typing stuff. I also think that it is a bit confusing to figure out for a beginner and could use a little more instruction.

k.berg , 01/30/2019

Returning user

I used TeuxDeux years ago and slowly transitioned to using reminders in Google Inbox. Now as Inbox is being shuttered and I won’t have that easy reminder button anymore, I remembered how much I loved TeuxDeux and thankfully it’s still here. I just like how tasks float to the next day, and I’ve really missed seeing a week of reminders all together.

The one thing missing in both the app and the web is the ability to set alerts on tasks on a per-task basis. Sometimes I need a push notification to motivate me! A badge count would be useful too.

Gogglor , 08/06/2021

A Godsend for My ADHD

Struggling to remember to do things is a constant struggle with my inattentive-type ADHD. This app had been tremendously helpful to me.
I love that the interface has today and “someday” as the two main buttons you can get to quickly. What that means for me is that every time I think of or notice something I need to do (“Call Uncle Joe” or “Clean out fridge”), instead of it disappearing into the void of “later,” which literally does not exist in my brain, I can very quickly write it somewhere I can revisit.
This app has really helped me get things done that usually wouldn’t be started until they were an emergency. Thank you so much for developing this great app.
