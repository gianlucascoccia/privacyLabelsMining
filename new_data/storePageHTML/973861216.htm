Not accurate.

I’m not sure if the algorithms are messed up or what but most of my information was wrong. What they told me somewhat described some things accurate, but some major things completely wrong. For example. I scored 99th percentile in creativity in real life on an administered test. Many times. It’s one of my most known traits to all of my friends and family. That’s what they tie me to first. This app told me that I was not creative at all. It also listed 3 main traits that nowhere near describe me. I’ve also been in a serious long term relationship for 1.5 years now and the app kept telling me I would find a great man soon telling me it as if I were single. The answers were extremely vague and general and even then with how general and vague they were, they still weren’t right. I like the music, and the colors, and the cool effects but it had red flags. The app tried to tell me I was 1 of almost 1,000,000 users and kept trying to match me with random people without even asking my relationship status. I find it incredibly sketchy how you say there’s almost 1 million people, yet, you have like 100 ratings. Inaccurate, misleading, and false.
