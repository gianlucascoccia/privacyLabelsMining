Description

Hunting Calendar is an advanced Solunar prediction calendar that will help you find the best hunting locations.

This app will show you the best hunting times for any location at any time using Weather forecast to help you improve your hunting success. It also includes animal calling sounds.

This powerful application allows you to take a photo of your trophy, add description, size, weight, age, locations, points etc... to build your personal database and share it with your friends!

Some of the features:
 - Prediction of the best hunting times
- Weather forecast
- Animal calling sounds
- Infinite prediction calendar with Calendar share
- Day, month and week view of hunting efficiency
- Moonrise, moonset and moon overhead times
- Moon Phase
- Sunrise and sunset times
- Minor and major periods
- Worldwide calendar for any location
- Imperial and metric units


We promise – you will love the Hunting Calendar!

Best tool I have.

I’ve been use if this app since it first came out. Obviously you don’t see game every time there is a spike, but I’d say 85% of the time I see deer, it’s close to a spike. Of course there are many other factors that control deer movement, but this is a great tool to use as well. It’s changed the way I hunt. I am a truck driver, and almost every time I see deer, I will fire up the app for that location. Almost always near a spike or bump in the chart. Only problem is I can’t find it in the App Store anymore when I refer Freinds to it.

Solid App

Been using this app for several years now and it works. You always have to take certain things into consideration that effect game movement; like a weather front/change in barometric pressure or changing food sources.I have found it to be accurate when taking in these consideration and typically the predicted game movement is within +/- an hour.
