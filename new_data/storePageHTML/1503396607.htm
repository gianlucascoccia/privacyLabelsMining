Description

EXPERIENCE THE FUTURE OF RIDING WITH BRP GO!
Your one-stop source for a connected riding experience on water, snow or the road. A ride on water? Explore places using the map, discover a multitude of points of interest and create and share your routes with friends to follow them on the map. Going on snow-covered trails? Same thing! And enjoy thousands of official snowmobile trails in total control with turn-by-turn navigation. Is the road more your playground? Take advantage of BRP Connect™ technology and connect your phone to your Can-Am Spyder's cluster to enjoy a range of on-road GPS navigation applications.

NAVIGATION FEATURES FOR SNOW AND WATER PLAYGROUNDS*
• Access thousands of snowmobile trails across North America (Snow playground)
• Access all bodies of water across the world (Water playground)
• Plan your itineraries
• Ride with turn-by-turn navigation
• Free-ride with the area exploration feature
• Find and ride to thousands of official points of interest
• Create custom points of interest
• Save and redo past rides
• Download your itineraries for offline GPS navigation
• Save and share rides with friends
• Locate friends in real time on the map (beta)
• Monitor your ride statistics
• Access navigation features directly from the vehicle display on some Ski-Doo and Sea-Doo models with BRP Connect™ technology

BRP CONNECT™ TECHNOLOGY
The BRP GO! app now includes BRP Connect™ technology, which enables you to use some of your favorite apps directly on your Can-Am Spyder, Ski-Doo or Sea-Doo’s 7.8-inch panoramic colour display. Riders can access road GPS navigation, music, and more, all at arm’s length. The compatible apps are optimized to help create a pleasant and safe riding experience. An easy-to-follow step-by-step guide is available in the app to ensure proper connection between your phone and your vehicle.

SNOWMOBILE TRAILS
Federations and other sources provide us with data to ensure you get the best snowmobile experience. The following provinces, states and territories are available:
• Canada: Quebec, Nova Scotia, Manitoba, New Brunswick and Saskatchewan
• US: Minnesota, Massachusetts, New York State, Michigan, New Hampshire, Wisconsin, Pennsylvania, Vermont, North Dakota, Idaho, Washington, Colorado, Montana, Utah and Wyoming
BRP works in collaboration with snowmobile associations. Their involvement in BRP GO! is at their sole discretion. Do not hesitate to ask your local club or province or state association to get involved in the initiative.

*Please note that BRP GO!’s navigation features for snow and water playgrounds are currently only available in the following countries: Canada, USA, Mexico, Australia, New Zealand, Sweden, Norway, Finland, France, Spain, Italy, United Kingdom, Brazil & Japan. We are working to improve the experience of other regions in order to offer the best navigation app.

400bird , 04/14/2021

Very disappointed with app and 7.8 gauge!

I ordered a new 2021 sled just to get the new gauge and have all of the great functionality that was hyped up by Ski Doo only to be sadly disappointed. I feel like I spent a lot of money for not a lot of return on investment! As stated by many other reviewers, the connectivity with the sled is very cumbersome and not at all user friendly. It is a very big pain to have to pull your phone out of the holder and unlock it in order to get it to connect to the sled every time you stop and restart the sled. With that said, the areas I rode in had decent maps, although there were definitely spots missing. I also experienced the sled randomly just disconnecting from my phone while riding. I would have to stop and go through the whole connection process again. I hope there are some very badly needed improvements to this whole system and at no cost to those of us who trusted we were getting a very useful and vetted tool from Ski Doo/BRP. It would also be nice if the app had a compass that would display your direction of travel.

Hi 400bird, thank you so much for your review and suggestion - We're sorry to hear that your experience was not as expected. We have a team of BRP Go tech available to support you. We could get you in touch with them. Feel free to reach out at brp.care@brp.com, or call us at 1-888-272-9222, we'd love to see if there is anything we can do to improve your experience. Talk soon!

2katz2 , 10/17/2021

GO is now better than Connect, but still needs a lot of work!

After loading the new GO for use on my Spyder and trying it out, Sygic now links and works better than it had previously.

However, the ability to listen to ‘my’ phone’s music is now gone, which, IMHO, is terrible. Sometimes I just want to jam while riding.

The first time I loaded the new GO App and Sygic, I hit the road. My music did play, sort of, but constantly cut out and finally just stopped, although the turn by turn instructions were fine. (Note: the couple of times I have used this so far, I used the bike speakers and didn’t try to connect to my Cardo)

Although I loaded a few other Apps on the bike, I have not yet tried them out, so can’t speak of any other potential issues.

Please fix the music ASAP!!

Developer Response ,



Hi, 2katz2! Thank you so much for your feedback! You can play music on your Spyder from your phone by connecting your phone to Bluetooth and clicking on the Audio section of your display. We suggest creating a music playlist on your phone, starting it on your phone, and then controlling the volume and previous/next songs directly from the Audio system. In our previous BRP Connect app, we were offering a "Music" application in the list of third-party applications. This application is not a part of BRP GO! 2.0, but we are working to adding it in a future version.
