Description

Write doorman story. Feel hotel mania & cooking ! Get yourself into this hotel life, feel cooking frenzy in Hotel Craze !

Want to be the hotel tycoon? Get yourself into this real hotel & cooking madness life, feel grand hotel mania in Hotel Craze & Cooking Frenzy. You will be able to open hotels all over the world, cook delicious food, get likes from your guests and upgrade your hotels to get more love and recognition.

People around the world love vacation, cooking. They travel for adventure or pleasure, and they will need the relaxing coffee time after one long day. It is time for you to run luxury hotels, build up your grand hotel team and show people the best hotel service and enjoy the cooking hot. Let's write the doorman story now and be the crazy chef!

Dash from hotel to hotel in our wonderful map. You can unlock more places as you progress in this time-management game. Build up your hotel team and attract more and more guests. Let the Hotel Craze start now!

Everyone loves the hotel which has delicious food. Of course you could cook all kinds of dishes for your guests. Pizza, Hamburger, Coffee, Green tea... Whatever they want! Practice your management skills, tap as fast as you can to fulfill guests' requests. Operating hotels has never been so exciting! Try different appliances to complete your game task, upgrade kitchenwares to get the best gaming experience.

The elegant environment is needed for our hotel. Get more and more money from hotel service, spend them on hotel upgrades. One high-end hotel can not only earn more money but also attract more guests all over the world. Collect their likes and be the best starred hotels.

What more Fun things bring to this game?
- Enjoy your craze time in more than 1000 hotel levels!
- Travel & open the worldwide hotels among different cultures!
- Be ready for your guests' whims and serve them in time!
- Cook all kinds of yummy food to satisfy your guests!
- Collect likes, earn more and more love and recognize!
- Upgrade your hotels! Operate high-end hotels and earn more money!
- The Booster system makes your game time more fancy!
- Don't forget to join our game task and events to get massive rewards!

What kind of hotel will you get? A little motel with hamburgers and drinks? Or one luxury hotel with an elegant environment? Now it is time to write your own hotel story.

Katonica , 04/10/2021

Huge problem with events

The app is fun and they have adjusted the difficulty so it is still difficult but not impossible to pass. It just may take a few tries. However the events are a problem. They only allow 2 hours to collect ingredients and that is simply not enough time. I play sporadically throughout the day and cannot immediately sit down with it and play for two hours straight. Also as I am progressing through a location it takes time to build up enough coins and diamonds to upgrade not to mention the time it takes for the upgrade to actually happen. I don’t understand why the ingredients and Easter eggs are not available at all locations either. That is the one thing that has put this app way above the other hotel apps is the fact you can keep playing locations that have been completed. I enjoy playing these other locations while I am waiting for the upgrades to happen in the newest location. It would make the events much more fun if the ingredients and event items could be found throughout all locations and that you would give the whole day to find them all instead of just two hours. I have not found all of the ingredients for the day yet. It’s just frustrating and takes the fun out of the game. I kind of want to stop playing it while the event is on just because it’s frustrating to know I cannot take part in the event for lack of ingredients.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for playing the game. Levels are designed to be fun and challenge enough. However, we value your feedback and will do our best to make improvement and provide the best balance of difficulties. If you have any suggestions, please feel free to contact us in the game.

ThunderChed98 , 06/15/2021

Level Difficulty

I really do enjoy playing this game and I have played A LOT of it! However… the levels are basically impossible to beat unless you have power-ups and other things to help you out. I like a little bit of difficulty but not too much that I can’t even play the levels in order without spending gems to get power-ups. It is literally IMPOSSIBLE to beat levels without something to help you!!! And I don’t like that…
I have got into the habit of replaying the first hotel map over… and over.. and over again to earn more gems. I replay that one over and over because it’s the easiest map. But, let me tell you, it takes FOREVER to earn enough gems to buy power-ups in this game!!!! And when you FINALLY have enough gems to buy a power-up, you only get a set of three. Once those are all used up, back to the grind we go… It’s pretty frustrating…
Trust me. I would really enjoy even more of this game if the level difficulty wasn’t so frustratingly impossible… and I would be playing a whole lot more of this game than I already do now if they just took some time to tweak it. Just saying…

Developer Response ,

Thank you for playing the game. Levels are designed to be fun and challenge enough. However, we value your feedback and will do our best to make improvement and provide the best balance of difficulties. If you have any suggestions, please feel free to contact us in the game.
