Description

Welcome to Home Design Makeover! Play the best Home Design match 3 puzzle game! Be the best designer by helping lucky families transform dreams into reality with amazing home makeovers! Solve fun match 3 puzzles to help design, customize and decorate the perfect dream home with beautiful decor. Your clients are counting on you to remodel their down-and-out fixer uppers!

Features:

- Remodel homes with a Farmhouse style or go Modern? You decide, you design!
- Design and renovate different rooms styles, including family-friendly living rooms, rustic kitchens, stylish bathrooms and chic bedrooms
- Help clients ranging from the newlyweds to Hollywood celebrities, and travel the world to Tokyo, Paris and Morocco
- Express yourself with an incredible variety of high-end designer furniture, lighting, flooring and other decor
- Experience addicting gameplay with offline mode so you can take the game from your home to the sky!
- Be challenged with over 1000 match 3 puzzles and dozens of game modes for endless relaxing fun
- Frequent, fresh and free content updates with new interior and exterior design challenges, floor plans, outdoor gardens, landscaping, seasonal items and more!

Let's get matching and transform outdated homes into fabulous family hangouts!

Must tune in! , 02/15/2020

I’m not the biggest fan of this game.

I loved the look of this game when I first saw it! The video ad I got in one of my games made it look like you could just design a home without having to do anything to get coins. I downloaded it right away seeing that it had good ratings. I went into the app after it downloaded hoping I would be able to design some rooms and houses without having to complete any games to get coins, like what occurred with some other design apps I downloaded and quickly deleted. Sadly, I was let down. I hate having to play games to get coins! If you create and game like this at least please make the game and challenge different! I’m tired of always having to slide my finger to make groups of 3 to complete the challenge to get coins!!!!!! Multiple design games have this and eventually it gets boring. There is one really great design game that includes no need to play any games! If you ever plan on making a Design game or any other type of game we’re you need to get coins to unlock things, please consider the fact that people are tired of games like this where you have to slide your finger to get groups of three. Either make it so you have to play no game at all, like maybe answer a riddle, or make no challenge or game at all. Thanks for reading this and I hope you realize my point!

vtlioness , 10/09/2021

Slow to begin, gets great, then prepare to empty your savings account!

When I first started playing this game, I found it slow and boring; but since then (at least 5yrs ago), the design graphics are actually very beautiful. For quite awhile I enjoyed this as my go-to for downtime. However, as I got up to level 9000, it started to become very hard. Now, at level 14,000+, it’s downright nearly impossible. Every fourth level is a hard level. They can take two full days, or an entire hour of free play to beat (total waste during competitions). Even levels not labeled hard, can often take up to two days to win. Unless I buy gems and boosters. Then I miraculously beat the level.
I find that I can enter a competition, begin within the top 15, progress to the top 10, hit a Hard Level. By the time I beat it, I’m often reduced to level 30. This obviously demonstrates that I’m not playing against ppl at my same level.
Love the designs, love the graphics; but I have wanted to throw my iPad across the room more often than not. It’s become more of a frustration than a joy. Out of work. Can’t “pay to play” anymore. Don’t get addicted unless you have disposable income. Also, game crashes a lot, lose a life or time in free play.
