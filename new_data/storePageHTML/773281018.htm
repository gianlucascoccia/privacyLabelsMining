Description

TenantCloud is a perfect place for landlords, managers, tenants, and service providers to have a simple and easy access to their property, units, leases, accounting and maintenance requests information on their iPhone/iPad managing their property from anywhere as well as collaborate together within the system.

Landlord Account Features:
• Free basic account up to 75 units/512 mb
• Manage residential & commercial property portfolio
• Manage unlimited tenants, owners, service pros and vendors
• Set up and manage a vacancy marketing website for free
• Receive, manage and send maintenance requests to your service pros
• Set up application fee and receive rental applications from your prospects
• Set up lease and receive rent and other payments online
• Set up late fees and track outstanding balance
• View and print receipts
• Screen applicants with built-in background check
• Add and track warranty expiration of property/unit equipment
• Customable dashboard and Calendar notifications
• View, customize and download Reports
• Communicate with your connected contacts and share files
• Email/Feed notifications
• Everything is synced directly with your account
• Unlimited support

Tenant Account Features:
• Complete and save time applying with a reusable Renter Profile
• Connect the landlord and view the lease
• Communicate with your connected contacts and share files
• Pay rent and other invoices online
• Submit maintenance requests
• Track renter insurance expiration
• Find a new home and apply online
• Email/Feed notifications
• Unlimited support

Service Pro Account Features:
• Complete business account and get more clients
• Connect with the landlord and receive maintenance requests
• Manage own request, change status, add notes
• Communicate with the landlords
• Attach and store files
• Email/Feed notifications
• Unlimited support

Owner Account Features:
• Connect with your managers and view your properties
• Communicate with your managers
• View your properties reports
• Attach and store files
• Email/Feed notifications
• Unlimited support
