Description

The fastest & most beautiful Seventh-day Adventist Hymnal App. Sing or play along with high quality piano tracks, scores & chords. Worship with your family on Apple TV.

The Adventist Hymnal app is easy to use and beautiful to look at. With a fast and always responsive user interface, you can now access all the hymns in the Seventh-day Adventist Hymnal wherever you are. Incontestably the best Hymn Book app in the App Store.

Listen to high quality piano tracks for your favorite hymns. Sing along and be inspired.

Know how to play the piano? Or a guitar? Play along your favorite hymns with the beautiful and crisp looking scores and chords on iPhone & iPad.

The following features are available:
• Speed Dial: Quickly open hymns by their number
• Fast Search: Search by title, lyrics or both
• High Quality Piano Tracks: Sing along to your favorite hymns
• Piano Scores: Play along with music piano music sheets
• Guitar Chords: Play along with guitar chords
• Create Playlists: Save and organise your favorite hymns
• Search by Category: Easily access hymns based on their category
• Present Hymn Lyrics to External Screens with TV Out (via a cable)
• Support for the latest Apple devices and iOS versions & features
• Download scores, chords and piano tracks and keep them on your device for offline access
• Export Scores & Chords as PDFs and share it to any app, or send it via Email or WhatsApp

We will continue to add new features so if you love this app, please get in touch with us to request which features you’d like to see.

But please note the following:
• There are over 600 hymns, all covered by your local church’s CCLI license.
• Not all hymns have the same special content (MP3s, Scores and Chords).
• You will need to confirm that your local church has a valid CCLI license to access some hymns as this app is intended for public worship.

That's it. See you inside.
