If I purchase a monthly subscription, how can I cancel?
The subscription will auto-renew monthly (every 30 days) until you choose to cancel. Initial payment for the entire month will be charged to your iTunes account at confirmation of purchase. Your iTunes account will be charged automatically for renewal ($39.99) each month within 24 hours prior to the end of the current 30-day period. Monthly renewals will continue to be conveniently billed as long as you require the valuable Foreclosure.com data. We do not offer pro-rated refunds if you decide to cancel the auto renewal with days remaining on the latest auto-renewal; however, you will continue to receive full site access until the current 30-day window expires. In other words, no cancellation of the current subscription is allowed during an active subscription period. You can manage your subscription from “Account Settings” after purchase to cancel the auto-renewal service at any time. That’s right, you can cancel your subscription at any time. It's a simple, hassle-free process. Our customer service contact information can be found in this prominent location, too (www.foreclosure.com/contact.html). More important information can be found in our “Terms of Use” (www.foreclosure.com/terms.html) and “Privacy Policy” (www.foreclosure.com/privacy.html).

Lots of potential but doesn’t deliver on service promised

I tried the subscription for 1 month and was very disappointed in the design and information provided by the mobile app and the website. All the information provided by the app and on the website can be found on other (FREE) service real estate apps. This app does not provide basic information you can find in other apps such as:
1. Public Records for recorded purchase price
2. Public Records for Taxes
3. Public Records on Auction information
4. No information in Tax Liens
5. No information on 2nd mortgage

Other issues discovered with the mobile app:
1. Information provided such as minimum bid is incorrect
2. Many of the listing pictures of the property do not load, the carousel my show X number of pictures but when you select to view them only 1 may load

The app has potential but needs significant improvements to compete with other apps that do deliver on its promises.
