Description

Want to learn English? Let us guide you. We’ve designed a guided course based on practice exercises and interaction with certified teachers and students who are at the same level as you. Whatever your goal is, we’ll help you find the path you need to take to achieve your goal.

Start today. More than 30 million students have already signed up.

HOW WILL YOU LEARN ENGLISH WITH ABA ENGLISH?
Welcome to the first Digital English Academy. Here, you’ll learn via our unique and guided methodology.

A course plan that’s designed for you and based on three main actions: LEARNING, SPEAKING and PRACTISING.

LEARN WITH A COMPLETE COURSE.
The ABA English course comes in all levels: Beginners (A1), Lower Intermediate (A2), Intermediate (B1), Upper Intermediate (B2), Advanced (B2/C1) and Business (C1).

> ABA Film
You’ll start each unit with a short film that includes conversations and everyday scenes, with subtitles in English or in several other languages.

> Thousands of exercises
You’ll practise all linguistic skills: reading, writing, comprehension and pronunciation.

> Video classes
Here, you’ll find all the grammar explained by our teachers in a clear and enjoyable way.

> Certificates
Every time you pass a level you’ll get an Official ABA English Certificate. You can share it on LinkedIn via the app.

> Level test
You can take a free level test to make sure you start at the right level.

SPEAK IN LIVE GROUP CLASSES
Live classes every day where you can practise speaking with our teachers and students who at the same level as you. You’ll find sessions on grammar, pronunciation, debates, culture and plenty more.

60 classes each week, more than 1,500 a year!

PRACTISE WITH MICROLESSONS
New content every day based on your interests. You’ll learn something new in just a few minutes with articles, podcasts, videos, interviews, short stories and music. Set yourself a Weekly Goal and create the routine that will be sure to lead you to success.

OUR PLANS
You can start for FREE with the Basic plan and see your first results. With this plan, you’ll get access to the 144 course units and can enjoy all ABA Films and video classes. You’ll also be able to practise with vocabulary exercises.

If you want to take it a step further and enjoy a more comprehensive plan, we recommend Premium. As well as being able to enjoy the course and all the activities, you’ll have unlimited access to group classes and microlessons.
