Gotta Yoga programs are carefully planned for learning yoga at home or on the go.
Our yoga classes are created by professional yoga teachers Anu Visuri, Vanessa de Haas, Blandine Soulage and Giuseppe Panarello.

Gotta Yoga is integrated with your Health app!
- When you practice yoga with Gotta Yoga, the app records a workout at the end of the practice. Also, it stores the corresponding active energy burned and Mindfulness minutes.
- If you have an Apple Watch, you will see your heart rate and active energy burned during your practice.

Gotta Yoga classes are perfect from beginners onwards. Start your personal yoga training by downloading Gotta Yoga on your iPhone, iPad and Apple Watch.

*YOGA PRACTICES THAT ALWAYS FIT YOUR SCHEDULE AND SKILLS*
Select your introductory program: beginner or advanced. continue with you preferred yoga program: for your morning, for your hips, for meditating etc. There are several practices that you can try for free. The rest can be used by subscribing to Gotta Yoga.

*RELAXING VOCAL INSTRUCTIONS IN SEVERAL LANGUAGES*
Videos and texts are available in English, French, German, and Finnish.

*YOGA PLAYER THAT YOU CAN USE AT YOUR OWN PACE*
Gotta Yoga player displays the yoga classes and asanas as videos with voice and text that support your learning. You can pause the player, and move back or forward in the yoga class as you wish.

*YOGA TREE THAT ENCOURAGES TO PRACTICE YOGA AND IMPROVE*
We have planted a seed for your yoga tree. Your tree will grow with every finished yoga practice. You can share your tree via Facebook, Twitter or email.

*APPLE WATCH*
Use Gotta Yoga with your Apple Watch. You can control the yoga player from your Apple Watch. Apple Watch records your minutes and calories in the Health App.

INFO ON USING THE APP AND REGARDING THE SUBSCRIPTION

The download and usage of Gotta Yoga is free of charge. You have access to 9 free yoga classes in the free selection of the app. Should you decide to subscribe to Gotta Yoga, you will have access to all current 150 yoga classes and 100 postures as well as all upcoming new yoga classes, meditations and postures during your subscription. All yoga content will be accessible in the app with a subscription of 1 month, 6 months or 1 year. If you decide to subscribe you will pay the price set for your country, as shown in the app. The subscription automatically renews if it is not cancelled within 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period. Your account will be charged for the next subscription period up to 24 hours prior to the current subscription expiring. It is not possible to cancel an existing in-app subscription. You can disable the automatic renewal function at any time by adjusting your account settings.
