Description

PicPlus, the photo editor for both experienced users and those just getting started. Not everyone is a graphic artist, but that doesn't mean your photos can't be works of art. 

Start using PicPlus and you’ll quickly discover it’s the only mobile all-in-one photo editor you’ll need. The app offers an extensive list of functions sure to satisfy anyone craving eye-catching pics for social media or any other use. No matter what you want to accomplish, PicPlus has features to give your pics that polish they need.  

Go beyond simple editing and create fantastic new creations with easy to use templates and editing features to take your pics beyond the mundane. Fulfill your fantasies with awesome stickers, templates, background options, live face filters and more. Get inspired.
