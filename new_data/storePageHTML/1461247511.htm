Description

Be inspired by the stories of individuals who have overcome the most incredible struggles to achieve their dreams. Learn about the icons of Hollywood, go back in time with musicians, journey with schoolchildren and even liaise with royals on the network that offers you a lens to the world at large.

Delve into the lives and passions of people around the globe through the eyes of Cinémoi. Explore a one-of-a-kind lineup of acclaimed programming that supports cultural diversity, women empowerment, and ecological appreciation.

Our premium programming gives viewers front row seats to a technicolor world of international cinema, film festivals, and an array of glamorous events, including the Cannes Film Festival, Paris, New York, Milan and London fashion weeks and the exclusive broadcast of the CinéFashion Film Awards.

App features:
• Unlimited access to Cinémoi’s highly curated library
• Discover exclusive international titles
• Access to the exclusive broadcast of Cannes, Berlin, London, Venice & Marrakech film festivals
• Seat front row to Milan, Paris, London, New York Fashion Weeks
• Watch original programming including the CineFashion Film Awards, Supermodels du Monde & the MET Gala
• Get personalized recommendations every time you watch
• Create your own personal video playlist
• Stream from your iPhone & iPad to your TV with Chromecast and Airplay
• Download your favorites titles and watch offline anytime
• Not seen on Netflix
