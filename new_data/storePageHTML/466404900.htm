What is Children's Prayer?
====================
• Children's Prayer is an app for iPhone, iPod Touch and iPad that daily provides a quote from the Bible and a question about it, as a basis to begin a prayer or a dialogue with your kid within the family, at school lesson or at Sunday school.
• The app, the daily prayer and the current prayer's series are free.

Why Children's Prayer?
==================
• If in this hurry-up world you feel the need to share some time with your children praying or talking about God, Children's Prayer is for you.
• If you believe that “All Scripture is inspired by God and is useful for teaching the truth, rebuking error, correcting faults, and giving instruction for right living”, Children's Prayer is for you (2 Timothy verse 3:16).
• If you are involved in a Children's Ministry and need a resource full of Bible quotes to comment and pray with kids, Children's Prayer is also for you.
