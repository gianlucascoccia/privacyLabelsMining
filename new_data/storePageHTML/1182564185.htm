Description

Care for your pet like never before with this companion app for Whistle smart pet monitors, including Whistle™ FIT™, Whistle™ GO Explore™, and our all-new Whistle™ Switch™ smart collar.

Since your pet can’t tell you where they are or how they’re feeling, we created the next best thing—a smart pet monitor that makes it easy to track and monitor your pet’s health, fitness, gps location**, and so much more—all from one easy-to-use app!

WHAT DOES A WHISTLE SMART MONITOR DO?
Whistle smart monitors give you a 360º view of your pet’s overall health by tracking and monitoring their unique health-related behaviors, fitness, GPS location**, and more—right from the Whistle app. You’ll get actionable science-backed insights that are unique to your pet, so you can make smart, informed decisions for your furry family member.

*Health Behaviors*
Your Whistle smart monitor tracks your pet’s scratching, licking, drinking, and sleeping patterns. If changes in their patterns might point to potential health issues, you’ll get notified so you can take action.

*GPS Location Tracking*
GPS, Wi-Fi, and cellular networks work together to inform you of your pet’s location at all times.

*Customizable Safe Places*
Create boundaries for your pet’s Safe Place locations. Plus, you can manage multiple Safe Places for home, office, or your favorite dog-friendly spots (Wi-Fi is recommended).

*Escape Alerts*
Get a text, email, or in-app notification when your pet leaves one of your designated Safe Place locations without an approved friend or family member.

*Activity Tracking*
Help your pet get the exercise they need with customized fitness goals based on their breed, age, and weight. Know your pet’s calories burned, distance traveled, minutes active, and more.


*Celebrate Achievements*
Earn badges when your pet meets daily activity goals, reaches new milestones, hits a new winning streak, and much more. Achievements are a fun way to look back and celebrate your hard work together!

*TeleVet*
Connect to a vet right from the Whistle app (limited time).

*Reminders*
Create custom reminders to stay on top of daily meds, monthly flea + tick treatments, bi-annual vet visits, grooming appointments, weigh-ins, and more.

HOW WHISTLE WORKS
1. Download the Whistle app (yep, this one right here)
2. Activate your Whistle smart monitor with a Whistle plan
3. Get notifications about your pet’s well-being & location*
*Only supported with GPS-enabled Whistle smart monitors

**HAVE A WHISTLE FIT? GPS/Location features like “Find My Pet” are not compatible with Whistle FIT. If you want to upgrade to a GPS-enabled Whistle smart monitor for location features in addition to health + fitness monitoring, email support@whistle.com.

Wonderful improvement!

The older pet tracker versions that Whistle offered were good, but the new Whistle 3 adds some well-thought-out improvements. The app is a bit more user-friendly, but the important improvements are in the device. It is smaller, lighter and more responsive than the older models. Plus, the home boundaries can be easily set in the app to match the property line - or any other dimension if you want. For me, this means that if my pet steps off the property I quickly get a notification. In my area, the coverage with AT&T is much better than the Verizon coverage on the old service and I get notifications much faster now. The notifications are more precise, too. They say my dog is “300 feet from home near 1234 Elm St.” I live in the city, so the faster, more precise notification could be a live saver. The only weakness, if I can call if that, is that when spending a great amount of time away from home (where battery power is saved by using WIFI), the battery life is less. But, Whistle notifies you when the battery is low and it can be recharged quickly while my dog naps. Last, but not least - Whistle support is wonderful. I have had their products for several years and they are helpful and very easy to deal with.
