Kingofthemustachemaker , 04/14/2019

The filter list is quite extensive and worth the price

For everything you get with this app (including the ability to apply filters to video) it’s worth it. Haven’t found out how to export the best quality videos and photos possible, but the app is fun to play around with. The only thing is I wish there was auto-generated dust/scratches instead of pre-made templates, like a similar app does. However, there are quite a few, so I don’t think your edited images would be so easily recognized as being processed through this app. I recommend using your iPhone’s camera and video app, especially if you have the newest iPhone. Video and camera in this app aren’t quite up to par, and as far as I can tell, it’s choppy in the display and doesn’t seem to be utilizing the Xs’s stabilization.
Now I have to figure out how to dial down on using these affects, as I want the subtle believability, not over-done.

I have one criticism so far, and that’s when you lower the opacity on the dust; it doesn’t remove the opacity evenly. Some of the “dust” will still be bright white marks on your photo, even at a low opacity.

Some other functionalities could be lens distortion affects. If they’re in the app, I can’t find them.

Developer Response ,

Thanks for the review and feedback.

- Photos are saved with 90% compression jpg and videos as original and max 1080p.
Dynamic dust is in the working with infinite variations of the particles so stay tuned for updates.

The dust opacity issue I can't recreate so could you please contact hello@ginnypix.com and let me know more. Also what kind of distortions you wish for the lens.

Ci Havlik , 04/08/2020

Best Editing App

I’ve had KuniCam for over a year now, I began using it for travels last summer. Now, I find myself using it multiple times a week!
I am a professional photographer who frequents DSLR and sometimes SLR (film) cameras, though film is more time consuming. That’s where KuniCam comes in. When I don’t have the time/funds to process film, I just click into the app and spend up to 30 minutes customizing my regular iPhone photos into something stylistic and unique. No two presets are the same!
This app offers dozens of filters, light leak presets, film grain(s), editing tools AND date stamps. Pretty ideal for a photographer on the go.
For what it’s worth, the edits I make reflect heavily on my social media profiles. They are often revered as awesome! Thanks KuniCam!
