Description

*2020 Webby Award Winner: Best Voice App*

Play Drive.fm - the world’s first and only app that lets you safely play interactive audio content, hands-free! Whether you are driving for your commute, drive for work, or on a road trip, Drive.fm helps to entertain you and others in the car in a fun and interactive way. Drive.fm can also be played hands-free in the home while washing dishes, folding laundry, or getting ready for the day. On top of that, it's family friendly too! Featured and supported by leading investors and press like Techcrunch, Mashable, Amazon, and Google.

Want to be more productive? Commute got you down? Road trip filled with silence? Now you can beat the boredom with Drive.fm!

QUIZ SHOW
- Play our hilariously hosted trivia show!
- Train your brain across seven trivia categories: Music, Literature, TV & Film, History & Society, The World, Science & Technology, and Sports & Leisure.
- Get to know Liam and Melissa, our friendly and funny hosts who quiz our players on a variety of interesting topics.
- Subscribers can play our whole back catalog 480+ Drive.fm Trivia shows!

SUPERFAN SHOWDOWN
- Movie buff? These quizzes focus on a specific movie, from the overall plot to the nitty gritty details.
- Make it a real Showdown by challenging your friends and family. See who knows more to determine Superfan superiority!

TUNETIME
- Test your music and song recognition skills with TuneTime!
- 80's Rock? 90's Pop? Choose your music genre and decade, and see how many songs you can get right.
- Play by yourself or with friends to see who is the master of the music.

SAFETY
- Completely hands-free gameplay and safe for drivers as the app is voice-activated so you can focus on the road.
- Drivers have the ability to play entirely in the background, so you can keep your map app in the foreground while you are on a commute or road trip!
- Your voice data is 100% private.

OTHER FEATURES
- On the road or at home with friends or family? Play Would You Rather to answer hilarious questions and scenarios!
- The latest speech recognition technology with near 100% accuracy.

KitKat0520 , 06/18/2020

Fun for Long Drives But Buggy

I downloaded this app because I tend to get bored on my hour commutes and I already like listening to trivia. I love the games and the snippets but don’t play to use this app if your drive will take you anywhere rural or where your service will go to anything below 4 bars. You could be screaming directly into the microphone and the app won’t pick it up. It’s very sensitive to this and will go out on a big highway in the middle of the city too. The creators seem to know this though because it says “you may have lost signal” and repeats the question instead of marking you wrong but it’s still frustrating. The app also doesn’t work as harmoniously with Apple CarPlay as you would hope. The creators must also know this because they have a prompt for that too. The content is currently a little bit on the slim side for its monthly price tag but it does look like they’re actively working to add more.

Unexpected plus side - I would say this app would be great for people that are blind/cannot use apps and games that rely on visual cues only. It seems like it’s very accessible with its voice prompts.

TL/DR: great idea but needs to be improved upon for its price tag.

Developer Response ,

We're sorry to hear about the issues you're experiencing. We aim to make the Drivetime experience as consistent and high quality as possible, which results in requiring a stable connection while playing. You're right about the content, we're adding and improving on our content library every week! If you have any other questions or concerns, you can contact us at support@drivetime.fm and we'd be happy to address them. Thanks!

AirynBoBerin , 09/03/2019

Bummer

This is app is a lot of fun, and the only one of its kind, I’ve looked.

That being said; it costs WAY too much money ($9.99 a month) for a trivia game. There is a free version but you get 1 episode a day (24 minutes long) so you have to chose drive to work or drive home, and my commute is almost an hour, so I did the free trial for 7 days, you can play as many episodes as you want and it was great, but when my trial ended I couldn’t justify $10 a month for a game. Maybe $10 for a lifetime, but a subscription it’s over the top. I also notice that when my trial ended, the game started acting up. It’s never on the right day so sometimes I’ll use my free episode and then it will get stuck for three days and it will say I’ve already played, so I can’t play for three days. I tried to clear the cache, sign out, and redownload the app to no avail. I think this concept was great if they would fix the issues and pricing.
