Description

Manage your Fingerhut account anytime, from anywhere! View your account information, pay your bill, shop and more.

Sign in with your existing Fingerhut.com username and password, or use Touch ID, Face ID, or 4-Digit PIN if you prefer.

Once you’re in, start managing your Fingerhut account with some of these exciting features:
• Pay your bill
• View your current available credit
• View your current balance
• View past orders and payments
• View scheduled payments
• Stay up to date on special deals and promotions
• Shop - something for every customer and every budget
• With push notifications, receive payment reminders and more
• Simple sign in using Touch ID, Face ID, or a 4-Digit PIN

We hope you enjoy the Fingerhut app!
