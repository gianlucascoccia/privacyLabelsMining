GET READY FOR BLACK FRIDAY 2021! Track Amazon prices, view prices history, earn money while shopping on Pricepualse.

If you are familiar with Honey, Camel Camel or Keepa you'll love this app on you iPhone: Track any product on Amazon, get push notifications when prices drop and find the best deal.

The best way to save money!

Save hundreds of dollars every time you shop with Pricepulse, a new shopping app that instantly checks every product’s price history and determines if you’re buying it a the lowest price.

We developed Artificial Intelligence technology to help create a price tracker that tells you the best time to buy and the best deals across million of products.

From diapers to laptops, to pet food, check Pricepulse before you buy!

Our algorithms automatically perform amazon price checks with tremendous accuracy. After Pricepulse weighs all possible purchase options, it tells you when exactly is a good time to wait for a price drop and when the price is right and your best move is... to buy now!

Top features:

DISCOVER the best deals *in real time* across millions of products.

SCAN BARCODES to perform price comparison and price checks while you’re in-store shopping and get yourself a price match from retailer.

PRICE ALERTS track products and receive price alerts via push notifications the instant prices drop.

FORECAST with the help of Artificial Intelligence technology so you know when product prices are predicted drop.

MORE PERKS:

SAVE TIME: Pricepulse does all the price comparison work for you, meaning money is not the only thing you save!

PERSONALIZED COUPONS: having Pricepulse by your side is like having coupons for exactly what you want.

FEEL POSITIVE: Using Pricepulse is a guarantee you are getting the absolute best price. This way, you can consistently feel positive about your purchases while saving tons of money in the process.

Quilk , 05/19/2021

Good app with some room for improvement.

This app offers some really cool features. I like seeing the price tracking of products as they fluctuate over time. While this is an offering, the prices aren’t always accurate. So you have to take this into account. Being able to earn points and gift cards for things you would purchase anyway is a huge plus. Unfortunately, on more than one occasion, upon opening one of the 15 minute challenges, where you’re rewarded with 50 points or 100 points for making a purchase within the time period, I have been deprived of the reward. While the items are things I wanted, I may have acted more impulsively in order to get the reward and then received none. That’s a huge disappointment, and support is nearly impossible to contact. The automated support system claims that my email is invalid, though it’s also listed as the email that’s attached to this application. It could earn a 5th star with some more consistency or with the addition of a functional customer support system.
