Description

Join more than 50 million coffee lovers around the world and unleash your entrepreneurial spirit in this FUN and RELAXING restaurant game. Decide on your cafe STRATEGY and earn income, even when you are offline!

REAL BUSINESS SIMULATION
● With My Cafe you can own and grow a coffee shop of your dreams
● Decorate the space as you wish, hire and train staff, decide on menu items and even set prices as you see fit

CAFE MAKEOVER
● Do you have a knack for design? Hone your interior design skills and do a total cafe makeover
● In this restaurant game, you can choose from numerous decor styles, position furniture as you wish, and make a place totally yours by accentuating with elements that show your true self

INTERACTIVE STORYLINES
● As a true coffee shop owner, you will get to meet your customers from various backgrounds and get to help them navigate through life. In this time management game you can pick between different lines of dialogue with customers and see where your story takes you!
● Do you like drama? Romance? In My Cafe, the choice is all yours!

GO SOCIAL WITH MY CAFE
● Play this cafe game with friends or meet like-minded people to form townships and compete against owners of other coffee shops.
● Fulfill festival tasks and lead your town to success together!

COFFEE MANIA: Do you love coffee as much as we do?
● My Cafe: Recipes & Stories allows you to unleash your barista superpower and create custom coffee and tea drinks for all your guests.
● Unlock unique coffee recipes and spice up everyone’s life in your cafe with distinctive drink combinations.
