Description

Hey there, we've had an amazing journey striving for the healthiest version of ourselves with you. Now we're working on an even simpler to use plan! ▶Stay tuned◀

Following the 30 day challenge with our meal plan is a life saver! You won’t have to worry anymore about what to eat or what not to eat. This Meal Plan app does all the work for you.

When you eat only whole foods something amazing happens - your innate ability to self regulate how much you eat turns back on. When you eat things like grains and sugar, your body has no mechanism to say, “hey stop eating, you're satiated.” But when you're eating real WHOLE FOODS, your body will know when you've had enough and this leads to reaching your goals. There are calories in the app for your information, but don't fret over counting calories on this diet plan.

This app will change your life and challenge you to succeed with weight loss and being the healthiest you’ve ever been!!

Includes a handy shopping list for the week. Choose to follow the plan “as is”, or easily replace any recipes you don’t want, for one you do want with one of the hundreds of extra recipes in the app to choose from. You can also customize the plan to feed just you or your whole family.

Start eating right, losing weight and feeling 100x healthier with this meal plan.

You can continue this plan for longer than 30 days if you’d like because with the subscription option we will keep you eating Whole, Healthy and Clean for life!

SUBSCRIPTION OPTION: There is a full free week trial subscription that gives you access to all features and if you decide to continue after the week it's $9.99/month with the option to can cancel anytime in your iTunes settings.

The app has been designed to work great on both the iPhone and the iPad.

So make life easier and get the results you want by subscribing to the 30 Whole Days Healthy Meal Plan app today!

Tasty main dish, but not meals

Many of the “meal” options are not meals. It gives some tasty recipe options for a main dish, such as a burger or sausage patty or pork tenderloin, but that doesn’t make a meal. We need at least a veggie option on the side, or ability to add sides in the plan, or add items to the shopping list for sides.

The variety of main dishes are great, the recipes are easy and tasty, and I like the shopping list feature, but this needs some enhancements for me to make it worth the cost.

Thank you so much for your feedback about this! We appreciate your thoughts.
