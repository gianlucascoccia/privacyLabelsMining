Description

PocketSmith Sidekick works alongside the web app so you can check your budgets, balances and spending - wherever you are!

This mobile app makes staying on top of your PocketSmith account a breeze. See all your bank, credit card, investment and loan accounts in one place without needing to use different apps. Bank Feeds keep PocketSmith automatically updated, so you can see where your money is going.

You need a PocketSmith web account to use the mobile app. Learn more and sign up at pocketsmith.com.

APP FEATURES

- Stay on top of your spending activity
- View, categorize and edit your transactions on-the-go
- Add notes and labels to your spending to record your memories
- View all your account balances at a glance
- Organize household documents like receipts and warranties by attaching them as images and documents to transactions
- Customize your mobile dashboard to see the insights that matter to you

We're constantly improving the app to help you make better money decisions.

isaacwalker , 10/25/2018

Desktop = Perfection. Mobile = not very useful

I love how the desktop app is so clean and yet also so powerful, focused on intelligent transaction rules -- filters, splitting, the works.

But on mobile I can't split a transaction, select the "Make This a Category Rule" checkbox, or even easily see what transactions are in a given category at the moment.

So I basically can't use the mobile app to do anything useful, just view totals and summaries - but automating and splitting transactions is what I need to do on the go day-to-day.

I'm glad the PocketSmith team has a laser-focus on the platform foundation (even offering an API!! Wow!!), but would love to see the mobile app be my day-to-day transaction home rather than the report-centric approach it has now, which I only need to see every now and then.

But thank you PocketSmith for being DIFFERENT in an ecosystem full of bloated / outdated personal finance apps with LIVE feeds!! I am a paid subscriber and can't wait to see where you take the app next. This is truly a 1-of-a-kind, best-in-class platform.

Developer Response ,

Thanks for the review Isaac. We are working on adding more functionality to our mobile apps so that they become more and more useful. We will be adding the ability to create Category Rules in the next couple of releases ⚡️I've also noted your request for the ability to split transactions. With regard to finding transactions in a particular category, you can quickly do this with the new search feature which was included in the latest release 🔎

Keith Nerdin , 07/23/2017

LOVE the website, but can't add transactions

The whole point (for me) of wanting to have a mobile app is so that I can manually enter transactions while I'm on the go so I can snap a picture of the receipt and know that I have it logged and an covered. I hate carrying receipts and without being able to manually enter transactions on the go, I'm entirely left up to automated bank transactions to populate. And actually, I opted to turn off automatic uploading. It is more convenient to do it automatically but one of the biggest personal benefits of using a service like this is for me to be actively, consistently engaged and aware of each transaction and to see how that's effecting my cash flow forecast. I've really enjoyed manually entering my transactions each morning.

But DESPERATELY waiting for the mobile app to allow me to do this in the fly. The mobile version of the website is responsive and is pretty much impossible to navigate efficiently on a phone. But....I do LOVE the website on a computer!

Developer Response ,

Hi Keith, we have just added the ability to add cash transactions from the app. This is available just for cash transactions at the moment, in order to prevent duplicate transactions from being manually added and imported from bank feeds or bank files. If you want to add transactions to non-cash accounts and don't mind these accounts being grouped incorrectly within net worth, you can change your accounts to the 'Cash' account type and they will then be available to add transactions to these accounts from within the app. We are looking at adding the ability to upload pictures for a future version.
