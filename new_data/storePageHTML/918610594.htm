Description

Use the WeRescue app to find your adoptable pet during Clear The Shelters from August 23rd - September 19th. Don't forget to apply early!

You've found the best app for dog adoption, cat adoption, and other furry and scaly creatures! WeRescue is designed to make it easy to adopt a pet, with over 70 available search filters, along with the best breed selection. Is there a breed you don't want to see? WeRescue lets you hide a breed from your search results!

Includes rescue dogs, cats, barn yard animals, birds, horses, rabbits, reptiles and small & furry pets!

• Best Breed search with upgrades for mixed breeds, designer breeds, and hiding of undesired breeds
• Extensive details on each pet – more than any other app on the market
• The largest number of filters of any pet adoption app
• Beautiful high-resolution photos

WeRescue app connects you with hundreds of thousands of pets across the United States – easily and quickly! Pets from shelters and foster-based rescues are shown in order of distance from your location. Enter zip codes for different areas. WeRescue can cap your search to a certain distance and let you specify gender, age, size, breed, and so many more. WeRescue offers more filters than any other adoption app!

WeRescue lets you share pets via social media, SMS text, Pinterest, Facebook, Twitter, Whatsapp and many more. Even if you're not in the market for a new pet, you can help save pet lives by sharing the beautiful pictures with friends. Your friends will see the exact pet you shared when they open the WeRescue app.

We're proud that we've brought innovative features to you like breed search, hiding breeds, mixed breeds, extensive filters, and beautiful high-resolution photos.

Condescending

I can’t stand apps with condescending or manipulative content. Fortunately, if you like either, you don’t even need to sign in to get both!
On the front page, there’s an option to create an account or “skip.” “Skip” simply opens a pop-up with an explanation of why it’s “important to sign in” to “save your data from being lost.” Thanks, I learned that when I lost my first important essay in grade school to our malfunctioning computer. I think my data is important to the developer, and it’s definitely important to adopt, not shop. I’ll just be using a different app for it, because I don’t want some developers with a freshman year’s grasp of manipulation tactics to have it.

2018dana , 12/29/2018

Repeated list of search results

So far I love the app (we are searching for our first puppy or young dog after the holidays).
I’ve noticed that when I do a search, I get a listing of dogs in the order of distance. Great. But then when I scroll downward I noticed that the list of available dogs is repeated 3 times. Not a big deal but I wanted you to be aware. So if I’m searching within a 100 mile range of home, I’ll get to the last dog at 84 miles out, and then I see the first dog that appeared at the top of the list (2 miles from home). Then I scroll through that list again and it repeats for a 3rd time showing me the first dog that’s 2 miles away.

I’m on iPhone 8 Plus, fully updated at iOS v12.1.2 (I also noticed it was happening with v12.1.1).
