Description

Black Friday deals. Buy clothes online, fashionable outfits

Women’s fashion: Women's fashion clothing store where you’ll find the best outfits.

Buy the best outfits from the Stradivarius app to enjoy the best shopping experience! Enjoy the best outfits for women and discover fashion trends.

◉ NEW FEATURES, PERKS EVERYWHERE!

- STORE GEOLOCATION. Find your nearest stores by activating the location on your device.

- QR CODE and VOICE SEARCH. Find the product you are looking for by scanning the QR code that you’ll find on the product label or use the voice search to find it.

- ELECTRONIC RECEIPT. Use the electronic receipt to have all your physical receipts saved on your app.

- FOLLOW-UP NOTIFICATIONS. Activate push notifications to keep up to date with news as well as knowing first-hand when your order will arrive.

- WISHLIST. Add items to your wish list.

- MANAGE YOUR CUSTOMER PROFILE. Through the app you can manage your personal details for making purchases: addresses, favourite stores and any fashion bookings you’ve made at physical stores.

Still don’t have the Stradivarius app? Discover a new way of shopping online for clothes and accessories, as well as creating wish lists for clothing, including your favourite dress trends for this season.

Store for girls’ and women’s clothing and accessories online, manage your orders, track your purchases and put together the most on-trend outfits. You´ll find dresses, T-shirts, shirts, jeans, trousers and accessories for girls.

◉ THE LATEST TRENDS IN WOMEN'S EVENING WEAR

Choose the best items for putting together on-trend and comfortable outfits every day. You can also create lists of trendy items, pay in-store and find the stores closest to you.

With the Stradivarius app, you can shop for women’s fashion clothing and accessories, put together the best outfits for each season, create lists of items from the catalogue, modify your customer profile and search for the nearest Stradivarius store.

◉ ABOUT STRADIVARIUS

We are Stradivarius. You are probably already familiar with our fashions and accessories. Our story began back in 1994 with a treble clef that became the hallmark of our firm. We celebrate our unique sense of style by inviting young women and men from around the world to discover all the melodies, energy and emotions behind Stradivarius - the fashion brand that moves to the sound of its own unique melody.

We design thousands of clothing items and accessories in Spain for exclusive distribution to our stores, which can be found in over 60 countries around the world, as well as to our online store, which started operating in 2011.

We are fully aware that what makes our customers happy today may not be what they want tomorrow, which is why we are constantly hunting down the latest trends to come up with the perfect looks for every occasion.

Each day, more than 10,000 employees with an authentic passion for fashion keep the Stradivarius facilities buzzing with activity, turning it into an authentic fashion hotspot. We take our passion with us wherever we go, enabling us to attract the world's most talented fashion professionals.
