Description

The GolfPass app gives you instant, anywhere access to exclusive GolfPass originals, your favorite Golf Channel programs, and much more, all in one convenient app. Take your favorite shows and pros to the course, the office, the airport or anywhere else, and watch original series and fan-favorites from the game’s best at home or on the go.

Keep the very best golf instruction close at hand, with thousands of on-demand golf tips and lessons from champion golfers like Rory McIlroy, as well as the same coaches who have helped major champions improve their game.

The GolfPass app works on your mobile device or connected TV, so you can enjoy the best golf videos and get closer to your favorite professional golfers wherever you want to watch. Catch up on the best Feherty interviews, new episodes of The Conor Moore Show, The Rory & Carson Podcast and others, along with exclusive access to classic shows like Big Break, iconic moments and clips from golf’s greatest events, and much more.

· Take your favorite shows anywhere you go with streaming access
· Watch new, exclusive shows and clips only for GolfPass members
· Stream live tournaments, updates and more
· Keep your favorite instructors in your pocket with on-demand lessons and daily tips.
· Watch 400+ hours of instruction and start improving your game immediately

Not Receiving Tee Time Credits with Annual Pass

I purchased the annual pass through the App Store on July 7 and still have not received any of the monthly tee time credits promised as part of the membership agreement. On the App, my membership card lists “member since July 07, 2021” and “valid through July 07, 2022.” It should be for this year, not next year.

I’ve called the customer service number several times to have these issues resolved, and have been told they will be fixed each time, but they still are not. There is no email address to try to contact for further support, only one phone number to call, where I end up having the same conversation each time.

In-app subscriptions broken

Got the in-app subscription, but it wouldn’t sync with my pre-existing golfpass account and I was forced to get a refund from Apple because the golfpass member services didn’t know what to do to link the subscription to my account. Basically to get this fixed I had to manually subscribe on the golfpass side for a slightly higher price, then sign into that account within the app. I want this fixed for others and for my troubles a compensating credit for the difference in monthly subscription price.

Developer Response ,

Hi Daniel, we appreciate you reaching out. Video-Only GOLFPASS subscriptions are available by Apple to offer golfers access to content on iOS devices. A full GOLFPASS membership purchased from the website includes additional benefits like a Monthly Tee Time Credit, Travel discounts, Shop offers, and more. For this reason, the full membership is a different price than the Video-Only option. Apple manages their subscriptions individually. Video-Only members can purchase the full membership online. We recommend canceling your Video-Only membership from Apple before enrolling in the full membership. We working on a way members can upgrade between memberships. At this time, iOS accounts are securely managed by Apple.
