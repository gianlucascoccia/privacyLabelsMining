Description

Looking for help or the latest tips for the game Stardew Valley? This all-in-one solution is exactly what you need: everything for the good game and improving your gaming skills. This app will be useful for both novice and experienced players. Does not require an internet connection to daily use, so that any relevant information is always available to you. It is unofficial.


● The database is available in the following languages:

• Chinese (中文)
• English
• French (Français)
• German (Deutsch)
• Hungarian (Magyar)
• Italian (Italiano)
• Japanese (日本語)
• Korean (한국어)
• Portuguese (Português)
• Russian (Русский)
• Spanish (Español)
• Turkish (Türkçe)

• Guides, hints, tips, and tricks
Tons of useful and nice guides that will be useful for both novice and experienced players.

• Items and recipes
Full info for each item or recipe: images, ingredients, stats, etc.

• News and videos
Follow the latest news and watch awesome videos about this game.

• Optimized
The app is fully optimized to work on all mobile devices from Apple Inc.

• Relationship
All objects (items, recipes, etc.) are interconnected with each other.

• Works offline
Once the database has been downloaded, the app can be used without access to the internet. This means that all the necessary information for a comfortable game will always be available to you even in places where there is no internet (for example, the subway). However, to view the news feed and such heavy files as videos or animated gif images, internet access is still required.

This database is a life saver!

I recently joined the ranks of SV enthusiasts (aka Addicts), and frequently found myself scribbling notes about characters’ favorites, when and where to fish in order to catch those Legendary fish, what day is Marnie’s Day off, when Caroline has aerobics class, and much more. As you can imagine, it was just as hard finding the information i needed when i needed it! Then it hit me: there’s probably an app for that!
I appreciate that this app is super easy to use, though i was so far into two different farms that i didn’t get the opportunity to update entries from my games. (Can you even log achievements from multiple games)?
The developer has done a marvelous job compiling so much game data, and has provided the ability to cross reference and navigate for ultimate flexibility. I highly recommend this app, and gladly send tips every so often. In fact that’s another plus - the database is free, and one can choose to gift tips, as compared with paying $1-5 or more for an app that’s not very good.
Thank you Kateryna!
One question: will you be providing support for iPhones 12? UPDATE: This database works perfectly on iPhone 12, it just took my phone about 24 hours to prompt me to download the data.
Additionally, i sent an email to the developer and the response time was super quick. Thanks again!
