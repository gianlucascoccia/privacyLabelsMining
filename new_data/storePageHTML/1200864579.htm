Description

Hi there! Welcome to the fun-filled tropical islands of Funky Bay!

Build your own town and farm in a tropical paradise. Set out on amazing adventures and find your fortune. Exotic plants, animals, and adorable pets await you! The charming and colourful characters have lots of interesting stories to tell!

Harvest crops, process them at your factories, fulfil your orders and sell goods to develop your town and farm. Explore new lands, mine resources, solve quests and unearth pirate treasures. Become an Adventurer, start your own business, escape your routine and get some real relaxation on an island paradise! What are you waiting for!

FEATURES:

• Breathtaking scenery and stunning graphics;
• Create and customize your very own farm with tons of different buildings and decorations;
• Harvest crops and fruit to craft valuable goods;
• Trade with island neighbours and friends;
• Begin your adventure with EXPEDITIONS on different islands in Funky Bay;
• Get pets and animals for your own farm zoo;
• Buy and sell valuables on the global marketplace with other players;
• Explore and expand your beautiful island;
• Embark on exciting and risky adventures for more resources;
• Play 100s of different quests with guaranteed bonuses and souvenirs!
• Interact with the fun local characters!

Funky Bay is free to download and install. However, some game items can also be purchased for real money.

Goozed4 , 06/01/2020

Nice graphics/glitchy game

I loved this game at the beginning, the graphics are great compared to other games. I like the regatta task and side adventures games but there is so much that the developers can’t seem to resolve all the constant glitches. The timed adventures were fun but not anymore due to amount of energy clear areas. I have never made an in app game purchase until this game because I wanted to finish one of their adventure games, that’s how much I enjoyed this particular app. But I was charged double of what I actually purchased. Luckily, I was able to report it to Apple and was refunded. One of the most frustrating part about this game is not receiving a response when sending a bug report. I lost 100,000 coins earned from the side adventure games, reported it but only got the automated generic email response that my report was received. My other reports also went through the same process, and still no resolution. I’ve only been playing the game 2 months and will be throwing in the towel. There are too many issues.

Developer Response ,

Hello! Sorry for the inconveniences. Please send a bug report in the game to our support team and describe your problem. The response might be delayed due to a heavy workload, but our support agents will reply as soon as possible. Also, could you please check you "Spam" folder and check If default e-mail of your device is the same as the one you use. Hope for your understanding!

TheLicensed , 03/10/2021

Nerfing the market

I have been playing the game for months now. And really enjoyed it. Finally found a fun and active neighborhood to join where we worked together to complete tasks and help each other out.
Now with the latest update they have totally nerfed the market. Instead of players buying and selling with each other and the market updates every 10 minutes with several players with multiple bins of items they have replaced it with one that has 10 bins. And those items don’t change for 6 hours unless you spend 10 gems to get new items. On top of that where we had basically from over 100 possible bins when buying from other players we are down to 10...unless you spend gems to additional bins. The items they have vary and you cab find items that sold fast in the player’s market and you pay top price for them which was common in the player’s market as well. But you could also sell items at the highest price as well. Now you sell to no one (so you can’t sell to mates in need) but you can sell through your silo and barn...at the cheapest possible price. This also caused the regatta task to sell items to go away. One of my favorites to perform.
Updates like this one will kill the game off because l am sure it is only the first in more to follow down the road to make you pay to play to get anywhere. I will give it some time to see what happens, but if money l spent on the game is going to upgrades like this...l will be keeping my money from now on.

Developer Response ,

Hello! Thank you for the feedback. We are sorry for any way in which we may have inconvenienced you. Right now players do not have problems with cheaters, sold out goods, glitches with the market and other issues. We are still working on the new market, so we would like to assure you that we have taken into consideration your feedback.

CherylStone , 06/27/2019

Cute but buggy

This is a cute game. I love the concept, the characters, the tasks and games, the islands. But it is buggy, even glitchy. The market is full of items that are already sold, making it nearly useless. Many players have complained but there’s no response. The cost of items increasing as you move up levels is ridiculous. An apple tree should cost the same on every level. The in-game chat for neighbors works sporadically and is very limiting. The islands are cute and I love this separate game, but the cost to clear them is high. Again, complaints from everyone but silence from the developers. I’ve paid real money for them in the past, as I want to support the game. It’s at the point that I am not sure that I will give the game any more money until problems are fixed. And finally, the regatta. Fantastic concept, I love it and my neighborhood. But it needs an overhaul. The tasks are too low in points to make a serious run for placing in top 3, and this is after an update. So they made it harder. And there are teams, regularly from outside the United States, that rack up impossible points in the first few minutes of the race. It’s impossible, so something is going on. And again silence from the developers. I love this game but it needs attention, and the developers need to start communicating more than silly polls on their Facebook page.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for your detailed feedback. We are sorry you have this feeling and impression about the game, but appreciate your review anyway. We continue working on the game design and balance and it is important for us to receive as much info from our players as possible. We changed many mechanics in the game, and also changed the points system in the regatta. Also, we changed the value of many items in the game, including trees. Now we are working on new ideas and hope that this will make the game more interesting for our players.
