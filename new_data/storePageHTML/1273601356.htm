Description

Who are we?
Whether you are dealing with depression, coping with chronic pain, entering retirement, working on increasing physical & mental wellness and motivation, or simply wanting to build your self-esteem, Wisdo has your back. With over 70 demographic-specific support communities and nearly 2 million users and trained Wisdo Helpers, Guides and Coaches, we harness the power of peer to peer support by connecting people with similar life circumstances. Wisdo is an extraordinary community that is available to support, listen, share resources & give/take advice 24/7. We are an award-winning mental-health & wellbeing app devoted to eliminating loneliness and maximizing wellbeing. Created as a solution to the loneliness epidemic, we have been voted #1 for Personal Growth & Social Impact, and have been voted App of the Day 5 times. Download Wisdo to find out why.

Key Features:
1. Check-in with yourself, share your story & set long-term goals
Share your story so we can get to know you & connect you to people who are facing similar life circumstances
Set long-term goals so we can help you track your progress, provide customizable, daily tips, and hold you accountable

2. Join any of our 70+ demographic-specific communities
We have safe, judgment-free communities all over Wisdo, committed to uniting people facing similar challenges. Use these communities to lean on peers for support, vent, learn, ask questions, and form meaningful connections.
Examples of our communities include for:
- Anxiety
- Practicing Self-Care
- Depression
- Relationship advice
- Building Self-Esteem
- Cancer
- Diabetes
- Parents of a child with special needs
- People who were adopted
- LGBTQ life
- People dealing with racism
And 60+ more!
Log onto our app to learn what other demographic-specific communities are waiting for you.

3. Maximize self-care by taking advantage of our Life Coach platform
We have over 30 professionally trained life coaches on Wisdo, eager to help you maximize your potential, identify obstacles in your way, and help you achieve long-term goals.
Life coaches on Wisdo exist to provide you with an informed perspective and to give you that friendly push that you have been needing.

4. Connect with trained Wisdo Helpers & Guides who are here to help & support
Helpers & Guides exist to support other Wisdo users through their journeys
Consider them the tour guides of Wisdo; here to orient you, help facilitate meaningful conversations throughout our various communities, and ensure that interactions stay safe and appropriate.


5. Put a face to the name: attend Wisdo Zoom events to engage with other community members on meaningful topics. Such events include:
Resilience Trainings by Dr. Gill
Wellness Wednesday - support session where everyone shares what's going on in their lives, receive support and return the same with others
Lighthearted sessions: games, icebreaker, & trivia

Hyguufcucuuc , 11/20/2017

Get this 👌🏻👌🏻👌🏻👌🏻

By first opening the app there are specific types of problems (mental or physical) to talk about in group chats. You can add more specific types of chats and people can join. There are ‘guides’ who monitor the conversation, keep it going and will talk to you with others in the chat and even direct message you if you don’t want all people to see. Even though the live chats are about specific things you can chat a guide or any person in general that’s willing to talk about any situation you might have.
Under another tab there are various subjects such as depression, MS, anorexia, child birth and even more mainstream things such as graduating from college, loosing a job, divorce and breakups that give some good advice (I’m bad at explaining just check it out for yourself). These really target specific problems.
I suggest everyone get this app. The only thing I don’t like is the Pinterest looking theme under the specific tabs and I also wish they could tell you what the specific thing is about Incase you just want to learn about it (but I guess part of the app is defining that for yourself per your own experience)
Overall great. (:
