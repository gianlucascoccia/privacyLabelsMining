Description

As the pioneers of Golf GPS, for the past 20 years, over 5 million golfers all around the world continually trust GolfLogix for exact GPS distances, full-color 3D course maps, 4-player scoring, pro-level stats, handicap tracking and discounted tee times. 
 
GolfLogix has recently invested heavily in mapping golf course greens to centimeter accuracy.  With these new green maps, we have innovated the art of putting with our new Putt Line feature which reads your putts and shows you exactly where to aim to sink it every time. Our new green maps also help you on all of your approach shots because from the fairway, you can see the contours and breaks of the green from afar so you'll always know where to aim and how your ball will roll when landing on the green.  No other golf app has all of this groundbreaking technology to help you shoot lower scores. 
 
Playing in an Official Handicap Tournament?  We've got you covered!  When starting your round, turn on 'Official Tournament Mode' and you will get to use all of the allowable features that keep you in compliance with the latest rules of golf.  You'll get to use 3D fairway maps, precise GPS distances to all hazards/layups/greens and see how your putts will break with our new detailed green maps (the Putt Line feature is turned off per official rules).  You'll have the same green map books that Pro golfers get to use in their tournaments, but you'll have all of them right on your phone, for thousands of courses!
 
The GolfLogix app was designed and built by golfers for golfers and is so easy, fast and fun to use that it seamlessly blends with your traditional golfing routines.

StarLakeGolfer , 08/16/2017

Outstanding GPS & scorecard

Love it, have been using it since 2013. Great GPS function & scorecard. I have the upgrade Premium Membership with the additional features & stats = great additional features for the small annual fee. Neat tracking for pitches, chip & putts... The most important strokes of the game. Wouldn't be without it on the course... Makes lay ups where necessary and easy to plan with the target layup feature.
Scorecard is customizable & easy to enter scores/stats for the hole. Haven't set up club tracking yet, but I believe it may "suggest" a club to use if properly setup. Allows team scoring, although I don't use that. My focus is to track & improve my game, with on the course assistance for distance - especially when playing new or infrequently visited courses.
Stat tracking is fun to watch - especially on putts... seems my most consistent "miss" is on the RIGHT. Probably relates to my setup for the putt (offline or incorrectly reading it - optical illusions?)... so it is targeting "something" to work on! All around a great & versatile App. Loads fast & is relatively easy on the iPhone battery... typically uses about 25 - 30% of the battery for an 18 hole round. I have done 36 holes without a battery recharge - started with close to 100%, battery was still ok for phone calls etc when done with golf rounds.

Great APP - Needs some work

I’ve been using this APP as a Champion Member for ~5+ years. It’s been a great APP up until recently. I purchased an Apple iWatch 4 with Cellular/GPS and was hoping I wouldn’t need to carry my iPhone 8+ everywhere on the course and could just leave it in the cart and use the standalone GPS feature in the iWatch 4. Unfortunately, there is times the standalone GPS feature in the iWatch 4 appears to work and shows a different distance than what is shown on the iPhone 8+ sitting in the golf cart - and there is times the distance to the center of the green doesn’t change on the iWatch 4 as I walk 25-50 yards in front of the iPhone 8+ sitting in the golf cart. Please work to improve this feature.

Additionally, the new updates are a bit annoying. Why is it the approach and green maps automatically pop-up? This slows me down too much - I would rather have the ability to access approach and green maps when desired. Please add a feature where one can select a setting where the approach and green maps can be set to AUTO ON at the users request. Thanks

I’ve been unable to leave my iPhone in the golf cart and receive a different distance on iWatch 4. gojandrooo , 09/10/2021

Good app, Mostly gets the job done

The course listing is good, haven’t played an unlisted course yet and 90% of courses have the green details included. When doing the green detail the GPS can glitch out-sometimes so your alignment to the green isn’t correct and you have to rotate your orientation to imagine how it should be aligned. The best part of the app is being able to plan two shots at a time, i.e. you can move the target around the hole to show if you hit it 150yds it will automatically display you have 100yds remaining to center green. However, this feature does mess up sometimes and the app thinks you are on a different hole than your actual current hole. It would be nice if there was a reset button to keep it from “sticking” on a particular hole. It would also be nice if you could customize the UI a little bit. My push chart has a phone holder and I constantly have to take the phone out of the holder to get to the arrow buttons. If I could toggle the arrows to be at the top it would be nice. All said it’s a good app that gets the job done. A couple feature updates and it would be 5 stars from me.
