Description

Join Buster Bumblechops in this goopendous adventure to complete the ultimate Moshling collection.

Catch and hatch up to six new Moshlings every day. You can even hatch Ultra Rare Super Moshlings using special Golden Spoons! Along the way you will adopt, name and customize your very own unique pet Moshling, play fun mini-games and solve puzzles as you explore the ever-expanding world of Moshi.

This app is completely free with no in-game payments or transactions - just 100% Moshi fun for everyone!

• Find, hatch and collect Moshlings

- Adopt, customise and name your very own unique pet Moshling
- Hatch up to six new Moshlings every day
- Care for your Moshlings, feed them and decorate their home to make them happy
- Find Super Moshlings, ultra rare superheroes in tough-to-crack eggs

• Play together

- Add your friends’ unique Moshlings to your collection
- Send your friends gifts
- Visit your friends’ homes to see who has the best items and rooms
- Browse your friends’ moshling collection

• Solve puzzles, play fun mini-games, collect items

- 19 fun mini-games including: Potion Commotion, Glumpageddon, Flick ‘n’ Mix, Moshi Slide, Math Mash, Monster Maze, Time Tangle, Ice Scream Parlour
- Learn as you earn Rox: memory, maths, spelling, shapes, mazes, geography, telling the time
- Take a spin on the Wheel of Moshiness for daily rewards
- Custom Moshling house with expandable floors, updated decorations and room items
- Moshi Shopping Mall with Snozzle Wobbleson’s Grossery Store & Dewy’s DIY Shop

Moshi Monsters Egg Hunt is free to play with your parent’s permission.

• SAFETY and COPPA compliance

We do NOT automatically collect personal information through this App. To save game data, users are be required to create an account and submit an email and password.

Great, but bring PC back!

The app is great, but there are still some crucial things that are missing! Moshi Monsters was such a fun online game for my friends and I to play when we were younger! We would talk on the land-line and play together. My main disappointment was not being able to pick my Monster at the beginning, the Moshi pets are way different. I love the ability to easily collect them all, but the “favorite” being the same size as the rest of the ones you collect makes it kinda lame. I haven’t had the opportunity to add anyone yet, but I assume that it won’t be like the original where you can see other Monsters walking around. I used to love making new friends that way! Dressing your monster was so fun!

And side note: I’d like it if the ice cream parlor game had more than one cone set-up.. it helps you go faster when you can prepare the different flavors before putting the toppings on..

If y’all make a comeback online I feel like there’d be a FLOOD of nostalgic young adults and their younger siblings dying to play! Hope to hear that announcement soon.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for your review. When you have friends on the app, you can go to visit their home and zoo and also send gifts. When you start the game and have created your Moshling, it is possible to change to a different one in the game when selecting a Moshling as a favourite in the Zoo. We will certainly share all of your feedback with the team. Happy Egg Hunting!
