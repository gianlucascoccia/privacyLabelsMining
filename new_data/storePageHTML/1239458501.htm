Description

Simon’s Cat in Crunch Time! - Connect treats with Simon's Cat
Published by Tactile Games.

The devilish crows have swiped Simon’s Cat’s favourite Tasty Treats and only YOU can help him find them!

Join Simon’s Cat, Maisy, Chloe and Jazz, and a cast of determined kitties on a colourful adventure through some pawsome new places where Treats are just waiting to be eaten.
Be ready to collect Tasty Treats, fight off big baddies and befriend a host of animal critters in this FREE puzzle game with added CATitude!

Simple and addictive puzzle gameplay! Connect the same coloured Treats to make a delicious chain!

Play your way through the neighbourhood in six different game modes and hundreds of challenging levels!

Challenge your friends! Can you beat your friends purrfect high score?
Can you beat the villains? Use your Treats and special boosters to fight off Sir Royston the Pug, Mr Potts, hungry hedgehogs and even a treat crazed vacuum cleaner!
Play with Simon’s Cat featuring brand new animations

Simon's Cat is the internet’s most mischievous cat and has become a global phenomenon. Star of over 60 films, which have been watched over 870 million times, and winner of several major industry awards, Simon's Cat has captured the hearts of a worldwide audience.

We are working hard to update the game with new levels that are purrfectly crafted and will leave you feline great!

Irish1280 , 03/11/2018

Love it

I play a ton of different match 3 games and this is definitely one of my favorites. The artwork is so detailed, sometimes I scroll through the levels just to see the scenery and fun animations in each section. I haven’t had the trouble a few others mentioned about having to spend money to progress through the levels. I’m currently on level 258 and have yet to spend a cent.

My only complaint is the ads. Well, one ad in particular actually. Now, I expect ads in a free game and I don’t mind the ones that are silent. I’ll actually watch those and occasionally click on them. But the ones that unexpectedly blast you with loud, garish music despite the fact that you have all sounds turned off in the game are unacceptable. When that happens I immediately scramble to exit the app as quickly as possible. Lately I’ve been getting the same ad -for the Family Guy game - nonstop after every single level. Seriously, I think I’ve only seen a different ad once or twice in the last month, and I play the game daily. This ad is loud and is driving me crazy! Please get rid of loud ads, and I’ll be one happy camper!

imlafn , 07/05/2018

Sales issues as others

Love the game and been playing it since day 1, too. But, man, what a waste of money when you use your boosters in a tournament and it doesn’t record your score that would put you in first place when you’ve earned it! Just beat the high score and it didn’t record the new score. I suspected it was happening before and I wasn’t playing close attention, but it was pretty obvious this time since my goal was to take first place. No celebration here. It’s either a bad bug or a way for the developer to get you to buy more coins/boosters.

Also, when you’re out of lives, you may watch a 30-second advertisement, only to come back and not have the life added. I usually quit the app, go back and watch another ad (*sigh*) and then it will award me the extra life. Good grief— I understand it’s a revenue-generator to have limited lives, but why can’t developers take a tip from Angry Birds and just have unlimited lives? I’d probably play all day, so perhaps it’s a blessing.

Either way, super-addictive, fun game, with an endearing theme, but get your bugs out or there’s no incentive to play if the game is going to cheat you of any accomplishments. Might as well throw my money into the lake. I’ve started deleting the app for a month or two and then playing for a week to get caught up. Way better than being at the mercy of limited lives, daily challenges, and frustratingly bad bugs.

Bear mind , 09/12/2018

Needs improvement

This is basically a match 3 game with cute graphics. I’ve played every level and while I like this game it needs a lot of improvement. Higher Levels freeze and you have to exit the level (losing all bonuses) to get it to work again. This happened a lot to me when I was at the max level of “Hot Streak” bonuses. They advertise a hard level challenge when the game starts up but I have yet to find it (this may be user error). The cost of a bonus is 70 coins which is very high, especially when the daily challenge award is 1 - 5 coins. 3 stars are very hard to achieve which is frustrating. You’ll end up spending a lot of money to buy bonuses. It will say you’ve missed a daily challenge even if you successfully play it daily and it disqualifies you from being eligible for the bigger bonuses at the end. This happens almost every time you spend 25 coins to play the next level on the same day. The developers are spectacularly unhelpful. I’ve submitted several problems to them and the responses have been amazingly unresponsive. It can take weeks to get a response. The most common is that unless you’re signed in with FB, they can’t help you and even when I signed in with FB, I received no help and they don’t fix the issues with the game even when you give them the levels that are problematic. I really can’t recommend this game until the issues are fixed. Play one of the many, many other similar games until they fix this one.
