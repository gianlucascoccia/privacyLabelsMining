Description

Become the Racer King!

Race against competitive opponents, make your way through a perilous track, and survive furious attacks. Be the last one standing to win the crown.

Press and hold to speed-up, drag to steer, and tap to jump.

Drive, drift, jump, bump, and collect coins to unlock dozens of awesome vehicles.

Game features:
• Super smooth arcade physics
• Intuitive controls
• Addictive gameplay
• 18 cars to collect

Fasten your seat belt and enjoy the thrill of battle royale with cars.

Great in theory not in execution

The ad is more satisfying than actual game play. The controls are jank, the servers are jank, the physics are a dice roll. Sometimes you have excellent traction and other times your wheels seem to be a corn cob over a stick of butter. The jump mechanic works 50% of the time. Winning doesn’t matter because you get the same reward regardless. Some may say that’s good, but it’s a battle royale game with cars. If winning doesn’t matter then why is it a battle royale? On top of all that nonsense you get ads as you’re trying to drive. I get you have to monetize your free game, but do you have to do it right at the start of a match as i’m trying to play? These are all frustrating things all sandwiched together. I went in with an open mind and i left with the urge to punch my fist through my expensive tablet. Wife, if you’re reading this, i did not do it this time. I was tempted, but rest assured the rage did not win over my logic this time. It’s been a very anger inducing day but i believe i can push that aside and give an unbiased opinion. I was thinking one 1 star but it seemed too harsh because like i said it does have a cool concept. It’s just put together more ham fisted than i kid playing with playdough for the first time.
