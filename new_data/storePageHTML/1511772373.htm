Prayer Times, Salah Guide, Dua

Amar Zeitoun

    • Free

iPhone Screenshots

Description

Shia+ is the latest, always free, Muslim companion app with features that will assist you on a daily basis.

Features:
• Accurate prayer times based on your location.
• Multiple Adhans or notification tones to choose from.
• Adhan Player to hear the full Adhan whenever you'd like.
• Countdown the remaining time of each prayer.
• Islamic calendar that will always remind you of important Islamic events.
• Description and history of each Islamic event.
• Wherever you are in the world, the Qibla compass will point you in the right direction.
• Prayer guide to assist muslims just learning to pray or anyone that just needs a quick reminder.
• Dua (supplication) guide for any occasion.
• Qadha tracker to make sure you never forget your qadha prayers or missed fasts you have left.
• 99 names of Allah will help you gain a better understanding of each of Allah's attributes by giving you the transliteration of the name and the meaning.
• Daily A'maal tracker to help you build a righteous and remember Allah throughout the day.
• Tasbih al-Zahra counter with audio pronunciation option.
• Tasbih counter with audio pronunciation option.
• Read a brief narrative of the events that took place during the tragedy of Karbala.
• Motivational Islamic videos.
• Home Screen iOS 14 Widget to get quick access to the day's prayer times, upcoming prayer time, upcoming Islamic calendar event, and Daily A'maal stats.
• Pray for Others feature allows you to set a prayer and add the names you wish to pray for every day.
• Many more features and updates to come!

No In-App purchases: All features and adhan's unlocked!
Ad-free: Enjoy Shia+ without any distractions!

What’s New

Nov 8, 2021

Version 2.1.1

Fixes and improvements included in this version:

• Adhan - Adhan 1, 2, and 3 have been changed with beautiful adhan's that include the phrase of wilaya
• Dua - Added Dua Faraj to Common Supplications section
• Qadha Tracker - Salat al-Ayaat has been added to the Qadha tracker so that you don't have to worry about forgetting to make-up the prayer
• Prayer Guide - Salat al-Ghufayla, a highly recommended prayer that is performed between maghrib and isha has been added to the prayer guide
• Fixes - Minor bug fixes and UI updates
