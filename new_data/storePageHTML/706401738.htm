Description

The KeepTruckin Driver App is one of the highest-rated ELD / Electronic Logbook apps, and it is trusted by over 1M drivers and 90,000 companies across the United States and Canada.
The KeepTruckin Driver App makes recording Hours of Service (HOS) on your iPhone or iPad fast and easy.
The KeepTruckin Electronic Logbook App complies with the FMCSA regulations, including Part 395. When used with the KeepTruckin Vehicle Gateway, the app helps fleets and individual commercial drivers meet their obligations outlined under the ELD mandate.

ELD-COMPLIANT ELECTRONIC LOGBOOK
Simply connect the KeepTruckin Driver App to the Vehicle Gateway to stay compliant.

SUPPORT FOR CURRENT CANADIAN FEDERAL HOURS OF SERVICE (HOS) RULES
KeepTruckin supports current Canadian Federal Hours of Service (HOS) regulations.

PROACTIVE HOURS OF SERVICE VIOLATION ALERTS
KeepTruckin will proactively alert you if you’re running out of driving time to avoid Hours of Service (HOS) violations.

COMPREHENSIVE RECAP
View your total hours worked for the week as well as your available Hours of Service for any given day and the following day.

SECURE DOT INSPECTION MODE
Inspection Mode allows you to show your ELD logs to an officer during a roadside inspection without sacrificing your privacy.

DRIVER VEHICLE INSPECTION REPORTS (DVIRs)
Easily complete pre-trip and post-trip driver vehicle inspection reports (DVIRs) so you can easily report and keep track of any defects.

DOCUMENT MANAGEMENT AND MESSAGING
Keep up-to-date with your fleet manager through the Messaging and Documents features in the KeepTruckin Driver App. Message your fleet manager directly and upload important documents, like bills of lading or accident photos, so you never have to leave your elogs app.

GET SUPPORT WHEN YOU NEED IT
Call or email our friendly 24/7 support team with any questions.

OVERVIEW
The KeepTruckin Electronic Logbook App is a user-friendly free elog and ELD app that drivers, fleet managers, carriers, and owner operators trust.
KeepTruckin has been featured in many popular magazines such as Overdrive, FleetOwner and CCJ. The KeepTruckin Driver App is one of the highest-rated free apps for the ELD mandate.
Apart from ELD compliance, the KeepTruckin Electronic Logbook App also improves efficiency and productivity with features such as Hours of Service violation alerts, DVIRs, document management, messaging, DOT inspection mode, and much more.
The KeepTruckin Electronic Logging App is compatible with iPhones and iPads running iOS 10.0 or higher.
For more information about the KeepTruckin Electronic Logbook App and the FMCSA-registered KeepTruckin ELD, visit keeptruckin.com.

BACKGROUND LOCATION DISCLAIMER
The KeepTruckin ELD App requests access to determine your location while the app is in the background. Continued use of GPS running in the background can dramatically decrease battery life. However, this is not an issue for the KeepTruckin App because it only accesses it occasionally. The app has been tested both with and without location enabled and the difference in battery life is negligible.
