Description

***Patients: this app is for doctors and healthcare providers. If you’re looking for the athenahealth patient portal, please contact your doctor’s office***

Designed for doctors and healthcare providers, the athenaOne app is a secure, mobile extension of athenaNet clinical workflows designed to help athenaClinicals customers deliver better care. Simply log in with your athenaNet credentials to get started.

- Access your daily schedule
- Quickly manage your clinical inbox 
- Prepare for upcoming visits ahead of time
- Use your existing documentation accelerators
- Document exams and place orders
- Create new patient cases and order groups

What’s New

Oct 25, 2021

Version 2.2.45

1. Orders: You can now edit and sign patient information orders.
2. Inbox: An alert now displays when you try to approve or deny a new or renewal prescription document that does not have a diagnosis and ICD-10 code.
3. Vitals:
- The Vitals section now displays results in order from oldest to newest.
- The Vitals section now displays blood pressure (BP) results as horizontal line graphs.
4. Exam tab:
- Capture images more easily with a new Capture image icon.
- View jotter images.
5. Summary tab: The Summary tab is renamed the Briefing tab.
6. Patient cases: When creating a new patient case from the Home screen, you can now select Patient Portal as the Source if the patient is registered on the Patient Portal.
7. Results tab: Links to PACS medical images in imaging results now work as expected.
8. Widgets: You can now add athenaOne app widgets to the Home Screen on your device.
9. athenaTelehealth:
- You can now rate the call quality and provide feedback when you end an athenaTelehealth call.
10. Profile:
- Push notifications from the athenaOne app are now cleared when you log out of the app.

Andrew07Mac , 03/30/2021

Great for adding photos, would like better writing support

This app is really wonderful for being able to quickly log into your patient list for the day. It is also very convenient for adding photos, for example of skin lesions, to a patient’s chart. No synchronizing your phone to a patient’s chart or anything like you have to with “Athena capture”.

There are a couple of issues I would love to see addressed. The first is that when you’re viewing the schedule, the app automatically right refreshes every so often which jumps you back to the top of the schedule making it very frustrating, if not impossible to follow along during a team huddle. The second thing is that I would love to see write to text support in the text fields. This only makes sense for a mobile app that many providers will use on an iPad to help quickly document things in a room.
