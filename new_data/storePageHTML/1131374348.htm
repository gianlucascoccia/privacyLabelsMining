Cg46 , 01/11/2021

Close to Perfect

We have used Albertson’s curbside pickup weekly since April, 2020. During that time, the store has enlarged the list of available items available to pick up curbside. There have been a few errors in our orders over the months, but dealing with those glitches is a minor issue when compared to the convenience the service provides. The ordering process is quick and easy. The timeframe for pickup is very accommodating. Substitutions are minimal. The employees bringing out our order are always friendly and helpful. We live very close to the store, so pickup is easy and quick. Since my hubby and I are retired and have a few health issues, this service is very important to those of us who need to remain at home and safe from potential virus spread. I hope that Albertson’s will continue the curbside pickup program even when the virus is a distant memory. It is a very valuable service to many customers.

Solitaire Granny , 12/11/2020

Albertsons Delivery and curbside pickup

I absolutely love Albertsons and for the past 24 years have shopped here in Great Falls. Being a gourmet cook snd a foodie, I prefer to shop myself, daily if possible. I can stand and look at dozens of pieces of produce before selecting just the right piece of fruit, mushroom, or cucumber.

A series of health issues and broken bones and now CoVid concerns have made it difficult for me to get to the store and shop safely, consequently I have had my groceries delivered for nearly two years. The personal shoppers are the best. If there are questions about something I have ordered, my shopper will call, the online order system is easy to use, and rarely is anything wrong with my order.
I make one big order a month and It is delivered right to my door. Actually, when I need it,
the delivery person will bring it right into the kitchen! Thank you, Debbie Haney and my Albertsons personal shoppers!

MD.mom , 09/05/2020

Excellent service and a real time saver!

I’ve been doing pick up for a few months from my neighborhood Albertsons and it has been wonderful. I almost always am able to get a same day pick up. I’ve had only a couple of items picked that were molded or spoiled, but with a call they corrected the issue. Since I live close it takes just a few minutes to return for the correct item and they are usually waiting for me when I get there.

Originally I still had to go in for harder to find items, but I’ve learned to scan that are harder to search for, now I don’t have to go in at all. On the rare occasion I go in because I only need an item or 2 immediately.

The service has been wonderful and a true time saver.
