Description

You've been asking us to change our app! ... so we did! Now we need your help to make it yours. The more you use it and give feedback, the more it evolves to be custom to what you want to see! And you can still gain access to The Zumiez Stash rewards you can’t get anywhere else, but now you can also shop the best brands and products we know you love. Because… it’s all about you.

As always, as a member of The Zumiez Stash, you’ll gain access to exclusive one-of-a-kind rewards, limited edition merchandise, and once-in-a-lifetime experiences from your favorite Zumiez brands. Log in and start getting points for doing the things you already do with Zumiez; shopping in-store or online, answering fun questions and surveys, attending Zumiez events, interacting with Zumiez everywhere, and more!

• Shop a customized feed of brands and products just for you
• Redeem and access rewards we know you’ll love
• Easily manage your points
• Help us continue to evolve the app: by reacting to products and rewards, answering questions and by sending feedback.
• Scan and enter receipts for points
• Quick access to zumiez.com, our customer care team, find a store and search jobs
• If you’re still reading this you should come work with us

Zumiez for life!

This store and all of the sales reps/employees are absolutely remarkable. I manage a successful business myself and I am overwhelmed by the quality of employees Zumiez has. They’re educated, kind, proactive, always looking to ensure the experience of their customers is a positive one. They’ve been outstanding on every one of my visits and I sincerely hope they’re recognized by their employers for all that they do and all that they add to this company as a whole. I’ve been in Zumiez on some of the very hardest days of my life and without me mentioning it the employees have taken note, and gone out of their way to make sure I was okay. They made sure I found what I was looking for and also reassured me on a personal level, showing genuine interest and compassion for what I was going through. I can find some of the products for lesser prices elsewhere, but I won’t go anywhere else due to the hospitality and empathy of the employees at Zumiez. The monetary value of the experience inside of these stores is valuable and I hope Zumiez realizes the worth of their employees. 🖤🖤🖤
