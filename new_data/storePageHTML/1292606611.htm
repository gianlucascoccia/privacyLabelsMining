Description

Looking for a kind and caring house and pet sitter, or love pets and want to find your next adventure? Download the award-winning TrustedHousesitters app and connect with the community of trusted pet lovers who are exchanging pet care for unique travel experiences.

“We cannot tell you how rewarding this lifestyle is. You simply have to try it..”
— Stephanie D, TrustedHousesitters member

Why do pet owners love it?
Rated 9.6/10 on Trustpilot with over 9,000 reviews, TrustedHousesitters helps you confidently connect and keep in touch with sitters, whenever, wherever. It’s pet care in your pocket that provides true peace of mind, offering sitter verifications, 24/7 support, and a dedicated vet advice line.

Why do sitters love it?
With thousands of sits in over 130 countries, TrustedHousesitters helps pet lovers like you find accommodation with adorable animals around the world. You can gain verifications and reviews to stand out from the pack when applying for sits, and even leave owners feedback.

What you’ll discover in the TrustedHousesitters app:

HOUSE AND PET SITTING AT YOUR FINGERTIPS
Use your free account to explore our community or start your annual membership plan to arrange house and pet sits, safely and securely. Already a member? Just log in to your app using your usual details.

FIND SITS AND SITTERS FAST
Choosing pet sitters over boarding or kennels? The app includes fantastic filters designed to help you find the perfect person to care for your pets. You can also use a wide range of filters, from ‘Small pets’ to ‘High-speed WiFi’, when searching for your next exciting sit.

NEVER LOSE A PROFILE OR LISTING
Discovered the profile of a sitter your cat will love or a listing with the dog of your dreams? You can store all your favorite profiles and listings safely away to view anytime, anywhere.

SAVE TIME WITH SAVED SEARCHES
Want to stay somewhere with a particular type of pet over a certain period of time? Simply save your search and you’ll get an alert straight to your phone as soon as matching sits are added.

MANAGE YOUR DASHBOARD
You can use your app to view your owner listing or sitter profile, and manage all your sits.

EASY-TO-USE MESSAGING
Contact other members on the go, and share updates and photos so you can stay connected wherever your travels take you.

SHARE YOUR EXPERIENCES
Tap and swipe to leave your dog’s much-loved sitter a five-star review or leave helpful feedback about your time caring for a cat in the countryside.

REFER A FRIEND
Met a fellow pet lover on your adventures? Use your app to share your exclusive discount code with ease. When they apply your code, you’ll get two free months of membership — a special thank you for growing the pack.
