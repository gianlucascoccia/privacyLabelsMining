Description

Plan your perfect trip from the comfort of home or on the road with the official KOA Camping App. Find new places to explore, reserve your stay, and make your way out!

Find Camping Near You! Whether you prefer to camp in your RV, enjoy the luxury of lodging or enjoy the classic tenting experience, KOA has you covered! The KOA app offers 500+ locations to explore across the US and Canada.

Where to Next?

Features of the KOA App:
• Make Reservations in the App
• Search and filtering capability allows you to find the perfect site in a campground that meets all of your needs.
• Manage your KOA.com account.
• Search by city, state, campground name, or nearby attractions.
• Map your nearby location or browse the map for your destination.
• The KOA App integrates with mapping services for hassle free directions to your campground.
• Campground information, search tools and driving directions all work off-line, making the KOA app perfect to on-the-go use.
• Set favorite campground or view recent campgrounds for streamlined trip planning.
• Customizable notification settings give you the inside scoop on the latest KOA hot deals and events from your favorite campgrounds.

Love the KOA app!

I don’t know how many times this app has helped us find a KOA when plans have changed during a trip. No more do we have to go online and scramble to find an available campground at the last minute- only to find out the office is closed and there are no online reservations. There are so many KOA campgrounds to choose from, and the way this app describes each campground, the amenities, and full descriptions of available sites, as well as being able to immediately make the reservation - makes it very easy to find an enjoyable place to stay. We have found places we love and have returned to, that we wouldn’t have found without this app. Love KOA!

mindctrl , 10/25/2021

Slow and forgetful

The good: You can book sites online. That’s about it. The not so good: It’s slow. Annoyingly so. The fact that the loading bar exists at all indicates they expect a poor user experience, otherwise it should be architected to avoid this. The older app didn’t do this and was much faster in general. The app doesn’t remember your selected preferences profile, meaning every time you check availability/make reservations, you have to wait for the page to load (red loading indicator and all), select your profile from the dropdown, wait again while the loading indicator makes you want say bad words, and then see it loaded. Want to change dates? Go through it all again. Want to check another campground nearby? Same. It’s just painfully slow to use this app. Oh and the random logouts are awesome. Start looking for a site, after waiting, only to realize the preferences option isn’t there because you were logged out of the app without notice. Navigate the app to where you can log in, log in, manually get back to the campground and spot where you were checking availability, and then wait numerous times while you select the profile, etc. etc. I know first hand that it’s hard to make good software, but this app is not fun to use at all, ever, under any circumstances. Please focus on improving the speed and usability of this app. Speed matters. It’s death by 1000 cuts trying to use this app.

Atalasky , 07/14/2021

Flies and dust

This KOA campground is situated in a place surrounded by cows and other animals. What the owner Helmet calls “country”. Needless to say you can’t stay outside your RV without being swarmed by flies and the fragrance of cow manure. Extremely unsanitary. It has a great mountain view but unfortunately the mountain is made of manure several hundred feet away. Its so a rough dirt road getting into the campground and the roads within the grounds are dirt with a sprinkling of gravel. Few obey the speed limit and the dust is endless. The owner Helmet is well aware of these conditions but he thinks simply calling “country” explains why this isn’t mentioned on his website. I find this very deceitful. I wish I had checked the reviews first because there are several others that mentioned this. When we ask for a refund for the next three nights as we were leaving Helmet said it was KOA‘s policy no refunds without a 48 hour notice he therefore only refunded one night I contacted KOA headquarters they said it was up to the owner to issue a refund. Who’s lying?? Not finished with this yet more to come.
