Description

Looking for help or the latest tips for the game Terraria? This all-in-one solution is exactly what you need: everything for the good game and improving your gaming skills. This app will be useful for both novice and experienced players. No ads! It does not require an internet connection to use so that any relevant information is always available to you. This app is unofficial.

Amazing

This is actually a really good wiki! It’s a lot better than most other ones on the App Store. It’s got Journeys End support, and works offline! Plus, while there are a couple missing items, there are plans to add them in. No ads is really nice as well. I didn’t think I would spend money on a wiki, but this is actually worth it! Thanks for making this!

There are some things that are bad: There is still a load of 1.4 content that needs to be added. I understand this takes time, but I do hope it happens. Some updates (like queen slime and empress of light not being in the boss section). I also think it’s odd that boss summons (like creepers, and ancient visions) are in the boss section. Also if you enter the news section without WiFi, you can’t go back and you have to close the app.
Thanks for reading this huge review!
