smacktalk fan , 07/15/2018

Love this app howeever...

This is such a great game and idea! I play with my brother and it’s so fun for us. I love the challenges. Most of those artists are top notch!
I adore all the colors that you can get. That makes me want to draw more detail as it’s so easy and fun to draw on my ipad pro. I would give this 6 stars (lol) but it runs out of ink before I can finish my drawing. That’s frustrating. It would be great if there was a way to be able to get more ink when you need it. Also, it would be awesome to be able to save games to a cloud or photos or something. I like looking back at the old games.
The only other issue is that sometimes, I’m noticing bombs disappearing when I haven’t used them. Those bombs are important because sometimes, there's no way to guess the word otherwise, especially in the challenges. Some of those words, I never heard of or are somewhat impossible to draw.
It might be fun to be able to log into different difficulty levels as well.

Dollprojectdotcom , 08/16/2020

Full of dark patterns

I wanted to play this game with friends/family. But it is just too shady. What are dark patterns you might ask? They are when the user interface tries to trick you into selecting certain items. And this game does it constantly. Buttons to say ‘no thanks’ are greyed out while the buttons that the app wants you to select are bright green. The game is constantly trying to get you to ‘Use up your hints so you can buy more!’ Or ‘Log into your facebook account through the app.’ You also can’t close the ads without accidentally hitting outside of the tiny x and launching the app store roughly 10% of the time. The constant ads and requests for me to join through facebook ruin the experience. Can you not just make a version someone can PAY for one time and remove ads and stop harassing customers about mining their personal data (facebook)? I just want to draw and guess drawings and this game is seriously complicating that experience. I cannot recommend this app to anyone as it is—especially kids. Please make a clean, pared down version someone can purchase to play with friends and family. Stop with the shadiness. You have a true opportunity to make a great app. Most of the drawing game apps are rubbish. This one is actually a fun, quality drawing app and would be wonderful without all the extra stuff that sabotages the gameplay experience. Tldr: Make a clean version someone can buy. Thanks.

percymcmuffin , 06/29/2018

The ads are utter torture and it’s glitchy across platforms even after a decade

I play this game daily, and I love it as it gives me drawing ideas and I can always find a great group of people to actually draw instead of just write unlike in the beginning. However, I’ve been playing this game for over a decade and played this on many different platforms from lg smartphones, the galaxy, my kindle, and ipad and it still has the same glitch where it freezes trying to load and the only way to get it to work is to restart my entire device. Also, a new glitch, the sensitivity is all wonky and when I start to draw it just creates straight lines and I have to undo my drawing... the ads are torture. Because they show the same ad often for a month, before you can move on to the next drawing unless you want to do a lower level drawing. I even paid for the premium version but it’s not the same. The palette is laid out different and the colors are really hard to find, I feel like the free version is much more developed but just tortures you with ads. I’ve tried inviting friends to bypass ads and I get the freeze glitch. After ten years I would expect developers to fix these dumb glitches and please stop showing the homescape ad before I become homicidal.
