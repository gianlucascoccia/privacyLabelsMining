Description

WDRB is the source for news in Louisville and Kentuckiana. With the largest team of journalists in our area bringing you daily in-depth reporting and breakthrough investigations, WDRB is the leader covering local news, sports, weather, and traffic. No other media company has more weather expertise with five meteorologists scheduled around the clock to bring you updated live or on demand weathercasts each day as well as daily blogs. You can also view our live weather radar and choose from several live streaming weather cams for an instant weather picture. When it comes to trending sports stories and unique sports takes, WDRB is staffed with a fleet of six anchor/reporter/columnists towering over the competition. We offer live streaming and on-demand Sports Page shows with constant perspective and takeaways from the top stories and sports alerts. To keep up on trending topics affecting Louisville, WDRB also offers Point of View editorials twice a week covering the big topics people are talking about in Kentuckiana.
