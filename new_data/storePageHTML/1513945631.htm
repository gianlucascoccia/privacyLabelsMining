Description

Earn money purchasing and delivering orders from grocery stores, specialty stores and more.

Be in control of your own time
You decide when and how long you want to be active on the Shopper app. Just sign in, wait for your next order and start shopping.

Commissions and bonuses
You can earn even more money with multipliers in areas with high demand as well as earn special bonus commission. All commissions are paid weekly.

Less stress on your car
You'll spend the majority of your day at the store thus limiting the wear and tear on your car.

Feel secure
Orders can only be worked on when stores are open. No 3am deliveries necessary.

Free Delivery For You
We also want you to enjoy your personal time. As a Shopper, you’ll receive one free delivery per month.

Terrible onboarding experience.

Onboarding experience was really poor for me. I signed up, got confirmation that I would receive bags, cards, etc and waited about a month. I finally reached out to support and told them I’d not received anything. They said they would send me the stuff and a few days later I got two shoppers cards, no bags etc. By the time I got the cards, the $125 bonus promo for my first 5 orders was expired. I have yet to use the app because I missed the promo (which was a great incentive to try it) and it’s unclear how the app even works. The last time I logged in, they assigned an order to me super far away and I had to abandon it because I didn’t know that it was going to assign orders as soon as I logged in and I wasn’t prepared to start working that day. I don’t have an opinion on how good or bad Cornershop is but the onboarding and lack of clarity in the app and around how it works and receiving materials has left me a bit soured. Instacart always has orders in my area and I get to choose them at places that aren’t 10 miles away. I’d love to try Cornershop but the current experience is not the way.
