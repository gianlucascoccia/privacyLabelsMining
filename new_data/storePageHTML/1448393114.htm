Description

Color My Life® is a cataloging app designed exclusively for paper crafters. Quickly and easily organize and catalog your paper crafting supplies. Spend more time designing and creating, and less time searching through disorganized inventory. Stop buying the same item twice (or more)! Add items to your wishlist for future purchases. Add a storage location so you know exactly where to find your supplies when you need them. We’re here to help you get organized!

Color My Life has an intuitive user-centric design and features these capabilities:
• Barcode Catalog: Quickly add an item by scanning a barcode or searching the barcode catalog. Color My Life automatically populates the item’s image and attributes. We are continually adding to the barcode catalog with new manufacturers and product releases.
• Duplicate warning: Adding an item with a duplicate barcode will generate a warning.
• Smart Color Tagging: Automatically create color tags based on the item’s image.
• Storage Location: Add a storage location, so you know exactly where to find your supplies when you need them.
• Searching and Filtering: Find your items by searching for both word and color tags. Use the filtering tool to select a Manufacturer or Category.
• Create sets: Items that are used together, like a collection or coordinating stamps and dies, can be combined into sets, so they are easy to find even if they are stored separately.
• Project Organization: Much like a recipe, Color My Life brings supplies and inspiration together as projects.

There is a 30-day free trial so you can get to know the app before subscribing. After the trial, you have access to all your content, but you need a subscription to continue to add new items. We offer monthly ($1.49 USD) and yearly ($14.99 USD) subscriptions.

By subscribing, you will have access to new additions in our barcode catalog, syncing your content to multiple iOS devices, online backups, as well as frequent app updates with new functionality. Try Color My Life today!

The subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Subscription and auto-renewal may be managed by going to Account Settings after purchase.

VisuMetal , 05/18/2021

Pro Artist’s Review - Self Limiting but Good

The craft world can sometimes be self limiting. But the app is a great start. I gave this app a try to see how flexible it might be for my design business because there isn’t really a proper app like this for artists in general. That might not be the intended purpose but it’s important for crafters too. I currently have to build my inventory in Airtable like many crafters might as well. And even that could be better and more accessible since Most apps are geared towards wealthy businesses. There are a lot of cool features in Color My Life like all the colors already built in and automatic palettes. Tags. Categories. Photos. Projects. But what really makes a craft? The Color My Life website says this app is made “exclusively for paper crafters”. I think that’s a massive mistake. The target audience for this app it too narrow. It might work well for pre-loading information like barcodes but it’s very limited in flexibility when you can’t alter categories or make a checklist for your projects. Especially with a paid service. But this app has a lot of potential as one of the only ones of its kind. It’s well designed and easy to use. It has a great framework for being more general. But the current state of it means crafters and artists alike must check the category options before committing to the use of this app.

Much like closet organizing apps like Stylebook, some fees are warranted. Most people are willing to pay a little. This app in particular is very affordable and low cost which is awesome. But, ultimately, there needs to be a consideration for the available features and different types of people who might use the app so it can be fitted to the user instead of just 1 purpose.

I see that notes and projects are in development but I’d really like to see a category editing option to really open this up. I’m considering a subscription but I’m really not sure yet.

Lots of Potential

I have this app on my iPad, and granted, there’s going to be some time before this is where it needs to be for a native tablet style feel. Regardless, I still prefer it to Evernote. With that said, I’m hoping that CML can partner with even more stamp, die, and paper manufacturers to add more barcodes and images to make this a truly seamless way to input database items related to crafting. I hope you guys have started reaching out to more companies to demo your product and get more support. I know many have had issues with the product not working without a small monthly paid subscription (with a free trial prior to the subscription onset). I have no problem subscribing for the product to work if it’s within my budget; small companies deserve an opportunity to make some money to develop, and having worked for a startup company, I am always happy to help as a consumer. I know all of the difficulties that go into new development, and how important even a small bootstrap income can be.
In any case, good luck, guys. Thanks for testing the waters with this, and I’m happy to provide any feedback you may require.
