Description

Master "Mined" Wholesale Diamond Prices and Markets. SAVE TIME! Compare quickly Diamonds by Shape+Size+Quality+Budget! Free Download w/Demo. Subscribe to Up-to-Date Data!

AN ESSENTIAL TOOL FOR GEMOLOGISTS, TRADERS, INVESTORS AND APPRAISERS OF NATURAL "MINED" DIAMONDS.

◆TIME SAVER: Quickly & conveniently compares in seconds between diamonds and over 2 million permutations of wholesale value!

DiamondMaster's "Bright List" from TROY DIAMOND REPORT® developed by Wayne D. Prentice, Graduate Gemologist and former Resident Gemology Instructor at the Gemological Institute of America (GIA), is the next generation global wholesale pricing guide that provides what Rapaport is missing!

Follow Wholesale Diamond Prices in 13 Mediums of Exchange and by 7C's!! The traditional 4C's: Cut Shape, Carat, Color, Clarity, plus "Cut Grade", "Cost" and "Change in Value" over last year and 5 years!! This App calculates % changes-in-value in your selected currency, Appreciation (or depreciation), which allows you to determine which "classifications" are maintaining best value in your local market. Quickly determine and compare each diamond's value in multiple FIAT currencies and exchange for Troy ounces of gold - the universal unit of utility!

◆ 10 Cut Shapes - diamond values on Round Brilliants and EACH "Fancy Shape" - Pear, Emerald Cut, Princess, Marquise, Oval, Radiant, Cushion, Trilliant & Heart vs Rapaport's 2!

◆ 46 Carat Categories - from 1 point to 10 carats vs Rapaport's 18!

◆ 10 Color Grades - D to M (GIA).

◆ 10 Clarity Grades - FL to I-3 (GIA).

◆ 13 Cost References: 11 Currencies - U.S. Dollar (USD), Euro (EUR), Swiss Franc (CHF), Russian Ruble (RUB), Canadian Dollar (CAD), British Pound (GBP), Japanese Yen (JPY), Brazilian Real (BRL), Australian Dollar (AUD), Chinese Yuan/Renminbi (CYN), Indian Rupee (INR), plus values in Exchange to Gold's Annual Moving Average stated in Troy Ounces (XAU) and a "Basket of Currencies Unit" - IMF's Special Drawing Rights (SDR). When diamond wholesale values change relative to the "Basket or Gold" they are truly changing!

◆ 4 GRADING SYSTEMS SIMULTANEOUSLY DISPLAYED: the Gemological Institute of America (GIA), International Diamond Council (IDC), American Gem Society (AGS), and the Russian Grading System (TU). The only diamond pricing App to display all 4 international standards!

◆ Dimensional Guidelines on every Shape & Carat Weight - very important when comparing cuts and verifying weight meets dimension guidelines!

◆ Per Carat and Total Diamond Value

◆ Displays Every Diamond's Percentage Change-In-Value over 1 and 5 Years!

◆ Determine which Diamond Classifications in what Markets Gained or Lost Value at the Highest Rates!

◆ Global Price Adjuster: Reset at any time the Wholesale "Bright List" reference to your personal preference or back to Bright List. Wholesalers can display at their desired discount, retailers at their desired premium and investors according to their needs whether selling, or buying for investment.

◆ Educational-Gemology pages on every cut shape

◆ Requires iOS 11.0 or later
