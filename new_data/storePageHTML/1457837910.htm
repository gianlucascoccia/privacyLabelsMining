Why use Cryptocurrency Sentiment Analysis for Trading?
Using sentiment analysis to estimate the signs of bullishness or bearishness helps indicate entry and/or exit positions with regards to crypto trading. The strength of the sentiment indicator tells you whether to enter a crypto position within your portfolio.

Application of Sentiment Analysis in Crypto TradingIn crypto trading so much of the change in price is dictated by emotions. Use sentiment analysis to:
- Monitor crowd psychology at an extraordinary scale.
- Classify the emotional state of the market.
- Anticipate price movement of assets.
- Build an investment portfolio strategy.
- Improve investment tactics.
- Create buy and trade signals.
- Maximise profit margins.
- Take advantage of arbitrage.
