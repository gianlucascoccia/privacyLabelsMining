Description

Fraction Calculator Plus - the best and easiest solution for fractions in everyday life. Calculate fractions: add, subtract, divide and multiply. Convert fractions to decimals easily.

- Would you like time to check your children's maths exercises? Now that's no longer a problem because you can review fractions in seconds.
- Do you need to adjust recipe quantities for a longer guest list? It will calculate the amounts in cups and teaspoons for you.
- Are you working on a craft or construction project with dimensions in inches? Forget about double or triple calculations on paper. Use fraction for correct calculations.

It's an easy-to-use fraction calculator with a large display and big buttons.

Features:

- The digits and buttons are clear and big, so they can be read at a glance from a distance.
- Convenient triple keyboard display enables quick typing. You only need to type three times to enter “three and three quarters”!
- Each fraction is automatically scaled down to its simplest form to make it easier for you.
- NEW! Each result is also displayed as a decimal number. That makes the conversion a breeze.
- Adding, subtracting, multiplying and dividing fractions has never been easier.

With Fraction Calculator Plus, your mobile phone or tablet is by your side in everyday life.

Great App, needs Metric translations

I do woodworking and use this app whenever I need to check myself (which is quite often). I like that tapping on the result gives the inch/fraction in 10,000ths of in inch, this helps when referring to many machining calculations (parts) but I still have to open a separate app to translate the .0000s of an inch into metric (which I do A LOT and I believe others are doing, too, as the world gets smaller). I'm giving it a 5 star rating based on my hope that the devs include a metric translation in addition to the thousands translation. Also, I love the simplicity and look of it. Good job.

Invaluable

The most useful support app I have for accurate measurements. Clearly a lot of thought went into designing this tool. The interface is designed the way a non-mathematician like myself thinks when trying to quickly convert decimals to fractions and vice versa.
