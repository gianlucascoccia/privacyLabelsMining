Description

The BEST Dance Cardio and Toning workout with celebrity fitness trainer, Amanda Kloots. Amanda Kloots created the AK! Rope class, the first ever jump rope class, toning your whole body using a jump rope. She went on to create AK! Dance, AK! Body, AK! Mama & Me and AK! The Two Minute Workout.

Amanda brings her infectious and inspirational attitude to everything that she does. Now you get to bring Amanda's positive energy into your home or on the go with the same in-studio workout experience. Start your experience creating your own workout from dance cardio, rope toning, to a quick arm and leg routine.

Inside this app you will find all of Amanda's workout classes including pre recorded LIVE classes, How To videos, and much more. Do you only have 10 minutes? Perfect, you can join Amanda for a 10 minute jump. You can pick your favorite videos add them to your playlist and have your own custom AK! Workout.

Amanda gives instruction and motivation to keep you going through the whole workout. A fitness full library of Amanda's workouts ranging from 5 minutes to 50 minutes that will give you options to select the right exercise for you anytime, anywhere. Each workout is targeted to specific body parts, class lengths, and NEW videos that will be updated and added every week!

Stream videos from anywhere in the world. Access all workouts on your phone, computer and smart TV. Gift the AK! Subscription to a friend and family member. No internet? No problem. Ability to download your favorite workouts for offline viewing .

Minimal equipment required, if you are ready to jump along with Amanda in her AK! Rope classes head over to amandakloots.com to purchase your own AK! Jump Rope. The Amanda Kloots Fitness app syncs with your devices to give you convenient access to your AK! workouts wherever you are.

Join the AK! Fitness community today and leave every workout sweaty and with a SMILE on your face!

willyjeen , 09/23/2021

If you’re here for the dancing … read this.

I’ve had this app for at least 3 months now and I primarily joined because of the dance routines. They are fun and really get the heart rate up. Amanda is a great teacher and I love her energy. However the quality of the videos, specifically the sound has really gone downhill. As a dancer, I simply can not handle taking a dance class where the steps don’t match the music. It ruins the entire experience. I reached out to the team last month when they posted a dance workout so mismatched they ended up taking it down. Today I took the newest dance routine and it was off. Again. With competitors like iFitness & Peloton producing quality content for less than this subscription I decided to cancel today. I’ll keep checking back in to see if the quality has improved but sadly, it’s goodbye for now.
