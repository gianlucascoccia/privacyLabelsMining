Description

Welcome to the next evolution of our app – introducing feel better by deliciously ella, helping you discover delicious ways to feel better every day.

We are passionate about making health & wellbeing as simple and accessible as possible. Using our holistic, science backed approach we’re here to help you with the four pillars of health: plant-based recipes, movement classes, mindfulness and meditation, and sleep. Updated weekly, feel better, will help you discover new & delicious ways to feel better every day, no matter where you are on your wellness journey.

Key features include:
- Over 700 delicious, plant-based recipes, from vibrant salads to nourishing pastas, warming curries and gooey brownies. Plus helpful tools such as a meal planner and shopping list.
- Over 250 movement classes with some of the world’s best instructors across yoga, pilates, barre, cardio, strength and stretching. We have something for every ability, including pre and post-natal, with classes ranging between 10-60 minutes.
- Mindfulness section to help you feel more calm and in control, with guided meditations, breathwork, sound baths, reflexology and body massage, and much more.
- Tools to improve your sleep and help you drift more restfully, from sleep sounds, to relaxing yoga & stretches and bedtime meditations
- Wellness tracker and planner across our pillars of health, helping you stay on track to reach your wellness goals. My Tracker is also fully integrated with Apple Health.
- New recipes, classes and articles added every week to keep you constantly motivated to discover new ways to feel better
- Access the app on up to 3 devices, including mobile tablet and desktop on our new all-access subscription plans
