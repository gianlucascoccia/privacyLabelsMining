Description

Vooks is a streaming library of read-aloud animated storybooks. Kid-safe, no ads!

Trusted by families & educators, enjoyed by millions of children around the world.

Do you have a child in Preschool - 3rd grade? Inspire a love of reading in them with Vooks. Vooks brings storybooks to life, expands vocabulary, promotes language development, and supports comprehension. Entertaining while educational, Vooks empowers families to read the recommended 20 minutes a day..

Vooks inspires and motivates young readers with intentional read-along pacing and highlighting to create a visual connection with the text. The stories teach valuable life lessons that promote social-emotional development to provide readers the tools to become leaders.

With hundreds of books and new books added weekly, you’ll always have access to the best in children’s storybooks.

Safety - Every child’s privacy and safety is important to us. FERPA and COPPA compliant.

Entertaining and educational!

Vooks is a unique and compelling streaming storybook site that brings books to life for preschool and elementary school aged kids. The stories aren’t only read aloud, but are also animated like a 10-minute (or so) film. The stories are narrated with various fun voices and accompanying music, keeping young readers and listeners attention.

And it’s easy to use! Just choose a storybook, click on play, and watch and listen. The text appears on each page and is highlighted as the narrator reads each word, encouraging the child to read along.

There are numerous types and themes of books, such as be brave, be creative, biographies, and more. So, the streaming storybooks aren’t only entertaining, but informative and character-building too. There are also lesson plans and other resources for extended learning.

This animated storybook concept is so fun. I wish I had Vooks when I was a child. I’m thrilled with my new subscription and look forward to reading and listening to the variety of animated stories with my grandson.
