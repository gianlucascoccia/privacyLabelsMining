forestyo , 06/03/2021

Live stream buffering!

We use this camera as our baby monitor. Was working GREAT at first. I keep the app running all night long so I can see and hear our daughter. At first I started to loose sound. I logged in on a different device and there was still sound. So all I had to do was unplug and plug back in, the camera itself. Now, we are 4 months in and there is VERY bad buffering on the live stream. Nothing is different than when we first started using it. It will send the live stream perfect for maybe an hour or so, then it starts to buffer very bad, to the point all feedback just stops. I have to completely shut down the app and restart it to get it playing again. But it’s just a cycle of buffering and restarting the app. I’m not sure if this is a camera issue or an app issue, but it does this on all of our devices we have tried it on. I’m not thrilled, and I certainly don’t feel 100% safe with our baby not having an eye on her like I should.

Developer Response ,

Dear Victure User, Would you contact us on app.support@govicture.com? We will check with you. Victure Team

kjb9870 , 07/02/2021

Great camera, terrible app

We were using this camera for our baby monitor. It was located close to our modem and router so we excepted no problems. The camera and app worked well for the first month or so, then I would wake up to loud baby cries, but heard nothing from my phone. When I’d look at my app (that I kept running all night), there would be a black screen with a buffer wheel. No video or sound until I closed the app and reopened it. Sometimes even doing that wouldn’t work. I would have to completely close the app and restart it. I reached out customer support who responded that they debugged the app and to try it again. It still had the same problems. I was then told to make sure I had the updated version. I did. Still the same problems. The camera is great. The app is garbage. We don’t use this any longer. I bought a new camera system for our baby’s room.
