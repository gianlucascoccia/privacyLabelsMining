Description

Warm Me Up! is the most comprehensive and varied vocal warm up tool available to today's singer.

Tailored to your voice type, there are 60 exercises designed to guide you through a warm up that will get your voice into tip-top condition and ready for your practice session, rehearsal or performance.

Warm Me Up! will create a unique warm up for you. It can generate a warm up based on how much time you have available or you can browse the individual tracks and create your own playlist. The exercises are recorded in high quality audio: a lush baby grand accompanies you through each exercise and a professional singer guides you through the first two repeats of each.

The Warm Me Up! team (Luke Hunter, Vicky Jacobs and David Young) are esteemed voice coaches and musical directors and have warmed up professional singers in Australia, the USA, New Zealand and England. Between them they have warmed up the casts of many mega musicals including Jersey Boys, Wicked, The Book of Mormon, Frozen, Come From Away and Moulin Rouge.

Warm Me Up! is specifically designed to be used by both the amateur and professional singer and can be used to warm up a soloist or larger group. It's a fantastic resource for singing teachers and choir directors who can warm up their ensemble away from the keyboard.

In the studio, backstage at the concert, in the corridors or in the car on the way to the audition, now you can warm up professionally... anywhere.

What’s New

Version 1.75.2

Warm Me Up has a brand new look and is easier to use! All your favourite warm ups are still here but with loads of new features. Now you can:

And, as always, we don’t track, record or pass on any information about you or the way you use Warm Me Up. We don’t even ask you for a subscription. We just want to get you singing and loving it!

Great warmups, but app could use some extra features

I use this daily to warm up. There are some really great warm-ups in this app, and you can chose your voicing (soprano, alto, tenor, or bass; also called high-female/low-female, etc.). However, at the base of the app, it’s just static recordings and a random playlist generator based on how long you want to warm up.

I’d love to see the ability to hear the sample from a real voice, but then actually chose your range and have a digital piano adjust (or even record live ones, but have more than 4 range options). I’d also like the ability to mark a warmup as a favorite and have the app go to those ahead of others. There are a couple warmups that just don’t work for me... but it’s literally 2 of them. But it would also be nice to have an “exclude from warmup” option.

I know I can make custom playlists, and add all my favorites to a specific list, or save one that was generated for me... but it’s not quite the same.

In the end, I got this app to help me warm up and it achieves that... and a lot less expensively than buying individual warm-up tracks on iTunes. So, definitely check this app out. I just hope the creators check out this review for some constructive criticism.

This app is AWESOME! Just if there could be a few more features

I use it with my vocal students and also with choir for warm ups! The only thing I wish for is if it has a way to be able to slow down or speed up the tempo. Sometimes the warm ups are too fast for people (particularly vocal children students) making it difficult to learn. If there was a way to change the tempo, making it slower. and then gradually making it faster and faster, that would be great.
Also, it would be great to change the keys and have more options for types of voices. There are already 4 types of voices you can choose, but also if it had exercises for specific sections (i.e. Alto, Soprano, Tenor, Bass, etc) Thanks!
