What does The Wheel do?

-The wheel acts as a Drop Location Picker to help mix up your drops and make Fortnite a bit less repetitive.

How do I use The Wheel?

1). Spin the Wheel when you or your squad enters the Battlebus.
2). Wherever the The Wheel lands is now your new target drop location.
