Description

Help reunite more lost pets with their families!

With the PawBoost app you can view, share, and report lost & found pets. You can also choose to get notified when a local pet is lost or found near you, and contact the owner or finder with helpful information. :)

Could use improvement.

Not too sure how I feel about the app. It’s very confusing and unclear if you are posting a found lost dog or that the dog was found and reunited. Also all interactions are done through email in a non conventional manner instead of interacting and receiving messages through the app itself which is the whole point and purpose of installing any interactive app.

And if you want to make a change to your post you can’t. The post you posted is shown to you as if you are a 3rd person seeing the post from a random user and because of that you can’t even adjust or change the status of your own post. It says message or call finder when I’m the finder.

Apparently you have to go through the email and click at the bottom small print that says change status if this pet has been reunited instead of clicking your own post in edit mode to change the status through the app. When you go under your account the only thing you find is for you to sign out. If I go under account in any app I expect to have access to edit my post, access messages/notifications and to have clear understanding of my options for what I’m posting or have already posted. As a user I am not very happy which is why I installed different apps to help me with lost and found post. I do however appreciate the thought behind pawboost. Just wish it was more user friendly and that it made more sense.

AHHHHHHHHAHHHHHHH , 02/28/2021

A useful little tool for any pet parents to have

A few days ago my blue pit and Brazilian mastiff made their great escape and hopped the fence while I was away. I hoped of face book at posted to every lost and found animal page there was for my area and everyone kept mentioning PawBoost. So I, being desperate to find my babies any way I could made a missing post on there and called ever animal shelter and the city animal control to give me a call if they had seen the boys. And within an hour of posting someone directed me to the “find my Fido” page on face book and there my boys were....at the next city over animal shelter. Over all a pretty helpful and friendly community and a nice little app to have. My only complaint is when you’re attempting to edit your post you made about an animal you can’t access it in the app so it can be a bit of a hassle...but over all 4/5 stars. If your a pet parent of any kind or like to help strays fine their way home. I would definitely have this app on hand.
