** Best Meditation App 2019 ** – Healthline

Unlock your fullest potential with the all new Omvana.
More than just 'another meditation app', Omvana is made for those who want to achieve more in life, and will help you elevate yourself to new levels of performance in multiple areas. Made for individuals who want to improve performance, elevate productivity, relieve anxiety, reduce stress, boost focus, get better sleep and much more.

What can you expect from Omvana app?
• Meditation tracks perfect for all levels, including beginners
• Personalized recommendations for your goals
• Customize your meditation with a background sound mixer
• Seamless navigation and saved playbacks

Omvana's library of meditation tracks covers key topics such as:
• Mastery of Performance
• Better Sleep
• Focus and Productivity
• Clarity of Vision
• Confidence and Self-Esteem
• Fitness and Health
• Happiness and Joy

Join thousands of people who’re living healthier and happier lives with Omvana to:
• Unlock your full potential and performance with guided meditation tracks and more.
• Transcend into higher levels of consciousness and practice meditation in a whole new experience.
• Achieve your goals through powerful guided visualizations from some of the world’s best personal growth teachers including Marisa Peer, Lisa Nichols, Bob Proctor, Michael Beckwith, Vishen Lakhiani, and many more.
• Create your own meditation track by mixing any two of your favorite tracks among 1000s of high-quality ambient sounds, binaural beats, and narrated meditation.
• Take control of your wellbeing and transform your life today.

Omvana saves your meditation sessions to your Apple Health app.
