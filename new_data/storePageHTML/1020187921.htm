Ok don’t get me wrong, this app is great. I’ve made tons of friends, and I really enjoy coloring and talking with others. However, there are a few issues. First of all, it will occasionally delete your shading which is frustrating when you’ve spent a while on it. There is also the issue of people posting inappropriate content. This may be an adult app, but that’s only for the complex pictures. It’s age range is actually 4+ and there are lots of kids on there. You also have to buy a subscription to access any pictures you missed or couldn’t get because you downloaded the game after they were available. Then there’s the issue of only three imports. A lot of times, many of us want to post more of our own art, especially when the pictures provided aren’t that great. There also should be an option to block users, especially if they harass you or your friends. You also are only able to view a portion of your notifications before they disappear, which is really frustrating when you miss someone replying to your comment, or miss when someone comments on your post. These issues aside, I really am grateful to have this game, and am grateful for the friends I have made🖤
