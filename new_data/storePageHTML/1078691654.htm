Description

Rack Room Shoes offers the latest, greatest shoe styles of the brands your family loves, plus an assortment of labels you won’t find with other retailers. Shop us for an incredible selection of athletic, comfort, dress, and casual shoe styles for men, women and kids.

Download the Rack Room Shoes Mobile App to:
• Get special App-only offers.
• Browse and buy shoes and accessories from our full catalog.
• Enroll or sign into Rewards to track your earning progress, plus see and redeem available Rewards.
• Receive alerts about recent orders and Rewards expiration.
• See your complete purchase history.
• Use the app as a digital receipt for in-store returns.
• Track shipping of recent orders.
• Find your nearest Rack Room Shoes location, and get directions in an instant.

-PLUS-

• Jump the customer service line with priority access to LIVE CHAT with Customer Care reps.

Much more coming soon!
