Description

Don't set goals, build habits.

Do you remember the last time you wanted to achieve something? You may have started strong, but after some time, maybe you lost motivation. Here is our tip – instead of setting goals, start building your daily routines, and improving your regular habits. Small and easy changes will compound into remarkable results. With our app, you will get better every day and achieve the kind of success that lasts.

Build good habits, break bad ones.
Want to cut down on alcohol, improve your healthy habits, or be more productive? Possibilities are unlimited. Get inspiration from our pre-defined templates or add your custom habit.

Achieve your daily, weekly, and monthly goals.
Using a simple overview, you can easily manage all your goals and see your achievements. Have you overachieved your goal? You can track that too.

Track your progress.
Browse history, check out your stats and see how you've improved over time.
Didn't have time to track a previous day? No problem, you can easily update any record in the past.

Features:
- Build good habits, break bad ones
- Daily, weekly, and monthly goals
- Track even overachieved goals
- History and statistics
- Widgets
- Reminders
- Notes
- Custom habit icons
- Apple Health support (requires iPhone or Apple Watch)
- Works on iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch
- iCloud backup & sync support
- Dark Mode support

flissyquokka17 , 03/11/2021

Best habit tracker app out there!

Believe me, I've tried a LOT of habit tracker apps, and this is really the best one I've found! I love the elegant yet efficient and simplistic design, as well as the widgets. I also really like how you can build or break habits, schedule weekly or monthly habits, and create custom units—everything that's been lacking from the other habit trakers I've tried!

Only a few minor suggestions:
1. I would appreciate it if the devs could add an option for habits to "repeat every x days" or something like that. I like having weekly and monthly options, but sometimes I want to do things every 12 days, for example, and it's not as precise to say 2 times per month.
2. Compared to other habit trackers that offer pretty much the same features, the premium yearly subscription is a good deal, but the lifetime upgrade is quite pricy. Since I am not really a fan of subscription models (and I noticed that the devs added the one time purchase option quite recently), I would willingly pay for the lifetime upgrade if it was slightly less expensive than $27.

Overall, I highly recommend this app! Kudos to the devs!!!!

If you'd have any other feedback or ideas for improvement, please let us know anytime at feedback@awesome-habits.com
