Description

Simple. Affordable. Weight Loss.

Health starts with what you eat, and ProTracker makes tracking your foods easier than ever.

Did you know that keeping a food diary is one of the most effective ways to manage your weight? Download the ProTracker app to easily track Calories, Food Scores, stay focused and achieve your goals!

Use ProTracker to:
• Log the foods you eat every day.
• Integrate with Fitbit, Apple Watch, Health app, and more!
• Create your own curated library of foods, meals and recipes.
• Scan barcodes or search our database to quickly track foods you’ve eaten.
• Use Quick Add to directly track Calories, Food Scores, and macros to your diary.
• Track your macros and micros, and set nutrient goals.
• Track your weight loss or gains.
• Group ingredients into meals or recipes and easily log one or more servings.
• Create your own custom reminders to help you remember to track.
• Visually compare your nutrient intake against your goals.
• Works for all Food Score based systems or simply choose to use Calories
• Sync your data securely across multiple devices.
• Share your premium subscription with up to five other family members with Family Sharing!

ProTracker is simple to use, highly customizable, and will quickly become your hub for tracking the foods you eat everyday.

Happy tracking!
