Description

1byone Health App is a smart app for recording health data. It assists users to better manage and take care of individuals’ health. The app is compatible with a series of 1byone health products including bluetooth body fat scales etc. It helps to measure and record, which can be used by multiple users.1byone Health can also sync health data with Apple Health and Fitbit.

[Disclaimer]
This application is only for fitness monitoring reference, and it cannot be taken as medical suggestion. For diagnosis and treatment, please consult doctors or other medical staffs for advice.
The TENS product（PL-029K16B） is only sold in the United States, not allowed to be sold in other regions.

stateslab appleid , 01/25/2019

Buggy display, layout broke after iOS update

After the iOS 12.1.3 update on my iPhone 10 XS max, the screen layout of this app has gotten out of kilter. It’s no longer possible to see all of the icons in the review/plot mode. From memory I can still use it. A new user, however, would find it very confusing.

Compared to the previous version which was kind of amateurish, this app has a “slicker” look. The plotting features have been improved making it easier to view a week, month or year timescale. Unfortunately, less data is available for plotting, and less data is reported to Apple Health. It used to be possible to plot bone mass, visceral fat and body water. None of those are now accessible in the plotting window. Also lean body mass is no longer reported to Apple Health.

I have had no problems with the basic functionality of setting up this app and pairing it with my scale.
