zilnessej , 04/07/2021

Great Selection!

There is an amazing selection of romance audiobooks! I’m happy to say that both traditionally published and indie published books are available. The price is unbeatable and I can’t wait to explore this app more.

A few things that could use improvement:

1. More speed selections. My usual starting speed for audiobooks is between 1.5x and 1.8x speed on other apps. The max on AnyPlay is 1.75x which is one of the lowest speeds I use. Also, a clearer speed icon for newbies to audiobooks. It currently looks like an old fashioned dial knob.

2. Better categories for browsing. Romance by itself is a huge genre and is divided into many sub genres. There isn’t a great way to discover new authors/books on the iOS app. You basically have to search authors by name to see if any of their audiobooks are available. I’d love to see just how many romance titles are available in total. Also, a filter of some sort to browse ALL romance titles and their sub genres (historical, contemporary, paranormal, fantasy, sci-fi, new adult, sports romance, dark romance, etc) is a must!

3. Time left update. When increasing the speed of the audio, the “time left in book” remains the same. I like to see how long it will take me to finish a book. It’s hard to tell when you’re listening on 1.75x speed and it still says 9 hours left.

Developer Response ,

Thanks for the kind review and we're always listening to feedback. Stay tuned for new updates and features. Happy listening!
