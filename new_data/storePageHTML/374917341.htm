Description

Welcome to the official GAY TIMES app, where GAYTIMES+ members can enjoy immediate access to GAY TIMES Magazine.

GAYTIMES+ is the new LGBTQ+ membership from GAY TIMES, offering unique benefits to those who help support queer storytelling.

The GAY TIMES app gives you access to not only the latest issue of GAY TIMES Magazine, but the back-catalogue, with much of the GAY TIMES archives available from early 2021 right in the app.

The app user experience includes a text-to-speech feature, explore section to preview articles before you buy them, and access to your membership account and curated content.

Through exclusive interviews and features with individuals from the world of music, fashion, film, TV, the arts, and community-led campaigns, our exploration of queer culture and the power it possesses to instigate real change continues to chronicle the long-fought journey towards true liberation.

GAYTIMES+ members should 'sing-up' to app access using their GAYTIMES+ email and password. Non-members can enjoy individual issues and read the latest culture, community and entertainment.

Visit support.gaytimes.co.uk for help

Subscription plans for non-members are available within the app. When purchasing through iTunes your account will be charged at confirmation of purchase.
Your subscription will auto-renew unless you turn this off 24 hours before the subscription runs out. You can turn off auto-renewal at any time from your iTunes account settings.
You can manage your subscription through your account settings after purchase on your device.
No cancellation of the current subscription is allowed during active subscription period.

I give GT 5stars and the iPad app 3!

I rediscovered GT on here, and what a refreshing experience to a really fresh and vibrant magazine that covers the silly, serious and salacious life of gay men, all with a wink of the eye and and wonderful sarcasm when needed. Since then loss of the Advocate in he US, this this sort of publication has been sorely absent in my life.

On the other hand, my New York Times all digital subscription, that costs as much per month as does my GT. Sub on here, is constantly forgetting I am paying through the nose for rapidly devolving news service and trying to get me to buy a new sub every time I turn on my iPad or change to my cell or one of my desktops. And the App just keeps charging away with duplicate Subscriptions for a sub that is supposed to cover all my digital devices but also seems to have advanced Alzheimer's disease.

Graydon's iLibrary , 12/08/2017

New paper subscription style

Hello, I have been a GayTimes paper magazine subscriber from 2008 until this year. I don't know what's gone wrong but the GayTimes I knew had more than what I received today which is a magazine which looks like it's trying to play catch-up and more than 75% of the entire magazine was of guys in clothing and not what I love to see. Even the front cover of the magazine has changed. The front of the magazine has no info what will be inside the magazine and the front page and back page are much thicker paper than what I've gotten for years. I like boy George and have no issues with him but if this magazine is a taste of what's to come in the new management, I may have to stop subscribing as if I want to see fashion and what's the newest type of clothing I can read "departures" magazine or Vogue. I know someone was fired for nasty tweets or something but I have literally never been as shocked as I was today when I opened the packing of the magazine and saw the font style changed and no info of what's in the magazine such as teases to get ones attention to read what's in the magazine. I look back to Novembers magazine and it has the image of "call me by your name" but also has things like "LOVE WILL TEAR OZ APART-The toxic marriage vote that's changing Australia forever" I just want to know that the next issue of Januarys GayTimes will have more than what I've received today for the December issue.
