Description

So you've built your first house in Minecraft... now what?
Breathe some life into your world and fill those empty rooms with the ultimate catalogue of Minecraft furniture items.

Content Notification:
-This app does not contain mod downloads or mod files for Minecraft.
-This app does not contain videos of each furniture item. Each furniture item is illustrated with detailed photos. Tap the zoom button to zoom in to see the finer details.
-It's up to you to recreate each piece of furniture that you want in your Minecraft world.

-Sections include Furniture, Rooms, Houses, Basics, Miscellaneous and Most Creative.
-My Materials: keep track of all the materials you need to build furniture in your world.
-Universal app designed for both iPhone and iPad.
-Upgrade to Furniture for Minecraft + to instantly unlock premium content and future updates, the Houses section and materials for premium items.
