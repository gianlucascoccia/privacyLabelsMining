NovaSiva11037 , 06/27/2020

One of the most fun mobile games I’ve seen yet

I see potential in this game, even though it is already developed with amazing L2D and animations. The gameplay is decent for a mobile game, turn-based attacks with bits of RNG rolled in here and there. I’m surprised by the amount of effort put into the art, with a lot of characters being animated. Are there issues with the game? Not any major ones that I know of other than the fact that you know, gacha. It gives you a choice of SS rank right at the beginning, so you already know it’s F2P friendly. You can spend money to get more in-game stuff, but it’s really optional and gives major benefits for a small price. Now, when it comes to the attacking animations, there are some really underwhelming attacks that have poor animation, but it’s decent for the most part. Other than that, gorgeous art and animations outside of combat.

Ezekiel IV , 06/22/2020

Amazing!!!

First off I would like to say I really appreciate the amount of fairness and quality that is in this game. This game has simple combat mechanics that doesn’t complicate the formula for competitive pvp. What propels combat and makes it challenging and refreshing is the diverse lovable characters of the game. I can’t help but compare this to Epic Seven and to me I would say this is better because of the f2p aspect and the gacha system. My only negative criticism would be that getting resources for building characters is a pain and repeating hard mode is limited to 10 times a day which I’m not okay with. If I wasn’t limited to farming in hard mode I could have more resources to build and try new characters and explore more characters instead of just sticking to the meta. Again I appreciate all the love that went into this game and I hope to continue contributing to this game...THANK YOU!
