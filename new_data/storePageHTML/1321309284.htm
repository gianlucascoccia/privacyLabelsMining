Description

Darkness Rises is a revolutionary Action RPG that blends gorgeous graphics, innovative gameplay, and intense boss battles, all within the palm of your hands.

Darkness spreads across the land, bringing with it a horde of ferocious demons ready to breach our gates. An epic fantasy RPG awaits - the path will be grueling, but you must persevere and descend into hell itself to destroy this evil before it ravages our world.

Legendary heroes are at your beck and call, from the earth-shattering Berserker to the magic-wielding Wizard. Character customization lets you choose from a number of classes that fits your playstyle and design your character as you see fit.
Hack and slash through fearsome monsters with a flurry of powerful skills. Enter the PvP arena and unleash your might against other players. This Epic roleplaying games pits you against demons and worse– can you rise to the challenge?

Conquer the darkness before it conquers you. Download now.

Customize not Working (UPDATED)

Okay, so first of all I play on an iPhone 8+ (in case it’s different for anybody else). I just finished the tutorial with a warrior class name LordTharle. It didn’t give me an option to customize my character, so I went looking for the option and found the “Beauty” tab. I selected it, thinking it was probably the place to customize your character. When I opened it said that I had to pay 1K gems just to customize it. Looking at other reviews and asking through the chat, it seems most people actually get the chance to customize for free one time. I’m not sure why I didn’t, but I tried signing out of my account and creating a new character. It didn’t work. I wish I could customize my character, and I may delete it if I can’t because character customization on a game that says you can is very important to me. Thanks for reading, hope you can help some way.

———————————————————————

One day after writing this I did get a free customization ticket. Not sure if this was the devs personally giving one to me or if it was sent out to everyone. I have changed my rating from four to five stars because I realized that it was user error that I did not get to customize. So far, this is a great game. Thanks.

Nick to Tricky , 07/01/2018

What an awesome game!

This is a really good game, I’m such a fan. Has great action, exciting combat, etc. Although it does need improvement like the selection part where you pick what class you want to be part of. I wish that a player could pick a class but then afterwards change their gender what they want. I picked assassin assuming I could change the character of my gender from male to female. Though I don’t mind playing as a female, I just wish I could make my character look more like me. I wish that when I opened the chests hat cost 5,000 that I would get a variety of different clothing. Instead I get the same clothing, some with different colors some just the same. Besides customization options, the game has a very bad lagging problem. When your out fishing or in the hot tub, or going to the spinning wheel. The game lags super badly and it is so hard to move around as the game lags more than it does when one is in the story. I also feel like there should be more super moves because your character ones character will do a super move but then you can choose to do a second one and they are usually all the same. Like when the assassin characters do the move where they wrap their legs around the opponent you can choose to do another super move on another opponent but out of all the super moves I used there are only three forms of combat for the super moves. Besides all that, kudos to this great game.

Erique purifoye , 07/06/2018

Would’ve been my favorite but.......

This game is one of the best rpg I’ve ever played. The graphics are amazing and the combat is good too. The game can get quite repetitive though. The PvP needs some balancing but can be fixed. My major problem is the customization. Not the character customization thing Like others have said. It’s the dyeing. I HATE the random pick thing. It’d be so much better to be able to just buy your choice of dye using gold. Better yet, include different textured dyes. Including different colored lava layers on your armor or a sort of aura. Like I stated before. A phenomenal game. Better than a few console games I’ve played. Also another problem I have is gender specific classes. It’s a very dumb decision to do that. As an RPG you’d expect to be able to make your ideal character. As a serious gamer I like role playing. It doesn’t help that you give players full customization over the character about everything except gender. Fix that as well. I still love the game just needs a few tweaks. Also the adventure mode shouldn’t be needed to progress the story. And the levels shouldn’t be the exact same other than the bosses at the end. The chances of the devs seeing this are pretty low. But please respond if you have a sense of where I’m coming from. And that you get my points. That’d help my issue with the game a lot
