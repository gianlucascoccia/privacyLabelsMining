Jorizondo , 08/20/2019

No support for multiple homes/locations

The app is good and the wireless dimmers are great, I liked them so much that I added them to our vacation home. To my surprise the app does not support multiple homes! Wow! I had other wireless switches in our second home which worked perfectly while being controlled by Google home and more importantly by voice commands as Google home DOES support multiple homes. I switched to Lutron in order to have only one system and now I basically have lost my voice command ability as you can not set up multiple homes in Google home since you would have to set up two Lutron accounts and link them to Google home in order to accomplish it. Google home/Lutron does not allow for this. So now I have lost my voice controls. The app needs still needs to mature a lot. Need to add multiple location support!!

Developer Response ,

Thanks for reaching out about supporting multiple homes in the app - Have you tried the latest 6.6 version of the app? We listened!

sbrunner , 08/26/2019

Rock solid

We have over 90 devices - on two bridges. The two accounts that are required are a bit of a hassle, Home brings them together - and this is what we use. After three years of service, the system has been running rock solid and dependable. The system deals perfectly with the many lightning related power outages we have in Texas.

I am super excited about the new fan controllers and will replace my eight fans that are on the old on/off switch today. Thank you for the 75 device expansion, not enough, though. A really nice feature would be the merger of the RA Select and Caseta lines for more flexibility. I do not believe it would eat into revenue as both lines serve completely different markets. But it would allow tinkerer to spend more money that they otherwise would not spend.

An in-wall outlet is missing from the line, the plug-in model is no replacement. I am running a couple of Z-wave outlets today for these for various things such as pump controller, outside lighting, and holiday illumination. Z-wave has been running fairly stable, but compared to Clear Connect, no comparison. Z-wave so and so often fails to trigger, whereas Clear Connect works every single time.

Developer Response ,

Thanks so much for sharing your feedback with us! Your feedback on upgrade options/compatibility, and in-wall outlet has been shared with our product management team.

FranceUSA , 04/12/2018

Siri phone works. Watch does not at first

Alexa will work but not as well as Siri. But Siri only works well with the iPhone. The watch does download the app but does not let you control anything. I had to delete My Home in HomeKit and reload the bridges to get it working. That is crazy. Also, the app watch does not let you access devices. It only lets you access scenes. So unless you create a scene you will not see your device. So create a scene to turn on a light and create a 2nd scene to turn off the light. Not very smart.

With all that said, I don’t think there is a better hardware device for switches. Add Hue Lights to Caseta and you have an amazing system.

The 30ft Range is not a problem for me yet but at a minimum they should create a mesh of Bridges that could add 50 devices each. So that with 3 Bridges one could handle 150 devices. 2 Bridges with 2 separate accounts is not an acceptable solution. And the expensive custom “RA2” which is a premium to the “Caseta” system is not HomeKit ready which means that even for free no one would want that outdated expensive system. Currently you can only do 50 switches on Caseta, which means you have to be selective on what to use Caseta. May be light switches using Caseta and use a different Brand to control power plugs.

Developer Response ,

Thanks for your feedback on our Apple Watch app and hardware!
You can customize what Lutron devices appear in our watch app from the iPhone app Settings menu under 'Apple Watch' - you can chose items from your scenes, lights, and shades.
We understand your concerns about the 50 device limitation and are committed to making improvements. Our currently available professionally installed solutions are RA2 Select (up to 100 devices), RadioRA 2 (up to 200 devices), and HomeWorks QS (large scale) - all which can be used with HomeKit.
Please let us know if you have any other questions, concerns, or feedback!
