Description

Season Two Begins!

[Beyond the Future]

New Story, New Karmas, and New Company's project...
More are waiting ahead!

The tapestry of time
Outlines the path of Fate

Who will be your soul mate? Mr Love: Queen's Choice is a romance simulation game that gives you the opportunity to text, chat and even call the main characters while developing your own career as a media producer.

In a world filled with superpowers, fantasy, and surprise, you will experience a girl’s whole life and get deeply involved with 4 male characters, feeling their romance, love, mystery and conflict in a deep story spanning dozens of episodes.

Voice Acting

Deepen your connection with your love interest by hearing his voice in English or Japanese. What type of voice do you prefer from a guy? Will it be soft, husky, lazy, sexy, pure, or deep? Mr. Love provides an immersive romance experience by allowing you to hear your love interest’s voice on your phone.
Japanese and English languages can be switched at your command, with famous voice actors providing voices in Japanese. The cast includes popular anime voice actors, including Daisuke Hirakawa (Free! - Iwatobi Swim Club, Rei Ryugazaki), Tomokazu Sugita (Gintama, Gintoki Sakata), Yūki Ono (Kuroko's Basketball, Taiga Kagami), and Tetsuya Kakihara (Fairy Tail, Natsu Dragneel).

High-quality Graphics
Want to watch the starry sky with him? Concentrate on work with him? See his genuine smile after saving a homeless dog? Ultra HD pictures, hundreds of delicate drawings, and CG will keep a record of every unforgettable moment between you and him.

Intriguing storyline
The world of Mr. Love is full of mysterious superpowers. Here you will experience a unique combination of fantasy and romance. As the story unfolds, you will unravel the mysterious secrets about his and your own identities through dozens of episodes, a deep plot, and a passionate romance simulation.

Message, Call, and Connect
Keep in touch with your love interest through messages, calls and social media. Chat with him via messenger and calls, and be the first one to comment on his online posts. You can even share your own moments and @ him.

Run Your Company
Run your own production studio! You will need to hire employees, produce TV programs (drama, episodes, interviews and more), keep an eye on each detail, and deal with emergencies. Make your own company succeed!

Genius scientist, resolute agent, demanding CEO, and popular superstar. You seem to know him pretty well. But…do you actually know the real him?

KaptainKaty , 07/24/2021

This game just keeps getting better

I downloaded this game on launch. I was obsessed with mystic messenger and I was sad it ended to I was looking at otomes on the App Store. They had this one for pre order and I was like sure why not. The art looked gorgeous so I figured it was worth a shot. To my surprise, the story was actually super good. My husband is in the military and he was put on deployment so I had a whole year to myself and this game got me through most of it. The voice acting is actually really clear and well done. I finished the storyline and put it down thinking no more content would come out. Okay so fast forward a year and a half later and I haven’t played at all. I log in and there’s SO MUCH CONTENT. I can’t lie, I’m shook. They have all these welcome back events (fully voice acted), things where the guy of your choice reads you to sleep, full on storylines in an alternate universe for limited time events with outfits for your best boy, and a new main screen of your favorite boy and you can change his outfits. I can really tell all the love the creators have put into it in just a year. This game is EXTREMELY well done. I’m honestly blown away. If you love mystic messenger, don’t sleep on this game. It’s probably the best otome I’ve ever played. Like ever. Thank you devs for putting so much work into it and offering so much content. ❤️❤️

My thoughts

Lol I’m sorry but GOD THEY’RE HOT. The voice acting is smooth and slightly soothing (unless we’re talking about victor). The music is unbelievably soothing and I love all the different things you can do. I love how each character has there own look and personalities. The only complaint I have is, there’s not all that much voice acting. The only ones who are voice acted are Kiro, Victor, Lucien, and Gavin. But the only times there voice acted is in phone calls and the actual story line which is great and all but I feel you should put a little more effort into voice acting all the characters. However, the voice actors voices are smooth and go with the characters really well. Another thing is I think you should add a feature to rewatch the tutorials for the features. It would be convenient for the player if they forget how to do something or just wanna rewatch it. Also I love the expressions and art. The art and colors are soft, they complement the feel of the game quit nicely. I love the seasonal challenges which makes the player more interested in the game and what’s in store. Of corse you don't HAVE to do any of this but I think it would add a few nice little touches to the game and make it more enjoyable. Sorry I feel like I’ve gone on rambling about artistic preferences. But if you do get to this please consider it.
