Description

Piano by Yousician is your personal piano tutor for the digital age! Learn and play thousands of songs on your real instrument with clear, instant feedback as the app listens along.

Start your free trial today and experience all we have to offer!

Learn at your own pace, whenever and wherever you want. The app listens to you play and gives instant feedback on your accuracy and timing. Our curriculum, designed by expert piano teachers, will help pianists of all levels improve, from complete beginners to professionals.

Step-by-step video tutorials guide you through each lesson. Fun and addictive gameplay tracks your progress and keeps you motivated to practice and learn. You’ll be amazed by how fast your skills advance. So go sit at your piano or keyboard, and start playing now!

Who is this for?
• Piano & keyboard players
• Complete beginners
• Advanced & professional musicians
• Piano teachers

What is included?
• Over 1,500 missions & exercises, with hundreds of videos, covering all the skills you need: sight reading sheet music, classical and pop songs for Piano.
• Yousician also teaches you music theory and Includes special trainers for each skill.
• A Weekly Challenge, where you can compete with friends and millions of Yousicians worldwide.

How does it work?
• It listens to your piano or keyboard
• It gives you instant feedback on your performance
• It takes you through awesome songs and step-by-step tutorials that suit your playing level
• It makes learning fun with rewarding gameplay and challenges
__

SELF-LEARNERS
Yousician has been developed with the world’s most innovative and experienced music teachers. It is perfectly suited for self-learning or as a supplement to learning with a teacher. With step-by-step tutorials and constant feedback you can always be sure you’re playing right.

PLAY WITH PIANO
Yousician is played with a real instrument, and requires no additional equipment. The phone’s microphone listens to you play and the app tells you how you’re performing.

This app is absolutely wonderful! I’ve really wanted to learn how to play piano so badly, and I think I finally found my answers! I’m so thankful for this app. I had a piano teacher, but back then I didn’t have as much logic sense as I do now, so I didn’t quite work out. When school was canceled back in March because of the current pandemic, I was inspired even more to do something with the piano in my house. And quite frankly, I was extremely bored. I would always play a few songs such as Gymnopedie.1 and Raindrop prelude and things like that but I never really knew how to play the piano. I’ve had the desire to learn this beautiful instrument since a young age, but never learned it. Now I’m 13 and I think I can finally fulfill one of my dreams. I highly recommend this app to anyone who has a desire to start learning a new instrument. You can even start with no experience and learn so on. The only thing that I don’t really like is the amount of time you have to play for free. It’s only ten minutes when you can learn so much more with five extra minutes or maybe even ten. Also, it would be great if it could teach how to read sheet music for beginners. Other than that, this app really is great.
