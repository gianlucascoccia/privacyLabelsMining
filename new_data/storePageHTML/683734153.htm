Description

Electro by Drum Pads 24 is the easy beat making instrument for creating EDM beats and music!
It's a new way to make EDM music. We've mixed synth with pads and now you can create amazing melodies improvisations on the go. You can play synth while you playing chords and drums.

Our electro presets will make your music performance sound like a popular EDM producer. Play on the go with professional studio samples, new free sound packs every month. With built-in LIGHT SHOW your performances will be even more epic!

Record your beats and music, share it with your friends or with our whole community: post your video with #drumpads24 hashtag!

Use effects to improve your beats:
- Filter
- Delay
- Reverb
- Pitch
- Distortion

Electro Drum Pads 24 has different type of sounds:
- Base lines
- Big kicks
- Synth chords
- Synth one shots
- Kiks
- Hats
- Snares
- FX
- Vocals

Suggest an idea for a new sound pack by joining us on Facebook and sending us a message.

What’s New

Apr 29, 2020

Version 2.7.0

Hey, beatmakers,

We have big news! As some of you requested before all sound packs will be moved to the Drum Pads 24 app! What it means for you:
* You can use just one app for all styles: Drum Pads 24 will contain all existing and all upcoming sound packs. Existing Electro Drum Pads 24 sound packs will be added to Drum Pads 24 within the next month.
* You can continue to use Electro Drum Pads 24 on your device. As soon as you delete it from your device it will not be possible to install it again since the app will be removed from the App Store on May 21.
* The app will be no longer supported: no new updates for the Electro Drum Pads 24 app.
* All upcoming sound packs will be released in the Drum Pads 24 app.

This update contains some improvements and bug fixes that will allow you to use the Electro Drum Pads 24 app if you wish to continue to use it.

Thanks for using Electro Drum Pads 24! It was a long way, now it's time for something new!

Feel free to contact us with any questions at drumpads24help+ios@gmail.com
