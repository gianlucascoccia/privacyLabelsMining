Universal Smart TV Remote App is an easy way to control your smart television and no longer have to deal with remotes that don't work and just get lost.

To make this app function as your remote, just:

1. turn your TV on
2. connect this app

This application currently supports:

2014 and newer Samsung Smart TV's
2016 and newer Sony Bravia TV's
2016 and newer Vizio SmartCast TV's
2016 and newer Roku TV's
