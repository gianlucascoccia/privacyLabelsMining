RebeccaLong , 06/25/2021

A lifesaver for the RD exam

I loved using this app because of the detailed explanations it gives with each question. It will usually not only explain why the answer is correct, but also why the other options were incorrect. This was very helpful in not just learning the material but also in starting to think in terms of ruling things out on the exam itself. I love the features, specifically the missed questions section and the ability to keep stats on yourself. It brought out the competitive side and made me go to town to try to beat the community average and keep upping my percentage. Would definitely recommend for all RD2Bs!

Greekfula , 09/16/2020

Can be used on laptop/desktop

I used this app for about 2-3 months to help study for a national registration exam to become a dietitian. I found the content very relevant and helpful. My only regret was not realizing I could log into the website using laptop or desktop computer... the particular exam I was studying for is separated into four different domains. Viewing the material on a desktop made it easier to see which questions were which from domain. I could then see my weaker/stronger areas. So yeah- great app! But better on desktop!

there’s another feature where you get quizzed on questions you missed previously. Overall great app!
