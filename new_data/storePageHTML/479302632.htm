Description

Feel better in the skin you’re in – with Health Magazine!

Get the magazine that recognizes “healthy” looks different for everyone. Use down-to-earth advice to live better and feel more fulfilled in every way. Find nutritious recipes that won’t sacrifice great flavor. Hear from the experts on the latest need-to-know facts in the medical field – and what changes you can make to benefit your life. Plus learn how to manage stress, have better relationships, freshen up your beauty routine – and so much more!

Download the app and receive a FREE ISSUE when you say yes to a monthly or annual subscription. Health Magazine is published 10 times a year.

Health® Magazine Subscriptions Available: 
• 1-month subscription - $1.99, automatically renewed until canceled
• 1-year subscription - $14.99, automatically renewed until canceled

SUBSCRIBERS’ AUTOMATIC-RENEWAL FEATURE:
Your subscription will be charged to your iTunes account at confirmation of purchase and will automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period. Your iTunes account will automatically be charged at the same price for renewal, as stated above, within 24-hours prior to the end of the current period.

Current subscriptions may not be cancelled during the active subscription period, but you can manage your subscription and/or turn off auto-renewal by visiting your iTunes Account Settings after purchase.

Downloaded easily

I love the app. I already receive the printed magazine so this was a free add-on. I loved the fact that once you went through the set-up steps that all the issues under your subscription number downloaded automatically and arranged on shelves. Much better than some of my other subscriptions that make you download each edition individually.

It is nice to have back options in one convenient place for easy access to some of the recipes and exercise ideas. I also like having my own reading material for waiting rooms, because I hate all the germs from the supplied reading materials in waiting rooms.

No longer free with print subscription

I have subscribed to Health magazine for years and previously enjoyed seeing my print subscription issues on my iPad. Then a couple years ago, I no longer could access my issues, even though my account ID and password was recognized and I was able to login. It seems that the iPad version is no longer available with printed subscrip. I had called Health earlier last year and they told me to contact Zinio, who supports the app. NO one at Zinio got back to me. I travel a lot for work and liked seeing my mags on my iPad. This mag is not worth me paying for a print AND iPad subscription. I just tried using the app just now, same thing. While I do still like getting the printed mag, I will probably let my current subscription expire at the end of this year. Disappointing. Health should clearly note the need to purchase the app subscription.
