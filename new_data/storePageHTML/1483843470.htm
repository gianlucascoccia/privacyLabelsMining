Gio-Unit , 10/18/2020

Review/suggestions

I don’t often review games, but when I saw this game in an ad in another one of my games I HAD to try it out. As an 80’s baby that grew up on TMNT the nostalgia was at 100! I love the art style and the animations and the overall polish of the game. It’s visually appealing to me and I like playing around with different team compositions.

Here comes my first suggestion. I would love to see bonus synergies with certain hero combinations. i.e. I would love to see if Casey Jones and April were both on a team they get some bonuses, Tokka and Rahzar, the 4 turtles, Bebop and Rocksteady etc etc.

Next, I fully understand this is supposed to be an idle game at its core. However, I enjoy it so much I wish there were additional game modes to enjoy. Once I hit that PVE wall and finish all my pvp battles I find myself checking the game periodically to see if there is something I can do and am sad when I realize I just need to wait for more resources to progress. Maybe something like a gauntlet mode or a way test various team compositions to see what you want to use. Etc. I didn’t play the beta so idk where this game started but I enjoy it and see a lot of potential. I hope to see a lot more coming to the game in the future.

Phildo2004 , 09/24/2020

OOZING SOOO MUCH POTENTIAL!!

Lemme start by saying I absolutely love the fact that someone made a TMNT game & didn’t use the “new age” design for them & instead used the OG classic design that all of us that were born in mid to late 80’s grew up watching & playing with the toys! Now being older I’m still a TMNT fan & collector so this game was a must download for me when I found it today! I didn’t even know it existed until it randomly popped up on my App Store but I’m glad it did! I think there’s a TON OF AWESOME POTENTIAL HERE! I’m not a big fan of “IDLE” games & I think if it were more like a team v team scenario where u actually got to control your team & not just hit an icon for a special when it happens it would take this game to bigger heights! Hopefully that will be added. Regardless I hope this game doesn’t wind up like “TMNT HEROES” where the developers just forget it & never update it anymore. Especially since I poured entirely too much cash into TMNT Heroes & now it’s boring bc there’s no new content or characters. Hopefully Kongregate does better & if it’s anything like Animation Throwdown then it’ll definitely be a ton of fun!

#BuyAstroTorfCustoms , 11/30/2020

This game is fun just like the show

The game is fun and gives you different variety on the enemies and your peoples attacks. It gives you a story like some games and is funny and is perfect on the characters personality in the show. I like how they also made the characters more crisp and modern, and the combat is fun. I just have 2 complaints. The first thing is that you don’t get to do much. You just watch and hope you win. The only time you get to press anything is when your choosing you characters to start the match, and when your fighting to get you character to do a special move unless you have it on so it does it on its own. Speaking of special moves, that brings me to my second complaint. The levels 99% of the time are so easy to beat. All you have to do is keep pressing buttons to get the characters to do their special moves. Other than that the game is fun and makes me feel like I am helping the turtles like I am in the show.
