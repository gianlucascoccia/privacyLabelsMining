Description

HiHello is the best digital business card maker app. With HiHello you can create and share your own digital business cards, scan physical business cards, and manage your contacts, all for free.

***HiHello has been featured in The Wall Street Journal, TechCrunch, Slate, and Axios***

HiHello is the best virtual business card and address book app that’s designed to help you connect and grow professional relationships.

CREATE CONTACTLESS DIGITAL BUSINESS CARDS
• Digital business cards are eco-friendly. They’re always available on your mobile device or desktop when you need them, so you’ll never need to order more.
• Include your pronouns, social media profiles, payment apps, badges, PDFs and more on your virtual business cards—they’re completely customizable and your information will always be up-to-date.
• HiHello Tip: We highly recommend adding a live photo or video to your vCards! A video business card makes a great first impression.

SHARE YOUR BUSINESS CARDS WITH ANYONE
• You can share your digital business card with anyone, even if they don’t have the HiHello app.
• Share your virtual card using your unique QR code, email, text message, social media, or an NFC tag. You can also send anyone the link to your business card.

MANAGE YOUR CONTACTS WITH A SELF-HEALING ADDRESS BOOK
• HiHello’s address book acts as a Rolodex and is your free personal and professional CRM. Your address book will heal itself, so you’ll always have your contacts’ latest information.
• Easily keep track of your contacts with a chronological timeline of who you met and when—we’ll even send you a reminder to follow up.
• Add notes and tags and search within your contacts.
• Sync HiHello with Google Contacts, Outlook, Zapier, and more so you can access your contacts across all platforms.

SCAN BUSINESS CARDS
•Scan any paper cards you receive and turn them into contacts with HiHello’s built-in business card scanner.

VIRTUAL BACKGROUNDS
• Take your video meetings to the next level with a free, personalized virtual background. These backgrounds are linked to your business card, so anyone on your video call will have access to your information.

EMAIL SIGNATURES
• Use your digital business card to create professional email signatures. With multiple templates to choose from, HiHello’s signature generator will allow you to easily create an email signature that links to your business card.

PRIVACY
• Our Privacy Policy ensures that your information is safe and secure.

ABOUT US
HiHello’s Digital Business Card Maker is made with love in Palo Alto, California. We welcome your feedback! Send us your comments from the HiHello app Settings page or email us at feedback@hihello.me.

We hope you love your digital business cards!
