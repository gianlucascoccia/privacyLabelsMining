STORY:
The little creatures of Minutia lived in peace and harmony until the meteor smashed into Mount Boom, bringing with it a strange force that transformed the Slugs who lived there into an army of greedy, greenery-gobbling pests. Now, the Slugs are taking over the world, munching a path through Minutia and sliming up everything they touch!

But a brave band of heroes is fighting back! Left alone when the Slugs conquered most of Minutia, these unlikely champions are on an epic quest to solve the mystery of Mount Boom and beat back the Slug advance.

To get to Mount Boom, they’ll need to become… the BEST FIENDS!

Editors’ Notes

A comet has struck Mount Boom, and it’s up to you to assemble a quintet of adorable fiends to stave off a slimy slug invasion. Best Fiends is a unique take on the classic item-matching puzzler. Your team of canny critters will launch attacks, power up special abilities, and uncover hidden goodies across dozens of levels. From tricky obstacles to devastating special moves, there’s plenty of strategy among the silliness.

Fish lover 2514 , 02/29/2020

Horribly addictive!! Seriously Fun!!

Ok, after playing this game since 2014, I think I can honestly say that this is one of the absolute best games you can download for your phone or tablet. Game play is creative, simple and challenging all at the same time. Collecting, improving and using your Fiends adds depth, and makes you really play around to find the perfect combo for each level. Plus, the constant trickle of new fiends keeps you coming back to complete your collection. I really appreciate that you can buy items and watch adds for bonuses if you want, but they don’t force it down your throat or make it mandatory to progress. Hats off to the developers, they really hit it out of the park with this one!!

After reading multiple reviews about how the game is rigged to make you buy gold because the levels are too hard or things similar to that, I just want to say that it’s not true. I’ve passed more than 2100 levels without paying a dime. If you can’t get past a level in less than 10 or 15 tries then your fiends are either not leveled up enough or you’re using the wrong ones. You should expect levels to get harder the further you go and this game does it without being cruel
