Description

Launch a car from a slingshot and watch it fly! Are you ready to make the craziest car stunts you’ve ever seen? Try for yourself!

This is it! This is what it all led to! All of the car driving, stunts, crazy shenanigans - this is what they culminate into! Are you brave enough, crazy enough, fearless enough to LAUNCH A CAR FROM A SLINGSHOT?

We bet you are!

In this awesome, one-of-a-kind arcade experience you will be able to:

- Adjust the strength of the slingshot
- Choose a direction
- Watch your car fly, bounce around the environment & reach the target…

… but only if you’re skilled enough!

Aim carefully & try to land in the marked spot! For the coins you collect in each approach you can buy awesome upgrades:

- Slingshot power: so that your initial speed gets even higher
- Engine: so that the cars drives faster
- Bonus: so that you earn more money for future upgrades

The further you get, the more challenging the terrain becomes and the more carefully you have to aim.

SDK51 , 10/19/2020

Please fix

I really like this game! So much that I paid to upgrade to no adds. Having no adds is not always a plus though, you have to get creative to add up points to upgrade your powers, rather than watch an add for an upgrade. You have to be pretty patient and repetitive. Because of this, I have a few issues with this game.
First: I takes forever for a new world to come out! So I go back and play the previous worlds again... and again.. The annoying thing about starting at the beginning of a previous level is you do not get to keep the power levels that you built while being patient through out the other worlds. I do not think that is right. Second, you have a new feature that will not stay running since it came out. As soon as you start pulling back on the car the app shuts down. When you reopen the game it starts over, and shows that you played the new feature and took a gem for doing it. Please fix this! For a game I ended up paying for this is extremely annoying.

PM871 , 06/27/2021

It was great for a while

I really enjoyed this game and even paid to get rid of the ads because it was worth it to me. The concept is different and thank goodness has nothing to do with the match 3 obsession with so many games lately. But I’m stuck on level 7-2. I have spent many hours of frustration because of inconsistent physics. I stretch back to 85% and it lands directly next to the button. So I stretch back to 85% again and adjust my aim slightly, but now it goes WAY beyond the button even over the railing behind the button! How does that make sense?? Worse yet, I HIT the button (finally) but it didn’t activate it! I also rolled over the button and it didn’t activate it. It seems impossible to pass this level. If it’s possible to pass it at all, it seems to take pure luck, not learning and adjusting from previous attempts which is what was originally attractive about the game. So it seems I can no longer play and I’m disappointed. Besides fixing the physics, it would be good to give some option to help when you’re completely stuck, like a hint, or pay coins to skip, or pay coins to make the button a bigger target, or whatever else gives some other option besides no longer being able to continue at all.

BrandonThomas97 , 09/17/2020

Bad physics, useless Engine upgrade, and ads even after paying to remove them

Ads: Even after paying for no ads, the app still occasionally advertises other apps made by this developer in a box in the bottom right corner of the screen. If I pay for no ads, I expect NO ads, not just seeing YOUR ads. After contacting the developer about this, they said in a Zendesk email reply “In the window you mentioned you can see the ads of our other games, we decided to leave this so players can see and check them out.” Nothing in their current TOS says they will do this, that would be fraud...

Physics and collision: Absolutely terrible. You can go in a perfectly straight line in flat terrain and still get knocked off to a hard left or right without hitting anything. It’s awful. On world 2 you get this new fun dynamic where if you hit a ramp too fast, your car clips on the ramp and you come to a complete stop with the front of your car stuck in the ramp at a 90 degree angle.

The “Engine” upgrade: No matter how much you upgrade this, there is no change to speed, control or distance you get from your vehicle. At the time of writing seems completely pointless unless we’re all missing something here. Would love to hear what the devs have to say and what kind of “update” we’ll get next.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for your feedback! Please be informed that the developer team has been notified about problems with physics and collision and will try to find a solution as soon as possible. Regarding the engine update, it reduces air resistance, so it influences how much the car loses speed. We're working on how to increase the visibility/feeling of this improvement.

Best wishes,
