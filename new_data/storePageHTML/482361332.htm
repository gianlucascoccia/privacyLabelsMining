Love this MOON app!

I have had the Deluxe app for some time. I love it! I especially like how you can add reminders to your i-calendar, such as Full & New Moon dates, eclipses, equinox’s, etc.—great feature! Having all the moon info I could want in one place is nice. I don’t know if this is something app’s have control over however, or if it is something that would need an update in the OS to work correctly, or ask you your preference.
