Description

• Monitor vehicle mileage, maintenance, licensing, fuel, accidents, recalls and order status, and access fuel PINs and documents.
• Request roadside assistance, glass repair, fuel cards and replacement plates or registration card.
• Receive notifications for critical alerts for maintaining your vehicle with ARI.
• Find fuel and maintenance vendors and schedule service for repairs.
• Report mileage for personal and business usage and enter inspection reports.

Not user friendly

I use ARI app for work in a fleet vehicle. We just switched from WHEELS. The WHEELS app was much better than the ARI app.

The mileage section only shows the last 10 entries. It would be much better if it showed the entire month.

The ARI forces you to use an email address for maintenance and services. It is not convenient if the employees at the maintenance location do not know how to use the app or have not seen it before. The app itself is not user-friendly. I have used much better wraps in the past by different companies. I do not like this one.
