Description

Join millions of families and discover BabyTV’s award-winning video app that’s made for little ones.

BabyTV is the world’s leading TV channel for toddlers and their parents. BabyTV provides quality kids videos that introduce your little one to the world around them. All our programming is created with the help of experts and includes short episodes, baby songs, toddler games and kids activities.

Age rating
Made for little ones aged 4 & under and BabyTV fans of all ages.

Kids videos
This must-have app is packed with early-learning themed baby videos episodes, nursery rhymes & songs, and is perfect for keeping your toddler happily entertained at home or out and about. We offer kids video for every age.

Toddler games
Games for kids which will help them with learning more about the world. Toddler games, so they can learn the colours, the numbers or different food. Our kid games for toddlers will keep your baby entertained while exploring new things.

Baby songs
Kids songs that will make your baby smile while listening. ABC songs, game songs with animal sounds, different musical styles - we have it all!

Bedtime stories
Bedtime stories for kids that will help you put them to sleep, but also will keep them asking for more! No matter if it’s one of the traditional bedtime stories or some new one that you haven’t heard in your childhood, we guarantee that your kid will love it!

Lovely Characters
Enjoy a host of BabyTV’s much-loved characters including; Little Lola, Billy BamBam, Oliver, Stick with Mick, Charlie & the Numbers, Draco and many more!

Offline mode
Downloaded episodes can be saved on your device for the duration of your subscription and are perfect for viewing in the car, on a plane, while keeping your device on airplane mode.
