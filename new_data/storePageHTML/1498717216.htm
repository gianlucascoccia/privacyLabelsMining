Description

Feel free to customize watch faces on your own apple watch with up to 5000+ wallpapers from Watch Faces Gallery Apps.

ESPECIALLY, WE ARE UPDATING MORE & MORE CHRISTMAS THEMES FOR YOU FROM TODAY!!!

Let the watch faces show how cool you are by your hand-picked photo! We collect all fancy apple watch faces from various and dynamic categories to meet the requirement of the pickiest person. You have a wide choice to set any photo to be your apple watch by just 1 tap. With new categories arriving regularly and a brand new wallpaper every day, this is the only one watch faces app that you need.

Features:
* EASY TO USE: preview watch faces on your phone, we also offer a detailed guide for setting wallpaper on your apple watch and many tips for utilizing your watch.
* FRESH LOOK: frequently update new faces with multiple categories: Christmas, Car, Sports, ...
* SORT BY CATEGORY: you won't feel lost in a sea of photos whenever you open the app. It can take you the shortest time to find your best one for today at a glance.
