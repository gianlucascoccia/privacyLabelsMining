Description

Stay on top of the latest with the Vikings, Twins, Timberwolves, Wild and the rest of Minnesota sports with the SKOR North mobile app.

Get the latest from Purple Daily, Mackey & Judd, Vikings Ventline, Judd’s Hockey Show and the entire catalog of offerings from SKOR North. Watch the latest videos, listen to the latest podcasts, read the latest articles and more.

Interact with SKOR North with a slew of new features like listener feedback and live polls. Receive breaking sports news notifications. Plus, be rewarded with chances to win great prizes!

FEATURES INCLUDE:
Listen live
Listen to SKOR North podcasts
Watch videos from the station
Read the latest sports stories
Stay up to date with breaking sports news notifications
Log-in/Registration – Complete with Touch ID/Face ID for easier log-in
Personalized profile
Notification center
Listener rewards – Including listening time rewards, promocode rewards, location check-in rewards and so much more
Listener feedback – Send us text, audio, photos or video
Vote in listener polls
Alarm Clock

The SKOR North app supports Apple Carplay!

Brian from MN , 09/08/2019

Skor North - Nothing better!

I’m 50 years old, lifelong MN sports fan. MN sports programming has had it’s moments but this is best I’ve ever heard. The combination of knowledge about MN sports, passion for sports and honest straightforward entertaining sports talk without a bunch of schtick is the best ever. Mackey and Judd with Ramie is pure gold. Judd is so underrated as a radio host, Mackey is a consummate professional who clearly cares about the overall product and Ramie is that perfect blend of comedian and passionate sports fan. Matthew Coller’s Vikings Daily with his guests Sage Rosenfels, Courtney Cronin and now Alex Boone is best Vikings coverage. Judd and Declan covering Wild, Danny C and Manny H and Raised by Wolves podcast, Detek Wetmore on Twins show are all podcasts that I can listen to anytime. I love that I can earn points and enter drawings for gift cards that are actually worth pursuing just for streaming live. And ... They still have Patrick Reusse in the fold. Love it! Keep it going!

WadeOfTheNorth , 02/14/2019

Motown listener

I’m a Minnesota transplant living in the Detroit metro area. The local Detroit sports radio shows are painful to listen to so I thought I’d check out Minnesota sports talk radio during the Vikings 2017 season. I have been listening ever since. The hosts are longtime Minnesota sports fans with fun insights into each of the Minnesota teams. My Random Minnesota sports player is Greg Gagne because when he first came up to the Twins team I was convinced he was mispronouncing his last name thanks to my familiarity with wrestling great Vern Gagne.
