Description

Need a better way to track your progress and to find the knowledge you need to reach your goals?
Then Ridely is for you!

What you get:
-> 100s of filmed riding exercises with some of the world's best riders and trainers - Meredith Michaels Beerbaum and Gareth Hughes, to just name a few.
-> A journal made exclusively for equestrians, where you can keep track of everything from shoeing to show results, giving you a perfect overview. It also lets you plan your upcoming week, month or season.
-> Define your goals and the actions you need to take to get there. Ridely will help you to stay on the right track and make progress.

And you know what’s even better? Ridely is free to download and use! We also offer a premium version, Ridley PRO, with unique riding exercises and extra functionality. There is a 7 days free trial so you can try it out.

Ridely is like a digital coach that will help you make progress towards your goals.

Some of our features:
-> Register one or several horses and share them with your team - a trainer, co-rider, parent or friend. You will all have the same information and get a notification when someone logs an activity with the horse. You can also chat with your team in the app.
-> Log your riding activities and add comments, as well as the feeling, intensity and time. Upload photos or a video (10 minutes of video on each activity with Ridely PRO). You’ll never have your riding videos lost again!
-> Track your hack with our tracker and get your ride on a map, together with the time, distance and average speed.
-> You can also log non-training activities, for example shoeing, deworming or a visit to the vet. Everything organized in one place!
-> Get statistics of your training - type of riding, feeling, intensity and time. Analyze and find patterns in your training and get a perfect overview.
-> Plan your upcoming week, month or season. Having a plan is really crucial to reach your goals.
-> Get inspired by 100s of filmed riding exercises within categories like dressage, jumping, US hunter jumping/equitation, western, in hand work, and more.
-> Write down your goals and break them down into bit size tasks that you plan in your journal.
-> Ask one of our experts for advice. Choose between all our expert trainers within different disciplines. Add a picture or a video and your question, you will get a reply within a week, directly in the app. A superb way to solve a problem or get a second opinion on your training.

Ready to take the next step in your riding? Download Ridely - we’ll help you reach your full potential!
