My game got erased after days, and hours playing it.

I played this game since it came out. I really enjoy the idle games. I can play this game all day just upgrading. I even ended up buying the no ads for the game which was a really time saver too to just speed things along the game. Today I was on the last unlocked level, and when I switched restaurants to check the progress.. an error happened where it went back to the very beginning with the tutorial which completely wiped out my progress. At first I hoped for it to be something else, and once I was done with the tutorial all over again I checked to see if my restaurants weren’t gone. Very sad to realize they were gone. I even checked to see if my in app purchase was still available, which I had to go to settings to restore my in app purchases. It did right away as if it still had my status still there somehow. Now I’m not in the motivation anymore as I was before to realize I can’t play the game I called my favorite. It was very disappointing. I emailed for feedback hoping for a restore. If that’s not possible. Then this game deserves a 2 star at least. Don’t recommend if you waste your time and effort towards the game for it just to be wiped out with a tap to another restaurant.

LadyofRoses , 02/08/2021

All In made me feel like a sucker

First off, let me say I've played Idle Miner (a different game by the same developer) for a couple of years. Perhaps that caused my expectations to be unfairly high, but them being from the same company is why I tried this one.

I managed to beat the first three scenarios in less than a week as a free to play. I did it by watching SO many ads. A nuisance, but doable. I was halfway through the Alpine scenario when along came an event. I wanted to support the game, but also thought it would be a great help for the event mine. I bought the All In package. Well, it turns out the only part of the package that helps is the ad skipping. I didn't have double income, nor the ability to be afk for more than an hour to accumulate money in the event.

The All In package isn't working correctly for the regular scenarios either. The money isn't accumulating properly. I'd give this game 4 stars if it had been honest about the packages. I'd also assume that with a bit more development time it can be a 5 star game like Idle Miner. Right now though I feel like a sucker. =(

Developer Response ,

Thanks for getting in touch! We're sorry to hear that you've had a bad experience with your latest purchase in the game :( Could you send us a message to community@idlerestauranttycoon.com so we can speak with you directly? Thank you in advance!
