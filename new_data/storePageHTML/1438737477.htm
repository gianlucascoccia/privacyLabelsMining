Videos are laggy and low quality

The subscription price is ridiculous for the quality of the content you get. Most of the videos are "live" and have the worst laggy quality. It's like a web cam from 1999. I didn't know such low quality equipment still existed.
There are a few videos in a nice studio, but not many.
I also don't understand the 10-15 minute "workouts." You're not getting a workout if you spend 10 minutes on it. You might burn 10 calories.
They tout that their library is large, but it really isn't because the majority of it is garbage and/or short.
Try Daily Burn instead. Half the price, huge library, tons of barre videos, good instructors, NOT laggy, high quality/HD. Also they give you a 30-day trial.
