Skhduehd , 03/26/2021

Love it - but there are some flaws!

This game is fantastic. Ive played it for hours. I had to redownload / restart my progress after I found out my game was bugged and wouldn’t let me find Atlantis, but that has been fixed. The music and atmosphere are amazing, and the bonus challenges really just keep the game going. Collecting all the sharks / predators is really fun as well.
The only negative thing I have to say about this game is about the ads - I don’t mind the abundance of them, because I understand that smaller developers need to make money. I get frustrated when the ads get bugged. I just collected 18k+ coins in a round, and clicked the ad multiplier. I watched the ad, and when I came back, the game was stuck on the same screen I had before and it won’t let me exit. The only way to get out of it is to force close the app and go back in, which loses all the money I made from that round. Its very frustrating, and its happened over 4 times now in the last 30 minutes of playing. I’ve missed out on well over 70k coins due to this. I really hope this gets fixed.
Other than that, I LOVE this game! Very relaxing and addicting. I’m excited to see where the developer takes this game. I’m sure with time, the kinks will get ironed out. :)

Amazing Game but I have some suggestions

I’ve been playing this game for about 2 days straight 😂i truly love this game. I rarely play mobile games because most of them are bad and all the same but this one is actually fun and calming. Whoever the dev is the music you put in is very very good. I love how smooth the game is and how ambient the fish are. This game is a very good time killer and there’s a solid amount of things to do in the game being that’s it’s a fairly newer game. the only complaint I have is the excessive amounts of ads and no way to get rid of them it kinda ruins the experience and peacefulness of the game. Also some suggestions I feel like if you added more biomes of maps to the game it could greatly increase the vibes the game gives off and make it a little more interesting. I understand that developing these types of things takes some time but I’m very excited for what the future of this game has in store it seems like your going in the right direction being that most of the reviews are 5 stars and agree with that I’ve never played a mobile game this much.
