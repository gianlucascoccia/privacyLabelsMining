Description

Order a Freaky Fast! Freaky Fresh!® sandwich with the Jimmy John’s® app. Order for contactless delivery, or pay ahead and pick up from your local store. Freaky Fast Rewards® members can earn and redeem exclusive rewards at participating locations. Terms and conditions apply. Plus, track your order and receive notifications when it’s out for delivery or ready for pick up. Sign in, find a location near you, place an order and enjoy the Sandwich of Sandwiches!

Features:
— Order delivery or pickup from your iPhone, iPad or iPod touch
— Order Catering
— Find your nearest Jimmy John’s
— Use a recent delivery address or pick up location
— Customize your sandwich
— Save your favorite orders to My Faves for quick reordering
— Check out our menu
— Accepts Apple Pay

Everything is great except for one important thing:

I usually don’t take the time to write reviews for anything, let alone petty ones like this, but I just wanted to say that this app is great except for the fact that it tells you your sandwich is ready ONE HOUR LATER. I ordered my sandwich through the app (so I could get rewards) and sat in my car right outside the Jimmy Johns waiting for the app to notify me when it would be ready. 20 minutes passed. 30 minutes passed. Still no notification. Finally I just tried calling because maybe there was a mistake. The girl told me, oh yeah your order has been ready for the past 20 minutes and I could get it in the drive-thru! Frustrated that I had wasted so much time and I was late to an appointment, I pulled in and got my sandwich. Then an hour later, I get a notification saying “your order is ready to pick up.” I know it’s such a little thing, but it’s also the most important thing! Getting the sandwich quickly 😒 Please fix this part of the app or I’ll probably just end up deleting it.
