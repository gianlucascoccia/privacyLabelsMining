Description

Download our app to get the most from your meal kit experience. Share your feedback by writing a review. We appreciate it.

Martha & Marley Spoon makes delicious weeknight cooking convenient. By replacing tedious meal planning and grocery shopping with exciting recipes and pre-portioned ingredients delivered direct to your door, you’ll save time to make time for more of the things you love. Use our meal delivery app to choose your recipes, order when it's convenient for you, and manage your account on the go!

Here's more of how our app makes life easier:
- Cooking simplified: use our digital recipes for a better way to cook your recipe choices.

- Manage your account on the go: set your delivery details and plan up to 4 weeks ahead.

- Liked what you cooked? Give us feedback and rate your recipes.

- Love Martha & Marley Spoon? Send free boxes to your friends - sharing is caring!

With more than 22 deliciously different recipes to choose from each week, our home cooking kit has something for every palate and dietary need. Healthy? Vegetarian? Family-friendly? We've got them all, and they're all Martha Stewart-approved. Start cooking quality weeknight dinners with fresh, seasonal ingredients today. Every day really can be delicious!

Hungry for more meal it how-to? Visit our website: marleyspoon.com/how
Like us on Facebook and get real-time recipe updates: facebook.com/marleyspoon
Find food inspiration on our Instagram page: instagram.com/marleyspoon

Inlet Kayaker , 08/17/2018

App works well. Martha & Snoop make great, albeit unlikely team....

...but, like other reviewers have pointed out, not seeing recipes, or current meals offered, without giving credit card info., and paying for first delivery, is extremely disappointing. I’m eager to give Martha and Marley Spoon a try. I’ll check back once or twice more (with fingers crossed) to see if this problem has been resolved. If not, I’ll move on. I’m sure this will be the case with most potential customers. No one wants to give their credit card info., and pay for something, that they haven’t tried, can’t see, etc. That said, the app’s functionality and ease of use are seemingly top-notch. NOTE: I tried accessing menus via link/home page as was recommended to previous reviewer. I had no luck. Nonetheless, congrats to the developer of this app for a job well done. It’s a shame the app’s reviews seem to reflect user’s complete dismay with the setup of the meal service, specifically that it requires payment without providing menus, sample meals, etc., and not the app itself. The app deserves five stars. The meal service is what’s lacking here.

dabar1 , 04/06/2019

The Meal service and quality are excellent!

We have been using this service for almost 2 months now. The food is super fresh, the instructions are step by step, with pictures, and it is easy to edit your future deliveries on their website. They include everything except salt, pepper, and oil for almost every meal. We have only had to add an egg to one and butter to another. That is all you can ask for when it is being shipped, we think. My husband enjoys them as much as I do and cooks a lot more now.
We have tried others and this is, by far, our favorite! I just downloaded the app and it works great! For those that want to see everything prior to committing a credit card, most apps require this before they give you their information. You can always cancel after looking. That is not a valid excuse to slam the app or business.

Blondie7885 , 04/30/2020

Customer service??

First, it was irritating I had to give credit card info and pick a date of delivery without even getting to see the recipes of that week. But I did. I got a charge right away... I didn’t think a whole lot of it. Then a couple days before my schedule delivery, I got another charge, then an email saying my order was on its way, then another email saying there was a mix up with the charges, I was being refunded one of them, but I should still expect my first box. Then an email saying my first box was cancelled. Box never came. So I emailed them to make sure I was being refunded both charges. Then I tried to cancel my account, but it gave me one more delivery before then that I didn’t want. So I emailed again and told them I don’t want that last delivery, so please cancel it. Someone emailed back, he had no idea what was going on. Didn’t even acknowledge the first delivery that never showed up, but said he cancelled the last one. After several emails back and forth, he sent one last really snippy one saying he explained it all already (No Felix, you didn’t). So I’m just praying I get both charges refunded and they didn’t charge me for anything else.
