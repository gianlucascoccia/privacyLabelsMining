The science behind Zova
-------------------------
Zova’s workouts are based on the science of heart rate training. What’s heart rate training? Basically, if you workout within a specific heart rate zone for the right amount of time, you’ll burn more calories. These extra calories burned mean you’ll get fit and lose weight faster.
