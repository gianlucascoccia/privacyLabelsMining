Description

Collect, compare and compete in epic car racing challenges with Top Drives, the ultimate strategy card game. Go turbo with super charged PvP and multiplayer battles.

Petrol-heads, motor-heads, turn to Top Drives, the high octane driving challenge - test your skills and knowledge to the limit. Think you know cars? Top Drives is a fantastic new card collecting game with over 1900 real life cars to collect, compare and compete in racing battles with other players.

Build a garage of the best customized cars and create your very own perfect racing deck for supercharged action against your friends or other drivers. Whether you prefer a tuned up American muscle, a feisty Japanese hot rod or go turbo with European and agile, you’ll find a car that gets your motor running in Top Drives - the #1 FREE car racing card collection game.

Top Drives is about racing cars across a variety of roads, tracks and challenges. If you like your driving fast and furious, you’ll love Top Drives. Hit the gas on asphalt circuits, slide your wheels around twisty circuits, master drifting in the G Force tests or speed to the chequered flag in a 1-100mph drag race. Choose Mustang, Camaro, Porsche Turbo, Audi TT or Nissan GTR, just make sure you’ve got the right car for the right track. You’ll need a garage of cars fast in the wet, good off-road, can take corners and burn rubber for drag races.

Why is Top Drives the ultimate motorsport experience for every car enthusiast?

- Turbocharged, addictive, fun racing strategy gameplay

Own the road, start collecting the coolest new rides, customize with upgrades then sell on your tuned- up cars to build the perfect garage! Get an edge by fusing your cars and push your hot wheels to the limit. Become driving champion with the best combination of performance, handling and grip. Start your engine. Hit the road with Top Drives and unleash exciting updates, take to the track with the coolest, fastest cars including Porsche, McLaren and Mustang, plus enjoy new game modes and new events themed around global car culture.

Jikan067 , 07/03/2019

Very Fun,But Needs More Work

I really like how the card system works, and the variety of car licenses rivals those of normal mobile racing games, however it’s very hard to collect the cars not just because of a limited amount of space, which has to be bought or earned by slots by completing the campaign and raising your experience, but because the pack system of cards falls short of nothing but spending the currency you earned just to get underwhelming cards that end up being discarded, whether by personal preference or wanting a better card. It doesn’t make sense that you have many cards that can be collected while having no space to have your own collection. There is still a good chance to help this issue be better, maybe by focusing more on the car packs than the slots or just taking out the slots permanently. The online is a great way to get players to come back, but limiting the matches by 5 takes time and is only a bait to spend your money just to skip the time, it ends up being a waste of time and currency.

Although the progression isn’t bad, and the campaign is great, it would be wise to expand both to benefit the variety of car cards you can earn, just a simple idea that could help the game be more fun and replayable. In conclusion, this game can be better, and maybe with enough feedback, the developers will listen to fans of this game and rejoice by expanding the game, fixing the issues it has, and maybe turn it into an even bigger success.

THE MONSTER MIDGET , 05/12/2020

I love it, but....

I absolute love this game. It is one of the only games I play and I could play for quite a while. Because I love this game I am willing to criticize it a bit. This is going to sound a little like a rant, but just hear me out. I think this game is pay to win. I am RQ 500 and I have never gotten a second legendary from any pack. The only reason I have a second legendary is because I got the VW Syncro when it was epic. All these events where people have these legendary Porsches and Jaguars, and I still have a Mclaren 12c. Don’t get me wrong the car is great, but I can only use it to mirror other 12c’s so I don’t lose 200 points! I don’t even get enough epics to upgrade my legendaries! Every epic I get is a celebration! I have opened ceramic after ceramic and gotten constant crap. I see al these YouTubers getting legendaries fr m ceramics every other month! I would open carbon fibers, but it takes me for ever to save up the gold to buy one. I love this game, I really do. I actually have a recommendation for the game. Why don’t you add a daily market, where there are certain cars available for sale, with a chance of legendaries being sold that day. You know, like the market in Clash Royal. I want this game to succeed. Thanks for reading.

Hollowtian , 03/03/2021

Almost perfect for me

I’ve been looking for a game like this for so long. I love cars, but sometimes I don’t think I always have to “drive” a car. This game is so satisfying for me because I can collect, and see many different models. That’s also one thing I like about Forza Horizon, you can see some cars from your daily lives. And I can still enjoy modification and upgrading in this game.
There are only two things I hope to improve. 1. Maybe a slight more accurate modeling, maybe not for all cars, but for some rare ones. I think adding details to all of them is not necessary.
2. If the first one is too hard, I feel this one is more realistic: add different sound for different cars. Right now I feel like they all sound the same. Again, not saying literally all of them, but maybe different types of car have different sound, like coupe sounds different from suv, etc. and maybe add some effect to indicate road noise. Because road type is also one of the major challenge in this game. Maybe sand sounds different from snow.
Anyway, it’s my first time writing a review for any kind of app. It’s not complaining, I love this game! Great work
