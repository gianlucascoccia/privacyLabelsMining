nugs.net Subscription Payment and Auto-Renewal Terms

nugs.net offers auto-renewing monthly and annual subscriptions to stream music from the nugs.net catalog.

Two subscription types are available, each with two tiers: monthly (30 days) and annual (365 days). All subscriptions auto-renew.

A nugs.net monthly subscription is $12.99 per month and the yearly subscription is $129.99 per year. A nugs.net HiFi monthly subscription is $24.99 per month and the yearly subscription is $249.99 per year.

All plans begin with a 30 Day free trial.

Payment will be charged to the iTunes Account at the end of your trial period if applicable, otherwise at the time of your purchase.

Subscriptions automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period.

Accounts will be charged for renewal within 24-hours prior to the end of the current period.

Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the users Account Settings after purchase.

Any unused portion of a free trial period, if offered, will be forfeited when the user purchases a subscription to that publication.

Corch McGuirk , 09/27/2019

Great Content, Poor Function

Love the content of the nugs app. They are unmatched in providing the quantity and variety of live music and it is updated regularly. However, the app constantly crashes, losing your place in the show or song you were listening to. Everyone that uses the app knows what I’m talking about.

Every time you lock your phone and use another app, nugs crashed, forcing you to reboot and go find your place again. With the amount of content on the app, this is an incredible inconvenience and seems to be getting worse with every IOS update. Before you spend the money on the monthly subscription service, please consider this glitch and it’s impact to your listening experience.
