MusicalMom1212 , 03/06/2020

Uniquely challenging restaurant game

(*Update: I’m at level 519, and it’s officially too hard, haha. I’m finally out of free gems to buy boosters. I haven’t spent a penny on this game. Sad to throw in the towel. It was a nice little app to challenge my mind when I needed a break from the daily grind.)

I really like this one! I like that there are different challenges to pass the levels, like serving the most dishes before the time runs out or making the most tip money from a limited amount of customers. It makes things much more interesting. The use of combos and “likes” is a unique twist. Be strategic about the way you spend your gems! Some levels need the right upgrade in order to win, and if your gems are gone, you’re out of luck unless you buy or earn more. Fortunately, there are plenty of free ways to earn gems. I haven’t had a reason to spend money yet! I’ve played this for several weeks now as my go-to for blowing off steam. I wish there was a way to purchase the game to remove ads. You can pay for an allotted time of unlimited play. I haven’t tried it, so I’m not sure if it removes the ads for that time. I usually just play until my five lives run out. Sometimes I’ll watch an add for an extra life if I run out too quickly. It lets you watch for up to five extra lives in a 24hr period. Pretty good for a free app!

c3rose7 , 05/07/2020

There’s only one problem...

I love this game. It is so much fun and was created in a way that you can keep going. In other cooking games, I find that you are not able to pass levels unless paying for upgrades. I love that I can play extra shifts on levels if I run out of money. I also love the special games (The easter and mother day special mini games are awesome) and the creativity with food and restaurants is so appealing!

There is only one problem I recently ran into. I was looking at the booster options but didn’t want to spend my diamonds right away. I went to click the exit button, but something went wrong. It ended up paying for a booster I never use, and unfortunately took half my diamonds. I now have 3 extra boosters of a booster I never use and don’t have diamonds for the upgrade I wanted to make. I would love it if there was a pop up when trying to buy things with diamonds asking “are you sure you want to purchase ‘x’?” Then you could confirm or cancel the purchase. Really small issue and I may be the only one who has this problem 😂

Fun but I’m Mad.

This game is pretty fun and I was cruising along enjoying myself but here comes level 22 and I literally can’t pass it because I keep being told I need more customers. So I can’t figure out if it’s because I’m too slow or if I’d run out regardless. But honestly I feel like I do a pretty good job. I’m filling all the orders, no one has walked away. The only level I’ve failed so far was level 6 of the valentine levels. So back to the customers I need: the only way to get more customers? Pay 67 gems for them. Guess what else? I don’t have enough gems. I’ve played this level like 5 times now. I started out like 20 or more gems short. I have watched ads and I get 2 gems per ad. How many ads do I have to watch? This makes me mad because I’m basically being forced to pay real money to get more gems or else I can’t advance in the game.
