Description

Track expenses and spendings on your phone!

If you are looking for the best personal finance application, this is the app for you and it's free! Millions of downloads cannot be wrong! Designed for iPhoneX!

iSpending helps you to track your income and spendings. You can add transactions under different categories, such as income, food, entertainment. Summaries for today/week/month/year are displayed right at home page. You can view detailed report for each month in beautiful graphs and find transaction easily through search. Try it yourself to see more features, you'll love it!

Features:
- Add income/expense transactions under different category
- Add customize spending category name
- Add transactions quickly
- Instant summary for today/week/month/year
- Passcode lock
- Export through email
- View history records and compare them
- Graph report for 1, 3 or 6 months time period
- Separate graph report for income or expense alone
- Search for transaction by name, amount, note or any of them
- Polished UI, easy to use

hkell1234 , 04/23/2020

Love this app!

I love this app because it is completely manual and I have total control over everything that goes i it, versus apps that are connected to bank accounts. The only reason I give it 4 stars instead of 5 is because I wish it had auto fill for the categories. I find myself typing in the same names again and again, which is a waste of time. The other thing I wish it had is subcategories. For example, I would like to have one category be “home” expenses, and then be able to break that down into subcategories like rent, utilities and repairs. And just a heads up for new users, it doesn’t save your info past the calendar year. It still stores it and you can get all the data by exporting an excel file, but it won’t show up on the app. So that’s pretty annoying.

Jjb1223 , 10/08/2018

Fully accessible; easy management; useful extra paid features

I've been using this great app for almost 2 years, and I have been fully satisfied as I was searching for an app like this for quite a while. First, simple bank balance management, logging of transactions, and viewing transaction history are simple operations to perform and all information on these screens is fully accessible to VoiceOver users. Further, not only can you organize your expenses in deposits into categories, you can add customizable categories as well. Just a note, if you are looking for an app to synchronize online with your financial accounts, this is not the app for you! But, if you pay a couple extra bucks, you gain the feature of logging recurring transactions, adding as many as you wish. This is definitely my favorite feature as I can see how my monthly transactions will impact my account before those transactions occur. Also, there is a budget management feature in the app. If you are looking for a simple financial account management app which is also fully accessible, this app will satisfy your needs!
