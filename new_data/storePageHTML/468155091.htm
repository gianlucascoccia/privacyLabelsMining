Description

The *best* app for Twitter followers growth, tracking unfollowers, and more! Find out how Who Unfollowed Me provides simple, smart, and efficient tools to grow your Twitter network, get analytics, and to use search and filters to engage with exciting new people!

Now with Dark Mode!

Why you’ll love Who Unfollowed Me…

**Security is our Top Priority**
In order to help you get the most out of your network, we ask for your Twitter data permission — and we handle this responsibility with utmost care. We take all precautions necessary to ensure your privacy is respected and your information is secure.

We never follow or unfollow anyone by ourselves. We also never post any Tweets at all! No spam!

8 years of trust to back the claims!

**Use Data Intelligence**
Visualize your relationships and opportunities! See people who don't follow you back!, or even your fans whom you don't follow back. Heck, even people you are mutually following, your "friends"!

**Monitor Your Growth and Stats**
See everyone who followed or unfollowed you, and even those who you followed or unfollowed! See a visual chart of your followers growth over time! And this is just the beginning!

**Manage Blocked and Muted Users**
Easily manage the people you're blocking and muting from one place! It keeps getting better!

**Search Profiles and Leverage Your Network**
Easily search for names, bio, locations, or websites of people in your network!

**Advanced Profile Filters and Sorting**
Filter and sort people in your network based on 23 different search parameters, including when they last tweeted, joined twitter, or how much followers-to-following ratio they have! That's not even all! It's crazy!

**Access Full History of Followers and Unfollowers**
Go back to your first followers and unfollowers from when you first started using the app!

**Handle Large Twitter Accounts**
Ready to manage accounts of any size! We're successfully handling celebrity accounts with 16M+ followers!

**Your Relationship History** with others!
Go to anyone's profile, and you'll know when they followed / unfollowed you, and vice versa! Or even when you muted or blocked them! It remembers everything!

**First Class iPad Support**
Who Unfollowed Me is designed to work perfectly on iPhone as well as the iPad!

**Build-in Support Chat**
Come ask me directly in the Support Chat! :-)

Developer Response ,

Hey Weather Connisour! Why a subscription and not a one-off purchase?

Every so often users ask if it would be possible to make our Premium Subscription a one-time payment instead of a subscription.

Everyone likes money, but I believe that great software is honest and sustainable. This means we are creating a service that helps you get your job done both today and in the future.

I understand that the subscription-based sales model is double-edged sword. Some users may prefer a one-time fee, but development, servers, catching up to ever changing twitter api policies, and security are expensive and the Premium Subscription funds these activities.

Above all, I believe in an honest and sustainable business model, and I have priced the subscriptions as such to reflect what it costs me to keep the operations afloat.

Thank you for your support.

PS: We show the data as we receive from Twitter's API directly. We do not trick or mangle it in any way. Why would we…?

Regardless, I'll be happy to do whatever I can to help you get the best value. Just send me an email on support@whounfollowedmeapp.com and we can get started there.
