Description

Schedule appointments for all your services from bang cuts to barre classes—all in one app. Because you’ve got more to do with your time than sit on hold with salons and studios.

Schedulicity connects you to local professionals from 50+ industries ranging from Instagram-famous hair salons to of-the-moment nail artists to your neighborhood Crossfit gym. See available providers, service menus, and class schedules, and browse photos to see just how bright and clean that yoga studio is. Then instantly book an appointment or class and pay for it just like that.

How it works:

Search through the best of the best: Filter down results using our special booking filters, including date, location, and price. Want the closest gel manicure? Go for it.

Book instantly: Schedule an appointment or class that fits your schedule— in just a few taps.

Never pay a cancellation fee: Easily reschedule when your Monday goes all wrong, rebook for Tuesday when it’s all going to be alright, or just cancel a scheduled event. Plus, get friendly reminders right before your booking date—so those accidental no-shows are no more.
