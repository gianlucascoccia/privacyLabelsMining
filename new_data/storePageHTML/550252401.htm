Description

Pac-12 Now is your home for Pac-12 sports! Enjoy unlimited access to Pac-12 coverage, including live events, school-produced live streams, in-depth features on student-athletes, studio shows, and other programming across all sports in the Pac-12, the Conference of Champions. Select your favorite Pac-12 teams and set alerts in the Pac-12 Now app so you don’t miss a second of the action!

IMPORTANT: Access to most Pac-12 Networks content is determined by your TV provider. Currently, the TV providers in partnership with Pac-12 Now include: Comcast XFINITY, DISH, Charter Spectrum, Cox Communications, Bend Broadband, Frontier, Suddenlink, etc. A full list of distribution partners can be found at https://pac-12.com/getpac-12networks.

Pac-12 Now features:

• Unlimited access to all Pac-12 Networks channels: Pac-12 Network, Pac-12 Arizona, Pac-12 Bay Area, Pac-12 Insider, Pac-12 Los Angeles, Pac-12 Mountain, Pac-12 Oregon, and Pac-12 Washington.
• Live events and video-on-demand on any device, including your big screen, with Chromecast or Apple TV
• Scores for top sports
• Notifications and personalization for your favorite teams
• Live game action, school-produced live streams, highlights, feature stories, recaps, and exclusive Pac-12 Now video content

The member institutions of the Pac-12, Conference of Champions, are:
