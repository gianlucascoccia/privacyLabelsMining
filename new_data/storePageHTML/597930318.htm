Description

Take your Catholic faith with you wherever you go!

Bringing Christ to the world through the media, Relevant Radio creates a community of hope. Through this personal and intimate forum of communications, marriages are saved, souls draw closer to Christ and His Church, and the Truth sets many free. Be a part of this amazing journey!

With the Relevant Radio App, you have easy access to our live stream, podcasts, audio prayers, and stay on top of the latest news. It's a great way to engage in your faith on the go.

The app includes these great features:

• Live Streaming of all your favorite Relevant Radio shows
• Watch our daily Masses and the Family Rosary Across America live
• Podcasts - so you can listen on your schedule
• The exclusive home of the In Conversation with God podcast
• Access to Father Rocky's Lenten Lessons on the Mass and his Teaching Minutes
• Audio Prayers - including three versions of the Rosary
• Over 100 text prayers with beautiful art includes Latin prayers with translation into English
• Confession Helper, Daily Readings, and other faith resources
• Latest news in the Church and around the world
• Download Podcasts to listen later
• Find your local Relevant Radio station
• All of our app content is 100% free!

We use location services to display Weather conditions.

Saw a bumper sticker!

We travel a lot and work many days of the week for our job... needless to say, getting to church had become difficult, still my heart was hungry for God and the Gospel. Ect. When my kids were young , I would get there, but not as often as I should because my husband would be working on Sunday morning... our life has been difficult as of late... we had been out of town on work together for months. I came home and went to our home Church in Chicago, actually, Palatine IL.,St. Theresa Parish. As I was leaving mass thatSunday morning, I saw the bumper sticker- 950 AM Relevant Radio. Quickly I turned to it... Grace has come into our lives! After being married for 28 years , I have finally got my husband going to church- not Sunday but during the week- he is taking a morning away from his job and we go together!! I have been asking steadily for awhile and suddenly it has happened! I listen to the radio constantly when in the car and it has blessed us and our lives sooo much. I have been very nervous about going to confession and I think I’m going to go, after listening to Revelant radio! It brings me joy and warms my heart and small miracles seem to be happening... thank you, thank you thank you to those that started it. Sometimes it’s hard to get to church and this reaches so many when they can’t! And now we are back at church as well! Thank you Jesus and God and the Holy Spirit for this blessing ‼️❤️

OmaFaith , 05/18/2019

Learned what being Catholic means

Raised a Catholic, I left the Church in my early 20s. After I was married and had children, I made a choice to come back to the Church to have my children receive the sacraments. My husband became a Catholic about the same time. About 10 years after that, I discovered Relevant Radio. Since then I have learned so much and have grown so much stronger in my faith! I realize I never really knew my faith before. I was born Catholic but not until I was an educated adult in the faith, did I choose to be a fully practicing Catholic.
I listen to you everyday and pass on the things I learn to my family and friends all the time. I encourage everyone I can to listen to Relevant Radio!
I am so grateful that there is a radio station like this that helps us to grow closer to God, teaches the truth in faith, offers encouragement in an often discouraging world, provides news not found in mainstream media, and in giving hope brightens our day. I am grateful, THANK YOU!
