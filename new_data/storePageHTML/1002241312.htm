Description

The ONLY app w/ real-world generated hashtags and analysis.
*NEW* Custom fonts enabled in themes.
*NEW* Hashtag tracking and tailored notifications.

Things on Instagram evolve pretty quickly, but one thing that hasn’t changed is the importance of hashtags.
To this day, building an effective hashtag strategy is one of the best ways to get your posts discovered by new and relevant audiences. Depending on how relevant your hashtags are, this can mean more engagement, more followers, and if you're a business, it even can lead to more customers! 

All tags are NOT created equally.

Hashtags by StatStory uses a unique hashtag search algorithm that works in any language to make sure you are always given the most up to date hashtags for your posts on Instagram. We don't use out of date hashtags lists like all the other guys. Every time you search a hashtag we find relevant tags people are using in real time! Remember, a tag that was once popular, might not be popular now.

Simply enter a relevant hashtag or a word that describes the post, and we will return a custom-tailored list of hashtags based on the most popular posts that are similar to yours! Feel free to use obscure or specific terms, in ANY language. The app will show you the popularity of that tag and how many posts use it. Use a mix of popular and less popular tags to broaden your reach. You can tap and remove any tags you don't want.

Use Themes and hashtag Layouts to take your Instagram game to the next level. Put together greetings, signature lines, and separators to make your post stand out! You can even add your custom tag lines, and lists. Go crazy and experiment.

You can also use our pre-made lists that are automatically updated regularly.

We have 100+ lists for:
- Fashion
- Motivation
- Travel
- Sports
- Animals
- Health & Fitness
- Food & Drink
- Seasons
- Electronics
- Art
- & so much more!

Worried about your post getting ghost banned because of bad tags? Unlike other apps, when you use Hashtags by StatStory you never have to worry about getting blocked or restricted tags that can harm your account.

Instagram only allows 30 hashtags per photo. Our app counts your tags, including your personal tags, to make sure you do not go over.
