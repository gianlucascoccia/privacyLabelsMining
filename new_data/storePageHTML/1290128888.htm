What’s New

Oct 31, 2021

Version 4.45.3

Thanks for updating the app to the latest version!

We're super excited to share a massive amount of features, enhancements and stability improvements to simplify your day-to-day life with the monday.com mobile app, such as a newly redesigned board experience, the ability to Sign In with Apple, a new Share Sheet and much more!

Here are some of the big highlights of this release:

- Board redesign: We're rolling out an entirely new Board Experience for the app; keep your eyes open for it! We've also reworked and improved many existing columns and edge cases around those.

- Sign in with Social: Signing in to your monday.com account is now simpler than ever, simply use your Apple account or other available providers for a seamless login experience.

- Share photos to item: you can now share up to 10 photos as an update, directly from the Photos app. Simply select some photos from the Photos app, tap the Share button and choose the monday app.

- The home screen now updates in real time as you add and remove items, making sure you're constantly synchronized with the web platform.

- Files: Huge revamp to the stability of file uploads and consumption.

These are just some of the HUGE improvements to this release, not including dozens of bug fixes across the app and many other enhancements. Go ahead and explore! ;-)

We're constantly striving to improve your experience with app in every release, so we'd love your feedback!
