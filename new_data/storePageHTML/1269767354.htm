Description

Use the NABP e-Profile app to access CPE Monitor® and stop scrambling to keep up with CPE and license compliance.
• Take care of all your CPE activity & tracking from one dashboard.
• Verify how much CPE credit you need to earn to meet license renewal requirements.
• Add, update, and verify your license information within your e-Profile dashboard.
• Export detailed transcripts to send to boards.
Download the app and log in with your e-Profile credentials to access CPE Monitor.

Terms of Use: https://nabp.pharmacy/policies/terms-of-use/
