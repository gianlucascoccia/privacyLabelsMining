Description

The best App for Singers

What happens when you…
- have to sing but don’t know how to warm-up correctly for the type of music are singing?
- have an audition or recording and your voice teacher is not available?
- need to teach a fitness class and your vocal cords have been pushed to the limit?
Try the new MF Voice app and find vocal exercises, video demos, extensive educational information and tips to take your voice and singing to the next level.

MF Voice introduces a new patented algorithm system where you will answer a series of questions about your voice type, experience, vocal range and song genre creating a warm-up designed specifically for your voice and the type of music you are singing. Feeling tired or not so healthy today? Not a problem! Matt included an entire pack of exercises to help you slowly warm your vocal performance back into shape.

Try our intuitive vocal methods where you are guided through your daily exercises with Matt himself demonstrating the “how to” and “why” you should execute each voice building technique. After the video demo, practice with the revolutionary keyboard (automatically programmed based on the information you provided)! This is the key to success!

Whether you are an aspiring or a professional singer, a dancer, a fitness trainer or even just a voice enthusiast, MF Voice will be very beneficial to improve your vocal health, stamina and overall technique and understanding of the voice.
Learn how to align your posture, activate the right muscles and improve your breathing with a quick & easy training program to unleash your potential.
It’s like having a trusted vocal coach at your fingertips!

This is the best new app for singing and fitness instruction! See what the buzz is about! Start Your Vocal Lesson today...Happy Singing!

Matt Farnsworth gathers years of experience as a top voice teacher, actor and pianist in NYC where he worked with thousands of world-class voices like Sara Bareilles, Carly Rae Jepsen, Constantine Maroulis and Pia Toscano. Many of his students are Tony Award® Nominees and can be seen performing on Broadway, off-Broadway and National Tours and Feature Films. Matt himself has performed on Broadway and regionally opposite Idina Menzel, Alice Ripley, Sutton Foster, Norm Lewis, Gavin Creel, and many other amazing singers. He is excited to share his vocal knowledge and expertise with you today!
