Description

Start living your best life with WBKfit. Let Katya, Daisy & Elise transform your body & mind to the healthiest & fittest it’s ever been. We’ve transformed some of the best booties & bodies around the world with our programs & meal plans, and with two extra trainers, we’ve upped our game. 

Workout at the gym or at home with a variety of program options & meal plans tailored to your goals.

Misleading

I’ve purchased Katya clothing for myself and i was not thrilled. Swore I’d never buy again. But i want to see MORE results and saw some girls have accomplished a lot by following her workouts and i love Katya she is my #1 fitness idol. there was a bundle special so i thought I’d give it another chance. I downloaded the app and browsed through it, seemed great. I didn’t want to start the program just yet bc I’ll be away from the gym a few days so i just wanted to take a look at what i was to expect. I noticed that you can NOT adjust your start date. Once you activate your program it begins. I had to email katyas support team to fix my start date because the app does not have that feature. After contacting them i also found out after a few wks you will NO LONGER HAVE ACCESS to the work out program, meals, etc. They DO give you the chance to download the programs to pdf and email to yourself. Still don’t know how it works because i don’t have access to my workouts bc of my start date. Which i think is ridiculous after spending that much money. I think you should have the freedom to access the program when ever you want once purchased that is linked to your email through the app. If i knew all this ahead of time i would’ve looked into something else. For the little access i had everything looked easy and pretty straight forward.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for your honest review, our programs do indeed expire two weeks after completion and if you want to change the start date our support team will assist with that. The app is a free download so there is a limit to what we can access and hold on the app, which is why we incorporated the download button which will send the workouts to your email address. Good luck in your future. - Team Katya
