Description

With MyStory.today you can write short stories and novels on your computer, tablet or smartphone. Write whenever you want wherever you are.

Features overview:
- Story management with chapters and scenes
- Corkboard with multi-labels support
- Character and places management
- Rich text editor with many formatting options
- Dynamic character and places linking
- Spell check and basic grammar check
- Auto-sync to your smartphone, your laptop and PC
- Export to MS Word or LibreOffice Writer

Start writing your own novel, your short story or your screenplay today. You can write on all your devices with internet access so you have your own book with you when you're on the couch at home, in the super market, on the train or in the nature. Write, when inspiration comes.


Organize your book with chapters and scenes:
To get an overview you can organize your book intro small blocks. They're called chapters which contains multiple scenes. You can scribble your ideas on the integrated corkboard and move them around as you like. Add summaries to the cards on your corkboard and get a rough outline about your story. The corkboard automatically syncs with your story outline so everything is always up to date

- Dynamic story corkboard with cards
- Move them around with drag and drop
- Add new scenes and chapters on the fly
- Write summaries for chapters to get a quick overview

Integrated rich text editor with spell check:
With the powerful integrated rich text editor you write your texts on the fly. Format your content with ease like bold text, italic or indented and give your story some style. You can use this editor for your scenes, character descriptions and places.

- Rich text editor with many styles for your content
- Integrated linking for characters and places
- Spell check and basic grammar check
- Full-screen mode


Dynamic character and places linking:
When you create a new character or a place you can use the rich text editor to add them dynamically to your text. This will create a link to the character and if you rename it later, they update automatically in all your texts. So don't care about manually rename everything by yourself and take the time to write a next chapters for your book.

- Create characters and places
- Link them to your texts and they'll update automatically if things change


Book export:
If you're ready or if you want to share a sneak preview you can use the integrated book export. This will exports all your chapters with the scenes to your favorite word processor like MS Word or LibreOffice Writer. You can use this for sharing or even as a backup for yourself.


Syncs on all devices:
With your free MyStory.today account you can access your work on all of your devices with internet access. On your smartphone on the go, on your tablet or even on your laptop or desktop PC. This happens automatically - you don't have to export anything manually.

If you're ready to write - download the free App today and start writing your book right now!

Mimsington , 02/07/2020

Almost perfect.

I’m currently writing a book using this app. And it is doing a amazing job of keeping tack of all my information. Being able to jump over to character summaries and back to writing chapters is nice. The chapters are organized, and when I email myself the manuscript to back it up and edit my latest chapter, everything is organized and laid out almost like a book. It is so much easier to write a book on this app lounging around having it auto fill words, auto correct words. All this while not having to maintain a composed posture behind a keyboard. I have a full time job, and am a full time dad. And yet I’ve been able to write over 18,000 words in the last 10 days!
I do have 2 complaints. The first one, I have seen other bring this up too. Having to scroll all the way back to the top of the page to turn on/off italics or change font or whatever when you’re 2000 words deep into a chapter is a huge time waster. So I’ve taken to just writing plainly and then editing after I send the chapter to my computer.
The other complaint is that if the app crashes, or even just has a hiccup, I loose everything I was just writing in that chapter. I try to save things periodically. But when the creative juices are flowing eh? A couple times this has happened and I can’t remember exactly what i wrote. This is how you make people go crazy.
And that’s it. A solid app. Needs very little change to be the best.

TheorigionalLythia , 04/20/2020

Love it but room for improvement

I love it! Definitely the best writing app I’ve come across so far. The organizational system is so helpful! It even includes the option to view your work as a story board. The only things it’s missing are 1. Better compatibility with the iPad keyboard cases. As of right now the up/down and left/right arrows do not work in the app removing one of the easiest ways to edit on the ipad. 2. A formate where you can view all the chapters and scenes together as if you were viewing/writing them in a word Document or any document that lets you read and write page by page and 3. A more customisable section where you can add, store, and organize characters. It would be nice if you can add your own custom panels to the characters section and write in them like you can with the chapter scenes. At the moment there are only 2: “short summary” and “main description.” For example, making a new panel/scene and naming it “relationships” or “character flaws” then writing more detail in that section. 4. The ability to copy/duplicate scenes and chapters in case you like the formate of the panels of one and want to use the same set up again or just want to keep a copy of an older version your rewriting. 5. The ability to add pictures. Still, I think this is the best writing app I’ve used since the days of A Novel Idea.

1234Horselover99 , 11/10/2019

Perfect!

This is a great app. I love the chapter and character organization. My only complaint is that I wish I didn’t have to scroll all the way up to italicize my words. But other than that, thank you for this app, I recommend it for authors, even kid authors like me, to use for ideas. Of course, if you really want to get a book published, contact an editor and use things like word documents. That’s what I use, anyways. I did manage to make a book called, “The Star Rider,” about a girl who goes to summer camp, meets a horse, and realizes that she and the horse have a bigger connection than what she realizes, and that she and her horse have magic and that their world is being threatened. She and her friends, Alex and River, set out to save their friends, Katie and Luna, and destroy an evil, extraterrestrial monster that has lived underneath their world in a separate dimension since the beginning of time. Guess where I made this book? On this app! I transferred it to Word for the revision process, and I’m currently on chapter 7 out of 18 chapters. After that I will print it out and contact my agent and my editor most likely. On that note, please be on the lookout for that book especially if you like horses and a bit of magic! Thank you for this app, again, it is my #1 idea source for books/book series’s.
