Description

Get the ultimate knowledge base for Monster Hunter World Iceborne. Information regarding weapons, monsters and more.

This unofficial guide app for Monster Hunter World assists your journey and features detailed statistics for:

- Weapons
- Weapon Tree
- Weapon Stats (Attack, Element, Sharpness, Crafting Materials, etc.)
- Monsters
- Loot
- Hit Table
- Kinsect Color
- Items
- Crafting Table
- How to get them
- Quests
- Target Monster(s)
- Optional Monster(s)
- Rewards
- Skills
- Armors
- Decorations
- Charms
- Set-Builder
- Set-Searcher
- Motion Values
- Specialized tools
- Kinsect crafting
- Arena (detailed quest info)
- Endemic Life

Unlock the app to get access to the app-wide searchbar and get the information you need even faster!
Unlocking the app gives you also the option to change the app icon to a monster of choice and different app themes.

Super Useful and Easy to Use. Worth the Money!

This app transformed my sets in MHW. I always felt like I wasn’t utilizing my decos and resources to make the best sets I could. I put in my decos, ran a search for the skills I wanted and bam - 10+ ways to do it. So I tried to cram more skills in with my limited resources and I got a surprise - the app tells you if you can make it or not, and when you can’t, it gives you lots of different options of how to get close and shows you what slots you have left. And you can save sets or share them. Worth it!
If I were to wish for any additional features it’d be the ability to change color themes to different neutral backgrounds and for the sharpness of weapons to update depending on the weapon and the level or sharpness you are trying to add. A damage calculator would be over the top great, even though I can’t give more than 5 stars.
