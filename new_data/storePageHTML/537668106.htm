Description

What’s in store for you at the Simpsons Store?

From A (for “¬Aye, carumba!”) to Z (for “Zazz”), Matt Groening, the master of mirthful mischief, presents over twenty years of titles from his very own publishing company, Bongo Comics Group! The new Simpson Store app features an ever-expanding library of digital versions of the best humor books in the biz, including family-friendly and familiar titles such as Simpsons Comics, Bart Simpson, Treehouse of Horror, and many, many more!

So tap into the information superhighway for a super reading experience like no other. Now available on your iPad, iPhone or iPod touch, our cloud-based platform will automatically sync across your never-without-it-by-your-side iOS devices.

Your wish list is our command. What are you waiting for? The Simpsons Store is open for business!

ClearShooter15 , 12/04/2016

Very Enjoyable

So far so good. It has a good selection of comics for a good price. (Not a lot of free full issues just one I believe. But would love to see more free issues) Prices range from $1.99-$3.99 I had recently purchase a print issue for $4.99 but discovered it on this app for $1.99 so it's definitely a cheaper alternative. It does have a few crashes here and there but nothing painfully continuous. Maybe once or twice a day. I definitely would recommend this app if you are either a comic or Simpsons fan. It won't disappoint from the looks of it it looks like it can only improve.

Josho soso15 , 08/28/2018

The Simpsons!

Long time fan of the comic, the selection is great but not perfect. I would like to see even more FCBD issues make it onto the store but that being said the prices are decent . Good fun comics!

Big D in Indy , 04/26/2018

An Extensive Collection

I have downloaded nearly 150 back issues of Simpsons Comics onto my iPad. I once had all of them in print form but had to sell that collection when my well paying job got outsourced. I was glad to have discovered the Simpsons Store app. The only gripes I have are that it crashes quite often; is sometimes inaccessible and is in need of updates. Otherwise I am able to enjoy reading my Simpsons Comics, even when offline.
