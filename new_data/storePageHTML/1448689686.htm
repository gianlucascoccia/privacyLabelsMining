Description

GPS Field Area Measurement - Find Near By and Street view

Map Measure helps estimating the area for a region given by a set of coordinates.When you want to purchase or sell some property & you want to calculate area or distance of your property ,this will help you .

GeoMap Calculator use to measure your land or distance as to put the points on the map.

Area Calculator is use for to measure your land or property area and set pricing from that , So You can measure area & also calculate the price of your property.

Distance Calculator is use for to measure your land or road distance from one place to another place , So you can measure distance & also calculate the price of your distance.

Location Tracker is use to locate your point & save it for later , Whenever you want the location you got from that.

You can calculate property price in the measurement.

** Features **
== Manual Measure ==
User can measure area or distance manually by dropping a pin to particular coordinates.That area or distance shows in My Area & My Distance

== GPS Measure ==
User can measure area or distance automatically by walking or running to particular location coordinates.That area or distance shows in My Area & My Distance

== Location Tracker ==
User can saved their parking, home, office location by simply dropping point.

== Speedometer ==
User can measure his/her speed while driving or running with the awesome gauge view

== Compass ==
When you are lost somewhere , this will help you to get the direction
