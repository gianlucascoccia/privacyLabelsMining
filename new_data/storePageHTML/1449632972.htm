What I’d like to see in the next update

I would like to be able to add a profile picture and see other people’s pictures. If we get new employees, it could provide an easier way for them to become aquatinted with everyone and at least have an idea of who their coworkers are.

I also wish that we would get more notifications from this app. For example, if a manager posts a new schedule, or we have a shift change, or our time off gets approved. Maybe even a notification that we have upcoming shifts. And I think we should be able to adjust and choose which notifications we want in a settings component.

I think this app could also benefit from a messaging option between the employees and manager. This would provide an easy way to connect with your coworkers and ask if they could swap shifts or cover for you. When I first started working at TSC, I didn’t know anyone and felt intimidated to ask for anyone’s contact info. Creating a messaging option would allow for employees to easily communicate when not at work, without the stress of someone having your personal phone number.
