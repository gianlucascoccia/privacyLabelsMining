Description

List your items, sort them into categories and associate them with places of storage. Easily manage your inventory level and value.
Conceptualize and hierarchize your categories and places of storage.

Thanks to the built-in search tools, you can retrieve your items and their location(s) easily.

Separate your items or objects into coherent groups (work-related items, personal objects, etc.) in order to manage different stocks simultaneously.

For users wishing to monitor their stock levels precisely, Stock Control allows refined management of quantities in the various places of storage, as well as a shortage alert for each item.

Import / export all your data thanks to a CSV file (spreadsheet program) in order to use them on your PC or Mac.
Transfer your data to your other iOS devices with iCloud and iTunes transfer.

Generate PDF catalogs based on your item lists, and send them by e-mail or print them using AirPrint.

Freemium version, limited to 15 items. In-app purchase unlocks unlimited item creation

Universal application for iPhone/iPad, iOS 8 and above.

ceezeepee , 09/10/2021

Great software for managing inventory locations

I tried dozens of inventory apps. All were expensive and few had location transfer capabilities. I almost ignored this one as it takes a little getting used to at first but I now have been using it for a few months and it’s very powerful especially for those not wanting to pay hefty monthly subscriptions. I’m honestly surprised it doesn’t feature higher in online reviews. I create groups for stock of the same type and then have locations under each group. I also include sold as a location so I can track my sales. The logbook and changelog allow one to view history. The notes function allows for additional notes per item. It could use some improvements but overall it’s a really solid app and a great deal. I’m tracking over 50 types of goods or bundles across many locations all on my phone and can therefore pick up any discrepancies with the warehouse stock recon. Highly recommend!
