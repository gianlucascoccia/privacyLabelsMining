Description

A DOCTOR’S CARE. ANYTIME. ANYWHERE.

Teladoc® connects you with a U.S. board-certified doctor via web, phone or app. Join over 50 million members and get quality, convenient healthcare and therapy on demand.

Teladoc provides quality healthcare services, including virtual primary care, everyday care, mental health, nutrition, dermatology and expert medical advice in all 50 states via phone, online or app. We have thousands of board-certified doctors, dermatologists, therapists, licensed nurses, nutritionists and leading specialists who can diagnose, recommend treatment and provide prescription medication for many medical issues, including:

- Non-emergency conditions like allergies, flu and sinus infections
- Eczema, psoriasis, rashes, acne, aging and skin care
- Depression, anxiety and relationship issues
- Nutrition and wellness consults
- And more

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Request a visit: Open the Teladoc app to request a visit with a doctor, therapist or dietitian. Then, provide a brief medical history and associated information about your health concern, and pay a copay as low as $0 (with insurance) or a flat fee (without).

Talk to a doctor: For General Medical services, a board-certified doctor reviews your medical history and any submitted images, then contacts you via phone, video or message center. Teladoc visits, including General Medical, Mental Health, Nutrition and Expert Medical Opinion, have no waiting room and no time limit; you can speak to the doctor for as long as you’d like.

Pick up a prescription: If applicable, a Teladoc doctor will listen to and discuss medical concerns with you, offer answers and recommend a treatment plan. If medically necessary, prescription medication can be submitted to a local pharmacy of your choice.

HOW DO I GET TELADOC?

Teladoc is a virtual care service provided to you as a benefit through your employer, health insurance or other organization. If you aren’t eligible for Teladoc through insurance or your employer, you can still access our telehealth services at a flat rate per visit. Visit Teladoc.com or download the app to learn more.

Some doctors on here seem hesitant to do anything when they can’t see their patients in person. I don’t use this unless I’m certain about an issue and it’s easier for me to use the app then go in or haul little kids in. One incident was when I knew my daughter had a UTI and we had a horrible blizzard coming in. I then had to rush to urgent care where it was confirmed but by this time the blizzard had come in and we couldn’t make it to the pharmacy due to there being accidents everywhere. We were lucky we made it home. I also know when my kids have an ear infection. It’s usually a secondary infection that follows a cold for them and then they get a fever and ear pain.
