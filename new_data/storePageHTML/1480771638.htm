Description

SHREDDY is the only app you need to achieve your transformation goals, no matter what they are. Join over 250,000+ SHREDDERS who are getting REAL results today.

Find a workout guide created by our SHREDDY PT with a science-backed technique that’s perfect for you. Whether you’re a beginner or experienced, if you’re training in the gym or at home, have complete control over your diary with our customisable calendar. Follow a meal plan specifically tailored to your goals and dietary needs, designed by our expert nutritionist and get a serious sweat on in 30 minutes or less with the real-time classes!

To keep you motivated we’ll be uploading fresh content every single month! From recipes to workouts, challenges to brand new features, we’ll be keeping it fresh and exclusive to the SHREDDY app.

Join Team SHREDDY and you’ll have access to:

WORKOUT GUIDES FOR ALL ABILITIES AND GOALS
SHREDDY is for everyone, whether you’re just starting out on your fitness journey or looking to build even more progress; we have exercises for all levels and all goals. Our workout programmes are tailored to your preferences and goals. With over 14 guides to choose from, there’s something for whatever your goal. If you’re brand new to training, short on time or looking to further your fitness journey there’s a guide on SHREDDY for you.

Following a workout guide in the gym has never been easier with technique recommendations and video references for exercises so you always feel supported, a weight and rep tracking feature for each set ensuring progressive overload for real progress and a built-in rest time feature.

Our home workouts are perfect for those who prefer to train at home or in a quieter area of the gym, minimal equipment is needed for the workouts and maximum results are guaranteed!

REAL-TIME CLASSES TO GET YOU SWEATY IN NO TIME
Our real-time classes range from 7-30 minutes to ensure you can get a killer workout wherever and whenever you need it. These workouts need little to no equipment making them perfect for when you can’t make it to the gym and are led by qualified PTs with regressions and progressions available for all exercises.

DELICIOUS MEAL PLANS FOR EVERY DIET TYPE
100’s of recipes formulated by our expert nutritionist covering all dietary types. We’ll assign you a meal plan to help you reach your goals or just maintain a healthy lifestyle depending on your goals.

A SUPER SUPPORTIVE COMMUNITY OF WOMEN
We’ve always been known for our interactive and supportive community on Instagram, and now you can find it on the app too! Start chats, get to know others, make supportive groups and join the in-app forums on everything from workouts to meals to general discussions.

YOUR FITNESS JOURNEY IN ONE PLACE
You’ll have the most comprehensive profile where you can stay on top of your fitness journey. Through Apple Health integration, progress pictures, measurements, write diary entries and check all your health stats in one place!

KIT LAUNCHES AND RESTOCKS BEFORE ANYONE ELSE
You’ll have early access to all new kit and supplement launches and restocks, so you can grab everything you need for your fitness journey before anyone else!

trixcore , 12/08/2019

Finally a workout app I can get behind

I find it really helpful to have my workouts written out before heading into the gym AND there are videos to follow along with. This is kind of standard for these apps but where Shreddy prevails is in having a tracker for your weights used, a community chat (where I’ve made actual friends), and additional workout videos you can follow along with. What really sold this for me was that the meal plans are vegan and plentiful. Any other app I’ve tried pretends to have ‘vegan’ and ‘vegetarian options’ but in reality it’s 2 basic meals on repeat. Shreddy has very thoughtful meals that are easy to prepare and are darn tasty.

There have, of course, been minor glitches with the app. BUT what I think is amazing is how darn fast the developers/app team has responded to fixing them. What I appreciate is that they really listen to their users and have been pumping out updates and fixes. I can’t wait to see what’s next for this app. Shout out to the hard working Shreddy team

okc52 , 10/28/2019

Great Content, App needs work!

I love the workouts and recipes provided, and the weekly setup and ability to change workout days and meals. But there are a lot of things I think the app is missing. For starters, I would love to see a rest timer that can be used in between sets and not just in between exercises. A timer that you can add or subtract 5 or 10 seconds from if needed, and that has some sort of bell or signal when the timer is done. Also when selecting the reps done for an exercise, if you change the number of reps, it saves that number for your next set, but I would expect it to reset to the original number each time since that is the number you’re supposed to be doing. For the meals page, it would be nice to have access to the total macros and calories of your selected meals so that I don’t have to look at each recipe separately and add them up. It could be a button you see at the bottoms that simply says “View nutrition” that way, like Grace wanted, the numbers aren’t all in your face. And lastly, my app never crashes, but if I leave the app during a workout it takes a few seconds to load back up, even if I just open my notifications bar. Would love to see a more seamless transition there.

Developer Response ,

Thank you very much for your feedback. We can reassure you that we have a passionate team in place which will continue improving the app and fixing all issues reported to us as quickly as possible. We are already working on some of the things you mentioned; going forward, it would be great if you could submit all of your ideas to us as feature requests as this helps our team keep track: https://shreddy.com/feature-request/
