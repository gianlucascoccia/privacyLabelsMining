Description

When all you do is serve fresh, delicious desserts to people in your community — every day feels like a celebration. Join in our celebration as we offer all the benefits of Sweet Rewards on your mobile app!

The Bruster’s Ice Cream mobile app is the most convenient way to manage your Sweet Rewards program, meaning earning rewards is easier than ever. See below for some of the apps key features.

Key Features:

Store Locator: Find a Bruster’s nearby no matter where you are.
Rewards: Every dollar you spend earns you 1pt towards a Sweet Reward.

Now available at participating locations in the United States.
