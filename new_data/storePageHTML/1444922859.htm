Description

** THIS APP WILL NOT FUNCTION WITHOUT HARDWARE. This is the companion app for Tempur-Pedic® Sleeptracker® AI Monitors. ONE OF THESE DEVICES IS REQUIRED TO USE THIS APP. **

POWERED BY SLEEPTRACKER® AI: BETTER SLEEP
Learn about your sleep - and improve your sleep - with nothing to wear or charge!

This is the companion app for the Tempur-Pedic® Sleeptracker® AI Monitor which makes your bed a smart bed. The Sleeptracker® system is the first cloud-based, non-invasive IoT sleep optimization solution powered by artificial intelligence (AI) and integrated into the smart home.

The app provides detailed daily sleep graphs and sleep metrics derived from accurately and continuously monitoring the sleeper’s respiration rate, heart rate, and movements throughout the night. Easy-to-understand sleep graphs visually distinguish between periods when you were in REM sleep, light sleep, deep sleep, or awake. The AI Sleep Coach delivers personalized tips and insights to help you improve your sleep every night.

AI SLEEP COACH
The AI Sleep Coach, powered by the Sleeptracker® Artificial Intelligence Engine, provides effective, easy-to-implement, personal sleep tips based on a comprehensive analysis of individual sleep patterns.

RESPIRATION AND HEART RATE MONITORING
The Sleeptracker® system monitors both respiration and heart rate accurately through the night for deeper sleep analysis, with your data displayed as easy to read charts in the app.

SLEEP CYCLE ALARM
Optionally select white noise that gently fades away after you've fallen asleep, then set a sleep cycle alarm that will help you Wake Up at the Optimal Time™ in your sleep cycle so you wake up refreshed and more energized.

AUTOMATIC SLEEP MONITORING
If you choose to record your sleep automatically, there’s nothing to do and nothing to wear. Optionally receive a notification in the morning when your sleep results are available and a daily email with your sleep summary and AI Coach insights.

APPLE HEALTH
Share your Sleeptracker® sleep data to the Apple Health app.

The Sleeptracker® system reports the following sleep-related statistics each night:
- REM sleep, light sleep, deep sleep, awake time
- Time at which you fell asleep and woke up
- Number of times awoken during the night
- Continuous respiration rate
- Continuous heart rate
- Sleep quality metric (scale of 0-100)
- Sleep efficiency (time spent in bed vs. total time asleep)
- Time it took you to fall asleep

New setup

I bought a cal king tempurpedic with the power base. I had a couple issues with setup. The barcode on the tag did not match the barcode on the tracker box. So after 3 failed attempts using the tag barcode, I had to get a flashlight and lay on the floor to get the barcode off the tracker box. Having the cal king means the two bases have to be slaved together. The delivery guys slaved them for the power controls, but not for the tracker. I had to figure out to disconnect one sensor from the right side tracker box and connect it with the other sensor in the left side tracker box. Not a big deal for me, but delivery guys should know this and the instructions were a little ambiguous. Since I just set up on the app, I can’t comment on the accuracy or sensitivity yet. Looking forward to it. So far the mattress and the power base have proven to be all I expected. I’ll know more about the actual tracker features later, but happy to see the right integration with Apple’s iPhone health capabilities.

Update -after two weeks, its almost scary how many metrics this tracker captures. Idk how it knows when I’m asleep or awake. Let alone deep/light or REM sleep. Heart rate and breathing rate without a device attached to me? room conditions as well. The long term analysis will prove useful to see if my sleep improves over time.

So far, I am very happy with the bed, the tracker and the app!
