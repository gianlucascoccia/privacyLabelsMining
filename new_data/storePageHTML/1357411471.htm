Vampyrez96 , 02/05/2019

Needs Fixing

My job recently started doing our schedules online and we would now view it on the app. However, there needs to be some sort of notification when anything pops up in the “Activity History” section. That way, I wouldn’t have to check every single day to see if anything on my schedule has been changed. I also wish I wasn't constantly being signed out of the app. It’s very annoying to be wanting to quickly check my schedule, then to have to waste time and sign in. And I also wish there was a way to view everyone’s schedule as a whole, that way, I know who I am working with, and who doesn’t work that day.

Developer Response ,

Thank you so much for your feedback, Vampyrez96 — we appreciate it, and want to apologize for our delayed response in getting back to you! As you may have already experienced, we’ve been making strides in improving the Employee Central app (as well as our full suite of products) throughout the pandemic. It is feedback like yours that led to updates such as fixing the logout issue as you’ve described, and also adding notifications to our roadmap for late 2021-early 2022, so again, thank you so much!


We hope you’ve had an improved experience since sharing your feedback and if so, we’d greatly appreciate an update on how things are working for you today. Thanks again for sharing with us!

We hope you’ve had an improved experience since sharing this and if so, we’d greatly appreciate an update on how things are working for you today. Thanks again for your patience and understanding.
