Description

Install now and enjoy a 4,500 UPX sign up bonus!!!

Built at the intersection of the real and virtual world, Upland gives you the power to buy, sell and trade virtual properties based on real-world addresses, just like the one you live at today… but in the virtual world. 

The digital properties you collect in Upland don’t have any association or rights to the correlating properties in the real world, however, the addresses do mirror real-life locations. Upland’s economy is powered by an in-game currency called UPX (pronounced “UP-EX”).

Once you own a property in Upland, you can start to collect UPX earnings on it every 3 hours. Pretty nifty, right?

Complete property collections in order to boost your UPX earnings and win one-time UPX award.

Trade your properties with other Uplanders in the marketplace - for UPX of for other properties in order to grow your property portfolio and UPX earnings!

Have you spotted a property that you want which is already owned by someone else? No problem! you can always make an offer for the property - even if it's not actively up for sale on the marketplace!

Huge investment potential

Started playing yesterday. Tutorials have helped understand how to play. Purchased UPX and bought over 30 properties so far. Mostly in NYC.

One thing that would be good for the Upland team to fix is the limits to sending and the awkward wandering the explorer does. Should have the ability to control wandering direction.

Payouts should continue to stack, that’s not the issue, so don’t change that. I suggest adding explorer leveling system where you earn experience by distance traveled, visiting properties, performing actions at properties, etc.. Make the levels increase the yield of properties you own. There need to be ways to take UPX out of circulation, which is where having other facets in the game could be leveraged.

johnny 4 3/4 , 06/11/2020

If you’re not willing to pay to play, PASS

This looks like a great game. It has a great concept and the I can only imagine what I could do with more coins. You have to acquire a net worth of over 10,000 coins to move out of “Visitor” status and become an “Uplander”. But that’s the problem; you’re gifted 3,000 or 6,000 coin to start and the minimum property value is ~2,400 coin. If your property accrues 1-2 coin/day (what my two are) it will take MONTHS, to achieve the 10,000 coin level. Full game function (the marketplace where you can buy/sell/trade properties) doesn’t become available until you cross that threshold. At the moment, the only way to get more coins is the wait (an exorbitant amount of time, seriously, do the math at 2 coins per day, needing 2,600 more coins would take 3 and 1/2 YEARS), OR you can BUY coins.

Great game, amazing process, bravo for putting together an awesome app where the blockchain integrates to real world assets. Needs serious attention to gameplay for beginners.

Everything about this game screams “PLAY ME!!” But I can’t. I can’t buy anything else, I can’t sell or trade what I already have, I can’t fill any more collections to generate coins faster, I can’t do anything but collect a few coins a day but at this point, I’ll probably just come back weekly to renew my earning timeline or cut my time lost, delete the app and let what I have cycle back into the system for someone who wants to pay to play. Shame.
