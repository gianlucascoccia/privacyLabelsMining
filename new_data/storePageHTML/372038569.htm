Description

Maximize your Hersheypark® experience with The Official Hersheypark Mobile App! Find information on rides, attractions, entertainment, food, shopping, and more for Hersheypark, ZooAmerica® North American Wildlife Park, and Hershey's Chocolate World Attraction. Plus, navigate through the Park with our customizable map and create an HPGO account to view personalized ride stats and ride photos.

Map Features
- Show and hide attractions of interest to customize your own map of Hersheypark and ZooAmerica
- Use the ‘Get Directions’ function for turn-by-turn navigation to any location*
- Create ride wait time alerts for your favorite rides
- Easily locate important services such as Ticketing Services, First Aid, Accessibility, Lost Children, Lost & Found, Barking Lot Day Kennel, and more
- Locate restrooms, ATMs, lockers, smoking gazebos, and stroller/wheelchair rentals

HPGO Features
- Create and manage your HPGO account to collect ride stats, view ride photos, and connect your Fast Track purchases
- Personalize your experience through your HPGO profile
- Create reminder alerts for your favorite shows
- Create a to-do list

Attraction Details
- Learn detailed information about all rides, shows, games, shops, services
- View current ride information including closed rides and ride wait times
- Search function to help find information about attractions
- Filter/sort results by height, distance, name, and more
- View daily entertainment and Hershey Character appearance schedules
- Find information on food and drinks, including menus, ingredients and allergens
- View hours for area attractions, including Hershey's Chocolate World Attraction, Hershey Story Museum, and more

*Continued use of GPS running in the background may decrease battery life.

Fun filled application to make your Hershey visit that much more enjoyable

This application is setup very user friendly. A must for a park visit if U are still learning what it has to offer. This application tells U everything, park hours, show times, rides (which U can filter by type or size restrictions). Application can even be used to save your parking spot. Tells U all wait times of rides and best of all directs U on how to get to everything with GPS. Description of all rides and even video descriptions. Worked very well with no crashes!! I will be using on every visit.
Wish there was a way to create a list of rides you personally want to go on and check off as U go or have application direct you in the quickest walk to them or sort by ride current wait times.
Currently U can filter by type and rider size and it does auto sort by closest to farthest which is useful.
