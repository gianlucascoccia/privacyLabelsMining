Description

The newest game from the Candy Crush Franchise! With updated graphics, fun new game modes and a host of friends to help you blast through hundreds of levels!

Your Candy Crush Friends have been scattered throughout the Candy Kingdom - Join this match 3 puzzle game to find them! This friendly bunch have their own unique powers to help you to create sweet candy combinations and blastthrough blockers!

Switch and match candies to unlock rewards, friends, and sweet collectable outfits! The more candies you collect the greater your power on the game board!

This fun new Candy game is filled with delicious treats - cookies, jam, chocolate and more! Your favourite game modes, but sweeter than ever!

Candy Crush Friends Saga features:
• Hundreds of levels to play – collect your favorite friends along the way!
• Your Candy friends are your allies – they are ready to help you blast through levels!
• Watch Yeti and his friends dance when you win levels!
• Fun new game modes; dunk the cookie in the chocolate & free the octopuses and the mammoths!
• Keep your friends in a sticker book and change their outfits!
• Explore sweet new worlds in 3D!
• Easy to play but challenging to master!
• Play whenever, online and offline. Easily sync the game between devices and access full game features when connected to the internet.
• Take on this epic saga alone or play with friends to see who can get the highest score!

makyahqassk , 10/22/2019

Good

So my last review got deleted due to foul language. I’m usually cursing while playing this game but anyways, it’s a good game. The concept of the friends helping you is the best I’ve seen throughout the CC Saga series. I’m never stuck on levels for weeks on end like it’s counterparts. The Bubblegum event is great, very rewarding and I’d hope to see it more frequently. But of course this game has its negatives. I don’t really like the rewarding system at all. You can’t start a level with 2x fish, or any special candies. Only the three boosters they provide, which in itself is frustrating. You get increments of the rewards instead of just a full reward. Then on top of that, you’re playing for costumes. Time and energy is simply going into changing how the characters look, not with how much they help you. Sometimes they don’t even help you and put their powerups in the most ridiculous of places. And that leads me to my final topic, the fish. In all CC Saga games the fish are pretty much useless but this game takes the cake!!! The fish go in the legit opposite spot that would help you. Swipe a fish which a wrapped/striped candy? It will go in the spot where is least helpful. I can’t stand it. You’ll have one move left and the fish won’t help you will whatsoever. But all in all, I play this game a lot. It isn’t my favorite CC game but it is fun and less frustrating at times.

Croftent , 10/16/2020

Only 1 Frustration

I absolutely love this game. I play a lot of different types of games, but this game is by far my favorite. I waited until I was far into the game to write a review, as we often feel differently about the game when it starts getting hard. I am on level 569 right now. I do wish some of the levels were easier, but I have only one frustration. When we earn rewards, I wish the hearts were refilled as well. It does not do much good when you earn a reward that is only good for 30 minutes or 2 hours if you only have 1 heart left or even worse, zero hearts! I really hope the developers will take note of this. Refilling hearts when earning rewards would be an awesome improvement!!

I am now on level 1197. All of the above is true, however I find it annoying to “win” prizes that are not prizes. Instead, the monitor moves up the scale so in reality, you earn nothing. I wish this Candy Crush game would take some lessons from the original game where there are lots of prizes and opportunities to earn unlimited hearts for a period of time - also, The team races on the original game are really fun. So - more prizes, please!!

Minnie8188 , 07/21/2021

Love it, buuuut...

This is my favorite game! It’s very fun. However, I only gave it four stars for a couple of reasons. 1. I get points after passing some levels, and they aren’t very helpful. Sometimes, you only get about two or three points. You have to play FOREVER to collect a lollipop hammer or a supercharge of a character’s power. Also, when I am able to open a chest, there is not much in it except for a few points! I don’t even worry about trying to get to it anymore. 2. Why are there people that I don’t know playing along with me that are showing up as if they’re my friends? They have animal faces and just say “player”. When I go to my profile, there is no option to change my picture to something else, so it truly seems like there are strangers showing up when I pass them or when they pass me. What is the point of this, and can I stop it?
3. It would be great if I could swap out more than just one of the three daily goals. Some days, I can’t fulfill any of the challenges because of the level that I’m on…for example: I needed the Red Rabbit (whom I dislike very much) to collect red candies, but the level I’m on doesn’t have him as an option to use. It’s happened more than once that the Red Rabbit was TWO of the three challenges on the same day! Horrible!
