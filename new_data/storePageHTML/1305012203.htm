Description

Florish is the complete indoor plant companion app, designed for those new to indoor gardening who may need help picking new plants for a space, or caring for the ones they already own. We are a new company and are looking for beta testers who want to stop killing their plants but don't know where to start.

Here are some ways you can use this app to grow a green thumb:

Use your phone's camera to scan the light in your room and understand what can thrive before wasting money on plants that are not suitable for your space. Our database includes many of the most common houseplants (no outdoor plants yet, sorry) and is growing every day. Don’t see your plant in our database? Submit a photo and we’ll add it to our queue of upcoming plants! All plant descriptions are written by experts who have actually grown the plants and can offer their actual pro tips, no recycled content here.

If you find a plant you like, buy it on our web store and have it delivered to your home or office (select plants and markets only).

Already own plants? Add them in the Florish app and start receiving watering notifications immediately. Our plant database also includes care instructions and common issues so you can troubleshoot problems.

Learn more about plant care and get inspired in our discover section where we aggregate tips and design trends in a regularly updated feed.

Have a new feature in mind you'd like to see? Email hello@florish.co to submit and thanks for Beta testing!

Don't forget to join our 10k+ strong @florish Instagram community for ongoing posts with plant inspo, tips, and tricks!

But now my light meter is t working at all and says that I need more light. I deleted the app and downloaded it again, but once again received the same message.
There a couple of other issues. The plant list they have is ok and growing I’m sure, but I’m not sure how long it takes for them to add new ones. Also, I wasn’t receiving reminders unless the app was opened, but that seemed to have fixed itself.
Besides those two things, he app is easy to use, the plant descriptions look good, and I like that it gives you the reminder.

SophiaDeStefano , 07/09/2019

Good for some things…

I really enjoy this app! The design is very sleek and it’s easy to maneuver. However, the default ’until next water’ amount of days it decides is frustrating because I cannot change or edit that amount of time depending on what my plant needs. If I didn’t know better and just followed this I might overwater my plants and we all know what can happen if we do that (for example any houseplant should have completely dry soil before watering, and depending on the size of the pot might not be dry after the default 7 days). Also, checking off plants that have been watered is a long process and is only available with those pop ups once you open the app which isn’t very simple and isn’t customizable. Besides those things, the app is interesting and generally helpful for new plant parents!
