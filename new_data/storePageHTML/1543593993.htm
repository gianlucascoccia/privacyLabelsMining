Description

Note Recognizer - App that Assists Music Learning by Detecting and Processing Musical Characteristics from Microphone in Real-Time

The Note Recognizer is meant for people who are seriously involved in active music making.

The Note Recognizer analyses the pitch frequency that you sing or play, and gives the corresponding note name. Very useful for singers who need to find the right starting note of a song. Or if you want to know the key of a song; sing the tonic, and you get the note name of the key. Also great as a tuner for guitar, or checking your intonation on flute, violin or any instrument.

User can choose from different note naming systems such as
- Standard (#)
- Standard (b)
- German (#)
- German (b)
- Indian Sargam (#) (Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Dha Ni Sa)
- Indian Sargam (b) (Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Dha Ni Sa)
- Indian Sargam (#) (सा रे गा माँ पा धा नि)
- Indian Sargam (b) (सा रे गा माँ पा धा नि)
- Do Re Mi (#)
- Do Re Mi (b)

Now with Live Notation: play your musical idea on an instrument and have it transformed to musical notation. Watch your score grow while playing.
