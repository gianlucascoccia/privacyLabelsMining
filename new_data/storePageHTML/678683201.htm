Description

Enjoy a seamless renting experience from start to finish with Zumper, the largest rental marketplace. Discover apartments, houses, or rooms for rent that match your preferences, request virtual or in-person tours, submit applications, and pay rent. All in one app.

BROWSE LISTINGS
Discover millions of places for rent throughout the U.S. and Canada with high-quality listings, images, and videos.

SET FILTERS
Choose your preferred price range, bedroom count, neighborhoods, and amenities, to customize your search experience.

CREATE ALERTS
Get real-time notifications when new places that match your preferences hit the market.

BOOK TOURS
Request virtual or in-person tours with just one click.

PAY RENT
Enjoy one-tap rent payments with credit or debit cards, Apple Pay, or bank transfers, and never write a paper check again.

INSTARENT
Reserve Instarent apartments and lease them completely online in 24 hours or less. Available in New York and Chicago.

MANAGE LISTINGS
Landlords can post properties for rent and manage their listings right in the Zumper app.

A friend recommended Jeremy to me and within a couple of hours we were on the phone going through 15 options he had pulled for me in the area based on my preferences. A few hours later I had found multiple apartments I liked and one that I loved - he even helped me to get some extra deals before signing the lease. After moving so many times and how stressful it was finding an apartment every other time, I never knew it could be this easy and downright enjoyable. I give all the credit to Jeremy and would recommend him to ANYONE who is looking to move to the Chicago area - he was excellent!

Scammers, unprofessional and unreliable

I did everything from filling out the financials, also paid to have an updated credit report ready for review so when I chose an apartment the owner or management company had access to it. Unbeknownst to me very apartment I was interested told me they cannot view it on Zumper. Why? Because they have to be on Zumper as well. The apartments are rented out and retrieved from another website. There’s no full disclosure telling you that some owners and management companies cannot view the credit report they had you purchase. The information is unreliable and a scam.

I tried reaching out to them to help me and they receive responded very late. Also the response was not pertaining to my question at all. I tried telling them and they gave another response not pertaining to my concerns. If you want to find an apartment in a certain area or neighborhood, I suggest to drive out there or have someone drive you around and look for yourselves, you get better deals and get to see the apartments up close and not in an app taken by professionals to make them look like they’re worth more than actual price. All the renting apps are alike.

Save yourself the time, money and headache. DIY!

Developer Response ,

Sorry to hear that and we're working hard to keep the community trustworthy and safe! Please email support@zumper.com with the suspicious properties and we'll investigate asap.

Very Helpful Most of the Time

Overall I had a great experience using Zumper. But I wish there was a way to hide listings from your results and to stop getting notified about them (maybe there is a way and I just could figure it out. I kept getting new alerts or clicking on listings I already knew didn’t fit my criteria but there was no way to hide them which made it confusing sometimes.
I also think having a smoke free option in the filters would be a great addition. Lastly, I know this isn’t so much the fault of Zumper, but I wish the realtors, managers, etc. that were responding to messages sent through Zumper had some kind of script or format to follow that at least had them say what their properties address or name was. I was continually receiving emails about properties that were vague and had almost no information so I had no idea what listing they were referring to.
But overall, good experience and very helpful for finding an apartment.
