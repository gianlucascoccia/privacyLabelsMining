Description

Addons Factory is a simple toolbox for creating your own mods and addons for MCPE. It is very easy to use but in the same time it has a lot of features and content. Addons factory contains more then 10000+ skins for any entities, blocks and tools that exists in Minecraft PE. With Addons Factory, creating addons has become even easier!

Features:
- Add Car in your world.
- Add Buildings and Castle in your world.
- Edit and create new mobs.
- Edit Biomes.
- Edit Block and model of the block.
- Edit Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, Boots, Armor.
- Edite Weapon
- Add Gun to your world.
- Edit Food proprieties.
- Create your Recipe.
- Create your Function.
- Also you can add Skulls, Heads, Paintings.

Just open the app, press the " Create add-on " button and add your first entity. You can edit every mob, armor, weapon, block, projectile, tool, food type that exists in Minecraft PE. After adding your first entity you can add another one or more. Addons Factory doesn't have limits you can add as many entities and changes as you. After your addon is done, don't forget to add a name, description and an avatar for it. Save it and install it directly to Minecraft PE by pressing the " Install " button.

Note: For full access of the features that Addons Factory provide, the app can ask your accept the access to your device camera and photo gallery. Your images will be used only to add avatars and icons for your addons. We don't save, stock and share any of your photos and images.

Addons Factory provides one Premium Subscription. The subscription plan is renewing automatically but you can cancel it any time. The name of the plan is: Addons Factory Special. The duration of the plan is one week (which comes with a 3-day free trial) and the price is 3.99 USD per week.
