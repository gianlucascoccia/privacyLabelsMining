Description

Get easy access to your ReadyRefresh® account and keep your favorite beverages stocked with a few taps on your phone. It’s simple and convenient to create and manage your deliveries, browse and shop products. You can even view invoices and make secure payments on the go.

Key Features:

-Manage your recurring products
-Make changes to your future deliveries and keep track of when to expect them
-Reschedule, skip or cancel deliveries
-Create new, one-time deliveries
-View past deliveries
-Find our latest beverages, water dispensers and supplies
-View product details and add the products you want to your deliveries
-Be made aware of out-of-stock items in your shopping experience
-Unlock savings when you add products with special offers to your cart
-Access your invoices if you are set up for recurring deliveries
-See account balances, make payments, update payment methods, manage autopay and opt-in or out of paperless billing
-Edit account details
-Manage notification preferences
-Manage multiple accounts
-Refer a Friend


ReadyRefresh® takes care of all of your healthy hydration needs at your home or office. We carry a variety of bottled water, sparkling water, flavored water, enhanced water, 3- and 5- gallon water jugs and water dispensers. We also carry iced teas, sparkling fruit beverages, energy drinks, hot chocolate and a variety of supplies. Some of our most popular brands include Acqua Panna® Natural Spring Water, Arrowhead® Brand 100% Mountain Spring Water, Deer Park® Brand 100% Natural Spring Water, Ice Mountain® Brand 100% Natural Spring Water, Perrier® Carbonated Mineral Water, Poland Spring® Brand 100% Natural Spring Water, S. Pellegrino® Sparkling Natural Mineral Water, Sanpellegrino® Italian Sparkling Drinks, Zephyrhills® Brand 100% Natural Spring Water and our own Nestle Pure Life® Purified Water and Nestle Splash Flavored Water Beverage.
