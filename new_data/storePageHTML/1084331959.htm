Description

Days is a simple easy-to-use personal and sobriety counter with a beautiful and clean interface. No bells and whistles. Just install the app and start counting. Best of luck!

You can choose to see the length of your sobriety in days, weeks, months, years or those combined. You can also override your counter by either providing a new number of days or a new start date.

If you want to keep a close eye on your counter, Days also has a small nifty Today widget.
