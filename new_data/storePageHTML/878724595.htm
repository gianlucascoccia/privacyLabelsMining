Description

***** WONDERFULLY COMPREHENSIVE READING PROGRAM FOR BEGINNING READERS (AGES 2-8).
*****Perfect for pre-K, kindergarten, and 1st grade children.
*****Join over 40,000,000 kids who love learning with award-winning 22learn apps.

Learn to read with monkey Abby! Abby's Reading Adventure is a wonderfully comprehensive reading program for beginning readers (ages 2-8). Discover how your child can turn from a non-reader to a reader with our step-by-step proven educational activities children adore.

Comprehensive early reading program: PHONICS, SIGHT WORDS, LETTER NAMES, TRACING

==== Hundreds of activities in 6 exciting reading missions with step-by-step learning paths.
==== Perfect for pre-K, kindergarten, and 1st grade children.
==== Members gain access to regular updates and new games.

Abby's Reading Adventure makes rigorous early reading curriculum engaging. Children join monkey Abby as she travels through space to explore remote islands full of adventure. Each island harbors an exciting mission that children help Abby to complete. In the process, children engage in hundreds of fun learning activities that develop their reading skills.

TEACHES ESSENTIAL EARLY READING SKILLS IN LOGICAL PROGRESSIONS:

* Letter-sound relationships
* Phonemic awareness
* Segmenting and blending letter sounds
* Letter recognition
* Letter names
* Letter writing
* Sight words
* Spelling
* Reading simple words and sentences

FEATURES:

+ Hundreds of teacher-approved educational and fun reading activities for children
+ 6 exciting reading missions
+ Step-by-step reading paths
+ Professional animations, voice-over, and sound effects
+ Colorful child-friendly design
+ Developed in cooperation with experienced teachers and parents
+ Play all pre-downloaded games offline
+ No third-party ads or links to social media
+ Regular updates

Abby's Reading Adventure takes children all the way from learning letter sounds to reading simple words and sentences. Prepare your child for success with this comprehensive early reading program by 22learn!
