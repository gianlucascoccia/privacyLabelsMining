Description

Introducing Congress in Your Pocket, Professional Edition, for the second session of the 117th Congress (2021-22).

Get the app members of Congress and their staff, pros and activists, rely on to engage on Capitol Hill. With your paid monthly subscription, this app updates every time you use it so you'll always get the very best information we have. In addition to having the most complete mobile directory of members and committees available, you'll be able to track legislation and your interactions with Congress like never before.

MEMBERS
Each bookmarkable member page offers a thumbnail and high-resolution photo, contact information, district maps, a short bio, social media links, capitol staff names and email addresses, committee and subcommittee assignments, and interest group ratings. Campaign information includes links to news, fundraising, recent opponents, election results, and win margins. We also offer political analysis from the Cook Political Report, Inside Politics with Nathan Gonzales, and Sabato's Crystal Ball.

COMMITTEES
Full committees have their own bookmarkable pages, and include the majority and minority office, contact, social media information, and a description of jurisdiction. Members are listed in seniority order and staff director names, telephone numbers, and email addresses are included. Subcommittees can be bookmarked, too, and have website links as well as membership ordered by seniority.

LEGISLATION
Learn about and track legislation with the ability to search and bookmark all bills introduced in the 117th Congress. Where available, each bill has the official descriptions, current status, sponsors and cosponsors, list of actions, and subject matter. The full bill text is linked to Congress.gov.

NOTES
Record or type secured meeting notes with members or committees. Edit and download all of them for use in outside the app.

MORE
Add widgets to your home screen showing when the House and Senate are in session. In addition to a powerful Search function, that covers staff and biographical information, you can see members of the House of Representatives or the Senate by last name or state/district, and by your current location. View lists of Freshmen, Open Seats, Vulnerable Seats, members by Political Party. Other features include the ability reorder bookmarks and navigation, see congressional office maps, and read Ken Mehlman’s strategic presentations.

APPLE WATCH
Add a quick-launch complication to your watch face and access the 117th Congress from your wrist, bookmarks from your iPhone, and members by your current location. Tap on the thumbnail photo and get a full screen picture. Tap to call, check the Twitter feed, dictate or chose a short note, or get the DC office map. Information includes office address, what percent they won their last election with, Sabato's Crystal Ball for the upcoming race, and a short biography.

What’s New

Jun 11, 2021

Version 17.1.5

17.1.5 Bug fixes. We fixed an issue in the search bar in the legislation section.

17.1.4 Bug fixes. We fixed an issue where the data might not update in certain situations. Now, when the app is displayed on an iPad in landscape mode it will fill the whole screen.

17.1.3 Updated Cook Political Partisan Voter Index (PVI) scores for 2021.

17.1.2 Never has it been easier to follow what’s going on in Congress. In addition to our session widgets, which show when the House and Senate are in session, we add elegant, ground-breaking, new widgets for tracking individual bills and resolutions during the 117th Congress. We also add a separate widget option to cover all legislation you’ve bookmarked, which rotates every few seconds. Tapping on bill widgets takes you directly to the legislation on the app. Available on iOS and iPadOS.

17.1.1 Adds Communications Directors to the Staff section.

17.1 The Legislation section of the app has been completely rewritten. The front page of Legislation features the 10 most recent bills from the House and Senate as well as the ability to search a database of bills for the 117th Congress. Each bill page lists the title, when it was introduced, the current status. With your paid subscription, it includes active links to sponsor and co-sponsor pages within the app. Where available, information for bills now includes the full title, actions, and subjects as well as a direct link to the full bill text on Congress.gov. Best of all, you can follow each bill's path through Congress to President Biden's desk by bookmarking them like you can for members, committees, and subcommittees.
