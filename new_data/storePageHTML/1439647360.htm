Description

Do you love getting exclusive deals on vacation homes? Then Whimstay is the app for you. Whimstay offers exclusive deals (up to 60%) on last-minute vacation rentals. Find your perfect vacation home and save hundreds of dollars with every booking on the Whimstay app.

Whimstay offers everything you need in a booking app:

• Exclusive deals on last-minute vacation rentals.
• Easy access to gorgeous, well-appointed vacation homes through our advanced search and interactive map.
• Safety and confidence of professionally managed vacation homes
• Filters by what is important to you: pet-friendly, ADA compliant, price and many more.
• High-resolution photos and traveler reviews
• Instant booking and secure payment with any major payment card in the Whimstay app
• A dedicated and responsive customer support team available to help, 7 days a week

Whimstay is also great for business travelers. Have an unexpected trip out of town? Stay at a property that feels more like home with a kitchen, living room, and the charm of a new city’s neighborhoods. Consider adding a night or two to the end of your trip – without breaking the bank. Or maybe book a larger place and invite friends or family to join you. The options are endless.

Whether you are planning a family vacation, a weekend getaway, or a business trip on a budget, book your next vacation rental on Whimstay and save!
