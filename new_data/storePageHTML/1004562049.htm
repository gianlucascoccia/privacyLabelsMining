Description

All pre-school subjects in one app! Boost your child’s all-around development while they have fun.
56 learning games have been carefully designed by our early childhood educators and cover 12 necessary skills:
• Learning letters and how to write them
• Mastering numbers and math
• Building logic and cognitive reasoning skills
• Sharpening memory and attention
• Boosting creativity and imagination
• Learning shapes and colors

Start your 7-day free trial of Funny Food World now!

LEARNING GAME SUBJECTS
• LEARNING LETTERS: Fun games for children that help them learn the alphabet in an easy and effective way.
• WRITING LETTERS: Games that develop fine motor skills, prepare your child’s hand for writing and teach them how to write capital letters.
• MATCHING LETTERS AND OBJECTS: These games improve language, concentration and memory.
• NUMBERS: Develop attention span and the ability to remember numbers.
• ELEMENTARY COUNTING: These games introduce numbers from 1 to 5 and teach basic math.
• COUNTING TO 10: Games that teach children how to write numbers and to count to 10 and which build familiarity with ordinal numbers.
• MATH ACTIVITIES: Learning simple addition, subtraction, comparison and fractions.
• COMPARISON: These games introduce concepts of "greater than or less than", counting and basic arithmetic for children.
• SHAPES and SIZES: Fun educational games that introduce geometric shapes and sizes.
• COLORS: Games for toddlers that introduce different colors and develop attentiveness.
• LOGIC: Games that introduce the concepts of consistency and continuity.
• SORTING: Fun games that develop attentiveness and organizational skills.
• MATCHING: Games for kids that develop memory and attentiveness.
• PUZZLES: Fun puzzle games that develop creative thinking and imagination.
• SILHOUETTES and CONTOURS: Learning games that develop creativity and cognitive reasoning.
• FIND and TAP: Fun games that develop attentiveness.
• CREATIVITY: These games develop creativity and imagination. The voice-over hints help perform the example tasks.
