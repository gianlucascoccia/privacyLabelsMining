Description

Choose from hundreds of workouts and train at home or in the gym with world renowned personal trainers, Krissy Cela, Danyele Wilson and Melissa Kendter. Build your strength and confidence by choosing a workout plan to suit you, whether you have limited time, no equipment or love training at the gym! There is a training plan for EVERY WOMAN.

GET YOUR FIRST 14 DAYS FREE! Feel motivated and inspired to DO THIS FOR YOU with your trainers there every step of the way!

Tone & Sculpt is built with you in mind, to push you to achieve your potential:
Choose from beginner, intermediate and advanced home and gym plans
Schedule your workouts using our weekly planner
Short on time? Try our 15-minute express workouts!
A wonderful in-app community forum where you can ask all your health and fitness questions and gain support every step of the way!
HIIT, LISS and exercise challenges to keep you motivated
Target area workouts for all muscle groups
On Demand workouts with Krissy, Danyele and Melissa working out alongside you from the comfort of your homes!
Achieve 50 stars from your workouts and receive an exclusive Tone & Sculpt t-shirt

And that’s not all – we’ve even got your NUTRITION covered:
1000s of recipes for a range of dietary needs
Full macronutrient calculations available for every recipe
Create your own recipes, save and share with the community
Schedule your meals and we’ll create your food shopping list automatically

Tone & Sculpt is designed to help all women, no matter who you are, where you’re from or what stage you’re at – there is a workout and meal plan for every single one of you! We want you to feel strong, confident, empowered and to believe you truly can achieve anything you set your mind to. And, we are here to help!

Every workout and training plan is fully customisable: simply browse our range of alternatives to find exercises to suit you. No equipment? No problem! We have a range of bodyweight only exercises too! Tone & Sculpt also has the capability of syncing your active minutes with Apple Health.

WE LOVE HOME WORKOUTS! Simply select the ‘home only’ option and the app will show you all of our home workouts without you having to search from scratch -we’ve got you covered!

Plus, our ‘offline mode’ lets you save each workout to access when you don’t have Wi-Fi available. We are on a mission to make fitness and nutrition possible for every woman around the world no matter your circumstances!

THE TONE & SCULPT COMMUNITY is at the heart of our app – join thousands of wonderful women from all walks of life – your personal cheerleaders, there for you no matter what! The Tone & Sculpt ‘familia’ are true athletes – we train to be the best we can be and we motivate each other along the way! You’ll be able to ask questions, seek support and interact with our Tone & Sculpt ambassadors too – all in a safe space.

So, what are you waiting for? Start now for 14 days, absolutely free!

Best workout app on the market!!

I’ve spent a good half of my life doing cardio, spin, and attempting ab workouts. I was always reluctant about strength training because I didn’t know where to start, and I didn’t want my body to look like a body builders. Then, I decided I really wanted to learn how to get lean and tone using weights, so I downloaded tone and sculpt after finding Krisy on Instagram. This app is hands down the best workout app out there. I’ve tried many, but this one is the best. You can select “home” or “gym” - I selected gym because I wanted to get familiar with machines, and it has built my confidence with weight training SO MUCH. The app targets exactly the right areas, and has such a variety with workouts. On your days off, you can select the “+” button and search through target workouts, 15 min workouts, etc. if you want to keep working on something specific. It’s very user friendly, and the workouts are challenging but doable and you learn so much all while building strength, and getting better everyday. This app is amazing. I’ve had my on and off cycle with the gym, but this app offers workouts that keep you coming back for more because you feel/see the results. The meal ideas on the app are also very feasible, and attainable in the kitchen. So much ease and variety! I will be a life long member of tone and sculpt. Thank you tone and sculpt team for building the best app out there!
