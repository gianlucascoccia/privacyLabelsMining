Description

RU There Yet?
Get where you need to go with the help of the best interactive map of Rutgers, featuring all the Rutgers bus routes, stops, buildings and parking lots. Here’s a quick rundown of the main features.

-Map-
Use the GPS technology on the Rutgers buses to track them in real-time, and see exactly where your next bus is. Customize which routes you want to see on the map.

-Directions-
Let the app find the quickest bus route for you, including transfers, and get step by step directions to your destination, whether its your next class or the coffee shop.

-Nearby-
See everything nearby at a glance. View predictions for nearby stops, and see nearby buildings and parking lots. Get fast access to Nearby right from the notification center.

-Browse-
Browse all the bus routes and stops, and browse buildings and parking lots for each campus. Build a list of all your favorites for easy access.

RU There Yet works with Rutgers New Brunswick or Newark campus ONLY.
