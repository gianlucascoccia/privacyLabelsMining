Description

The Trip Plans app lets you view your detailed itinerary from your travel advisor on your iPhone. No data or internet connection? No problem! All your trip details will still be there presented in a beautiful and easy to understand format.

- See a summary of your trip with how many events you have on each day.
- On each day, view detailed information for each event.
- Quickly pull up phone numbers and navigation for the places on your trip.
- Chat with your travel advisor directly in the app. No more messy email chains!

Your travel advisor will send you an email with a link to open your specific trip. Bon Voyage!
