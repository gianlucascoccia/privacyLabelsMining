Room for Improvements

I primarily use the app for macro tracking, and it works well enough for that. However, it would be beneficial to be able to remove food entries when you click on things in error. This is not a current feature. The data for foods in the system can also be inaccurate and you aren’t able to adjust it. I also find the photo recognition feature too inaccurate to use. It’s a great idea, but needs fine tuning.

Jssjsssk , 02/18/2018

Bad app

This app is terrible. For starters, what they call a workout is actually just an exercise, so it’s difficult and takes a long time to record an entire workout. There are not enough foods in the database, and my biggest complaint, the app does not give you kcal burn on any exercises. I understand that it is more macro focused, but I still like to get a decent idea of what my calorie burn was for any given workout. Do not under any circumstances buy this app. My Fitness Pal combined with Runtastic and Sworkit is still the best way to track, imo. I was hoping this app would combine all of those into one, but unfortunately I was wrong.
