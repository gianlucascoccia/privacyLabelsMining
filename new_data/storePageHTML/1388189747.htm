Description

ConjuGato will help you master the trickiest part of Spanish – verb conjugations. Try it free and become fluent faster!

With ConjuGato, you don't need to type the answers – just think or say the correct verb and tap to check for yourself (you can enable the typing option in settings, if you wish). This is the perfect app for training your conjugation skills on the go, whenever you have a free minute.

Feature Highlights:
• Flexible settings: practice verbs based on irregularity, endings or popularity
• Conjugation tables for each verb, with highlighted irregular forms
• Mnemonic flashcards to learn similar verbs together
• Audio pronunciation for answers, infinitives, conjugation tables
• Dark mode
• Free sticker pack for iMessage
• No ads
• No internet connection required

We are a two-person team who moved to Chile without knowing any Spanish. Back then, conjugating even in the present tense was a struggle. We couldn't find a decent training app, so we created ConjuGato! Using it greatly improved our own conjugation skills, and it has since helped thousands of other app users – check out all those 5-star reviews!

ConjuGato is free for the first two tenses and 250 of the most popular verbs. If you need more advanced practice, there’s an affordable one-time upgrade that unlocks everything in the app forever. No subscriptions or hidden fees!

If you are missing some features or need assistance – please contact us, we are always here to help!

Free version:
• 250 verbs, 27 rhymed flashcards
• Indicative mood
• Present and Preterite tenses
• Present progressive (continuous) verb forms

Pro version:
• 1000 verbs, 104 rhymed flashcards
• All moods: Indicative, Subjunctive, Imperative
• All tenses: Present, Preterite, Imperfect, Pluperfect, Conditional, Future, plus their perfect aspects and progressive (continuous) forms

This app is suitable for both Spanish and Latin American dialects — just disable ‘vosotros’ and you are good to go.

Happy learning!

UPDATE: ConjuGato is adding updates that address all my suggestions, WOOHOO!!

So far I’ve researched other apps and this is my favorite one. Other ones you have to write out but I am more interested in quickening my ability to speak. I have a good baseline for Spanish and vocabulary but the first thing I lose is verb conjugation when it’s been a while since I’ve spoken. Some suggestions I would add that I would pay additional for are if they made it so you could rate how well you know each card so that the algorithm makes that card show up more frequently and ones you don’t know as well not show up. Another suggestion I would also pay additional money for is instead of showing the card that asks for the preterite form of the word “acabar”, in context sentences would anchor more in actual language use. That said, this is my favorite app so far, thank you Conjugato for not just making a great app but also one with a cute app icon because details matter!

Developer Response ,

Thank you for the review, glad the app helps! 😊 A rating algorithm is coming in the next update: there will be a "minus" button to decrease the frequency of the currently displayed conjugation. We are also considering adding the example sentences but it will definitely take some time because of the number of verbs in the app.

DJYossarian , 04/21/2021

Incredibly helpful and easy to customize

IMHO ConjuGato is THE model for how a verb learning app should work. I first downloaded it to be able to see all the conjugations of a given verb in one place, and it does that wonderfully.

Then I discovered the Practice feature, and that was a game changer. It gives you a verb and its translation, and you conjugate it in the tense and person you're given. It gives you feedback as you're typing -- correct letters are black, incorrect letters are red, correct letters with incorrect accents are orange, and when you've got the word right the whole verb turns green. You can get hints one letter at a time.

Already mastered the present tense? No problem, just exclude it. Want to just focus on irregular and stem-changing verbs in the future tense? Easy as pie. The present subjunctive for only the 100 (or 200, or 343) most used verbs? Yep, it can do that, too. This app is great for really narrowing in on the areas you need to improve, and getting you to fix those areas FAST.

The interface, BTW, is clean and elegant and easy to figure out. It's a delight to use this app.

My only grumble is that I can't seem to find how to upgrade to the pro version. Not that I feel like anything is lacking in the free version; I just want to support the developer.

Also I would be thrilled if they came out with versions for other languages.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for such a detailed review!! 😻 Glad you are enjoying the app! The upgrade page should be available from the center button in the bottom tab. But it gets removed after upgrading, and you also mentioned having more than 100 verbs (the free version includes only the first 100), so it sounds like the Pro version is activated already.
