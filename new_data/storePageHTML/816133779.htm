Description

Hear without any limits!
The application restores your sound perception to the natural level.
Now you can hear even the whispers.

Petralex Hearing aid will automatically adjust to specific features of your hearing.
Use the power of your smartphone and latest technologies for maximum sound amplification.
No registration and no advertisements.
You only need a simple headset in order to use it.

Hundreds of thousands of people have chosen Petralex to solve their hearing problems.
The application was chosen as Microsoft Inspire P2P contest winner in 2017.

FEATURES:
-- Automatic adjustment to hearing specifics - FREE;

-- Adaptation to different types of environment - FREE;
-- Full acoustic amplification up to 30 dB with a wired headset - FREE;
-- Built-in hearing test - FREE;
-- Amplification of quiet sounds without losing the overall volume (dynamic compression) - FREE;
-- Using 4 options of sound amplification - FREE;
-- Speech recognition - FREE;
-- Support of Bluetooth headsets and Air Pods* - FREE.

I loved until....

When I first installed this app it was wonderful. It was a music player and hearing aid app all together. It was wonderful, I set up the profile and was able to listen to my music with the eq set up for my hearing loss. It was like rediscovering my music collection. I honestly didn’t use the actual hearing aid app because When not using headphones I had my own hearing aids. When listening I just selected the profile for the headphones I was using.

Then everything changed. In one update they separated the music player from the hearing aid app. While the music player worked with the selected profile, if I changed headphones (and yes there are difference) I had to leave the music app and open the hearing aid app to make the change. I don’t think it was intended that but the button I assumed would be for that purpose was grayed out. Not the best but sill usable. Though i would have helped if that upgrade had not wiped out my profiles. Then they did another upgrade. Great, I thought maybe they fixed that problem. Nope, it was worse. Now the music
Player would only play one song at a time. No matter what I did to the settings I could not get
