Description

We built the first iOS client dedicated to Twitter threads.

With Threader:
• Use our share extension to compile and read a thread directly from Twitter
• Open them in a well-designed single view for a smooth reading
• Save a thread in your bookmarks so you can read it later

Upgrade to a premium membership and unlock special features:
• Retrieve the threads from your timeline (don't miss what people you follow on Twitter write)
• Receive an email daily digest of the top threads from your timeline, as well as your most recent compilations
• Subscribe to your favorite authors and receive email/iOS alerts when they write a new thread
• Convert and export any thread to a PDF format
• Search through all of the threads compiled on Threader (we have the largest database of compiled threads, after Twitter)
• Get unlimited bookmarks and compilations
• Read your bookmarks offline, without an internet connection
• Turn on the dark mode
• Enjoy your premium features on both our web and iOS apps

For $3/month or $30/year (save 2 months with the annual plan).

We hope you’ll take as much delight as we do in discovering these great stories and pieces of knowledge.

Enjoy your read!

OhMiimmaa , 01/06/2019

Love this app & the support!

I rarely write reviews; actually, I’m trying to reduce 3rd party apps from my phone. (See: news. hacks. data breaches. I am the commodity. etc)

But I’m a curious person, a news junkie & our current US political scene could not be more fascinating. So, I love twitter. I love reading scientists, journalist, wonks, gossip & memes.

Long way to say I’m so glad for this app! I’m so grateful to find a way to gracefully bookmark important stuff. Then, a few days ago the app stopped working. So I contacted the devs, Marie & Vincent. They are a delight! Ethical & hardworking. While talking w/ them I asked @ privacy & like their answer: “... we don’t sell you data. We don’t even put ads on our apps. We really care about the privacy and are working hard to make a clean product. We decided to chose premium subscriptions as a business model to rely on anyone except the ones who pay for our service.”

My personal financial model is to support developers (especially small devs) & businesses that respect me & their customers, and make great products & services.

Keeping this app & my annual subscription.

Developer Response ,

Thank you so much for this review and your kind words! <3
