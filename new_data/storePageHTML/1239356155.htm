Description

PLAY the best online bubble shooter game for free and solve all the fun puzzles. Clear all the bubbles and beat the challenges. Download now and enjoy a classic ball shooter featuring hundreds of addictive levels, smooth game mode and awesome boosts and power-ups.

Clear the board!
Drag your finger to move the laser sight in the direction of the bubbles. Lift your finger to take a shot. Match at least 3 bubbles of the same color to pop the group and win points. Shoot and pop all the colored bubbles to level up.

Reach the Target
Beat all the challenges and win hard levels. Reach high scores and try to get 3 stars on every level. You can swap bubbles without limit, simply tap on your bubble to change its color.

Special Rewards
Unlock amazing boosters that will help you crush the bubbles and complete levels:
* Pop 7 bubbles in a row to get a fireball that will burn up bubbles on the way.
* Drop 10 bubbles or more to get a bomb that will take out surrounding bubbles.
**Remember: don’t use the special boosts right away but save your power-ups until you need them.

Game Features
* Awesome levels
* Plenty of colorful bubbles and exciting challenges.
* Amazing graphics and designs.
* Join the bubble mania!
* Easy to play but can be tricky to master.

Play anytime and Anywhere! No internet connection is required.

Ready to hit and break some bubbles?
Pick your target carefully and fire away!
