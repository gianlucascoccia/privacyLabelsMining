Decent blemish smoother

Free version seems to work well, tho many options seem to be tuned in a way that will make you want to purchase upgrades (and it constantly begs you to upgrade features) in order to make it look more natural. But for a basic blemish smoother (which I was looking for due to a couple fun photos my gf has been embarrassed about because she had a breakout that day) I have been able to “smooth” them out tho if you zoom in it does took a bit fishy and obvious something has been altered. There is an upgrade that would allot the smoothing tool to be tailored which could allow for a more natural editor.
(I’m leaving this review to have their watermark removed, and I’m still trying to get it to recognize that I have done so)

XtineInJax75 , 12/15/2018

Love it but . . .

I love this selfie editor app but I'm having major issues with it. My teenage son got his braces off today and I wanted to show off his new smile on FB. I wanted to remove a little of the acne breakout he's currently experiencing and the app did that beautifully. However, it keeps telling me that in order to crop the picture I have to upgrade. I bought the whole package months ago. So I thought okay, whatever, maybe somehow the cropping tool wasn't included?? So I was willing to pay the $4.99 to upgrade AGAIN. Then it said I had already purchased the upgrade. Okay great. Went back to editing my sons picture and it's saying the same thing. I can't get past the message that I have to upgrade and when it says upgrade now and I try, now it won't let me. What gives? I just want the picture of my son. Am I asking for the world?

Dimz101 , 09/08/2020

Teeth whitening is good

I like the auto editor it looks natural and the filter options are nice however the advanced editor changes are very obvious, sharp, and unnatural. there are some changes that could be made to the auto editor lip color changer, it goes outside the natural outline of my lips and when changing the color there seems to be a glossy finish that makes it obvious. I don’t like that the first thing when downloading the app is asking to upgrade. it would be better for it to be asked after using the app for a little. The auto smoothness isn’t a feature I like because it smoothes things that make the picture worse not better. The teeth whitening is probably the best feature on this app but unfortunately in some pictures, it doesn’t detect my full smile so it’ll only whiten some parts or go outside my actual teeth.
