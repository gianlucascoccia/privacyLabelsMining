Description

Thumbnail maker is the easiest app to create stunning channel arts & thumbnails for your YouTube Videos.

Perfectly cropped to 2560x1440, you can create Youtube Channel Art & 1280x720, you can create the perfect video thumbnail for your youtube videos.

Here are some of the awesome features:

- 1000+ Backgrounds
Huge selection of awesome backgrounds to create your thumbnails or simply import your own backgrounds.

- Typography fonts
Over 100+ uniquely designed fonts to personalize your most stunning thumbnails and channel art.

- Stickers
Add unique stickers to make your thumbnail awesome.

- Filters
Enhance the visual appeal of your thumbnails with 30+ professionally designed filters
