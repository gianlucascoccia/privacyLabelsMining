Description

Glo is a daily online yoga, meditation, Pilates & Fitness app designed to help you feel better in body and mind. Practice at home or on the go with the world’s best teachers. With classes for every life stage, level, and need from beginner to advanced, we'll help you create a practice that’s uniquely your own.

KEY FEATURES:
• Wide variety of classes for every level of experience
• Personalized workouts tailored to your needs
• Take a live class with other Glo members in real time
• Save your favorite classes
• Create personal collections and enjoy curated ones
• Download classes and practice from anywhere
• Professional Instructors
• Self-guided programs
• Track your progress on your Apple Watch
• Google Chromecast compatible, stream any of our 4000+ on-demand classes

YOGA CLASSES THAT FIT YOUR LIFE
Glo is a yoga app designed for real people—work up a sweat with our yoga classes for weight loss or learn the basics with our beginner yoga classes. The Glo app offers personalized yoga routines to fit every lifestyle!

PERSONALIZED CLASS RECOMMENDATIONS
Find class suggestions designed for you, easily access your go-to practices, view curated class collections, and stay up to date on our latest classes and community favorites on the For You page.

LIVESTREAM CLASSES DIRECTLY THROUGH THE APP
Join your favorite professional instructors with members from around the world with our live classes that you can schedule in advanced.

START YOUR JOURNEY WITH BEGINNER CLASSES
Glo's introductory courses cover all the basics of yoga, meditation, and Pilates—perfect for beginners!

EASY-TO-USE SEARCH TOOLS
Easily find the classes you're looking for with the in-app search feature. You can narrow your results by practice, duration, teacher, level, body part, and more.

LEARN FROM PROFESSIONAL INSTRUCTORS
Our world-class teachers provide the perfect mix of knowledge and support and are globally renowned for their expertise in yoga, Pilates, meditation, and beyond.

REACH YOUR GOALS WITH A GUIDED PROGRAM
Our programs are guided class series designed to take you on a journey. From inversions to better sleep and mindfulness basics to core work—choose a program that aligns with your current goals or inspires a new one.

FILL YOUR LIBRARY WITH YOUR FAVORITES
Keep all your favorites in one place with the Library feature! Save your favorite classes and teachers, create personal class collections, and keep track of the programs you've joined.

CREATE YOUR OWN COLLECTIONS
From “morning energy” to “lower back love”— create your own collections to organize your favorite classes by theme. You'll also find new collections curated by Glo each month.

SELF-GUIDED MEDITATION TIMER
Practice guided meditation with our Meditation Timer to sleep better and reduce anxiety. Begin a self-guided session at any time, choosing from a variety of relaxing ambient sounds and interval chimes.

TAKE CLASSES ON THE GO
Download your favorite classes so you can practice offline. From your hotel room to your living room to the great outdoors—enjoy the freedom to practice anywhere with the Glo app.

TRACK YOUR ACTIVITY WITH HEALTH APP AND APPLE WATCH
The Glo app connects to your Apple Watch so you can track your exercise duration and heart rate to contribute to your Activity Rings and meet your daily goals.

Downloading the app is completely free. To continue your practice, you'll need to subscribe to Glo for only $22.99 USD/month purchased with your iTunes account. After the free trial period, your subscription is auto-renewed every month so there’s no interruption in your membership or practice. You can cancel or edit your subscription in your iTunes & App Store account settings. For more information on our terms and conditions and privacy policy, please visit https://www.glo.com/termsandconditions/ios

Life saving

Seriously. As a person who lives with depression, anxiety, and addiction, YogaGlo has had a significant and lasting effect on my body, mind and spirit. On the days when I can’t even get out of bed, there is a meditation or breathing exercise. When I manage get up, but I’m tired and anxious, I can use the filters to narrow down my search to fit what I need. Coming home from the gym, a bike ride or a hike, I can find recovery and restorative classes that fit the time I have. Whether I’m sore, energized, grieving, struggling in sobriety, tensed, stressed, sad, angry, grateful, motived, have an hour or 15 minutes, in need or strength training, tutorials, or spiritual healing, YogaGlo is always right beside me. There is such a wealth of knowledge, intention, customization, and variation in types of yogic practices as well as professionally trained teachers. I have been using YogaGlo for about 4 years now and am very pleased with the app for my iPhone or iPad. I seriously can not recommend this app and program enough. The membership is very reasonably priced for how many options are available, especially when you consider you would be paying near or more than the monthly fee for JUST ONE yoga class at a studio or gym. I hope you find what you need or realize you already had it. Love.

This has nothing to do with the quality of the yoga classes and instructors, which is great.

Matsubayashi-Ryu , 04/13/2019

Jason Crandell changed everything.

As a yoga teacher, I used to be a die hard fan of Alo Moves and all the flashy fun it has to offer. There’s some neat-o tricks on there! It wasn’t until I heard of Jason Crandell on the Yogaland Podcast through his wife, Andrea Ferretti, that I immediately knew I HAVE to learn more from this guy.

After taking his online sequencing course, I immediately canceled my membership with Alo and signed up for Glo. If you haven’t already, I can’t recommend learning everything you can from Jason Crandell. My teaching as well as my personal practice has grown so much and I can’t even begin to explain how thankful I am.

I think it’s worth mentioning that Glo lets you “gift” a certain number of classes to anyone you want, something I’ve been taking advantage of! I love sending them to students I’ve become friends with so they can continue to learn. Whether you’re a teacher or practitioner, Jason Crandell and the other amazing teachers on this app will take you to a whole new level!
