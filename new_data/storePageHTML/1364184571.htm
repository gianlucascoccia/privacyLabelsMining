Description

align27 is a journey of personalized self care and time management using the ancient wisdom of astrology.

It’s like having a secret superpower in your pocket - empowering you to manage, maximize and master your own time.

align27 will tip you off about the best time to do things and vice versa, so you can maximise your 1,440 minutes a day and get things done quicker with the results you want.

Have you noticed some days everything just flows and other days it's a struggle - no matter how much effort you put in, things just don’t work out? Why is it that some days are better than others? That’s because, ‘Timing is Everything!’.

This ‘time’ is unique for each of us - one-size-does-not-fit-all. Therefore, personalization is key.

Using advanced technology we decode your birth chart using thousands of time-tested ancient astrology techniques to give you hyper-personalized, real-time personalized data every minute of the day.

Dive into this magnificent world of aligning your work patterns, scheduling your meetings, reserving tricky conversations to optimum times and asking the bank for money, just by referring to align27 for less than 2 mins a day.

You’ll know when to wear your crown and hustle, when to zip your mouth shut and meditate and when to snuggle up and camp out on the netflix-ready couch.


Features
• Is Your Day Green, Amber or Red?: A world-first traffic light system to tell you how ‘rocking’, ‘mediocre’, or ‘so so’ your day will be.
• The Cosmic Weather: Another world-first; your own day in a Cosmic Timeline graph showing the high’s to fly and the lows to doze.
• Moments: Timing is everything - ‘Golden’ for power moments, ‘Productive’ for action and ‘Silence’ for lay low - moments guide you to plan your day better.
• Planner: An ultimate personalized 90-day calendar mapping out all the above features for the months ahead.
• Calendar: Sync your align27 data (Green, Amber, Red days and Golden, Productive & Silence moments) with your device calendar
• Rituals: A vedic toolkit of personalized rituals and tips, equipping you to deal with any moment.
• Profiles: Add profiles of friends and family so you can check their data too.
• Personality Profiles: Find out what your friends and family are really like.
• Manage time like a pro: We’ve got you pro enthusiasts covered with Advanced Rituals and Hora, Panchapakshi, Panchak and Ascendant shown on a real-time grid.
• Widget: See Now, Hora, Panchapakshi, and Rituals on your home screen
• Transits: Long-term planetary influences based on the transit of Saturn, Mars, Jupiter, Mercury, Sun, Venus, Rahu and Ketu.
• Daily Horoscope: Daily insights based on planetary transits and your birth chart.
• Discover: Dive into astro news, tips and how-to’s.
