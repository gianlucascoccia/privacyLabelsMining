Description

Invoice for Business is an enterprise-class invoice management system that allow multiple users working together in the most simple way. Whether you sell products or provide services, Invoice for Business is ideal for billing your customers without the hassle of paper trail invoicing.

Email or print invoices directly from your iPhone and get paid faster by invoicing your customers on the spot.

Specially designed for professionals, businesses and organizations.

Includes role-based security, access lists, multiple branches, barcode generation and scanning.

FEATURES
- Cloud based multi-user app.
- Create invoices and estimates for services or goods.
- Logo is displayed on invoice, contract and estimates.
- Mark your invoices as paid once you collect payment.
- Email your invoices as sleek printable PDFs.
- Print your invoices directly using AirPrint.
- Multiple Currency Support.

CONTRACT & TEMPLATES
- Save time by creating a Contract to invoice goods or services periodically to the same customer.
- Create templates for common set of products.
- Customize payment methods and terms, currencies, taxes, discounts and units for each contract.

ROLES
- Predefined roles are the easiest way to limit users to perform a specific task, such as viewing, creating or modifying items.

ACCESS LISTS
- Allows you to grant access to each item.
- Assign users, roles, and branches to lists in just a few steps.

BRANCHES
- Allows you to limit access and separate operations by branches, whether a branch is a store, location or department.

LISTS
- Predefined system lists (favorites, flagged, expired/expiring and others) allow you to organize items more efficiently.
- Create and share item lists among members of your team.

SECURITY
- Manage users from the app, assign roles, and monitor access.
- Authorize each device before use.
- Touch ID ready, for fast and secure access.

CUSTOMIZABLE
- Create your own set of invoice and item attributes.
- Show user photo or company logo on sign in.
- Customize header and footer information in reports.

BARCODES
- EAN 8, EAN 13 and UPC.
- Code 39/93/128/QR for custom SKU codes.

REPORTS
- Print or email sleek PDF files.
- Import items from a CSV file.
- Export invoices to a CSV file.
