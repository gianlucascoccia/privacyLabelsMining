One of the best Apps in n the App Store

After using this app for many years, I signed up to become a Gold (paying) subscriber, not for the additional features it offered, but because I love this app and want to support them. Shopper is an app I use every day. When I run out of something, I immediately add it to my list in Shopper. I have a grocery list, a drugstore list, a hardware list, a list for our vacation home, and holiday lists. If my husband calls on his way home and asks if I need anything from the grocery, I simply sync my grocery list to him. He checks items off the list as he gets them. If he isn’t able to get an item, it stays on the list. A few years ago, I was asked to give a presentation to the members of a ladies club, many of whom were older and struggled with technology, on how to get the best usage from their smart phones. As part of the presentation, I recommended three apps, and Shopper was the first one on the list. It is the one app I wouldn’t want to live without. Kudos to everyone at Shopper. Well done!

Awesome app with super pro-active customer support!

I have been using shopper for years. I tried a few others but decided to go with this one. It is so easy to customize your lists and create new ones. I was able to take my own pictures to use as thumbprints on my lists. Pet Supplies has a picture of my best friend, Jack, a rescue Eskipoo.

Ckirsano , 07/10/2017

Great app for shopping and recipes

I love this app for 3 main reasons. First, it lets me create template lists, which I use these for specific stores and recipes. That way, I can have a default list of products I usually buy at specific a store, and I simply uncheck the ones I don't need that day. Or if I'm making one of my frequent recipes, I just select that template and it adds everything to my shopping list. Second, it intelligently combines everything...so if 3 different recipes call for garlic, it will add up the total number of cloves in my shopping list. And third, it organizes everything by aisle/department, which you can further customize to match the layouts of your stores. It has made dinner and party planning so much easier for me!
