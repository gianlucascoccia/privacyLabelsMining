Description

Build and Defend to become an Epic Legend.
A new style of Gunship Battle MMO!

** This game officially supports English.

Battle enemy units to defend your base and save the world. Gunship Battle is now back as a new MMO Strategy game, Gunship Battle Total Warfare!

Become an Admiral and lead your troops to end the battle and dominate the ocean.

Game Features:

BUILD YOUR ARMY BASE
- Build powerful Ships, Jets, and Armor Units and form your own strategy against Armada.
- Defend your Headquarters from incoming enemies trying to take over your land.
- Battle Real-time PvP with other Admirals from around the world.

CUSTOMIZE YOUR ARMY
- Camouflage your Ships with your own design and patterns.
- Research various subjects and advance battle strategies.

JOIN ALLIANCE
- Dominate the ocean by creating or joining an alliance and assemble an unstoppable force.
- Help your Alliance by supporting their actions.

Rule over not only the ocean but also the sky with your army!
Download now to start your new journey as Admiral.

sapperdaddy81 , 01/26/2021

Addictive and Fun!

The game is great. Its got a lot of aspects to keep you playing for a very long time. Competitive and easy to spend money. But at the same time, you can easily keep up with others if you play for free. Ive noticed very few bugs. Which is great for an app.

I think i would like to see more tutorials on things. More how-tos would be great. I think that some things could be a little bit more informative as to what some upgrades offer when it comes to alliance buildings. As an example: How do you gain access to more items in alliance shop when buying items with coins?

Some ideas that I think might be worth mentioning:
1. Carrier levels above 5.
2. Make more mines and anti air available to build for alliances.
3. Having a choice of country your admiral belongs to. This would give you access to different ships, aircraft, etc. Think along the lines of command and conquer.
4. Adding in Helicopters, hovercraft.
5. Being able to upgrade specific parts of units. Examples: larger fuel tanks to increase jet move distance. Uranium shells for tanks for more damage.
6. Maybe adding a stealth buff. You would not be visible to anyone outside of alliance during movement. Would go away once you attack or when time expires (whichever comes first).

I gave five stars easily. Highly recommend!
