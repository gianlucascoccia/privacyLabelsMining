App_5t0r3_N1cknam3 , 12/26/2020

AirPlay Fails if Auto-Lock is Not Disabled

The big strongpoint of this app is that you can watch the shows with it. For an app to do what it is primarily designed for is expected. The worst flaw of this app is that in order to watch a show using AirPlay you need to turn off Auto-Lock in display settings. I have no idea how this hasn’t been fixed. I turn off Auto-Lock to watch shows with my Apple TV and then turn it back on when I am finished. This should be easily fixable with an update. Several people write off that bugs like this are to be expected because it’s a free app. I don’t feel it’s at all free with the number of ads I have to view when watching shows through the app. Someone is getting the ad revenue. I’m just stating that I would be more inclined to watch more if they fixed this feature.
