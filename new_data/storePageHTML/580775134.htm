Description

Read your favorite light novel and manga eBooks on the go or at home! BOOK WALKER is one of Japan's most popular reading platforms for digital light novels and manga. There are over 18,000 English light novels and manga being offered from publishers like Yen Press, Kodansha Comics, Seven Seas Entertainment, J-Novel Club and more.


▼Features▼
■Exclusive titles & short stories
・BOOK WALKER has a selection of exclusive light novel and manga eBooks, as well as short stories provided only to BOOK WALKER users.
・Exclusive Titles: "Why the Hell Are You Here, Teacher?!", "TenPuru -No One Can Live on Loneliness-", "The Ryuo's Work is Never Done!"
・Exclusive Short Stories: "The Combat Baker and Automaton Waitress", "Arifureta: From Commonplace to World's Strongest Light Novel", "How NOT to Summon a Demon Lord Light Novel"

■Unique bonus contents & giveaways
・BOOK WALKER not only offers users unique purchase bonus items, such as illustrations, but also holds regular giveaway promotions on social media with exclusive prizes, such as author signatures, BOOK WALKER coin and more!

■Manga simul-publication (simulpub)
・Read chapters of selected titles (almost) at the same time as they come out in Japan! BOOK WALKER provides their users with a selection of simultaneous chapter releases for popular titles with weekly and monthly updates.
・Popular Titles: "So I'm a Spider, So What?", "Edens ZERO", "To Your Eternity", "The Case Study of Vanitas", "Goblin Slayer", "Grand Blue Dreaming" and more!

■Special promotions & sales
・BOOK WALKER hosts many types of sales promotions to help users catch up with the latest series or discover all-time classics.
・New users will get a 50% Coin Back on their first purchase. (There is no limit to the amount purchased)
・Monthly store-wide promotions will reward a great amount of BOOK WALKER coins.
・Theme promotions will help users to discover new series and get them at a discount.
・Title promotions provide limited-time bonus items for users to enjoy.

■Earn coins with every purchase (1 coin = 1 JPY)
・Earn BOOK WALKER Coin every time you purchase eBooks during a promotion or based on your Membership Rank! Coins you earn can be used to purchase eBooks.

■Personalize your library
・The BOOK WALKER app has been renewed! Now it's easier than ever to create your ideal light novel and manga collection and take it wherever you go.
・Customizable "Cover Images" for your bookshelf
・Bookshelves can now display 1,000 books
・Display series together!

Harold S. W. , 11/24/2017

Nice platform, but app experience needs improving

I really like BookWalker as I can integrate and view my purchased items from all its three sites (Taiwan, global, and Japan) in one single app. The app itself, however, definitely needs a lot of improving. Typography is really badly designed. I hope the app could support more fonts and allow for more reading options such as line spacing. I understand it is common to read a Japanese book that is vertically aligned, but since Japanese do read horizontally aligned books, I wish the app could support horizontal alignment as well.
