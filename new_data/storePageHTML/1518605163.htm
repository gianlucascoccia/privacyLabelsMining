Description

Meet Magic Drums AI Rhythm Games!
- Speeding Up For Each Track With 3 Lives
- Leaderboard To Play With Friends
- All Tracks Are Visible Now

Do you love music rhythm games as we do? We created a new ability to make fantastic BEATS in your own real magic studio right here, right now. What are you waiting for?! You don’t need any special equipment, just your hands, your curious imagination, and pencils or chopsticks to get started.

[GAME RULES]

We offer you two different game modes: [Unique Beats] and [Tapping]

If you choose Tapping Mode, you need to tap colorful circles when they have reached the bright activation zone with rounded areas. Use your fingers to tap them on the screen. Try to catch the rhythm and don’t miss any of the circles to create cool music beats.

If you choose Unique Beats Mode, headphones are required to play in this mode. Before starting you need to go through a one-minute calibration. You need to take two similar objects in your hands and choose surfaces you want to calibrate drums. It depends on your imagination, of course. You can choose a notebook for the first drum sound, a table for the second, and a cup for the third. Each colorful circle will be matched to a definite calibrated surface. When the circle reaches the bright line of the activation area, don't miss it - BEAT the surfaces.

Expect to have a speeding up for each song while playing in any mode. If you are a free user, you will have 3 lives that save you when you miss the beat in a song. These songs are waiting for you to conquer. Show your talents!

Open your mind and be ready for surprises! It’s easy to start even if you are not a genius at rhythmic games!

We built the app so you can experience music not just as a listener, but as an artist and a creator too! Believe in yourself and let the world hear YOUR BEAT.
