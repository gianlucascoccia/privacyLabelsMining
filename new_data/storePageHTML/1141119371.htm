Description

Manage your fantasy football lineups and dominate your leagues with My Playbook from FantasyPros.

My Playbook is a suite of powerful tools to help you make better decisions, manage your lineups, and win your leagues!

Created by FantasyPros, the #1 fantasy sports advice and tools provider in the world, this app will let you import your team to get custom fantasy football news, rankings, and analysis.

Download now for iPhone and iPad for the ultimate edge.

-------

What do you get with My Playbook?

*Custom Advice*
Import your league for custom news, rankings, and analysis.

*Breaking News and Updates*
Stay on top of the latest player news and expert advice.

*Accurate Player Rankings*
View consensus rankings from the most accurate experts.

*Quick Access to Stats & Projections*
Timely research and reports in one convenient spot.

*In-Depth Expert Analysis*
Comprehensive articles, dynamic podcast, and live video streams.

More Useful Tools:

*Start/Sit Assistant*
See what your optimal lineup is each week based on the most accurate experts.

*Top Available*
Identify the top fantasy players to pick up in your league.

*Waiver Assistant*
View suggested players to add and drop.

*Trade Assistant*
Find out if you should accept or reject a trade.

*League Analyzer*
See how your team stacks up against your league.

*Who Should I Start?*
Easily compare players side-by-side for smart lineup decisions.

*Game Day*
Real-time fantasy football feed, leaderboard, and league matchups with live fantasy scoring for your teams.

*Scores & Plays*
Live NFL scoreboard, play-by-play, team and player stats, betting odds, and more.

Teewhy92 , 10/25/2019

Perfect app for fantasy ADVICE

This app is great! If you’re like me and are constantly looking for ways to improve your team, tweaking your lineup and getting 2nd(or 3rd/4th/5th lol) opinions on trade values/waiver adds this app is a great tool for you! The app allows you to import your entire league(s) and it will rank all the teams based on a week-to-week basis as well as the entire season. I find that to be what sets it apart from other apps/sites I’ve used because it takes into account how any transaction affects your overall ranking as well as the other teams involved. My only recommendation would be not to “live and die” by their suggestions for your weekly lineup because they are are purely based of statistical analysis although they are oftentimes accurate. Sometimes that “gut feeling” you get about a guy you’re thinking about streaming in your flex spot because you saw something in the week(s) prior, will work out better than projected. Trust me, you will feel a whole lot better if you go with your gut, and fail, rather than going with their suggestions and seeing that guy have that day you knew he would! Best of luck, and don’t tell your league mates about this app lol

Silv on the Street , 12/02/2020

Great for Multi-League Management!

Second year seriously using this app and upgrading it to MVP for this year. It’s been a life saver for me. I initially started out this year in 2 leagues and ended up getting in a 3rd league that I didn’t even get a chance to draft my team. The great thing about this app is the ability to start/sit - autopilot assistance for the leagues. I definitely prioritize one league more than the others and that’s why it comes handy, for instance I was busy on a Sunday morning driving and forgot to check my lineup in one of the leagues that I had Julio Jones in (who was out because of a hamstring) and it auto switched my lineup to put in a starting WR and save me from a 0 on my points for the week. Ended up winning that week because of that auto-switch! The ability to go through 3 different leagues in one app is honestly game changing. Crunching numbers, reading articles, evaluating trades, whatever you need to get done this app can help you out a ton. Definitely has been huge through this unique year to say the least and my team management has been greatly improved. If you’re looking to elevate your fantasy game, this is where you need to go!

EZE Ruthless , 09/23/2018

And right back to a 5-star rating

Admittedly, I was pretty unhappy with this app being changed in the middle of football season (as opposed to during the off-season). And my feelings about losing the ability to rank players by filtering experts? Well, those feelings ranged somewhere between “quiet—yet dignified—disappointment” and “apoplectic rage”.

But here’s the thing: these developers actually listen to their customers. The expert ranking issue? Fixed by the very next DAY (on a Sunday, no less). To me, that kind of responsiveness is not only impressive—it is also more than enough to cancel out any misgivings I originally had about the app, and “My Playbook” is once again very much deserving of its 5-star ranking.

I’d also say that these folks have earned a bit of trust with the way they’ve handled this, so if an update glitch or temporary omission occurs in the future, I will be a lot more patient. Sorry for underestimating your customer service team, FF Pros—thanks for all you do.
