Description

With a subscription to EPIX NOW, you can watch new episodes as they premiere each week, plus stream 1000s of movies, documentaries and more live or on demand - with no ads.

Enjoy streaming with spatial audio so it feels like you're watching in the theater.

Lean back and enjoy access to our Live TV channels so you can watch what’s airing now, uncut and commercial-free.

Download full episodes and movies to watch offline or on the go using your iPhone or iPad. Want to watch on a bigger screen? It’s easy – simply tap to cast to supported TVs.

With picture in picture support, you can keep watching while multi-tasking.

Go behind-the-scenes of your favorite TV shows to get more of what you love to watch, including interviews with the cast and crew.

Keep track of all the stuff you want to watch and personalize your experience by adding movies or series to your queue to watch later.

Curated collections make finding something to watch easy. You can also discover movies and shows your favorite actors, directors or other crew members are in by clicking their names on movie and show detail pages or by using our advanced search.

Sign up now with a special introductory offer and stream anywhere, anytime. Then pay just $5.99 a month. No cable needed. Cancel anytime.

Introductory offers are for new and eligible returning customers only. The introductory pricing lasts for 3 months and unless you cancel, your credit card will be charged after the introductory period had ended. Your subscription to EPIX NOW will automatically renew each month unless you cancel. Your subscription is subject to the EPIX NOW Terms of Use and Privacy Policy at all times.


EPIX NOW is only accessible in the US and certain US territories. Select titles not available in HD. Some restrictions may apply.

Well, 4 days later on July 4th I noticed ANOTHER $6 charge on my bank account. Upon investigation, it was a second charge for a separate epix service THAT I NEVER SIGNED UP KET ALONE AGREED TO PAY FOR. After several back and forth emails with epix customer service, i received an email bluntly stating there was nothing they could and to contact iTunes. So I did. iTunes informed me this was a completely separate service for “apple tv” and could not tell me when or how I was signed up for it. I canceled both services and iTunes informed me that I would receive a refund for the second $6 charge “sometime in the next 30 days”....now living pay check to pay check ad a lot of people do these days, this has caused my bank account to be overdrawn by a friggin dollar.
