Description

myStop® Mobile puts real-time bus information and trip planning in your hands. Quickly and easily access interactive location and schedule information for more than 39 public transit agencies across the US.

myStop® Mobile has been updated to bring you improved performance and usability with an enhanced look & feel. Use myStop® Mobile for:
• Trip planning enhanced by Google Search
• Quick access to service alerts
• Integrated email & SMS notifications — so you don’t miss your bus
• Navigation to the nearest stop
• Real-time graphical bus tracking — see where your bus is on the map
• Determining bus capacity — so you can ride in comfort
