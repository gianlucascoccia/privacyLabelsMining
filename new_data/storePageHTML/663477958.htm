Important Disclaimer: The functional features of the Golf Handicap Tracker were not prepared, approved, or licensed by USGA or R&A, and the Golf Handicap Tracker app is neither sponsored nor endorsed by USGA or R&A. This app does NOT provide an official USGA or R&A handicap index. USGA is a registered trademark of the U.S. Golf Association, which is the exclusive owner and licensor of its trademarks and service marks.


Supports new handicap rules. This simple and intuitive app will help you find out your handicap now. See below for feature highlights:

Accurate. We have full confidence on our handicap calculation.

1. Calculate your handicap based on new 2020 handicap rules.

2. Support 9-hole scores combination!

3. Thousands of courses with rating/slope are available for download.

4. Calculate handicap for a specific course and maximum stroke (net double bogey).

5. Backup and restore your scores to/from server.

6. Has option to manually input rating and slope.

7. Keep all your handicaps over time.

8. Print and share your handicap card.

9. Follow your friends' handicaps and scores.

10. You will get push notification when your friends post scores.

11. Implemented a Soft Cap and Hard Cap to limit the extreme upward movement.


Since hole by hole scores are not required, please post Maxed-out score rather than gross score for the round.

Glime28 , 08/11/2020

Just ok

I’ve been using this app for quite a while. Purchased the advanced version this year since I was playing more golf. It’s a very intuitive app - almost too simple. I’d like them to add some sexiness to the app. It looks like an app from 2010. It works fine, my 2 complaints is that 1. You have to manually backup your scores. I recently lost my phone and started from scratch. Logged into the Handicap app expecting all my data to be there, but it wasn’t. I tried to use the “restore” feature and nothing happened. I had no idea this wasn’t automatically backing up. So now I lost my entire summer of 30+ rounds of golf. Really upset about that. And 2. I find this app only has about 75% of golf courses. It’s missing Courses that have been around for decades which is odd to me. You can manually add a course but that’s a pain. I’m going to look at other apps considering I just lost ALL of my data.
