Description

The UISP app gives users a single-point device installation and management tool for Ubiquiti’s extensive line of ISP-grade products. With the app, you will be able to:

• Integrate your ISP devices into the UISP controller.
• Quickly and easily setup ISP devices and align links with bluetooth or a dedicated management radio.
• Manage and monitor every aspect of your ISP network from a single app.

Works well

I don't have much going on in my home network. 1 edgerouter x-sfp, and Unifi AP AC and then several wired and wireless clients. This app allows me to change some basic settings in the router. Obviously you can't change anything with the Unifi AP in this app (that would be in the Unifi app.) However, my only issue is doing firmware upgrades on the router within the app. The request always times out and the upgrade fails.

Mcnibblet , 06/19/2020

Minimal features that don’t even work

Has a bps over time graph for my router that works until you tap to a different part of the app or switch apps. After this the graph remains frozen and doesn’t change until you completely exit the app and restart it.
There is a firmware update page that has only worked 1 of 4 updates I’ve tried (errors and blank screens). After getting one that worked, the “available firmware” section then showed an older version, despite clearly showing a newer version installed just above that section.
The web interface is unusable from a mobile device so your stuck with this if you want to check something when out of range of a computer.
