Description

Download and order from Two Crumbs. Wait til we get our crumbs on you!

We here at Two-Crumbs specialize in only the best! From our delectable desserts to our “made from scratch” entrees, we put passion and pride into all of our creations to give you the best of both worlds!

Our savory crumb is an energetic and enthusiastic cook, Tara Fleming. Tara’s passion and inspiration comes from the love of food itself. Tara sees food as being the core of everything and her prepared meals are bound to leave you with a smile in every bite.

Our sweet crumb is an aspiring baker and cake artist, Melina Adams. From gourmet cupcakes to elegant wedding cakes. Melina is inspired by the joy she brings to each and every client as she helps to make each special occasion a little extra special.
