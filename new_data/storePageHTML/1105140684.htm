Long Time Swagbucks User

I've been using Swagbucks for about 5-6 years now. I just recently discovered the Answer app for Swagbucks. I like that's it's an easier way to just do the surveys (which I enjoy doing often). I've always had mixed feelings about the Swagbucks surveys though. Most of the time it's good, but then other times it kicks me out when I've just spent 30 minutes trying to complete a survey, rewarding me 1SB instead of the full credit. I think if they're going to have you get that far into a survey and not let you finish it, then they need to reward you with more for your time. That's really my only complaint. But I've also completed many surveys without issues. It's a nice thing to do in your spare time, to eventually be able to redeem your points for giftcards of your choice that do work.

Do not do it until you read...

This app robs you of your time. Survey after survey, repeatedly takes you through a long qualifying process, only to say you do not qualify time after time. Many times they are not honest: they don’t count the qualifying time in survey time, they’ll list a survey to be 8 mins, you invest that time answering their repetitive qualify questions, then they reveal the actual survey time is longer than what was indicated when you selected it. Add to that how often you get deep into a survey, many times all the way to the end, only to be DQ’d.
Every time you attempt a new survey they re ask the exact same exact same qualifying questions again, god forbid you make an error, hit a wrong key (Who has never typo’d for christ sake) and ban you, daring to call you dishonest!!!
Funny thing is these solutions are very simple to fix: 1) have every user create a user profile that contains every possible qualifying question, occasionally ask them if they have any changes orbupdates to report. This will eliminate robbing them of their time, re-asking the same qualifying questions over and over again for every single survey, while also reducing the risk of errors. 2) if there does need to be a few additional qualifying questions at the beginning of a survey, the time answering those should always be included in the survey estimate time.
Until they fix these very basic problems, stay away from this app.
