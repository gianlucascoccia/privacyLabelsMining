Description

Daily Thrive was created to help you become the best version of yourself. By eliminating barriers to fitness and making workouts enjoyable, we give you something to look forward to every single day.

Key Features:

full length workout classes, including warm up & cool down
fits any schedule; workouts range anywhere from 10 mins to over 1 hour
low impact & no equipment options
new workouts every Monday
workouts for all fitness levels
workout challenges + schedules

& so many more features coming in the near future!

Daily Thrive is your virtual workout studio, with Vicky Justiz as your master trainer! Vicky Justiz is a NASM certified personal trainer who has built a community of over 1 million women through her relatable approach to fitness. She aims to empower women and make fitness approachable for everyone.

Together, you will sweat, laugh, struggle, be challenged, get stronger and grow. You will never feel alone on this journey - Vicky will be here with you, every step of the way. Join Daily Thrive today and get ready to thrive from the inside out!
