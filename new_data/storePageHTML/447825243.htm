Description

The Virtual Keypad™ App gives you control of your security system on-the-go. Connect to your home or business system to arm or disarm, control lights, locks, thermostats and more, easily with your app. Even view and record video from your security cameras.

With the Virtual Keypad App, you can:

• Arm and disarm your system
• View what’s happening at your home or business
• Control Z-Wave-connected devices such as thermostats, lights, locks and garage doors individually or all at once
• Set up Actions to automatically control your system throughout the day
• View and control Z-Wave devices using a photo or a security camera
• Receive notifications when doors are opened or closed
• View security cameras and your recorded clips
• Add users to your security system from your app
• Search your complete system event history
• Cancel or verify alarms with live video view

The app requires a compatible control panel and connected service. For more information about upgrading your alarm system, contact your DMP installing alarm company today.

All features may not come standard with your app. Continued use of GPS running in the background can dramatically decrease battery life.

Good but...

First off let me start by saying that the app is a GREAT piece of mind. It is extremely convenient and easy to use. The design is nice and looks great. However, recently there has been some glitches that, at times, won't let the app arm, disarm, and will timeout and ask for you to sign in although it shows in already signed in. I use the face recognition which is awesome but when the app is glitch in it will have me use my code...but then will time out. Then I have to sign completely out and log back in and then it takes some time for it to get back on track. The Wi-Fi is consistent and so is the data whenever I'm not using Wi-Fi. Just thought maybe the developers should be aware of this. But again other than that, great app!

Claybirdie , 11/02/2021

Disappointing

This product is promising, but not effective. It’s tied to a cellular communicator so if you have any type of cell service issues, it’s worthless. We will have stretches of time when the app works, and stretches where it times out and will not connect. The whole point is to make life easier by arming/disarming through the app so you can arm without having to stand by the door and wait, or disarm as you are pulling into your driveway so you don’t have to hear that annoying alarm as you enter. Very, very disappointed in this. My alarm company said the only option would be for them to switch the communicator’s carrier to a different carrier, but that likely won’t solve the problem due to where we are located. I miss the text messaging functionality they had with their old communicator. Except towards the end when they were phasing it out, we never had any issues and always received notifications of arming/disarming/burglar. Now, with this app, half the time it doesn’t even update with the current status and only pushes notifications randomly. So disappointed. Love their wafer sensors and their old school button alarm panels. This app, and the finicky touch panel, definitely do not measure up.
