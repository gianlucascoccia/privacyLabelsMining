Description

Succeed with 100+ weekly live & recorded classes to choose from. Yoga, HIIT, Pilates, Meditation and more.

Our inspiring classes help you create your own exercise and wellbeing routine, with classes designed to increase physical strength, movement, energy and relaxation. With the latest insight from our team of experts and customer experiences from tens of thousands of happy Yogaia customers, we bring you something new every day.


With our classes you can bring balance and joy to your everyday life, in just a few minutes a day! Experience Yoga, HIIT, Core, Barre, Pilates, Healthy Back, Neck and Shoulders, Meditation and relaxing classes right from the convenience of your own home. Classes from 5 minutes when time is short, and up to an hour or more when your schedule permits.

You too can reach your goals with our international team of inspiring yoga teachers, mindfulness instructors, and personal trainers. Classes for an array of levels and interests. Join us live with camera opt-in for a personalized experience, or select classes from our video library where new classes are added daily. You’ll never be bored!

Not everyone has a yoga studio nearby for their downward-dog and warrior-pose fix, which is why Yogaia brings the studio to you. Recorded sessions from studios across the world are always available, but the real fun is connecting with live classes from the comfort of your living room so instructors can offer feedback and encouragement. With its crisp design and smart search features, Yogaia makes it easy to book sessions with your favorite yogi, letting you get limber without ever leaving home.
