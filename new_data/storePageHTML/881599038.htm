Description

The official Google Analytics mobile app lets you monitor all of your Analytics properties so you can keep track of your business while you're on the go. With this app, you can:

1) Check key metrics in built-in reports
2) Monitor real-time data
3) Compare date ranges and apply segments
4) Build your own reports with any combination of metrics and dimensions
5) Save any reports to your dashboard so you can easily come back to them

Super helpful for mobile!

I absolutely love being able to look at this info on a phone- there are plenty of times when I want to check on my active audience numbers/basic audience stats but don’t necessarily have my laptop with me, and this is great for that.

One thing that I often find frustrating is that there are many metrics in the desktop browser version of Analytics that I can’t construct with the supported views/data streams/comparison options in the mobile version of the product. It would be great to either expand the app’s feature parity with the browser version, or to enable it to load/sync and render packaged/serialized representations of the dashboard views that had been constructed in the browser version of the product.
