Description

Hours is the easiest and most convenient way to keep track of your work hours. Either use the Clock feature to “Clock In” and out, or add your hours manually to the Log. At any time, you can see a weekly overview of the hours worked and salary earned.

As one of the leading time tracker apps on the App Store, Hours offers many useful and unique features, including:

CLOCK TIMER
Keep a live timer during a shift and track hours and earnings by the minute.

MANUAL ENTRIES
If you don’t want to use the Clock feature, you can also add work hours manually.

WEEKLY LOGS
On the Log page, you can see weekly overviews of the shifts you have worked, as well as the total hours and earnings for that week.

EARNINGS & TAXES*
Calculate earnings and deduct taxes.

MULTIPLE JOBS*
Working more than just one job? Create multiple jobs with different settings.

EXPORT*
Export your work history in multiple formats.

REMINDERS*
With Clock Reminders, you will never forget to report your hours.

PASSCODE PROTECTION*
Keep your work log extra secure with a passcode lock.

While the core functionality is complete free to use, some of the more advanced features (denoted with an asterisk above) require a paid subscription. Please see the subscription details below.

It helps to keep me organized

I recently took up 4 different jobs as a personal assistant where I have to keep track of my own hours. I purchased the premium version and it is a life saver! My only problem is that it automatically goes to the first job that you put in instead of giving you a choice on a separate screen before starting the job. I’ve clicked start on multiple occasions on the wrong job, but thankfully it’s very easy to change even while it is still tracking for you. I love how I can leave it and forget about and it will still be tracking for me.

KnightAngel87 , 10/21/2020

Works but is frustrating

I us the app because I paid for the year subscription. But I will not do that again unless some things are fixed. For one, there is no way to delete a job if you no longer have it. Also, I am constantly having to restore my purchase. Every time I open the app it has reset to free and will not let me do anything until I go into the settings and restore purchase. I am very frustrated with this issue. I have never had an app that you purchase a subscription for where I have to constantly restore it. The only time I have ever had to restore a purchase is if I had to reset my phone, got a new phone, or the app got deleted and reinstalled. With this app, I have to every time I open the app. If that doesn’t bother you then This app works great. If you are like me and monitor more than one job it will be irritating.

MaxK440 , 06/15/2021

Handy app / would love more options

I do like this app so far, it’s user friendly and has the option to customize different jobs which is great for the freelance work it do. I like the notes tab so I can document what specific task I was working on in that time slot. You can adjust your salary per job. You can filter which jobs you view per work. This is great when wanting to view the total hours for only one job. You can manually log hours from the past or track in real time with clock in.

I didn’t rate this 5 stars only because I would like to see even more. For example the option to view my hours per month and per year. I’d like to also be able to see the sum of one entire project through months or years. I’d like the date to show the day of week or maybe a calendar image.
