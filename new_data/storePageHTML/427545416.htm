Description

Online Doggy is the #1 provider of webcam systems to Doggie Daycares, Kennels, and Veterinarians. Our systems are installed in over 400 locations in the US. With this App you can view the cameras at your Online Doggy equipped Pet Care Provider. First, download the App to make sure your Pet Care Provider is offering the service. If so, subscribe and enjoy!! If not, tell your Pet Care Provider you would like the service to be available.

This is a subscription service to ODoggy for you to enjoy.
Details:
-1 Year Subscription to View Available Pet Care Providers - $4.99
-30 Day Subscription to View Available Pet Care Providers - $1.99
-Payment will be charged to iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase.
-Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period.
Account will be charged for renewal within 24-hours prior to the end of the current period, and identify the cost of the renewal.
-Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user’s Account Settings after purchase.
-No cancellation of the current subscription is allowed during active subscription period.

-Charges for the subscription will be charged to your iTunes account.

Pathetic!

Just as the title states, this is a pathetic excuse for a video app. I used it because I was boarding my dog and this is the service they use. Don't expect live footage due to the app freezing every few seconds. To make matters worse, you have to sign up for recurring monthly charges in order to use it, so you are charged if you don't cancel before the next billing service. I have to go by the boarding business to see if they can help me cancel the service because the information for cancelling auto renewal provided by Online Doggy is not accurate! So for now I have removed all methods of billing so I will not continue to be charged for a service that was crap from the start.

katielyly , 10/29/2019

Decent App/Great Customer Service

To be honest, the quality of the video wasn’t that great but it got the job done. I only need it to check in a few times a day on my dogs while they were boarded & it allowed me to do that. The only downfall is that you do have to sign up for a subscription, you are able to cancel that subscription at any time with no fee added. I did cancel & end up getting charged one additional month which come on... it’s under $2.00 so not that big of a deal. But I did contact the Online Doggy customer support team by Email & within a matter of minutes a very nice gentleman called me & apologized for the inconvenience, he helped me step by step on my phone to make sure I was completely unsubscribed. All & all I do think it is a good app, sometimes they just have to work out all the kinks to get them right. I think the customer service is what really sold me on it & I would definitely use it again in the future if I need to!
