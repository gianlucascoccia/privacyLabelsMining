Description

Check out the new Apple Health Data display features to check your trends.

Early humans perceived time in continuous arcs, the movement of the sun, the moon, and the constellations across the sky. With the demands of modern civilization, we began to chop time into discrete, finite pieces. Boxing it in static grids of days and weeks and months. Ordering it in chronological lists of appointments and events.
KRONxARC technology by Nectelier transforms our perception of time. Combining the beauty of continuous arcs with modern smartphone and tablet gestures, the KRONxARC user interface provides a more elegant, intuitive, and productive way to navigate time-based information.

KRON, by Nectelier, uses KRONxARC technology to change your perception of time, reinventing your calendars . . . and much more. View health data, photos, music listening history, and news feeds using the beautiful, intuitive interface. Navigate a holistic view of your life with simple, familiar smartphone gestures.

What’s New

Nov 12, 2021

Version 3.2

Refinements and Updates to Apple Health Data display and Annotation features:

A. Apple Health Data Display Expanded:
1. Daily Trend at a Glance:
-Main “Arc” Display: Color intensity heat map of each hourly Health Data “Arc” based on the daily maximum value.
-When zoomed in where the “Beam” is spanning less than 60 minutes, the Summary Section lists the hourly value for each Health Data (see “Notes” before for additional details.)
-When zoomed out where multiple days are in the “Beam” of the Main “Arc” Screen, the Summary Section under the “Arc” displays the average per day across the multiple days.
2. Daily Summary Plot in the Detail Pop-up:
Daily Detail Pop-up window shows a quick summary followed by a bar chart plot of hourly health data. Horizontal scrolling is activated for the Detail Pop-up for ease of navigating between days.
3. Daily sum or avg. for each Health Data are listed in the List View Screen, for quick continuous vertical scroll across days. (Friendly Reminder: the ListView Screen is accessed via the Main “Arc” Screen by clicking the “->” button or by tapping the header area of the Summary Section.)

Notes:
-For Active Energy, Exercise Time, Steps, Standing Time, Workouts data - sum or average values (counts or minutes) are displayed hourly on the Arc and displayed daily in the Detail Pop-up.
-For Heart Rate data - the beats per minute (BPM) is the unit of display; the average BPM per hour or average BPM per day are calculated based on where the beam is pointed.
-For Sleep Analysis - in this initial display implementation, “Asleep” data from Apple Health are displayed where one “Sleep Event” is the grouped data display, and a “Sleep Event” is defined as sleep segments that are separated by no more than 6 hours.

B. New KRON Annotation Features:
1. The new Label feature (first introduced in version 3.1.1.) is now available for each health data daily Detail Pop-up. The Pop-ups are accessed via clicking the Health Data in the “Summary” Section under the Arc or by tapping the “Arc” and selecting “View” on the Main Arc View screen.
2. A Label icon is present on the top left of each Pop-up, clicking this brings up the Label Window.
3. Previously created labels can be marked or unmarked for any individual Detail Pop-up by tapping and uncapping, when marked, a check mark icon will appear next to that label in the Label Window.
4. New Label(s) can be created by clicking the “+” icon available in the Label Window.
5. All labels created are automatically added to the “Labels” section, accessed from the menu.
6. Labels are stored on the user’s iCloud.
