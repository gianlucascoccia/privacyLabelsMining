Description

Meet your dream match – just one swipe away!

Lovelink is an interactive virtual reality romance game that lets you navigate your chats with your choices! With interesting, exciting and thrilling characters to browse through, dive into an all-new narrative dating experience.
Make your fantasy a reality, date the kpop star or flirt with a royal prince, Lovelink has a perfect match for everyone! Each character has a unique story for you! Make the right choices and you never know who will ask you out on a date!

Get to know your potential suitors! Learn about their lives and experience their unique stories. Collect steamy selfies, send flirty emojis and make choices that influence your interactions and the outcome of the story, and who knows, maybe they’ll want to meet you for a date!

- SWIPE through over 85 characters, of all backgrounds and desires, waiting to chat with you!
- CONNECT with your dream partner! Congratulations, it’s a match – time to get to know them better and get to chatting.
- COLLECT selfies from your matches and view them any time in the Photo Gallery.
- FIND your perfect match. Match with your zodiac sign for the ultimate romance.
- CHAT and react with your match to level up your relationship! Chat away and make your choices. You never know where it may lead…

Time to get swiping in Lovelink to find the love of your life!

heyibba , 10/13/2020

One of the best apps I've played in a long time, but way too expensive

I am simply addicted to this game - however the amount of gems it requires to make any substantial progress with your matches it ridiculous. The amount of free gems we receive daily is not enough to even cover the lowest premium answer. It's extremely frustrating because I often feel forced to pick the premium option because the non-premium response is usually pretty mean!! I would be willing to pay for a monthly subscription for this game if it meant getting ridiculous premium prices. If this continues, I can't imagine I'll end up keeping this game. It is ridiculously expensive to open pictures or give good responses.

I also found out in another response that characters have counterparts (aka the same exact character but a different name/appearance), and I was actually extremely disappointed once I looked into that. For example, I matched with Angel, but then I found out her counterpart is Emmalyn, who I personally find so much prettier. However, I've put enough energy into developing my relationship with Angel that unmatching her just to get Emmalyn seems like a giant waste! On the other hand, it makes me less excited about Angel now, knowing Emmalyn is out there but I'll never get to meet her.

Additionally, I'd like some way to be able to customize my character.

Thanks for rating our app, heyibba. We appreciate you taking the time to give us a review and providing feedback on areas we could improve. We will pass along your suggestions to our development team for them to review.

Great game and some suggestions!

I love this game. It is very fun and makes lose track of time. I do think it is a bit pricy if you want to get gems in order to do the fun stuff, which is understandable. I was thinking that maybe there could be balance where people can get some stuff that usually cost a lot of gems with time and then others that can afford it can still choose to buy them. It is pretty impossible to unlock all pictures and choices when you only get about 15 gems and pictures alone are like 300. Maybe, we can be allowed to unlock a picture a day for free, or watch an ad instead, or maybe after a time window it will unlock by itself? Same thing could be done for the choices, though i think the option to watch an ad instead might be better. There is also the possibility of increasing the amount of gems we receive, that way we can actually save them up. Also, i was thinking of maybe adding more personality to the character so that when you swipe right it will actually match you with characters you are compatible with? Or maybe adding a “what are you looking for?” section. Lastly, i think it would be great if we had more choices and maybe the ability to react to the texts we want with our emoji of preference, instead of pre-selected texts. Thank you for making this awesome game!!
