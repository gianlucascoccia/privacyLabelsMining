Description

DIY is an online learning community to learn new skills. We believe that creativity can be at the core of learning anything and so any skill can be a gateway for family time and helping kids get ready for the future. Thousands of families have registered to use DIY to meet others who share their interests, explore more than 50+ Courses and TV Shows, 3500+ projects across 150+ skills, and earn badges for their achievements.

Courses on DIY span the spectrum of science, tech, arts, building and engineering, covering a range of topics from Digital Art to Stop-motion Animation to Coding to Crafts. While TV Shows cover themes like outdoors, pets, craft using junk, fun facts and much more - its entertainment that inspires creativity and learning.

DIY closes the loop - watch engaging videos or join live sessions, do projects and activities offline and share creations in a safe & moderated community to get comments from peers and feedback from mentors. DIY requires an in-app purchase of a monthly or a yearly subscription plan.

Families subscribe to 'DIY - The Learning Community' for its huge library of learning content - courses & TV shows, hands-on projects, live sessions and an awesome kid community where kids can safely share online. There’s something for every kid: Art, Photography, Slime, Minecraft, Machines, Sewing, Animation, Cooking, Singing, Coding, Shows … you know, the stuff that makes the world great!

The thing that makes DIY different from all other screen time is that your kids will get inspired by other kids who love learning and share their passion and curiosity.

With DIY, you can discover everything your child can be.

Developer Response ,

Thanks for your feedback and we hear you. After running DIY as a free resource for 8 years we can no longer sustain its operation without support from our members. However, we realize our previous pricing may have limited kids and families from enjoying our unique online community.

We have decided to lower the cost of our subscription from $19.99 to $4.99/mo and if you have multiple kids, our family plan is now $7.99/mo from $24.99/mo. If you prefer a yearly subscription our Explorer Yearly Pass is $49.99 and Family Yearly Pass is $79.99.

We wish we could make our content free to every kid. Sadly there are costs that go into running DIY.org, creating new content, and ensuring an ad-free, bully-free, and supervised space for kids as they explore cool hobbies and interests.

We hope you will consider giving us another try! Shoot us an email at help@diy.org and we’ll help get you set up.
