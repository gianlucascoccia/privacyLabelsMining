Description

Your club, your channel – instantly stream your favourite Manchester United programmes on iOS and tvOS devices, or on the web.

Includes:

• Live match action: Watch pre-season games live, Women's team matches and see the stars of tomorrow with full U23s and U18s coverage 

• The best matchday coverage: Follow the action with exclusive pre- and post-match interviews, expert studio pundits and live audio coverage of every first team game in 2021/22

• Match analysis: View full-game replays, extended highlights and unrivalled reaction and in-depth analysis

• Manager's press conference & interviews: Hear from the boss every week, live and uncut, as well as exclusive interviews with all your favourite players

• Award-winning, original shows: Access groundbreaking films and documentaries featuring the manager, players, legends and famous fans.

App Features:

– Watch on demand: Catch up on your favourite Man Utd programmes at any time
– 24/7 streaming: Can't decide what to watch? We're playlisting your day – all day, every day
– Audio-only mode: Listen uninterrupted to the live stream while using other apps
– Cast to your TV: Watch live or on demand on TV using AirPlay or Chromecast
– Voice assistant: Ask Siri to "Play MUTV" at anytime.
– What's on guide: View the full MUTV schedule for the next seven days
– Remind me alerts: Never miss your favourite shows; set alerts for upcoming programmes

Voodoopiper , 11/09/2019

The best there is period!

What a great app period, hands down! This app provides me with flawless connection to MU and all the current updates and events, but more so ongoing historic events and matches. It’s great to be able to remain connected to MU’s past and be able to keep up with its current progress with so many interviews with former players and staff, informative commentary, and News. There are times that I can not pull myself away. Love the live match commentary, but would wish for a better display of graphics other than the score screen. Perhaps player profiles, ongoing shots of Old Tafford or anything to break up the monotony a bit...would love for actual live feed of the game...Now that would be true nirvana! For me it is often difficult to find a pub or obtain connection to live games so I rely heavily on this app to keep me informed. I have compared this app to that of Liverpool and Arsenal’s (my mates teams) and they are both crap compared to this one. .Glory Glory Man United! Well done!

Mulletproof22 , 08/16/2021

Crashes and spoilers

I love the content that this app offers, and the picture-in-picture feature was a big improvement. That said, I’ve been using this app for about two years, and it remains very frustrating. You’d better hope that you don’t get a phone call while watching a match, because if you leave the app for longer than about 30 seconds, the match will reset and you’ll have to scroll through and try to remember where you left off. It is maddening! This used to be my only frustration with the app, but I used to really appreciate the care that went into the content of the match notifications. It seemed that whoever was managing the notifications would purposefully avoid spoilers. Now I have to turn off all notifications because I don’t want to know the score of the game at halftime or full time, as I won’t be able to watch the match until several hours after it has ended. PLEASE, STOP WITH THE SPOILERS!!! Nobody wants to watch a match for which they already know the outcome, especially if your app doesn’t allow for watching live matches. The final suggestion I would make is the addition of quick skip buttons to skip +/- 15 or 30 seconds while watching a match. This would be a nice addition.

karan301 , 11/14/2020

Much improved

I was wholly unimpressed with the app when it first came out but over the years, it’s become a solid offering. The recent update adding Picture-in-Picture is a long awaited feature. The native iPad and Apple TV apps are much appreciated as well.

The one thing missing for me now is landscape support on iPad. I spend most of my day behind an iPad with Magic Keyboard attached, and opening the MUTV app rotates it to Portrait. This isn’t good. Fortunately I can quickly choose what I want to watch and get back to work while using PiP. (Bonus points if we ever get support for Split View and Slide Over for iPad, but I’d be shocked if that ever happens.)
