Description

From the makers of the award winning Just Press Record comes Perfect Tempo, the app which lets you speed up or slow down any song on your iPhone or Apple Music without affecting pitch.

It's an ideal practice tool for musicians and dancers. Play along or dance to any song at the speed you want without losing fidelity.

Access over 50 million songs directly on Apple Music*, play from your iPhone’s Music Library or from iCloud, Dropbox and other cloud services.

You can even change the tempo of any of your recordings made in Just Press Record!


FOR MUSICIANS

Learn any song at your own speed.

Repeat any section of the song to practice a particular passage.

Rip your backing track CDs and play along to them at your own speed.


FOR DANCERS

Practice your routine at the speed you want.

Put any section of the music on repeat to learn complicated steps.


KEY FEATURES:

• Fine control over playback tempo in 1% increments with a simple slider.

• Search for and play any song from the entire Apple Music catalog *(Apple Music subscription required).

• Direct access to all the songs you already have in your iPhone’s Music Library.

• Import backing tracks and other audio files stored on cloud services such as iCloud Drive and Dropbox.

• Direct access to all your recordings made in our app Just Press Record.

• Repeat the whole song or just one section to make practicing tricky sections easier.

• Quickly access your 'Recently Played' songs.

• Use your Apple Watch to control tempo, volume and repeat right from your wrist.

• AirPlay support for output to wireless speakers or Apple TV.

• Dark Mode support.

• Change the key (transpose up or down) of locally stored DRM-free songs imported into the app or from your iPhone’s Music Library. (Missing the pitch controls from the last version? Re-enable them in the Settings app > Perfect Tempo. Then swipe left on the album artwork when playing a song).

Emily in MVCA , 12/06/2019

Would be 5 stars if it would play the next running song on the playlist

Was delighted to find this super simple option for matching songs I love to my ideal running cadence. I am happy at 162 bpm but several of my favorite running songs are 158 bpm. After a few days playing with different software including GarageBand, I stumbled across this app! You do have to do a little math but it seems that 2% increase from my playlist is perfect. The interface is simple and familiar, and lets me access my playlist, any of my purchased albums, and Apple Music. It lists everything by song title but you can filter a little. So I set like 9 songs on a playlist, with a mix of original tempo and +2% tempo. The songs keep the choice I made, and there’s a toggle button to “repeat” or “off” or “repeat section”, but even when it’s off it still just plays the same song again. If it would go forward to the next song on the playlist, I’d like it better. As it is, I’ll have to mess around with my phone to manually choose the next song. Luckily they’re each pretty long!

Jwelchez , 03/29/2020

Mostly love it

I absolutely love the ability to manipulate the tempo of streaming music. As a transcribing musician, it’s an invaluable tool. It would be infinitely better if the fast forward and rewind could be done to the second more easily. Sometimes when you’re trying to hear a single 16th note you don’t want to have to scroll back an entire 5 -10 seconds. Perhaps the ability to change to a sideways/landscape orientation of the app would allow for a wider scroll bar and make it possible to forward/rewind more precisely. If that were to be fixed, this app would be a 5 star A+ 100% perfect app all day long. I’m a college professor and have recommended this to several students as a transcribing tool. This is a complaint that I’ve also heard from several of them, as well. Please fix it in a future update.

Yellow blackbird , 12/07/2019

Almost perfect

This app does just what I need it to EXCEPT that it cannot move to the next song on the playlist. I use the app to change the speed of the songs in the fitness class I teach and while I LOVE that it can change the tempo of Apple Music songs, it’s very frustrating to have to change to the next track manually each time. If that feature could be added this would be a five star app!
