Description

SCREEN-FREE ENTERTAINMENT FOR KIDS 3-12!

Pinna is the only screen-free, ad-free audio streaming service custom-made for kids ages 3-12. With thousands of premium podcast episodes, audiobooks and songs all in one app, parents and teachers can access entertaining and educational content to activate kids’ imaginations!

Pinna is free to try before subscribing! Listen to a selection of full episodes of Pinna Originals and upgrade to either a monthly or annual subscription to unlock the full library. Our subscriptions include a free trial before any payment is collected. Monthly subscriptions are $7.99 USD and annual plans are $79.99 USD (that's 2 additional months of free listening after the free trial!).

Great for Long Drives

There are so many quality shows on Pinna, I don’t know where to begin - driving 20 minutes to daycare or driving hours to a nearby city, there are so many shows that keep my boys entertained - from the short Sugar Monster to the medium lengthen Grim, Grimmer, Grimmest. Christmas is Coming is great when the season is coming - ExtraBlurt is one we all played - Savvy and Wonderhair is pretty good - especially the planet episodes, my oldest loves Mars Patel - and there are countless books, like Shredderman, which are quite good - the only problem is the cost. Most shows are entertaining and there are tons of things to discover but At 7.99 a month (after the first free one), make sure you get your moneys worth. There is enough here to keep you busy listening for months ... but eventually ... we pulled the plug but after six months we came back to get the latest episodes and check out the new shows - I guess the price is worth it if you are anti screen and don’t mind letting your kids listen to programs / podcasts instead of listening to the podcasts in your own queue
