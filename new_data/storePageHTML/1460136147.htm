Description

SlimQueen is brand new Home Fitness app.

New workout every day, bodyweight exercises, 100% Personal Training Plan, Challenges - stay safe & healthy with SlimQueen.

The app has women tone of voice. As women we know how hard it is to stay on healthy track and find self-time between family and work. We experience the same difficulties as you do so we’ve developed the app which helps you create healthier and happier life.
 Workouts include only safe bodyweight exercises which are proven to improve physical condition and health.

SUBSCRIPTION UNLOCKS LOTS OF FEATURES:

- Never-ending Program of daily workouts adapted for your activity level and your goals.
- Everyday workout: daily set of exercises personalized by intensity.
- 30-day Workout Challenges: exercise plans with gradually increasing intensity.
- Collections of exercises suitable for particular occasions: “In the office”, “On the beach”, “Before running”.
- Collections of exercises targeted for particular areas: “Waist trimmer”, “Abs killer”, “Booty booster”.
- “Stress Relief” collection of exercises which help you to come down.
- Special “Mom Programs” which were designed to take care of yourself after having a baby.
- “Wed Shredding” to tone up before wedding.
- Brilliant progress tracker made for you to stay motivated.
- Instructions for all exercises with useful tips and text guides.
- Gentle notifications which help you to stay on a track.
- Weight loss tracker and integration with the Health app.

HOW TO USE THE APP AND SUBSCRIPTIONS

Any unused portion of a free trial period will be forfeited after subscription purchase made.
