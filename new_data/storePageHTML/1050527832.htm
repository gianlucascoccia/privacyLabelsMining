Description

Most folks meet by seeing each other and think that’s enough. At Vorail, we feel sight is just not the right way to meet someone new. When you use the Vorail app, you meet people by their voices, their contributions to conversations and the questions they ask. No pictures. The true beauty of a person lies within and if you hear our voices, you’ll quickly understand. Come hear what you’ve been missing.

Vorail is controlled by gestures, not by pushing buttons. List of gestures at About page of http://www.vorail.com

Listen to highlights by finding "The Vorail Roundtable" podcast on iTunes.

wa4wga , 03/12/2018

No serious intent to reach out to the non disabled community.

Claims to the contrary, the developers of this application appear to have made no attempt to reach out to those who are not disabled. I’d say this appears to run counter to the stated objectives of the program developers. Also the developers are socially inept and not as receptive to suggestions as they claim. For example, I have suggested that there should be a delete button for in box messages or at the very least that in box messages should delete after X value. Play with the app if you like but be aware that any suggessstions you are likely to make will fall on deaf ears. Finally, if you want non disabled users to be aware of this application then it falls to you to make them aware of it as the app developers have shown no such interest.
