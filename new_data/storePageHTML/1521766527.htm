Description

Introducing the Protect App from Protect Young Eyes!

MOMS AND DADS unlock your digital parenting superpowers in 5 minutes a day with The Protect App! Hundreds of bite-sized, swipe-able panels that break down complex internet safety issues.

Would you like to teach your kids how to be predator proof, porn resilient, and screen wise? After performing over 1,000 internet safety presentations, we’ve learned what it takes to prepare kids for the digital world. And we’ve poured it into Protect.

Lessons on: YouTube. Disney+. Snapchat. TikTok. Discord. Digital mental health. Social media tips. Digital rules for co-parents. Router set-up. Instagram. Pornography talks. Tricky People. Screen time. Gaming. iPhone set-up. Chromebook basics. Creating Digital Trust. Popular games like Among Us, Minecraft. Emergency steps for porn, sexting, predators, mental health. And more!

Imagine every internet safety blog post, presentation, and technology course you've ever read or taken. Now imagine all of it in one place.

The Protect App is a modern way to learn the very best internet safety information in the middle of your crazy, awesome life. Find 5 minutes a day! Quick tips. That’s it! In the school pick-up line. Waiting for the pediatrician or for practice to finish. Have 5 minutes with your child? Then watch a video about online predators to start an awesome conversation.

KEY FEATURES:
* Hundreds of mini lessons on the most important digital topics.
* Videos produced with teens and young adults to watch with your kids!
* Highly memorable, bite-sized chunks for busy families.
* Optional faith application using 3 Bible translations (including Catholic).
* Progress tracking.
* The entire app is available in Spanish!
* SOS button for critical events (pornography, sexting, predators, mental health).
