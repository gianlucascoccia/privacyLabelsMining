Description

Welcome to Townest: Alfred’s Adventure - a magical match 3 puzzle game! Check out this magical town full of mysteries! Help Alfred the boy renovate this place, bring happiness and warmth back to his hometown, and pass levels to find clues on his missing grandmother!

Plan your moves by matching 3 or more puzzles in a row, using boosters wisely to beat hundreds of levels! Quickly gather energy to summon crows to help you overcome those sticky puzzles!

After clearing each colorful match-3 level, you can unlock different regions to go to, restore and decorate the town along the way! Clear levels to find clues on Alfred’s missing grandmother, which will lead you to the final revelation. So, what are you waiting for? Install and design your own town!

Townest game features:

EXCITING MATCH 3 LEVELS
Enjoy hundreds of levels with awesome boosters and explosive combinations! Take part in time limited events to earn more rewards!

TOWN DESIGN
Move buildings freely, renovate the entire town, and design the town the way you want！

SPECIAL MERGE
Get higher level buildings by merging 3 of the same lower level versions!

MAGICAL MATERIALS
Merged buildings can also produce magical materials while offline. Shake your phone to obtain all the magical materials. They can be merged into magical books to summon various buildings as rewards!

UNIQUE STORYLINE
Help Alfred restore his hometown, search for clues on his missing grandma by matching pieces!

LIVELY CHARACTERS
Various characters-such as Alfred, Vincent the crow, bookstore manager, etc. They will all accompany you on your adventure.

HUGE MAGICAL TOWN
A mysterious magical town for you to explore, discover the secrets and gain the power to restore it to its former self.

Townest: Alfred’s Adventure is completely free to play but some optional in-game items will require payment. Levels range from easy to hard for all adults to enjoy - solve puzzles with quick thinking and smart moves, progress to the next level for more rewards! It would also be the best game for you to spend time and share with your friends. Come and play it now!

Hokey Pokey!

This game was quite frustrating when I started, mostly because it was too slow and took too long to progress. The developers have worked to fix this with periodic side ‘competitions’ and it’s somewhat better now, and I look forward to it getting even better. Some levels are nearly impossible- one shouldn’t have to play a level for several days.
Challenging is one thing, impossible is another.
The graphics and story are fun. But, it still needs to move faster to sustain interest.
The team feature is new, but it’s not yet shown to an asset for the player. I’ll stick with it but won’t spend $$ as the purchases aren’t really worth it.
The positive aspect is the improvement -indicating developers are interested in making the game more playable with options to make it move a bit more quickly.

Overall a waste of time

Don’t get me wrong they have some interesting concepts like merging houses and other items. The story is decent (that is part of the problem also) and the animations are fun but on the tech. End it breaks down. The levels overall are decent challenge but it’s a obvious attempt at a cash grab because every time you get a bonus multiplier the level all of a sudden becomes impossible to beat. As soon as you run out of those multipliers then it magically becomes available to beat and move on. Then when you finally get to the last level (based on the update) you are stuck there until they update and you can’t even do a repeatable level to at least entertain yourself. As of this writing the last update was a month ago. Then there is the special events you get to do. The last one they did you could only participate if you were BELOW a certain level. So those of us that were at the end level could not even participate. As I mentioned earlier the story is also part of the problem. It’s not complete. They dribble out a bit of the story with each update. And because the story progresses on the number of stars you collect the story stops long before you get to the current end level. So really you are doing levels to collect stars for the next bit of story and basically is done before you even start new levels (rinse and repeat). So I’m sorry a game with so much potential has lost me and I must say to everyone..... skip it.
