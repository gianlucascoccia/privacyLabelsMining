"Making Drinking Fun Again."-TGDaily

"Stay Hydrated and Have as Much Fun."-Alphadigits

Drinking water is essential for life, and a less water drinking problem can cause dehydration, fatigue, skin problems and other serious health issues. Plant Nanny² is a cute water reminder app that keeps track of how much water you drink, motivates you to drink water daily and solves the low water drinking problem that most of us face.

Each glass of water you drink grows the cute plants in Plant Nanny² so you can both thrive! Set a daily schedule so that you can collect, grow plants and take care of cute plants to get hydrated together!

Grow plants in Plant Nanny² to practice self care and make yourself healthier with our in-built drink water reminder and water tracker.

Wondering how much water to drink? Plant Nanny will provide you with a customized water drinking plan with interactive charts and reminders so you know your water consumption schedule. Plant Nanny²'s little plants will boost your spirit, improve your health, and help you cultivate good habits for drinking water!

I TELL EVERYONE I KNOW TO GET THIS APP

If I had a dollar for every person I got to download this app I could buy so many more clovers. This is by far the cutest and most successful app I have ever used to track water intake and form the healthy habit of trying to reach my goal everyday.
Nothing is better than randomly saying “oh, I need to water my plant!” while at school, the office, dinner table, etc. and having people look at you with funny faces of curiosity before you pull up THE CUTEST APP EVER, Plant Nanny, and explain that every time you drink a glass of water, you get to water a virtual plant, name it, love it, and watch it grow, while you build an adorable collection. Plant Nanny Forever. 💙 P.S. (Plant Nanny 2 is far better than the original app, however, I have been a fan/ user since the very original version.) In the first version, your plant would die if you hadn’t “watered it” often enough, and then you would have to start all over. Very sad and discouraging.. Now, in Plant Nanny 2, the plant will never “die” the animation will instead look as though your plant has “wilted” since the last time you opened your app. Now if that happens, no worries, just drink a glass of water, and your plant perks up and returns to its lively animation! This update encourages you to get back into the habit of drinking water & growing your plant, even if you forgot for a few days. Instead of starting all over. Overall, Best. App. Ever.

LittleBunnyV , 01/28/2019

Falls short of the original Plant Nanny

I hope the creators will still be updating the original Plant Nanny, because this new version falls short on the following points, for me: (1) no more options for different glasses of water - a feature I thought distinguished the original app from other health tracking apps. We don’t drink the same quantity of water every time we go for a glass of fluid! In the new version, I have to go change the custom glass size in the settings for each drink - that’s pretty inconvenient. (2) This is a personal preference, but I think the new aesthetics are too cutesy, and focused on the character rather than the plant! I want a plant, not a tamagotchi. I also like the window sill and the options for backgrounds.. Sure, the graphics could use an update, but the concept was nice - I found comfort and familiarity with having a houseplant by the window. The new background is not as relatable. (3) User interface is, yes, not as primitive as the original, but that also makes it a tad less simple and friendly. (4) This is so minor, but I thought the golden clovers was popcorn. Hehe (5) Finally, the advent of a new version seems to signal to the fact that the original will no longer be updated, and will slowly get phased out via incompatibility with newer software updates (ie. we will lose all of our precious plants and an app that works better than its successor). PLEASE KEEP UPDATING THE ORIGINAL PLANT NANNY 🙏!
