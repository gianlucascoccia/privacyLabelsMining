Description

SimDif is the first website builder to be fully functional, with the same features, on a phone, computer and tablet. This allows you to easily switch from one device to another to create, edit and publish your site.

SimDif website creation app is designed to help you build a site that will be easily understood by your visitors, and increase your visibility on search engines. Forget about the technical frustrations. Focus on your expertise and get straight down to telling your story by creating and organizing your content.

Create your own professional website by simply presenting what you know about your business, field, activity, or hobby.

KEY FEATURES

• The optimization tool helps you to understand what is important to make a good website and shows you what to work on, before you can publish. This will ensure that your website has the essential qualities appreciated by search engines.
• A clear and intuitive user interface. Even easier to navigate than the original SimDif.
• Brand new graphic customization tools.
• Respond instantly to your site’s visitors with the in-app chat.
• Rotate your phone to view how your site will appear on a computer.
• Observe the behavior of your site's visitors with new simplified statistics.
• The app is like a coach, with built-in contextual tips and guides to help you build and learn.

SimDif has 3 different versions. Starter, Smart, and Pro.
All versions include free and reliable hosting. SimDif is available on most systems on phones, tablets, and computers.

STARTER (Free)

A free Starter site helps you to organize your content into a simple and effective website.
Up to 7 pages | 14 color presets | Free .simdif.com domain name | the Pre-Publish Optimization tool | Site visitor statistics.
To keep it online for free, all you need to do is publish the site at least once every 6 months.

SMART

A Smart site offers more choices at a good price.
Up to 12 pages | 56 color presets | Enable and moderate your visitor’s blog comments | Control the way your site is shared on social media | Install and use Analytics | In-app hotline for direct contact with the SimDif team.
More shapes, more fonts, more customization.

PRO

The Pro version offers more specialized features and customization control.
Maximum of 30 pages | Create your own color theme and shapes | Customizable contact form | E-commerce buttons | Password protected pages.
Enhanced features and finer customization.
