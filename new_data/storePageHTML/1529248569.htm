Description

A journey for the truth, a story buried for years...
Being a celebrity may look like fun and sun on the outside, but behind it all are endless secrets.
Your life should have ended 5 years ago, but you have miraculously been granted a second chance.
From A-List celebrity to fresh newbie, the entertainment industry has become so much more different and dangerous than the way you knew it.
Luckily, you are not alone: helpful friends, partners in the industry and four guys that change your life forever.
You might think yourself lucky for getting it all,
But the truth is... you’ve lost even more.
When the truth is revealed, who will be the one you fight for?

Aniko_Maple , 01/18/2021

Great Game but Way Too Much Flaws

I love the concept and the storyline, but the English is pretty bad... it seems like it was translated using a machine.
The fonts used in this game gives me headaches. The fonts just looks like a rip-off. There are better options and buying font licenses aren’t that expensive compared to the revenue. You can easily obtain them from websites such as Blambot.
As well as everything in the app is just unnecessarily shiny and animated. It would be many times better if the flower petal animation is gone and everything isn’t shiny shiny.
I’m also confused as to why this game makes my phone (iPhone XR) so hot so quickly. In ten minutes of gameplay, my phone gets as hot as when I’m playing Genshin Impact. Other competitors in this game’s field such as Wannabe Challenge and Helix Waltz don’t make your phone this hot and their games take about 4-6 gigabytes.
This game will significantly become better and get better ratings if the reasons listed above were fixed. I’m having high expectations for this game though! I’ve been looking for this type of game for quite a while.
