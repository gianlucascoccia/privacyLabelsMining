تسوق بضغطة زر، من السوبرماركت لبابك

‏‎شراء المقاضي لم يكن يوماً بهذه السهولة. سهولة استخدام تطبيقنا تمكنكم من شراء المقاضي بضغطة زر. وفروا أوقاتكم من خلال طلب المواد الغذائية والمنزلية أون لاين من بنده و هايبر بنده وفي أي وقت وسيقوم متسوقكم الخاص بجمعها لكم وتوصيلها من السوبرماركت لبابك.

‏Now shop with a click!

‏Grocery shopping has never been easier. With our user friendly mobile app, you can now shop your home essentials with a click. Save time by ordering groceries and your home essentials from Panda or Hyper Panda, any time, and a personal shopper will deliver from store, to your door.

‏- Friendly interface and easy navigation

‏- A big selection of products for you to choose from, and growing.

Great App but service depends on store

One of the best online supermarket apps ever. Excellent to use, easy to search and finding by brand is unbelievably fantastic. Unfortunately the actual collection and delivery depends on the store. I tried three around Khobar. The first actually called and commenced shopping, submitted substitutes and delivered on time. But I can’t make them my default so I had to change. The other two were pathetic(Rakha & Rayan). Average was a 5 days from order to delivery. They scale back the time to days from minutes and one experience was so horrific, it took the head office to have them start shopping and they wanted to deliver at midnight ! The shopper couldn’t care that I had work in 6 hrs. They seemed angry I complained. Even the driver was unpunctual and kept me waiting for more than one hour. So great application but after +20 years as a customer, no more shopping at Panda for me or family. COVID showed me which companies really care.
