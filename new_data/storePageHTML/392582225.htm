Description

Get the sharpest news coverage from Victoria, Australia and the world with the reimagined Herald Sun app. Here’s what you’ll love:

24/7 NEWS YOU CAN TRUST

Get access to the latest news and biggest stories as they happen, 24 hours a day, seven days a week from our team of award-winning journalists in Melbourne and right across the globe.

CUSTOMISE YOUR NEWS FEED

Set your local news area, follow journalists and topics, plus your favourite sporting codes and teams. All of your customised preferences will appear on the home screen so you can get straight to what matters to you.

NOTIFICATIONS TAILORED TO YOU

Receive up-to-the-minute alerts on the news you’ve asked for. Tailor your notifications from a range of topics from breaking news, sport and entertainment to business updates and lifestyle.

READ TODAY’S PAPER ON YOUR DEVICE

Read the paper anytime, anywhere with the digital replica of today’s newspaper. Access the past 14 back issues, and all your favourite liftouts like Escape, body+soul and Stellar magazine.

DISCOVER MORE THAN JUST NEWS

Our app is packed with loads of extra features so you’re always in the know and entertained with daily Podcasts, Sudoku and Crosswords.

So, whether you’re interested in the latest news, politics, crime, opinion, entertainment gossip, NRL or other major sports, this app has got you covered.

How to gain full access to the app's content and features:
1) Members to the Herald Sun can log in to the app when prompted using their membership details; or,

2) Become a member by subscribing through the app (via iTunes) for just $14.99 per month for the first 3 months. After the first 3 months, subscriptions will automatically renew to $29.99 per month.
• The subscription period will automatically renew unless turned off no later than 24 hours before the end of the current period.
• Your credit card will be charged through your iTunes account with the subscription price of $29.99 per month.
• You can manage your subscription any time from your iTunes account settings, this includes renewal options and cancellations.

For full Terms and Conditions please visit https://myaccount.news.com.au/heraldsun/help/subscriptionTerms?pkgDef=HS_SDO_F99914_PEP#subscribe and our Privacy Policy, please visit preferences.news.com.au

Unable to read offline

I anticipated that if I download the paper at home, I should be able to then read it offline on the bus or train but stupidly, the app wants to reconnect online before allowing you to open the app. Extremely frustrating and disappointing. Why bother having the download feature at all. Please fix.
The Age and Sydney Morning Herald do not have this problem.

Extremely Impressed!

This update is an impressive step up in line with other highly rated magazine type layouts. (Flipboard, Currents & Zite). The zoom in and read works well. Highly recommend.
