Description

Put on your roller skates now!

Drag your finger left to right to avoid the obstacles and race down the path! Are you skilled enough to have zero hits?

Unique controls - control your legs and stretch them to choose the right way. Be reactive and get to the bonus level!

Sky Roller Lover , 11/19/2020

Awesome! Could use a bit of work though.

This is a great game and I really enjoy playing it, and I haven’t encountered many bugs or glitches. I just wanted to write a couple suggestions for new designs and such. First, I think you should add different ramp designs, you could have preset color combos, or you could have custom colors that the player could make, that you would have to buy. I love all the characters and skates, but in my opinion, after you buy all the common and uncommon characters and skates, you have no other use for diamonds, so I would love to see an option to buy the hero’s and legendary characters and skates. I also think you should have an option to create a custom character that the player could make to make the game more personal. I think you should also make more emotes that you could buy for the characters to do when you finish a level. That’s only a little bit of the suggestions that I thought of, but it’s a great game overall and doesn’t need much fixing. Do what you can!

Great game, just a few things...

I really love this game. Ever since I got it, I’ve been playing it nonstop. One time, my younger cousin wanted to play, and he tried to change the colors of the roller skates. He obviously couldn’t. So, I would recommend adding that to the game. Another thing is, maybe different dances for different types of characters. For example, the Common characters stick with the dance they have, and then different dances for the Uncommons, Heroes, and Legendaries. It might take a lot of coding, but I just thought it was a good idea. Last but not least, the advertisements. I get it, there’s a thing about sponsors, and possibly paying for ad-free, but unfortunately, some people don’t have the money, and there are way too many ads.
