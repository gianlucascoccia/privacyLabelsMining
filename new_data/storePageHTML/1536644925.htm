Description

From the creators of top NBA basketball games NBA 2K22, NBA 2K22 Arcade Edition, and NBA 2K Mobile, NBA SuperCard is the legendary sports card battle game pitting NBA and WNBA All-Stars and legends from your favorite teams against each other in an all-out battle on the court. Play now as the new Season 1 tips-off this fall featuring brand new card tiers, innovative new designs, and new events with more to come!

Sports card collecting meets epic card battles! Collect cards across generations of NBA and WNBA all stars. Train them, level up their unique stats, and bring old and new rivalries to life. Build your deck from hundreds of collectible cards and form the ultimate team of your favorite basketball stars!

Battle cards across a variety of game modes such as always-on Quick Game, Champions Mode, Team Battleground, and Head-2-Head. Experience the challenging new game mode: Ball Hog, and face off against powerful players in epic boss battles to improve your deck.

Card collecting games and the NBA make a dynamic duo! Hit the hardwood and take on special challenges to unlock exclusive new basketball cards, new packs, and new ways to have fun this season. Participate in new events to earn rewards and special edition cards!

Build your deck, get on the court, and immerse yourself in the new Season 1 of NBA SuperCard!

The General Consensus

Very slow start as far as content and game modes but it’s new so eventually those things will come. Great time killer and very addictive! I believe everyone in the community can agree on changing 3 key things:

1-Packs in store either need to be cheaper or have their odds increased, right now they just aren’t worth even half the value.

2-please add either a skip animation button or have it in the settings to just turn off animations like the old 2k games used to have so we can get through our games quicker.

3- the grind is set far too high. The drop rates are low and the gauntlet takes too many games to complete. Can we take like 5-10 games off the final 5 cards? Playing the same deck 40+ times over and over just get an undercard is far to tedious.

Other than those three things the game is perfect for it’s intended purpose and extremely easy to just pick up, learn, and play!

JJMH2424 , 01/17/2021

Great game with a lot of potential

This game is somehow very addicting but it just doesn't play super well. It has a ton of potential and I think there are already a lot of great things about it. Some things that could change is making quick games quicker with a 2x button, being able to swap players in and out of lineups easier, and more things to do within your team. There could be team events where all the points from everyone on your team gets an extra reward and there should also be an option to trade or donate to players on your team. I think the store is fine being able to buy packs to improve quicker but there should be a way to get all players pro for ftp players like the Jamal Murray and Jokic cards that just came out in quests. This game will grow fast with some simple quality of life changes and more things to do within your team. Keep up the good work!

snwns , 07/24/2021

Pretty good

If you’ve played WWE SuperCard this is basically the same, but easier to get into because it’s new and there aren’t a ton of card tiers just yet. Most of the modes are the same but I do like how player’s positions actually matter which is a nice twist. The game rewards you the more you grind, it does have timers but you don’t wait forever and grinding quick play is legit. Much like WWE you either pay or be patient or RNG will brutalize you with the packs though. Champions requires more effort than King of the Ring which is a blessing and a curse but worth it for the rewards. Much like WWE, it’s easy to pick up and play but with enough depth to sit and play for a while when you get into it. It is still missing quite a few things though, additional modes, Fortifying cards and gear being the main two I can think of but this is a solid start so far. Would be awesome to see an NFL or UFC SuperCard btw!
