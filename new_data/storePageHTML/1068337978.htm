Description

Stream free TV and movies on your iPhone, iPad or Apple TV. XUMO features 160+ channels, thousands of Hollywood movies, 24/7 network news, popular TV shows, sports coverage and plenty of kids and family friendly programming. No credit card or sign up required.

“...this is better than some of the paid streaming services. Lots of interesting channels with original content...it streams perfectly.”


Watch what you want on XUMO. Our app offers a wide variety of programming that will entertain, educate and enthrall viewers of all ages.

Stream FREE Hollywood Movies
Check out XUMO’s impressive selection of free, on-demand movies. Relax with an old time classic, a hair-raising thriller, a laugh out loud comedy, or tear-jerker drama. When you open the XUMO app, you open the doors to a film library that contains over 1,000 movie titles in every genre.

Stream FREE Family Movies and Kids Shows
XUMO’s selection of free family movies and kid friendly TV shows are available on 13+ on-demand channels including Ameba, batteryPOP, Toon Goggles and Cool School. Animated and live-action shows, engaging games, educational content, and kid-safe radio captures the interest and imagination of toddlers to tweens.


Stream FREE News
Stay on top of the latest national headlines and world news events with 24/7 news programs from trusted networks including: NBC News, CBSN, Newsmax TV, Time, Bloomberg News, and USA Today.

Stream FREE Sports Channels
Enjoy live and on-demand game highlights, commentary and coverage on 25+ free sports channels including FOX Sports, PGA TOUR, Sports Illustrated, STADIUM, MMA Junkie and Nitro Circus, just to name just a few.

Disappointed!

I got the hang of using the app, but I was surprised when the movie I was watching was interrupted by the beginning of another movie. I thought that maybe I missed something about how to use the app, but after 8 or 9 times, just how patient should I be? I gave it a fair chance, but somehow the moments of movie magic are completely obliterated when an entirely different movie interrupts the original actors' dialogue. Too bad. The whole point of the app, I thought, is to engage the customer in interesting programs or movies. Nope. So sorry. If I want to ditch a movie I'm watching in order to watch something else, then I should be the one handling the process.
You're not taking any money from me, but you are taking minutes of my time that I can't recover. The experience is doomed to be a waste from the beginning. I sure have enough opportunity for that without looking for yet another chance to waste more time!

Ruined One of my Favorites

I just wanted to show V/H/S to my family. This was the only free option. Xuno interrupted the quick-cutting horror anthology action by interrupting the flow of the film literally every 10-15 minutes with an average of 7 commercials each time. It doesn’t tell you how many commercials you’re getting each time or how long it’s going to last. What was a barely two hour long movie was instead around 2:40 of repeating uninteresting commercials that cut into the pacing abruptly and disturbingly. I am not watching anything on here again - all the other options such as tubi and Peacock do free better.
