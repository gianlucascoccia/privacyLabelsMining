Description

The CBS46 News app brings you breaking news, weather, traffic and sports headlines for Metro Atlanta and North Georgia. Activate the push alerts and get breaking news the moment it happens. Get your news and weather on the go. Watch our streaming newscasts live on your phone and TV. This app allows you to pause on your phone and resume watching on TV. Track storms down to your street with our interactive radar. Plus, Atlanta’s most accurate forecast from the CBS46 Pinpoint Weather Team is updated throughout the day and available anytime.
