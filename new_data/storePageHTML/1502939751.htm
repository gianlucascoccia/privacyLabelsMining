Description

XLaunch is a SpaceX launch tracking app for space enthusiasts with an iOS-centric design and packed with information so you can stay updated on the latest SpaceX launches & related news.

Features:
- Notifications for upcoming launches
- Browse all past and future SpaceX launches, and view details about the rocket, payload, launch pad, landing site, and more.
- Watch livestreams directly from SpaceX right in the app.
- View hi-res photos from each launch.
- Live countdowns so you know when the next launch hits T-0.
- Read the latest SpaceX and related space news in the News tab.
- Bookmark your favorite launches and news articles for quick reference later.
- Links to SpaceX press releases, Wikipedia, Reddit threads, and more to stay in the loop for each launch.
- Widgets for iOS 14
- Full Dark Mode support
- 7 custom app icons
- Choose a color theme from 10 different colors, or create your own unique color

Rocket and Payloads
Read about SpaceX rockets and learn about their capabilities, including technical details about size, engines, payload capacity, orbits, and more.

Launch and Landing Pads
Learn more about where SpaceX launches and lands their rockets with details and maps for each site, including the historic Launch Complex 39A at Kennedy Space Center and the Of Course I Still Love You drone ship.
