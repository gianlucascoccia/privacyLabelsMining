Description

Connecting you to fresh, family and fun has never been easier than downloading the new Stew Leonard's mobile app. Earn, save and redeem points for free Ice Cream easier than ever before. Discover the latest Stew's Products and be the first to know about our weekly specials, events and other special offers.

- Earn points towards Free Ice Cream or Coffee each time you shop at Stew Leonard's
- Easily track and redeem rewards right from your smartphone at checkout
- View weekly specials and offers for great savings while you shop
- Conveniently browse StewLeonards.com for special events, new products, and Stew's fun from your phone

Thanks for shopping with Stew Leonard's. We look forward to adding additional enhancements, features, and benefits in the coming months.

Aggravating

The app is good, however, it is very aggravating when you cannot log into it when you are in Stew Leonard's. Most of the time I have to try and try and try. I get so annoyed I forget about it even when I know I can give phone number to cashier. Sometimes that doesn't even work. I'm told I can go to customer service for help but by then I just want to leave. Something has to be looked into to make it easier.

Billy Birdfan , 06/07/2020

Frustrating

This app frustrates the heck out of me! Out of the last 3 times that I’ve had my app scanned at the register it’s only posted once. I went to Customer Service and she was clueless as to why it wasn’t posting properly (me too). She then asked somebody who said the the points take 24-hours to post. Four days later . . . Nada. I emailed Stew’s from their website and nobody cared enough to respond. It did work on my most recent visit (yesterday) but I’m still missing points for the two previous visits. Why bother having an app if it’s not going to award the points you promise??

hamyls , 08/08/2021

Needs Apple wallet integration

The app is okay but it’s annoying that you have to open the full app for the store to scan your barcode and that only works when connected to Wi-Fi or cellular data. It would be nice if the app had Apple wallet integration so you could use all those features without the hassle of opening the app every time at checkout.
