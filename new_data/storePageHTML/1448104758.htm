Description

Ship makes finding a date more fun by bringing your friends into the dating app experience. On Ship, your friends find matches for you. Even your friends in relationships can help set you up, so you can all share in the crushes, the ghostings, and the situationships together. It used to take a village, now it takes a group chat.

How it works:
• Set up your profile, as either a dater or a matchmaker
• Start matching for yourself or for your friends
• Chat about prospects
• Follow each other’s dating activity
• Catch yourself actually enjoying a dating app

Download Ship for free to find out why Engadget says “Shipping – or swiping for a friend – is taking off”.

Danibea76 , 02/01/2020

Not great...

My friend asked me to join her “crew” since she’s had bad luck with finding good guys, and I was excited to help; however, after opening the app, I very quickly realized I’d be zero help in her search.

I found you typically get the very basics - pictures, height, job title, alma mater, and distance from you. I just wish they’d maybe make more sections mandatory? Or ask users right away if they’re interested in a hook-up or relationship or something, and maybe allow the hook-up profile to be more sparse (not my thing, but no judgements here), but ensure other users have a more robust profile.

If you’re like me and are more interested in finding someone you could actually click with intellectually as well as physically, you might want to get something else.
