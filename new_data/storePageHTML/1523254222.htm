Description

Take the auto-battle sim genre and add a splash of ridiculous by adding dozens of cute and vicious animals to the fray! Pack your animal army with lions, dogs, bears, and more before sending them into battle to win gold and glory in Animal Warfare.

Each animal fights differently and no battle is the same! Build and upgrade your army your way to take on challenging fights.

Animal Warfare features:
- Deep and addictive progression systems
- A crisp and minimalistic art style
- An expansive campaign with 100s of unique levels
- A huge roster of animals to collect, each with unique perks and abilities
- A Challenge Mode where you can earn bonus rewards
- Epic Boss Battles to test your skill!

lisanae562 , 02/21/2021

Great game!!

I, personally, like this game. I got tired of those games with a bunch of adds and a lot of repetitive things, but this one is genuinely nice. Even though it is warfare between animals, there isn’t any gory or too violent things. For example, once you defeat an animal, it turns into tiny blocks and there is no blood marks or deadly attacking left. Also, you won’t get pestered by ads all the time. You could probably play up to 8 or 10 games before you get another ad. The rewards are really cool as they usually give you chunks of coins as you improve your animal army. You can merge animals to make them level up to have more easy and quick attacks and most of the animals are free.(depending on what card you can pick) Anyway, these are just the things I personally like about this game. Thank you to the developers who made a wonderful game like this!

Very Fun And Very Good Game! Just Some Feedback

To the people who are just quick reading the reviews to see if this is a good game, in my opinion, it really is. If your a person who likes animals, merging, and battling, this is a game for you! But I have some issues. My game started crashing a lot recently. One time, I pressed play level and the screen became black. I waited a little to see what would happen and it just logged me out of the app. And, every time there is an ad, which there are not many :), almost ever time it ends and I try to tap the X, it crashes. And some feedback is that instead of having a million animals in your stable, you can upgrade the slots for your army with coins or gems or even both! Like maybe 3000 coins and 100 gems. It’s getting kind of annoying to put all my animals that I’m getting into the stable. Next, maybe you can do online, multiplayer battling. I like the way it is right now, but maybe the option to online battle with other people would be pretty cool, but make sure they’re close to your level! And maybe some ideas could be that you can partner up with someone else and battle another pair of partners together online. This is just feedback and an issue, but overall, this is a very fun and addicting game! I hope you can fix my crashing difficulties, but other than that, I cant wait to see more updates from you! Have a good day!

Good premise!

Original review removed by author due to issues being resolved after recent update. Great idea to have a pseudo-idle game with bears that shoot laser beams. You aren’t so much involved in the battles as you choose your animals and their levels after purchasing cards, and from there your animals fight the reddish colored bad guy animals. You have your typical chests you watch videos to earn for animals, your premium gem currency, your skippable videos after every 3-5 battles, and x3 coins to earn after battles with full length videos. Some days, the daily challenges take 30 min, some days they take hours, but they’re doable even if you don’t watch videos to open chests early. Looking forward to the new animals and the new tree of life feature from this update (1.4.6, I believe?). You will have ads after battles, but they’ll be skippable, so the full length ads are only by choice. Only reason for 4 stars is because I’m leery of putting 5 when there were so many initial difficulties that may come back soon. So far, no trouble with new update with freezing and lagging.
