Description

Follow a colorful blessing of globetrotting unicorns as they gallop around the world on an incredible whistle-stop tour.

From a star-studded film premiere and a flamboyant festival, to a wild safari adventure and a beautiful tropical beach, there are 20 intricately illustrated scenes to search, and seven sensational unicorns to spot in every location.

This ultimate search app challenges you to find 7 friendly unicorns among each colourful and vibrant scene in their active adventures. Follow these cute, legendary unicorns as they jet off across 20 exciting game levels.

Take your search skills to the extreme - once you've found all seven unicorns, you'll be given a checklist of ten 'star find' hidden items to spot once you've located each and every missing alicorn.

Includes a relaxing soundtrack of music specifically produced for the app including pieces by composers such as Debussy, Chopin, Mozart and Liszt as well as several original compositions to help you unwind and focus on finding the mysterious unicorns in their artfully drawn universe.

Based on the best-selling 'Where's the Unicorn?' and the sequel 'Where's the Unicorn Now?', by Michael O'Mara Books.

Marenlaurence , 09/25/2020

Fun and great artistic graphics but a little clunky

Used to have a where’s Waldo app that sadly no longer exists, so I was excited to find this! It’s fun and the scenes are well done. The issues I have with the app are it’s user friendliness and typos. For example, to start the app, the “play game” button is the third small button in a list.. after about and “how to play”. The buttons are all super close together , and I sometimes press the top button out of habit. It’s just a weird format. Also, there are a couple typos, I don’t think it’s made by an English speaker. But you can generally figure out what it means. For example, one task is to “find a child on a ‘sledge’”. (??) but you can deduce it means sled. Lol

Overall great concept, but could stand to be improved! I recommend it.
