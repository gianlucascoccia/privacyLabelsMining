AmyB8484 , 09/29/2018

Works! Just one thing...

This app really works and gets you into a trance faster than other hypnosis apps I've tried. It actually gets me way more relaxed than any guided meditation I've ever done. I wish they'd add more tracks, but they've got a decent selection to start with, and it's worth the money to upgrade.

I wish the part of the recording at the end of each session that brings you out of the trance were on its own separate recording that you could put at the end of the playlist, rather than at the end of each individual track. There's one recording for the intro part where they get you into the trance, separate recordings for each topic where they give you the actual affirmations that you set on your playlist to play right after that first track, but the end of each recording brings you out of the trance, so you can't stack more than one together, and they're very short. Because when you would finish the first one, now it's brought you out of the trance at the end, so when the second set of affirmations would start to play, it's not going to work because you're not in a trance anymore. You'd have to play the beginning part and get hypnotized back into a trance all over again. This completely defeats the purpose of having a playlist feature. On top of that, it's obvious that the outros are all the same recording just tacked onto the end of each track anyway, so it might as well be on a separate track.

But overall, great app, very cool.
