The only Holy Bible with Augmented Reality (AR) Support that talks to you! Approved to be read in churches & for personal use. Full offline, audio, daily verses and more

The much anticipated and sought after Offline Smart Bible is finally here, An award winning app! Not just any offline Holy bible in King James Version(KJV) or New International Version(NIV). This is a smart and intelligent holy bible app approved and appointed to be read in churches. With augmented reality support plus a user friendly interface, It’s easy to use, completely offline and millions of people are using it. What are you waiting for?

Some top features of Holy Bible Offline AR:

- Augmented Reality Support - Get closer to the word with an Augmented Reality experience

- Holy Bible talks to you! High quality audio allows the Bible to be read to you out loud

- Auto scroll allows the Bible to scroll automatically

- Full offline use. You don’t need the internet to use it.

- Add your own text notes, voice notes and sermons, and record preachings, sermons, praise and worships, church services and personal notes.

- Complete Holy Bible containing both Old and New Testaments

- Full offline Speech and audio support - Smart Noah like Siri can automatically scroll and read out loud scriptures to you.

- Daily Verses : Get encouraged with daily devotional messages, motivational verses and inspirational verses to help you in your day to day life and overcome life obstacles

- Highlight, bookmark and save sections of scriptures.

- Copy, share, translate scriptures, daily verses, bookmarks and devotions with friends and families via Whatsapp, Google+, Facebook, Twitter, email, & SMS


The Holy Bible in King James Bible (KJV) and New International Version (NIV) has been downloaded and installed by millions of believers around the world. It’s FREE, easy and a friendly way to read and listen to the whole Bible with no need of internet connection.

The smart Holy Bible in your pocket! The Holy Bible in the new testaments and old testaments. Study the bible daily to empower you spiritually through your walk with God.

This top app comes with complete offline use and supports pre-recorded speech with high quality audio, smart sermon, voice notes, prayers and personal recordings. Backed by the personal digital artificial intelligent John - he can automatically read scriptures to your hearing, scroll verses intelligently and read out devotions and daily verses to you. Just select the chapter and verse and Noah will recite it for you. You can also record live church services!

This Holy Bible(KJV, NIV) allows you to highlight and bookmark any part of scriptures like you would in real life, add personal notes, voice recordings and sermons, copy, paste, share scriptures with friends and family on WhatsApp, Facebook, twitter etc.
