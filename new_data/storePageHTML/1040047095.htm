Description

Join Spoon, the social audio platform loved by 30M+ people worldwide — and growing fast! Discover music, talk shows, news, and other audio content. Livestream conversations and meet friends with social chat!

Spoon is an interactive audio livestream platform that lets your voice be heard. Starting your own podcast has NEVER been this easy!

There’s so much to explore with live streams, music, debates, and just talking with new friends. Host a stream, spark a conversation, and meet listeners and fellow creators with just your voice and your phone.

AUDIO CONTENT YOU WANT
Talks, lives, and much more. Plus, you can easily search for the topics you want to listen to like comedy, anime, ASMR, and more! Our team also hand picks content by emerging streamers for you to check out. Did we mention our original live podcasts? Listen (and even call in) to Spoon Originals weekly live streams.

STREAM TOGETHER WITH LIVES
Live streaming on Spoon is more fun. Period. As a streamer, you can invite listeners to co-host at any time. Need help choosing the next song? Set up a poll in the stream for listeners to decide! Want to help out your listeners? Get interactive! Ask anonymous questions with our request box feature. Support your favorite streamers with likes and animated digital gifts. After all, streaming is better together.

PODCASTS? WE’VE GOT ‘EM
For Creators: Forget about fancy mics and hours of editing — go live, and save it as a podcast! Make your podcast more interesting with listener interaction, and spark conversation with live social chat.

For Listeners: Missed a livestream? No problem! Listen to recordings of live streams whenever you want without any annoying ads. And discover podcasts you enjoy faster with our enhanced search and recommendation system.

For Musicians: Hit record, perform, and share your music on Spoon to interactive audiences.

LET’S TALK SAFETY
One of our biggest priorities is making sure our users are able to stay safe on our platform. Our moderation team actively monitors content and is regularly trained. Spoon is absolutely committed to creating a uniquely empathetic and non-toxic space online. For more information on how we keep Spoon awesome and safe, you can check out our community guidelines here: https://bit.ly/2Q31f54.
