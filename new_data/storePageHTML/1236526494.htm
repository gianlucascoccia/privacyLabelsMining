Exclusive Features:

Paytrack holds innovative technologies and algorithms to be exact with every calculation it makes. Here is a list of conditions that the app will consider:

- Sick Pay

Paytrack will allow you to set a Pairing as “Sick” and depending on how many hours you have in your Sick Bank, it will process the calculation accordingly.

- Keeping Track of your Sick Banks

The Sick Banks will be automatically updated every month, and you will be able to see them under your user's settings. This way you may keep track of how many hours you have and how to use them if necessary.

- Vacation Pay

When you set your days of Vacation, Paytrack will recognize them and add those hours into your schedule.

- Lead Pay

With various algorithms inside Paytrack, all you need to do is let it know if you decide to fly Lead and you'll know exactly how much you will earn.

- Pay Protected

Pay Protection is also in Paytrack. If any of your trips are canceled and you are Pay Protected, simply go into your Pairing, and drop it with Pay Protection. This will allow Paytrack to know what to do next.

- Account Protection & Cloud Synchronization

By creating an account, Paytrack will automatically save all of your information in the Cloud, allowing it to synchronize with all of your devices at once. If you ever need to change to a new phone, all you have to do is download the application, log into your account and all of your information will be downloaded instantly.

- Overtime Pay vs. Time & Half

The confusion of not knowing when your Time & Half kicks in is over! With Paytrack, you will be able to distinguish how much you are making of Overtime versus Time & Half.

- Junior Assignment

Yep, it will also calculate Junior Assignments.

- Check Breakdown

By clicking on the Checks icon, you will see a breakdown of BOTH upcoming checks. It has been designed to resemble the company provided Pay Stub, so that you may compare it accordingly when it is Pay Day!
