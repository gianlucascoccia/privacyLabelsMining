Editors’ Notes

Whether your groove is ’60s rock, Taylor Swift, or anything in between, this expertly crafted music-trivia game has all the tunes you’ll want to name. With clips from more than 10,000 songs, it’s easy to get hooked on its multiple-choice competitions, and the excellent new Party mode tests your skills against the masses in daily tournaments. Thankfully, if you played the first SongPop, your progress, coins, and so on carry over into this impressive sequel.

So sad the owners got so greedy and ruined this game😢
