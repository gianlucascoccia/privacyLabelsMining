Description

The variety you crave, the instructors you love, the results you want: Go all in, full out with obé Fitness. Look forward to your workout—wherever you are—with up to 20 daily LIVE classes and 7,000+ on-demand. Try 7 days free and cancel at any time.

Founded by entertainment execs Mark Mullet and Ashley Mills, obé was born to bring the best in boutique fitness to your fingertips. No commute, no waitlists, no pricey hardware—just amazing variety, world-class instructors, and a community that cheers you on.

Whether you’re craving dance cardio, feel like lifting heavy, have a passion for Pilates, or want to take a Ride, obé has you covered. Fit in a 28-minute session, take a 10-minute Express, or level up with a 45- or 60-minute challenge!

SWEAT WITH THE BEST: obé brings the top instructors in the fitness biz directly to YOU. Each instructor brings their unique personality and experience to the platform, providing an engaging, studio-style experience wherever you are. Tuning in LIVE? Expect fire soundtracks and listen out for personal shoutouts!

CURATE YOUR OWN COLLECTIONS: Do obé YOUR way by organizing classes into categories like Bodyweight Strength, #NoExcuses Express, or Rest Day Stretches.

TUNE INTO POPULAR MUSIC: With Replay, you can take most of our popular music classes, including our only-at-obé themed classes, once if you missed them LIVE.

STAY ACCOUNTABLE WITH PROGRAMS + CHALLENGES: Our curated programs and time-based challenges help you stay on track to reach your fitness goals and see results that really last. Boost your mood with the Endorphins Program, or level up with Hard AF to build strength, power, and endurance.

JOIN THE COMMUNITY: Meet the fiercest feel-good community in fitness! Join the obé fam on and you've got a powerful crew in your corner, whatever your goals are.

STREAM ON YOUR FAVORITE DEVICES: Sweat at home or on the go: Watch on your computer, phone, tablet, or cast to the big screen with Chromecast, Apple TV, FireTV and Roku.

GO ALL IN, EVEN IF WIFI IS FULL OUT: Download up to 7 classes to your device and stream offline, wherever you are.

SUPPORT: Reach out to obé with questions, comments, or a little love at any time directly through our in-app member support portal.

KendallAsh24 , 06/17/2020

Everything I Need!

I’ve been using the Obé app for three months now and there’s nothing more I could possibly need! I never write app reviews but If you’re looking for a new, challenging and diverse fitness plan, you should get Obé! You really get such a great fitness routine for the price per month compared to in studio costs and can get your workout done faster by doing it at home. There is such a wide selection of types of workouts that I never get bored and can do something different every day. It’s really simple to navigate the app and stream to your TV. I work out later in the day so I take full advantage of the replay classes and there’s always so many to choose from. The instructors are all great, innovative and explain things really well. This is the first time taking classes “somewhere” where I don’t have favorite instructors because I like them all. The 28 minute classes fly by and I love the option of level up 45 minute classes and the short 10-11 minute classes are a great add on if I want to work something a little more. You can sort by type of equipment you have to find classes you can do or sort by type if class. You can even save 7 classes to has offline which I just did for an upcoming trip! Really the only tiny thing I wish the app had was an option to sort by most recently saved in my favorites but really there is literally nothing else that Obé doesn’t check off for me. I’m seeing great results for these 3 months of work which keeps me motivated!

I've been running injury free the last year + and my running times have all improved.

Sarita from Chicago , 10/11/2021

Love the content but app itself needs improvement

I love the wide variety of classes that obé offers and how straight-forward and accessible the instructors are. The instructors are very encouraging and they make the workouts challenging no matter the level. Thanks to obé , I’ve been able to get fit and keep a consistent workout schedule, something I was never able to do when I was doing exercises on my own. I always want to workout bc of the fun workouts, particularly as obé makes it really fun to work out with friends! My minor complaint on content is it would be nice if they had music rights for replays; the generic music isn’t fun. It’s particularly a bummer that when there are fun themes- like 90s pop-those aren’t available for replays bc of the music rights issue. Other than that, I love the content!

My biggest problem I have is with the actual app itself. I’m not sure if it’s due to my having an outdated iPhone but it is really challenging to mirror onto Apple TV from the obé app at times. If I don’t do it in a certain order, it doesn’t work (I don’t have a problem with it with other apps). Also, sometimes the app doesn’t even start and I have to give it a little bit of time, which is a problem when I’m trying to join a live class. I also don’t appreciate that it periodically logs out you out for no reason. Again, it’s annoying to do that when you are trying to join a class. The filtering could be more user friendly too as it’s slow.
