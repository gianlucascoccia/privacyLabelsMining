Description

We’ve updated the app based on your feedback:

• Upload photos for your auto and pet policies

• Choose a repair shop, drive-in, rental car or other service when filing a personal auto insurance claim

• Track your claim

• Face ID & Touch ID login

• Apple Pay

• Performance – Better, faster and more stable

• View pending payments and future bills

• Scan the VIN to add or replace a vehicle on your policy

• Refresh main screens by “pulling down”

Other features:

• Quickly pay your bill

• Add, edit and delete preferred payment methods

• Add, edit and stop automatic payments*

• View, save and share auto & pet insurance ID card

• Manage your auto policy

• View and save Auto and Homeowners policy documents

• Accident Checklist – Steps to consider if you are in an accident

• Get roadside assistance

• File an auto or property claim

• Agent contact info

• Manage your document delivery and communications preferences

• Get an auto, renters or homeowners insurance quote



* Subject to eligibility

Nucky for short , 05/11/2021

Quite literally useless app

The company I work for uses nationwide for their 401K provider and apart from their app, I've found them to be pretty good. That being said, I don't know how a company this size can't get someone to improve this app, it honestly might be the most basic, useless app I've ever used. I can literally only do 2 things on the app, see by balance and YTD return or see my balance and my yearly return. That's it. I can't see what funds I'm holding specifically in my target date fund, no graph showing growth since starting my 401K, no option to allocate my funds differently, etc... I can only see my balance and that is it. I use other brokerage apps and you can do so many things but for some reason nationwide has decided to put 0 effort into their app. I do most things on my phone like a lot of younger people and this fact alone would stop me from investing any other money I have with them but unfortunately I don't have a choice in my 401K provider. Seriously nationwide, hire someone to make your app even a little bit useful.
