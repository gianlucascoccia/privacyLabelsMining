Description

GAME GOLF is a FREE app that tracks a golfer’s on-course performance and provides you with Rangefinder distances as you play. This is not a trial version and comes with major updates for the 2018!

OVERVIEW
GPS SHOT TRACKING & RANGEFINDER DISTANCES
Improvement comes from knowing your game and GAME GOLF will help you to manage the course like a pro. Get instant feedback on every shot you hit with precise distances to greens, hazards and landing zones on over 36,000 courses worldwide.

PERFORMANCE ANALYTICS
STATS INCLUDE:
- Club distances, Strokes Gained Analysis, Tee and Approach shot accuracy, Greens hit, Fairways hit, Scrambling, Putts per hole, Average distances, Distances per club and MORE.

“GAME GOLF’s cutting edge technology allows the many golfers who play this game the opportunity to keep track of every shot they hit in a seamless fashion; and the feedback they receive will provide both them and their instructor with a roadmap to improvement that you just can’t learn on the range alone.”
Sean Foley, world renowned golf instructor


NEW FOR 2018!
SMART CADDIE (Premium)
GAME GOLF smart caddie helps golfers make data-driven decisions as they play. It’s like having your own personal caddie who considers every shot you’ve ever hit, has identified all of your tendencies and considers course and weather conditions.

KEY FEATURES
- Advanced Hole Strategy
Gain insight for every shot on every hole you play.
- Real-Time Playing Conditions
Suggestions factor in weather conditions and elevation.
- Club Recommendations
Based on your historical data, location and playing conditions.

COACHING DASHBOARD (Free)
Designed with the input of PGA of America Golf Professionals, the new Coaching Dashboard not only allows you to accurately determine the parts of your game that need improvement, but it also makes it easy to share your data with your PGA Professional.

It allows players and PGA Professional’s to speed the improvement process by tracking a player’s strengths, weaknesses and tendencies on one page. It also allows the PGA Professional to build lesson plans based on a players on-course performance analytics.

APP FEATURES
TRACK
Seamlessly track your game and we will generate your statistics based on performance.

VIEW
Get a visual representation of your game, showing your location on the course and what clubs you used and your performance analytics for the day.

COMPARE
Compare your game to your friends and the pro's. See how others play the same courses you play and how you match up.

GAIN INSIGHTS
At a glance, understand the meaning behind your data. View your trends over time to learn about your game.

SHARE
Allow friends, coaches and PGA Professionals to see your progress via email, the app and Social Media.

KEY FEATURES
Live shot tracking and club distances*
Live shot editing in-app*
Live scorecard*
Lock screen mode: save battery and have the most essential rangefinder information at a quick glance

*Not with GAME GOLF Classic

SMARTWATCH
Support for Apple Smartwatches, featuring quick access to GPS distances (front, middle, back), add and edit shots.

HealthKit integration counts your steps during your round.

Data usage: 5-10 MB per round depending on the course and usage by the user. This includes the map download.

Note: Continued use of GPS running in the background can decrease battery life.
GAME GOLF smart caddie subscriptions are available in Annual ($49.99) and Monthly ($5.99) terms. Payment for Monthly subscriptions will be charged to the user's iTunes account at confirmation of purchase, and Annual subscriptions will be charged upon expiration of the 30-Day free trial. GAME GOLF smart caddie subscription will automatically renew for the original subscription price unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period from the user's Account Settings. Any unused portion of a free trial period will be forfeited when the user purchases a subscription.

No support

When I first started using the Pro version, it worked great. At the end of last year, it stopped working in the middle of a round. I had to reset the medallion. When I started using it this year, it took the entire round to download the course even though I downloaded the course the night before and didn’t track any shots. The next time I tried to use it, I started the download when I got to the course and it finished downloading on the 3rd hole. Then, the smart caddie feature wasn’t working. The same thing happened the next time I used it. It was tracking my shots but stopped tracking them on the back 9. The smart caddie feature still wasn’t working. I tried to look on the website for support, I kept getting an error message saying that page wasn’t available. I tried to use the support on the app and it wouldn’t go to the support page. I also tried to email the support website and haven’t gotten a response. I don’t know if they want you to buy another medallion when it quits working but if I have to buy another one, I will buy another brand.

xCaptFrostyx , 05/18/2020

App and tags are good but that’s it

I’ve been a GameGolf user for years and overall I’m happy with the product. The reason for this review is solely based on the app and the support. First of all, the app is very glitchy, and requires a few restarts to get it going. I’ll download a course beforehand, then the app will want to download it again. Second, whatever you do DO NOT subscribe to the Smart Caddie. The two times I tried it it took over 20 minutes to work after it was done “downloading the weather”. It isn’t worth the money. And finally, don’t ever expect to get any kind of support. Their support page is no longer working and if you try to email them, it bounces back.

Overall I’m happy with the hardware and basic app usage, but that’s it. If it wasn’t so expensive I’d switch to Arccos.
