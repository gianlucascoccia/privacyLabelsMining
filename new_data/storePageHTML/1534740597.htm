One-Dimensional, room for improvement

I loved the game as I progressed through, but after unlocking everything, I didn’t find a lot of motivation to play. I wish I could play with friends. There is a ton of potential for improvement: *tournaments can be several matches; the single match is no different than any other match *PvP, I’m sure this is more difficult than people realize, but it would be worth it *make skins/boards/bags/effect have special traits (i.e. ice bags slide easily, “kapow” effect can push opponent’s bag away, make the desert board sticky, etc) kind of like fruit ninja *achievements come too quick. *different play modes (play to 21, alternate who starts, partner/team play, etc) *$7/week is a quite steep for a one-dimensional game. $60 in two months is what you would pay for a AAA game. Pros: The art design is great! Mechanics/physics are spot on and simple enough to play for a few minutes and then put down. Fun unlockables. Cons: No PvP - it’s all simple computer AI and offers no depth other than skin changes.
