Description

Digital note-taking, simplified! Feel the joy of modern note-taking through one of the most fluid productivity apps. Convert your iPad into a digital notebook and create beautiful handwritten notes. A feature-rich notes app, useful for students, working professionals, bullet journalists and other note-takers. Noteshelf app supports PDF annotations, audio recording, conversion of writing to text and more!


EXPERIENCE NATURAL HANDWRITING
* Note your ideas with a natural handwriting almost as if writing on a real notepad
* Create good notes by customizing your writing. Choose from a range of pens, pencils, and highlighters
* Switch between pens, eraser and highlighter with a single tap


SEARCH/ CONVERT HANDWRITTEN NOTES/ OCR
* You can search through your handwritten notes and even convert them to text
* Noteshelf recognizes over 65 languages of handwriting


MARKUP DOCUMENTS & IMAGES
* Import PDFs, MS Office documents or images; or add them to your existing notebooks.
* Highlight, underline or write notes on your documents/images


BACKUP, SYNC & SHARE
* Backup your notes to Google Drive, Dropbox or One Drive and retrieve them any time, anywhere
* Sync all your notes via iCloud and use them on all your devices seamlessly
* Export and share your notes as a pdf, png or as a noteshelf file


EDIT YOUR NOTES WITH EASE
* Select and move your text anywhere, change the size and colors in a snap
* With the Undo and Redo options, corrections can now be made more quickly than in a notepad
* Erase entire strokes or just the highlighter with the new eraser modes
* Write precisely by using the zoom in feature
* Draw perfect geometrical shapes for all your mind maps


AUDIO RECORD NOTE
* Record audio as you take notes so you never miss out on anything important at school or work
* Add as many recordings as you want onto your pages and play them back anytime, even as you handwrite notes


PRESENTATION MODE
* The new presentation mode allows you to turn the app into a whiteboard and give engaging presentations
* Use the laser pointer and neon pens in the app to bring the presentation to life


MULTI-TASK LIKE A PRO
* Open multiple windows of Noteshelf and work simultaneously with ease
* This multi screen workflow of using 2 notes side by side will work only on iPads running on the iPad OS


GO HANDS-FREE WITH APPLE WATCH
* Create voice notes with Noteshelf using the Apple Watch
* Auto-save all your recordings to the iPhone and use iCloud to sync them across devices. Take notes later while you listen to those recordings


DO MORE THAN JUST BOOKMARK
* Sometimes, digital notes might run into hundreds of pages. So, bookmark important pages, name and color them to search with ease


EVERNOTE AUTO-PUBLISH
* Auto-publish your notes one-way into Evernote and access them on Evernote, from just about anywhere


SOME OTHER FEATURES
* The expansive set of paper templates and covers in Noteshelf will keep you and your note-taking experience fresh. Use the new Custom Digital Diaries to create diaries based on your needs
* Add your most used pens and highlighters to the Movable Favorites Toolbar for easy access
* Secure your notes with passcodes. Use Face ID or Touch ID to lock and unlock your notebooks
* Use Siri commands to create a new notebook, search for an existing notebook, or open a notebook in a snap
* Keep your personal and business notes separate. Drag and drop notebooks into groups or categories easily on your iPad notes
* We support a dark color scheme - as beautiful as the system-wide dark mode. (Only on iOS 13 for iPhones and iPad OS for iPads)
* Noteshelf is on the way to delight it’s users with amazing features which makes it one of the top paid apps on your iPad.

Noteshelf is really great, just one thing keeps me using Goodnotes

Writing on Noteshelf is simply a pleasure, it is so smooth, the virtual pencil options are very elegant and well designed, it even seems my handwriting becomes (barely) legible. Evernote integration is very handy, much easier than exporting each note (though it could be much easier if you could set it to sync all new notes by default...), it was what made me buy it.

There is one small detail that makes me continue using Goodnotes: the paid Mac app, which costs $9 (while Goodnotes offers it for free for its iPadOS paid users). Given the limited function a Mac handwriting app has (how many of us have a tablet, my older Mac won’t even run Sidecar), it seems really foolish to pay extra just to be able to see and export my notes from an app I already paid for. Change this and I will become an active user and an advocate...
