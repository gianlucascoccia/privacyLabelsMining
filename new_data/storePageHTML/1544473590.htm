Description

Scanner-App is a user-friendly multifunctional mobile scanning app that turns everything from IDs to receipts into digital information.

No more queuing up near a huge scanner. Tiny and fast Scanner-App will scan and share docs via email or massager in some minutes. Forget about tons of paper. Organize, manage and store documents in the app or in a cloud.
With Scanner-App, you don’t need to wait until you get to your office or home office. You can scan and sign documents and send them while you are in a transport or walking through the street.

Main features:
- High quality PDF and JPG scans
- Live border detection and perspective auto correction
- OCR (convert image text to word format editable text)
- E-sign any document by adding your signature to scans within the app
- Full featured file and folder organizer
- Edit docs: move, rotate, add or delete pages
- Print your scans with any Wi-Fi printer
- Cloud services like Dropbox or Google Drive
- Multiple pages in a single document
- Scan QR codes
- Share your scans via email, massager send to iBooks.

Unique technology of image transformation allows the algorithm of the application instantly detects borders, removes unnecessary shadows and edit perspective. In 5 seconds the Scanner-App creates a full scan of any document. It will not lose clarity and color. There is also the possibility of flexible color adjustment of the document.
