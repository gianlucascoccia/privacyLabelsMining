Devs Rock

HODL listens to their community and thinks two steps ahead. Wow, thanks devs. I leave a review and you guys had already made an update addressing exactly what bothered me from the previous update. I shouldn’t have doubted the heroic HODL, hercules of coin apps, guardian of the gates at the zenith of crypto olympus.
Previous review: Guys, I love the app. Used it for many months now and it’s been everything I want. Every new coin is uploaded quite expediently, it’s great. But the new update- not so much. I’m struggling to get used to the automatically collapsing coin info area at the bottom of the list, and how each coin is pushed to the bottom when selected. This change has bothered me to the point that I had to download another crypto app. The greatness of Hodl was it’s efficiency. I loved being able to just scroll right through my list and check all my current coin details, one after the other. It was quick and easy. Now I find myself constantly scrolling when I just want to quickly go down my list of coins by tapping on the next one, and only scroll when I hit the bottom of the current coins displayed. Now you must scroll to each coin because the selected coin automatically gets pushed to the bottom. This little feature has greatly reduced the user experience in my opinion. I just think it was much smoother when the info pane was manually collapsible.
