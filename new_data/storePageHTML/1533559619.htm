Description

WidgetPet! Meet your new best friend. Ever wondered what your pet is thinking? Pets might not be able to tell you about all their adventures, but your widget pet can!

• Hop on into the WidgetPet pet shop and pick a companion.
• Incubate and then hatch your widget pet.
• Care for your widget pet and they'll share stories about their day and tell you about their adventures.
• Feed, water, love and even put your widget pet to sleep if they're feeling tired.
• As your widget pet grows they'll unlock new capabilities such as you being able to "pet" them or engaging in fun mini-games or "playdates"
• Send your widget pet on fun holiday escapes and celebrate the seasons with them, and they'll be glad to share their reactions!

Keep your widget pet close at all times and monitor their feelings and health stats. You can add their widget to your iOS device and monitor them on your Apple Watch so you can keep them right by your side! Take good care of your widget pet and they'll be happy to share their emotions throughout the day (and night!) as their widget updates with their latest activities.

Widget pets are the perfect virtual pet companion. Pick one out (or be surprised!) and hatch yours today for Free!

Hilary Reed , 03/22/2021

I love this so far.

I really like this game so far. I like virtual pet/parenting games. The one thing that I really like is that this game supports VoiceOver, the text-to-speech software built into iOS for use by people with limited or no useable vision. To be honest, I did not expect this game to be at all accessible. This is because most games like this are not accessible, and they contain many graphics and other items that cannot be read by a software program that is only able to read text. I have just a few things so far. When I went to name my pet, I came across an area of text on the screen that read “gender, optional.” I’m not sure what this means, so maybe you could make this a dropdown or something to select the pet’s gender. Unless this goes in the name field? I couldn’t get it to work. Also, in the widget, there appear to be quite a few images that are not labeled that pertain to my little mouse friend Bianca. Yes, that is a Disney Rescuers film reference :-) maybe you could provide text descriptions for this? Also, when you provide the pet age stats, maybe you could remind the player without vision what the pet is? For example, you could say “baby mouse.” I haven’t gotten to the play dates yet, as she is only a few hours old, but I will update this once that happens. If you need any additional information about VoiceOver, please let me know by responding to this review. Thank you so much, and apologies for the lengthy review.

Miles123123123 , 12/15/2020

A really great app!!🌟🌟

I love having a widget pet its adorable and its nice to have something to check up on every once in a while (its kind of like a tamagotchi) but I have a couple of requests.
The first thing is, I love the system where you have limited resources. It makes it a little more real to take care of the pet and honestly is what really kept me continuing through the game, however there is one tiny flaw in it. I understand you guys need to make money, and its fair enough to make resources cost an ad. But there need to be more ways to earn stuff. Once I ran out of turns and I had to watch an ad, I couldn’t because I don’t have wifi a-lot of the time, so I think there should be mini games you can play with your pet to earn turns to take care of your pet. Or even better, there should be an option to put your pet to sleep and as it sleeps you earn turns. Its really unfortunate when your pet runs away and theres nothing you can do about it.

My second suggestion is that pets should have animations. Maybe your dog is walking around the widget, or maybe its lying on the floor or barking at objects. This would be really cool because would make the widget pet seem more ‘alive’ and wouldn’t be just a pet staring at you. Maybe the widget could have a full background and could interact with stuff there. So its like an entire home in a widget.
