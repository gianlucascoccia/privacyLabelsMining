Description

UWM InTouch
By United Wholesale Mortgage, LLC

UWM InTouch is an easy and convenient way for mortgage brokers to access UWM’s EASE platform. View your pipeline, lock a loan, check current rates, and more, anytime from anywhere. This app from United Wholesale Mortgage, LLC makes it easy to get more done while you’re on the go.

Among other things, the mobile app allows you to:

• Lock or extend your loan
• Submit, review, and track progress of change of circumstances
• Upload and review cleared conditions/invoices
• Create and manage all client requests
• Receive push notifications on all your loans

With UWM InTouch downloaded on your mobile device, you can stay productive no matter where you are.

So close....

Love this app for a couple practical reasons:
1. Get notified when a loan changes status
2. Get notified when a lock is expiring
3. Upload conditions from my phone

What it doesn’t do well - wish I could do in the app:
1. Change fees - feels like a simple feature we should have
2. View documents - if you try to view a doc, it pulls up an entirely different document. I selected the insurance policy, but it pulled up the credit report. Super weird

Other than that, it’s good for what it does well. And for those reasons, if you’re a broker, download the app

No App for customers

I just refinanced my house and UWM purchased our loan. I am very, no, I’m extremely surprised that UWM does not have an app that their customers can use to make payments and review loan activity. It’s bizarre that in this day and age a financial institution wouldn’t have something so basic. It’s like buying a 2021 Ford Explorer and discovering that it doesn’t have Bluetooth. That is a standard feature for all new cars. 10 years ago it wouldn’t be a surprise, but today it would blow my mind. That’s sort of the same thing here just in a different context.
