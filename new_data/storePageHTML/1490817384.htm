Description

Tattoo Studio Pro makes managing the most critical parts of your tattoo and piercing business easy, from front counter check-ins to weekly payouts.

Are you a shop owner, studio manager, or tattooer looking for a way to simplify your shop's operations? Tattoo Studio Pro makes managing the most critical parts of your tattoo and piercing business easy.

It has essential tools that every shop needs, from the front counter to the weekly payouts.

Try it for free!

PAPERLESS RELEASE FORMS
Tattoo Studio Pro has a fully digital waiver form system that is so easy to use that anyone can do it with minimal staff involvement. Just verify and photograph the ID, then let the client do the rest.

Every form gets saved as a PDF file that you can easily access with a simple search. Download, save, print, or share any form at any time.

WALK-IN QUEUE
Each new release form has the option to save into a queue in the order they are completed. This allows you to edit the form after a session to fill in details about the cost and other notes like information about needles, inks, jewelry, or anything else you may need to document before saving the form as a PDF file.

APPOINTMENT BOOKING
Book appointments from anywhere. Pick a client from your list, or add a new one. Choose a time slot, select the artist, and schedule the appointment.

And with automated SMS text message reminders, your clients will be reminded the day before their appointment, reducing the number of no-shows and late arrivals.

CLIENT PROFILES
Easily find, update, and contact your clients from any device. Every client's release forms and appointments history are accessible from their profile too.

FINANCIAL REPORTS
Managing money and payouts doesn't have to be complicated. You can track sales with every release form and get daily, weekly, and monthly financial reports with one tap.

Export your selected report as a CSV file for use in your favorite spreadsheet editor or bookkeeping software.

ADDITIONAL FEATURES
• A modern interface that's simple and easy to use.
• Support for underage clients with parent/legal guardian.
• English and Spanish release forms (and more languages on the way).
• Always in sync across devices.
• Affordable and predictable subscription pricing.
• Cloud-based Database.
• Automatically syncs all of your devices.
• Unlimited cloud storage and automated backups included.
• Customize the branding by using your shop's logo.
• Fully editable legal templates for tattoo and piercing consent forms.
• Every waiver signed generates a printable PDF document.
• Privacy and Security-focused design.
