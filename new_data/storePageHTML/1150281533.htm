Description

Gro CRM the top-rated, friendly, award-winning small business Mac CRM iOS and macOS platform. Gro CRM is made exclusively for Apple Users and their iOS devices ( iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, Messages & Apple TV).

Gro CRM, the all-in-one friendly Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform designed to help small businesses, teams, and individuals, manage their contacts (leads, clients, and more), deals, calls, projects, appointments, documents, email, inventory, expenses, sales, invoices, quotes, receipts, payments, reports and much more!

Our friendly CRM app is made just for individuals, small businesses, and teams just like yours. We have helped countless companies and individuals jumpstart their growth and productivity, and we can help you too.

Gro CRM is a super friendly, modern, and customizable CRM platform experience that you will love. Work smarter, be more productive and win more business with our easy to use CRM app for Apple iOS devices.
