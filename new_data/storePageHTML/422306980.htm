Description

Order groceries for convenient curbside pickup or home delivery.

We’ve taken your shopping list a step further. Shop for groceries directly from your phone or tablet and pick them up at the store without ever getting out of your car.

- Search for any item and view details like price, size, and nutritional info.
- Scan items in your pantry and add them to your cart.
- Shop from past purchases up to 6 months.
- Add items to your cart directly from the Weekly Ad.
- Like your bananas extra ripe? Add a note to your order and your personal shopper will select your produce exactly how you like it.
- When you are ready to Checkout, select your pickup time and we'll have your order ready when you arrive at the store.
- Same day delivery and pickup options
- Clip digital coupons
- View your Fuel Points
- Order party platters, holiday meals, deli meats & cheeses, pizza, and fresh sandwiches with Order Ahead
- Refill and transfer prescriptions to your Harris Teeter Pharmacy
- View your VIC card and enroll in e-VIC for personalized savings

Sherryljnc , 08/19/2020

Great but a few Improvements needed

I love the online contactless shopping - thank you so much for this option! There are a couple of changes that would help me and probably others. If there could be a profile setting that could save the senior discount option and also the no substitution settings that would be great. I keep forgetting to specify them in the checkout process. Also it is frustrating that you have to delete items that are out of stock before you can check out - you aren’t charged for items not actually in stock when the order is filled so that’s not a problem. Removing items means even if the item is restocked before your order is shopped you don’t have a chance of getting it. There are items that are in stock when I start my order but always out by the time I check out. Being able to edit the order after checkout would be a real advantage - you can specify the discounts and claim your pickup slot early and then add or remove items as needed until a specified time before the pickup time. I build my order all week. Also I used to save a lot of time by using the favorites list by just clicking on items on the list I wanted to add to my cart, but recently it seems to work backward. If i click on an item it crosses it off my favorites list and I can only add the other items to my cart so I have to delete all the items I don’t want and then add the remaining items to my cart. That takes so much longer it’s not worth using the list at all.
