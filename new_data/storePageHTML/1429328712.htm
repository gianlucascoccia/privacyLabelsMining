Description

Welcome to the method designed to reduce everyday pain and build total-body strength. P.volve’s home workout app offers everything you need for incredible results: an extensive library of on-demand workouts and a wide-range of clinically backed series that range from strength to cardio, recovery and more.

Our method pairs low-impact, functional fitness with resistance-based equipment to activate and strengthen every muscle while improving mobility, stability and balance.

BACKED BY EXPERTS

Our Clinical Advisory Board and team of trainers work together to develop a wide range of series based on your individual needs, limitations and preferences. Personalize your routine with series that can help you:
• Get started with our method and equipment kits
• Work through sensitivities and injuries in the lower back, hips, knees and more
• Modify workouts as needed with small adjustments to keep you moving
• Optimize pelvic floor health for proper function through strengthening and releasing programs
• Keep moving at every stage of pregnancy with pre and post-natal workouts
• Get in sync with your body by matching your workouts to the ebb and flow of your menstrual cycle

UNLIMITED WORKOUTS ON DEMAND

Get access to hundreds of workouts, with new videos added every week. Filter workouts to find what works best for you and your fitness goals according to:

• Class type: Our class types work together to bring you a well-rounded, holistic routine.
-- Strength & Sculpt: Strengthen and tone your entire body with a low impact workout
-- Cardio Burn: Increase the intensity and work on heart health
-- Recover & Stretch: Use deep stretching techniques to increase flexibility and restore the body
-- Mat Definition: Build ultimate core strength with Pilates-inspired workouts

• Length: Find videos ranging from 15-70 minutes long to work best for you and your schedule.

• Equipment: Get moving with workouts using your favorite pieces of equipment or no equipment at all.

• Focus area: Keep working toward your goals with workouts that strengthen and reduce pain throughout specific areas of the body.

And more...

PERSONALIZE YOUR ROUTINE

Curate your own plan by favoriting the workouts you love, joining a series and tracking your progress along the way. Stream to your favorite casting device for anytime, anywhere workouts. Plus, use weekly and overall workout stats to stay accountable.

CarrotVK , 11/29/2020

Amazing workout, clunky app

I love P.volve and have been using it as my exclusive form of exercise for about 5 months, however I am finding the latest app update very difficult to navigate. To start with, the interface does not rotate horizontally during exercise selection, but only during streaming videos, making it very difficult to use on my iPad with the magic keyboard, which is the main device I have been using for streaming. The old app allowed for easy access to a variety of different programs, organized by difficulty level, this one limits to only a few choices. It’s tougher to search workouts by focus and difficulty level as well. The old app also selected a variety of exercises as part of personalized “programs”, which became longer and more intense with time, now it seems to just show workouts from programs you have enrolled in and does not suggest videos from the library, which does not really offer much variety. Overall, it’s usable, but wish the interface was friendlier and easier to customize.

Developer Response ,

Hey there! Thank you for this thoughtful feedback, you bring up some really great points. Our app when used on iPads definitely has considerable room for improvement right now, and it's an area we'll be scheduling some important updates on — so stay tuned.

We did receive feedback similar to yours about workout levels, and we're happy to share that these are now back in the app and on our streaming website. You can now head to Workout Library > Filters to sort by difficulty level. In addition to that, we recommend joining a workout series, a feature that will add those videos to your 'My Plan' section + to your calendar, and can help keep you on track with your workouts.

Thanks for sticking with us for these 5 months, excited to bring you more improvements all the time.

taila , 01/01/2021

Love new UX changes but still glitchy

The new UX changes to this app make workouts easier to find and save for later. Everything is just a lot clearer and easy to navigate. The one thing I’d mention is that the automatic logging of workouts doesn’t work consistently. I did a workout yesterday from start to finish and the check mark at the top says I didn’t workout yesterday. Normally this wouldn’t be a big deal bc I’m not obsessed with tracking myself, but the issue is that I’m doing the Summit Series. So the app is suggesting I re-do the same video (as if it’s Groundhog Day) and I can’t seem to manually log a workout or otherwise trick the system into registering the workout. Not sure how much longer this glitch will persist, but pretty soon the suggested “workout of the day” section will no longer serve as a helpful feature. At first it seemed like the day of the week feature would be super motivating and useful, but at least for now it’s just kind of a disappointment...

Developer Response ,

Hi Taila, thanks for sharing your thoughts with us on this. We've been made aware of some technical issues with the tracking of your workouts, particularly if it's a workout in a series you've joined.

New versions of our app will address this in the near future, but as a tip in the meantime — if you complete a video and restart that workout, we recommend watching it all the way through so that your progress is not undone. We know this isn't ideal and we are hard at work on improvements, so keep an eye out for app updates coming soon.

LOVE the program, hate the user interface

After years of pain from back injuries and sciatica, P.volve has quickly become my solution for feeling strong and fit, without recurring injury. However, the user interface design is not intuitive or easy to navigate, both online and in the app. For instance, I’m doing the Summit 60 series and it is frustrating to have to scroll through the app to find the program and the scroll through all the days of workouts to find the one I need to do. Also, the video keeps freezing mid-workout, so I have to do it all over again (and again). Plus, the video icons are so tiny (especially the start/pause button, which is in the very corner of the screen, making it difficult to use...) and there should be a skip forward/backward 10-15 seconds option. PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE do a redesign to make your site/app more user-friendly!!!
