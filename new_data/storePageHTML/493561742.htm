Description

Introducing Indian Ride Command, the ultimate accessory for Indian Motorcycles. Ride Command is designed to enhance your riding experience by providing ride planning, maps, vehicle diagnostic data, and Bluetooth connectivity.

No stats on main page

All it says is “Pair your bike with Bluetooth to see vehicle data” and has not worked from day 1.

Very disappointed overall. The RZR RideCommand is far superior, it records tracks on power on, the Indian version does not. This doesn’t support WiFi, the RZR does. It seems buggy, driving at night it randomly will go to daytime mode and blind the rider, a good way for an accident.

It seems like the Indian version of RideCommand was a fork by developers and forgotten since updates are rare. CarPlay is nice but I paid another $99 for a wireless CarPlay adapter.

I want this to work but for me but it just doesn’t. I hope they spend more developer time fixing this app and the Indian RC in general. I had high hopes but so far am underwhelmed.

dadio6255 , 03/31/2019

Great App

Indian has done well upgrading this app! You can now log into Ride Command on your computer, and plot out a route. Then Bluetooth the route directly to the bike! This works very well. Make sure you update the latest firmware on the bike, and add the app to your phone. It works well syncing to your iPhone! Also, add many way points to take the roads you want to ride on, so the navigation software doesn’t vary from your route! I entered several planned routes with many way points and they all take the roads I entered. Thank you Indian for finally getting this right!
