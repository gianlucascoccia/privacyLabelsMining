Description

Calling all card collectors, organizing your PSA-graded collection is now easier than ever. The free PSA Set Registry companion app lets you upload card images and compete for the best sets from your iPhone or iPad.

From vintage to modern trading cards, the PSA Set Registry companion app offers an extensive list of sets to choose from, allowing you to quickly add your baseball cards, football cards, hockey cards, non-sports cards, tickets and unopened packs to the app.

Stay organized and edit your collection’s information by uploading images, adding your purchase dates, purchase prices, current trading card values, comments, notes and more.

Did you sell a card from your collection? No problem! Simply change its status from “active” to “sold” and add the buyer’s name, sale date and sold price! Stay organized as you buy and sell your PSA-graded cards.

Improve your trading card, ticket and unopened pack collecting experience with an extensive features list:

· Add cards, tickets and packs to your collection with the Quick Add feature by scanning the cert verification code with your iPhone or iPad.
· Scan a PSA-graded collectible’s barcode to determine if it will upgrade your set using the “What If?” function
· Manage your collection & review set rankings and completion percentages
· Compare your sets with other users within the PSA Set Registry

App requires internet connection to use.

For more information on this and other PSA apps, visit http://www.psacard.com/apps.

N1ghtfox31 , 02/20/2018

#1 (and 2) Chipper Master sets and I love it

Lets face it... when building a giant set like the Chipper Jones Master Set with 2,639 unique cards and counting, its easy to mess up and buy duplicates. When building a set that massive and not being someone of unlimited means, a dupe I buy today may mean the budget is tapped out for a card I need tomorrow.

When new cards arrive (or the 1000 I just mailed in today come back in a few months) adding them by scanning the barcode makes it quick and painless. I also think the photo upload option is great. When I decided to add photos of each card in the set, it seemed a daunting task, but chipping away (get it? GET IT?) has been much easier with the app.

When selling extras, being able to quickly see what I paid helps as well. The one thing I would love to see (not just with the app, but in the app and on the website) would be a linking if the new Auction Prices Realized to the cards automatically in the way that the SMR is. Thanks for a helpful app!

KB_LETLOW , 07/08/2021

Inventory Management

Gives you a “what if” option to type a CERT # into and compare how adding a card will rank your set among others. Allows you to customize and build sets for nearly any thing you can imagine. If you’re searching for a certain card, it does the research for you and finds the card being sold all over. I had A few glitches when adding some cards to your inventory but not to your set. Reported it and it was handled IMMEDIATELY.. very very very prompt to solving any issues! Highly pleased and 100% recommend this app!

PmcCollectibles , 05/17/2020

A few kinks, very cool app overall

I like this app and I’m sure it will get better and better. When using Quick add sometimes it buzzes like it read the card but never actually loads. Closing out app and restarting fixes. Can only add a photo when quick adding. There are other ways that allow for picture adding after adding a card but the “save” button doesn’t seem to work in those features. Starting a Set is only possible if the set is preloaded into the app. I can’t seem to just create my own set or subset inside a set. The Showcase feature is pretty cool. Overall a really cool app for keeping track of PSA graded cards.
