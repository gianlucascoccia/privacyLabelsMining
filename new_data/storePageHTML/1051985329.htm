Description

CrewLounge PRO is a feature rich productivity application designed exclusively for LAA & LUS FA employees only, not for Retirees. For years CrewLounge PRO has been an FA's best friend, now version 13 represents best version yet. Get known crewmember locations, save important documents graphically, weather, calculate crew breaks efficiently, decode airports, and with additional available subscription get schedule, current sequence,TTS, CREW PORTAL, ETB,NS/NST, and much more at a touch of a 1 button. Features requiring data access require monthly subscription. The subscription called 'CrewLounge Pro Data Access' Monthly subscription costs $4.99. For your convenience the subscription automatically renews every month unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period. Account will be charged for renewal within 24-hours prior to the end of the current period, the renewal monthly cost is the same $4.99. Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user's Account Settings after purchase, instructions are in the app's manual button on the main screen. For full details you can visit the privacy policy and terms of use url below. CrewLounge PRO offers unpresedented usability and productivity when it comes to managing these online services. It speeds and improves the flow of information securely and reliably. CrewLounge PRO puts the full power of automation in the palm of your hand. Works with WiFi as well as cellular connections. No more struggling to remember transactions or cryptic codes, no more struggling with your keyboard, the applications intelligence handles both automated and manual features. With CrewLounge PRO you can spend your time more productively. CrewLounge PRO will fit like a glove and you will quickly wonder how you ever got along without it. For more information visit www.crewapplications.com/crewlounge.html (Now compatible with iOS8 your minimum iOS version must be 8.0)(Authorized users only) For privacy policy and terms of use please visit http://www.crewapplications.com/privacypolicy.html
