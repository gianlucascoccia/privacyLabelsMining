Description

Freedom Call

Freedom Call is the market-leading second phone number app — a second line for calling, texting, and picture messaging in everyday situations.

Get set up with your second phone number in under 3 minutes.

-- More Features --
* You can send SMS & SMS to all your friends, even if they don't have Freedom Call!

* Receive verification code & SMS

* Custom Voicemail. Record a custom voicemail greeting to prompt callers to leave a message.

* Voicemail to Text. Get your business voicemail automatically transcribed to text to save you time.

* Set Business Hours. Choose when you’re available for business calls and send callers to voicemail after hours.

With Freedom Call on your iPhone/iPod touch/iPad, you can make and receive international phone calls with CRYSTAL CLEAR SOUND QUALITY and at SUPER LOW RATES.
