Description

Podcasts are a perfect screen-free activity for kids. Listen to favorite shows, and discover new ones, with the Kids Listen app!

Kids Listen is a nonprofit made up of podcasters, parents, and teachers advocating for high-quality audio content for kids. We understand that kids are deep and thoughtful listeners. In our survey of family listening habits, we found that 74% of kid listeners initiate discussion after hearing a podcast. 80% request to listen to an episode again… and again. 20% listened to an episode 10 or more times!

Parents told us that they wanted an easy way for kids to find great, age-appropriate podcasts. That’s why we created the Kids Listen app.

FEATURES:

THE BEST PODCASTS FOR KIDS
* Listen to 30+ Kids Listen member shows, including podcasts from NPR and other top podcast networks, independent podcasters, and kids!
* Fun and educational content – science, literature, history, bedtime stories, serialized stories, mindfulness/meditation, music, inspirational interviews, and more

CURATED BY PODCASTERS
* Discover your next favorite show while listening to themed playlists
* Updated each week

PODCASTS BY AGE LEVEL
* Find age-appropriate content easily
* Kids can discover their own podcasts

DOWNLOAD EPISODES FOR OFFLINE LISTENING
* Create your own playlists by adding to your “stash”
* View your listening history, to easily listen again
* Filter playlists

ABOUT KIDS LISTEN

Kids Listen is a grassroots organization of advocates for high-quality audio for children. We’re podcasters, parents, teachers, and listening advocates. Our members create the best in podcasts for kids.

Our mission is to build community, advocate for the growth of the medium, and create standards and ethics that serve as best practices for development, production, and monetization. Kids Listen is creating platforms for producers to share ideas, and publish data and information relevant to creators, consumers, and supporters of podcasts for kids.

Kids Listen, Inc. is a California nonprofit company that has not yet obtained 501(c)3 status.
