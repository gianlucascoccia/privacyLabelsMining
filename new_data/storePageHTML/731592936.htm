Fun, but keep your head

This game is a fantastic way to not only have fun with a rollercoaster tycoon, but the little details and world building truly do create this game. So many interesting references and hidden features that honestly it can be quite impressive. Though people may look at it and see the many transactions and purchases and feel a bit overwhelmed; just remember that they don’t matter. They truly won’t make a difference and it will feel much more rewarding to actually work and plan for the items you want. If I could suggest anything, I’d honestly like to see a bit more literal Theme Park representation. Everything is ‘vaguely’ related to them however even though it’s pretty much useless; possibly adding some sort of nod to the hotels of the theme parks or even having certain gift shops be creatable as opposed to just concessions. Another thing is the characters in the park. Due to only being the Magic Kingdom, it limits itself on its amount of characters. Maybe separate the different areas a bit more to where there could be something of an Epcot, or an Animal Kingdom so you don’t feel weird and jarred when the Carousel of Progress is right next to California Screamin’. And possibly some more unique and obscure characters would be nice like Oswald or even Br’er Fox and Br’er Bear. Heck even the country bear Jamboree could both be an attraction and set of characters.

Overall a great game though opportunities are missed

sheledonae , 05/22/2019

No longer fun

When I started this game about 2 years ago, I honestly enjoyed it so much! I am a huge Disney fan and loved getting to build my own Disney park. However, the game makers have made it impossible to have fun with it anymore. I used to not have to spend any money and still got most of the characters and rides, but they have gotten ridiculous with it. Each event now requires over $10 if you want all of the rides and characters, and the difficulty levels have increased exponentially so that a new player would never even get close to getting what they need in the event’s 26 day time frame. Also, they keep giving new rides (3-4 with each event) and hardly ever release new land to place them on. I have at least 15 rides in storage that will never see the light of day. We also used to get a lot more time off between events to level up characters and other such things that you can’t really do during an event. Now, the events are back-to-back-to-back! And to top it all off, the latest update with the happiness rapidly decreasing so you would have to log in constantly throughout the day to keep it up, is absolutely ridiculous. I have never complained or even left a review on here for anything. I just am trying to be honest and say it’s just not worth it anymore.
