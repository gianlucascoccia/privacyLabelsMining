Description

Carpenter Calculator lets you calculate feet and inches easily and display the results on a visual tape measure!

Features:
- Quick measurement calculations
- Support fractions and mix decimals
- Results are displayed in feet, inches and centimeters
- Results are marked on a visual tape measure for convenience.

Note: To use the + button, you will need to purchase Pro Features using In-App-Purchase.

turnoffyourradio , 11/28/2020

Lite version - almost caused a divorce

I don’t see any indication this is a lite version. I don’t mind paying for a useful app but I downloaded this freebie in the middle of helping my wife with a project and now I’m writing a review instead of helping her and she’s looking at me with that look that says “I just needed you to convert that measurement to a fraction and now you’re writing a frustrated review on an app you tried once instead”

The lite version does not have the calculator functions unlocked and I didn’t see an easy way to convert decimals to fractions. I’m deleting the app.

-edit : I figured out that the lite version will let you do basic math OTHER THAN ADDITION. You have to tap the unit buttons at the top (ft, in, cm, mm) to enable the division or subtraction buttons, but the addition button is still locked out and takes you to a different app entirely, not an in-app purchase. That’s some shady stuff if you ask me. Also, wife forgave me. We’re ok.
