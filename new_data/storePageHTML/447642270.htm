Description

With Starr Cards you can make your own fully customized, professional-quality baseball trading cards to share on Facebook, illustrate a blog, print in ultra-high definition, or email to friends.

Starr Cards Retro 75 Baseball evokes memories of a time when gas cost forty-four cents a gallon, Jaws captivated movie goers, Aerosmith rocked the airwaves, Jimmy Hoffa went missing, and Cincinnati's Big Red Machine was in full swing.

Create an entire set of baseball cards featuring your kids, friends, school team, or amateur-sports league. When you're done, the cards are automatically arranged in a gorgeous collector's album for easy access and browsing.

Starr Cards is loaded with goodies so you can produce the ultimate baseball card. Every aspect of your card is fully customizable:

• player name, position, and number
• team name, stadium, and mascot
• color scheme of the card's frontside
• season and lifetime statistics
• biographical data including birthplace or hometown
• anecdote, trivia, or highlight

Starr Cards has custom stat formats for each player position, and even a special landscape-mode for producing team cards. No detail is left unattended — each and every card is sure to be a collectable keepsake.

The backsides of cards are replete with entertaining baseball trivia including a cartoon Q & A section, fast facts, insider capsules, and the best quotes from the history of the sport.

Discover for yourself why Starr Cards is being hailed as "the most exciting development in sports card collecting in the new millennium."

If seeing is believing, then Starr Cards is sure to make you a believer and diehard fan. If you like baseball, you're gonna LOVE Starr Cards!

Garrulous , 08/16/2011

Buyer Beware!

I was impressed with this software. Until I decided to edit the image I CREATED with the software I PAID for to make a collage of the cards to share with friends.

I was stunned and upset to learn that the PDF exports of the cards could not be altered (at least not without adding a step or three) due to the PDF's being locked and password protected.

I don't see any reason why this would be the case, or what the purpose of doing this is, unless as one other reviewer mentioned, they fear someone removing the company logo from the card. Which is truly lame in a paid app.

Box Seats Brad , 07/23/2011

Take me out to the ballgame

I've tried custom baseball card services a couple times before, and was not particularly impressed with the results. The cards always looked a little bit amateurish--especially on the backside. I was prepared for more of the same when I first bought this, but I have been blown away by the quality of the cards Retro 75 is able to produce. For coaches, like myself, this app is a dream come true. I can make professional quality cards for my team using the PDF output uploading to a professional online printing service. You can even print giant-sized cards with no loss of quality whatsoever. When printed on glossy cardstock, the cards are "even better than the real thing" to quote the U2 song.

Please consider making football and basketball versions too. While I coach neither, my friends who do have already said they will be first in line to purchase if you build the apps. "Build it and they will come!"

Best of the iPhone , 07/08/2011

Grand Slam

A lot of baseball metaphors came to mind after I had played around with this for a while. Most succinctly, I would have to say that Starr Cards is more than just a home run, it's a grand slam. The quality of the cards is just crazy good. I made a card using a photo I had taken with my high-end Nikon and then sent the output to the large format printer (2 ft x 3 ft) we have at work. Incredibly, all the lines and text remained razor sharp and I suddenly had the world's biggest baseball card ready for framing! To say the cards look professional, is like saying Babe Ruth was just another professional. This is all-time All-Star good (pun intended).
