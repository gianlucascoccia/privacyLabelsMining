Description

The power of Relisten.net in the palm of your hand! Listen to any song from over 100 artists!

- Listen to music in the background, with full streaming capabilities!
- Thousands of concerts and 10s of thousands of songs from the Live Music Archive at archive.org, etree, phish.in and more!
- View taping source and taper notes
- View concert rating
- View shows by venue
- View random show
- Last.FM scrobbling
- Listen to bands like Phish, Widespread Panic, Grateful Dead, Dead & Company, Umphree's McGee, String Cheese Incident, Marco Benevento, Lotus, STS9 and more!

Follow @relistenapp or @alecgorge for updates and information!

jrod1978. , 09/05/2018

Supremely Grateful!

We've come a long way from trading tapes in the parking lot! Can't say enough good things about this app. It's the holy Grail for any Dead fan. No it may not have EVERY single show the Dead ever played but they're getting pretty close and the quality on most of the recordings is impeccable. So many of them are sourced directly from the sound board and sound as good as any studio recording.
The only complaint I have is a small one and it has to do with the general operation. Occasionally when I first open the app it freezes and I have to shut it down and restart it to get it to work. Sometimes it even happens a couple times in a row. This is a mild inconvenience and I'm sure y'all could fix this with an update at some point. To be completely honest, this little technological flub is worth the hours of brilliant music I am afforded through the apps existence which is why it's still getting 5 stars.
Anyway, thank y'all for creating this app and making everybody's world a little bit brighter. I will continue to spread word of it's existence to heads everywhere.
Peace/love,
-jrod.
