Description

The rapid!Access* mobile application is designed for on-the-go access to your rapid! paycard account. With rapid!Access you gain secure and immediate in-hand access to view and manage your account information, all in real-time. With your iPhone, iPad, or iPod Touch, you can use rapid!Access to:

- View current balance
- View recent transactions
- View transaction details
- Transfer funds
- Enroll & Manage Text Alerts
- Locate an ATM

And more . . .

*This app requires Apple iOS 5.1.1 and later.

App and website are both excellent

I can remember when I first started with the app and website; at the beginning of 2019. Both were awful to be honest. I could hardly ever get on, as the website was always having issues.
I can’t remember when that changed because that was time ago, but I can tell you that I love both the app and website! I absolutely never encounter an issue on either. It would be nice to withdraw money without getting two withdrawn fees. I have not found a bank they are affiliated with. Also, taking out $100 from Walmart or wherever when you do cash back counts as your one time free. Though you won’t be charged for those, if you use that as your first withdrawal, and then go to the back you are now on your second withdrawal. Aside from that, I don’t have any feedback. This card and one other card I have are my most used cards because going on the app as is quick, efficient, and makes my life easier.:)

Unnike minks ebb , 02/08/2021

I hate banks

I hate banks and that is why I continue to use this card that I got from a temp job a few years ago. It handles most of my needs. I love the new feature that I can login with my fingerprint on the app. The biggest problem I have with the card is getting cash without paying a fee. I think you get one free transaction after a deposit. But after that even with the unlimited debit plan you still get charged for an ATM withdrawal. I think that’s bogus if you’re paying for the unlimited debit so you should be allowed to get ATM withdrawals for free as well. Allow me a way to get more than one free cash with drawl and I would rate this five stars. Actually I just remember you can do a cash back transaction but the only options I’ve found for that is $100 at a time from Walmart. So you’re stuck doing many transactions to get the cash if you need more than $100.
