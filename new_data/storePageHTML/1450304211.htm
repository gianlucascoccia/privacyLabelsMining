Description

Unlock exclusive pricing on hotels that you cannot find elsewhere. Whether you are craving a staycation or a far-away getaway, Snaptravel has the best deal tailored to your budget.

Best deal for your budget
Choose from more than 200,000+ accommodations worldwide from the best hotels, motels, hostels, all-inclusive resorts, and more for the best price in the market.

No hidden fees
We do not charge extra! Our prices do not include any surprise fees.

Stay flexible
Everyone's plans can change. Most of our properties offer free cancellation for your flexibility.

Last-minute bookings
Looking for a last-minute deal? You can find the best hotel deals in the app. With the app you can make a booking in under 5 minutes and secure your room for tonight (or later).

VIP Upgrades available
Are you looking for ways how to make your trip a bit more unique? Explore our room upgrade options, late check-outs, and other perks.

24/7 support
We're available instantly over SMS or FB Messenger in multiple languages. Never be put on hold again.

Manage your booking
Enjoy easy management of your booking on the go directly in the app.

Book & share
Share the booking with people you are traveling with and enjoy an additional discount when they sign up to Snaptravel.

Easy to use app:
1. Search - type your dates and destination in a chat or search tab
2. Get the deal - browse through the best hotel deals in your dream location
3. Book stay - fill out your information and confirm your hotel booking in 3 simple steps
