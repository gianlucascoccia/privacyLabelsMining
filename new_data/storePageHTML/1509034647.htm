Description

CLOS is the world's first app for shooting high resolution photos and videos remotely. App of choice for Vogue Italia's remote shootings.

Using CLOS app is just as simple as creating a virtual room and sharing the link with your friends. Once your room is set up you can manage the process remotely just like if you were in the same room with the participants, whether they are across the street or on another continent.
Make photos, record videos, save them in high resolution straight to your CameraRoll or to the Clos Cloud right inside the app.

Features:

- Host and manage remote photo shoots

- AI CAMERA

- Get high resolution photos (4032 x 3024) and videos (3840 x 2160) regardless the internet speed

- Control the focal and exposure point manually

- Save images to Clos Cloud or Dropbox

- Use the flash

- Choose where the pictures go and who will have access to it

- Explore the new kind of photography

By default, the pictures are saved on the model's device only. Use this option when the model's privacy is a priority.
With Clos Cloud enabled, photos will be saved to the room creator’s account.

All video calls are end-to-end encrypted.

Clos has recently got a new feature - AI Camera. Artificial Intelligent photographer that knows exactly when to press the shutter button. Check it out!

Great, But Needs 2 Features

I’m constantly amazed by the quality of the images that I’m able to capture with this, especially in good lighting. I shoot raw with it to allow myself much more flexibility in editing. When you switch between the different cameras, it reverts to jpg every time, which interrupts your flow. It would help tremendously if the setting stayed the same unless changed manually.
Also, an optional shutter sound would help the model to know when to change poses, as well as allowing the photographer to have confirmation, rather than having to watch for the number at the bottom. I intend to keep using Clos for a long time, but these features, which seem easy to implement, would make the experience significantly better.
