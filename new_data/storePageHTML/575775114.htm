Description

Notice: You NEED an account with CellGate before you can use this application. This Application is designed and intended for the sole use by CellGate Access Control and Monitoring Subscribers.

The CellGate Access Control and Monitoring system lets you control who enters your property and when—from any location, at any time. By simply logging in to your online CellGate account, you can create and customize an unlimited number of access codes and maintain a detailed historical record of all gate activity, including photos.

Our solar- and AC-powered system packages contain everything you need for remotely controlling automatic and manual gates, and the system's camera options help ensure you receive photos of all gate activity directly to your mobile phone. Built specifically for use with cellular networks and the internet, the CellGate system allows you to monitor and control access to your property 24 hours a day, directly from your mobile phone or any internet connection.

No matter where in the world you happen to be, when a visitor arrives you will receive text, email or voice alerts to your cell phone. Then simply send an open command to any gate anywhere in the U.S.

Working with manual or automatic gates and powered by solar or AC, the CellGate system can operate in a variety of environments such as Farms and Ranches, Oil and Gas Sites, Residences, Deer Leases and Self-Storage Facilities.

Exotic/Wild Game Traps are also controlled and monitored by CellGate with the added option to disable motion triggered image messages after the trap has been closed.

AceFence , 07/12/2021

Cellgate Cellular Telephone Entry

I have installed several Cellgate systems for both multi family and single family applications. This is a very simple to use and functional entry system which allows the user a lot of flexibility in obtaining information regarding usage.
The system is perfectly capable of telling the user whether the gate is open if magnetic sensors are installed on the gate. This works good on slide gates but due to movement of swing gates by the wind, the system will show the gate being open when it is closed. This is not the fault of the Cellgate system but a function of slop in the gate hinges and gate operator connections.
The app is easy to use and easy to understand. The photo of the person at the gate is crisp and clear and comes to the user's cell phone very promptly and allows clear voice communication. The ability to roll from one unanswered phone to several others is a very good feature.
These units can be set up with standard 120 VAC or solar so they can be installed anywhere there is cellular service.

twigger333 , 07/29/2020

Needs cell signal video streaming option on the app

This video streaming for this app works beautifully as long as the device that is called from the gate or door requesting access is currently connected to Wi-Fi. If you are out of Wi-Fi connected areas when someone calls from the gate, the app won’t connect and the call is redirected to your regular phone number as an incoming call and you will not get video streaming. When this happens, you will have to separately access the app in order to open the gate. That would have been a good thing to let this user know because I thought the video streaming just wasn’t working when I tested it while 200 yards away from Wi-Fi at the gate.

The app should have the capability to select the option to: stream via WiFi, or through cellular data when WIFI is unavailable-similar to FaceTime. You would have to understand that this would increase data usage and additional charges might apply but people should at least have the option to do so.
