Description

Colorgram is the original time-lapse coloring book. A relaxing, therapeutic game for all ages.

The app features,

▸ New pictures everyday
▸ 2500+ of Coloring Pages
▸ 500+ flat/gradient colors / Custom color palettes
▸ Pictures of mandalas, girls, patterns, animals, florals ,etc
▸ Creative time-lapse community
▸ Backup your custom color palettes


Coloring is the perfect way to distress, reduce anxiety and relax whether you’ve just finished a long day at work or you need something to pass the time – any time is the perfect time to dive into our family friendly coloring book!

With a huge range of colors and pictures to enjoy plus DAILY artwork updates, there’s endless hours of relaxing fun to be had with ColorGram!

I’ve thrown all my games away 😂 this is more fun😊

This app keeps you busy coloring and at the same time you get to see all the incredible artists and their perceptions of the drawings. I probably can’t mention names so I won’t 😢 but there are about twelve artist that really really ROCK when it comes to coloring and then there are some that are so so good and what I mean is they are pretty decent and I enjoy looking at their work and then they come up with something that blows your mind and soon you find yourself falling in with all the pictures that they have all painted....whoops.... colored. Editors give you, the artist, one picture a day in which you color then they pick one of those pictures for the following day and label it “Editors Choice”. I figure when I color the same picture a few different times and in different ways and then publish them I am out of the running for “editors Choice” but I am okay with that because I have a blast coloring these fun, cute pictures. I would say that for those who are looking for complicated and detailed coloring like 3D etc. this APP might be a little to simple for you but, for me who hasn’t really taken art classes since about forty years ago this APP is perfect and FUN..... and I want to say “Thank You” to the editors and APP designers for keeping it simple and fun.
