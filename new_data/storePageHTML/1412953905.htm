Description

The Yoga with Kassandra app is your #1 source for online Yin & Vinyasa Yoga classes. Members get access to over 550+ videos that can be streamed or downloaded for offline use to any device!

Enjoy the collection of classes from the Yoga with Kassandra YouTube library as well as some exclusive members-only classes, monthly calendars and programs. New exclusive classes, programs and monthly calendars are added every month. You can't find those anywhere else!

Find the best yoga class for you by searching by Style, Length, Level and Focus. Videos range from 5-90 minutes and include Yin, Vinyasa, Hatha, Power & Restorative Yoga. This app also gives you access to guided meditations, yoga pose tutorials and so much more.

The in-app calendar allows you to schedule your yoga classes ahead of time and stay accountable throughout the week! When you do a yoga class it will automatically log it in your calendar so you can track your progress and reach your goals.

Deepen your yoga practice by reflecting on your experience with the journaling function.

Whether you’re an advanced yogi or just unrolling your mat for the first time, you’ll find plenty of fun and easy to follow yoga classes that fit your style within the app.

With your Yoga with Kassandra app membership, you’ll receive:
• Full unlimited access to ALL Yoga with Kassandra YouTube classes and exclusive members-only classes only found within the app
• Full unlimited access to exclusive monthly yoga calendars and programs
• A range of classes that vary by style, length, focus and difficulty to suit your every need!
• High quality, SD and HD streaming videos
• Automatically beam videos from your phone to your Chromecast or AirPlay enabled devices
• No Internet required! Download videos to watch offline, or connect with WiFi, 3G and 4G

A Great Program for all Abilities

First off, let me say this is a timesaver!! No running to and from yoga classes at the gym or studio!
Secondly, there is something for everyone! I initially began going to Yin yoga classes to improve my tight runner’s legs. As I began to tire of one more thing in my to-do list, I began searching for classes on the internet. I found Kassandra’s You Tube videos and wondered why anyone would purchase the app when we could access her videos for free. I’ll just say the app organizes videos by type, ability, length of time, time of day, etc. There are hundreds of options and there are other classes and opportunities available to those of us with the app. I’m SO glad I purchased it. I’m doing yoga more often, eliminating travel time and have an amazing instructor right in my living room. And I’m super pleased that I can now access my app via Roku!!
