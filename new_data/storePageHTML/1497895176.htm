Thegirlwhosings , 02/16/2021

Very disappointed.

Enjoyed the free trial, signed up for the $20/mo unlimited plan and was immediately disappointed. I used the service to schedule two doctor appointments, both of which required my personal info each time (why can’t I just save my date of birth and whatnot to my profile so I don’t have to do it every time). They do not sync to your calendar app, so you have to create a calendly to easily share your availability — but don’t waste your time there because your idiot assistant you get assigned won’t even bother and will schedule outside of your availability. You also can’t request quotes for anything (I.e. I’m moving and wanted a quote for movers and apparently that’s too advanced for them). They can’t cancel any appointments they made, which is inconvenient as I had last minute travel come up, and now I need to take more time out of my day to personally call the office, because they can’t. They send you calendar invites for your appointments (woohoo, something nice!) but they don’t change the event name and 50% of the time will have the wrong time so it just f**ks up your calendar. Requested a refund, and they said “sorry but no” so their customer service is awful. I had the subscription for three days, and every task I asked them to do, something was wrong, but yet no refund or even attempt to fix the issue. Not worth the hassle at all. Waste of time.
