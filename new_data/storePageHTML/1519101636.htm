Description

SouthState's new mobile app is bringing you enhanced features for streamlined access and more peace of mind.

Stay on top of your accounts.
- Check balances and view your account activity
- Look up check images
- Quickly deposit checks with your device's camera
- View your balance instantly on your Apple Watch
- Enable alerts to be notified of account activity
- Sign up for eStatements

Transfer money, pay bills or pay people.
- Send money with Zelle®
- Easily pay your bills in minutes with BillPay
- Transfer funds between SouthState accounts or to other financial institutions

See your full financial picture.
- Link accounts from other financial institutions
- Use budgeting tools to get insights on your spending

Open additional accounts or apply for a loan
- Open a checking, savings, or money market
- Apply for a personal loan
- Apply for a mortgage

Other helpful tools
- Find the nearest location or ATM

Be sure to keep your app updated for future enhancements. We're working hard to provide you with even more tools tomorrow.

Lucas Christian , 06/02/2021

Honestly Pretty Good

I’m new to SouthState (coming over from CenterState, and coming over from NBC before that). From NBC to CenterState the app got worse. Fortunately this appears to be an improvement.

The design of the app looks nice, which is great given CenterState’s app was doggone ugly. My inner web designer is happy. Most of the app is still basically a glorified webview, so the way you navigate around isn’t particular fluid or intuitive. There is currently one tab bar and two slide-over menus, one on each side. Just having one of those three should be sufficient.

What I do really like is the “financial tools” feature, which they seem to have bought from a vendor, but it’s really integrated well. In particular I like being able to assign categories and custom descriptions to my transactions so I can see exactly what it is without having to guess what “CRYPTIC MERCHANT NAME HERE” is. Not strictly essential functionality, but cool to see them thinking out of the box.

Developer Response ,

We appreciate your insightful feedback, Lucas! Great to have you banking with us.

trammmq , 05/30/2021

Date and time

Only been using the app for a few minutes so I can’t say much but there are a few things that I picked up on immediately. Number one, the layout and design isn’t great compared to the centerstate app. The centerstate app had everything you needed in one spot so it was very convenient and easy to navigate. This app is still pretty easy to navigate I guess but I can’t understand why they wouldn’t stick to the simple layout that they had before instead of complicating things. Also, it’s ugly. Looks cluttered and not as organized as the centerstate app was. But that isn’t a huge issue for me.

What IS a huge issue is the lack of date and time on transactions. I’m hoping that this is just some type of glitch or one of the kinks theyll have to work out since the app is new?? Because I know d*mn well they couldn’t have possibly thought that getting rid of the ability to see the date and time of a transaction was a good idea. No one in their right mind would take that feature away. It’s important for obvious reasons that we get to see when exactly our money is coming in and going out. I REALLY hope this is fixed soon.

Developer Response ,

We appreciate your review and feedback. We will share this information about the transactions with our team.
