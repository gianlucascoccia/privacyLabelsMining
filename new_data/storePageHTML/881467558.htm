Description

Be an expert on the Science world! New discoveries, research, interesting interviews and studies. All you wish to know about the current science world brought to you in an easy to use, simple and efficient app!

Features include -

- A news summary covering stories from all sources ! Clean feed with no repeated stories. For each story - see all sources that covered it with a simple tap !

- Subscribe for push notifications on the topics you care about!
Also receive push notification on prominent stories in the science world, making sure you're up to date, even when you're busy !

- New discoveries, interviews, research results, studies and interesting talks - brought to you from the leading Youtube channels!

- Read later - save interesting stories easily and for free!

- Your very own news feed - choose the topics you want to follow, the topics you want blocked, and the topics you want to get notifications about! Don't bother yourself with news that don't interest you!

- A community of Science lovers! Post stories, videos or polls, comment on stories, tag articles, earn points and badges!

- Block source - filter out unwanted sources

iPhone only features -

- Collapsed mode - an effective reading mode! Skim through the news and decide what you want to read, save or share!

Enjoying the app? Not satisfied? Whatever it is - we’re waiting to hear from you. Please write us what’s on your mind to support@newsfusion.com

Science News & Discoveries

Science News and Discoveries is a simple yet resourceful news source for those interested in the daily ongoing of astronomy, Earth based science, biology, individual health, physics and a bit of technology. Users have the option to customize their news feed according to the subjects they want to appear so you can simply tap into the app and be greeted by stories you are more likely to be interested in. Articles can be tagged for what you thought of them, badges and points can be earned for reading, they can be saved to read later and they can also be shared with others. The interface is easy to navigate whether you are filtering the feed by topic or just going around the menu out of interest. Science News and Discoveries is well worth a download for those interested in seeing what's new in our world as well as other worlds.
