Description

Meet Annie! A reliable app for baby monitoring that works on any network. All you need is to connect two devices and transform them into an audio & video baby cam. Annie is always ready to help you watch your kids when they’re sleeping. Make parenting just a little bit easier with Annie!

VIDEO BABY MONITOR
- Not only can you perfectly hear your little one, but you can also see your baby's every move. Annie is a video nanny that will let you watch your baby through the camera of your device while the baby sleeps soundly. Feel free to use back or front camera.
- Night light feature helps you recognize what is your baby up to at night. Turn on the light on the baby's device and see for yourself.
- Use the mute button to turn off the audio recording if you do not want to be disturbed by the sound.

BABY MONITORING WITH UNLIMITED REACH
- Experience unlimited reach with Annie Baby Monitor. The app is working on WiFi, 3G, 4G, and LTE. It will keep you in touch with your baby wherever and however far you are from your baby as long as you have a working internet connection.

MOTION DETECTION
- Annie baby monitor will send you a notification when any motion is detected

MULTI-CHILD & MULTI-PARENT
- If you need to babysit two children at once, no problem. If your grandma wants to join the monitoring of your newborn, also not a problem. Annie allows you to add as many devices to your account as you need depending on the size of your family.

CUSTOMIZE YOUR BABY’S PROFILE
- Make the app yours by adding your baby's name along with their photo to the baby's personal profile. That way you can easily monitor several children at once.

STAY NOTIFIED
- Annie Baby Monitor will always let you know about your baby's activity. A notification will let you know if there's noise in the baby's room depending on the noise sensitivity setting. The baby alarm will let you know exactly when it's time to check on things so you always have everything under control.

SIMPLE TO SET UP
- Turn your devices into the best video monitor by entering a five-digit code from one device to the other. Open the app on two devices, create a code on the device you leave with your baby and enter the code on the device you take with you - all within 30 seconds of opening.

TRY BEFORE YOU BUY
- Annie Baby Monitor lets you try all the features and video monitoring during the trial. And if you're happy with Annie, you can buy a subscription - weekly, monthly, or yearly depending on how long you plan to use Annie. You are the one in charge!

Why still use expensive hardware baby monitors when you can have a modern video baby monitoring app for a fraction of the cost? Download Annie Baby Monitor, a video nanny camera that will help you watch your baby.
