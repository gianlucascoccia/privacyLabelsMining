A GREAT WORD GAME

This is one of several word games that are put out by the same group but under different names.
I find it very useful in keeping my mind active through searching for words from a given set of letters.
I am 66 years old and this game is an asset to me.
I’ve looked at and played some of the games developed specifically to enhance brain function. This game allows me to mess with words, which is something I enjoy, and my ability to figure the words out increases quickly and I think it enhances brain performance better than those games which tout themselves as being designed just for that purpose.
These kind of things are more important than a lot us know.
I appreciate the game and my biggest problem when playing it is myself and my reaction to any given problem I find in the game. I get bothered when it won’t accept words that are real words, which really isn’t an issue of great merit.
I uninstalled one of these games yesterday when it presented me with the same puzzle I had just completed. I liked that game like I like this one, and I hope to reinstall it and carry on where I left off.
Again, that problem is not that big of a deal and I am too impulsive at times.
I would ask the developers to keep my name with my review, if there’s a means of doing so, and I want to thank them again for working on these games.
Their efforts and the results of their efforts are very worthwhile.

PICOBOY BIRDY , 03/03/2021

Frustrating

I used to really enjoy this game. Not anymore! I agree with other reviewer‘s regarding the daily puzzle. It used to be difficult to obtain three stars, since the latest version it is almost impossible. Twice I have received three stars in the last few days and did not receive the coin rewards. When contacting the developer, I was advised that I had used the hint button more than one time. Because there are so many four letter words that will fit within the context of a specific area of the puzzle, it’s almost impossible to get it right on the first try. Previously, I was usually able to figure out the correct words for the specific space. Now, because there are so many possibilities, it’s all luck and I have been using hints, which I never had to do before. I also agree with the other reviewer‘s regarding the vast amount of missing words from their dictionary. Many common words are missing, and even the plural of a word that it will except is rejected as a plural. This is not a calming word game it is a frustrating word game and I am deleting it. Sad to see it go as I have been playing it for several years, but enough is enough.
