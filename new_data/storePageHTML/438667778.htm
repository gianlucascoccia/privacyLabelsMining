Description

Join us on a divine journey with the real Music Visualizer or in the words of a fan: "A Mature Masterpiece of Living Art”

* The only particle based visualizer with beat detection technology!
* Over 9.5k App Store reviews world-wide & about 1k Facebook fans
* Now with MIC ON "dancing flow" option for your favorite streaming media
* Quick help with guides and user feedback via built-in UserVoice
* Stun your friends with amazing abstract line art drawings!

Take an unforgettable trip into a world of thousands of particles with the ultimate multitouch particle visualizer and its unique music visualization capabilities!

Gravitarium has evolved into an amazing tool you can use to visualize your own iTunes tracks or draw amazing abstract line art that makes a personal, elegant wallpaper for your device.

In the traditional sense you can always just guide the particle flow with your fingertips to achieve 10 different animations depending on the number of touches on the screen:

1 - Rocket, 2 - Sparkle, 3 - Energy flow, 4 - Atomic, 5 - 3D freeze, 6 - Circularium, 7 - Fish, 8 - Vortex, 9 - Lasers, 10 - Lightning.

Gravitarium Plus Features:

► Sticky flow option allows for the animation to stay active without any fingers on the screen
► Dancing flow animates the particles at 60fps while sensing and responding to background music
► Lines / Specks Drawing modes provide for unique abstract art creations
► Ability to Save, Edit and Share your creations or use the built in “Wallpaperizor" to make a parallax optimized image
► Includes 10 original tracks by Silent Strike + allows for selection from your iTunes library
► Provides the ability to use the built-in microphone and have the particles react to other sound activity (such as Pandora or own voice)
► Incorporates a multiplayer dexterity training game and a multiplayer drawing / flow mode to play with a friend via Game Center
► Animation options with the ability to save and load presets
► Cross-device play capable: iPad to iPad, iPhone to iPhone, iPhone to iPad…etc.

Included Apple Watch App adds the ability to control options and playlists from your wrist like a remote. Tip: Got a Projector? Try it with AirPlay mirroring for the ultimate party fun ;-)

Unleash your creativity and make your own unique masterpiece today!
