Description

The Official Girl Scout Cookie Finder App
(Designed especially for Girl Scout Cookie customers.)

Girl Scout Cookies are only in season for a few months! Make sure you don’t miss out. Find Girl Scouts selling cookies in a snap. Use the Girl Scout Cookie Finder app to find a complete listing of when and where you can support Girl Scouts selling delicious cookies near you or anywhere in the USA and Puerto Rico.

Key features include:

- Sortable listings by date, radius, or map
- Search functionality and GPS capability by zip code, and the ability to save cookie sale dates to your calendar
- Link to nationwide shipping options

The Girl Scout Cookie Program® prepares girls with the business smarts they need to take on the world! Proceeds stay local to the zip code you order from. Each purchase supports all the amazing girl entrepreneurs who dream big —they’re bold, they’re brave, and they don’t let anything get in their way!

Thank you for supporting Girl Scouts' across the country!

Not a cookie crumb to be found...

I agree with the other people that are saying this app is useless. I went to 7 booth locations at the time and place this app said they’d be set up and there was nobody there. Decent App

This app does exactly what you’d want it to do. It shows you where the Girl Scouts will be set up, and even though the distance can be kinda wonky, you can just enter the addresses in your own maps app for a more accurate distance. A lot of people complain about showing up and there not being scouts set up, but...they’re children? They’re not setting up for a 12 hour day so you can get cookies when it’s convenient for you. Also there’s sooo many reasons a troop may not be able to set up one day. It’s pretty sad to see so many grown people complaining about children who sell cookies for free. Just say you like child labor.
