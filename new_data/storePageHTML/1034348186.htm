Description

A studio in your pocket! Everything you need to make modern beats and electronic music. The easiest way to get started with real music production.

CREATE AND MIX LOOPS
Build melodies and drum loops by drawing notes. Arrange your loops with scenes to explore ideas or make full-length tracks.

DISCOVER GREAT SOUNDS
Browse thousands of high quality synth patches, sampled instruments and one-shot samples. You can import samples to the drum machine and build your own melodic instruments.

ADD EFFECTS AND AUTOMATION
Add studio-grade effects like reverb, delay, distortion and chorus. Fine-tune sounds to find the perfect mix. Create modulation and transitions with powerful automation.

SHARE AND COLLABORATE
Upload your tracks to SoundCloud or export them as audio or video files. Quickly share links to your projects if you want to collaborate with other producers.

EXPORT TO YOUR FAVORITE DAW
You can export projects to Ableton Live, Bitwig Studio or Studio One, which makes Auxy a great sketchpad for producers on the go. You can also bounce projects as separate audio and MIDI files, which can be imported into any popular music-making app.

JAM WITH FRIENDS
Auxy supports Ableton Link, which lets you jam in sync with other apps and devices connected to the same Wi-Fi.

TRY IT FREE
Auxy is free to download and evaluate with a limited set of sounds. You also get a 7-day free trial to evaluate all the sounds and features before you start a subscription.

MADE IN SWEDEN
Auxy is built by a small and independent team in Sweden. Our mission is to put a studio in everyone's pocket and push music forward as an artform.

Editors’ Notes

A refreshingly simple take on music-making, Auxy lets you create beats simply by placing colorful squares on grids. With the intuitive modular interface, it’s easy to arrange blocks, tinker with their composition, and create complicated beats. Once you’ve found the sound you’re looking for, you can adjust the tempo, key, and effects on the fly—then record it and export to MIDI.

VokonMusic , 08/31/2021

A game changer in mobile DAWs

I have used auxy since around 2014, and have gotten to know the ins and outs of this app in that time. Everything about it, from the UI to the sound design itself, is incredible. Not only is the app great though, but the community as well. Many of my best friends online are from the auxy collective discord, and it’s truly amazing to see such a concentrated amount of talent in one area. As well as this, the auxy staff repost songs they like, which is a really great way to keep in touch with the community and reward experimentation. I don’t think I’ve ever seen another mobile daw where the community put together a full on awards ceremony for people who make music with the app. As well as this, working with groups or collabs is incredibly easy and efficient too. Being a member of many groups and collabs in both auxy and FL Studio, i can safely say that Auxy is the better of the two when it comes to collaborations. This is mainly due to the fact that there are no VSTs in auxy, but it’s also just because the projects can be shared through a link as opposed to a file. Incredible work lenberg, and I can’t wait to see what you do next with the app!
- Vokon
