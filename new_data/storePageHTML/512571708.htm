Description

Earning miles is now even easier. With the Miles & More app, you always have all the important information and opportunities to earn at hand.

These benefits await you:
• Daily updated offers and news tailored to you and your Miles & More status
• Our goal centre where you may choose an award you would like and set it as a mile goal
• Your mileage account balance for status and award miles as well as current mileage transactions, so your status goal is always in sight
• The digital service card – always ready to use whether it is with a Miles & More Partner, for Lufthansa Check-In or Lufthansa lounge access
• With My Challenge, you can fill up your mileage account more quickly through personalized challenges that are always tailored to you

Not a Miles & More member yet? Then simply register directly in the app so you can start earning valuable miles right away and redeem them for great awards. It’s all easy and free of course. Your Miles & More Team wishes you a lot of fun while earning and redeeming miles!

Good App with Potential

Does what it’s supposed to do and a bit more. Like the feature to save miles for goals.
Two new features I would really like to see coming:

- Family and Friend Sharing. Add family or friends to earn and save miles together for trips.

Trotzi , 09/16/2018

Has Potential

I use the app in order to calculate mileage for future flights, since MnM discontinued the web-based lookup tool that was just a click away when booking flights via a PC-based web browser.
A tip: When entering the flight details, it does not appear to be necessary to put in the correct flight number for many queries; any input seems to do the job. Not sure why the date input is needed - both of these should probably default to some random date in the future or buried in some expert level section. In short, too many inputs are required to do the most common queries, when only uncommon queries should require more inputs.
I just wish LH would build this tool right into the booking process. Maybe I am the exception, but I use it to justify purchasing a higher booking class ticket to keep or achieve frequent flier status.

jhlc5 , 10/14/2018

Super Glitchy, has frustratingly limited functionality

I don’t understand how people give this app a positive rating. I’ve had a lot of difficulty logging in, prompted to reset my password multiple times, not because of user error but because the app seems to arbitrarily reset and doesn’t register entries. Also, the app itself has puzzling limitations in functionality. The landing dashboard is more oriented to advertising Lufthansa flights and services than about getting an overview of one’s account. There are other frustrating limitations. For example, it’s apparently not possible to request to mileage credit for past travel on Lufthansa or Star Alliance partners. This makes it feel more like Lufthansa etc are trying to create barriers for loyal customers to accrue and manage miles, rather than facilitating and encouraging them.
