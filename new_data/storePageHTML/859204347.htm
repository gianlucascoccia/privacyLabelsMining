Description

How about plunging into the world of esports and receiving a mountain of rewards? Join us for Blitz Cup streams and get cool prizes!

Meet a truly legendary MMO shooter for your mobile device! Become part of a community of millions of players from across the world, take a vehicle into your first massive tank battle, fight in a 7x7 format, and win! Check out the extraordinary variety of vehicles, maps, modes, and possible strategies that this tank shooter game has to offer!
You'll discover an enormous and action-packed world of tanks—historically accurate World War II vehicles from the USSR, Germany, France, Japan, Great Britain, China, the USA, and many other countries. You’ll also drive experimental vehicles that have been brought to life based on blueprints created by famous real-life engineers, vehicles inspired by well-known anime series, and armored monsters from popular alternative universes—everything you might need to have fun fighting in a multiplayer action game with friends both old and new. There are over 400 vehicles to choose from!
There's always room for growth as you progress in this PVP shooting online war game with its well-developed progression system. You’ll research a wide array of vehicles, from Tier I tanks to monstrous Tier X machines. Change the guns, customize the equipment, and apply camouflage to increase your chances of survival and domination. Tune your combat vehicle to specifically match your playstyle, the battle type specifics, or mode.
You’re unlikely to get bored. No battle is the same. Regular in-game events that feature Collector and Premium tanks as rewards alternate with new game modes that will change the way you think of army multiplayer shooting games. Gravity Force will make your tank float above the ground. Burning Games will put your survival skills to the test and surprise you with the possibility of healing your allies. Mad Games brings the meaning of "mad" to a whole new level: become invisible, play dead, surge forward, ram the enemy as though your life depends on it (oh wait, it does!).
Discover a multitude of maps. Tank battles will unfold on a variety of different and distinctive arenas in which victory depends on your team's strategy and knowledge of their surroundings. Normandy at the moment when the turning point of WW2 occurred. The post-apocalyptic Wasteland, scorched by an oppressive radioactive sun. The Mediterranean coast with its ancient ruins. A snow-covered base at the edge of the world, a metropolis, desert sands, an eastern city... And even the Moon!
You're never alone. Want to play with a friend? Create a platoon. Want to collaborate on a bigger scale? Join a clan to enter battle as part of a pack of like-minded players and seek glory in Rating Battles or participate in tournaments with prizes! Coordinate your strategy and annihilate your enemies together!
Expect it to be spectacular. The game is automatically optimized for your device. Enjoy the eye-catching features of every battle arena, highly detailed tank models, massive explosions, and flying blown-off turrets. Manual settings will help you find the balance between fantastic visuals and high FPS.
WoT Blitz is a tank universe within your phone or tablet. So hurry up, enter the game, and start your engine!
The game is suitable for adults and children over 12 years old. The game requires at least 2.5 GB of free space and an Internet connection. Learn more at https://wotblitz.com/

Editors’ Choice

While the premise sounds simple—defeat the enemy combat vehicles or capture their bases—World of Tanks Blitz is a fascinatingly deep experience that requires careful strategy in each lightning-fast battle. A buzzing multiplayer community and well-designed in-game chat make it easy to work together, whether you’re flanking foes in a snowy trainyard or scouting from the sweeping vistas of a rural countryside. With a complex upgrade system and more than 90 tanks that represent Germany, the United States, and the USSR, you’ll find plenty to unlock as you hone your skills.

Best Game I Ever Played, But Needs Some Repairs.

I think wot blitz is a game like no other. It has really great graphics, real existing and prototype tanks, the flexibility of the game being on almost every device I can find, and you can be creative on how you try to win and use your tank. I can't think of any better game or even one close to this one. It really takes tanks to new levels.

However, when the new update comes out (update 8.0) it will add some cool new tanks that would seem fun to use, but two of these tanks remind me of the old sports car like tanks the " BT series's " because these two American tanks can move backwards with their tracks broken and the " BT series " had the ability to move forward and backwards without any tracks. If you were to add that new thing to the BT tanks, I would appreciate this game a ton more but I would understand how that would make the " BT series " too supreme since it's a tier three light tank. I respect the game a lot but maybe wot blitz could find a way to make something LIKE this happen.

Update 8.1 came out and it seems good except for one thing. When I played the game, I had no account connected to my player so when I got back on the game after the update, it got rid of it! I wish war gaming did not do that. I still love the game but they need to pay more attention to what they do. If you are reading this, make sure to have an account attached to your player ID so nothing like this happens.

IGUN4FUN , 07/19/2020

Great Game! Small request though

I think this game is hands-down one of the best games for mobile devices. The game has exceptional graphics for a mobile game, as well as an amount of content comparable to large computer games. The quick battles and great pacing make progression smooth and easy. I do have a small request however; After update 5.5, most of the lower tier tech-tree vehicles were removed and given collector status, and all had only one module of each type. While this was typically not an issue, seeing as most vehicles were given the best modules, I think it would be great if all the tech-tree vehicles had the option of equipping original modules. This change could be because some vehicles have somewhat better stats with other modules they had instead of the ones given. Some good examples are the low tier American tank destroyers T18 and T82. The T18 and T82 are rather lackluster (The T82 might be considered unusable) with their current guns. I think mounting the 75mm howitzer on the T18 and the 105mm howitzer on the T82 would make the vehicles much better. Not only that, but these are also the guns that were historically mounted on the vehicles. This would make most of the players who originally played these tanks normally a lot happier, as they were accustomed to the vehicles being like this. Overall, I can see this as being a small improvement to the game, and one I personally think would make lower tier battles more fun.

Developer Response ,

We appreciate your feedback and wish you a great game!
We do our best to improve the game in every its aspect. If there are any changes, we will announce about them on our official portal: http://wotblitz.com/news/.
Stay tuned!😊
