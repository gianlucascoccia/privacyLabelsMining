Description

Travel smart with ParkQuest – the all in one USA National Parks & Historic Sites guide. ZERO ads or tracking. NEW iOS 14 ParkFinder widget showing local parks near you!

Your Comprehensive Guide to America’s National Parks

Find your way to every national monument, to every national historic site, to all of the campgrounds and campsites that you’ve seen on Instagram. From there, traverse every national forest, dive deep into the depths of Yellowstone, Yosemite, Grand Teton, and Grand Canyon national parks with detailed maps, comprehensive trail maps, tips, and advice all in a simple, easy to use app.

Who would have thought that finding your way through America’s national parks could be so simple?

With ParkQuest, you can plan your next great National Parks adventure in minutes.

Plan Your Visit

Plan your visit to a wide range of national parks with the simple, easy to use ParkQuest National Parks Guide. Featuring an interactive dashboard that supports Dark Mode, displays beautifully detailed maps of parks, trail maps, and area maps, and utilizes advanced smart technology to save your content for offline viewing, ParkQuest National Parks Guide is the only national parks guide you’ll ever need.

It’s Time To Discover The Secrets of America’s National Parks

ParkQuest National Parks Guide is more than just a map app – along with providing users with detailed information for historic sites, campgrounds, and visitor centers, you can use ParkQuest’s database to learn important information related to every park in our system. From there, learn all about special sites, interesting locations, and important facilities with up to date information that is constantly updated and maintained.

View park hours, find directions, and verify entrance fees before you arrive to plan your days accordingly. And from there, you can rest assured knowing that your visit to America’s national parks will be one for the books.

From California to Washington D.C., ParkQuest can guide you through all the parks, forests, monuments, and preserves in the United States.

With ParkQuest, you can access quick directions to nearby points of interest, including:
- Scenic lookouts, vista points, and scenic views
- Hiking trails, biking trails, and popular treks
- Restrooms, visitor centers, and park facilities
- Campgrounds, campsites, and RV parking

ParkQuest gives you ad-free and spyware-free access to thousands of detailed maps, helping you find park facilities, hiking trails, and more.

Every park includes detailed interactive charts with helpful data for planning your trip:
- The average monthly daytime visitors to each National Park Service site for each park
- The typical monthly tent campers, backcountry campers, RV campers, and overnight lodge stays at each park
- The average daytime high temperature each month
- The average nighttime low temperature each month

Typically, you would need to carry a map, travel book, and notes from a park ranger to get the information easily while you’re on the road. Now, you can install ParkQuest.

Beautiful app with tons of useful info

Great app! I'm honestly surprised there wasn't already an app like this for national parks. Most of the other national parks/federal lands apps I've seen have really outdated designs, but this one presents information in a clean and modern interface. The part that shows the temperatures and how busy the park is each month is super useful for trip planning. It's also fun to just explore the map. Lots of national monuments and historic sites all over the place that I'd never heard of before. I have an old phone so the app is sometimes a little laggy, but it seems like they release updates pretty often so I'm not too concerned about it. Definitely get this app if you're into the outdoors-- it's awesome.
