Description

The #1 app for churches without musicians! Problem solved.

Just when you thought it couldn't possibly get any better, The C-Dub Brand has released this revolutionary new app called Whoop Triggerz Plus, that makes it possible for anyone, yes ANYONE, to back up a preacher during service, and sound like a 7 pc band while doing so! Not only that, you can now play behind a singer and sound like a skilled musician. It just takes one finger!! Pastors, your days of having to preach without musicians backing you are finally over! Church members, you now have the power to assist your pastor and music ministry like never before. This app is designed for the church by the church. It’s easy to download and get started, and we guarantee your church will never be the same! You’ve been looking for a way to enhance the music ministry at your church, and it’s finally here!


Amazing Features:


-Back up the preacher in any key without having any advanced musical knowledge.

-Play behind a singer without any musical knowledge! Just tap the blue button when you see the "cue words" displayed on the screen!

-Key Detection! Can't figure out what key the preacher is preaching in? Hold down the key display for about a second, and soon it will find the key for you and highlight it in green!

--Sound like a 7pc band with just the push of ONE button using the revolutionary Easy Play mode! All you have to do is tap the button, and each time, a new chord or band hit will play a realistic sequence that will instantly have you sounding like a pro!

-Be creative and create your own sequence of Whoop chord progressions while playing in Pads view. You can take full control of the moment and easily flow as the atmosphere of the service changes!

-Tons of collections to use giving you the ability to carry an entire service musically from you mobile device!

-The ability to change keys at any given time

-Tap the S button and the congregation will be ready to shout!

-Swipe right and instantly access 4 levels of shout music that you can switch between on the fly. You can change the key and the tempo at any given time!

-Tap the T button and you’ll get endless organ talk music in any key!

-Swipe left, and instantly access 4 levels of worship music that you can switch between at any point in time! You can also change the key and pitch at will.

-Change keys while preaching and the music never stops!

- Ability to do a full praise and worship set with all the transitions in between!

The app blogger , 11/12/2019

As. MUSICIAN Pastor

I have been a minister of music for 25 years and a licensed and ordained preacher for 15 years and a pastor for 1 year and a half. I STILL have to play and the. Get off the organ to go preach. The keyboardist we have does have to tip out as I get up to deliver the message. This product was made for me as it has the potential to help me be two places at once in closing. HOWEVER, the flaws are that the 2 piece collection has very limited stabs and runs in the first 9 sets. Adding 9 more ages matching the chord stabs the full band has plus several runs respecting those chords would make for a much more natural build up as well as keep the preacher in that collection without ever having to use the band just to get those chord stabs. There needs to be more chords runs between 2-3 as well as between 4-5. I kinda wish there was a feature to record your own stabs and sets so the musician can be two places at once. I know how ai would back myself up is what I mean. Also I would include some transitional sets for the move from high praise to worship or vice versa. Basically the app is a good start but it’s missing some elements that would make it flawless. As a musician I totally respect it. As a pastor I totally could use it. As both though it’s missing some sets that would make it perfect. Please offer an upgrade with more in the 2 piece set and focus on the ‘tuning up’ portion.
