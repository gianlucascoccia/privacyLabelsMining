Description

Read China Daily in its original format

You can now have CHINA DAILY at your fingertips. The China Daily iPaper app from NewspaperDirect gives you every section of CHINA DAILY every day -- full-content digital replicas in their original layout. The app is free, and you can link your existing China Daily Smart Edition account to download the paper wherever you are. Flip pages, pan and zoom in to view any page, article or photo in stunning clarity.

For US$4.99 per month, you get China Daily iPaper in all four editions: China Daily, China Daily USA, China Daily Hong Kong and China Daily European Weekly. The cost for an individual issue is 99 cents (US).

• Payment charged to your iTunes account
• Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the subscription period
• You can manage your subscription, including auto-renewal, by going to Account Settings after purchase
• Cancellation can be activated only after the current subscription expires (no partial refunds)
