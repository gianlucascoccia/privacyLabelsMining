Description

Everyone's favorite Custom Pokemon Card Maker.

Make custom parody Pokemon cards using your own photos and pics. Customize everything on your card and see it in a cool 3D view.

- Super easy to edit.
- Select a photo from your photo library.
- Swap the card type.
- Edit the card's name, description, stats, attacks and more.
- Save your card to your photo library and add it your your deck to view it in the app later.
- Share your card on social media from directly in the app.

OK, I love this app. I like to draw stories in my journals for fun then I put it in a Pokémon card. So when I found this app I knew it was going to be good because of the rating. But it wasn’t all I wanted...Here’s a list of what I want:

1. Events
The events would be stuff where you could submit your Pokémon cards and other people could like them and there would be a winner (you don’t get anything it’s just for fun).

2. AR mode
I saw this in lots of other mobile Pokémon card making games. You could pick a card to summon, and your friends could summon there’s and you could play against each other! I just thought this was SO COOL because it was like real life.

3. Expansions
Ok, so there are 3 expansions as I am writing this. And I think there should be a option to put a expansion with your Pokémon cards.

4. More card types
With card types, it just makes the card different. There could be gold, or black, and maybe if you won a event you could win some different card types? That’s your choice though.

5. More health
I think you should be able to give your Pokémon as much health as you want. It would just make it more fun when making cards.

Ok, thanks for reading the whole thing. You don’t have to add this stuff but I would absolutely LOVE if you did. Bye!

Good, but needs work

This is a pretty good app, especially considering that it’s free, but there are issues with it nonetheless. For one thing you can do stage 1 or 2 cards, which is kinda an issue, would be great if you were able to add that. The retreat cost and move energy things need to be able to have more then 1 or 2 energy on them, it would make the cards seem more authentic, which would add to their creative value. And then the last thing that would really kinda be needed to make them seem more authentic was to add the ability to put a “Pokédex #” on them, that would just be great but it’s honestly not really important compared to the other things. An additional thing to add is entirely unnecessary but I feel would improve the app a lot, and that is a question of whether or not you would be willing/able to add Poké-bodies and abilities to the cards? Lastly, if you could add a feature for making Ex’s and Gx’s, that would be awesome, but I am aware that that is likely too much to ask. Again, this is a pretty good app, I think if it just added those things it would be absolutely perfect.
