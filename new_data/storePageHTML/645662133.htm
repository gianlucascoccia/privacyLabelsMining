Description

Get even more out of MyShiftPlanner - try Pro features, free for 3 days and take advantage of sync, more calendars, annual leave tracking, icons, reporting and lots more!

If you work shifts you'll love MyShiftPlanner. The awesome color coded shift work planner - designed to help shift workers gain back control of their shift work diary. 

300,000+ shift workers around the world, from nurses to retail workers, are already back in control of their shift work lives.

KEEP TRACK OF YOUR SHIFTS
- Automated shift schedules via an easy to use color-coded calendar
- Full customization of shift type names, times and colors
- Many common shift patterns built-in
**4 On/4 Off
**DuPont Schedule
**Days/Nights
**Early/Late
**Continental Pattern
**Custom Repeating Patterns
**Non-repeating patterns
**And many more
- Change your rota and set up future rotas in your calendar*
- Set reminders and customize alert sounds*
- Create multiple personal calendars*

TRACK SHIFT HOURS, LEAVE & OVERTIME
- Add annual leave, rest-days, overtime, on-call, training and recurring paydays
- Calculate hours, overtime and annual leave via time reports and tracking tools*
- See reports for normal and overtime hours worked*
- Track your holiday/annual leave allowance*

SHARE YOUR SHIFT ROTA
- Sync with your device calendar to manage work, social, and family events in one place*
- Sync shift data between your devices
- Sync your shift rota with your device calendar*
- Show personal appointments from your phone in MyShiftPlanner*
- Email, print and share your shift pattern information

CUSTOMIZE THE APP FOR YOUR NEEDS
- Caters for public holidays in most countries
- Supports split shifts and week numbers
- Supports 24 hour shifts
- Choose from 3 app styles - Light, Dark and Gray
- Today Widget included
- TouchID and FaceID protection for your app
- Works on both iPhone and iPad
- Regular updates with new features, fixes and improvements

**Calendar Sync - pull events from your device calendar to show in the app, and/or push your shifts into your device calendar for use with other apps
**Multiple Patterns - add future rotas to your calendar for when your shift schedule changes
**Multiple Shifts on a day - add a second shift to any day
**Multiple Calendars - create more calendars to track second job, or a partner's shift rota.
**Calendar Overlay - see two of your calendars, or a shared calendar together
**Custom Icons - add to your calendar to keep track of personal appointments or special shifts
**Sharing - share your calendar with other MyShiftPlanner users (such as partner or colleagues)

**Work Time Report - track worked hours, overtime and annual leave for any period
**Shift Reminders - make sure you never miss a shift. Setup for all or individual shifts
