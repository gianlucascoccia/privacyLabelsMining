Description

Tattoo & Piercing can makeover your Body with cool designed tattoos and piercing. It helps to create and share your tattooed or pierced images with your friends and family.

MAIN FEATURES:
+ Choose photo from album or camera
+ Crop your photo if needed
+ Add Piercings and Tattoos on yourself Virtually
+ Add customize Text or Effects
+ Save and share your creations to social medium [Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Flickr, Tumblr or Email]
