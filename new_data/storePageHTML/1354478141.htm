Description

Fight to become the biggest crab on the island.
Intense multiplayer fun, battle with up to 100 real players per game!
Scrap, scavenge and slaughter your way to becoming the King of Crabs!

Collect weird and wonderful crab species, upgrade and customise them to help you climb the ranks. Team Up with friends to help or fight each other - it's up to you!

A huge island world is waiting to be explored and conquered. Find a variety of funny and dangerous weapons to give you the edge.

This game combines easy to use touch controls with fun packed game-play.
Incredibly detailed 3D graphics, animation and sound make the experience come alive.

BECOME THE KING
Climb to the top of the leader-board to prove you’re the mightiest crab in the land!

CRAB ‘EM ALL
Collect, upgrade and grow a variety of Crabs. Discover advanced skills and collect unique skins.

WEAPONS GALORE
Find an array of crazy and deadly weaponry to help skew the odds in your favour.

MULTIPLE GAME MODES
Test your worth in the intense PvP game mode, or try PvE for a more friendly time!

REGULAR UPDATES
Look out for new Crabs, skins, maps, special events and games modes in the future.

* Game requires a persistent internet connection.

Please note: King of Crabs is free to download and play, however, certain items are available to be purchased for real money.

Rialcm , 02/17/2021

Focus on 1 crab if you are F2p

I play on iPhone SE 2016 At first I was ready to uninstall the game because of how fast it drained my batteries but this was pretty much solved by changing graphics from mid to low. After you get past the quick tutorial of this game you get put into a battle against other players, what u don’t know is that this sort of easy noob arena where you can easily become king crab continues until you reach a certain rank, after that you suddenly find your self in the arena with the strong players and you will keep dying to what you would call Pay 2 win players. A lot of people say that the game is pay 2 win, yes there are consumable boosts like “faster growth” and “magnetic”. spenders would definitely have a lot of these. But The mega and legendary crabs and the upgrade points for the crabs are not pay 2 win, these are fairly easy to obtain as a F2p by simply playing the game and climbing the ranks / completing quests for gacha spins. Upgrade points are rotated in the shop. Here’s what I recommend, if u are going to be F2P you must focus all your attention on and main 1 crab. That 1 crab will gain levels as you play, each level represents 1% growth to your crab, so let’s say your crab is level 80, it will enter the game at 80% growth. All your earned pearls you would spend them on buying upgrade points from the shop rotation and soon you have your self a crab that easily competes with the pay 2 win.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for the very detailed review and 5 stars! We continue to balance the game and as you mentioned, introduced ranked servers so that similar skilled players will battle against with each other. All crabs have a strength and weakness, even the legendary crabs. Finding the crab that best fits your play style and levelling it up is good advice. Worth noting the more crabs you have levelled up, the more scavenger coins will be available to collect. We have plenty of updates planned for the future!
