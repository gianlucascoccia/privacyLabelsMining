Description

The advanced viewing and selling solution for artists, galleries, collectors and interior designers to instantly view and buy/sell artworks on personal wall spaces, no measuring! Fast and easy to use. Import images of art, shoot a photo, start sharing your new walls and selling your art! Sell original artworks, your own fine art prints, or enable iArtView prints and wall art in one click.

Whatever your medium - fine artist, photographer, mixed media painter or digital artist, iArtView will help your art look fantastic. This app is the perfect tool for artists who want to sell more art.

Features:
iArtView allows artists to sell original artwork
iArtView automatically scales artwork to your wall space. No need to measure.
View single or multiple artworks on your personal walls.
Not near your wall? No problem! Select one of the designer interiors provided.
Adjust lighting
Alter the perspective and angle of your art on the wall.
Choose a frame and mat combination from our contemporary selection.
Get inspired! Choose a new wall color with the scaleable Color Wall.
Save art-on-wall images to your camera roll or email for handy future reference.
Share walls with clients and friends via email, text, Airdrop
Share to all platforms like Facebook, Pinterest, Twitter,
Purchases are a breeze. Buy and sell artworks directly in the app. Receive commission on prints made via iArtView. No fees for listing artworks.
Favourite the art you love the most
Place Favourite artworks on interior walls to find your favourite combination
Professional and varied social media content creation is easy
Save time on styling your beautiful quality content for social media by utilising our designer interiors
New interiors and frames added regularly
Additional features for paid subscribers:
Customize gallery profile screen with gallery logo, information and GPS map directions.
Discover tab where buyers will discover your art and you can find new art to be inspired by

JB9111 , 01/07/2018

Simple to use but limited walls offered

Easy to use for busy and artists. Just snap a picture and put it on a wall, any wall or background. There’s a good number of walls offered in the package but you run out of walls fast if you produce constantly. There should be more walls added to the inventory. I have the Plus subscription and I think there need to be more walls for the price. I can see how other artists complained that it can be pricey. The top of the line subscription is too pricey for me at this point. I’m ok (not absolutely satisfied) with this app, until I find another one.

Hubcatsmuse , 10/19/2021

My Abstracts…

Love seeing my abstract paintings in a room setting and on the walls. The thing I would love to be able to change is to be able to move the position and enlarge my works so that their new positions and size will stay when I send them out. Nancy Belle

V8748855 , 08/21/2020

All the functions but ridiculous pricing

I think this could really be an amazing app. I’ve looked at several similar apps and in terms of functionality, I like this one the best, love that you can change the perspective as well as the size on your pieces.

My only issue (but BIG issue) is with the pricing. Very few wall options available even for the plus version, I would be willing to pay those prices if they had more to offer but there are far too many other apps with more reasonable pricing and significantly more walls to choose from. If it weren’t for this, I would rate this app 5 stars. Just doesn’t make sense to purchase unless you have money to blow.
