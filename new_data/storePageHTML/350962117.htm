Annoyed by bugs! , 09/09/2018

Video playback and image display bugs!

Ever since a big update several months ago, the videos on the front page can’t be viewed properly in landscape mode. A huge portion the picture would be cut off on the top and bottom, so only the middle section of the whole picture shows. Even when the videos are viewed in portrait mode, edges of the picture are cut off slightly, which doesn’t really affect the whole picture, unless there are words at the edges. The videos will be shown in full screen automatically and cannot, I repeat, CANNOT be resized, regardless of which mode viewed.
Some images and gif will be oversized as well when clicked on, and requires to be resized, but some images cannot be resized and would require the viewing device (in my case, iPad mini 2) to be switched to the upright portrait mode.
The videos in SinaNews that shows up in “messages” can never be played out ever since who knows when.
Oh, another thing, automatic video playback cannot be turned off when viewing videos in series, once one video finishes, the app will scroll down and play the next (sometimes I like to watch the same video more than once, I would be happier without having to keep scrolling up to play it over!)
Other than those annoying problems aforementioned, the app is fun for entertainment and socialization.
