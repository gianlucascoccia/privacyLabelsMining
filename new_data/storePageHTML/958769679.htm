Description

Hunt.com is an apartment finder app designed to find you the perfect home. Search through all the best local apartments and houses, use detailed filters, contact landlords, save your preferences, and share with friends.

Key Features:
• Huge inventory of affordable apartments, homes and condos
• Intuitive, easy-to-use interface
• Search results available in list or map-form, whichever you prefer
• Interactive map which automatically groups rentals for easy browsing
• Helpful filters to narrow your search; specify preferences for location, price, neighborhood and even pet-friendliness
• Personal account to store your favorite rental listings
• Email or call properties directly from the app
• Nationwide coverage in the U.S.
• Download the Hunt app and find your perfect rental today!

Share any comments, feedback, bug reports at hi@hunt.com. We’re always excited to hear from users - Happy Apartment Hunting!

I have given up using this for the 5th time. Every time in the last few years I have needed to move, I download this app, put in the information for what I need, and it gives me NOTHING. I am starting to think either listing creators don't know about this app or that there is zero reasonably priced housing on this app at all and that they focus exclusively on high end or out of my region housing. Rheapxoxo , 06/08/2017

Too good to be true

I was recently looking at properties on this app and when I contacted landlords they told me that their properties were managed through Airbnb and they would be handling payments. Then I received "from" Airbnb which fake. It also requested that payments be made via iTunes gift cards. THIS IS A SCAM.

So disappointed with this app for not doing proper background and security checks on their tenants/landlords.

After doing research on all of this I found out the same guy who tried scamming me has scammed many others in various cities throughout the country.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for surfacing this! We work hard to keep scammers off our platform, but sounds like we may have missed one. Please contact us at hi@hunt.com so we can investigate this specific case and avoid such scams going forward. Thank you in advance!
