Description

Let Homes.com assist you in finding the perfect rental home. Instantly find your next rental from an intuitive map search view in your target area. Whether you’re looking for an apartment, condo, town home, or a home for rent Homes.com makes the search process easy.

Adjust filters to see house rentals that are pet friendly, include a gym or other amenities important to you. Finding your next rental home has never been simpler.

Key Features:

• Map search made simple. Slide and tap to quickly find rental homes.

• Filter results with easy taps. Pet friendly? Washer & Dryer? Pool? Find what is important to you.

• Save and quickly view your top choices.

• Bright full screen, landscape oriented photos are easy to view with a quick swipe.

• Connect instantly with rental property managers to check availability.

• Natural gestures make navigating the app user friendly.

Homes.com is your partner in finding the perfect rental for you to create your home moments.

Scams

I’ve talked to 2 people about looking into condos, both with really good deals that sounded awesome! Both of them wanted to work through Airbnb and wanted a down payment that was 100% refundable before being able to see the place. So I looked into it and they only want you to pay through bitcoin. Fake Airbnb site with cryptocash, which is not refundable. The “owners” also live across country and don’t actually answer any questions. They just want to deal business to ASAP to get your money. Do not trust any rentals if they want you to deal with it. One person was Anne Sanders, who was in England, and the other person was Mario Ballesteros in Spain. Both of them also had stories like “my daughter lived there for 2 years and decided to move out. So we wanted to rent it”. DON’T DO IT! Don’t trust them if they want to work through Airbnb.

Hi Tael! Thank you for taking you time to leave us a review! We’re sorry you had a bad experience while using the app and running into what might be a scam. We will definitely look into this and find out what’s going on.
