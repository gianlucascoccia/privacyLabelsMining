Description

It’s never been easier to set up your T-Mobile Internet service and manage your network.

Use the T-Mobile Internet app to quickly and easily set up your High-Speed Internet Gateway. You can also control which devices connect to your Wi-Fi network, edit network settings, and more, all from your mobile device.

Note: To use this app you must be a current T-Mobile Internet customer.
