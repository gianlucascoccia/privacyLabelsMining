Description

** Featured in Apple Apps We Love 2019, The New York Times, Forbes, Wired, and more! **

INSCAPE Meditation App: Reduce stress & anxiety, improve focus, and sleep better using daily mindfulness meditations, breathing exercises, music, and sounds. The app includes personalized content recommendations based on your goals, time of the day, and experience.

Make meditation and mindfulness part of your health, fitness, and wellness routine with INSCAPE guided meditation.

Research shows the benefits of meditation to include:

IMPROVED
* Sleep
* Happiness
* Heart Health
* Cholesterol Levels

DECREASED
* Stress
* Anxiety
* Blood Pressure
* Sadness & Depression
* Aggression & Irritability

The INSCAPE meditation app includes guided meditation sessions for beginners, intermediate, and advanced meditators and range in length from 5 minutes to 85 minutes.

INSCAPE meditation and mindfulness sessions focus on
Sleeping Better
Reducing Stress
Relieving Anxiety
Breathwork
Increasing Happiness
Love & Relationships
Fear of Flying
Boosting Creativity
Improving Focus
Building Acceptance
Commuting
Kids Sleep & Relaxation
And more...


Inscape has been designed to fit around your busy lifestyle. The simple habit building exercises have been designed to deliver maximum impact, with only a few minutes a day to help you get to a better headspace.

The Inscape meditation and relaxation app can help you enjoy a happier and healthier life. Download now for free. For our friends in NYC, come visit our guided meditation and relaxation studio in the Flatiron district.

Subscription pricing and terms:

INSCAPE offers auto-renewing monthly and yearly subscription options.

Your INSCAPE subscription will automatically renew at the end of each term and your credit card will be charged through your iTunes account. You can turn off auto-renew at any time from your iTunes account settings but refunds will not be provided for any unused portion of the term.

INSCAPE integrates with the Apple Health app and tracks mindful minutes. Your profile in the INSCAPE app also tracks average time spent meditating, and a streak counter to track progress in building a meditation habit.

So far so good, except for the bugs...

So far I’m really loving this app. I’ve never been great at meditation or taking time to calm down and reduce stress, so having an app on my phone - which is basically my lifeline - that I can just press when I need to wind down is extremely helpful. However, I’m having some issues with what I can only assume is a bug; the home page isn’t loading, making it hard to interact with the lessons/sessions I want. I have full signal so I know it isn’t my internet, and I’ve tried clicking on the lessons I’ve gotten emails about, but it still won’t load. I’m not sure where to report this bug, so here I am.
Also, as nice as it is to have a free option and the trial available for premium, the constant reminder by placing a big green bar over items I want to click saying “try out trial!!!” Is OBNOXIOUS. No matter how many times I dismiss it, it returns 30 seconds later and it’s irritating. I know that premium subscriptions are how you make your money, but as much as I like this app it’s making me reconsider using it when I can’t click on things. :/

Hi! Thanks for the feedback and sorry you’re having an issue. There’s a bug fix coming for your home screen spinner in the next update. In the meantime if you log out (You > Settings) and log back in again, it’ll go away.
