Description

Just as the Byga service platform has set the standard for how large elite club directors and administrators get everything running smoothly behind the scenes, the Byga Mobile App sets the standard for how families, team staff and administrators coordinate, collaborate, and ultimately develop better clubs and better players.

The Byga Mobile App provides the following features:
- Schedules and Results
- Player Attendance
- Teams and Group
- Messaging and Chat
- Club Notifications
- Access to Club Resources
- And More

With the Byga mobile app:
- Everyone stays on the same page with reliable communication and timely instructions.
- Parents enjoy their children’s games and support their team / club more.
- Everyone is more engaged in the club community through collaboration beyond the team.

Finding the right club operating system for your club is a crucial step in ensuring smooth operations of your club, meeting expectations of coaches, parents and players, and providing solid financial footing. Considerations such as club size or complexity, field scheduling needs, and the need to support multiple leagues are important considerations that will lead you to consider the Byga solution.

Jim@Hicks , 10/06/2020

I REALLY like BYGA

I am a team manager for a girls competitive soccer team, have used other team management apps before, but man, oh man, is this nice. We used BYGA for tryouts, where team parents entered their own registration info via the app, then team managers completed the registration by checking in players at the field by taking their picture and entering their jersey #. Coaches then had the player picture, jersey # and all the checkin info at their finger tips, on their phones, which aided in their confidential evaluations. Practices and games are easy to set up, they can be set up at the club level, or at the team level. Rosters and player availability are both easy to use. Contacting the team is easy, can text, email or push notification. And the support is awesome! We had an odd issue where the league made a change to the schedule, that change flowed through to BYGA’s calendar but some of us team managers did not receive notice of the change. BYGA’s support was all over it and had that issue straighten out quickly. Absolutely the best team management app I have encountered. Highly recommended !

Hasseaf , 04/12/2019

Fantastic app

I’m reviewing this as a parent whose daughter is in her first season of competitive club soccer. I have been using Byga, both on the web and through the app, for a few months now.

I already love Byga in general and the app is a perfect companion to the more full-featured web product. It has the most important pieces I need on the go: a detailed schedule and the ability to read and send messages to the coach and other parents on the team.

The interface is simple (in a good way) and intuitive. I haven’t encountered any bugs or rough edges. Love it!
