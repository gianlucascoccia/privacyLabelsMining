Description

Stars Discovery - Glitter of all stars in your hand now.

Ever wondered how many stars and constellations we are surrounded by?
There are countless stars and lots of constellations named by space explorer agencies, and here all are wrapped up in one app, to give everybody the exact information of their location, shape, appearance in iPhones & iPad's

Enjoy the view around earth from earth without telescopes and without searching for a place away from your home in search of glittering stars of sky in night.

All the human searched constellation till now are updated in Stars & Constellations app for iOS
