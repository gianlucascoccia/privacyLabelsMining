leannalady , 09/01/2021

Great app but would be better with a tracking option

I really like this app. Honestly, I was a little skeptical since I’ve read about the equipment needed for Kirlian photography and didn’t think my phone camera could replicate it. However, I’ve read my own aura before and it’s often primarily purple, but I wanted to try this app since I can’t always read it in detail. I also saw that they use a hybrid reading style and don’t just use the phone camera somehow. Purple has popped up in my readings every time and it even shows up as my dominant aura color most of the time, even if other colors shift. This gives me more trust in the app’s credibility. The analyses and other colors also make sense with what’s been on my mind.

The only thing I don’t like about this app is that there’s no way to access your previous reading analyses and the photos are not automatically saved. You can see your last reading, but that’s it. It would be lovely if there was a diary option to save your readings and track your progress rather than just being able to save the photo to the camera roll. As a slight data geek, some kinda chart or graph for tracking the colors/intensity/placement would be awesome as well.

Overall, great app! Would def recommend if you have trouble reading your own aura and want to check it every day. I also have the paid subscription and I feel that the pricing is fair for the extra daily readings.
