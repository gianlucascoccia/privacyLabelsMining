Some extremely important advice

The story is well written and the graphics are phenomenal.

As far as cost, it’s a little pricey. However, you can spend more on other less visually stimulating games. I would recommend you at least give this one a whirl.

Start a new player and only spend a dollar to get yourself a feel for whats going on here, because there is a lot going on here, very overwhelming in the beginning. Once you learn the ins and outs, start another account with a new player. Trust me, this is the best way until you get a foundation under your feet.

Zsenine , 04/18/2020

Enjoyable

I am liking the game. Good graphics, cool character and world designs. Even a cool storyline. It is, like every “free” game, very p2w, which I expected. All of the good costumes, you have to buy with real money. And drops from bosses are all based on chance. Sure, you can get wings and good equipment from bosses, I know people who have got them, but with my awful luck I get garbage equipment from bosses, every single time. It gets kind of frustrating. It is very hard to dodge bosses, since the controls aren’t good, and the boss attacks are stacked one after the other so you literally cannot dodge AND attack, just one or the other. So bosses are either very easy or ridiculously hard. I especially dislike the attacks where the system tells you to dodge, but also stuns you so you can’t move. But every game has its own issues, and I will stick with this one for a while since there are good things about the game, too.
