FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

• Can I rely on Rocket Lawyer documents?
Yes. Rocket Lawyer documents are vetted by lawyers and legal staff so you can use them with confidence.

• How quickly will an attorney respond when I ask a question online?
Our nationwide network of On Call attorneys responds to all questions within 1 business day. Many questions are answered in less than 4 business hours.

• Are RocketSign digital signatures legal?
Yes. RocketSign digital signatures meet all requirements set forth in the ESIGN Act, making them fully legal and enforceable for most purposes. For more information, see our RocketSign Terms of Service.

• What does the free trial include?
During your one-time 7-day free trial, you can make documents, get legal answers online from a lawyer, and RocketSign documents.

• How much does a Premium Membership cost after the free trial?
At Rocket Lawyer, we believe everyone deserves access to affordable and simple legal services, which is why we keep our monthly membership fees as low as possible. After your free trial, it’s only $39.99 per month to enjoy the full suite of legal services available on the Rocket Lawyer app.

• Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. You can deactivate your Rocket Lawyer membership during your free trial or at any time thereafter.

• How can I get started?
To get started, simply download the Rocket Lawyer app. We’re ready to help!

• Can I view the Terms of Service?
Yes, you can view the Terms of Service here: https://www.rocketlawyer.com/general-terms-of-service"
