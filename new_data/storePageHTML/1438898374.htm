Description

Ever wanted to know what it feels like to run your own interior design studio? Now you can with TapBlaze’s newest home design game, Homecraft! Do your best to satisfy your client's design request while staying true to your interior decorator intuition. Become the master homecrafter of your neighborhood... your city... your country ... your world!

Solve match 3 puzzle levels and unlock hundreds of new decor items to help your clients create their dream home! The final makeover design is entirely up to you!

FEATURES
* 1,000s of different home decor makeover items
* Home design simulation with match 3 puzzle challenges
* Unique homes, rooms, houses and clients
* Unlock rewards as you polish your design skills as an interior decorator
* Cute story and relaxing gameplay

From the living room to the bedroom, dining room, rooftop garden, chef's kitchen, luxury bedroom, and backyard pool, you have the opportunity to express your home design style while renovating without getting your hands dirty.

Download and start homecrafting now!

imlizr , 07/23/2019

I like it a lot, but progress is slow and/or expensive

Could be more than 3 stars with improvements. I am *deleting* for now, but might reinstall in awhile to see if it is improved. I do really like that the coins I earn go towards decorating rooms and the match-three games for earning the coins are challenging and varied. However you can only store up to 5 hearts (energy allowing you to play one match-three game each) at a time. And the amount of time it takes to earn those hearts is way too slow. Also the diamonds that can be traded for hearts and boosters cost too much money. I suggest we earn/store 10 hearts in the time it now takes for five. Packages of diamonds and “specials” should cost less. I expect to spend some money when I first start playing a game because I want to learn it and play a little faster, but this one takes too much. I know game developers need money to start and keep developing a game, but I will be more loyal and keep spending some money consistently overtime if it is more balanced and the game company doesn’t seem too greedy. Also, more bonus options in-game or via FB page would be helpful and fun.
