FEATURES
• Sophisticated user interface for guitar playing
• Scrollable fretboard with 15 frets

• Chord table mapping on the guitar fretboard
• Strings enable/disable mode, strum with precision
• Guitar orientation for right & left handed people
• Different guitar sounds to choose from
• Sharing with your Facebook friends
• Tested and designed for the latest Apple phones and tablets

HOW TO PLAY
Tap on the fretboard to select the frets you want, swipe over it to move to higher and lower frets.
Tap on the buttons at the edge of the fretboard to enable and disable individual guitar strings.
Pick and strum the guitar strings, either manually or automatically by pressing the strum button.
Select the chords you want to learn by using the chord selector at the top of the screen.
