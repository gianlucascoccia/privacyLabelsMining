Description

Slime Making Games with MANY cool slimes such as: Unicorn Slime, Rainbow Slime, Glitter Slime, Candy Slime, Chocolate Slime and much much more!

From the creators of the super popular Unicorn Chef brings you the newest UNICORN SLIME!

Features:
- A super fun slime DIY game with TONS of CRAZY SLIMES!
- Keep updated with new slimes EVERY MONTH!
- DIY super cool slimes such as unicorn slime, glow in the dark slime, bubblegum slime, magnetic slime, marshmallow slime, shampoo slime, color changing slime, edible Nutella slime, edible Laffy Taffy slime, and so much more
- Tons of realistic DIY tools to play: bowls, spatulas, spoons, measuring cups, plates, store boxes, food masher, needle tubing, and so much more
- Tons of slime materials to try: spring water, school white glue, clear glue, baking soda, glitters, food coloring, contact lens solution, borax powder, hot water, color changing pigment powder, glow paint, glow glitters, shampoo, marshmallows, chocolate, candies, sprinkles, and so much more

How to play:
- Use interactive controls to play the game
- Follow the recipes to make the slimes
- Mix various ingredients to create the unique flavor and color of your slimes
- Decorate your slimes with beautiful sprinkles, glitters, and unicorn toys
- Store the slime into the storeroom or refrigerator
- Eat edible slimes to trigger the rainbow magic
