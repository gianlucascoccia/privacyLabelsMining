Description

Descartes MacroPoint™ for Truckers is the most trusted and secure app used by drivers. Enjoy automated communication of arrival, departure, and in-transit location data to your customers in real time. Descartes MacroPoint™ for Truckers is free to use. Automatic location updating keeps the app hands-free. Ensure a safe and uninterrupted driving experience with MacroPoint for Truckers.

Benefits & Features

• Automatically sends in-transit location data
• Reduce check calls & increase on-road performance
• Receive real-time notification of load assignments
• Instant tracking connectivity to all your customers
• Improve customer satisfaction

Descartes MacroPoint™ for Truckers enables location sharing and other communication with your trusted partners during active load assignments only and always over a secure connection.

To read more about our terms and privacy policy, please visit: www.MacroPoint.com/privacy-policy/

**Continued use of GPS running in the background can dramatically decrease battery life. However, MacroPoint for Truckers doesn't run the GPS services in the background continuously. The app uses GPS only when the device moves significantly (i.e. 100 meters).

PrivacyIsKing , 11/23/2020

Privacy concerns

Going to be honest with you here. The app works as advertised but I seriously do not like having to download this app onto my personal device in order to complete a load. Every time I have a load that requires me to use the app I only download it when I am actively delivering the load and immediately delete the app afterwards. I feel it is incredibly invasive to have this app on my PERSONAL device. What information does the app have privileges to? My phone contains private data that the app is not clear on whether or not it has access to (financial records, personal pictures/videos, contacts, call/text records, etc.) There should be an opt out option available but apparently I’ll be fined if I don’t download this invasive application on my phone. I am a company driver, my job does not pay my cell phone bill. This is not a “work” phone device. I’m honestly questioning the legality of such a system.

Developer Response ,

Hi there. We understand and encourage you to read our privacy policy. We can also state that we only ask for location permission. If we wanted access to other files or text history etc.. the app would have to ask you for those permissions to have access. We do not ask for those. We used to but do not any more. The ELD market is strong and we connect to those systems as well. Have your company contact us and we can connect to the truck for location instead of the mobile app.
