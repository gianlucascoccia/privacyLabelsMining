Description

Welcome to the mixing master class that offers an unparalleled mobile DJ set-up as well as a massive variety of learning resources that will teach you how to mix like a pro!

DJ it! condenses a fully functioning DJ set-up into your mobile phone without sacrificing a single feature. Your fingertips will have access to all the tools the pros have at their decks except now they're on your mobile. Better still, DJ it! showcases precisely how to use these tools effectively as it offers a personalized educational experience to guide new DJs and veteran mixers alike!

Learn to mix.
Having the perfect tools is one thing, but you need to know how to use them! Thankfully, DJ it! is all about doing just that. Whatever your ability level, DJ it! offers lessons that will help you master critical techniques that are essential for every DJ.

Quiz
Start by taking a simple questionnaire to let DJ it! analyze your skill level! Study up and attempt more challenging quizzes to earn high scores and expand your knowledge.

Glossary
Faders, Pitch Sliders, Filters, Channels, Turntables, Decks, Mixers... Don't panic! Our extensive glossary covers every piece of language that every Dj needs.

Dj Tips
Master looping, explore creating hot cues and learn the ins-and-outs of the mashup with quick tips tailored to a variety of skills levels and requirements.

Tutorials
Here you can learn how to use every single aspect of DJ it! incredible turntable set up. Explore every feature of your mobile mixing desk and tap on whatever you need to learn more about. With tutorials, you'll be mixing like a pro in no time!

Practice makes perfect.
DJ it! also offers an unrivaled mobile mixing set up that makes creating fresh music just as easy as it is to get up and dance to it! However, that doesn’t mean it’s not for experts too! Aside from lessons, it offers a complete Djing package that any advanced Dj could use to scratch, mash, edit, loop and fade their way to the top, all from their pocket!

Your set-up includes:
- Automatic BPM detection
- Tap to adjust BPM, merely tap the track to sync!
- All the PRO FX you need including Delay, Reverb, Flanger, Gate, High/Low-Pass, Phaser, Bit Crusher, Roll, Reverse.
- Loops: from 1/16 to 64
- Up to 4 Hot Cues per deck!
- Automated audio FX sync on the BPM.
- Three band EQ.
- HD recording with automatic saving and uploading to your library so you'll never lose a mix.
- Support for all major audio formats including MP3, AAC, WAV, AIFF

Welcome to DJ it! - your new one-stop shop for all your mixing needs.
Study up because it’s time to learn how to mix your masterpiece!
