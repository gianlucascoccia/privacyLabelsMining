dev_macd , 06/15/2020

Fun But Flawed

The PhiGolf app is decent and has moments where it’s fun to mess around with. The accuracy with the swing stick isn’t very good though. The distance is all over the place. The accuracy is pretty constantly off. Almost every shot starts out way right, which isn’t close to my real life trajectory at all.

I was excited for the WGT subscription, but that’s even worse. The distance on WGT is much more accurate, but the accuracy is awful. It’s to the point where I’ll probably cancel the monthly subscription even though it’s so cheap. Every time I try it I’ll get through 1 or 2 holes before it gets too frustrating to enjoy.

If I had to do it over again I probably wouldn’t have purchased the swing stick.

Developer Response ,

Thank you for choosing our Phigolf App.
First of all, sorry about the issue you've experienced.
To tell you the truth, there is a limit to motion sensor technology & we don't want you to change your swing.
There is no way to get equal result from swinging a short stick provided and real golf clubs. Shaft length, shaft flex, weight of device, etc. lt’s all different.
Our engineers always have been working on to provide better quality game to users.
Thank you again,

Works great with my IPhone X

Having a good time in the garage with the boy drinking beer. Think we played 36 holes last night. No connection issues or any problems except putting would not register a swing some times. Not a big deal, and fun to practice with for sure. It’s far from perfect but it’s fun and after a golf lesson I do some practice swigs at work. I don’t think the face angle is reading right. I’m always on the plus side and it reads negative every time. Would be great if it explained what your motion should be or how you should be swinging. Any kind of lesson help would be awesome. No updates for a long time so not sure what’s going on with the company. Hope they continue to support and sell the set up it’s fun.
