Description

A simple and easy to use app to track transactions and balance of your accounts like checking saving, credit card and cash etc.

Checkbook Wiz is a convenient way to manage your personal daily finance, check or add transactions anywhere with your device, keep an eye on balance of your accounts, see clearly where you money comes and goes.

You can set multiple currencies for this app, and it support iCloud auto sync, iCloud & Dropbox backup and restore, makes it easier for you and your other family number to manage accounts together.

Key features:
⁃ Track total balance, cleared balance of total accounts and each account.
⁃ Track transactions of each account.
⁃ Supports different currencies for each account.
⁃ Show transactions by daily and monthly.
⁃ Show daily and monthly total income and expense by calendar.
⁃ Supports adding expense, income and transfer transactions.
⁃ Supports splitting a transaction across multiple categories.
⁃ Add receipt for a transaction.
⁃ Add check number for a transaction.
⁃ Mark transaction as cleared or not directly in main screen page.
⁃ Kinds of default categories and you can add your own.
⁃ Supports adding recurring transactions.
⁃ Search transactions by name, category, account, amount, type and note text in a specific date range.
⁃ Comprehensive reports including:
a). Total income & expense of all accounts and each account.
b). Transactions by category.
c). Transactions by payee.
d). Monthly income & expense cash flow histogram.
e). Monthly account balance chart view.
⁃ Export data inform of PDF, CSV and HTML.
⁃ Supports Air Print.
⁃ Password protection.
⁃ Supports Touch ID as passcode.
⁃ Add unlimited reminders.
⁃ Set Primary Account, primary category and home currency.
⁃ Custom month start day.
⁃ Show or hide reconciled transaction.
⁃ Supports iCloud auto sync.
⁃ Support both iCloud and Dropbox backup and restore.
⁃ Supports Apple Watch.

Nice program. Big flaw.

I’ve used this for almost two years. Simple and flexible, and just what I wanted: a check register that makes it easy to keep my accounts balanced. But…the recurring entries function has simply stopped working reliably. Some entries are duly entered into the register, then “rolled over” to the next month. Others are rolled over without ever being entered. For example, I have a regular monthly insurance payment. The app is set up to enter the payment into the register on the first of each month. Sometimes it does; sometimes it doesn’t. But either way, the list of recurring payments rolls on to the next month. So I’m scrambling to find the disparity between the bank’s records (which show the payment) and my own (which show the next payment will be recorded in October even though the September payment was never recorded). I hope that is reasonably clear. I’m bringing this up because I have contacted the designers twice but have never received a response. I did upgrade to the paid version this year, but probably will not renew.

I recommend this to everyone

Do you want to be on top of your finances? Track your everyday spending and what not? Then this app is for you. This app has tremendously helped me with managing my money. By just taking an hour every couple of weeks and entering all my bank transactions, I can see what I'm doing with my money. Especially since the app lays it out SO beautifully. It is so detailed and precise and I have never been in so much control of my money. I love the ease of use. I love the layout. I love how I can see where every penny has gone or come in from. There is a wonderful search bar too, in case you needed to look something up. You can look up by category, keyword, bank account, and more. It's so precise too. If you search "food", you can see all the times you spent money on food. And you can choose the time frame that the search uses. You can search just the month, or one day, or the whole year. And when you hit search it will bring up EVERYTHING related to what you put in there. It's a powerful app and I highly recommend it.

It does what I need it to do...

I have had this app for a couple of years now and I am pleased with what it does. It allows me to keep track of multiple accounts just like I would with an analog checkbook register with a whole host of features that help keep me organized. It is relatively bug free and with the exception of the ads it operates pretty smoothly (I can’t tell you why I have not purchased the upgrade yet but I most likely will now that I have shamed myself into it). I cannot speak to the more advanced functions like reports and such as I don’t use them. I highly recommend this app for someone wanting a good, complete and relatively simple way to track one’s bank and credit card accounts.
UPDATE: So I upgraded and I’m so glad I did. Getting rid of the ads made a difference and the ability to print reports, compile data and print them is pretty nice. It’s not QuickBooks Pro but it does the trick for what I need. Still very happy with this app.
