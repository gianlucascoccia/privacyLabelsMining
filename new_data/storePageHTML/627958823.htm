Description

JusTalk is a FREE high-quality video, voice and group calling app for everyone - simple, reliable, secure and fun, so you can enjoy life's moments together.

WHY USE JUSTALK:

• FREE CALLS
Make HD-quality voice or video calls with your friends and family for free over Wi-Fi or on-the-go (2.5G/3G/4G/5G)*, even if they're in another country.

• MORE THAN CALLING
Doodle, record your call, play games, and share photos with your friends and family to liven up your conversation during calls.

• SAY MORE THAN TEXTS
Send and receive pictures, voices, videos, locations, stickers, GIFs, and more 1:1 or in a group for up to 200 members.

• GROUP VOICE CALL AND VIDEO CHAT (MEETINGS)
Create as many groups as you like to make group voice calls with family, friends or coworkers up to 50 people. Enjoy high quality audio and super high definition video calls.

• NIGHT VISION
Light up the background of both you and your friends in the dark.

• JUSTALK TO PHONE
Make domestic or international calls from JusTalk to phone. Click on your avatar in JusTalk and find JusTalk to Phone in Wallet to learn more.

• CROSS-PLATFORM
JusTalk is supported to use on different operating systems and all kinds of device sizes no matter it’s a smartphone or tablet.

• PRIVATE AND SECURE
All users' personal information (including calling and messaging data) is end-to-end encrypted and is split into multiple random path which ensures it can't be monitored or saved by servers. Moreover, all the personal data is never shared with any third party. Enjoy safe and free calls.

• MORE FEATURES
Create your own unique JusTalk ID and make your friends easy to search your account; add new friends by shared name cards or QR code.

• LOW DATA USAGE
Save 40-90% of the voip or vowifi network traffic during realtime video calling with 720P HD quality. Like a fun video walkie talkie.

• JUSTALK PREMIUM & JUSTALK PLUS
Upgrade to JusTalk Premium: enjoy all exclusive features including 10 free credits and special price of Out Call, 720p HD quality video calls, ads free, special ringtones and themes.

Upgrade to JusTalk Plus: enjoy ads free, special ringtones and themes.

*Data charge may apply. Contact your provider for details.

Good app, but I have a few recommendations...

All in all, I enjoy this app. It's very convenient for texting and calling when I don't want to use minutes or texts. But the video chat feature lags quite a bit, and sometimes the camera has glitches. I don't use very many emojis (in fact I deleted my emoji keyboard so that I don't get into that annoying habit) but people who do use them in most of their texts may be disappointed when they realize it costs premium to use most emojis. But if you're okay with limiting your emojis, having slight lag in your video calls, and a few pop up ads here and there, this is the app for you! It still allows you to connect with people across different platforms, the texting feature sends texts faster than iMessage, and the video chat isn't THAT terrible, there's just some freezing, but you can still hear each other. Emojis shouldn't be a problem, since I'm pretty sure most of us can express emotion just as much through words as we can through cute little smiley faces. I would definitely recommend this app!
