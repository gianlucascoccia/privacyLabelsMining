Description

Let's Find Friends!

We all meet great people while playing multiplayer games.
But we can't chat them once the game ends - that's where AmongFriends comes in!
You can use this app to keep in touch with your crewmates from games like imposter, roblox, fortnite and more!

AmongFriends is the perfect place for you to keep in touch with these friends, and find new ones.
Roblox, Minecraft, Free Fire are examples for the common games friends here play - make sure to add the best tags to your profile!
Make sure you don't share your private information, and use the app in accordance with our ToS.
Remember to not reveal any personal information while chatting!

In our 'add' page you can find many people who wants to chat with you, and their short bio describing themselves!
You can add your room code, and wait for other people to join your in-game room.
This app is a must-have to find new friends for the game, and keep in touch with existing ones.

Disclaimer:
1. This is a fan-made app and not officially related to any game. All rights reserved.
2. Use this app to keep in touch with your friends , but always treat the other person like he is an impostor - don't reveal personal information!
