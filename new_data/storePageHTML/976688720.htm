Description

Brace yourself for an age of massive medieval war, where you will march over your rivals’ burning remains in an all-out quest to conquer!

Fortify your castle, forge an army and enter the ultimate MMO of global domination! But as your civilization grows, you’ll clash with ambitious powers, and only one king can claim the throne! Do you have the strength to unify the land under your banner? Or will you watch your kingdom crumble before you?

◉ A KINGDOM IN YOUR HANDS
• Dispatch armies, construct buildings, clash with barbarians, conquer territories and reshape the balance of power every day.
• Every inch of your realm comes to life with amazingly detailed graphics

◉ CHOOSE YOUR FACTION
• Choose to be a Highland King, a Northern Tsar or a Desert Sultan.
• Each army has a special advantage that emphasizes a unique playstyle, whether you prefer to quickly build up your kingdom, strike with charging knights or fiercely defend your civilization from invasion.

◉ TERRITORIAL DOMINATION
• Clash and conquer to grow your global influence daily.
• Unlock special powers and bonuses as more lands fall to your army.
• Fortify your defenses to ward off enemy invasions.
• Fight your way to the throne to become High King over the entire realm!

◉ FORGE A CHAMPION
• Choose a unique Champion – ranging from those as noble as knights or as merciless as Vikings – to lead your army and help build your kingdom.
• Find and craft equipment to boost your Champion’s skills.
• Command heroes and adventurers to set off on quests for treasures that will pave your path to the throne.

◉ BUILD A THRIVING NATION
• Build regional structures that you and your many allies can benefit from.
• Establish thriving Trade Centers, forge sacred Temples, and clash over Stockpiles of treasure.

◉ TACTICAL MMO WARFARE
• A wise king chooses battle formations carefully! Cunning tactics hold more power than massive numbers.
• Time your commands wisely to take advantage of the ever-changing seasons.

◉ COMMAND A GLOBAL ALLIANCE
• The Alliance management system makes it easy to organize with allies.
• Intrigue will pave your path to the throne as you craft schemes with other Alliances. In this MMO world, there’s as much to gain from helping as there is from betraying.
• Chat with players to forge friendships or verbally clash with rivals.

◉ CLAIM THE SEATS OF POWER
• Capture the five highly fortified castles that hold the Seat of Domain, Seat of Heroes, Seat of War, Seat of Order and Seat of Accord.
• Each Seat grants its controlling Alliance unique perks and the ability to propose world-changing Policies.
• Every player can take part, as all Policies are voted on by the entire Realm’s player population!
• Enter a new level of diplomacy and intrigue as your Alliance must negotiate with rivals, and players at large, in order to win the votes needed to pass the Policies you want.
• At the center of it all lies the Throne of Might, which only one player may rule to wield the greatest power of all!

Five castles hold the crucial thrones, each possessing a unique influence over the entire realm that only one Alliance can hold.

Download it now and forge your path to the throne!

What’s New

Nov 4, 2021

Version 6.0.1

Hear ye, hear ye, my Lords and Ladies! Our jubilee Update 50 has marched into the Realms, bringing new heights to conquer, lots and lots of holiday content and a number of balance changes and quality-of-life improvements. Without further ado, let’s jump right in and see what the future of the Realms holds:

[NEW FEATURES]
1. Castle level 34
- Castles' level cap has been increased to 34
- Upgrading Castles above level 33 requires Special Building Plans—a brand-new resource that can acquired by completing Daily Quests

2. Advanced Research
- New types of Research that can be unlocked at the end of each Era
- Max out all Basic Research of an Era to unlock the Advanced Research
- In addition to basic resources, Advanced Research also requires Insight—a brand-new resource that can be acquired from Daily Quests and the Daily Urn

3. Gemstones level 8

[QUALITY-OF-LIFE IMPROVEMENTS]
1. Throne of Might changes
- The Throne of Might is no longer claimed by the player who is currently holding it at the end of the Initial Reset period; instead, it is claimed by the player who managed to hold the Throne of Might for the longest time during the Initial Reset period
- If two or more players managed to hold the Throne of Might for the same amount of time during the Initial Reset period, the player who reached the time first is considered the winner
- Banish: 5 minutes before the end of the Throne of Might protection period, all players within the Throne of Might region will be Banished, clearing the region for active players

2. Path to Glory progress has been added to the Board sidebar of your screen for ease of access

[HOLIDAY CONTENT]
1. FIGHT THE FRIGHT – multi-stage Halloween event
- Experience a spooky new Halloween story—overcome different solo challenges every day, conquer milestones and contribute towards activating precious buffs for the whole Realm
- Join forces with all the rulers in your Realm to stand a chance against the final Boss stage of the event, where every point of damage dealt matters

2. Join the Halloween Pumpkin Hunt event and make the most jack-o'-lanterns for your Castle

3. Undead Horror: Stop the Merciless Raiders and complete this Halloween-themed Personal Story to obtain great rewards

4. Banish the Headless Knights in the new PvE season—defeat their camps on the World Map to obtain the spoils of war

5. Halloween Event Shop offerings: Complete limited-time events to gain Event Shop currency and use it to obtain the items you desire

6. Halloween Calendar Rewards: Log in every day during the Halloween celebrations and claim your daily gifts

[BALANCE CHANGES AND IMPROVEMENTS]
1. "The Brotherhood's Assault" Alliance Raid will now award one Radiant Key

2. Reduced prices for Equipment Chests at the Exotic Dealer

3. Alliance Encounters will now award more Solo Encounter & Alliance Encounter Tokens across all levels and difficulties; their strength is also reduced for Castle Levels 12-30, again for all difficulties

4. The cost of initiating or joining an Alliance Adventure has been reduced to only 25 Energy, instead of 100

5. Initial Solo Events will now award more Gold, and some of them more Glory Emblems

6. More and better rewards for the initial Personal Events "Muster for Battle" and "Gain Might Challenge"

7. Reduced prices for numerous items in the Event Shop's Special Selection and replaced the Governor's Chest with a Treasure Chest

8. Loading hints updated

Editors’ Notes

An intriguing chapter in the build-and-battle genre, March of Empires makes a grand entrance with striking visuals that showcase impressive details in every farm, barracks, and castle. The gameplay is just as epic, moving steadily from the opening phase of building a home castle to conquering nearby lands and forming alliances with other players to become emperor. As the seasons change, weather impacts your tactics, and your continued success boils down to how shrewdly you approach everything from battle formations to slyly negotiating with other players to overthrow the leader of your alliance.

However, if your looking for a game that you can choose to either wait for your upgrades or pay and progress quickly then you’ve come to the right place. Keep up the good work in developing this game. There are times where I do wish that the packages contained more variety but over all there a great price for what you do get.
