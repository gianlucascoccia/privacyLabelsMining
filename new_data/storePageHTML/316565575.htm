Description

The only (free!) wedding planning app you’ll ever need! Download the Wedding Planner app by WeddingWire—we make planning easy.

From booking your wedding venue to your final wedding dress fitting, we do the hard parts for you. Customizable tools like our Wedding Checklist and Wedding Budget will help you stay on top of every wedding planning task. Plus, browse our Wedding Directory of over 250,000 local vendors to find top-rated wedding professionals in your area.

The Wedding Planner app by WeddingWire comes with the following wedding planning features:

- LOCAL VENDORS: Search through over 250,000 local wedding professionals, get pricing and availability details and read 3+ million vendor reviews written by couples like you. You’ll find wedding photographers, wedding florists, wedding caterers, wedding DJs and more.

- 360º VIRTUAL VENUE TOURS: Save time and make more informed decisions by taking an interactive tour of local wedding venues before you visit.

- FREE WEDDING WEBSITES: Customize your free wedding website, add photos of your relationship and share key day-of details with guests.

- WEDDING CHECKLIST: We’ll create a list of wedding tasks for you based on your wedding date. Your to-do list is completely customizable and accessible from anywhere.

- WEDDING BUDGET: Keep track of how much you’re spending on your venue, wedding dress, flowers, caterer, cake and more! You can even use it to schedule payments.

-WEDDING GUEST LIST: Organize your guest list right from your phone. Track meal selections and RSVPs for all of your wedding guests.

- FREE WEDDING REGISTRY: While you're at it, why not create a wedding registry? Browse special offers and wedding deals from some of the most popular wedding registries out there—and keep it all in one place.

- INSPIRATION: Get inspired by photo galleries of real weddings, wedding dresses, flowers, cakes, invitations, favors, jewelry and so much more wedding inspiration.

- COMMUNITY: Gather ideas and advice from other engaged couples who are also going through the wedding planning process.

- WEDDING COUNTDOWN: Track the time left until your wedding day—just pop in your wedding date and begin counting down!

Download the Wedding Planner app by WeddingWire—the best wedding planning app for staying organized and getting things done.

srjallad , 12/07/2018

Wedding website issues

I really do love wedding wire. They have been such a useful tool for planning my wedding. I love their layout and all the tools they provide. However, the only reason for giving it 3 stars and not 5 is due to the wedding website feature. Unfortunately, they do not make it very easy to navigate. I also feel it does not give the user full capabilities. I accidentally clicked on the “disable function” one time and little to my knowledge that meant deleting the entire website. Support told me they could not re-enable it and that it should have asked me if I was sure I wanted to disable it before making the choice final. It never asked me if I was sure, it automatically did it. It was so sad to learn my hard work and careful planning of over 6 months for my website had gone to waste. I know The Knot gives you a couple of days to reactivate your website if you delete it. If only wedding wire would make it easier to navigate and not as easy to delete your website with the click of one button, then I would have loved to rate it 5 stars. I have heard too many people have issues with their website. My registry on WeddingWire also does not show the correct amount of items I’m registered for. Had I known all this before hand, I would have gone with the Knot for my wedding website specifically. Hope this helps others not make the same mistake as myself!
