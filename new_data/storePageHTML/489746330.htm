Description

Writing can be hard. Werdsmith makes it easier. 

Werdsmith turns your iPhone and iPad into a powerful writing studio that inspires you to write. With features to motivate you and help you break through writer‘s block, you‘ll find yourself writing more than ever.

--Here's how it works.--

1. Capture your ideas
Whenever inspiration strikes, Werdsmith makes it easy to quickly add it to your Ideas List, before it gets lost. Stuck for ideas? Ghostwriter, our AI, writes unique and interesting writing prompts for you.

2. Start your project
When you‘re ready to start your project, move it to your Projects List. From here, choose from one of our four writing modes, including our Novel mode, that automatically formats your novel as you type. This lets you leave the formatting to us, so you can focus on writing.

3. Create a writing ritual
Many writers have a ritual - a time they set aside every day to write. Choose a time that works for you, and Werdsmith will help you build up your daily writing habit by reminding you to write.

4. Set a goal
Add a word goal to your project, and we'll show you your progress as you write. We‘ll even how many words you've written and how long you've spent writing, and you can track your daily stats in our handy widget.

5. Get feedback and publish
When your project is finished, you can share it privately with your editors, publish it publicly with your readers, or export it to 1000s of other apps or your computer for printing or editing.

All your projects stay up to date between iPhone and iPad, automatically backed up and protected. Werdsmith can fit into your existing writing process, or you can treat it like a fresh start. It's up to you.

Over a million writers have entrusted Werdsmith to get their projects finished and become better at their craft. Join us!

---

Things you might want to know:

You can use all of Werdsmith's features for free, but there are limits, for example in the number of projects you can add, the number of writing prompts you can create, or the amount of time you can spend in Novel Mode or using app themes. You can increase or remove these limits by becoming a Werdsmith member. Sync, backup and sharing features are optional, but require a free Werdsmith account.

If you choose to become a member, you’ll be charged through your iTunes account at the end of your one-week free trial period, unless you cancel your membership at least 24 hours before it begins. Your subscription will renew automatically, unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. You can manage, upgrade, downgrade or cancel your subscription in the app's Settings or by going to your App Store account settings. Keep in mind that any unused portion of your free trial period is forfeited if you purchase a membership before the end of the trial. Please read our Privacy Policy at werdsmith.com/privacy and our Terms of Use at werdsmith.com/terms. Feel free to contact us if you need help!

A big thanks to the writers who've supported Werdsmith over the years. We couldn't do it without you.

What's new in Version 5:

Werdsmith has been completely rebuilt from the ground up with new features, faster performance, and our new AI writing technology, Ghostwriter.

Here are our 10 favourite upgrades in Werdsmith 5:

1. WRITING PROMPTS
Starting with a blank page can be daunting. Now, whenever you start a new project, you can ask Ghostwriter to start the story for you. The ideas can be interesting, amusing, or even moving, and they’re a great way to spark inspiration for your next big project, or to practice your writing skills.

2. WRITING SUGGESTIONS
Writer’s block is a thing of the past. Now, whenever you’re stuck, Ghostwriter can finish your sentence for you or write the next one.

Ghostwriter understands your style, plot and characters and suggests ideas relevant to you. It’s like having a collaborator that knows your writing intimately.

3. SEARCH & PROJECTS
The top of the main list now has a search bar, to easily find old projects or ideas. We’ve also moved the projects section to the top of the main screen for even faster access to your current projects.

4. NEW EDITOR
The editor has been completely redesigned to give you the absolute maximum amount of space for your writing. We’ve hidden the status bar while you’re editing, and on iPad, we’ve moved the controls to the keyboard. This gives you up to 16% more space for writing.

We’ve also moved the main projects list to a sidebar on iPad, making it faster to switch projects and putting the focus on your text.

5. NEW FONTS
This version has four beautiful new fonts, one for each writing mode.

6. NEW THEMES
All four themes have been completely recreated and now come in a Light and Dark version.

7. PROFILE PICTURES
You can add your profile picture to the app, and we've redesigned your writer‘s portfolio to be simpler and sleeker.

8. IMPORT/EXPORT TO 1000s OF APPS
You can now import to Werdsmith or export from Werdsmith with any app which supports exporting plain text, for example Notes or Files. This makes it easier than ever to get your projects into Werdsmith for editing, or to send your projects out to your computer or your readers and editors.

9. IMPROVED PERFORMANCE
We’ve supercharged the app’s performance with large projects, to the point where you can edit a 1-million-word novel on a 6-year-old device. We don’t necessarily recommend it though…

10. LOTS OF SMALL IMPROVEMENTS
- Your projects will now automatically resume where you last left off.
- Formatting characters (like * and #) are automatically hidden while you're not editing.
- The larger widget now has an add button to quickly add a new idea.
- You can now set up your studio again by tapping the button in Settings.
- Login is faster and easier.
- Support for large text and VoiceOver have been improved.

NEW MEMBERSHIP
Our new AI is available to all users, but the number of prompts you can generate are limited. We have a new membership option that unlocks more prompts from Ghostwriter. If you're an existing member, nothing changes about your membership, but you can choose to upgrade at a discount. See the app for more information or to sign up.

We love hearing your feedback (good or bad!) so please get in touch. All the best with your writing!

Developer Response ,

Hi there! We believe this version is a big improvement, but totally understand your concern. The major change is that your projects list is now on the top of the main screen for faster access (rather than in the second tab). There's also a search box to find older ideas and projects. If you'd like to be extra safe, you can back up your work before you update - just go to Profile > Settings > Archive > Export. Please keep in mind the custom 4-digit passcode has been removed (it was an unpopular feature that caused some issues) but you can still use Touch ID/Face ID or your device's passcode. All that said, if you'd prefer to stay on the old version, the update is completely optional! Get in touch if you need more help -Nathan
