A thing to remember about this sort of game is that they make them so that people will pay to put as many ads as they can on it. ( which is really annoying )

The reason why I’m giving it 4 instead of 5 stars is because I got really bored really fast and just deleted it. It’s not a game that I would want to play when I’m bored because it’ll make me even more bored. 😐

Could be better

First off I wanna start with the ads. There are WAY too many! Each little level lasts about 10 to 20 seconds, then you get a 20 to 30 second ad. This gets really annoying, so I usually I turn off my Wi-Fi to prevent ads. The second thing I wanna talk about, is that every single level is the same. Then once I try to skip, it doesn’t let me press the skip button. There are some huge bugs in this game. This is usually not a game I would go to if I was bored on my phone. And I do not play often. You could get it if you wanted to, but I honestly wouldn’t say it’s worth it.
