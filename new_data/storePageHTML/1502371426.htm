Description

Toddler World is the #1 learning app for kids from 1-6 years old. With more than 500+ teacher designed learning games, used by millions of toddlers and parents across the world

* #1 play-based preschool learning app in more than 35+ countries *
* Winner of Appnetreviewkids.com Best kids App 2020 *
* Tailored for kids aged 1 to 6 *
* Ad-Free, Kids-Safe, COPA Compliant *

Discover endless learning fun with a complete set if early development learning games. All including counting, language, songs, self-expression and motor skills while engaging kids with captivating animation, music and learning fun.

* Meet the Tap Toy characters *
Kids will travel with Snickysnoo, Bea, Ben and Jade as they fly around the globe, making discoveries as they go - all in one exciting and learning-rich Toddler World.

Kids and toddlers will learn essential early curriculum such as:
• ABCs
• Number Tracing
• Early maths
• Patterns
• Shapes
• Colors

Toddler World offers hours of creative activities, games and open-ended play including
• Music Making, Magic Piano & Musical Instruments
• Sounds of the World
• Baby and Toddler Songs and Nursery Rhymes
• Puzzles & Jigsaws
• Memory Games
• Spot the Difference
• Colour Matching
• A to B Associations
• Shape Match Learning
• Logical Problem Solving
• Focus and Concentration Development

There is great opportunity for kids to gain development skills with Toddler World:
• Kids can enjoy learning about the countries of the world
• Toddlers can learn about social skills and community
• Toddlers will learn the names and sounds of animals and dinosaurs!
• Kindergarteners will enjoy geography as they learn the names of continents and oceans

Toddler World also has tons of creativity enhancing games for preschool kids, including:
• Building things
• Playing Cowboys, Pirates and other role-playing games
• Baking cakes and making milkshakes
• Coloring and drawing

We have also included endless informative, and educational role playing games, great for your child’s character development
- Become an astronaut, make your own space ship,
- Build you own house
- Play handyman and customise your world

Toddler World has also added a special feature for parents; Baby bedtime lullabies. Use this helpful feature to calm and put your child to sleep.

* Offline family focused *
• Everything in Toddler World is already downloaded - No Internet connection needed.
• Great tool for quality learning time during long trips and waits.
• Offline family-centred activities designed to let families join in the fun!

* About Tap Toy *
Tap Toy is an award-winning toddler learning-lab devoted to play. We make learning apps and puzzle games for preschoolers worldwide. Learning that seed imaginations bringing joy to life, for kids, for parents, and for fun!
