Description

Want to meet people you just click with? Have you got aspirational squad goals but lack the squad? Or what about finding the one you call Bae? Omi is THE app connecting like-minded people in a safe, secure way.

IRL it’s not always easy to make great connections but at Omi, we know what it takes. That’s why we designed the app to be a pressure-free meeting platform where you choose what the match means; chat, make friends, date, it’s up to you.

Our simple, intuitive design makes Omi really easy to use:

Explore
The app shows you profiles of people around you based on your preferences. Swipe through and like people you want to connect with.

Connect
If you both like each other, it’s a match and you can start chatting. Remember, it’s pressure free, so this is your opportunity to tell your match what you are looking for.

Chat
Every type of a relationship starts with a conversation, this is the time for real talk. Break the ice with a voice note, express yourself with a unique Omi sticker or write what’s on your mind. Above all, just be yourself!

Playing it Safe
At Omi we’ve created a safe, secure community where people feel comfortable connecting. We are proud of this and it is important to us that we maintain it. This means we have a zero-tolerance policy for bad behaviour. Our anonymous reporting feature lets you report any unwanted behaviour while keeping your identity private.

Omi Premium
Omi is completely free to download and use, but if you want to make the most of your time you can upgrade to our premium experience! This means you get unlimited likes so you can swipe as much as you like and gives you access to see who has liked you, so you can match with them instantly.
