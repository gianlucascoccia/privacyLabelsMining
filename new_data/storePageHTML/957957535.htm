M R B G , 01/05/2018

Very Basic, Not User Friendly, Could Be Better

The reviews that say they have to go through several clicks to get to their school are correct, but that is only the first time. Once you click “Favorite” for that school it does add to the favorites. HOWEVER, you can only see your favorites by clicking out of the whole process!! This is NOT obvious, and really annoying, hence the negative reviews. They need to add a favorite button at the bottom to easily switch between schools.

The other issue is the interface is clunky and only has one view available, and that view is by day. You can’t see the whole month’s menus at a glance. For parents who only let their kids buy lunch a few days a month this is REALLY annoying.

Also, the nutritional information is helpful, but a list of ingredients is needed as well. This would help parents who want or need to avoid some things in their kid’s diets.

The app also only has an interface for a phone format, not a tablet. So the app looks ridiculous on a tablet, think phone image enlarged.
