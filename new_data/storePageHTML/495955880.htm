Description

Beyond Any App of its Kind!®

With 24/7 access to our extensive inventory, the Bed Bath & Beyond app makes it easy to stock up and spruce up your home on the go. Find your favorite furniture and decor brands, pick up all of your household essentials (bedding, bath towels, cookware and more) and save big every time.

Shop faster and smarter with these sanity-saving features:

• Coupon wallet. Organize all of your offers in one easy-to-access spot so you’ll never miss out on savings.

• Easy-to-update gift registry. Use your mobile phone to scan barcodes of in-store items and add them instantly to your registry. Need to give a gift? Shop any Bed Bath & Beyond registry from your device.

• Shop-ahead service. Reserve items right on your phone and pick them up at your convenience at the Bed Bath & Beyond store of your choice.

• Store locator. Need a Bed Bath & Beyond store on the fly? Find the nearest one—whenever you want it, wherever you are. (Map and directions included.)

Bed Bath & Beyond is a member of the Bed Bath & Beyond affiliated companies.

smart spender 101 , 08/15/2021

Love BB&B!

BB&B has been my go to store for all things related to home. I can always find exactly what I am looking for, from cooking and dining utensils, to bed and bath supplies, not to forget the great variety of decorative items, and even self care products such as shampoo, conditioner and other items often found in department stores or pharmacies. Prices are very competitive, and with the purchase of the annual membership - you receive a 20% discount on almost all purchases as well as other perks such as shipping fees being waved for online orders. My only complaint is that seemingly the availability of many products in store has been reduced, often eliminated entirely. I do not like having to order products online without having the opportunity to see the items in store first. Of course they can’t carry everything in store, but I am seeing a lot of empty shelf space in stores, to the point that they often look like they are going to be closed down. Still, it is my favorite, go-to store for so many things! Highly recommend!

Developer Response ,

Thank you for taking the time to share this feedback! We're glad you're enjoying those excellent Beyond+ benefits and we'll be looking toward more availability for our in-store products as much as possible. Best wishes and have a fabulous day!

T. Vinson , 08/26/2021

The perfect store!

Having been a BB&B customer for 40+ years, I’ve bought several homes full of household items. Never once have I been unhappy with their level of service; returns and exchanges have been easy and pleasant as well with the help in finding a particular ‘thing’ whether online or in-store. When they offered the one-time fee to get the automatic 20% off entire purchase for a year, I jumped right on that! No more coupons in my car or purse! It also entitles you to free shipping which is MUCH faster than they say. Their prices are almost always cheaper or at least competitive with discount stores with their quality and selection being far superior. During COVID they couldn’t have been more accommodating and continue to be. An excellent store that still makes shopping FUN!
