Description

Are you ready to start your career as an ACE mech pilot of unstoppable war machines in robot battles you always imagined? Dynamic combat, breathtaking animations, and riveting stories are waiting for you!

- Over 500 animated humanoid mecha with unique designs ready to battle under your command!
- Over 150 gorgeously-drawn characters with distinctive skills, themed skins and stories.
- Customize your battle setup freely and crush your opponents worldwide!
- Explore the massive mecha world and immerse in the heart-pounding stories.
- Free up your hands and collect resources at any time in Expedition system.
- Captivating soundtrack written by the world-renown composer Hiroyuki Sawano.
- Classic mecha design by the anime director and character designer Masami Ōbari.
- Voice actors featuring Maaya Uchida, Rie Kugimiya, Yuichi Nakamura, Yui Ishikawa, Marina Inoue, Saori Hayami and more!
- Greet friends daily to earn bonus rewards, challenge them and join in clans together!


-----PROLOGUE-----
The world was once engulfed in a sea of flames caused by twelve battle mechas, later called the ‘Grand Gods’.
Fast forward centuries, and the war is but a legend forgotten by time – it is a memory that humanity can barely recall. But then, the emergence of "Battle Mechs" suddenly jolts the entire world awake. Forces of all manner and kind conspire in the shadows, all eager to lay their hands on this world-destroying technology – and they will rekindle the fires of war among the world’s armies, mercenaries and bounty hunters to get it.
Meanwhile, ACE pilots from all over the world are scrambling to assemble. The legend of the Battle Mecha begins now! Ace commanders, assemble!

MikoZeroTwo , 01/20/2020

An Amazing Game!

This game starts with a fluid, easy to follow tutorial for new players. The characters talk with you through interactions. Such as when you touch them. At every section of the game, there is music to accompaniment. You can change the music to the one that you like. The characters have a function called Live 2D, so it makes the characters look more realistic.
I wish I could control the mecha during battles with a omni-directional joystick. Since the mech moves by itself, and the only thing you can do is to drag your finger from the mech to a point on the screen to dash and to prioritize targets.
The downside to the game currently is the dialogue during cut-scenes. Where you see characters talking to one another. There isn't much background information that eases you into it's direct story. The characters are fun to play around with, but I wish you can have a conversation with them like in Z-Girls.

PeachyIldi , 10/03/2020

Didn’t think I’d love it but...

Very free to play friendly, although it does take TIME and some grinding. Completing chapters and getting all the stars grant massive in game premium currency and leveling up gives a lot too! Devs are generous and events are very free to play friendly! Battle pass gives the most bang for your buck and helps push you to get those OP banner mechs. DO NOT use pilot gatcha unless it’s the new player one where you’re guaranteed one of your three pilots you pick: recommend Slokai as one of them! Also the SSS vassago unit is very good later on in its plane form and future kai!

Only con is that is can get very addicting and grinding rare mechs and parts can be a pain.

I’d overall recommend this game to anybody and say it’s player friendly though you might want to check out Reddit for mech guides so you don’t feel you’re team is bad early game

Quecojo , 11/05/2020

Good game overall

So been playing for around 4 weeks, and I have so far mostly enjoyed my experiance with the game.

Interesting story, great character and mech designs, good voice/music.

We don't get full control over our mechs in the battles, but we can at least see them move around and carry out there attacks.

F2p for the most part, and while some rewards are decent, what we get from mission to mission is next to nothing in regards to the premium currency or upgrade materials. And the Skins!!!! While very well done, many cost in excess of 1000 of the premium currency... for comparison a 10 pull of rare mechs or characters (in reality i’ve only gotten 2 characters on a 10 pull, the rest were affection gifts for increasing stats) will cost you 2000 of the premium currency. For something that is cosmetic only I feel its a bit high, but just my opinion. Its an optional feature, but one that can get very costly.

Events are all doable f2p, but you will have to grind them. From the 2 events i have seen, each usually lasts a month, so plenty of time to get all the associated rewards.

The game like all f2p is a GRIND when it comes to trying to get the more rare mechs and items, but is fun overall.
