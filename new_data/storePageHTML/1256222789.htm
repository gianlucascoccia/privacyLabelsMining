Description

You can enjoy exclusive discounts and extended free trials of popular apps designed to benefit small businesses with Offers! In addition, we also launched our Community!

Hashtag Expert is the premier destination for small businesses and influencers looking to grow their social media presence. We offer a suite of technical tools combined with deeply useful tutorials and content designed to help your business take off. Growing on social media has never been easy—now, it feels more difficult than ever. Small businesses already have enough on their plate and *that's* where we come in. We analyze market trends, consult leading experts, and run experiments to figure out the absolute best ways to grow. We then turn those insights into automated software tools and polished educational content to make your life easier.


We got started with hashtags and our app is still very much centered around them. We have the #1 hashtag generator in the business that is based on millions of data points from Instagram. That combined with tons of editing tools make Hashtag Expert the best app for planning, using, and analyzing your tags for social platforms. You can see hundreds of thousands of trending groups of hashtags and search/filter by various categories including Dance, Photography, and Cooking.


Membership—Hashtag Expert members get access to our entire content library and all software tools for one affordable price. We're committed to adding more and more tools that make your life easier so managing your social media presence is as seamless as possible.


● Learn - read dozens of exclusive articles that answer social media's toughest questions like should you switch to a creator/business account? When is the best time to post on Instagram?


● Keyboard - install a custom-designed Hashtag Expert keyboard that gives you quick access to our millions of hashtags. With the keyboard, you'll be quicker than ever at coming up with hashtags.


● Community - join our member-only community that has other small business owners just like you. Discuss strategy, brainstorm new ideas, and get feedback on post concepts. You can also talk directly to the Hashtag Expert team.


● When to Post - using data from our integration with the Instagram API, we're able to find out when your followers online and use that information to predict when you can post for the absolute most engagement.


● Captions - tired of struggling to write a caption? Or need some new ideas for what to post about? Our library of thousands of captions solves this problem and makes it easy to come up with amazing, catchy captions for your posts.


● Curated Hashtags - holidays and other big events are some of the most active times on social media, especially for businesses. That's why we prepare curated lists of the top trending hashtags for events like Thanksgiving, New Years, and other holidays.


● Performance - see some of the most in-depth analytics on hashtags out there. We show post counts, predict how well hashtags will perform for you, and let you visualize metrics in beautiful graphs.


● Offers - get access to exclusive discounts and extended free trials for popular apps made for small business owners. You won't find these anywhere else.


For support, you can email us at support@hashtag.expert


Follow us on Instagram @hashtagexpert for tips & tricks
