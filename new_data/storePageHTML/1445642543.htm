Richard C II , 07/28/2021

Underrated

First off, I want to say this is one of the most fun games I have played in a long time. There is no pay-to-win element. You earn a generous amount of in game currency just from playing, and you can only use the money for cosmetic changes to your character. The ads are frequent, but not after every fight like plenty of other games. At least in this game, all of the ads are either actually intriguing or skippable after no more than 10 seconds. Most console games have loading screens 10 times as long!

The combat itself is my favorite thing about the game. It’s not so quick that it encourages button spamming but it is fast enough that skill and timing truly matter. Every hit you give/take feels that it has weight to it. The blocking and damage systems are unique and add another level of challenge. And the way you can trade blows with your enemy while damaging yourself as well makes you think about every move you make. All fighting games could learn a thing or two!
