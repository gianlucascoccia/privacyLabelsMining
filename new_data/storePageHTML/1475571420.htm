Description

Who’s Elmo? You’re Elmo! Who’s Cookie Monster? You are! Play games and sing along with your favorite characters and AS your Sesame Street friends, too! Dress up with furry, funny character filters, masks and dress-up costumes in a kid-safe, Sesame Street-created play environment.

All songs, games, masks and videos are FREE to try for 14 days :)

Then unlock UNLIMITED ACCESS to all songs, stories, videos, and masks within the app with a MONTHLY or YEARLY subscription, see details below.

Everyone can join in the fun, the app comes FREE with six character Masks, two Immersive Games, as well as Dress Ups so you can play and sing as your favorite Sesame Street friend!

*Featured App at Apple's Keynote Event*
*Multiple Award Winner*
*Forbes Kids AR App of the Year*

Fun masks and filters let you dress up as beloved Sesame Street characters: Sing, dance, collect shapes, even nosh like Cookie Monster...and giggle together as you pretend!

Sesame Street masks and games utilize ground-breaking AR technology! This magical AR recognizes facial features and movements so Elmo’s mouth sings along when you do. Cookie eats as you pretend to gobble cookies, and Abby’s bright eyes move when yours do.

Interactivity inspires imaginations and encourages dancing and physical play.

Safe and Ad-Free. No info is collected about you or your child. Even the FREE content is ad-free! Instead of ad sponsorship, this safe environment is made possible with in-app purchase, accessed via a “parent-gate”.

FEATURES

Sing and Play! Become the star of Sesame Street songs when you’re dressed as one of your best-loved characters.

Record the Fun! Press record, make an instant video only you can access, and then watch the fun all over again!

Dress Up Fun! Get creative as you pretend to be Elmo, Cookie and friends – dress up in crazy costumes, hats, glasses and fun moustaches!

Watch the full Sesame Street songs in the video viewer, many with ALL-NEW intros from Elmo!

We’ll be adding new characters, dress-ups and more Sesame Street fun! So check back often!

iPhone X + EXCLUSIVES:
Cookies Monster’s Cookie Caper
Help Cookie Monster collect the ingredients to make his favorite food...Cookies!
Cookie Monster pops into your world via AR – your movements help him collect ingredients for his favorite food!

Multiplayer Games
Bring together as many as three friends and family to wear different masks and play together at the same time!

ABOUT WEYO
Sesame Street has partnered with Weyo to provide positive, immersive experiences that encourage active connections between children, their parents and their favorite Sesame Street characters.

PRIVACY
All images seen through the camera in this app or saved while playing inside stay right on your phone. Nothing is sent to the cloud. We don’t track or store ANY images–videos, scans, or faces. We can’t see them, save them, distribute or share them. Only you can do that.

Weyo is committed to creating a safe and secure place for families. We aim to offer full transparency about any information collected. To learn more about our privacy policy, visit www.weyo.app/privacy Terms - https://www.weyo.app/terms/
