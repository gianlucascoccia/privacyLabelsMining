Description

Just press play. With 73 million tracks, playlists and podcasts plus personalized recommendations just for you, Deezer is your free music companion. Listen to the songs and podcasts you love and discover your new favorites with the music player. Your life deserves its own soundtrack.

Enjoy the best features with the free Deezer music app

• Browse 73 million songs for free
• Identify tracks with SongCatcher
• Find new tracks with Shuffle mode
• Sing along with Lyrics
• Play by genre and moods
• Get personalized recommendations with Flow
• Add songs to your Favorites collection
• Discover and create your own playlists
• Make collaborative playlists
• Choose a time to stop your music with the sleep timer function
• Share your favorite songs or podcasts on social media
• Listen to radio stations plus audio channels, and podcasts

Want more? Go limitless with Deezer Premium, Deezer Family, Deezer Student & Deezer HiFi and enjoy an ad-free, offline listening music player

• No ads
• Offline Mode: download and listen, no wifi needed
• Unlimited skips
• Find favorite artists
• Superior Sound Quality (320 kBps)

lpbennett , 12/21/2020

One Huge Design Flaw

Since the demise of Google Play Music, we’ve been shopping around for a good music player app. Deezer has come highly recommended, but there’s one design flaw that I just can’t get around. I am home full time and like to stream my music to my TV or other player. When it finishes playing my selected album, it starts playing other, random music from the same or similar artists. Even if I set the album to repeat, it STILL plays other music and albums. I want it to stop playing all together over choosing music for me. If I want a radio station inspired by a specific artist, I’ll use Pandora. Because I am currently using a variety of players right now, I’ve also noticed Deezer is less intuitive than other platforms at finding music based on my search credentials. For example, if I want to listen to Christmas music from a certain artist, it will only bring up albums that specifically have “Christmas” in the album title, not all of their holiday music. Just today I wanted to listen to an album called “Christmastime” by Michael W Smith. I typed “Michael W Smith Christmas” in the search field and it wouldn’t bring up the album 🤷🏻‍♀️ I had to go through his albums to find it.
