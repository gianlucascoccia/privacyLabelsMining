Description

Start an annual subscription & unlock every issue published in the last year for FREE! Limited time offer.

Today’s Quilter is the NEW magazine with a fresh take on traditional quilting, on sale every 4 weeks. With projects, features, techniques and news, each issue offers intermediate to experienced quilters 100 pages of beautiful, colourful and inspirational pages of everything they love.

At the heart of Today’s Quilter are projects, created by the most well-known and talented designers. Exquisitely photographed quilts start inspired creativity, while expert technical features ensure quilters are always improving their skills for stunning results.

Hours of reading pleasure in the form of the latest news from the world of quilting, events and shows to visit, in-depth designer profiles, explorations of the heritage behind the quilts, plus lots of chat and fun from the quilting community!
