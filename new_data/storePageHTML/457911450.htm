Description

The WellSpan app provides quick access to the WellSpan Health network of physicians, hospitals, urgent care and specialty centers serving South Central Pennsylvania and Northern Maryland.

Use our app to:
• Access your MyWellSpan account to see your health record, schedule an appointment, message with your physicians or pay a bill
• See the urgent care locations closest to you, the number of patients currently waiting and “Reserve My Spot”
• Find a WellSpan primary care physician, specialty physician or care facility
• Upload health and fitness data, including data from the Apple Health app, to your MyWellSpan account when enrolled in a self-tracking program
