Description

WANELO (Want, Need, Love) is a marketplace connecting you to stores with the best deals on over 20 million fashion, beauty and home products.

1. Save big! Get 60-90% off shoes, accessories, dresses, tops, swimwear, home decor and more!

2. Buy with trust! Millions of products have reviews with photos posted by REAL shoppers. Every purchase is always secure and backed by The Wanelo Guarantee.

3. Every day, Wanelo will show you new products, personalized just for you. As you save products you like, Wanelo learns your style. The more you use it, the better it gets!

4. Use Wanelo to browse across categories and search for items from 17,000 different stores.

5. Our loyal shoppers get special promos weekly!

P.S. New shoppers get $5 off their first purchase, and you can earn money when you invite your friends to shop with you!

Ease of shopping

I must say that I am relatively new to shopping replicas. But I got the bug! This site, WANELO, has been the easiest to navigate so far. The pictures are plentiful and the description is detailed. I have not yet received any items, but I am looking forward to seeing how the item and the pictures match. I believe that my first impression has been five-star!!
When navigating through the other sites, they either don’t have a quick picture of your product in detail, or it takes you a few tries to get the piece that you are specifically looking for, or it takes you to totally different piece merchandise. On this site, it groups like-items together which eases honing in on the actual piece that you are looking for. Because, let’s face it, when we shop, we already have something in mind.
I will write another review once I receive my merchandise (LV Tri-fold card holder with a rose-pink button and pink interior). I cannot wait! Talk to you soon, LizLev!!
