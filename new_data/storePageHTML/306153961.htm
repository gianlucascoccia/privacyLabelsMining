Persian Calendar Pro able to access all events of any selected month that you have in your Calendar application on the iPhone/iPod/iPad. Persian Calendar Pro is the ONLY Persian Calendar able to fully interact with your iDevice Calendar default application including adding new events to your calendar.

Apple provides the tools to enable the development of the most powerful Persian Calendar for the iPhone, iPod and iPad! Even in the computer world, you can’t find a Persian Calendar as powerful!

The most important function with any calendar is to display the CORRECT date. Many similar calendar shows bank holidays and special dates are based on the Hijri (Islamic) calendar. Persian Calendar Pro is the only Persian Calendar application that accurately converts all holiday dates from the Hijri calendar.
