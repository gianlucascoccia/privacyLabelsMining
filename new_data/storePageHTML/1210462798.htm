Description

Passport America is the Original 50% Discount Camping Club since 1992. Members, fellow Campers, and RV Enthusiasts alike can hit the road with confidence with our Redesigned, ALL NEW and Improved My Passport America app! Our FREE interactive app allows you to quickly find over 1,300+ affiliated Passport America campgrounds and RV Parks by City, State and Country. Now, finding nearby campgrounds and RV Parks is easier than ever using our "Nearby Parks" feature, based on your devices current location.

Additionally, these NEW and Improved features will make your experience easier and simpler:
- Easy to use Campground Search Features and Mapping
- Mark your Favorite or Visited Campgrounds, and see those on a map as color-coded pins.
- Save your Favorite Parks, Visited Parks, Park Notes, and Trip Routes across all your devices – save them on your smart phone, then view them later on your tablet or desktop.
- Map Navigation and Directions
- Get Directions directly to a Campground using the Navigation app of your choice.
- Search for Campgrounds and RV Parks based on Name, Address, City, or State and include selected amenities, such as amperage, dump station, full hookups, pull thrus, wifi and many more.
- Trip Routing will allow you to route your trip and show all Passport America affiliated Campgrounds/RVParks within a distance that you specify. You can even save your Trip Routes and view them later.
- Plus, Much, Much MORE!

Our mobile app is available for FREE to everyone who would like to explore our participating campgrounds. However, only Passport America members Save 50% on campsites at over 1,300+ participating campgrounds all across North America.

Our members are our #1 priority and we want to hear your experiences with our new My Passport America App. Please send all comments, questions, suggestions and concerns about the App to apps@passportamerica.net.

Wishing you safe and happy travels.
