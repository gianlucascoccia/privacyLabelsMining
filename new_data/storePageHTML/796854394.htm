Description

EASILY PLAN YOUR DAILY DRIVE WITH REAL-TIME TRAFFIC


Choose the best route to take before you get in the car. Set up your personal traffic checker for any delays on your commute and see where traffic is right now. Optionally share your destination with a TomTom GPS device* for navigation through traffic.

BENEFITS:
>> Know before you go: We're passionate about traffic so you don't need to be. Get highly accurate traffic information, in real time.
>> Set your personal traffic checker: Want to know about traffic on your commute? Get reports before you go in case of delays on the way.
>> Set your destination and sync it with your GPS*: Set where you're going using phone contacts, saved favorites, a tap on the map, or just type it in. Then send it to your GPS device* and it will guide you through traffic.
>> Make your map personal: Easily add and manage your home, work and other favorite destinations.

GOOD TO KNOW:
-Please note that this app will not update your GPS device software. Instead, you can download the latest map or software for your GPS device by visiting tomtom.com/getstarted from your computer.
-You can get TomTom's world-class, turn-by-turn navigation by downloading our navigation app: TomTom GPS Navigation Traffic.

*Supported TomTom models include:
TomTom VIA 52 / 53 / 62
TomTom GO 40 / 50 / 60 / 51 / 61 / 400 / 500 / 510 / 520 / 600 / 610 / 620 / 5000 / 5100 / 6000 / 6100 / 6200
TomTom Rider 40 / 41 / 42 / 400 / 410 / 420 / 450 / 500 / 550
TomTom GO Professional 520 / 620 / 6200 / 6250
TomTom Trucker 500 / 5000 / 6000
Your TomTom device needs to be connected to the Internet to exchange information with the MyDrive app.

Great device, obnoxious app

I love my new go 40s device, and like the traffic feature through my iPhone, but each and every time I start my car, the app asks if it can connect. You get 2 choices. Ignore, but I presume I won't get traffic. The other selection is connect, which launches this app. I just want to connect, but no launching of the app. There's no setting that I see to do that. Oh, if you don't select either, and just drive, you get connected. Okay, I could just always never do anything, but I'm usually wanting to do something - one last SMS - before I start to drive.

2018 November update:
I use my Tomtom all of the time, but have given up getting real traffic updates or sending my destination from my iPhone because it never works. It is still amazing to me that when I start my car, the app breaks into whatever I am doing to ask if I want to connect. I want it to **always** connect without fail, but there’s no setting anywhere that says always connect. My old radio-based-traffic Tomtom never asked, and somehow it figured out that there was traffic ahead. As for transferring my address from my iPhone, my Tomtom address entering interface is so slow and nonsensical (It should guess that I’m looking for an address at least in a 50 or 100 km radius instead of suggesting the first item in Colorado when I’m in Los Angeles) that it makes the lack of being able to send an address from my iPhone all the more painful.
