Description

Search for ships all over the world and enjoy real-time live AIS positions with great coverage at your fingertips!

FleetMon is the companion app of our vessel tracking website that lets you look up ships and gives you instant access to the extensive vessel information of FleetMon, the open ship database.

A FleetMon Open account is recommended to make best use of FleetMon. You can register on our website. It is free!

The list of your favorite vessels will then be synchronized with your "My Fleet" on our website. This way the app makes it easy to stay up-to-date about your fleet, even when you are underway.

Search for your specific vessel of interest and store it into your "My Fleet" for alerting and monitoring. Inspect name, type, speed, draught, deadweight and destination of searched vessels, their schedule and recent port calls.

Our ship and maritime focused maps with ferry lines, berths, windparks, industrial landuse (terminals, etc) and more, help you understand while inspecting various situations.

* NOTES:
If you have problems with the app, please send us an e-mail to support@fleetmon.com. Thanks!

Visibility of vessels in the App depends on AIS signal availability. Subscribers of our "FleetMon Unlimited Sat" plans are able to locate (almost) any active vessel in the world through satellite AIS, while standard plans offer near-shore coverage only. See our website for details. Completeness and accuracy of the information provided cannot be guaranteed.
