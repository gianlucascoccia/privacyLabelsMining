Description

By using this app * Smart Receipt App * there would be no worries if you have lose or destroyed your paper receipts. You will able to keep all receipts and documents on your mobile phone.
you can easily move your data to another iOS device.

Current abilities:
- Define custom categories. (active & inactive category)
- Choose category for each receipt.
- Save Receipts in your mobile.
- Take photo/Scan receipt papers.
- Attach related pictures (4 images) to each receipt.
- Generate report and send to Email, as CSV file to use in
Excel & Text.
- Auto Tax calculation by setup tax percent in application setting.
- Edit & update receipts .
- Show list of stored receipts, filter and search
- Select standard currencies.
- Show monthly & yearly view of receipts
