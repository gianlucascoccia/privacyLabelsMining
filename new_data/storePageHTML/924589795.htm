Description

{<3} , 02/18/2019

People are wrong

I’ve played highrise for years now so I know a bit about how it works. There’s a lot of reviews that say you need to spend money in order to get “good” items. That’s not true. I had good items and friends before I started to spend a little money on the game. You can join crews and get items to sell that way. There’s plenty of non-eventing or crews intended to help out new users. There are some people who aren’t nice on the game, but if you stay away from people like them, then you won’t have a problem.
There’s so many fun things to do in highrise. You can buy and sell cute items and clothes. There is also weekly eventing for you to participate in to earn ranks and rewards. Joining a crew is also a really good way to make friends. A crew is a group of people that participate in events together and get to know each other in the crew chat. Some crews have requirements for the events (this just means how many tickets you need to get in an event) but there’s so many non-eventing crews to join when you are first starting out.
When you first start out, don’t ask people for donations or free items. There’s so many ways to earn items to sell that you don’t need to ask people for free stuff. There’s giveaways you can join and possibly even win. If you want to get on someone’s bad side, ask for free items. That will surely do it.
