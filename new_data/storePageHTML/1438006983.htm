Description

Step is a mobile banking experience built for Gen Z. It gives families and teens the tools to manage their money and become financially independent at an earlier age. Sign up for your first bank account and Step Visa Card in minutes.

With Step, you can shop with your personalized Step Visa Card, get paid up to 2 days early, and send and receive money with family and friends instantly - all without paying any hidden banking fees.

Why we’re great for you:
- Learn good financial habits by tracking your spending and balance
- Never pay monthly fees or typical banking fees
- Take Step anywhere with you with Apple Pay
- Customize your Visa Step Card
- Send and receive money instantly
- Get paid up to 2 days early with direct deposit
- Earn money for referrals and growing the #StepFam
- Keep your money secure with an FDIC insured bank account
- Shop online instantly with your Step Virtual Card

Why we’re great for parents:
- Send money to your teens instantly in case of an emergency
- Automate your teen’s allowance, skip or adjust at anytime
- Monitor your teen’s spending
- Freeze your teen’s card in case it gets lost
- Start teaching your teen financial literacy
