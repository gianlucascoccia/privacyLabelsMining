Description

ModeSens is the premier luxury fashion shopping assistant. Explore, search and find millions of the latest designer products in all one place.

ModeSens is your free, digital shopping assistant — helping you shop from 12,000+ premium brands and 400+ store partners like Gucci, Fendi, Net-A-Porter, Farfetch, Nordstrom, Saks Fifth Avenue, Burberry, Louis Vuitton, Balenciaga, Dior, and many more.

ModeSens offers full designer collections for men, women, kids and home — with powerful filter capabilities, link search, barcode scanning, and informative product pages. Our smart shopping tools make it easy to compare price and availability, set sale alerts, curate collections and so much more. Real-time updates allow you to never miss an item in your size at the right price!

Join our community to share your favorite pieces and best looks, while also finding inspiration from style lovers like you.
