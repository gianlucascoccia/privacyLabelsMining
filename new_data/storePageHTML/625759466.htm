Description

The world's best cycling map lets you navigate and track your rides and inspires you with over 8 million routes worldwide.

Everything a cyclist needs in an app—that's Bikemap. Map, track and navigate your rides with ease. Harness the power of our active cycling community by accessing our user-generated cycle route collection with millions of routes and trails worldwide.

DISCOVER OUR EXTENSIVE CYCLE ROUTE COLLECTION
Get to know the most popular cycling routes and MTB trails near you and beyond. Discover millions of bike routes in over 100 countries. It doesn't matter if you ride a road bike, e-bike, mountain bike, or city bike. With our extensive route library (+8M routes), you'll find a suitable path no matter your bicycle of choice. Our collection keeps growing thanks to our users who continuously plan, track, and add new routes.

FIND THE PERFECT ROUTE TAILORED TO YOU
Search for your destination. Filter by route length, ascent, bike type, and surface. Sort by popularity, relevance, length, or ascent. Then browse the results to find the perfect ride that meets your needs and wants—your next cycling adventure is about to begin. Not finding what you're looking for? Plan your own route from scratch and share it with our community of cyclists and mountain bikers. You can also plan routes on our website and have them effortlessly sync with the app.

NAVIGATE SAFELY WHILE RIDING YOUR BIKE
Navigate confidently from A to B with stopovers. Our intelligent GPS navigation system leaves the choice up to you: take the fastest route or prioritize bike paths and lanes. Turn on voice navigation to get spoken turn-by-turn directions without having to look at your screen. Don't worry if you take a wrong turn—the Bikemap navigation will safely bring you back on track.

EXPLORE OUR DETAILED CYCLE MAP
Find essential points of interest (POIs) on your rides, such as public restrooms, bicycle parking, water stops, repair shops, bike rentals, and e-bike charging stations. Avoid any unpleasant surprises like construction, potholes, and slippery roads by checking out our community reports. Is something still missing? Help out fellow cyclists by reporting both hazards and useful infrastructure encountered along your route. Build a top-notch cycle map with us to make cycling safe and hassle-free.

TRACK YOUR RIDE
Turn your iPhone, iPad, or Apple Watch into a bike computer. While riding, you'll see real-time ride stats alongside your navigation—current speed, distance, duration, elevation, and the ETA. The ride stats are saved to your user profile to let you track your bicycling activity over time. Bikemap also supports HealthKit.

GET MORE WITH BIKEMAP PREMIUM
• Offline Maps & Navigation: Download maps and routes to ride offline anywhere in the world.
• Bike-Type Optimized Routing: Optimize the routing for road bikes or mountain bikes.
• Advanced Cycling Maps: 3D, Night mode, OpenCycleMap, OpenStreetMap.
• 3D Route Preview: View your route projected in 3D, including its height profile.
• Fall Detection: Ride your bike safer—alert others if you fall off your bicycle.
• Premium support: We have your back if you encounter any problems while using Bikemap.
