Description

Record video, pause and continue recording whenever you want - when you ready, edit and save your recordings as one complete video to your camera roll !

FEATURES:
- PAUSE and RESUME your recording,
- add TIMESTAMP to recordings,
- add TEXT to your videos,
- add PHOTOS / IMAGES to your recordings,
- add animated content (GIF, stickers, emoji, texts) to video,
- record as many video segments as you want,
- IMPORT videos from your device,
- add FILTERS to your videos,
- create animated GIF from your video,
- Anonymous Faces Filter - finds faces in video and pixellates them so they can’t be recognized,
- CHANGE ORDER of your movie segments,
- DELETE any video segment before saving,
- TRIM video segments duration,
- ROTATE video segments,
- export all segments or just selected ones, as one video to camera roll,
- SHARE recordings using Facebook, YouTube, Instagram etc. directly from app,
- zoom in/out with pinch gesture or slider while recording,
- change camera to back/front while recording.
