Description

Athan is the most favorite Islamic App to get accurate prayer times, read the Holy Quran, Qibla Finder to find accurate qibla direction, find nearby mosques, Hijri Calendar to track Islamic events, Dua & Azkar, achieve prayer discipline and stay organized in their busy life.

Now you can keep track of your prayer performance by logging them in the PrayerBook in a fun and easy way.

Trusted by Millions of Muslims worldwide, Athan by IslamicFinder is a platform that provides useful Islamic features at one place.

Features:
-Prayer times of your location with adjustable prayer alerts.
-Finder of nearby mosques, organizations, schools and businesses, along with their contact information and directions
- Integrated Qibla direction for thousands of cities worldwide
-Ability to track your prayers by logging them in your own prayer book
-Hear the Athan at every prayer time
-A widget on the home screen allows you to keep track of your prayers on the go!
-View the remaining time for each prayer and see the prayer timings for the entire day.
-Track your prayer progress with detailed graphs and timelines.
-Islamic Calendar; to notify you of all upcoming Islamic events (includes both Hijri and Gregorian dates)
-All new Hijri-Gregorian date converter.
-Quran with 45+ translations

In-App purchases: Now enjoy a smooth Ad-free experience & SEVEN new Athan voices from all around the globe.

fareehasadiq , 04/07/2021

Prayer tracker glitches and issues for sisters

Salam! This app is overall amazing. I love the features. Features such as the prayer tracker, gallery, Quran, community and qibla. These are so helpful and useful and are just spectacular. But there are a few issues with the prayer tracker. It works amazing and it pleasing to the eye but there are some problems and inconveniences. For starters, the punctuality badges do not work. When you go to the prayer tracker for the day and scroll down, you can see it always deletes the data from 5 days before. So it never shows a full week, even if you had been praying, because the last 2-3 days are always being deleted. The punctuality badges aren’t unlocked because of this. Once you fix this, there is still a major problem for the sisters. As you know, we are excused from praying for a week every a month. That means most of us never pray more than 3 weeks in a row. We can never get to or past the one month badge. There should be a way we can mark that we are excused. Or maybe a way for a period tracking app, such as Flo or Clue, to be connected to this app. The badges are just badges and are not that important but they are still helpful and motivational. Please consider these fixes. Thank you so much for making this app which has assisted many people, including myself, onto the right path. JazakAllah Khair, may Allah SWT bless us all.
