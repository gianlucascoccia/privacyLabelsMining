Description

- Designed for children with special learning needs
- including 100+ curated learning videos and games
- Created by kids experts to develop multiple skills for life

The subscription offers curated EDUCATIONAL VIDEOS, LEARNING GAMES, and BOOKS that promote socio-emotional, life, academic, and cognitive skills for children with special learning needs and their parents, carefully built on visually engaging tools and an independent learning process.


Why iCan ™?
- Focus on the education on special learning needs
- 100% Ad-free and child-safe design
- Facilitate communication, basic writing & math learning process
- Develop multiple intelligences: social stories, cognitive games, creative activities
- Created by top developmental experts
- Fun learning with educational Videos, learning games. books and worksheets!
- Empower children in the spectrum to practice daily routines and independent life skills
- Grown-up area: for parents and caregivers

So Parents, what's the Educational impact of iCan?
- social skills
- speech and language skills
- cognitive skills
- life skills
- self-care skills
- mathematical reasoning skills
- writing foundation skills

iCan was specially designed for children with special learning differences and needs, from verbal to nonverbal, diagnosed with Attention-Deficit / Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD); Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD); Cerebral Palsy; Fragile X syndrome; Kernicterus; Language and Speech Disorders; Intellectual disabilities; Developmental delays; Chromosome 8 deletion; Aphasia; Down Syndrome; Sensory Processing Disorder, and other disabilities and diagnosis.
