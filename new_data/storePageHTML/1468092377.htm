Description

Keep track of your work hours with this easy time tracking tool!

Track your work hours with Hours Tracker! Enter your start and end time each day, and calculate your total work hours and pay. At any time, you can export your time sheet as an Excel or CSV file. Weekly time tracking has never been this easy!

• Pick any time to start and end the daily entry
• Deduct time for breaks
• Get an overview of daily work time
• Calculate total weekly work time
• Enter your hourly salary and calculate total pay
• See work and pay history of previous weeks
• Export time sheets as Excel or CSV files
• Set log reminders for start and end times
• Use your local currency

And it is all for free!

ChristieKay143 , 09/13/2021

Perfect for what I was looking for

I hire babysitters, yard people, random help from time to time and this app has helped me keep track of the hours. What I like the most is the reminder to add hours. For example… Our babysitter comes every day for approximately 4 to 5 hours. This app reminds me 15 minutes after she gets here and a couple of hours after she leaves. (I set the reminder times) I’m able to update her time on this app and it works out really well. At the end of the week it tells me how much I owe her and how many hours she worked (all info I enter). I don’t think it could get much better than this…. unless you were to write it on paper and then you don’t have the reminder.
The only downfall I could see would be if you wanted to add Taxes… I don’t see a spot for that but that’s not something I need. So I’m 5 star happy.

JayKing0422 , 07/11/2021

Overtime feature?

I have only been using the app for a couple weeks now, and so far I do like the simplicity. I have a couple suggestions I’d like to make however. First and foremost, would be nice to be able to add an overtime rate for more than 40 hours. (This has been mentioned by others too, I know). Additionally tho, might want to think about having a setting for OT to be calculated either after 40 hours for the week, or after 8 for the day as some companies are that way. Have a setting to be able to select either time and half or double time.

Secondly, it would also be great to be able to select your break times that will automatically be entered for each day instead of having to manually add them per entry. For instance, most get either a half hour or hour for lunch, so to be able to set that accordingly as a default would be nice.

Aside from that, so far app is working as expected based on the current available features. Just some minor improvements would be greatly appreciated. Can’t really complain too much for it being a free app, and also ad free to boot. I would gladly even be okay with paying a small fee for the additional features to be added.

Jimwymr , 03/04/2021

A nice start but...

I need to be able to count for my time in multiple chunks of time. If I work for a few hours at one project and then lunch and then a few hours at another project, then possibly dinner, and then a few more hours at yet a third project, I would like to be able to account for these hours independently. I would also like a small narrative for each of these locations so that I can describe what I did at those places. So far this app is always at least half wrong. Either the hours are incorrect because it does not account for breaks, or the total number of hours is incorrect because it does not account for breaks. An example; 9 to 5 is not 8 hours. 9 to 5 is 7 1/2 hours because there was a half an hour in there for lunch. Fix the app please.
