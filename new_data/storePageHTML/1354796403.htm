Rival update

I see the complaints out there that seems to center on unfairness in elements like the wind or club performance. I don’t play video games. None. Obviously this is the first. So for me personally, it’s up there with a baseball game that I played in the 60’s at the Ocean City, NJ boards. It’s great really. The graphics and such are done well. In regards to the unfairness, (which is usually felt if you’re losing), I believe that it levels off in both directions. Sure, there are times when I shake my head but often it’s me not the app. Each club has different characteristics that are meant to give us a choice as to what to play.

In summary, I thinks that the game is terrific. You just can’t take it personally. It’s a game, that’s it. So if your attitude is in the sense of a defeatist, you’ll lose more than win. Don’t worry about other players. You take it to the basket and dunk the crap out of it. They’ll do what they do and you need to play your own game, just like the pro’s do. I’ve come across some truly great players that I’ve watched carefully so I can learn. A few of them(5 to 6), really stand out and are most cool to watch. It’s a fun game folks. Don’t take it so seriously or else you’ll be frustrated and want to quit which may be best for you.
