Description

Turn your memories into movies with music. The app helps you to create beautiful SlideShow from your photos. It's completely FREE.

Simple steps:

• Pick your photos
• Organize them in the right order (Long tap a photo and drag to reorder it)
• Select a transition to animate between photos
• Choose your favorite music from our gallery or your own
songs.
• Select the slideshow's length to change the duration per photo.
• Share it on Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat, YouTube or save it to your camera roll.

So, let's make your memories even more amazing

It’s better than most

I gave this app 4 stars because it’s better than most slideshow apps I’ve looked at because almost every app makes you to pay to use it or pay to do more than 10 pictures. This app is good because it allows you to put however many pictures you want and what music you want (as long as you have it already) a couple downsides is it doesn’t allow you to choose exactly how long each slide will be it only allows you to chooses the duration of the whole video. One thing I would like it to have was the ability to choose different transitions and for the pictures to be up longer.
