Description

Connect with God daily with guided meditation and prayer. Find bible study plans that work for you, develop spiritual routines, and deepen your relationship with God.

Glorify is your #1 Christian Daily Worship & Wellbeing App. Join a community of over 1 million Christians connecting with God on a daily basis through Christian Meditation, Bible Stories, and Prayer.



‘At a time when making space for the sacred practice of personal devotion is both increasingly difficult and an absolute necessity to maintaining a healthy soul; Glorify is a Godsend, in every way.’ - Joel Houston, founding partner at Glorify & lead singer at Hillsong United



Calm your mind with Christian meditation and devotionals, designed to inspire tranquility and improve the quality of your sleep.
Prayer and devotional topics include:
- Calm and Tranquility
- Better Sleep
- Growing Confidence in God
- Unlocking Inner Peace
- Overcoming Anxiety
- Healing from Grief
- Parenting
- Marriage
- And much more!



Explore the Bible from Genesis through to Revelation with a personal bible journal designed to encourage reflection and learning, in turn enhancing your spiritual journey.
- Bible Stories for Kids
- ESV Bible, NASB Bible, NIV Bible
- Bible Journal
 
Develop structure for your daily worship with handpicked Bible verses, devotionals, and Christian meditation. Create space easily every day for reflection with music and prayers.
- Daily Devotionals
- Christian Meditation and Wellbeing
- Verse of The Day
- Prayer Plans


Connect with God on a daily basis through daily worship. Grow in faith and love through the word of God, whilst advancing your bible study with journaling. Calm your mind and achieve better sleep with Christian Meditation and Prayer. Install Glorify and start your journey today!

lovebuglarry , 11/16/2020

Amazing

This is such a wonderful app with so many resources and guided devotionals and even reflections. It really helps break down the word of God and most importantly helps us to apply it to our everyday lives. There are however a few things that I think need improvement.

There should be a way to save and go back to each days daily worship. That way If you wanted you can go back and reflect upon them or if you missed a day of worship you can go back on the calendar, click a day, and it’ll have the scripture, devotion, and reflection for that day. I also think it would good to have an unlimited amount of what a person can type into the prayer section of the app.

Thank you to the creators of this app it is such a blessing and such a useful resource for believers and new believers to build up a habit of getting into fellowship with the lord. The listening portion of the app is wonderful too with its many guided meditations. Thank you, this app is amazing!

Developer Response ,

Thanks very much for taking the time to leave this review. We are thrilled to hear that you are enjoying the Glorify app!

We greatly value your input and will discuss your suggestion regarding the space in our prayer tab within our team as we are always looking to improve our app experience. Currently it's not possible to revert to previous daily worship days but we are working on introducing this feature very soon!

Stay tuned for improved and new features coming your way with our upcoming app releases!

God Bless,
The Glorify Team

Best Hallmark Pod , 12/23/2020

Life-changing beautiful app

I have never been one who has kept up with regular daily devotions from one single place until this app came along. I told my brother about it because I was just blown away with how grounded the lessons are and how easy they are to understand and follow along. Because of this app my brother and I have grown much closer in our spiritual walk and we’ve even grown closer as brother and sister. We have daily text conversations because of this app. One of my favorite things about this app is that you can listen to everything and you don’t even have to read it. While I sometimes may not agree with absolutely every theological aspect that is presented, the most important things that are presented—the tenets of faith—are right on Scripturally. I highly recommend this app to anyone who is looking for a way to be more intentional with their devotional life. It will revolutionize your daily quiet time with God.

MaleahBean , 02/04/2021

This app is amazing!

I love this app! As a college student it’s often hard to set aside time for reading and reflecting on the word, because so often I only remember all the assignments I have to do. I love this app because not only can you read the scriptures but you can listen to it too! I also really love the reflection and meditations that you can do after you read. This really gives me time to reflect and pray on the words I just heard and not just to rush through the reading.
The music that they have for rest is also really great! They don’t have as much of a selection for that which is a bummer, but I really love that feature!
I recommend this if you are struggling to set aside time to read the word, or if you are wanting something that can help guide your reflection and meditations of the word. 😊
