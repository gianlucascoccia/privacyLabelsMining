Description

Discover and save the best recommendations from your friends and favourite content creators: your next favourite movie and tv show, the perfect gift for your friend, or the community preferred trendy restaurant in town. What's more, you can also recommend everything you love!

How does it work?

● Discover the best Recommendations curated for you every day: whether you are looking for a good restaurant or your favourite Influencer beauty routine, you will find it here.

● Search and find what you need: Recommendations around you, TOP recommended things every week or the best in every category.

● Save and organise everything in Collections: what's better than having everything well organised? Create private or public Collections to share with your friends.

● Recommend. What movie did you find mind-blowing? What book left you with an existential void when you finished it? Recommend everything that you love and have everyone discover your good taste.


Peoople improves with you every day, if you want to become our particular muse, you can send your idea to hey@peoople.app
