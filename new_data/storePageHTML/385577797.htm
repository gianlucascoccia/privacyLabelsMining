Description

Official Mobile Guide to Alaska's Best Places
Powered by Alaska.org, The Alaska App is your ultimate interactive Alaskan discovery guide -- whether you’re planning your first trip to Alaska or live here!

Plan Your Trip
• Expert Advice: When is the best time to visit Alaska? What are the must sees? How many days do you need for your trip? What do you do if you encounter a bear or moose, and much more!
• Guides: The best Alaskan hotels, cruises, tours, attractions, restaurants, shopping, and nightlife
• Trip Planning Help: Request free itinerary recommendations from local Alaska experts

Like a Local
• Hidden Gems: The best glaciers, mountains, parks, trails, scenic viewpoints, ghost towns, and wildlife-viewing spots only locals know about
• Outdoor Recreation: A comprehensive list of Alaska’s public use cabins, RV parks, campgrounds, and trails
• Dining: Best restaurants, pubs, breweries, and coffee shops
• Local Knowledge: Listen to bush pilots, park rangers, and wilderness guides tell you about their favorite places in our audio guides
• Videos: 100+ videos, produced by us, showcasing Alaska’s top destinations and tours

Updated Review

I was disappointed a few months back when all the weather data was stripped from the app. I wrote the developer and after several exchanges, a promise was made to reinstate those features.

This latest update has done that. Weather, sunrise/sunset times, aurora forecast and web cams are back!

There’s more that can be added and I’m holding off on a five-star hoping that other features are coming.

GREAT informational app!

Loved loved loved this app. We traveled around the Kenai Peninsula and up to Denali and always found great stuff using this app. It has all sorts of great recommendations and we learned a lot about Alaska. Also great for my kids to listen and learn about flaura, fauna, history and sites we were visiting. Highly recommend to anyone traveling (or living) in Alaska.
