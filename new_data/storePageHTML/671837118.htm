Jumpy, freezes & can’t force quit

This used to be a great app to get headlines worldwide. It had the fastest Push Notifications. Now it’s extremely frustrating to use and I no longer get Push Notifications.
The barrage of ads make the app jumpy. It freezes or scrolls backwards. Ads load, pictures may load. The story may be there for a second and then vanishes. Comments take to long to load if the do. The longer you’re on the app the worse it gets until it freezes completely.
You need to cut back on the ads to make the app functional. As it is they use up all the bandwidth so there’s nothing left for the articles. It’s been updated several times and seems to get worse with each update. Please fix it or remove it until it’s usable. Right now it’s complete trash.
