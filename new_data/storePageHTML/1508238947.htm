Description

Livestream your wedding, baby shower, or other celebrations with Lovecast. You can schedule your livestream, invite remote guests, designate a co-host to handle your livestream, and instantly view and download your wedding video playback. Now you can bring all your family and friends to your big day, even when they can’t be there physically. Treat them with 1080p streaming and fun features such as virtual rice toss, and a digital photo guestbook.

Perfect for marriage proposals, weddings, elopements, baby showers, graduation ceremonies, and other special events.

Why Lovecast?

* Easy workflow at your event site
Just press the “Start Live” button. No camera rental, special crew, or complex setup is needed.

* Watching is instant and easy
Your guests can watch the exciting moments live on your website on any device with a single click—no need to ask them to download any app.

* Amazing virtual event experience
During your livestream, your guests can join the live chat, throw virtual rice, and leave a photo guestbook anytime on your beautiful website. Impress them with your own event website as opposed to a plain Zoom meeting.

* Photo guestbook
Your guests can post a photo guestbook on your event page, and you can download all their well wishes into a printable guest book as a keepsake.

* Your event is livestreamed and saved with the best quality
Lovecast uses the highest quality audio and video encoding (1080P) to ensure your event is captured at the best quality. In comparison, Zoom only does 360P for its video quality.

* HD video recording
No need to wait for months for the videographer to get back to you. Watch your live stream HD recording anytime after the event.

* Manage live stream logistics ahead of time
Lovecast lets you designate a co-host to help manage the event & perform the livestream.

shark8me2 , 11/25/2020

Great stream quality and an amazing service!

I just used the app for my wedding on November 22nd, and it worked perfect! I used it on my iPhone12 for our outdoor ceremony, and had it run on data (the WiFi from the venue did not reach the spot where we said our “I do’s”). A friend of mine videoed the entire ceremony from start to finish. The stream did not interrupt or glitch out and all of our friends from around the world (US, Canada, Japan, Europe) were able to watch!

The best part about Lovecast is that it is simply a stream that everyone watches. Your guests click the link and they can view the ceremony. There is no need to worry about anyone else accidentally sharing their screen, not muting themselves, or ensuring that those features are disabled before the stream goes live! There is no option for them to video-in or speak/interrupt. What we do like though is that they can write comments on the message board that you can read afterwards. It was fun to see our guests write about the little things that occurred during our big day.

My wife and I are so incredibly thankful for Lovecast making it possible for our guests to virtually attend our ceremony (reception has been postponed until late next year). For those couples looking to have smaller ceremonies this pandemic but want their friends and loved ones to still be present with them (virtually), Lovecast is the way to go!
