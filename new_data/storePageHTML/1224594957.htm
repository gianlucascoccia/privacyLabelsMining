Charles is Doing Fine TY , 10/05/2021

Nice short-term play

If you’re just trying to kill a few minutes here and there, this game is an ok option.

Graphics in movies are literally Lego Ghostbusters adaptation of movie scenes, which I found humorous! The game itself is simplistic in terms of both graphics and gameplay. Move left and right, up and down stairs. Ghosts appear (levitating at near ground level) from left and right. Zap them with your photon beam depletes them of energy, at which point a trap is automatically thrown for the capture. Bring traps to a containment unit to empty them for reuse. If the ghosts make contact with you, it depletes your life. After enough captures, you get upgrades, with options like beam power ups or more traps.

Overall, reading this review is more fun than the game itself, but the game is a good distraction for a couple minutes, if that’s all you’re needing.
