Description

A simple and beautiful app to show the current phase of the moon.

- See the current phase of the moon in gorgeous details.
- Swipe your finger to change the current day.
- See the next full moon, the next new moon or any date between 2011 and 2030.
- Use the almanac to browse the predictions.
- Set up reminders for full moon or new moon.
- Know exactly at what time the moon will be perfectly full.

Additionally:

- Fully localized in english, french, spanish, italian, german and russian.
- Works well with all locales and calendars.

Used a long time & love this app

This app is really perfect for me. It’s elegantly simple and intuitive, and allows me to easily see a range of future or past moon cycles for whatever date i wish quickly, and that i can scroll through. If you wish to have full and new moon alerts, the wolf howl chime is a really nice touch….and it’s one i definitely hear! I also like how the app names the moon phase and the % illumination of any particular date of interest including the current date. This is the one moon app i use regularly, as it is kept informative yet brief sticking to the basics - not astrology or anything like that.
