Description

Daily Harvest helps stock your home with clean, delicious food made from real fruits + vegetables. We do the hard work for you, meticulously selecting the best ingredients from passionate, organic farmers who freeze everything right on the farm to lock in nutrients and peak-season flavor. Our team of talented chefs and nutritionists take allll of these ingredients and craft delicious food without fillers, gums, or refined sugars. In just a few clicks, we deliver everything right to your door, ready to enjoy in minutes.

Great Food, Decent App

I use DH because it’s so easy to throw together a yummy meal. Takes less than 5 minutes to put together and easily meal prep. Makes creating a filling meal with fruits and veggies so much easier. Only need condiments and protein to spice up the meals. I just buy low sodium chicken stock for the base. For smoothies, it is absolutely customizable and provides a hearty amount great for breakfast, pre and post workouts. Wish I could review items within the app and save recipes, tips, and suggestions from other users for repeated use. Would also be a great idea if you could create lists and meal plan directly through the app, and/or export nutrition facts for macro and calorie counting. Also, would be an awesome touch if I could save different shipments on an alternating schedule so I don’t have to manually add and delete different items for variety. Otherwise, DH is doing a great job at listening to their customers tummies and providing delicious meals that are healthy and diet friendly. Keep adding and improving! So much potential!

Welcome to the future , 03/24/2020

Setting up first time

CAUTION! if you select a plan before doing the math for box price, like I did, once you build a box, set up your account, and get to check out, it is IMPOSSIBLE to change your plan to one less expensive. Even chat help couldn’t come up with a way. Even closing the site, going back in and trying to start over only popped me back to the same checkout page with the same plan originally chosen. Even trying to skip the log in didn’t work. All roads led to that same final checkout page.

SO BEWARE, pick your plan ONLY AFTER CALCULATING THE BOX COST BY MULTIPLYING THE NUMBER OF ITEMS IN THE PLAN BY THE PRICE PER ITEM. For me, it was a surprise how expensive it was for the “most popular” box, 12 items weekly. Before agreeing to pay, I tried to back out and rechoose a different smaller box (or monthly instead of weekly). Anything to get the bill down to a smaller dollar amount.

COULD NOT CHANGE TO A DIFFERENT PLAN NO MATTER WHAT I TRIED. So I haven’t eaten anything, or experienced any deliveries, but I already see a SERIOUS FLAW in the web site. Suggestion to the company: either show the TOTAL BOX PRICE in addition to the per item price ON THE SAME EXACT PAGE, or create a user friendly path to back out of a final checkout page back to a place to change the specific plan now wanted (other than the one first chosen.) I think there is a way to change specific items chosen, but not a way to change the overall plan wanted.
