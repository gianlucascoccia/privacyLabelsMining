Description

Always stay a step ahead of the weather with the 10 Day Weather app. Plan ahead for the following weekend, that party, sports competition, outing etc.
The stunning HD weather scenes also make it ideal for you office desk, bedside table, or living room shelf.

Choose from a variety of HD animations to match the weather conditions in your area, your mood or show random scenes. Beautiful ocean views, calming rain scenes, fog fly-bys, magical thunderclouds and many others bring the weather to life to your screen.

Weather Features:
+ Detailed 10 day forecast accessible with a single tap
+ Hourly forecast for 3 days
+ Sleek screen displaying current weather conditions with matching real animated weather backgrounds
+ Swipe screen to select between your favourite cities
+ Both imperial and metric units supported (Fº/mph or Cº/kmh)
+ Automatic location detections or add your favourite locations with a simple search

Additional features:
+ HD Animated weather background scenes matching current conditions, today’s forecast or random weather scenes
+ Sleep timer
+ Shake to turn on Flashlight
+ On main screen weather display can be hidden so only the relaxing animated scene is visible

Urchin Tender , 01/29/2021

Effectiveness of 10 day outlook.

In the North East it is difficult to determine a 10 day forecast. The Atlantic ocean is itself a weather maker. The lands in the warmer months heat up determining weather. The winter months are a completely different set of circumstances. Predominantly North West air flows clocking through the compass card in anticipation of moisture/precipitation. Summer time weather is dominated by a wind direction from the South West. An accurate 10 day forecast is possible but it’s more of a gimmick. I like the app though, it gives enough of an outlook with wind direction noted so that the viewer can figure out what the planet has been doing for centuries.
