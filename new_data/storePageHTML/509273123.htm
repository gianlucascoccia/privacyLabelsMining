Description

Do more for your retirement anytime, anywhere.

With this app, you get on-the-go access to your Transamerica retirement account, so you can do more at your convenience.

Manage your account: Access your account balance, investment performance, and make changes to how much you save and how you invest for retirement. Please note: Some services and features may not be available on certain plans.

Get OnTrack®: Use our powerful OnTrack® tool to learn how increasing your savings rate, changing your investment strategy, or updating your retirement goals might brighten your retirement.

View your account's transaction history, outstanding loan information, and more.


Have a Transamerica retirement account? Log in using your username and password to manage your account anytime, anywhere.

*For Terms and Conditions, visit transamerica.com/individual/terms-of-use/.

TransAmerica’s whole interface is garbage

They provide zero useful information, either on the app or on their online portal. If I wasn’t locked into their 401k retirement plan by my employer, I’d move my money out in a heartbeat. The lack of transparency by this company says to me: “Thanks for your money sucker!”

If I’m going to be giving you thousands of my dollars, I want to know the details of what you’re doing with it. But no, all they show is my account balance and a supposed overall rate of return (which is never right, on any site I might add). However on other sites, I can view my transaction history to see how much I’ve contributed, how much they’ve charged in fees, and what returns I’ve gotten on my investments. This information is nowhere to be found with TransAmerica.

Additionally, they don’t even have the courtesy to narrow the investments list to just the assets I own. So I see the entire list of their offerings with zeroes in 80% of the columns. How annoying is that? And since they don’t even do that, they definitely can’t be bothered to show me a breakdown of my own cost basis and gains for each asset. THIS IS BASIC INFORMATION that every investor should have access to in order to manage their investments.

TransAmerica, I hope you see this and understand that the lack of information you’re providing to your customers is almost criminal. Again, if I was not forced into your 401k account by my employer, I would be gone in a heartbeat. Please, do better.
