This App Is A Must Have

I am blown away by this app! I love the sounds I can choose from (or music), the ability to mix up to 4 customizable sounds. By customizable I mean that each of the 4 sounds has its own volume bar and I get to decide which sounds are foreground and which are background. Normally I would recommend the sound list be a bit bigger, but with this app allowing me to mix up to 4 different sounds, I essentially create my own sounds from the ones I mix. It also has an alarm clock with changeable backgrounds and digit colors. I do wish there were more backgrounds to choose from, or even the ability to use my own photo. It works flawlessly on my iPhone and is very easy to navigate and use.I like the Sleep Cycle Calculation. If you use a sound soother or music to sleep, think, escape, whatever - THIS is an app worth having!
