Description

Welcome to Fish the Gulf!

This is a recreational fish identification guide for the state waters of the Gulf of Mexico. It includes links to fishing regulations for the gulf waters of Florida, Alabama, Louisiana, Mississippi, Texas including Federal waters.

The application includes the following sections;

• Saltwater Bag Limits, Rules and Regulations.
Links to the selected states fishing regulations with the ability to update the link if and when the URL changes.

• Fish identification
Artist illustrations of over 100 fish common to the Gulf of Mexico.
The fish are listed by species and detailed information about each fish is furnished including habitat and food value.

• Knots
A small but useful selection of illustrated fishing knots.

• FishyScale
This is a popup fish weight calculator.
Select the type of fish and then dial the length and girth measurements into the scale.
The scale will display the approximate weight of your fish.

• Measurement Instructions
A popup guide to fish and crustacean measurement regulations.

• Locations
A popup page with predefind locations such as boat ramps and bait and tackle shops.
Click the sign post of predefined destinations to view a map where pins will show all the locations near you.
