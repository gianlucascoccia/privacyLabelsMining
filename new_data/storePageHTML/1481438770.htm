Description

We don’t have any tricks, but we’ve prepared some delicious treats for you! Enjoy our new story Hot and Haunted, and don’t miss out on the amazing Halloween offer!

Have you ever imagined yourself as the main character in your favorite romantic movie? Tabou Stories lets you do just that! Make choices that matter – wear beautiful outfits, navigate the drama, and fall in love. In Tabou, you make your secret dreams come true!

• Customize your avatar – you choose what you want to look like
• Wear the outfit you like best from a wide variety of choices
• Develop relationships with other characters in the story – will you be friends, enemies or lovers?
• Make choices that will affect the ending of the story – you are the master of your destiny!

Flirt with celebrities, become famous, and find your perfect match in Matchmaker! In an unexpected turn of events, you've ended up on a famous reality show. With contestants including a bad-boy singer, a famous Hollywood actor, and your model ex, things are about to get interesting! Are you going to be a flirt that wraps everyone around her finger to win the show, or are you looking for true love? Are you a good girl or a scandal queen? YOU choose what you want to do!

In Billionaire's Darling, you are a normal college girl until you meet billionaire Henry Lockwood. You get an offer to be his fake girlfriend… an offer that sounds too good to be true. Henry is a complicated man and his family is full of secrets… Can you find out the truth? Are his feelings for you real, or is it all just for show? Take part in high society, wear expensive outfits, experience thrilling moments and uncover mysteries, all in one story!

Your new boss is arrogant, but incredibly handsome and charming. How long until the sparks start flying? Find out in Office Affair! Can you keep things professional? Are you going to focus on your law career, or on your gorgeous boss? Or maybe it's your kind-hearted client who caught your eye instead? It's your choice! Be a lawyer superstar, fight for justice, help people, and maybe even find your happy ending!

And that’s just the beginning! We are constantly working to bring you new and exciting stories. Whichever story you choose, hours of fun are guaranteed! Download Tabou Stories now, and start living your secret life!

Musicnerd2002 , 08/02/2020

It’s a great app! But unfair

I haven’t had this app for long, but I’ve quickly come to enjoy the stories and the plots. It’s so easy to get invested into this game. I badly want to give it five stars, but that would just be false advertising.
The deal with the diamonds has been a problem I’ve noticed many people are having, including me. It’s difficult to get diamonds yet the choices cost so many of them. I don’t have the money to pay for more diamonds every time I run short, which seems to be frequently. I’m sure there are plenty of people who think the same way. It’s hard to enjoy the game when you’re rarely able to afford the premium options.
The challenges to gain the diamonds are contradictory. One of the challenges suggest you choose three premium outfits to get three diamonds. Well how could someone possibly choose three premium outfits if they can barely get enough diamonds for one? I’m expected to choose 5-25 diamond outfits so I win three diamonds. It doesn’t make sense.
Also, having to wait 2-3 hours for each key isn’t really ideal. This isn’t as much of an issue though, as long as the person playing the game has a lot of patience.
Overall, the app is very good. It definitely stands out compared to other interactive story apps. If adjustments can be made to the diamond issue, then I would love to give it five stars.
