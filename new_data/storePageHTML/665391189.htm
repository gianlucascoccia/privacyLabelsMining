Solobo_ , 02/05/2021

Update Headphones to Fix Bluetooth Issues

There’s a lot of negative reviews for this app and most of them mention connectivity issues. Different people suggest different solutions to the problem but what worked for me was updating the headphones with a computer. At first I was having the same problem where the headphones connected via Bluetooth but the app wasn’t able to recognize it. One of the first things the guide says is to make sure the device is running on the most recent software so I decided to check if there was an update available by connecting it to my computer and what do you know, there was an update. After updating it I tried connecting it to the app again and it worked instantly. This might be the problem some of you reading this have so I hope this helps. I don’t think this app deserves the low rating it has since it’s simple and straightforward. There’s really nothing bad about it. Hopefully the connectivity issues go down and the rating goes up in the future.
