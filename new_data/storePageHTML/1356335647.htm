Description

Answers and care made simple

The Horizon Blue app offers easy access to your insurance information, including benefits, coverage details and status of your claims. On-call Horizon BCBSNJ support is always at your service to answer your questions or concerns.

You can also chat with a nurse or see a doctor virtually directly on your mobile device.

The Horizon Blue app is free and lets you:
• Access the details of your plan
• Speak with a nurse or doctor
• View and print ID Cards
• Locate doctors and hospitals
• Easily track your costs
• Get quick claim status updates

ari7935 , 01/27/2021

Unable to upload docs/pictures

The app is super convenient and great to use. I think the nurse chats and telehealth video conferences with the docs are awesome features (especially now with COVID). I would give the app 5 stars but I believe there is a glitch on the iPhone app that doesn’t allow you to upload your pictures/documents before conferencing with a doctor. Yesterday, I desperately needed to talk to a doctor but could not proceed with anyone because the app did not let me do all the steps when filling out my information so I could not upload necessary documentation. After the first initial step where you fill out your medical history, it does not lead you any further to billing, etc. After inputting your name, allergies, and any medication you may be on, the app takes you directly to the waiting room to see the doctor. I tried deleting and reinstalling the app, updating it, and restarting it but still I could not get to the proper page I needed to be. When compared to the computer version, the laptop version of the website is much simpler and easier to use. On the laptop, I was able to fill out much more information, upload my documents, and confirm my billing. Please look into this issue because this really is a great app.

Developer Response ,

Thanks for taking the time to detail your feedback. We do agree with your suggestion and are looking into adding some of the functionality available through the member portal to the mobile app.
