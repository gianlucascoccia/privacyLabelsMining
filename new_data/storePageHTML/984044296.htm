Description

Live better, live Rappi

Discover an app created for you, available 24/7 for whatever you need.

When we say that we want to make your life easier, we mean it. That's why you can send anything from anywhere in the world with your SuperApp, to more than 9 countries, everything you want to your family and friends.
So if you need to go to the supermarket, a restaurant, or the pharmacy: We’ll do it for you.

With Rappi you can order:

Restaurants
Always choose the best variety of food, from pizza to international dishes, we will deliver everything you want in minutes.

Supermarkets
Great for ordering your monthly groceries and having the help of an expert who chooses the products just like you would. We also deliver it to you by car so that you receive it in perfect condition.

Last-minute shopping: Express
If you need an ingredient or have a special craving, order it through Express without lines and receive everything in 35 minutes or less.

Pharmacy
Stay healthy while taking care of your family no matter what time it is, we deliver your personal care products 24/7.

RappiBank & RappiCard

We launched RappiCard, a credit card wit no annual cost, offering many benefits; cashback, high security, 24/7 support, discounts and promotions. Ask for it in 5 minutes from the App! (available only in Mexico, Colombia, Peru and Brasil)

Online Stores: RappiMall
The perfect place for people like you who love to shop at their favorite stores and malls from anywhere and have it delivered to your door.

Courier Service
When you need to send the keys to a friend or even a birthday gift, anything you need to send or recieve, we help you. If you run out of cash, we will also deliver it to where you need it.

Travel: RappiTravel
We are all taking care of ourselves right now, but we know that we will travel again, that is why we are here to help you, even when it comes to planning your next trip.

Download it now and live better with everything that Rappi can do for you.

uislas1 , 04/23/2020

Lifesaver

I was really hesitant to use rappi at first but a friend insisted. Once I started I am hooked. I am very busy and rappi helps with everything! From going to the market for food, bringing breakfast, coffee and lunch to work, to picking up my dry cleaners, buying meds in the pharmacy, even buying a baby shower gift on one occasion, bringing cash from the ATM, picking up my contact lenses and paying for them, and now I can donate food packages during the pandemic. Yes, fruit is not ideal for them to bring, but when you have no time one or two super ripe bananas doesn’t affect me as much as having no food in the house does. You can ask them to bring food from any restaurant you want and not have to rely if the company works with them or not. Many Hunan and Shake Shack purchases made like this. I highly recommend. Of all the times I have used it, about 5-6 times have I received the order slightly off- a pepperoni less, an order askew, no knives and forks, etc but rappi has always helped in refunding those times.

Yezhez , 05/15/2021

Needs lots of improvement

I have been using this app for almos 6 months now on a daily basis. I have used it as much as 5 times a day. I use it for the convenience yet needs much improvement when it comes to “communication.” Support is fast and good if you pay for the membership, and they have so far taken care of any issues. The problem is that 1 in 3 orders is wrong or missing something. Now keep in mind I use it every day 3 times a day or more, this means that one of my orders will be an “issue” everyday. It would be okay if it was once every other week or so. The other issue so to say is, all my orders are usually from the same places and the same order. Once there are 3 strikes I choose another place but the problem persists and it is very time consuming having to contact support and complain all the time. I do not like people that complain so much and now I am that person. I do think service can improve as far as always getting orders wrong or with missing items. Who is responsable to check an order prior to being sent/delivered, the driver or the restaurant? Neither will take the “blame” or responsibility.
