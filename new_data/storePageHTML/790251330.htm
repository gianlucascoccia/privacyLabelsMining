Description

Beautiful sayings of our beloved Prophet Muhammad (S.A.W). Access thousands of Hadith from a collection of four Authentic Hadith Books:

* Sahih Bukhari
* Sahih Muslim
* Sunan Abi Daawud
* Al Muwatta

Sahih Bukhari
Generally regarded as the most authentic collection of Ahadith, It covers all aspects of life. Iman Bukhari took 16 years compiling the hadeeth.

Sahih Muslim,
Muslim ibn al-Hajjaj (204 AH to 261 AH) traveled widely and gathered 300,000 hadith of which approximately 4,000 were extracted for inclusion into his collection based on stringent acceptance criteria

Sunan Abi Daawud
Abu Dawuud collected 500,000 hadith, but included only few thousand in this collection. It took him 20 years for collecting the hadith.

Al Muwatta
Al Muwatta is a complete record of the legal and social parameters of the original Muslim community. Imam, Malik ibn Anas compiled the 'Muwatta' over a period of forty years, he selected only about 1% of authentic Ahadith for inclusion into the Muwatta, from the corpus of 100,000 narrations available to him.
