Description

A shopping list that improves the quality of your grocery shopping by making it easier, faster, and most importantly smarter. It is all you would want out of a grocery list and more.

To make sure that Listonic saves you time and money, we focused on speed, simplicity and a user-friendly, practical interface.

Why use Listonic - The Smart Shopping List

- Easy to use - about 70% of products on your shopping lists are recurring items. That’s why Listonic now suggests your favorite products first. You’re also able to add quantities with one simple tap.
- Your shopping lists are always with you - on your phone or on the web. You can access Listonic through your web browser at http://listonic.com, then create or edit a grocery list on your computer and have it ready to go on your mobile!
- Sharing lists with others - it makes shopping with family and friends much easier. Your lists are synced, so any changes are instantaneously seen by others.
- Smart item prompter - suggest the most relevant products as you type, understands your actual shopping history and that generally 70% of the products you buy are recurring purchases
- Smart sorting - the products on your lists can be sorted automatically according to store aisles to make your shopping easier and faster.
- Control your spending - add prices to the items on your shopping list and Listonic automatically calculates your total.

People all over the world are already enjoying the convenience of Listonic – with over 8 000 000 products added to lists each month.

If you see anything we can improve, please, let us know! New reviews and support emails are really helpful to make our app better and smarter! Contact us via Twitter (@listonic) or at support@listonic.com (but please mind that our support team speaks only in English :))
